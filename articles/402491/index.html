Programming the human nervous system. Reciprocal reflex reflex

The main muscle, which is pumped in fitness clubs and gyms, is the rectus abdominis muscle (the so-called ‚Äúcubes‚Äù, or ‚Äúchocolate bar‚Äù). And no matter how much swing this muscle, it is still flabby in most people. Why it happens?

There are reflexes of inhibition of antagonist muscles. I will show with the example of the arm: if you bend the arm at the elbow, the biceps work, the opposite antagonist triceps must relax, otherwise you will not bend the arm.

In the spinal cord, everything happens unequivocally - if you activate the stretch reflex (read the article on Hicktimes about it) - the impulse is transmitted to the intercalary inhibitory neurons, they inhibit the alpha motoneurons that control the antagonist muscle fibers, and the antagonist relaxes, allowing the muscle to agonist to contract under the action of the stretch reflex.

At the same time, higher motion control centers and higher muscle tone regulation centers operate in a similar mode, only the antagonist muscle relaxation relaxation with contraction of the agonist is not always performed.

When overstressed, the lower back muscles relax the abdominal muscles. In our society, most people are slightly stooped, and one of the compensatory patterns is to bend over in the lower back to see something in front of you and not look at the floor. Add joy and high heels for women. The high heel unfolds the pelvis, naturally, there is a deflection in the lower back, tension in the lower back muscles.

How long do you think you can pump a press a day? Probably ten to twenty minutes. Even if you are a fan of fitness, then more than an hour is unlikely you will be zealous. Now calculate how much time your lower back muscles are strained with your usual stooped posture - usually all day and often at night also do not relax to normal.

Reflexes of antagonist muscle inhibition at tension of the lower back muscles will constantly lead to "flabbiness" of the abdominal muscles. As soon as the tone of the lower back muscles is normalized, the tone of the abdominal muscles is normalized.

Thus, it is possible to tighten the abdominal muscles, without resorting to rocking the press. In fact, active people who walk a lot, without any problems, climb and descend the stairs, bend over, rise, and the abdominal muscles are strong enough. Such people, with normal muscle tone of the back, will not have the problem of "flabby stomach".
