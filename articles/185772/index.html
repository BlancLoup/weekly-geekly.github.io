Technology companies recognize sarcasm

The French company Spotter has developed a tool that, according to them, is able to identify sarcasm in comments on the Internet.

The created software platform scans social media and other Internet sources to create reports on the reputation of its customers - among which are the European Commission, Air France and other large customers. Like most of this software, the application is engaged in the analysis of semantics, linguistics and heuristics.
