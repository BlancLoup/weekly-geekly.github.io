Interactive Git Branching Tutor

A certain Peter Cottle ( Peter Cottle ) made an interactive tutorial on the basics of branching in Git. There are several simple learning levels where you need to make a couple of commits, and then merge or rebase, there are also difficult levels to think about. You can also save levels and share them with friends.

Although the author himself claims that the application is still raw, I advise anyone who is interested in git to take a look at this cool thing .



Supported commands:
  • commit
  • branch
  • checkout
  • cherry-pick
  • reset
  • revert
  • rebase
  • merge
