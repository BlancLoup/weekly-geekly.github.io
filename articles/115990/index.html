Samsung Galaxy Tab with Android 3.0 - thinner iPad 2, and the price is the same

Today, at CTIA, Samsung presented 2 versions of their tablet PCs running Android 3.0 - 10.1 and 8.9 inches.
The devices turned out very tasty.

Price




As can be seen from the illustration - the price of 10.1 is similar to that of the iPad 2, and at 8.9 it is even slightly below $ 469 and $ 569 for 16GB and 32GB, respectively.

Common to both versions


* 1 Ghz dual core, but not clear NVidia Tegra or Samsung processor
* WXGA TFT LCD display (1280 x 800)
* Pls display, it is almost like IPS
* microSD slot (up to 32Gb)
* Bluetooth 3.0
* Gyroscope, accelerometer, compass, light sensor
* SIM slot
* 4G version supports HSDPA (21 Mbps), and in the future they promise versions with LTE and WiMax
* WiFi a / b / g / n
* rear camera - 3 megapixel with LED backlight
* front camera - 2 megapixel
* 720p video recording
* 3.5mm headphone out
* stereo speakers
* Hardware-based encryption of internal and external memory
* 1080p playback
* Adobe Flash 10.2
* TouchWiz UX (4.0)


Terms of appearance in the US market

10.1 - June 8
8.9 - the beginning of summer





Differences


Version 10.1
8.6mm thickness (8.8mm for iPad 2)
weight - 595 grams (for iPad 2 - 589 grams)
battery - 6800mAh according to the presenters provides 10 hours of video


Version 8.9
thickness is also 8.6mm
weight - 467 grams.
battery - 6000mAh


TouchWiz UX


They said that they reworked it under the tablet form factor.

The main chips:

* full customization - widgets, etc.



* tray with mini applications (ala doc on iPhone)



* integrated communication through a social hub



* reading books and bookstore
