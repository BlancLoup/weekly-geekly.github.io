We invite everyone to the first hackathon in the history of the Stavropol Territory



Good afternoon friends! Congratulations to all with Maslenitsa!

We want to invite all interested Habr's visitors to <hackathon_weekend /> - the first hackathon in the history of the Stavropol Territory! Theme: " Development of a mobile or web application ." Hackathon will be held from March 18 to March 20, 2016 in the building of NCFU in the city ‚Äã‚Äãof Stavropol at the address: ul. Pushkin 1.

You can develop on any existing mobile or web platform, but you must be ready to submit a mobile device (or devices) with a prototype to the judges for a short time evaluation, the application itself, as well as to submit source texts and materials. A narrower topic of the competition will be announced on the day the hackathon begins.

All the necessary development tools need to bring with them, from us - space, electricity, food and the Internet. In other matters, backup sources of Internet and electricity are also better to take with you.

Invited to participate teams from 3 to 5 people, lone programmers who want to create a team on the spot, as well as students of master classes. The master classes will be held on the 19th at the same time as the hackathon, so it will only be possible to participate in one thing.

For participants of the hackathon from other cities, 15 paid places in the hotel are provided on a competitive basis. Preference is given to guests from the most remote regions.

The event is non-commercial, admission is free by prior registration . The organization of this event involved people who are directly involved in the development of ReactOS, but the matter is not limited to them. We have partners and sponsors, and partners and sponsors, in turn, have prizes for the winners and participants.

Also, sponsors and partners are never enough, so the organizing committee of the event is open for cooperation.
