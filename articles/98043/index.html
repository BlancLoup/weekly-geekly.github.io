"Feedback" is a convenient way to report inaccuracies in the data of DoubleGIS

Every day, millions of users of DoubleGIS throughout the country are looking for organizations, addresses or driving directions. They periodically encounter inaccuracies in the data and wish to inform us of this misunderstanding. However, until now, this could be done either by telephone, or by e-mail (which still needs to be found), or by some other not very obvious way.

However, yesterday we released an update with the so-called "Feedback", with which you can not only add a new organization to the directory, but also report errors in the directory, on the map or transport routes.

It looks like this:


Or, for example, from the context menu by right-clicking on the map:


Yesterday, the update became available immediately in all our cities, except Magnitogorsk, Nizhny Novgorod and Rostov-on-Don. These cities will add from August 1.

During the first day, we received several hundred reports of new organizations or inaccuracies in data. All messages are sent directly to our specialists reference and map production. They check the information and make changes to one of the next updates of DoubleGIS.

Please inform us about the defects found in any quantities. We love and appreciate such information very much, because it helps us to make DoubleGIS better, and you - to rarely encounter inaccurate information.

PS By the way, already this month DoubleGIS will appear in two more cities: in Naberezhnye Chelny and Yaroslavl .
