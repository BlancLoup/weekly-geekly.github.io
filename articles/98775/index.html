RetroN 3 is a versatile gaming console for nostalgic gamers.



I do not know about you, but I still sadly recall Rock'n'Roll Racing on the Sega Genesis and Phantasy Star 2 on it. In addition, at the very beginning of my gaming "career" I really liked the game Dizzy on Dendy. By the way, a couple of cartridges with the aforementioned games remained. That's the trouble - there is no console. Of course, I know about emulators of various consoles for PCs and communicators, I myself went through Dune 2 again and on an emulator for a PC, and even on my HTC Mogul. And even the joysticks from Sony and Sega were soldered to the LPT port of their old computer. But all this is somehow wrong, I just want the original console. In general, for all nostalgic gamers, the golden time has come - the game console RetroN 3 has appeared on the market, which supports almost all cartridges from your old consoles. Details - in continuation.

So, the company Hyperkin has released a new type of game console, which will allow everyone to remember childhood or adolescence. RetroN 3 supports all old cartridges from Nintendo, Super Nintendo and Sega Genesis video consoles. It also supports game controllers for these consoles, it has all the necessary inputs.



RetroN 3 comes in two colors - red and black. Interestingly, with the console go two controllers (wireless), which are exactly the same design of controllers from Sega.

And you know how much this fun is worth? Nothing at all - 70 "evergreens" and your console. And yes, of course, this is not very practical, no one argues that RetroN 3 cannot be replaced by one of the dozens of cool emulators, or simply forget about the old games. This console is exclusively for those who are nostalgic for the games that served as forerunners for the current industry. This console is for those who remember "how the mountains were high, and the trees - low," as they say in an old Georgian joke.
