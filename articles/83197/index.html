The results of the ACM ICPC 2010 World Championship finals

The results of the XXXIV World Programming Championship finals are announced in Harbin.

The Shanghai JTU team won gold medals and became the 2010 World Programming Champion!
The Moscow State University team took 2nd place and received gold medals.
The National Taiwan U team took the 3rd place and received gold medals.
The Kiev NU team took the 4th place and received gold medals.
The team of Petrozavodsk State University took the 5th place and received silver medals.
The U Tsinghua team took the 6th place and received silver medals.
The team of Saratov State University took the 7th place and received silver medals.
The Warsaw U team took the 8th place and received silver medals.
The SPbSU team took the 9th place and received silver medals.
The Zhongshan U team took 10th place and received bronze medals.
The Fudan U team took 11th place and received bronze medals.
The KTH team took 12th place and received bronze medals.
The team of the Ural State University took the 13th place and received bronze medals.

The team of British U in Egypt became the champion of Africa and the Middle East.
Shanghai JTU team became the champion of Asia.
The Moscow SU team became the champion of Europe.
The UF Pernambuco team became the champion of South America.
Team Stanford U became the champion of North America.
The U of Western Australia team became the champion of Oceania.
