How to make a small contest in one day

Not so long ago, I caught up with a global event - a wedding. For me, frank introvert, such an event is akin to Armageddon. If I were a spherical horse in a vacuum, I would have arranged some small local party. But it turned out 80 of the most diverse guests. And here arises the main question. Like all this assorted crowd to entertain. I am fundamentally opposed to forcing someone to something. And if you look for ‚Äúcontests for a wedding,‚Äù then it becomes scary to see this at your wedding. As a result, we had to invent our contests with the presenter, taking only a few known and simple ones. About one competition which is related to Habr's subject I will also tell. I have not seen similar contests for a wedding. Maybe someone will help. I think that it can be used not only at a wedding. Sources are attached.

Idea


We had the idea to make a game that would take place in the backstage of the holiday, without announcing the lead. So that people accidentally bump into him. Any quest. But the quest is "social". To interact with people. For example, go and get acquainted with an arbitrary person. Try to find the answer to a strange question. How to find the quest? Hanging QR codes or text links. It looked something like this:



Considering that almost everyone has a mobile phone with an apple / android - to participate is not a problem.

Is there something ready?


I could not find. Maybe blind. There is a paid platform for conducting city quests that would be convenient for a wedding quest. But they need an operator to count the answers. There are many paid services for "carrying out quests in the corporate party." But why should they, when my entire quest should have 4-5 questions and should not attract undue attention?

Filling


The questions were of several categories:
1) Learn the name of the person depicted in the picture. The simplest and, in my opinion, the most interesting part of the competition. The question was asked twice. The first time one of the groom's friends was generated, the second time - the bride.
2) Find the answer to the text question. The implication was that the people should ask others. But the people are mostly googled.
3) To do some kind of action for which you need to attract another person. In this category, we had only 1 question.
