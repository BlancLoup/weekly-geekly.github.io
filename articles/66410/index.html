VISA Virtual from QIWI. Soon!

In blogs and on news resources, including on the habr , already passed the information that QIWI is testing a virtual VISA card.

The key word here is testing, since the product is currently available on a limited number of QIWI payment terminals and on the website of the payment system QIWI Personal Cabinet .

A full-scale launch is planned for the end of August of this year. And that will be .

At first, I think it should be said about how much it will cost, since this is the issue that concerns users most of all. At the moment, the interest rate, frankly, is not quite attractive and amounts to as much as 11%. By the way, even with these percentages, for example, it is more profitable to pay for access to the same WOW game through the QIWI virtual visa than to purchase payment cards. More profitable by as much as 10%! The same can be said about Skype vouchers, because when paying by card, VAT disappears.

In late August, it will become even more profitable, since the interest rate will drop to 5% .

Now about the other parameters of the map.

The minimum amount you can put on the card is 100 rubles , the maximum is 15,000 rubles .

You can purchase a card in the QIWI payment terminal (QIWI) or on the website mylk.qiwi.ru. When purchasing, specify a valid number of your mobile phone. An SMS message with the card number, expiration date and secret code will be sent to this number. Record this data and keep it in a safe place.

What can be purchased using virtual VISA? All global e-commerce is at your service!

At your service are PayPal, air tickets, hotel reservations, the best western online games, shopping on Ebay, Amazon, the most popular poker rooms and much more. Virtual QISA from QIWI will be accepted there without question, unlike some real cards issued by Russian banks.

Another important aspect is that QIWI's virtual VISA is much easier to get than a card in any bank. There are no queues, the payment method is usual and does not cause questions; There is a fee for replenishment, but there is no fee for annual maintenance.

It is important to use the card safely . You can put as much money on the card as you plan to spend and you will not have to worry that your data will be leaked and scammers will take advantage of them. Let me remind you that last year alone in the world more than 4 billion dollars were stolen from bank cards all over the world!
