LifeTrak Zone C410: smart fitness tracker photo review



Smart watches and fitness trackers are becoming more sophisticated, helping to monitor their own activity and body condition. Probably, after a while, such gadgets will be able to contact the attending physician on their own, reporting any problems if they are detected. All this goes.

Nevertheless, now the functions of sports gadgets are very diverse. And a bright representative of the family of fitness devices is a hybrid of a fitness bracelet and a smart watch LifeTrak Zone C410. Looking ahead, you should immediately say that this model has a function for tracking the quality of sleep - so if you have problems with sleep, you definitely should try a gadget (if not this one, then another, with a similar function).

Learn more about the features of the Zone C410 - in the sequel. In the same place we will carry out and anboxing of the device. Soon we will also post a full analysis of one of the models of fitness trackers with electronic commentary.

LifeTrak Zone C410 Features



  • Display: digital, monochrome, there is a backlight;
  • Protection: can be immersed under water to a depth of 30 m, shockproof, FCC, CE, ROHS standards;
  • Battery: Round flat replaceable battery CR2032;
  • Compatibility: iOs 7 and higher: iPhone 4S, 5, 5C, 5S; iPad 3, 4; iPad Air; iPad Mini, Retina; iPod Touch 5; Samsung Galaxy 4, Galaxy 5 and Note III, LG Nexus 5, Motorola Moto G and HTC One M8;
  • Chronograph range: 9 h., 59 min., 59 sec.
  • Range of steps / goals: 0 - 99,999 steps
  • Calories / Goal Range: 0 - 99.999
  • Distance / target range: 621.3 miles / 999.9 km
  • Sleep: sleep / target range: 1 h. 00 min. - 14 h. 50 min.
  • Memory: 7-day memory
  • HR: 30 - 240 beats per minute
