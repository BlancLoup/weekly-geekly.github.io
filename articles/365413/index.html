Smart watch premium: what offers are on the market

Luxury brands for some reason entered the market of smart watches, and offer a limited number of functions for an unlimited amount of money. In today's collection of the most vivid instances from Frederique Constant to Bulgari.



Montblanc - electronic strap for mechanical watches

The strap design was created for the Timewalker collection and will be synchronized with IOS or Android devices via Bluetooth.



The touchscreen is located on the wrist and is a monochrome dial with a size of 0, 9 inches, which allows you to receive notifications about calls, sms, meetings and events, and due to the built-in pedometer, the user will receive basic "fitness functions".



The cost of models in this collection is from $ 3,500

Frederique Constant - Analog Dial Smart Watch



MotionX watches are synchronized with a mobile IOS or Android device to display data on physical activity and sleep phases. In addition, the information will be available on a special dial, set in the "six".



  • Dial: 42 mm
  • Materials: Steel, rose gold
  • Glass: Convex, sapphire
  • Battery life: 2 years +
  • Water Resistant: 5 ATM


The collection is divided into male and female with similar functions.

Cost: 1295 Swiss francs

Breitling B55 Connected - between classic and smart

Watches with analog and digital displays are very limited in their capabilities. In the arsenal of "skills": two reminders a day, approach and correction of time and date, automatic highlighting of the dial when turning to 35 degrees.



  • Contact with the phone via Bluetooth.
  • The screen is not touch
  • No smart notifications




According to experts, the cost can be approx. $ 8,000

Modillian - universal smart buckle

Despite the fact that the cost of the fastener starts on Indiegogo from $ 10 and reaches only 1,900, you can fix it on any watch, turning it into a smart one.



Thus, those who are not in a hurry to change their Hublot to Apple Watch Gold can get a number of advantages in the form of smart notifications.
