Google custom servers.

The network has new information about Google custom servers.




Yesterday, at the Data Center Efficiency Summit conference, the company showed one of its custom servers, information about which was previously hidden. The presence of a built-in uninterruptible power supply in each server was shown. This constructive solution, according to Google, allows to achieve higher energy efficiency compared to centralized power backup systems.
