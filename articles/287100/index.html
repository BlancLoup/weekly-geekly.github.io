Worried about your blog? Make a backup!

I stumbled upon a curious program, which I especially have nothing to do, but someone may come in handy.

Her name is Blogger Backup and this all said
A few words from the developer:
Blogger Backup is a utility whose goal is to easily and quickly copy all or some of the posts from your Blogger account.
The work uses the C # library named GData; Saving goes in reverse chronological order, and when the program ends, you receive a local Atom / XML file.

From myself: the last line does not tell me anything, and I do not think that Blogger is so popular in Russia. There were two goals for this publication; Firstly, the program can be useful to someone, and secondly, it can encourage someone to write a more flexible analogue, for example, through RSS with any blog engine.
