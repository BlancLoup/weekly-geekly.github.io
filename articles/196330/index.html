Definitions

Near space - Earth, near-earth orbit, orbit of the moon, inclusive.
Middle space - Venus, Mars, asteroid belt.
Deep Space - The entire solar system
Inaccessible space - star systems.

This contradiction (near vs deep space) must be solved in the administrative space: the near space is for state corporations and service structures, the far space is for national states and humanity as a whole.
