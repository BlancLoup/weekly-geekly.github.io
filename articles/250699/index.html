The magazine "In Oblaka.RF" looking for talented authors who are well-guided in the clouds

At the end of 2014, the publishing house "Media Grus" launched a new project with a talking title - " In Oblake.RF ".

After the release of the first issue of the journal In Oblake.RF, we received a lot of reader feedback. Let's say right away: most of them are negative, and one cannot but agree with them. The first cloud came out lumpy, but I would very much like it to be a coma named after Thomas Edison, who claimed that a negative result is also a result. We are not going to follow the example of a patient American who has succeeded after thousands of unsuccessful experiments. We have taken into account all the constructive criticism and will soon be ready to bring to your attention a new issue of our magazine In the Cloud. RF, which will become your navigator in the cloud sphere.

"In the Cloud. RF" is intended for a wide audience, because today the situation is as follows: almost everyone uses the clouds, but not everyone does it meaningfully.

In the process of rethinking the concept of the magazine, it became obvious: the popularization of the topic of cloud computing implies the opportunity to speak on the pages of the magazine not only to experts and specialists, producers and providers of cloud services, but also active consumers of cloud products.

In this regard, we are looking for talented authors experienced in the cloud theme, and we announce a competition for the best material about clouds.

The competition is held in two categories:


1. The best cloud publicist



Do you have something to say about:
  • technologies for building cloud solutions
  • economic aspects of the use of clouds,
  • security (or danger?) of work in the cloud,
  • legal nuances of the purchase and use of cloud services,
  • latest trends in the industry, news, achievements of problems and ways to solve them, or any other phenomenon of the cloud sphere.


In this case, you present the information in an accessible and fascinating form, which means that you have every chance to win the title of "The Best Cloud Publicist."

2. The best cloud futurist


You can not live without cloud technology? Do you have a rich imagination? Can you predict (or know exactly?) How cloud technologies will be used in the foreseeable or distant future? Then you will definitely become the winner in the nomination "The best cloud futurist".
To participate in the competition in this category are accepted the work of not only gifted masters of the artistic word, but also talented artists.

We are waiting for your materials at a.zhiteneva@mediagrus.ru
