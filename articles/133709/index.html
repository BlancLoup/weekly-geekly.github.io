Video review omnivorous reader PocketBook Pro 602

Recently, the market of electronic readers has increased significantly. One of the most popular players in the CIS (and not only) is the Ukrainian company PocketBook . Its main advantage is omnivorous in terms of formats, the main ones are fb2, ePUB, djvu, PDF . In today's ‚Äúreview without rules‚Äù we will talk about the 6-inch PocketBook Pro 602 reader:



The device has a neat case, presented in two colors: in a dark and light range, and good characteristics:
  • Processor : Samsung 533 MHz
  • Display : 6 inches, E-Ink, 16 shades of gray
  • Dimensions : 182 x 132.3 x 11 mm, weight: 255 g.
  • Formats : FB2, FB2.ZIP, TXT, TXT.ZIP, PDF, PDF (ADOBE DRM), DJVU, DJV, IW44, IW4, RTF, RTF.ZIP, HTML, PRC, MOBI, CHM, EPUB, EPUB (ADOBE DRM ), DOC, DOCX, TCR, JPEG, BMP, PNG, TIFF, and mp3 audio
  • Memory : 2 GB (952 MB available), microSD slot
  • Interfaces : microUSB, audio 3.5, WiFi, Bluetooth
  • Software : Widgets, browser, ABBYY dictionary, Text-to-Speech, mp3 player
  • Battery : Li-Polymer (1530 mAh), 14000 pages

The main advantages of the PocketBook Pro 602 e-mail reader include a compact form factor, a long battery life (about a month without recharging), and similarity of the image with paper carriers. However, to read in the dark you will need a special flashlight, which is sold as a separate accessory. The recommended price of PocketBook Pro 602 in Russia is 8290r.
