Scribd will scan your documents for free.

Startup Scribd, which specializes in publishing documents via flash widgets, yesterday announced the launch of the unprecedented Scribd iPaper program. In order to attract more files to their hosting, they decided to make a free scan and OCR of all the documents and books that will be sent to them by mail. This is a truly unique offer, because our prices for scanning and text recognition start at about $ 0.17 per page, and here everything is free.
