Workshop from GFI: "Solutions from GFI Software to protect corporate email"

BAKOTEK company, the official distributor of GFI Software in Ukraine, invites you to participate in a free practical workshop on GFI MailEssentials, GFI MailSecurity and GFI MailArchiver products, which will be held on March 24, 2011 in Kiev.

Seminar held for IT professionals

The event focuses on acquiring practical skills for installing, configuring and using the functionality of the following GFI Software products:
  • GFI MailEssentials - a server solution that provides a 98% spam detection rate;
  • GFI MailSecurity - reliable protection of corporate email from viruses, trojans, exploits and email attacks using several anti-virus mechanisms;
  • GFI MailArchiver is an email archiving and email management solution that allows you to store all company email in a database.




Training is conducted by: Vladislav Samoylenko, GFI Leading Product Specialist, BAKOTEK

Date: March 24 (Thursday) 2011
Venue: Softline International, Kyiv, ul. Igorevskaya, 14-A, 1st floor
Registration: 09:30 - 10:00 Workshop Time: 10:00 - 16:00
