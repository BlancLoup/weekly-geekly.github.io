DevOps Moscow meetup: Monitoring

On May 16, we invite engineers to the next meeting of the DevOps Moscow community in Avito's office. The second meeting after a long break will be devoted to monitoring. The program includes reports on both monitoring tools and incident management in general. Representatives of Avito, Okmeter.io and Badoo will speak. Register for a meeting and invite colleagues. Under the cut - theses of speeches, links to the registration and video broadcast mitap.
UPD: the places are over, join the live broadcast of the meeting on the youtube channel DevOps Moscow .
