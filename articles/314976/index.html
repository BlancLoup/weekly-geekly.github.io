Competition for ZeroNights 2016 from Mail.Ru Group



Friends, we invite everyone interested in cybersecurity to the sixth annual conference ZeroNights 2016 ! This time we invite conference guests to look for vulnerabilities in the Internet of things. Winners will receive valuable gifts. Details under the cut.

So, if you came to ZeroNights 2016, then you probably understand something about hacking and network security. We suggest you try your hand at hacking the Internet of Things sample network. The rules were developed in close contact with our DIY-community. Before the conference, we will not disclose the details so that you do not do homework. All instructions you will receive directly on the stand.

According to the results of solving the problem, two winners will be determined who will receive IoT equipment sets as a reward. Dare! Also at the conference will be held a quiz on Internet security, for the victory in which we will give the hoverboard.

And do not miss the speech of Alexander Leonov, information security analyst at Mail.Ru Group. He will make a presentation on Enterprise Vulnerability Management, along with Ekaterina Puhareva, senior information security specialist at QIWI, in the Defensive Track section.

See you at the conference! Do not forget to purchase tickets . Date: November 17-18, 2016. Location: Moscow, Prospekt Mira, 150, Cosmos Cos.
