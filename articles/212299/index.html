DigitalOcean opens data center in Singapore



A well-known hosting provider DigitalOcean today has delighted everyone with the news about the launch of a new data center in Singapore. The data center was launched in collaboration with Equinix . This is a long-awaited and excellent news story for those who want a presence in the Asia-Pacific region.

This region became the sixth in addition to the already existing ones, in which the creation of droplets is possible. Thus, at the moment the following regions are available for selection:
  1. New york 1
  2. Amsterdam 1
  3. San francisco 1
  4. New york 2
  5. Amsterdam 2
  6. Singapore 1
