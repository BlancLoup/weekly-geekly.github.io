Microsoft has banned other browsers and search engines in the integrated search for Windows 10


Virtual voice assistant Cortana with elements of artificial intelligence

Cortana (Cortana) - a virtual voice assistant with elements of artificial intelligence - can no longer send search queries to other search engines, only to Bing. It also denied the right to open third-party browsers, such as Firefox or Chrome. Now, the Edge browser will become the exclusive browser for integrated search in Windows 10, announced Microsoft.

By default, Internet search is activated in Windows 10. It can be disabled through group policies, in the registry, search settings, or firewall. Otherwise, an Internet search is offered in the Windows 10 local search window or when communicating with the virtual assistant Cortana. At the same time, search queries are sent to Bing, and when you click on the link, the Edge browser is launched.



Until now, it was possible to change the behavior of the inline search by setting another default search engine in Windows 10 and an alternative default browser. Then the search queries were sent not through the built-in search engine Windows 10, but through the search engine of another browser. When you click on the link in the integrated search, another browser was launched, for example, Firefox or Chrome.
