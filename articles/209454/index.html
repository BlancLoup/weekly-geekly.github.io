Laboratories for penetration testing "Test.lab"


PentestIT "Test.lab"


About the laboratory

PentestIT "Test.lab" penetration testing laboratories imitate the IT structure of real companies, with servers and workstations. The purpose of the laboratories is to provide an opportunity for everyone to test the skills of pentest in conditions as close as possible to real ones, while being completely legal. Tasks that are laid out in "Test.lab" are taken on the basis of completed pentest in an impersonal form and are always interconnected. For example, the found logins and passwords stored in the user's browser can be used to access the servers of the virtual company under attack. In this way, Test.lab laboratories are more realistic than they are different from regular CTF competitions.

Attack vectors and tools

In the process of developing pentest laboratories "Test.lab" various attack vectors are laid, affecting almost all areas of information security: network security, systems, and application software. Performing tasks requires not only deep technical knowledge, but also the ability to use special tools: BurpSuite, IDAPro, Metasploit, etc., as well as specific thinking.

In addition to the practice of penetration testing, participants are given the opportunity to communicate, share knowledge, and the spirit of rivalry only adds an incentive to win and be in the hall of fame .

Conclusion

We invite everyone to take part in PentestIT laboratories, which gather around them people with completely different levels of training - from beginners to professionals in the field of information security, while everyone discovers something new in the field of information security.
