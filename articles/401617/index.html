Welcome to the DIY Meetup March 4



Hello! On March 4, we invite all the makers to our next Do It Yourself Meetup, which will be held at the Moscow office of Mail.Ru Group. This time we will talk about aviation, teach the cat to use a sensor controller, find out what a sequencer is, take a ride on an electric bike - and that's not all. Register and come.
