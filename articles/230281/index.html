Bing has become the next search engine where you can request the removal of search results.

This feature is available only to residents of the EU, because is a consequence of the decision of the European Court, which decided that users have the "right to be forgotten" - right to be forgotten, this is what it is called. The resolution was made a couple of months ago and specifically related to Google, but apparently, Microsoft decided not to wait until it was their turn.



Users wishing to erase themselves from the memory of the World Wide Web are invited to fill out a detailed form , on the basis of which they will decide to remove the requested information from the search.

Let me remind you that Google decided instead of deleted search results to show a message that there was once a result that they had to delete. He plans to produce something similar in the search for Bing at the time of writing the note was not known - it may be necessary to wait for the first deleted results.
