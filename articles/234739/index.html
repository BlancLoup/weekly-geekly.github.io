InFocus M512 - a budget device for Snapdragon 400 and with support for 4G networks

Immediately catches the eye often mentioning the company Foxconn , they say they are the manufacturer of this smartphone. The desire of the latter to get out of the shadow of Xiaomi and Huawei led to the creation of a line of smartphones under the InFocus brand. Now on sale are two models, the older M512 and the younger M310 - today we talk about the first.

As for the workhorse of contributing to the consumption of content from the world wide web, calls and other devices subject to smartphone, the device looks just fine.
