The study inside the egg

You must admit that the main problem in office work is the impossibility of getting rid of the surrounding fuss. Even headphones do not always save, colleagues still flicker before my eyes. The same problems with freelancers working at home, with family.

Danish designers GamFratesi have come up with a cardinal solution for sound and visual isolation. It is a small egg-shaped cubicle that allows you to retire even in the most densely populated office.



More photos under a habrakat.



This option is also ideal for coking , because in a small room 3 x 4 meters can be placed close to a dozen offices, and people will not interfere with each other work.



For their ideas, designers from GamFratesi have already won the main prize at the local exhibition of furniture design. This is not surprising, because they de facto came up with a new type of furniture.
