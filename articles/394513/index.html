Tim Cook called the price of the iPhone overvalued



Apple CEO on Indian national television told what exactly the company is going to take to stop the drop in iPhone sales. In almost all countries of the world, the iPhone gradually reduces its market share, yielding to the position of Android phones.

Whether such a price is justified by a small gadget, the host of the NDTV channel asked a rhetorical question. After all, almost everywhere the phone from Apple is more expensive than in the US? With the American price of $ 598 in China, the phone costs $ 693 (average for 2016, according to Deutsche Bank statistics ), in Russia - $ 695, in India - $ 784, in Brazil - $ 931. And you cannot say that these countries are richer than the USA.

Tim Cook agreed that the cost of the iPhone in other countries is too high. He recommended citizens from other countries to make purchases of the iPhone in the USA, where draconian duties on import of electronics, such as in Brazil, do not apply. Brazilian national authorities impose prohibitive duties in order to encourage foreign corporations to locate production in the country, encourage import substitution and support domestic producers.
