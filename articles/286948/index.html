The top 50 fastest growing companies include 5 Russian

In Switzerland, published a list of the 50 fastest growing high-tech companies. It includes 5 Russian startups: Avito, ivi.ru Media, KupiVIP, Lamoda and Ozon .



75% of all companies from the rating were founded in the last 10 years. The average time from the moment of foundation of the company to the first stage of financing is three and a half years. On average, companies went through four stages of investment. The total number of investors on the list of 50 companies is 223. Of the 50 startups, 47 were financed by at least one US investor.

The main criteria for inclusion in the rating were the company's growth indicators from the date of its foundation to the current moment. 366 startups claimed to participate. Of these, companies were selected with revenues of more than $ 10 million per year, sales growth of 50% from 2011 to 2014, and costing more than $ 100 million. Then, from the remaining 148 applicants, the special committee selected the 50 fastest-growing and most potential start-ups.

The commission included Alexander Galitsky, Almaz Capital . Full list of companies published on the site .
