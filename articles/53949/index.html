Idea: purchase and exchange service of used subscriptions for a fitness club / gym / pool

Summer is coming soon.

Despite the IFC and BAK, some people still buy subscriptions to fitness clubs and gyms - so that by summer they look a little less fat, and a little more muscular.

As a rule, in fitness clubs, subscriptions are always available for sale, incl. for long periods - for 6, 8, or 12 months. Usually it brings great benefits when buying - up to 50% savings. However, when summer comes, people leave for resorts, summer cottages / vacations, or find themselves more enjoyable on summer evenings than going to the gym.
