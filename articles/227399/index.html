We invite you to take part in the Russian Developers Cup



Today, registration for the second Russian Developers Cup, a championship of mobile and web services developers, as well as games, has begun. You can register for participation until July 22.
This is a competition of development teams, each of which creates a project. If you do not have colleagues and friends with whom you could create a team, then you can join one of the existing ones or create your own Dream Team.

The jury is absent from the Russian Developers Cup. Totally. Winners are chosen by popular vote among the participants of the championship. And it will last a whole week, so everyone will have time to vote. The best projects cannot avoid a severe award: $ 10,000, $ 5,000, and $ 3,000 for the first three prizes, respectively. But this is not all - the winners will be invited to the Autumn Technology Forum, where they will be awarded honestly earned award medals in a solemn (moderately) setting.

This will be the second Russian Developers Cup championship. Last year's winner was the project Funky Quest , which is a service for creating and conducting games and quests on the ground. In addition to the RDC, a number of other championships are held annually for IT specialists: the Russian Code Cup, the Russian Design Cup, the Russian AI Cup. The total number of participants has already exceeded 10,000. These are Russian-speaking programmers, developers and designers from more than 34 countries of the world.

By organizing all these events, we pursue an obvious goal: to develop the IT industry in Russia. Well, and forge professional staff, which could be proud of. What can give you participation in the championship? Quite a lot: this is a great opportunity to make new useful contacts, find partners, gain new and important experience in your field, develop teamwork skills, and express yourself among professionals. Yes, and the prizes are good.
