Google launches Contributor, a crowdfunding service to support publishers.

Now advertising from your favorite site can be removed for a dollar a month.


Google has big problems with content providers (media and blogs). If the search engine shows a part of the article in the results of the search for news, then who will go to the website of the newspaper, if the main points have already been learned? Is that some particularly meticulous reader, but some visitors have already been lost. At least, publishers think so, they close their eyes to the number of people the search leads to the site.

Problems were, for example, in France. In 2012, Google wanted to make pay for access to content. In the end, the problem was solved, but it cost the search engine 60 million euros . This was not the end: the French media received a discount on advertising.

Despite such a reputation, Google is really thinking about helping publishers. Their new initiative is designed to help content authors get paid, and end users will be able to get rid of advertising.

There are two main ways to monetize websites: advertising on the page and the paywall (paywall), a paid subscription to access content. The first is annoying, the second does not give access to anyone. Google Contributor combines the merits of both ways.

The user can choose how much he wants to donate - 1, 2 or 3 dollars per month. The subscribed user will no longer see ads on the publisher‚Äôs site, to which he donated money. Speech, most likely, is only about advertising affiliate programs "Google". The money will be distributed through adsense.

Instead of ads will display a message of thanks. In some versions of the site banners will be removed altogether.

So far, not everyone can use the service. According to the Gigaom website , at the moment agreements are concluded with 10 sites, among them are the hosting of imgur.com pictures, the Urban Dictionary jargon, the wikiHow guide database, the editions of Mashable , The Onion and ScienceDaily . Those who wish to add their site must leave an email address in a special form for invitations. We are accepting applications from the USA only.

In essence, publishers sell some of the user's attention on a page with content to advertisers in the form of advertising elements. Attention traded on the auction scheme in Google AdWords. Now the user is given the opportunity to redeem his own attention back for personal use.

Of course, almost nothing will stop browser extensions that cut ads, but Google Contributor provides an alternative to such a radical way of solving the problem of annoying banners at the expense of the site owners.

Earlier , a similar project for YouTube was presented called YouTube Music Key. For 10, or rather, 9.99 dollars a month, you have access to music and 30 million tracks on Google Play Music. The first month is cheaper - $ 7.99. For the period of subscription to YouTube, advertising is disabled.

In September of this year, YouTube Funding crowdfunding tool was launched for some countries.
