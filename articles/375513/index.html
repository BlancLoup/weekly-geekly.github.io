Welcome to XinCheJian - or Shanghai Hackspace



We continue our series of reviews of the Middle Kingdom.

We have already talked about the Hackspace of Shenzhen ( TechSpace , Chaihuo Make Space ) and the Hackspace of Beijing . They also talked about hackspace in Hong Kong .

And it was the turn of Shanghai - the hackspace Xin Che Jian (by the way, the first hakspace of China, opened in 2010).

Some facts about Shanghai, this is a city where:
- the longest metro in the world
- Shanghai port, including the deep-water port, is the largest in the world
- there is a monument to Pushkin (the only one in China),
- trains from the airport go at a speed of 430 km / h (breaking 30 km in 7 minutes),
