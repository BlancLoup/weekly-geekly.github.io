2. Familiarize yourself with system requirements .

Windows Insider


Our colleagues in the OS development team thought a lot and finally came up with this program.

By registering with the Windows Insider program, you‚Äôll receive the latest builds and be the first to learn about new features and products.

By joining the program, you will have the opportunity to take an active part in the development of the operating system - to give feedback on the product, its capabilities and send us your wishes and comments. There is no guarantee that everything that you see or not see in the preliminary version will be part of the final release or vice versa will not be implemented. But with your feedback, you can influence this process and help make Windows 10 even better, more useful and more convenient for you.

The Windows Feedback application is built into the OS distribution, with which you can share feedback on the preview version with us.



There is a forum for program participants in which you can ask questions, get answers from experts from all over the world and search for necessary information. Already, the forum posted more than 1000 topics.
