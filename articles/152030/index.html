Wideband audio eliminates some of the bandwidth limitations and transmits audio in the range of 50 to 7,000 hertz and higher, which allows you to transmit human speech in a ‚Äúcleaner‚Äù form, with minimal distortion.

Ironically, in the USA this function is not supported by any of the major operators that started selling the iPhone 5. Earlier in Russia, the service was provided only in Moscow and Sochi, in 2012 appeared in Novosibirsk. Since September 6, 2012, its support has been announced throughout the Siberian branch of MegaFon (the Republic of Tuva, the Republic of Khakassia, the Altai Republic, the Altai Territory, the Krasnoyarsk Territory, the Kemerovo Region, the Omsk Region, the Tomsk Region, the Novosibirsk Region).
