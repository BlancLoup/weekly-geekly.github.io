In this publication, I would like to find out which of us is right, the employer or me is an applicant for the vacancy "Interface Developer". I sent my resume to the employer and solved the tasks that he proposed, among them was this:

It is necessary to draw a table with a large number of columns. To fit the table into the screen, the column headers decided to display vertically.
Design and implement a cross-browser solution for displaying vertical headers. Browsers: IE6 +, FF3.0 +, Opera 9.5+, Chrome 4.0+.
