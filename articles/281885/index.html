April 23 - Russian final of Imagine Cup and Student Day in Moscow

Microsoft is trying to do a lot of good for students, but the highlight of the year is certainly the final of the Imagine Cup student projects competition . Traditionally, the finale is not only a competition of teams, but also a set of lectures and activities interesting to a wide student audience - so we also call it Student Day. This year the final of the Imagine Cup and the Student Day will be held in Moscow this Saturday, April 23, 2016 , in Digital October , starting at 10:00.



We recommend all students in Moscow and the surrounding area to attend this event - for this you must first register . For students from other cities will be available online broadcast . Below - in more detail about what will be at the event and which teams will perform .


Team performances


The most important thing in the final is still the performances of the teams! This year in Russia there was a very big interest in the games - it is not surprising, after the Russian teams won the international final two years in a row. As a result, 12 games projects will be presented at the finals this Saturday, and only 4 projects each in the categories of ‚Äúinnovation‚Äù and ‚Äúsocial projects‚Äù. But this does not make such projects less interesting - we will have a holographic teleportation project (without using any augmented reality helmets!), And an Internet of things device for helping people with disabilities, and virtual educational space travel .

More information about the teams and their projects, you can read on the site .

Master of Code


In addition to student teams, schoolchildren will also compete for a trip to Seattle at the Master of the Code competition finals. First, the finalists of the competition will present their projects to the jury and the audience, after which the super-finalists will compete directly on the court for the right to go to the international final of the Imagine Cup in Seattle. If you are also a master of code, or are planning to participate next year, or just go to school and love computers, you will probably be interested to watch this process!

Lectures


In addition to the performances of the teams, we have prepared several fascinating lectures for you:
  • Andrei Egorov , Executive Director of the Skolkovo Open University, TEDx Ambassador to Russia, Advisor to Singularity University will tell us about the technological future in the lecture ‚Äú The future has a present ‚Äù.

  • The winners of the 2014 International Imagine Cup - the Brainy Studio team - will share the secrets of how to win the Imagine Cup , as well as how to get the most out of your project.
  • I will tell you about the recently announced at the conference // BUILD technologies related to artificial intelligence: Cognitive Services and Microsoft Bot Framework


Master Classes


The most valuable thing at an event is usually the opportunity to do something with your hands! To do this, we will organize a series of master classes that will be available only to those lucky ones who have time to register for them (for technical reasons, the number of participants is strictly limited).   • Andrei Ustyuzhanin, Yandex and CERN cooperation project manager, in practice will tell you about using DevOps in scientific research . To participate in the master class, you need a basic knowledge of Python and a laptop with Docker installed.
  • Irina Svyatitskaya from Career.ru will hold a master class on resume writing and interviewing
  • The best speaker of Microsoft, Andrei Beshkov, will tell you how interesting and incendiary it is to speak to the public , and also help you to master it in practice.
