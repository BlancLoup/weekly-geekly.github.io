Results of the city hackathon 2GIS

Hello! Last weekend (June 17-19), 2GIS was held by City Hackathon in Moscow. As you may have guessed from the title - the event was dedicated to improving life in urban environments - such a concept of "smart home", scaled to "smart city".



The main criterion by which the projects were selected - the connection with the urban theme and the solution of the problems of residents. There were additional bonus points for using the provided gadgets and technologies, but even without them, as many as 37 teams came to the start, and the choice of the winner made our experts break their heads. That was interesting on the hakathon, how it went and who won - under the cut.

About projects


All projects could be divided into 4 categories: solving technical or organizational problems, solving logistics and transportation problems, projects for commerce and projects for leisure.

According to the hackathon participants, the main problems of the big city are the ecology, trips to work / home and difficulties in organizing rest and walks.



This time one of the most popular ways to solve the problems found was the creation of telegram bots, which, however, is clear - this is not a service + mobile application with all the charms of the backend / frontend / interface type to do. Faster, easier and with the demonstration of special problems will not be. :)

Commerce projects and aggregators


List of projects

Schedules


Mobile application designer for scheduled organizations. Any hairdresser, clinic or technical inspection center will be able to create their own application and receive clients at the appointed hour.

Simple interface, ease of use, interaction with social networks - and even automatic creation of a mobile application. Cool!

Foodsharing


The service (website and applications for iOS and Android) will combine restaurants and shops that want to give free fresh, but not sold on time food, with charities and those who need it.

Aggregator of car sharing


Platform for combining carsharings. Users will help to use different services under one account, and operators - to connect to a common database of drivers, even without their equipment and system.

Heycoffee


Mobile application with a map of Moscow coffee shops and the possibility of pre-order. Now coffee is waiting for you, not you!

Create your event


Service for registration of guests at events. Individual chat bots that collect contact details of participants and send notifications.



Solving organizational and technical problems, self-management


List of projects

building manager


Telegram-bot, which will unite the neighbors and help solve domestic problems. All you need to do is specify the address. The bot will connect to the chat of your home, after which you can subscribe and chat with previously joined residents.

We are neighbours


A social network to solve the "neighbor" problems - to vote, discuss the problems of the yard, ask to look after pets and warn you that you will be doing repairs.

Bots designer


Platform for creating city bots in Telegram, Facebook and other instant messengers. From interactive helpers with basic functions to complex ones with integrations, script add-ins and analytics. Users do not need to know programming, a convenient wizard will do a lot for them.

Medical record


Personal space to store all digitized health data. The data are summarized by one type in the versioned directory. All indicators are defined immediately, and it is possible to estimate the percentage of digitized (and relevant) human health. For example, to collect all the X-rays and watch the dynamics of changes, whether it is a fluorography or the healing process of a broken bone ‚Äî the photos will overlap each other and highlight the changes with contrast.

Audio guide with links to routes. The application will tell a lot of interesting things about the usual places that make up your daily routes.

We are promoting the bicycle and helping newcomers in the city. List of projects

Moscow walks


No, not another guide, but an app with beautiful photos, routes from guides in love with Moscow, interactive hints and unusual facts.

Virtual routes


An application for interesting workouts in the gym. Sit on the exercise bike, put on 3D glasses, choose a route and travel around the virtual city.
