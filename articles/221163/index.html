InfoboxCloud opened a new location in Krasnoyarsk

We are happy to inform you about the launch of a new location in the data center of Krasnoyarsk in addition to the existing data center in St. Petersburg!



At the moment, in a new location, hosting and VPS services are running. Already, the use of several locations will allow you to be closer to customers and create more responsive services. Also, for our customers, scenarios of geo-replication of data into a separate location became reality.

In the near future, we will announce a few more new locations for cloud infrastructure and vps in different cities and countries.

If you need a VPS trial in Krasnoyarsk - write a letter to Yury Trukhin, an expert on cloud technologies . We pay attention that cryptocurrency is forbidden on the trial, but it is possible and necessary to evaluate the work of the service.
