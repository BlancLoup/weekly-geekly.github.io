How to get natural backlinks?

When you have a product, project, service, or application, you want people to know about you. You have many ways to achieve this, including - to interest others to tell about you. If you are lucky, natural links will appear on you - not only the most logical option, but also, from relatively recent times, it is a requirement of Google , which has learned to distinguish between natural links and purchased ones. Yandex, at least in words, would like from us the same . In this regard, it is interesting: what does the civilized practice of obtaining natural backlinks look like?



Why people might want to link to you? Two aspects are important in obtaining natural backlinks: planning and time. Each new link for Google can be a signal to rank you higher, especially if you are not on the first page - then it makes it more likely. However, for this link must have certain parameters. She must be:

  • Relevant, i.e. go from a site with a similar theme.
  • Natural, i.e. not purchased
  • Going from a domain with a good rating (DA).

The success of the event depends on how skillfully you plan your next steps in obtaining these links. Let's see how to do this.
The easiest way to satisfy two of the three conditions: to find relevant domains with a good rating. To do this, do not even need to type them in the search. It is enough to skip the sites of competitors through the backlink check services:

  • Comparison of Donor Links, MegaIndex - for free, you can compare several sites at once.
  • Open Site Explorer , Moz - for free, more suitable for English-speaking domains.
  • MajesticSEO - good, but heavily paid.

After such a check, you will have a list of target sites from which you also want links for yourself. It remains to think how to get them.

Use a scientific approach




Any company has data about its own audience, market, industry. Tell about them and what conclusions you draw on the basis of these data. This is one of the most difficult ways, but here you have far fewer competitors - not everyone can competently submit analytics, moreover, such texts, as a rule, take almost as much time to create them as high-quality video or infographics. And, of course, do not forget to scatter this research to people, including professional and news sites.

Most often, data for research can be found in the manual, which uses analytical sources for the decision-making process, such as Gartner , Forrester , Ovum , Altimeter , Corporate Executive Board or their Russian counterparts. From such reports, you can create surveys of market trends, they provide an opportunity to share figures.
