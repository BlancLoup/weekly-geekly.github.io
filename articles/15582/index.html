Mail.Ru is recruiting programmers in Nizhny Novgorod

Mail.Ru has announced the opening of an R & D center in Nizhny Novgorod, which will focus on developing the software part of the company's new projects. At the moment, there is already a set of web programmers and HTML-makers from among local residents.

The office in Nizhny Novgorod became the first Mail.Ru development center outside of Moscow. Since the rent of offices and programmers' salaries in the province is several times lower than in the capital, this will significantly reduce the costs of one of the largest Internet companies in Russia, whose staff has already grown to 250 people.
