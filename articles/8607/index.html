"Chocolate" laptop from LG

Explicitly pleased with the popularity and demand for the Chocolate phone lineup, LG is launching a Z1 laptop with a similar design.

Remarkably, the computer has, in addition to the main 12.1-inch (1280 √ó 800 pixels) display, a second, 2.5-inch external screen that supports SideShow technology (present in Windows Vista). The title of "chocolate" laptop Z1 received because of the touch buttons and finish a la "black piano": this is the combination used in a similar line of mobile phones.
