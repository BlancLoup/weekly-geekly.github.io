Startup lynch from investor: first issue

Last week we announced a new habraproject : a startup lynch from investment analyst Fast Lane Ventures. I remind you that the conditions are very simple: you leave a request for your project, we analyze it in detail and publish it after a while.

In just a couple of days, 20 applications arrived, which is, in principle, a great success of the project - given the traditional desire for secrecy among our person and, especially, our entrepreneur.

This week, as promised, we will publish the first Lynch. Why the first? First, to fit all 20 applications with comments in one post is unrealistic. Therefore, we decided to break them into several cycles and lay out parts in two weeks. Secondly, we hope that this is not the last experience of this kind, and there will still be those who want to get a qualified expertise - the application form still works . The authors of all applications will receive a mail notification of the status of the consideration of the application and whether it will be published.

For the first publication, we chose the most complete and informative applications in those areas of the Internet business, where Fast Lane Ventures has more experience. They were five projects:
  1. Regional resume sites
  2. Selloby - a mobile service and free classifieds website for sale, purchase, job search, rental housing and dating
  3. Sanviz 3D - a program for visualizing tiles of various designs for bathrooms
  4. Carmio.ru - an online service for calculating and comparing retail insurance products
  5. Znauvse.info - a project that provides free answers to all questions by SMS

The structure of comments is simple: application (spelling and punctuation of the original), pros, cons, general conclusion and answer to the sacramental question - is Fast Lane Ventures ready to give money to this project? Useful reading for all who have or who are just preparing to launch their own startup. Well, just for everyone interested in the topic.

So, let's go:

Carmio.ru is an online service for calculating and comparing retail insurance products.

Describe your project in one or two sentences.

Carmio.ru is an online service for calculating the cost of various insurance products in various insurance companies in Russia. The service is designed for quick and easy calculation of insurance and is aimed at both insurance market professionals (insurance brokers, agents) and ordinary users who want to calculate and issue insurance.

What is the business model of the project, how will the revenue be generated?

Project business model:
2.1 Sale of licenses for the required period of use of the system for insurance brokers, agents. The calculation of the cost of a month of use is carried out using a flexible system of discounts depending on the number of licenses required, the number of months ordered and the intensity, which makes the system accessible even to individual agents, and creates large discounts for large brokers.
2.2 Selling insurance to ordinary users who need to insure something. This is implemented as follows - the client calculates the cost of insurance, if he was interested in the offer,
he leaves a request on the website, or calls the operator, then we redirect the request to our partners who are directly involved in insurance, as a result, the client prepares
insurance from our partners, and we get a certain percentage of the transaction. In the future we plan to open our own brokerage insurance agency.
2.3 Subscription fee for insurance companies for their presence in the settlement system.

Who are the customers, why will they use your project?

Our clients are insurance brokers, agents who every day need to calculate insurance costs for customers, while spending too much time, because each insurance company has its own calculator for calculating insurance, as a result, to calculate, for example, 15 companies You must have 15 different calculators. With the help of Carmio.ru service such a calculation can be done in just 1 - 2 minutes. Also, customers may be ordinary users who need to insure a car or any other type of insurance.

Who are the partners, suppliers, why would they be interested in working with you?

The partners of our project are, first of all, insurance companies, because this is a direct advertisement of their services, we introduce their tariffs, methods into our service, as a result, users calculate insurance and subsequently acquire insurance policies. Also at the initial stage, a large insurance broker acts as a partner for consulting and identifying the needs and necessary functionality of the service.

How do you see the competitive environment and the market as a whole (the main
competitors, segmentation, total adressable market)?

Our service competitors are companies that have implemented their settlement systems for 3 types of insurance: CASCO, CTP, Green Card. But the difference is that our service will be much more functional and offers the consumer a wider range of possibilities for calculation. This is the calculation of insurance for Personal Protection, VHI, Mortgage insurance, Tourism, etc. for various insurance companies.

Why is your project better than existing competitors?

Carmio.ru - will be the first project in runet, with which you can simultaneously calculate several different insurance products. To date, there is not a single service in RuNet with such functionality. We plan to make it possible to calculate insurance not only for well-known types of insurance CASCO, OSAGO, Green Card, but also for other types - LCA, personal protection, mortgage insurance in various insurance companies. Those projects that are now implemented on the Internet are sites that offer to calculate insurance and at the end of the calculation they need to leave contact details so that you can contact the client and offer him services. The difference of our project is that we do not require any contact information, the client, after calculating insurance with our system, sees the results of the calculation, and already decides whether the price suits him or not. Also, the main difference between our calculation system and the existing ones is that we have implemented the maximum possible number of parameters for the calculation, which is the main advantage. After all, the service is aimed at insurance market professionals - brokers, for whom the use of such a system will help reduce the time for calculating insurance, and consequently increase labor productivity.

Describe the development plan for one year and the strategy for the next 2-3 years

Development strategy of the project: Registration as a legal entity, search for premises for a company, refinement of the necessary functionality, organization of a large-scale advertising project company, search
partner clients from insurance companies, processing applications from users for the purchase of insurance. If we talk about plans for the next 2-3 years, then this is, first of all, the refinement of our service to a full-fledged insurance portal for those who directly work in the field of insurance. Those. creating the most convenient environment for the work of insurance brokers / agents. Also in the future prospects of opening your own brokerage insurance agency.

How much time and money is needed to develop, before the project
will the first client appear?

At this stage of the project, customers already have. But there are not as many of them as we would like, money is needed to finalize the project and advertising. An investment of 1.5 million rubles is needed.

What are the main achievements of team members and how are these achievements
will help your project succeed?

The project participants include 5 people: the project organizer, a programmer who has extensive experience in implementing complex Internet projects, a web-design, 2 insurance consultants who have higher education in this area and have been working in the insurance business for more than 5 years. As a result, we have a close-knit team, each of the participants knows his business perfectly, which certainly helps in the implementation of the project.

A promising market that is growing organically and by increasing the role of the Internet in attracting customers

From the point of view of usability, Sarmio makes a comparison across several companies, simplifying the user's life, but then complicates the decision-making and purchase process
