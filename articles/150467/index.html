IOS weather station



Netatmo is an aluminum cylinder, inside which are all the necessary sensors to determine the weather conditions and chemical composition of the air. Communication with the smartphone via WiFi. The plans - the application is not only for iOS, but also for devices on Android `e.

Sensors:
- humidity
- pressure
- temperature
- noise level
- CO2 level in the air.

It is possible to set up alerts on the phone - for example, if the level of CO2 at home has suddenly increased, an alert will immediately come.

Issue price - $ 179.
