4 reasons not to use Linux on a laptop.

I use Linux almost every day when working on a laptop, for several years I have accumulated enough experience and impressions from working with it.
Many things are very convenient and very well implemented, but there are also disadvantages that sometimes cause decent difficulties during work. I found 4 good reasons not to work on a laptop with Linux ...

Webcams, card readers, PCMCIA devices are raised either with the use of many hours of ritual dances with a tambourine and mana, or not at all. The same applies to the support of external monitors \ TVs \ projectors - we hope that with the advent of Ubuntu 7.10 (Gutsy Gibbon) they will solve the last problem ...
