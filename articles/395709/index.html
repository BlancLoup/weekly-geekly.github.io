HIPER POWER BANK RP10000


Official page of the model: hiper-power.com/products/hiper-power-bank-rp10000
Declared capacity: 10,000 mAh

The appearance of this model is quite standard for such devices. Black square box made of thick plastic. At one end there are two USB type A connectors for connecting powered devices. There is also a microUSB connector for charging the most external battery, a set of four blue indicators and a large power button.



At the opposite end there is a brief technical description of the model, its full name and badges of certificates. On the top cover there is a positive sticker ‚ÄúBe charged‚Äù. Everything is quite concise, nothing superfluous.





Connectors for gadgets are divided into two groups, the first with one "lightning" is intended to charge devices with a maximum current of 1A, and the second (two "lightning") - a current of 2.1A. By connecting two devices at once, each of them will be charged with 1A current, which is quite logical.
It is worth noting not very large weight of the model - only 261 g. On the other hand, the edge of the case is not very convenient when shifting the device from hand to hand. Although it is convenient to carry it in your hand, since the case is somewhat rough and confidently rests on the palm of your hand. By the way, this is the cheapest model among the participants of this review. In general, HIPER has a lot of external batteries, and everyone can choose to their own taste. Alas, as is usually the case, not all models can be bought, and some are already discontinued.

Brief conclusions



If we talk about the conclusions that were made after this small test, I personally noted several facts for myself. Do not order from China "branded" electronic gadgets, even the simplest. Some kind of watering can for the shower - please, but everything that carries electronic components in itself is better to buy in a normal online store. Let it be a little more expensive, but still the risk of getting the cat in a bag, or even completely empty bag, is much lower. Xiaomi devices are notable for good and high-quality stuffing, but, alas, because of the desire to be similar to Apple, design and design are often higher than some consumer qualities.

External batteries HIPER inferior in quality toppings, but on the other hand, in my opinion, the most practical to use. As a result, the external battery HIPER POWER BANK SP20000 was given to the daughter. Capacity here, of course, decides the key role, but since there are always many small things in the handbag, the case protection here is also an important criterion. In any case, the full capacity of the battery is enough to charge its iPhone 5S for 8 times. For personal use and charging, the Zuk Z1 was chosen as a good model HIPER POWER BANK RP10000, and my wife got the original Xiaomi Mi Power Bank 16000 RM89 battery, match for Xiaomi Mi4c.
