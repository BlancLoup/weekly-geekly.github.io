How PacketZoom Mobile Expresslane Increases Application Performance

We at GlobalDots use different tools to increase application performance. In the list of popular problems of mobile applications, low conversion often emerges, a large percentage of disconnections of connections, and slow work of third-party APIs. CDN does not save from all ills. We want to talk about PacketZoom Mobile Expresslane - a software solution to accelerate the transfer of content between the clouds and mobile applications through its own UDP-based protocol for processing HTTP requests instead of TCP / IP.

PacketZoom Mobile Expresslane SDK is not the only solution of its kind, but it is widely distributed and can solve various problems. In this article, we have compiled a sample of various cases, so that you can figure out whether you will be interested in PacketZoom Mobile Expresslane, whether to look further to it.




In fact, PacketZoom Mobile Expresslane is an SDK that is embedded in a mobile application and does not require any changes. The whole procedure usually takes less than an hour.

PacketZoom uses a large variety of technology to optimize the connection. This includes saving TCP sessions, choosing the optimal connection speed, optimizing the cache, and using specific network speed data. One of the advantages of the PacketZoom technology is that the connection within the proprietary protocol is not established with an IP address, but with a device; therefore, when switching between networks ‚Äî switching between different types of mobile networks ‚Äî it recovers faster with less packet loss, that is . The protocol is initially focused on networks with low bandwidth and zone coverage. In some scenarios, the solution provider promises data transfer acceleration up to 10 times. Inshorts is a mobile news aggregator that shortens texts to 60 or less words. In the news he keeps the headlines and basic facts, removes expert comments and other related information. The main market is India, the country is not the most stable mobile Internet.

Inshorts uses the Akamai CDN, but it does not solve the problem of slow data transfer and permanent disconnections. After the introduction of PacketZoom Mobile Expresslane, the volume of complaints about the speed of the application has decreased by 70%. The number of views of pictures in the news increased by 7%. The change in the number of breaks can be seen on the chart - it has decreased by 3-4 times.



Story 2. A dynamic application and many APIs in the Indian wilderness


Netmeds Marketplace Limited is an Indian online pharmacy that delivers everyday medicines. The Netmeds App forms about half of the pharmacy traffic, and the second half comes from the website. The application is popular in small towns where ordinary pharmacies are few. And the quality of mobile communication also suffers. Therefore, after introducing the CDN, the company tried PacketZoom to optimize the speed of the application.

As a result, the download speed increased by 1.64 times for 3G networks and 1.5 times for Wi-Fi and LTE. The number of user gaps due to network problems has decreased by 5 times.

Story 3. Online shoe store with lots of pictures and Amazon CloudFront

The SDK was implemented in an hour, resulting in a three-fold increase in data transfer speed in all types of mobile networks, and the number of breaks was reduced by 63%. Due to the fact that PacketZoom takes away caching of mobile data from a CDN, the cost of it has decreased to 10 times.
