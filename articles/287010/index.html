Google will allow you to pay bills from Gmail using the service Pony Express

The new service will work in the Gmail mail and allow you to pay for services from mail applications. The project code name is Pony Express. According to Recode, the feature may appear in the fourth quarter of 2015.



To work with Pony Express in the United States, the user must provide personal information, including a social security number. After identifying not only Google, but also a third party, the user is able to pay bills in Gmail and Inbox email applications. Payment will be possible by credit or debit card.
