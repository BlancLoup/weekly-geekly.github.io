How we chose the theme for our second template


After our first Profi Theme template fell into the Corporate category, we decided that the next template would be narrowly targeted. We made a small survey on categories and decided that the category of Real Estate is quite suitable: up to 100 wordpress templates, and sales of the most successful exceed thousands. As I wrote earlier, they decided to start with the new category Sketch Templates. We already had some ideas on the subject of renting the premises, it remained to promote the topic and prepare the Sketch-file for release. After processing the software, which rested solely on increasing the volume of the template, we finally uploaded our template to the market.



Based on previous experience, we did not bother to develop the HTML version, but immediately began to develop a WordPress template. After a month of productive work, the first build for the review was ready. We realized that it was not perfect, but we still decided to try it in the hope of getting the software rezh. And during the test, which at that time was about two weeks, the remaining nuances were finalized.




And we already thought that it was practically at the goal, however Themeforest.net reviewers did not think so. Check our template lasted more than two months, or rather 63 days. 63 days to review! What to do?
