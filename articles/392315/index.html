Mobile application to communicate with celebrities



TipTalk offers an unusual service for fans eager to get news from their favorite star. The user who installed the application has the ability to pay the attention of his idol and get an answer from him in the form of a text message, photo or video.

Famous personalities, movie stars, sports stars, and other celebrities get the opportunity to monetize their fame by setting a fixed cost to communicate with fans. Currently, text message prices start at $ 20, photos start at $ 50, and videos start at $ 100.

Fans can choose a star from the list of registered in the service and ask them their questions, express admiration, ask for unusual greetings on the holiday, etc.

Stars get 50% of the money listed, Apple keeps 30% for itself, and TipTalk gets the remaining 20%. Fan money is returned to him if he has not received a reply within 48 hours. All the stars are checked by the company for the authenticity of the person.
