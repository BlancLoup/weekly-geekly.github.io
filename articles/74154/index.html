The new generation browser for Nokia communicators



November 3, 2009 - Oslo, Norway Opera Software has announced the release of a beta version of Opera Mobile browser 10 for Nokia communicators and other mobile devices running on the Symbian / S60 platform. The most notable innovation of Opera Mobile 10 beta is the interface, first introduced in Opera Mini 5 beta. Now Opera Mobile users have access to the Express panel for quick access to their favorite websites, tabbed browsing and password manager.

Opera Mobile 10 beta is optimized to work with communicators equipped with both touch panels and keyboards.

You can download Opera Mobile 10 beta at: www.opera.com/mobile/next

One of the most important priorities in the development of Opera Mobile 10 beta was the speed of the browser. The current version for Symbian / S60 works twice as fast as the previous one. Users will experience a significant increase in speed when loading web pages, scaling and framing them. In addition, the browser supports Opera Turbo technology, capable of compressing the contents of web pages when they are transferred to a mobile device. This technology allows you to increase the speed of downloading content sites and reduces the cost of mobile Internet.

Updated interface. Opera significantly redesigned Opera Mobile browser design, making it even more simple and intuitive for mobile phone users. The same approach is used in the development of the Opera Mini 5 browser.

Availability
Opera Mobile 10 beta is available for Nokia, Sony Ericsson and Samsung communicators running on the third and fifth Symbian / S60 platforms.
