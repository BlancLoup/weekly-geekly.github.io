Surely, many have already heard about the aka powerball wrist exerciser. Most often it was mentioned on Habr√© in the light of the prevention of tunnel syndrome .

Introduction


We must begin with the fact that the purpose of the purchase was precisely the prevention of tunnel syndrome, since although the work is not related to coding, hands are always on the mouse and clave. Therefore, it was decided to start prevention immediately.

I started my acquaintance with gyro-trainers from the site powerball.ru , where all the models from NSD are given, and there are customer reviews about each of them. What it is and how it works


Each powerball consists of a housing, a rotor and a groove.


The main components of the gyro. A pale blue color indicates a translucent body, a light purple circular groove, black - the axis of the gyroscope, yellow - a rotor.
From the name of the gyroscopic simulator it becomes clear that the powerball is essentially a gyroscope .
All the specifics of the work of powerball is perfectly described in the article on the wiki , but if you say in human language about what happens during training, then it can be described as:
When spinning the rotor and making rotational movements with a brush, the speed of spin of the rotor increases (how, by how much and by what physical laws, again, described in the above wiki article). As a result of the rotor spinning, forces are created, which are manifested in the fact that the powerball is pulled out of the brush, and you have to apply the efforts of the hand and forearm in order to hold the ball in your hand and maintain rotation. As a result of the hand rotations, as I understand it, the desired effect on the joints occurs, which is the ultimate goal in the prevention of diseases of the hand.
