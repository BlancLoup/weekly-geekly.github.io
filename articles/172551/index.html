Dropbox 2.0.0 released

An amazing innovation has been added: a new drop-down menu combining the convenience of the old menu and a new implementation of the chronology of the most important events of your Dropbox account! For example, if someone shares a link for you, you will see it instantly! You can also immediately accept or reject the invitation directly from the drop-down menu, directly from the desktop. All events are displayed not only in real time, you also have access to them at any convenient time. In addition, files that have been recently changed are also displayed in chronology, and you can easily share them.




In addition to the new release menu, less visible improvements were made:
  • Error correction using discrete graphics on laptops with OSX.
  • Adding support for Brazilian and Portuguese.
  • Other minor fixes.


Download new 2.0.0 version: www.dropbox.com/install

Spoon of tar: the new menu is available only for OSX and Windows, the implementation for Linux is not yet ready.
