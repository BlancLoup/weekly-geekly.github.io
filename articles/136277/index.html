Pinkle is a mobile service for communication and dating.

I present our project - Pinkle , a mobile application for communicating and meeting people around. Already running Android apps . But first, a few words about how the idea was born and why we are doing our service Pinkle.ru .


How many interesting and fun people are around, but because of natural modesty or excessive good breeding, we are afraid to start a conversation?

Our application for Android helps to get acquainted and communicate with people around, see what people are writing about, what is happening near you and participate in what is happening.

The idea was born in late spring, and a fully working version of the application for Android was ready only by November. Constantly finalizing the service and making adjustments to the functionality.

Always wondered: "How to organize communication of strangers who are near or at the same event?". And there are many such events, be it just a conference, an exhibition, a snack in a cafe or a boring lecture. To be very clear, according to Pinkle, it is a Twitter implementation with geo-referencing and the absence of such concepts as ‚Äúfriend‚Äù, ‚Äúfollower‚Äù, etc., thereby occupying the very, not yet developed niche, means of communication with surrounding people.

Examples of using


For example, if I am at a conference or meeting, I will see what is relevant at the moment, i.e. thoughts and ideas of people who surround me here and now, and are not thousands of kilometers away.

Or you walk down the street and choose which cafe to go to. There is nothing easier than just asking people around. To do this, just write messages and get an answer, and maybe an invitation to join, if you like the person who answered you.

Another example. You are sitting in a lecture and falling asleep. With our service you can communicate and discuss the most pressing problems with your fellow students who are in the same audience.

And there may be many such examples, maybe some of the most sought-after ones, we do not yet guess. No limits and restrictions are imposed, the deeper you scroll through the "message feed", the more distant and outdated will be the displayed messages of users and events. In other words, at the very top of the list, only the most relevant and interesting.

How does the application work?


At the moment, only the application for Android is completely ready, and we will talk about it. After launch, the user is logged in via Vkontakte or is registered in the old-fashioned way. Next, the application determines its location and displays a list of recent messages from other users, sorted by distance to us and time. On the other tab, we see a list of people sorted by distance from us and you can start communicating with each of these users by previewing his profile or winking at him:
