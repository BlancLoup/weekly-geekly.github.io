Ainol V9000 HDX Megatron - player playing 1080p video

Chinese manufacturer Ainol may not be the most well-known player in the media player market, but he has released a product that can plug such popular devices as the iPod Touch or Zune HD.


The new player Ainol V9000 HDX Megatron is based on the SoChip SC9800 chip and allows you to play HD-video with a resolution of up to 1080p. Megatron has a 4.8-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 800x480, which, of course, does not reveal all the delights of high-definition video, but you can always connect the player to the TV via the built-in HDMI output.

The gadget is equipped with 16 GB of internal memory and has a battery capacity of 2450 mA / h.
