Luminous BT Smart Bulb: What's Inside the Smart Bulb? Review + disassembly for parts



In the world of smart things a special place is occupied by the so-called smart-lamps. These are bulbs equipped with additional modules and sensors, with the result that a conventional LED lamp turns into a smart device.

In recent years, several companies have begun to position themselves as manufacturers of smart bulbs: Philips, LG, and some others have become quite famous in this market.

Of course, there are less eminent manufacturers, whose smart lamps are not much inferior to the developments of large corporations, but still for one reason or another remain in the shadows. Luminous BT Smart Bulb features



The developers have equipped the lamp with a Bluetooth wireless module, thanks to which the device is controlled from a smartphone.



For mobile devices on Android OS and iOS, the corresponding application is written that extends the functionality of the device.



Main functions:
  • Own mode of lighting of any room (you can create settings in the application, for several rooms at once);
  • The ability to change the color of the lighting;
  • Setting the lamp operation mode (for example, turn on the lamp at 19.00 and turn off at 23.30). If necessary, the lamp can increase or decrease the brightness and change color over time;
  • Setting the color music mode (lamps change the luminance level and color to the beat of the melody).
  • There is no need to purchase an external controller for the lamp - for many models of smart-lamps from other manufacturers such a controller is required, and the controller is purchased separately from the lamp itself. Savings in this case - more than 30%, compared with peers;
  • Turning on / off according to the schedule is made without having a smartphone nearby;
  • The lamp does not require additional settings - we screw it in the cartridge, download the application, and that‚Äôs it - the system works out of the box;




Since the lamp is light-emitting diode, installing such kind of smart-bulbs in a home or office, besides the convenience of controlling the lighting, also saves energy.
