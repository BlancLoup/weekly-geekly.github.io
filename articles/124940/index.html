Payweb.com is a convenient and profitable tool for remunerating freelancers

Hello, honorable users of Habr!

In our first post I would like to tell you about the possibility of using our service as a means of remunerating remote workers. Discuss with you the pros and cons, get your opinions and listen to your wishes.

Having studied the methods and possibilities used today, we have developed a tool that, in our opinion, will significantly simplify and cheapen the process of transferring regular payments.




How it works:

- The company / payer initiates an electronic account in the system where it can transfer money via bank transfer. Our maximum commission is $ 30, so it is reasonable to transfer the entire amount of monthly payments in one tranche.

- The counterparty / beneficiary opens a bank account with a MasterCard card. I want to emphasize that we are opening not just a debit card, like most other systems, but a full-fledged offshore bank account, which can be used not only for receiving payments from a particular employer, but also for other purposes. Details

- Further, sending transfers within the system costs only $ 0.5 to Payweb electronic accounts, from where an employee can transfer the necessary amount to his bank account. Either $ 2.5 for transfer directly to a bank account (this option is available only for corporate clients).

- Having received the transfer, the employee can immediately withdraw money at any ATM in the world. Up to $ 2,000 per day with a commission of $ 2 per withdrawal. Or pay by card without any commission up to $ 25,000 per day

Thus, even if the company / payer assumes all the costs of opening and maintaining an account and commission, the transfer of payments still turns out to be more profitable than most conventional tools.

And in addition, there is a special promotion now, during which we give a 50% discount on incoming payments for the first 3 months and a 50% discount on opening accounts for 100 contractors of the company. Attention: for users of Habr the bonus is increased to 6 months and 200 users respectively! (to get your bonus, please contact me after registering the company).
