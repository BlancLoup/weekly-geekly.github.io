Catch under the Christmas tree-2: Fibrum works with any smartphones from 4 to 6 inches

Have you read about a virtual reality helmet for Galaxy Note 4 for $ 200? An expensive toy for owners of Galaxy Note 4, which in itself costs more than 30 thousand rubles. And what can you say about a virtual reality helmet that would fit any smartphones from 4 to 6 inches, including the same 5.7-inch Galaxy Note 4?



This helmet was shown on December 9 in Moscow by the Russian startup Fibrum : Fibrum Lite virtual reality helmets with a viewing angle of 90 degrees (‚ÇΩ 2,990) and Fibrum Black with a viewing angle of 110 degrees ( ‚ÇΩ 4,990 ). The helmet supports Apple, Android and Windows Phone smartphones.

Later, they promise the Ultra version with its own screen, processor and video card, as well as a joystick and a virtual reality glove.

As it is now fashionable to say, this is a completely Russian gadget: the company's head office is in Moscow, all production is conducted in Russia. However, moving to Chinese production facilities is not excluded. The Fibrum team consists of fifteen people, the development was started in 2013.

Our company was one of the first to understand the prospects and growth potential of the virtual reality market, starting the development of the Fibrum helmet in November 2013, long before the news about the purchase of Facebook by Oculus Rift, as well as before the presentation of the Samsung Galaxy VR.

- quote from Fibrum press release

Fibrum says that at the SEO conference, the AR Conference, the Robotics Expo, the Fibrum press conferences and during private screenings in their office, the helmet tried on more than 6,000 people. Among them was the ni404 , which found the Fibrum is not very convenient: according to him, because of badly sweating face, I want to take it off after five minutes. The developers promise in the consumer version to take into account this problem, how exactly, do not specify.
