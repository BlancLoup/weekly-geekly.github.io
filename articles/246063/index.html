Results of the competition of Android developers Epson Moverio BT-200

Recently, we summed up the competition of Android-developers for Epson Moverio BT-200 . In the contest, participants loaded their applications into three categories - games, AR- and non-AR applications in the struggle for Epson equipment.

It's time to announce the results!

The head of the VI- and Business-Directions of Epson Europe BV in Russia, Evgeny Jaksimov, presents prizes to the winners of the competition

Applications and prizes


Category "The best application without augmented reality"


  1. 3D Photos: Russo-Japan War 1904 - 3D photos of the Russian-Japanese war of 1904-1905
    Developers - Yana Artischeva, Anastasia Godunova. Prize - video glasses Epson BT-200 .



  2. BeBack Light - voice GPS navigator mushroom picker-angler.
    Developer - Vladislav Pomelov. The prize is an Epson EH-TW490 projector .



  3. Colorizer is an assistant application for people with color disorders.
    Developer - Andrey Popleteyev. The prize is Epson L355 Printing Factory .



Category ‚ÄúBest game (AR- and not AR):


  1. No safe Place - an arcade game in which the player needs to collect coins, avoiding collision with computer enemies, "worms" (Honestly, long thought how to describe =)
    Developer - Roman Bryukhanov. Prize - video glasses Epson BT-200 .



  2. GyroCar - AR-game, which allows you to control a virtual toy machine.
    The machine is "superimposed" on the surrounding space, and it can be followed by head movements thanks to the use of a gyroscope built into the video glasses.
    Developer - Roman Bryukhanov. The prize is an Epson EH-TW490 projector .



  3. Lyfos (Lyfa) - a logic game in which you need to collect balls of the same color into flasks.
    Developer - Anar Ibrahimov. The prize is Epson L355 Printing Factory .



Category "Best Augmented Reality Application":


  1. ARPiano is a piano teacher app.
    Allows you to display the keys that you need to press the user in order to play one of the two in-app tunes.
    Developer - Igor Shadurin. Prize - video glasses Epson BT-200 .



... and everything. Alas, no one took the second and third place in this category. This is due to the fact that we received applications from several developers, but did not find these applications in the Moverio Apps Market. Attempts to contact the developers, unfortunately, were unsuccessful.
