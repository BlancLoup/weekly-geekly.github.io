Qualcomm introduced the new flagship Snapdragon 800



Qualcomm presented at CES its new flagship system on a chip, the Snapdragon 800, Mashable writes . As stated, its performance is 75% higher than the previous flagship Snapdragon S4.

Snapdragon 800 is based on the Krait 400 quad-core processor and is capable of speeds up to 2.3 GHz. Phones with Snapdragon 800 can record and play video in 4K resolution. Snapdragon 800 will support camera resolution up to 55 megapixels, as well as multi-channel HD audio.

The new Adreno 330 graphics processor is twice as powerful as its predecessor, and, as stated, is equipped with a new technology that extends its use beyond the limits of graphics for general computing tasks, which improves overall performance.

The Snapdragon 800 is fully integrated wireless - there is multi-band support for LTE and Wi-Fi 802.11ac, which allows speeds up to 1 gigabit per second.

In addition to the 800, Qualcomm also introduced the Snapdragon 600 with a frequency of up to 1.9 GHz and a performance 40% higher than the S4. More detailed specifications are in the Qualcomm blog .
