Toshiba Satellite T230 and T210

Toshiba Computer Systems presented one of the thinnest laptops in the history of the company in the line of Satellite.

The Satellite T210 with a 29.5 cm (11.6 '') display and the Satellite T230 with a 33.8 cm (13.3 '' display) are distinguished by their elegant shapes: the front section is only 17.6 mm thick. On sale in Russia, the device will arrive in the third quarter of 2010.




The weight of the models starts from just 1.48 kg (Satellite T210) and 1.75 kg (Satellite T230).


The full-size silver-colored keyboard is complemented by a no less comfortable large touch panel with Multi-Touch technology and chrome-plated buttons built into the textured wrist surface.


The attractive design is complemented by a well-balanced set of functions, covering all everyday computer tasks.
