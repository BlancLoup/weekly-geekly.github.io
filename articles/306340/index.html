2. Weight of votes from owners of badges



Until today, badges, with the exception of participation in PPA , did not give their owners any tangible benefits, in addition to cosmetic.


However, to get them is not easy, which means that their owners have pretty tried. It is reasonable to give such users the privilege, because they understand what topic is in demand, and can distinguish a good publication from a bad one.


We decided to correct this situation and increased the weight of the vote for the publication of users with icons. This innovation will help quality notes quickly gain a good rating and get to the top.


So, today the owners of the following 4 icons are given super power:


- ‚ÄúAuthor‚Äù, ‚ÄúStar‚Äù and ‚ÄúOld-Timer‚Äù - instead of the 1st plus, 2 (2 248 users) are added at once;
