Skolkovo Robotics International Conference will be held May 20, 2016

The fourth international conference Skolkovo Robotics will be held in Skolkovo on May 20, 2016. More than 3000 participants are expected. Outstanding international experts in the field of robotics, representatives of venture funds, research and research teams, start-ups and other participants are planned to gather at one site.
Under the report of a report from the last conference, a short interview with speakers, stands, master classes. Link to the registration form.



Robots are increasingly becoming a reality. These developments, which only recently seemed to be just a flight of fantasy by filmmakers, are gradually entering our life, making it even more amazing.


The first conference took place one day and attracted about 700 participants . The second conference lasted two days, and she managed to gather at least 900 people . The third conference gathered more than 1,000 participants in 2015.
An even larger event is expected this year: plenary sessions, an exhibition of robotic projects, investment and mentoring sessions, finals of competitive selections of robotic projects and a show program. More than 3000 visitors, 80 + speakers, 40 + projects at the exhibition, 72 hours of talking about robotics. Now about everything in order.
