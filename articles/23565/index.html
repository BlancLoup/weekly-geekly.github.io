IKEA. Ideas for the Japanese subway.

On the occasion of the opening of a new store in the Japanese city of Kobe, IKEA decided to conduct an unusual advertising campaign. Since yesterday, trains, painted in the style of designer fabrics sold in IKEA stores, have been traveling on a monorail. In addition, the standard interior of the cars was also changed, with the help of all the same fabrics. In general, the photos speak for themselves.
