7 days left to buy Early Bird tickets for MBLTdev 16

November 17 in Moscow will host the third international conference of mobile developers MBLTdev 16 . Invited experts from the UK, USA, Germany, Romania, Denmark and Russia will share their development experience for iOS and Android.



The program will be divided into several streams:

For developers

Hardcore reports, hands-on workshops and live coding:

  • Features of the choice of design patterns on Swift 3 for unit testing and TDD,
  • Google Cardboard chips and Daydream SDK,
  • detailed analysis of Firebase,
  • testing using Espresso, UiAutomator, Appium and using the Firebase Test Lab and Amazon Device Farm,
  • Improving the readability of the code through the competent organization of callbacks,
  • ensuring the consistency of the stored data using access synchronization,
  • dependency handling with asynchronous methods
  • interaction with Robolectric,
  • working with data that does not yet exist when creating interfaces,
  • alternative application development without fragments,



For designers

Track with lectures and workshops from the teachers of the course of design of mobile applications of the British Higher School of Design are specialists of well-known companies with significant experience in the field of UX / UI service applications and digital products, mobile development and marketing.



Until September 1, you can purchase an Early Bird ticket at the lowest price - 2500 rubles. Details on the official site .

Follow the news on Facebook . See you at MBLTdev 16!
