Google Glass to help people with Parkinson's disease

Google launched a campaign to use Google Glass to those who can solve difficult issues with this gadget. Five pairs of glasses were donated to the University of Newcastle, where experts began making an assistant out of the device for people suffering from Parkinson's disease. Researchers want to help patients live more independently - and they already have results.
