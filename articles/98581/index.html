iPhone 4 with DSLR lens



As the guys from Hypebeast write , the iPhone 4 exceeded the expectations of many, but its 5-megapixel camera is inferior to some of its competitors - for example, the HTC Evo 4G with an 8-megapixel camera. However, a solution was found - using a special tripod, you can attach a DSLR lens to the iPhone 4.



Due to the brevity of the publication on Hypebeast, some commentators thought it was a fake, but this or similar tripod, Modo Pocket, was found to be Technabob. It can be ordered in the French store Digit-Photo.com for 18.90 euros.
