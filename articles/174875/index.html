Already 6 days after the start of the company on indiegogo, the guys from the Aerospace Industries Association (AIA) managed to raise the necessary $ 33,000 to show a 30-second video dedicated to NASA on the main movie screens in the US immediately before the show of the Star Trek space saga - Into Darkness ( in the Russian box office - Star Trek: Retribution). The video is planned to be shown within 8 weeks from the premiere of the film. Now, AIA has a new goal - to collect $ 94'000 for a video showing in at least one cinema in each state.

The company's goal is to raise the prestige of NASA, above all in the eyes of young people, to remind that there is much to be proud of and much that remains to be implemented by the new generation in the new space age.
