Great Belarusian Firewall


In Belarus, create a "black list" of sites. The authors of the project believe that the new rules should protect users from extremism , pornography , violence , etc.

The ‚Äúblack list‚Äù is formed at the suggestion of authorized government agencies. Legal entities, individual entrepreneurs and ordinary citizens who should send their suggestions on blocking websites to government agencies can help in drawing up a list.

It is planned to make two access lists. The first will be publicly available, the second closed. The first will include Internet resources registered in the national segment of the Internet (it is still empty ), and the second - the other unwanted Internet resources. To obtain a closed list, providers are invited to do the following:
1) To issue a LETTER to the BelGIE RUE
2) To the authorized person of the Internet service provider ARRIVE in RUE "BelGIE" (Minsk, 1 Kharkovskaya str., Office 3) with a LETTER and a certified LICENSE for the right to carry out activities in the field of communication
Get a login and password to access the list of restricted access through the SITE RUE "BelGIE"

Formally, the new law applies only to:
to state bodies and organizations, as well as educational and cultural organizations - without fail;
other users of Internet services - on their request.
The decision on a general ban remains with the provider. But having on hand a list of "forbidden" sites and complete dependence on the state, it will be difficult to keep.
