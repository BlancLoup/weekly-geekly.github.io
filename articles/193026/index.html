We invite you to participate in the Intel Software Conference 2013


Dear habrovchane! We invite you to take part in the annual Intel Software Conference 2013 , which will be held on September 17 in Irkutsk and September 19 in Moscow .
You will be invited to the reports on two of Intel's flagship software products for developers - Intel Parallel Studio XE and Intel Cluster Studio XE. Specialists involved in developing software solutions for embedded systems on Intel platforms will also be interested in a review of the new specialized toolkit Intel System Studio. Developers of computing-intensive and cloud solutions are reports on porting applications to the Intel Xeon Phi platform.
During the day, a demo zone will be organized in which leading Intel experts will demonstrate the capabilities of the latest development tools, including tools for creating and debugging multi-threaded programs and the capabilities of the new Intel Xeon Phi computation processor.
Register for the conference.
