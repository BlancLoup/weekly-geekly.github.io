Giving a T-shirt to a web developer


Actually, a friend and I decided to give this t-shirt to our boss.

At the beginning there was an idea to order something like that.

from here for $ 18, not counting the delivery.

But there was little time left and it was necessary to quickly deliver a gift ... So it was necessary not to order via the Internet, but urgently to invent something ourselves. And we had to take one place where they put stickers on clothes. Having traveled there and talked to the responsive staff, we bought an ordinary black T-shirt and brought them. On hand was a screenshot of the code.

Code and color scheme compiled in vime. In general, the inscription was ordered and in a day the T-shirt presented above was ready. The total price of this T-shirt turned out to be one and a half times cheaper than from the online store.

Happy birthday, Anton)
