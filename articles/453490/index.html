Education


  • The GIScience research group at the University of Heidelberg, using the example of health-related tags, studied how data in OSM changes over time. The results of the study allowed a deeper and better understanding of the typical mapping models, including understanding the reasons for filling certain territories with these tags, the appearance of specifications ( amenity=hospital -> amenity=clinic ), and also showed that such tags begin to be used randomly if one or another the country begins to provide humanitarian assistance.
