Everyday robot


Every year, robots increasingly penetrate into various spheres of human activity. Indeed, in comparison with computers and mobile devices, more interactive, mobile and social functions can be put into the robot. For example, in addition to reading the mail, reminders of upcoming meetings and organizing a video call, your home robot will be able to clean your apartment, bring a cup of hot coffee, feed the dog or water the flowers.
Today, the market of service and personal robots directly interacting with humans is considered promising. Modern robots already know how to conduct excursions in museums, entertain the public, advertise new products, advise clients, protect territories, serve the elderly, deliver mail. In the near future, as the development of artificial intelligence, popularization of robots and cheapening their production, they will become good friends and helpers for each person.

Product Promotion and Promotion


Developing a business in a modern, highly competitive environment is not an easy task requiring creative approach. The sophisticated buyer is not much surprised, and few people pay attention to simple advertising. A robot promoter is a new way to stand out among competitors, increase public interest and make an advertising campaign more effective. Such a robot will be the focus of presentations, exhibitions, conferences and any public event.

Robot Advee - a member of the advertising company HTC

Office robot


An office robot is able to bring new colors to ordinary working days and become a full-fledged member of the working team. Most models of office robots are positioned on the market as telepresence robots. Their main goal is to create a plausible effect of a person‚Äôs being in a room, which can be achieved by using several means of communication at once: a monitor, a camera, a microphone, feedback sensors or hand-manipulators. With such a robot, you can remotely work, participate in conferences or control the workflow in the office.

Robot teleprinting iRobot Ava 500 with a large screen and the ability to transfer HD-quality video
Robots in this category support various remote control tools: computers, smartphones and tablets, IR consoles and set-top boxes. Often, this versatility can be achieved through the use of a web server, which can be connected via a standard Internet browser. Strict requirements are imposed on the quality of video conferencing.
Another category of office robots is characterized by greater autonomy and the availability of useful intellectual functions. Agree, at least, it will be fun to have a robotized assistant in the office who will greet employees with various remarks, carry coffee and cookies, strongly remind you of the inevitability of the deadline, tell jokes during the break (about office plankton, yes) and conduct other socially attractive activities .

A miniature office robot EMIEW-2 with an original adaptive suspension is able to independently build a room map and find items
Navigation of office robots can be done manually, in standalone or combined modes. For example, the operator can set the final point of arrival, and the robot itself will pave the way for its achievement. After the battery is discharged, it will return to the base for recharging. The autonomous navigation feature will be especially useful in large office spaces.

Scientific research


An autonomous robot must have the ability to make decisions and truthfully play the role intended for it. Today, many scientific institutes conduct research in the field of AI, and the robot becomes a suitable platform for experimental confirmation of scientific hypotheses and development of intelligent algorithms. Tasks, the implementation of which for a person is not difficult, for the robot can be of great difficulty. Recognition of objects, capture and movement of objects, building a map of the room and navigation - there are many unsolved scientific problems in the field of intellectual robotics.

Willow Garage PR2 playing billiards
The most functional and, unfortunately, the most expensive solution for research can be called Willow Garage PR2 model. The used PR2 operating system ROS provides hardware abstractions, device drivers, an extensive set of ready-made libraries, simulators, a package management system, and other tools that allow scientists without inventing bicycles to focus on the research task. Libraries of visual and navigation algorithms, in the development of which the best universities of the world are taking part, are in open access. PR2 already knows how to open doors, fold towels, play billiards and treat you to beer. With the help of the community formed around the PR2, scientists can share their work and receive confirmation of their results in the scientific community.
