Kingston MobileLite Wireless G2 Wireless Card Reader Review

Of course there are others, but since the device, about which the post will be, decides exactly these - let's make them basic. Kingston Mobile Lite Wireless G2 is not the first attempt to cover the immense, it was already the first version of such a device .

I will begin the life story of Kingston Mobile Lite Wireless G2 ( MLWG2 ) with its positioning in the world of money. Yandex.Market says about the price of 3542 rubles per unit. Search on the Internet leads to the price of 3300 rubles .



What%% username gets for this money:

  • Powerbank with a capacity of 4640 mAh
  • Card reader for SD / SDHC / SDXC and microSD / microSDHC / microSDXC memory cards
  • Wi-Fi router
  • Photobank

The package also includes a microUSB cable, which is suitable for charging the built-in battery and for recharging Android devices. Cable for "apples" is not included.

Powerbank


For comparison, similar standalone devices cost from 1000 rubles per piece. A battery of such capacity is enough to charge the iPhone 5 twice (data from the official site of Kingston). The reality is this: the iPhone 6 Plus could charge one time from 20 percent to 100% and one more time from 20 percent to 53%. That is, it is impossible to count on a week-long trip without a mains charger, but MLWG2 can help out during the day perfectly.

Card reader


It's hard to invent something new, but the MLWG2 reads all the existing volumes of memory cards of the stated formats, including the latest 256 GB SDXC and 128 GB microSDXC. The delivery comes with the original microSD-> SD adapter in case you bought a microformat card without it or lost your native adapter.



Wi-Fi router

This is one of the key differences between the first and second generation Mobile Lite Wireless. Thanks to the integrated Ethernet port, you can organize a network of wireless access to the Internet in any place where there is a wired network. In addition, it supports the installation of USB 3G modems, that is, you can arrange a network on the road. Power will be supplied from the built-in battery and you will have to choose - either a powerbank or a 3G Wi-Fi router. The function is triggered simply by long pressing the power button.
