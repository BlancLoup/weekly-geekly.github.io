Where to go? To Switzerland?

Summer, it's time to relax. Just a little bit to keep up with competitors. I started by searching for a good site where you can not only choose a tour according to certain criteria, but also read reviews and see photos. Unfortunately, for me and fortunately for entrepreneurs, there was no high-quality resource on the Ukrainian Internet. But I found a very interesting Internet project in Russia - VeniVidi . This is a tourist social network. The resource is very good, there would be a good advanced search there. Here you have a business idea: a tourist social network in WaNet. Money is easy enough to get out of here, the tourism business is well developed in Ukraine. You can engage in the sale of advertising, mediation in the organization of tours and, finally, to become a tour operator yourself. In my opinion, a very promising direction. I would take it, but also so full of work.

Let's return to our sheep, namely to the choice of tours. I do not like the rest in the style of "beach, sea, sunbathing". I am more attracted to the mountains, extreme rest and so on. After a painful search, I decided to stop at Switzerland. Holidays in Switzerland are offered by two leading operators, the Old Yard and SodiS . The old courtyard specializes in Switzerland, so the choice was on it. For $ 1000 you can relax and pick up a tour for every taste.

Maybe someone knows a good place to relax, share your impressions, advise something else. Thank you all in advance.
