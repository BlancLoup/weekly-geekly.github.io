Movies winners of the New York City Drone Film Festival 2016



The New York City Drone Film Festival was founded in 2014 by director, landscape photographer and aerial cameraman Randy Scott Slavin. This project provides a platform for the demonstration of films of aerial cinematographers. Slavin says that he has launched a film festival of films made with camera drones, since he has no place to share his experience. More importantly, it is an opportunity to evaluate such tapes.

The mission of the New York City Drone Film Festival is to help in the development of cinematic art with the help of unmanned aerial vehicles. This year the event turned out to be quite successful, as can be seen by watching the films of the winners :
