Hi, SaaS | Russian SaaS 2016 - results



Traditional subjective and expert post about the Russian SaaS - the results of 2016. In 2016, I fought with the Romain and won , and looked at SaaS with one eye and that's what I saw.

Sentence


Offering cloud services rolls over. In 2016, the respected site startpack.ru added 552 services. SaaS has ceased to be something incomprehensible to consumers.

I recall that in its Cloud Computing Hype Cycle diagram (cloud maturity cycle) for 2015, Gartner placed SaaS at the Plateau of Productivity point, a plateau of productivity, and this is the final stage of development at which the technology is applied everywhere.

According to bcsg.com, 60% of SMB companies already use SaaS and 78% of respondents plan to buy SaaS in 2017. These are indicators of a survey of 600 companies, and I think metrics can be projected onto the Russian market with some kind of insignificant reduction factor. UPD - A decreasing use factor of about 0.8 compared to current survey data on iksmedia.ru. In a rather conservative segment of medicine according to our research ( Dentalcloud ), 60% of dentists are already using cloud services and the most popular service, attention is Google Forms.

Trends


Most development companies take understandable approaches to development - development of a platform (1C) or API and integration. An interesting case of the year - vendors of integrations appear on the Russian market. For example, UniSender and Bitrix24 Pinall microservice integration declares integration as an independent product and talks about thousands of installations.

A global example of an API service like SaaS - Enrichment API, which is interesting from the point of view of implementing the API itself - it can be used without programming skills. The essence of the service is not obvious, from the point of view of value - search, collection and addition of contact data from everywhere.

Last year, the development of niche stories became apparent. So for the 2015-2016 year in the segment of the automation of dentistry, in which I play with DentalTap , 5 new players have appeared. At the same time, the traditional leaders of the segment are still not in a hurry to go to the cloud, which is very strange.

Newbies


2016 was not without surprises - the global market for tight integration with Google left a service for organizing workflow processes Kanbanchi , which is being developed by Saratov web-studio Lar Team. The application is used by 165 thousand people and it is developed on the API "Google Drive". Fine, really!

Now big players are playing in the field of BPaaS - Rostelecom has been actively promoting this kind of service on thematic events throughout the year. The national cloud platform O7 has been enriched with the offer of virtual infrastructure and nothing new has emerged from SaaS.

Opinions


As usual, I invited the best cloud colleagues - market participants who perfectly showed events and trends in SaaS for 2016.

Katerina Kostereva
CEO Terrasoft
According to IDS, SaaS is growing 5 times faster than the traditional software market. According to Gartner, this year in the world it will grow by 20% compared with the previous period, and this trend will continue for the next 3-5 years. According to our observations, the Russian SaaS market does not lag behind the world trends, and this year it is even slightly ahead of them, showing an increase of more than 25%. Terrasoft cloud service sales growth only confirms these trends.

In addition, the enormous development of the technology itself is affecting the cloud market. Intelligent business processes, predictive analytics tools, integration with IoT, big data - all of these technologies require powerful computing resources and the ability to quickly scale to meet the ever-growing demands of the business. Of course, only the largest enterprises can afford to support the necessary infrastructure locally. While the cloud provides the company with the necessary resources, removing the headache regarding server performance and availability, security, speed, and many other similar issues.

Alexey Fedorov
CEO startpack.ru
Using SaaS in the work becomes commonplace. Users do not need classic arguments about the benefits of the cloud approach, their attention is shifting to the study of consumer properties of products and the search for reasons why you should stay in the current service or why you should change it to another. The trend of 2016 is SaaS solutions for corporate chats and bots. Bots will continue to improve in the direction of artificial intelligence. Their development will be in demand in corporate helpdesk, customer support, personal assistants and concierge services.

The market is oversaturated with services that solve universal tasks that are not related to a specific industry: CRM, task management, cloud storage. Each category has its own hard-to-reach leaders, and in the total mass it is difficult to distinguish one service from another - the opportunities provided are identical. It is impossible to stand out or compete in such an environment, therefore more and more services appear that specialize in certain business sectors: CRM for a dental clinic, task management for a courier company, etc.

The direction of narrow specialization will develop deeper, down to atomic app-s created by teams of 2-3 people, SaaS-add-ons and add-ons, and even just a bare API. Mobile First's SaaS strategy will also be affected by specialization, the availability of a mobile application that replicates the capabilities of the web version of the service is no longer enough, mobile versions of services will expand the functionality of mobile devices. The narrow specialization of services is a consequence of the proliferation of the SaaS market, where the blue ocean strategy is the only way to survive.
