MODX Club in Moscow

So, immediately about the main thing: I announce the opening of the MODX-club in Moscow :-)

We will be based in the anti-cafe YoStudio . The place is simply wonderful! We will have our own room for 25 people, large tables (which can be arranged as it will be more convenient), Wi-Fi. In general, everything you need. In the photo, the hall looks naked, but this is just a bad photo. There are windows and a closet with books and stationery, as well as a stand for presentations.

The proposed activities are of the following format:
  • Training seminars for end users (basic management of MODX sites, etc.)
  • Advanced training courses for MODX programmers
  • Just pleasant and useful meetings to chat about your favorite System.

The first meeting will be held this Monday, that is, on January 28 at 16 o'clock.



The financial side of the issue: being on the territory of the anti-cafe is paid, but the price is quite insignificant - 2 rubles / minute. At the same time, you are not limited to just visiting our events. There are game rooms with board games and other little things in order to just relax.

Once again I will clarify: this will be a permanent place of our club, that is, there we will gather constantly. How often - will depend on attendance. If there are many people willing, we will meet at least every day, there is an agreement. I will bring an iMac so that examples of MODX can be considered objectively, but it is advisable to come with my favorite laptop so that no one takes away the mouse ;-)

In general, we are waiting for everyone! :-)

I'd love to hear questions, suggestions, etc. Everything will be necessarily considered.

UPD For those who still do not know what MODX is : it is such a very powerful platform for developing web projects. Who wants to know more about it, just come, I will tell everything in detail and show it.

UPD2 Who plans to go exactly, immediately unsubscribe in the comments, since we can take 30 people at a time as much as possible.

UPD3 Tomorrow (01/31/2013) next meeting .
