Atari 2600 Emulator - Stella


A long time ago I played with my cousins ‚Äã‚Äãin the clone Atari 2600 (pirate without cartridges, but who then had a crap) brought from Germany. At that time it was something amazing, but then Dendy, Sega (all also pirated) appeared and, finally, a computer on which all this is wonderfully emulated. Recently I remembered Atari and I want to tell you about its emulator - Stella

Stella is an OpenSource emulator whose builds are available for Linux / Windows / Mac systems. It is also ported to run on J2ME, PSP, Dingoo A320 and a bunch of platforms and consoles. I use it on Linux, since the build is in the Mandriva PLF repository, but even if there is no package for your distribution, there is nothing complicated in the build from the sources.

The main window of the emulator in combination is a window for selecting rum. Unfortunately, the interface is not standard, like most GTK applications. He borrowed from another great project - ScummVM. Google says that there is a frontend for KDE - Kstella , but I have not watched and tested it.

The user has a lot of settings. For some reason, Stella with the OpenGL renderer did not want to work for me (in other applications / emulators / games, everything is fine), saying ERROR: Unable to open SDL window: I could not experience the video effects, I would like to. Okay, let's be content with Software, it will be even closer to the original.

With the setting of the gamepad, I did not find any problems, it wasn‚Äôt even necessary. All 4 directions and the whole one button worked by default. Unless you can bind Start and Reset to any gamepad buttons.

In the rest of the settings, nothing particularly interesting.
If we want to play fullscreen, but press Alt + Enter during the game, but do not forget to go back, otherwise the unpleasant resolution of 640x480 awaits us after closing the toy.
Although the console is already more than 30 years old, the emulator is still being developed (the last commit in SVN is 3 days ago).
The emulator supports zazipovanye roms, but at 7z swears, which is pretty annoying, because all the Roma on Good2600 are clamped in 7z.

Boxing
Simple boxing, you can play together. It took me 10 years to understand that these are not two figures 3, but two boxers with a top view.
