Text translation Apple Special Event (broadcast is over, all new at the end of the post)

In the small presentation hall of the company campus in Cupertino , today, Apple will show some new items. New iPhones and iPads are expected, but to say for sure what they will show, as always is impossible.

For favorite readers of Geektimes this evening we will hold a text translation of the event. Those interested are encouraged to add this post to their bookmarks in order to return to it at exactly 20.00 Moscow time.

In general, the presentation was initially expected on March 15, but Apple moved the event for almost a week and chose March 21. The press has received invitations and there is definitely a hint in their design - on the image you can see the colors of the product cases: gray, silver, gold and pink gold.

So what do we expect to see today?


iPhone 5SE


5SE can mean 5S Enhanced. But why confuse shoppers with quintuples when the sixth iPhone life cycle is on? Rumors speak of the name of the iPhone SE, Special Edition.


Amateur renders of putative devices

Little iPad Pro


This is only a characteristic, not a real name. Other
