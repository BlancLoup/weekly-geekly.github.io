National Geographic has released a hard drive with all editions of the magazine 1888-2009 and bonuses



Of the entire mass of TV channels that are now available, I watch only 2-3. These are National Geographic, Viasat HIstory and sometimes Discovery. If it were not for these channels, probably there would not be a TV at home. At National Geographic especially a lot of interesting programs, and the magazine itself is very interesting. True, it is not always possible to view and read the materials of National Geographic. But today I accidentally read the news that National Geographic put on sale a 160 GB hard drive, with all the issues of its magazine (1888-2009) and a lot of bonuses.

Bonuses include thousands of digitized cards, which are laid out at all for the first time. There is a tool Geobrowse, which helps to find articles, photos and maps related to the selected location.

There are also tests that help find out how well you are oriented in the geography, history, and culture of certain regions.

In addition, the proposed bonus DVD with tips from professional photographers, as well as all sorts of moments from the lives of journalists, who usually remain behind the scenes.

And yes, the price of all this pleasure is about $ 200. ($ 199.95), which is not so much. I myself am thinking about buying all this as a gift for myself for the New Year. It is a pity, of course, that there are issues only until 2009. At least by the beginning of 2012 posted materials, it would be generally super.
