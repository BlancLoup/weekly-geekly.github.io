The main quest of summer: GAMER LIVE 2010

Gamer.ru, who still strongly wants to reach the hearts of Habravchan, is preparing to celebrate a birthday and organizes a very worthy event for gamers in my opinion - Gamer Live 2010. As a bonus, I promise to put a picture of one girl in the comments, which is almost the most important the organizer if you like the idea :)




On warm August weekends, from 6 to 8 August, it is proposed to tear away the soft seats from the cozy chairs and replace laptops, consoles, iota and the rest of the computer artillery with a saturated two-day program outside the home - at the Vympel recreation center 100 km from Moscow.

The organizers promise football competitions (skills acquired on habrafutbol ), basketball, volleyball, tetris, WoW, AION, as well as horseback riding, evening gaming performances, creative competitions and, of course, will be a lot of prizes from sponsors and partners . Not yet hardcore gamers will perhaps rejoice at master classes and trainings, and promise to brighten up the nostalgia for the network and computers with a show match for popular e-sports disciplines. Rumor has it that these disciplines will be Guitar Hero and something more sporting;)

Even at the festival, I noticed that a clan of 10 of the cutest gamers was traveling to please your eyes and just relax. There will be a pool in the Twix advertising. There will be a fire, a guitar and, probably, soundtracks from any Dyuk Nyukem and Mario.

The number of tickets is limited (keep up), details and pictures of the base here (click on spoilers), good luck and good luck to all!
