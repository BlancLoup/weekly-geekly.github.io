DevFest season 2016 in 10 cities of Russia

The DevFest Conference is a global event held worldwide by the Google Developers Group (GDG) communities with the support of Google. As part of this event, a presentation and discussion of the latest technologies and development of Google. This event will be interesting to developers, IT-specialists, students of IT-specialties and everyone who is not indifferent to modern technologies.


This year in Russia there will be a record number of DevFest conferences - as many as 10 ! Therefore, it will be easy for you to find the DevFest running in or near your city.

October 29 Kaliningrad - http://goo.gl/tMPjFH
November 13 Rostov-on-Don - http://goo.gl/thJgLH
November 19 Siberia (Novosibirsk) - http://devfest.gdg.org.ru/

December 10, Nizhny Novgorod - http://devfest.gdgnn.ru
December 10 Krasnoyarsk - http://goo.gl/iiyK1z

Each DevFest is unique in its own way, but each of them will bring together hundreds of participants interested in the most modern technologies and many speakers who will be happy to share their experience.

As always, DevFest conferences will be primarily devoted to the most modern and sought-after Google technologies. This, of course, Android N and Firebase. In addition, the popular topics will be virtual and augmented reality, the Internet of things, machine learning, as well as Go, Angular and other technologies for developers. So stay tuned to the news of the GDG group in your city, so as not to miss one of the coolest IT conferences this year!
