Navdy: Projection Notification System for Gesture Driven Cars



Correctly they say that the safety instructions are written in blood. Of course, this also applies to cars, many of whose owners still continue to write SMS and talk on the phone while driving.

The other day there was information about an interesting notification system for cars, which allows you to communicate without problems while driving, using gestures to control the system itself. Navdy, the so-called device - a projection device with a transparent "display", which displays information such as the current position of the car, the caller's phone, SMS and anything else that might be needed while driving.

In this case, as mentioned above, the system is controlled by gestures. If you need to take a call, for example - lift your thumb up. The system also has voice control (Navdy is synchronized with the voice control system of Android and iOS). So you can set the voice, for example, the direction of movement with a demonstration on Google Maps.



The system projects notifications / messages / map in such a way that the driver does not have to strain himself in order to view the details. At the same time, Navdy is easily installed in any car, from the smallest to the largest.



The system, among other things, shows the current speed of the car, the amount of fuel, the distance that can be traveled on the current fuel stock and other data. True, the device will show this information only if there is a car onboard computer, if you put Navdy in Zaporozhets, for example, the driver will not receive such information.



In general, quite an interesting system, which is another attempt to make the movement in the car more secure, while expanding the capabilities of the driver.

The price of Navdy cannot be called low: now the pre-order for the system will cost $ 299, and a little later - at 499.
