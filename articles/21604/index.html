Architect IE8 will arrive at RIT!

The architect of the latest versions of the Internet Explorer browser, one of the developers of the CSS standard, the legendary Alexander Mogilevsky, will speak at the RIT 2008 conference!

During the hour-long guest speech, Russian web developers will have a unique opportunity to see the latest features of Microsoft Internet Explorer 8, which was presented a couple of days ago at the MIX'08 conference in Las Vegas.

This will be the first presentation of the new version of the most popular browser in Russia. Also, you will have the opportunity to ask any questions to a person who knows what tools for working on the Internet, we will use in the future.

In the comments to this post, you can ask questions to Alex, the best of which I promise to ask during an interview.
