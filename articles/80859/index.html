Underwater Mask



Those who combine diving with underwater photography, know how inconvenient to dive with a camera. Not only that, the extra weight, but also the hands are busy, if you have to drive away the hungry sharks. Chinavasion offers a solution to the problem for only $ 116.99 - a diving mask with a camera. The mask, which is logical, is equipped with a handset, and the camera has 4 GB of memory and takes pictures and videos with a resolution of 1280x960 pixels. True, the quality of photos and frame rates is not known. It's extremely easy to use - rub the magnetic ring for a second to get a photo, and two seconds for video. The vibration of the mask will confirm that the command has been completed.
