Three intrigues for two startups or how to properly blow up your rocket engine


Already two weeks have passed since the explosion of the Atar rocket engine on the tests of the Russian space startup Lin Industrial. In the first days after the incident, the Lin Industrial page was asked to stop spreading gossip and guesswork and wait for the official press release. We did not wait for him, and in the discussions following the traces of the incident some interesting facts came to light.

Required disclaimer


The task of creating your own launch vehicle is very difficult, and I have great respect for people who are trying to solve it. But respect does not exclude bewilderment about strange actions and indications of seemingly obvious mistakes (and let me correct it if this is not so).

For those who are not in the know. On December 11, the first fire tests of the Atar rocket engine were carried out with a load of 100 kgf. But, after working for about four seconds, the engine exploded. The first official information was very vague.

Strange place


In the plot of the TV channel "Russia 24" was shown the scene.

Many media outlets described the incident as the first private liquid rocket engine in Russia. But it is not.
