Submit and Archive

In addition to the two cappuccino, my usual morning includes the processing of new mail that arrived in my mailbox at night. Most often, I reply to a message and want to archive it in order to enjoy the clean inbox. Clicking "Send", then "Archive" several million times, I begin to dream of the existence of a method in which it is enough to press once, and perform both actions at the same time. So I decided to turn this idea into Gmail Labs as an experiment. Turn on "Send and Archive" on the tab "labs" in the settings and you will see a new button with the same name. The button makes an action, the name to which it responds: it allows you to reply and archive the letter in one click.
