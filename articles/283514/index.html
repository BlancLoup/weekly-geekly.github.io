Bushido Mobius: Member Path

The main problem in choosing a technology conference is to separate the wheat from the chaff - the speakers and organizers love biting names that have nothing to do with the essence of the performance. The result is that the reports of the average mobile conference are divided into three categories:

Dmitry dbelob Beloborodov , a regular participant of our conferences, spoke about how to survive at the hardcore conference from the JUG.RU Group. More precisely, he made a real manual (or, if you will, a bushido) for participation in a technical conference:

Before participating in the conference:
  1. View the current list of reports . She is already 95% ready.
  2. After the appearance of an accurate program of reports with parallel tracks, select reports to be viewed during the conference.
  3. Organizers should send a letter with a request to share information about what reports you plan to visit. This information is needed to clarify the size of the rooms for specific reports. The grid of reports after this may be slightly adjusted.
  4. View video reports of previous conferences on the channel JUG.ru on YouTube . Most speakers have previously spoken at other events. The selected new Mobius presentations may be a continuation or similar in scope to previous reports by the same or other speakers.
  5. Subscribe to Habrahabr to the blog of the company JUG.ru Group. The announcements of the conference have already been published, interviews with speakers and news are published.
  6. Subscribe to a @MobiusConf twitter account to track news about the upcoming conference.

During the conference:
  1. If you plan to write a review of the conference, it is recommended to take a camera. The quality of smartphones cameras is not enough to publish, for example, on Habrahabr.
  2. Before the conference, make sure that the grid of reports has not changed unexpectedly. If this happens, adjust the list and procedure for visiting reports.
  3. If the venue of the conference is not familiar, study the layout of the halls for quickly moving from one report to another, coffee breaks, lunch.
  4. Feel free to ask questions to speakers. Relevant, interesting questions complement the report well and are useful both for the audience and for the speaker.

After the conference:
  1. If you have the desire and opportunity to write a review about the conference - do it. It is advisable to illustrate a review with photos taken at the conference. The best place to publish a review is Habrahabr.
  2. View the #mobiusconf hashtag on Twitter for feedback from conference participants.
  3. Rate the reports of the conference on the sent link in a letter from the organizers of the conference.
  4. After a while, Habrahabr should have a review ‚ÄúVideo of the best reports of Mobius 2016‚Äù, which will help you choose the following videos to watch.
  5. Share impressions about the conference with friends and colleagues. If you like the conference, do not hesitate to recommend it to others.
