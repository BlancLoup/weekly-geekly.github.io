Electronic Arts officially opened the company in Russia

Taking into account the growing market of interactive entertainment, the company Electronic Arts (EA) , the world leader in the development and publication of interactive entertainment software, decided to focus its efforts on the support of Russian retail chains and users. In this connection, on July 26, 2007, the opening of the EA company in Russia was officially announced.




The opening of the new EA office in Moscow is another step in implementing the company's development strategy in Central and Eastern Europe and strengthens the global presence of EA in more than 40 markets outside of North America.

EA intends to contribute to the formation and development of the fast-growing market of interactive entertainment in Russia. The Moscow office of EA will focus its efforts on providing Russian users with a wider range of localized games, building connections with the gaming community, and closer cooperation with retailers.

At the event, which took place on July 26, 2007 in Moscow, EA hosted representatives from industry, business and the media and spoke about its plans to bring the best and most recent innovations in the field of interactive entertainment to the Russian market.
