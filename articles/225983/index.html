Here is a photo of the audio equipment in the rack:



In the hall, 6 PA-20T (ROXTON) two-way ceiling speakers are embedded in the suspended ceiling in the 20 W mode. However, as practice has shown, it will be necessary to rewire to 10 W mode, now there is a very big difference in the sound level in the hall and on the street, but there is no separate adjustment by zones in the amplifier. Internal loudspeakers are loaded on the no. 1 warning zone.



Outside there are two outdoor all-weather speakers ROXTON CS-840T with a power of 40 W (notification zone No. 2)



For users (of course, not for everyone, but in accordance with the assigned access rights), the sound system can be controlled in two ways:
  • using a computer in the workplace. VLC player is controlled via web-interface.
  • using a mobile device (phone or tablet) that is running the Android operating system. Uses the VLC remote application. A tablet or phone is connected to the school network via a WiFi access point.


Files are replenished into the media library (a network folder on a media server with the appropriate rights assigned) by users themselves (they already know how to do it, hurray - two years have passed for good reason!).

For filing voice messages was the idea to use Zello, but the delay was more than 1 second, and this option had to be abandoned.
