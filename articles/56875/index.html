Web services monetization: business models

We spent a few hours exploring Webware 100 Top Web Apps 2008 online services and studying their business models. The following diagram shows the results of the study - 34% of services use an advertising model, 12% different subscription schemes, 8% sell virtual goods (most often in the form of data for download), the same share of services offers related products (usually large development companies offer a free option product to attract to their platform) and another 8% of services use a ‚Äúpay per use‚Äù scheme.

Meta models
