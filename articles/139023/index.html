The second long-awaited Dropquest 2012 from Dropbox.com



Good day to all, though a couple of topics have already been written about the quest, I want to remind everyone about this event, to say something, and more ...

The main thing:
Page DropQuest2012 (Not valid until the start of the quest) .
Beginning today, on Saturday, May 12, 17:00 GMT (21:00 Moscow time and 20:00 Kyiv time).

Remember the prizes:

1st place (1 participant) - a hooded sweatshirt from Dropbox, a T-shirt from Dropbox from Hack Week, a picture drawn by the entire Dropbox team, an invitation to help design the next Dropquest, 100 GB of additional space in Dropbox for life.
2nd place (10 participants) - a Dropbox hoodie, a Dropbox T-shirt, 20 GB of extra space for life.
3rd place (15 participants) - T-shirt from Dropbox, 5 GB of extra space for life.
4th place (50 participants) - 2 GB of additional space for life.
5th place (100 participants) - 1 GB of additional space for life.

In any case, all participants who have completed the quest will receive a guaranteed + 1GB for your box. If you take the 5th place, then you will get 1 + 1GB.

You can read more about DropQuest 2012 here!

According to the authors, the current DropQuest will be more difficult than last year, and they hint that you cannot count on an easy victory. Also, the administration promises to prevent injections of answers to the forum and their blog. But the entire network is not monitored.

As always, there is a lively discussion in IRC conference irc.synIRC.net port 6667, the #HDB channel in which you will receive a small surprise and help for the quest.

The fastest will be rewarded with valuable prizes. Well, we stock up on poporna, coffee, and check our ingenuity.

In the meantime, we are waiting for the quest, you can see how it was in the previous year.
