Your curious girl can unlock your Iphone 5s with your finger while you sleep


Lock your phone by access code is still alive. Access to the Iphone 5 through the fingerprint recognition system can be obtained even when you are sleeping. This means that a jealous partner can press your phone to your finger while you sleep and read all your messages, view the list of calls, email and so on.

Apple confirmed that the dead finger does not work. Slowing the victim, of course, is possible, but international spies will not be able to cut off the finger of Prime Minister of the Minister in order to gain access to his secret and personal files. Apple also does not store copies of fingerprints on the server, instead it stores them in the secret zone of the A7 processor, which was specifically designed to be inaccessible to hackers and other applications.


Apple did a great job in making the Touch ID protection system easy to use. Therefore, the Iphone 5s can be easily unlocked by your cat , toe, or even ... any part of your body if it is in the base of the phone. Despite this, the real problem is that the Touch ID does not store information about whether someone has used it.

Come on, come to your senses. You can wake up after drinking and see your friends post messages to your ex and creatively rewrite your Facebook profile.

It is not news to hear stories about suspicious girls or guys who got access to the phones of their boys / girls while they were sleeping, found their correspondence with someone else and left them. A digital access code would protect them from this.

Touch ID is vulnerable to "hacking on a dream."

As soon as someone finds out which finger (s) you have registered in the Touch ID system, they can take your phone off the bedside table, lean your sleeping finger on the screen, and voila, the phone is unlocked.

If you have dubious information in your phone, you need ... not to have dubious information in your phone. After all, if someone close to them will search your phone while you are sleeping, you will have big problems. But if you have to sleep with suspicious individuals, you should go into the settings, activate the lock by access code and remove the fingerprints that were previously entered.

In fact, it all comes down to the fact that there is not a single access system that is both convenient and 100% reliable. No character sets, no face recognition, no fingerprint system.
