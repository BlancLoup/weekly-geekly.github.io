In this series of articles, we will describe how you received accreditation, how you can get it yourself and share with you our developments. We in Begat want the development of not only us, but also the entire industry as a whole, since competition creates good products.

The first article will talk about the administrative procedures that will need to be performed to obtain the status of an accredited registrar. In the second article we will tell and share our developments for integration with the CC (Coordination Center).





Why we became a registrar and what guided us


Before we received accreditation, we registered domains .RU / . through different registrars: the main supplier was reg.ru , we also worked with r01 and nic.ru. Some of our domains are still serviced by these registrars. In addition, we work with these registrars on domains in areas other than .RU / .. If the client wanted to transfer the domain to us for service, we went to meet him and transferred him to our personal account of the current domain registrar.

This created a large number of problems both for us and for our clients. There were cases of delimitation of domains, about which we had to learn from clients, errors on the part of recorders software. The very support of three different APIs created difficulties. As for domain prices, our partner prices were only about 10% higher than the price that the Coordination Center offered, so this was not the main reason for becoming an independent registrar ...

The main reason is the complete management of the domain name by our company and the lack of dependence of us and our users on possible failures and errors on the part of our partners.

The first attempt to apply for accreditation was in the summer of 2014, but then, unfortunately, in connection with other matters (at that time we were actively planning to enter the international market, there will be a second attempt ahead =), the folder with the documents remained just a folder with the name "Registrar Project".

The second time we decided to become a registrar in .RU / . zones at the beginning of 2016, when it was reported that the transfer of domains between registrars would become paperless. Of course, we were extremely disappointed that, unlike international zones, our focal point of national domains did not adhere to the same scheme of work, and the money is taken only for the transfer, and not for the extension at the moment of transfer (which is clearly not conducive to market liberalization ). It can be said that it was impossible to do so due to the fact that the maximum term for registering a domain in .RU / . zones is 1 year, but what prevents to make it longer?

Nevertheless, the transfer procedure between registrars has become easier, which means that those who wish can transfer domains to us, and since we have already started studying the question of how to become a registrar for the second time, we decided to finish the job.

What you need to do to get accreditation


Domain zones .RU / . regulates the Coordination Center of the national domain of the Internet (https://cctld.ru/) .

To date, 46 registrars are accredited ( https://cctld.ru/ru/registrators/ ), and it is noteworthy that 13 of them, including us, were accredited last year. Separately, I would like to congratulate our colleagues in the field of hosting, who have received accreditation recently - this is a step in the right direction. Perhaps other companies, like us, have decided to do this due to a change in the policy of domain transfer between registrars.

All information on how to get accreditation can and should be found on their website - https://cctld.ru/ru/docs/

We read requirements to the future registrar and we correspond to them.


An accreditation can be obtained by a legal entity of the Russian Federation having a working office and a minimum number of employees to work as a registrar. It will be necessary to prove (confirm) the coordinating center a stable financial position, the ability to ensure the sustainability of the software when working with the Registry and data backup.
And also: insure activities as a registrar and prepare a prototype of the site, which will contain all the information regarding future work as a registrar.

Fill out the form and prepare a package of documents




If you feel the strength and ability to meet all the requirements described, you need to open the Accreditation Agreement ( https://cctld.ru/ru/docs/project/accr_polo_21042014.pdf ), find point 2 and prepare a package of documents in strict accordance with the list and then send it to the mailing address of the Coordination Center.
If formally all signs are observed and the documents correspond to the above list, then the Coordination Center will send you an accreditation bill within 3 days after receiving the package of documents. On the day we receive accreditation, the cost of checking your competence to register domain names was 80,000 rubles. This amount is not refundable, even if the Coordination Center will refuse to accredit you.

  • We pay the bill for the accreditation check.
  • We are waiting for verification of documents and compliance with all requirements.

During the verification process, the Coordination Center will contact you and, possibly, point out some flaws in the documents or in the questionnaire. Documents are checked within a month.

We get a decision on accreditation or rejection



When you receive a refusal, please note that you can re-apply only after 3 months. If you are lucky and the Coordination Center has made a positive decision about your accreditation, then you will be invited to sign the Accreditation Agreement. This Agreement must be signed within 20 days from the date of the decision on accreditation.
