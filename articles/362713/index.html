New children's watches from AT & T

My post ‚ÄúOn health: 5 gadgets for the little ones‚Äù can now be supplemented with one more novelty: AT & T smart watches designed specifically for children. Or rather, for their parents.




We are talking about the device FiLIP 2, an improved version of FiLIP. One of the advantages is the ability to replace one link from a bracelet (with a lock) with a link of a different size in order to more precisely fit the small owner to the arm.



The watch is available in four colors: blue, orange, red and green.



What about technical specifications? The bracelet is equipped with a robust 1.8 "inch display with a resolution of 128x128 pixels, which, according to the manufacturer, will now proudly endure any bullying on the playground. The 390 mAH battery should be enough for 50 minutes of conversations with mom and dad, for which, by the way, headset required, or 48 hours standby.

The device works with the usual SIM card in GSM / EDGE 850-1900 MHz and UMTS / HSPA 850-1900 MHz networks, it is also equipped with aGPS and WiFi modules. It is compatible with iOS 6 and above and Android 2.3 and above.

The cost of such a gadget, which is also waterproof, is only 99.99 dollars. And for the connection will have to pay 10 bucks a month.
