IDeco gateway specifically for tablet computers

Moscow, April 1, 2011 - Aydeko presents a revolutionary concept - the world's first Internet gateway, designed specifically for tablet computers. It is planned that the first release will take place exclusively for Apple iPad models.





According to a recent study by analysts Gartner, in the period from 2010 to 2015, the year sales of tablet computers will grow from 17.6 to 326.3 million units. Today, this trend is one of the strongest and most interesting for the global market for computer devices, analysts say.

The Aydeko company, trying to always keep up with the times and often overtaking it, has developed a unique concept - the iDeco internet gateway specifically for tablets.

Use iDeco - manage your time efficiently

The problem of inappropriate use of their working time is a serious question for owners of tablet computers. With the release of new levels for Angry Birds, they actually become hostages of this undoubtedly ingenious game. The iDeco gateway allows you to completely block access to similar applications during business hours. Disable this function is possible only in the evening. Blocking Angry Birds at work is a sure way to boost.

With iDeco, control Internet traffic everywhere

The iDeco gateway will enable the owners of tablet devices not only to distribute the Internet from the gadget (subject to a 3G connection), but also to control its consumption. With our new application, the safe distribution of the Internet to friends in the woods near the fire is done with a single click.

Keep secrets with iDeco

Very often, the preservation of human secrets determines a lot: from family happiness to career advancement. The iDeco gateway allows you to hide any secrets from the owner of the tablet from unwanted viewing: photos from the bar, private correspondence with friends and much more.
