QIWI company buys Money Mail.ru payment service

The QIWI group announced its intention to purchase the Money Mail.ru payment service owned by the Mail.ru Group. At the same time, according to the representative of QIWI Xenia Vasilyeva, this is not a purchase in its usual sense, but the placement of QIWI payment instruments on Mail.ru sites.
