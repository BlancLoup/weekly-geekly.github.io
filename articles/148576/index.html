Botiful - miniature telepresence robot



Now in most regions of our planet there is no problem in a couple of seconds to contact a person located thousands of kilometers away. This is facilitated by the development of network technologies and the miniaturization of gadgets. Now, even most entry-level smartphones can be used as a means of video communication, provided there is a high-speed wireless network, of course. As a result of this rapid development of network technologies, various devices began to appear, providing telepresence.

Some of these devices are very expensive, others are more or less available. But soon the smallest telepresence robot in the world, the Botiful bot, may appear on the market for such devices.
