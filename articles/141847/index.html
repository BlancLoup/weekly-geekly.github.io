Published video reports of the conference WebProfessionals



The WebProfessionals Conference is Microsoft's largest regional web development conference. The conference was held on April 3 in the city of Samara, gathering more than 300 participants from around the region and about 6,000 online viewers. The conference discussed the latest innovations in the stack of Microsoft web technologies: from solutions based on its own architecture based on IIS to cloud technologies based on Windows Azure .

An important part of the conference was reports on the actual use of Microsoft technologies in practice from large Russian companies. During the conference, Visual Studio 11 and other development tools were presented, solutions from partners that allow you to create effective web projects.

We are pleased to announce that the video of the conference reports is already available for free online viewing .
