Auto.ru wants to close 55 sites with similar names

Web Studio LLC Meta Dizayn became the defendant in a lawsuit of the company Avto.ru LLC owned by Yandex. The lawsuit was filed on January 13, 2015.

The plaintiff requires the closure of 55 websites owned by Meta Design LLC and to seek compensation from the defendant. All sites are named after the same pattern XXXauto.ru, where instead of XXX the numbers are usually indicated: 04auto.ru, 06auto.ru, 07auto.ru and so on.
