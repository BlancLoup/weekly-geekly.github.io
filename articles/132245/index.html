LG Optimus Smartphones and Upgrades to Ice Cream Sandwich (Android 4.0) and Gingerbread (Android 2.3)

Hi, Habr! Hello, friends!

By numerous requests, we summarized all the information on the release of firmware for Ice Cream Sandwich (Android 4.0) and Gingerbread (Android 2.3) on our LG Optimus smartphones.

Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich and LG Optimus smartphones.



Important : recent news about the fact that LG Optimus 2X will not be updated to the new version of Android OS (4.0 or Ice Cream Sandwich) is misinformation and is NOT true .

We are currently in the process of planning an upgrade for the Ice Cream Sandwich (4.0) firmware for the LG Optimus 2X, as well as for the other flagships of the LG Optimus (2X; Black; 3D) line.

Detailed information about the schedule of Android OS upgrades to the Ice Cream Sandwich (4.0) firmware version for specific models Optimus will be announced after the public release of the firmware for all manufacturers takes place (currently 4.0 firmware is available on exclusive rights for only one model).

Android 2.3 Gingerbread and LG Optimus smartphones.



We express our sincere gratitude to all owners of Optimus smartphones, who patiently waited and waited for the Android-smartphones to be updated to the Android 2.3 version of the Gingerbread firmware.

We are sincerely pleased to see such a high interest in the improvement of our products, which was shown by the owners of the LG Optimus series, and we take all possible measures to offer a timely and smooth transition to the new version of the mobile OS.

Dates of the release of updates of the version Android 2.2 to 2.3 in Russia and the CIS:

LG Optimus 2X: the end of November - the beginning of December (11/28/11 - 12/09/11)

LG Optimus 3D : the second half of December (12/19/11 - 12/27/11)

LG Optimus Black : the second half of December (12/19/11 - 12/27/11)

We appreciate your time and attention, and we are very sorry that such a delay has occurred. At the moment, we are doing everything we can to ensure that the current firmware comes out on time, and we are working to ensure quality service for smartphones in the future.

Once again we apologize.

All additional updates on firmware will be promptly published on our pages on Facebook , Vkontakte , Odnoklassniki , Twitter .

And also on our website.

Anticipating questions - information on updating the LG Optimus One (P500) to ICS from HQ has not yet been received. As soon as the information is available, we immediately relay it to all sns and not just the channels.

Thank you very much.

Respectfully,
LG team.
