Half of the reports of TechEd 2012 conference have been published, wait for the second half after // BUILD /

It is very important for us that the participants of the conference know the contents of the conference as early as possible for planning their time and participation. At the same time, our goal is to provide the latest information and announcements to TechEd.
On October 30, at the headquarters of Microsoft in Redmond, the global BUILD 2012 conference will take place, the speakers and organizers of which will speak at TechEd Russia and which will significantly influence the program TechEd Russia 2012.




Great news for all Russian specialists - speakers and organizers of the BUILD conference will present on TechEd Russia 2012 with their presentations on key topics - Windows 8, Windows Phone and Windows Azure.

Windows 8 will be covered by Drew Robbins - director of the client operating systems and tools team at Microsoft. At Microsoft, as director of a team of experts in client operating systems and software development tools, Drew Robbins helps developers use the latest technologies to create the best Windows applications. Drew has repeatedly spoken around the world to developers, telling how to use Visual Studio to develop platforms such as Windows, Windows Phone, and Windows Azure.

Windows Phone will be highlighted by a world-renowned expert, author of popular books and Windows Phone product manager at Microsoft - Larry Lieberman . Lari Lieberman is the product manager for the Windows Phone Application Platform. Lari coordinates the creation and publication of all kinds of materials on the development of applications and games for Windows Phone, including training documentation for developers, labs, videos, speeches, etc. Lari is an invaluable source of information about Microsoft's new mobile platform, and he will be happy to share this information at TechEd Russia.

From a Windows Azure point of view, a unique composition of key experts and key development managers will arrive in Russia at TechEd Russia, starting with Scott Guthrie and Mark Russinovich, whom we have already written about, as well as the legendary Dmitry Robsman - Director for Development, Windows Azure Application Platform and Slava Kavsan - Development Manager Windows Azure Security.

In order to simultaneously give you detailed information about the conference program and ensure the availability of the most recent and relevant reports that cannot be announced before the BUILD conference, we publish half of the sessions for your information. We still do not fully publish the tracks on Windows 8 and Windows Phone, as they will be especially connected with the new reports presented at BUILD. Also, for the time being, round tables and laboratory works are published in a limited volume.
The publication of the final conference schedule and the launch of the schedule builder is expected in early November immediately after the BUILD conference.
