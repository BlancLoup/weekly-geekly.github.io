How I went to Italy to do research on Computer Science

How did I find out about the University of Trento?


There was a great desire to study abroad. I was twice in the United States on the program WORK & TRAVEL and I think that this has largely changed my outlook. So, I studied, like many other IT-students, in the Ufa State Aviation Technical University, where I received bachelor's and master's degrees. How did I get a grant for the student?

A resume should always be honed and ready to be thrown into battle. As a result of my correspondence with the professor, he gave me a link to the grant (research in the field of social networks). To receive it, I had to create a certain package of documents and send it all by mail to the university secretariat. After the documents were sent by me, it was necessary to wait about 20 days to receive a response.
Finally, in early September, I received a letter of happiness saying that I had won this grant and that they were waiting for me at the University of Trento. My joy knew no bounds. At work, I immediately warned the management that in about 1.5 months I was leaving (this was how much I took to prepare the documents, including the visa and tickets).
As a result, I arrived in Trento on October 19th, from where I write this post.





What am I doing here?


At the moment I am not a PhD student (graduate student) of this university - I have a contract for six months, during which I will conduct research in the field of social networks and related fields and, if everything goes well, then I become a full-fledged PhD student. I deal with the fact that I read scientific articles on this topic, analyze what the world has already achieved in this area, put forward hypotheses, trying to understand what else can be applied, or how one can apply already existing knowledge.
