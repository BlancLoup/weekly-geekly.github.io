Drupal nostalgia

Do you know that according to historical data - nostalgia was considered a disease?
Since the term nostalgia appeared in 1688 (wikipedia) , a lot of time has passed and now the concept and feeling has been slightly rethought by us.

According to logic, nostalgia is a sense of reality with a sense of grief about the past.
This past is not always good, we can nostalgic for not quite positive events of the past.

Often the reason for this is the desire to correct past mistakes by actions in the future.
But, more often, we are nostalgic for the good events that have caused these or other positive feelings.

As a consequence of past events, we have additional experience; at the heart of our perception of the world, new fundamental building blocks have appeared, on which we rely now and in the future.

And it can be called the word "cool."
