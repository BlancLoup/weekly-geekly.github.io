Make Me Passport - the story of one idea

On the one hand, this is the main conceptual component of any project, on the other - it alone does not mean anything at all. It remained to resolve the issue, in fact, the printing of passport books and their further personification. The stated goal was highly presumptuous: souvenir passports should not differ from real passports in terms of the quality of materials or other visible signs. We have been looking for a suitable printing house for a long time. Somewhere they could not make the covers like a real passport, somewhere they did not want to sew a book with threads.
