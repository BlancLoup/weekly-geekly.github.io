Invest Fest - money is waiting!

One and a half summer months in St. Petersburg was SumIT - this is:
  • free coworking for projects
  • startup university
  • meeting-place IT-Schnick
  • platform for attracting money to startups



It's time to attract investment!

On August 24, a final event of the summer start-up marathon will be held at SPSU ITMO - Fest Invest, to which we invited not only investment funds and leading IT companies: Runa Capital, Almaz, ABRT, VTB, Mail.ru Group, EMC, Intel, RVC, RSV Venture Partners , but also real private investors. Igor Rubenovich Agamirzyan, General Director of RVC, will come as a special guest.

Now it's up to you: only the application and our experts, who will select only the top 10 projects, separate you from the opportunity to attract investments . We will invite them to a closed working session with investors. Where each project manager will have the opportunity not only to talk about his startup, but also to work with all interested investors.

The application must be submitted before August 22, 21:00.

The remaining projects will also receive a chance to attract investments, speaking at a public session, which will be held that evening at 19:00. About 15 projects will be selected for the public session.
