New Sony eBooks



Judging by the images appeared on the network, Sony has completed the development of two new models of electronic readers: PRS-300 and PRS-600.

The PRS-300 (red) is distinguished by a small screen diagonal - 5 inches, can not play music and does not support flash cards. The company offers to write the entire library to the built-in memory, which is 440 megabytes.



The PRS-600 (silver) screen is six-inch, has audio output and support for memory cards. The volume of the built-in memory of this reader is 380 megabytes.

The expected module Wi-Fi did not receive the book. With the screen backlight system at Sony also decided not to strain.

Readers will be available in red, silver and black versions. When the sales start is unknown, but the first news from Sony will be able to buy the citizens of Canada, the United States, England, Germany, Holland and France.

The price of readers is still unknown, but it can be assumed that it will be no higher than 300-400 dollars.
