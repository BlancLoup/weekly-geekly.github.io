IoT, AI systems and network technologies on VMware EMPOWER 2019 - we continue to broadcast from the scene

We are talking about new products presented at the VMware EMPOWER 2019 conference in Lisbon (we also broadcast in our Telegram channel ).




Revolutionary network solutions


One of the main topics of the second day of the conference was intelligent traffic routing.

Global data networks (WAN) are rather unstable. Users often connect to corporate IT infrastructure from mobile devices through public hot spots, which entails certain security risks. Therefore, companies are forced to apply additional protective measures: redundancy, load balancing, encryption. Software-defined networks help solve these problems.

One of the most significant events of the day was the review of the VeloCloud network SD-WAN solution. VeloCloud is a provider of cloud-defined, distributed networks acquired by VMware last year . The organization offers a custom solution that automatically routes, encapsulates, monitors and restricts traffic.

The system includes a software platform and devices installed along the perimeter of the corporate network (in the branches of companies, data centers, etc.). In the latter case, we are talking about VeloCloud Edge hardware. They allow you to build tunnels of various types: Branch-to-Branch, Branch to SaaS, Branch to DC, there is also support for legacy-networks through IPsec.

The system analyzes the type of transmitted packets and the performance of network channels. Based on these data, the most appropriate channel is dynamically selected or a decision is made to aggregate several of them. VeloCloud SD-WAN separates the control and transmission levels, which are responsible for the logic of the network devices and the traffic broadcast. This approach makes the network more productive and flexible.

The capabilities of SD-WAN by VeloCloud are impressive - during the tests, the system reduced the average ping by three times and solved the problem of packet loss during a UDP connection via LTE. Representatives of VMware claim that you can now forget about the defects in voice and video communication, if at least one channel (LTE or Fiber Channel) has sufficient bandwidth.

The work of the solution in the production environment has already been evaluated by large organizations. For example, in February of this year, the SD-WAN by VeloCloud system was introduced in Singapore Telecommunications Company SP Telecom. It successfully routes traffic on a network connected to more than one hundred data centers and commercial buildings connected by a million kilometers of optical fiber. SD-WAN users also include Redmond , Deutsche Telekom and Triton .

IoT on the pulse


The company actively joins the growing segment of corporate IoT. In this area, VMware has the following goals:

  • Simplify the management of individual smart devices and their clusters due to centralized administration, customizable templates, detailed and timely information about failures (and not only).
  • Increase the security of IoT infrastructure, especially at critical corporate sites. As examples, the speakers cited the experience of VMware with railway companies and medical institutions.
  • Allow partner companies to implement a flexible and scalable infrastructure without the need to involve specialized administrators.
  • Implement native integration with various types of cloud environments.

The company will try to achieve all these goals using its IoT infrastructure management platform - Pulse 2.0. It is a solution for monitoring, administering and protecting Internet of Things devices. The purpose of Pulse is to simplify the management of large-scale IoT-based infrastructure through a RESTful API and more advanced update procedures ‚Äúover the air‚Äù.
