DotNext Conference returns to Moscow - with new forces and updated name

This is an announcement for all .NET-programmers who are interested in what is happening with their platform.

December 11 in Moscow will be held. NET-conference DotNext. The most important .NET-conference in Russian. And the biggest konfa for dotnetchikov in Russia. Well, in general, it seems like the biggest .NET-konf in Europe: yes, there are more large-scale conferences, but in order to make it so that we can do it - perhaps we are the largest, yes. Last year, 400 participants gathered in Moscow (together with the speakers and organizers - 450) and about the same number watched the conference online. This year the conference will be even bigger - we want to gather 500-600 people at the venue.



TTDTH! (Only technical reports, only hardcore!)

Traditionally on DotNext much attention will be paid to the insides of the platform itself. Just look at the first published reports: Karlen szKarlen Simonyan will talk about atomic operations and the Memory Model, and Andrey DreamWalker Akinshin will give a report on micro-optimizations.



No cost and without applied (and sometimes funny) reports. For example, Dmitry mezastel Nesteruk from JetBrains will talk about how to write his bot on the dotnet.



In general, reports will be about 25 pieces, each lasting 50-60 minutes. If you want to try yourself as a speaker - submit an application !

  • talk with you about your experience using their products (ReSharper, dotTrace, dotMemory, etc.)
  • find interesting developers in their offices - Petersburg and Moscow
