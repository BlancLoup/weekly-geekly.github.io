Charge four devices from the cigarette lighter at the same time!



If you have a GPS, iPhone, iPod, MP3 player ... Or another gadget capable of receiving power via USB, then it is very likely that you want to charge them in your car from the cigarette lighter socket. It's no secret that there are appropriate devices for this, but they are not perfect. Many of them occupy the cigarette lighter and, until you pull them out, it will not be possible to smoke from it. Yes, and USB-connectors on them is not enough ...

But this device should solve this problem. It contains four USB connectors and a standard car cigarette lighter.

Features:
- Ability to insert one such device into another;
- Neat design (it is so often not enough!);
- High power charge - 2000mA!
- Can be used from both 12- and 24-volt on-board network!
- Replaceable automotive fuse.

The manufacturer identifies the following devices that can be charged from this charger:
- iPhone
- iPod Touch
- iPod Classic (6G)
- iPod 5th Gen (Video)
- iPod 4th Gen (Photo)
- iPod 3rd Gen
- iPod Nano (2nd)
- iPod Mini
- iPod Shuffle
- MP3 Players
- GPS Devices
- PSP / NDS
- Some phone models
- Delll Axim
- HP IPaq
- The rest of the device with the ability to charge via USB.

Device Features:
- Input: 12 to 24 Volts
- Output from 5.0 to 5.5 volts
- Output current: <= 2000mA to all 5 ports
- Output power: <= 10W
- Power surges: <= 0.5V
- efficiency:> = 65%
- Dimensions: 99.5 x 4.75 x 4.75mm
- Weight: 55g.
