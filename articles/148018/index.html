IDCEE 2012 - Investor Day in Kiev

The Central and Eastern European Investor Day will be held in Kiev on October 18 and 19 - IDCEE 2012 .

We are the general mobile partner of the conference and will be glad to see you in the fall in Kiev.




IDCEE is an international conference designed to become the main platform for the development of Internet entrepreneurship in the region of Central and Eastern Europe with a focus on the CIS countries, held in Kiev for the third year in a row. In the new year, 2000+ participants and 200+ investors from around the world are expected.
