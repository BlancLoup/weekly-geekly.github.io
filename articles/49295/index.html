Android smartphone, Kogan Agora, delayed


Well, actually, as some predicted, Kogan Agora could not be done on time. At the moment, Kogan has announced that the device will not be released in the current version. The main disadvantage is that the screen resolution is not enough for applications that will appear in the near future.

In order to fully comply with modern standards and not become obsolete immediately after the release, Agora must be redone.

Anyone who made a pre-order, will be refunded within 7 days.

Company blog

It is very strange, despite the not very outstanding specs, the device could well get a decent market share. Apparently, not all the reasons were voiced.
