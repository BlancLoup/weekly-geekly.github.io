Open Call or 10 Android / Arduino Ideas for Google Developer Day 2011


Hello to all!

I am glad to inform you that now Google has decided to include in its events a whole mini-contest for Arduino development!

Video conference of the global project coordinator Phoebe Peronto with the Open Call organizational group

The first stage of Open Call will begin at 20:00 Moscow time on Monday, July 18th. This will be a series of test questions. Anyone who passes it - will directly develop competitive ideas. Ten best ideas will be presented at GDD2011 in Moscow on October 10th. Authors will receive invitations to GDD and the opportunity to demonstrate their own development to the participants of Google Developer Day and key Google specialists!

Your community can take a direct part in all this IT riot.
First, of course, the presentation of their competitive ideas
Secondly, you can delegate from your community to the Open Call jury up to 3 most intelligent forms.

The requirements for jury members are as follows:

Acquaintance with ADK and other hardware programming languages
Understanding Android Development
Basic knowledge of Arduino OS

All information, in particular the conditions of the competition on the official website of Open Call for GDD2011
