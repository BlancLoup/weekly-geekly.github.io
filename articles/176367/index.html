4G on smartphones: in Russia from 04/10/2013

Yesterday I participated in the work of the press club dedicated to the launch of 4G on smartphones.
Megaphone managed the impossible: journalists and representatives of most smartphone manufacturers (HTC, Huawei, Nokia, Samsung, Sony, ZTE) and mobile chipsets (Intel, Quallcom) gathered in one room. The conversation was very interesting.


In the photo: Yevgeny Kozlovsky asks tricky questions for the Samsung representative.

Now 4G Internet can be used not only with the help of special modems and routers, but directly on smartphones and tablets that support the Russian 4G standard. Due to legal problems, voice over 4G VoLTE (Voice over LTE) is not yet used (all 4G licenses only allow data transfer), so the Internet traffic goes through 4G, and when you call, your smartphone switches to 3G or 2G. You can read more about this technology here: habrahabr.ru/company/megafon/blog/156051 . Judging by the comment left by the author of the article, the switching time from 4G to 3G is about 5-7 seconds, but when switching from LTE to 2G, this time will be 24-26 seconds.

It is alleged that the average speed of 4G Internet in Moscow is now 10-20 Mbit / s. For high speed inevitably have to pay increased energy consumption. Exact numbers were not mentioned, but according to the experience of those who have already used the new technology, in 4G mode, the battery sits 10-15% faster than in 3G.

I want to note that the advantage of 4G is not only in speed. Due to lower pings, it becomes possible to use such Internet for online games. For example, avid players claim that it is impossible to fully play Tanks Online via 3G, and through 4G without problems. In addition, the ability of the smartphone in 4G can significantly improve the situation where the 3G network is overloaded.

Now 4G network operates in 81 cities of Russia, in Moscow the network operates within the Moscow Ring Road and in many cities in the Moscow Region. In 2013-2014, Megafon plans to invest 15 billion rubles in the development of 4G networks. According to forecasts, by 2015, the share of 4G smartphones in total sales will exceed 15%.

Starting 4G on smartphones has a useful side effect: due to the fact that the cheap Internet XS option now works with 4G, those who bought 4G modems a year ago and then abandoned the use of 4G due to high tariffs (for example, I) can use 4G again.
