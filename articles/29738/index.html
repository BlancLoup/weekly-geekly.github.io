Mobile Report Opera Software. June 2008

The next report of Opera Software on the state of the mobile market according to Opera Mini servers for June 2008 has been published. Here are some excerpts from it.

Top 10 most popular tubes in the world
1. Nokia N70
2. Nokia N73
3. Nokia 6300
4. Sony Ericsson K750i
5. BlackBerry 8310
6. Nokia 6233
7. Nokia 6600
8. Samsung E250
9. Sony Ericsson W810i
10. Nokia 5300

In June, Opera Mini users viewed more than 3.2 billion pages, which is almost 10% more than in May. Meanwhile, the total number of Opera Mini users was about 14.5 million, 1.5% less than in the previous month (apparently summer, vacation).

With regard to information on the countries, Russia still occupies a leading position. At the same time, there were minor changes in the list of the most popular websites visited by Russian Opera Mini users.

Top 10 websites (by number of unique visitors)
1. vkontakte.ru
2. mail.ru
3. odnoklassniki.ru (previously 5)
4. google.com
5. yandex.ru (previously 3)
6. wapos.ru (previously 8)
7. rambler.ru (previously 10)
8. seclub.org (new)
9. jimm.im (new)
10. dimonvideo.ru (new)
