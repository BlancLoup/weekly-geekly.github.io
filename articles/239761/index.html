"Photoshop" goes to the clouds: the main announcement from today's presentation of Adobe

At the Adobe MAX event in Los Angeles, the company showed a lot of interesting things, but one of the coolest announcements is the Photoshop web version shown today, providing a number of impressive features right through the browser:



Even if you don't have Photoshop on your computer, but you have an account in the Adobe Creative Cloud, you can drag and drop the received .psd file into the browser window with a simple drag-and-drop and not only see the picture, but also be able to edit it, including working with layers, choice of colors, etc. Particular emphasis in the presentation is on the fact that everything works very quickly - perhaps faster than on your computer.
