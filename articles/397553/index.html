LVL - bracelet, recording the level of dehydration



The BSX Athletics development team has managed to design an unusual accessory for people leading an active lifestyle. Outwardly, their LVL looks like a regular fitness bracelet, but it has a very wide functionality. The developers promise to register the level of dehydration, monitor the pulse, activity, mood, sleep and calories burned.

All this is packaged in a miniature case, and should work 3-4 days without recharging. Optical sensors BSX Athletics are used to collect data. How many promises correspond to reality is not clear.

But the application is serious. Therefore, the developers managed to collect 430 thousand dollars instead of the planned 50 - and the collection will last another 37 days.

ONEmicro - the right wireless acoustics




Engineers project OneAudio seems to have studied all the shortcomings of Bluetooth speakers . And they suggested using the almost forgotten DECT technology for sound transmission. According to their assurances, it will provide excellent sound quality without loss, full stereo, extended coverage. ONEmicro system - the beginning of the series, the most simple and logical development.

In the future, the team plans to replenish its set with additional playback devices. But already for the starter kit, work in multiroom mode is available.

This is not the first action of OneAudio. The first project has already taken off on Kickstarter, and in June 2017 will begin to be sent to buyers. ONEmicro should be launched at the end of August of the same year. Instead of the required 15 thousand dollars, 25 thousand have now been collected - there is still 15 days ahead.
