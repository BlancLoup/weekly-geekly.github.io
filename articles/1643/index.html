North-West Telecom will connect up to 70 thousand ADSL subscribers by the end of the year

The number of subscribers of North-West Telecom OJSC (NWT) using the ADSL Internet access service will increase to 70 thousand subscribers by the end of 2006, 50 thousand of them in St. Petersburg, the company said in a press release.

One of the main projects of 2006 for North-West Telecom is the provision of broadband Internet access services. At NZT telephone exchanges, all the branches of the company installed xDSL equipment with a capacity of more than 90 thousand lines, of which 34 thousand lines were involved (37%). As of July 1, 2006, the number of users of dedicated Internet access, including using ADSL technology, was about 33 thousand, which is several times higher than the same indicator as of July 1, 2005 (4 thousand users). The six-month increase in ADSL subscribers was more than 24 thousand.
