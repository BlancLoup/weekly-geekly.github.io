Back links from Yaha soon sink into oblivion

Yahoo and Microsoft have agreed that Yahoo's search will use Bing algorithms - a new search engine from Microsoft. This agreement could be completely imperceptible for Russian search marketing, if not for one big but: at the moment, Yahoo is one of the best sources of information about competitors backlinks, and, as a result, information about external optimization budgets. In addition, now Yahu has data export in various formats, which is also very convenient.

Now, after replacing Yahoo, SEOs can lose this fairly handy and, most importantly, fast tool. The agreement states that although Yahoo will use Microsoft's search technology, Yahoo will be able to manage its search engine user experience, but the necessary functionality is unlikely to be preserved.

Therefore, it is time for Runet optimizers to think hard about finding new solutions for collecting backlinks and estimating budgets.

Let me remind you that three years ago, Runet lost its backlinks from Yandex , which caused a lot of noise in the optimizing environment . However, the optimizers quickly settled into the new conditions, switching to the collection of links with the help of Yahoo.
