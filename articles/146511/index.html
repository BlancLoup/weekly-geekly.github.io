Google TV will be sold not only in the US



Google has officially announced that its set-top box Google TV will soon be sold outside the United States. In general, there are a lot of fans of all sorts of gadgets who are waiting for this moment. True, in Russia, this joy can not wait: first, Google TV will be sold in the UK (starting from the 16th of July), and after a while - in France, the Netherlands, Canada, Mexico, Germany, Brazil and Australia. Nothing is known about the CIS countries.
