How to make money on streams


Each streamer pursues its own goals, whether it is communication with the audience or a desire to boast the skill of the game, but one thing unites them all - the desire to earn. And you can do this in several ways at once. For example, consider the most popular site - Twitch.

  • Built-in advertising . On the stream with a large number of viewers Twitch "hangs up" advertising. Everything is simple here: the more your viewers see it, the more you earn.
  • Paid access to stream . Subscribers will not see ads and will receive smiles in the chat, but a significant part of the audience will be lost.
  • Direct advertising on stream . Upon reaching a certain threshold of the audience, the streamer becomes interesting for advertisers. You can tell about a product on the stream itself or put a link to it under the broadcast.
  • Affiliate programs . It differs from the previous version by the absence of a direct agreement. You register and get the opportunity to attract people by referral links.
  • Donati . Donation from the viewer to streamer. Today it is the most common way to monetize the stream. And there are no restrictions here: how much the viewer will like it - so much will it prick up.

Before the advent of special services for collecting donations, streamers collected donations directly to a card or electronic wallet. Needless to say, this was inconvenient at once for a number of reasons? First, it distracts both the streamer and the viewer. Secondly, there was no interaction with the streamer: he just once an hour came in and watched receipts in the Internet bank and thanked everyone. Of course, it could not continue for a long time and instruments began to appear, seeking to make the life of the tape drive more comfortable. Now in the West it is Streamlabs / Twitchalerts, Streamelements and Tipeeestream.

The emergence of such a service in Russia is also not long in coming. A few years ago, a self-taught programmer from Omsk named Sergei Trifonov watched foreign streams, and he liked how simple and convenient everything was: a couple of clicks ‚Äî and money from a streamer. In foreign services there was no localization and support of our payment systems. Then Sergey decided to write his own service, adapted for Russia, and he became Donation Alerts - today the most popular tool in RuNet.



The service is free from all the drawbacks of a ‚Äúmanual‚Äù collection of donations and adds several convenient and useful features, while combining a friendly, user-friendly interface and ease of use:

  • Save time and convenience . Stimer simply place a link to the donate under the video, and the viewer click. No need to go through a complex authorization system every time. The service supports all possible payment systems.
  • Instant transfer of funds and ease of withdrawal . Receipts from all users are collected in one place and automatically displayed once a day.
  • Visualization is the most important element of interactive on the stream. All donations are displayed on the stream, causing a violent reaction leading. Also on the stream, you can add voting, viewing media and displaying paid subscribers.

To register with Donation Alerts, just log in with your account on social networks. The service is not an electronic wallet and does not store money for more than a day, therefore every night all funds are automatically withdrawn and sent to the user through the payment system chosen by him.

During the broadcast, you can collect donations for a specific purpose and record the final amount (for example, buying a new instrument or device, upgrading a computer - something your soul deigns). The progress indicator of the required amount will be visible to all participants. You can set several goals at once, then the viewer will decide for himself what to donate. In the statistics control panel, you can analyze the activity of the audience at one time or another stream and customize the operation of the widgets. This will help make your stream even more efficient, as well as assess and eliminate shortcomings.
