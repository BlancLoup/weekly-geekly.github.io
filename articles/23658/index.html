iCamp - Informal internet conference

iCamp is the first informal conference of Internet activists in Russia. The conference will be held in barcamp format. iCamp will be held from July 30 to August 3 in the cultural and educational center "Etnomir . "

In 2008, iCamp will be devoted to the development of new Internet projects, social networks, educational opportunities of the Internet, blogging and citizen journalism, the creation of cross-media content, as well as advanced Internet technologies.

The main goal of iCamp is to unite talented people who determine the development of the Internet - its driving force, to organize the exchange of experience and knowledge, to create an effective environment for professional and personal development.

The iCamp program is built according to the open principle, when each participant of the event can state his theme, gather like-minded people and hold a seminar, master class, open workshop, competition or presentation.

Special attention on iCamp will be paid to the development of start-ups, commercial and social Internet projects. The informal conference will serve as a platform for their discussion and the impetus for further development. Projects that will participate in the startup section on iCamp will be able to get funding.

iCamp is supported by the MGIMO Internet Policy Center .
