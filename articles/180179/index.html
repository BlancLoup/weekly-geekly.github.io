Smartphone with Full HD screen for $ 200? Easy!

Meet iOcean X7 is a 5 inch smartphone with a screen resolution of 1920x1080 pixels:


4 Cortex-A7 cores, 2 SIM cards, a good 8 megapixel camera and all this at a price of just over $ 200.

‚ñ∫ Specifications

Screen: 5 ", 1920x1080, IPS
Processor: Mediatek MT6589 (Cortex-A7 1.2GHz, 4 cores)
Video accelerator: PowerVR SGX544
Operating system: Android 4.2.1 (Jelly Bean)
RAM: 1GB
User memory: 4GB + microSD slot with card support up to 32 GB
Camera: front 1MP, rear - 8MP

The 4-core Mediatek MT6589 chip has already become the standard for smartphones manufactured in China in 2013. Energy efficient Cortex-A7 with a frequency of up to 1.2GHz shows excellent performance results. The only disadvantage of the platform is the lack of devices with 2 GB of RAM. Initially, it was about some compatibility issue of the MT6589 and 2GB of memory, but at the moment there are rumors of problems with the supply of 2GB of chips. The first devices with 2GB of RAM were Dakele Big Cola 2 and UMI X2, the start date of sales of which is scheduled for the end of May.
