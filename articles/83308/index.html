Online store and other updates

A little more than a month has passed since the launch of the service I'm Sync . During this time we managed to work hard and made a huge number of fixes and improvements. However, the main news is that now users have access to a new component of the publication, namely the online store.

Embed an online store in your site using JavaScript!

Online store


Now, in order to add an online store to your website, simply place the following JavaScript on the page where you would like it to appear:



With the help of appropriate tools you can upload information about products, photos and prices to your website right on your website. An example of an online store embedded in the blog can be seen here .



While the service does not allow you to add products to the cart and place an order, however, in the near future, these functions will appear.



As part of this topic, we would like to contact the owners of sites that are already using some solutions for organizing an online store or are planning. We are interested in constructive suggestions, questions and comments. What may not be enough for you personally, so that you start using the service.

Now, any site owner can easily organize their own online store absolutely free. Those who do not have a website can create it on our service.

Other updates


In addition, changes were made to other components of the publication, such as Events, Photos, Videos and Places.

In particular, the way of creating events has changed. Now, when creating an event, it is not necessary to specify a place (although the possibility of creating events on the map is still available). It is enough to specify the name and date. We remind you that with the help of the Poster event publication component you can embed convenient tools for publishing events, photos and more into your website. More details can be found here .

There were also corrected information editing pages. Now they look neat even with a small width. This is useful if you add a publication component to a blog or just a bottleneck on your site.
