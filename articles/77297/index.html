Recycle, Recycle and Recycle (VI Lenin)



It was always interesting to watch how some innovative ideas are announced loudly somewhere, and somewhere quietly and without a special pump are implemented (nanotechnologies, energy-saving and others). And then the example itself came into the hands, by mail. I ordered samples of microchips in one very well-known company. A cardboard parcel arrived from the states, opening which I was at first surprised at - what kind of straw they stuffed there. And when I reviewed the content I was simply delighted with the idea - it is so simple, just as useful. Useful to us all.


As far as I can tell, now almost everyone is packing fragile materials using bubble plastic packaging. Having unrolled the goods and having cracked enough bubbles ( it turns out , this process has a calming effect due to the harmless realization of the destructive impulses inherent to the individual), we send the used film to the garbage. We usually do not care that, at best, this film will be burned at an incineration site, releasing into the atmosphere the toxic products of combustion of polyethylene.
But in this case, paper was used, and the quality is quite characteristic of the already processed material. And paper has a softening property due to a kind of perforation, which turned it into springy wrapping material. Even if this paper is thrown away, it will not do any harm at all whether it is burned or simply rotted in the soil. The package also has a reminder of how important it is to recycle waste. Now it is easy to imagine how such a small, but very innovative idea in scale, even if it is one, overseas country, will help to reduce the ecology and health of people. And how many of these ideas apply with us? Are they interesting to anyone at all?
