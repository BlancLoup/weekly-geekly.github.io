Some tips for job seekers

The time is now that the labor market is filled with a large number of IT specialists. With what it is connected, many, I think, know. A fairly large number of candidates responds to the vacancies of our company, in a couple of weeks a few people have already got a job. Facing directly with the search for employees, I want to give a couple of simple tips for those who are still in search.
<br><br>
1) Always indicate your contact phone number in the resume. Best of all - cell.
