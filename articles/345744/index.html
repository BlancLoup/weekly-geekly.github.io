New release Oh, my code! How I became a VKontakte developer at 16


A few months ago, we launched the Oh, My Code interview series on Tehnostrim channel. It's time for a new release, and we have it special. In the interview, we spoke with the youngest employee of the Mail.Ru Group, VKontakte business product developer Seva Zhidkov and discussed the development of technologies, the future of programming, and how the data of 100 million users are processed.


Seva also shared what skills and knowledge a young man must possess in order to become a successful developer, what goals need to be set for himself, to create successful and modern products, as well as how to get on an internship on VKontakte. You will find out what the 16-year-old developer is doing now in the VC, what is the internal kitchen of some IT-processes in the largest Russian social network, how the VC recommendation system works. Seva spoke about emergency situations, about code quality analysis and testing. He shared the story of creating a dating app in VK, told his self-development and about people who inspire him in life and career.
