Let me tell you a secret: there are no ideal employers, there are no ideal vacancies, and there are no ideal candidates. Therefore, in the labor market there is space for sales: the recruiter ‚Äúsells‚Äù the vacancy to the candidate, and the candidate ‚Äúsells himself‚Äù to the recruiter.

For a person looking for work in standard ways (posting resumes on job sites), the main task is to write the relevant desired position of the resume and attract the attention of the recruiter.
* Recruiter - a person who is looking for a resume of candidates for a vacancy and conducts an initial interview. As a rule, telephone.

Let's write the correct selling resume. Let us consider the example of a well-known Hh.ru template. By the way, my resume helped me get a job without experience and education in exactly the area I wanted to - IT recruiting.

So:

One resume - one position

While reading ‚Äúweb-programmer / IT specialist / system administrator‚Äù one can not help thinking about whether this candidate is able to do anything at all in these areas or is catching ‚Äúgood luck‚Äù.
If you are considering different work options for yourself, write a separate resume for each position.

Contacts

It is useful to specify in the summary except phone and mail, also Skype. This will help the recruiter to quickly contact you, see when you are online and interact quickly in the course of your selection process for a job.
Do not worry, you will write only in the case. And time is saved enough. Sometimes for 5 lines in Skype chat it is possible to understand whether the candidate and the recruiter have mutual interest in each other. In addition, contacts in Skype remain, the recruiter renames them (changes the nickname to the first and last name, also indicates the area, for example JS) and when a new vacancy appears, offers it first of all to ‚Äúwarm contacts‚Äù in Skype and in professional networks.

It is no secret that in addition to the job sites there are also professional social networks. Linkedin is the most popular among them. About 40% of Linkedin users work in IT and, as a result, recruiters are frequent guests there. Having a profile in ‚ÄúLink‚Äù, and specifying a link to it in the resume, you increase your rating in the eyes of the recruiter and increase your own chances of finding work in a large company and in an interesting project.

On Hh.ru and other specialized sites for job search only 20-30% of vacancies are posted. At the other positions, recruiters are looking for candidates on their own, without having an incoming stream of responses. As a rule, this search is not limited to job sites. Linkedin and Moy Krug are excellent platforms for job search (in the professional networks vacancies are also posted) and for searching for candidates.
I am actively looking for Linkedin. By the way, from observations: PHP programmers respond well to vacancies when I write them to Vkontakte.

Possibility of moving

There was not a day when I would not call nonresident candidates with a job offer in St. Petersburg.
Seriously thinking about moving somewhere? Ready to leave in 2-3 weeks? Do you know where exactly you would like to move? - well! Then feel free to indicate the possibility of moving and do not forget to mention the city (s) where you would like to live.
Nonresident candidates are called frequently. And it is precisely in connection with the fact that they said in the resume that they were ready to move.
So if you are not ready, or strongly doubt - do not write about the move. Do not mislead recruiters and do not waste your time.

If you are ITshnik, specify in each place of work those technologies that you use. Just writing, for example, PHP is not enough. In the work experience, write at least a couple of sentences about the essence of the project.

If you are a system administrator, and you do not have a project as such, specify the number of computer users in the company to which you have been working or are working now. This will help assess the scope of your business and the complexity of the systems you have encountered.

Show your professional growth. Manage overall experience experience

If there were a lot of work places in your work history, but in many of them you worked for 2-6 months, do not include everything in the resume. The employer is incredulous about such a candidate. It is clear that you had good reasons to quit after several months of work, but for the employer this is not an argument.
Take a general look at your job experience. Do not split it into a large number of companies. If you are 23-28 years old, then 3-5 companies in this section are enough. More - bust.
Specify only those companies in which work has brought you real benefits: you have grown professionally, gained valuable experience, worked in a high load project, managed the group.
Make sure that from your first job in the current growth was traced. If there was a moment when you briefly lost ground - it is better not to mention this, so as not to spoil the overall picture.
How to hide temporary gaps in work experience? Add a month to the previous and next job. Say that you rested for some time on available savings, left for a long journey. At first nobody will check this information in detail at first, and then you will surely show yourself to be a real guru at a new place of work and it won't come to your head to delve into your past.

Separately, I want to note the situation when you write a resume for a position in a new field for you, or when you have really little experience, but you are 100% sure that you will cope with the duties you are applying for.
