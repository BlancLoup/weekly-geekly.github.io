Intel announced the purchase of Altera for $ 16.7 billion

Intel has officially confirmed the purchase of Altera, one of the largest manufacturers of microprocessors. The amount of the transaction is quite large, about $ 16.7 billion, writes Slon.ru. The entire amount of the transaction will be paid in cash, with the purchase of Altera shares at a price of $ 54 per share. The participants in the transaction report on its closure within 6-9 months.

It is worth noting that the price of shares, proposed by Intel to Altera, is 11% higher than the value of Altera securities at the time of the end of trading on May 29 ($ 48.85). At the same time, in just three days, starting from May 26, the value of the shares of the purchased company increased by 56%. The reason for this rapid growth is the news of the merger with Intel.
