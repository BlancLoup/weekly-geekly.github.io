Search users in social networks

YoName is a service that helps to find any user in social networks.




The service looks quite normal and looks like a search engine. A user just needs to enter the user's first or last name, his nickname, or email address. YoName after the search will display all the results for each of the social networks ( Facebook , Friendster , MySpace , Xanga , Match , LinkedIn , Digg ). Search results are displayed as a photo / avatar of the user, his place of location, age, and the URL in the corresponding network. If YoName cannot find a person exactly according to the request, then all possible matches will be displayed on the output. I warn you, YoName does not offer updated information on this or that search, and does not save the search results. So ...
