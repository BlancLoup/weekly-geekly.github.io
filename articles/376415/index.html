The myth of the benefits of alcohol in small doses debunked

It is believed that moderate alcohol consumption has a positive effect on health, in particular, a good effect on the cardiovascular system. Of course, first of all, this argument is used by companies that produce and sell alcohol-containing products, and sometimes even doctors recommend their patients alcohol in small doses. However, the last thorough study shows that the positive effect of alcohol is greatly exaggerated - if it exists at all.
