Convenient search for ATMs on the map of Ukraine

Hello, my name is Andrew and I usually do online shopping.

But today I want to tell you about a project that I created a few weeks ago for myself and friends, and now decided to show people. This is a search service for ATMs in Ukraine - gdebankomat.com .



The service was created primarily for themselves and friends, who travel a lot on business and often have to look for the nearest ATM on these business trips. Especially for them, we have already collected more than 9,500 addresses of ATMs of 52 different banks in Ukraine. And we continue to collect.

Our service is based on Google Maps and Google geocoding - and we show the location (exact or approximate) of ATMs in most regional centers and major cities of Ukraine. But besides the card, we also have a simple search for ATMs in a particular city - for example, all ATMs in the city of Nemirov, Vinnitsa region
