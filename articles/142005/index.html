Meet - the portal we use within the company in which I work. I am pleased to share my personal experience (more negative).

The product is not user friendly, not at all. Navigation is very different from the products that people are used to using (VKontakte, facebook, classmates, and many others). Want to read a private message? - On!, Get!, Everything is simple:

  1. Find the activity icon
  2. Not found? It is in the top menu, you need to drive a mouse over it until you see the hint ‚ÄúCommunication‚Äù
