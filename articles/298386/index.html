200 TOP CPA networks to promote products



Quite often the question arises, where, how and by what means to promote their products and solutions. One solution to this problem is the CPA network. CPA - Cost Per Action (price-per-action) is a payment model for online advertising that only pays for certain user actions on the advertiser's site.

The CPA model is one of the most cost-effective advertising payment options, since the advertiser does not pay for impressions or clicks, whose effectiveness is extremely difficult to measure, but for specific consumers who have confirmed interest in the product with targeted actions.

Since, at the moment, there are quite a few such networks, a natural question arises how to choose the right CPA networks in Russia, Ukraine, or how to evaluate foreign CPA networks for maximum impact. Given this goal, a collection of 200 networks was assembled. Enjoy learning!
