Universal cooler Hyper 212 from Cooler Master


Cooler Master definitely makes good products. I think the next novelty will not be an exception - the CPU cooler Hyper 212 , which due to its mounting allows it to be used with Intel processors, including Core 2 Quad, Core 2 Extreme and Core 2 Duo, and with AMD production CPU, including Athlon 64 X2 and Athlon 64 FX.

The fan Hyper 212 has a size of 120 millimeters and, thanks to the crescent-shaped blades, has a low noise level - 19 dBA at 2000 revolutions per minute.

The novelty radiator is also interesting. These are two "towers" through which four heat pipes are drawn. This design allows to reduce the resistance of the radiator air flow, and at the same time reduce the weight of the device.
