Anonymous collapsed servers with child pornography

There is no need to remind the respected Habr audience about the well-known hacker group Anonymous - they were mentioned in a rather large number of topics; for example, in the context that the group was able to get to the secret documents of NATO and the promise to "destroy" Facebook . Then the nature of the news about Anonymus was mostly scandalous, but now the opinion about these hackers will have to be changed, at a minimum.

About a week ago, Anonymus discovered a certain Hidden Wiki site containing links to hundreds of child pornography resources that were not available to search engines and used the services of Freedom Hosting. Further actions of the group are best described in their own words:
Putting the server Freedom Hosting, we removed more than 40 sites with child pornography, among which was Lolita City - one of the largest sites containing more than 100 GB of child pornography. We will continue the collapse not only of any other server that will be noticed by us in the distribution of child pornography.


As proof of their actions, Anonymus provided relevant screenshots and technical information about the underlying Freedom Hosting, among which is the link /users/lolitacity/index.php (although it is hardly possible to hope that visitors register on such resources under real names). It is also curious that the hosting owners tried to resist the attack and took characteristic actions to resist it, but this did not stop the group.

Thus, it is possible to draw a consoling conclusion that at least one of the problems of the modern information society will probably get its solution, since it will not be a particular person or group who can be pressured in one way or another, but something intangible and quite powerful, in addition to attacks on NATO servers, to accomplish something really useful.
