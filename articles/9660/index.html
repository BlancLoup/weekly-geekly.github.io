As you know, nothing stands out in the crowd like a correctly chosen ringtone for a phone. The art of its proper selection has not yet been mastered by all. And indeed: it is dangerous to put something you love as a call - because after a few days, the former favorite song will be so opposed that it cannot be said. Put unloved - wildly. And leave the phone in the factory virgin - frankly stupid and unfashionable.

In theory, companies selling this kind of mobile content can weigh in with a good ringtone. Money, in principle, is small, and in general: ringtones are a product of impulsive demand, the purchase of it by the majority is not perceived as a waste of money.
Unfortunately, unexpected problems may arise with the acquisition of the ringtone you are interested in. The fact is that this sphere of services is oriented (according to the sellers) ninety percent to girls and teenagers. You will laugh, but most of them still do not know how to use file transfer and it is much easier for them to pay for a ready call to emp-three.
Thanks to this clientele, the range from sellers appropriate. As the same vendors told us, during the boom of a song, its ringtone version brings up to 150 thousand dollars of income per month. With the fact that the "cut" ringtone from "Black Eyes" and any Shostakovich cantata is the same, calls are made from what is capable of bringing maximum profit.
As a result, people walk the streets, with their mobile callsigns reminding either of the chicks from the incubator, or the congregation of one sect.

Meanwhile, making a ringtone from a melody or song is fairly easy. Especially if you use a special converter for this. Such as One-click Ringtone Converter 1.9.
The program is extremely convenient and well organized. The first step is to find the desired melody or song on the hard disk. The second step is to select the necessary piece in it for conversion (if this is not done, the melody will be converted completely). Step three - in the list of manufacturers and specific cellular models, select the phone of interest to us. Step four - select the folder for storing the final material (ringtone) and the operator, through which we can immediately send the received ringtone to the desired phone (the transfer goes via the Internet connection and works only for US cellular operators). Everything!
