Nipper - the smallest charger in the world



I am sure, it makes no sense to talk about how unpleasant it is to be with a discharged phone in your hand when the smartphone is needed the most. Everyone was in that situation, yes, everyone. Well, now there are external batteries that save. True, they also have a problem - the external battery is not that small, plus, the battery also needs to be charged.

An external battery can be forgotten, it can not be charged, and then the problem remains a problem. Another interesting solution is the Nipper portable charger, which does not exceed the size of the key ring, plus it does not need to be charged. How it works?

Yes, very simple. The fact is that this charger is powered by ordinary AA batteries. Such devices are on sale, only they are not very convenient in operation, but Nipper - if not ideal, then close to it.

So what is Nipper?




This is a very small charger. It does not take up space, and you can use the gadget at any time. Once you have found a stall with AA batteries, you can assume that the charge problem has been resolved.



Nipper was originally conceived as an emergency gadget. When there is nothing else at hand, and a discharged smartphone is very necessary to charge, then Nipper comes to the rescue. He is always there.

The size of the gadget is only 17 * 17 * 17 mm, and weighs 10 grams. Nipper can be used as a key fob to never forget it anywhere.
