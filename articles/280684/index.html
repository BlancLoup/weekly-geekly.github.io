We invite you to the spring UX-conference of the Russian School of Service Design on April 16

On April 16, we invite participants to the Spring UX-conference of the Russian School of Service Design , which will again be held in the office of Mail.Ru Group. At UX-conferences of the School, participants share their copyright experience from the design of services and services based on customer-oriented values, share their UX practices, ideas and secrets, and answer questions.

Among the topics discussed are customer focus as a technology and basis for designing products and services, digital as a key environment for interaction between customers and services, customer-oriented organization of company processes, current trends in service design, and much more.
