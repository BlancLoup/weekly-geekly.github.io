New CSO rates! (Ukrtelecom, Ukraine)

Now for tezhe money you get speed 2 times more! I also ask you to pay attention to such a nuance: the tariff plans will be changed automatically within 3 weeks from the 5th to the 21st. Manual switching to new tariffs will become available after the 21st, so do not overload technical support with useless calls.
