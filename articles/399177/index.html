Recently, more and more laptops began to appear, which in some incomprehensible way just do without a fan. Just like phones, but only laptops. Silently do not rustle to themselves, and so on, interesting is direct, ultra. And so, in an impulse, so to speak, of youthful enthusiasm, I decided to change my “ still very good laptop ” to something like that. Moreover, the diagonal of 14 "I was too big, and for a long time I wanted to return to 11.6, but with the preservation of Full HD.

The choice fell on the average model in the 700-11 line of Lenovo Yoga. An excellent ANSI keyboard with separate Home (+ PgUp) and End (+ PgDn). True, without illumination, but I do not need it. 3 USB connectors: one of them is combined with power. Declared and as a result achieved 4 hours from the battery. Weight a little more than a kilogram. In general - beauty. Yes, and the red color suits me perfectly, all the same, the stickers are usually all sealed.

The only caveat: it works "without complaints" under the standard Windows Home 10. Well, that is, how - "without complaints" is if you do not take into account the fact that it is Impossible to Work the Same. No, I can, of course, but - this is if you pay a lot. And for the usual work I have Ubuntu, more precisely Linux Mint Mate Desktop. And, everything would be fine, but after moving - transferring partitions from the old to the new - Sarah refused to be friends with my new laptop:

  • Heated terribly , immediately and all the time, degrees to 70 in Idle mode
  • Very often it did not load at all

Of course, everything under the cat is a subjective opinion, but I think it is suitable for all fresh Ubuntu Based distributions, as well as for other Linux, since most of the information does not relate to a specific distribution.
