The new Xbox will not appear before 2013



Finally, Microsoft has responded to rumors about the imminent release of the new generation Xbox console. The web is now quite often published news from "anonymous sources", with the statement that the Xbox 720 will be announced at the June E3 exhibition. The same sources told about some details of the configuration of the game console of the new generation from Microsoft. Apparently, all these news and rumors forced the corporation to give an official comment on this issue.

So, the other day, a spokesman for the Redmond giant said that all these are not true, and the next generation Xbox will appear only in 2013, perhaps the console will be announced at E3, but only next year. Now, according to a spokesman for Microsoft, work is underway, and every day, but the final version of the console, ready for sale, will appear only next year. In addition, a representative of the corporation declined to comment on the possible configuration of the console.

These knowledgeable sources say that the next video set-top box will be about six times more powerful and productive than the current device. Most likely, the AMD Radeon HD 6670 platform will be selected for the graphics system.

In August of this year, developers associated with the Xbox, will receive the SDK. But this will happen after it is known exactly what the Xbox 720 is and what the device configuration is. Apparently, this year the Xbox 720 really will not be presented. A pity, I would like to see this console in action.
