Apple announces Trade-in for broken iPhones



Welcome readers of the blog iCover ! According to the Internet publication The Verge, in the coming days, Apple plans to launch a new Trade-in program within which owners of malfunctioning iPhones will be able to exchange their idle gadgets for new ones with a fixed surcharge. The cost of compensation for iPhone 5S , iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus models will be $ 50, $ 200 and $ 250, respectively. Compensation funds will be credited to a special gift card. Cash withdrawal from the card is not provided.

Thus, the scheme of the Trade-in program is almost no different from the previously announced Apple Store Reuse and Recycle iPhone. At the same time, there is one fundamental difference in it. Devices accepted for replacement with surcharge under previous programs should not have obvious mechanical damage: cracks on the screen, case or camera.

As part of the new program, which, according to The Verge, Apple launches literally this week will be taken iPhone with obvious defects: cracks on the surface of the screen, injured camera and buttons. Assess the degree of damage that must remain within "reasonable limits" (no comments) will be experts authorized service centers. That is, The Verge jokes, if your smartphone has moved a truck or has fallen out of the window of a high-rise building, then, most likely, you will not be lucky again.
