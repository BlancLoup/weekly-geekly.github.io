WHAT IS INNOPOLIS UNIVERSITY?
Innopolis University is a new Russian university specializing in training IT specialists in accordance with international educational standards and proven successful Russian practices. The key goal of the University Innopolis is to prepare highly qualified specialists of international level who can become the center of positive changes within the company or organize their own project or profitable company.

WHAT IS A SOFTWARE ENGINEERING SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT MANAGEMENT PROGRAM ??
The program was developed taking into account the best world and Russian experience in training software developers and is aimed at meeting the needs of the Russian labor market for highly qualified specialists.

The basic principle of the program is expressed by the formula ‚ÄúLearn by doing‚Äù. More than 40% of study time is devoted to practice, which, in addition to the projects included in each academic discipline, includes the implementation of the project for an external industrial customer by a team of students under the supervision of experienced mentors. Thus, during their studies, students acquire versatile practical project experience, equivalent to 3 to 5 years of work in the company.

HOW DOES A CANDIDATE SELECTION FOR A MASTERS PROGRAM HAPPEN?
Competitive selection takes place in several stages:
  • consideration of application documents by the representatives of Innopolis University;
  • Interviews of successful candidates with representatives of the Admission Committee of the Innopolis University in the city of Innopolis, including with representatives of the industry;
  • Deciding on admission;
  • Invitation to study according to the program;
  • A student's confirmation of the offer.

HOW DO I FIND OUT THE DECISION OF THE RECEPTION COMMISSION?
Successful candidates will be notified of the decision of the Admissions Committee on an individual basis.

THE LIST OF REQUIREMENTS TO CANDIDATES INDICATES ITEMS WHICH ARE NOT IN THE FORM OF APPLICATION ON YOUR SITE. WHAT ARE THESE DOCUMENTS NEEDED FOR?
Documents specified in the file "Requirements for candidates" are necessary to continue participation in the competition. Candidates send a full package of documents after the relevant proposal received from representatives of Innopolis University.

All documents must be in English or with translation. It is not necessary to notarize the translation of a diploma, its annexes, academic transcript (or extract from the record book).

What does the TOEFL TEST represent? AND WHERE I CAN GIVE IT TO?
TOEFL is a standardized test that reveals the level of proficiency in English as a foreign language. The test consists of four sections: reading, listening, speaking and writing. TOEFL can be taken in one of the certified centers located in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Kazan, Naberezhnye Chelny and many other Russian cities. Writing to TOEFL takes place at least three days before the planned test date. The results are published on the personal page of the test 10-14 days after passing the test.

The recommended test result for participation in the master program is 100 points (TOEFL iBT), and in each section you must score at least 22 points.

Candidates can also provide IELTS test results, in which case the minimum recommended result is 6.5 points.

Testing should be completed no earlier than two years before the date of delivery.

I HAVE IELTS CERTIFICATE. CAN I PROVIDE IT OR DO I NEED TO PASS TOEFL TESTING?
Yes, you can provide an IELTS certificate if the results you receive meet the requirements for admission. The recommended total score is 6.5 points, while the test results in each section should be at least 6.0 points, in the oral section - 7.5-8.0 points.

Testing should be completed no earlier than two years before the date of delivery.

CAN I APPLY ADDITIONAL DOCUMENTS TO THE APPLICATION: USE RESULTS, DIPLOMAS AND CERTIFICATES? WILL IT IMPACT ON THE DECISION OF THE RECEPTION COMMISSION?
Yes, we recommend that you submit the results of the Unified State Exam (if available); as well as diplomas and certificates confirming your achievements related to the specialty (participation in competitions, contests, conferences, etc., starting from the senior classes of the school), as well as any other achievements.

I COMPLETE THE TRAINING, BUT AT THE MOMENT OF THE SUBMISSION OF THE APPLICATION, I DO NOT HAVE A DIPLOMA FOR EDUCATION. CAN I PARTICIPATE IN THE COMPETITION?
Yes, you can participate. Instead of a diploma, provide a scan of the academic certificate (this is a document of the established sample), which is issued upon request at the dean of the faculty of your university.

After graduation from the university, it is necessary to make a copy of the diploma supplement, make its translation, provide it to the admissions office.

I DON'T TRAINED IN IT SPECIALTY, BUT I HAVE PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE IN DEVELOPING SOFTWARE. CAN I PARTICIPATE IN COMPETITIVE SELECTION?
Yes, you can participate in competitive selection if you have not been trained in an IT specialty, but are qualified in the field of natural sciences and have experience in the industry (software development).

WHAT IS A MOTIVATIONAL LETTER? HOW IT MUST LOOK OUT?
A Statement of Purpose (SOP) should show why you are a worthy candidate and why you are interested in the proposed program. Describe your dream job and explain how learning at a partner university will help you get it. The motivation letter must be in English.

Please do not describe the general merits of the master's program.

WHAT SHOULD INCLUDE A RECOMMENDATION LETTER?
According to the requirements, candidates must send three letters of recommendation - one each from the employer and the educational institution, the third - alternatively: from the employer or the educational institution.

A letter of recommendation usually has the following structure.

  • How long and in what capacity have you known the applicant (student, employee, etc.)?
  • What is the difference between the applicant in professional and personal terms and how do you assess his potential?
  • Please rate the applicant by the following parameters by comparing it with other students / staff (0 - low level, 5 - excellent level)

  1. intellectual abilities;
  2. hard work;
  3. motivation to continue education in the magistracy;
  4. emotional maturity.

Additional information about the applicant, his strengths and achievements.

The recommending person must provide their contact details.

If possible, the letter is signed by the referring person and sealed by the organization.
