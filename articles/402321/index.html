Livall review: a smart helmet with a controller, microphone and own application

The main task that the bicycle helmet should perform is to increase the level of security, however, additional functions can be useful!



Livall - a helmet that keeps statistics of activity, indicates the direction of turns, allows you to quickly answer the call and listen to music. And yes, it serves as a means of protection!

Livall is a helmet gadget that gives its owner additional features. It works in tandem with applications for iOS and Android (Russified).

The software allows you to perform some settings, set the signal strength of the indicators or turn them off, and also saves the race statistics. In order to start recording a workout, you need to click in the main menu ‚ÄúGo to cycling‚Äù. After that the training window will open.



Then all the statistics will be saved in a separate section of the menu, where you can see the details of the races for different periods of time.

Also, the application is a means of calibrating devices and connecting them together. Livall is sold with the controller.



The device itself is uncomplicated. Among its features: indicate the direction of rotation, control the music and camera of the smartphone, if you suddenly need to take a photo. The controller is mounted on the steering wheel using a special "clip".



But in order to connect it with a helmet, just the right thing - the application. All connected devices are saved in the list:
