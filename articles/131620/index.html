Software, work with the device




What can you say about the software? In principle, the standard interface for Android devices is user-friendly, intuitive, intuitive.

For its immediate task A10 copes well. Firmware makes reading comfortable and convenient. To move through the pages, you can use not only the touch screen, but also the individual arrow buttons. The program remembers the position of the reader in the book, and also supports many other related useful functions.

And although everything is sharpened for reading books, the functionality of the device goes far beyond the e-book: watching videos, playing music, browsing the Internet (browser, mail, Skype and other software, connecting via wi-fi), dictionary and translator, organizer, office programs. Plus, everything that you install yourself, including games - among the programs there is the Android Market.


Camera

On the front of the A10 posted 2MP camera. Frankly, the camera is weak. It is difficult to make high-quality photos because of its location and the small number of megapixels. But for video conferencing, it is not very good because of an unsuccessful angle. There is, of course, a solution - you can simply flip the device 180 degrees.


Conclusion, conclusions

What can I say - the device pleased me pleasantly. A10 is a tablet rather than an e-book. This is manifested both in the appearance of the device and in its software and hardware. It's great that the Ukrainian company has released such a worthy product. I heard that the PocketBook has high hopes for this model, and it seems to me that they are mostly justified.

Now it is difficult to say how much the device will cost, but according to preliminary data it is $ 400 or (most likely) even less .
