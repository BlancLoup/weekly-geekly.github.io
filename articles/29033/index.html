Sms feedback for sites

Another of my ideas for taking part (willing) or criticism (constructive) -sms feedback.

The bottom line is as follows:

Owners of websites, newspapers, booklets and reasons - those who want to get this type of communication, enter into a partnership agreement with the content provider providing the service and receive a unique code and place it in the form of an icon (with information about the short number and participant code) or links to their resources. The user will be able to send a text message to the site administrator or other contact person to a short number with the participant's code as a prefix. From the user's point of view, this will give a GUARANTEE for consideration and response from the recipient. The obligation to give any response to the request within nn time must be specified in the content provider agreement with the partner. From the point of view of a partner (website, newspaper, etc.) - this
extra income and spam protection. Few people will write nonsense even at the rate of $ 1, but those who really need to contact and get a reasonable answer will write.
