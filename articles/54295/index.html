P2P right in the browser

The new version of the p2p plugin Littleshoot makes it possible to download torrents directly in your browser. Works great on Macs and Windows; in IE, Fox, Chrome and Safari. It also supports the Gnutella protocol and Youtube integration.

Project creators have experience in developing p2p projects, such as Poisoned, Kazaa, XFactor, Pando, BitTorrent Inc., and Gizmo Project.

The first real p2p plugin for browsers (built on NPAPI and ActiveX) supports bittorrent, gnotella (a truly decentralized protocol) and its own Littleshoot p2p protocol.

www.littleshoot.org/beta (via torrentfric )

You also see that , not so long ago, the realization of the idea of playing downloadable video and audio directly from p2p networks directly in the browser is not so far.
