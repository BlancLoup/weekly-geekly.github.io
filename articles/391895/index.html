Computer User Corner: Civic Activism

At a time when bureaucratic procedures are less frequently performed on paper, each user of a wearable computer has the opportunity to send, with the help of his device, allegations of violation of the landscaping procedures, the work of departments and traffic regulations to the appropriate executive agencies.



Regional governments and initiative citizens of the country have created electronic services, presented in the form of websites and applications for OS, wearable devices, using which one can send appeals on violations of improvement, and in some cases also work of state institutions. The principle of operation is the same: the user, by selecting the appropriate category, creates a message, including in it a brief description of the problem, the exact location and, if desired, a photograph; then the message is checked for compliance with the selected category and clarity and sent to the appropriate department, which, in turn, must give an answer within the stated period and solve the problem. Services for receiving reports of violations in the field of traffic and parking work on the same principle, but are presented exclusively in the form of applications for wearable devices and the use of photographs for them is mandatory.

Government services


Moscow


The Moscow government was the first in the country to create a service of this kind, calling it " Our City ". The service can be used when specifying the name and surname, as well as the e-mail address for working with the site and the phone number for working with the application for wearable devices. The stated deadline for receiving a reply by post is 8 days. It is presented in applications for Android , iOS and Windows . According to the portal, since October 2011, 1,008,029 has been solved out of 1,563,416 problems stated. It was found that some of the responses of officials were supplied with fake pictures .

The Moscow Helper , released in August 2015, the first seven months of existence allowed only to report cases of non-payment of parking. In the middle of March of the current year, the possibility was added to declare about parking and stops in prohibited places. In the future, it will be possible to make appeals about the cases of parking on sidewalks, pedestrian crossings and lawns. It is possible to send applications only from the place of violation and at the time of its occurrence. Developed by decree of the government of St. Petersburg, " Our St. Petersburg " in many respects repeats the look of the Moscow counterpart. Tatarstan


For residents of Tatarstan, the local government has created " People's control of the RT ." The term for consideration of an application is 10 calendar days, and in the category of Housing and Communal Services - 3 days. To use, you must register with a phone number to receive a code, and specify a name or nickname. The functionality of the service, which Moscow and St. Petersburg lack, is commenting and evaluation of applications by other users. Presented in applications for Android and iOS . According to statistical reports on the portal, since April 2012, out of 65,609 applications, 51183 have been satisfactorily resolved. If we believe the indicators on the main page, out of 18,071 applications, 13,791 have been satisfactorily resolved.

None of the government services, oddly enough or surprisingly, works according to Federal Law 59 " On the procedure for considering appeals of citizens of the Russian Federation, " according to which citizens' requests to state bodies, local governments and their officials are considered 30 days, not counting the three days allocated for registration.
