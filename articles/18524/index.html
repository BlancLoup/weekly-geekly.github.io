Enterprise 2.0: What does the coming year prepare for them?

Well, of course, the coming year is preparing something new, not only for them, but for us, for all of us. However, in order to avoid another stream of indignant comments from supporters of the uniqueness of the Russian IT market (see, for example, discussions [ 1 , 2 ]), we will assume that we are only talking about "them."

I understand well that everyone is already tired of the New Year predictions. But, nevertheless, I still venture to recommend that you read another prediction:

Dion Hinchcliffe: 12 predictions for Enterprise Web 2.0 in 2008

I recommend, firstly, because it concerns the area (Enterprise 2.0), which has not yet been spoiled by forecasts. And, secondly, this forecast was made by Dion Hinchliffe (Dion Hinchcliffe), from my point of view, one of the strongest authors writing on this topic. I have already mentioned it several times in my blog (the last time it seems here ).

What else characterizes D. Hinchlif is his amazing understanding of the organic links between such modern IT technologies as SOA (service-oriented architecture), SaaS (software-as-a-service) and the social component of Web 2.0.

If you are interested in all this, be sure to read the indicated article . Her essay in Russian can be found in my iTech Bridge blog .
