Panoramic video from the cab of a Formula 1 car

The car is driven by the Swiss racer Sebastien Olivier Buemi on the Rudskogen Motorsenter in Norway.

The technology of obtaining such a video is using a special camera ViewCam 360, created by the company Making View, which with the help of a spherical lens system can receive an equirectangular video with a resolution of 4K x 2K and a frame rate of 25-50 fps. The weight of the camera is about 600 grams, the internal memory is 224 GB; The camera is controlled via a wireless interface.
