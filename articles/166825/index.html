Not so long ago, the international consumer electronics exhibition CES 2013 was held, and many companies showed their innovations, developments, visions of the future and prototypes of devices that, in their opinion, people will use in the near future. And there are companies that brought the technology of tomorrow to today.

What does LG Smart Home Services consist of (and how it works)?


It is worth starting, perhaps, with communication between devices. It is carried out over wireless channels: NFC, Wi-Fi, 3G / 4G. At the core of the control protocol is the SmartShare standard that has already been proven by time, supporting not only video transmission, but generally any information that can be used by this device.

The second important component is the LG Smart Control application, which allows you to control connected LG home appliances using a smartphone or Smart TV. Synchronization of the ‚Äúremote‚Äù (in the face of a smartphone) with the equipment can also be carried out via NFC, which eliminates the tedious setting and connection in ‚Äúmanual‚Äù mode.

Virtually any information (and function) is accessible from your phone, both on the couch and outside the house. It remains to teach the washing machine to hang out and iron the laundry, and the intelligent trash can - to empty yourself in the right place.
