Chinese air mashes

Cheap air masks appeared quite recently. They allow you to move the cursor around the screen, moving the hand with the aeromouse in the air. In this case, only the hand should move, not the whole hand. The principle of operation is based on the use of a gyroscopic sensor. Of course, using the aeromouse is less convenient than using an ordinary mouse. But it is not intended for normal use with a computer. The purpose of the aeromysh is to control a computer, a media player or a TV from a chair or sofa.

I have long wanted to try out aeromashes, so when the opportunity arose to order goods for a test from another Chinese online store, I ordered two aeromys at once - one in the form of a console, the second with a built-in Russian keyboard.





The first aerial mouse in the form of a remote called the 2.4GHz Mini Wireless Gyroscope Flying Mouse USB TTH-117941 Flying Mouse Air Mouse with USB Receiver costs $ 17.23 .



A micro-receiver is connected to the USB port. The device is defined as a standard mouse and keyboard. Aeromouse transmits signals at 2.4GHz and works confidently at fairly large distances. She confidently worked for me from the farthest point of the apartment through two concrete walls. Eats air gun from two AAA batteries.
