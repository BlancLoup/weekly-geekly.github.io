Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine will be released on the PC


Action Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine, on which the Canadian studio Relic Entertainment is working, turns out to be released only on Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3. The game was announced in May 2009 and then THQ positioned the project as a console exclusive.

I must say that the Warhammer 40,000 gaming universe is very lucky lately - a great strategy of Dawn of War 2 has appeared with the continuation of Chaos Rising, an ambitious Warhammer 40,000: Dark Millennium Online is being prepared ... Let's hope the Relic new project will be executed at the traditionally high level, especially considering THQ publisher decided to release the Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine in the PC version.

Relic head Jonathan Dowdeswell (Jonathan Dowdeswell) said that a dedicated RS audience is very important for the company, and I am glad that the developers had the opportunity to release their offspring on personal computers.

In Space Marine you have to feel yourself in the shoes of one of the Space Marines and thorough war with orcs, demons and heretics. On the shelves of the game will appear in early 2011.
