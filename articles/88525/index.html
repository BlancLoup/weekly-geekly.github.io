MTS canceled tariffs with unlimited Internet



About a year ago, the MTS company put into operation in Kazan the first tariff with a reasonable subscription fee for 3G Internet: for 650 r the subscriber received 4000 MB of traffic. Some time later, almost real unlimited tariff plans appeared: 128 Kbps for 350 rubles (no more than 1 GB of traffic for 3 days) and 512 Kbps for 545 rubles (no more than 3 GB for three days).

But the joy did not last long - the tariffs were closed for connection, and on March 10, the site received news that from March 22, the parameters of these TPs are changing. Now for 350 r the subscriber received only 1500 MB of traffic at a speed of 128K, and it will be unlimited only from 2 to 6 nights. For 545 r the subscriber will receive 3000 MB of traffic at a speed of 512K, no-limit is also only 4 hours a day. For the most voracious, 10,000 MB without speed limits for 950 r.
