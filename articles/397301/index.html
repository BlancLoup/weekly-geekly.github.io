The highlight of the program: Lenovo YOGA Book at IFA 2016

Transformers notebooks of the YOGA Book series have become a real discovery of the IFA 2016 exhibition. This is one of the few devices that has two versions with different operating systems - Windows 10 and Android. But the touch keyboard Halo Keyboard from the first show causes a lot of controversy.



Appearance


Lenovo's branded hinge design immediately hints at the accessory of the YOGA series laptop. It allows you to rotate the screen to full 360 degrees and consists of 130 parts, in addition resembles a watch strap. The hinge is designed for serious loads and is able to withstand the constant unfolding back and forth.

YOGA Book received a thin body - the thickness of the laptop is only 9.6 mm, weight - 690 grams. This was made possible thanks to the used parts of the body of magnesium and aluminum alloy. There are three possible hull colors. YOGA Book on Android is presented in gray and golden colors, laptop on Windows - only in black.





Special features


Keyboard Halo Keyboard - touch. Yes, there are no familiar keys, not so many favorite key moves and everything related to classic keyboards. But she knows how to give a tactile response and is able to learn. Key illumination only works when you need to type text. The rest of the time, the matte multi-function panel with anti-reflective coating (also known as the keyboard) can be used as a touch panel for drawing.



The demonstration of drawing possibilities was impressive - a cartoon artist painted all comers, of course, with the help of the YOGA Book. Practically it looks like this: a sheet of paper is placed on the keyboard panel and the image is applied to it using the Real Pen stylus. The stylus is able to simultaneously write and ink on paper, and produce graphic information on the screen. For the transmission of images, electromagnetic resonance technology (EMR) from Wacom is used. The panel recognizes 2048 options for pressing the pen and allows the pen to deviate to 100 degrees.



Laptops are equipped with the Book UI interface that works with both Windows and Android. It provides work with several applications in separate windows. Windows can be moved, scaled and in every possible way to adjust for themselves.

Specifications


Both versions are equipped with IPS-display size of 10.1 inches with a resolution of Full HD (1920x1200 pixels). The heart of the devices is the Intel Atom x5-Z855 processors, integrated Intel HD Graphics 400 are responsible for the graphics. The LPDDR3 RAM is 4 GB, the main drive is 64 GB flash memory. Laptops support work with microSDXC memory cards up to 128 GB.

Naturally, there is a full range of wireless connections: 2G / 3G / LTE, Wi-Fi (802.11 a / b / g / n / ac), Bluetooth. YOGA Book works with nano SIM cards and supports 4G networks.

Laptops are equipped with connectors micro-USB, HDMI, 3.5-mm audio. In addition, in both versions there are light, vibration, Hall sensors and an accelerometer.

Both versions are equipped with batteries with a capacity of 8500 mAh, while the version on Android will autonomously last about 15 hours, the version on Windows - about 13 hours.

Instead of conclusion




Lenovo YOGA Book can be called a pioneer in the world of new technologies. This is a completely new model transformer, fully capable of speaking out for a laptop, a tablet, and even a graphic tablet. No longer needed bulky third-party drawing tools, no need to carry with them both a laptop and a tablet. One small, compact YOGA Book - and you write notes, make sketches, answer mail, sit on the Internet, watch videos, read books.
