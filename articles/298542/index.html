IBS Group has reduced its share in Luxoft



Today, IBS Group has sold 4% of the shares of Luxoft , a software development company. In total, IBS Group has about 37% of the company's shares in the amount of 11.87 million, and the value of the shares sold was about $ 71.1 million.

The main reason for the sale of the stake could be the decline in the market value of Luxoft by 14.06% from $ 67.28 to $ 57.82, amid the fall of the Down Jones index by 1.29% and the NASDAQ by 3.25%. In the 2015 report, the IBS Group reported in a similar way about possible plans to completely abandon the share in Luxoft or its subsequent reduction.
