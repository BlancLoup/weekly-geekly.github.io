University timetable s01e03: season finale

Earlier in the series
We do the mobile service "University Schedule" . Schedules are added by entire universities or faculties, which distinguishes us from projects, where adding schedules is just one of the organizer options.

In 2013, we received about 50,000 users, of which about 20% are active. We added over 60 universities across Russia and hooked Ukraine, released applications for iOS, Android and Windows Phone and made a mobile site for all other phones.
