February 8 - free webinar "Using AutoCAD 2010 in construction design and design"

Softkey's online software supermarket and Autodesk hold an online seminar for design engineers and architects.

The webinar is dedicated to the new features of AutoCAD 2010 and its use in construction design and design.

Date: February 8, 2010.
Time: from 11:00 to 12:00 (Moscow Time).
Theme: "Using AutoCAD 2010 in construction design and design."
Speaker: Dmitry Rusin, chief specialist in architecture and construction at Autodesk Inc.

You can register for the webinar here - www.softkey.ru/webinars/detail.php?ID=1101
The event itself will take place on a special Softkey platform - 217.74.43.246/r74018291

In the webinar program:

* Presentation of the product.
* Show new features:
- The creation of arbitrary forms;
- parametrics;
- Publish DWG. in pdf. work with pdf. underlay.
* Tips & tricks:
- Select commands with Tab;
- Dynamic blocks.
* Scale annotations.
* 3D (work with the network, anti-aliasing).
* System variables:
- DYNMODE;
- FILEDIA.
* Species cube.
* Record action.
* Workspaces.
* Combined callouts.
* Setting up callouts according to GOST.
* Customize dimensional styles according to GOST.
* Link to Excel.
* Calculator.
* Hatching.
* Dynamic input.
* Visualization.
* Cameras (bypass, circling).
* Viewports.

AutoCAD 2010 allows you to solve the most complex design problems.

By means of creating arbitrary shapes, a wide variety of bodies and surfaces are modeled; project verification time is significantly reduced; parametric drawings help keep on hand all the necessary information.
Project ideas can be visualized in PDF format, as well as implemented in layouts, obtained through 3D printing. Not even when ideas turned into reality so quickly.

Note.

* Adobe Flash Player 8 or newer;
* headphones or speakers;
* microphone (if possible).
* You also need to open TCP port 1935 on the firewall.
