Silent computer is needed by all!

A couple of days ago I published an article about building a silent computer for my home. And I hurried a little - I did not post pictures of what eventually happened. Today I made out a working sistemnik and took a couple of amateur shots.
But for a start, the historical frame "as it were." I could not resist :-)


Silence is important to all! :)

The following photo helps to estimate the height of the Scythe Ninja radiator. Body fans blow directly at him. So CPU cooling is, so to speak, semi-passive. The top fan (clearly visible in the next photo) is going to turn off. And close the vent hole in the top case cover. I doubt only one thing: it blows the passive video card downstairs - will it overheat.



General view of the system in the collection. Please note that the board is fully open - all wires are hidden behind it and fixed to the side wall with special clips. The hard drive is in the bottom basket. It is removed if you unscrew the bolt and pull the ring. I removed the second basket from the case to increase the free space for better cooling.
