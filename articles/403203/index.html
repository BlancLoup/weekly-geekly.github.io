Exhibition VacuumTechExpo 2017

VacuumTechExpo is the only international exhibition in Russia of vacuum equipment, technologies, equipment and materials, cryogenic equipment, as well as equipment for applying functional coatings.



The exhibition widely presents vacuum technology and vacuum equipment, vacuum technology, materials for vacuum technology, cryogenic equipment and components, analytical equipment and measuring instruments.

The VacuumTechExpo exhibition is held in Moscow annually and contributes to the improvement of the efficiency of the Russian industry by promoting high-tech vacuum equipment and modern vacuum technologies in strategically important sectors of the economy.

The exhibition is actively attended by Russian and foreign manufacturers of vacuum pumps, vacuum chambers and other vacuum production technologies and materials.

Subjects of the exhibition:

- Vacuum pumps
- Vacuum chambers
- Vacuum gauges, pressure transducers
- Vacuum fittings
- Vacuum installations for the application of functional coatings
- Vacuum furnaces
- Cryopumps, coots, coolers
- Leak Detectors

Participants: manufacturers and distributors of high-tech vacuum, cryogenic equipment and technology, vacuum materials, equipment for spraying, interested in actively promoting their equipment on the Russian market.

GNB Corporation, MSH Techno, VACOM, ZENCO PLASMA, BLM Sinerzhi LLC, VACUUMMASH JSC, Vacuum-Service LLC, Glinved Rasha LLC, Intek Analytika JSC, Cryosystems LLC , OAO NII TM, LLC Sigm Plus Engineering, ZAO Ferry Watt and many others.

Visitors: general and technical directors, heads of departments, chief engineers, chief technologists and specialists of research organizations and manufacturing enterprises.

Fields of activity of enterprises that are represented by visitors: engineering, instrument making and electronics, metallurgy, chemical production and refining, energy and nuclear industry, medicine and pharmaceuticals, optics, construction.
