Kontur.Kampus: we invite you to a free student camp of industrial development near St. Petersburg

The campus is a student camp for novice programmers, where the knowledge of the Contour developers is shared. For five days we will learn to write clean code, test and design. And in the evenings, drink tea with cookies, play board games and work in a team of smart guys like you! On Campus you will gain experience in industrial development and find new friends with whom you will have a great time :)


April 15 Campus goes to visit the Northern Capital! If you are from the Leningrad, Pskov, Novgorod regions or Karelia, we are waiting for you!




And the Campus is an opportunity to come for an internship and stay to work in the Contour.


For you, free of charge, travel and accommodation we take on. Selection by test, details on the site kontur.ru/kampus .


We will be happy if you tell friends from St. Petersburg and the surrounding regions about the Campus :)
