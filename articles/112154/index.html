1. Virtual QR-camera for code recognition

The technology of QR codes, of course, is not new. This is a kind of transfer of two-dimensional barcode technology to the Web. Barcodes were invented in the United States in the late 1940s by two students: Norman Woodland and Bernard Silver, as a means of automated information processing about goods. Based on Morse code, Woodland developed a similar system that could be applied to product labeling.
