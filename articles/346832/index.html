Forsyth study of blockchain technology

We are starting a foresight study of the blockchain technology in various sectors of the economy. And today we need your help - thoughts on the format of the forecast and participation in a small survey. According to the results of the study, new articles will be published here.

The purpose of this study: to make a forecast for the next 5-10 years (from 2018 to 2028) about how this or that sphere of human activity will change with the use of blockchain technologies.



We hope that in this IT community we will be able to get the most constructive comments, ideas and suggestions. And, perhaps, proposals for further mutual cooperation in research.

Who are we? Undergraduates and advisers of Krauslab magistracy in the Baltic Federal University them. And Kant (Kaliningrad). Our target audience: these are entrepreneurs and representatives of business structures who have just become interested in a new trend and want to get more structured and high-quality information about its areas, as well as understand how their business may look in 5-10 years.

While this is a draft, it makes no sense to publish here without elaboration. And the final product of the research should become more attractive, usable and with a clear range of metrics.

Therefore, now our task is to collect as much feedback as possible on the format of the forecast . If you have 5 minutes for thoughtful answers, then please open this survey form on Google with 7 very important questions for further research.
