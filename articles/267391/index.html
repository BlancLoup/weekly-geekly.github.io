A little bit about droidcon



Oddly enough, droidcon is not really a Google story. This is the story of the initiative guys, who in 2009 gathered and made the first conference dedicated to everything related to the Android platform and the development for it. It turned out well for them - so good that next year the conference "spread out" in several countries. Well, and then Google joined - but not as an organizer, but as a sponsor and participant. Such independence allows inviting any speakers to the conference and discussing any issues, including ‚Äúinconvenient‚Äù ones.
