Promotional codes for students at the launch of Visual Studio 2010

As you probably already know, in mid-April, Visual Studio 2010 will launch in Russia .



We still have promotional codes for registration in St. Petersburg (April 14) and Yekaterinburg (April 16).

If you are a student or graduate student and want to attend one of these events, write your course (year of study) in the comments , the university and the city of the event - the first 10 who wrote (for each of the cities) will receive a promotional code.
