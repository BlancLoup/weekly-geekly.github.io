Meet Evernote for Mac 1.6

A new Evernote for Mac (version 1.6) has been released, with many notable updates. Download it here.

Piles of notes

In Evernote for Mac, we were able to significantly improve the speed and performance of working with a large (even so - BIG) number of notes. This update should be felt and appreciated by active users of Evernote. All operations with the service are now much faster. If you are still busy building up your base of notes in Evernote, then you can be calm - Evernote is ready to calmly accept any volume of your records, thousand after thousand. What we expect from you.

Mailing notes




Another nice change to Evernote for Mac is the ability to send notes via e-mail. Now you can send them directly through our service, which ensures greater reliability and a uniform format of sent notes, no matter how Evernote client you use. Well, and besides, it simplifies the life of those of us who avoid email clients, preferring the web interface. The email address of the sender will be the address to which your account is registered. It can be changed in the settings in the Evernote web interface .

And much more...
  • increased performance when displaying search results with large PDF files;
  • dragging the link to the application icon in the dock now works more correctly;
  • another round of struggle with the problem of the clipper in Safari, which was discovered by some users;
  • Solved the problem with the "Save As ..." item in the context menu;
  • fixed bug with free space indicator;
  • many other fixes.
