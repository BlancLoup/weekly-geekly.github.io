Team goals. Moving towards a goal is easier

Today we want to tell you about a new function that opens up new opportunities in working with our service - team goals. If you are not familiar with us, please read the introductory article for a start.




Team goals are easiest to present with a live example.
Suppose you gathered in a major campaign in the mountains for a month or more. The most important thing in such a journey is to properly prepare and not forget anything, it will be impossible to buy something else during the trip. It is necessary to distribute the role - one of the team members will take a tent, and someone will take the dishes for food. As soon as you are ready, you indicate whether a particular point has been fulfilled and what is most important (especially if there are points on the order of 100) - everyone sees the current progress in staffing. Conveniently! This is not a piece of paper or a notebook, which may be lost.
This goal can be made not public and keep it hidden from prying eyes.

Take another example from the IT field.
You are a young team specializing in the development of aerial robots. It will be logical to tell interested people about your work, since a loyal audience is needed, which can later become clients of the company. Here it is already logical to make the goal public and to lead it by different team members. Ivan can write about the collection of components, and Andrew about programming on the arduino. Very much it will be interesting to follow precisely the process of creation, and not the finished product. Such information is now very popular and helps attract a lot of fans. For example, in the advertising industry, sometimes the process of creating an advertising video is much more interesting than the video itself.
