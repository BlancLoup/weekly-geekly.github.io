The TinkerForge do-it-yourself kit will make your home more comfortable

Hi, Habr!

Have you already gotten a smart home? TinkerForge plan to help you with this by releasing a controller to control your home in order to make it cozier and smarter. The cost of the controller at the moment is only $ 70, for which you will receive the Starter Kit. The kit will help you manage sockets, lights, blinds from your smartphone.

Opportunities:


Allows you to manage devices over the Internet
Powerful external antenna
Managed via a web interface or from a smartphone

The manufacturer promises that the platform will be comfortable, understandable and secure, even for beginners.





Video summary: Bob sits in a meeting and thinks if he turned off the light at home. To get up and go home in this situation would be pretty stupid. His nerves and time can save Starter Kit: Internet of Things. A small gadget will help manage devices in the house via a smartphone or a web interface. These devices include sockets, lights, air conditioners, blinds, coffee machines, and more. Now Bob can turn off the light without leaving the workplace.

Configuration can be configured on the site to manage devices through a web interface.





For $ 70 (after May 24, the price will go up) you get the Starter Kit. It includes a main unit, a remote switch, a cable, two sets for mounting and self-adhesive rubber feet.



Assembly promises to be simple.





Specifications


Radio module: RFM69HW
Power Consumption : 10mA (standby), 40mA (sending data)
Frequency: 433MHz
Parameters: 11cm x 6.5cm x 4.5cm
Weight: 130 grams
