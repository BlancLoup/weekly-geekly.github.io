Richard Stallman is 61 years old

Better late than never.



While everyone is busy discussing the Russian firewall, copyright and Ukraine, we completely forgot that on March 16, 1953, a brilliant man of genius was born, an evangelist and philosopher of our time, Richard Matthew Stallman

A high-tech guru, founder of the free software movement, the GNU project, the Free Software Foundation, and the Freedom of Coding League. Wrote the legendary Emacs editor, the GCC compiler, the GDB debugger, and the concept of copyleft.

Richard has won numerous awards and prizes.
In 2013 he joined the number of honorary citizens taken to the Hall of Fame

Let us all together congratulate Richard the eccentric on his birthday and wish him strength, health and creative success!

Happy birthday to Richard!
