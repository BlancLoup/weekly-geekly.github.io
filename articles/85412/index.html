Astri MyID - a two-screen reader for 150



According to slashgear.com , the two-screen Astri MyID reader shown at the last CES 2010 exhibition is at the stage of a late prototype. This means that there are only a few months left before the start of production.

Let me remind you what is interesting about this e-book. Firstly, Astri MyID has two displays - one traditional for readers based on e-Ink, the second - color LCD. Secondly, the book promises to be very cheap. According to the source, the cost of components is only $ 65, that is, the minimum retail price will be somewhere around 150 US rubles, which, you see, is good.

The cheapness of Astri MyID is explained by its modest display size. The diagonal of the paper screen of the book is 5 inches, the liquid crystal is 4.8.

Reader allows you to simultaneously keep open up to 6 documents. It is assumed that the LCD screen will be used exclusively for navigation, while the e-Ink is intended for reading itself.
