3D scanning with a RangeVision Spectrum scanner

December 2016 was marked by the release of a new model Spectrum professional 3D scanner from the company RangeVision.

The Russian company itself does not need a big presentation. RangeVision has been around for years in the field of professional 3D scanning. But the new model is not so much information.

Specifications:

  • Scanning method: Structured backlight, optical triangulation
  • Number of scan zones: 3
  • Cameras: 3.1 MPix (Please note that the Spectrum model is equipped with 2 cameras to more accurately determine the 3D point).
  • Operating range (minimum): 133x100x100 mm
  • Operating range (maximum): 520x390x390 mm
  • Scan object size (min): 1 cm
  • Scan object size (max): 3 m
  • Scanning accuracy: 0,072- 0,26mm
  • Data Format: STL



Contents of delivery:



RangeVision Spectrum is a new universal 3D scanner that is perfect for scanning objects from 1 cm to 3 meters.

Benefits of RangeVision Spectrum:

  • Three customizable scan areas for professional digitization of objects of any size, from 1cm to 3m
  • Two industrial cameras for color 3D models with high details (from 0,072mm)
  • Depending on the features of the object and working conditions, you can choose any convenient scanning mode - for scanning objects of any size and shape.
  • High resolution texture scanning support

RangeVision Spectrum - a very accurate 3D scanner allows you to get 3D models of objects with high accuracy (up to 0.04 mm) and degree of detail (0.072 mm).

The scanner is perfectly equipped, easy to use and is perfect for scanning various items: from 1 cm to 3 m.

The kit includes a turntable that allows you to scan small items without any problems.

Dry figures and beautiful photos are of course important data. But everyone is interested in how to work on this equipment. And how it can show the result.

For you, we want to present a video of the 3D process of scanning the glue gun body. This video is just to demonstrate the capabilities of the equipment. And at the same time is a training material for working with the scanner.



From the video it becomes clear that the scanning process is not very complicated. And you can, quickly enough, to teach to use the scanner, any person. At the same time, the achieved result is at the highest professional level.
