Yandex finances "Zenmoney"

Yandex has decided to finance the project " Zenmani " - service accounting and personal finance planning. Financing is carried out in the framework of the program Yandex. Factory, launched in April of this year. The only condition that Yandex put to the authors of the project was the need to use the Yandex API in further development.

Zenmani participated in the Yandex.Start event held on March 15, 2011. Now we are actively working with the authors of the project and are thinking about further investments in the service.

The nearest Yandex.Start will be held on June 28th. We received more than 600 applications, but only about 30 projects met our interests and requirements and were shown to Yandex managers. Nine projects got to the stage of personal meetings, of which only three are currently invited to Yandex.Start. If your project meets our requirements , we are waiting for your application for participation in the nearest Yandex.Start until June 15.
