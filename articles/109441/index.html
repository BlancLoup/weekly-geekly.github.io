New Facebook Profiles

Facebook programmers have completed work on new profiles, which will be enabled by default for all users by the end of the year. The gradual migration began this morning for selected users, but everyone can turn on a new profile without a queue. There are three major changes.

1. Brief business card. A listing of two or three lines of basic facts from a person‚Äôs biography and photos that he considers important (at first, the system automatically selects the last pictures marked with the name of the user).



2. Selected friends. Now everyone can make a list of best friends that will be displayed all the time in the profile. Separately - lists of relatives, classmates, colleagues.



3. Individual projects in the profile. In the new profile, you can paint in more detail what you do at work. Specify the names of specific projects and the names of your colleagues who participate in the same project. For example, one of the developers of new profiles indicated this project (Profile revamp) and a list of programmers who also participated in the development in their profile.



In addition to projects at work, you can describe your hobbies, list special courses at the university, etc. - all with the indication of friends with whom you are doing this.

Along with the three main innovations, some additional features were added, for example, a quick display of your common interests on the page of any user, endless scrolling of photos, new features on the people search page (search by school, city, etc.).

If you have not yet been transferred to a new profile, then you can learn more about the innovations, and on this page you can join the protest group in favor of returning the old system.
