The year is coming to an end. It's time to sum up.
Top 50 .com domains (ranked by number of unique users).

Main trends (Gainers' List - winners):

  • Among the top ten are 5 content sharing sites (You Tube, Flickr, Facebook, Wikipedia, Digg content sharing sites).
  • The traffic on Facebook.com grew by 14.6 million, but attention! MySpace.com is not lagging, traffic has grown by 10.2 million over the same period.

  • Digg is growing faster than Facebook!

Basic trends (Losers' list - losers):
