Payoneer: Your Affiliate Partner

In one of our previous posts, we began a story about various industries of the Internet business, in which Payoneer cards are used to receive various payments, fees, salaries and bonuses. Judging by the number of comments, the topic aroused interest in the Habra community and whether we would like to continue it today.
So, today we will talk about our interaction with advertising and partner networks.

These people are really pushing forward e-commerce, they are the workhorses of the Internet, to which we owe to the fact that over the past twenty years, most modern companies, one way or another, moved their activities online, drastically changing our lives. Today webmasters are everywhere. Webmasters participate in advertising and partner networks and earn money for the Internet traffic their websites bring, and for many years Payoneer has been specializing in paying them these very incomes. Using our cards, a large number of partner and advertising networks make payments to webmasters and affiliates around the world - from the CIS countries and Eastern Europe to Brazil and India.
Among our partner companies, which we organize the payment process, there are networks that are fairly well known in the Russian- speaking part of the Internet, for example - MediaShakers , Convert2Media , Smowtion , Copeac , DSNR , ROIRocket , Ybrant Digital (Lycos / Oridian) , Conduit and many others.

Now let's analyze the above examples of affiliate programs by type, in a little more detail:
MediaShakers , Smowtion , DSNR , ROIRocket and Ybrant Digital are all-the-art banner networks that redeem all traffic and show the main types of advertising banners: fixed pay per impression (Flat Fee Advertising), pay per click (CPC) and pay per click action (CPA). Based on the overall indicators of the banner banner impressions, they derive the overall traffic cost factor, cost, and conversion rate ‚Äî this is quite a popular solution for monetizing sites on the ‚Äúwestern‚Äù Internet. Payments are made with a delay (hold) for 30 - 45 - 60 days depending on the specific conditions of the banner network.

Convert2Media and Copeac - these affiliate programs specialize in converting traffic to specialized sites (thematic traffic). Typically, in this segment, popular advertising campaigns are pay-per-action, although they also skip campaigns with a percentage of sales commission (CPS), pay by the number of conversions, and simply pay for clicks. As a rule, a strict geographic or sociological targeting is established for each campaign, the availability of various promotional materials depends on the advertiser's desire: these can be banners of all possible formats, ready-made static payment pages, or a set of text links.

Well, the last (in a way, but far from important) partner company from our today's story, the Conduit Network , does not need any special presentation; this network is the leader among toolbar manufacturers, allowing site owners to create customized toolbars for their visitors. Payment here takes place according to several schemes but, for the most part, they pay for the use of this or that search engine. In the light of the growing competition in the search engine market, the possibility of earning money on toolbars has recently become more than relevant.
