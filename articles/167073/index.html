THL A1 - budget smartphone with IPS screen


Today in the new review from the company Box Overview we will get acquainted with the budget THL A1 smartphone with very good characteristics: 3.5 "IPS screen, 2 SIM cards, Android 4.0.4.

I think recently little attention has been paid to reviews of budget decisions. Everyone is writing about Hi-End, or something supernova. But in fact there are people who do not need half of the proposed functions. For them, a budget solution will be enough. But in order not to go with the Nokia 1100, but to look more modern, we will consider a budget Android-smartphone called THL A1.

Sound quality


In this regard, A1 is very pleased. The interlocutor is perfectly audible, no extraneous noise. The microphone works fine too - no echo or other spurious noise is noted on that side.

Hardware


ThL A1 is built on the MT6515 chip, which is a stripped down version of the MT6575 chip. The differences are in the absence of GPS and 3G. The chip consists of a single-core Cortex-A9 processor with a frequency of up to 1 GHz and a GPU PowerVR SGX531.
The performance of the CPU + GPU combination is good, the only thing that spoils the whole picture is only 256MB of RAM. Too heavy programs / games can just unexpectedly be unloaded from memory.

Working hours


The THL A1 uses a battery capacity of 1100mAh. With an average load (for half an hour of games, calls, surfing, running various tests), the phone can work on one charge for about 2 days. If you consider the presence of 2 batteries in the kit, then with moderate (minimum) use, you can go with the phone the whole working week.
With more active use of the battery will last no more than one day.
