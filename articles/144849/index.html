Having started, the bootloader will get used to the phone, raise a full-fledged high-speed USB interface, to which the system, as always, will ask for the driver. ERRATA (known problems and their solutions)

Of course, no phone is complete without glitches and problems.
What are the disadvantages of this phone?
  • A strange charge controller (although most likely a problem in firmware version 2.3) - first, the phone drops to 1% of the charge, and then it works for several hours. This error does not affect the time of work, but the impossibility to find out the remaining charge of the charge at least the last half of the time of work is inconvenient. There is no solution yet, some were helped by several charge-discharge cycles, after which the system begins to more accurately predict the remaining charge. Well, you can always learn to determine the balance of the voltage that can be displayed on the desktop. 4.2 - full charge, 3.6 - is now cut out.

    Here is an example. With a balance of 40% interest, the device suddenly began to show 1 sharply, and showed it until shutdown, after which I put it on charge.
    Systems with 4, this problem applies to a much lesser extent, I have seen such a schedule only a couple of times, immediately after the firmware.
  • There may be poor GPS antenna contact. In some devices, GPS does not work at all, or shows a very small signal level - so much so that it does not determine the position at all. It is treated by disassembly, and by putting a piece of scotch tape or paper under the antenna's contact - for better contact.
    I am fine:

    Moreover, due to the fact that the device supports A-GPS and EPO - downloading ephemeris of satellites and predicting their movement, as well as has constant access to the Internet, GPS starts really very quickly.
