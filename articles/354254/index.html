What would you do if you got a billion?


Unfortunately, the motivational charge after visiting a cinema or an incendiary performance quickly diminishes, and all because you really wanted to get close to the picture that appeared and even may have made any efforts, but when the incendiary effect passed, you were too lazy and switched to something habitual.
