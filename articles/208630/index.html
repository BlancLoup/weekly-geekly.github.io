Turning a GoPro 3 action camera into a photo and camcorder with c-mount lenses

In December 2013, the Canadian team of BACK-BONE specialists implemented the final modification for the GoPro 3 and GoPro 3+ action cameras with mounts for c-mount photo lenses called Ribcage .
The modification is quite interesting and useful, provided that the price of it is $ 249. Judging by this topic , the team assures that the maximum gain of the camera in weight is +44.3 grams with a full complete set without a lens, of course.
They also provide a video installation video for a GoPro 3 camera.



With this modification, you can get a quality tool for photographing and filming with their own features and not a big budget. The thing is certainly worthy of attention.
