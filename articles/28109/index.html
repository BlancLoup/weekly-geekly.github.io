Some details about Diablo 3

Today, as already mentioned, the company Blizzard announced the long-awaited game Diablo 3.



A few details and a couple of screenshots under the cat:


- The game is developed with an eye on the PC and Mac. Console Diablo 3 bypasses.

- The game's plot starts 20 years after the events that took place in Diablo 2: Lord of Destruction, and tells about the eternal struggle of good (in our face) with the fiends of Hell, who are eager to die to all organic life forms. The scene is a dark fantasy world of The World of Sanctuary.



- The engine that will be used in the game, is a proprietary development of Blizzard Entertainment. Its name has not yet been officially announced. But now it is already known that the Havok technology is responsible for the physics in the game.

- The main sources of inspiration for Blizzard have become the universe of games of the Legend of Zelda series and, of course, World of Warcraft. The influence of the latter and prevailed.



- Now confirmed the existence of two classes of characters in the game - Barbarian (known Cleave, Ground Stomp, Leap, Seismic Slam, Whirlwind) and Witch Doctor (Firebomb, Horrify, Locust Swarm, Mass Confusion, Soul Harvest) skills. Each character will have its own story and plot. We will be able to choose the gender, but we will not be able to customize the look of your character.



- The development team consists of 50 people.

- The duration of the game - not less than Diablo II. And also all the same random generation of maps, monsters, etc. But with your chips. Everything will change, down to the entrances and exits to dungeons and more.



- It is expected to support the Battle.net system.
