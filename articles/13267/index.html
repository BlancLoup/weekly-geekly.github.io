New children's phone from Firefly Mobile

The company Firefly Mobile , which has developed an inexpensive phone designed specifically for children , will introduce its new version in the near future. The model, called the W100, will be significantly different from the previous 5-button keyboard, with which the children could only call several memorized numbers.


According to the illustrations found on the web, it‚Äôs quite difficult to imagine that the beast would be this Firefly cellphone W100. Yes, and the characteristics of the device the company has not yet made public. But it is already known that the phone will be equipped with a touch keypad that changes depending on the operations performed. So, if you need to dial a number, then a numeric keypad will appear on it. And if any game is loaded, the monitor will be reconfigured to display the arrow keys. In addition, the device will be equipped with a digital camera.

Since the price of the previous Firefly children's phone model does not currently exceed $ 60, we should expect that the W100 will cost quite well. The time of the appearance of the phone on sale is not reported.
