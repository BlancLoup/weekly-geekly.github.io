Photo of Pegatron on ARM (i.MX51) and Asus Eee PC 1005HA

Comparative dimensions of two netbooks - on top of Pegatron on ARM, bottom of Asus Eee PC 1005HA.
Pegatron weighs 850 grams and, thanks to the ARM processor, runs on a single 2-cell battery for as much as 8 hours.
We look forward to whether it will cost $ 200, as the company promised.



Comparing these two devices as the photographer did, of course, is not very correct, because they have different size screens (8.9 for Pegatron and 10 for Asus). Nevertheless, everything that becomes known about the coming Pegatron is incredibly curious.

It is already known that he will have a 2 cell battery, 4400mAh. It would seem a little - but due to the profitability of the ARM processor (the Freescale i.MX515 processor, this is ARM Cortex-A8), which is herted by the Pegatron at 800 MHz - it runs about 8 hours from this battery.

It weighs 848 grams Pegatron (1.87 pounds).
If he really goes on sale at a price of $ 200 ... Or at least - let's be realistic - close to that ... it will be a success.

Although, of course, a real breakthrough in this niche will go from models to ARM with a frequency of 1 GHz and 1.3 GHz. On the other hand, it will strongly depend on their price. More precisely, as far as it will be lower than netbooks on Intel architecture with similar performance, functionality and battery life.

The photo shows a special pink model. Do not be afraid, in fact, the main colors will be normal.
