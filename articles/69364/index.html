BambooInvoice: A Billing Web Application

Many freelancers often face the need for billing their customers. Usually, Excel or similar programs are used for this. However, there are free web applications, one of which I want to tell briefly. It is called BambooInvoice and has the following features.

- Maintain customer base and their contacts
- Creating accounts
- Sending invoices by soap
- Creating pdf-accounts
- Printing invoices
- Tracking overdue accounts
- Notes to bills and their payments
- Export to excel or xml
- Accounts reports

Technologically, BambooInvoice is a set of PHP scripts based on CodeIgniter that is installed on your server, MySQL is also required (drivers for MSSQL, Postgre, OCI8, SQLite and ODBC are included).
