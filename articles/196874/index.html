New HP Chromebook 11 is available on Google Play

Today, Google has officially unveiled its new product of cooperation in the production of various devices. Partner in the release of the next Chromebook'a has become the company HP, which has already released a model running Chrome OS - Pavilion Chromebook 14. HP Chromebook 11 is equipped with a 11.6-inch IPS screen with a resolution of 1366 x 768, the viewing angle is 176 ¬∞, the color gamut is 60%, and the brightness is 300 nt.



A dual-core Samsung Exynos 5250 GAIA processor with a frequency of 1.7 GHz, 2 GB (4x 4Gbit) DDR3 RAM and 16 GB flash memory is installed.
