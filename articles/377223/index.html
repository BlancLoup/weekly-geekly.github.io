Turkey wants to ban Minecraft



The popular game Minecraft attracted the attention of the Turkish authorities, and from an unexpected side. Yesterday the commission issued a verdict: yes, the game is too cruel and should be banned.

The report of the Ministry of Family and Social Policy has been sent to the relevant authorities, which should develop a legal procedure for the implementation of the ban. After that, the final decision will be made by the court.
