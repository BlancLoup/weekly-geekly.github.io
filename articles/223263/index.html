Types of targets


Goals can be divided into two types: linear and hierarchical. Linear goals consist of a list of understandable small steps. For example, "read the book" X "for the month." The goal consists of: buy the desired book, calculate how many pages per day you need to read in order to invest in the planned period and, in fact, devote time to reading every day.

Hierarchical goals - consist of steps, each of which can be distinguished into a separate goal.
Consider a simplified example. You want to drive around America in a rented car, but you have no rights, no passport, you absolutely do not know English, etc. It would seem that it is better to change the dream, but with SmartProgress dreams become goals and come true in life.
