Electronic card file of arbitration cases

The time of endless running around the offices, paperwork and long waiting for answers to requests is over!

Now you have at your disposal - a modern electronic help system that allows you to:
  • Get comprehensive information on any case that has been considered, is being considered or will be considered in the near future in the Arbitration Court in any (!) Corner of our vast country.
  • To track in real time what stage is the consideration of the case, who participates in it, and what decisions are made on it.
  • Subscribe to the newsletter and receive information on arbitration cases of interest to your email!




Previously, it would take you weeks or even months to get this information. Now it will take a few minutes.


How to find the right thing?


To do this, enough to be able to use the Internet and know the location of the letters on the keyboard of your computer.
Once logged in , fill in the boxes on the left side of the screen (Case participant, judge, court, case number), and specify the search period. After this simple procedure, click the "Find" button, and the automatic search system will display in the right (large) window a full list of tasks requested by the user.



And it does not matter if you find it difficult to fill in any of the columns. The computer is not a callous official! He will not send you back because of the ‚Äúlack of information for searching.‚Äù Help system will find the right case for the data that you specified. Or provide a choice of several cases with similar parameters, and you choose from the list of what you need.
If, upon request, the system has not issued any information, it means that the requested case is in the process of being processed and will appear in the database in the near future.

How to work with the selected case?


When the specified parameters have been entered and the necessary case has been found, the electronic card of the case will open in a new window.
If you do not consider yourself to be an advanced lawyer, the abundance of information that appears may confuse you. How to understand all this, and how to work with it? No more difficult than a paper file, but much faster. At the same time - no allergy to archival dust!



Here we have collected all the information that directly relates to this case: information about all the defendants (plaintiffs, defendants, judges, third parties, etc.), about the courts that considered the case, and about all judicial acts adopted in the framework of the case.
The main events of the trial are displayed in the calendar, where all the dates on which this or that event took place in the case are highlighted.
Here you can find the production numbers, the date of registration, the amount of the claim, as well as download some documents that are freely accessible. Such documents are marked with the ‚ÄúPDF‚Äù icon.

Track Case Changes


Electronic Guardian - this feature allows you to subscribe to information on current arbitration cases and receive it in your email box. Very convenient for super-business people who have no time to search for information on the site, but you really need to know what processes are taking place in a particular case. And this feature will be useful for potential sloths: a smart help system will monitor the situation and inform you about all changes. To use the subscription, you must register.



Court Calendar


The calendar of court sessions - allows you to find out what cases, in which courts are conducted on a particular day and when it is held, for example, your meeting will occur. It is enough to specify the necessary parameters and the system will issue a list of cases that are considered on this day in various arbitration courts of Russia.
