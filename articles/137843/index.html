Nginx share has grown up a bit again.



Netcraft, as always, keeps us abreast of global trends in terms of the dynamics of the web server market and the total number of sites in the world. So, the other day a regular report was published, according to which in just a month the number of sites on the web increased by 30 million. This is an increase of about 5.2%. If it goes on like this, the HS will soon fill the Internet with the number of sites going to be in the billions.

As for the web servers, on the basis of which it all works, the positive trend of increasing market share for Nginx is observed now. So, for the month, the share of resources that work on the basis of Nginx increased by 0.27%. Numerical growth is quite significant (for Nginx
these are 12 thousand hosts), but the most significant in this case is the percentage.



This can be seen in the example of servers from Microsoft and Google: the number of sites operating on their basis is growing, but the market share for these web servers is still falling. And growth is significant. as for Microsoft, here we see an increase in hosts of 3.9 million, and in respect of Google - 450 thousand.

Well, Apache, of course, is the leader - although its share has not increased (the number of hosts based on it has fallen by 18 thousand, but all this is trifles).
