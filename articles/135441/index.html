JXD S7100 - portable gaming console on Android OS



In general, the JXD S7100 device with a strong desire can be called a tablet, because Android 2.2, installed on this creation of Chinese engineers, is suitable not only for games. But still, the device is best adapted specifically for games, with side d-pad and other function buttons. The diagonal of the display device is 7 inches, which should be quite enough for all sorts of games, and "native", and games run with the help of emulators.

Equipped with this tablet console is pretty good. There is an ARM Cortex A9 processor, with a frequency of 1 GHz, and a graphic system Mali 400, and 512 MB of RAM. Plus, the developers have equipped the device with an HDMI port to enable display of the image on the big screen of the TV. Depending on the model, the amount of internal memory ranges from 2 GB to 16 GB. There is also a wireless module Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g / n (as without it), plus USB 2.0 ports.





As mentioned above, Android is installed on this device as an OS. And yes, the dimensions of a portable tablet / console are 238.5 * 121.7 * 12.35 mm. The price of the device is $ 139, which is not so much. It would be interesting to know whether the emulators are preinstalled for popular gaming consoles or not.
