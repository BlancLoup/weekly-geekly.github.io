Video: HH.ru founder Yury Virovets personally about recruiting, prospects, investments ...

On June 15, a meeting was held with Yury Virovts, President of the HeadHunter Group of Companies.
Yuri has been working in RuNet for more than 11 years and has a huge experience in building a business and investing.

It turned out an excellent two-hour dialogue, during which two stories were told: about the invested HH and about the investing HH. At the same time, they invented a new method for evaluating startups - not in money, people, prospects, or degrees of trust, but in Moscow apartments.

At a meeting on investment, Yuri Virovets covered the following topics:
- How HH received investments and invested itself;
- What does the investor pay attention to when deciding
- What are minor at first glance, mistakes can break a business;
- How to present the project as attractive as possible for the investor.
- A specific case about investing in ITmozg was considered
