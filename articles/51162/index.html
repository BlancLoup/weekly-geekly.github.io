Mobii NB9010 - mini laptop for students



Point of View - nVidia's colleague for the release of video cards, continues to develop its notebook business. Recently, the company showed the model NB9010 - the generation of the compact Mobii. It is assumed that the machine will be focused on schoolchildren and students. Like almost all modern mini-laptops in the chest Mobii NB9010 beats "atomic" heart. The machine is based on the Intel Atom N270 processor, equipped with 1 gigabyte of RAM and 160-GB hard drive. According to the manufacturer, the 6-cell battery with a capacity of 4400 mAh should be enough for 4 hours of battery life (probably lying).



Resolution ten-inch screen - 1024x600 pixels. Again, according to the company, the chip of the laptop is the ability to connect a normal display via the VGA connector - they say, to the university with a laptop, and at home to make cabs, on a modest 24-inch. The model is also equipped with a card reader with support for SD, MS and MMC card standards and a Wi-Fi adapter. Among other equipment worth mentioning is a 1.3-megapixel webcam, three USB 2.0 ports and a D-Sub output. As well as the fact that the hull design provides for a subsequent upgrade. In particular, there is easy access to the memory strip and hard disk.

The dimensions of the laptop are 254 x 189 x 39 mm, and the weight is 1.25 kg.
