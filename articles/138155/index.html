TOP 10 services provided by freelancers


At PROhq, we write a lot about the fact that freelancing is an intensively developing model of business relations, however today we decided to provide our users with more specific data obtained as a result of a kind of research and analysis of the remote work market.

One of the main objectives of our study was to understand which specialties are most popular today and why. What provokes people to leave offices and develop them as freelancers?

As a source of information, we analyzed offers on several popular freelancing exchanges where the majority of specialists are concentrated. So, we present


TOP10 services provided by freelancers in RuNet:

1. Web programming
The dynamic development of technology leads to the need to adapt new devices to work with the Internet, as well as the emergence of new applications that will be of interest to modern users.

2. Website Design
Website design development is necessary for the individualization of the company, its separation from the rest. Today, there are fashion trends on the Internet, following which the organization positions itself as a modern, dynamically developing brand.

3. Copywriting
Well-written texts contribute to fueling interest in the company from the potential consumer, which entails an increase in sales. In addition, with the help of copywriters it is possible to convey any information to the reader in a language he understands. 4. Translations

With the help of translations, it becomes possible to learn about situations and opinions of experts in all spheres of life, as well as to establish stable relations between business partners from different countries.

5. Printing Design
Print design is a huge success due to the development of outdoor advertising. There are more and more carriers with which you can tell about a product or service, and the more interesting the design of a particular carrier, the greater the likelihood that a consumer will pay attention to your company.

6. Logos
The logo is the face of the company. Games have enjoyed success at all times, but today their number is rapidly growing due to technical capabilities and the development of Internet technologies. In addition, games have become part of the corporate strategy for promoting a product or service.

8. Interior Design
Interior design is necessary in connection with the development of the construction industry and the emergence of opportunities for the realization of any ideas in interior design. Specialist in interior design takes into account all the features of the room, as well as the preferences of the owner and fashion trends. That is why the rooms decorated with the help of a designer are close to the ideal.

9. SEO specialist
Search engine optimization (SEO) is necessary to promote the company's website to higher positions on the necessary queries in search engines on the search results pages in order to attract a potential consumer to the site.

10. Corporate identity
The development of a corporate style of a company is working on how effectively a company will be represented on the market, how recognizable and memorable it will be for customers and how seriously it will be able to attract the attention of the consumer so that he becomes interested in the product or service she offers.
