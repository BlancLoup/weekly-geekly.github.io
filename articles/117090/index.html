Startup Weekend American

We have great news! The fame of the performance of the Russian Weekends reached the creators of the Startup Weekend format.
Ideologists and authors of the international startup movement will come to St. Petersburg, on the second St. Petersburg Weekend (April 15-17), to share experiences and see how this happens in Russia.

For the first time, Startup Weekend will be fully open to viewers who are awaited by a special program of seminars and communication with the leaders of major Internet projects.

Marc Nager, Clint Nelsen and Frank Nourigat , two Americans and one Frenchman who created Startup Weekend, will tell about startups in the US, share secrets of how to launch their project on the international market, and answer questions from the audience.

Ekaterina Skorobogatova, Development Director of Russian Facebook, will tell you how to promote your own project using the Facebook platform. Also, especially for developers of social applications, Catherine will give a lecture on the development and promotion and will answer all the questions of participants.

Arkady Moreynis , head of Glavstarta, will come up with business models for the authors of bare technological solutions, and will also offer teams of the best projects about $ 10,000 to bring the idea to the prototype.

Participation is free .
Venue : St. Petersburg, Kronversky Prospect, 49. St. Petersburg State University of Information Technologies, Mechanics and Optics

Required registration for viewers and participants: startupweekend.timepad.ru/event/4560
