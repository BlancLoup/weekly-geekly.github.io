TANDBERG in the organization



In the process, the task was to organize video conferencing between departments. It was necessary to connect the audience with a bunch of people, while ensuring two-way audio and video communication.
When organizing such installations, you need a competent approach, you need to solve the following questions:
  1. What communication channel in our realities have units?
  2. What is the audience reach?
  3. What information will we transmit during the meeting (presentation, documents, video content, DUO video, face-face, face-face, etc.).
  4. What functionality do we need?
  5. Who will be the central hub with a thick channel and where are we going to record and store all this?
  6. Providing the hall (projectors, presenters, acoustics, stands, fixtures, corny connection of electricity and the Internet)?
  7. Who will supply all this to us?
  8. Who will serve all this, train, make installation?
  9. How to integrate all this into the overall network of the organization?
  10. Are we ready to spend the N-th sum of money to implement the system?

About service and training is a separate topic, it must be solved immediately, people are painful about changes in their work, they have to go through 3 phases: denial, anger and acceptance of the situation.

Communication Channel Requirements
It is important to consider not only the connection speed, but also the quality of the connection, which includes such concepts as packet loss and jitter. Packet losses can be conveniently checked using the built-in utility in Windows PathPing , a Linux equivalent - mtr, also a very handy utility iperf or jperf with a graphical interface in Java , using it you can generate traffic by controlling deviations.

If you choose between fiber-optic communication channels, copper or ADSL, then it is better to choose the first one, if possible and take the channel ‚Äúwider‚Äù, you will need an external ip-address for each terminal, or if you are connected to a network in a VPN, then select a separate ip range addresses in the internal network. We also launched on ADSL, at a speed of 128 kbps, of course, the conference resembled a slideshow, but ‚Äúit works.‚Äù Tandberg has developed a special technology for updating images; the whole picture is divided into sectors and only active ones are updated, i.e. if you wave your hand, then only the zone with the hand will be updated, not the whole picture, which allows you to greatly reduce the requirements for connection speed, channel quality and traffic consumption.

In Tandberg 770 MXP NPP - the threshold is set to the maximum connection speed for H.323 and SIP up to 768 kbit / s (you can increase the maximum threshold by purchasing an additional option), you also need the NPP option to transfer presentations. On average, with active discussion, for one hour of traffic work at ~ 300-350 mb, this is with a picture quality of 720p .

In the Tandberg 1700 MXP NPP - with the minimum configuration (without Tandberg MCU) the MultiSite (multipoint) option is needed, it allows you to connect to 3 codecs at the same time. It is a 22 ‚Äùtouch screen with a camera that can be used as a regular monitor. Installation
Well, we have dealt with the connection and roughly vaguely imagine what equipment Tandberg is needed, go to the installation.

ATTENTION!!! Update firmware. Firmware updated with a special request to the service center Tandberg, only for official suppliers.

Questions that we have to solve:
  • With the help of what to look and what to look at?
  • What to listen to?
  • What to say?
  • What additional equipment do we need?

With what to look and what to look at?
If the audience is not large, you can and should use LCD or plasma panels, the pictures are richer on them, immediately take 2 identical panels of the maximum diagonal and scatter the picture with the presentation and ‚Äúfaces‚Äù. If the audience is large, you need to use a good projector based on the parameters depending on the audience, the illumination: 2500 lumens for an audience of less than 100 people and daylight, 3000 lumens for an audience of 100 to 200 people and daylight, 5000 lumens for an audience of more than 100 people and bright daylight.
Not unimportant is the parameter of maximum resolution, the size and quality of the projected screen. The best surface for the screen, in my opinion, is silver-gray with a reflection coefficient of less than 1, it is more stable in extraneous light sources and its color reproduction is the highest, and of course it is necessary to select it depending on the projector resolution and aspect ratio.

What to listen to?
On the market a large selection of different acoustics. For an audience of 50 people, a full-time 100 watt of power is enough. It is better to use pendant subwoofers, you can even take it already with a built-in amplifier.

What to say?

Omnidirectional microphone copes with its functions, if you doubt that one will not be enough to buy another one (put at the beginning and at the end of the audience). But for an audience of 50 people, it is quite acceptable for one.

What additional equipment do we need?
It is important to provide equipment with good protection against static electricity, to purchase a good UPS, given the power of the projector.
For presentations, there is a nettop with a monitor in each audience. DropBox is installed on them, during the meeting, through which files are exchanged, everything is simple.

Training and control system management
It is better to immediately appoint a person responsible for this system, so that he feels the whole process of installation and setting up the equipment, he himself participated in the installation. He should deal only with training and installation, as well as monitor all systems in real time.

Management can be done using a convenient Web-based interface and telnet. Screenshots of the web-face, you can see here. To control the camera via telnet, you can use the special emulator of the MXP Soft TRC4 control panel .
Well, for clarity, what is happening in the audience, you can install cheap ip-cameras aimed at the screen.
It is better to immediately connect and configure SIP, this is a workaround that will save the situation if something gets mixed up on the highways.
