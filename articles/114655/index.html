Release Flare 0.11

Flare is a young, open-licensed RPG game. Many have long dreamed of an open analog of Diablo, and now there are shifts in this direction. At the moment, the game is developed by one person, there is no script, no possibility of buying things, but the engine and good isometric graphics are ready. Available and 3D-models of all objects in the format of Blender.

Innovations in 0.11 :
  • Enemies have four abilities like a hero;
  • There are new enemies who shoot a bow, own spells, throw darts, can teleport and much more;
  • Most abilities have moved to the configuration file;
  • New Cave (east of Goblin Warrens with sixth level creatures);
  • New enemies: skeletons-archers, magicians-skeletons and others;
  • New magical ranged weapons: fireballs and ice spikes;
  • Various bug fixes.
