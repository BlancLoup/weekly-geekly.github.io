On Thursday, I propose to view the third edition of the kedDroid video preview, devoted to the review of software for Android. As usual, today we are talking about three applications: one program, one game and one utility (this is either live wallpaper, or widget, or a small utility).
Enjoy watching!





In today's review:

Google Reader is the official application for reading RSS feeds. And how good it is - look in the review!


Dungeon Hunter - a beautiful and interesting RPG from Gameloft. The app was free, but now costs $ 5. In addition, there is no game in the Market, you will have to go to the Gameloft site.

Backup To Gmail is a useful small application that will allow you to back up all SMS, MMS and call logs to Gmail. Unfortunately, the application is paid, if you have the opportunity to buy, it will cost $ 2.
