According to the law of the Russian Federation, which in this part fully corresponds to the natural law, the top management of rich large enterprises should have done this:
Labor Code of the Russian Federation, Article 134. Ensuring the increase in the real content of wages
Ensuring an increase in the real content of wages includes the indexation of wages due to an increase in consumer prices for goods and services. State bodies, local governments, state and municipal institutions produce wage indexation in the manner prescribed by labor legislation and other regulatory legal acts containing labor law norms, other employers in the manner established by collective agreement, agreements, local normative acts.


Officials of the Federal Labor Agency official letter in 2010 gave an explanation that it is the responsibility of all enterprises, including commercial ones.

In 2014, this was confirmed by the Constitutional Court:
Last year, in the Definition of July 17, 2014 No. 1707-, the arbitrators of the Constitutional Court of the Russian Federation indicated that the employer does not have the right to deprive employees of the statutory guarantee and evade the establishment of an indexation procedure in a collective or labor contract or in a local regulatory act.


Only the difficult financial situation of the company can be the basis for refusal from indexation of salaries:
The Definition of the Moscow City Court of September 12, 2014 No. 4g / 1-9076 states that the employer's obligation to index salary is not unconditional, but depends on various factors, including the economic indicators of the organization. If the company has unfulfilled monetary obligations, including debts owed to employees on the basis of court decisions, this indicates the difficult financial situation of the company and, therefore, the absence of conditions for salary indexation.
