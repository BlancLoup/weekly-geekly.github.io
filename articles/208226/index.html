This is not enough, and considering the increased risks associated with some kind of suspicious activity of the Central Bank on revoking licenses from various banks, it looks rather ambiguous.

As for the deposit insurance system, it is not very convenient, if only because in order to protect yourself somehow with more or less large amounts (even if a million rubles), you need to open several deposits in different banks. In addition, in the event of a large-scale banking crisis, when several banks close at once and people start demanding reimbursement of their deposits, will the DIA have enough money for everyone? Stock market




Ideally, the stock market should fairly accurately reflect the situation in the country's economy. In Russia, as we all know, the outgoing year was not remembered by any particular economic growth, rather, on the contrary, stagnation takes place and even the expectation of some future changes for the worse.

Deaf side and the drop in volatility in the market is confirmed by the fact that the distance from the maximum to the minimum in 2013 is only 295 points on the MICEX index. Even in 2012 there were about 400 points, despite the fact that the market levels were about the same. If we calculate the trading range in 2013 in relation to the annual maximum and compare it with previous periods, then it turns out that it was the lowest volatile year for the entire, albeit short, history of the Russian stock market. This is quite an interesting fact, if we recall that in parallel the same US market during this time showed a completely opposite situation, when the trend movement upward continuously continues throughout the year. ( Source )

Our market is lagging behind in growth from the world - this is a fact. The S & P500 index grew by almost 30% over the year, having updated historical maximums more than 4 dozen times, and the growth of domestic indices, except for the period at the beginning of the year when the maximums were set, almost did not grow - MICEX rose only by ~ 1%.



In such a situation, it is not possible to count on the opportunity to earn money on some large price fluctuations, even if it is late to the beginning of the movement. For IT specialists, the good news is that in order to engage in so-called algorithmic trading, it is not at all necessary to have such large movements. Now there are quite a lot of literature on stock trading, various companies provide platforms for writing robots (from domestic, I found StockSharp and TSlab ) and training programs. With due diligence on this, obviously, you can earn by resorting to various arbitration strategies, but efforts will have to be made very much.
