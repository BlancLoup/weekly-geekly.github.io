Agile Testing Days Conference in Potsdam (Germany) in November

From 19 to 22 November in Potsdam, near Berlin, the Agile Testing Days conference will be held. So far there is an opportunity to go through early registration and save 400 euros for three days of the conference, plus one-day optional training. Trainings are conducted by Lisa Crispin, Gojko Adzic, Ola Ellnestam, Scott W. Ambler, Lasse Koskela and many other famous experts in the field of testing! By the way, it is not necessary to register for all days - you can choose those that will be interesting.

The Agile Testing Days 2012 conference is an annual conference held in Europe for and for professionals from all over the world who are involved in the agile movement. Since 2009, the conference is constantly growing and improving and the organizers are happy to present the 4th version of the largest European agile-party. What is expected:
  • 4 busy days
  • 9 inspirational keyouts
  • 10 workshops
  • 10 speeches from sponsors
  • 40 reports
  • more than 70 speakers
  • more than 400 testers from all over the world

In addition, there will be a number of workshops in the following formats: Open Space, Testing & Coding Dojo, TestLab, Test Clinic, Product Demo & Consensus Talks.

By the way, there is a chance to get a free ticket - for this you need to take part in the voting MIATPP - Most Influential Agile Testing Professional Person. The winner of that year was Gojko Adzic.

Who is this conference for? For testers, test managers, project and team lead managers, test department managers, developers, analysts, CIO, CTO and CKO.
