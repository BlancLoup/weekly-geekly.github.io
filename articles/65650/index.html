As we thought out and drew the interface interface

For our own project, Iconza, we needed an interface. I really wanted it to turn out beautiful and fashionable, and also convenient and easy to use. The essence of the project is that this is a one-page web application that distributes icons for free by set or by piece. Icons can be repainted in any color, and you can also change their size. We took a board, colored markers and sketched out a prototype of the future interface.



Following the modern fashion, we chose gothic black for the base and dark gray controls. First, they are beautiful. And secondly, they do not distract from the main thing - from the icons.



The main part of the interface is implemented using the Mootools canvas and JS library. On the server side is used Django .



We have drawn a preloader of a special design (in the form of a juicer), so that the visitor has more fun to while away seconds before loading.



To make it more interesting to pick up icons, we came up with funny and very talkative characters - a worm and a mole. They appear at the bottom of the screen and comment on the user's actions.



The interface turned out beautiful and easy to use. Many people have already taken advantage of Ikonsa and got unearthly pleasure.
