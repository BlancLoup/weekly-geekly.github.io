The immediate future through the eyes of futurist artists



On the Internet for a long time go all sorts of drawings, which depict our time, as it was represented by people about 100 years ago. The drawings are quite interesting, they were clearly created by people (or people) with fantasy. Our time is very different from what his ancestors imagined, but there are ideas that have become reality.

Now things are changing much faster than before. Work, people, life - and all this is caused by the rapid technological process. Some aspects of the near future of mankind were discussed at the next meeting of the World Economic Forum. Among the other visitors to the event, representatives of the AKQA agency and the Misk Global Forum attended. The ideas heard were then presented as illustrations.

The future depicted on them is the year 2030, plus or minus a few years.

Some illustrations look fantastic, but some of them can be implemented now, using existing technologies. For example, "printers for super homes" - their prototypes are already there and are working.
