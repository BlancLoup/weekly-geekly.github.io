An animatic is a draft of a future video, in our case, a cartoon, this is the next stage after the storyboard. In essence, this is the animated storyboard.
At the production stage, animatics approves the composition, frame duration, etc. In principle, the Animatic is the final link in the most creative part of the work on the cartoon.

Animatics as well as later cartoon includes several components: scenes, plans, frames ...

Each scene corresponds to its own place of action, for example, a gypsy in the field drags a tent - this is one scene. Gypsy in the forest is another scene.
Scenes, in turn, are divided into plans, and plans are already on frames ...
A plan is a fragment of a video (or film) from gluing to gluing (and not any gygygy !!), as well as from start to stop the camera.
