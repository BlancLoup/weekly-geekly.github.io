A bit of background


Once on a sunny Friday morning in June, after finishing all the current affairs, I sat back and plunged into the jungle of the Internet. At some point, the FilegiverCom article about Myo came across to me. Before that, I had already heard about this thing, but at that moment I somehow did not betray this meaning, but here she became interested in me. And not only me! After reading the article and my colleagues, too, we had a heated discussion about how this device can be used in everyday life. Not having come to some kind of agreement, we decided that we should touch it personally, and I placed an order.
Until October, there were no changes, except for receiving mailings with the latest company news. And on October 24 (almost exactly in four months) I received a text message from the bank about the withdrawal of funds, after which my wait was delayed.
I checked the mailbox every day, but the cherished check in it was never there. In early December, writing in support with the question: ‚Äú Where exactly is it? "- I received the answer that the device was sent, we can not give you a tracker, but usually it comes to six weeks. And I waited on.
And so, on my way back home on the 18th , I almost no longer hoped for anything, looked into the drawer and found a notice there. My joy knew no bounds, but I could get to the post office only on Saturday. On Friday, I announced the news to my colleagues and, what was my surprise when one of them showed me this article.
In any case, on Saturday I got to the post office, took the device and, having tinker with it for a while, decided to tell you about the impressions.

Using


At first I tried to use Myo to control the VLC player. In principle, everything was not bad, but for some reason switching the songs did not work. Experiencedly achieved that he was able to shift the position in the current composition forward or backward (the team should be quick, not long), which suggests that the teams have changed in the new version of the player.
For the smartphone installed this application. With the management of Google Music, it coped well, but its sensitivity to the teams is too high.
By the way, about the teams. In standby mode, Myo listens to only one: double-tap with your middle finger over a large finger, after which it goes into control mode. Actually, there are only four teams:
  1. Spread your fingers
  2. Take your hand away from you
  3. Take your hand to yourself
  4. Squeeze fist

Myo is also well-versed in space. This is used, for example, in the ‚Äú Fist ‚Äù command: by turning the hand clockwise / counterclockwise, you can increase / decrease the volume in the same VLC .
