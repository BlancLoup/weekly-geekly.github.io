SLS


The SLS technology is one of the most demanded and used in industry, in particular, in mechanical engineering and foundry.



Its main advantage is that there is no need for support elements, because the surrounding powder - print material - serves as a support for the model. Due to this, many of the most sophisticated forms become available that are impossible to print on printers with other technology. In addition, the technology allows you to fill the entire volume of the working chamber with prints, since there is no need for their contact with the platform.



Products after printing can be used as a final product, as their strength characteristics are as close as possible to cast and molded specimens.

The cost of this decision previously started from 10 million rubles, so that it was not available for most educational institutions.

SLA




SLA is a generic technology name. It has several proprietary analogues of different manufacturers, such as PolyJet and MJP, as well as variations on the light source used - DLP, LED.

The essence of the technology is layer-by-layer curing of photopolymer resins with ultraviolet light.

By virtue of its main advantage, the highest printing accuracy has the widest application: medicine, dentistry, jewelry production, printing foundry equipment, master models, burning out models.

Due to its popularity in production, this technology is also in demand in education. Since there are specialized SLA-printers for the dentistry and jewelry industry, and it can be very expensive to purchase several such devices in an educational institution, we offer you the most versatile and applicable printer for training.
