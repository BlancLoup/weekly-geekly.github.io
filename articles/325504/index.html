So. What kind of OneBox is this?


Imagine a unified system that records calls, letters, customer payments, employees and suppliers, incoming and outgoing orders, maintains a database of contacts, warehouse and finance. And based on this data, CRM OneBox generates a list of recommended actions: who to call, who to write, what to say.

Learn more about the system here .
In short, this is a universal tool uniting all business processes in a company. Convenient not only to the head, but also to all employees. Binding all employees of the enterprise into a single, harmonious organism. Control system and basis for effective teamwork.

The basic idea is that you can automate almost everything. Already, One Box Next:

  • records calls, letters, employees and suppliers, customer payments, incoming and outgoing orders, maintains a database of contacts, warehouse and finance,
