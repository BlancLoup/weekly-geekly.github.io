Amazon has announced a new generation of readers Kindle Paperwhite

A letter from CEO Jeff Bezos appeared on the Amazon.com homepage.
We believe in the power of reading, we are very passionate about books. Books convey knowledge, spread ideas and talk about other worlds. We worked hard to make reading even more comfortable and convenient. Today we are pleased to announce the release of a new Kindle Paperwhite.


The new Kindle promises to be 25 percent faster than its older brother. In the new device, the backlight is better and the screen is more contrast.
A complete list of improvements under the cat.

New display technology:
white has become whiter, and black is blacker.

New generation backlight:
LEDs are located around the perimeter, reading has become even more comfortable.

Processor speed increased by 25 percent:
Now the pages are turned so quickly that you will not even notice.

Improved touchscreen technology:
A 19 percent increase in touchscreen grid provides sensitive responses even to small touches.

The transition to other sections of books has become easier:
Now you can skim through the book, page by page, jump to other chapters, pry into the end without losing the current page.

Smart search in the dictionary:
Advanced Word Search with X-Ray and Wikipedia. For more information, you can simply tap on the word.

Ability to create your own dictionaries:
The words viewed in the dictionary are automatically added to quick access pages where you can check yourself using flash cards.

Integration with the Goodreads service:
Now you can browse the books that your friends read, share notes and rate the read books.
With Kindle Paperwhite, the announcement of new generations of Amazon devices has begun. I think that within a month we will see an updated line of other devices.

PS And the smell of freshly printed paper was not added :(
