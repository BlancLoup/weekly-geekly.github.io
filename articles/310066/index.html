31 best email marketing automation tools

Do you want to automate your email marketing campaigns? Marketing professionals know that using the right tools is crucial to creating scalable campaigns. In this article, we will share the top 31 e-marketing automation tools to do this or that job.

While digital marketing is just beginning to grow rapidly, e-mail marketing is still one of the tools that ensures high return on assets. Consider some statistics related to the automation of email marketing:

  • E-mail marketing is a tool used by 82% of all companies.
  • 95% of companies that conduct marketing automation invest in email marketing.
  • Automatic e-mail messages generate a 70.5% increase in the number of views of messages and a 152% click-through rate.

Companies that send automatic letters are 133% more likely to send believable messages than those that do a general newsletter.

These are just some statistical examples that show how important e-mail marketing is in general and automation in particular. Below are 31 tools for automating email marketing that are advisable to adopt.

Tools "drip" marketing

1. Sendloop



An important aspect to get rid of the "drip" of marketing, is targeting a specific target audience. Using Sendloop, you can create a list of contacts in a particular company and track their behavior on your website in order to further adapt your messages.

2. ActiveCampaign



The service allows you to create "drop" letters that are formed based on a survey of subscribers, their behavior, interests, location. Using this tool, you can automate the entire life cycle of a subscriber or client.

You can also use ActiveCampaign Tags + OptinMonster to start dropping marketing based on subscriber behavior on your site.

3. BombBomb



Text only messages are very boring. Even messages with different images will seem boring, given the information richness in the Internet space. If you want to effortlessly add some zest to your posts, use BombBomb to diversify them with high-quality video content.

4. Drip



The Drip application is designed to simplify the process of "drip" marketing. This is surprisingly a simple tool that has a strong impact. Characteristics of the application - collecting email, the availability of ready-made templates, the function of moving elements from panel to panel to create the necessary material.

5. MailChimp



The service allows you to send "drop" letters based on the activity on a particular site and orders that are in the basket. Gumroad is widely known as a place to sell digital products and services. Now the service still has an e-mail function that allows you to collect and store data on potential and existing customers. Then you can produce segmentation, relying on numerous features and send updates about your products.

Another great tool for determining the characteristics of potential buyers is CallidusCloud. This technology allows you to automate the process of identifying prospective potential customers, excluding those that are high or low risk.
