Xperia X10 - official announcement

Sony Ericsson unveiled its first XPERIA X10 mobile phone running the Google Android operating system, which is positioning as a device for communications and entertainment.

The X10 smartphone in the monoblock form factor is equipped with a 4-inch OLED touch screen (480x854), a Qualcomm Snapdragon QSD8250 1GHz processor and an 8.1 megapixel autofocus camera, FM receiver, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules, a GPS receiver, 1 GB built-in memory, 3.5 mm headphone jack and memory card slot and microUSB.

In addition, with X10, the company introduces the new UX platform, which is an add-on to the main platform and combines the capabilities of entertainment applications with the integration of social services and rich user interface graphics. In particular, support is provided for applications for sorting SE Mediascape data, designed for working with photos, music and video files, and SE Timescape, which collects all the data history of communication in various services with one person in one place.
