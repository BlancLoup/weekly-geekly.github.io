Melodeo will provide mobiles with music from iTunes

The company Melodeo decided to launch a joint project with iTunes , in which the songs purchased in the online store can be listened to on mobile phones.

The principle of the service is approximately as follows: Melodeo downloads tracks from a custom playlist and transmits them to mobile phones. Income is planned to be extracted from subscription fees and advertisements.

Now the system is working in test mode, but by the middle of summer we can expect an official opening. By the way, in the future we plan to integrate not only with iTunes, but also other music services.
