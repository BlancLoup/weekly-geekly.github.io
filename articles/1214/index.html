Competition >> Identity: Best of the best 2007 <<




I invite everyone to take part in the international logo competition Identity: Best of the Best 2007

The previous competition collected 970 papers from 26 countries in 10 nominations. This year two new nominations appeared in the competition, the competition itself received official support from ICOGRADA, and the jury became even more stellar. All works will compete with each other in 12 nominations (6 nominations of marks and logos, 6 nominations of corporate style and packaging).

<lj-cut>
The members of the jury and the selection committee do not see the names of the authors during the evaluation of works, therefore, they are absolutely impartial in their assessments. A preliminary selection of works in the longlist (a total of 50 works will be selected in each of the logo nominations and 25 works in each of the corporate style nominations - a total of 450 works) will be carried out by a selection committee, which will include designers from different countries: Robert Paulmann ( Robert Paulmann) - Germany, Olga Purtova (Olga Pourtova) - Russia and Bill Gardner (Bill Gardner) - (USA).

All works that have passed the preliminary selection will form a long list and will be evaluated by each of the ten members of the international jury on an 11-point scale (from 0 to 10 points). The opinion of the members of the selection committee affects only the formation of a long list, but is not taken into account in the final vote of the international jury. The unprecedented level of ten experts of the international jury, each of whom is a world-class specialist, will provide an honest and objective assessment of the work.

The Identity: Best of the Best 2007 jury included: David Carson (USA), Eric Spieckermann (United Designers Network, Germany), Keno Cato (Cato Purnell Partners, Australia), Emma Buti (Landor Associates, United Kingdom) , Alexander Faldin (fallindesign studio, Russia), Tony Spat (identityworks.com, USA), Christian "Whale" Paul (Brandient, Romania), Ruth Klotsel (Estudio Infinito, Brazil), Minato Ishikawa (Minato Ishikawa Associates Inc.), Ivan Chermayeff (Chermayeff & Geismar, USA).

Works for the competition are accepted until January 30, 2007. The cost of submitting one work for the competition is 250 rubles (until December 15, 2006) and 400 rubles (from December 16, 2006 until January 30, 2007).

Full information on the results of the international competition of logos, trademarks and corporate identity Identity: Best of the Best 2007 will be published in the 10th issue of Identity magazine, which will be distributed at the International Branding Conference HiBrand 2007 and the Design and Advertising exhibition. The awarding of the winners of the Identity: Best of the Best 2007 competition will be held at the Design and Advertising exhibition on Design Day on March 21, 2007 in the conference hall No. 2. Two members of the jury of the Identity contest will take part in the award ceremony: Best of the Best 2007, whose candidatures are now being agreed.

Do not forget to indicate in the questionnaire if you learned about the competition from this community :)

Sign up and participate!
