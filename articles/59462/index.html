Folding skateboard



Korean hobby Jin Hokang (Jin-Seok Hwang) - design boards for skateboard. A couple of weeks ago, he laid out the concept of a folding board on his blog . The advantages are obvious - such a board can easily fit into a backpack, which means it becomes much easier to move around the city on foot. You can also take a gurney to the office or university without any problems.
