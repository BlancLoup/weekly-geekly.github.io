Samsung Introduces SE790C - Ultra WQHD 34-inch Curved Monitor

Samsung Electronics officially unveiled the new curved SE790C monitor, which could be seen live earlier in September at IFA 2014. Along with the ultra-high Ultra-WQHD resolution (3440x1440 pixels) and an unprecedented 3000: 1 contrast ratio, the 34-inch display will provide improved performance and functionality .



The monitor is based on a VA (vertical alignment) liquid crystal panel with wide viewing angles of 178 ¬∞ horizontally and vertically, with an aspect ratio of 21: 9 and a radius of curvature of 3,000 mm.
