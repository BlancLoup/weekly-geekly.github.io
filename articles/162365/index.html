Especially it concerns programmers, testers and business analysts, people who spend most of their lives at the computer and are in a constant process of intellectual activity. They just need to sometimes receive a charge of positive emotions and impressions - this is what makes us carefully approach the organization of holidays, cooperatives and just small gatherings in the office.

And the reason for the event is never difficult for us to find! We have a foreign company, and we can take the opportunity to celebrate any foreign holiday. Also, our guys often go on business trips abroad, and on arrival we will definitely get together to give them the opportunity to share their impressions and treat us to good foreign alcohol. ))

This time we had 2 good reasons to have fun: Halloween was coming, the mysterious celebration of the supernatural and unexplainable, and the long-awaited extension of the contract with one of our key clients.
