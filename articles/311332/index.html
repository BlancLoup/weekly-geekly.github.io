Moscow Droidcon 2016: a side view

The team of our Android developers visited Droidcon 2016 in Moscow and was not very pleased with the event. We share our opinions about the program, reports and the general atmosphere on Droidcon and call the community for discussion. We hope that an open discussion of the conference will contribute to its improvement in the future.
