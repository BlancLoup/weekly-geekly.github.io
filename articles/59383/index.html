Dell's new Mini

Dell has announced the upcoming launch of the new netbook Inspiron Mini 10v.


Inside the new Inspiron Intel Atom N270 1.6 GHz processor, 1 GB of RAM (standard), 120 GB hard drive. As an option, you can change the HDD to a 16 GB SSD. The novelty is equipped with a display with a diagonal of 10.1 inches and a resolution of 1024x576 pixels.

From the wireless interfaces in the Inspiron Mini 10v there is Wi-Fi. For an additional fee, you can install a Bluetooth adapter. There is also a 1.3 megapixel webcam. Additionally, you can buy an external DVD.

Works new netbook running Microsoft Windows XP SP3.

The cost of the Inspiron Mini 10v as standard (HDD 120 GB, 1 GB of RAM, without DVD and Bluetooth) is approximately $ 300. The novelty will be on sale at the beginning of summer.
