E-generator.ru summed up the year

The project E-generator.ru , owned by Finam investment holding, almost tripled its revenue in 2006. The Finam press service reports that the number of orders received at E-generator.ru has increased by 25%. About 30 tasks for creativity are received by project participants per month.

At the same time, the number of ideas proposed by the authors in one project has significantly increased, the source informs. This figure increased by 40%. At present, there is an average of 500 solutions for one naming task, 300 for slogans, 25. for commercials and promotions 25. Such dynamics were achieved, in particular, by increasing the number of authors of the system by 73%. Now among those - about 14.8 thousand people.

It is reported that the growth of income E-generator.ru occurred also due to the launch of new directions. So, after the purchase of the Ukrainian company ONtext (the deal was made by Finam in January 2006), the E-generator.content project was launched. The demand for promotional blogging services at E-generator.ru is growing rapidly. Now among the company's regular customers there are several hundreds of companies, among which are Yandex, AlterVEST, STS TV company, Sport-Express, etc.
