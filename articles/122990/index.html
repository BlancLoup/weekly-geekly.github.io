Google Takeout - take away your data from Google

Google has introduced a new service - Google Takeout . In short, this is an opportunity to upload all your data from all Google services in a single archive. This is useful both as backups and in case you decide to change Google services to something else.

So far, only a few services are available, such as a Google profile, contacts and Google+ circles, messages in Buzz (along with comments), Picasa albums, Stream Google+.

Unlike competitors who are trying in every way to limit your ability to upload your own data from their services, Google is a good corporation and does not deter you at all.
