Facebook Corporation is building another data center in Sweden: a new concept of "accelerated construction" of data centers

Facebook announced plans to expand its server capacity in Sweden, namely, the plan to build the second Data Center in the city of Lulea. When building a data warehouse, the newest design approaches will be used, thanks to which the company expects to double the effective capacity of the building and build it much faster than it was possible before. The advantage of the selected project Data Center is that it can be geographically erected anywhere.



The first Data Center of the company in Lulea, which was commissioned back in 2012, became the data center pioneer of Facebook, for the first time the data center was moved outside the territory of the United States.
