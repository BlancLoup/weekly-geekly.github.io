We provide a service for the creation, storage and legal distribution of copyright and licensed content with the possibility of receiving remuneration. The service is called Content Placer.

The main advantages of this service

1. Original author's content

Content Placer can only publish copyrighted content, or content for which there is distribution rights. Thus, in the event of a dispute, using Content Placer is always possible to confirm authorship.

2. Widget

A small application that displays any post created in Content Placer, on any website on the Internet. The widget of the post that is inserted on the site is displayed in the design style of this site and is no different from the "native" post.

Here's what it looks like:





The widget has a panel of elements:
Asterisk - add a post to your bookmarks on Content Placer;
Signature of the author - a link to his page in Content Placer;
Embed button - insert a widget with a post to any other site;
Pages - post pages;
Post creation date

Through widgets, any post from Content Placer can be displayed on an unlimited number of sites; all his readers will know the author and see the link to the source.

3. Remuneration

The author can set the price of his post, starting with 1 cent and above. Users who distribute this post through a widget on other sites are called resellers and receive a portion of the copyright income from the sales of the post.
Users who are ready to buy a post, can get acquainted with its free and pay for the full version.

As a result, users were able to easily and quickly buy and legally distribute high-quality content on the Internet, and the authors were able to receive remuneration for their work.
Commission Content Placer total - nothing, on average 10%
Payment for posts is made through PayPal and Content Placer internal micropayments system. Contributions to authors are made through PayPal, Visa and other money transfer systems.
