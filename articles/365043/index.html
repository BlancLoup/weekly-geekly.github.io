InnoCentive Challenge - tracking of books for $ 120000 (deadline 1.04.15)

In developing countries, books on the way to children disappear, but the government and non-profit funds do not know at what stage this is happening.
Technology is required for fast and easy tracking of books for schools and learning centers in low-income countries.
Users: from parents of a child to the Minister of Education, non-profit funds.

The competition is divided into 3 stages.
The first stage requires a written description of the proposed innovation by the author and his experience in this field. Successfully passed the first stage? Get $ 20,000, supplement and develop your work with partners to start it in pilot mode in stages 2 and 3.
Upon completion of stage 3, the author (s) of the best solution will receive $ 100,000.

Innovation should have 4 main components:
(1) A process for tracking books;
(2) Related Software;
(3) Associated iron and equipment;
(4) User friendly interface.

The primary stage in the system under the terms of the competition should be the order of the book (Minister of Education or another user).

Full information: www.innocentive.com/ar/challenge/9933724
