Extending large missing parts of the image in photoshop: multigradient

Good afternoon friends!

Being engaged in photoshop image editing, I often stumble upon the task of the so-called "patching holes." We are talking about the restoration of remote areas of images due to the presence of a number of edge, mostly smooth areas. For example, from the image where a bridge is depicted against the sky, it is necessary to remove this bridge, filling the hole with a new sky from the remote bridge, so that it coincides with the edges of the originally present one.




The task essentially comes down to completing the image by context. The largest scientific computing communities and groups have long been engaged in this, offering their remarkable methods.
