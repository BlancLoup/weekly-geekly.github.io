HyperBaton: IKEA for sites


When I was preparing this article I talked with friends working in various Internet studios.
Despite the autumn growth in demand and the change in price policies of orders from large studios, it has become much smaller.

In many companies that develop websites, recently there is little or no work. And despite all the efforts of managers, trips to exhibitions, attempts to develop new sales channels, the number of customers is sharply reduced. Someone survives providing an account for old customers, someone enters international markets, but practice shows that the market is stagnating.

From the point of view of the customer, the situation on the market of production sites resembles an attempt to catch a taxi in the middle of the night in the center of Moscow - cars to the voting customer are lining up, but having learned the price that the customer can offer during the crisis - they refuse him and continue to rush around the city in search of customers with a big wallet.

Similarly, most large studios offer customers prices that are justified by high margins, sifting out most of the mid-level customers.

It was these prerequisites that made us think about the organization of a low cost manufacturer of high-quality websites.

Allow me to submit: low cost site developer giperbaton.ru

Ideology:

HyperBaton is a literary word, a synonym for the term " anaphora " - meaningful change in the places of words in a sentence. Remember Master Yoda? " We need to knock down these starships " - this is a hyperbaton.

If you have ever flown low-cost airlines, then it will be very easy to understand the ideology of HyperBaton in our work with customers.

For example, the renair airline discounter prohibits employees from charging mobile phones in the office, airports where the company's fleet is located are far from large settlements, your departure is likely to be in the middle of the night, and you will not be fed on board a plane. But with the help of renair, you will receive the main thing - transportation from point A to point B at the lowest price.
Well, or buying furniture for IKEA, you will most likely have to take the boxes home by yourself. However, you are ready for this, because you get the main thing - quality furniture at a low price.
