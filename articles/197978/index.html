Test on the knowledge of the agency digital market

Hi, Habr!



We have compiled a comprehensive professional test of 32 questions, which should show how a person understands our market. The test is divided into 8 blocks with 4 questions each:

  1. Logical thinking
  2. Digital arithmetic
  3. Managerial skills
  4. Using different channels
  5. Communication, conflict resolution
  6. Technological aspects
  7. Market knowledge
  8. Industry terminology

We tried to make the test quite complex and universal, so that the result would give a certain general assessment of the knowledge of the human market. We offer to try their hand and discuss the issues. The goal is to ultimately make a product that an arbitrary HR-manager could give to the interviewee, and by results understand - to what extent a person has an overall view of the industry, a kind of consolidated digital-IQ.

Some questions have one uniquely correct answer, some - a few. We are pleased to discuss the questions in the comments, and also take suggestions for what we are missing.
