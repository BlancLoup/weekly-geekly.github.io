TeamViewer QuickSupport for Android is now not only for Samsung

I hasten to share the news, which, I believe, will make life easier for many developers and administrators - I have not found any information in runet.

The TeamViewer QuickSupport mobile application, which previously was available only to owners of Samsung phones with a TouchWiz shell, now works on any other android smartphones, although this has not been officially announced. At the same time, the application began to require root access.

In Google Play, the application is not available for all phones, but the apk from the manufacturer‚Äôs web site is wonderful.
