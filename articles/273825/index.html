Hub AI & BigData meetup # 1



On December 26th, our FlyElephant team will take part in the Hub AI & BigData meetup meeting dedicated to big data and artificial intelligence. The event will be held in Odessa and will begin at 11.00. For anyone who can not come, will be organized online broadcast .

The program of the meeting includes two reports and informal communication:
  • Introduction to machine learning and an overview of popular algorithms
    Dmitry Spodarets (CEO at FlyElephant )
    In the report we will get acquainted with one of the subsections of artificial intelligence - machine learning and its history. Consider the popular machine learning algorithms, as well as run their examples using the FlyElephant platform and find out how it is possible to use this platform for solving artificial intelligence problems.
  • How machine learning helps reduce power consumption
    Vadim Kuzmenko (Software Engineer / Data Scientist in Opower)
    Machine learning algorithms are one of the cornerstones in the motivation of our users to deliberately consume electricity. We will look at how our products use analytics and why it works.

Registration for the meeting .
Admission is free, registration is required.
Meeting point: Odessa, Hub IT School, st. Greek, 1a, -1 floor (entrance from the street. Y. Oleshi through the Hub Living room).

Registration for the online broadcast (link to the broadcast will be sent before the event).
