The working process


However, it's not about the games. And although gamification has a positive effect on employee productivity, this is just one of the ways to increase interest in work. And on interesting things, if you are not tired, you will always find time and energy.

And it's not the choice of work, although many authors argue that if you are tormented by procrastination, then you are said to be busy, not your business, look for your purpose in something else. Others believe that there are interesting projects and not interesting ones. Accordingly, the first you perform easily and quickly, and the second, unpleasant to you - you put off and pull. But the main thing is not what you do. The main thing is the work process.

Someone may have been fantasizing that he had in vain chosen the IT industry, that it was boring and depressing here and that if he were, for example, a person who is engaged in the creation of movies, he would have been burning with work and full of interest. Not here it was! It would be necessary to get to the studio, where dull workflows, a harmful director, endless edits, etc. etc. In general, everything is the same.

Whatever you do, come up with a fantasy to your business - you can make it much brighter and more positive than the routine shooting of pixel enemies or reading entertainment sites. Your interest in work (and not at all on the work itself), and as a result your productivity, depends on the way your workflow is built, communication with the team, management / customer. You must, in spite of everything, build your processes so that they are fascinating, regardless of what tasks you are facing. So that between entertainment and work you can choose work without violence against yourself.
