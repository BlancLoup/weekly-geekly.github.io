Top4Top.ru: debriefing. May 31, Moscow

The developers of the highly controversial Top4Top project will tell about its structure, client-server solution and implementation of the Flash client (most of their report will be devoted to this: the reasons for choosing a pure ActionScript3 (not Flex), as well as the development of problems and their solutions.

Then Artemy Malkov will talk about Flash Player 10, namely: the Open Screen Project , the File Reference, powerful text processing, UDP, 3D effects, shaders, the Drawing API, video, sound, and an improved clipboard.

Update: the meeting took place, already have a report .

This will happen at the 6th meeting of the Russian Adobe Flash Platform User Group , which will be held on May 31, 2008 at 12:00 in the Adobe office, Moscow. Address: metro Paveletskaya, Sadovnicheskaya str., 82 Building 2, Aurora Business Park, 2nd floor, Toronto conference hall.

Please note - registration for the RAFPUG-6 is obligatory (due to the passage of the meeting participants to a protected room).
