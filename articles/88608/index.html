Overview of electronic payment systems. What to choose?

In the process of developing a rather interesting project for a travel company, it became necessary to integrate one of the payment systems on the site so that site users could pay for their own orders without any problems and restrictions.

So, the task is rather trivial, and I began to solve it by studying the subject area.

We have a small travel company (who cares: www.go-baikal.ru ). It is necessary that the user has the opportunity to pay orders, regardless of the type of electronic currency / credit card (acquiring).

The main players of this type of activity on the territory of our country were considered. In addition to the above criterion, several important criteria were of interest: the amount of the transfer fee (range and what influences the percentage), how long they connect (range by the number of days) and from which year the payment system exists (this is also quite important).
