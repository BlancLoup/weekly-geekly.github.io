System Restoranonline.ru successfully launched and is looking for partners in the regions


The Restoranonline.ru system is a unique service that unites all sides of remote ordering food from restaurants to customers.

The uniqueness of the Restoranonline.ru system is as follows:


  • The restaurant receives absolutely free personal, full-featured website delivery service with its own domain name (for example - www.restoran-idzumo.ru ).
  • In its own advertising, the restaurant advertises only the address of the site of its restaurant, connected to the system, and not the site of the System.
  • Full automation of all internal processes, workflow, reporting, billing
  • In the restaurant for the acceptance of the order in general may not be the Internet!
  • The system has its own call center for processing and approving applications
  • The system has its own courier service, but you can connect and third-party
