Preview Rambler.iOS # 9


"Even now, your enemies are eagerly watching Rambler.iOS videos"
Motivation for Harvard students

After a long break, we hasten to announce the spring meeting of Rambler.iOS # 9, which will be held on May 25 in our office at the Danilovskaya Manufactory.


The heroes of this release will be:

  • Denis Batvinkin with the report " Life without memory ". In his report, Denis will talk about what is out of memory error, analyze the causes of this error and show useful tools to deal with it.


    Discussion report: GitHub



  • Sergey Krapivensky will talk about static code analyzers , how to use them, what problems they solve, how to incorporate them into the usual development cycle.


    Discussion report: GitHub




  • Samvel Mejlumyan will consider Swift as a server development tool and compare it with popular platforms. Issues of microservice architecture, problems and best practices in backend development will be addressed.


    Discussion report: GitHub



Traditionally, in the interval between reports, a mini-game will be held. To make it as interesting as possible, now we are taking cool questions from you . If you want to become a member and boast your erudition, then send your question related to iOS development or development in general to rambler.ios@rambler-co.ru . The only requirement is that the question should have an unequivocal answer, which can be reached by a logical way.


You can also participate in Tech Talks and discuss with experts from RAMBLER & Co, Mail.Ru, Yandex such topics as React Native vs Native, ObjC vs Swift and a secret question from Ash Furrow .


And, of course, as always in our program:


  • Afterparty, where you can continue to communicate with speakers and other conference participants.
  • Raffle licenses for software from our partners, as well as RAMBLER & Co.
  • Live broadcast - follow the news on our Twitter and Facebook group.

Registration required: Timepad
