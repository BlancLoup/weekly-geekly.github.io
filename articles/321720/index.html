Procrastination is a phenomenon whose victims are all, even animals cannot withstand it. Several years ago, Tim Urban vividly described the problem in the pages of his blog, Wait But Why. And since procrastination is inevitable, it is worth trying to benefit from it - I have compiled a collection of six online services (and their alternatives) that will help you learn something new, even when there is no strength to work.

Lumosity : online games and brain training


The industry of cognitive simulators (services for ‚Äúbrain training‚Äù) has been actively developing in recent years. One of the most striking projects is the Lumosity service, whose users can play online games that are specifically designed to develop memory and other cognitive functions.

Before using the service, the user asks the user to specify data about the field, age, education, profession, and also about what time of day he works most productively, whether he does sports and how many hours he sleeps. Based on this data, a program is generated that includes several games to determine the basic level of the three cognitive abilities - mindfulness, memory and flexibility.

Voriginale : watching movies and learning English


The Cinema Original service allows you to watch your favorite movies and TV shows in good quality with interactive English subtitles.

For each film and series shows the level of language proficiency, which is necessary for understanding. The developers have implemented a player with a lot of convenient features. For example, when you hover the cursor over a word in the subtitles, the video stops and its translation is shown, pressing the space bar displays all the latest lines - you can start watching from the one that seemed incomprehensible. In addition, the film is accompanied by a mini-dictionary with vocabulary, which is also broken down by difficulty levels.


In addition, there is the possibility of adding unfamiliar words to the dictionary, which is synchronized with the application for training in memorizing.

The project is being developed in partnership with the Skyeng English Learning Service, which provides technology for effectively memorizing words. To work using the platform Vimbox .

What else : useful lessons (including video) for learning more than three dozen languages, in addition to English, are collected by the BBC Languages project.
