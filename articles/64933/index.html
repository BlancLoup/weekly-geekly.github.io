Any modernization of the site should be carried out after a deep analysis of the issue. Statement of the problem, the need, methods / methods, material and time costs, the preparation of TK and the EFFECTS of implementation. The latter is not just an analysis and forecast of the result of introducing a new functional. During planning, it is necessary to calculate the consequences for each item.

The formulation of the problem depends on how well the formulated task will be, depends on how easily it will be solved, how much money will be spent. In addition, a detailed description of the task helps to see more subtleties and details in the implementation, and to find new ways to solve it during the preparation of the TOR. The consequences of poor development of this stage are an increase in time and material resources for implementation.

Necessity - are changes necessary at all? How profitable are they? Are there any more pressing issues? Consequences - if it turns out that you solved a particular problem, instead of solving global issues, in the future the created functionality may be irrelevant and subject to remodeling.

Ways / methods - look for a long time, painstakingly, dig deep, find many different ways to solve the problem. Approach systematically to develop a method for solving the problem. Analyze links tied to future functionality. Plan how the new functionality will work in conjunction with the rest of the engine. Consequences - innovation simply can not fit into the existing system, if you develop it in isolation from the functionality of the entire engine.

Material and time costs - as already mentioned, you can find a middle ground. Need to find.

The result of the introduction of new functionality - how to perceive its end customers. How much will be clear and accessible to customers a new feature. Consequences - ignoring service, deterioration of conversion, loss of money.
