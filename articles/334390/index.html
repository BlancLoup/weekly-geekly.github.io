Magento Dare to Share - Open Platform for Reports on Magento, PHP and eCommerce

Abstracts of Reports


Magento Community Engineering



Magento Open Source is not just a rebranding of CE, but a new approach to working with the community.
How Magento helps the community, and how it can help you. How you can help Magento, and what you will be for it. The first steps. Where to begin.

Release Automation for Magento 2. The Irish Store Company Experience



Implementing the Blue-Green deployment technology for projects on the Magento 2 platform to reduce downtime risks and reduce the release time from 6 hours to 15 minutes.

Story of a Transformation



The report is devoted to changes in business processes in an IT company. How is the transformation from one business model to another. How does this affect the company itself and the people in it. The social component of change and the need to change. The story of Atwix, which worked centrally and moved to the distributed development model, and how it affected it.

Dynamic caching of personalized data in Real Life



How important is eCommerce personalization. Caching using dynamic blocks using ESI (Varnish and Akamai), which are calculated for each individual client by the system of search and recommendations.

SOLID programming on Magento 2



As an adherence to the principles, SOLID helps to simplify development using the example of Magento 2 code.
Examples of good practices, as well as violations of the principles. SOLID is the first step towards a proper modular and extensible system architecture.
