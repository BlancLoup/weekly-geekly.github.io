Transfer of corporate mail to mail for a domain from Yandex

Disclaimer


It may seem that in this topic I am scolding Yandex, but in fact everything is completely different. This story is about how little things can seriously affect the impression of a product, or help in solving the problem. Also, I hope that this article will help "fellow sufferers" while in Yandex are engaged in product modifications.

In general, compared to what it was, Yandex.mail seemed to be something far and beautiful ...
