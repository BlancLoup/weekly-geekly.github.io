Meet Steam Machines - new game consoles from Valve



As expected, the second announcement this week was the gaming console. After SteamOS it was not difficult to guess. True Valve and this time managed to take the original and decided to distribute the console for free. You will not get bored with them.

Generally speaking, Steam Machines are not consoles, but their drawings.
