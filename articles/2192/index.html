From the British schools remove access points

A large number of parents concerned about the health of their children, forcing schools to get rid of WiFi-transmitters. In their opinion, the electromagnetic radiation generated by wireless access points adversely affect the physical condition of children, although this has not been confirmed by any research.

Many schools, according to Times Online , obey the requirements of parent committees. The publication also cites the words of one of the teachers, who claims that under the influence of the fields from WiFi transmitters, he became very sick. Parents believe that until the security of access points is proven, they cannot be installed in schools.
