Dmitry Satin. Availability of information for people with disabilities
Accessibility of information for all people is a concept that has become widespread in the USA, the UK and the European Union, thanks to the adoption of laws in these countries that require equal rights for people to access information, regardless of the physical and cognitive difficulties that they may experience due to temporary or chronic disorders and diseases.
Anatoly Kostin. Review of the conference "The human factor in aviation and astronautics"
On June 13-15, 2007, in the ancient village of Yaropolets near Volokolamsk, the 3rd Conference on Human Factors in Aviation and Astronautics was held, which gathered both veterans of the national ergonomics and young researchers. Despite the fact that the problems of usability were not actually considered at the conference, its generic connection with ergonomics makes it interesting to understand the situation in this industry. This is all the more significant if you remember that it was in aviation and astronautics that ergonomics received its main development.
