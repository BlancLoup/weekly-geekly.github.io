Since the New Year holidays are so long, I decided to put things in order at home, and I was very surprised when I brought out the Toshiba Satellite T2400CT laptop from the dusty shelf. Honestly, I thought I threw it three years ago. But instead of correcting this misunderstanding and sending the laptop in the trash, I decided that such a fate was unworthy of him.



And for good reason - when I connected it to the network and pressed the power button, the laptop booted and showed me Windows 3.11. Well, I thought, and decided to write a review.

And if you have already stopped celebrating the New Year, or you just ran out of alcohol, you are welcome to look at pornography for robots. (Be careful, there is almost no text, some photos)



Toshiba Satellite T2400CT laptop with its characteristics does not shine:

The processor is 486DX2, 50 MHz int./25 MHz ext
Memory - 8 MB RAM, expandable to 24 MB
Hard Drive - 320 MB
Display - 21 cm (8.4 ") with a color active TFT LCD matrix that supports a resolution of 640 x 480 (256 colors)



On the left side of the case there is a power connector, a power button and a PCMCIA 2.0 Type II slot (5 mm).



On the right side of the case there is a PCMCIA 2.0 Type III slot, a plug where there could be an audio card and two PS \ 2 connectors for connecting a mouse and a keyboard.



On the back of the case, under the caps, the VGA, LPT connectors, the port-replicator connector (in the middle), the SCSI 2 interface, and the COM port hid.



The dimensions of the notebook are 300 x 223 x 56.6 mm, and its weight is 3.2 kg. From above - the Odeon TPC-10 tablet for comparison of dimensions.
