ShopConf-2016: we are looking for speakers



This year, on June 17, 2016, an international conference ShopConf-2016 devoted to the development, operation and promotion of online stores will be held in Skolkovo as part of DevConf-2016.

What kind of event?


Now not only a large, but a huge segment of the web development market is the creation and promotion of online stores. Analytical agencies write about the development of the e-commerce market, marketers vied with each other about what is best to sell, and SEO people tell you how to attract a customer. E-commerce is not just a market segment, it is the engine of web development. That is why the organizers decided to bring together two worlds - business owners and web developers.
