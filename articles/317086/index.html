The access to the resume database service works as follows:

New search capabilities in the database of resume:

  • Look for only those professionals who have indicated work experience, education, expected salary.
  • Search for specialists in several specified cities, companies or educational institutions.
  • Exclude from the search for cities, companies or educational institutions, of which you do not really need specialists.
  • Specify exactly which fields to search for: by the name of the specialist, his specialization or skills.

How it was and how it became

To this day, we had a bonus program , according to which the placement of one vacancy gave us monthly access to the resume database. Now the placement of one vacancy gives one day access. To gain access for a longer period, you need to purchase access separately or place more vacancies.

For each job, we used a special algorithm to select suitable specialists for the skills and display them all on the job page. In addition, we made a part of these specialists a mailing list about a suitable vacancy. All this remains a free bonus when posting a job and now.
