Banim someone else's account on Free-lance.ru

It so happened historically that I had two accounts on Free-lance.ru. I used to start one one myself, and the other started my hired employee. None of the designated accounts was used, they were once once established, partially filled and abandoned, I broke up with a former employee a long time ago, but access to both accounts remained.
Today comes another letter from the service with the text:
We invite you to visit again Free-lance.ru
We noticed that you have not visited Free-lance.ru for a long time. Meanwhile, the site publishes about 40,000 projects per month, and the average project cost is 20,000 rubles. Surely, many of these projects will be interesting to you.
