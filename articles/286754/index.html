Mail.ru Group monetizes cloud storage

According to Vedomosti, data storage with a capacity of more than 25 GB on the Mail.ru Cloud will become a paid service for newly registered users. Mail.ru Group considers this fact to be a new opportunity for users to expand their cloud space.

In August 2013, when the service just started, users were provided with 100 GB for free. And for all registered users at the time, these conditions will indeed remain.
