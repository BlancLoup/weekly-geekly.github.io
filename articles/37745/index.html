Interesting online store

I stumbled today on an interesting online store castingservice.ru. It seemed interesting to me because of its extremely low prices. I decided to look into the section "About the store", and there:

"
Here you can purchase Laptops, Mobile phones, Digital cameras, Handhelds / PDAs, etc. for only 55% -60% of their real value in regular online stores. You ask, where do we have such good prices? we honestly answer - we deal with carding , i.e. we buy goods in online stores on stolen credit cards.
We also provide other services:

- manufacture and sale of finished plastic Visa Electron;
- sale of fresh cardboard, both with the code (CVV2), and without it ...
"

I was slightly surprised by this admission. Maybe someone knows why they so brazenly admit the origin of their goods and are not afraid. Are these holes in the law or a simple divorce?
