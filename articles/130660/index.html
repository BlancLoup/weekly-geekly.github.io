Online identification in Yandex.Money through Equifax



I have long wanted to identify an account in Yandex.Money, but did not have time?

Now it can be done without leaving home . For the citizens of Russia, we have developed a new service - identification through the credit bureau Equifax . If you have ever applied for a loan in any Russian bank (whether you received it or not), you can try to identify yourself online.

It looks like this:
  1. You fill out the form on our website, in which you provide your passport data (name, surname, document number and address). Then pay Yandex.Money the cost of Equifax bureau services - 90 rubles.
  2. Equifah checks if there is a person in its database with such data, and asks you questions to verify your identity. At the same time, Yandex.Money does not get access to your credit history: the check happens on the Equifax side.
  3. If your data was found in the database and you answered all the questions correctly, you become an identified Yandex.Money user.

The questions that Equifax will ask you may concern both the loans that you have taken in the past and the data of your documents (TIN, pension certificate, etc.). In total, you will need to answer (more precisely, choose the answer option) to four questions. If the answers match the data stored in Equifax, the identification in Yandex.Money will be considered as passed.

The whole process takes only 10-15 minutes. And do not go anywhere!

If you cannot answer all the questions (for example, you do not have the right documents at hand), the money will be returned to your Yandex.Money account within an hour. Do not be discouraged if you did not manage to pass the test from the first time - you have three attempts per day.

Recall that identification is necessary to increase the limits on payments, account replenishment and withdrawal of money .
