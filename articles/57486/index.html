Ringtones Manager on Windows Mobile


Perhaps someone remembers the wdoneManager widk topic . It took a lot of time, and now the author made improvements. Alas, due to negative karma, wdk cannot publish this topic by itself, so I publish it as one of the users.

The program is now able to put a melody on incoming SMS. For owners of devices with WM5 on board there are some minor limitations - the melody should be in wav format, and there should be free space in the device‚Äôs internal memory, since the melody will be copied to the \\ Windows folder. It is also desirable that the wav be saved at 44100 Hz, Mono, PCM. WM6 users have nothing to worry about.




Now you can change the melodies of standard alarms. Here, the restrictions for SMS in WM5 apply to everyone, without exception, the melody should be in wav, and it will be copied to \\ Windows. It is possible that in WM7 they will still make the full path to the melody for the alarm clocks, as happened with SMS in the WM5-WM6 transition, but so far only.

For people experiencing difficulties with free space, made a separate lightweight assembly, which does not have a built-in listening to melodies, clicking on Play will open the external player.

Here, in general, that's all. There are few changes, but I think they are worthy of the new version.
