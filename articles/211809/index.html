Bitcoin exchange Btc-e.com suspended the input and output of rubles, and MtGox.com temporarily stopped the withdrawal of funds in dollars

A small news note. Btc-e.com suspends operations with Russian payment systems:

In connection with the decisions taken in relation to the cryptocurrency in Russia, work with Qiwi has been suspended indefinitely, as well as with other payment systems in Russia.

All financial obligations will be fulfilled in full by accessible means without charging additional fees. We recommend you use the okpay system (USD, EUR), the interest on the withdrawal of it is reduced to zero.

At about the same time, MtGox.com publishes news on the suspension of withdrawals from the stock exchange, explaining this decision by the increased number of withdrawals and related technical problems. As users of this exchange note, problems with cash withdrawal from MtGox have been around for a long time, and as a possible reason for the increase in these problems, for example, the recent removal of a single Bitcoin application from the appstore. February 10 Goksovets promise to clarify the situation with cash withdrawal in more detail.

These news are only related to the date of publication.
