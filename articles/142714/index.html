With MarketGuid'om turned a separate story.

600 rubles were transferred to the balance. The advertising campaign was set daily limit of 10 dollars. After about 25 clicks on the last banner, we stopped him, because The web visitor showed an average time on the site equal to 2 seconds, from which it followed that the transitions on it were completely disinterested users.

The teaser was stopped, however, his shows continued. For some reason, the 10-dollar daily limit on the advertising campaign also did not work. In the end, the account balance was -40 dollars. Technical support did not give absolutely no intelligible answer to the question why it happened so and why the automatic stop of the advertising campaign did not work.
