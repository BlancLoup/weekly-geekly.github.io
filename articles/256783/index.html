Frontend Dev Conf'15 was held on April 18 in Minsk

On April 18, Frontend Dev Conf assembled frontend and UI specialists for the third time. Participants were more than 300 developers. The event was held in a friendly atmosphere, pleasant backstage conversations and heated discussions with speakers.







FDConf'15 traditionally consisted of two streams and included 14 speeches. At this time, an even greater emphasis was placed on filling the program and technical reports. The level of reports was monitored by the Program Committee of the conference: Dima Dudin, Vova Dashukevich, Yegor Myasnikov, Yura Tatenko, Misha Larchenko and Valera Kaminsky.
