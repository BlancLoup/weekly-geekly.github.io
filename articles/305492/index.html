Launch of free school for Android developers in Kazan postponed to September



Due to numerous requests, it is not working people at all, but having a rest, classes in the school for Android-developers in Kazan are postponed to autumn.

Enrollment continues until August 15.
School starts on September 6th.

The teachers, the leaders of Google Developers Group Kazan, the leading developers of e-Legion and DZ Systems, will train students for 3 weeks in the development of Android applications. The course program includes:

  • Current trends in development: requirements and architecture;
  • Client-server interaction: from classic A / B / C patterns to JobScheduler;
  • Reactive programming with RxJava;
  • Modern architectural patterns: MVP and MVVM;
  • Testing Android applications.


Training is full-time and free. Classes will be held 3 times a week.
Each graduate will receive a certificate confirming the course. The best students will be invited to work in the e-Legion team.

Detailed information on the website: www.e-legion.ru/lp/android
