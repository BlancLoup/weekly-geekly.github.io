Easy way to share the route

Recently I ran into a small problem, namely the lack of simple and convenient services for laying a route. It seems to me that it should look like this - I came in and waved a little mouse, copied the link and gave it to someone.
Therefore, having studied the API from Yandex maps, I decided to do it myself. Of course I killed it much more time than I planned, but I did it anyway - gpstracks.ru .

There are not many opportunities yet, actually laying a route or downloading it from two formats - GPX and KML. Who cares about GPX - this format is often used by GPS receivers and KML is used in Google Earth. The resulting track can be downloaded in the same two formats, so the service can be used as a converter. The tick "publish" is just a start for the future when I do a search on published tracks.

PS And yes an example of a track here - http://gpstracks.ru/1v
