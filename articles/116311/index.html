Watching video without leaving the site

Everyone has long understood that video on the Internet is a necessary thing. But many forums, social networks, including habrahabr do not always allow to embed a video in a comment or topic. Fortunately, only in comments Habrahabr does not allow video to be embedded, and on most forums, even in the subject of the video is not embedded. And this script will make your life online a bit easier (it works on all sites, not just on habrahhabr).

The script replaces the processing of links to video sites: youtube.com, vimeo.com and twitvid.com in such a way that by clicking on the link (video), a video player will be embedded directly in the page. It turns out that you do not need to leave the site to watch the video! Clicked on the link, looked and closed.
