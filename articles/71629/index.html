Perhaps the most convenient online music player in RuNet!



Why the most convenient? - All the details and odes of praise under the cut :)

We all know the problem: go to a website, turn on the music player and open a bunch of tabs. And, to switch a song or add a new track to the playlist, you have to go through all the tabs in search of the one in which the music plays.
Agree, not very convenient. But it is even more annoying when you inadvertently close the tab with the player.

Well, forget about it! We did everything to make our listeners as comfortable as possible, and nothing prevented them from enjoying their favorite music! Now, if you have several windows open with Thun , then all will be shown the same player. And you can control the playback of music from any window, and not search for the one from which the sound comes. And this concerns not only neighboring tabs, but neighboring browsers!

Perhaps, there is no such thing anywhere. Not only here, in RuNet, but nowhere else at all :)
But this is not the only feature of our player . It should be noted:
  • auto selection of new tracks based on the listened;
  • convenient work with playlists;
  • composition evaluation;
  • and many many others!
