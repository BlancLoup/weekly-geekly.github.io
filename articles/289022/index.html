Google received a patent for smart contact lenses

The US Patent Office has granted Google patent No. 8,985,763 on multi-layer contact lenses with sensors and a microcontroller. The device can be used to measure temperature, glucose and cholesterol levels and blood alcohol levels. The lenses will leave their primary vision correction function.



In January 2014, Google received a US patent for smart lenses with a video camera . The documents described a device in the form of a contact lens containing an electrical circuit, a camera and sensors. The sensors of light, pressure, temperature and electric field were mentioned.
