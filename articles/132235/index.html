IOS Developer's thorny path

Hi Habr!
I recently hooked on your website, in particular, the section dedicated to iOS development. I have been doing this professionally for 5 years now and have seen a lot. Now I decided to share my experience, maybe someone will find it useful.

First about the most unpleasant. Many have already written about this here because I will describe the most important, from my point of view, problem - this, of course, is the process of application validation. Over the years I have seen quite a few editions of this, perhaps the most important document for any developer ( App Store Review Guidelines ), but sometimes it just comes to the point of absurdity. For example, a client came recently: ‚ÄúMake me a very simple application. I want a picture of an electric razor to vibrate and buzz + advertisement. ‚Äù Work for an hour, two, especially not straining. I did it, I uploaded it on iTunes, we are waiting for 5-6 days ... Rejected, they say it is too simple and does nothing:
  • 2.21: It can be rejected

It does not matter, added a couple more rubbish and nonsense. But I want to say right away, life has taught and I feel the approach of such moments even at the stage of discussion of the project.
I have written programs in appstore, probably more than 50 lying around, of which about one-third had problems at this stage. And these are only those that I know about, but in fact the statistics are even more sad.

Now they are trumpeting everywhere about the new iPhone, Siri ... The other day, I got a website , caught fire, decided to write a similar program for the iPhone. In due course, and with desire, I always had a hard time on my projects (as the saying goes, a shoemaker, yes without boots), because I threw everything I could out of it, leaving only the main thing. Somehow I assembled the interface on Photoshop and sent it for validation.


The result was not long in coming.

I have several more such examples, but despite all this I want to say, the development for this platform is a very entertaining occupation, which completely provides me and feeds, and also remains for gasoline for a two-wheeled friend.
