Web options in Google search

On the Google search results page, pleasant changes are coming, announced in May at the Searchology conference .
When searching through google .com on the left, the options menu for displaying search results is displayed.
There is a display only video, pages from forums, and reviews. There were options for displaying results with pictures (not image search), outputting more text, and many other interesting and useful options.
What to tell, try it yourself .
