DASH Evolution Announced as Social Payment Network

Pseudonymous identity

Another key feature claimed is related to DASH identities. We are talking about meta-data that can be specified by the users themselves, for example, their E-mail address. This, according to Evan, will allow ordinary users to quickly and easily exchange public keys (billing addresses) over a secure protocol between themselves without the need for direct interaction with their private encryption keys. Users will be able to exchange lists of their public keys in the background, which will make the execution of all transactions much easier and provide users with advanced privacy tools DASH - by default.
