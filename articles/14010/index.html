Technology, look into the future

From reading the most striking is the synthesis of gold, the single currency kilowatt-hour, portable quantum generators, the development of artificial intelligence. If we take together these four technologies, then there will be a global redistribution of funds in the world market. So, we sell the accumulated gold?

Arthur Clarke Predictions

2009 - an atomic bomb explodes in one of the third world countries, after which the UN will decide on the destruction of nuclear weapons

2010 - deployment of a total electronic tracking system for the eradication of crime and the emergence of portable quantum generators that extract energy directly from a vacuum

2012 - regular flights of space planes

2014 - space hotels will be opened in orbit

2015 - the flowering of nanotechnology, full control over the atomic structure of matter, the production of gold from other metals, the triumph of alchemy

2016 - the introduction of a single world currency - kilowatt-hours

2020 - artificial intelligence will reach the human level, two kinds of mind will coexist on Earth, but one will develop faster than the other.

2021 - landing of people on Mars

2023 - dinosaur cloning due to computer-generated DNA structure. Small dinosaur individuals will replace guard dogs

2025 - discovery of the mechanisms of functioning of the senses. Replacing the nose, eyes, skin with more efficient artificial organs

2036 - China surpasses US gross domestic product

2040 - reproduction of molecular duplicates of any objects and substances. It will be possible to make products, clothes, diamonds from street mud. Industry and agriculture lose their meaning. A person goes into art, education and entertainment

2045 - creation of a closed-cycle dwelling with complete self-sufficiency and recycling of waste

2050 - mass freezing of people who in a cryonic dream rush into the future

2051 - settlements on the moon for pensioners who have low gravity

2090 - the onset of a new glacial period, a person will begin to intensively burn fuel to create the greenhouse effect

2095 - the creation of vehicles with speeds close to light.

During the Second World War, Arthur Clark served in the British Air Force and, when the volleys died down, made several predictions based on a good understanding of the technical systems and the possibilities of their development. The young officer guessed the appearance of radar to search for rockets and airplanes and indicated reliable frequency ranges. He got a taste and, having entered the literary path, for several decades now has published a summary of the expected scientific breakthroughs. Among the predictions come true - the location of communications satellites in geostationary orbit (in the West it is called the Clark orbit), the emergence of the World Library called the Internet, machine translation, development of nuclear energy and automation, progress of antibiotics and computers, work on artificial intelligence, landing people on the moon (with a minor error in the date), human cloning, which coincided in time with the dubious operations of Dr. Antinori. The date of the forthcoming landing on Mars according to Clark agrees with the plans on this subject of US President Bush.
