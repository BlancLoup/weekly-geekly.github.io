Motorola will be producing sunglasses with headphones

The Federal Communications Commission (FCC) has approved the release of a Motorola combo gadget. By the way, I have no idea, and here the FCC, maybe someone in the know? ETS Motorola S71, and this is the working title of the new product - this sunglasses with integrated headphones. The idea, of course, is not new, but the thing itself is interesting.


You can connect a Bluetooth adapter to the Motorola S71 ETS (I wonder where?), Which will allow you to use glasses for their intended purpose - i.e. Listen to music from any compatible player or mobile phone. Also somewhere on the headphones there are special buttons with which it will be fashionable to control playback. You can charge O ROKR Pro (the second name of the gadget) from an ordinary power outlet (the charger is included) and via USB.

Unfortunately, no other details about the Motorola earpieces could be found. Neither of the manufacturer directly points (in the picture they are clumsy, but you never know, it will be some Gucci that will make the price transcendental - with Motorola will), nor about the volume of the battery. Price and date of entry into the market is also not advertised. However, the summer ends and the sunglasses are no longer as relevant as three months ago. So, by next summer, when the ETS Motorola S71 is not only released, but also cheaper, you can already think about purchasing.
