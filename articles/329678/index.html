English for beginners and forgotten


  • English as per notes


    Pros: the author with a good sense of humor, teaches concisely and intelligibly. Learning English with music, movies and games.
    Minuses: the amount of video is not very small and the author, for some reason, does not add new ones.

  • English at Jobs school


    Pros: a convenient and interesting channel. There is English for beginners, analyzes of your favorite films. Analysis of metaphors, idioms and comparisons in English, and in order to make it easier to absorb the material, for example, the author uses songs.
    Minuses: beginner grammar lessons are given in a random video and, apparently, they will not be added anymore.

  • English Galaxy


    Pros: a very useful channel. Detailed and at the same time very simple lessons. And for zero students, both for beginners and advanced - this channel will be useful to everyone. Users claim that the author has a pedagogical gift :)
    Cons: lessons of the same type and the lack of video or audio inserts from movies or songs.

  • EnglishDom.YouTube


    Pros: the channel, which contains fragments of serials and films, funny videos, webinars on various topics - from exam preparation to interviewing in English. In addition, you can find mini-lessons on grammar and introductory videos of teachers.
    Minuses: new and cool videos should be searched already on the site in the training section , where there are tasks for the video.


English with carriers for advanced



  • EngVid


    Pros: perhaps the coolest channel known to us, on which a whole team of native speaker teachers works. Particularly interesting is the manner of presenting the material - everything is very fun, accessible, at ease, there is a video on the subject of grammar, features of the English vocabulary, typical mistakes and much more.
    Cons: I would like more videos on grammar topics.

  • BBC Learning English


    Pros: the channel has a lesson schedule for each day - on Monday we learn the words, on Tuesday the grammar, and on Thursday they learn to speak. In addition, on Wednesday, the Air Force is taught to perceive the news, and on Friday is drama day.
    Cons: new videos are released only on weekdays and tasks for beginners may seem difficult for those who are just taking the first steps in English.

  • EF podEnglish - LearnEnglish


    Pluses: on this channel everything is presented in the form of analysis of situations. Do you remember Dasha, a traveler for children? Here is the same channel for adults. And at the end of each video you will be very soulfully praised.
    Cons: new videos are released only on Tuesdays and many videos from the channel are already a bit outdated.

  • Fluency MC


    Pros: a specific and interesting form of education. Small video lessons are submitted in the form of rep-texts with a vivid picture. The author of fun with his repchik literally knocks verbs into your head.
    Cons: the style of filing may not be all.

  • Learn English with Let's Talk


    Pros: the feature of this channel is in the analysis of the most common mistakes, the comparison of words, prepositions and expressions, which are often confused and used incorrectly.
    Disadvantages: some videos have been dubbed using bots, so sometimes there is not enough liveliness and intonation on the video.


As you can see, there are a lot of interesting resources, thanks to which you can have fun and relax learning English, allocating at least 5-10 minutes a day. And if you also have your own personal dictionary and add new words or grammatical structures from the video there, and then repeat them from time to time, you will manage to get the most out of your holiday and return home not only tanned, but also pumped in English!

We wish you good luck in learning and look forward to your comments!
