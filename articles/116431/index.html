Internet advertising: developed a unique content targeting system

A new technological solution has appeared on the world market, which allows to automatically display contextual advertising based on visual content.

Currently existing web advertising services offer advertisers to place ads depending on the theme of the site, based on keywords or taking into account socio-demographic characteristics specified by users. However, on the Web there is a huge amount of visual content that is hosted by users from different parts of the globe. The uniqueness of the service lies in the ability to automatically select advertisements depending on the content of the visual content of web pages. In this case, the presence of text descriptions is not mandatory.
