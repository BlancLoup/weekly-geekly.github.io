Sandisk has released a 32 Gb microSDHC card

microSDHC is one of the Secure Digital family of memory cards that we hear about quite often. It is becoming increasingly popular in mobile devices due to its compactness. Until yesterday, the official map was available in the size of 16 GB. And now ... it happened: the SanDisk company released a 32 Gb card. Production of this card was made available thanks to the 32nd nanometer technology of the third generation (32nm X3).

Since yesterday, the map is available on the manufacturer's website. In stores, it will appear in the next month. Estimated cost will be $ 200, and the warranty period - 5 years.
