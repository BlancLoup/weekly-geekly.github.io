GoTech 2016 participants: facts and figures

This year, 723 projects from 32 countries and 120 cities became GoTech participants. More than 60 representatives of venture capital funds, technology companies and business accelerators took part in the evaluation of projects at the first stage. Named semi finalists GoTech 2016.



The final of the main competitive nomination will be held at the GoTech forum on October 6 at the Physical Park (Moscow). Participation is free. Register by link >>

In addition to the competitive program, the GoTech Forum will feature: Prisma founder Alexey Moiseenkov, MSQRD founder Yevgeny Nevgen, Yulmart board chairman Dmitry Kostygin, IMI.VC founder Igor Matzanyuk, Runa Capital managing partner Dmitry Chikhachev, GridGain founder Nikita Ivanov, co-founder of Acronis Stanislav Stanislav Zathachiv other.

The closed GoTech After-party will be held in the evening. This is an opportunity to meet with representatives of successful IT companies, investors, journalists and founders of cool startups (for example, Prisma and MSQRD!). Admission is by invitation only. Or you can take part in a raffle on our Facebook page and get tickets.

If we evaluate the technological component of the participants, then in the Top-3 projects in the field of mobile and cloud technologies, as well as work with big data. % shows the proportion of technology among the entire set of GoTech applications, while each participant could choose several directions.



Whatever you do, on GoTech you can find projects that are useful for this area.



84% of participants target the global market.



34% of companies have already attracted external financing in the form of investments from funds or business angels, as well as grants. 49% develop the project only at their own expense.

At the same time, 35% of companies are already profitable or will come to payback in the next six months.
