New cartographic portal

The portal of the Federal State Unitary Enterprise Gosgiscenter presents digital topographic maps of open use of scales of 1: 200,000, 1: 100,000, 1: 50,000, 1: 25,000 to the territory of Russia. Users are given the opportunity to view a rasterized version of the nomenclature sheets of maps.



The portal is new, so we will take into account all your suggestions and questions.

PS Since I took part in the creation I could not help but promote the project.
PPS There is a beta version of the portal for Android and iOS.
