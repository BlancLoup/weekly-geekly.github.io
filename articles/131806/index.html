Tenzor company has announced a programming contest, the prize is a scholarship from them for the whole academic year.

Yaroslavl company "Tenzor", dealing with everything from writing software to recruiting staff, announced a very interesting competition for students of 2-5 courses of any university.

Everyone can participate, simply fill out a form on the site. But this must be done before November 7 !

A small quote from the site :
How is the competition held?

1. APPLICATION
First you need to fill out an application on the site or a paper form in the service of the personnel of the company "Tenzor".

2. MEETING
The second stage is a story about yourself in any form. The main thing that we want to hear from you is your achievements, in other words, that you can do better than many.

3. PROJECT
If you have passed the second stage, we will send you a task that you will have to complete within 3 days. Then it goes to the jury, consisting of the leaders of the development department of the company "Tenzor".

4. FINAL
If your work has struck the imagination of our jury, we will invite you to the final. At this stage, the main task will be to protect the project.

5. REWARDING
We invite participants to the cafe and give everyone valuable gifts from the company "Tenzor". The winners will get something even more interesting: 10 main prizes are scholarships in the amount of 3,000 rubles. monthly during the school year. In addition, some lucky ones will receive a special "Jury Prize" - a cool gadget from America.

PS: Tenzor Company was a sponsor on HackDay in Yaroslavl.
