Mikhail Chabanov proceeded to the creation of the Russian OS

As it turned out recently, the overwhelming majority of Russians support the creation of a Russian operating system. The results of a survey conducted recently on the Mail.ru portal were a revelation: 55% of respondents consider it reasonable to create a Russian OS from scratch, and another 30% to create their own Linux distribution.

Naturally, the first enthusiasts immediately appeared who are ready to embody the people's will in reality. On launching a project to write the core of the Russian OS, a certain Mikhail Chabanov said.

Deputy Viktor Alksnis received a letter from Chabanov and published it in his blog :

Hello, Victor Imantovich!

Today I wanted to leave a message in your LJ on the topic of creating an initiative group of developers of the Russian OS. Leaving comments is not allowed anonymously, but I do not have my LJ page. Therefore, I decided to send this message to you by e-mail. If you see fit, publish it in your journal:

! urgent! - READ ALL -! urgent!

I propose to get down to business without too much chatter. We will have our own OS only if we start working on it. I am a developer - I live in St. Petersburg. Surely the blog is read by other programmers. I propose to assemble an initiative group of developers of the Russian OS. These people should be interested in the design and do from the heart. In the first stage, you will need experienced software designers.

Anyone who feels the strength to write: russian-os@mail.ru.

And further. Linus Torvalds one wrote a kernel for one of the best OS. Can it be that dozens of Russian programmers will not be able to do the same?

Sincerely. Chabanov Michael.
