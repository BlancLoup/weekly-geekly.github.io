About the cleanliness of the table

Somewhere in here it is necessary to insert the idea that in one of the practical systems of restoring order in affairs a very interesting way is suggested: at the end of each working day you need to tidy up on the table (physical) and leave it clean . Do not rush to argue, think about how to do it:
  • Transfer small entries to a diary, organizer, etc.
  • Documents to put in the correct storage location (Tray / folder "archive", "current project", "drafts", "English", etc.)
  • Finish started on a piece of paper and throw it away.
The table must be clean and tidy. And behind it - and consciousness. Leaving home, you realize that things have been completed. Try it, it works.
The following consideration has two goals: the table should be the minimum necessary and the table should please the view. Therefore, we will put on it a beautiful picture. As they say now, to "reflect individuality."

Proposed concept

  • The desktop is used for quick access to key applications.
  • Objects with which current work is conducted are placed on the desktop.
  • Objects are moved across the table to different areas depending on the current state of work on them.
  • Desktop is used to navigate long-term storage.
  • On the desktop there is a picture that defines the designated places for documents and shortcuts.
A software service can be offered that automates the processing of objects on the table (auto-archiving of unused shortcuts, auto positioning, timing control, etc.)
Below I will describe a specific desktop configuration and some rationale for these ideas. However, it is absolutely clear that approaches can vary and be combined.
So, the central part of the table is given under the picture. To please the eye and did not allow to litter the table.

Launch shortcuts

On the left are the permanent program shortcuts. In the upper left corner - Basket. And then I will explain why. If there are too many programs on the left, then firstly, consider whether they are really needed every day, and secondly, you can use two vertical columns of icons. Our solution is scalable.

Working Papers Panel

The top line is the most important. She is constantly in front of her eyes, it is most convenient to use her for current work. I offer the following structure:
The left third is for temporary files, they are processed, printed, added, sent, copied where needed - and sent to the recycle bin. That is why the basket is right there on the left of them.
The average third is for the current documents that are currently being worked on. Upon completion, they can be sent to the destination and move to the archive or to the trash.
The right third is for documents that linger on the table for longer work.
It seems to me that modern wide monitors are just enough for the optimal for consciousness number of current working documents (tasks). But if there is a need, we can use two rows and above.

Document repository

On the right we will have a tool for organizing long-term storage. Symmetrically from the Trash in the upper right corner - the folder "Archive" or "Warehouse". Upon completion of the work on the document, everything that it is a pity to delete is sent there, but it is too lazy to classify. Leave this job with built-in tag and content search tools. I still rejoice every time I transfer another incomprehensible document from routine affairs to such an archive. It is impossible to create a system for everything, especially if it is not very necessary. An unstructured archive for junk, content indexing and searching is the easy way to free the table and the mind. I am stepping on a song about the archive, because I want to say a lot, but not all at once.
Next, the actual storage structuring tool. You need to create a simple file storage system and place shortcuts to key points on this system. That is, useful documents after processing are not in the general archive dump, but in the right place. And the entry points to such places we place on the right in a row.

Dynamics of the movement of documents

Thus, we have the following dynamics: documents get to the top, are taken away to the basket to the left, to the archive to the right or to the permanent storage to the right and down. The emergence of new objects on the table forces us to remove too much from the table. Take it as a rule - do not have more than 5-9 current documents, which are under active work. The rest must be completed, canceled, delegated, transferred to the future. Yes, this is one of the ideas - a time machine for files. After completing the work on the document, put it in the right place (green arrows in the figure above).

Well, and in order not to get up twice, a couple of words about the structure of the document storage (My Documents folder). We are talking about this here, because the right side of the table is reserved for navigating through the repository.

Storage structure

Mail - folder with the local cache of the mail program. Does not need backup. Rename to Cache.
Music - a folder with music and video for work. Rename to Media. Does not need backup.
Temp - folder for temporary objects when copying, etc.
SkyDrive - a folder for all documents that are synchronized with the cloud and home computer.

In SkyDrive are:
Office - office documents (travel, instructions, orders, etc.)
Personal - folder with personal files
Work - folder with working files
Photos, Pictures - pictures, pictures
Public, Shared - folders available on the Internet for sharing via SkyDrive with other people.


Further division does not make sense to describe in detail. The working folder contains the archive of projects, current projects, presentations, etc. The main thing - on the desktop there are shortcuts to the key points of this structure. And to current active projects. This allows you to effortlessly fold documents into folders for long-term storage.
