International blockchain-conference Genesis Moscow Conference will be held in Moscow on May 26

On May 26, the Waves Platform together with the Digital October Center will hold the Genesis Moscow Conference - the first international blockchain conference in Russia, during which representatives of the foreign and Russian blockchain community will meet.

The event is intended for communication, creation and strengthening of business connections, discussions, as well as exchange of views on the development, implementation, application and regulation of blockchain technology. Visitors will be able to chat with colleagues in the industry, discuss prospects, convey their point of view and dispel the myths that still exist.

Each of the eight international speakers of Genesis Moscow Conference, after its 45-minute presentation, will answer all the questions of the conference participants.

At the event will perform:

  • Alexander Ivanov, founder and CEO of the blockchain platform Waves.
  • Charlie Schrem, evangelist of blockchain technology, ex-director of BitInstant and one of the founders of the Bitcoin Foundation.
  • Mika Lammi, Head of IoT Business Development at Kouvola Innovation Oy, Head of the Baltic project for SmartLog.
  • Paolo Taska, FinTech economist specializing in P2P financial systems.

At the Digital October Center site, blockchain technology events have already been held with Vitalik Buterin and Sasha Ivanov, as well as Blockchain Weekend educational seminars for entrepreneurs, business executives, top managers and the general public.
