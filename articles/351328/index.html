Uncompetitive wrestling: how crossover tournaments have changed in their first year

Last Saturday, March 10, Crossover held the next Moscow Hiring Tournament for the Software Engineering Manager vacancy. It was quite an ordinary event in a series of our events, not highlighted by the theme of the vacancy - the position of SEM occupies the middle level in the hierarchy of our specialists - not the format. Many have already heard about tournaments for Crossover job opportunities - and many have criticized them. Moreover, tournaments are beaten not even on the passport, as an idea, but on the face - for the name itself. The combination of the words "vacancy" and "tournament" cause people to have different, often heavy, associations, and the sometimes differing real formats of events under a common brand of tournaments confuse and probably frighten a number of potential applicants. And we agree with this criticism.
