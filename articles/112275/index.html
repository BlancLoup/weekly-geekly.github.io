Spring is coming and CodeFest with her

In total, some 3 kopecks a month ago, we happily summed up the first CodeFest in Novosibirsk and responsibly promised to return in the spring. And since we keep our promises ... We are starting to prepare the spring CodeFest!

What will it be?
In September, CodeFest was held in two streams and in three sections: development, testing and project management. This time we decided to keep the concept, but at the same time slightly expand the direction of development and experiment.

As a result, we have four additional areas:
  • Enterprise - it will be interesting to developers of corporate information systems;
  • Mobile - mobile development: platforms, frameworks, technologies, rakes, trends, etc .;
  • UI - UI place in development, usability testing and other joys;
  • HR is a special, highly experimental section for managers and HR-services of companies large and small.
As you see, we once again expose ourselves to experiments, but how else can you do the coolest IT conference outside the Urals? :)

As it will be?
Two days in Novosibirsk. Both weekends: Saturday and Sunday. Whiskey-party between them. A mountain of knowledge, contacts and experiences for life.

When?
CodeFest will be held in late March, get ready.

Looking for talents!
Last time we independently searched for interesting speakers and practically left no chance to those who contacted us after the announcement of the CodeFest. At this time, everything is different: we announce the event in advance and we will be happy with tasty, juicy, ripe reports about how and what you have implemented and on the basis of which technology.

Do you have such a report? Urgently email us at sheldon@codefest.ru .

Join us, it will be cool!
