"The Witcher 2" with all additions released on Linux

On May 22, on Thursday, Polish game studio CD Projekt RED released the game The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings. Extended Edition "for operating systems on GNU / Linux. Within two days, the publication can be purchased with 80% discount for any platform via the Steam digital distribution service for 83 rubles.


Illustration: screen shot of The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings. Extended Edition, CD Projekt RED.

The game is guaranteed on Ubuntu 12.04 and SteamOS, as reported in the press release I received. The publication includes all previously released additions and sound in 5 languages, including Russian and English, as well as subtitles in 12 languages.

Playing Witcher 2 on GNU / Linux will work on computers with GeForce 9800 512 MB or higher graphics cards, Intel integrated graphics are not supported. Separately, in the received press release, the temporary lack of compatibility with AMD video chips is underlined, the driver promises to notify about changes in the situation with the drivers for the cards from this manufacturer.
