HUD and notes for road users

Periodically I play poker online. In all rooms it is possible to write a note (note) to another player. These notes help with the next meetings with this player at the table. I write there some crazy actions, something like all-in with garbage or a stake with garbage, or, on the contrary, tricky, not obvious actions, for example slow playing pocket aces.

There is still such a thing as HUD - displaying statistics on the screen during the game, there are several such programs, for example, poker tracker or hold'em manager. They collect information on the players during my game and provide me information about the style of playing opponents, and I can already adjust my game.
