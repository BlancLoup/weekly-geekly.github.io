"Oh! Picads - advertising in microformat ¬ª

What is a picad? Picada (from English pic'ture and ad'vertisment) is an advertisement in microformat (22x22 pixels). In this small box combined graphics and a small text message. Placing and payment picada made one click!

"Oh! Pikady "is a promising new project in the field of online advertising and at the same time an additional way to monetize websites and blogs. The project offers a simple and convenient way to buy and sell advertising on the site, the cost of which is very low.

Because of the surprisingly simple placement of advertising, we decided that the placement would be accompanied by this exclamation: " Oh! Picads - I will post here, too, and here I am my picada." In addition, as it seems to us, we gave emotions to our project - and this is good.

Advertisers



It is enough to see a picad block on the site to know for sure - here you can place your advertisement with one click.

Details
  • The picada is located exactly where the advertiser clicks.
  • You can select a picad from an existing list, load your own, or create a picad using a constructor (in development).
  • The placement period is either 30 days or forever (which is convenient for blog posts).
  • Cost of accommodation from 100 to 500 rubles.
  • Payment is made by SMS, WebMoney or Yandex. Money (we hope that we will connect).
  • In addition to posting on the partner site, the pick is placed in a catalog on the site of the service.

Webmasters, bloggers, site owners


Place on your site a picad block and get a commission on the cost of the placed picad on your site!

Details
  • The partners themselves can provide their advertisers with everything described above. :-)
  • The commission of the system is 50% (when paying by SMS, the commission of mobile operators is taken into account).
  • Picad blocks can be placed directly to the posts. By assigning a minimum cost and an unlimited period of placement, you can make this method of placement attractive to the advertiser.
  • Submit an application for registration of your website oyy.ru/registration , quite a bit of time will be required for approval or disapproval.
Next - add blocks, insert them on the site, get a commission from the sold picad on your site.
