Chocolate 3D printer - coming soon



Choc Edge was launched in 2012 as a research project at the University of Exeter, Devonshire, United Kingdom. The project aimed to create a 3D printer that can produce objects from material not previously used for 3D printing. Since we love chocolate everywhere, it was decided that it would be the ideal material to turn to 3D print enthusiasts and attract people who were not interested in this technology before.

As a result, Choc Creator was created.



Choc Creator has become the world's first commercially available 3D printer for printing chocolate. The first Choc Creator immediately captured the imagination of the public and the media, and after it printers such as the Choc Creator V2 and V2 Plus were released.



Main characteristics




The goal of Choc Edge is to revolutionize the creation of chocolate products and gain experience using 3D technologies for printing chocolate. With Choc Creator, an unprecedented level of individualization, flexibility and control over their creations became available to users. We believe that this technology will allow users to take the ability to create chocolate products to a new level.



In addition, it increases the potential for creativity while reducing the size and weight of the printer, offering an indispensable experience in creating chocolate products.



For chocolatiers, bakers and cooks, food innovators, marketing companies, as well as those who are looking for innovative ways to produce non-standard chocolate products.

Choc Creators V2 Plus was designed with the same ideas, but with a deeper understanding of the needs of the market. It is ideal for the manufacture of logos, more complex 2D designs, as well as small 3D shapes, great for marketing companies that produce corporate gifts, chocolate shops that produce personalized items, as well as for any food manufacturer who is looking for a new way to enhance individuality of their products.

Main features


New solutions have made Choc Creator even more reliable and easy to use.

30 ml syringe. stainless steel with removable nozzle that heats evenly, specially designed for printing without clogging.



Large printing area (180x180x50 mm.) Allows you to create more volumetric products than was possible earlier.

2D printing art


Choc Creator is capable of producing fine, detailed and artistic chocolate models,
the creation of which is difficult when using traditional methods of manual work.
To create a truly artistic chocolate masterpiece, one-line printing technique is used.



2.5d print art


For more stable, bright and interesting chocolate printed products, Choc Creator uses graphics and silhouette printing, and also automatically fills the models. Such sweet products can be tasted either immediately or decorate them with a cake.



3D inspiration


Choc Creator can be used to create simple 3D shapes, play back scanned 3D objects, and create a set of items with a more complex structure.



All models use melted Belgian dark chocolate. The use of other types of chocolate, or improperly melted chocolate, can lead to a deterioration in the quality of products.



Applications


Choc Edge allows users to create their own texts and models using simple web applications.

Mix & match

In Choc Edge's Mix & Match app, you can choose a model from our existing library, add any text to it, or simply load the model for printing separately.



Choc draw

Choc Draw is available to Android tablet users with a wireless connection to Choc Creator. Using the stylus in the application, the user can create a unique design that can be immediately started to print. For easy re-creation of portraits, you can add translucent background images to track inaccuracies.

Ease of use:

  1. Install the included Choc Print software.
  2. Open the model you need and generate a print code.
  3. Melt chocolate
  4. Fill the syringe and insert it into the printer.
  5. Click the "Print" button. Your chocolate model will be created automatically.
