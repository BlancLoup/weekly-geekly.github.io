What do they return?


Because of this, after the Black Friday in the United States begins a stir. Sometimes the queues for the return of goods exceed the queue for the purchase. But the basic rule of the great American law of commerce: the customer is always right.

The largest returns, according to tradition, will fall on consumer electronics and children's products. TV sets, washing machines and refrigerators go to the shops in the hundreds. Smaller items, such as game consoles or components, are returned no less willingly. In this category, the return reaches 30-40 percent of the amount purchased on the day of the sale.

Other smartphones are returning less willingly. But laptops (especially the first) come back very actively. Apparently, the point is quite wide customization possibilities in the USA - if 3-5 modifications of popular MacBook or Dell XPS 13 models are available in Russia, then a choice of a couple of dozen options is available for residents of the United States.
