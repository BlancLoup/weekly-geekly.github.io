ASUS F83T Review


One day my old Toshiba Sattelite Pro refused to work, and I was already tired of wearing service centers. It was decided to buy a laptop.
I limited myself to a budget of $ 500-600. Choice fell on ASUS F83T

Specifications


Screen: 14 "(1366x768)
Processor: AMD Athlon Neo MV-40 (1.6 GHz)
Chipset: AMD RS780 + SB700
RAM: DDR2 - 800: 3GB (2 Kingston slats)
Hard Drive: 320 GB
Video Card: ATI Mobility Radeon HD 4570
Weight: 2.39 kg
Dimensions: 34.5 x 24.1 x 3.45-3.78 mm
Audio: Built-in stereo speakers, SRS Premium Sound
Wireless interfaces: WiFi 802.11b / g, Bluetooth 2.1
Battery: 6 cells, 4400 mAh

Using


The laptop comes with DOS installed, but the disks come with all the drivers and additional software for Windows XP, Vista, 7.

The screen is good, supports a maximum resolution of 1366x768. The screen does not recline by 180 degrees.
On top is a 1.3 MPix webcam

Also, there is a built-in microphone, which is located on the left above the keyboard.

The keyboard is waterproof, the buttons are comfortable, the press is smooth and quiet.


The touchpad is made under aluminum, which at first is not very familiar for tactile sensations, but after an hour of work you get used to it. The buttons are made under chrome.


The indicators are located just to the left of the touchpad: Turn on, charge, WiFi (Bluetooth), access to the hard disk.
