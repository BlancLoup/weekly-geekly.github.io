How does our stress level change during the day? One day with Emvio on the wrist

Good day!



Our project (creating the Emvio emotional stress tracker ) is moving in a measured manner . The last couple of months we have devoted to the manufacture and debugging of prototypes. And one of them got to testing one very good person.

Peter took part at the start of the project, having completed a couple of tasks in the programming part (at that time Emvio was still at the layout stage). But after that I switched to another project and was not aware of the latest changes in ours. Therefore, the proposal to test the prototype for compliance with the testimony of the device internal sensations was for him a pleasant surprise. We got the opportunity to take a fresh look at our device. And Peter - see first-hand how his stress level changes during the day.

Conclusion


In general - quite interesting sensations. You can ask the question - why measure the level of stress, if your own emotional sensation is usually always understandable?

First, it is not always possible to feel that you are really under stress. Very often in some situations you catch yourself thinking that you are in a nervous, stressful state for quite a long time. If this happens with a certain periodicity, then sooner or later it will begin to cause severe discomfort and health problems. For example, most doctors agree that many gastrointestinal diseases, such as gastritis, are the result of constant excitement or stress. As a confirmation, I can give an example from my own life, since this is a story from the first person. Several years ago I was in the clinic with suspicions of gastritis. After all the examinations and the discovery of some problems, the head manager of the gastroenterology department advised only one thing at discharge - stop nervous and change lifestyle (as a result). And these tips helped. If the device will give signals immediately upon the occurrence of certain levels of stress, this can help control it, if it occurs in your life.

Secondly, even if you control yourself well, then with such a device you can take a more objective look at your own stress indicators, test yourself.

I would like to give an analogy with the calculation of own costs. We all consider our own money, expenses, incomes, we save somewhere, we plan somewhere, we build a budget, and so on. However, many put special software for the accounting of own money and house accounting and more carefully approach to the analysis of expenses. After all, when there is a complete picture of actions before your eyes, it is much easier to analyze something for yourself.
