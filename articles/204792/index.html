A1Agregator terminates agreements with medium-sized partners

A1Agregator stops working.
A few minutes ago, the payment aggregator A1 sent the following letter to its customers:
Dear partner!

Processing company A1 Aggregator from 06.12.2013 stops accepting payments by plastic cards, mobile commerce, electronic currencies, as well as sms-services. Payments for services rendered will be made in full.

Attached to this letter - notice of termination of the contract.

We kindly request that information on short numbers, prefixes, and payment forms be removed from their resources.

We apologize for any inconvenience. To speed up the processing of your questions, ask them in response to this email.

Such are the cases. However, this was not a surprise.

This was preceded by delays in payments, inexplicable technical failures, monthly silence of support and managers.
And if everything is clear with cards and electronic currencies, there are no problems, then with SMS payments there is a question: are there any worthy alternatives working with small partners (i-Free wrote yesterday that they work with turnover from 100,000 per month).

UPD: Not quite stop. On the official website there was news as follows:
From December 6, 2013 LLC company "A1 Agregator" introduces new rules for working with clients. The company will suspend work with legal entities whose turnover is less than 300 thousand rubles. per month under agency contracts and subscriber acquisition agreements (sms-services), with termination of contracts. Partners have been notified in advance by the relevant informational e-mail until 04.12.2013 inclusive.
