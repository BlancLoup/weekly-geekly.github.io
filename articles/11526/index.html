Russian sites are recognized as the best by FWA

The Red Keds Creative Agency once again proved that the Russian web-art can compete with the Western; two sites developed by the Agency won the title SOTD (website of the day) in the British project Favorite Website Awards, which presents the most spectacular and high-quality flash-sites from around the world. The favorite site becomes the first site of the day (SOTD); at the end of the calendar month, the site of the month (SOTM.) is selected from the sites of the day. 12 sites of the month at the end of each year participate in the People's Choice contest - People's Choice Award (PSA), and the winner becomes the site of the year (SOTY).

The awards were awarded to two sites developed by the Agency: Converse Russia ( www.converserussia.ru ) and Honda CR-V ( www.crvrussia.ru ).
