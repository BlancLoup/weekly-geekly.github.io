Homemade stabilizer for digital cameras

Engineers from Microsoft Research have developed an interesting nozzle , which is mounted on the case and connected to the sensor of a digital camera. During the exposure, the stabilizer accurately registers the movements of the camera, and then automatically corrects the sharpness of the image.



The construction of inexpensive gyroscopes and an accelerometer is assembled on the Arduino board and, according to the developers, works better than the built-in stabilizers in even the most expensive modern cameras.
