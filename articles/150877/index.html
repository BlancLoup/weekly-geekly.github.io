Philips THE SPECKED with a braided cable: Headphones that do not get confused?

Once I got tired of unraveling the puzzle that came out of my headphone pocket every time, and I was bothered to look for headphones that would be free from this shortcoming.
As the first test instance, the headphones from the company THE SPECKED series were selected:


Packaging:

Cute box, born obviously a man with a sense of humor:

Dense to open it must try.

Inside it turned out to be a scary look of a bundle of wires connected by flexible ties:

However, it is not as difficult to untangle it as opening a box. The convenience of quick use was sacrificed for the beauty of the front side.
Inside there were three sizes of cushions:

And the headphones themselves:


Headphone type - in-channel, half-open type. The only difference with a closed type is a tiny hole.

Capsule - dynamic, one on the earpiece. Neodymium magnet, Mylar (it is a lavsan, if someone does not know. Mylar sounds more impressive) dome, a coil of aluminum copper coated wire. Everything has been standard for about two years now, the solution is time tested.

The wire is the joy of an audiophile, oxygen-free copper (hfc, no one knows that ALL copper wires are now oxygen-free), the plug is gold-plated, the body of the plug is made of metal.

The sound is completely normal, no debris, no distortions. Although I am not picky, but in my opinion there is nothing to complain about.

The length of the wire is 1.2 meters, Y-shaped (the length of both ends is the same - the rays of hatred of the headsets, which spread over the neck), the place of separation is a metal cylinder:

At the ends goes silicone tie, for tightening the wires under the chin:

The rubber wire itself is fabric braided. Pleasant to the touch (yes, the braided wires are my fetish). The sheath made it possible to reduce protection at the outlet of the connector and the connector to a minimum, I hope this solution is reliable, and will not fail in a year. But in my experience - braided wires live the longest.

The headphone case is plastic, with a completely ingenious solution - there are bumps on the left earpiece, but not on the right one. All, the letters L and R can not be searched (they exist, but they are made very stylized).

Now about the main thing. Are you confused?
The miracle did not happen, they are still confused in their pockets. But one big plus - the braid nicely lowers the friction between the wires in the coil, which is why it is much nicer to unravel them - I shook them a couple of times, they unwound themselves. I hope the sheath will save them from the winter, which I have not experienced even a single headset.
