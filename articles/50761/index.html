Killing Forum

Inspired by the inhuman act that happened yesterday in the Russian WAP community ...

Probably all the old mobile phones were known for the WAP-resource of Siemens-Club, in particular its forum. At one time, in 2005-2006, he was on the first lines of the rankings by attendance, until the moment they stopped producing phones of this brand. Strangely enough, after this event, the attendance of the resource has dropped, but not very much. On the other hand, the forum has evolved from a purely technical to a freer one, where issues of already extensive subjects were discussed.
Of course, the backbone of the forum, "techies", remained, but talking already more on an equal footing with noobs (there was no one to pray) ... I referred myself to the oldies, because communicated there practically from the creation of this forum. But as they say, there was no sadness ...

It all started around the beginning of 2008, when, due to the perpetual employment of the administration, the forum was almost neglected. It is rather difficult to follow the whole case (average attendance is more than 200 people online).
