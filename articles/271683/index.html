Come November 26th to Visual Studio Workshop

November 26th we invite you to Visual Studio Workshop . During the event, you will be offered a number of deep sessions, immersing you in the methodologies and processes of developing and testing software based on Visual Studio, TFS, Git, Visual Studio Online products, ASP.NET and Azure platforms. Experts from the Microsoft team will share best business practices on monetization, project management and teamwork with participants, and during the roundtable you can get answers to your questions.



Key topics of the event:
  • Business development - project management, best practices and methodologies;
  • Effective management and control of the source code;
  • Continuous integration and deployment of projects locally and in the cloud;
  • Approaches and testing tools;
  • Practical experience in the use of ALM tools.

The event is focused on developers and system architects.

More information about the program of the event can be found on the event page .

Participation is free. Registration is required.
