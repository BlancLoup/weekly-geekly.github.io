Technical drawings HTC Dream with Android OS made public

The resource androidguys.com published technical drawings of the HTC Dream phone (aka G1). The main feature is that the lower part of the communicator is slightly protruding (like the RIZR Z8 in the unfolded state). The screen of the device moves to the right, exposing the QWERTY-keyboard.


The thickness of the device will be 16.35 mm (the notorious iPhone - 12.3 mm). On the phone will be the logos of T-Mobile, HTC and Google.

According to the source, the black version of the phone will look particularly well.
