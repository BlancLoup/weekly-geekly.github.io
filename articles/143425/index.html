Samsung competition

Samsung announced another application contest with big prizes. Last year there was a competition for smartphones on the Bada platform, this competition is designed for Android smartphones.



A total of 2 categories are proposed: for games and non-game applications. In each category, standard applications and programs using the S Pen will be separately assessed.

Applications published in Samsung Apps starting from today until September 30 are allowed to participate in the contest.

Competition website with a description of prizes and conditions - http://www.smartappchallenge.com/eng/challenge/awardsJudging.do .
