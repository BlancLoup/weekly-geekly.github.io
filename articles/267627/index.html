Dell Solutions Forum 2015: registration is open

Dear Colleagues!
We are pleased to inform you that in a month, October 21, the Dell Solutions Forum will be held in Moscow for the fourth time already! Our meeting place again will be Radisson Slavyanskaya, and we invite everyone to take part in it.




What is the Dell Solution Forum?
Dell Solutions Forum is a large-scale Dell event held in Russia as part of the global series of Dell Solutions Tour events. That's how the Forum went last year .

Dell 2015 Solutions Forum is held under the slogan "IT: a step into the future." Every day the needs of organizations are growing: in order to remain competitive and be ready for tomorrow, they need more efficient virtualization technologies, large storage capacities, more powerful analytics tools, secure access to data from anywhere. Dell will provide complete solutions, products and services that are characterized by maximum efficiency, ease of use and flexibility. Moreover, during the forum examples of successful implementation of technology projects for companies from various industries will be demonstrated. This year, thematic sessions will be organized so that you have the opportunity to study in detail each of the areas and discuss them in the framework of a dialogue between Dell experts and Forum participants.

Mobility. Employee mobility.
How to ensure the mobility of employees and not lose control over the infrastructure? Dell experts will talk about how to integrate corporate devices, applications, and their management services. Our solutions will help you get the most out of your personal mobile devices at work and reduce risks by reliably protecting corporate and personal devices in a physical, virtual and cloud environment.

Cloud Increase IT efficiency and flexibility.
The benefits of cloud technology for business are obvious. But an ill-conceived implementation strategy and the use of isolated solutions can lead to chaos and confusion in IT architecture. You can switch to cloudless infrastructure without losses using a universal approach that allows you to choose the platform and supplier that meets the needs of your business.

Security. Data protection: from user devices to the cloud.
Security technologies today must take into account the realities and established working methods of employees, take advantage of cloud technologies, the ability to work on personal devices and mobility - to ensure the productivity and efficiency of employees and support innovation. We will explain how Dell integrated solutions provide an exceptional level of security for data, personal information, networks and services across the organization.

Big Data. Effective use of analytics to convert data into knowledge.
How to convert structured and unstructured data into useful information for you faster, easier and more efficient? The Dell Big Data Analyzer solution combines natural language processing, self-learning and tonality analysis with easy-to-use search and visualization capabilities and allows you to gather information, identify relationships, and get the most out of big data. We will help you extract and analyze useful data to use this information to make more informed decisions.

How is the Forum going?
The Forum itself can be divided into two parts. Before lunch, you will be able to hear the keynote address of the company, presentations by our key partners, Intel and Microsoft, the IBS Platformix report, and also to witness a panel discussion on the trends in data storage technologies (software-defined storage (SDS), three-level flash memory (TLC) ) ). In the second part there will be presented thematic sessions and seminars of specialists of our company and partner companies, including Citrix, VMWare, NVidia and others.
In addition, you will be able to see for yourself all the key Dell products at the exhibition, as well as get acquainted with the ready-made solutions of our partners in the demonstration area.

Read more about the program of the Dell Solutions Forum 2015 here .

Who is this event for?
Our event will be interesting first of all to managers in the field of IT: directors, heads of departments, consultants and specialists.
As part of our Forum, you will have an excellent opportunity to talk with Dell experts, their colleagues and colleagues. This is a great opportunity to build partnerships, and often friendly relations with experts in all relevant fields.

Where, when and how to get?
What: Dell Solutions Forum 2015.
When: October 21, 2015, Wednesday, from 09:00 to 18:30.
Where: Moscow, Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel, Moscow, pl. Europe, d. 2 (m. "Kiev").
How much: for free.
How to: register here .
