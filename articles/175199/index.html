Panic. Now litecoin

Recently an article appeared that the price of bitcoin reached $ 100 . But this cryptocurrency is not calmed down.

This post is not a call for buying litecoin. It is purely narrative. All further occurs exclusively in the framework of a single exchange.

Just yesterday morning, the cost of one litecoin was 20-23 rubles. In the evening of this day they were already at 45-48. In the chat, everyone is shocked, they write off on April 1 and other absurdities. On the afternoon of April 2, they settled down for 60 rubles, but ...

The volume of litecoin exceeded $ 8.5 million. In less than a day, $ 5 million was reported. On April 1, there was only $ 2.1 million.
Why such a jump? My opinion is speculation and the desire of people to cash in on litecoin, since they missed the growth of bitcoin. Began to spread the rumor that once bitcoin has grown so much, then all other cryptocurrencies should also creep up sharply. On this occasion, the number of users has increased dramatically, which resulted in a huge influx of money and an even greater demand for litecion.

The horror itself was going on from about 19:30 to 22:00. The number of guests exceeded 10,000. The number of registered online users reached 5,000.
