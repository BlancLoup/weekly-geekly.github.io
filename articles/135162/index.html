Work with two SIM cards. Software and stuffing.



The device is based on Android 2.3.4 Gingerbread, with some additions to the part of the work with two SIM cards. Physically, the slots are located as in 1310 - one above the other. The main sim card is lower, secondary - upper. Why do I call them that way? Everything is simple, in order to save energy, the second SIM card is limited only by the GSM band, although in the settings you can assign your role to each SIM card - voice or data. In any case, even when selecting data for the second SIM, it will function only in GSM.
It can be seen that the software has undergone some processing compared to the previous model, the role of SIM cards is defined in a separate item of the main settings menu, and more detailed settings for each SIM are located in the menu of wireless modules.
