Microsoft Quality Assurance Day

Hello, the community!

February promises to be rich and interesting: in addition to the already announced HTML5 Camp (February 17), MSQA Day will be held at the company's office on February 22 - an event for those who care about the quality of the software.

The conference is devoted to Software Quality Assurance as a whole, that is, how to develop high-quality software, what processes, methods and tools are needed to create high-quality, and therefore more reliable, convenient and functional software.

At the conference, a significant part of the reports will be focused not only on testers and QA-managers, but also on programmers and project managers and teams, because software quality is the result of teamwork. Brian Keller (Brian Keller), a leading technical evangelist of Microsoft, who is a world expert in application lifecycle management and Visual Studio, will tell about this in his report.

I draw your attention that participation in the conference is paid. Under the cut there is information about how you can get an invitation for free (as well as an MSDN subscription). You also have the opportunity to join the online broadcast on the site .
