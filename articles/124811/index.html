Google is going to open an office in Indonesia



Google is going to open an office in Indonesia, and this year. In this country, the number of Internet users is constantly increasing, and the company decided to open its own office in Indonesia. The news about the new office was reported by the vice president of Google in Indonesia Boediono.

The decision was made after Eerik Schmidt and Boediono held talks. After opening a representative office in Indonesia, Google is going to invest in various Indonesian projects. Unfortunately, the exact dates of opening a company office in this country are not known.

Currently, the population of Indonesia is about 230 million people, of which about 40 million are connected to the Network. Indonesia now represents a very attractive market for various Internet companies, and Google is determined to start working in this country.

Interestingly, Indonesia is the third country in the world in the number of users registered on Facebook (20 million people). In the first place in this indicator - the United States, in the second - England.

Among other things, in Indonesia now there is an active growth of the economy, which further attracts investors to the country, including companies operating in the IT sector.
