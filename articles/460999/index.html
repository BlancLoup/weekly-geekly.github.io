Let's play a little game with Lamoda

Hello! My name is Pavel Savelyev, I head the department of automation of business processes in Lamoda. I want to invite you to play PHP Quest. To participate in the quest, you must fill out the form . After filling out the questionnaire, we will send you a puzzle in the mail. Until August 12, August 5, players send their decisions to quest@lamoda.ru. Spoiler: the puzzle has many solutions, and we are ready to consider any.

UPD: we make decisions by August 5 in connection with a large number of applications, we apologize.



We will invite everyone who successfully completed the puzzle to the finals, which will take place on August 16 at our office in Moscow. Players from other cities will be paid for tickets and accommodation.

On August 16, you will find the final competition, games and a party in the best office according to the Best Office Awards 2019 . Winners will receive a Job Offer from Lamoda on the same day.

As it will be?


  • 13:00 - 13:30 Registration of players and draw
  • 13:30 - 14:30 Welcome speech (Pavel Saveliev, Head of Business process automation; Alexander Afenov, Team Lead; Alexey Felde, Tech Lead)
  • 14:30 - 16:30 PHP match
  • 16:30 - 17:30 Lunch of heroes
  • 17:30 - 18:30 Sergey Zaika, team leader of the call-center team will tell you how Lamoda worked with kafka and give a prize for the most tricky question
  • 18:30 - 19:00 Awarding and presentation of JOB Offers
  • 19:00 - 22:00 Party

Key internal automation systems are written in PHP in Lamoda: delivery service, photo studio, payment management, order management systems, as well as B2B-direction. Our technology stack is PHP (we are writing on the latest versions), Docker, Symfony 3.4 / 4 in conjunction with Doctrine 2, PostgreSQL, Kubernetes. The level of development can be estimated by our github.com/lamoda and many speeches at conferences and meetings. Read more about performances and technologies at tech.lamoda.ru .

For any questions, write to me at quest@lamoda.ru.
It's time to play the game.
