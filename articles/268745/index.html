Moscow Atlassian Meetup October 20



On October 20, members of the Moscow Atlassian community will gather in the office of the Mail.Ru Group for the second time. Like-minded people will share practical experience and discuss the subtleties of using Atlassian products. In the program, as usual, several reports and guests talk over a cup of coffee.

Nikita Teplov , Mail.Ru Group, "Seven deadly sins or how not to break JIRA"

A few tips on how to avoid serious errors when administering JIRA. How to prevent a decrease in performance with the growth of the system and prevent long downtime due to their own laziness. Based on real events.

Georgy belgeor Belousov , CJSC PETER-SERVICE, "Migration of data to JIRA through DB"

We in the company are actively moving from three different tracking systems to JIRA, or rather, we are already at the final stage. All entities for transfer - about 500 000. In the report, I will tell you how it was. At first, the approach to migration was through the API, then we were faced with the need to transfer history, dates, attachments, etc., as well as a too long migration time. The approach has changed, and in the end, through trial, error and experience with the structure of the database has been completely redesigned in the direction of direct migration to the database. The total time has decreased significantly, the speed has become more than good, the purity of the data has increased.
