Sony Xperia 1 in Russia - price and bonuses for pre-order

June 14, 2019 Sony Mobile announced the launch of the long-awaited flagship smartphone Xperia in Russia 1. In Sony Xperia 1, everything revolves around outstanding opportunities for creating and consuming content, and the most notable innovation is the use of a new 6.5-inch 4K HDR display with a cinematic aspect ratio of 21: 9, manufactured using OLED technology to ensure perfect color reproduction, superior contrast and flawless black colors even in complete darkness. To be limited to only one picture would be strange. Is it possible to get an impressive feeling from watching movies without really sound quality? For those who pre-order before June 23, Sony Mobile offers the WH-1000XM3 premium wireless headphones with advanced noise cancellation technology. However, it is not only the screen and sound that attracts the attention of Sony Xperia 1. The smartphone is equipped with a triple camera with a resolution of 12 megapixels, a large pixel size in the matrix and 16 mm aperture optics for shooting landscapes and beautiful panoramas, a universal 26 mm lens, and millimeter telephoto for portraits.
