Android 2.x is currently used on 70% of all active Android devices.



According to the latest statistics, the share of Android 2.2 Froyo on Android devices (of those that visited the Android Market for two weeks before September 1) is already 28.7%, gradually catching up with Android 2.1 Eclair and almost equal to the total number of Android 1.5 Cupcake with Android 1.6 Donut.

Considering that in May Cupcake and Donut together occupied 62%, the statistics look impressive.
