Oculus Rift opened pre-orders of VR helmets

Hello everyone, we decided to open our corporate blog VRDevice with great news:
The company Oculus Rift, which is developing a virtual reality helmet has finally opened up the possibility of pre-ordering the first consumer versions of their devices.





Brief history of Oculus Rift


Oculus Rift are not pioneers in the field of VR. In the 90s, the first attempts of other companies to create such devices failed with commercial success due to the lack of the level of development of computer and mobile technologies necessary at that time. Oculus presented their first version for Oculus Dev Kit 1 developers in 2012 on kickstarter, successfully collecting the required amount. The first version had 3 degrees of freedom, the screen resolution was 1280 x 800 and the viewing angle was 110 degrees.
In the spring of 2014, Oculus was purchased by Facebook for 2 MHR. $. In the summer of 2014, the second version of the Oculus Dev Kit 2 was released, which already had a 1920x1080 screen, 6 degrees of freedom and a viewing angle of 100 degrees:



Oculus Rift Consumer Version 1


The first consumer version of the device has a surround sound system in the form of built-in headphones, Positional Tracking and (according to not yet fully confirmed sources) 2 screens with a resolution of 1200x1080.



You can check the readiness of your PC for Oculus virtual reality with the help of the utility .
Included with each helmet will be supplied Xbox One controller, Oculus Remote, wires and 2 free games: Lucky's Tale arcade and EVE space battles simulator: Valkyrie



The kit does not include wireless controllers Oculus Touch, the release of which they decided to postpone in the fall of 2016.



So far, pre-orders are available only for 20 countries, which, unfortunately, did not include Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, etc.
The cost of pre-order is $ 599 and 699 euros for the European part. Delivery times vary from March 2016.

If you have the ability to deliver to the address in the number of allowed countries, then pre-order can be done on the official Oculus website .
For those who supported Oculus in the first campaign on kickstarter devices will be sent free of charge!

Trend of Virtual Reality


2016 is already marked as the year of virtual reality, which can undoubtedly be considered as the starting point for the popularization and growth of virtual reality technology.
In addition to the Oculus Rift this year, other major players, such as the HTC Vive and Sony with their helmet for the Play Station 4, should also enter this new and promising market.
In Russia, there are also projects of devices and technologies of virtual / augmented reality. Representatives of some of them are currently at the CES 2016 annual consumer electronics show in Las Vegas, which runs from January 6 to 9.
We wish them all good luck!
