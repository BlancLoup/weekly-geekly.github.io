Designer Lego.cuusoo "Back to the Future" on sale from August 1



All geeks favorite company Lego officially announced that from August 1, the stores come in a new set of "Back to the Future."

The set was proposed on the project ideas.lego.com , which I wrote about last October. This is a crowdfunding service. Anyone can offer their project, and if he gets more than 10,000 votes, Lego begins to consider him as a candidate for production.

Specifically, this project was created two years ago on August 1, 2011. 5000 supporters scored in March 2012, 10,000 votes were collected already in April. And in December, Lego announced that it would release a designer.

August 1, the designer appeared on the official websites of Lego in America and Europe. In Russia there is no.

Project creators


The creator of the project Masashi Togami is entitled to 1% of sales. But he has already stated that he transferred this money to the foundation of Michael Fox , the same actor who played Marty McFly in Back to the Future. The fund is funding drug development in the fight against Parkinson's disease.

True, there is one thing: Masashi Togami works at lego.cuusoo. So it may be that giving up royalties is an elegant way to get away from arguing about whether lego.cuusoo employees can participate in the development on an equal footing with ordinary fans.
