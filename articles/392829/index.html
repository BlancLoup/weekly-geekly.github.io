PO2RT: coworking on top



The first stage of MEGA Accelerator - the acceptance of applications and the selection of winning teams is over, the guys have three months of training in co-working PO2PT, which was chosen as a place to accelerate projects. Recall: MEGA Accelerator is a project of IKEA Centers Russia, managing MEGA shopping centers throughout Russia, to search for innovative, unusual and cool ideas to improve consumer experience in the MEGA shopping center and beyond.

March 11 was the opening of coworking PO2PT. Our correspondent went to the event to see everything with his own eyes, to get acquainted with the teams that passed the selection and tell Geektimes readers about everything. Photos of coworking and not only under the cut.

Facts and figures


As planned by the organizer, a special meaning is put into the abbreviation PO2PT:

  • Port - start, the starting point for the departure of a large navigation.
  • The port is a transport hub that connects cities, different people, different spheres of life.
  • Port - the way / place to transfer information, experience.
  • O2 - the formula of the oxygen molecule.

We would, perhaps, add another hidden meaning for the Russian-language outline - software (software) 2 (to, for) ReTail - PO2PT. After all, it is in this coworking that teams that doom retail and shopping malls for a technological future work and develop.

The business center in which the co-working is located was built in 2008 - by the standards of the builders the building is quite young, and by the standards of the tenants it is already inhabited and endured all the childhood diseases of the new buildings. What you need!

Despite the fact that the business center is located 3 km outside the Moscow Ring Road, its location is extremely successful. It is very easy to get here: in the morning everyone goes to Moscow, so the lane to the region is free, in the evening - on the contrary. This is convenient for everyone, but, above all, for residents of the North-West of Moscow and the Moscow region. Now, freelancers, startups, young businessmen can not waste time trying to get to co-working or a tiny office in the city center. Rent a place in coworking can any individual or company, prices do not bite:

For comparison, the price of a month of renting co-working in major cities of Russia (except Moscow and St. Petersburg) ranges from 5,000 to 12,000 rubles. At the same time, in most cases coworkings are located not in large business centers, but in buildings with a non-cost of buying and renting housing.

PO2PT occupies the last, 15th, floor of the glass high-rise of Khimki Business Park. The co-working area is 700 square meters. In addition to the huge workspace there is a parking for cars and an open roof terrace. Can you imagine summer sunsets and views from there?



View of the parking lot from the 15th floor

The building itself is a triumph of glass and concrete, but not to which residents of Russian cities are accustomed. Light, seemingly almost weightless design resembles a mosaic of bright clean windows: the architecture of the building is based on panoramic windows. In our northern latitudes, this solution allows us to receive our portion of daylight even in the most severe, inclement weather. And it always has a positive effect on health and mood.

Scientists from the University of Exeter conducted an experiment and found that if you decorate the interior of the office with plants, paintings, photographs, the staff will work on average 32% more productive.
