Despite the decline in quarterly revenue of Megafon, analysts positively evaluated its results.

Megafon has published financial results for the second quarter. OIBDA margin increased by 0.9 pp to 45.6%. Free cash flow increased by 35% - to 19.3 billion. Total operator revenue fell year on year by 0.7%. GDR "Megaphone" on the London Stock Exchange on July 30 rose by 3.67% to 12.7%. The company's mobile revenue on the Russian market grew by 0.6% compared with last year and reached 65.8 billion. Analysts praised the results of the company.

Two quarters to a row, MegaFon's mobile revenue in Russia decreased by 0.4% and 1.7%, respectively. Now the company managed to reverse this trend. According to analyst Raiffeisenbank Sergey Libin, mobile revenue increased due to the acceleration of the company's revenue growth from data transmission. This quarter, they grew by 20.8%. While in the previous two quarters, their growth was 15.4% and 10.1%, respectively.

For three months, the number of subscribers of data transmission services has increased by more than a million, and the entire subscriber base - by 2 million SIM cards. This is due to the start of sales of MegaFon contracts in Svyaznoy stores.
