PROLiNK Glee TA-009 - netbook from Singapore



Singapore company PROLiNK could not resist, succumbed to the general trend and also released a netbook.

By and large, the machine is only interesting because it comes preinstalled with Windows 7 Ultimate 32-bit RC1 (Build 7100). All other characteristics fully correspond to the netbook menstrim. Here you have the Intel Atom N270 processor, and gigabytes of RAM, and 10.1-inch screen (1024x600 pixels), and 160 GB hard drive, and ... and so on.

Dimensions of the device - 264x180x300 mm, weight - 1110 grams (with a 3-cell battery with a capacity of 2200 mAh). Details can be found in the vr-zone.com review.

The cost is 485 US dollars with a battery for 3 cells and 555 dollars with a battery for 6 cells (2400 mAh).
