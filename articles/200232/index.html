COLT 1.2.4 released with LESS / SASS support, CoffeeScript

A new version of COLT was released, in which we fixed a large number of errors (thanks to bug reporters!) And added support for LESS / SASS , as well as CoffeeScript . Now, if you change the styles to LESS / SASS, as well as the CoffeeScript code, the changes will be delivered to the application without overloading and losing state.

A big request to the community is to test livecoding for CoffeeScript and go through your cases. Ideas and bug reports please send to our tracker .

The other day we will add support for TypeScript and improve support for CoffeeScript - pre-compile scripts will be supported in the Live phase.

In our immediate plans ( thanks to this survey ) add COLT / JS support for IDEA / Webstorm and SublimeText .

And in the ActionScript version we are working on support for MXML and FB plugin. (We released the latest AS for IDEA and FlashDevelop last week.)

Send bug reports to our tracker .

Download the new version on our website codeorchestra.com
