One look ahead

I love futuristic videos. Even if it is related to the vision of the future of one individual company. They have something so ... fascinating.

This week turned out to be quite fruitful for Microsoft's clips: first came Productivity Future Vision 2011, where Redmond is trying to extrapolate today's developments five years ahead. Then a video about the cloudless future of Kinekt, and after - a wonderful video about the same Kinect, but linked to PowerPoint. Interested please under the cat.

So, the first video. Productivity Future Vision 2011. Hints on WindowsPhone stick out of all slots, but it turned out beautifully. In addition, they showed almost Babylonian fish.



Video second on Kinekt. It seems that Microsoft is well aware that they invented something more than a game controller.



Video three, again about Kinekt. Here is a specific implementation of binding to Excel (yes, I did not make a reservation, the coordinates of the objects on the screen were written in Excel, although the effects look like typical PowerPoint). Particularly pleased with the change in the layout of the text depending on the location of the speaker
