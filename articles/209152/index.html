On January 15, the registration of the participants of the annual contest of developers - ‚ÄúNative Speech-2014‚Äù opened. The winner will receive 120,000 rubles, the silver winner - the iPhone 5, and the finalist who won the third place - the iPad 4.

Participants will need to create a workable algorithm for converting the recognized sequence of phonemes into text that meets the norms of the Russian language. When creating the algorithm, it will be possible to use in-house developments and open software packages intended for research, such as: HTK, Kaldi, SRILM, mitLM, OpenFST. The use of commercial products and online services is not permitted.

Already on the MDG website, you can familiarize yourself with an example of tender data and ask questions to the organizers of the forum .

The opening of access to the current competitive database and, accordingly, the start of work of the teams is scheduled for February 15.
