Do you remember myspace? Do you use MySpace? On the one hand, the questions are stupid, because The audiotria of the network is quite extensive, on the other - MySpace is no longer that :) But the struggle between Facebook, which is actively winning a new audience, and MySpace continues.
Anyway, you probably heard that Facebook recently introduced the ability to update its status on Twitter directly from its pages (but not for ordinary users, but for administrators of special pages of any products or celebrities); However, MySpace has stepped further and introduced the mutual synchronization of MySpace Twitter statuses, which can not but affect the growing popularity of Twitter among young people who communicate in huge amounts on MySpace, as well as vice versa, will allow those who have long left the network to revive their profile, broadcasting there their messages from Twitter and thus will give an opportunity to their friends, who have not left anywhere, to be aware of all events again.

Enable and configure this option in your profile . Keep in mind that it is still in beta and is only available if you use the American site MySpace.com (this option is not available on the Russian site).
Moreover, I have set myself an American site, but still updates are only from Twitter; MySpace statuses don't appear on Twitter. (I suspect that this is somehow connected in time zones, as the widget indicates the time 7h ago)
