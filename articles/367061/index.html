First, let's talk a little about one extremely popular technology that sets the weather today in the virtual market for legal video production. It's about smart TV.

Smart tv


What is Smart TV? In short, this is a software platform that allows the TV (or receiver) to directly access online interactive resources. If at all in a nutshell - this is a software for connecting the TV to the Internet. The technology supporting this technology makes it possible to painlessly abandon regular television broadcasting - the user can choose what to watch and when. In addition, Smart TV allows you to use social networks via TV, access news resources and, for example, store photo collections. In short - TV with Smart TV is actually a powerful multimedia center, almost comparable to a working computer or laptop. Now more than 90% of modern TVs are equipped with this platform.

The advent of interactive television has strongly spurred the development of sites and applications for access to legal video content, whether it be movies, TV shows or broadcasts.
