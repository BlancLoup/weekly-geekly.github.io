What prompted me to research?
It all started with the fact that at the end of August all developers on Admob had a die at the top that appeared Admob 2.0 . Fashionable, modern, innovative. Fortunately, in Russia at that time there was no functional opportunity to switch to it. In Ukraine, the update was available, because I learned a lot of interesting things from the Internet.
Firstly, Admob in Russia and Ukraine no longer supports transfers to a bank account.
Secondly, checks will be transported by a tortoise slave on galleys Russian Post.
Thirdly, there is some kind of Rapida ( link ), which I have not used in my life. Courier delivery, etc., but still, there $ 15 will have to spend. Correct if not so. Still did not understand in detail.
