Available to order kit to build your own tablet with a touchscreen OLED display

The popularity of Internet tablets could not fail to open new niches in the emerging market.
So, after the products from well-known brands, Liquidware presented its touchscreen tablet, which can be assembled by the buyer.


Liquidware offers to purchase a set of components, which includes:
  • Motherboard BeagleBoard Rev C4 with integrated OMAP3530 720 MHz processor, which also has RAM on its chip
  • Beagle Touch Touchscreen OLED screen with a resolution of 480 * 272 pixels, capable of displaying 262 thousand colors (18-bit depth?)
  • 2600 mAh BeagleJuice battery, which is connected to the device using a two-pin connector
  • As well as a 4 GB SD card with Angstrom Linux pre-installed OS
Developers also prudently placed a 20-millimeter speaker into the module with a touch screen.
