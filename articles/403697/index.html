Russian monitoring system RedPine - from the problem to the birth of a new product

10 years ago, the company Hited, which, among other things, offers diesel generators for rent, took up the efficiency of its rental fleet, which is more than 250 diesel generator sets operating in different parts of the country. For the effective operation of such a fleet, a monitoring, accounting and dispatching system is needed, otherwise everything can get out of control. We must see the location, statistics and mode of the equipment, remotely turn it on and off. And at first it seemed that it was not very difficult.



But in practice it turned out that the solutions available on the market cannot provide all the necessary functions in real conditions. Therefore, it was decided to create our own monitoring system that meets all our requirements, and the result was a product that can be refined and offered to other companies to solve various problems.

We called it Red Pine , and now it is a family of products, covering not only power supply, but also other engineering systems.

It all started with a task, continued with problems, and ended with the creation of a product


So, at first there was a task that consisted of a number of requirements. And we quickly realized that it would hardly be possible to solve it with the help of third-party products, because these third-party solutions often presented their counterclaims to us, and as a result they could offer us only a certain compromise between our desires and their limitations. This did not suit us at all, since these restrictions became real problems.
