Total


What should the perfect personal information manager look like?

  • It should contain a slice only for you personally useful (or useful in the future) information, and it should be possible to simply filter by context (Mathematical analysis -> Integration by parts -> formula)
  • All information should be stored in one place.
  • All information should be structured for the fastest and most efficient search (full-text search, categories and tags, hyperlinks), and a minimum amount of effort should be made to structure it.
  • Information should be available always, everywhere and from everywhere, with instant speed.
  • Information must be securely stored, we do not want to lose all the developments of life due to an oversight or accident.
  • Change history should also be kept.
  • It should be possible to present information in any convenient form, be it a text, a mathematical formula, a picture, a graph or a graph.
  • Recording information should be as simple, quick and accessible as possible; a mathematical formula can be drawn by hand, you can quickly drive in text with auto completion, generate a graph or diagram with a couple of simple actions. And it's better not to type and write anything at all, but just think about it.


I know only one tool, as close as possible to these criteria - the human brain. And his main problem, which does not allow him to be considered the Ideal Personal Information Manager, is his limited resources.
