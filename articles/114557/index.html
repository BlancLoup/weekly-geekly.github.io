The second conference. NET developers in Chelyabinsk



We invite you to the second meeting of .NET developers, which will be held March 25 in Chelyabinsk. The official address on the network http://www.dotnetconf.ru , meeting VKontakte and hash tag on Twitter #dotnetconf



What for?



We want to be strong developers and improve engineering approaches to software development. Principles and approaches to application development are important for us. We believe that the most valuable and interesting for developers is the use of best practices such as Agile, TDD, BDD, CI, Refactoring, DDD and many others - everything that makes developers more productive and eliminates routine and boring work.

The format of our conference is different from the Platform or similar meetings . It is more like the ALT.NET community . To convey the essence, an excerpt from the article ‚ÄúThe Microsoft culture relating to the toolkit, tutorials, and the .NET environment, in general, does not reflect or support an important set of core values ‚Äã‚Äã[of software development].‚Äù

We are interested in practical experience . You can not leave with a report about the fact that a new version of ASP.NET MVC or SharePoint has been released. You can read about it on the Internet faster than the speaker will tell about it. The experience of our speakers should be related to the use of a library or development environment in 1-2 commercial projects so that you can go out and say ‚Äúthis is really useful, but you should take a closer look at this part, I got these problems with it, I suggest solving them like this".

In addition, at our conference you can present your OpenSource library on .NET. At this conference there will be an announcement of the library for working with OpenXml via the Fluent interface .

Who needs it?



We are pleased that this is the second meeting. About 50 developers from various organizations came to the first. We received very positive feedback, so we decided to go back. Thank you all for the criticism, we took it into account at the second meeting of .NET developers.

When and where?



The conference will be held March 25 in Chelyabinsk. The exact location will be known closer to March 10th. This will be either one of the conference rooms in Chelyabinsk, or the audience of one of the universities of Chelyabinsk. Notifications will be sent to all registered people about the exact venue of the conference.

How to get there?



Admission is free after registration . The number of places is limited, less than half is already left. Registration is obligatory, because we need to know when to close the registration, so as not to encourage those who do not have time.

Those who come from other cities, we will sign travel.

What will happen?



The first reports are already known:

  • Cucumber acceptance tests
  • Pragmatic approach to the development of flexible software systems
  • ASP.NET MVC - how to build a truly flexible web application
  • TDD or how not to shoot yourself in the foot
  • Announcement of the OpenSource library for creating and modifying documents in OpenXml format via Fluent interface
  • Programmer's Kata
  • Build the testing process
  • Career in IT
  • Metaprogramming Interfaces in ASP.NET MVC


The program is still under construction. Contact us if you want to offer your report to our conference.

Training and discount



For the first time in Chelyabinsk, our training partner company ScrumTrek will conduct Agile Development with Scrum training. Training for those who plan to implement Scrum in their project or organization, or for those who want to compare their ways of working with the best industry practices.
