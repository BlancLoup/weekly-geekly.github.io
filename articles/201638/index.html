The transition to Windows Azure will speed up the deployment time of the 1C solution in enterprises, taking into account the implementation of industrial needs and individual customer specifics. Partners of the company will be able to improve the quality of service, for example, to improve technological support: since most operations can be carried out remotely, they will be able to serve more clients per unit of time and also concentrate on solving specific tasks.
