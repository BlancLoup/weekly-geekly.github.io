But to whom is the very first Macintosh Plus released? Fly!

The Profiles in History website put up for sale the very first Macintosh Plus computer that was donated to Star Trek screenwriter and producer Gene Roddenberry.

A great gift for real fans of Apple and Star Trek. Moreover, the computer has a serial number F4200NUM 0001

The device has 1 Mb of RAM (expandable up to 4), a 3.5-way floppy disk drive, a SCSI interface for hard drives and peripherals, a 9-inch monochrome screen with a resolution of 512 by 342 pixels.
The buyer will also be given a letter confirming the authenticity of the computer, signed by Rod, the son of Gene Roddenberry.

If you hurry, you can have time to become the owner of a historical rarity. At the moment, the price has reached 1600 USD. In order to cover the last bid, you need to declare a price of 1700 USD
