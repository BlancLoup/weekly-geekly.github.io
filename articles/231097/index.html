Boxed CMS for an online store: a review of popular engines

From the very beginning of the review, the task was defined - from among those presented on Runet today to consider the most popular and progressive CMS, focused on the creation of online stores. However, for a number of my own considerations, I had a desire to work with the ‚Äúdomestic‚Äù product as it were. Accordingly, each of the engines considered, which took part in this review, had to meet a number of criteria. The criteria themselves were selected on the basis of trends in technological development, on the one hand, and from the specifics of the work of such resources, on the other.
