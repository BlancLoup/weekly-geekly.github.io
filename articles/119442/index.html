5) probkiizokna.ru



"Traffic jams from the window" develop a fixed network. Any owner of a computer connected to the Internet, whose windows face the road, can download a special program from the site, install a webcam on the windowsill and take part in the project. This program by video image of a webcam determines the road congestion in the surveillance zone and transmits this data along with the individual frames to the server.



1) About the service
The project was launched in September 2010. The own funds of the founders were used as sowing investments, and also a grant was received from the Foundation for Assistance to the Development of Small Enterprises in the Scientific and Technical Sphere (www.fasie.ru). Within 3 months after launch, the threshold of 80 cameras was crossed online. After the first 3 months of operation, the project got into the investment pit, when the founders' funds ran out, and the necessary camera base - at least more than the market leader - was not recruited. It took several months to find an investor. For this period, the number of cameras online has decreased and stabilized at the level of 50-60. Finally, in late April, an investor was found who provided funds for development.
