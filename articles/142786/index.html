PhoneSoap kills microorganisms on the surface of the smartphone



Over the past few years, many experts conducted research, the purpose of which was to study the cleanliness of the surface of smartphones and mobile phones. The results of these studies were unambiguous - even in a public toilet there are fewer harmful microorganisms than on the surface of a smartphone. In general, the results of some studies can be trusted, and many smartphone owners are looking for a way to clean their devices from harmful bacteria.

It is clear that after such intimidation by medical professionals, many owners of mobile devices are looking for a way to clean their device. Well, since there is a demand, it means there are offers. Some of them are reliable, others are not. The other day, another interesting solution appeared, this device, called PhoneSoap.

The device is an aluminum case, inside which are emitters of ultraviolet radiation, deadly for 99.9% of microorganisms. Here are samples of microorganisms from the surface of the smartphone before processing and after (a sample is taken, placed in a nutrient medium, then experts analyze the species composition of microorganisms in the cup).



The case does not interfere with connecting the device to charging, so you can clean it simultaneously with charging, for example, at night. True, there are some problems - for example, how quickly can you reach the phone that is in the case at this time?

The developers claim that their device can not prevent the owner to hear the alarm. PhoneSoap is not yet available for purchase, it is exposed by developers on the Kickstarter service. Developers need about 18 thousand dollars, and the ‚Äúsoap for the phone‚Äù can be launched into production.
