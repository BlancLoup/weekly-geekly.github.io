Google is preparing to acquire the company Global IP Solutions

It became known that Google wants to buy a company Global IP Solutions.

GIPS specializes in VoIP and video conferencing services. The company is known for creating the narrowband ILBC codec and wideband iSAC. Her software is installed in more than 800 million points. GIPS helps companies reduce the load on the network in the transfer of audio and video in real time. Among the clients of Global IP Solutions there are such companies as Nortel, Oracle, Samsung, WebEx, Yahoo !, AOL and a number of others.

Google offered to pay $ 68.2 million, or $ 2.12 per share, which is 27.5% more than the current market price.
