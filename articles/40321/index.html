TechDays.ru - over 120 webcasts for developers and IT professionals

So, the TechDays.ru project officially started .
TechDays is the conclusion of traditional seminars from Microsoft for technical specialists - Developer Days (DevDays) and TechNet Seminars to a new level:
  • First, it is a single resource where you can get information about upcoming technical events for both developers and IT professionals. Now all the information about them will be in one source - subscribe to RSS .
  • Secondly, it is not only materials and recordings of speeches, but also a very large number of additional technical webcasts ( over 120! ) In Russian. It is important to note that some of the broadcasts have a basic level of 100 and allow you to get acquainted with the technology in 15 minutes ! Moreover, new webcasts will appear there constantly, and not only in the spring and autumn seminars - subscribe to RSS . These webcasts are created by both Microsoft employees and independent experts, which makes the content even more diversified and interesting.
  • Thirdly, it is an opportunity to prepare for listening to the reports of the seminars through webcast broadcasts.
  • Fourthly - listening to the reports, you can answer questions on them, win prizes ( ITQuiz project) and get the right to register for a seminar in your city.

Welcome to TechDays.ru !
