Gambling Offline

More recently, ABBYY moved to a new office. It was certainly the biggest move in the entire history of the company, because the number of employees is growing every day. The process went very smoothly: on day X, all things were neatly packed in special boxes, which some ABBYY-people recruited up to 5 pieces. But at one of the jobs of the movers waiting for a surprise in the form of 30 such boxes.
They were carefully packed collection owned by my colleagues. And not all, but only a part, because the owners transported the most valuable copies themselves.


Meet Maxim Kalenkov ( mkal ), a programmer and co-owner of a collection of more than 300 board games. He will tell you what games are popular with our employees, when they have time to play them, and how this passion began.



- How long have you been collecting all this? Do you remember how it all began? What game was bought first?

From 1.5 to 2.5 years. In my opinion, the initiators were our employees from among programmers Timur and Dima. Timur brought the "Colonizers", Dima - "Civilization", and then it went on ... At some point, they ordered the huge game "Twilight of the Empire", then I joined the players.

- Tell me where and how often do you play?

We meet every Saturday except holidays, because many leave. Usually we play in the dining room, it happens that in the negotiation room. Sit for 6-9 hours. Earlier, when they were going to be irregular, they did a newsletter, but now we write only if the meeting is canceled. Sometimes even over tea we have time to play some quick game.

- How long does each game take?

Well, the games are different. Now there is a tendency to reduce the time of the party. For example, Twilight Empire takes from 8 to 16 hours, and those games that are released recently have a format from 45 minutes to 1.5 hours per game.



- Mainly on the shelves of the game of foreign manufacturers. Why is this?

Several times I bought the games that we released under license, and always had some problems either with the bundle or with the quality of the translation. In addition, we publish very few games. - By what criterion you choose the game?

We prefer strategies, where the game is based on competition between players and you need to constantly make difficult decisions, calculate situations, make choices, sometimes bluff. - Do you play corporate parties?

Here at the last celebration of March 8, we were asked to organize a small game library. Although usually do not play. A bit wrong format of the event. We prefer to play thoughtfully, in a relaxed atmosphere.

It is quite difficult. Tastes change: every month or two there is a new game that becomes favorite for a while.
