I smoke a month from USB: normal flight

In the fourth year of smoking, I was thoroughly sick with shortness of breath, difficulty breathing in the process of falling asleep and a stable fee of 37 rubles for a comfortable stay of a new day. I did not want to quit smoking, so I decided to move to another level of development of this bad habit. Disclaimer: I do not call for the use of nicotine in any form: through smoking or chewing tobacco, using nicotine patches, candy or chewing gum, as well as using the ‚Äúelectronic cigarette‚Äù described in the topic. Also, an electronic cigarette is not a wonderful cure for nicotine addiction. The post is written in order to increase the amount of useful information about the gadget, about which the majority has a very vague idea, based on rumors and prejudices.

I decided to switch to smoking electronic cigarettes. Another couple of friends told me that "they will not help" (although, I remind you, quitting smoking was not in my plans). I confirm: electronic cigarettes are a purely individual substitute for regular cigarettes. These are completely different things, with different taste and smell; with a regular cigarette, an electronic cigarette is related only to the similarity of a substance inhaled by a smoker with tobacco smoke, so that -

Theory and blah blah


A nicotine inhaler was invented in 2003 by the Chinese scientist Hon Lik - the inventor smoked two packs of cigarettes a day until his father (also a smoker) died of lung cancer. The device was called the "electronic cigarette", and in 2005 the company owning the brand Golden Dragon Group Ltd began exporting from China.
In fact, there was nothing special to invent. A standard electronic cigarette consists of three parts:
  • battery - with a button or with built-in pressure sensor that responds to tightness. Charging, most often, from USB;
  • atomizer - heating nichrome spiral + metal foam;
  • cartridge - a container with a special liquid.



When a smoker presses a button (in the case of a button battery) and pulls on an electronic cigarette, liquid enters the atomizer from the cartridge, the nichrome filament starts to heat up, and the incoming liquid evaporates. That is, the smoke does not enter the lungs of the smoker ‚Äî an aerosol with a solid dispersed phase, but a vapor. There are quite a few types of atomizers, they have recommended themselves types 510, 801, 901, each gives its own quantity of steam and in its own way reveals one or another taste and aroma. Accordingly, the appropriate battery is selected for the type of atomizer. Also there are so-called. cartomizers - refillable cartridges with integrated atomizers.
The main component of an electronic cigarette is liquid. It consists of propylene glycol and / or glycerin, liquid nicotine, and food flavoring. I am not a chemist or pharmacist, so I can not declare 100% harmless use of such a mixture in the lungs, as well as the absence of consequences from the daily use of propylene glycol. However, the fact is that I began to use nicotine, which I needed in cigarettes, without concomitant inhalation of over 9000 tar, carcinogens, carbon monoxide, polonium -210, lead-210 and radium-226.
A separate song - the taste and smell of liquid. There are fluids with tobacco, flower, fruit and specific tastes - for example, with the taste of condensed milk or energy (and the Italians make liquids with flavors of roasted bacon, onion, anchovies and fried potatoes). On many sites, you can order both a ready-made liquid and ingredients for its independent preparation. Standard doses of nicotine are 0, 6, 12 and 18 mg / ml.

For me, this turned out to be completely different from all past attempts to quit smoking; there was no annoying itch in the subcortex.
