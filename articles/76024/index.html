How to do everything



Preface.


For the time that I have, I want to do as much as possible. Moreover, I want to do everything at the same time. I have a way. I'll tell you about him.

People have many interests. Often these interests do not just arise and last for several hours, but last a long time. Hobbies are many, they arise regularly, but what about the rest? What to do? My technique.

Key ideas.


  • Interests must be recorded
  • Interests must be visible
  • Interests have no priorities

Must be recorded. If all your interests are recorded, you will not forget about them. Visual memory will help to return to them more often.
Must be in plain sight. If the names of hobbies before the eyes, then this is the key to motivation.
No priorities. If interests have priorities, the leading one will appear; there will be no time left for the others.


How it works?


Everything is very simple. It is enough to take a sheet of A4. make 4 bends and roll with a triangle.
On the side that is in front of you, write what you want to do. Write only the name. For example:
  • Sport
  • Electronics
  • VashBlog.ru
  • Music
  • Tree
  • Spanish
  • Programming
  • Psychology


This list is what you wanted to do for so long but did not reach for a long time. Only titles. Meaning you invest yourself. For example: VashBlog.ru will mean that it‚Äôs time to add a new blog post; Sport symbolizes your interest in chess.
Now the list on the sheet. Here are 8 points: 4 in 2 columns. Place this folded sheet near your workplace. Let you pay attention to him more often.
Sheet stands on the table and waits for you to return from work and institute. You sit at the computer, now is 20.00, there is 2 hours while the movie is swinging. It seems to have free time. It's time to choose a random number from 1 to <number of items in the list>. This can be done through the program, which I brought to the application. If there is no computer, then, taking the book, in advance thinking of the stock, open any page. Then count the number of consonants in this line, which they made. If there are more <number of items in the list>, then count again from 1.

What's the point?


You have no choice, the case decides for you. Whether you want to or not, now you have to open a book to yourself, take a file or go to solder your scheme. Depending on what each item from the leaflet means. You have 2 free hours, spend it for your favorite thing.
