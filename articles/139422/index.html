Yandex Mobile Camp at the conference # MBLT12

A week later, on March 12, an international mobile conference # MBLT12 will be held in Moscow.



The format of the event includes 7 thematic sections, which involve the moderator's communication with experts. In total, more than 40 experts will take part in # MBLT12, including:

Igor Pusenyak (Lima Sky), Raphael Stoffman (infoGin), Gerhard Gunter (Digital Sunray Media), Vangelis Stamatukos (Velti) , Ola Larsen (BlackBerry), Raimo van der Klein (Layar), Zoya Adamovich (Xyologic), Eldar Murtazin ( Mobile-Review.com), Mazkhar Can (Fly), Alex Patsay (Mail.Ru Group), Dmitry Tarasov (Tarasov Mobile), Ilya Shpankov (Opera), Dmitry Satin (UsabilityLab).

Full guest list with the schedule at http://mblt.ru/ru/timetable .

In parallel with the main program, Yandex Mobile Camp will take place in the small hall with the participation of Yandex mobile developers. From 11 am they will start talking about developing for iOS and WP7, using Core Data, auto-assembling iOS projects in Yandex, localizing iOS applications and much more.
