Notification about new letters in jabber

In gtalk there is a nice option for notifying users about new emails, but since I use another jabber server, I thought that it would be convenient to hook up such a feature on myself. Maybe I was looking bad, of course, and there is such an opportunity in gmail, but I did not find it and wrote a small script that I hung in cron. And now he happily notifies me of new letters every 15 minutes, if of course there are such letters.

To work with jabber I used the XMPPHP library.
