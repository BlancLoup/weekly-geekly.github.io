Terrible view. Rigid carbon construction and aerodynamic design that provides minimal wind resistance. The use of such a system allows not only to fly in the wind up to 10 m / s, but also to achieve a horizontal speed of up to 22 m / s.


Side view, "chassis lift mechanism"




Patented technology. Patent US20140263823 . Due to the rise of the "legs" propellers do not fall into the frame


Battery with charge indicator.
All quadcopter devices are powered from one 22.2V battery (6S). The ‚Äúsmart system‚Äù of the battery allows automatic maintenance. New algorithms calculate the distance and the remaining flight time to return home. The battery has the ability to report voltages on each cell, which is important for monitoring and maintaining battery life.


Data on the degree of battery charge is constantly displayed on the smartphone display



Camera and stabilizer Zenmuse X. Ability to shoot in any direction 360 degrees. The camera is made by SONY, has a viewing angle of 94 degrees and, most importantly, thanks to aspherical elements, it is free from barrel distortion. The camera allows you to take photos in RAW (DNG) format for further processing in Lighroom or analogues.

Video stream is up to 60 Mbps. Shooting resolution from 1080p / 50-60fps, up to 2.7K / 30 or even 4K / 25fps. Available manual control of camera parameters - ISO, shutter speed, white balance.



Camera, docking mechanism


Docking for camera




The system of visual and acoustic positioning.

This solution allows you to fly in the premises and keep at the same time the position and height. Sonar is responsible for maintaining the height, and the camera is constantly producing a series of shots that register and correct the displacement relative to the floor surface. Now flying in large halls has become even safer and more stable.




The antenna is packed in the legs (they are shock absorbers)




Engine

The remote looks like a separate finished product (its price is comparable to the iphone 6). The remote allows you to fly a distance of 2 km


Stylish and sleek remote, with ergonomic and pleasant lining on the sides and mount for the tablet. Video is transmitted in resolution up to 720p on Android device

Automatic take-off and landing are available. Also, the new mode - the dynamic point ‚Äúat home‚Äù when the pilot moves - the position of the house is constantly updated, so in case of loss of signal or charge termination, the copter flies not to the takeoff point, but to the pilot. This is due to the constant connection with the GPS receiver in your tablet.


Remote, top and top view feet

From the remote you can quickly and without looking to take a photo, start and stop video recording, playback, but the wheel responsible for exposure compensation has become the most important and noticeable.

Also, innovation, probably more enjoyable for video operators, is the appearance of a zebra reporting overexposure. If you have RAW in photo mode, you can still pull light overexposures, but for a video - what has been shot is taken.


Rear view, here you can also see ergonomic overlays that create pleasant sensations and increase grip.


It is possible to control 2 consoles. One pilot controls a quadcopter, and the second controls a video camera.
