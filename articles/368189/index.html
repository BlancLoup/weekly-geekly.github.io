Sound


I am not a great expert in sound quality, but the fact that the music in the headphones blocks the noise from outside (especially on an airplane) is a plus for me. The sound is smooth, I did not notice any distortions in the low or high frequencies, while watching the movie it seems that the characters speak right in your head. That is, what you need. Autonomy and ergonomics


The stated 11 hours of sounding is quite a real figure. From the moment I received it, I only charged the headphones twice - once the initial charge was out of the box and a second time before it was released, just in case. However, after the bus from the airport I turned off the headphones and turned them on only on the way back, so the waiting time could not be measured.

Connect and control


The connection is standard: for the first time you press the Power button for a few seconds - the phone or tablet searches for the device and remembers it. Next - just a button for two seconds. Convenient, but this is probably the standard.


For the first time you need to get into the "Settings". Further it will not be required

Three buttons, as I said above, are terribly convenient. Upper control the main functions, the lower two - the sound and scrolling forward / back. True, the increase in sound with a long press (about a second), with a short press unwinds one song back. A bit unusual, but this is a matter of habit. All the buttons are on the right ear, so when driving from the side it seems that you scratch the turnip.
