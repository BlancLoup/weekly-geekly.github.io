Translation PS3 Slim and Firmware 3.0 in September!

This article is a free translation with remarks from the post dated 08.18.09 in the blog HaxNetwork . Original article here.

Today, Sony has introduced a new Playstation 3 - Slim, the price of which in the US will be $ 299. The new PS3 Slim looks quite vivid, it is smaller in size and has reduced power consumption. Sales of PS3 Slim (already equipped with firmware Firmware 3.0) will begin on September 1st.

The following are the main features of the new PS3 (the full list at the end of the article ):

- Product Code: CECH-2000 (A) (for charcoal black)

- Hard Drive - 120 gigabytes
(When certification in the US FCC declared the same amount of HDD in 150gb (?) And 250gb (CECH-2000B), approx. Ed.)

- Removed the function "Other System" ( now you can not put Linux )

- Memory: 256 megabytes XDR (RamBus) RAM and 256 megabytes of video memory of the standard GDDR3
It's all the same as the "bbw"

- Dimensions: 290 x 65 x 290mm (width x height x length)

- Weight: 3.2 kilograms

- 33% less and 36% lighter than previous PS3 models

- Will go on sale with already installed software Firmware 3.0
