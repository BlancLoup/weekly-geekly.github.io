GeekBrains launches free online marathon dedicated to remote work


From March 18 to March 22, the educational portal GeekBrains will hold a free online marathon for those who want to change office work to freelance in four areas: marketing, design, management and programming. In five days, participants will be ten webinars, and the most active will receive prizes and a discount on training in GeekBrains.


Marathon participants will learn how to change their profession and get their first order in freelancing, what employers pay attention to when looking for remote employees, as well as trends in the freelance market, the secrets of remote work in management, design, marketing and programming.


The founder of the CHYRKOV studio Sergey Chirkov, HR-expert, career and
Life coach Anna Rudnitskaya, leading designer of INVITRO digital projects Ilya Polyansky and other speakers who built a successful freelancing career.
