Arctic Cooling MX-2 - thermal grease with a "service life" up to eight years

The company Arctic Cooling announced the release of a new effective thermal paste. MX-2 is perfect for overclockers and is able to maintain its properties for a long time.


MX-2 paste does not contain silicone and metal particles, due to which problems associated with the possibility of shorting the tracks or conclusions in case of contact with them paste, was avoided. The main binding liquid is a mixture of polysynthetic oils, which ensures stability and preservation of heat-conducting properties for a long time. According to the manufacturer, MX-2 does not lose its properties and does not require re-application within eight years.

The cost of one package (for convenience of application made in the form of a syringe) MX-2 weighing 4 grams is $ 8.
