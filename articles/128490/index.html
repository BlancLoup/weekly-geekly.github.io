New-old scammers

Today I received a letter from sweb about phishing links. Below the text of the letter, from myself I would like to add that the method of deception is suitable not only for sweb users. Well, the phishing link looks pretty good :)), not everyone will figure out what the final domain is. In general, be careful and cautious. 

 Dear Clients!

 Some of you have received fraudulent letters allegedly from SpaceWeb with a notice of exceeding the load limits.  These letters suggest following the link to learn about ways to reduce the load.

 Do not go through this link and do not enter a password!  This kind of letter - fraud (phishing).  You can read more about it on the corresponding page in Wikipedia: http://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A4%D0%B8%D1%88% D0%B8% Distribution of messages occurs via email addresses taken from public sources (in particular, from the whois service), in order to steal usernames and passwords from the account control panel.

 You can distinguish a fraudulent letter by the type of link on which it is proposed to pass.  SpaceWeb uses only the spaceweb.ru (sweb.ru) domain to allocate service resources.  Link of the form: http://help.sweb.ru._server-support.atmyspace.net/?domain=domain.ru is fraudulent, as in this case the site is located on the domain atmyspace.net.

 If you have already passed a similar link and entered your password, please, as soon as possible
 rather, change it and report the incident to our quality control service at service@sweb.ru.
