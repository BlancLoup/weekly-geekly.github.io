Collection of Opera Mini User Signatures

In the Russian version of the browser for mobile phones and smartphones Opera Mini, for some time now, the default is to search from Yandex, which cannot be deleted or not done by default - after closing the browser, the settings are again installed on Yandex. The situation is especially pitiable for Android users who cannot choose the international or English version.

This approach shows disrespect for users, limits them.

We, the undersigned, ask to give users the opportunity to truly choose a search engine, since an alternative browser provides freedom of choice, and the concept of Opera Software - a full-fledged Internet on any devices.
