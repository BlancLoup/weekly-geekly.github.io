The opening of the Hackspace "Steel"

In the course of further development of my ideas with an electro-scooter start-up, by the will of fate, I happened to attend the opening of the Moscow hackspace / co-working "Steel" on the territory of the former All-Russian Exhibition Center VDNKh. This event took place on July 17 in pavilion 322, having started at 18.00 and lasted until 6.00 (already on July 18). According to the project of the organizers, the building plans to implement a technological space equipped with all the necessary equipment to create a working prototype in the field of the Internet of things, microelectrical transport and energy efficiency. Actually, this institution is viewed in perspective from the perspective of the upcoming prototyping - the benefit is in September it is planned to equip the hackspace with the appropriate equipment. In the meantime, I managed to admire the hackspace with non-ferrous metals and blast furnaces at the grand release stage.





So, grabbing a reflex camera with a bright fifty dollars and wearing a waistcoat- isserman , stuffed with two mirrorless cameras, a shooter and a few more gadgets, I rushed to the event.



I managed to arrive shortly before the event, thanks to which I was able to capture the exposition with the existing equipment, until it was taken up by the giktolps.



Basically there are now motley widescreen plotters and 3D printers. I hope that by the autumn, CNC machines, high-precision welding machines, pipe benders, sheet bending machines and hydraulic presses will be delivered. Although not the fact that the adherents of the Internet of things and energy efficiency share a similar desire for metalworking. Well, let's hope for the development of the institution mainly in the direction of a warm tube hackspace, rather than a glamorous casual coworking.

And currently, further plans have been voiced by inspired speeches by the organizers of the hackspace and high-ranking capital figures in the official part of the event.



Further, the informal part followed, during which I was able to encounter a lot of interesting things. For a start, I visited the technology showroom, where the cream of electric transport of our glorious default has gathered. As it turned out, the country has already launched serial production of rather compact folding electric bicycles.



Regarding the production localization of the product, it was possible to achieve success mainly in the bicycle aspect, since almost all cycling kits are made in domestic factories. But the electric component is almost entirely ordered in heaven, but the targeted work on localizing the production of motor-wheels, controllers and battery assemblies is already underway.

The electric transport product itself was able to capture in more detail at the open-air site, located in front of the building.
