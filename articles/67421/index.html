ADSL modem loses connection before rebooting? No problem!

It all started with the fact that one day I became the owner of the D-Link 2640U ADSL router. Since I also distribute the Internet to my friends, it is turned on for me around the clock, and then one annoying trouble was revealed - he periodically lost connection with the provider and did not want to connect before rebooting. Searches and firmware updates did not solve the problem, and since I sometimes don‚Äôt have a home, and I need to overload the modem, one day a script was written that does it automatically.
