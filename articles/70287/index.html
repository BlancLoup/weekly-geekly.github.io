file.qip.ru and egg virus

Literally today, through ICQ and the file.qip.ru exchanger, an interesting enough virus has spread, interesting at least with what it can say :)

It all started when I received a message from my friend.
(23:26:41) ***:
Link to download the file Egg.rar
file.qip.ru/file/104633322 *** (950 kb)
[- File sent via file.qip.ru. Read more at: file.qip.ru -]

(23:28:08) Screatch:
what's this

(23:28:17) ***:
something positive)


Previously, the sad experience made me ask every time I get such links, but when I received the answer I realized that there was nothing to fear ... I opened it, paid attention to what is written Tested by Kaspersky Anti-Virus 2009-10-18 and without fear downloaded and launched, I saw scrambled eggs, closed. But here I was once again saved by Linux.
A few minutes later, I learned that as it turned out, it was a virus, and it seems to be quite well planned. As of 01.23 Moscow time, the file has been downloaded 13,897 times. Such a rather large army of bots is obtained.

I was surprised by the organization of the virus itself, the developer of the virus had thought out in advance that the majority would ask a question like ‚ÄúWhat is it‚Äù and prepared an auto answer. After a virus is infected, the virus steals your ICQ account no matter what IM client you use, then the virus is sent to the entire contact list.
