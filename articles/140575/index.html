Report from InterSystems Global Summit 2012

The InterSystems Global Summit 2012 took place from March 19 to March 23 in Orlando, Florida, USA. This is an annual event that attracts thousands of developers from around the world using InterSystems technology.


We present a photo report of the head of the branch of InterSystems in Russia, the CIS and Baltic countries, Nikolay Krechetov.
Caution! Under the cut a lot of photos.


Orlando The road from the airport to the hotel at sunset


Before the start of the conference, the most energetic were able to familiarize themselves with the achievements of the American space program and to go to the space center at Cape Canaveral for half a day.


The event began, as usual, with a social event on Sunday evening - everyone gets to know each other, communicate.


Joerg Klingler, Managing Director for Central and Eastern Europe, meets a new partner from Kazakhstan, Ruslan Bezrukov, Elsi Tech .

This year the conference gathered more than a thousand participants from 25 countries of the world. The program is very rich - there are four events in one Global Summit - Developers conference (for application developers), Executive Leadership conference (for leaders of InterSystems partners), Healthcare Leadership Conference (for medical IT managers) plus trainings before and after the event.
