DevConf 2017 will be held in Moscow on June 17

DevConf 2017 , a big conference for web developers, will take place on June 17 in Moscow. Every year more than a thousand participants gather to tell and learn about the newest in the world of programming for the web.

There will be a big party, reports will be read by experienced specialists in Go, MySQL, PostgreSQL, PHP, Javascript and mobile platforms.

We invite everyone to take part. If you want to speak - apply on the conference website. For speakers there will be free participation, nonresident - accommodation, and in general a lot of nice buns.

We are waiting for applications for reports in the following areas:

- Using Golang - practical experience, problems and solutions, frameworks on it, what interesting projects you do on it.
- Architecture. SOA, REST. Theory and practical examples of problem solving.
- HTTP / 2, TLS, traffic prioritization and website loading acceleration
- Javascript - news of the world front-end, mobile cross-platform development, Javascript offline.
- DevOps. Continuous integration, monitoring, organization of collaboration.
- Project and team management - experience, mistakes and achievements of real projects

Current applications can be found here.

Registration for the conference is already open
