This imperfect reality requires correction: a selection of AR-programs

There is reason to believe that this year we will experience a rapid increase in the population of augmented reality programs. In the latest version of iOS, Apple has built in support for mechanisms that allow programs to supplement with any information any objects that are in the field of view of the camera of a smartphone. This not only greatly facilitates the work of programmers, but also makes AR technologies available to hundreds of thousands of iOS developers. In the meantime, the projected abundance is not pouring on us, we decided to make a selection of interesting AR-applications.
