Mobiado Professional 105GCB Rose - Nokia deep tuning



Canadian Mobiado specializing in the production of ultra-expensive mobiles yesterday announced the Professional 105GCB Rose phone yesterday.

Strictly speaking, the Mobiado phones themselves do not produce. Canadians are just developing the case. The basis for the Professional Model 105GCB Rose was the Nokia 6500 Classic, so it comes as no surprise that both tubes are very similar in technical characteristics.

The new Mobiado model will use a screen with a resolution of 320x240 and a standard 2MP camera. The device has a Bluetooth module (version 2.0 with EDR). The user has access to 1 GB of memory, it is impossible to expand this amount with cards - the manufacturer decided that a gigabyte is enough for everything that is needed. Contact with the computer through microUSB port.

But the parameters of the case are never banal. The front and back panels are carved from a single crystal of artificial sapphire and inlaid with 18-carat gold. Keyboard buttons are also made of sapphires. Mobiado also claims that weapons steel was used in the manufacture of the phone and points to the unique fastening of the battery cover.

The phone works in GSM / GPRS / EDGE 850/900/1800/1900 MHz and UMTS 850/2100 MHz networks.
