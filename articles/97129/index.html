Let's discuss the portal TVzavr.ru

April 7, 2010 started our portal tvzavr.ru . We set ourselves not the easiest tasks, the implementation of ideas and technical support required a lot of effort. Now we are ready to take the first results, talk about our goals and answer your questions, dear users. We will also report on new opportunities that will be provided on our website and listen carefully to your opinion about us and how we can improve our service.

Our portal is an Internet cinema, which presents a wide variety of genres, from Soviet classics to modern Russian and foreign films and documentary videos. Here you can find a wide range of educational films. A collection of selected anime, the largest collection of legal anime in RuNet, is already more than 350 hours. And we are constantly updating our video library for you.

For registered users, we offer features such as creating playlists, maintaining a list of selected films, fixing already watched films, saving search queries, the ability to participate in discussions and contests.

For all viewers, even those who do not register, the TV shows are automatically viewed as playlists. And if you watch the movie again, then you will be offered to start watching either from the beginning, or from the place where you finished watching the previous time.

If you like to watch movies in a separate player, retaining all the advantages of local viewing - instantly moving through the film, including just a few frames, resizing the frame, hanging the player over all windows (button on the right of the player control panel), then you just click on the button ‚ÄúWatch in a separate window‚Äù, which is located in the upper right corner of the video player.
In the near future, you will be able to watch not only movies in a separate player, but also playlists and channels. In fact, our viewers will have a small TV, the program for which you can create yourself.

All films are equipped with numerous tags (by directors, actors, years of film production, genres, etc.), by which you can quickly find similar video materials.

Our correspondents keep track of all the most interesting shooting projects (both film and television projects). We offer exclusive materials from the set of NTV, StarMedia, TNT, Ren-TV, Forward Film, Amedia, Triiks Media, RWS (Russian World Studios), Mostefilm and others, including exclusive photos and videos, interviews with actors, analytical reviews.

In general, we have tried to make a portal on which you will find for yourself both interesting films and information about new video projects that are still at the production stage. All that is interesting to this film fan.

We will be glad to constructive criticism.
