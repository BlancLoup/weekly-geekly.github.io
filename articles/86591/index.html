Nettop Zotac ZBOX HD-ID11 on NVIDIA Ion 2



At CeBIT 2010 , taking place these days in German Hanover, Zotac presented a new generation ZBOX HD-ID11 nettop on the NVIDIA Ion platform. The heart of the Mini PC is the 1.66-gigahertz Intel Atom D510 dual-core processor with an integrated graphics core, Intel NM10 Express Chipset (Intel Pine Trail platform).



The computer is equipped with Gigabit Ethernet and 802.11n Wi-Fi adapters, an eSATA port and six USB 2.0 ports. To connect the monitor are the HDMI and DVI outputs.



The package includes a VESA kit, designed to mount a nettop on the back of the monitor.





Features ZBOX HD-ID11:

  • Windows 7 operating system with Aero interface
  • Intel Atom D510 processor (1.66 GHz)
  • New generation NVIDIA ION graphics
  • DDR2 SODIMM slot
  • Compartment for 2.5-inch hard drive
  • 6-in-1 card reader (SD / SDHC / MMC / MS / MS Pro / xD)
  • Communications - Ethernet 10/100/1000 Mbit / s, Wi-Fi 802.11n
  • Ports - HDMI, Dual-link DVI, eSATA 3Gb / s, RJ45, USB 2.0x6, S / PDIF
  • 7.1 audio support
  • DirectX 10.1, OpenCL, OpenGL 3.2, NVIDIA PhysX, NVIDIA CUDA, NVIDIA PureVideo technology support
  • Dimensions 188 * 188 * 44 mm


That is, this MiniPC has the main components and the necessary connectors, and you are offered to install the RAM or drive yourself.

The price of this designer is $ 210.
