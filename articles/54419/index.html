Something about Opera

Several, in my opinion, quite good methods of working with the Opera browser.

Mouse.

Work with the mouse "manipulator" in the Opera is thought out better than anywhere else.
1. navigation through the history of pages.
With a short delay, we press in turn the right, then the left button. And we get history (-1) or in Russian on the page back. Pressing the buttons in the reverse order takes you forward to the page.

2. navigate through tabs.
Clamp the right button and twist the wheel at the outside. A list of tabs and selekotr appears.

3. Close / open a new tab.
Wheel click on the name of the tab and it closes. A click on a free space opens a new tab. It is a standard technique, the same as in FF and possibly in IE.
