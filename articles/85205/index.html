Feelings and initial setup


What did it give me - the average user - other than the awareness of what is higher / higher / next? And gave, I must say, a lot. To be completely clear - I did not have the slightest claim to the A-110, but in the new A-200 the sensations improved by an order of magnitude.
I think those users of the A-110 (and other syabas-based consoles) will be interested to learn about one feature of the A-200 even before its purchase - there will be no such surprise as mine. The fact is that the A-200 can install its applications on the screws, taken out of the "old" consoles, only by preformatting them. Surprise-surprise, I have a half a terabyte of information that I don‚Äôt have to throw myself. I wriggled out by connecting the console through the USB-slave to the computer, and draining all the information on it (about 20 MB / s speed) - it took about six hours. If possible, choose a 2.5 "screw - significantly reduce the heating of the viscera, and the console will weigh a little bit smaller. By the way, now you need a screwdriver to install the screw. Hanging


I get stuck. They are associated with the work of the torrent rocking. The forum is trying to understand how this is related to the number of simultaneous downloads, peers and the maximum download / download speeds. I also believed that hangs slightly depend on these parameters. The hangs occur at the moment when the download of the torrent is over and verification has begun. The load on the processor from verification + some network activity (someone is watching a video on the network or continue torrents) and hello. But! What is nice, the A200 is quietly reloaded from the console, unlike the A110. A110 had to constantly click the toggle switch, but in the A200 it simply does not exist. Press the off button for 4 seconds and the console starts blinking bright LED, indicating that it completes all the processes and goes to the shutter.
