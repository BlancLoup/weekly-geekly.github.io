The report on the results of "My Circle" in October 2015

We, at My Circle , finally set up all the statistics and now we can share our analytics on the state of the IT job market.

We will try to periodically cover the following questions: the need for which specialists in the IT industry now exist, what is the competition between specialists in a particular area, what is the level of salaries in each of them, and much, much more.

Let's start with a very simple report on what happened in October 2015.



In October, a little more than 500 vacancies were placed on My Circle. On average, 6.73 specialists responded to each vacancy. That is almost one and a half times more than in the IT industry as a whole (for which general indicators are answered monthly in hh.ru ).

The easiest way to "My Circle" is now to find a suitable designer. On each design job responds an average of 15 specialists. It is also not very difficult to find a coder, programmer or administrator. In these fields of activity, the number of responses for each vacancy is on average more than 7. It will be a little more difficult to find a marketing specialist, manager or developer of mobile applications. The number of responses to such vacancies is about 5.
