Dev Generation 2010: developers, start!

This year we are again holding the Dev Generation contest, which has become a traditional spring contest for young developers. Dev Generation-2010 started on April 14 and will last until June 2010.

The conditions are the same - you are developing a project or idea in the field of software development and Internet applications, send us a request and if your project is the best, you will receive an investment of $ 100,000 from us to make the idea a reality. Last year, for example, $ 100,000 received the project of interactive hypervideo Actio.



Developers who have provided a project worthy of a second place will go to the world conference on Internet technologies LeWeb'10 in Paris, which will be held on December 8-9, 2010. By the way, the history of the conference LeWeb is quite interesting, however, like the conference itself. If you become one of them, tell me later, how cool is that? :)

Developers whose ideas will be the best according to Microsoft experts will receive a 3 coupon from the company for a free visit to the Start In Garage software for entrepreneurship. In addition, there will be gifts from other partners and sponsors of Softline Venture Partners in the competition.

In general, there are prizes, and quite impressive, the matter remains small - send ideas!
Now about the participants. The Dev Generation competition is aimed primarily at students, so students or teams with at least one student or graduate student from Russia, the CIS or the Baltic countries are allowed to participate. So if you are 18 years old, you have a great idea in the field of software development and you are a student or a graduate student - it's time to send an application for participation in the competition!
Full rules can be read here www.softlinevp.com/devgeneration .
