Zune Phone to appear in December

It is rumored that a Zune-based mobile phone will appear in December 2007, just a few months later than the Apple iPhone. Just yesterday, a meeting was allegedly held at Microsoft, where they made this decision.

Rumors about the Zune Phone are spreading rapidly in the blogosphere - it causes a feeling of deja vu because just the rumors around the iPhone have been spread for years . Now, it seems, the same begins with the Microsoft phone.
As blogs reported yesterday citing an anonymous source at Microsoft, the Zune Phone smartphone will be homogeneously integrated into the Zune online platform and, most likely,
will not work on the Windows Mobile operating system. Instead, it will have an interface that looks like a modern Zune device.

The smartphone will be able to establish a wireless connection with the Xbox 360 game console and broadcast the video stream to the phone screen. This is a very good solution, because through the Xbox 360 console, you can buy video files from an online store.

The release of the console in the Christmas season of 2007 is a very good moment. First, there is enough time to prepare. Secondly, the successful start of sales is almost guaranteed: the main competitor of the iPhone will not be such a novelty by that time, and should not be in high demand because its announcement is planned for June 2007.

It may be recalled that as early as September 2006, before the release of the Zune player itself, information about the Zune Phone smartphone was confirmed by Zune global marketing manager Chris Stephenson himself. True, he did not name the specific time of the device.

Be that as it may, any information about the Zune Phone today belongs to the category of rumors. However, these rumors have already become a cause for ridicule - what will the Zune Phone really be (see video).
