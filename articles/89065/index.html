This black brick will be today's guest of my article.
This is a modern navigator MW-500 from the company with the simple name PocketNavigator.



He can do a lot of things besides the fact that he can lie and give a beautiful view to the table or hang
in the car and serve as a mirror.

UPD: Updated photo and added important price info.


As you already understood, this is a GPS navigator from the Pocket Navigator company (Pocket Navigator, trite, but understandable)
The company specializes exclusively in the production of navigators and a lot has progressed in this. Of course, most functions
navigators depends on the software that is installed in them, but sometimes the developers fill these helpers with motorists and some extra-functions, which I will talk about later.

When you purchase the MW-500, you get an elegant glossy box in black and blue. In principle - the standard.



Its filling consists of:
* Navigator MW-500 itself
* AC adapter 220-230 V
* Car holder for mounting on the windshield
* Car charger
* Wire for PC connection
* Disk with user manual
* Stylus
* User's manual



In principle, everything is the same as in other navigators, but it is good that everything is present and you do not have to buy the mini-Usb wire to connect to a computer.

I also liked the fact that there is a stylus in the navigator. It will be convenient for accurate use and route guidance, as sometimes with a finger
