Smash Bros. ported by calculators TI-83/84



Texas Instruments TI-83/84 type calculators are specialized computing devices that are far from those calculators that many of us are used to. The capabilities of such devices are very extensive, and thanks to this functionality, some craftsmen even run games on Texas Instruments calculators.

For example, a couple of years ago we launched Portal on calculators. Now it is the turn of the classic arcade Super Smash Bros.


Portal example on TI-83/84

Of course, this is not a modern incarnation of Super Smash Bros. under 3DS and Wii U with 40+ heroes and extensive levels. There is only Fox and Falco, but the game does not make it any less interesting. On the contrary, a monochrome 2d toy should appeal to gamers of the old school (which, of course, must also have a corresponding calculator).


And here is the port of Super Smash Bros.

Ported a toy programmer with the nickname Hayleia. In this case, the source code can be downloaded , it is open, and it can be changed to your liking. By the way, there is even an option for multiplayer .
