Call the phone through Miranda

With the help of the MirOUT plugin for Miranda, you can call any person from your contact list on the phone. A call to a Russian mobile operator will cost $ 0.076. You can make a test call for free.
Update: In the comments, the MirOUT developers were noted and clarified that prices for Russia were revised and now they are: Russia $ 0.046 (Landline) $ 0.055 (Mobile). Details in the comments.
