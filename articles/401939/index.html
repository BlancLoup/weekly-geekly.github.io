Children's carpentry machine PLAYMAT: do-it-yourself woodworking is interesting


A whole industrial complex can be assembled from several PLAYMAT machines.

Many children from an early age tend to do something on their own, helping their parents or just having fun. Someone with his mother sweeps, someone learns to hammer nails with dad. Unfortunately, it is often no longer the case that children do the simple work. But it is known that the kids learn very quickly, often showing excellent results. What if you teach your child to perform some difficult tasks so that the skills gained can be useful in the future?

One option is to teach your child the basics of electronics and programming. For this there are various toys, designers, mini-PC with a set of sensors. In addition, you can teach a child carpentry and carpentry. Here you can guarantee that it will be extremely interesting for the child, and the skills acquired in childhood will help to gain confidence that he can do everything he needs with his own hands. But how is this possible? After all, all these saws, nails, drills, blades and other things are very dangerous for kids! In fact, there is a way out. It would be a desire, and the way to implement it can be found almost always. In our case, the output is a multi-function machine for the PLAYMAT tree. Children's machine, designed for use by older children of preschool age.


The design of the machine thought out as far as possible. Despite the fact that it can create a lot of different things, all the nodes and elements are completely safe. Moreover, the developers pay attention to the fact that it is still necessary to teach the child the basics of safety rules when working - this will help in the future to be more careful and to receive less injuries.

How it works?


The machine is modular, the developers themselves call it a transforming kit. The fact is that this kit contains all the necessary parts to create four different machines: turning, jigsaw, drilling and grinding. All four are designed for processing wooden elements. Safety comes first


We like the fact that the developers paid great attention to the security of their complex. For example, a jigsaw machine will not be able to damage the skin of a child, despite the fact that it allows you to very accurately grind out any form of wood. Touching the moving elements of the machine, the child will receive only slightly unpleasant sensations that will not harm him, but will allow him to understand that it is necessary to work carefully.



The electric motor receives a voltage of only 12 volts, so the child will not receive an electric shock, the power part of the system is harmless, as well as the mechanical part.

Also, wear safety glasses while working (they are not included). Shavings and sawdust fly off from the blanks, so it is worth taking precautions.
