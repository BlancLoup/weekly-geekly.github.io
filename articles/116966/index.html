Problem Solving with E1tele.com



When I left for the USA this summer, I had 2 tasks:
Problem number 1: Cheap to call abroad without changing the phone number.
Problem number 2: Cheap calls home from abroad.

My solutions to these problems using E1tele.com you will find under the cut.

Here is what I did to solve problem # 1:
  • Step 1. Registered on the site E1tele.com
  • Step 2. I added my usual mobile phone to the list of registered phones (For example, my number in Moscow is +7 (903) 1234567, I added it).
  • Step 3. Established the status of this mobile phone as "confirmed", and then made it "manager" (so that you can manage this phone number using SMS messages).
  • Step 4. Changed the access code in the section ‚Äúmy account‚Äù - ‚Äúsecurity settings‚Äù.
  • Step 5. I got my personal number on E1tele.com and set my mobile to redirect to my personal number.
  • Step 6. Next, I boarded a plane and ended up in the USA. I carried out further actions already there.
  • Step 7. I buy a foreign operator's SIM card (note that for some countries you can buy cards from E1tele.com ).
  • Step 8. I sent a message from the new phone to the SMS center with the following text: # 11 * 79031234567 * 1111 (# 11 * Native_mobile_number * Access_code) In response, I received a confirmation. Thus, I can make cheap calls abroad without changing the phone number.
Problem number 1 is solved.

Solution of the problem number 2:
To solve this problem, I call via SMS-callback of my American mobile.
For those who are reluctant to type an SMS message, there are various solutions to this problem:
  • Option 1. You can save an SMS message and send it to our SMS center
  • Option 2. Save your contacts on E1tele.com as short numbers. Here is the instruction.
  • Option 3. Install on your mobile app WingBee for smartphones. I wrote about it in detail in my blog.

Thus, problem number 2 is also solved.
