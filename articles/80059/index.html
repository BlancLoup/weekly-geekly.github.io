English speaking resources



Computer science



OpenCourseWare MIT




One of the largest repositories of training courses. Here are collected almost all the courses that are taught in Massachusets Institute of Technology, which is considered one of the best in the world. The site has a total of 1900 courses, and I have counted more than 200 courses taught at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science . The composition of most of the courses: brief lecture notes, a detailed description of the course, a calendar plan, laboratory tasks, course project tasks and exams, intermediate modules. Video lectures are attached to some courses.



Videolectures.net



Very good resource with a huge number of video lectures on computer disciplines in English. All lectures are categorized.

UC Barkeley Webcasts



Webcast site (both in audio and in video format) of the University of California, Berkeley. Except as a computer theme, there are quite a few other general educational disciplines. Sami webcasts conducted since 2001.

ShowMeDo



The site is dedicated to learning free software, in English. All lessons are presented in the form of video tutorials and screencasts. A lot of lessons on programming in Python.

YouTube EDU



The well-known YouTube channel dedicated to education. Here are collected together all the video lectures of world famous universities - MIT, Stanford, Cambridge and many others. There are also channels of 2 Russian universities - SZTU and MGIMO.

Academic Earth



Free video courses from leading universities - Harvard, Stanford, Berkeley, Yale and others. In computer science, 14 training courses are presented.
