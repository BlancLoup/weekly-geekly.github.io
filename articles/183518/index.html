Siklum announces Amazonization of .NET developers in Kharkov!

The Kharkov office of Siclum announces a two-week campaign for all .NET developers, which will allow not only to evaluate their professional skills, but also to get a gift from Amazon for this!


In the period from June 17 to 28, take part in a technical interview for one of the .NET vacancies opened in the Kharkov office of the company - and choose yourself a gift for Amazon yourself! Applicants can take part in the promotion of a Middle-Senior level who meets the requirements specified in one of the vacancies at www.ciklum.com/join/careers/vacancies/by-vacancy/city/Kharkiv/skills/.Net-vacancies . Each participant of the action, regardless of the outcome of the technical interview, but successfully passed an HR interview that meets the requirements for one of the vacancies and at least appointed a technical interview until June 28, inclusive, receives a $ 50 certificate from the technical interview for self-selection and ordering a gift from Amazon.com

Being modern companies, Siklum gives the right to choose a gift: books for technical development, professional literature or, for example, flippers? You decide!

For more information, please contact the Siklum Kharkiv office team by e-mail Kharkov@ciklum.com or directly from the recruitment team leader Olga on Skype olga_didich
