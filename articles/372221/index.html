Since May 9, Opera VPN mobile application is available for iOS devices.



Thanks to the efforts of Opera developers from May 9, 2016, iTunes has a free unlimited VPN application for iOS available, and iPhone and iPad owners will be able to increase their personal data protection and access more content. The new application will be especially useful in places with limited access to social networking sites and streaming video.

Recall that Opera recently introduced the VPN function in Opera browser for personal computers, and Opera VPN for iOS is the first full-fledged VPN application from Surfeasy for the Apple mobile platform.

Opera VPN for iOS is available in selected countries in English, Arabic, French, German, Indonesian, Japanese, Portuguese, Russian, and Spanish.
