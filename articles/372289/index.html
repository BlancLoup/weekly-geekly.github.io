I have an engineer with my mother: the end of the NTI Olympiad



At the end of April, we had a chance to visit the first All-Russian Children's Engineering Olympiad of NTI (National Technology Initiative). In total, almost 5,000 children participated in the competition, but less than 100 reached the final. The organizers of the first All-Russian Olympiad of NTI were 3 polytechnic universities: Moscow, St. Petersburg and Tomsk. Since our studio is based in the building of the Moscow Polytechnic University, we were invited to cover this event on our channel.
