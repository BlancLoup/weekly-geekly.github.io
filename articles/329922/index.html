The science of neural networks. Live

There are 213 days left until the end of the year, so it's high time to start learning something new, for example, to plunge into the science of neural networks. Today, in one day, we will get acquainted with the installation of neural networks on the air, starting with simple architectures and ending with deep learning - networks with dozens and hundreds of layers. Also consider the convolutional networks used for image recognition, and recurrent networks for sequence analysis. And you will be able to train a neural network with us to solve non-trivial tasks - from recognizing handwritten numbers to recognizing cats in photos.
