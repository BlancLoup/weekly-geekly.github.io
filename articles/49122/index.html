Intel Atom N280

The first serial "Atomic" processor from Intel was launched into production a little more than six months ago (N270). In 2009, we expect a new wave of netbooks on the updated version of the processor.


Compare N270 and N280:
Atom n270 Atom n280
Chipset 945GSE GN40
CPU frequency 1.6GHz 1.66GHz
FSB 533MH 667MHz
Price US $ 46 US $ 60-65


The first netbook on the Atom N280 was shown at CES'09.

In addition to Asus, several other companies announced the development of devices on the new processor: Acer and Gigabyte Technology.
