Our conference has several formats of performance, and for each of them their own conditions:

#one. 40 minutes report

In this format, the speaker is allocated 40 minutes for a report with a presentation and five minutes for questions in one of the two event rooms. The speaker will be compensated for transportation costs (the road to Moscow and back by economy class) and booked a hotel room near the conference venue for three nights. Compensation for the road can not exceed $ 1000. All additional terms are discussed separately.

# 2. FastTrack talk

In this format, the speaker is allocated 15 minutes for a report with a presentation and five minutes for questions in a separate room. Speaker is not compensated for travel expenses and accommodation is not provided. Applications for participation should be sent to: fasttrack@zeronights.org.

# 3. Master Class

The speaker prepares training materials, presentations and other materials. He is allocated at least 2 hours to conduct a master class in a separate room. The speaker will be compensated for transportation costs (the road to Moscow and back by economy class) and booked a hotel room near the conference venue for three nights. Compensation for the road can not exceed $ 1000. All additional terms are discussed separately.
