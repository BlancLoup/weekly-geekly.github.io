Do you use the mouse while working?

Hello.

I think we all saw beautiful photos of Yandex offices, Google, and other well-known market players. Usually there someone sits in a comfortable, easy chair behind his MacBook Pro and writes code. I wondered if it was true that most developers do not need a mouse to work? I propose to discuss this.





Personally, I have been trying to part with my little, gray friend for a long time. However, so far, unfortunately, without success. Without a mouse, it is sometimes quite difficult to quickly search for something needed on the Internet (even using the touchpad of a laptop). In addition, it is sometimes not very convenient to navigate through the code (in rare cases when the wonderful IDEA or ReSharper is not enough).

It will be interesting to hear about your experience without / with a mouse / touchpad / trackball in the comments. Perhaps you are using some additional programs that create some hot keys for more convenient work behind a laptop without a mouse. Perhaps you know some other tricks and tricks on this topic.
