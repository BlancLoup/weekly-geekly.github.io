GTA 4 game hit the Guinness Book of Records

Crime action Grand Theft Auto 4 continues to beat all imaginable and unimaginable sales records. Although the game has not yet been ported to the PC (and this is unlikely to happen in the near future ), but thanks to it the retail sales of the Xbox 360 console have increased by 125% . The lack of a PC version does not interfere with the victorious march of GTA 4.

On Tuesday it became known that the game GTA 4 will be included in the Guinness Book of Records as the most successful project not only in the history of video games, but in the entire entertainment industry. The adventures of American crime set an absolute record in sales for the first day ($ 310 million) and the first week ($ 500 million). Previously, the record belonged to the book "Harry Potter and the fatal relics", which on the first day of sales brought $ 220 million.
