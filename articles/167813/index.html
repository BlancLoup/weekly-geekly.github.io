How google & yandex find pictures



Google & Yandex search engines recently changed the design of their image search services. For the user, probably, it really became more convenient - now he can say for sure that the abstract from Google has downloaded the picture from Google Dex.

Search engines are also in the black - PR of their eternal love for the user - and by covering their reluctance, he shares traffic and, as a result, money.

How? These are such good companies ... and you webmasters are all evil, greedy and, in general, SEOs - a typical Internet user will say.

And everything is simple - almost every web resource owner will answer them.
If earlier when searching for a picture - when clicking on a preview - the site of the source opened, now the big picture itself opens - without the site \ server on which it is located. Without counters, without the text of the document, and yes without advertising ...

The user is of course pleased - the picture was found - everything is super!
The search engine also - the traffic is not going anywhere, Yandex also will show its own advertising right away - though NOT under its content.

That's just the most web resources such innovations as a knife in the back - on average, the search traffic for pictures all sank by 40-50%
No traffic - no money - no new content. The same abundance of pictures and their interest in creating a resource is less.
