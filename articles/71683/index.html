HTC HD2 went hunting


Finally, a monster among WinMo-smartphones has been released. Today, HTC introduced the HTC HD2 officially. It is under this name that the smartphone will be sold on the world market. A distinctive feature of the smartphone is a Qualcomm Snapdragon processor operating at 1 GHz. The volume of ROM is 512 MB, RAM is 448 MB (which is a record for the moment), as well as the eye-candy interface HTC Sense UI, which will look just great on a 4.3 inch display. The smartphone runs on Windows Mobile 6.5. Expected cost - 600 euros.
