Habrastruchta in St. Petersburg

Finally. Next Wednesday, November 30, the First St. Petersburg Habravstrecha will take place. Details, organizational issues and other announcements under the cut.


Photo from panoramio.com

The meeting will take place on November 30 at 20:00 , but the place has not yet been determined. Concerning

1. I ask St. Petersburg to write my suggestions, where to meet, in the comments or on the post egorkotkin@gmail.com. The requirements for the place are as follows: a decent quiet institution such as a cafe, somewhere in the center or on Vaska, a table for 15 people, or the ability to move a few tables, a corner of the hall or a separate room.
