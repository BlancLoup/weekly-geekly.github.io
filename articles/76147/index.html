The action "Interface = understanding": the results of the first stage are summed up

Good evening, dear habrudruzya!

The action "Interface = understanding" continues. We spent two full days with us for consideration of your applications, which - to our great joy - were sent a lot. We want to thank all the participants for their interest, correctness and ... understanding.

Unfortunately, the number of projects that we would like to help for free, exceeded the planned rate. For a number of participants, we decided to make an exception by sending them individual offers of cooperation. We also increased the number of projects that will be implemented for a symbolic amount - from 1 to 8 thousand rubles.

However, there are no losers in our promotion. To each user who submitted the application, we are ready to offer professional implementation of their projects at prices 30-40% lower than the market ones. Within two days we will finish forming price offers and, together with links to the work of the organizers of the action, we will send them to the email addresses of the other participants. Thus, about a hundred more projects will get a chance for their good-quality and high-quality performance at prices comparable to those offered by novice studios.

Today we publish a list of 15 projects in the Interfaces category that we are ready to implement for free and shareware.


Free projects



Topbot


Rostislav Dmitriev
beta.topbot.ru | topbloger.ru | top4blog.ru

What is: An unusual blogosphere statistics engine with an interesting presentation of posts in the issue (images and other additional information are displayed).

What will be: Full-fledged statistics services with an optimized for reading interface, advanced search, categorization and adding to favorites.

OfficeSIP


Vitaly Fomin
www.officesip.com

What is: Free server and client for SIP-telephony. At least additional functionality, simple interface, indistinguishable from hundreds of competitors.

What will happen: Applications that differ from competitive solutions in the convenience of access to basic functions, combined with a simple and pleasant design.

Graffi


Stepan Goncharov
www.androlib.com/android.application.app-graffi-free-qxqC.aspx

What is: An attempt to create a simple graphic editor on the Android platform. Modest features, the interface is only for portrait mode.

What will be: A simple but pleasant graphical editor that uses all the features of Google's new platform to implement the interface.

Bileterra


Ekaterina Krivich and co.
bileterra.ru (in development)

What is: The implemented service concept for the purchase and sale of tickets, developed uskeys and interface sketches.

What will happen: A friendly and convenient platform for buying and selling tickets, designed for both regular users and natural visitors.

myCRIB


Oleksandr Rakovets
(in developing)

What is: A developed concept of student service for the exchange of lecture materials, sketches, notes, timetables, etc. with social functionality.

What will happen: A complete knowledge-sharing platform for students with a flexible design, optimized for everyday and mass use.
