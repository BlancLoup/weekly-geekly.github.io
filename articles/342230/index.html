2. Customer Relationship Management. CRM


Customer information comes out on top in the competition. The more information you collect and analyze, the more chances you have to sell the product. Contacting support, downloading the free version, visiting the site, participating in a webinar, shopping - all these are sales funnel steps and it is extremely important to keep track of all these touches.



What you can do in Redmine using the CRM module:


  • maintain a database of customers, partners and counterparties;
    • Segment customers with tags and filters;
    • see the history of communication with the client;
    • see the point of interaction with the client (sales, registration, correspondence, calls);
    • bind tasks to the client;
    • send email messages;
    • organize customer portal;
    • organize the sales process.


It is worth noting that thanks to the user access settings, you can organize a real personal account for your customers, where they can see all their requests, download products, pay for licenses.


Practical example
In one of the SaaS projects, users of the trial version were not converted to a paid one, so it was necessary:
  1. Find a bottleneck in the sales funnel.
  2. Identify and work out the main causes of failure.
  3. Hold "Customer development" current customers.


In order to determine the bottleneck, I had to build a separate funnel to promote the users of the trial version. Problems were identified at the stage of authorization and customer involvement. I needed to understand why there were 20 registrations per week, and only one became a paid subscription.



To find a bottleneck at the stage of using the free version, I had to add touch points to the client, analyze the behavior inside the trial, and fix feedback for each client. As a result, we added new stages of interaction with the client: an online presentation and training training and began to analyze. At the same time, it was necessary to keep current customers, identify the collective image of the customer and improve the value proposition.



As a result, I set up my own ‚Äúboards‚Äù for each type of clients and organized work with both leads and current clients. With the help of filters, I built the reports I needed, and by the responsible executives I could see who was working, and the visualization of the funnel by the sales stages gave me food for thought.



I managed to organize the work of five employees at once, identify and eliminate the bottleneck (migration of data from other systems), and, as a result, increase the average check and income.


3. Organization of customer service. Helpdesk


If communication with clients began to bring inconvenience to you (loss of information, failure to meet the deadline, no person in charge), then the time has come to organize the work of the support service.


Each appeal from the client has its own status, the author and the responsible executor. All actions on a ticket are stored in the history, and you can always track the current status of the call, unlike the mail client.



What you can do in Redmine using the Helpdesk module:


  • helpdesk will automatically create a ticket from the email client (or from the contact form);
  • for one and creates a contact card (if it is a new appeal);
  • You can bind the task to the contact (the history of customer calls);
  • save you time for answers (response templates);
  • privately communicate with colleagues, discussing how to help the client;
  • organize support levels (SLA);
  • track support service metrics (time of first response, etc.);
  • get a score for the answer from the client (manager's score);
  • automate support (Set up auto-reply with a link to the FAQ);
  • initiate a dialogue with the client first (Pop-up feedback form).

Practical example
An appeal came from the client. Following the link from the notification letter, we go to the client's card and see: well, firstly, this client tried the trial version, secondly, he has downloaded free versions of the product, deals are made on it, and there is contact information.

Without leaving the contact card, we respond to his appeal, having at hand the necessary information about him. This is important for building personal and trusting relationships.

We all love the attention to our person, and if you mention, a la: "I see that you tried our trial version of the cloud solution last year, but you didn‚Äôt have enough support to migrate data from Trello", then for the client it will be It sounds like this: "You are a very important client for us, and we all understand the responsibility as one and really want to help you and your business!"


Integration of data from CRM and Helpdesk plays a very important role in organizing interaction with customers. And now let's see how the work of the team is organized.
