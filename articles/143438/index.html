Ultramodem for ultrabook

Intel is not only fast processors, but recently c and chipsets for 3G modems.
The first such chipset was the Intel XMM6260, and the hero of this review will be the Hi-Universe E369 modem developed by Huawei based on it.
Today it is the most compact 3G modem with USB interface.



The device weighs 20 grams, and its thickness is only 8mm.
The modem supports 5 ranges: 850/900 / AWS / 1900 / 2100M. Thus, the user can access the network from anywhere in the world. The pleasures are not exhausted. It is stated that the modem on an Intel chipset consumes 30% less energy than models on chips from other manufacturers.

Ancient wisdom says: "Everything is relative," so I picked up our opponent's hero - the branded Megafon E173 modem, also produced by Huawei. I think I will not be mistaken if I call him one of the most common.
