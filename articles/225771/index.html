Personalized brain cancer treatment



In the New York Genome Research Center , a lot of work is being done on the personalized treatment of one of the most dangerous types of human brain cancer, glioblastoma .

Cancer is a complex disease, the treatment of which is complicated by two major obstacles. First, it may happen that in search of an effective treatment method, patients will have to try different options, losing time. Secondly, oncologists simply do not have time to get acquainted with the avalanche of data on cancer of even a certain type, not to mention additional data on unusual types of the disease, and, moreover, data on the genome of a particular patient.

A doctor can be a very capable specialist, but the human brain is simply not capable of containing all the information that can help a particular patient.

Opportunities supercomputer "sharpened" to work with Big Data, a huge unstructured arrays of information. In this case, it is an analysis of hundreds of thousands of medical certificates (about 600 thousand), as well as data on the patient's genome. Plus, the system analyzes biomedical literature and data on relevant drugs.

With the help of IBM Watson, doctors obtain the necessary information after analyzing terabytes and terabytes of data by the system, and use this information for personalized patient care. This allows you to increase the chances of a successful recovery, and each new case further increases this probability .
