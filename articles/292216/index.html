In Russia, will reduce the fee for electronic public services by 30%, as well as increase the labor mobility of citizens

In only 22 regions of Russia, more than 35% of citizens use electronic public services. The leader was the Ural region. There, this figure reached 47%. And in the North Caucasus, it does not exceed 17%. However, the government planned to reach this level throughout the country by 2015. In this regard, measures will be taken to increase the interest of Russians to e-government services.

One of these measures is a 30 percent reduction of state duty on these services. She was offered by Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev. For some services, the state duty has already been reduced earlier.
