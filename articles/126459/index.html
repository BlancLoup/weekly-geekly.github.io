Life abroad



It all started with the fact that I started thinking about where I would go to live. I wanted to move somewhere close to a warm climate, in one of the European countries, where you can move without being a millionaire. I began to study, read ... and then I realized that I lack a site where I could find information about any country, talk with people who already live there and those who are planning to move. Of course, there is not one forum and blog in each country - but such a site that would unite all Russian-speaking
Emigrants - I did not find this. And what I found did not completely satisfy my needs.

That is why I decided that you need to make such a site. About this site and will go on.

about the project


I'll start with the name of the site. For about two weeks, my friends and I came up with a name. The first thing they decided - the site should be in the zone. Com. It is prestigious, promising, etc. And all the popular social networks in this area. Even the beloved / hated (underline the necessary) contact on vk.com gets over. But it turned out that there is a problem with the choice of name. After all, as you know, this is the oldest and most popular zone. Most
delicious titles were taken. Began to think about what should be in the title. We thought that the site is about people and about countries. From here people and accordingly countries have taken. And they combined these two words.

It turned out - peopleandcountries.com - and fortunately - this domain was free. Hurray, move on :)

The site I wanted to do with a social focus. So that people can find each other in any country, including the district and the city. That there were photos, blogs, ratings, personal messages, groups and other things of social orientation. That is what I began to create.

About where and how I advertise and what resources were the most effective.



I tried a lot of different tools: advertising on twitter, facebook, vkontakte, directadvert, advertising from bloggers, Yandex - direct, Google - AdWords, SEO, on specialized websites for directions. Checking returns through www.google.com/analytics , metrika.yandex.ru and liveinternet.ru.

Briefly write on each.

Twitter microblog advertising is effective, but very short-lived.
Facebook turned out to be one of the leaders in terms of efficiency, the cost also turned out to be very attractive - qualitatively and not expensive.
Advertising VKontakte - the quality of traffic is lower than from Facebook, the price is more expensive. Apparently due to the fact that the audience is younger than the one that is needed for my project. On average from 25 years.
Directadvert is an interesting option. Something like contextual advertising, but it looks like news on sites. More expensive than social networks, but a good conversion is obtained, recorded.
Advertising with bloggers is effective. Short-term effect - a large number of transitions at once. And over time, it gradually falls, but it does not reach zero. Bonus - SEO effect.
Yandex is a big traffic, low price, but low percentage of conversion from visitors to registered users. Compensated by low price.
Google is the most unexpected failure. Low traffic, expensive conversion is close to zero. Maybe I cooked it wrong, but the results are not pleased.
SEO - for new projects - is tight. But this is a promising channel. Therefore, work is underway and a small effect is already there.

To summarize , in terms of price / quality for our project, the most effective was (to my surprise) Facebook. The second is direct from Yandex. The third is advertising with bloggers. All the others are about the same. The worst are AdWords from Google. Perhaps this information is useful to you :)

The story about where I am moving and why it is there :)



Perhaps the reader will be interested - where I decided to move. I'll tell you about it. I am moving to live in Bulgaria . From the east it is washed by the Black Sea. It borders with Greece and Turkey in the south, with Serbia and Macedonia - in the west and with Romania - in the north.
Why Bulgaria? Because:

1. Sea
2. Next to Russia
3. Real estate is cheaper than 2 times in comparison with Russia
4. Prices for cars are lower times ... sometimes 4 (!!!) than in Russia
5. Prices in general. For products - below. For rental apartments - below. Prices are generally lower for almost everything. It seems apart from gasoline and cigarettes :)
6. Language. People over 35 mostly know Russian, says the forum about Bulgaria . Learning Bulgarian is easier, because there is Cyrillic, just like us. Many words are alike.
7. Making it legal to live in Bulgaria is easier than in most European countries.

But about moving to Bulgaria, I think, I will write in detail separately. I think this information will always be interesting and relevant.
