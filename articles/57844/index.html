9th StartupPoint at KIBORIFE - an important and major event for a startup in one place

On the 23rd of April (Thursday) at 4:00 pm and at 7:00 pm, 2 StartupPoint sections will be held at KIBORIF. More than 30 venture funds and private investors confirmed their participation in the meeting, among them: Apaxys Global Ventures, ru-Net limited, Alliance ROSNO, InVenture, VDI, Inkubator.ru, Wermuth Asset Management, MICEX, ABRT, Almaz Capital, SBAR, Media Cartel, ValueTech Advisers, Adobe Systems Russia, Alawar Entertainment, Microsoft, Roiber, Rambler, Intel, FINAM.
Already more than 20 startups have applied for participation in the 9th StartupPoint. If you want to get professional feedback on your startup, show it to investors, find like-minded people in the team - these 2 sections are for you. You can apply for participation with a startup on StartupPoint.ru .

Why 2 sections at once?
First of all, there are too many projects that want to shine in front of large market players for one section; we almost never deny this unique opportunity to anyone (because for a startup this is a really important development step).
Secondly, it is an opportunity for start-up teams to combine their participation in StartupPoint along with the organization of their mini-sections running in parallel at the same time.

But its implementation is highly dependent on the width of the Internet channel. In any case, all captured video will eventually appear on StartupPoint.ru in the profile of each of the startups that were presented (an example of a pitch in the profile of a Wi2Geo startup ).

For questions about the organization of the meeting, write to mimic@startuppoint.ru

PS If you are not able to attend the meeting, but are interested in attracting investments in a startup and / or feedback from fellow start-ups and expert investors, register your startup in the StartupPoint community. Get in the field of visibility of investors and startups, let those who can be interested in your startup know about you.
