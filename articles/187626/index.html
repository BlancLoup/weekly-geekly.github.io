Each of us has needs. The need for family, friends, home, work, hobby, ...
But I would like to talk about one of the most important and always relevant: the desire to live well and earn well.

Most people have this need. And everyone is trying to satisfy her through work, profession and career. And it seems quite logical to achieve this goal through professional development and self-realization. Who among us would not want to become a high-class specialist, a professional of international level: recognition, respect, high incomes, great opportunities - sounds great.

And what is the plan of action for all these millions and billions of future high-class workers? Quite often, this plan looks like this: finish school, prepare for a university, enter a university, study, finish a university, find a good job, work, make a smart career, go on a well-deserved rest.

This plan only seems to be correct, but it is not.

The right plan is different from the wrong plan in that the right plan leads to a goal, and the wrong plan doesn't.

Competition




1 Winner takes all


5% of the best specialists receive 50% of all money. 20% of the best professionals receive 80% of all money.

Some companies are looking for a better employee, while others are cheaper. The first are not afraid to overpay, but they want to get the best for the money they have. The latter want to pay the smallest money for the minimum quality that suits them.



You will begin your career / professional path from the leftmost point of the graph. And it is best to be in the rightmost part of it. You have a long way to go. You need as quickly as possible to move at least to its right half. The specialist from the right side of the graph differs from the specialist from the left side by the presence of experience, and, moreover, of qualitative experience.

While you are in the left half, the number of such specialists as you in the labor market is much more than the demand for them. So this is a buyer's (employer) market. And you have to compete with the same as you, for the most lousy job.

But as soon as you have gained enough experience, and moved to the right half, the rules of the game begin to change. Demand begins to exceed supply and wages begin to grow. 5 years of good experience can result in a 10-fold increase in salary. So, think, look around, and learn.

But even better is to enter the top 5% of specialists. Here your fees will be limited only by the stock of money from your clients / employers. He wants to hire the best - let him pay better than anyone. Just like at auction.

A smart and hardworking person is able to enter the top 20% of specialists in 5 years. And over the next 5 years - in 5% of the best specialists. Here, of course, you need a lot of self-education and often change jobs. Sometimes and work beyond measure.

But do not need much (long) work. The best specialist does not do more work, he does a better job. The best. Therefore, it cannot be replaced by ten average workers.

If in the presidential election you received 48% of the vote, and the second candidate is 47%. You did not get the support of the majority, you did not surpass your rival twice. You bypassed it by one, only one percent! But you will become president and get everything , but he will remain nothing and will not receive anything.

I am the best


Well, if you decide to become the best, you will also find many "magic surprises."

1 The best is the enemy of the good



To be better is to be better than others, to transcend them and to be different from them. You cannot do the same as anything to become the best. You need your way.

You cannot be the best in everything: while you study everything, someone specializes in one thing. The best way to become the best is to choose one very narrow specialty and become a world-class specialist in it.

If you practice ballet from five years old to 8 hours a day, there will always be someone who practices from three years to 10 hours a day. By the age of fifteen his experience will exceed yours by 5000 hours. But there are also geniuses, for every hour of which you need to study three. And the best teachers in the world, and you, for example, are self-taught.

The only way to become the best without having your own unique way (to be like everyone else) is to work harder, be talented, have good teachers and rich parents. Although - this is not quite "like everyone else", right?

But even the fastest and most hardworking horse in the world will not overtake the car. You need your own strategy, your own unique plan to become the best without sacrificing everything.

2 The best not just to be


There is always someone who started before you. Someone has rich parents, someone studied at the best university in the world. Some parents took me to work. Nothing wrong. It happens. This is called - different starting conditions. But such people are a minority, the world is full of people who have themselves achieved success, thanks to their innovative thinking, hard work and the ability to constantly learn everything new.

Life is like a card game. Anyone will win if he is given only trumps, a professional wins regardless of the cards distributed. He minimizes their influence to a minimum by his skill.

No one understands this as well as professional athletes. Many of them have only a few years to realize their chance and achieve success.

3 There is always someone who is willing to work more than you.



There are many such people. Among them are workaholics, perfectionists and people who are simply in love with their work. Yes, many of them donate family, friends, and are ready to work 80 hours a week. Work is their life. This path is not for us. But they are perfectly able to push you to the roadside. You are not ready to spend 6 months a year on business trips to get a promotion, and they are ready.

The average Chinese student is more diligent than ours, and the Chinese worker is ready to do your job for a quarter of your salary.

To work a lot is not a way to success, but to work a little is a way to failure.
