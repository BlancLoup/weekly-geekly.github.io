World Drone Prix Super Bowl Race: $ 1 million prize pool



Top drone pilots from all over the world will compete in Dubai. The prize fund of the largest drone racing championship is $ 1 million.
The first World Drone Prix World Championships will be held March 11-12 in Dubai. 32 participants will compete in high-speed piloting of multikopter on distances with obstacles. Broadcast championship in real time - here .

Initially, more than 100 teams were invited to participate in the competition. Previously, a number of qualifying tournaments were held in Los Angeles, Seoul, Berlin and the cities of China, as a result of which the number of finalists was reduced to 32 riders.

The prize fund of $ 1 million will be divided between the winners of various categories, including such as "Best route team", "Best time to complete the distance", "Best free style team" and "Best designer" (this nomination is available for teams that collected their own drones on their own). In addition, the audience will vote for the best team.



This event will be the first in the calendar of world competitions organized by the World Organization of Racing Drones and Sheikh Hamdan, Crown Prince of Dubai, for the development of sports and technology related to drones.

Pilot drone Rio Rex (Ryo Rex), who founded the drone racing Aerial Grand Prix in Los Angeles in 2014, is now working with the World Drone Prix and believes that this is the direction of the future. The race track itself was specially created for this competition and includes a number of obstacles for pilots.
