MasterCard MoneySend: A New Way to Transfer Funds to Bank Cards



As you remember, the money transfer service VISA Money Transfer has been operating for a long time, successfully, and in many banks already online (instantly). Today we can report on the connection of the new service: MasterCard MoneySend . It allows you to transfer money from the QIWI Wallet account to the MasterCard and Maestro bank card, issued by any Russian bank or banks of the following countries: Albania, Armenia, Bulgaria, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Hungary, Georgia, Israel, Kazakhstan, Cyprus, Kosovo Macedonia, Malta, Moldova, Poland, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Turkey, Ukraine, Croatia, Montenegro, Czech Republic.

This service will be available today on the QIWI Wallet website, and will soon appear both in mobile applications and in QIWI terminals.


Currently, the transfer rate ranges from 1 minute to 2-5 days, depending on the particular issuing bank. When testing the service, we conducted a test payment to an account at Alfa Bank - the payment came in 5 days. In the future, as in the case of VISA Money Transfer, most banks will switch to an accelerated transfer system. Thus, the money will be transferred to the recipient‚Äôs account almost instantly.

To transfer funds in any QIWI Wallet interface, it is enough to enter the number and validity period of the beneficiary's card in a special field. The maximum amount of transfer is 15,000 rubles. Commission when transferring funds to the account of Russian banks is 2% of the amount of payments + 20 rubles, banks of other countries - 2% + 40 rubles. The total amount of transfers with a commission per one recipient or one sender per month cannot exceed 600,000 rubles.
