Bitcoin has an official bank



One of the oldest e-currency exchangers Bitcoin Bitcoin-Central.net has received a banking license.

The license was obtained in France, which means that the exchanger will receive its IBAN (International Bank Account Number) over the coming months, this will make it possible to use it as a regular bank, making transfers of ordinary currency, and the bank, in turn, will automatically convert it in bitcoin.

After completing all the legal subtleties, bank customers will be able to get a payment card and use it as usual - making transfers and replenishing it with a certain amount.

The bank will take on all the difficulties associated with currency conversion.
As a result, buying something for, for example, the euro, the bank in real time will convert Bitcoin into euros and pay.

Thus, access to Bitcoin will be received by those people who were afraid to use it earlier because of various difficulties and because of the lack of specific knowledge.

Bitcoin is a digital decentralized currency that cannot be regulated either by the government or by banks, and the key difference from conventional currency is that the transactions made cannot be canceled.
