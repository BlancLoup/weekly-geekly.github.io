Pre-election race through the eyes of search robots



We perform daily cross-section of documents located on the main pages of the million most visited sites in the world. Today we will look at how the number of references to presidential candidates correlates with ongoing events.

Link to chart, scale - linear

The number corresponds to the number of references found in the text from the body of html documents received by the crawler daily, which allows us to display an index for each candidate. Usually we build similar indices by brand for marketers / pr-specialists, but the election is a historic event.

Frankly, we approached this issue with some negligence, Sobchak was added by some miracle before she announced her desire to nominate a candidate, Grudinina already when it became clear that without him the banquet would not take place. On the other hand, we did not have a goal to make a thorough analysis of the candidates' technologies, we wanted to see how the tops behave.

On the graph at the beginning of the topic, we can observe the dynamics of references to candidates. The scale is linear, Putin flew away, Sobchak (when he announced his nomination) and Navalny (when the CEC denied registration) approached him most of all. Here the scale is logarithmic .
