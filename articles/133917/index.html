1 minute for good

Most startups in Runet pursue a ‚Äúsimple‚Äù goal - to make the world a better place, and, in the meantime, to earn N millions of dollars for their creator. But in this topic we will focus on the project, which focused only on the first component.

Charity startup 1minute.ru was launched on November 14th. Its essence is that the user spends one minute of his time, for example, he watches a video paid by the advertiser, and the funds received for this action go to give gifts to children or other good deeds. The user himself does not spend money. One of the first advertisers was Pushkino Bank, Comdi Internet broadcasting service, Google search engine and several other companies.

Now the first action is going on - funds are being raised for New Year presents for pupils of 37 orphanages. Over the past three weeks since the start of the action, more than 9,000 people took part in the project, spending a total of more than 10,000 minutes on the proposed actions. The funds received are already enough for 624 gifts (at the time of writing the topic). These numbers are constantly increasing. You can follow them on the online counters on the site.

You also have the opportunity to spend 1 minute of your time for good. To do this, just go to 1minute.ru and perform one of the proposed actions.
