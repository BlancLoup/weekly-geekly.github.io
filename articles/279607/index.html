Download, raspars, poured into the database. Well done. We look. Now the truth will be revealed - when is it necessary to publish, so that there will be many views, comments, the rating grows and ponies run across the lawn. For a start, let's see when you can get the most rating. 

SELECT CEIL(AVG(rate)) AS avg_rate, COUNT(id) AS quantity, CEIL(AVG(comments)) AS avg_comments, CEIL(AVG(views)) AS avg_views, WEEKDAY(created)+1 AS DAY, HOUR(created) AS HOUR FROM posts WHERE DATE_FORMAT(created, '%m-%d') NOT IN (SELECT `date` FROM holidays) AND rate > 0 AND comments > 0 GROUP BY WEEKDAY(created), HOUR(created) ORDER BY AVG(rate) DESC

Explanation: we exclude from the sampling holidays and posts that have a rating and the number of comments not more than zero.
