UXCamp 2011 - open-air conference

Hello, dear readers.
We would like to invite you to UXCamp 2011 a wonderful event, which will be held in Kiev on July 30-31. This year, by itself :)



A two-day workshop for UX-specialists and designers of Ukraine in one team with the customer and honored experts of the CIS will allow you to get new skills, see the experience of colleagues and hear the opinion of experts.
The presence of the customer throughout the work guarantees new emotions and the opportunity to show the value of design to project owners.

And this is the first step to the emergence of many useful user-oriented interfaces.


The event will come great experts in the design of user interfaces, including the same:
1. Dmitri Satin. Leader in UsabilityLab .
2. Yuri Vetrov. Head of design and interface design in Mail.Ru.
3. Andrei Sikorsky. Quality Director at UsabilityLab .
4. Alexey Kopylov. Technical director, partner in UIdesign .
5. Sergey Kudryashov from Interface Control.
6. Katerina Umnova. Independent expert.



In 2010, such an event has already taken place and you can watch a report about it directly on the official website .

Register and come to Kiev on July 30-31, it will be fun and practical :)
