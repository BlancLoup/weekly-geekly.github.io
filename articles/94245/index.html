Google AdWords Remarketing

In this review, I want to talk about a new, fairly powerful tool for behavioral targeting in contextual advertising.
Remarketing is a function of interest-based advertising. It allows you to contact users who have already visited your site and display ads that can interest them. Ads can be viewed while browsing sites on the Google Content Network.

Some time ago the ‚ÄúAudiences‚Äù tab appeared in the Google AdWords interface. This tab is the formation of audiences for remarketing campaigns.

Now all visitors to the site are marked with special cookies, which allows them to be identified on all sites of the Google content network and show them their ads.
