As a gift by February 23, Microsoft offers students to pass an exam for free!

You might be surprised - why should a student take another exam when the session has just ended? Probably many of you would not agree to take extra exams even for money!

However, this is a certification exam, which allows you to become Microsoft Certified Professional , which in many cases simplifies the subsequent employment (see the funny American video on this topic ). The usual exam cost is about $ 50, for students $ 30. Only now you (if you are a student) have the opportunity to receive a free voucher to pass any certification exam 072-xxx in the center of Prometric. You can get a voucher according to the DreamSpark program, but only until June 30, or until a certain number of vouchers are spent. Hurry up, click on the banner below and reserve your exam right now!
