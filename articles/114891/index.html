FIXber: first results and some statistics

Hi, Habrazhiteli!

Two weeks passed after the closed launch of the FIXber site (software testing services market).
We received from testers and customers a huge number of feedbacks, most of which were really useful, some were not very good, apparently due to a not deep understanding of what we were doing.

The first testers have already scored an initial rating, having made an excellent start for themselves before the official launch of the site, and the first customers received many problems found in their projects.



The other day we collected some statistics and are ready to share with you real figures - the main activities of testers and customers are under the cut.

I want to express my gratitude to everyone who helped and now helps to make FIXber better.

Now everything is moving as planned, and we are going to launch the platform fully next week - the programmer does not get enough sleep and writes code, at the same time correcting bugs, all day and night.
