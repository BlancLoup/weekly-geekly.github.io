Launch of the first P2P search engine



This is my second experience in creating search engines. The last search engine was based on Last.fm and vkontakte. After I tried my hand, I decided to start something more serious and put my old idea into reality.

Currently searching for P2P network DC ++. If you like my creation, then pretty soon I will make it possible to search on the torrent network.

To explicit supporters of torrent, I can say that the download speed through torrent is not always higher than DC ++, and sometimes the download is faster.




To the question why this search engine is, I will answer:
1) No need to install additional clients
2) When downloading via P2P networks, you have to keep the client constantly turned on, and our server works around the clock.
3) It often happens that it is impossible to technically connect to DC ++ in active mode, which causes many problems.
4) No need to keep track of your ratio. The service takes these problems on itself :)

In general, please try .

PS At the moment, due to technical limitations, the delay before issuing results is approximately 30 seconds.
