Gazprom-Media plans to create a competitor YouTube in Russia, combining Rutube and Pladform

Gazprom-Media Holding will buy 33.3% of Plaedform LLC and merge it with its video service Rutube . Yesterday, the holding filed a petition for a deal with the Federal Antimonopoly Service (FAS). According to a Kommersant source, the company is scheduled to be renamed Ruform LLC (Ruform). Pladform is the largest distribution network of legal content in RuNet, allowing the right holder to receive income from advertising in the player.

The founder and CEO of Pladform Armen Gulinyan will remain the general director of Ruform, and the head of the digital sub-holding of Gazprom-Media Vadim Fedotov will be the chairman of the board of directors. The merged company will operate on the mass market for viewing videos under the Rutube brand, and the Pladform brand will remain for Internet sites and copyright holders.
