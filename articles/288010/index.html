What appeared earlier: content or links?

I, as a fighter against the copy-peysta, I want to share in my opinion useful tips from Lorelle VanFossen .
- There was such a question: will the unique content be sufficient to achieve heights in blogging?

Experience tells us that unique content brings traffic, but I would agree with a few reservations. This refers to the fact that traffic always has a source. If they do not know about your site, then there will be no visitors, respectively.

This is a question of the same level as the question of what came first: an egg or a chicken. I heard that scientists have proven that the first egg ever appeared. Serious SEO experts and the best bloggers know that, from the very beginning, there must be quality content, and then links will appear.

You can promote your site wherever you want: Technorati, Digg and even Slashdot, but until you have valuable reading material for visitors, until your site is filled with high-quality content, all your energy for promotion will be wasted.

Here's what new bloggers write me: ‚ÄúThe traffic to my site is very small. Where to get it? I've been writing my blog for a week now, but nothing happens. What should I do? ‚ÄùPeople, a week is a very short time to create high-quality content. Ask the same question after three, four, six or even twelve months, when you have valuable content on your site.

If you start writing a blog for profit, start with quality content. Try your hand, work on yourself, correct mistakes and learn to just work with a blog.

When content gets bigger, open up to the world: work with links! But keep creating quality material. Comment on other people's blogs, write letters to other bloggers about your topics, asking them to comment on them. Participate in online discussions, chat rooms and be visible on public blogging events. Find friends among bloggers.
