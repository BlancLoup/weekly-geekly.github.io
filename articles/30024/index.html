3D printing via the Internet

Philips has launched a unique startup Shapeways , which will carry out cheap three-dimensional printing via the Internet.

It works simply: you create a 3D model in one of the 3D editors and upload a file to them on the site, then choose what material to print the product (bronze, plaster, plastic, etc.) and what it should be colors. After that, the model is sent to you by mail.
