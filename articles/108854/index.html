Glavstartom on TechCrunch

On December 13, for the first time in Russia, a meeting of TechCrunch Europe will be held at the Red October.



Moscow TechCrunch will be dedicated to the development of the Russian start-ups market and support for investment programs. In addition to short reports and speeches, we are waiting for the leaders of the IT industry and an interesting program.

Within TechCrunch, there will be a Startup Elevator Pitch Battle, in which about a dozen promising Russian projects will take part. Incendiary (as always) to lead the battle of startups will be Arkady Moreinis, chief director of Glavstart.

Glavstart will accept applications from those wishing to submit their project to TechCrunch.

Requirements for participating projects :
  • The presence of a prototype or finished product (not necessarily released on the market).
  • Ability to make a presentation and participate in the discussion in English (preferably, understandable).

Applications are accepted at the mailbox of idea@glavstart.ru. In the subject line of the letter it is necessary to indicate ‚ÄúTechCrunch‚Äù.
Deadline for applications is December 8th.

Those who wish to receive advice and expert advice, pump up and bring their project to mind before a public speech, we invite to the third Moscow Startup Weekend, which will be held on December 3, 4 and 5 .

You can register for the event and buy a ticket here - techcrunch.timepad.ru/event/4011

By the way, participants who submit their projects will not have to buy tickets. :)
