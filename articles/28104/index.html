Do entrepreneurs need education?

Due to the fact that Y-combinator AddVenture and Web2People clones appeared in Russia, the Start in Garage training has been around for a long time, and I have plans to launch the same program in Kiev, I would like to know the ideas of users on this issue.
And questions to which I would like to get answers:
1. How much will such a course be in demand in Russia-Ukraine?
2. Would our start-ups agree to give a minority stake (5-15%) of the company for methodological, practical legal assistance, as well as acquaintance in a narrow circle with investors and successful entrepreneurs?
3. What would entrepreneurs like to receive from this course as a result?
4. What topics should you pay special attention to?

I will be glad to hear the answers in the comments, or in my blog
