Light barrier



A very unusual art installation that uses artificial smoke and a matrix of dozens of round convex mirrors on servo drives to create a three-dimensional, three-dimensional image. A small source of light is directed at each mirror. Reflected rays are focused in different parts of space, creating a glow due to reflection from smoke particles.





Hummingbird Aerodynamics



According to the results of high-speed video (1000 frames / sec), scientists have created a model of created turbulence during the flight of a hummingbird. This bird flaps its wings like an insect, making about 40 strokes per second (!). At the same time, the hummingbird is kept in the air by no means due to the Bernoulli equation, unlike airplanes and other birds. Instead, nectar-eaters with insane speed "sweep" the air forward and down. This experiment confirmed the assumption that about 75% of the hummingbird's lift force is formed while the wings are moving forward, and only the remaining quarter falls on the movements of the wings backwards. However, it still remains a mystery how a hummingbird manages to hang in the air and make three-dimensional evolution in space.
