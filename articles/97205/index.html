AlterGeo geolocation with refreshing bonuses

Habravchane, we suggest you make a difficult, but still a pleasant choice: a cup of invigorating coffee, a glass of refreshing juice or mojito?

Now, a geolocation application for iPhone, Android, as well as the AlterGeo web service will not only find all the establishments nearby and show you where friends are, but also allow you to receive tasty bonuses from the establishments .

Starting today, becoming the mayor of the well-known restaurant chains IL Patio and Planet Sushi, you can get free coffee, juice or non-alcoholic mojito.

How it works? The title of mayor is received by guests of establishments who attend and are marked in it through AlterGeo more often than others.

You will see the information that you have become mayor in AlterGeo on your laptop or phone immediately after you check in. Also information about the "merstvo" is displayed in the profile of the institution.


Show this message to the waiter of the IL Patio restaurant or Planet Sushi, whose mayor you have become, and will gladly treat you to free coffee, juice or non-alcoholic mojito - your choice.

So far, the offer is valid only in Moscow restaurants IL Patio and Planet Sushi owned by Rosinter, but very soon bonuses to mayors will be provided in all Rosinter restaurants, in regions, and also in other establishments in different cities and with new bonuses - stay tuned!
