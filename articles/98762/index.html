Let's start, as usual, with the external charms and finish on the possibilities.

Appearance




The device is black glossy, on the front side there is only an IR receiver for the remote. On the back panel is a row of connectors: here is USB, and HDMI, and Ethernet, and AV, as well as a green on-off button. The device looks very beautiful and, I think, will fit into the design of any interior, because black is a universal color. The only drawback of the case is, of course, that it is glossy and leaves all prints on it.



There is nothing except the rubber feet and the sticker below. This is right, because you will not hang a device on the wall, since it will be inconvenient to manage, but stability must be present.
