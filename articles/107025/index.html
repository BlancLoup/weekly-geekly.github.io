Universal Digital Radio / Podcast Terminal



The company Grace Digital Audio released Wi-Fi Internet radio GDI-IR2550p , which is ideal for the service Pandora . On the case are the same buttons as in the web interface (Play / Pause, vote, rewind).

For users outside the United States, Pandora radio is available through the stump-deck , but the radio can play not only music from Pandora, but any other Internet radio station, of which there are now more than 50,000, as well as podcasts. It can connect to a personal computer and play music from the iTunes collection.

Grace GDI-IR2550p is sold through an online store for $ 169.99 (the site now lies, of course).
