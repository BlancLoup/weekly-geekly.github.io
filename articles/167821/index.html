Impressions of the Enot V134 tablet


After using and reviewing the tablet, the Enot j101 turned its attention to another tablet with quite good Enot V134 specifications . According to the manufacturer, this model is also in the budget price category and the recommended price is $ 169.


Specifications


  • Screen size: 8 inches
  • Resolution: 1024x768px
  • Processor: Allwinner A10 1.2Ghz Cortex A8 ARMv7
  • Video accelerator: Mali400 MP
  • Operating system: Android 4.0.4
  • RAM: 1024MB DDR3
  • Internal memory: 8GB + microSD slot with card support up to 32GB
  • Wireless: Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g / n
  • Camera: Rear - 2MP, Front - 0.3MP
  • Interfaces: Microphone, power connector, Micro USB (HOST), 3.5 jack, MiniHDMI, MicroSD
  • Battery: 4200 mAh 3.7V
  • Case Material: Plastic, glass, rear aluminum cover
  • Dimensions: 198x154x7mm
  • Weight: 450 grams


Packaging


The tablet is packed in a glossy box, with bright green inscriptions in the corporate style Raccoon, the package opens quite easily, the upper part is fixed with the help of side magnets. With the packaging, we can immediately see clearly how the tablet looks, the screen size and the version of Android itself.


Packaging at a high level, all the necessary accessories and adapters are present (unlike miniHDMI-HDMI), I think the manufacturer should pay attention, the kit includes:

  • The tablet
  • Protective film
  • Case
  • microUSB
  • MicroUSB to USB adapter
  • Headphones
  • Charger
  • User's manual
  • Official warranty






Appearance


The default tablet is already in the case, but the tablet itself is in protective packaging, so to work with it you need to get it from the case and at the same time you can see its bare appearance. The case is made of PU (a kind of leather deputy), inside it is sheathed with something resembling velor, pleasant sensations when in contact with it. The appearance of the cover resembles a book, and if you open the cover, the tablet itself feels confident inside and the chances of slipping are equal to zero. Naturally, all the holes for the camera, the speaker and connections to the ports are provided on the case. Also, the tablet can serve as a stand for watching videos, and for this purpose a leather clip is provided on the back.







Without a cover, the tablet looks very attractive, the back of the tablet is made of brushed aluminum, which gives an additional heat sink for the processor. The screen is covered with glass, on the tablet itself there are 3 physical buttons - a volume rocker, a back button, and an on / off button. On the front panel we have a VGA camera, and no touch buttons, which means that the tablet was originally sharpened for Android 4. On the back of the Enot V134 we have a 2.0mp camera and a speaker.







At the bottom of the tablet are the connection ports: microSD (up to 32GB), microphone, DC charging jack, miniHDMI, microUSB (host), 3.5 jack.



Camera


In the tablet there are 2 cameras, front and rear. The cameras will not be able to boast outstanding pictures, the front camera does an excellent job with video calls provided there is good lighting. The rear camera is designed for better pictures and videos, but again, provided a good consecration, the resolution of the pictures is 2592x1944px.

Sound


On the back of the Enot v134 tablet is a speaker, the sound is loud enough, but there is no bass. There is enough dynamics for watching video clips, movies, but for those who like to listen to high-quality audio compositions I recommend using headphones.

Autonomy


The tablet is equipped with a polymer lithium-ion battery, 4200 mAh 3.7V. According to the manufacturer, the tablet operating time in non-stop video viewing is up to 6 hours, according to personal feelings it is less, but in a sparing mode, you can safely and up to 8-9 hours without recharging. Charging time is certainly not the fastest, up to 3 hours from the power supply, but charging from the USB cable is much faster.

In conclusion, I can emphasize that the declared price is fully justified. Fully equipped, including an adapter and a cover, a protective film on the screen. Ergonomic and easy in the hands, a guide to use in Russian, official technical support , warranty and maintenance on the territory of Ukraine.
