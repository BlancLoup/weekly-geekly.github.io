Nokia N96 - the first official information

Nokia has lifted the veil of mystery regarding the new smartphone codenamed N96.





The new device in the slider form factor will have 16 gigabytes of internal memory, a 2.8 "screen with a resolution of 320x240, built-in GPS and WiFi modules, mobile video support (DVB-H), as well as a 5 megapixel camera with the world-famous Carl Zeiss optics and support for shooting VGA-video up to 30 frames / sec.
Of course, all this will work under Symbian OS (Symbian S60 3rd Edition with Feature Pack 2).
