What is the actual problem?



Another cat Matroskin spoke about the need for the correct spatial orientation of objects, explaining to Uncle Fyodor the technique of using a sausage sandwich. As it turns out, methods of handling the newest Apple smartphone also significantly affect its ability to communicate with the base station. The first owners of the iPhone 4 discovered that under certain conditions, the signal level drops to zero, accusing the manufacturer of defects associated with the antenna of the smartphone.

Apple on the second day after the start of official sales of iPhone 4 made a statement on this issue, along the way explaining to users how not to keep a smartphone while working on the network. The fact is that the side metal panels of the iPhone 4 case are part of two antennas oriented to work in Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS and GSM / 3G networks, respectively. By closing the lower left corner of the iPhone 4 with your hand, the user may obstruct the operation of the antenna.

...
According to independent experts, for the first day of the start of sales, Apple managed to sell about one and a half million iPhone 4. Users complain about other shortcomings of the smartphone. If you do not take into account the easily scratched glass panels of the case and their fragility, then you can think of yellow spots or stripes on the screen of some specimens. The defect disappears on its own after several days of active use, it is associated with the costs of manufacturing the screens. Another defect of the screen is expressed in the presence of a group of white dots resembling a constellation. Methods for its elimination in the home is not developed.

Mythical news:
The problem with the reception of the signal in the iPhone 4, about which they wrote a lot there, seems to be resolved in the coming days. According to some sources, Apple will release an iOS 4.0.1 update already on Monday, June 28th.

Recall that the problem with the reception of the signal is felt on the iPhone 4 if you touch the bottom left of the smartphone. However, this problem is software, not hardware as previously assumed (one western resource even offered its solution for $ 0.001). In particular, the same problem appears in the iPhone 3GS after an update on iOS 4. Just a few days later, the well-known observer Walter Mossberg reported that the software problem will be solved soon.
