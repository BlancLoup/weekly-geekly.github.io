Recruitment to the Developers School of 3D solutions: free places


Frame from the final stage of mounting a 3D cube to visualize engineering solutions

Now in Russia, 3D modeling is used in the oil sector, nuclear energy, engineering, shipbuilding, aircraft manufacturing. Companies in these segments acquire special high-resolution 3D systems for operation. Designing in 3D makes it easier and easier to imagine the work of complex systems and their structure in space.

Relevant specialists are very necessary in Russia, so we are opening the 3D School. The most important thing: the cost of training - $ 12.000 . At the same time there are 15 free places. Training will be held in English. Students of the 3D school will be among the first in Russia to introduce 3D solutions and will have access to the expert environment and practice in a leading IT company. Details below.

How many places in the school?


There are 15 free places in the competition and paid places for $ 12.000 for the course. There are already very few vacant paid places, a number of large domestic companies have already sent specialists to us for training.

How will the training go?


The course begins in October and lasts for 9 months, of which in total 3 months of theory and 6 months of practice. The tasks will be real: modeling of industrial facilities (for example, oil rigs), buildings, their engineering systems and interiors, complex high-tech devices (for example, different types of engines), biosystems (for example, the structure of the human body) and so on. And all this with the smallest detail and the most realistic animation.
