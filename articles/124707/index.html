About GeeksFreaks:
Pilot Developer Garage, dedicated to the development of Android, was held on June 28, 2011 in Nizhny Novgorod and gathered about 150 mobile application development experts for various platforms. In the near future - holding similar thematic conferences in various regions of Russia.

One of the tasks that the GeeksFreaks team sees for itself is to form a community of mobile application developers. To this end, the company organizes large-scale events that gather about 500-1000 people, where developers share experiences in an informal setting, get to know each other and the trends in the mobile industry market:

- Mobilefest - 2009 - festival of mobile developers (Nizhny Novgorod, September 2009)
- Mobile Developer Day 2010 - a major conference of mobile developers, held in Moscow (December 2010)
- Winter Mobilefest - 2010 (Moscow region, Volen Park, March 2010)
- MDDAY # MoCo - conference within the framework of the Mobile World Content Business Forum (Moscow, June 2011)
