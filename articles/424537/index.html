Sberbank launched its own operator SberMobile

On September 26, after testing, Sberbank launched its own virtual operator SberMobile (the previous name is ‚ÄúLet's talk from Sberbank‚Äù), which works on Tele2 networks. At the moment, the operator‚Äôs services can be used only by residents of Moscow, St. Petersburg and their regions; by the end of 2018, plans to add 13 more regions: Tula, Tver, Bryansk, Ryazan, Kaluga, Smolensk, Kostroma, Voronezh, Belgorod, Lipetsk, Kursk , Tambov and Oryol regions.



The name for the new operator seems to have been chosen quite recently, since the domain sbermobile.ru was registered only 8 days ago.



Tariffs at SberMobile start at 200 rubles.

The operator does not offer fixed tariffs, the subscriber has the opportunity to collect the tariff for his needs. For a minimum amount of 200 rubles, the user receives 150 outgoing minutes throughout Russia, 3 GB of Internet traffic and 50 SMS messages. The most expensive tariff for 1150 rubles includes 1500 minutes, 30 GB of traffic and 50 SMS.



50 SMS messages are included in any tariff, and, as far as I understand, this number cannot be changed. Of the options that do not consume the Internet, the following are available:

  • Messengers - 49 rubles or free of charge at rates costing from 300 rubles.
  • Social networks - 69 rubles or free of charge on tariffs costing from 400 rubles.
  • Music - 99 rubles or free of charge at rates costing from 500 rubles.
  • Video - 199 rubles or for free on the tariffs cost from 800 rubles. (however, this option can be connected only by selecting a traffic volume of 15, 20 or 30 GB)

In fact, the spread in tariffs is not very large, and it is difficult to call such a possibility to select a tariff as a calculator.
Minutes can be selected from the following values: 150, 300, 600, 1500
The amount of traffic can be selected from the following values: 3, 7, 15, 20, 30 GB
