IMHO VI is betting on video advertising with Tvigle

The largest Internet advertising reseller in Russia, IMHO VI, has signed a contract with Tvigle Media for the realization of the advertising opportunities of the site Tvigle.ru.

Tvigle.ru is a major entertainment video portal with absolutely legal and high-quality content. On the day, the site is visited by 100 thousand unique users who view more than 6 million videos per month. The collaboration between Tvigle Media and IMHO is logical, since the agency does not sell advertisements in unlicensed or pirated videos.

Demand for video advertising in RuNet is growing rapidly. Thus, according to experts, this year this segment of the market will grow by 80% compared to the past and will amount to about $ 5 million. The largest advertisers have already become interested in this trend. For example, Tvigle Media conducts advertising campaigns for clients such as: Unilever, Nokia, Sony Ericsson, Philip Morris, Red Bull, BeeLine and other major brands.
