Instead of conclusion


Actually, what was all this about. Here are just a few conclusions and tips:
  1. Lead the development from the idea, not from the implementation.
  2. The application should not weigh hundreds of megabytes and have awesome graphics to get to the top of the App Store.
  3. Users are often much better than we think about them.
  4. Do not be afraid of minimalism. Read Rework , it is correctly written about functionality.
  5. Do not be afraid to leave something for future versions. Candy is always more important than wrappers. There will be sweets - we will make a wrapper.
  6. Good still sometimes triumph over evil. So the idea can still beat marketing. Even when it comes to Need For Speed!
  7. A free application may well occupy top positions and have an honest rating of more than 4.5 stars.
