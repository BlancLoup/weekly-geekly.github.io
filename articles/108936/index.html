Unusual laptop with two touch screens: Acer Iconia



Not so long ago, a laptop from Toshiba , similar to this one, was already demonstrated on Habr√©, and then quite a few negative reviews about the device from those who tried it in their work appeared in the comments. Perhaps a similar model of a laptop without a keyboard and with two touchscreens will be more convenient than its predecessor. True, so far it is only a working concept, which is not yet on store shelves. More information about Acer Iconia - in continuation.

So, this model has two touch-sensitive displays, both with a diagonal of 14 inches and a resolution of 1366 * 768 pixels. Displays are covered with Gorilla Glass, which is famous for its durability. It is worth noting that the device displays support multitouch in the amount of up to 10 points.

The working concept of the device was demonstrated at the next Acer press conference, where new company products were highlighted. Without a doubt, this laptop was the main novelty of the press conference. As with similar models, during operation, you can use one of the displays as a keyboard. The device configuration is quite interesting: the entire platform is based on Intel Core i5-480M / i5-560M / i5-580M (2.66 GHz), the laptop is equipped with 4 gigabytes of RAM, integrated Intel HD graphics, internal memory of up to 750 gigabytes, wireless modules Wi-Fi connectivity 802.11b / g / n, Bluetooth 3.0 and even 3G, plus there is a web browser. The device has a large variety of ports, USB 3.0, VGA, HDMI, RJ45, S / PDIF, 2xUSB 2.0.

As a result, we get a fairly powerful device that allows you to work and play - the laptop is truly universal. Interestingly, in contrast to similar models of other companies, this laptop, according to the journalists who tested it in its work, is quite convenient. Unfortunately, the release date of the laptop with two displays on the market is not yet known, but it is known that it will cost no less than 2 thousand US dollars.
