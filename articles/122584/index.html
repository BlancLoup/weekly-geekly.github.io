Vacancy and project today (June 19-24): the project Pravo.ru needs a PHP developer (Samara).

1.) Pravo.ru


Reference and legal system Pravo.ru - a new word in the Russian segment of legal services. ATP is a single aggregator of regulatory documents. Users have access to federal and regional legislative acts in all editions, resolutions and decisions of all arbitration courts. Access to the ATP Pravo.ru is provided around the clock via the Internet, which means at work, in the car or at home - all the necessary information is always at hand. For the first time, you can communicate with colleagues without interrupting your work with regulatory documents. ATP Pravo.ru gives you the opportunity to add to your friends, sharing documents and comments, without leaving the system.
