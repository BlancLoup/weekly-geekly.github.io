Is it safe?



In general, yes, but there are, of course, some peculiarities.

For example, large online stores value their reputation, so the risk of getting a marriage from there is almost zero.

With eBay, the situation is more complicated. At an online auction there is always a certain risk of running into a scammer who, instead of an iPhone, will send him a battered version or three rolls of toilet paper. However, such cases are quite rare, and in order not to become a victim of fraud, it is enough to follow simple rules of caution.

Mail can fail - it happens that the brave workers of this deliver the parcel for too long. However, recently the situation and with this almost settled.

When making large purchases more expensive than 10 000 rubles or buying something suspicious, various unpleasant questions may arise from Russian customs. But if you correctly and honestly filled out the customs declaration and do not send 10 kilograms of cocaine in a package, then these difficulties are most often resolved after a visit to the customs terminal.

So, although some difficulties sometimes happen, but for the most part they are completely solvable, and the process of shopping in the US using EbayToday is quite simple, pleasant and useful for you and your wallet. We think that more than 50,000 successful purchases of our customers are a clear confirmation of this.
