ASUS N750JV Multimedia Notebook Review

Index N says that this device belongs to a series of multimedia laptops with top configurations, high-quality sound system and stylish design. The number 7 in the title means that we have a laptop with a 17-inch screen. As can be seen from the size, this device is a desktop replacement class. Let's take a closer look at this entertaining laptop.

Equipment


The box in which the laptop comes in is surprising in its size. After tablets and ultrabooks such dimensions seem to be too large, although even before all laptops were in bulky packaging.


The box is framed in the usual ASUS style: matte printing with a laptop image.


Almost the entire area inside is occupied by the laptop itself, laid in a cloth envelope.




On the right is a small niche for the charger and a remote subwoofer.


The charger with a matte finish and glossy ends has impressive dimensions. A voltage of 19V and a current of 6.32A are as much as 120 W!


Appearance


Unlike last year's model, the design of the laptop has changed. ASUS N750JV has an impressive size and weight of 3.2 kg. The design of the notebook alternates in 2 colors: the cover and the bottom of a dark brown color, and the case itself is silver. The design of the body is completely metal.

The lid is made of brushed aluminum, and although some of the fingerprints remain on it, they are not so noticeable and are erased quite simply. The center has an engraved ASUS logo, highlighted from the inside.




The bottom of the notebook is a solid metal part.


In the middle is the ventilation grille, in the front part there is a perforation for the speakers.


Another ventilation grate is located between the casing and the screen.


The mounting design is simple: just unscrew the screws around the perimeter and get access to the insides of the laptop.
The thickness of the case at the edges is somewhat smaller, which makes it convenient to take the laptop from the table and visually makes the laptop thinner. The ribs around the perimeter of the body are smoothed with a small bevel.


The screen is mounted using one large hinge and has a opening angle of 120 degrees. The hinge has a tight turn, which allows you to lock the screen in any position. In the closed position, the laptop lid is held on magnets. Engineers have perfectly calculated the effort, and the laptop lid is quietly opened with one hand, without the need to hold the case.

Interfaces


The main connectors are on the left end of the laptop. The first is the power connector; then a gigabit RJ45 for connecting to the network, it has a flip cover, which allowed to reduce the size of the connector. Next come HDMI and miniDP connectors for connecting a monitor and a TV, two USB 3.0 with support for charging in the off state, and an SDXC card reader (SD / MMC / MS / MS Pro).


On the front side there are LED indicators of beautiful white color.


The right side is occupied by the Kensington lock; 2.5 mm output for the included subwoofer; as well as the Blu-Ray combo drive, which is just useful for watching movies.


Then there are two more USB 3.0 connectors, a microphone input and a headphone jack with S / PDIF support.

It implements the technology developed by ASUS experts SonicMaster Premium, representing a set of hardware and software tools to improve sound quality. A special "highlight" is an external subwoofer, which is used for high-quality transmission of low frequencies.

Input Devices


Before us is the usual island full-size keyboard with a separate digital section. The keys are plastic, exactly the same "aluminum" color as the case itself.


The keyboard has a standard set of keys. On the one hand, such minimalism and simplicity pleases the eye, on the other - there are users who like various additional keys, such as adjusting the sound and controlling the media player.


In general, the keyboard is comfortable, thanks to the large backing under the wrists, it is convenient to type. The movement of the keys, equipped with backlight, small, with tactile feedback.


All the characters on the keys are laser engraved and glow in the dark.


The backlight can be turned off, there are three levels of brightness.


Below the keyboard is an impressively sized touchpad. The panel has exactly the same color as the case, and fits perfectly into the overall design. The dimensions of the touchpad are 10.5 by 7.5 cm. It has a rough, pleasant to the touch surface.

Battery and working time


Capacity of the built-in battery is 6260 mAh. Full charge takes 2.5 hours. Due to the compact design of the laptop from the removable battery had to be abandoned.
When viewing FullHD-video with an average brightness, the laptop lasted 4 hours.
With a minimum screen brightness and surfing the Internet, the operation time was 6 hours.
If you turn off the network, set the minimum brightness, turn on the energy-saving profile and limit your work to office applications, you can reach 7 hours.
