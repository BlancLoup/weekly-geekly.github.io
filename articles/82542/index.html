Monopoly online



Already a month I can not tear myself away from the online implementation of the economic game Monopoly. It has all the same as in the classical monopoly, with some differences. This is the presence of Russian brands, which is a pleasure to take possession of, for example, Sberbank or Lukoil :) In this game, like in any other online, there are commercial services, but you can do fine without it. A credit system was introduced into the game, which allows you to use a loan if there is a shortage of funds (for real money, of course).

Of the advantages of the online version, I would like to point out the moment of closure of transactions, i.e. Negotiations can be conducted both openly and privately through an internal message system, which allows you to develop your strategy for acquiring real estate, because when exchanging companies, the possibility of private negotiations is necessary in order not to disclose their cards to all participants.

For gambling people have the opportunity to play for real money.
