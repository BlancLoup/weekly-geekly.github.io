Discogs - put your collection in order

I have long wanted to clean up my music collection, namely, to get rid of unknown artists, wrong tags, and other nonsense. As a player I use foobar2000 , we will check with the base Discogs.com

The first thing you need is a plugin to interact with the database discogs.com foo_discogs

Open the tracks of interest (in my case, this album is Live & Rare). Select them from the menu (right mouse button) discogs -> write tags



After that the plugin window will appear:



After selecting the artist and the album, click next and check the names of the tracks and the contents of the albums.



here you can also change the genre and style of the album, click on write tags and after a short wait we get the decorated tracks. But it remains to find the "cover". To do this, select any track, select the menu discogs -> update album \ album image
