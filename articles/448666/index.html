1. Minimum rates

5. Mobile solution


It seems that not the most popular feature among the services for the exchange of electronic documents. We found out only that there is an application on iOS and Android in Contour. Plus Synerdocs offers mobile Viber and SMS solutions for signing documents, and VLSI - for notification of new documents. For other services there is no information.



6. Exchange in roaming


Finally, talk about the most interesting. As in the mobile communication, in the electronic document flow there are difficulties with the interaction of subscribers in roaming. Only it is not about services outside the service area. In the case of document flow, roaming implies the possibility of exchanging documents between customers of different operators.


Again, there are more than 30 services in Russia. And not everyone has a roaming connection configured with each other. You can see who the EDO operator already has, here .


On the one hand, almost all operators are connected to roaming centers - platforms that provide secure exchange of invoices between customers of different services. On the other hand, the readiness of the operators themselves to set up roaming is different for everyone. Judging by the experience of our partners, the process is delayed in the case of Kontur.Dyadok and Taxcom / 1C: EDI, faster - VLSI and Synerdocs. For other operators there are no reviews.
