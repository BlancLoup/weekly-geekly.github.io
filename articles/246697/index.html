AWS and edX offer $ 1000 for completing online course

AWS and edX offer $ 1000 to any person who has completed one of the entrepreneurship courses 15.390.1x Entrepreneurship 101: Who is your customer? or 15.390.2x Entrepreneurship 102: What can you do for your customers? . True, not in cash (by check, gift card, etc.), but by Amazon Web Services for that amount.

Partnership page with information and links to enrollment: www.edx.org/AWS-activate

What do you get
  • $ 1000 loan in AWS, valid for 2 years
  • Free access to AWS Essentials: one-day web-training with an instructor (usually course price ~ $ 600), and 8 tokens per lab (regular price ~ $ 30 per lab)
  • Month premium support
  • Free access to individual consulting with solution architects
  • Special offers from other companies


Subjects Offer What are the conditions?
  • It is necessary to enroll in the course Entrepreneurship 101 or Entrepreneurship 102 (probably better to do it on the page, the link to which is higher). To receive the award, you must obtain a paid certificate of completion. The minimum payment is $ 50, but you can pay more, and the money goes to the development of the course and platform.
  • Get passing score
  • You can only get one reward per person.
  • The award is not transmitted, i.e. she is tied to a specific person


How to get a reward?
  • When you complete the course, you will receive an email with an AWS code (you must have Verified Student status)
  • Step 1: Create an AWS account using code
  • Step 2: Fill out the form that edX will send you using your email address (which is registered with edX), your AWS account name and code. After about 3 weeks, AWS will confirm your participation and provide access to services and credit.


Good luck!
