Cost and delivery



Packaging


It is packed in an orange box, without any inscriptions (some sellers send in black boxes with the bright inscription DROPAD)


Being quite tough, it nevertheless came a little wrinkled from the sides, but it saved the tablet from our mail.

The packaging, in fact, is quite ordinary - a cradle of foamed plastic, cardboard partitions, and wire bags.

There is a small problem in the package - due to a slightly ill-conceived construction, the power button was pressed against the wall and pressed during the journey. Actually, the tablet comes seriously discharged, and there were problems with it - either the battery does not hold at all, or the input transistor breaks through the input with a large current. It would be good to check with the seller whether a piece of foam will be cut so that the button is not pressed.

It goes well, but the box looks ascetic and crumples. No longer donate.

The box contains: the tablet itself, charging (with a carefully placed adapter under our outlet), headphones, a cable for connecting to a computer, an adapter cable for usb-host, and instructions in Chinese.

Here it is, our handsome. It is assembled with high quality, nothing crunches inside, does not falter and does not hang out. Structurally, it is assembled in such a way that it simply cannot creak. Rubber covers of additional ports do not inspire confidence - they do not provide special protection, but they look frankly flimsy - stagger, are very easily removed, but at the same time they complicate access to the connector and card (and the reset button, which is no secret)

Working hours


I understand that each person has his own tasks, and the tablet will work at a completely different time.
After a week of use with this style of work:
It charges all night, in the morning I take off the charge, I download articles to the RSS reader via Wifi, so I switch it off and use it all day. Three or four hours of reading a book, about an hour of total navigation / map viewing, and an hour of games.
Total about 6 hours of work (with the screen on), and 4-5 hours of waiting. Coming home, I finish off the rest of the charge with the Internet, and put it on charge.

If used only as a reader, the work time will increase to 9-10 hours.
Work time on: 4
Solid four. Of course, it would be good to have a work time of 24 hours, but the main thing for me is enough for a day. Arranges.
