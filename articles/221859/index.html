In February of this year, on the basis of the training center (TC) of the MUK group of companies (Kiev), a unique seminar ‚ÄúTechnologies and Storage Systems for SMB Customers‚Äù developed at TC MUK was held for the first time. Specialists in the field of pre-sales training (presale), working with customers from the category of small and medium business in data storage systems (DSS). Since the market offers solutions from several key storage vendors at once, the knowledge and practical skills acquired by the listeners were not tied to a specific manufacturing company.

On the contrary, for the participants a comparative analysis of relevant product families from different companies was carried out, and already on its basis solutions that were optimal for customers were formed according to the stated requirements. The workshop is held in two modules, two days each. With a goal of minimal impact on the schedule of the participants, it was found that the second day of each module is held on Saturdays.
