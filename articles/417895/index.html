Repair of 3D printers and machines: how the Top 3D Shop service works in Moscow and St. Petersburg



3D printing and manufacturing products on CNC machines have become an integral part of life, they are used by everyone: home-made craftsmen in home workshops, small business creating custom-made things, development engineers to create prototypes and parts of new devices and many others. The number of daily working 3D-printers and machines is constantly growing. The same can be said about robotics, equipment for three-dimensional scanning and vacuum casting.



Like any technically sophisticated equipment, all these devices need maintenance, repair and upgrade. In this article, we describe how the service of the service of Russia's largest 3D printing and engineering company Top 3D Shop works.


How does this happen


For maintenance, the client contacts his problem with technical support, by mail at supportmsk@top3dshop.ru or by phone 8 (499) 322-23-19. In the course of communication, information about the printer is requested, as well as a description, photo and video of the malfunction - to provide primary advice on its elimination. Often it is possible to troubleshoot even during the consultation. This is convenient - the client does not need to carry his equipment anywhere.

If the equipment still needs qualified repair, it is possible to order the necessary spare part from a remote warehouse in advance, find out the approximate timing of diagnostics and repair.

Upon receipt of the equipment by the service center, an acceptance certificate is issued, which will need to be presented upon receipt.



Diagnosis is carried out within three days after receipt, in some cases a little longer. After the diagnosis, if the repair is warranty, it starts immediately.



If the guarantee is over, or the equipment was purchased not from us, which is also often the case, the cost (in accordance with the price list ) and the terms of the repair are agreed with the client. If the client is satisfied with everything, the service engineer proceeds to repair. After the repair is completed, a test print is carried out and a statement of the repair work completed.



What does service do besides repair


In addition to repairs, the Top 3D Shop service also deals with equipment diagnostics, modification of printers and CNC machines, as well as pre-sales preparation and installation of equipment. All of this also applies to robotics, 3D scanners and ancillary equipment, such as molders, ultrasonic washers, high-temperature furnaces and vibrating tables, injection molding furnaces and injection molding machines.



NDP (commissioning)


The service of the Top 3D Shop carries out the commissioning of 3D printers, CNC machines - laser and milling, as well as other equipment supplied by the company.



Our engineers go to the buyer in order to install the purchased equipment, test its work and train the client in handling it.



This service is provided to both individuals and organizations.



For example, we recently made a selection of equipment, pre-sale preparation, installation and instruction of client personnel at a factory engaged in the production of electronics for river and sea vessels.

What kind of breakdown you have to face most often




The most common problems associated with the print head: plastic jams in the thermal barrier, clogged nozzles, fusing plastic on the print head, insufficient pinch gear, loosening gear on the motor shaft, temperature sensor malfunctions and so on.



Next come the problems with the belts, the calibration of the table, with the sensors of the end of movement and electronics.



As for photopolymer printers, their most frequent problems are related to calibration and resin spillage into the printer body.



What problems may arise during the repair and how they are solved


During the repair, problems may arise with nodes that the customer did not indicate during the printer registration for repair. For example, they can bring a printer with a completely disassembled print head, or any other failed node, and after its restoration, a malfunction will be revealed with anything else.

Before performing repair work, the client is warned that such a situation is possible.



Do I need to improve my equipment


New printers and machines usually do not require any modifications and work fine out of the box, or after a small initial setup. People who purchase such equipment want to get results quickly, without wasting time studying the technical part.



RepRap and DIY kits are usually modified, the quality of the components in which often leaves much to be desired, and the assembly by an inexperienced user can lead to malfunctions.

The modification allows you to replace or supplement some nodes to improve the operation of the device. For 3D printers, for example - add a heated table, blow up models, a closed chamber, replace sensors, guides, strengthen the frame, etc., on machines - replace a spindle or laser tube with more powerful ones, add a cooler or compressor more powerful, install optional knots.

Sometimes the manufacturer produces additional kits for modification. Such kits allow you to optimize equipment for specific tasks.


The simplest and most difficult cases of repair in our practice


The simplest thing that happens is fitting and switching loose or incorrectly connected contacts, replacing components and calibrating the printer.

Sometimes, after the elimination of such problems, the problems appear more complicated.
The complex can be attributed to work with printers of unknown origin.

Non-standard design solutions, lack of documentation and the use of unknown spare parts make the maintenance of such a printer an interesting but long and laborious quest. Often, it is necessary to refine the design, functionality and firmware of the printer so that all nodes work correctly.


Interesting and informative cases


Any complex repair is interesting in terms of experience. Repair of printers of unknown origin and making improvements to the design of printers - just from this series.



How do Top 3D Shop maintain quality of service?


Any equipment, upon completion of work on it in our service, is calibrated and test printed. During work, all actions are recorded, which are then recorded in the act of completed work. There is a photo report - before and after the repair work. All work and deadlines are negotiated; if something changes, the new information will be consistent with the client.

Advice to owners of 3D printers and CNC machines from our engineers




Like any complex technical device, 3D printers and CNC machines require careful handling and timely maintenance. It is necessary to monitor the cleanliness of the surface of the working platform, the print head and moving parts, to lubricate and clean in a timely manner. Observe the rules of operation and safety.

Novice users must read the instructions that are attached to the equipment in paper or electronic form or taken on the manufacturer's website. The manual contains most of the information that will help make the first confident steps in the work.

If you did not find the information you need, contact the manufacturer directly, and in case of a breakdown contact the service center, then your printer will work long and productively.

Top 3D Shop service is always happy to answer your questions about the repair and maintenance of CNC machines and 3D printers - write to us at supportmsk@top3dshop.ru or call the phone numbers listed on the site .
