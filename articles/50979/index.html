First release of StartupIndex Review



The first issue of StartupIndex Review was released - a quarterly collection of topical innovative projects in the IT and media industry, addressed to the professionals of the Russian venture capital market.

StartupIndex Review includes descriptions of the most relevant and promising startups that have passed StarupIndex expertise. In addition, each issue contains an analytical review of key market segments and interviews with one of the well-known authoritative experts in this field.

The total circulation of the publication is 1000 eczemplars. StartupIndex Review is distributed through targeted mailing to representatives of industry and portfolio investors, media holdings, venture capital funds, incubators, as well as authors and managers of successful projects in the field of IT and New Media.

How can a startup get into StartupIndex Review?

In order for the project description to fall into a regular release, its authors need to register on the website startupindex.ru and place their project in the catalog. If the project, according to analysts of StartupIndex, has good prospects for development, an expert council is formed according to it and an investment attractiveness rating is assigned. StartupIndex Review includes projects rated BBB and above.
