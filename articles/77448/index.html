More about publishing components

Last week we announced the imminent launch of the project I'm Sync . Today we will talk about what information you can upload to your site using the components of the publication, as well as show how it will look.

Event poster


With this component you can upload event information to your site.



You can mark events on the map, upload photos and videos to them.



This component can be useful for sites of organizations, communities, personal sites and just blogs.

Photo


With the following component you can upload photos to your site.



The user can conveniently view your photos.



You can upload and edit photos right on your site.

Video


The video publishing component will allow you to work with video in the same way as with photos.



Contacts


And finally, the last component, with its help, you will be able to mark the places of your company on the map and indicate their contacts.



This component is ideal for publishing coordinates and contacts of the company's offices on your site.

After you have added the publication component to the site, you will be able to edit and add new information using a convenient interface.

We hope that the components of the publication I'm Sync will make your site more convenient and functional. We also remind you that through Twitter you can receive news about the project and participate in its discussion in real time.
