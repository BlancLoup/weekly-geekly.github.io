Christmas gifts from 3CX

Before the advent of Christmas and New Year, 3CX offers great gifts to its partners. In monetary terms, the amount of gifts to active partners is more than 1000 Euros!
Firstly, any partner who sells at least one perpetual license for 3CX Phone System during November-December of this year receives for free:

  • The license for hosting on 4SC Standard Edition for 12 months for resale. EUR 175 equivalent
  • Unlimited Voice Application Designer license. Equivalent to 875 Euro.

Recall that the Voice Application Designer is a 3CX PBX visual programming environment that allows a partner with minimal VoIP experience to create powerful voice applications, for example, for the customer voice self-service portal. The partner can then deploy these applications remotely at the client‚Äôs site or on the virtual PBX server.

Note! In order to receive these gifts, you must complete the sale before 11:00 AM 24.12.15 CET (on the eve of the Catholic Christmas). All licenses sold must be credited to your affiliate account before this time.

Secondly, as you already know, you can now offer a virtual PBX 3CX Phone System hosting service to your customers. By itself, this opportunity is a great way to enter the new market of virtual PBX with minimal cost!

But we give even more! To make it easier for you to demonstrate the capabilities of the hosting PBX to customers, you are allocated a new NFR license (in addition to the existing NFR license corresponding to the partner status). This NFR license will allow you to host the 3CX 4SC Pro Edition system on 3CX hosting (not to be confused with your own hosting server, which not everyone has). Now you can use your old NFR key for your own needs, and demonstrate the cloud version to the client. Or vice versa.

Go to your 3CX Affiliate Panel to activate these licenses.

Unlike competing hosting platforms, the 3CX Hosted PBX solution focuses exclusively on the partner. 3CX guarantees retention of the client, because his registration data and settings always belong to the partner. Each virtual entity of PBX is fully virtualized and separated from the others. Thanks to this, 3CX can guarantee high reliability of operation of each virtual PBX - in case of failure of one entity, the problem will never touch other clients, as it happens in competitors' solutions.

Additional information about your NFR license:

  • Virtual PBX 3CX, activated by NFR-key does not stop working after the first month of use.
  • The NFR key is received by all Silver Partner level partners.
  • All affiliate level partners receive an NFR key for a period of 12 months. After that, the keys are deactivated if the partner does not receive the Silver Partner status.
  • NFR-key designed for 3CX Phone System v14
  • Trial versions cannot be updated with this NFR key.
