Sale of tablets from Barnes & Noble - up to 44% discount

The American company Barnes & Noble , specializing in the sale of books and digital content, organized a real holiday of prices for its tablet devices of the NOOK HD series.



NOOK HD + (16 Gb) is now sold at a price of 149 dollars (it was - 269 dollars, a discount of 44%)

NOOK HD (8 Gb) is now sold at a price of $ 129 (it was - $ 199, a discount of 35%)

Rumors that Barnes & Noble wants to close its direction of electronic devices have been going for a long time and, apparently, such extremely low prices are clear evidence of this.

In any case, the price of a 9-inch tablet NOOK HD + with very good technical characteristics becomes almost on par with similar tablets of most Chinese manufacturers.
The only negative is the need to purchase through forwarding services such as ship.to and others (tablets are sold only in the USA).
