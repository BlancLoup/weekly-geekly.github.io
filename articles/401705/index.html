Mighty babe - TrueRMS multimeter Aneng AN8001

A few years ago, it was impossible to imagine that a TrueRMS multimeter with automatic range switching, capable of measuring direct and alternating voltage, direct and alternating current, resistance, capacitance and frequency with 6000 readings and 0.5% accuracy may cost less than $ 15. Today this device is in my hand.


After my exact multimeter stopped being accurate due to some kind of malfunction, I was about to buy the same one again, but I found a small Aneng AN8001 instrument made on the same chip and promised to be just as accurate.

Measured parameters, ranges and accuracy:

Resistance: 600 (0.5% + 3), 6K / 60K / 600K / 6M (0.5% + 2), 60M (1.5% + 3);
DC voltage: 6V / 60V / 600V / 1000V (0.5% + 3), 600mV (1.0% + 10);
DC current 600uA / 6000uA, 60mA / 600mA, 10A (1.5% + 3);
AC voltage: 6V / 60V / 600V / 750V (1.0% + 3), 600mV (3.0% + 3);
AC current 600uA / 6000uA, 60mA / 600mA, 10A (1.5% + 3);
Frequency: 5/50/500 / 5k / 50k / 500k / 5M / 10M (0.1 + 3)
Capacitance: 10nF (5% + 20), 100nF / 1uF / 10uF / 100uF (2% + 5), 1000uF / 10000uF (5% + 5);
Size and weight: 130 * 65 * 30mm, 200 g;
Power supply: 2xAAA.

In fact, the multimeter is much more accurate than stated.

I checked the measurement of constant voltage using a reference voltage source on the AD584 .


In the first column the value of the reference voltage, in the second - the readings of the multimeter.

9.99724 9.99 (0.07% discrepancy)
7.49970 7.49 (discrepancy 0.13%)
4.99729 4.996 (0.03% discrepancy)
2,49909 2,498 (0.04% discrepancy)

AC voltage at the output of the stabilizer Calm .


Accuracy is about 0.1 V (a device tested with the help of a standard showed exactly 1 volt less than the real voltage, the discrepancy between that device and this is exactly 1 volt).

Compared with the Holdpeak HP890CN, built on the same chip, the Aneng AN8001 is very small.


Unlike large devices, the small multimeter does not have a rubber case for the case, but there is a stand.


Included probes, instructions and a bag with a fairly strong chemical smell.


Probes are equipped with caps that cover almost the entire tip of the probe.


If you press Hold for a long time, the backlight turns on for 12 seconds.


There is a sound dial, which beeps for another 0.5 seconds after opening the contacts. The device is very fast - 3 measurements per second. After 15 minutes, auto-off is triggered (first warns with a single peep, and then turns off after a minute if you do not press the button).
