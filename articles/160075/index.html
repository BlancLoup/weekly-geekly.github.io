Amateur video camera

It was always interesting how they make movies, whether it is possible without a large budget to get a high-quality picture using amateur equipment and amateur skills.
I'll tell you how I shot a video for the contest and what I used for it.
It was necessary to remove a parody of any film, lasting 60 seconds. Chose "Leon".
There is no experience in such filming, just a couple of times I shot reports.
After I came up with a small story, I began to make a storyboard, I do not know how to draw, but for myself, in principle, I understand.




It was necessary to be limited to a minimum of actors and a minimum of scenery, ideally one actor and one room, but in my case it didn‚Äôt work out that way, I stopped at 3 characters and 3 locations. I chose actors from friends, found two and decided that for the third character I can stay in the frame myself.
I started the search for props, the main difficulties were delivered by round-rimmed glasses, I don‚Äôt know why, but this is a shortage in our city, I finally found it through my friends. I took the rest for a while too, bought only a sheet of A1 format, a tablecloth in a cage, printed out photos.



The next stage is equipment. A Canon 550d camera ($ 590), I have two lenses for it, a Canon 50 1.8 ($ 120) and a Sigma 30 1.4 ($ 490).



Tripods, constantly writing everywhere, that the main sign of amateur video is shaking. $ 30 for both.



When shooting on open apertures, there is a very high probability of focusing errors, to make this probability a little less, you need an external HDMI monitor or such a device



View Finder, attached to the camera with a magnet, a Chinese noname, costs about $ 20.
In order to record video in FullHD with sound without any problems, the camera needs a memory card of at least 6 classes, I have 10 classes, so I did not have any problems, I have a SanDisk 16GB 16G Extreme SD SDHC Class 10, 45MB / S card - it costs 25 $ 30. You could get by with cheaper options.



Of the three locations for filming, one was in the room, I decided not to complicate the situation and remove this scene at home. I used ordinary, economical lamps, a rack and a holder with cartridges for lighting. For all together about $ 35-40.



In the video I wanted to add a voice-over text and in one place the text with articulation. I worked with sound for the first time, I will say in advance that it turned out to be of very poor quality. I used the RodeVideoMic microphones for $ 160 and the Chinese radio loop for $ 20.

Next was the installation, used Adobe After Effects for slowing down, color correction, etc., Sony Vegas for the main roller assembly
