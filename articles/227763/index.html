Payoneer and Ball To All - a ball for each child

Working with thousands of companies around the world, Payoneer helps a wide variety of organizations to provide payments to their employees. We recently entered into a partnership agreement with the non-profit organization Ball To All, and are proud of this collaboration.


Ball To All is an international non-profit organization whose mission is to keep children as children. Employees of the organization, the so-called "ambassadors", work in countries such as: Ethiopia, India, Jordan, Kenya, Zimbabwe and many others, providing children in difficult situations with soccer balls. The founders of Ball To All believe that by encouraging children to play, they help them stay children, stay out of trouble, grow up healthy and happy.
