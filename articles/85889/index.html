What is Fireworks and why is it needed?


In our country, this graphic editor has not gained popularity. It so happened that the average cost of Internet traffic to download it, or the cost of the media on which it is recorded, is on average equal to the Adobe Creative Suite package. This is partly why everyone uses Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to create web design. Well, now, for me personally, it's time to think about software licensing.

I myself am not a web designer, I am a programmer. The main tasks that I accomplish with the help of a graphical editor are a minimal modification of raster and vector graphics, cutting of the finished design, image optimization, prototyping, etc. Photoshop and Illustrator solve these problems without problems, but Fireworks is also able to solve them. And now let's remember about the cost of Photoshop + Illustrator ($ 700 + $ 600) and compare with the cost of Fireworks ($ 300).

It often happens that Fireworks cannot cope with the display of native Photoshop and Illustrator files. There are glitches with the display of some effects. In such cases, a reference screenshot usually helps, which allows you to understand where the problem is and, if possible, correct it. There are also alternative editors (Gimp, Pixelmator), which open the source file, but with their own glitches that are not in Fireworks.

Thus, Fireworks is unlikely to interest designers already working in Photoshop, its main users are programmers, layout designers, designers and a small number of designers (at least in Russia).
