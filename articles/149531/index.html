RuTracker hacked

On the night of August 14, the torrent portal RuTracker.org stopped opening for most users.
Apparently, the resource was hacked by hackers. When entering the site, an automatic redirect to the external page was installed under the heading Ita est! ("This is so" - lat.)

In the credentials of the rutracker.org domain, the lines typed in Latin appeared on the Whois service: ‚Äújust don't bother a person‚Äù, ‚Äúyour power has been crushed‚Äù, ‚Äúlisten to the will and perform - no pornography, no cheeky, vulgar, stupid advertising‚Äù.

Rutracker.org is the most popular Russian torrent tracker. Initially, the resource existed under the name Torrents.ru. In February 2010, the delegation of the Torrents.ru domain was terminated due to a criminal case on the fact of unlawful use of copyright objects.
