Mobius Announcement 2016: Mobile Development Conference



On Saturday, June 4, 2016, the third Mobius conference for mobile developers will be held. Venue - St. Petersburg, Park Inn Pulkovskaya hotel.

We notably rummaged in the feedbacks of our past conferences, and a couple of weeks ago we had an open meeting where we discussed the 2015 conference fails and how to turn them into 2016 win years. As a result, it became clear the main problem: the past Mobius turned out not so hardcore as they thought.

So what to expect from the conference in 2016?

First, of course, the reports, the development of which we have engaged a whole team:

  1. Andrei Dmitriev , our program director, who has a dozen professional conferences for programmers behind him - four JPoint, four DotNext, three Joker, etc .;
  2. Matvey Malkov , Nexters - one of the best speakers of the past Mobius, who talked about reactivity ;
  3. Vladimir Ivanov from Apollophone, again one of the best speakers of Mobius 2015
  4. Dmitry Evdokimov , Director of the Digital Security Research Center;
  5. Vladimir Mironov , develops at Kotlin in production, recently spoke in Moscow with a corresponding report ;
  6. Denis Neklyudov , the first Google Developer Experts in Russia;




And so that you do not think that the guys are fooling around, here is a list of the already confirmed speakers:

  • Wiebe Elsinga , Google Developers Expert for Android, Co-founder GDG DutchAUG, Mobile Technical Lead;
  • Royi Benyossef , Google Developers Expert, Developer Relations Manager at Stealth;
  • Andrey Belenko from Skype, CISSP, on Mobius 2015 talked about encryption and security in iOS ;
  • Almost all program committee members (see above)
  • Developers of the Kotlin language from JetBrains and libraries for it (who exactly - we find out now);
  • Someone whom you suggest to us - in the comments below, at the meeting with the organizing committee (see below)
  • One of you - submit us an application for a report !
