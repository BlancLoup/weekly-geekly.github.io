CeBIT 2013. Mobile devices. The final post about the exhibition

Almost a week has passed since the end of the CeBIT exhibition in Hanover. I probably will not repeat and tell about the novelties of the computer market. Let me just remind you that you can read about them here: day one ,
second day, third day .
As a final report, I propose new items of the mobile world, which are slightly dimming against the background of the announcement of the Samsung Galaxy S IV, but still deserve attention. For example, Lenovo presented a phone with FullHD resolution.



In comparison with the Galaxy Note II, it is slightly larger than the Samsung.


It should be noted that this is an atomophone, that is, the base has become the mobile platform Intel CloverTrail with the Android 4.2 Jelly Bean operating system. The basis of this phone is the Intel Atom Z2580 processor (two cores with a clock frequency of 1.8 GHz), PowerVR SGX544 acts as a graphics accelerator. The amount of RAM (2 GB) and internal memory (32 GB + MicroSD slot) is large enough, but you have to take into account that the Lenovo K900 is equipped with two cameras - 13 MP and 2 MP for the front, so the photos quickly consume extra gigabytes. The battery that has to cope with this celebration of life has a capacity of 2500 mAh.



Another super fun thing was shown by the guys from Durabook. Panasonic Toughbook main competitors brought a tablet based on the third generation Intel Core i5. Up to 16 gigabytes of RAM, one HDD or a 2.5 "SSD and another mSATA SSD can be installed in the device. The display with a diagonal of 10 inches has a resolution of 1024 * 768 and a maximum brightness of 700 nits. Of the required interfaces are: LAN, WLAN , Bluetooth, WWAN (LTE / 3G), GPS, two USB 3.0 ports, miniUSB 2.0, SD-reader and PCMCIA slot. The battery has a capacity of 5200 mAh, in addition it is possible to attach the second one to the same. But the battery life even with two batteries will be 9-10 hours - not enough ...





At the Samsung booth, of course, SGS IV was not there yet, but it was possible to look at other innovations. The main event at CeBIT was Galaxy Express. Outwardly very similar to SGS III, it has a low screen resolution (800 * 480) with a diagonal of 4.5 ". Otherwise, it is a decent device: a dual core 1.2 GHz and 5-megapixel camera can attract buyers (I myself would I did not buy such a screen.





Protected smartphone Samsung X Cover 2 has an improved screen compared to the first version. More dramatic changes have occurred.





A huge miracle, called Galaxy Note 8 LTE, seems to fill the gap between 7 and 10 inch versions of tablets.
The characteristics of the new device are also good: Cortex A9 is about four cores, 2 gigabytes of RAM and a S-Pen stylus. Compare the dimensions with the Galaxy Note II can be in the photo below.
