ASUS N90Sc laptop for $ 1600 on SiS chipset. What for?!

I can not share my impressions about the laptop ASUS N90Sc, positioned by the developer as multimedia. I had a model ASUS N90Sc (N90S-P870SFJVAW). This mobile PC is equipped with a large LCD screen (18.4 ") with LED backlight and a resolution of 1920x1080. Watching movies on this display is pleasant. Moreover, the N90Sc model is endowed with four-channel Altec Lansing acoustics. The sound is cool.



I read the characteristics on the sticker.




I liked everything except for the weak GeForce GT 220M DDR2 video card . Well, what kind of multimedia is it, if it actually contains the renamed GeForce 8600M GT, albeit on a more advanced technical process. I launch information utilities.
