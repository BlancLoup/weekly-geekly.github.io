Basics of the ICF



Go ahead, the magistracy is built on the basis of the Interuniversity student business incubator QD . In 2010, the first enrollment of students is carried out, so the atmosphere, literally, is as innovative as it may be possible.
The educational process promises to be exciting and interesting, judge for yourself - the day is divided into two parts by lunch.
The first half of the day, future masters attend lectures by leading world-class specialists . Including classes will be conducted by teachers not only from other cities, but also countries.
The second half of the day is devoted to your own business, which is your master's project. You are allocated a workplace, as well as the attention of the ‚Äúmentors‚Äù of the magistracy (if in Russian, mentors), who will help you with experience, knowledge and connections to realize your goals.
If you don‚Äôt have your project when entering, it‚Äôs not a problem - you can take part in a team of comrades or choose a project from those offered by mentors and teachers of the ICF.

One of the mentors, by the way, is Sergey Kotyrev , the founder of UMI Studii and the founder of UMI.CMS .

Admission to the ICF


For admission to the ICF, you must successfully complete the following steps:
  1. Preliminary interview and submission of documents.
  2. Examination system analysis.
  3. Interview.
The interview plays the most important role, since you yourself must clearly understand why you are going to the ICF, what role the faculty will play in your development / work, and what knowledge and experience you want to gain by becoming a master of the first edition of the ICF. Duration of training - 2 years.
