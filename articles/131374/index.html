Acer Iconia Tab A100 - video review of the first 7-ki on Honeycomb

Yesterday we published a brief first-look on the Samsung Galaxy Tab 7.0 Plus tablet, and today we will show the Acer Iconia Tab A100 tablet, which has already been sold in Russia for several months. It became the first 7-inch device that comes preloaded with Android Honeycomb . After testing, we identified several of its advantages and disadvantages. More about them in the next "review without rules" :



Acer Iconia Tab A100 in filling is almost not different from its 10-inch brother Acer Iconia Tab A500 .

The same platform NVidia Tegra 2 , the same 2 cameras, 5 megapixel main and 2 megapixel front. The main differences are in the dimensions of the devices, battery capacity and the amount of RAM (A100 - 512Mb, A500 - 1Gb). In addition, the A500 has a full USB connector, while the A100 has a microUSB.



Specifications:
  • Processor : dual-core 1 GHz Nvidia Tegra 2
  • Memory : 512MB (RAM) 8GB of internal memory, microSD slot
  • Screen : 7 inches, LCD, resolution 1024X600
  • OS : Android 3.2 Honeycomb
  • Camera : Main: 5 MP, LED-flash, front: 2 Mpix
  • Data transfer : Bluetooth 2.1, USB 2.0, Wi-Fi 802.11 a / b / g / n, microHDMI
  • Dimensions : 195 x 117 x 13.1 mm, weight 470 g
  • Battery : Li-on two elements at 1530 mAh (3060)
  • Network : GSM 3G / 2G / HSDPA available in version A101
  • Optional : accelerometer, compass, gyroscope, proximity sensor, pressure sensor

As for the main advantages of the Acer Iconia Tab A100 , these, of course, include the price, which on the Internet starts from 12,500 r. Another positive factor is the speed of the device, the untouched Android 3.2 Honeycomb and the presence of many ports. The disadvantages include the thickness of the device and the insufficiently capacious battery.
