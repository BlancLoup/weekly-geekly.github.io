Domain Registrars


You can register a domain with accredited registrars, or with their partners - domain resellers.
Accredited registrars in the zone .RU 24, in the zone. RF - 18. The list is published on the official website of the Coordination Center of the national domain of the Internet

The largest registrar in this market is Ru-Center (nic.ru).
Domain name resellers are partners of one or more accredited registrars. These are hosting providers, design studios and specialized domain resellers. And it can be both private individuals and official companies. Resellers earn on the difference of partner prices with prices for the consumer and can only accept payments and change settings - they cannot change the owner. Those. in fact, the domain is also registered through an accredited registrar.

Myth - domain documents

Registrars often provide in a different form "documents for the domain", confirming the owner. Only, these documents do not have legal force and do not protect from anything - in terms of the rules for registering the domains of the administrator (owner) of the domain, it is not the paper document that determines, but the whois data. This is to nothing - services are completely different. A hoster doesn't care who owns the domain.

What to do if incorrect registration data is specified?


Write to the registrar. Actions are largely dependent on the accredited registrar. The question is solved.

Myth - everything does not suit me in the registrar, but I can not do anything!

You can always change the recorder. All registrars provide information - how to do it, but often this information is hidden away. The difficulty is that it is required to submit the notarized original of the application or to come personally with the documents and powers of attorney.
However, if you move from one reseller to another or transfer control directly to an accredited registrar without changing the registrar, everything is much simpler. For example, Ru-Center allows you to do this online, and Webnames (Regtime LLC) - with a simple scan of a letter with a statement, you do not need to notarize.
Consent reseller is required. You can always conclude a direct contract with the registrar.

How to identify a registrar?


Use whois. In the registrar field, see the entry * -REG-RIPN

Myth - I transfer the site to another host - I need to transfer the domain

Not necessary. Simply paying for a domain and hosting services can be paid either in one place - or in different ones.

Myth - I was sold a domain - I was given a password to the questionnaire

When selling a domain, you need to create your profile with the registrar (if not), and the seller to send a notarized application following the sample of the registrar to transfer the domain to another administrator (owner). Only after the domain appears in your profile and whois is displayed with your data - it is yours. Yes, it is not so easy, but then stealing a domain is problematic.
If you are afraid - use auctions or the service ‚ÄúDirectional domain transfer‚Äù, this is now offered by many registrars, for example: nic.ru.
When transferring / selling a domain, you can either leave the domain with the same registrar or transfer it to another.

You can change the administrator (owner) of the domain only 90 days after the domain is registered. and this is done only by an official statement certified by a notary (this does not cause any problems, since notaries not certify the document, but only your signature).

Myth - if the registrar closes - you lose the domain

ICANN periodically announces termination of accreditation (Russian registrars do not meet there). In this case, on a competitive basis, domains are massively transferred to other accredited registrars.
In the case of resellers, it is even easier - the domains remain with the accredited registrar.

Recommendations


  • Choose a registrar you can trust.
  • Register only on the actual owner of the domain.
  • If you register for a promotion - carefully read the terms of the promotion.
  • Be sure to send scans of documents (RU, RF), even if the domain has been registered for a long time.
  • A few hours after registration, check whois - there should be your data and the status of VERIFIED (after checking the documents, usually from the 1st day to a month).
  • After domain renewal (within 3 days) - check with whois that the domain registration period has changed (paid-till field: YYYY.MM.DD).
  • You must obtain a login and password to manage your domain. In the case of buying a domain from a reseller, you must be given a login and password from the domain control panel of an accredited registrar.
  • Use caution when registering email addresses received from free mail services - they can be disabled for inactivity and take control.
  • Use complex and complex passwords to access the panel and mail. Do not store passwords in mailboxes, do not save them in browsers and mail programs.
  • Do not forget to renew your domain registration - in the RU zone this is done once a year.
  • To hide your data - use Private Person and provide individual contact information for the domain (only for individuals). In this case, you will avoid fraud using publicly available whois data - as information will be published:
    person: Private person
    And not the address and telephone number that you provided when registering a domain, but contact information specifically intended for public access.

  • If you plan to register a dozen domains - become a partner of an accredited registrar. Many offer very competitive prices starting from 1000 rubles of general expenses or special conditions for mass registration. There is also a minus - most likely it is necessary to draw up a paper contract.
  • Read the Rules for registering domain names in the domain RU and the Russian Federation - so that the terms of separation and other conditions are not a surprise for you. Please note that some domain zones have special conditions.
