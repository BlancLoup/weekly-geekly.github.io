We can not pay so much money to PPA members

April 1, 2016 marks exactly 5 years since the start of our Promotion of Authors Program (PPA) , giving users the opportunity to earn money by publishing high-quality and interesting articles on Habr√©. During these 5 years, hundreds of authors joined the program, earning a total of hundreds of thousands of rubles and thus proving the effectiveness of PPA, but ...



But we decided that it could not go on like this. We can not pay as much as we pay now. Therefore, we have well weighed the pros and cons and made a decision ... pay even more!

Yes, from this day on, we will increase the amount of rewards and introduce thresholds for a rating that affects the payout amount:

  • If the publication dials +30 , the author receives 3,000 rubles ;
  • If the publication gains +50 , the author receives 5,000 rubles .

Moreover: for each subsequent rating point over one of the thresholds, the author now receives twice as much as before - instead of 5 rubles for each item we will charge 10 rubles. For example:

  • The publication, which collected +30 , guarantees the author a reward in the amount of 3000 rubles ;
  • A publication with a rating of +49 gets 3000 + 190 = 3190 rubles ;
  • The publication, estimated at +151 , earns 5000 + 1010 = 6010 rubles .

For publications with the type of "translation", the mechanic is saved, and the amount, according to new thresholds, is divided into two:

+30 gets 3000/2 = 1500 p.
+49 gets 3190/2 = 1595 p.
+151 gets 6010/2 = 3005 p.

And no, this is not a joke. With money, as you know, they are not joking.

So join the Authors Encouragement Program , post interesting articles and earn money.

PS: Articles published since April 1 will be calculated according to a new scheme. If the text was published earlier (say, March 31 at 23:59), the calculation will be made according to the old scheme.
