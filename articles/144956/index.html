Opera Mini 7 for J2ME, BlackBerry and S60

Today came the final version of Opera Mini 7 for J2ME, BlackBerry and S60 platforms. The main difference from the 6.x family was the emergence of a new start page containing several convenient (we hope) blocks:

Sites
Here, in the course of working with the browser, recently visited web pages will be automatically displayed, so that resources you frequently visit will always be at hand.

Social networks
This section displays the latest information from your social media accounts. While Facebook and Twitter are available, VKontakte and Odnoklassniki will soon be added.

news
This is the ‚Äúsecret‚Äù section: if the websites you visit have an RSS feed, it will automatically connect to this section so you can see the RSS news from your favorite sites automatically.

Suggested links
Here, popular local resources are sorted by category.

Of course, not all of the new start page may be useful, in this case it can be turned off in the browser settings.

Another good news is that the Express panel has not gone away, but is located next to the start page. And doubly good news - now the size of the Express panel is not limited to 9 cells - add as you see fit. And already triple pleasant news - we fixed Opera Link's work with the Express panel in Opera Mini, now you can synchronize absolutely the entire list between the desktop and the mobile phone.

Once again we remind that all Opera browsers are absolutely free and you can download them from the official page of m.opera.com
