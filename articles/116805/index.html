iNT PRO and iNETTRADER - an app to trade stocks using your iPhone and iPad

Until recently, Russian iPhone users did not have the opportunity to make securities transactions through their phones. However, this issue has now been resolved. The broker company NETTRADER.ru developed applications for trading stocks on the MICEX Stock Exchange using the iPhone and iPad and became one of the first in the world and the first broker in Russia to release such software. The release of the viewing application took place in May 2010, the trading application - a few months later in October 2010.
