onlineMerchant: a convenient payment solution for online stores. Part I

Getting acquainted with the increasing number of services from PaySto , I conclude that it is organized very reasonably, rationally and conveniently, and that each of its components functions together with others, helping our domestic entrepreneurs to develop their business. In this case, all their attention can be directed specifically to work, the provision of services, the organization of services to their customers, but not to solve problems with payment, accounting and distribution of bills. This role takes just the system PaySto.


This time I want to talk about another service from PaySto - a service for accepting payments for services provided or goods sold. It is called online Merchant . With this feature, sellers of goods / services on the Internet acquire the opportunity to receive remuneration for their labors without any difficulties. This feature is used by merchants and service providers when the value of their goods is predetermined and indicated in their online store or other web page.

The beauty and convenience of onlineMerchant is that there is no need for a merchant to register with different payment systems and control the receipt of funds in each of them. On the other hand, the payer, on the contrary, can use a convenient payment method available to him at online Merchant. All work on currency conversion, payment processing and transfer of funds from one account to another takes on PaySto.

Of course, the functionality of onlineMerchant is a third party in the process of buying and selling. However, the merchant must understand that the benefit of cooperation with PaySto in this case is just that in order to receive the desired payment, he will only need to register with PaySto.

Next, he must integrate the payment instrument into his online store or relevant page and simply get paid. All intermediate actions like currency exchange, turning electronic money into real money and transferring money from one system to another (or from one account to another) are taken over by PaySto. The seller does not create different accounts in several banks and does not activate different accounts in domestic and global payment systems. He only offers goods and receives payment for them.

Of course, PaySto service, in this case, an online Merchant, is paid, and it will cost the entrepreneur 5% of the amount of the payment received. It is also important that both individuals and legal entities, regardless of the type of business they do, can use onlineMerchant to receive payments.
