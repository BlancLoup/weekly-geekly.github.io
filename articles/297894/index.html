Alibaba Group's development strategy for 2016 and plans for cooperation with Russia

Alibaba Group holding CEO announced development plans for 2016. He identified several priority areas. Among them - the promotion in rural areas, major cities and global expansion. It is worth noting that the company is ready to help Russia in the development of online export of goods.

Further development in large cities


"We are going to strengthen and expand our position in the market, increasing our presence in major cities," Chang said at a speech in Hangzhou in front of company employees.

According to Chang, within the framework of development in the largest cities of the PRC, Alibaba plans to increase its reputation among residents. At present, the retailer has already managed to achieve some success in Beijing, where he focused on online sales of products and household appliances. It is planned that the capital will be the link between Alibaba and 400 million people in northern China, where the infrastructure is less developed than in the southeastern provinces.

Strengthened engagement with rural regions
