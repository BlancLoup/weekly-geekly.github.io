Working in several web studios provided me with many useful acquaintances, so the designer and programmer were found fairly quickly. A couple of brainstorming in a cafe with Wi-Fi and the project was solemnly laid out on a large sheet of paper.

Professionalism is not the only thing a patient needs. A doctor works with people; therefore, his purely human qualities are important: attentiveness, tact, and even kindness. And this can only be learned from those who have already been at his reception.

Therefore, the function of reviews on the site is critically important: be it a specialist three times, have 2 diplomas and 3,000 publications, if you want to forget a visit to him, like a bad dream, this should be reflected adequately in the rating.
