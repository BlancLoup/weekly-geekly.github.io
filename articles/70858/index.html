Car body



The company PC Rides, it seems, intended to make a small revolution in the housing market. The guys are going to serially produce a computer in the form of a Dodge Charger SRT8 sports car.

The first thing that pleases in the case is the presence of a competent light indication. While the machine is on, the LED headlights are lit, the dimensions blink when the hard drive is addressed, the size flashes. A DVD drive is integrated into the front bumper, DVI, HDMI, VGA, 6 USB ports, audio output and a network connector are mounted in the rear bumper.

When the sales start is unknown, but the model will be offered in three versions:

Standard - with an Intel Core 2 Duo 2.26 GHz processor - for $ 1,850;
Turbo - with Intel Core 2 Duo 2.4 GHz - for $ 2175;
Super Turbo - with Intel Core 2 Duo 2.53 GHz - for $ 2,495.
