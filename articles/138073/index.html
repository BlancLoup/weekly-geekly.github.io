We vote for the whole world with golosim.ru

It was getting dark. Suddenly, we ( iklementiev and AlexeevGena ) decided to make a vote in which a huge number of people could take part (let's dream: tens to hundreds of thousands). After a brief search, we realized that at the moment there are a huge number of places where you can create voting ballots, but which are in no way connected with each other (the most striking examples are Habr, VKontakte, Facebook, etc.). All of them are limited to their audience.

At this point, we decided to make some efforts and make a service that would allow collecting an audience from different social networks for voting, as easy and convenient for the user as possible. Therefore, we are connected through oAuth to several networks.



On the main page, 10 top pollsters are displayed, the rest of the user gets either through links from social networks or through a search.

After login, the user can see the voting results and filters with his participation.



Future plans:
  • API implementation for communication with external services
  • smart search
  • infographics
  • geo buns
