Limited edition EeePC 901 and 1000H will be released in Korea



Starting today, pre-order sub-laptops EeePC 901 and EeePC 1000H are available in Korea in limited edition in Pink and Jell-O green color solutions. And the feature is not just in color, but also in the fact that the laptop lid and its working panel are covered in the case of a red laptop with snowflakes, and in the case of a green laptop with leaves (according to some sources - mistletoe leaves). They will be available on August 22nd, but the price for them has not yet been announced. Whether these laptops will be sold in other countries is not known.
