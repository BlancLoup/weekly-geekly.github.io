Fragment of the discussion of the translation of the "computer hockey" into Russian in an echo conference Ru.Hokku in October 2003

Our colleague zhylin yesterday brought a translation (probably his own) of a number of such computer error messages that took the form of hockey (Japanese tricycles). In the same place , KirillGolub rightly remarked to him that the translation was imperfect: the triniges should follow the classic number of haiku syllables (5 + 7 + 5).

But in my time (about seven years ago) I myself devoted some time to the creative search for such a translation into Russian of hockey-like error messages, which would be more perfect in the number of syllables.

I think it is not superfluous to bring on Habrahabr the then result of my creativity - a fragment of the message I made in the Fidonet ehoconference of Hokku on October 8, 2003. It was this translation of mine that was later distributed over Fidonet and the Internet (for example, [ 1 ], [ 2 ], [ 3 ], [ 4 ]):
