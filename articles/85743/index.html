Two mice and two pointers on one PC

Two mice and two pointers


I have long dreamed that in windows you could connect two mice, and at the same time control two pointers. So to speak, with two hands to shoot files and links in the browser. A couple of days ago I found such a program - Peanut Butter Pointer. About a miracle - she got on windows 7, though in compatibility mode, but it works with a bang.
The official website of the company http://www.peanutbuttersoftware.com/ . The program was created so that adults could spend time on the same computer with their children, protecting their psyche on the Internet.
In my opinion this is the perfect solution. You can spend time on the same computer with your girlfriend, friend, friends - according to the concept of gaming consoles, such as one monitor - two manipulators and a bunch of programs for playing together: Word, IE, explorer ...
I ran a test of flash games and, like a true cowboy Bebop, shot from two hands. By the way, it was necessary to shoot by turns, since at the same time you can select or click only with one mouse.
By pushing the tachpad, I got another (third) pointer.

My impressions of the program are on the solid four, since it does not implement fine tuning of the mice.
