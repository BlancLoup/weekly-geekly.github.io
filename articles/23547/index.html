How to move your home directory to another section

Moving your home directory to a separate partition will be useful if you ever need to reinstall OS X, you can just format the partition with OS X, reinstall OS X, and be ready to work in less than an hour, without having to spend time backing up your personal data and files. The whole task can be performed by several terminal commands and yes, it is easier, I can explain where to poke in Finder and NetInfo Manager ( in which I did climb and do everything with pens ).
