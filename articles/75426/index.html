Communicator Highscreen Zeus based on Android OS



Vobis Computer (Vobis) introduced its new Highscreen Zeus androidophone to the market.
The device is a side slider, equipped with a QWERTY-keyboard. At the same time, the thickness of the device is only 15.1 mm, which does not exceed the thickness of many classic candy bar monoblocks. The device does not have a standard catalog of Google Android Market applications, which is usually installed on full-featured Google phones (for example, HTC Hero and HTC Tattoo). However, two alternative solutions are pre-installed on the device - clients of two application directories at once: the English AndAppStore (free applications) and the American SlideMe (paid and free applications). The purchase of paid software through SlideMe will be possible on the territory of Russia (payment by credit card).

Devices under this brand are manufactured at Taiwanese plants Mobinnova, a division of Foxconn Group, a contract assembler of all Apple iPhone devices.
