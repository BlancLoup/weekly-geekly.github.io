HackDay for Superheroes



December 12-14 in Naberezhnye Chelny will host the final HackDay # 35 for Superheroes. We collect participants from all over the Volga Federal District, projects from Moscow, St. Petersburg and Kaliningrad and the best HackDay teams of this year. What awaits the participants of the event - look under the cut.

HackDay will arrive in Tatarstan for the first time. Tatarstan is a region with the highest entrepreneurial activity in the field of IT. A lot of events held, a strong IT-community, universities that produce technical specialists, dozens of Internet projects, successfully operating in the global market - all together form a strong ecosystem for the birth and growth of Internet start-ups. HackDay # 35 will be an entry point for people with ideas who do not yet have a team and a clear vision of the product and business.



For the grand finale, we will bring mentors and speakers with applied management and business development experience:

At the demofest, we traditionally celebrate the best projects in 4 nominations : the best business solution, the best technical implementation, the solution that can change the usual course of life, and for the first time, a project executed at the interface of programming, Internet technologies and medicine.

HackDay # 35 opens with 90-second project pitches. We start on December 12 at 18:30.

Registration and details.

On Friday, participants will present projects and form teams; on Saturday and Sunday, teams will work on developing projects. You can work at night.

We will organize an applied educational program that will avoid pitfalls when launching a prototype project and at the first steps after its launch. As part of the mentoring sessions, each project will receive individual advice from HackDay mentors.

The event will end with a demofest , during which participants will present the developed prototypes.

Participation in the event is free, however, you will have to write a letter of motivation.
