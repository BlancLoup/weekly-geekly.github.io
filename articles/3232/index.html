QIP messenger purchased by RBC

The QIP messenger popular in Russia, working on the ICQ protocol, was acquired by a company affiliated with RBC .

Messages about this appeared in runet blogs today. Checking some technical information indicates RBC.

- First of all, whois qip.ru and qipim.com point to Valento Commerce Limited, which is most likely registered in the offshore zone (Virgin Islands). This company has already been mentioned in connection with RBC.
- NS of the above domains - relax.ru. The whois of this domain contains the telephone numbers of the RBC call center.
- The site qip.ru is hosted at RBC facilities (and not at the RBC hosting center , but on another server owned by RBC CJSC, and access is provided by Golden Telecom). At the same time, at the bottom of the site pages (for now?) It is indicated that Spaceweb provided hosting .



- A new Delphi programmer is working on the QIP project. Judging by the vacancy published before, his salary ranges from 40 to 55 thousand rubles (the vacancy message has already been removed from the QIP forum). A non-profit project can hardly afford such expenses.
