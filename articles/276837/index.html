New 2GIS for Android - we begin public testing

New 2GIS for Android went into public testing. To install it, go to the Google Play page or search for the search query ‚Äú2GIS beta‚Äù. Available for devices that work on Android 4.1 and higher.




The current version of the application is still the main one. It is not deleted when installing 2GIS beta and it maintains a page in GP .

Android fragmentation is a well-known theme. Devices of all shapes, with different screens and incredibly different in quality. Developing an application that works well on all or at least most Android devices is not an easy task.

Since May, in the "closed" mode, the application tested on 1079 devices 16,000 people. All this time we have worked to make the product public.

We tried to take into account the experience of updating on iOS. We will transfer to the new 2GIS only when we are fully confident that we have eliminated all the important errors and most users are satisfied with the application.
