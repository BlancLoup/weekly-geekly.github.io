Developer Meeting on Sphinx, June 18 (Saturday)


Since the last mitap about Sphinx, more than a year has passed, so it's time to get together again. On June 18, the second SphinxSearch meetup will take place, geographically again in Avito, register and join ! Something interesting is promised to tell Avito, Ozon.ru and Netology, well, I will also be present, participate and consist. Again, pizza, coffee, cookies. Under the cut a little more about the speakers and the program. Well - and where exactly to come there too !!!


About the main thing, that is, the program and our wonderful speakers.

Once, Anton Kovalev, a developer from Netology-groups, will share his experience of quickly deploying the Sphinx on the example of the Foxford project . Tutorial : how to quickly autocomplete, get the beautiful context of the found word after the search (snippets / excerpts) and clear the text from the markup (HTML, LaTeX).

Two, Igor Chakrygin will talk about how Sphinx is used in Ozon.ru, including listing methods for updating indexes that have been tested, and will also reveal approaches to solving specific problems encountered during operation.
