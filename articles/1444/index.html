Wikimapia - a symbiosis of Wikipedia and Google Maps

On Wikimapia, any visitor can select a site on the world map and edit it, that is, add a text description. For example, you can find your home on a city map and mark it for everyone to see. The Russians have already marked all Moscow with marks.

The creators of Wikimapia made a tool with which, in theory, you can mark all the significant geographical objects in the world. Actually, this is exactly what the official motto of the site sounds like: "WikiMapia".

To add a new object to the map, just click the ‚ÄúAdd New‚Äù button and specify a place or area. After that, a window will appear in which you can create a text description. Each text field is edited as in Wikipedia . Other site visitors can add something to your records or correct them. As if in a folk encyclopedia, the history of edits is kept here.

The authors of the interesting project are Alexander Koryakin and Evgeny Savelyev. They write that they were inspired by the success of Wikipedia and Google Maps to create Wikimapia.
