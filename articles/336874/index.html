Event digest for HR specialists in the IT field for September 2017



September is the time to get new knowledge, and for HR specialists who work or want to work in IT, new training courses will start in September. The second half of September is rich in meetings and seminars where you can share experiences and just enjoy communicating with colleagues.


Immersion in IT recruitment (course of training webinars)


When: August 4 - September 22
Where: Webinar
Participation conditions: tuition fee of 10,000 rubles (20% discount on the promo code "MY CIRCLE")
Organizer: IT Recruiter School

The course is designed for recruiters who are just getting acquainted with the world of IT. You will have 3 weeks of theoretical and practical classes, 6 speakers who will help you understand the technological stack, marketing chips, and also tell you about the right technical interview, networking rules and much more.

Speakers: Ilya Popov, Sergey Khudovekov, Svetlana Petrovicheva, Ekaterina Zhdanova, Evgeny Maslov, Victor Bakhmetyev.

Details and registration


IT Recruiter 2.0 (training course)


When: September 5 - November 9
Where: St. Petersburg, Zanevsky Ave., 30, K.2, BC Rostra, 5 fl., SoftBalans group of companies
Participation conditions: the cost of training is 26,000 rubles for individuals, 30,000 rubles for legal entities
Organizer: IT-Dominanta School

The course will be useful both for experienced recruiters and recruitment managers who are planning to find work in the IT sphere, as well as for beginner IT recruiters who are striving for the heights of professional skills. Course duration - 2 months. Participation formats: full-time (personal attendance at classes in St. Petersburg) and online (video broadcast).

Speakers: Olga Vlasenko, Anastasia Likhodievskaya, Dmitry Laabe, Vitaly Khudobakhshov, Sergey Atroshchenkov, Alexey Akopyan, Eleonora Yakimenko, Faina Lerner, Denis Kalanov.

Details and registration


Recruiting in IT. Practice (seminar)


When: September 16, 10:00
Where: Moscow, Butyrsky val. 24/13 p. 1, office 32
Conditions of participation: tuition fee 5,000 rubles
Organizer: Valuable Experience

The expert will tell in detail how to navigate the current labor market of IT specialists, take into account its specificity and trends in the selection.

After training, you will be able to find IT-specialists not only on job-resources, but also on social networks, professional communities and on the open spaces of the Internet, as well as to minimize budgets and search time.

Speaker: Julia Kartashova. Business trainer. She graduated from the Russian Academy of Entrepreneurship. Experience in IT - more than 10 years. She worked in such companies as "DIRECTUM", "Headhunter", "Spargo Technologies".
