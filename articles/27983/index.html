New network hard drive Seagate Central Axis 1 TB

Seagate has released a 1TB Maxtor Central Axis network drive. The novelty supports the ability to remotely access content via a web interface using Central Axis technology, which allows working with the hard drive from a browser without the need to open ports in the firewall. Just enter your username and password.

Of the technical characteristics of the Maxtor Central Axis, only its volume is known - 1 TB, the spindle rotation speed is 7200 revolutions per minute and the cache size is 16 MB. Winchester is equipped with a USB port (two pieces) for connecting a printer or other drives. New comes with software Drag and Sort and Maxtor SimpleView.

A new drive will be available in July (this is in the USA). It will cost $ 330.
