Pokeda.ru - Revenge of a Former Friend


A few months ago, an interesting project was launched revenge former friend . Decided to share with the community and write about this project.

In RuNet, there are projects for girls only, in which they can publish photos of their ex-boys with comments and stories.

Now justice is restored. A similar project for men has been opened - pokeda.ru .

You can argue long, ethically or not. The project is scandalous, but once it lives, once people come, read, write reviews, comment, it means that people need it.

PokeDa.ru is a kind of place where you can let off steam. After parting with a girl is not always so easy to go through this period. You need a place where you can speak out and cry out.

In some cases, even publications become an extremely important warning: look at this case, because such scary stories must be made accessible and open so that no one else will fall for this hook.

This project was created by several people, as they say for fun . At first there was an idea, then it turned into the kind that can be observed at the moment. The assembly was taken as a basis for the popular "engine" (knowledgeable people will immediately understand which one), something has been redone, refined and written on its own, generally optimized for this idea. While the project is in beta (some flaws are possible), but we are trying to correct the shortcomings and implement something new.
