Who are we?


Ontiko - on the one hand, we are a development company specializing in the development of large and high-loaded projects, on the other hand - an organizer of professional IT conferences. I am sure that you know us better than we do.

For example, the superpopular social network VKontakte - one of those whom we helped cope with high loads.

For us, a typical project is a social network with photo and video elements designed for medium or high loads. We have the experience of creating the largest sites on the Runet. Through the organization of conferences for web developers, we maintain friendly relations with many leading experts of the Runet, which allows us to attract them to the most complex areas of development and to solve specific customer issues.

We hold conferences for many years. And this year we are holding several thematic professional IT-conferences covering all areas of the development of Internet projects.
Very soon (in April) there will be RIT ++ 2010 , which includes RootConf (conference of system administrators), a conference on managing WhaleRider Internet projects in September and ending the year with a conference for developers of high-loaded Highload ++ systems in October.
