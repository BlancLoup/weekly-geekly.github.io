RIM lost its BBX brand. The next OS will be called BlackBerry 10



Thanks to the efforts of RIM, their ardent fans will have to postpone their plans for stuffing a tattoo with the text " BBX ", because A federal court of the United States of America has prohibited the company from using this trademark, thereby deciding in favor of BASIS International , which, in fact, belongs to it. As a result, the Waterloo guys announced on BlackBerry DevCon Asia that the next OS would be called " BlackBerry 10 " (by the way, the Roman number 10 means ... We see what you did here). Hopefully, RIM plans to create a single OS for tablets and phones will be implemented much better than the process of renaming it. Next time, obviously, they should leave it to the machines .
