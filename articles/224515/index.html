Few people think of making a move out of turn in the real world, but, in essence, this technique has been used since time immemorial. For example, after one of the battles of the American Civil War, General Robert Lee announced to his officers that General Grant would march on Spotsilvaniyu, since this is the best solution for him. Lee's troops made, so to speak, a turn out of turn and arrived at SpotSilvania before Grant‚Äôs army could reach it.

Turns out of turn are a common phenomenon in the business world. When Tylenol manufacturers learned that a similar Datril anesthetic would be sold at a significant discount, they made a move out of turn. They set the price below the Datril cost even before the manufacturers of the latter were able to announce their price. The advertising campaign failed and Tylenol kept its position in the market.

We are in the company, often we are ahead of the game - even on a preliminary study of the automation object we are developing a prototype system. This approach allows us to deeper understand what needs to be done, and the customer to see the seriousness of our intentions.

Be flexible (and properly set tasks)


You can win in the "tic-tac-toe" or solve other complex problems, if you apply a flexible definition of the term "victory". Allow your row to bend, and the victory is in your pocket. The task should be set constructively, based on non-trivial solutions that are different from your original expectations.
