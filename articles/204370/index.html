LTE in Moscow

Good day!

Today is our holiday. We had a full-scale launch of LTE in Moscow. Covered most of the city within the Moscow Ring Road, until the end of 2013 coverage will be 80%.

Here is a coverage map:



To connect to the LTE network, you need to update your SIM card.

We offer you to simply get a new USIM card with 4G support in any Beeline office. If you are already a Beeline subscriber, the exchange of your SIM for a new one is free. The number is, of course, preserved.

Our devices (routers, modems, and so on) that support 4G can choose 3G and 2G when they go beyond 4G coverage. The latest versions of modems support LTE FDD (800/1800/2600 MHz), UMTS (900/2100 MHz) and GSM (900/1800 MHz).
