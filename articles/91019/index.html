StarCraft II: Wings of Liberty - beta testing

Are you still not invited to test the second part of the famous Starcraft?
There is no beta account, but I want to play. What to do?



You can go two ways in general: buy or get it yourself.
About the first way I will not speak. Google has it all.
The second way is much more interesting.

Everyone who wondered about getting the key probably knows that in order to open a beta profile you must have at least one game attached. And what to do if it is not?
This is where World of Warcraft comes to the rescue. Already understand what I'm getting at?

The procedure is as follows:
  • register a clean account on battle.net
  • we drive in all our data, subscribe to the news, choose the format of the appeal to you
  • activate the trial version of WOW for 10 days (key moment)
  • now (!) beta profile is available - go (even after the expiry of the trial period, the beta profile will work)
  • we swing softina which transfers technical characteristics of the computer
  • we check once again whether you are subscribed to the news, whether the appeal format is selected and whether all the checkboxes are on the beta profile page
  • waiting for the next wave of distribution of elephants, not forgetting to glance in the mailbox


Well, and most importantly, perhaps, the fact that a working invitation to participate in the beta test came to me for such an account.

Immediately I will warn you, do not make several accounts - they will ban everything.
