Q: What is a FXS gateway and what is it for?
A: FXS (Foreign Exchange Subscriber) - analog voice interface (RJ-11 connector) for connecting a two-wire PSTN line to a telephony device. In the simplest case - a wall telephone jack.
definition taken from: voip-tel.ru

Careful, a lot of text!

AudioCodes is one of the leading manufacturers of equipment and software for building VoIP networks. The main activities of the company are the development of the element base, software and hardware systems, as well as extensive research activities. The most well-known AudioCodes developments are the G.723.1 voice codec and VoIPerfect software hardware platform. The company was founded in 1993 and today has sold about 12 million VoIP ports from AudioCodes. A wide range of high-tech, reliable, cost-effective equipment company AudioCodes can be successfully applied in the construction of operator and corporate networks of VoIP. Komptek has 2 AudioCodes equipment lines: Mediant digital VoIP gateways and MediaPack analog VoIP gateways. Company information taken from www.comptek.ru

The MP-202 is a great 2-port gateway that works over both UDP and TCP and TLS protocols.
It has a wild bunch of settings, it seems to me that you can configure everything on it that your heart desires (regarding voip telephony and the fact that this is still an fxs gateway).

Convenient construction dialplan, extremely simple and at the same time very capacious.
Here is an example:

You can set up speed dialing, i.e. I dial 001 and get on the number 73522666666 - for example.

Also, the creators have not forgotten about security:

  • You can set access rules.
  • Port Forwarding
  • Specify DMZ host.
  • Port Triggers.
  • Close access to certain resources.

After reading what is described above, you guessed that this is not about the FXS gateway, but in combination - the router. Yes, you are absolutely right. In addition, this is a very good router (especially considering that he can QoS).
Accordingly, you can customize the routing.
