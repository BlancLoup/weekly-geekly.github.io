Free webinar "What's new in Kerio Operator 2.3"



Introducing the new version of Kerio Operator 2.3! With new features, using Kerio Operator has become much easier for both users and IT administrators.

Among the new features:

  • The Click to Call feature available to the Kerio Connect client and Chrome and Firefox browsers - using the new plug-in, you can now make calls by clicking on the number in the Kerio Connect Address Book of the client or on the phone number listed on the website.
  • Call Monitoring - allows you to listen to telephone conversations in real time, as well as indicate the level of interaction with participants in the conversation to improve the quality of service.
  • New automatically supported phones - thanks to the addition of Aastra and Yealink brand handsets, IT administrators and business owners are now more free to choose devices of different brands.
  • And much more...


Webinar date: June 6 at 11.00 (Moscow time)

Webinar presenter: Anton Tikhonov, Kerio Technologies Technical Manager
