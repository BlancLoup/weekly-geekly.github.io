Problems of attracting an audience early

Where to talk about your ambitious project, if you only have an idea? Is it possible at the initial stage to find like-minded people and simply interested people. The article will discuss the problems that can be encountered and how to solve these problems.





Problem number 1.


Currently, there are countless sites where you can declare your ambitious project in one form or another. News sites, blogs, twitter, videos on youtube, everywhere they say that this or that project started - they invented a new type of solar panels, built a giant robot, jumped from the stratosphere. But how terribly it becomes to me to find out how people achieved this, what they went through, what difficulties they experienced! The No. 1 problem emerges - we, simply interested people and professionals in our fields, want to see not only the result, but also the process of achieving this result. In English, this concept is denoted by the phrase "Making of".
