Big report from InfoCom - mega-interesting exhibition of screens



Hello! We returned here three weeks ago from the jubilee exhibition Infocomm in Florida and slightly took pictures of all sorts of interesting pieces from there. Infocomm is one of the few world exhibitions that you definitely need to visit if you want to stay up to date with all the new products. For the most part, everything is connected with different screens and display facilities in general.

The exhibition is held in two stages - 2 days for partners, 3 days for the press and all people from the street. The first two days, different closed decisions are shown, individual presentations are made. For example, two years ago in Amsterdam, Mitsubishi invited us to show off their innovative geometry screens, which then, unfortunately, did not go into mass production. First, a little about the exhibition

There are four more things that make you drive:
  • At exhibitions often show new items. To this we were waiting for a laser TV, which was not supplied outside the States.

  • And, of course, you can manage to agree on cooperation with everyone personally, which is often much simpler and faster than through the network.
