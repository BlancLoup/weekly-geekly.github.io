Launch of the Orthodox search system "Rublev"

Today, February 3, director and producer Yuri Grymov posted an ad on his Facebook page about the opening of the first Orthodox search engine.



The search engine proudly carries the name " Rublev ", it can also be used as a database on Orthodoxy.

As Grymov himself writes on his page, this project has been developed for about two years, and now its creators are actively raising funds to develop a mobile version of a search engine.
