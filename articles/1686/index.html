Microsoft releases an amateur platform for creating games

Microsoft has announced the release of the free platform XNA Game Studio Express for creating games for the new Xbox 360 console, as well as under Windows XP. A test version of the platform will appear on August 30. The final release is expected towards the end of the year. For developers of games for Windows, the platform will be free, and developers for Xbox 360 will have to pay $ 100 per year for membership in the club.

It is assumed that the development tools will be accessible and understandable for millions of simple gamers, even for children, who will be able to create their own toys from scratch for the first time in their lives. This step of Microsoft is quite logical now, when a new trend of growing popularity of amateur content is noted on the Internet.
