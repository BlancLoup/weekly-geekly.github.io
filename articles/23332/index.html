Alternative GBIC Modules for Cisco Catalyst

Given: Cisco Catalyst 4503 switch with multiport optical module supporting GBICs.

Not given: native tsiskovsky GBIC WS-G5486 (now you will not find them in Moscow and, besides, they are expensive).

Task: to find a module from another manufacturer that would work with a katalist.

I heard that 3Com has the appropriate modules, Allied Telesyn and D-Link, but I was told that they might not be suitable. I would not want to spend money on 8 glands, which then have nowhere to go.

You do not know?

UPD: As it turned out , there are hidden commands for iOS that allow you to install any module that is physically compatible. But, in some cases, it is for Catalist 4500 that IOS SG is required.

service unsupported-transceiver (spelled that way)
no errdisable detect cause gbic-invalid
