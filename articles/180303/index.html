Nikon Coolpix AW100. One year later

A year has passed since I purchased this camera. More than 2000 shots were taken during 4 seasons in the temperature range from -20 to + 40 in all possible conditions: snow, rain, sea, mud, ice, sand, shaking on a bicycle and cars, etc. I decided to share my impressions of the use of this protected, designed for extreme and harsh conditions of soap dish.

I am engaged in foot, led, water tourism, rock climbing, winter sports. Sports were added an average of 1 per year. At first, the old Canon PowerShot A520 was enough. But after 4 years of use - he began to die rapidly: a bent and beaten body, a crash when the lens came out, the shutter was often wedged, it was necessary to fix it with hands, the screen was completely wiped and scratched. Battery


As you have noticed 1400 mA. In the characteristics written 250 photos. Looking ahead to say - in principle, the truth. But I mainly use it in these modes:
1. During the day (the flash does not fire) + compass. Enough for 300 + - photos.
2. During the day (the flash does not fire) + compass + GPS tags on the photo (coordinates). Enough for 150-200 photos.
3. Happy with a GPS track recording. Used a couple of times. You can make about 150 photos, but only for 6-8 hours, since the GPS works, even when the camera is turned off.
4. Mostly during the day + compass + GPS tags in the photo (coordinates) + about 10 photos in the evening or at night (in the parking lot). 200-300 photos.

When using 1,2,4 "mode" quietly enough for a hike (7-10 days). Now there are plans for 14+ days, thinking about buying a second battery.

The convenience of use


Well what can I say. I put 4+. It lies comfortably in the hand, even the wet almost does not slip. The buttons are quite comfortable, but the place is still there, it was possible to make a bit more.
There are a couple of "minuses", but for me they are minor:
- It is convenient to photograph in gloves (winter). But to change the mode or to carry out viewing of the made photo is quite difficult.
- No button illumination.

You can say pleased. Good quality. The focal length is quite small: about 1 cm.

I use the highest quality. 1 photo on average weighs about 6 megabytes. On 8 GB flash drive fit about 900 photos.
If we evaluate on a 5-point scale, then on 3+, 4, depending on the conditions. Albeit not a mirror quality, but you can catch any moments, always at hand, no need to stop to take a picture. Conveniently take pictures of yourself. Photos are shown on the map (in Google, Nokia, VK, etc.)
