The service helps young professionals get a loan of up to $ 25 thousand at a rate of 6% for a period of two or three years. In addition, they cannot rely on favorable credit terms due to the prevalence of quantitative criteria in the rating, such as the amount of previous credit accounts and so on. To assess the solvency of this category of citizens, Pave changed the FICO formula, raising the importance of criteria such as education, work experience and hiring potential in the future.
