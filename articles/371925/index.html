Honda has launched its own hydrogen fuel cell vehicle.


Presented a new car president of Honda Takahiro Hachigo

In September of last year on Geektimes there was news about a new Honda project - a car on hydrogen fuel cells. Then it was also reported that the stock of the car from Honda will be more than that of Mirai from Toyota. Now the company has presented not a concept, but a commercial version of the car, which was called Clarity. The cost of new can not be called low - $ 67,000 is still a solid amount.

In the first year, 200 cars will be sold, but they will not be sold in showrooms, but will be provided as part of leasing to government organizations. Some Clarity will be made available to commercial companies, which, however, have not yet been named.

After some time, hydrogen cars will go on sale in California at a price of $ 60,000, and leases will be provided for $ 500 per month.

In Japan, leasing will cost $ 880 a month, and the total cost of a car here is $ 67,000, as mentioned above. State "green" benefits may reduce this amount.


Honda headquarters visitor to Tokyo takes pictures of Ckarity

Harmful emissions of a car on hydrogen fuel cells does not, at the exit - only water. Honda is one of the first (if not the first) company to create a commercial version of hydrogen fuel cells. They were first certified by the US Environmental Protection Agency and the California Air Resources Board in 2002.

When the tank is fully loaded, a sedan (the form factor is exactly that - a five-seater sedan) can travel 750 kilometers without refueling. The company during the work on the project was able to reduce the fuel cells, while increasing the number of seats in the cabin to 5.
