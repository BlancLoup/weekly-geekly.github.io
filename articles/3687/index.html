The first seminar in the framework of the conference

On March 6, at 19.00, a seminar will be held on the problem of the development of the server side of web solutions that have reached a high load.

The event is held on the eve of the RIT-2007 conference. The class is taught by high-level specialists, program committee members and conference speakers. This seminar is the first of a large cycle of studies designed to share knowledge with leading experts of the Russian Internet segment.

The main focus is to create a community of developers and businessmen, where discussions on the development of the domestic software market in the Internet development sector will be held in a convenient form. This seminar will feature an introductory lecture on the technical challenges faced by any major project on the web. In addition to the lecture, there will be an opportunity in an open dialogue to discuss key issues of your concerns regarding the design and development of a server solution.

The lesson plan will cover the following issues:
  • Tuning the OS, fine tuning the web server, network and database
    Code and development culture
    Scaling and clustering

    The cost of participation is 800 rubles . The classroom will be held in the Extropolis conference center at 9 Trekhprudny Lane, Moscow, Bulletin-1 Hall.
    More information you can find on the seminar page http://rit2007.ru/class/924.html
