The second Ukrainian forum of Internet leaders Iforum (report + photos) was held in Kiev

I have just arrived from the second forum of Internet figures and I want to share a little of what I heard and a little more seen.

The forum is held for the second time, the first time was last year and the second time it gathers the most active audience and the largest number of interesting speakers. This is explained simply, unfortunately at the moment it is the only event of this format where you can hear something useful for yourself and meet interesting people. The rest of such conferences held in Kiev have long been turned into a platform for self-promotion, where companies advertise with each other, as there are fewer regular users.

The entire forum was divided into 4 streams (business, advertising, technology, and Web 2.0) and at first it was quite difficult to get people to disperse into the other sections. As it turned out not in vain, most of the most interesting was in the central section about the Internet business.

According to information from Alexey Masya, the event was attended by about 2,500 people, for comparison, there were about 1,000 last year.
And what did they do there?

The event began with the performance of Anton Nosik, known as dolboeb, before that I heard about him a lot of interesting things, so I expected to hear something unusual.
