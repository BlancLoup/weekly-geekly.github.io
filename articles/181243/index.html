Getting access to new Google maps without invites

A few days ago I requested an invitation for new Google maps, but so far I have not received it. I did not put up with this and began to look for ways to access the cards by other means. As usual, in such cases, access to new Google services can be obtained by adding yourself special cookies.
So, in order to gain access, you need to register yourself such a cookie:
