Clever roulette will help not to be mistaken with the size of clothes when buying from an online store



When buying clothes in the online store, there is often a problem - the goods received do not fit in size. Buyers often struggle with this by ordering the same thing in two or three sizes. After fitting, they keep for themselves what came up and return the rest to the store. Of course, you must first pay for things. Yes, and with the return may have problems.

On the other hand, for shops this practice is also inconvenient and expensive. If you want to offer good service, you have to take the goods back. And he seems to be sold. It is necessary to check the returned goods for damage, deposit it in the warehouse, transfer money to the buyer.

The Italian company Xyze, which created the clever roulette ON, wants to solve this problem.

With the help of a tape measure, a person can fix his basic dimensions, which are important for choosing clothes, such as chest volume, waist or hips. Roulette transmits the measurement results in a special application, Xyze ID. The application also tells you where to measure correctly.



Xyze ID translates all measurement values ‚Äã‚Äãinto international standards so that you can buy clothes for yourself even in Europe, even in Asia.



If the store where the product is ordered, supports the interface of this device, then the data will be transferred directly to its system and the size of the clothes will be automatically selected. Already, the application contains several of the most popular online stores.



Roulette itself is not much different from the usual one. Flexible measuring tape has a length of 160 centimeters (this is enough, even those whose dimensions differ from the reference 90-60-90). 3 volt battery lasts for 2 years of work.



Actually, everything is not simple, but very simple, while also practical. The roulette price is about 40 euros - now the developers are trying to raise funds on Indiegogo , but they say that the device will hit the market anyway.
