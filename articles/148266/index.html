Corky

In December last (2011), before Christmas, the type | depot type foundry (which is from Sofia in Bulgaria) a free distribution was announced (under the terms of CC BY BYND 3.0 license ) of the new Corki header family of four faces: Corki, Corki Rounded, Corki Tuscan, Corki Rounded Tuscan.

Each of these fonts contains 134 characters: the Latin alphabet, the Cyrillic alphabet, eight characters of a hand with pointing finger (four directions: up, right, down, left - and two variants of the hand: the back or palm of the hand to the reader), numbers, different characters ( including inside circles), various arrowheads.

The two Rounded styles are different from the other two by the roundness of the outer corners of the letters.

The two Tuscan styles are distinguished from the other two by the presence of grooves in serifs in places where the vertical or diagonal line of the letter fits on the opposite side.
