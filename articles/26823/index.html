Electricity from heat. Option from ElectraTherm

ElectraTherm has developed a prototype of an electric generator that uses heat as a raw material for generating electricity. Thus, the Green Machine (and so called the generator in ElectraTherm) can immediately solve two problems: obtaining cheap electricity (three to four cents per kW / hour) and recycling industrial waste, which includes heat.


Another advantage of the Green Machine generator is its relative, compared to conventional heat turbines, simplicity of design and, accordingly, low cost. Well, do not forget that for energy with it does not require burning fossil fuels. Those. It is a source of renewable energy.

In general, ElectraTherm announced its development very timely. The US government believes that the generation of electricity through the utilization of industrial heat will soon be able to surpass all other renewable energy sources. So, when ElectraTherm starts mass production of its Green Machine in mid-June, customers are likely to find them.
