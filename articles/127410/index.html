Complaints about problems with QIWI-wallets



QIWI is the most innovative IT company in Russia. Recently, she made a real breakthrough, offering new convenient services and technologies. In any corner of the country you can find a blue or orange terminal with the QIWI logo.

Unfortunately, the flip side of the introduction of new services is the emergence of new errors inevitably arising in advanced products. Security failures in payment systems are particularly sensitive, and the popularity of the system can turn such a crash into a nightmare.

On the forum of a specialized provider portal nag.ru , an angry topic of a single user has recently appeared who has a substantial amount of money from a QIWI wallet. It turned out that QIWI experts read the topic, and, after some altercations, the issue was resolved in favor of the victim, which indirectly indicates the company's problem.

However, after some time, the topic got into the search engines and the offended and injured were pulled into it in the hope of help. Below, the original citations of the victims, the grammar and punctuation in them are preserved!
