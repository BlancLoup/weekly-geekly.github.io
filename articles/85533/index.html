ASUS ROG Ares Specs and Images

For more than a month there have been rumors about the release by ASUS of the world's most powerful video card - ASUS ROG Ares. As expected, the video card is based on two overclocked GPU RV870. The frequency of the chip will be 850MHz, and the memory will operate at 1200 MHz just like the single-chip sibling. Memory for one video card is an incredible amount: 4 gigabytes.

If we already assumed the characteristics, then the most important thing to look at today is: the graphics card model in 3d.




The cooling system resembles the cooling of the second generation GeForce GTX 295 video cards and occupies almost three slots. The power of the video card is supported by 8-pin + 8-pin + 6-pin connectors. The device as well as ASUS ROG Mars will be released in a limited edition, with an exorbitant cost. In 3DMark Vantage, the original 5970, Ares is nearly 30% ahead.



Now we are waiting for the test results from enthusiasts, and the prices of this monster.
