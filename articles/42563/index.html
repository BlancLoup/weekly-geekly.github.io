Now the GeForce 9400M is not only officially available to Apple.

NVIDIA has officially unveiled a new GeForce 9400M laptop graphics processor for everyone . It is an integrated solution in a single chip, characterized by high performance. Previously, this processor was only available to Apple, which used it in its MacBook, MacBook Pro and MacBook Air. Now, all other notebook manufacturers can count on deliveries of the 9400M.

This new graphics chip has 16 cores. Calculation speed - 54 gigaflops. Nvidia claims that it is five times more productive than the video embedded in the Intel GM45 chipset (Centrino 2). But he also, if you believe the words of nVidia's chief vice president, Jeff Fisher, is frugal in terms of energy consumption. That allows you to play on a laptop for a long time or fully watch a movie in high definition resolution.

I especially liked the fact that nVidia speaks about new chips: it was tested and approved by Apple.
