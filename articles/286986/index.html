The Bortnik Fund plans to invest up to 6 million rubles in a Mobiforce startup

According to Rusbase.vc, the Russian startup Mobiforce received a grant from the Bortnik Fund in the amount of 1 million rubles under the START-2015 program. Within the framework of the program, the project may receive up to 6 million rubles within 3 years.

The selection criteria for the winners of the program are :

1. Scientific novelty
2. Relevance of the idea
3. Technical value of products
4. Plan for the implementation of ideas in the final product
5. The prospect of commercialization
6. Evaluation of their capabilities
7. passion for the idea
