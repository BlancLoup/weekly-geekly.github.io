The free Kyiv application has been updated with the Kyiv poster, now there is a map and places nearby

The application from the site Gloss.ua contains a full poster of the events of Kiev for every day + a detailed database of city establishments http://gloss.ua/iphone .

In the new version added binding institutions to the GPS-coordinates. The application will show which cafes, restaurants, cinemas are located near the place where you are. It will also let you know in which street, at what address there are exhibitions, museums, restaurants, cinemas, night clubs.

Switching from the ‚ÄúMap‚Äù tab to the ‚ÄúList‚Äù tab, you can see which establishments are nearby within a radius of 300, 500, 700, 1000 meters.

The new version adds the ability to go from the institution's profile directly to the card and made the phone numbers active. You can immediately call to clarify the time movie shows, book tickets or tables in restaurants.

From other useful features:
Cinema: the schedule of all film shows, the coordinates of cinemas, detailed descriptions of movies in the box office.

Restaurants: a complete and detailed database of Kiev restaurants, filtering by city districts, options (for example, wi-fi, hookah), cuisines and prices.

Theater: playbill, announcements of performances, profiles of each theater, cast of actors, scenes of performances.

Music: event poster, coordinates of concert venues and institutions ala Docker Pub.

Exhibitions: poster of exhibitions, information about all Kiev museums and galleries.

Nightlife: a nightclub database, event schedules, DJ profiles.

Direct download link on the App Store http://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/gloss-ua/id339003573?mt=8

Over the past few months have received a lot of feedback on the application, most of the wishes implemented. There are already about 2,000 downloads, mostly, of course, from Kiev.
