OT Routes - 8 New Cities and a Pedestrian Update

Today, Yandex.Maps for six Russian and two Ukrainian cities have earned a service for building routes on public transport.

We will name all new cities where it has become more convenient to navigate around the city on buses, trolley buses and trams: Vladivostok , Orenburg , Dnepropetrovsk , Tolyatti , Kirov , Kostroma , Ryazan and Nikolaev .

Here is a complete list of cities where the service of building routes on public transport works.
