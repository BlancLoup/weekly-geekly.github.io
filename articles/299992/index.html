Sales of devices on the VR market will be $ 895 million



So far, only the Oculus Rift is an important player on the VR market, but this year several virtual reality helmets should come out at once. Moreover, the cost of the Oculus Rift kit is $ 600, which is quite expensive for a device in a new segment. One of the most awaited decisions should be the PlayStation VR virtual reality headset, which will be released later this year. Taking into account new solutions on the market, Analytics Analytics suggests that sales of new devices will exceed more than $ 895 million in 2016.

Almost 77% of the profits from sales in this segment should come from Oculus, HTC and Sony, but by the number of devices sold, these brands will occupy only 13% of the market. Total sales will be approximately 12.8 million copies, but the vast majority of devices sold will be in the budget segment of mobile devices.

It is in the mobile sector that sales primarily depend on proper marketing, which fully compensates for an insufficient amount of ready-made content along with a relatively weak technical implementation.

Sales of devices of the highest price category, designed for PC or consoles, will be only 1.7 million. Nevertheless, smartphones should be a potential factor in the growth of sales of devices in this price segment. According to analysts from Strategy Analytics, mobile devices should potentially become a link that will attract new users to this niche and subsequently acquire virtual reality helmets for the console or PC.
