Competition Project Anarchy Mobile Game Dev Challenge

Intel has opened the acceptance of applications for the Project Anarchy Mobile Game Dev Challenge - a mobile games contest with a grand prize of $ 100,000. For the second place they will give $ 25,000, and for the third - $ 15,000.



The competition will last until May 31, 2014, and the winners will be announced in June, but those who have time before February will be able to get feedback from the Havok support team. In addition, developers will be able to get marketing and PR support, which, for example, is expressed in the presentation of games at the Havok booth at GDC 2014.
