Daylight saving time begins on the second Sunday of March at 02:00 in the USA and Canada, and back - at 02:00 on the first Sunday of November. Not only Hawaii, Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands.

In the middle of March for two weeks we change the schedule of students from Russia and European countries, since at the end of March, the time in Europe and the USA with Canada will be correlated.

This usually happens on the night from Saturday to Sunday, but before that, on Friday, Israel switches to summer time. Since on Saturday night there is a religious Sabbath.

Accordingly, we must make small shifts for Friday lessons before the global shift for 500 students on Sunday.

Many Skype schools probably use some built-in systems for automatic transfer of time and notifications for students and teachers, but I cannot imagine how automatic systems can be used in our case.

Since each student needs an individual approach. For example, one student can take classes late at night, while others can not concentrate at 18.00.

Despite the fact that we are turning upside down and asking other students to move, some of the students have to change teachers every time.

This means the organization of additional test lessons, psychological discomfort and disruption of the educational process.

Students and teachers usually attach to each other and do not easily agree to replacements.

In March 2019, all EU member states last switched to summer time, and by October of next year, each EU state will have to decide for itself whether it will remain in summer time or switch to winter time.

It looks like this innovation will add us a headache.

In addition, the Russian government all the time makes proposals to return to daylight saving time.
