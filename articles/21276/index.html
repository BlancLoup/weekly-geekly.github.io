Started rating TOP-100 web studios 2008


Hi, habra people! Yesterday Tagline started the next, third in a row, rating "TOP-100 of the leading web studios Runet 2008" . Now there is a stage of preliminary registration of representatives of web studios.

Last year, the project caused a lot of controversy and discussion. This year, apparently, the organizers adjusted the methodology, added new criteria, and also plan to build a number of local ratings for the largest cities of Russia and the CIS: St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Nizhny Novgorod, Yekaterinburg, Samara, Omsk, Kazan, Chelyabinsk, Vladivostok , Perm, Ufa, Krasnoyarsk, Irkutsk, Kiev, Minsk.

The project, as I understand it, is still one of the few who are trying to somehow analyze the situation on the site development market and give some quantitative assessments of the situation ‚Äî market volume, number of players, segmentation, technologies, etc.

Also this year, an evaluation of the work of studios from TOP-20 on the quality of work was announced.


The meaning of the rating and the method of construction is simple:

In the questionnaire on market leaders, respondents will be asked to name 10 market leaders (this year, according to two criteria - the company's turnover and its level of fame), giving each of them a rating score from 1 to 5 (5 - maximum score), as well as giving an opportunity to comment. own choice. The TOP-100 rating will be built by summing up the points scored by the studio for all profiles.

Independent experts this year include Ryzhikov (1C-Bitrix), Ramensky (ROCIT), Andreev (Webplanet), Filev (Rambler), Synods (Roem), Smirnov (TelNews), etc.
