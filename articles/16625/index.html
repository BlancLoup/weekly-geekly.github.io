ISpidy online web services aggregator

Good day.

This post is published at the request of the user goldeneye . Therefore, if you decide to add karma, then do it to him. If you decide to reduce karma, then do it to both of us (for uninteresting material to me, because I decided to publish it).

So, here is a startup that recently appeared on the network.

The service combines the main features of popular blogs, social networks, online photo albums and video services, such as LiveJournal, LiveInternet, Blogger, Picasa, Youtube. iSpidy improves usability and efficiency by providing a convenient editor, a single interface to services, and simple cross-posting capabilities.

Main features of the system:

  • Powerful wysiwyg text editor that supports insertion of photos, videos, flash games and flash animations, as well as special livejournal tags
  • Simple crossposting posts to various blogs
  • Convenient online photo album publishing on their blogs
  • Reading user messages, as well as its friendslongs, in a single list, organized chronologically
  • iSpidy is a news aggregator and allows you to read news feeds of any format (rss / atom)
  • Displays all friends of the user in the common list
  • Conveniently ordered chronologically view photo albums


    • Personally, I tried this service and I can say that it is quite convenient. The only thing that worries me is that you need to communicate your data (username and password) to a third-party service. For example, having reported the account data on a blogger, I, in fact, will give access to my Adsense account. The same goes for other services. If it were not for this circumstance, then I would definitely become a regular user of the new iSpidy service.
