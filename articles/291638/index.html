Xiaomi has concluded an agreement on the distribution of mobile devices in Russia

The Chinese mobile device manufacturer Xiaomi has entered into an agreement with the Russian company Marvel-Distribution . She will start selling the first batch of Chinese devices in the coming days. For now, only Mi Pad tablets will be sold. They have undergone compulsory certification, and now in Russia their warranty and service maintenance will be carried out.

Previously, Xiaomi tablets Russians could buy only through the Internet. The price of the device ranges from 13.5 thousand to 19 thousand rubles, depending on the configuration. Popular models Asus, NVIDIA and Samsung have similar characteristics, but they are more expensive - from 18, from 24 and from 25 thousand rubles, respectively.

Distribution agreement was concluded in the spring. But it was not reported, as they were waiting for the delivery of the first batch of goods, representative of Marvel-Distribution Yulia Salagaeva explained to RBC.

"Megamind" wrote that in 2015, Xiaomi plans to increase sales of smartphones by 30%. Such a statement was made by Lei Yong, executive director of the company during the congress of people's representatives of China. The company decided to increase its influence on foreign markets: in Russia and in Brazil.

According to company forecasts, revenues will exceed 100 billion yuan ($ 16 billion). Last year, sales of their smartphones grew by more than 100% to 74.3 billion yuan. It sold 61.1 million devices. This year, Xiaomi plans to sell 80 to 100 million smartphones.

And in the fourth quarter of 2014, the company ranked fifth among global manufacturers of smartphones, having received 4.4% of the market, reports IDC.

Recall that at the end of 2014, Xiaomi has become the most expensive technology startup in the world. At the same time, the company's value is growing on the expectations of investors, who believe that Xiaomi will be able to turn customers of its smartphones into customers who pay for various services of the same developer.
