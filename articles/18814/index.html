Question 1: Who needs it at all?
Answer : Buyers. In the presence of large amounts of such information, the selection process for the purchase of a product can be very convenient and fast. Question 2: Why has this not happened before?
Answer : There are plenty of websites where people could leave feedback on their possessions, but only within the scope of the site. But, on these sites the emphasis is on several other points: news on the topic, articles, reviews, tests ... but, again, only on the specifics of the site. The main theme of the site on possessions is directly the possessions of people themselves. Not only mobile phones, not just computer equipment, but any (ideally, all that he has, and which can appear in his life in the future) possessions of any person. All this and combine on one site. Indeed, in its pure form in the RuNet nothing like this existed.

Question 3: Privacy, security, how with these concepts?
Answer : That's right, these and similar questions can and should arise. As soon as it comes to people's possessions and property, issues of ethical, privacy and protection are raised immediately. This is the main thing! By the way, as always in all media, not just on the Internet.

Here is what should be considered when organizing the work of such an Internet service:

1. The inadmissibility of viewing personal information about the owner without his permission.
2. The inaccessibility of viewing the entire list of possessions of an individual;
3. Organization of the website with the support of secure protocols;
4. The inaccessibility of viewing information on possessions without registration and authorization on the site;
5. Hard moderation;
6. Operational communication with the administration of the resource, both on general and technical issues;
7. Hosting a reputable hosting provider;
8. Sophisticated regulations and rules of the website, consistent with the law;
9. Perhaps cooperation with insurance companies. Offer users to insure their property.
