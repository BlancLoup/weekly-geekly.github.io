The Windows Store apps I use everyday, part 1

best application reference guide

As you all know, yesterday Microsoft released Windows 8. I use Windows 8 from the earliest version and felt the development of both the operating system and the application store. In this article I will tell about those applications from the Windows Store, which I use daily. I hope this guide will help beginners.

I have Windows 8 installed on 5 different PCs: a stationary desktop that is already 6 years old, a two-year-old Acer Iconia Tab W500 tablet, a three-year-old Dell laptop, a relatively new working laptop Lenovo W510 and a new MacBook Air 11 '' (2012 model). On each of these different-looking devices, all the applications presented work equally well: on the tablet, I use the touchpad, on the PC with the mouse, on the laptops touchpad.

The article contains a list of the best in my opinion Windows 8 applications that any user of this system with a Russian account can install on their PC. Almost all applications have support for the unique features of Windows 8 ‚Äî live tiles, search contracts, or files.

This is the first part of the guide, in the sequel I will talk about numerous applications for popular services like Film Search, clients of online services like Wikipedia and others. In the final third part there will be a big story about numerous games for the Windows Store.

Skype




Skype needs no introduction. I can only say that under Windows 8 it looks just fine.

MetroTwit




MetroTwit may be familiar to Windows users by desktop client. Now it is for Windows 8.

Analogs: Twitro - use less, Rowi - almost never use.



Mobli




Mobli is a cool Instagram-like service that allows you to share videos and videos with friends. The service has applications for iPhone and Android, now for Windows 8. By the way, the client is very beautiful.

Mail, Calendar, People, Messages




This set of applications is included in the standard delivery of Windows 8. The applications are very convenient, I use my email client for my personal account on Outlook.com. I use the calendar least of all, as the calendar is in my work mail, and here it only shows my friends' birthdays. It is convenient to use Messages in the snap-mode (last screenshot) when replying to messages and working on this article, for example. The People app aggregates and connects all contacts of social networks and Skype.

Fliptoast




FlipToast is a universal client for social networks: Twitter, Facebook, LInkedIn and Instagram (view only). The client is very beautiful and functional, just nice to use, flipping through the message feed.

Kindle




Kindle needs no introduction. I recommend to all users of Kindle devices. The application is functional, constantly updated. Today there is only one complaint to it - the pages are scrolling slowly, which is strange.

Analog: Kobo is an e-book store client, there is a small selection of books in it for my taste.



Photos and Camera




Another standard application that aggregates many sources of your photos: local collections, SkyDrive, Flickr, Facebook, external media. Live Tile twists pictures and photos.

Standard Camera application allows you to work with the camera, take photos and shoot video.

Lync




Lync is a client to the corporate communications system of the same name. If your company uses Lync for instant messaging and video conferencing, be sure to check it out. In another case, the application is useless for you.

Skydrive




SkyDrive is another standard Microsoft application, a client for cloud file storage. I highly recommend the SkyDrive service and the application itself, which integrates into file access contracts and allows you to access files in the cloud from any application in Windows 8.

Analogs: Yandex.Disk (at the time of writing this article has disappeared from the store) - an excellent client and also with a file access contract! Box.com is a client for the cloud file storage service of the same name.



Muz.ru Amuze




Muz.ru Amuze is a client for a music service that gives you free legal streaming of our and foreign music for every taste.

Evernote




Evernote needs no introduction. Install. Although, personally, I prefer to use OneNote.

4th & Square




4th & Square is an unofficial client for Foursquare. Constantly updated. From pleasant - notifications when you came to a new place that you can make a check. From not very pleasant - a rustic interface.

News bento




News Bento is a convenient and beautiful RSS feed reader and for my taste is the best client for Google Reader. In the presence of a set of popular resources for those who do not have their tapes and uchetki in Google Reader. Separate tapes can be taken out in live tiles in Start.

Tom's Hardware




Convenient official client to the popular resource Tom's Hardware. Lots of authentic material and interesting news.

Analogs: AppyGeek is a technical news aggregator, it is convenient to select one topic and put it on a live tile. The Register is the official client for a popular resource.



News@Mail.ru




To the delight of a cool and beautiful official client for the service of News@Mail.ru. Put it boldly!

Bing News




News client from Bing, very comfortable and beautiful. Use is a pleasure. I chose the USA region for news, there are many cool content providers.

Analogs: BBS News Mobile (unofficial) is a beautiful client, copies some ideas from Bing News. News 360 (at the time of writing it disappeared from the Store) is a unique Russian news personalization service that has gained worldwide fame. Sky News is the official client of the popular news channel.



Bing




The search engine Bing is almost unknown in Russia, but in the USA it already occupies more than 30% of the market. Bing.com chip can be called daily background pictures, videos and even 360-degree panoramas. In the application, this feature is inherited, you can scroll through the background images and download them to your computer.

Analog: Google - the first official Google app for Windows Store.



Bing Weather




Weather app from Bing. Very beautiful and informative.

Analogs: Gismeteo - the official application of the Russian service. WeatherFlow is a beautiful application from CocktailFlow authors with animated weather backgrounds.



Bing Sport




Sports lovers in general - is dedicated .

Bing travels




The app from Bing for traveling and interested in other cities and countries. There are 360-degree panoramas of beautiful corners of the world.

Finance




Cool application from Bing to monitor the stock quotes of various exchanges around the world. Interested in the state of the company? Install safely. Preferred list for monitoring IT companies in the screenshot above.

The engadget




IT audience to represent Engadget does not make sense. Others should know that this is one of the most popular resources on the Internet on the topic of IT technologies. Swing boldly official client !

Analogs: The New Web is a popular news site.



USA Today




The official USA USA app of the same name media publishing house in the Windows Store appeared one of the first and was and remains one of the most beautiful. Just worth a look!

Analogs: The Wall Street Journal , The New York Times



Music and Video




In countries where Xbox Music and Xbox Video services are officially launched, these applications have access to online content streaming services. For Russia, at the present time, these are simply media players of files on a computer.

The uniqueness of the Video application is provided by streaming video on the Xbox with a single button. Very comfortably!

Analogs: Multimedia 8 is a great player. Press Play: Video is also a cool player.



IVI.ru




IVI.ru is a Russian service for accessing video on demand. The client gives free access to a bunch of movies.

Tune.in




Official client for access service to dozens of radio stations in the world.

Tuba.fm




The official client to the service of streaming music of choice, depending on the genre or artist. Very nice.

Analogs: 7digital music store.



CyberLink PowerDVD Mobile




This is a very functional player and media cataloger from a reputable developer. Today, this is probably the only app in the Store that understands the mkv format. However, the high price will scare away many (for free for buyers of the desktop version).

There are some more quality apps from CyberLink in the Windows Store: YouCam Mobile, PowerDirector Mobile.

Ashampoo ImageFX




Simple image editor .

YouTube player




There are already a lot of unofficial YouTube players with search and catalog published in the Store. I found this cute, sometimes use.

Evernote Skitch




The official client for the popular service from Evernote.
