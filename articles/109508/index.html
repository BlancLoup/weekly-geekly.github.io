mini NB520: netbook with premium audio system Harman / Kardon

Do you often pay attention to the sound quality when working with a netbook? Do you know which audio system is used in your device? The recently announced new mini NB520 has become the world's first netbook equipped with a built-in premium speaker system Harman / Kardon. We are waiting for a new product on sale already in the first quarter of next year, but for now we offer to admire the device‚Äôs photos and video releases.

The sleek mini body of the NB520 attracts the eye thanks to its brilliant rubberized surfaces and the dotted texture.


The mini NB520 is one of the thinnest and lightest netbooks on the market, based on Intel Atom processors. It would seem that this can be limited, but Toshiba went further and, in fact, suggested sound quality and volume level, which are usually not associated with small-sized systems.


The sound quality offered by the built-in Harman / Kardon stereo speakers with a power of 2 x 2 W makes you forget that a netbook is in the user's hands. The speaker system supports Dolby Advanced AudioTM technology, which provides natural sound when playing music and watching movies. In addition, the netbook supports playback of movies in HD format (with a resolution of 720p). The Sleep and Music technology allows you to connect an MP3 player to the mini NB520 and listen to music on Harman / Kardon speakers without turning on the computer.

Available in a choice of colors - blue, brown, lime green and orange.
