Pebble Time - new Pebble smart watches with color E-Paper display



Today Pebble announced a new model of smart watches Pebble Time and at the same time launched a crowdfunding campaign on Kickstarter to collect money for the first batch. Supported companies will be able to get watches for $ 159.

It took the company less than 30 minutes to collect the required $ 500,000 - an absolute record for Kickstarter.

The main feature of the novelty is a constantly working color display. The Pebble Time case is made of aluminum, and the display is protected by Gorilla Glass. Despite 20% more thin case in comparison with original Pebble battery life remained the same - 5-7 days.



Among other innovations: a simple replacement of the straps, a microphone for quick response to notifications and a port for connecting additional sensors. Their production will be engaged in third-party manufacturers.



Pebble Time will be released on an updated version of Pebble OS with more convenient delivery of information and notifications. At the same time, support for previously developed interfaces and applications will remain. The clock will connect to devices on Android and iOS.

Pebble Time can be bought on the site Madrobots.ru for 14,990 rubles.
