Application of GSM gateway Termit Sprut Universal

Why do we use this or that device? The answer is simple: the human desire to simplify your life and save yourself. The described multifunctional analog cellular gateway (sometimes called GSM-bridge) is no exception.

Termit Sprut Universal has the main function of a GSM gateway - the implementation of high-quality communication. It can work with pulse and tone dialing. Can be used for billing. It has an internal subscriber list for 300 users. May collect statistics on calls. It has the ability to adjust the sound volume and the system of echo suppression.

For example, you open a business: a shop, office, gas station, warehouse, production. To build new communications or rent existing ones is very expensive. And if you have a GSM network, it will be enough to choose a SIM card of a local operator, purchase Termit Sprut Universal and connect an ordinary telephone to this gateway. And you will instantly have a telephone office with minimal investment.

Moreover, even in cases of poor reception of a cellular signal in the room where the gateway is installed, it is possible to connect an external amplifying antenna.

If your business is temporary or constantly moving, you do not need to build telecommunications in each place and spend money. The gateway can be used both for reservation (in order not to remain without communication) and to replace existing telecommunications.

In addition to the main functions of the GSM gateway, this device has: an FXO port for connecting a city line, a CallBack function, a conference. It can forward, route, and filter calls. Which in turn leads to cost savings.


With proper planning (analytical mind and direct hands), you can fully use the telephone for less money. For example: the tariff for a SIM card inserted with Sprut Universal is lower than yours or is unlimited. Then you can make it so that with certain actions the gateway will call you back (CallBack function).

After connecting, you can call another mobile phone using the conference function, switch to the FXS or FXO port. Through the FXS port, the gateway can be connected to a telephone or an office PBX. Through the FXO Sprut Universal port you can connect to an existing PSTN.

Another Sprut allows you to transfer data using it as a GPRS-modem.
