GoPro Camera Mounts


It is this GoPro model that comes with one large suction cup with a dual-axis swivel head, as well as 5 mounts on 3M double-sided tape: 3 pieces for flat surfaces and 2 for curved ones. In addition, there are 3 easy-detachable mechanisms for attaching the camera to 3M-fasteners, two ordinary and one curved. They also provide additional. rotary mechanisms that can be fastened together and for rotation in two planes.


The suction cup is very comfortable and holds tight, but only on perfectly smooth surfaces such as glass or a car body. If there is the smallest texture, such as laminated chipboard, then over time the suction cup is lost and the camera falls.


3M-mount allows you to install the camera inside the car, and on the outer surfaces of the car or motorcycle, you can even helmet:


Of course, 3M mounts are consumable and will quickly become unusable, but you can buy an extra $ 4 per piece. In our realities, it is easier and cheaper to buy not fastenings, but a sturdy mounting double-sided tape.

According to the description, this sticky tape 3M reliably glues, but then it is easily peeled off with a slight heating, for example, with a hair dryer :)

If necessary, purchase additional attachments - on the head, on the chest, etc. :)
