Launchlist for your site

Hello, dear Habravchane.
We want to publish the result of our small research.

The essence of the experiment

There are quite a few sites, including regional ones, where people discuss the work of web-design studios, as well as the sites they have developed.
Sometimes such discussions lead to popping opponents with poop about implementation details.
Agree, it is a shame when a large (not for one month!) Work is carried to school by critics, because of little things in usability, laziness, or simply because of a small forgetfulness of the developer.

We have monitored dozens of similar discussions over the past few months and revealed the following ...

The main claims that are made to the sites are very easily formalized.

So, we offer you a Launchlist compiled by us , which will help you to prevent annoying mistakes and control the quality of the project.

Of course, the idea is not new. However, we could not find complete and detailed Launch-lists in the public domain, and we set about creating our own.

This launch list contains requirements for design, layout, usability and functionality that should be met in most cases. Some requirements are not particularly critical, others are required. The list was formed on the basis of our own experience and analysis of messages on many sites where other projects are discussed. For specific projects, we create shorter launch lists based on this.

We welcome your additions.
