Watching IP-TV from Rostelecom on the computer

Many use digital television provided by various Internet service providers. But few, for one reason or another, have more than one set-top box. What to do if you want to watch your favorite channel, if the TV is busy firmly and for a long time? The answer is in this article.



When moving to a new place of residence, first of all, I connected the Internet package and interactive television from Rostelecom.
Rostelecom employees installed their favorite Sagemcom F @ st2804 router and Motorola VIP1003 set-top box.
The connection diagram was originally as follows:



On the router, the first of the four available LAN ports was configured as a WAN, the second was set up by a bridge (let's say, the pass-through channel between the first and second ports), and on the third and fourth ports the Internet was heard. The first port was connected cable from the provider, the second - a TV set-top box, and the third and fourth ports were occupied by two desktop computers.

This solution had one very serious drawback: if the router was hanging, then television was also cut short. It was necessary to restart the router and wait about a minute before establishing the connection.

After another hang of the router as an experiment, the set-top box was connected to the network via a switch, bypassing the router:

And here I am again faced with a nuisance: having two parallel connections, the computer becomes confused where its internet is and stops opening pages.

At random, I determined that television is really far and deep, which IP address is in the network settings. You can specify for example 192.168.137.1/255.255.255.0. The local network will no longer be defined as a home network, and internet connection will again become available.

Do not forget to select the network in the player settings again.

If the laptop has no shortage of network devices, then you will need a second network card to watch television on your desktop computer.
