Accelerator StartupMonthly (USA, California) will hold the Olympics in Moscow

Startup Monthly is a business accelerator from Silicon Valley. Its representatives travel around the world to host Startup Olympics . The winners of each regional tour will have the opportunity to participate in the international startup tournament Startup Year . A series of events, which will last from March 29 to April 7, will be the first such event in Moscow.

A distinctive feature of Startup Monthly is its own event format. In the Moscow session will be presented two of them.

  • Ice breaker workshop
    • For whom: Business founders and technical specialists, start-ups with and without ideas.
    • What to do: Meet each other, learn how to properly voice your ideas and form a team for the further implementation of the project.
    • To the winner: The best teams will be able to participate in the Pitch Accelerator Workshop, get training from the best mentors of the Startup Monthly school, test their team in the battle of start-ups and possibly become the best project to receive free training and further investments in Silicon Valley.
    • When: March 29 and 31.
  • Pitch Accelerator Workshop
    • For whom: IT-entrepreneurs who have a prototype of their project, and the best selected teams from Ice Breaker Workshop.
    • What to do: Learn how to competently develop the business model of your project, make a 5-minute presentation for the investor, which will convince the feasibility of investing in the project. You are also waited by a seminar of one of the most successful mentors of Silicon Valley, Nathan Gold, the founder of The Demo Coach, the author of more than 15,000 startup presentations.
    • To the winner: Startups with a ready-made prototype of the project will be able to take part in the battle of startups, following which the jury will select one of the most viable projects, the team of which will receive free training at Startup Monthly school (USA, California) and the opportunity to invest in a project in Silicon Valley.
    • When: April 5 and 7.
