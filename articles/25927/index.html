By the nature of my work, I regularly monitor interesting Web 2.0 startups in runet. When choosing startups, which can be attributed to the spring of 2008, I was guided by the presence of references to the startup on specialized resources during this period, as well as functional readiness.

BestPersons.ru



The computer allows you to solve problems that before the invention of the computer did not exist. Similarly, the BestPersons service allows you to solve a problem that did not exist before the invention of social networks - combining dozens of accounts on other social networks. About two years ago we would have said that the problem was sucked from the finger. Now in my password manager there is a special daddy - ‚ÄúSocial Networks‚Äù, which contains dozens of elements. Yes, it turns into a nightmare: if friends, then immediately everywhere, if you post, then immediately in LiveJournal, in Liveinternet, and in Blogspot, and in a separate blog. The idea to make a universal business card in the network, where everyone could post information about themselves was vital for a long time, but BestPersons.ru chose the most correct and relevant approach - the aggregation of already existing information.



Investment Information: according to rumors> 500 thousand USD
Possible startup problems: it is likely that the portal will be used as a means of searching for information about users, and registered users will rarely visit the portal, which will lead to a lack of a regular audience and an increase in the budget for promotion.
Ways to solve problems: the introduction of a large number of own social services
Probable monetization model: advertising, due to the add. services. Successful monetization will require attracting a large number of participants.
