Interview with Nikita Sherman (Odnoklassniki.Ru)

Less than a couple of weeks, as Nikita Sherman, the current head of all classmates, sent us the answers to almost all the questions asked to him . Moreover, Nikita chose the best of them and is ready to give the author some valuable prize :) We read:

What did you think when you were invited to head Odnoklassniki? How long have you thought about the offer?
When I received an offer to head Odnoklassniki, I was just thinking about where to go with my family to relax in the summer. Of course, such decisions are not made in one day, so there was a rather long period of negotiations. The decision was made shortly after returning from vacation.

We are equally loyal to absolutely all users. Moreover, we are not forcing anyone to pay anything, this is a purely voluntary matter. There has not been a single restriction for existing free services. On the contrary, we propose to increase the limits for those who are willing to pay for it. The range of free services, of course, will actively develop. I will say even more, next year the most attention will be paid to the additional free functionality. Much will be trite redone and will become more convenient, there will be new sections and services.
