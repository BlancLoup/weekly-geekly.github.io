The robot that will overtake you (video)

Hi, Habr!

Quadcopters and other cars - this is yesterday. it's time to run! Enthusiasts raise money for the mass production of OutRunner, a runner robot with many legs. The bar is set at $ 150,000. Robots will be available in two versions that can run on different surfaces, including grass, dirt and asphalt.



The basic OutRunner Core model is equipped with one and a half feet and weighs three pounds. Six legs will accelerate the robot to 10 miles per hour.

Too slow? Choose OutRunner Performance: This model is equipped with an HD camera and is capable of running at a speed of 20 miles per hour on its twelve robo-legs (2 feet high, weight - 5 pounds). The battery is enough for two hours of work. In addition, you can control this model using the application from a smartphone while watching video from the camera.
