Think about links

Annoyed when the link-image and the link-signature to it are two different links leading to the same place. In this case, suggesting a link-image, the link-signature, of course, is not highlighted. At this moment I start to think that perhaps the link-signature leads to another place, and move the cursor to it, and begin to compare in memory the URLs of these two links. Although you could not do this, if you hover over the image, the signature to it would also highlight.

In my practice, in 90% percent of cases, it was possible, after some thought, to make them one link, including the image in the link-signature and, then, rendering it visually using CSS .
