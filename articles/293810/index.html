Over the past year, about 10 Chinese software and hardware developers have entered the Russian market.

Since 2014, about a dozen Chinese manufacturers of telecommunications equipment and software have entered the Russian market. In addition to such large companies as Huawei and Lenovo , smaller companies are also trying to occupy free niches.

In Russia, according to Kartoteka.ru , in August 2014 LLC Inspur ru was registered. This is the representative office of the Chinese manufacturer of telecommunications equipment Inspur . The company has entered into a contract for the supply of servers at FSUE Scientific Research Institute "Voskhod" for more than $ 200 million. Voskhod is a government agency dedicated to automating the work of the authorities. Further, Inspur's partners were the system integrators I-Teco , Krok and the distributors Navikom , Merlion and Marvel Distribution . Inspur positions its products as a replacement for IBM servers. According to Gartner, in the first quarter of 2015, Inspur was in fifth place in terms of server shipments worldwide, with a market share of 3.4%.

Three months ago, the Taiwanese Askey Computer Corporation entered the Russian market. It is a subsidiary of Asus. Askey Computer develops and manufactures telecommunications equipment: routers, gateways, set-top boxes. At the end of 2014, Askey revenue amounted to 34.9 billion Taiwan dollars ($ 1.1 billion).
