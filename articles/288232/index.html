Wikileaks returns to the bunker and changes the DNS

The Wikileaks website with all secret documents is now available on the Swiss domain Wikileaks.ch . The hosting, apparently, is French ( 213.251.145.96 ). After the failure of Amazon to provide hosting services and the removal of DNS records, Wikileaks had to seek refuge in Europe.



There is also information that Wikileaks is returning to its old Swedish bunker, an air-raid shelter, which has been preserved since the days of the Cold War, and is now used as a data center by the Swedish provider Bahnhof AB ( photos ). The site is now available at http://46.59.1.2/ .
