The best search engine for your company.

Friends, this article was in the drafts a long time ago; I was encouraged to visit it by a client who was again unable to find documents from SharePoint in the catalog.



In my work practice there was a period in one organization when I formally did not manage anything, but the bosses did not want to say goodbye to me. At that time, I was the quick problem manager. He begins to introduce you to new people. If he has enough patience, then gradually your contact base grows. At some point, the situation comes that you already know all the key people, you figure out who does what, you know what to ask. If a question arises, contact the right person and learn everything from him. So here I offer an interesting alternative. If you have Bitrix24, Jive, Yammer, DaOffice or IBM Connections, then they most likely work for your entire organization and everything that people in the system do is indexed there.
