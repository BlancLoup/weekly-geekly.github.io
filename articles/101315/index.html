The agreement of television companies on the main trends of the television industry

A few weeks ago in Monte Carlo, leading television companies agreed on some major trends that are changing the face of the industry.


  • Television companies understand that their advertising business is recovering faster than expected.
  • Mobile and social media and their association are the main themes - players recognize that social media has become crucial (for most of them even more important than Google) as a source of traffic for their sites.
  • Media companies also take into account the fact that many users are more tied to their devices than their content (it‚Äôs more important for them to have an Apple iPhone than to access ESPN content)

  • Universal TV / network (as opposed to specialized) thus recognize that they need to pay more attention to the formation of brands, in particular, with a multi-platform approach, and they need to remain connected with a younger audience: HBO, MTV, ESPN models rather than NBC or BBC1
  • Now the focus is on innovative program formats and new types of content, because they represent the core competencies for players who are developing new formats on TV and associate them with new positions in entertainment and social media.
  • Business models are focused on different advertising options: advertising + paid services (subscription, fee for use); advertising plus deals (e-commerce, affiliate marketing, lead generation).
