The father of the Internet calls to prevent a split of the web.

The inventor of the web, Sir Tim Berners-Lee (Tim Berners-Lee ) addressed the annual World Wide Web conference in Edinburgh, which opened on May 23, and urged not to allow the introduction of different prices for certain content categories on the Web, reports Lenta.Ru with reference to the BBC .

According to Tim Berners-Lee, it is very important to keep the Internet united.
