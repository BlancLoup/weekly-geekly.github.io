Live Labs - Color Picker

Live Labs does a lot of interesting things, among this set there is a special gadget for sidebar whists - Coloir Picker , at first glance, a placer of strange colored circles.


When you click on a circle, the RGB code of the corresponding color is copied to the clipboard.

Palettes, you can customize by entering / adding / deleting the desired color. True, it is strange that the color can be edited only in numbers.



An interesting palette can be found by keywords through the website COLOR lovers , on which enthusiasts store, preserve and share interesting collections of colors and patterns (patterns). Here, by the way, you can edit colors through the palette :)



Palettes can be saved and loaded at any time.
