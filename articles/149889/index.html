We test Kyocera FS-C8025MFP

APPEARANCE
The device is made in a white case. Hardware parts most susceptible to pollution - auto feeder, panel
and the niche of the output tray is made of black plastic. The plastic used in the product is easy to clean.

Sophisticated ergonomics allows the use of multifunction devices in any, even limited space. So, the stapling finisher (option) is attached to the side of the machine and takes up much less space than floor finishers.
For easy operation, the MFP can be mounted on a cabinet equipped with wheels, the inner space of which is provided for storing paper. Execution under color of the device does it almost imperceptible.

PAPER FEED
The capacity of the paper trays is calculated on the basis of a maximum sheet thickness of 0.11 mm. The MFP in its basic configuration is equipped with a duplex, a 50-sheet automatic document feeder, a 500-sheet cassette and a multifunctional tray. The ability to work with A3 format and print in color on paper with density from 60 to 256 g / m2 allows you to perform
up to 80% of office tasks, including in marketing departments. Additionally, you can purchase a second cassette by increasing the paper supply to 1600 sheets.



FUNCTIONALITY
The noise level at work is comparable to the noise of desktop models. In the basic configuration, the device offers network printing, copying, network scanning and (optional) fax. Driver installation and device connection are standard and take a minimum of time.

The driver is Russified, as well as the device itself (we had a new MFP on the test with a non-Russified control panel, however, for the Russian market, the models will be completely Russified).

Management - intuitive. The device is equipped with a wide touch panel with a detailed illustrated color menu with step-by-step navigation, thanks to which the user can easily find the function he needs. The "back" function also works step by step. For the most frequently used functions, a quick access setting is proposed. Printing from USB drives is supported, for which there is an appropriate connector on the device panel.

The FS-C8025MFP prints grayscale, fine graphics, color images well. It is important that the MFP has a real toner saving mode when printing and copying, the use of which reduces toner consumption by a third. The device supports many functions of printing and copying - they are respectively selected in the driver or on the control panel.

When copying provides the ability to interrupt jobs, electronic sorting, copying
with separation, there is even the prevention of accidental overruns of toner (for example, when printing white texts on a black background). The model easily handles not only printed but also digital documentation. Can scan to PDF, compress and encrypt files. Scanned documents can be easily sent to a folder on a computer, to an FTP server, by e-mail, saved on a USB drive.

As standard, the MFP is equipped with an authentication system that provides access to documents only to a specific user, which helps maintain confidentiality. There are options for secure deletion of stored information.
