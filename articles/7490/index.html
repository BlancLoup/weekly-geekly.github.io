Astronomers have found a habitable planet

What many have been waiting for has happened!

Astronomers first discovered the planet, which by its basic parameters is very similar to Earth and may contain water in liquid form on its surface, reports the BBC.

The planet is located in the constellation Libra at a distance of twenty and a half light years from Earth. This is the smallest exoplanet (i.e., a celestial body orbiting a star outside the solar system) of all known scientists. She turns around her fading sun, called Gliese 581, for 13 days.

Gliese 581 is smaller and colder than our Sun, so the temperature on the planet roughly corresponds to that of the Earth. At the same time, it is 14 times closer to its star than the Earth to the Sun.
