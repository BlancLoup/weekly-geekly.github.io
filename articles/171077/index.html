Dennis Tito plans to send people to Mars in 2018

Another serious player joined the race for Mars.
Yesterday, a press conference was held of the recently created non-commercial foundation of the Inspiration Mars Foundation, which presented its project of a nonstop manned flight around Mars in 2018.




Let me remind you that NASA is planning a manned flight to Mars in the 30s of the 21st century, the program of the European Aurora space agency suggests a manned flight in 2033. Roscosmos is more streamlined in preparing a manned flight to Mars in the first half of the century. Until recently, government projects had a shortage of finances, leading to a reduction in previously planned programs.
Since 1990, the topic of the colonization of Mars has been actively developed by the non-profit organization The Mars Society of Robert Zubrin , which has established test scientific stations on Earth, where various aspects important for a successful mission to the Red Planet are being worked out.
Since the beginning of the century, the private company SpaceX has joined in the creation of a relatively cheap booster rocket capable of providing a flight to Mars.
In 2012, the private project of the European company Mars One , which gathered to overtake all state space agencies and send the first colonialists to Mars in 2022, became a sensation.

And now the Americans again declared the first manned flight to Mars.
This time the initiator was the American billionaire and the first space tourist Dennis Tito, who is planning a nonstop expedition to Mars in 2018. According to the plan, the spacecraft should start on January 5, 2018, reach Mars on August 20, 2018 and return to Earth on May 21, 2019.


Unlike other companies that require significant investments from outside to fly to Mars, the American multimillionaire takes the bulk of the costs and acts as a donor for the development necessary for the success of this mission. It is also planned to attract private donations and participation of charitable foundations. The flight does not involve spacesuits and the exit of the spacecraft, which requires the installation of all life support systems inside, and possibly makes the addition of external inflatable modules problematic. Whatever design is adopted, in any case the ship will be cramped. The entire apparatus must be put into orbit from one launch, which significantly limits the payload. A mission that requires more than one launch increases dramatically in price and complexity.

The cost of the mission has not yet been finalized and is estimated at $ 500 million, experts talk about a billion (the cost of the Curiosity mission is $ 2.5 billion). And unlike other space projects, Tito is a very rich man, ready to write some pretty big checks, which increases the credibility of the mission. But the time factor is not on the side of the project, and it requires a very tight schedule. To launch in January 2018, the project must be completed in just a year, including the choice of launch vehicle, which must be ordered and built by the beginning of the mission. The launch window is an objective thing and cannot be delayed. It is associated with such a phenomenon as the confrontation of Mars - the smallest distance between our planets.
A simple online program allows you to visually observe the mutual position of the Earth and Mars, depending on the calendar.


It is for 2018 that the Great Opposition falls - when Mars is as close to Earth as possible. The main risk is associated with radiation hazard during the flight, from which people on Earth are protected by a magnetic field.
