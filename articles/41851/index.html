Choose Opera!



The release of Opera 9.6 is a good thing, but we know that Opera is not only a browser, but also a huge number of users who constantly take an active part in testing and distributing Opera Software software products. Therefore, the Norwegian company pays no less attention to the development of the popular My Opera portal, constantly improving functionality or opening new sections. Not so long ago, the portal Choose Opera . Moreover, not only externally, although this aspect should be given special attention only because Oleg Melnichuk, who since August of this year has been working at the company's head office in Oslo, has made us all known. In fact, from the inconspicuous section on My Opera, where rarely had anyone visited before, it turned out to be a real portal for people who are not only in love with Opera, but who want to spend a small part of their personal time promoting this browser.

According to his idea, this Choose Opera portal should become a meeting and communication place for all who want to take a more active part in the distribution of their favorite browser. Here you can organize any event in your city or university, find a lot of promotional materials or just chat with each other and directly with Opera Software employees on a specialized forum . Already, the active participants in the portal are more than a thousand opera fans from around the world, and this number is growing every day.

By the way, one of the latest innovations is a very nice video about the current release of Opera 9.6.



By the way, anyone can post this video on their website or blog - let others be happy. ;)
At the same time - you get a small contribution to the further distribution of your favorite browser.

In general, if you want to help your browser become even more popular and, as a result, better - welcome to the new portal.
