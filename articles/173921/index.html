PayPal is close to officially appear in Russia

The inconvenience associated with the inability to use all the functionality of PayPal in Russia is likely to disappear soon - the Pay Pal Roux limited liability company was registered in Russia on March 13, 2013 as a non-bank credit organization (NPO).

The question of further functioning of the payment system in the territory of the Russian Federation rests on obtaining a license, for which it is necessary to make authorized capital in the amount of 18 million rubles during the statutory period, which has not yet ended.
