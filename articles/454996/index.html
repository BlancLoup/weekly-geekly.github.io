Cosmonaut Training Center named after Yu.A. Gagarin and Roscosmos began open recruitment in the cosmonaut corps



In FGBU "Research Institute of CPC named after Yu.A. Gagarin ‚Äùlaunched a set of candidates for test-cosmonauts of the Cosmonaut Detachment of Roskosmos in 2019.

But not simple, but engineering-sport-volitional.

Applicants must have a higher education program (only the first three are listed below, the rest are listed in the publication) + experience in the received specialty for at least 3 years:

  • computer science and computing;
  • electronics, radio engineering and communication systems;
  • photonics, instrument making, optical and biotechnical systems and technologies.

Volunteers should have endurance, strength, speed, agility, flexibility, special physical fitness - resistance to the effects of adverse factors of space flight (congestion, weightlessness, hypoxia, vestibular stimuli) and airborne physical fitness.



Applications and documents (copies of originals) from applicants for selection are accepted from June 3, 2019 to June 1, 2020.

Documents are sent by Russian post with the notification to the address: 141160, Moscow region, Star City, the head of the FGBU "Research Institute of Central Testing Complex named after Yu.A. Gagarin "marked" In the commission on the selection of candidates for astronauts. "



The decisions of the IAC on the selected applicants are published on the websites of the State Corporation "Roskosmos" and the FGBU "Research Institute of Central Testing Industry named after Yu.A. Gagarin ", as well as personally communicated to all selected candidates for cosmonauts.



Requirements for applicants for participation in the competition for the selection of candidates for astronauts

1. General requirements.

A candidate for a cosmonaut candidate in the Russian Federation may be a citizen of the Russian Federation.

The age of applicants should not exceed 35 years.

The following persons are not allowed to the competitive selection of candidates for cosmonauts:

  • having citizenship (citizenship) of a foreign state or a residence permit or other document confirming the right of permanent residence of a citizen of the Russian Federation on the territory of a foreign state;
  • in respect of which the criminal prosecution is conducted;
  • those who have not removed (outstanding) conviction;
  • violating the legislation of the Russian Federation on the protection of state secrets;
  • having in their respect entered into force decisions of the court on declaring incompetent or partially capable.

2. Requirements for education, professional qualifications and work experience of applicants.

Applicants must have a higher education in the program of specialty or magistracy and a specialty from the list of the following integrated groups:

- computer science and computing;
- electronics, radio engineering and communication systems;
- photonics, instrument making, optical and biotechnical systems and technologies;
- electricity and heat;
- nuclear energy and technology;
- mechanical engineering;
- Physical and technical sciences and technology;
- aviation and rocket and space technology;
- air navigation and operation of aviation and rocket and space technology;
- nanotechnologies and nanomaterials;
- mathematics and mechanics;
- physics and astronomy;
- chemistry;
- biological sciences.

Persons with higher education should have experience in the received specialty for at least 3 years after graduating from the relevant higher education institution.

Applicants who have a higher education in the field of medical sciences (therapeutic and preventive care) with at least 3 years experience in the field of aerospace, underwater, military medicine or disaster medicine are also admitted to the selection.

Flight contenders should have higher flight education and flight work experience (service) in military units, organizations, institutions engaged in the operation, use, and testing of aviation or space technology for at least 3 years after receiving a document on higher flight education.

Priority in the selection are those with experience in the aviation or rocket and space industry of Russia.

Documents on the received higher education should be of the state sample, and the higher educational institutions that issued them should have state accreditation.
Applicants who have documents of foreign countries on education must submit documents on the nostrification of relevant diplomas.

The list of personal documents submitted by applicants to the competition commission at various stages of selection is given in Appendix No. 1 to this Regulation .

3. Medical requirements.

Medical requirements that determine the degree of fitness of candidates for cosmonauts are set out in the Regulations on Medical Examination and Monitoring of the Health of Candidates in Cosmonauts, Cosmonauts and Cosmonauts Instructors, approved by order of the Minister of Defense of the Russian Federation and the Minister of Health of the Russian Federation of January 9, 2001 No. 14 / 7, and in Appendix No. 2 to this Regulation .

4. Psychological requirements.

When selecting applicants for compliance with psychological requirements, the level of development of professionally important psychological qualities necessary for a successful cosmonaut's professional activity is studied and assessed.
In the process of psychological examination are analyzed:

  • biologically stable psychophysiological parameters (typological properties of higher nervous activity, psychomotor qualities, reliability of operator activity, etc.);
  • features of individual mental processes and properties (emotional, cognitive, volitional);
  • socio-psychological characteristics (degree of professional self-determination, personality orientation, sociability, tendency to leadership, conformism, etc.).

Psychological qualities of applicants are assessed according to the results of special psychological studies, relevant interviews with applicants, studying documents describing their livelihoods, studying observation data by expert psychologists for applicants as they pass through other types of selection.

The reason for the rejection of candidates for selection is the presence of adverse individual psychological characteristics.

5. Requirements for physical fitness.

The composition of professionally important physical qualities, assessed in the selection of applicants for compliance with the requirements for physical fitness, includes: endurance, strength, speed, agility, flexibility, special physical fitness - resistance to adverse effects of space flight (overload, weightlessness, hypoxia, vestibular stimuli ) and on-board physical fitness - the ability to effectively use in flight on-board simulators for physical training in order to prevent ki adverse effects of prolonged stay in weightlessness (hypokinesia, hypodynamia, microgravity).

These qualities are assessed on the basis of the results of the performance of the standard exercise by the applicants.

The actual level of physical fitness of applicants is estimated based on the levels of these physical qualities.

The final conclusion on the physical fitness of applicants is based on the ratio of actual and required levels of preparedness.

Regulatory exercises for applicants are presented in Appendix No. 3 to this Regulation .

6. Requirements for professional fitness.

Applicants must comply with the following requirements, which are necessary for the subsequent preparation for space flight:

  • possess the necessary minimum of general knowledge in the field of the basics of manned space exploration;
  • have the ability to study space technology (to demonstrate the ability to understand the basics and principles of building technical systems, understanding their physical nature, the ability to memorize technical information, terminology and technical characteristics);
  • to have abilities to operator activity (determined by the results of special tests).
  • know computer equipment at the level of requirements for computer science in higher education and be able to use a personal computer, e-mail, antivirus programs (determined by the results of special tests);
  • own one of the foreign languages ‚Äã‚Äã(English, Italian, Spanish, German, French) within the framework of the requirements of the programs of non-linguistic universities of the Russian Federation;
  • have the necessary level of knowledge of the Russian language (written and oral);
  • have the necessary awareness in the field of the history of world and domestic cosmonautics;
  • possess the necessary minimum of general knowledge in the field of cultural studies.
