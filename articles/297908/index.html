IIDF in 2015: the fate of startups that received support and plans for 2016

An overview of the IIDF projects in 2015 showed an unbounded imagination in the world of high online technologies. Strategic partners of project support were the following companies: Rambler & Co, Yota, Microsoft, Rostec, Renaissance group, etc.

For more information about the IIDF and the participants of 2013-2014, you can read in the previous article .

Observing the development of venture financing and technological innovation, it can be concluded that the state is interested in scaling the information security market, developing software and advertising technologies. We will tell you about the most interesting projects today.

FRIA participants: from concept to monetization

www.group-ib.ru

For information security, she took on the hard mittens Group-IB, the largest company in the fund's portfolio. Since 2003, she has been closely following cyber threats, investigating crimes in the field of high technologies. Such major tasks as the suppression of a DDoS attack on the Aeroflot website were carried out, the international payment service Qiwi was supported.

At the stage of transition from the service company to the product manufacturer, it was necessary to promote new directions in IT employment. Group-IB is a storehouse of innovative solutions, and they always have projects in the launch queue that require cash investments.
IIDF invested in the project 4 million dollars., Skolkovo - 88 thousand dollars. Financial investments and skills in capturing new market niches gave impetus to the development of the company.

Judging by the information posts, Group-IB plans for the future are enormous: the construction of branch centers of cyber security in the regions of Russia, the development of computer forensics, the fight against cyberterrorism. We wish them good luck, in such a difficult task!

turbodealer.ru

Turbodiler is an online project on the accelerated sale of vehicles, implemented by replicating ads through many popular thematic partner resources. Project promotion cost 25.4 thousand dollars., Microsoft's injection amounted to 5.45 thousand dollars.

Internet platform allows you to perform transactions for the sale of used cars. The startup offered a new method to accelerate sales in the car market, given the competition for the buyer in the first quarter of 2015.

Now the project continues to be improved: technical changes were made to improve the service, the sales department increased, resources were concentrated on collecting statistics. In 2016, Turbodiler plans to become a powerful CRM system for car dealerships, introducing customers and managing the sales department.

habitek.ru

Habitek - a system in the clouds for self-creating projects of low-rise residential buildings. The user creates the space-planning solution of the object online; he is freed from the need to explain his requirements to the designer, to expect the results of his work. The client, using adaptive prototypes and other platform tools, gets a viable habitat, simple communication with developers, professional support.
The project to receive investments from IIDF in the amount of 20 thousand dollars.

This is the only project from the Voronezh region, which also takes into account the desires and whims of the user through self-design. There are already real customers, there is an additional set of programmers.
Startup participants plan to receive 1 million rubles. arrived in a month, for a start by placing the project line on partner sites.

tehnovizor.rf

TechnoVisor will provide you with an ideal safety analysis of loading / unloading. The service literally controls the work of the loaders, sending reports on the work of the drivers in the warehouse and contributing to the increase in the efficiency of the auxiliary business process. The project was implemented at the expense of the Free Media Foundation - 25.4 thousand dollars. and Microsoft - 5.45 thousand dollars.

Technovizor uses a data collection terminal and an access card for operation. The first sale amounted to 608 thousand rubles, focus on retail!
The project participants set non-child goals: to compete with Toyota, Linde, Still, Crown. In the future, they see cooperation with BigData, plan a spectacular exit to the countries of Eastern Europe.

cloudstats.me

Cloudstats.me is another lucky person who received an infusion into the development of cloud information technologies from FRIA and Microsoft - 25.4 thousand dollars. + 5.45 thousand dollars.
A simple but incredibly powerful service monitors servers and websites on cloud technology, which requires minimal technical skills for the user. Soon, the innovation platform will finally be transferred to the cloud, where it will undergo load testing.

In the hope of receiving advice and assistance from Microsoft, developers see a way out to the full stability of the server and unprecedented popularity in the next couple of years.
