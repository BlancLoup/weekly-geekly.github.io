Kerio Control 8.2 received the certificate of FSTEC of Russia

Kerio Technologies announces the receipt of the FSTEC certificate of conformity for the version of Kerio Control 8.2 for a period of three years.

The received certificate will certify that the Kerio Control 8.2 firewall. is a software tool for protecting information from unauthorized access from external computer networks and systems, and complies with the requirements of the governing documents.

Kerio Control 8.2 received certification for class 3 security as a firewall. The control of undeclared capabilities was carried out in grade 4.

The certificate of conformity provides the ability to use the product Kerio Control 8.2 in government, education, medical and any other institutions that require the use of certified products.

The Kerio Control 8.2 solution is available as a software package and does not require a separate operating system for its operation. The certified product Kerio Control 8.2 is already available for order, and the certified distribution can be downloaded from our website .
