Free VM with Windows for testing sites on older versions of IE

Microsoft has been distributing free virtual machines with Windows XP, Vista and 7 for several years to test websites on older versions of Internet Explorer.

All images are equipped with the appropriate version of IE and security updates. For XP, updates were made by October 2012, for the rest - by March 2011. The rest is a clean, fresh installation of Windows without any special modifications. VHD images can be downloaded to Microsoft Virtual PC or another virtualization package. RAR self-extracting archives are divided into 700 MB for ease of download.

Windows_XP + IE6: 415.5 MB

Windows 7 + IE8: Part 1 , Part 2 , Part 3 , Part 4
Windows 7 + IE9: Part 1 , Part 2 , Part 3 , Part 4 , Part 5 , Part 6 , Part 7

Password for authorization to all systems is the same: Password1.

Immediately after starting Windows 7 or Windows Vista, they will be asked to enter an activation key.
