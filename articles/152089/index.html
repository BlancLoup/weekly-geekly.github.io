Ultrabooks Acer Aspire S5 video review



Acer Aspire S5 is a new ultrabook, replacing the models S3. Laptops are equipped with glossy displays with a diagonal of 13.3 inches and a resolution of 1366x768

Ultrabook case is made of magnesium alloy, and they work on low-voltage Intel Core i5 and Core i7 processors of the third generation. The amount of RAM is 4 GB without the possibility of expansion. For data storage, solid-state drives are used that work in pairs in raid 0. The Core i5 model is equipped with two 64 GB hard drives, and a model with a Core i7 dual 128 GB SSDs.

The most interesting thing about the new model is the unique panel of I / O ports. When you press a special key, the panel with ports comes out of the laptop case, which, firstly, is quite original, and secondly, allows you to reduce the thickness of the laptop during transportation.
