What awaits us in ReactOS version 0.4.5?



Greetings to all Habrazhiteley

We decided to break the prolonged silence and lift the veil of uncertainty over the fact that the interesting lies in wait for everyone in the upcoming release of version 0.4.5. Wait for the release of a very short time, just two weeks.

Themes


For the past six months, Janis Adamopulus has been hard at work on correcting the bugs in the visual design of the operating system. As a result, more and more themes are applied almost without distortion. In some cases, the system is almost impossible to outwardly distinguish from XP or other operating systems.







Virtual machines


Thomas Faber turned the old hack into a full-fledged fix, and now in Virtual Box a dynamic screen resolution change is available over a wide range.



Alexander Telyatnikov released a new version of the universal driver for ATA-devices, and as a result, ReactOS was launched in the emulator of old PCem computers.



Office 2010



The result of the work of Hermes Beluski brag on this video. There is a slow version without music. Now anyone can install and run another office suite from Microsoft.
Please note that for successful experiments with the office you will need to install the Samba package from the application manager.



Open Awards 2017 competition


The ReactOS project takes an active part in two nominations of the Spanish open project competition. Now comes the last hours of voting. You can support us. Instructions on how to vote are available here . Every voice is important! (at the time of 23:27 on April 30 there are not enough 25 votes )

We did not have 2 votes in one of two nominations to reach the final. It is sad.
