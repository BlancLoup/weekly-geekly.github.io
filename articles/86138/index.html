Few words about intellectual property and common sense

I am very glad that my previous article was relevant and interesting. Today I want to touch on the topic of intellectual property.

Intellectual Property Protection Methods:
  • In scientific articles publish the results of research, new methods, algorithms, approaches, schemes. Publication of a scientific article does not guarantee the protection of rights to the content, but is an important point for its recognition;
  • A patent is obtained for an invention, scheme, device, model. A patent is a guarantee of respect for your intellectual rights;
  • A certificate for a software product is obtained to confirm its authorship for a certain developed program (most often, the name of this program is reserved for itself);
  • evidence for the algorithm - everything seems to be clear here.
