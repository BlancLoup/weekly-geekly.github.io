Citizens and the state: access to public services will be available through the bank


Photo: Oleg Kharseev / Kommersant

In the near future, citizens of the Russian Federation will be spared the need to go through complex procedures to gain access to public services. The Ministry of Communications is currently implementing a new project for cooperation with major banks, Kommersant reports. Banks and connect their customers to public services. Plus, banks will simplify access to the information of state bodies on citizens (provided that the citizen has given his consent).

When you click on a button, a citizen receives on his phone the information necessary to enter the portal, and may begin to work with state services.
