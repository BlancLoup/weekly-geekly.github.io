Seven-inch netbook Kohjinsha SK3 UMPC



Production of Kohjinsha SK3 UMPC laptops began in Japan. The new model turned out very compact: dimensions 193x132x30 mm with a weight of 720 grams.

The netbook features the SK3 touch screen, an Intel Atom processor at 1.33 GHz and 1 GB of RAM. On board the device is also a 60-gigabyte 1.8-inch hard drive (4200rpm), WiFi b / g / n, Bluetooth 2.0 and GPS. The list of ports includes: ethernet 10/100, VGA, audio in / out, slot for microSD cards, two USB 2.0 ports, a universal card reader and a slot for ExpressCard 34.

It is expected that the mini-laptop will be equipped with two cameras - a low-resolution webcam for video calls and its three-megapixel option for photographs of fixed objects.

After conducting the test, Jeita - known for being too loyal to testing devices - the manufacturer promises battery autonomy for 3.2 hours. But one of the users of the portal umpcportal.com says that in real life, the battery holds at best 2.5 hours. Probably, this opinion is worth listening to.

However, there is also a positive - the laptop will be sold in Japan at a price of $ 733, including local taxes.
