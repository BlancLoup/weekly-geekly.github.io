TargetSummit 2017: Prisma, AppsFlyer, Aviasales, KAYAK and 30 more speakers will speak in Moscow on March 29

March 29 in Moscow will host the fifth international conference on mobile marketing TargetSummit Moscow . It will bring together more than 600 participants and 30 speakers from around the world to exchange experiences in mobile marketing of applications and games.



The TargetSummit Moscow program is divided into 3 thematic blocks: product stories about mobile apps promotion, ASO discussion panels, analytics, user acquisition and growth hacking, as well as practical workshops.

Conference speakers include KAYAK, Prisma, AppsFlyer, Mail.Ru Group, Nordeus, Aviasales, SkyEng, Maps.me, Lamoda and many other companies from Europe, America and Russia.

More information about the speakers and the conference on the official website . There you can buy a ticket.
