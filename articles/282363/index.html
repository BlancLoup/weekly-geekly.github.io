Open DevOps Expert Meeting April 28 at Microsoft Technology Center


We are pleased to inform you that on April 28 an open meeting of the IT community will take place at the Microsoft technology center located in the center of Moscow , for which any specialist who is interested in organizing software development and creation on the Microsoft technology stack can register. It is not the first time that community experts hold similar meetings and each time provide all participants with the opportunity to share their experiences and talk with the same representatives of the industry. To participate in full-time or online format, you must register by the link .

A little talk about the content of the meeting. Traditionally, IT-specialists and developers are engaged in solutions in their own separate areas, which, as previously thought, little overlap. However, nowadays, in order to effectively cope with their duties, the system administrator must in one way or another have the skills of a developer, at least for writing scripts and understanding what others have written. The experts will look at DevOps - the increasingly popular approach to developing, deploying and maintaining software products in the enterprise, and also talk about container technologies as an important component of the DevOps infrastructure.

Participants will have the opportunity to spend a Thursday not only in a pleasant company, but also with benefits - at the meeting, in addition to the live communication of the community at dinner, two reports are scheduled from industry experts.


What is DevOps and why it helps to make software products better, and users are happier told by Vladimir Gusarov - MVP Visual Studio and developer technologies and the author of many recorded reports on TechDays.ru.
In the report, Vladimir will talk about the principle of ‚Äúcontinuous delivery and deployment‚Äù (Continuous Delivery and Deployment), as well as show the main stages associated with the deployment and maintenance of software products in the enterprise.


Georgy Gadzhiev, who needs no introduction and, perhaps, one of the leading experts in Microsoft on Microsoft technologies, will tell us about the containers and their place in modern Microsoft technologies.

The meeting will be held at the Microsoft Technology Center (MTC) in Moscow, located near the Belorusskaya metro station, from 19 to 21 hours. Registration for the event is strictly required. When registering, you will have the opportunity to choose the option of attending an event or online participation. How to connect to the broadcast, you will be informed in the letter confirming your registration. To enter the MTC, a passport or a substitute document is required.
