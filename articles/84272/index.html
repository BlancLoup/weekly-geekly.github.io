C & C Classic: download for free, Commander!

The news is outdated for a couple of days, but the attention of all connoisseurs is definitely worth it: Electronic Arts on Saturday, February 13th, laid out for free download the gold classics C & C. These are Command & Conquer: Tiberian Sun (1999 release) and the Firestorm supplement, Command & Conquer: Tiberian Dawn (1995), two disks, for GDI and NOD and Command & Conquer: Red Alert (1996), two disks, for Allies and the Red Army.

The last two gifts are no longer gifts, as they were available before. However, on the Command & Conquer Classic page there is now a whole set of favorite old but not outdated games of the C & C series. Moreover, EA was not lazy and made a patch that allows you to run the 20th century C & C on the new Windows 7 operating system. The patch was provided with detailed instructions.

Well, aren't they great? ;)
