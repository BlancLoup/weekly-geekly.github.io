Danish company Lumigon presented a smartphone with a night vision camera.

While the flagships of the mobile industry are competing in the number of gigahertz, inches and megapixels, little-known manufacturers have no choice but to surprise the world with fundamentally new technological solutions. It turned out perfectly with a small Danish company Lumigon, who recently presented the flagship version of its small line - the T3 smartphone with the ability to shoot photos and videos in complete darkness.



June 2, 2016 in Copenhagen, the announcement of the smartphone T3 from the company Lumigon - a stylish device durable steel case and support for several innovative solutions. Among them are a night vision camera and Back Touch technology, which are still not found in any of the smartphones on the market.

T3 hull elements measuring 137 √ó 69 √ó 7.9 mm and weighing 145 grams are made of molybdenum and high-strength 316 stainless steel. Hardware stuffing and functionality

The performance of the Lumigon T3, which is worthy of its flagship, is provided by a 64-bit MediaTek Helio x10 processor with eight processing cores with a maximum clock frequency of 2.2. GHz with the possibility of their simultaneous operation. Correct and productive video subsystem is provided by the IMG PowerVR G6200 graphics controller, which operates in tandem with 3GB RAM.

A bunch of cameras in T3 looks very promising. On the rear panel is a 13-megapixel main module with a phase PDAF autofocus and a two-tone flash. And it is on the back that an additional 4 megapixel module of a 2K night vision camera with a dual IR flash is located. With the help of the latter, it becomes possible to take photos and videos in complete darkness.



On the front panel is a 5 megapixel 2K camera with its own flash. Supports 4K video recording and H.265 4K video playback.

The manufacturer promises ultra-fast autofocus, excellent image quality regardless of the time of day or the light. Front camera with Front Flash technology will allow you to make great selfies even in low light conditions.

At the bottom of the front panel there is a fingerprint sensor with the ability to read a fingerprint pattern attached at any convenient angle.



A set of sensors is represented by an accelerometer, gyroscope, electronic compass, altimeter, light sensor and thermometer. Navigation capabilities of the device provide modules GPS / GLONASS / BEIDOU. IR transmitter allows you to use your smartphone as a remote control. It is possible to charge the battery via USB Pump Express port or wirelessly.



Multimedia standards support

Supported

Audio formats: 3GPP, MPEG-4, AAC, AMR-NB, AMR-WB, FLAC, MP3, MIDI, Vorbis, PCM / WAVE, Opus

High-quality playback of audio content (up to 24 bit / 192 kHz) and a minimum of extraneous noise is guaranteed by ICEpower technology from Bang & Olufsen.

Communication capabilities, methods of data exchange, internal memory

T3 allows you to stay connected to the networks of two operators simultaneously, supports work in GSM networks (850/900/1800/1900 MHz), 3G UMTS (850/900/1900/2100 MHz), 4th generation LTE (800/900 / 1800/2100/2600 MHz). Available data exchange via Bluetooth 4.1 and Wi-Fi 802.11a / b / g / n / ac, via infrared (16-60 kHz + 455 kHz), external HDMI via MHL. Supported and the possibility of NFC-payments.

The built-in memory is designed to save 128 GB of data, and this figure, according to Lars Gravesen, will be fixed by the company in the future as the bottom bracket. The management of the software functions of the gadget is entrusted to the Android OS 6.0 Marshmallow. For ordering on the website of the company are available colors of silver, black and orange. As an additional premium version, the version is offered in a case using 24-carat gold in combination with one of four color solutions.



Dock station

The glass and steel docking station used to charge and transfer data is not included with the T3 priced Lumigon at $ 59. The device allows you to adjust the phone to the user's preferences. When installed in the docking station, the phone can go into silent mode, activate an alarm clock, turn off / turn on Wi-Fi, light, automatically perform other preferred settings entered in the menu by the owner, both at home and in the office.



Like the previous Lumigon T2HD model, priced at a launch in Denmark at $ 1028, the new T3 is located in the upper price segment. The cost of the device, declared by the manufacturer is nearly $ 925, and this is without taking into account the cost of delivery and related fees. The package includes a waterproof headset, USB-cable and adapter, manual and warranty card.
