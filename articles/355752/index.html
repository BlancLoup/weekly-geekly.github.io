US Department of State allocates $ 60 million to support independent media in Russia



The item on the allocation of funds to support independent media in the Russian Federation is in the law, which was signed yesterday by US President Barack Obama. In addition, the same law contains another clause, which states the need to maintain uncensored Internet access in Russia. This item will also be funded from the amount indicated in the title. The law itself was named the Law on the Support of Freedom of Ukraine, and allows the US president to introduce new sanctions against the Russian Federation.
