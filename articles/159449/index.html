High-capacity batteries from Mugen Power Batteries


Our company is engaged in the development, production and distribution of high-capacity batteries for mobile devices, and has established itself as a leader in its field, mainly due to high quality products and customer support worldwide.
Our plant has complied with CE and ISO 9001 quality standards , as well as RoHS and has been working in the battery industry since 1979.

Why you need high-capacity batteries


Since Google introduced its new Android mobile OS to the world, we started to produce lithium-ion (Li-Ion) high-capacity batteries for flagship phones of the time, such as HTC Desire A8181, HTC EVO, Samsung Galaxy S, Google Nexus One and other smartphones, as the weakest link on Android phones was and still is the time to work on one charge from a regular battery.

The Mugen Power high-capacity battery solves this problem and gives the smartphone owner the opportunity to use all of its functions, such as GPS, WiFi, 3G, games and multimedia, and not constantly thinking about finding an outlet or a free USB port.

At the moment, our collection of high-capacity batteries has more than 600 models for all major hit phones on various OSs (Android, iOS, Windows Phone, webOS, Symbian, etc.), such as Samsung Galaxy S III, Samsung Galaxy S II, Samsung Galaxy Note , HTC Sensation, HTC One X and iPhone 4S.
