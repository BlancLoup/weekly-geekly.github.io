Apple iOS 4.1 beta 3

Literally just on the apple website, the third beta version of iOS 4.1, as well as Xcode 3.2.4 and iOS SDK 4.1 beta 3 (Snow Leopard) has become available for developers.





In general, according to tradition, Apple releases beta firmware once every 2 weeks, this time, the new beta came out a week earlier, is this related to a hole in the Safari browser, through which you can jail?

If you are a developer, you can try out the beta on your iDevices.
The following phones and players are supported:

iPhone 4 ( iPhone3,1_4.1_8B5097d_Restore.ipsw )
iPhone 3Gs ( iPhone2,1_4.1_8B5097d_Restore.ipsw )
iPhone 3G ( iPhone1,2_4.1_8B5097d_Restore.ipsw )
iPod Touch 2G ( iPod2,1_4.1_8B5097d_Restore.ipsw )
iPod Touch 3G ( iPod3,1_4.1_8B5097d_Restore.ipsw )

xcode 3.2.4 and ios sdk 4.1 beta 3
