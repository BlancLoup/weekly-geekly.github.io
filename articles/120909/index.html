The Ministry of Communications and Information of the Republic of Kazakhstan by its order approved the Rules of registration, use and distribution of the domain space of the Kazakhstan segment of the Internet . Here are two quotes from this document.
2.4 For the registration of a domain name, the registrant shall submit an application with accurate and complete information in electronic or written form in accordance with the annex to this Regulation. The requirement for server hardware is its physical location in the Republic of Kazakhstan. All registrant applications are reviewed in the order in which they are received by the registry or registrar.

2.9.3. Domain name registration is suspended by the registry or the registrar when finding server equipment that uses an Internet resource with a registered domain name outside the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Google responded to the decision of the Kazakh authorities: now instead of www.google.kz, users from Kazakhstan are served by www.google.com (redirected to the Google page in the Kazakh language).

‚ÄúWe are in a difficult situation: an attempt to build borders on the Internet poses a number of important questions for us, concerning not only the efficiency of processing requests, but also the protection of personal data and freedom of speech.
