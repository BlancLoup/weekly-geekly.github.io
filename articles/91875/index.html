Interview with organizer iPaddevCamp, San Jose, CA about opportunities

During the first iPaddevCamp, which took place on April 16-18 in San Jose, Silicon Valley, I interviewed several organizers of the competition. Briefly about them and what they think about the iAppContest competition, which began on the same day as the campaign;

Do you have a ready application? We invite you to take part in iAppContest www.iappcontest.com in the First All-Ukrainian competition among iPhone / iPad programmers. Just upload your video to www.iappcontest.com .

The contest is held in three main categories.
1. Entertaining
2. Social
3. Business (you will find many subcategories in which you can define your application)

Any ready-made applications can take part in the competition whether they are located in the Apple Store or not.
Results will be known on June 19th. Three of the best will go to Silicon Valley on a specially prepared program!
