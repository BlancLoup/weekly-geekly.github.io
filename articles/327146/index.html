YiiConf 2017, Moscow, June 16

This summer, June 16, 2017, a web developers conference will be held in Moscow, dedicated to the PHP framework Yii .



The speakers will talk about how to work with the framework correctly, present the best practices, and give practical examples of optimizing Yii-based applications under high loads.


Members of the framework development team and authors of training materials have already submitted their applications for participation:


  • Alexander Makarov (@samdark), core team:
    • Yii. What's next? .
    • OpenSource from the inside .
  • Pavel Klimov (@ klimov-paul), core team:
    • Project installation and database migration: typical tasks and errors .
  • Dmitry Naumenko (@SilverFire), core team:
    • Domain Driven Design - just about complicated .
    • Recipes for Yii 2 .
    • Profiling in PHP with XDebug, XHprof, Blackfire .
  • Dimitri Eliseev, ElisDN.ru, author of courses and screencasts:
    • Semantic programming: Where to put the code .
  • Mikhail Artemyev:
    • We create and deploy the application framework in 10 minutes .
  • Dependency Injection, Container and Service Locator .

Carsten Brandt (@cebe) will also arrive with a report.


You can also apply for a report or a master class . For speakers, participation in Yiiconf and Devconf is free.


At the end of the conference, we will arrange a question-answer session, where you can ask questions to speakers and authors of the framework. During the conference, you can express your thoughts or make a story on an interesting topic using flipcharts.


The cost of participation will grow! Now the price is from 4000 p. depending on the package .


Note for residents of Ukraine: you can go to Russia freely, on a civil passport. Visa or additional documents are not required.


You can follow the news of the conference on Twitter using the hashtag #yiiconf .


For all questions you can write to the mail to the organizers: 2017@devconf.ru


See you at YiiConf !
