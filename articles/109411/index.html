Meeting Donetsk Drupal developers

We invite Donetsk CMS Drupal developers and just interested in Donetsk Drupal Group Meetup. DDGM will be held on December 11, 2010, format - Drupal meetup, the meeting will end with a party at the club.

The official website of the event is meetup2010.drupal.dn.ua .

Participation is free, prior registration is required.

For a professional drupal developer, meetup is an opportunity to share experiences, patch up your skills, get answers to questions, find collaboration partners and / or a customer, and make a presentation.

Beginners will have the opportunity to get an idea about the system, its features, strengths and weaknesses, as well as to understand how it is suitable for solving the set goals. But first of all, this is an excellent opportunity to start developing websites with the help of Drupal.

The customer will be able to find a professional team that quickly and efficiently solves the task with minimal risks, or receive free consultation from professionals.

Sponsors of the conference will provide an opportunity to expand their sphere of influence and gain useful experience investing in Open Source.

The organizers are a group of professional developers who love Drupal from Donetsk and Zaporozhye, each having a wide experience in developing and using websites based on CMS Drupal:

- Vadim Mirgorod (DonNTU graduate student, freelancer);
- Vladislav Bogatyryov (head of development and internet marketing at gorod.ua).

Organizational partners: DonNTU, Trellon, GOROD.ua.
