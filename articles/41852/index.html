YouTube has been selling music

Good afternoon, dear habrakrady!



Leafing through the news portals came across such an article:

YouTube users will now be able to buy their favorite music without leaving the video hosting, writes The New York Times.
It is known that the music will provide online stores Apple iTunes and Amazon. When buying a music video, simply click on the icons with the names of the respective stores.

So far, through YouTube, you can buy only music belonging to the record labels EMI and Universal Music, as well as the game Spore. In the future, Google intends to expand the range of products sold through YouTube.

Currently, the new service works only in the United States.

Recall that the other day YouTube launched a new cinema mode, as well as a new project called Screening Room (Viewing Room), to provide support to novice filmmakers. A source


Starting sales, Youtube may finally begin to make a long-awaited profit. Google (bought YouTube 1.5 years ago for $ 1.65 billion) did not disclose the financial indicators of this video hosting.

Well, what do you think will come of it? Big will be the income?
