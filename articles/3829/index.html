"Yandex" moved to the Urals

The company "Yandex" announced the opening of a development office in Yekaterinburg. Pavel Braslavsky, a web search officer, became the branch manager. This is the third branch of the company - in September 2006, an office was opened in St. Petersburg, and a year before that - a Ukrainian representative office.

The task of the Ural development office is the development of existing and the development of new search technologies and communication services of Yandex. To date, the Ekaterinburg office has 10 employees. Yandex plans to expand the office in the near future and is looking for specialists in Yekaterinburg in the field of information retrieval, as well as experienced programmers in C ++ and Python.
