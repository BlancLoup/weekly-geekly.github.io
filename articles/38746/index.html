RBK Money: on the road to recovery?

The market of electronic payment systems is a bit like a madhouse: there is a Napoleon - Webmoney, a patient who considers himself a doctor - Yandex. Money and other patients with the syndrome of schizophrenic psychosis, expressed in complete illogical thinking - such as Rupay. About him, and will be discussed - because the case is atypical and to some extent even interesting.

So, Rupay apparently realized that he was sick (you must admit that it was a great rarity for the mentally ill) and demanded another doctor. In the role of a new doctor, RBC Holding, who took the patient on bail and even adopted him, gave him his last name.

From that moment on, Rupay became RBK Money. However, the course of treatment turned out to be longer than expected, and the announced Rupay extract (now RBK Money) was postponed time after time.
