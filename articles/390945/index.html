Yuriev night


Unlike Cosmonautics Day, St. George's Night is a more informal event. For it is suitable not only a popular science event, but also a themed party, watching fiction at home, but almost anything. At the same time, the number and themes of the events noted make it possible to assess well the interest in space in the country. For example, the second place in terms of the number of events in 2015 was taken by Iran. And this is despite the fact that the events were serious, were held in planetariums and universities, and the date April 12 is in no way connected with the Iranian space program. At the same time, Russian events are rarely marked on this map. In my opinion, the Cosmonautics Night perfectly complements the Cosmonautics Day and these names do not interfere with each other. Accordingly, if you have not yet thought about how to celebrate this wonderful holiday, I dare to offer a couple of ideas and brief instructions on how to register an event and try to overtake at least Iran (the United States had 72 events in 2015, they will have to catch up with them for a long time) .

There is just enough time to ask around your friends if someone had a telescope or good binoculars lying around in the closet. In the evening in the area on April 12, the Moon will be in the first quarter. This means that it will be visible in the evenings, and its lighting conditions will be very good for observation. Immediately after sunset, Jupiter will be visible in the southern part of the sky, its four satellites can be seen even with good binoculars. And after midnight Mars and Saturn will ascend.
