Cute and capacious external HDD from Iomega

Iomega company releases a new external HDD eGo Helium , designed for both Mac and PC. The 2.5-inch 320-gigabyte hard drive, which is hidden in a silver case made of anodized aluminum with a thickness of 16 mm, weighs only 200 grams.


HDD is formatted under the HFS file system, created using Iomega's Drop Guard design, due to which, in principle, it is protected from falling from a height of 1.3 meters. Of course, equipped with a USB 2.0 port.

On the shelves eGo Helium will appear in October. It will cost $ 150.
