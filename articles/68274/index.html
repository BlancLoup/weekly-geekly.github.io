Online applications in your browser: size does not matter


The specialists of Legco.net (in my person) analyzed a large number of feedback from netbook owners and found out the reasons for the disappointment with the purchase. The main disadvantage of netbooks is the small size of the hard drive - there is nowhere to store software for comfortable work.
However, consumers forget about the important part of the functionality of their devices, the one that is used in the title itself: NetBook is a laptop for working on the Internet! The new catalog of services for online work on the Internet Orqwe.com has gathered the best resources and combined them for the convenience of users in a single menu.

Principles of selection of resources are few, the proposed service should:
1. It is necessary to be accessible through a browser with Flash installed and not require exotic plug-ins and / or downloading and installing additional software.
2. Be sure to be free, or as an option - have a free plan.
3. It is necessary to have an English version (by the way, about a dozen services over the past few months have a Russian version and are dragged directly from Habr!).
4. It is advisable - not to require registration, most of the functionality of the services is available to any visitor directly from the main page (went in, did what was needed - and walk, all without much fuss).

The functionality of online services covers all aspects of activity: office applications, work with graphics and color, editing audio and video files, programming and entertainment; You can design your home layout right in your browser, choose a design for your homepage, draw a unique avatar for a social network, manage a list of urgent tasks for today and a month in advance, unpack the archive document, create a ringtone from your favorite song, save the document in a password-protected vault and send a link to it to a friend, create an image with an encrypted message, and check the suspicious file for viruses ... almost everything you do on your desktop PC is available online, will get In person, select the desired application from the Orqwe.com menu. Do not look for the Start button in the left corner of the screen - it has moved to the network, and the size of the hard drive now really does not matter!
