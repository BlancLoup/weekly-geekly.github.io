Live discussion. Artemy Lebedev. Megan Donahoe. Design, Metro, Windows Phone

Hi, habrayuzer!

As part of UserExperience 2011 , one of the creators of Metro-design, Megan Donahue, will arrive in Russia.

Megan leads developers and UX managers on the Microsoft Windows Phone Design team. Megan has more than 10 years of experience creating user interfaces, including computer interfaces, mobile operating systems, websites and games.

Megan graduated from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, has bachelor's and master's degrees. Megan conducted research for her final work at the MIT's Media Laboratory, where she studied wearable technology and computer design.

After that, she worked as a producer of the gaming portal Shockwave.com (MTV project). Then there was a project manager in the Apple Ipod design department. Currently, the Megan group in Windows Phone brings together developers, user research specialists and engineers in the design and creation of mobile systems and applications.

We decided to take advantage of the opportunity and, instead of simply sending design Artemy to Lynch, to have an open discussion between him and Megan. You can watch the live broadcast on the project page , and you can also ask questions via Twitter #temametro
