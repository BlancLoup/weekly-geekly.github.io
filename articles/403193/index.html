How we did our audio exhibit and demo.

From April 20 to April 23, 2017, we invite everyone to visit the audio equipment exposition in the updated Audiomenia showroom at 4/4 Drum .

Our audio exhibition will be held at the same time as the Hi-Fi & High End Show 2017. We decided to prepare a solid program, built our own cinema and equipped all the necessary infrastructure to show other audio products (players, headphones, amplifiers).

A new cinema hall of Audimania in the showroom on Metro

Why do we do it


The growth of the personal cinema market led us to create a separate lineup and launch our own production of specialized acoustics. Such acoustics will allow you to create a home cinema that will be as close as possible to the environment familiar to us from ‚Äúbig‚Äù cinemas, and in some ways will be able to surpass it.

For us, this decision has become natural. We have been engaged in the production of acoustics for many years and can perform the corresponding tasks in almost any direction.

We decided to give our customers the opportunity to evaluate a product such as speakers for personal cinema halls, in person, in an environment that will be prepared and equipped accordingly. For this purpose, we have allocated two large rooms in our showroom and built our own cinema halls. Today they are ready for viewing.



Own infrastructure


The display of any audio solutions requires compliance with the necessary conditions. We decided to create a platform that would exceed the capabilities of any exhibition rooms and allow us to demonstrate the acoustics in an environment as close as possible to the ‚Äúcombat‚Äù.

Our cinema halls were specially designed for additional sound insulation of the room. They are built on the basis of the "acoustic envelope" or "room inside the room." This design consists of modules that are filled with sound-absorbing elements.

In addition to preparing the premises, we calibrated the cinema halls. Today, they meet Dolby Atmos cinema requirements and support configuration 7.4.6.

One of the cinema halls is made in the form of an ordinary living room. Here you will not see the acoustics and other audio equipment, but you can listen to it in full.

In this room we have placed the "invisible" columns Cerasonar. They are mounted in the walls, can be applied on top of a decorative coating (wallpaper, paint for walls) and allow you to achieve a full-fledged cinema sound, but at the same time completely ‚Äúunload‚Äù the visual component of the room.
