Synology¬Æ introduced DiskStation DS412 +

Another new feature introduced today by Synology to users is the 4-disc NAS Plus series - Synology DS412 + , for small and medium-sized businesses. This is the first model of the 2012 model year among 4 desktop disk drives, replacing the DS411 + II .

High system performance allows demonstrating significant results when exchanging data: reading data at a speed of 205.68 MB / s, and writing - at 182.66 MB / s using a RAID 5 storage array and activated by the aggregation function of network ports. Compared with its predecessor, speed indicators improved by 85% and 73% respectively.

Synology DS412 + has high reliability and the ability to work for a long time without rebooting, which is achieved by a combination of passive processor cooling technology and an integrated fan duplication system (two 92 mm fans are used). DS412 + is also equipped with two network ports, in case of failure of one of them or a network failure, and supports ‚Äúhot swap‚Äù of disks, which together guarantee continuous data availability.

Synology DS412 + NAS is equipped with:

  • 2.13 GHz dual-core processor
  • 1GB DDR3 RAM
  • Two ports Gigabit LAN and Link Aggregation
  • Two USB 3.0 ports, one eSATA and one USB 2.0

Key Features:
  • Hard disk support up to 4TB (sold separately)
  • Ability to replace hard drives in hot mode
  • RAID types supported: Synology Hybrid RAID, Basic, JBOD,
    RAID 0, RAID 1, RAID 5, RAID 5 + Spare, RAID 6, RAID 10

  • Low power consumption - 60 W (operation mode), 20 W (sleep mode)
