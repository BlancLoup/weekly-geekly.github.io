Video Captcha from NuCaptcha

Canadian startup NuCaptcha introduced a new kind of protection against robot commentators - video captcha. The developers claim that people solve their video puzzle at once, but programmers will have to work hard to create an automatic recognition program.

In essence, a video captcha is a string of characters running along a curved line, from which a person is asked to recognize the last three, made in red. The background on which the letters are running is customizable. The number and color of key letters is not; These parameters are controlled in NuCaptcha and can be changed at any time if the developers decide that this is necessary to strengthen the protection against robots, as well as people recognizing captcha for a fee.

However, some additional protection in the system already exists. For example, if the system suspects in user kapcha- "Negro" (the method was not specified, but is likely to analyze the frequency of the input responses to videokapchi with the same IP-address), running a string of characters in the following captcha will slow down more and more. Accordingly, the appearance of key red letters on the screen will be delayed, so that a professional captcha recognizer will take 15 seconds for each video captcha. Obviously, NuCaptcha believes that it will make the job of recognizing video caps economically unprofitable, even for Indians .
