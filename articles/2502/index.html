Microsoft will make their cards even better

Microsoft has signed a contract with GlobeXplorer, a major supplier of aerial photographs and satellite photographs.

Over the coming months, GlobeXplorer high resolution images will be integrated with the Virtual Earth service. Currently, about 400 thousand square miles (more than 1 million square kilometers) of the territory of the United States have been filmed.

This innovation will allow Microsoft to better compete with Google, Yahoo and other market players. In addition, by November it is planned to create three-dimensional maps of one hundred cities of the United States, writes InfoWorld .
