Domain RF bends under any federal authority.

Today, July 13, 2010, the Coordination Center for the national domain of the Internet, notified all registrars of the sole decision to change the Rules for the registration of domains of the Russian Federation.

Official appeal


proof
Dear Colleagues,

On the voting in the Council of the CC there is a question of granting the right of priority registration of domains in the zone of the Russian Federation only for those media that were registered before May 12, 2010. See details below.

Please immediately bring this information to your employees who are responsible for receiving and processing applications for domain registration in the RF zone.

Detailed information:

Yesterday, June 12, 2010, the Coordinating Center for the National Internet Domain received a letter from the Federal Service for the Supervision of Communications, Information Technology and Mass Communications (Roskomnadzor), which provides information on the recent significant increase in the number of applications for registration of electronic media. In particular, it was stated that if in April 2010 3 applications for registration of electronic media were submitted to Roskomnadzor, then in May there were already 36, and in June 2010 the number of such applications increased to 900. At the same time, most of the applications contain the names of the media, consisting of commonly used words, for example - books.rf, flowers.rf, construction.rf and others.

Roskomnadzor assumes that the increased number of such applications is associated with the entry into force of the Regulation on the priority registration of domain names in the domain. RF for certain categories of users (hereinafter referred to as the Regulation), and applicants who register media with commonly used names have the goal of receiving name in the domain. RF corresponding to the name of the media, for unfair use, including for resale.

In order to suppress the possibility of obtaining the most requested domain names by means of ‚Äústreaming‚Äù media registration, Roskomnadzor requested the Coordination Center to make changes to the order of priority reservation of domain names for the media.

Fully sharing the concerns expressed by Roskomnadzor, the Coordination Center held consultations with representatives of the Ministry of Communications and Mass Communications and Roskomnadzor, which resulted in a proposal to amend the Regulation on the priority registration of domain names for the media. Currently, the Council of the Coordination Center is considering the possibility of amending the Regulation regarding the introduction of an additional criterion for those media outlets that can apply for registration of domain names in the domain. RF in a priority order. In accordance with the proposed wording of the relevant provisions of the Regulation, the right to submit applications for priority registration of domain names will be granted only to those media that were registered before the Regulation entered into force - May 12, 2010 (that is, no later than May 11, 2010).

In order to ensure the rights of bona fide users, changes need to be made before the start of the priority registration period for the media, i.e. until July 15, 2010.

The Council voted on the issue of making the Regulation on the priority registration of domain names in domain. The Russian Federation for certain categories of users of the following changes:

1. To state clause 1.4.5 of the Regulation on priority registration of domain names in domain. The Russian Federation for certain categories of users in the following edition:

"The founders of the media (media) registered before May 12, 2010 in the manner prescribed by the legislation of the Russian Federation, whose products are intended for distribution throughout the territory of the Russian Federation."

2. To introduce changes to the Regulation on the priority registration of domain names in the domain. The Russian Federation for certain categories of users from July 15, 2010.

Voting will take place on a calendar day on July 13, 2010. An additional message will be sent on the decision taken as a result of voting.

We apologize for the inconvenience arising from the short period of consideration of the issue
