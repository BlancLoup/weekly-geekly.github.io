Transfer from your Payoneer account to your local bank account

Hello, dear users. Payoneer is pleased to announce the launch of a new service: transfer from your Payoneer account to your bank account. Currently, the service is available in 50 currencies (including rubles) in more than 200 countries around the world.

The new service makes it easy to withdraw funds to your local bank account. In order to use this service, open the "Withdrawal" menu and select "Withdraw to my local bank account".



First you need to add account data. The account must belong to you and be registered in your name. After submitting the account details, our staff will check them and email you as soon as the check is completed. This process may take several business days.

After receiving confirmation you can transfer funds. At the moment, the minimum amount to transfer is $ 500.

The transfer arrival time to your bank account is up to 3 business days for local bank transfers and up to 7 business days for international transfers.

This option is already enabled for most accounts registered with Payoneer. If this option is not in your account yet, contact our support in any convenient way to connect the service.
