Startup Negotiations with the investor "amateur"

Greetings
No claim to epic, I want to share a story.

So, the person got in touch with me, let's call him "M". The essence of his words was as follows:
I know people who may be interested in buying such a project , if it is developed.

Before that, we were superficially familiar with us. I knew that he lives in Hong Kong and does some business with the CIS countries.
Well, the whole "business with China" looks like: bought there, sent here.
Those. at least he is not a professional investor / angel / fund, but a person who knows this business from the inside.

Distribution of shares

This, of course, depends on the amount of attracted investments.
And they, in turn, depend on what goals should be achieved, say for a year.

Version 1 (the simplest and most obvious option):
