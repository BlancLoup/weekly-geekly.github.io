What for?


On May 19-20, the 2nd Obninsk Innovation Forum was held in Obninsk, where, as you understand, they discussed how to save our country with the help of those innovations. Prior to this, in April, the city administration decided to conduct a request for quotations for the development of " PROMO applications for Apple iPad ." On the 21st of April an order was placed.
