Open all links in the same tab.



" Open a link in a new window "- it's just disgusting! I myself want to decide when, what and how to open! If I need to open a link in the new tab, then I will hold down the Ctrl key (in Opera - Shift).


And all this is due to the fact that target="_blank" registered for the link, which, as it were, tells the browser what should open in the new tab.

The Opera will come to the rescue remove_blank.css . Thanks to him, the links will be opened in the same tab. In Firefox, you can try removeblank.user.js in about: config for browser.link.open_newwindow to put "1". For Safari like this .

Remove_blank.css is not new - it was made by profiT in 2006. But I used it, it seems, I am alone.

Download remove_blank.css

Added: in Yandex and Google, you can configure how to open links in search results.
