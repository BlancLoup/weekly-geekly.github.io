Bags with built-in outlet

American Josh Cross launched on Kickstarter fundraising for the project GoPlug Charging Bags - a set of travel bags with its own built-in battery. GoPlug bags can charge equipment through standard interfaces (socket and USB) while the user is on the go or out of reach of the sockets.



The GoPlug project includes several types of bags: a laptop case, a travel case of various modifications, a backpack, a bag for photographic equipment. At the same time, they are all waterproof. The capacity of the built-in battery is enough to fully charge the smartphone six times, the tablet - twice, the laptop - about two times. All sockets are mounted on a swivel mechanism so that you can connect gadgets inside bags while carrying them, or outside while they are being used by the owner. About how much batteries increase the weight of the bag, the author, is silent, but writes how much they will be worth on his site - $ 65- $ 85




The price of GoPlug bags starts at $ 139. For a successful start of the project, Josh requires 65 thousand dollars. Until the end of the fundraising, 7 days are left and a little more than 25 thousand have been collected. Given how clumsy the pictures of the prototypes look, the project is unlikely to collect the required amount. But as an idea for DIY, such a suitcase can easily fit.
