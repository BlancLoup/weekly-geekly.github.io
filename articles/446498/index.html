Raiffeisenbank is looking for speakers at

On May 18, in Omsk, Raiffeisenbank holds a regular conference for specialists in the development and maintenance of information technology <code / R>. For several years, it remains the largest IT event in the city. And this time we invite you to become the stars of this event and make a presentation.




A bit of history


The story goes back to the distant 2016 year. Then the conference was created for the exchange of experience and knowledge between the distributed team. At first, as always, there was not enough communication between the two cities, and this event became one of the most important links in terms of uniting the children scattered across the vast expanses of the country. Over time, of course, the situation has changed, and the urgent need to hold a large internal event has disappeared, but the guys didn‚Äôt want to share their knowledge, so they replayed the format and turned <code / R> into an open Omsk IT conference.



What is it now


Now it is the largest IT event in Omsk, where colleagues from different companies can share their experience and knowledge. In fact, the main task is to create and strengthen the IT community in the city. This is a very noble and bright mission, and we believe in its success.

The conference program itself is divided into several streams: lecture and master classes. It was specially designed so that visitors of any level of training could find something to do to their liking: some of the speeches deal with highly specialized topics of interest to experienced specialists, others are aimed at beginners.



How to become a speaker and what awaits me


The conference audience is 400-500 guests. Specialists of completely different technologies with different levels of knowledge and experience. Expanding your horizons and speaking to such an audience may seem like a very laborious task, but the organizers will not be afraid to help prepare and finalize the report.

The 50 to 50 conference grid consists of reports from our experts and external experts. For the first time, we not only share our experience and want to know yours, but also do it in an open format with open submission of reports for everyone.

Rules:

  1. The advantage of technical reports. As in any conference, we want more meat, but also reports about soft skills, flexible practices and management always come in handy.
  2. Application can be left until April 10.
  3. Theme
  4. Description (2-3 sentences)
  5. Theses
  6. Level of listeners: initial, for practicing, expert
  7. Performance format - 40 minutes report
