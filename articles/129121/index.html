Report on meeting Apple Developers Community # 8

On September 2, the eighth meeting of the Apple Developers Community , a community created for the introduction and communication of Apple developers, took place in St. Petersburg.

The meeting was timed to the first release of iOS-school .



In addition to the cake and champagne, all the guests were greeted by all graduates with final projects, as well as a report by a special guest, the founder of iBearSoft , Andrey Pilyugin ( Andrey_Pilyugin ).





Having waited for the Ingria business incubator to fill the hall, the moderator, and part-time the CEO of e-Legion - Alexander Zverev took the lead.

Alexander talked about his company, the future of ADCSpb and the meaning of the iOS school. After listening to the parting speech, the hall was ready for further information battles.



The first (and only) invited speaker was Andrey Pilyugin from iBearSoft . Andrew, we found just on Habr√© on the wonderful article "How Bills for iPad was the number 1 in the US . " A couple of days of correspondence and the date September 2 in his calendar was already busy.


Text transcript

Talking about the creation and promotion of applications (for example, their cases), Andrew answered many questions from guests and went to the hall.

The iOS-school teacher Stanislav Krasnoyarov followed the scene. He spoke about the training program, his thoughts on the first graduation, and congratulated all the students who had come to the end. In addition, the set was announced in the second, autumn school iOS-developers .



Having finished his short speech, Stanislav sat down in the front row to watch his graduates from outside. Each of them had to make an iPhone application as a graduation project for the week.

The final applications were mostly seemingly completely normal applications, without any technical frills, demonstrating the ability to work with standard tools. But this was the essence of learning: in 2 months Stanislav taught graduates the basics, and now they will decide whether to develop further in this direction.

One of the final projects was a client for an online store, made by Anatoly Burov .



Unfortunately, other final projects could not be mounted. And among them were applications for: a jewelry store, a fitness center, a restaurant, etc.



In addition to the diploma and applause, each graduate received invaluable experience and knowledge in the sought-after field.

For dessert, literally and figuratively, a big cake in the shape of an ipad was waiting for all the guests, which was symbolically taken by the school teacher to cut it.



We are very pleased that with each meeting of the members of our community of Apple-developers it becomes more and more. On October 18, already the ninth meeting is planned, at which we expect guests from all over Russia, as it will be very interesting. Follow the announcements and thank you for your attention.
