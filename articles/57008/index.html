Sony Ericsson Idou - new SE with 12.1MP running Symbian OS

A new smart communicator from Sony Ericsson, Sony Ericsson Idou, has been announced. The features have already been announced:
  • An operating system developed by the Symbian Foundation
  • "Large display" with an aspect ratio of 16: 9
  • 12.1 megapixel camera
  • Xenon flash

The name under which the new smartphone will be available is not yet known. We are looking for a new device in stores from the second half of this year.
