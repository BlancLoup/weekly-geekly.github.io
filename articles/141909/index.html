Ways to view

IPTV via set-top box



If you already have Internet from Iskratelekom and you have a router supporting Bridge, then you are lucky, you will not need any additional equipment, after calling the company an engineer will come to you who installs the prefix, activates it, demonstrates the work.

If you do not have a router, you will have to purchase a switch, any unmanaged one will suit you, the company offers D-link DES-1005D or ASUS GigaX 1005B.



When connected, the subscriber receives the Sagem iad85hd prefix, in rare cases Amino 130 or ADB-3800W.

The fastest of them - Sagem. The slowest is ADB (it works slowly only with HD resolution).

It looks simple, quite modern, it does not overheat in the process. When viewing HD video does not slow down at all, the menu responds quickly. The console does not stand out with anything - it is simple, it lies comfortably in your hand (unlike the Amino remote control !!).
