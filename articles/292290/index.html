PayPal valued at $ 44 billion before secession from eBay



The cost of PayPal is $ 44 billion - that's exactly how experts estimated the payment system before separating it from eBay, FT writes . Yesterday, the company's shares appeared on the Nasdaq Exchange list, at a final cost of $ 36.4 per unit.

Thus, PayPal is estimated at 60% of the value of eBay. Starting today, eBay shares are available in two varieties, with or without the right of ownership of PayPal. After July 20, eBay shareholders will receive one PayPal share for each eBay share.
