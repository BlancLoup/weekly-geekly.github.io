Mail and Microsoft: a shoemaker without shoes

It recently took me, for the first time in many years, to write to Microsoft support, I usually got used to solving problems on my own, but the question here was not technical.

It turned out that it was not so easy to do for a mere mortal, but in 20 minutes I found a page on microsoft.ru with which you can send a message. I was promptly answered by e-mail and requested additional information. I answered, and it was already decided that the deed was done, but then the answer came:
