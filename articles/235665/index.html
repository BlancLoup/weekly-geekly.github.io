Lumia 830, Lumia 730 and Lumia 735 announced in Berlin

At the heart of the Lumia 830 case, as well as the flagship presented in the spring, is a monolithic metal frame in silver or black, which is also a design element of the smartphone. The frontal part is almost completely covered with Gorilla 3.0 protective glass of slightly convex shape, and on the reverse side there is a colored protective cover made of polycarbonate. The smartphone is presented in a completely black color, as well as combinations of silver frame and back cover in white, bright orange or bright green. Such a design "recipe" is becoming traditional for Lumia and allows you to focus on lovers of bright colorful design, and conservative users.

Another important point regarding the physical characteristics of the new smartphone is its thickness and weight. They are 8.5 mm and 150 grams, respectively, so that the Lumia 830 becomes the thinnest and lightest device in the line at the moment. The length and width of the new smartphone are 139.4 x 70.7 mm.

Lumia 830 is equipped with a basic 10 megapixel camera with PureView technology, LED flash, ZEISS optics (the optical system consists of 6 lenses) and the easiest and thinnest optical image stabilization system of all that we have ever created. The camera has a high sensitivity, allowing you to take high-quality images in low light conditions. The sensor size is 1 / 3.4 inches, the focal length is 26 mm, and the f / 2.2 aperture. The front-facing camera has a resolution of 0.9 megapixels and a wide-angle lens for better self-portraits and video calls.

The smartphone supports SensorCore technology, which allows you to collect information for fitness applications (such as Health and Fitness and Adidas miCoach) in low-power environments.

The new Lumia 830 is already pre-installed the latest Lumia Denim update with Windows Phone 8.1 Update 1. We will tell you more about it later in the post.

Nokia Lumia 730 DS / 735




The Lumia 730 Dual SIM and Lumia 735 presented today are two versions of the same model with similar characteristics and design, focused on Skype video calls and taking self portraits (or so-called selfies). The main difference between the models is that the Lumia 735 supports LTE networks, and the Lumia 730 DS supports simultaneous operation of two SIM cards.



A key feature of the new devices is a powerful front wide-angle camera with a resolution of 5 megapixels and the ability to record Full HD video. The focal length of this camera is 24 mm, and the aperture - f / 2.4. Thanks to the above characteristics and a wide-angle lens, the front camera is well suited for taking group photos with friends and sharing in Skype. Especially for the presentation of the smartphone was developed a special application for shooting high-quality self-portraits - Lumia Selfie.

The main camera in the new Lumia 730 DS / 735 smartphones has a resolution of 6.7 megapixels, autofocus, ZEISS optics and an LED flash. The sensor size is 1 / 3.4 inches, the aperture is f / 1.9, and the focal length is 26 mm.

The deep integration of Microsoft services in Windows Phone 8.1, pre-installed in new smartphones, allows one-touch switching from voice calls to video calls via Skype. A system consisting of a pair of microphones and intelligent voice processing technology ensures clear sound transmission during conversations.

In some markets, users of new smartphones will also have a three-month free Skype Worldwide Unlimited subscription to make international calls to a mobile or landline phone.

The devices have a 4.7-inch OLED display with a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels with an aspect ratio of 16: 9 and a pixel density of 316 pixels / inch. The smartphone supports ClearBlack technology and, like the Lumia 830, has a high sensitivity sensor.



As for the other technical characteristics of the smartphone, here Lumia 730/735 smartphones will offer users a 1.2 GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 processor and 1 GB of RAM. The internal memory of the device is 8 GB, but it can be expanded with microSD memory cards up to a maximum of 128 GB and cloud storage 15. It is worth recalling that with the recent update of Lumia Cyan, all Lumia devices were able to install applications on a memory card (respectively, if device supports work with them).

A battery with a capacity of 2220 mAh will allow you to use your smartphone all day without thinking about charging. The maximum talk time on the 3G network is 17 hours. The Lumia 735 has support for QI standard wireless charging (but only with the optional Wireless Charging Shell (CC-3086) - UPD ).

New items are presented in a recognizable Lumia palette: bright green, bright orange, dark gray and white. The dimensions of the smartphone are 134.7 x 68.5 x 8.9 mm, and the weight is 130.4 grams.



Lumia 730 and Lumia 735 also come with a pre-installed Lumia Denim update, which includes a new application for shooting "Lumia Camera". Also, users will have access to a package of mobile applications Microsoft Office, detailed maps and satellite navigation (HERE Maps and Drive +), clients of leading social services such as Instagram and Twitter, as well as the latest fitness applications that use the benefits of SensorCore technology, such as Fitbit and Adidas miCoach.

Deliveries of the affordable and powerful Lumia 730 Dual SIM and Lumia 735 to Russia are expected in October. Price will be announced additionally. The estimated cost of the 4G / LTE modification will be about 219 euros, and the 3G Dual SIM version is about 199 euros (all prices are exclusive of taxes and fees).

Nokia Denim update


Windows Phone 8.1 Update 1

We have already written in detail about the major changes in Windows Phone 8.1 Update 1, so in this post we will simply list them.

"Real" folders - now, to create a new folder on the start screen, you do not need to resort to the help of special applications, simply drag one of the tiles to another. Each folder can be renamed in the text box above.

Application Corner - Customized Start Screen, on which only certain applications and games are available. This feature will be useful for corporate use, but other usage scenarios are possible.

Improved work with SMS - now users can select individual text messages in the dialogue to remove them from the conversation or copy and paste into a new dialogue for sending.

Alarm clock - now you can set up a replay in the Alarm application.

User VPN - ensuring privacy and security when working with data in public Wi-Fi access points or in a home network.

Improvements in IE - faster and smoother browser operation.

Unique Lumia features

The Lumia Denim update also includes a number of exclusive features. Many features are hardware dependent and are being announced for Lumia 930, Lumia 1520 and Lumia 830 only. Information about the availability of certain features on other Lumia devices will be provided simultaneously with the distribution of this update.

‚Äú Lumia Camera ‚Äù - will replace the Nokia Camera application. It features an improved shooting algorithm, new features and more intuitive controls. Lumia Camera will allow users not to miss an important moment due to the quick turn on of the camera and continuous shooting with a pause between frames of several milliseconds.



The Moment Capture function, which is activated by a long press of a camera button in an application, automatically starts video recording at 4K resolution and 24 frames per second. Each video frame has a resolution of 8.3 megapixels, which means that each frame can be saved as a static image.
Rich Capture will add HDR support and allow users to first take a picture, then adjust the settings and select the best option.

Improved Glance screen (the screen displayed in the lock mode of the smartphone) - Lumia devices that support it can, in addition to the date and alerts, display other information, for example, from Weather or Health and Fitness apps.

The Lumia Denim update will be available for download by air in the fourth quarter of 2014, after testing and approval by partners.
