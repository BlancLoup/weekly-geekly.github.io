10% cashback on Apple payments with Payoneer

Payoneer provides iOS developers with payment solutions that make it possible to receive payouts, no matter where they are in the world. That is why thousands of developers around the world receive their payments with Payoneer. You can read about some of them in our blog .
We decided not to stop there, and we are pleased to announce that from August 1, 2013 we will return 10% of the first transfer that the account holder receives from Apple!



Learn more about this promotion and open a Payoneer account here .
By the way, this is a great chance to use the Refer a Friend program and invite other developers. For each referral who receives at least $ 100 payment, you will receive $ 25 (as well as friends who registered on your link).
If you already have a Payoneer account, simply activate the US Payment Service and start receiving payments for your account details.
We have not forgotten about those cardholders who have ever received payments from Apple. You can also participate in this promotion! Just send us a request by email as soon as you receive your next Apple payment (after August 1), and we will be happy to return 10% of the transfer back to you.
The maximum amount of cashback is $ 500.
