Tovarix - product search service

New, original startup in the field of e-commerce:



Problem
Today, the product range in any market segment is so huge that not every buyer is able to understand this ocean of goods. To select, for example, a mobile phone, it takes several days, or even weeks, to spend on studying different models and technologies. At the same time, the risk of buying inappropriate, low-quality or unreasonably expensive items remains rather high.

Idea
Simplify the process of choosing a product for a modern customer. Rid him of the need to study reviews, reviews, specifications, compare models and prices.

The buyer should not understand the entire product range on the market. It is enough that he knows what he wants and has a certain budget for the purchase. To help him decide on the choice should expert goods expert, consultant product range (online) store. It is desirable that several experts from different stores work with each buyer, who will offer him several models of products that fit his requirements and fit into his budget.

Principle of operation

- The buyer from the main page leaves a request to search for the goods, indicating the category of goods and price range. Sample request: "a mobile phone for a girl, with a touch screen, a good camera, GPS, 8 GB of memory and a powerful battery."

- All experts in the selected category of products receive this request by mail and can add to this request a product offering indicating the specific product model and price. Recommendations should be offered in strict accordance with the requirements of the buyer and with an optimal price / quality / reliability / functionality ratio.

- Several experts can answer one request.

- The buyer studies the recommendations of experts and follows the links of experts to the pages of online stores, where they can make a purchase.

- If the buyer is satisfied with the recommendations - he closes his request.

Detailed project description: www.tovarix.ru/about

What does the buyer get?
The buyer receives several optimal in terms of price / quality / functionality offers of products that exactly meet his requirements. This saves time on studying the model range in the market, reduces the risk of buying useless, inappropriate things, saves time and money.

What does the expert get?
The expert attracts new buyers to his online store, who know exactly what they are looking for, have finances and are ready to make a purchase. Such visitors are the most valuable customers for any store. If they are satisfied with the purchase - they will come again.

How much is?
For buyers, everything is completely free.
For experts - registration is free. For each external transition to the pages of online stores - 3 rubles are withdrawn from the account.
When an expert is registered, a bonus of 300 rubles is added to the account.

Who can become an expert?
Any owner or manager of an online store who professionally understands the product range of his online store.

Rating system
For experts, there is a rating system that allows you to determine whose recommendations are more authoritative. The rating depends on the expert's activity on the site and on the buyers' assessments of the usefulness of expert recommendations. Recommendations from experts with higher ratings are displayed above in the list of recommendations for a query.

Summary
We invite buyers to leave requests for the search for goods, and also we are looking forward to representatives of online stores on the project! Please register more actively and wait for requests from buyers!

And of course, criticism of the project is welcome!
