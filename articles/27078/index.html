MTS connected to the turnstiles

Probably all Muscovites remember how recently the new metro stations Trubnaya and Strogino were opened in Moscow. But not many are willing to recall that at conferences and meetings devoted to this issue, there were many innovative proposals for the development of the Moscow Metro.
In one of these statements (I do not remember whose it was), it was just said that it would be nice to pay for travel in the subway using a mobile phone. Then this proposal seemed to many impracticable and even fantastic ...
And so, quite recently, the first information appeared that the development of this original fare collection system is the responsibility of MTS.

I think many Muscovites who have to take the subway will like this decision. After all, if this idea is implemented, it will not be necessary to stand in lines and constantly search for a card in the bag or in the portman.
