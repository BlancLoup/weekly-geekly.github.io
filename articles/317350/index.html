Presentations and video presentations from the VR-Today virtual reality meeting

On December 10, our second open meeting took place in Moscow, dedicated to the development, launch, positioning and monetization of products in the field of virtual reality. Actually the meeting was called - VR-Today: a virtual reality party. Under the cat, we bring to your attention all the videos and presentations of speeches about VR-cinema, about VR-iron existing and future, about augmented reality, about mobile VR, about investments in innovative technologies and much more.



In general, the event itself took place in an interesting informal setting, right in the BarDisco90 bar, and besides reports there you could play Sony VR, test and buy Homido mobile VR helmets, products from the VR-Store virtual reality gadget store, or even win prizes from colleagues involved in the production of "cardboard" helmets - Bosglass VR and Pompeii Art. By the way, they can make branded helmets for events quite easily and quickly. But the main goal was to communicate with everyone who came with each other, to establish intra-industrial relations, find joint win-win cooperation stories and just get acquainted with colleagues on the interest in new technologies.
