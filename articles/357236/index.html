Russia included in the top five countries with the most developed cyber war



Domestic supplier of DLP-systems Zecurion Analytics in its own estimates suggests that Russia may have the fifth most advanced cyber armies, the newspaper Kommersant reports . The Zecurion assessment claims that the cyber capabilities of the Russian Federation are bypassed only by the USA, China, the United Kingdom and South Korea.

In the understanding of Zecurion, cyber warlike people are engaged in espionage, blackmail, cyber attacks and information wars. Such units affect the mood and behavior of countries. The more developed the country, the more vulnerable it is: the equipment is more and more dependent on the Internet, notice the company. Vulnerable devices of individual users, their machines, the infrastructure of countries.

The data presented by Zecurion was criticized by Solar Security's marketing director, Valentin Krokhin. In the commentary for Kommersant, he noted several problems with a similar assessment:

  • The UK Government Communications Center (GCHQ) employs 6,000 people. It is unclear how Zecurion analysts have determined that only 2,000 of them are engaged in cyber attacks.
  • Espionage, cyber attacks and information wars are the competence of technological intelligence. As a rule, these are civilian agencies that cannot qualify for military budgets.
  • There are cyber commands in military structures, but they are - at least de jure - protecting the infrastructure of military cyber systems. In this case, do not distinguish protection from attack.

Krokhin notes that the cyber parts of South Korea, Israel, Iran and Estonia are the most developed, Italy, the Netherlands, Turkey and France are in doubt. Now no country recognizes that it is attacking information systems of other states, Krokhin said.

Zecurion is a Russian company that develops DLP systems to protect against information leaks, is engaged in research and consulting in the field of information security. At the end of last year, the Ministry of Communications and Communications included the Zecurion DLP solution for monitoring information leakage channels in the Russian software registry.
