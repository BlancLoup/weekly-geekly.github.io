Inviter - Invites Exchange


I think most of you, dear readers, do not need to explain what invitations are, what they are for, and what works sometimes are worth making them. Newly habra-users will not allow to lie ... The concept of invites is a good way to form a high-quality audience and minimize the impact on the resource of different inadequacies. But at the same time, limited registration greatly complicates the lives of people who seek to join the community with the best intentions. So how can you earn a precious invite with a little blood?

Everything is changing!


The most interesting thing is that the ratio of people who are looking for invites and users who own these invites and are willing to share is not unique. For the same GoogleWave, for example, the supply is four and a half times the demand.
