Bartesian: get any cocktail from the capsule



Capsule coffee makers are familiar devices to everyone. Not everyone likes them, but there are also quite a few lovers of making coffee in just such a coffee maker, having received coffee with exactly the taste and smell you expect. It turns out that the capsule principle can also be used to prepare other types of beverages. For example, cocktails.

Bartesian is a cocktail preparation system that will help you create the most complex cocktail in seconds. You do not need to remember complex recipes, buy rare ingredients and interfere with all this, wasting precious time. All you need is the appropriate capsules and basic drinks at the bar (vodka, rum, gin, tequila).

To prepare a cocktail, you need to insert a capsule, select the desired mode of operation, and Bartesian will prepare a cocktail - just the way you expect. 1 cocktail = 1 capsule. In addition, it is necessary to pour the four basic strong drinks listed above into their respective tanks.



Now the "cocktail liberator" (that's what the developers call their device jokingly) can make six different drinks. Three classic and three - bonus. Over time, the creators of Bartesian will add new types of cocktails, creating new capsules.
