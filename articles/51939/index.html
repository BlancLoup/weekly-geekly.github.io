Protection list of private trackers

People can argue for a long time that trackers are theft from a developer, this is bad. Publishers can make countless attempts to crush and affect trackers. But the fact and the urgency of their existence remains a fact that can be taken as a modern trend and with which nothing can be done. After reading the article not so long ago, I didn’t particularly like the 7th paragraph, regarding the ease of circumventing the traffic accounting, which I would like to elaborate on.

Just a few words and go to the main idea.
