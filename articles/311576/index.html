The history of creating an application for finding nannies in Ireland and an electronic waiter



At the summer internship at the European Innovation Academy in Nice, students of our university Aydar Akhmetzyanov and Igor Shulgan developed mobile applications through which you can call a child care assistant and order lunch in a restaurant.

Students of Innopolis University had summer internships at Russian and foreign IT companies. Aidar Akhmetzyanov learned about an internship in Europe from a university newsletter, went through an online interview and went to France for a month.

‚ÄúIn the fall, we are launching a project in Ireland. In this country, education and certification of nannies is regulated at the state level, therefore there is a guarantee that a reliable person will look after the child, ‚ÄùAydar comments.
