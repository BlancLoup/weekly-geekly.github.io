RBK Money wallet service becomes free!

Hello! In June of this year a new instrument was introduced in our payment system - a professional wallet. This innovation was intended primarily for freelancers who use RBK Money in their work. A professional wallet provides its owner the opportunity to place on his personal website a form for receiving transfers from other Internet users. Initially, the commission for processing transactions from other users was 3.9%. We decided not to reduce this rate gradually, but to cancel it completely. From October 10, the commission when transferring funds to a professional wallet - 0%.

You can transfer funds to a professional wallet account in any convenient way: using self-service terminals, money transfer systems, in Russian Post offices, communication shops, banks, etc.

In order to get a professional wallet RBK Money, you must register in the payment system and go through the identification procedure. For 1 month, up to 100,000 rubles can be received via the site of the owner for a professional wallet.
