Star of David Filter



There is such a phenomenon when when parking vehicles because of the curves of Chinese hands that collected GPS antennas (options are also possible with the installers' hands or simply because of the dense building in large cities), during the night / parking there is an independent release of coordinates, and the system counts them as mileage, which sometimes can be up to 20 km (or even more). You can get rid of it in several ways. The easiest -program filter, but it will lead to errors in traffic jams, or when the vehicle is moving slowly. The second method (the most correct , implemented by us) is to connect the ignition as an event for which the motion will be processed, i.e. key turned \ inserted - we consider the movement. You can develop this option indefinitely - you can connect the cheapest accelerometer for motion processing, included dimensions, lowered handbrake, etc.
