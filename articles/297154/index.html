For the first time in Russia: the Yandex.Money service began accepting payment for the games of the world's most popular console

Yandex.Money has entered into an agreement with Sony PlayStation Network, a manufacturer of game consoles.

Yandex.Money is the first payment operator in Russia to pay for games for Sony consoles. Now 22 million users (this is the Yandex.Money audience) will be able to use their e-wallets for this.

The service previously sold payments for games on PCs, Nintendo consoles, offered to replenish an account on Steam, buy codes for iTunes and Xbox, a company spokesman Yevgeny Arnautova lists. Through the payment service, users can pay for games on other sites - for example, Wargaming.net , Origin.com or 4game.com .

No user fees are charged, which is not the case with counterparties. On the website of the service in standard conditions for digital content providers, for example, the commission on Yandex.Money wallets is 3%.

According to Json & Partners, the audience of game consoles in Russia in 2014 was 4.2 million people. But at the same time, ARPU (average revenue per user) in this segment is more than $ 4 per person, the highest in the market as a whole. "Traditionally, the console segment products are the most expensive on the market: both the set-top boxes themselves and the content bought on them require serious financial investments from the buyer," concludes Json & Partners analysts.

About 4% of payments come from cards linked to wallets, 8% of payments go through Internet banking. On average, for each user who pays for game content, there are two or three payments per month.
