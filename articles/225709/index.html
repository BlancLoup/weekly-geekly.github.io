Mobile version of Habr: version 2.0

Hello! Another good news for our readers who own mobile devices (for all users of the site?) - we are finally ready to present a new mobile version of "Habra".

Remember this simple address:



For those who have not been able to go to this address until today, we recall that it used to be a mobile version of Habr:

- Chaotic list of posts (due to the lack of personalized tape)
- Comments

Outdated design (although, rather, it was the lack of design), no authorization and other simplifications - in this form the mobile version was almost from the first days of its existence, no changes were made to it. Nevertheless, thousands of people used it daily, which could not but rejoice. Therefore, we decided to give them a pleasant surprise.
