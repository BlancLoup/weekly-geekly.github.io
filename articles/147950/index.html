The commercial story began 30 years ago - in 1982, the Telenet network was launched, which was accessed via subscriber terminals - Minitel, consisting of a monitor, keyboard and modem. Later they evolved, there were models with a color screen (Minitel 1 Couleur), with the ability to set a password for the terminal (Minitel 2), which did not overcome the Minitel Photographic prototype stage and others.

The number of pages in the Minitel system reached 25,000, users were offered a wide variety of services: train schedules, airplanes and ticketing, stock reports, tournament sporting tables, exam results, email, recruiting, etc. There was even a red-light district. While in the whole world the Internet carried business to new opportunities, sales channels, development directions, in France, on the contrary, it became a competitive technology for companies that already earned good money with Minitel.
