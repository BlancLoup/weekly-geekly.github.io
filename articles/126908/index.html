Navalny presented the official iOS application for RosYam



Alexei Navalny presented the official iOS app for RosYam, a project for fighting pits on the roads, launched in late May.

The application is free developed by Unreal Mojo . The application itself is also free.

As Navalny himself describes: ‚ÄúThe application is excellent: simple, very convenient and utilitarian. We saw a hole on the road, took a picture of it, launched the application - the complaint is ready in a second. ‚Äù

You can download it in the iTunes App Store .

Unlike the unofficial iOS-application released by Pravo.ru, in this application you will have to send a complaint to the traffic police yourself. Attached from Prava.ru, you need to pay $ 3 for sending a complaint.

As for Android, then it already has a beta version of the unofficial application from the "Redsolution". In addition, the desire to make a similar application for Android said the same "Pravo.ru", and the official application of the director of business development Unreal Mojo Alexander Patsay (alexmak) promised to release a week and a half.

Judging by the official statistics of RosYam, the effectiveness of the project is quite good. 1223 defects out of 7341 are now fixed.
