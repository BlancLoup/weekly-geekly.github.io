"Heavyweights":

Social network. There is everything you need to have a progressive social network, from groups to video uploads. Started very recently - July 1.
Prospects: It can become a social network number 3 after Odnoklassniki and Vkontakte. The project is engaged very seriously. This is spoken of as a domain (apparently, it‚Äôs not bought for one hundred thousand dollars), a list of services by the time of launch, a strong team of developers.

life.ru - L! fe
The project of the newspaper Life. Launched the project in April, is already included in the 20 most popular sites on the Runet. Yellow news.

gdeetotdom.ru - Where is this dom.Ru
Portal about real estate, the main feature of which is the ability to see photos of all real estate and its surroundings. Photos are taken by specially equipped cars plying the streets of the city.
Prospects: Excellent, if you look at the American equivalent. On our Internet market, things can be more complicated. But the project is really interesting and original for Runet. Thanks to the authors.

dribbler.ru Dribbler
Football social network from developers HabraHabr. The site was launched before the start of the 2008 European Championship, but so far has not gained much popularity. The part of users there was attracted in the first days from Habr, and then the flow of visitors began to plummet. Now on Dribbler 2-3 new users are registered per day.
Prospects: foggy. Immediately, the project did not go, that the owners will take to popularize it is still unclear. Until now, Dribbler advertising, except on habr.ru was not observed.

drive2.ru - Drive 2.0
Social network from the Internet magazine Drive.ru. The ability to tell and show your car. The project is implemented at a high technical level, but so far there is a shortage of services. Just wander and evaluate other people's cars quickly tires.
Prospects: The project will not achieve wide popularity and will most likely remain as an entertainment supplement to the main drive.ru resource.

All radio stations Petersburg. You can listen to songs from the air, create your own playlist, find the song that sounded on the air earlier at a given time
Website functionality, as I understand Moskva.fm clone. Both projects are executed immaculately high quality.
Prospects: popularity among residents of St. Petersburg and not only.

livehh.ru - Headhunter Live
Business-oriented social network: corporate blogs, professional communities. Another project of the company HeadHunter.
Prospects: There is a chance for success among office workers. Given the size of the audience of friendly resources hh.ru and webby.ru, some of them can go to this service.

top4top.ru - Top4Top
Pseudo-intelligent portal. The main failure of the year. Neither the names of the project's masterminds (Dibrov and Novozhenov), nor the $ 15 million allocated by investors for advertising and development, helped.
Made on flash, at the time of launch, the site had a lot of small and large flaws, for which bloggers simply pecked it. After that, a fat cross was put on the top4top, and the majority of users did not return there again.
Prospects: in this form there.

vshtate.ru - In the state
Social network for employees and employers. A new project by Pavel Durov, the creator of vkontakte.ru. The network offers to share experiences, summaries and be always up to date with the events of its industry.
Prospects: To become one of the main Internet projects of the new type for job search. Given the audience Vkontakte project can quickly gain popularity.

www.bestpersons.ru - The best people
An interesting project that combines the user's online business card, connecting all of his profiles in social networks and blog hosting sites. It is possible to write posts to different blogs from a single interface on bestpersons.ru.
Perspectives: Brilliant. It can really become the service that will unite all the services used by the user; passport user and his homepage on the Internet.
