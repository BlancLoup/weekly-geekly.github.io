How to clean the monitor screen

I share experience.

For several months, I endured divorces and blemishes on the monitor that were not rubbed off. Right here is absolutely nothing. Even a bottle of vodka was uncorked.

And yesterday I cleaned it so that now the screen of the switched off monitor looks perfectly black, without any stains or marks at all. At once I will make a reservation that what we cleaned is not suitable for LCD monitors, because their screen is made of plastic. I have a real pipe, 22 ", the screen, respectively, glass.



So here. I needed:

Brake cleaner


Plain kitchen (clean) rag


Cloth with microfiber


First, well sprinkle the brake cleaner onto the screen, so that it starts to drain, and then quickly and carefully (because it instantly disappears) we wipe the screen with a regular rag. If it is good to rub, then any stain will be erased. After there are no stains, there are still a lot of stains from the rag on the screen. We take a rag with microfiber and wipe everything well too. After that, the screen becomes perfect. I'm happy: )
