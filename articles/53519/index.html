ASUS Eee Keyboard in May

On the first day of CeBIT 2009 in Hanover, Asus officially unveiled the amazing Asus Eee Keyboard, announced earlier at CES.


This cute and compact keyboard is equipped with a 5-inch touchscreen display and ... a 1.6 GHz Intel Atom processor, 1 GB of RAM, a 16 or 32 GB SSD, as well as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules, two USB 2.0 ports, a VGA and HDMI outputs.


And Asus announced two models. Wired and wireless. The first will cost $ 400, the second - about $ 600.


To buy Asus Eee Keyboard happy bourgeoisie will be able in the end of May - the beginning of June.
