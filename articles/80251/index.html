And now the idea itself


I want to make an online encyclopedia of characters. As I see it.
  • Any user can register on the site and upload their image with the character. When downloading, he chooses the type of license for the image (BY, BY-SA, BY-ND, BY-NC, BY-NC-SA, BY-NC-ND
  • Search the site for the name of the characters
  • Alphabet Search
  • Character drawing lessons

Further development:
  • If you liked the image, then you can always contact the author and order his work
  • Contests! And what about without it?
  • Integration of different social buns.
