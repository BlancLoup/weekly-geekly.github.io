Crackulous: a simple program for hacking iPhone applications

Hacker sites report that Crackulous has been published on Cydia.hackulo.us, which allows hacking protection from any program from the App Store . The source is also coming soon. This is terrible news for developers who earn money by selling applications for the iPhone, and there are more and more of them.

The program is quite easy to use and, unlike its predecessors, has a graphical interface. It is easily installed on your iPhone or iPod Touch. Hack software can be literally in one motion, after which it becomes available for installation on any other device iPhone or iPod Touch.

Similar tools were available before, but because of their complexity, they were not widely used, so finding the hacked versions of programs for the iPhone on the Internet was rather difficult.

Interview with the author of the program can be read here .

By the way, as some examples show, hacking software for the iPhone can sometimes increase its popularity by orders of magnitude and even increase the number of sales.
