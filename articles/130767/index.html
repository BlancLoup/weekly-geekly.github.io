How to find a common language with the developer? Master class for project managers

There are plans to create a web project, there is money, there are people who can write code. What's next? Select development tools, plan the architecture, distribute tasks, organize work. If you do not feel confident in these issues, it's time to learn from someone else's experience. October 25 Sergey Boronin will just share his experience in the InCube.
Sergey will hold a master class "Anatomy of web projects, the minimum subsistence level of competence for a startup . " The master class will be useful to the creators of projects who need to achieve mutual understanding with the "techies" and articulate the requirements for the project.
Often business decisions are closely related to technical implementation issues. The choice of technologies can play a significant role in risk management, become decisive in the competition and, ultimately, have an impact on attracting investment. Therefore, technical literacy of a project manager is a factor that determines the success of his decisions.
Sergey Boronin will not only improve the technical literacy of the master class participants. He will talk about the architecture of a large web project in terms of risk management, help you understand how to choose the most successful business solution.
The following questions will be addressed:
Web Project Classification
Strengths and weaknesses of development tools
The impact of project scale on the choice of development tools
The impact of technical solutions on new stages of investment
Types of hosting, including in the clouds
CMS, CMS types
Frameworks, their types and limitations
Support for various browsers and its payback
Types of licensing their choice, verification of copyright infringement

The cost of participation in the master class is 1250 rubles.
Duration - 3 hours
The master class will be held on October 25 in the business incubator of the ANC InCube (Vernadsky Avenue, 82. Academy of National Economy, location map ).
