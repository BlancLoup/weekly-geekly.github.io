Does Microsoft have discipline? Windows 10 will be updated strictly in March and September



Until today, it was difficult to guess when Microsoft will roll out updates with new features (feature upgrade) for Windows 10. From September 2017, the release schedule will become more stable: the company announced that major updates to Windows 10, Office 365 ProPlus and System Center Configuration Manager go out twice a year on a fixed schedule: in September and March. The combination of updating the office suite and the operating system is due to the fact that Office Pro Plus and Windows 10 often install and update together, so this will make life easier for many.

Thus, the nearest update of Windows 10 and Office 365 ProPlus will be held in September 2017, and the next - in March 2018.

Established another clear rule. Each version of Windows 10 will be serviced and maintained for 18 months. In principle, this corresponds to the current practice, Microsoft writes, but the output along with Office and the official explanation of the policy will simplify the life of corporate sysadmins. It turns out that to maintain continued support, it will be sufficient to install the update clearly once a year and a half.

Microsoft hopes that the addition of semi-annual updates to the package also System Center Configuration Manager will also simplify the procedure for periodic updates on a schedule.
