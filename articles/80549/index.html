Online and helpful quotes

Let me bring to your attention the project " Useful Quotes "
In essence, this is a regular directory. With one difference only - the sites are provided with the information that they contain. This information is framed in the form of quotations, and the source is indicated for each quotation.
The purpose of this project is to contribute to the systematization of sites on the Internet.
Using the site is recommended as a quick reference guide, where the best excerpts from various sites are provided.
In the process of filling the site was read a huge amount of text and scrolled through thousands of pages.
Experience has shown that no matter how good a site is, only a small amount of information can be provided in the form of quotes in the ‚Äú Useful Quotes ‚Äù project.
Among the pages viewed should be noted multiple duplication of texts, often the same text was presented in dozens of resources.
Many viewed sites were created on the principle of copy-paste, but you could also find text copying followed by high-quality formatting, which improves text perception.
Most of the information read was transfusion from empty to empty. Often there were attempts to retell information at a conversational level, when an understandable phrase through the addition of emotional statements and "personal opinion" increased in size several times with almost complete loss of meaning.
We were pleasantly surprised by the articles, which had to be almost completely broken down into quotations and transferred to the project - they were written so well and adequately. It was even a little embarrassing to do this and feel like the last copy-paste.
So, the site " Useful Quotes " is presented to your court. It is interesting to know your opinion on this controversial project. Your comments, criticism and advice are welcome.
Is this site something useful, or does it not make any sense, and is it just a waste of time?
