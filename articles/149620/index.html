Business is like football

I love to watch football and play it. I follow the news of different clubs. And I often wonder that football is a projection of our business in all successes and failures, breakthroughs and failures, laws and contingencies.

Relatively speaking, the team of our company is a football team. The criterion of victories or defeats is the profitability of the business. If you can score goals and not miss, you grow. You know how to not miss at least - already a sign of stability. You skip and don't score - it's time to change the coach at least, and maybe go down to the lower league, rethink the key issues and then prove that you can fight along with strong competitors.

The basis of the team is midfielders. In our case, these are the developers. Here also directors and testers. That is, those who create the product or service. Technical support is protection. If the defense is attentive and experienced, then it copes with clients with high quality and well, retains their loyalty and leaves no doubt about the future profitability of the business. Forwards is a sales department. It is clear that a good salesman should be aggressive, persistent, restless, but at the same time correct (technical) jewelry maker of his craft. Agree, this is exactly what the attacker should be. Otherwise, he can inflict a lot of punches, but they will not hit the shots.



The role of a manager in different companies is different. Ideally, this is a coach. Desirable good, experienced, "experienced". He played at one time in different parts of the field. He knows what and how to demand from the players in different situations. It builds understanding in the team, if necessary, rotates it, motivates, scolds, comforts, etc.

The leader must be a tactician and strategist. At meetings, he gives the team installation, sets tasks. Well, everything is like in football. And the outcome of the match depends on the correct installation of the game. If his business idea is correct, then there will be a result. How many times do we hear the phrase that one coach outplayed another. Competition in business lives exactly according to the same laws. And there are not so many accidents.

If a business fails, then the manager, as responsible for the final result, has to play the role of a goalkeeper. The midfield missed an opponent, the defense does not cope, and now the goalkeeper has to catch shots on goal in the form of customer complaints, partners' claims. And the more unbalanced the team, the more strikes will be inflicted by the opponent.
And it is even difficult in this situation to say whether it is a good leader or, on the contrary, a bad one. On the one hand, instead of embarking on the coaching bridge and establishing ‚Äúconnections‚Äù in the team, he is not doing his job. On the other hand, he stands at the final line and saves the whole team. Being spectators or fans, we look at this situation and think about whether it is worth saving such a team. Maybe she needs to lose hard and seriously and rethink everything ...

A strong captain can shake the team at the right time, charge with emotions. By the way, is it necessary to be such a person? A weak captain is a coach's mistake, it would be better if there was no captain at all. In such a team will be anger, misunderstanding and understatement. Here I look at Arshavin and I think that the player is technical, good, and as a leader, it probably hinders. Let's hope today's friendly match will answer this question.

The important point is not only the game of the team on the field. But the microclimate outside the field. Each team has its own microclimate, and it can change.
