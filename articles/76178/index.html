List of socials for feeding google.de

If for you the word Lesezeichen (German bookmark) doesn‚Äôt say anything, then this list is created for you, cousins. But you already know the advantages of Pligg and have read the first commercial thread on serche about Western social affairs last year, and I think you will find a worthy use of this list. What to do to appease google.de? Feed him social networks in German ...

In Russia there are no optimizers working under .de and German-language sites. That is, their number is calculated by the fingers of one hand. Not much better is the case with .com, but there are many atrocious affiliates, although they often get so-so because of their poor English proficiency. But suppose you were assigned to promote a resource in German, and you need to start with something.
Start with social programs. Google loves them and all German issuing including MF is all social.
The bulk of the German social networks are gray and inconspicuous. They are built on a slightly modified pligge, so the registration forms are about the same (somewhere the password is generated by the site, somewhere both the login and the soap are asked, although one soap would be enough).
For some bookmarking services, you will need to export bookmarks from the browser. For FF3 (Bookmarks Menu -> Manage Bookmarks. In the window manager of the menu Import and backup -> Export to HTML) this is elementary, and I do not know optimizers who do not use Firefox.
