Briggo: a coffee bot that brews coffee as good as a barista



I think on Habr√© a large number of coffee lovers. There are probably just fans of the taste of coffee drinks, and those who use coffee only as a stimulant (especially in the morning). So, recently information was published about the robotic barista Briggo, able to make a huge amount of coffee drinks, including those that are prepared according to the recipe of the client.

Briggo does not look like a humanoid robot, by itself. Outside, this is a kind of kiosk, the size of which is comparable to the size of a telephone booth. Briggo is a serious startup that has already received about $ 11 million in investment; this is not a concept.

Managing positions in the company are held by people who previously worked at Motorola, Dell, Johnson & Johnson, and other well-known organizations.

As mentioned above, Briggo is able to prepare almost any of the famous coffee (and not only) beverages. Espresso, American, iced coffee, mokachino, latte and others - all available to the client who wished to taste something special. According to the developers, the machine can also prepare drinks based on tea, but the main attention was paid to the preparation of coffee and derivatives. Soon a device for cooking, something like a multicooker , can be shown, only larger and more productive.



Briggo is interesting not only because it can make many types of coffee drinks, but also because its work is automated.
