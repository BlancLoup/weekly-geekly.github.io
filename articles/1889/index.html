Blinkx.TV will provide Microsoft with its search engine

Blinkx.TV will provide Microsoft with its search engine for use in MSN and Live Internet resources.

The method of online search for audio and video data, proposed by the software giant by Blinkx.TV specialists, involves the use of special software that performs speech recognition. According to the developers, this method of detecting multimedia files is more efficient than those used by Yahoo, MSN and Google.

The agreement between the two companies gives Microsoft a direct license and does not provide for the distribution of advertising revenue. The financial side of the transaction is kept secret, reports TechWeb .
