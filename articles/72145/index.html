Optimization of expenses for mobile Internet



Having looked at the tariffs and packages of Russian OpSoSov, I saw a sad picture. The prices for the mobile Internet are clearly overstated and with constant use of the Internet, be it you or your devices that are closely tied to it ( for example, Android OS communicators, WM, etc. ) you will have to pay more than one thousand rubles for it.


And so, if your device constantly looks at the weather, checks mail, twitter, RSS, then the average Internet consumption is at least 5mb per day, if you update all services at an hourly interval on Android, then the device already eats 15-20mb per day, which is quite a lot - 70 rubles per day at a cost of 1mb - 3.5 rubles.

Of course, gigabyte is not so much, but enough for basic needs.
