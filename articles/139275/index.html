Boot to Gecko - a look at Mozilla's mobile OS [Video]

Perhaps some people know that Mozilla , the developer of the popular Firefox browser, is preparing to release its own mobile OS , codenamed Boot to Gecko . The main focus of the creators are on the web platform orientation, to be more precise, all the shells, applications and interfaces will be written in HTML5 .



At MWC, we were able to learn a few details about the unusual development and look at the alpha version of the system itself.

As a test device was taken smartphone Samsung Galaxy SII . Boot to Gecko (B2G) itself was installed on top of Android , and in the future, users themselves will be able to install it on rooted smartphones. So far, the system is damp and slows down a lot, but you shouldn‚Äôt demand too much from such an early build.

The undoubted advantage of B2G is that applications written for it can work on any device with a browser. In addition, it is likely to be distributed free of charge, just like Mozilla Firefox . The company is negotiating with various phone manufacturers about possible cooperation.
