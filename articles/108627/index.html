Apacer AH522 (8Gb)







The flash drive is made of plastic and metal. It weighs only 18 g, its dimensions are quite ordinary 59.1 x 21 x 7.2 mm. The USB connector here is inside and is pulled out with a special lever.



This model is available in two versions: a capacity of 8 and 16 GB. The device is able to read and write data at a speed of 30 Mb / s and 12 Mb / s, respectively, and is also equipped with a blue LED status indicator.



By the way, the owner of the product is given the opportunity to download the Apacer Compression Explorer (ACE) proprietary software from the official website of the manufacturer, which allows not only to protect files stored in the drive, but also virtually to increase its capacity almost five times by compressing the recorded information.

Apacer AH128 (8Gb)







Apacer Handy Steno AH128 is positioned as a flash drive for men. It is available in red and silver colors, as well as celadon. The case is framed with black edging, the drive is also equipped with a leather strap with a zinc alloy buckle.



This is a modern and stylish accessory for men, with protection from water and dust.
The model range of Handy Steno is represented by high-tech devices for data storage, produced by Chip-on-Board (COB) technology, which connects the flash drive chip and the board into one and protects the device from moisture, dust and mechanical damage.
