Navigator Contenter

Many sites have poor navigation. In the event that the site to which you came is one such, the Contenter navigator can help you.

The idea was born in the process of discussing PreScroll and is as follows.

The Contenter plugin scans the html-code of the page, tearing out the headers of different levels (h1..h6) from it, and builds it in its window. The result is a tree of headings that you can conveniently navigate to different parts of the page.

Since not all sites use headings for their intended purpose, or are not used at all - as an option - you can build a tree depending on the font size of the design elements.

Another feature of the plugin can be an automatic site map. The map can display the first level titles and / or headings on the pages. You can also display short descriptions or keywords. You can make it possible to choose what to display.

Design add to taste.
