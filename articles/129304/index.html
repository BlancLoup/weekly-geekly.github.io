Useful applications, games and documentation for modern DesktopLinux-systems

Two useful additions have been published for modern DesktopLinux-systems containing programs and games that are not in the standard repositories of Linux-distributions.

The DVD contains over 30 interesting applications.
In addition to the programs, the disc contains video tutorials, tutorials for working in Linux, as well as electronic versions of popular free magazines and books on the subject of open source software and Linux.
