In Tomsk, create a team of robots, football

Last year, students of Tomsk State University of Control Systems and Radioelectronics (TUSUR) won the qualifying stage of the Robocup Japan Open 2013 - the football championship for humanoid robots. The students of the laboratory of robotics and artificial intelligence of the Institute of Innovation TUSUR won the discipline of 3D Simulation Soccer, that is, in a competition of programs for controlling 3D simulations of robots.

Now it's time to take the next step. In Tomsk, they started the creation of the first in Russia team of real football robots.



The team consists of two strikers and a goalkeeper. Currently, software for players is being created in TUSUR.
