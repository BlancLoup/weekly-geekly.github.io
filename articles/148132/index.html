Microsoft believes in the Mayan calendar

Today I downloaded a trial version of Visio Professional 2013. The description is worth a separate topic, but I can immediately say - a very nice Metro interface and nice updated templates. You can also go to www.microsoft.com/office/preview/en/try-more-products (Thanks to Vladimir Yunev for the link) and choose one of the new products of the family. But our conversation is not about that. As it turned out, Microsoft firmly believes in the end of the world 21.12.2012.

1. Go to the Visio Professional Preview and create a Timeline.
2. Drag any of the Timeline Shapes into the workspace and the Configure Timeline window opens.
3. We see that any project ends 12/21/2012



Now I am plagued by vague doubts - Can it really postpone the end of the year to 12/21/2012?
