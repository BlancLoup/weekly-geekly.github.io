Ted Talks - Silverlight 3 TED Video Player

The development team of thirteen23 never ceases to amaze us with its interesting developments. They combine excellent services (such as Twitter , Netflix , etc.) with powerful technologies (WPF and Silverlight), and eventually produce applications that we enjoy using. Let me remind you that these very people released Twitter client Blu .

And today, their new development - TED Talks. What it is? This is a video player that allows you to view videos from TED.com in a convenient way (smart people share their vision of the present and the future on this site). Moreover, this application was made using Silverlight 3 technology. It was in the third version of SL that support appeared to work outside the browser, which uses TED Talks.

Actually, to install the application you will need to pre-install yourself Silverlight 3 Developer Runtime (supported by Windows and Mac) from this page.
