Nokia Belle FP2 is available for the Nokia 808 PureView

Arrived update Nokia Belle FP2 for camera phone Nokia 808 PureView. The update, in my opinion, is pleasant, but not fundamental. Upgrading to it is only for those who use this mega-camera phone more like a smartphone. But for whom the Nokia 808 PureView is primarily a unique camera, the update is not necessary. There is an update over the air and personally I have all the data and settings remained unchanged.




The most striking change is the virtual keyboard. The keyboard was the element of the interface, which almost everyone and from the very beginning caused complaints. And this is despite the fact that Nokia has samples of great virtual keyboards. In N9, for example, or in the same new Nokia Asha Touch line, which devices have displays only 3 inches in diagonal, Nokia managed to make an ergonomic and comfortable keyboard.
