Disrupt NY 2012: the best of the best this year

Recently, Disrupt NY 2012 was held in New York - a start-up competition organized by TechCrunch. For three days, on May 21-23, an unprecedented lynch of young projects took place, seeking to win the coveted prize of $ 50,000. Let's look at those five software projects selected for participation in the final contest.



Ark


A search engine that helps you search and build samples on Facebook, Google +, Twitter and many other services, so you can see if your classmates live in the same city as you, are not married, and so on. Unlike Google‚Äôs social search, more than thirty formal conditions and filters are available in Ark . If Google is already showing success in this direction, then the social network number 1 is not very good: Facebook has already tried to buy Ark, but without success.

Babelverse


The service offers a universal solution for speech translation based on the community. Since machine recognition of speech is inadequate in quality, the founders decided, then let those who can do it - people will do the translation. In fact, the project is a platform, on one side of which there are translators who translate speech in real time, and on the other side - poor tourists who get lost in an unknown country or journalists who are too lazy to decipher the recording of the interview. Who knows, maybe the guys were inspired by Rajesh's dream about Siri :)

Nevertheless, if the project really gains popularity, then an online exchange of professional translators with an open market pricing mechanism will actually arise.

Uberconference


Most startups were born and are born in the process of automating and simplifying the needs of their authors and founders, and this project is no exception. According to the creators, Uberconference is an easy-to-use cloud solution for conference calls. Killfiches of the service is postulated as follows: to enter the conference, just click on the link; there is no need to register and log in to the service and enter other codes and passwords. A convenient conference management and the ability to view information from the social profiles of the interlocutors during the conversation have been implemented. The project is monetized according to the freemium model, features such as subscription and decryption of calls are assumed to be paid. The service is executed in two isostachs - browser and for iOS.
