For the developer, it is absolutely natural for the desire, if not to come to the ideal, then bring its brainchild to mind. In order to do this, we need feedback. The Internet provides a unique opportunity to get feedback (criticism, advice) with minimal effort. I got very interesting people, professionals who spent their time to help the project. Prospects and opportunities for crowdfunding


Leading a crowdfunding project, I made important conclusions for myself:
  • First, crowdfunding is a very real opportunity for developers who do not have the money to release a series. If we talk about production, then there has long been the practice of single-piece sales of their products to a narrow circle of consumers. Such products are very high quality, but usually have an uncompetitive price. The release of the series with the help of collective sponsorship allows you to significantly reduce the price due to the bulk purchases of components. Yes, you have to make an experimental copy for your money - but this is no more expensive than doing amateur radio. Here, instead of money, time is invested.
  • Secondly, crowdfunding is a universal way to test the adequacy of any new product, even if we are talking about a new line in an existing company. Crowdfunding technology eliminates the need to make high-risk investments. At best, you can sell a batch, and at worst - get a bunch of useful analytics. Even in case of failure, you get new acquaintances and a significant upgrade of the original product: those who pay money are interested in improving the final product.
