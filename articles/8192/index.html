"Stream": new tariffs

Broadband Internet access provider Comstar-Direct (Stream brand) this month introduced a new line of unlimited tariff plans. In addition to the cheapest “Start” anlim tariff on the Moscow market (128 Kbit / s for $ 8 per month), Stream tariffs were launched - 3072 Kbit / c for $ 25 per month, and “Mainstream” - 4096 Kbit / s for $ 32 per month , “Streamer” - 6144 Kbit / s for $ 40 per month, etc. In addition, a new limit tariff “Time” was introduced, within which 1000 MB of traffic at a data transfer rate of 6144 Kbit / s is purchased every month for $ 10.

Subscribers can also choose from a set of television packages. For a surcharge of $ 3-10, you can buy a monthly package “Basic” (54 channels), “Advanced” (91 channels) or “Unlimited” (98 channels + “Games”, “Radio channels” and “Video on Demand” services). If the subscriber does not plan to use interactive television, he can remain on the tariff with “pure” high-speed access.
