The social network Comby.ru is one year old

April 26 social network Comby.ru celebrates its first birthday.
During the first year of its existence, the number of registered users on Comby.ru reached 154,255 people, more than 20,000 of whom come to Comby every day to chat on blogs, upload photos or videos, find friends or former classmates.

Designed for an audience consisting of young and active people (students, schoolchildren, office workers, etc.), Comby unites more and more participants - sociable, creative, seeking to express themselves and know the world around them.

In April 2006, Comby became the first Russian social network and still holds the leading position among universal social networks.

Users find on the site interlocutors, new and old friends and classmates, draw up profiles, lead blogs, upload videos, photos, participate in groups. Unlimited space for hosting photos and videos, the ability to insert HTML into the profiles of participants and groups give complete freedom of expression.

Over the year, Comby changed several times according to the wishes of users - the design was improved, user profiles were improved, OpenID authorization was introduced, a video service appeared that supports all popular video formats, and the users saw a new home page for their birthday.

350 thousand pages are viewed daily by social network members. Comby continues to grow: in search of modern, convenient communication and entertainment on Comby - Russian MySpace - about 900 new users are registered every day.
