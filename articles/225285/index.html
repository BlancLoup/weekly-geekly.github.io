The first Omsk Geo-Hakaton completed

On June 1, the first Omsk geo-hakaton ended.
Recall that it was held in two stages: the first, the competition of ideas , and the second, the implementation of the proposed idea. We are in a hurry to tell about the second stage, we have succeeded!





After the first stage, the teams had a week to refine their ideas, and at the end of the second stage, submit a working prototype. The second stage of the geo-hackathon took place over two days - May 31 and June 1.
