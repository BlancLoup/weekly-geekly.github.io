Round table "Autotest and Android"


Alexander Smirnov, Alexander Ezhkov and Dmitry Voronin talk about auto tests on Android. They share their experience in using UI tests that have been developed in projects of various sizes: from start-ups to large companies with dozens of full-time developers, answer questions from the audience, speak extensively about the feasibility of writing UI tests, possible pitfalls, and features of testing on real devices.
