Original gameplay ideas

In the meantime, as most of the more or less large companies make monotonous games, independent developers are looking for, inventing, trying new opportunities.
Under the cut a small selection of games in which there are fresh ideas.

Unfinished Swan : around monochrome space, the player bullets balls of the opposite color to mark the surface. The game is in development.


Flipside
The mod for Half-Life 2, the main character is crazy with a split personality, you can turn the world on the other side. And there - it's different.


Tempo
The player controls the lady in an elegant hat, moving in a wheelchair, but with super-abilities like telekinesis and "out of the body." The video is nothing special, but the idea is rich, I think.


World of goo
But this is not just a curious concept, but a successful game, with interesting tasks, stylish graphics, and atmospheric music.
