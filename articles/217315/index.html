Photo report from the exhibition of retrocomputers in Minsk

An exhibition of old iron is held in Minsk this week.

Excerpts from the exhibition website:
Cult computers 80-90-ies of the last century. More than 100 unique exhibits. Most of the exhibits presented at the exhibition are part of the private collection of Alexander Alexandrov, a 4th year student of the faculty of computer systems and networks of the Belarusian State University of Informatics and Radioelectronics.

Warm, lamp exhibition :) All the time that I was there, new people came there. At some time there it was already difficult to approach the tables on which the elderly equipment was located.
