Javascript pong

I decided to support the series of posts ‚ÄúMaking XXX on JS in 30 lines‚Äù. Took for example Pong in close to the classic design:

It turned out not quite in 30 lines, but as many as 38, because Logic came out decently, but did not want to completely kill readability or compress with minimizers / obfuscators.

In the demo:
  • Management by Up / Down;
  • Enemy AI;
  • Accelerate the ball with each rebound;
  • Keyboard handling, independent of ball movement;
  • Waiting for keystrokes to launch the ball;
  • Support for virtually any size of the field and the height of the "players";
  • Maintain statistics.
