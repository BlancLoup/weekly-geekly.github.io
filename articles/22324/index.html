Small twitter life hack for beginners

In a nutshell. Twitter is a microblogging platform. In fact - a group chat with its own circle of contacts and a completely indescribable aura.

There is a very simple and effective way to start working with twitter. It will help beginners to easily learn how to work with twitter and easily get hooked on this nice igloo :)


First you need to register and it is very desirable to enable working with jabber / gtalk (yes, it is called devices).

Then we send the message to the twitter bot on , we get a confirmation:
twitter (00:00:00 00/00/0000)
Notifications are on. Turn off by sending 'off'.

Now you need to add someone active on Twitter.
This is done by the NIK follow command.

For starters, you can add the following comrades:
follow numberone
follow rulix
follow nafigator
follow BBSoD
follow ELIZIUM
follow nomoretears

... and life hacking, by the way, is primitive to disgrace!
We look to whom the listed comrades answer, and do follow for those to whom the answers go.

That is, we see a quote:
twitter (00:00:00 00/00/0000)
dorofeevilya: @ maksim77 in my opinion, this is Greek cognac, right?

And then do follow maksim77 .

Thus, you can quickly dial the contact database, then simply filter out not very interesting friends with the NIK command.
