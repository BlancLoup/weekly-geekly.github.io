We invite mobile developers and startups on AppClub {build, monetize} # 4 in Kiev

February 21 in Kiev will be the fourth meeting of AppClub {build, monetize} .



AppClub {build, monetize} - professional communication on mobile development, promotion and monetization, as well as the opportunity for mobile startups to talk about themselves and get live feedback.

What's included?

Domnikov Vitaliy dotCypress is a professional developer of applications for the web, as well as Windows Phone and Android mobile platforms.

Interesting facts about Vitalie:

  1. Not only knows, but also knows how.
  2. Charismatic and interesting speaker, knows such scary words as F #, monads and MongoDB.
  3. He was one of the first in Ukraine who learned about the Metro interface and learned how to use it successfully.
  4. Nyashka.

His report will be devoted to the creation of an MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) framework for Windows Phone and Windows 8 platforms.

Vladimir Kirillov darkproger - iOS developer.

Despite his youth, Vladimir is a frequent visitor to various hardcore conferences and events.

Interesting Facts:

  1. He loves his iPhone (but in his heart he dreams of a Lumia 920).
  2. It has a profile on github.
  3. This Linux guy!

The topic of the presentation: "Accelerating the development of applications for iOS", which will be devoted mainly to the issues of executing code in isolation from the application context.

Krakovetsky Alexander sashaeve - the author of Windows Phone 7 Rocks! . Head DevRain Solutions . Microsoft Regional Director, Microsoft MVP, Telerik MVP, Ph.D. Information Technology, Forbes Columnist .

Interesting Facts:

  1. He spoke at the first three AppClub meetings. People still greet him.
  2. Look like that's it.

Alexander will talk about intelligent algorithms for processing textual information (text mining), in particular, about how the Readability services work .

Andrei Yermolaev a1ndrey - iOS application developer, idevelops.com .

Andrei is the author of Interlocutor HD (an analogue of Siri in Russian), which occupies top places in the Russian AppStore.

Interesting Facts:
  1. Unlike many, Andrew not only develops applications, but also earns them.
  2. He knows a lot about how to look for investments.

Andrey will share his experience in the development of the Interlocutor, as well as answer questions from start-up entrepreneurs.

Meeting details


When: February 21 18: 00-22: 00.

Address: Kiev, st. Kudryasheva, 14b, first floor (office of Epam Systems).

Entrance fee: symbolic $ 15 (120 UAH). For students - 50% discount (you need to send a student scan to info@devrain.com). You can pay via Webmoney (details will be sent on request to info@devrain.com) or via Eventbrite .

Pizza / coffee included.

PS Those who wish to host or support AppClub in their city can write to info@devrain.com. Also, if you have experience in designing, developing, promoting and monetizing mobile applications, you can become the speaker of our next meetings. Let's get acquainted!
