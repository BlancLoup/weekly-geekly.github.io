The developers of a number of companies created the Expert Council of the gaming industry (ESII)



Developers from Mail.Ru Group, Riot Games CIS, Wargaming and other companies created the Expert Council for the gaming industry . It includes people like Ilya Karpinsky (Mail.ru), Vyacheslav Makarov (Wargaming), Alexey Krainov (Riot Games CIS), Anton Yudintsev (Gaijin Entertaiment), Orlovsky Sergey (Nival).

The Council has many goals. Among the main ones, the quality of games created in the region, the support of developers and the development of e-sports can be highlighted.

The CIS gaming market is considered one of the fastest growing in the world. According to statistics from NewZoo in 2014 , the Russian gaming market ranked 12th in the world and showed rather serious growth. The Russian-speaking gaming audience is estimated at almost 40 million players, much of which is willing to spend money on games.
