Rambler Cards Restarted in New Quality

Today we present the beta version of the updated Rambler-Kart . This is the first service that is restarted in the new concept and in the new corporate style of "Rambler".

The amount of data provided makes the Rambler-Card, even in the beta version, the most detailed in runet. The service provides detailed cartographic information about 2,000 settlements, with an accuracy of streets and houses. Nobody has such coverage on the market. The basis of the service is a city with a population of more than 100,000 people. For the first time, users will see maps of many such cities on the Internet.



Actual traffic information is provided for 86 cities in the European part of Russia - this is also the widest coverage in runet.
