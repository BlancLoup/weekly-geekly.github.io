New analytics service offers data for 1000 days on Twitter

A new service, Reseach.ly , has been launched , which allows you to see the history of Twitter messages for the last 1,000 days.
Also, the new platform is equipped with a powerful analytical module, using which, users can get data based on keywords, twitter demographics, geographic location, time frame and much more. The result of the analysis is given when classifying microblogging into community types (news, business, etc.)
The service is conditionally free; when ordering specialized reports and more in-depth analytics, there is a paid subscription.
The first users of the platform and TechCrunch readers have already noticed that despite the decent data of the service, any analytics requires explanations, even if it concerns Twitter. And even the results of research.ly clearly need improvement, such as, for example, a module for constructing graphs and diagrams.
