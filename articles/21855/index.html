Survey using the tag cloud

I am not a designer, but sometimes interesting ideas come to mind, some of them even manage to be realized. Here is one of them (maybe the idea is not new, then sorry).
I propose to use a tag cloud instead of the standard vote (using radiobutton, checkbox and button). Firstly, it is more beautiful (dianolne) than the standard form, and secondly, this type of voting reduces the number of button presses. With standard voting, we first select the option, and then click "Send". Immediately simply click on the option you like. As an addition, the total number of votes can be indicated in parentheses, or how many votes have been added today.
