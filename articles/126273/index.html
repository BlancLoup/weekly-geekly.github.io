Place your price - it's easy

It was with this motto that we began to write our small and modest project. The service is focused on small and medium business. The meaning was as follows: the seller has a lot of goods and there is a customer base, customers constantly call the seller to place an order. What happens when customers get a lot? - Busy phone, annoyed customer, tired seller from endless calls.

How to solve this problem?



The easiest option that everyone uses is to put a girl in to receive and process orders. Actually they do that.

Minuses:
  • Salary. For a small business, hiring a new employee is very noticeable.
  • 1 person can handle a limited number of orders. Over time, the girls need more and more.
  • Order can be made (call) only at certain hours.
  • Orders on pieces of paper are easily lost.

pros
  • It works.

What do we offer?


The bottom line is this: You place your price with us and redirect your customers to our website, where they make the goods themselves, and then they simply come, pay for and take away.

Pros:
  • Orders on the server are never lost.
  • The buyer places an order.
  • The site is always available for purchase.
  • Time only to view the order.
  • You can get by with fewer employees.

Minuses:
  • Not all customers own a computer.
