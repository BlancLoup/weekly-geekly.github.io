Don't startups need money? #StartupPoint

"Startups don't need money." In any case, this is stated by more than 50% of startups presenting their projects at StartupPoint meetings.
The initial capital is often not the main problem of a startup. It is more important to get an expert assessment of the project and the feedback of colleagues, as well as to get contacts of potential partners and get to know them. StartupPoint community meetings are aimed at solving these problems.

This Sunday ( March 28 ) the 17th Moscow meeting of startupers and investors StartupPoint will be held.
On it, developers and entrepreneurs will be able to present their projects to a circle of experts and investors, get practical advice (on a number of issues: legal, technical and financial).
The event can be visited (come to it), and see (subscribe to the online broadcast) to anyone who wishes.
Registration is open.

The following projects will be presented at the meeting:
  • wobot
  • MobietyPolls
  • b2bvideo.ru
  • guider.mobi
  • Bytefy.com
  • Electronic pharmacist
  • Intraservice
  • SaEx
  • Dvazap
  • POIdo

On Sunday, our experts will be:
Maria Chernitskaya, CEO and co-owner of iConText ;
Gaidar Magdanurov, Web technology expert, Microsoft ;
Alexey Dolotov, Head of Research and Analytics, Yandex .

The program of the event, the conditions of participation and the latest information will be available as it arrives on the event page .

We invite service providers for start-ups for free (advertise) to tell about their services. To obtain accreditation of the service provider, write through the feedback form .

If you have a project, add it to the startup directory , get the attention of investors, experts and potential partners.

Where and When : March 28, from 15-00 to 19-00. MIRBIS Institute, Moscow, st. Marxist 34, Bldg. 7, floor 7. (View on Yandex.Maps )
