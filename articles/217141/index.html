Microsoft Research Director for External Research Programs will deliver a lecture at Moscow State University

April 7 at 17:00 at MSU. Mv Dr. Daron Green, director of Microsoft Research for external research programs, will address Lomonosov. The lecture is aimed at students, graduate students and young scientists studying computer science or using computational technology in their studies or research.
Speech: Innovation and Research at Microsoft Research.

Short description


Microsoft Research is a global organization developing modern science and expanding the capabilities of computers. Today, Microsoft Research is one of the largest research organizations in the world, where many well-known scientists work.

In his report, Dr. Daron Green, director of Microsoft Research for external research programs, will talk about the future of collaborative computing technologies and the global strategy of Microsoft Research. He will demonstrate how Microsoft Research explores and creates unique research opportunities on flexible experimental devices using cloud infrastructure.

The presentation will take place in the building of the Faculty of Computational Mathematics and Cybernetics in the audience of P-13. To register in the list of participants (to the list at the entrance to the Moscow State University building), you must send a letter to contact@mstlab.org by April 2, 2014, indicating your name and place of study or work.

Biography of Dr. Daron Green

Biography of Dr. Daron Green


Dr. Daron Green is the director of external research at Microsoft Research, he is responsible for the collaboration of Microsoft scientists with scientists from around the world and determines the research investment strategy. External research is one of the key components of Microsoft Research, which includes close cooperation with the academic and scientific environment to solve the most complex global scientific and social problems. The range of tasks is varied: health and welfare, education, computer science and environmental science. In the past, Dr. Green was engaged in research in modeling molecules and equations of state of fluid mixtures - in his work for a bachelor's degree in physical chemistry (1989, Sheffield), and PhD thesis on molecular simulation of fluid mixtures (1992, Sheffield). Then he was engaged in the simulation of the packing of polymers and proteins (1993-4). His research required the transfer of software applications and optimization for highly parallel and distributed computing in a number of areas, including computational chemistry (molecular dynamics and applications for quantum mechanics), radiography, hydrodynamics, and fundamental engineering. Dr. Green then focused on high-performance computing and was responsible for Europe‚Äôs largest parallel computing programs for the European Commission, the main suppliers of high-performance computing in the United Kingdom for research and military organizations. Then Dr. Green moved to SGI / Cray - where he helped found the European branch of professional service, on the basis of which he organized a small team for Selectica Inc. Selectica specialized in online configuration technologies and logical systems provided as a web service. Due to experience in high-performance and distributed computing, as well as web services, IBM invited Dr. Green to develop the initial strategy of GRID computing in Europe, Central Asia and Africa, which soon became global. Dr. Green moved to the USA where he worked on the IBM EBO. Dr. Green moved to Microsoft Research from BT, where he was responsible for the proposals for all sectors in the global services system. He also worked on strategic activities and business development activities in a variety of business areas, including healthcare, security, working with the public sector, energy, and environmental sustainability. Within the latter direction, he founded BT program, which is responsible for business proposals to commercial customers, allowing them to reduce environmental pollution by carbon and develop business practices that focus on environmental protection, and take into account the impact on the economy and society.
