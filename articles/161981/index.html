Promotion of mobile applications

According to statistics, 59% of mobile applications do not pay back the costs for its development.
An original idea and a good implementation are only half the way to success.
A good application, but without marketing, is likely to fail, and a bad one with proper promotion can pay for itself tenfold.

Next, we analyze the techniques of promoting the application without investing.

ASO optimization

It takes two steps and each has its own purpose:
1. Directly search engine optimization (SEO),
2. Visual optimization.

The purpose of the first is to increase searching ranks. That is, make it so that your application can be found by as many people as possible using a search engine.

There are three main free admissions.
1) The name of the application. It is very important to choose the correct name. It mainly depends on him how many people will find you using the search. Try to make the title include the main keyword search words.
Example: an application on the theme ‚ÄúEntertaining games for parties‚Äù is better called ‚ÄúEntertainment for parties‚Äù. Immediately 2 keywords: entertainment and parties. It is not necessary to call, for example, "Entertaining" or "Mimosa".
2) Description. Most users read only the first paragraph. Try to make it as informative and attractive as possible. Also use maximum keywords in the text.
Example: an application on "Cooking". Use in the description of the following words: cuisine, recipes, delicious, cooking, and so on.
3) Translation. User growth up to 15%. Use the main languages ‚Äã‚Äã(English, German, Chinese, etc.). Try to provide a high-quality translation and not translate the text in Google translator, other programs.

The purpose of the second step is to increase the attractiveness of the application.

1) Icon. First of all, users look at the icon, then at Screenshots, and only at the end of the description. Therefore, pay special attention to the application icon. The icon should reflect the essence of the program and stand out from the competition.
2) Screenshots. Also very important item. Make screenshots that show your application at its best.
3) Promotional video. You can make a demonstration of the program, where try to hide the roughness of the graphics application, show only the most successful solutions.
4) Translation Screenshots. Up to 30% user growth.

Downloads on different days of the week and holidays



As we see on weekends and holidays, there are more downloads than on weekdays.
Try to publish the application before weekends or holidays, it will help you quickly get into the top "new free \ paid". It will be even more effective if you get to other tops, on the highest line possible during the weekend or holiday period.
