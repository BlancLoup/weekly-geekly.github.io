Types of communication for business: which one to choose?

A modern company, no matter how large it is, is simply impossible to imagine without a telephone. However, there are quite a few varieties of telephone connections today. What kind of communication is more profitable?

IP telephony

Recently this type of communication is gaining momentum. What is its essence? In simple terms, this is an opportunity to make calls worldwide via the Internet. To do this, of course, you need to connect a special service provider.

Among the advantages of IP-telephony is the possibility of multi-channel connection, as well as the network nature of the connection. In the first case, you can set the number of interlocutors, in the second - use free calls between offices in different buildings. In addition, tariffs for IP-telephony is indeed lower than that of a regular telephone connection. This is especially felt when communicating with foreign employees and partners.

Among the downsides of this type of connection is the need for a stable Internet connection and a speed of 512 Kbps. For large cities, this is not a problem, but not every provincial Russian city (not to mention the countryside) can boast a stable signal and high speeds.

Analog connection

The traditional form of telephone service, available almost everywhere and familiar to the majority of the Russian population. Communication is carried out using an analog signal, which is transmitted via the line drawn for this.

The advantages of traditional analog communication are clear: availability, wide range of distribution, as well as the low cost of the necessary equipment.

But there are significant drawbacks. The first is tariffs, which are still high compared to IP-telephony rates. The second is the additional costs of equipment and line when connecting one more (two, three) numbers.
