Programming Contest: JSDash (Results)

Today we publish the final results of the programming competition and reward the top three winners.

Congratulations to the winners!

  1. Yuri Chechushkov - 55954 points. The prize is 3000 USD .
  2. Ouanalao - 55653 points. The prize is 2000 USD .
  3. Anton Ivakin - 54100 points. The prize is 1000 USD .

(The results above are for 100 test levels.)

Test results are published on GitHub .

Due to the very small gap between the leaders on the results of testing at 20 levels, we decided to increase the volume of the test set to 100 levels. The gap has increased quite a bit - from 0.34% to 0.54%, but the decision of the leader retained an advantage. At the same time, the third place was taken by another solution, not that at 20 levels. Perhaps it would be worthwhile to make the test suite even more, but then we would have to postpone the announcement of the results for a long time. We accept the set of 100 levels as official, since testing at 100 levels is obviously more accurate than at 20.

The res directory contains the protocols of 100 tests for each solution. If you come up with some interesting analytics on the basis of these data, which we have not thought of, please let us know!
