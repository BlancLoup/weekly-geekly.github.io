NVIDIA Delivers Part of Video Card Documentation to the nouveau Team

Yesterday, September 23, Andy Ritger, the leader of the team responsible for developing drivers for Unix systems, on the nouveau developer mailing list , the open driver for NVIDIA video cards, published some of the documentation for Device Control Block, a table containing information about topology and content of video cards.

The report says that NVIDIA plans to continue to publish documentation on its video cards in the future, as well as that developers will pay attention to the nouveau mailing list and clarify incomprehensible places in the documentation.

On the mailing list, these applications were greeted with enthusiasm.
