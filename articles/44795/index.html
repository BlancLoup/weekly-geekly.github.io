Photos in Google profiles

Google Profile used to be just an account to provide a single login to various Google services. But now, on the basis of profiles, they make a platform with various attributes of life-streaming .

The other day, Google added another element of social networks to the database of registered users. From now on, each profile owner can add a stream of photos to him, that is, an updated photo album. You simply specify the source of the photos (Picasa, Flickr or other), and they will be broadcast to your standard profile.
