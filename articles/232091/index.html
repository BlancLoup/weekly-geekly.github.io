Intel Pro 2500 - new SSD series with hardware encryption

Reliability, usability and security are the main requirements for products for business users. All these qualities are fully possessed by the new series of professional SSD Intel Pro 2500 . The new SSDs have an extended lifespan, support data encryption with a 256-bit key, comply with the security standard of Opal 2.0 storage devices and can be controlled remotely using Intel vPro technology.
Intel SSD Pro 2500 will be available in capacities ranging from 120 to 480 GB in 2.5-inch form factor and M.2. The maximum sequential read speed is 540 MB / s, sequential write - 490 MB / s.
Sales of new SSD models will begin this quarter, starting at $ 104.
