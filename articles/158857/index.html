Hacking the Adobe Connectusers.com Forum



Names, email addresses, phone numbers, hashed passwords for more than 150,000 users, including Adobe employees and government customers, are the catch of a hacker who cracked the Adobe forum.

At the moment the forum is closed. From the attack suffered: Adobe, Google, NASA, the US Air Force, educational resources (the forum contained data from employees of these companies and institutions).

Adobe officially recognized the hacking of the Connectusers.com forum and reset all users' passwords, reminding that the use of different logins and passwords on different sites is the key to security on the web.

The hacker under the nickname ViruS_Hima assumed responsibility for hacking. Then he also said that he had sent all the technical details of the hacking to the Adobe security service.
