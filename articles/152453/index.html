Calls using iPod, or turn MP3 player into a phone

Let me introduce you, dear habrazhiteli, my idea!




As everyone is well aware, the main difference between the iPod and the iPhone is the absence of the call function of the first one, which proves not a small difference in price.
I would like to suggest an idea for creating a device similar in size and design to the iPod Shuffle. Let's call it iCall.
In fact, iCall is a box with 3 modules:
- sim card module;
- GSM antenna;
- Bluetooth;

A sim card module is needed to receive data from a mobile operator, and bluetooth to establish a connection between the device and the iPod.

The necessary functions for dialing, taking a call, sending messages will be provided by the application installed on the iPod.



Any headphones with a microphone (for example, new Apple headphones), which continue to perform the function of headphones for the iPod, are connected to iCall. Just like in the phone, in the case of a call, the music is muffled and communication is carried out using the built-in microphone.

From the above, it turns out that iCall is a small mobile phone, in which the role of the screen plays the iPod. And with the help of a small accessory, the mp3 player turns into a great mobile phone.



The idea is posted on the website www.quirky.com . The desirable price of the product is $ 50-70. If someone wants to vote, I will only be grateful Link .

ps This device can be connected not only to iPod, but also to iPad.
