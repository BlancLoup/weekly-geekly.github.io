The gathering of system administrators live

Attention-attention to all those who have already firmly decided to go to the fifth All-Russian gathering of system administrators, still doubts or does not even know what it is about.

On July 1, on Thursday, exactly at 15.00 Moscow time, the studio of the SLTV channel will gather the organizers of the fifth All-Russian gathering of system administrators and will answer questions you can ask in real time.

The live broadcast will be broadcast on the website www.sletadminov.ru , and anyone can take part in it by asking a question on the Skype hotline (dsa.hotline) or in a special section of the forum (by the way, you can start asking questions in the forum so that during the broadcast the answers to them should be voiced first).

We are waiting for you on the air!
