PHP Digest number 157 (May 20 - June 3, 2019)


Fresh selection with links to news and materials. In the release: PHP security updates, commercial company Doctrine, PhpStorm 2019.2 EAP, Symfony 4.3, a portion of useful tools, video recordings, and much more.

Enjoy reading!


News and Releases


  • PHP 7.3.6
  • PHP 7.2.19
  • PHP 7.1.30
  • The creators of Doctrine after 10 years of existence of the project founded the eponymous commercial company . Now the doctrine can be officially funded, buy trainings or consultations, and also sign up for paid webinars.
  • PHP Version Statistics - 2019.1 - Traditional collection of statistics on the distribution of PHP versions based on the data Composer sends when connecting to packagist.org. The use of new versions of PHP is growing, and the total share of all PHP 5. * is about 10%.
  • PhpStorm 2019.2 EAP - The PhpStorm early access program was launched , thanks to which you can feel the new IDE features for free. Among the innovations available now: autoinject and highlighting of regular expressions in PHP, support for typed PHP 7.4 properties, copy-paste search on the fly right in the editor, support for the cURL syntax in the HTTP client, EditorConfig out of the box, and more.
  • The PHP-FIG ended the election of members of the Core-Committee - Asmir Mustafic became the new secretary, Woody Gilk and Matteo Beccati joined the former members of the Beau Simensen , Larry Garfield and Matthew Weier O'Phinney committee . Read more about the structure of PHP-FIG .
