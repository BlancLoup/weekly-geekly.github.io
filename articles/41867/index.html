SSUZ website

About a year ago the technical school turned to us with the task of creating a site for an institution. In this post I want to introduce you to the general concept of the resource. My goal is to get your opinion (preferably students) about the site. And also hear your suggestions.

System features:
  • Access from outside only by personal accounts
  • Unique design
  • Timetable of classes
  • pupils
  • E-library
  • Questions and answers
  • News and photo stories
  • Memorable dates
  • Hall of Fame
  • Newsletters
  • Testing system


Timetable of classes

The schedule of classes is displayed in tabular form. This takes into account the group in which the student is studying. Those. It does not display the entire schedule for the entire institution (this option is available through an additional link), but the schedule for a specific group.

The table displays:
  • Item Name
  • Time and duration of the lesson
  • Audience number
  • Full name of the teacher

pupils

This section displays the full list of students. At the beginning of the school year, students are automatically transferred from one group to another.

In the future, based on this information, it is possible to create a service "Search for classmates . "

Also in this section displays information about circles, sections and students who participate in them. Creation of new groups is allowed. For example, if several students participate in a research project.

E-library

The library contains documents (books, magazines, textbooks) in electronic form. This can be the following types of documents:
  • Archives
  • Text documents
  • Audio files
  • Video files

Some documents can be opened directly on the site. At the same time, statistics on the number of downloads is kept.

Questions and answers

In this section, students can ask their questions directly to the leadership of the PCF. Technical workers can:
  • Answer the student personally and at the same time remove the question from the list
  • Reply as an e-mail and make it so that only the one who asked the question sees it.
  • Reply as an e-mail and make the answer publicly available.

Unlike the forum (conference), the section does not allow discussions.

News and photo stories

Those. several news feeds (several topics, for example, "News of the Technical School", "Competitions") in each of which contains a list of news. Inside the news there is an opportunity to add photos.

Memorable dates

The section displays information about:
  • Birthdays of students, employees, famous people
  • Holidays
  • Technical events

Hall of Fame

This page is divided into several sections. For example: "Best Athlete of the Month", "Competition Winner", etc. Each section displays a list of students in the format:
  • A photo
  • Full name
  • Group number
  • Brief description of merit

Newsletters

All interested users of the system can subscribe to the list. Mailings can be of several types:
  • Site news
  • New documents in the library
  • Timetable of classes

Testing system

LMS connection. Currently, there are two systems under consideration: moodle.org and www.atutor.ca

Instead of conclusion

They immediately refused to lay out grades on the site: this is a burden on teachers (no AWP), students are adults (up to 45 years old) and for a number of other reasons.

The project has not been implemented. Moodle is currently being deployed.
