SolidLogic GS-L10: mini PC in a silent radiator case

The company Logic Supply, specializing in the supply of small form factor systems Mini-ITX, announced the release of a new compact computer SolidLogic GS-L10. This new product is interesting because it is equipped with a powerful dual-core processor with a clock frequency of up to 2.53 GHz and at the same time boasts exceptional silence due to the absence of fans in the case.


The secret of effective "fanless" cooling lies in a special case, covered with huge radiators. You can clearly see it in the photo.
The novelty includes the motherboard MSI MS-9818, the microcircuits of which are cooled with the help of radiators with a system of heat pipes. The computer can be equipped with a 2.5-inch SSD-drive or a protected hard drive. Of the supported processors are marked Celeron M CM858, Core 2 Duo P8400, Core 2 Duo T9400. Two SO-DIMM sockets allow you to install up to 4 GB of DDR2 memory.
A set of ports includes two PS / 2, four USB, two Gigabit Ethernet (RJ45), as well as VGA, HDMI, DVI-D, RS232. There are expansion slots CompactFlash, PCI Express Mini, 32-bit PCI, PCI Express x1. Computer dimensions are 225 x 195 x 80 mm.

New comes preloaded with Ubuntu 8.10. Price SolidLogic GS-L10 in the minimum configuration is $ 1020.
