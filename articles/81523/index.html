There was a patch for Internet Explorer (Operation Aurora)

As promised, Habr's readers will be the first to know about the appearance of the update :)

Just a couple of minutes ago, we published an update that closes the vulnerability in all versions of Internet Explorer. You can download the update through Microsoft Update (Microsoft Update) or download it manually through the Download Center

We remind you that in order to increase the security of your computer, we recommend upgrading your browser to Internet Explorer 8 .

Threat And do not disable auto-update in Windows, it will allow you to regularly receive the latest security updates.

You can learn technical information about usability here: www.microsoft.com/rus/technet/security/advisory/979352.mspx + information in English here: www.microsoft.com/technet/security/bulletin/ms10-002.mspx
