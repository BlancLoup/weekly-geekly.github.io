AWS_Ru meetup at Raiffeisenbank

We invite you to the meeting of the AWS_Ru community, which will be held at the Raiffeisenbank site in Nagatino, on July 4th. We will talk about IoT and AWS solutions, learn how to squeeze everything out of the cloud and still listen to the secret speaker. Prepare your questions and come to share experiences, it will be interesting.



Borisenko Petr, Synergy team


AWS IOT. Fast (but not too) solution
We will tell you about our experience in developing an IOT platform for managing and monitoring telecommunications equipment facilities. How the application works and which AWS services are useful to us. About why we chose AWS and what problems we encountered on our way.

Andrey Ivakhnenko, Antiplagiat.ru


How to squeeze the clouds
Anti-plagiarism is a special-purpose search engine with tens of millions of checks for borrowing per year. Andrei Ivakhnenko, will present a case of efficient use of AWS, will talk about ways to save on infrastructure and some pitfalls. How to squeeze the maximum performance at minimum cost: Reserved Instances, Spot Instances, different types of EBS disks, Autoscaling Groups.
