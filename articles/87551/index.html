Notebook Cooler ZM-NC2000 Review


Notebook cooler ZM-NC2000 is a cooling pad for a laptop. I have long wanted to buy something like this, but I did not dare at all, and suddenly they gave it to me. For about a month I have been using this device and decided to tell about it. First of all, the packaging pleased me with its pleasant appearance, even despite the complete absence of Russian letters on it.



The photographer out of me is not very good, so then the photos are not mine, but from the bourgeois site ( http://www.techpowerup.com/reviews/Zalman/ZM-NC2000/2.html ), and all are clickable.

Open the box and see: the stand itself, the USB power cord and instructions.
Immediately drew attention to the pleasant aluminum surface. Bottom black plastic was quite strong to the touch.
The packaging says that the surface has 928 vents - I did not try to calculate it, but it is very similar to the truth.
In addition to the holes, there are protruding rubber inserts that prevent the notebook from sliding on the stand: two points at the top and a strip across the entire width at the bottom - these things really work! )



The stand has two USB ports, one of them (optional) is plugged into the USB power cord from a laptop, and the other becomes an additional free (!) Port for connecting any device to the laptop.


Also it is necessary to mention folding legs, as on the keyboard.
