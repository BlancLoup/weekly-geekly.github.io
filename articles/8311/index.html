QIP Infium: first information and screenshots

The developer of the popular ICQ-client "QIP 2005" recently published information about the new product, about which there were only rumors - QIP Infium .

I will cite two key developer quotes:
Using Infium, you can now connect to various instant messaging networks, ranging from Jabber to Mail.Ru@Agent, everything will depend on your choice and desires. We would also like to avoid chaos with samopisnykh plug-ins and therefore all of them will be controlled before they are available to users.

QIP Infium is also created by one person and is created almost from scratch, i.e. this is NOT a converted qip2005, only some stable parts of the code are taken from qip2005. Infium has implemented the very best that has been accumulated over the years of creation and testing of qip2005. As they say, recreating the wheel once again, you already know where you will meet the pitfalls and you will not repeat the previous shortcomings. Of course, this would be ideal, but still. Labor and patience this time took a lot more, and I think it's worth it, you can appreciate it soon when we post the first test version.
