We consider the tasks of the hackathon from Promsvyazbank. Why can a hackathon?



Promsvyazbank opened accepting applications for the hackathon PSB SAMARA BATTLE , and the Technoserv company decided to support the event and act as its technology partner.


Hakaton will be held in Samara from September 30 to October 1. His goal is to find the best developers, designers and even marketers in order to develop a prototype of a new product or solution to one of the topics relevant to Promsvyazbank. Below we look at each topic from the point of view of which prototypes of which products can be developed within the framework of the hackathon, as well as what to prepare for and what to expect from the event.


What topics are interesting


Topic 1. Gamification


Any solutions that in the game format help the client to better understand the brand, products and stocks of the bank, for example, while waiting in a branch line. The idea may be to launch some kind of viral internet game.


Topic 2. Proactive financial assistant


Any products that help people plan and control the state of their personal, family finances and investments, achieve personal and family financial goals. A distinctive feature of a proactive financial assistant should be the possibility of self-study based on analyzing customer behavior and receiving feedback from users. Both the ideas that solve the problems proposed in the subject are interesting, as well as any ideas that expand the boundaries of a personal financial assistant in the classical sense.


Topic 3. Big data and artificial intelligence


Any products based on big data technology and artificial intelligence aimed at: increasing sales of banking products (deposits, loans, cards, payments, transfers), providing personalized offers, improving the quality of banking services, retaining existing customers.


Topic 4. Instant payments and transfers


Any products that allow you to simplify and speed up making payments for goods and services or transfers to each other. New ways of payments and transfers. Any solutions that allow the customer to solve his regular payment tasks in the best way possible.


Topic 5. Helper gadgets for business


Any products for portable devices (smartphone, tablet, smart watches, GPS tracker, etc.), which will be assistants for solving some business problems of legal entities and individual entrepreneurs.


Topic 6. Opening an account for legal entities


Any products / services that will solve the problem of attracting new customers, including assistance in choosing a service program (tariff).
