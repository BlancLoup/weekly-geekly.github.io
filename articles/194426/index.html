Training courses and books about Unity


Scream School in Moscow! A huge and serious course on game development with a focus on Unity. On Friday , September 27, we present the course, tell and show. Register and come . Is free!

Real Time School in Moscow announced intensively on Unity . I really like the idea and format - I worked on the weekend and went enlightened :)

Unity School in Kaliningrad has already begun work, actively and seriously . I am inspired by the target audience of Kaliningradians: to create games - after all, it develops a child much faster than playing them!

There is still Unity in render.ru and a number of teachers and schools who have just started developing the course. Did I miss someone? So I do not know about you - write to oleg@unity3d.com, get buns and bonuses for your desire to teach Unity.
