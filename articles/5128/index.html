RuTube opens an affiliate program: Video on your site is just

Video in RuNet is gaining popularity by the day: it's not just fun for the lucky ones with anlim. Good channels spread outside the Moscow Ring Road, and with them video clips. And this is not only "fun" - begins to gain popularity viewing important news on the network. Attendance of sites with video clips increases significantly. Video is becoming not just popular, but also regularly discussed.

Previously announced RuTube API development is complete. Russian web masters are offered a simple and convenient way to organize a section of video clips on their website. It will interest the owners of news, entertainment portals, gaming resources, sites about cinema, sports. Add a simple view and share videos on your site. And it looks like this is your site, not RuTube.

Register as a RuTube partner and get the ready-made html codes needed to create your own video section. Using them is no more difficult than Google Adsense. Visitors of your resource will be able to watch videos, record video from the webcam, leave their comments. And those who are capable of more than copy-paste can use the styles of their site in code, write their own javascript functions and make the added pages indistinguishable from other pages of their resource.

The first partners of the video hosting RuTube are the entertainment resources of Webpark , Anekdotov.net and the sports portal Eurofootball .
