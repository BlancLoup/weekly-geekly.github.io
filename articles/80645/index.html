Copyright and AppStore

Hello to all,
I want to share an amusing case that happened happening with my iphone game. I think that it is no secret to anyone that there are practically no unique ideas. Practically, on any game that is available you can find an analogue on another platform. Let's see what is really subject to copyright protection and what to do if you complain of a violation in the app store for your game.

Checked, this is not in the app store, and started to implement. The code was completely and was written by me from scratch, and I asked the graphics to draw a familiar designer.
