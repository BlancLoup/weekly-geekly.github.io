Considering the above, in the sale of goods by the agent KKT is applied on a mandatory basis.
At the same time, in accordance with the aforementioned clause of a transaction concluded by an agent with a third person on behalf of and at the expense of the principal, the principal and rights arise directly from the principal. In this case, the CCP is applied by the principal.
