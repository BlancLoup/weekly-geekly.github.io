Development of HD-interface for the phone

The process of developing a new interface standard for connecting mobile phones to high-definition displays has begun. For the project are responsible specialists from Nokia, Samsung Electronics, Silicon Image, Sony and Toshiba. They called the whole thing Mobile High-Definition Interface Working Group.

The goal of the project is to create a standard interface that will allow connecting a mobile device to HD TVs and monitors. One of the conditions - the ability to transfer pictures of quality 1080p and audio, as well as simultaneous charging of the gadget.

It is based on Silicon Image technology called Mobile High-Definition Link, which is designed to exchange data between portable consumer devices and HD TVs.
