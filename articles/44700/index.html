Currency Converter (UA)

You know, if you enter into Google, for example, 100 usd in uah - it will return the amount in the right currency?
The advantage of this feature is completely killed by the fact that Google uses a familiarization course , but it does not closely correspond to reality.

Therefore, it was necessary to transfer currencies manually, looking at an objective course on finance.ua .

On this lovely Saturday night, my patience was exhausted, and I wrote a currency converter . He accepts the same request as Google, and is familiar with this course. For convenience, the converter is embedded directly into the Firefox search bar (and IE7-8).

Waiting for suggestions and comments.

Disclaimer: written for myself, because the course is Ukrainian. If there is a demand, I will do the translation at the rate of the Central Bank of Russia.

PS: if you can transfer prices at the real rate, it is somehow more convenient - I will be glad to hear.
