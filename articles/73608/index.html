Ghosts in Canon EOS 7D

In the digital camera Canon EOS 7D an interesting glitch was found . On individual frames, subtle "ghosts" may be noticeable.



The phenomenon manifests itself in speed mode (continuous mode) and when shooting a fast moving dark object on a bright background.





Samples of "buggy" photos are published on the Dpreview forums here and here , as well as on the Japanese forum ( translated by GTranslate).

Canon engineers are now studying the problem and will later say exactly what it is related to (most likely, something is wrong with the CMOS settings). Anyway, the bug will be fixed with the next firmware update.

Canon EOS 7D - 18-megapixel advanced SLR worth about two thousand dollars. In high-speed mode, it produces up to eight frames per second.
