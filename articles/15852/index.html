Give the GeForce 8800GT at a normal price!

Despite the fact that the price recommended by NVIDIA for GeForce 8800GT ranges from $ 199 to $ 250, the real market value of new video cards in different regions ranges from $ 270-300, and in some cases even higher.

NVIDIA did not calculate whether its capabilities, or the needs of the market. It turns out that the increased cost of the GeForce 8800GT is caused by the extreme shortage of G92 graphics processors, which currently have only about 30 thousand pieces.

We very much hope that in the near future, the company will solve the problems associated with mastering the 65-nm process technology and will start producing chips in the quantities required by the market. And we, respectively, will finally receive the long-awaited 8800GT for $ 200.
