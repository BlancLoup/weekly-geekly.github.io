Sony Vaio X Netbook

Not wanting to lag behind Nokia, Sony showed reporters a new netbook, the Sony Vaio X Series. Fujio Nishida, head of Sony Europe, showed a carbon black 11.1-inch netbook at a special press conference. The weight of the novelty is only less than 700 grams (almost a pound less than the weight of most netbooks), the thickness is less than 1.5 centimeters. In addition, Sony promises to build 3G support and a battery that will provide a whole day of work.
