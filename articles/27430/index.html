iPhone 3G quadrupled

The staggeringly low prices of the iPhone 3G, called Steve Jobs, have proven to be subsidized. That is, it will only be possible to buy a phone for $ 199-299 with the simultaneous acquisition of a contract for the provision of communication services. The cost of "unsubsidized" iPhone 3G will be significantly higher: $ 770 for the 8 GB version of the device and $ 880 for the version with 16 GB of memory. Thus, the real cost of the mobile phone will be comparable to the cost of other similar innovations, for example, HTC Diamond.



The operator Vodafone announced prices for an unlocked iPhone 3G in Italy. Its cost in the configuration with 8 GB of memory will be about $ 770, with 16 GB - about $ 880, which, respectively, is almost 4 and 3 times more expensive than the previously announced prices, reports Engadget. Taking into account the cost of the first generation iPhone, it is assumed that the cost of the iPhone 3G in other European countries will be similar.

Recall that the new model of the iPhone - with the support of third-generation networks - was presented last Monday by Apple CEO Steve Jobs (Steve Jobs) at the annual Apple WWDC Developer Conference in San Francisco. He said that the cost of the new phone will be $ 199 for the 8 GB version and $ 299 - for 16 GB. However, no details were reported on that day.
