Yandex still retains leadership over Google in the Russian segment of the mobile Internet

The latest report from Opera Software, Mobile Internet Status, reports that Russia remains the leader in the number of Opera Mini users. Russians are fully using the Internet on their mobile phones, checking emails on the go, chatting with friends on social networks, and playing online games using the Opera Mini, which is fast and easy to use. Also, residents of Russia still prefer Yandex search system, rather than Google, which remains in third place in popularity after vkontakte.ru and mail.ru. While large sites attract a lot of attention and traffic, smaller sites collectively collect more users.

Top news from the State of the Mobile Internet report for August 2008:

- In August, the number of Opera Mini users was approximately 17.3 million, which is 9.1% more than the previous month and 357% more compared with the data for August 2007.

- Opera Mini users viewed over 4.1 billion pages in August. Each Opera Mini user has viewed on average approximately 242 pages. Compared to July, the number of views increased by 11.7%. Since August 2007, this figure has grown by 337%.

- In August, 17.3 million Opera Mini users used more than 60.3 million megabytes of traffic among world operators. Compared to July, the amount of traffic used increased by 12.1%. Transmitted data in Opera Mini is compressed by an average of 90%. Without compression, Opera Mini users would use more than 603 million megabytes of data in August.
