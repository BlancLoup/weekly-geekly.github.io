Evernote for Android now supports handwriting.


Great news for Evernote lovers on Android. Now, when you need to quickly make a note, you can make a sketch by hand directly to Evernote. If you carry a notebook with you, just because you don‚Äôt like typing notes on your phone, starting today, updating the Evernote application will be your salvation.

To quickly make a note by hand, just click on the pencil icon in the upper right corner, after which a spotty grid will appear, and start creating! At your disposal are four colors and three options for the thickness of the lines. Moreover, you can add audio files and text documents to your note.

You already know that in Evernote there is a text search system. Handwritten notes are no exception. In addition, the Evernote update for Android includes an improved text editor and increased speed of creating snapshots. All this you can get right now by downloading the update from Google Play.
