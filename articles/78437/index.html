Sapphire Cards or Tale of Six Radeon

Sapphire has been represented in Russia for several years now and is one of the largest suppliers of AMD video cards in Russia. The company has earned itself a good name on the Russian market, above all the quality of its products, attractive prices, as well as good overclocking potential of its products, which is very important for computer enthusiasts. Sapphire was again ahead of the rest of the vendors with the release of AMD fifth-series AMD video cards. It is necessary to say a few words to readers about the innovations in this series, which abound. The main chip products, positioned as "evergreen" is DirectX11. Without exception, all cards support the eleventh DirectX and allow you to enjoy great effects. Why "evergreen" you ask? From now on, AMD decided to abandon the letter designations in the name of its graphics cores. Cypress - cypress, Hemlock - hemlock, Juniper - juniper, Cedar - cedar, Redwood - mahogany are the names for the new GPUs. All GPUs have performance differences, but support a common set of technologies. The basis of DirectX11 lay tessellation - the process of splitting the image into smaller forms, such as triangles or quadrangles. This is because the increase in detail in games, so the need for better textures all the time grows.





As the Sapphire products were the first to appear on the Russian market, we chose the products of this brand for the test of the fifth series of video cards from AMD.



What and how we tested?

We selected a pair of video cards for each model from the Sapphire HD5xxx family and compared them to each other in 3D benchmarks and games. CrossFire configurations also took part in testing. All video cards were tested at nominal frequencies, and we, OCClub.ru , as a project oriented to overclocking, checked the video cards for maximum overclocking.

List of tested video cards:

* 2x Sapphire HD 5970 (735/1010 MHz)
* 2x Sapphire HD 5870 (850/1200 MHz)
* 2x Sapphire HD 5850 (725/1000 MHz)
* 2x Sapphire HD 5770 (850/1200 MHz)
* 2x Sapphire HD 5750 (700/1150 MHz)

These video cards, both in single-player mode and in CrossFire, passed the following tests:

* 3DMark06 is a popular graphic benchmark from Futuremark 2006, which still shows excellent graphics performance. Default settings.
* 3DMark Vantage - the latest graphic benchmark, originally honed under Nvidia. But with the release of HD5xxx, everything changed. Default settings.
* Heaven - the first DirectX11 benchmark with impressive graphics. Surely, this test in the future will be mandatory in the set of testers.
* Crysis Warhead is a game that has conquered millions of people all over the planet. Settings: 1920x1200 resolution, very high graphics quality.
* FarCry2 - a game on the Cry Engine engine with beautiful graphics and an exciting plot. Settings: 1920x1200 resolution, Ultra High quality graphics.

* number of stream processors - 3200

* number of ROP-blocks - 2x 32
* core frequency - 735 MHz
* memory frequency - 1010 MHz

The top two-headed product from AMD turned out so powerful that the gap in performance from the previous generation of video cards turned out to be many times as much. Honestly, no one expected this level of performance. Hemlock is a dual Cypress, but due to the presence of two cores on one PCB, the core frequencies are reduced to 725 MHz instead of 850 MHz. Also, in order to fall within certain limits, the TDP voltages are also lower than usual for Cypress 1.16V. However, the HD5970's fervor is very hot. Cards from Sapphire were able to accelerate to frequencies of 950/1200 MHz, which is a very high figure. The disadvantages of this card include a high retail price of 27,000 rubles. A bunch of a pair of such video cards can become a utopia in the dreams of avid gamers.
