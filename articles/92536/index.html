Publisher Snowball became part of the "1C-SoftKlab"

Appeared today on the sites Snowball.ru, Games.1C.ru and SoftClub.ru official press release reports:

Moscow, April 30, 2010 The Russian publishing house Snowball, created in 1996 to develop, localize and promote computer games, and 1C-SoftKlab, which surely occupies a leading position in the Russian market of computer and video games, have announced the signing of an agreement to merge the companies .

The merger will result in the merging of publishing directories of companies and the integration of Snowball Studio into the structure of the 1C-SoftKlab group as part of strengthening the specialization of the group on key expertise allocated to separate publishing areas. The studio team will lead the work on projects that are most demanding to localize and market, including projects in the genres of RPG, RTS / TBS and Quest / Adventure, going under the brand name Snowball.
