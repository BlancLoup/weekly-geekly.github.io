Reminder: 5 days left until HackDay # 7


Congratulations to all the programmer's Day and remind you that in 5 days HackDay # 7 will begin in Moscow.

At HackDay you can:
  • assemble a team under your idea or join an existing one;
  • listen to practical workshops and develop your project based on them;
  • learn how to develop applications for the iPhone / iPad;
  • develop a virtual reality application;
  • develop an augmented reality application.

And also get new contacts and spend time fun and profitably.

Register here .

Participation in the event is free.

Invited:
  • programmers;
  • designers;
  • copywriters;
  • interface designers and usability specialists;
  • sysadmins;
  • marketers;
  • and all the others, without whom not to create an interesting project.

To participate , be sure to register and post your project idea with a description and a list of necessary people, or join an existing project. The work will go on two days - Saturday and Sunday. Master classes will be held on Saturday morning. Demofest will be held on Sunday evening - a presentation of the developed projects.

Master classes will begin with one general theoretical flow, and then they will be divided into applied master classes, on the basis of which you will be able to make your own project under the guidance of specialists.

11:30 - 12:45 Theoretical flow in one audience


  1. Mikhail Kechinov, " Where to start and how to lead the development of the project to meet the 48 hours ."
  2. Alexander Komarov, Rasim Isaev, Alexey Meander. A cycle of three 15-minute master classes " How to turn a garage project into a successful business ."
  3. Andrei Sapozhnikov, project management training .

13:00 - 14:00 Practical flow in different audiences


  1. Gaidar Magdanurov, Microsoft. " IE9, HTML5, Dynamic Data ".
  2. Inga Borovkova, AR Door. " Let's add our reality together " (during the master class, participants will develop an application for phones based on augmented reality).
  3. Alexander Babaev, Inru, Roman Busygin, Yandex. " Basics of development for iOS (for iPhones and iPads) ".
