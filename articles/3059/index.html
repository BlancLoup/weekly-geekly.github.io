Gmail is open to all

Starting today, you don't need an invitation to register with Gmail! Now every visitor to the Google homepage will see the sign up for Gmail. You can also use the direct link mail.google.com/mail/signup .

Google gradually approached this event, making the registration open only in some countries. By the way, Russian users could also register with Gmail without invitations.

In addition, now the ability to collect mail from other mailboxes via POP3 is available to all users of the English-language interface of Gmail. It is located in the settings tab "Accounts".

But, despite free registration, Gmail is still in beta testing. Experts suggest that the "final release" will occur on April 1, the third anniversary of one of the best postal services.
