Opening of the fall semester 2011 at –°omputer Science club



As in the last semester Computer Science club operates in two cities: St. Petersburg and Yekaterinburg. Opening will take place on September 11 and 30, respectively.

St. Petersburg


September 11, 2011 at 10:30 am, the fall semester opens at the Computer Science Club . As always, the doors of the Marble Hall of POMI RAS are open to all comers.

At the opening will be briefly told about the club and upcoming courses. Those interested will be able to get answers to questions about the new initiative of the club - CS center (see topic about opening ). And at 11:15, Andrei Mikhailovich Raygorodsky (Yandex, Moscow State University, MIPT) will begin to read a mini-course on web graph models.

There are usually quite a few courses:
Web graph models and their applications ( A. M. Raygorodsky , Yandex, Moscow State University, MIPT)
Computability and Logic ( D. M. Itsykson , POMI RAS)
Computer graphics ( V. A. Galinsky , FML 30)
Seminar on circuit complexity ( A. S. Kulikov , POMI RAS)
Introduction to word combinatorics ( A.E. Frid , IM SB RAS)
Computer Science Workshop

Yekaterinburg


Semester opening is scheduled for September 30th. The first will be Denis Raskovalov (Yandex) with a mini-course "Information Search from the Inside . " The program for the entire semester and details about the club in the Urals will be published later.
