Photos for Lumia. What's new?

Most recently, we had a post about third-party accessories that enable us to expand the capabilities of the Nokia Lumia smartphone. We make every effort to ensure that the software component of our devices satisfies most of the wishes of users. And developers are always striving to come up with something new that the manufacturer has not foreseen. And it's great, because users, because of this, there is even more means of expression.

We chose the most interesting, in our opinion, photographic applications from third-party developers, which allow us to look at the process of photographing from a new angle.





Projekt tripod



This application allows you to take a series of pictures with an unlimited period of time between frames. As a result, even without the use of a tripod, a sequence of photos identical in composition and separated by time is obtained. From these frames, you can create an unusual collage, using all the shots of the series. By the way, this application has received a grant for the development and development of the project AppCampus .

Refocus

This application works on the same principle as Lytro digital cameras. In such a camera, when recording, not only information about the color and intensity of light is recorded, but also vector information about the direction of light at each point in the image. This allows you to select a focus object after the picture is taken.





Possible as an automatic focus on the selected object, and manual adjustment of parameters. The result can be shared on Facebook, Twitter, by email or via Bluetooth. The application allows you to upload the full file to SkyDrive, display a photo on the live tile, and also edit RFI files shared with friends, for example.

The cost of Refocus in the "Shop" is 34 rubles, you can also install a free trial version. It is fully functional, the only limitation is that only three images can be stored at a time. Not much, but to test the work and evaluate the capabilities of the application is enough.

3D Photo Camera

As it is easy to guess from the name, in this application you can make not simple, but three-dimensional photos on the smartphone. It's simple: you only need to take two photos in a row, slightly moving the camera between them. The application will show the first frame when capturing the second.

The result can be saved in several modes: color anaglyph, black and white anaglyph, stereo pair. Anaglyph images can be viewed through 3D glasses for a volumetric image effect.

Intellicam express

In the previous post, we mentioned programs that allow you to control shooting with your voice. And Instacam Express is a good example of this class of applications.

The program is able to take pictures by voice command, while recognizing faces and focusing on them. In addition, she is able to work in locked screen mode.
