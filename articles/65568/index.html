New demo version of the game Hammerfight

A new demo version of the awesome Hammerfight game (previously known as Hammerfall ) has been released. The game was in development for about 2 years, and here is the long-awaited continuation of the story. This game is notable for its control method and physics. You only need a mouse, you can even without buttons;)


Hammerfight is 2D battles on flying machines using a variety of piercing-cutting and strike weapons. A unique combat system based on honest physics, binds the rider's movements directly to the movement of your mouse. Swipe the mouse and the rider swings his hammer with a hammer to drive the enemy into the wall! There is an incomparable feeling of a real blow, the feeling of a massive weapon in your hands. Honest physics and direct control of the mouse give a huge variety of combat techniques and unlimited field to improve the skill of martial arts.

The game is really great, created as if in an era of great Vangers . The same original and atmospheric. Dialogues in oriental style and excellent design are fascinating, and when controlling a fighting machine, you just climb into a mouse. The game is undemanding to resources, I have an excellent Eee PC 701 to deal with it. If there were more such games, the authors really invest a bit of them in them.
