The killer feature of OpenCL technology, Virtual OpenCL, comes onto the scene and allows you to combine many accelerators installed in several computers into one high-performance cluster.

Virtual OpenCL


VCL is available for free and works with any hardware that supports the standard OpenCL 1.0 or 1.1, allows you to combine various devices into one computer network and provide its power for any applications that can work with OpenCL.

As an example of the use of such technology, I would like to talk about a monstrous password bust farm consisting of 25 AMD GPUs.

Password cracker


Breaking a password with brute force often rests on the computing power of the computer that will perform the search.
