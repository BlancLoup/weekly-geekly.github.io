A network of 34 charging stations for electric vehicles opened across Ukraine



In Ukraine , 34 electro-gas stations have recently appeared , and 9 more are planned to open this year. Gas stations appeared on the most popular highways, including Kiev-Chop, Kiev-Kharkov, Kiev-Odessa, Kiev-Dnepropetrovsk, Kiev-Lublin, Kiev-Peremyshl. According to the initiators of the project, the average distance between gas stations does not exceed 200 kilometers.

According to the project representatives, not only Tesla electric vehicle owners, but also owners of other electric vehicles, including Renault Zoe, Porshce Panamera, BMW i3, can take advantage of the new charging stations. Also, with the help of an adapter, owners of Nissan Leaf, Chevrolet Volt, Mitsubishi iMiEV, Renault Fluence can refuel.

Electric cars are now going on the offensive on all fronts. Many manufacturers of cars with an internal combustion engine create their own versions of electric vehicles (Toyota, by the way, went its own way, developing cars on fuel cells). But not all countries, owners of electric vehicles feel at ease.



The main problem is the lack of infrastructure electroazs. In Russia, Ukraine and some other countries on an electric car you will not go further than the power reserve of a car, and the battery can be recharged far from everywhere. However, this problem gradually ceases to be a problem. For example, some Ukrainian companies - owners of gas stations open electric stations. In addition, Tesla Motors branded charging stations will appear in Moldova, Ukraine and Russia this year and next.
