Bethesda and Fallout: all news from the conference at E3



The company Bethesda presented a lot of interesting developments and news at E3, and many of them are related to Fallout. Among the most notable news is Fallout 4 news, the new Fallout Shelter game and, of course, the real Pip Boy!

But let's get everything in order.

Fallout 4



Imagine now you can even fly!

First, the release date of the game has become known - it will happen on November 10 of this year. The toy will appear on the platforms Xbox One, PlayStation 4, PC. In this case, the collectible version of the game will include a wearable device - the very Pip-Boy!



The plot of Fallout 4 starts a few hours before the apocalypse - "one beautiful Saturday morning." However, most of the game takes place in the future, distant from the date of the disaster for 200 years. In the game you can customize the hero, looking at yourself in the mirror in the bathroom. A character can be either male or female. As far as you can understand, the hero of the game can have a partner - in this case a dog.







Fallout 4 works on the new Creation Engine. The game has a large number of branches of the plot, side quests, RPG elements. A character can even build his own house using wood, metal, and other materials. The more build, the more people will come. In the end, marauders with robbers will come. Construction is an optional feature.



Here you can modify armor and weapons (50 types of weapons, plus 700 types of modifications of both weapons and armor).

Pip-boy



Not the smallest device

As mentioned above, the collectible version of the game now includes a real Pip-Boy, which is worn on the arm, as in the game. For the device mates with a smartphone, and works as a smart watch with some reservations. An iOS and Android application has already been released for the device. The cost of the Pip-Boy Edition is $ 119.99.

Fallout shelter




Finally, Bethesda announced the game Fallout Shelter, it's something in the Sims style, but with an apocalyptic slant. This toy has been released (yes, it has already been released) only for iOS, so that owners of Android and Windows Phone smartphones can only wait for now.

The game has a lot of black humor, unexpected decisions and opportunities.
