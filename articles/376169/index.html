For 7 years, Wikipedian has fixed the same error 47,000 times

Do you think, know what obsession and perfectionism are? This is probably not the case. Meet the Wikipedia editor under the nickname Giraffedata , his real name is Bryan Henderson. From 2007 to the present, he has made more than 47,000 edits on Wikipedia .



Henderson is something of a cult character in the Wikipedian community. When they talked about him at a community conference, the audience applauded the hero .

The 51-year-old programmer from the USA is among the 1000 most prolific editors . But, unlike the others, he did not use bots: all edits were done exclusively by hand.
