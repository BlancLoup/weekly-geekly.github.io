Paradise perfectionist or what should be cable management

Choosing a good and reliable cable is certainly not easy, but it is equally important to be able to properly organize all these wires: so that it is not only functional and convenient, but also beautiful. And some craftsmen are so confused that they even invented a special term for this - cable porn "cable management". As Friday, we offer to see how not just good cable management looks like, but real cable perfectionism.



The most obvious tool for organizing cables is the good old buckles, time-tested. But they also have drawbacks: firstly, they are disposable, and secondly, this solution is more suitable for the home than for large commercial premises. In data centers, they have long since switched to reusable velcro - they are slightly more expensive, but no less reliable, and you don‚Äôt have to write them off after one use.

Another important point is the labeling of cables. Even if there are only a couple of things, the absence of marking can play a cruel joke, for example, when changing the employee responsible for feng shui in the server room.
