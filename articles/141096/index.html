Video review tablet Samsung Series 7 Slate

Samsung Series 7 Slate (XE-700T1) is a Samsung tablet with a 11.6-inch PLS display and Intel Core i5-2467M processor based on the Windows 7 operating system. It installs 4 GB of RAM and a 64 GB SSD drive. WiFi (+ N) and BlueTooth 3.0 are available from wireless communications.



In the software part, Samsung has added some interesting solutions designed to simplify the touch management of the operating system.

The estimated cost of the Samsung 7 Slate is 40 thousand rubles.
