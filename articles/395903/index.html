Acer Liquid Z630S review: technology long-lived

In September 2015, the Taiwanese company Acer released a budget smartphone Acer Liquid Z630, and six months later, at MWC 2016 in Barcelona, ‚Äã‚Äãshowed an improved S-version of this device - Acer Liquid Z630S. In it, the developers have increased the amount of RAM and improved the processor, and also retained the original software features like Acer Bluelight Shield, reducing the intensity of the blue color on the screen and DTS Studio Sound technology, which provides high quality sound. Another advantage is that the smartphone has become attributed to long-livers, since it has a 4000 mAh battery installed on it, which provides good autonomy.



Technical characteristics of Acer Liquid Z630S (t04):

  • Screen: 5.5 "IPS HD 720x1280 (267 PPI), multitouch with up to five simultaneous touches
  • Processor: MediaTek MT6753 1.3 GHz
  • RAM: 3 GB
  • Built-in memory: 32 GB
  • Memory card support: yes, microSD
  • Operating system: Android 5.1 Lollipop
  • SIM card support: microSIM + microSIM
  • Connectivity: 2G / 3G / 4G
  • Wireless interfaces: Bluetooth 4.0, Wi-Fi
  • Main camera: 8 MP
  • Front camera: 8 MP
  • Sensors: accelerometer, light sensor, G-sensor, Hall effect sensor
  • Battery capacity: 4000 mAh
  • Talk time: 21 hours
  • Standby time: 710 hours
  • Dimensions: 167x88x8.9 mm
  • Weight: 165 mm

Equipment


In addition to the smartphone itself, the manufacturer put in the box Acer Liquid Z630S a headset for talking, a microUSB cable, a power adapter and instructions for use. The box itself is compact, made in a dark blue color, the main characteristics of the device are applied to the lid.

Design




The design of the smartphone uses a combination of dark gray plastic and golden elements - the bezel around the main camera, the Acer logo on the back cover, the frame around the case. Acer‚Äôs signature features are noticeable: rounded shapes and original metal speaker.



Most of the front side is occupied by a screen, above which is a golden speaker grid and, to the right, a front camera. Under the display is a silver company logo. The control buttons are located on the right edge - the power button on top, underneath the volume change swing. The headphone jack is located at the top, and the microUSB connector for charging and synchronizing with a PC is located at the bottom.



Ergonomics


The large diagonal of the smartphone will appeal to those users who are using the multimedia capabilities of gadgets with might and main - watch videos, read and play games. For this, all Acer Liquid Z630S with its 5.5 "diagonal is great. In addition, thanks to the rounded shape, the smartphone is comfortable to hold in your hand, although it is large: the gadget fits perfectly in your palm and does not slip.



To remove the back cover (a special groove on the bottom end is used for this), you need to apply a little effort. Under the cover is a removable 4000 mAh battery and slots for microSIM and microSD.



Screen


The 5.5-inch Acer Liquid Z630S screen is manufactured using Zero Air Gap technology, which means there is no air gap between the display and the glass. Such technology is also involved in providing good viewing angles. The touch panel of the screen has good sensitivity and response and recognizes up to five simultaneous touches.



The display uses a resolution of HD (720x1280 pixels), which may seem to someone insufficient. Small details and text are really not quite smooth - pixelation is visible - but this pixel density is quite enough for comfortable use in a variety of tasks, and from the ‚Äúpluses‚Äù - a screen with a lower resolution consumes less system resources and battery power, which increases the autonomy of the smartphone.

On the Acer Liquid Z630S there is an interesting feature of the Acer Bluelight Shield, which allows to reduce the intensity of the blue color of the display. One of the options for using the mode will be its inclusion before bedtime, to ‚Äúcalm‚Äù the nervous system (here was an article on this topic). Also, this mode is recommended to use when reading in order to reduce eye strain.



The maximum brightness of the display is not enough for use in bright sunshine, but in most cases (room, cloudy weather) the picture is clear and well distinguishable. The minimum brightness threshold allows you to comfortably look at the screen in complete darkness.

Smartphone at work








In addition to the basic Android 5.1, the company generously supplied the Acer Liquid Z630S with proprietary software. On all models of the line, there is an Acer QuickMode interface that prompts the user to choose one of three display modes - standard (this is normal Android, familiar to experienced users), simplified (for those who have not yet mastered) and basic (very simple mode, even for children). Well, the package Acer Aid Kit, power management and the leading statistics of battery consumption, as well as tracking the level of memory usage. With this package, you can also quickly back up data and restore it later.



As for the iron, the Acer Liquid Z630S received a 64-bit eight-core MediaTek MT6753 processor operating at a clock frequency of 1.3 GHz. The amount of RAM is 3 GB, there is a built-in storage capacity of 32 GB. Despite the fact that the device is gaining average points in synthetic performance tests, in everyday work the processor power is more than enough: the interface does not slow down, applications run quickly, the animation in the system is smooth.



You can use the Acer Liquid Z630S and for games. The Asphalt 8 racing simulator went great, the only disadvantage was only a slight body heat.



The smartphone supports two SIM cards, both micro formats, both connectors work with LTE networks. There is a full set of wireless interfaces: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS.



The main feature of the smartphone is high autonomy: the capacity of its battery is quite impressive (by modern standards) 4000 mAh. The manufacturer promises 21 hours of talk time and 710 hours in standby mode, and in our test for continuous video playback (on the way to Minsk), the Acer Liquid Z630S lasted eight hours at maximum screen brightness - this is an excellent result, indicating a really high autonomy . This was confirmed ‚Äúin the field‚Äù: in active use mode (Internet, reading, camera, applications), the smartphone works without recharging for at least one and a half to two days.

Not all games will go smoothly on it, but in other daily tasks there will be no performance problems. In general, this is an excellent phablet with increased performance and LTE support.
