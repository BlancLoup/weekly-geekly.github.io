"Yandex.Money" issue plastic payment cards



Today it became known that the popular payment system Yandex.Money issues its own Mastercard plastic cards. The card, according to representatives of Yandex.Money, is issued not by the payment system itself, but by its partner, Tinkoff Credit Systems Bank. Card receipt is free of charge, so that any user of the specified payment system can receive a card for their own needs. The card allows you to pay in the network, and in reality.

It is not required to create a separate bank account, the money will be charged directly from the Yandex user account when paying. Of money". Interestingly, the map is not local, it works in any country of the world. So, in order to pay for goods or services abroad, you do not need to have a bank account, you can pay directly from your Yandex.Money account. Plus, confirmed accounts receive a PIN code, which opens another opportunity - cash withdrawal at ATM.

Card service is completely free, such a card is valid for three years. It is interesting that payment through the terminal goes without a fee, but when withdrawing cash, there is a commission, making up 3% of the withdrawn amount, plus another 15 rubles. All account operations in Yandex.Money can be tracked, plus there is the ability to connect informational SMS.

The card can be obtained in any country of the world, the company sends it, after filling in all the necessary data, with a letter (registered).

By the way, Webmoney can also order such cards, I previously worked with gKard, and after these cards became unavailable - with PaySpark. Cards from Webmoney are issued with the participation of foreign partners. In contrast to Yandex.Money, PaySpark takes a commission for crediting money to a card, for withdrawing, and paying for goods / services in real life. But the card is quite convenient, because it allows you to instantly withdraw money from your Webmoney account to plastic.
