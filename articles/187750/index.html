LinkMeUp. Issue 5. Guest from Norway

Hello, fellow communicators.
This time our guest is a network engineer from Norway - Mikhail Shchedrin ( awesomebanana ). Therefore, most of the fifth issue we will talk about moving, working in Norway and the practice of building networks "at them".

In addition, according to tradition, we will talk about some interesting news:

  1. The Ministry of Communications offers to select LTE licenses from the Big Four, which were issued to them as part of the competition in 2012.
  2. Mikran will supply Mobile Communications Complexes to the Ministry of Defense.
  3. Ethernet Alliance announced the creation of three new committees: 400 Gb Ethernet, Access Networking and Roadmap.
  4. Cisco is preparing an Open EIGRP RFC and has announced a new EIGRP OTP mechanism.
