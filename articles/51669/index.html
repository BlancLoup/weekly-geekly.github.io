My Phone - Microsoft Storage Service

Microsoft announced the imminent launch of the My Phone service, also known as SkyBox . Let's see what happens, as always hope for the best .

He will probably start working on the day when the announcement of Windows Mobile 6.5. According to available data, owners of mobile devices with Windows Mobile will be offered the opportunity to automatically synchronize various information. For example, it will be possible to place a backup copy of data from the phone (multimedia content, a list of contacts, messages, etc.) on the service. At first, the amount of allocated memory will be limited to 200 MB.

You can access the saved files from any device running Windows Mobile. As representatives of the software giant, My Phone will be the first of many mobile services that Microsoft has planned for the current year.
