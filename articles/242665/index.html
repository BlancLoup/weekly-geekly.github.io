An example from life. A large company, the head of the sales department became pregnant, well, it happens with women. When she returned to the organization, her boss forced her to quit, explaining that as an employee, she was no longer. New example. A large hydropower plant was built in the Siberian region, people had to be evicted from the flood zone, with minimal financial outlays for the state.
