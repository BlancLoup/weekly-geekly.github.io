Sony VAIO Duo 11 Ultrabook (tablet) video review (SVD-1121QR)



Sony VAIO Duo 11 is a hybrid ultrabook slider with a touch-sensitive 11-inch FullHD IPS display. The SurfSlider design allows you to move the screen up to open the keyboard. Thus, the device can be used as a tablet or as a laptop, depending on the situation. The VAIO Duo also comes with a stylus with a pressure sensor that allows you to draw lines of different thickness or use handwriting.

The devices are running Windows 8 (regular or Pro) based on low-voltage Intel Core i3 / i5 / i7 processors. The amount of RAM is from 4 to 8 GB without the possibility of an independent upgrade. For data storage using solid-state drives with a capacity of 128 or 256 GB.

The disadvantages of the device include quite large dimensions and weight, as well as not too long battery life.
