Noise Security Bit # 9 (about Black Hat 2014 and Defcon 22)

Not so long ago two, probably the most significant conferences in the field of practical information security were held in Vegas. Of course, we are talking about Black Hat 2014 and Defcon 22 . Reports have long been posted online, and Habr has already been distributed in several publications on this topic, so here we will talk about the Noise Security Bit # 9 podcast. This issue was recorded almost immediately after the end of the final day of the Defcon conference with Russian-language re-detectors, or rather with the most persistent ones that we managed to catch at the time of leaving the Rio Hotel.



The ninth edition was recorded from beginning to end in one of the rooms of the Mandalay Bay Hotel, consisting of: Anton Karpov ( @ toxo4ka ), Ivan Novikov ( @ d0znpp ), Svetlana Gayvoronskaya ( @SadieSv ) and Ivan Petrov ( @_IvanPetrov_ ).
