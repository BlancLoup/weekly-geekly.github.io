Registration for WebCamp 2011 is open.

From April 30 to May 1, the Second Specialized Conference WebCamp 2011 will be held in Odessa. It will be communication and recreation of experts in the field of web-technologies.

The WebCamp 2011 conference will be held in barcamp format, i.e. active involvement of all participants in the organization and holding of the event. As part of the official part of the event, WebCamp will discuss issues related to internet marketing, design and usability, as well as web technologies and project management.

In addition, a special section "Blitz-reports" is planned. Within its framework, you can tell the audience about your projects within 5-10 minutes.

How to apply for participation in the conference as a speaker and what requirements for reports can be found here .

Participation in the conference is free by pre-registration. You can register for WebCamp 2011 on the event website .

The unofficial part of the event will take place on the 1st of May (climbing wall of the Odessa Mountaineering Club, the Black Sea coast). For guests from other cities there is an opportunity to put up tents in a closed climbing wall.
