Briefly about the project:
The name " Air People " was coined almost immediately. It, perhaps, best reflects the essence of the project - this is a place for people who regularly fly both in Russia and abroad.
Those who fly from time to time or seasonally - will also be happy)

An important difference from other websites about airlines will be not simple posting reviews about the quality of service / meals on board / flight delays and other things, but more extensive topics about offers and promotions from various airlines, airport news, reviews, photo reports and of course free exchange of information between network members.

Not without romance - for some people, including me, aviation is associated with the sea, the sun and adventure.

Thus, the target audience is very extensive: from people who flew economy class at least once in their lives, to oligarchs who have on their own aircraft.
We will also be glad to see those who have never flown, but on a subconscious level, are in love with the sky.

Development plan:
Until mid-August, we find out which airlines will enter the top in the opinion of users, and then immediately open.
