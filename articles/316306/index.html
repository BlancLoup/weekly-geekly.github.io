Andrei will tell how they in DINO Systems (Ring Central) made a telephone platform using GridGain . In order to understand most of the report, knowledge of the GridGain or the In-Memory Data Grid as a whole is not mandatory.

At the beginning of the meeting, participants unfamiliar with IMDG will learn what GridGain is. Then Andrei will demonstrate the solution of the tasks taken from the project using GridGain and show that the solution that comes to mind first is not always the right one. After a series of practical tasks in which potential bugs will be demonstrated when using GridGain, Andrei will proceed to consider the most interesting bugs of GridGain itself, which his team encountered in the process.
