IBM Watson will be twice smarter

Victory in the game Jeopardy, successful study at the doctor, culinary success. What will Watson look like after a major upgrade?



At the 2013 Hot Chips conference, IBM said it was implementing a major update on the Watson supercomputer. Not only the CPU will be upgraded, but also a number of system nodes and components.

In 2010, IBM released the Watson supercomputer based on the Power 7 processor. Last year, the company upgraded it to Power 7+ . Now IBM is preparing to upgrade to a 12-core Power 8 chip, which will give two-, and in some cases, a three-fold increase in performance compared with the first system.

According to forecasts, IBM Watson will become twice smarter, and become the first true AI will certainly surprise us with new achievements.

The review of the new Power 8 processor was prepared by the site overlockers.ru.

PS There are comments asking why the supercomputer is called Watson, not Holmes. Answer: Thomas Watson (Thomas J. Watson) is one of the founders of IBM, and the supercomputer is named after him;)
