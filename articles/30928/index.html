Top 10 freeware for Mac OS by lifehacker.com

Top free programs:

  1. Quicksilver . He knows how to do everything with the system, and does it with the help of a unified interface and only with the help of a keyboard.

  2. TextWrangler. Free Textmate replacement. Younger brother BBedit.

  3. SilverKeeper . Allows you to make backups on an external disk of arbitrary data from your poppy on a schedule.

  4. Adium . No comments. Great IM client.
  5. HandBrake . Allows you to backup your DVDs in MPEG-4 format.

  6. iSquint . Video Converter for iPod.

  7. VirtueDesktops . Virtual Desktop Manager.

  8. The Unarchiver . Multi-format archiver.

  9. VLC . Open source video player and support for a huge number of formats.

  10. iTerm . Terminal software supporting tabs, transparency, with rich configuration options.
