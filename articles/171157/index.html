Bitcoin exchange rate hit a record high.



For a rather long history of its existence, the Bitcoin cryptocurrency exchange rate against the dollar has both grown and fallen. Bitcoin had the most problems when the system started having problems with ensuring the security of users' wallets (for example, an attacker somehow stole $ 250,000). But now, it seems, the problems of Bitcoin have receded into the background, since the exchange rate of e-currency has reached its historic maximum. For 1 Bitcoin at night / in the morning of February 28th they were given from 32 to 33.22 US dollars. By the way, the previous record of $ 31.9099 was recorded on June 8, 2011.

It is worth noting that in 2011 the rate was less than 2 dollars for one Bitcoin. So those users of the system who have accumulated a sufficient amount of coins at the time of the crisis of the system can be satisfied - their confidence in the ‚Äúgolden age‚Äù of cryptocurrency can be justified. The past few months, the Bitcoin exchange rate grew smoothly, there were no excesses, so the currency rose in price.



At the same time, some resources, including Mega, Reddit, Wordpress.com, began to accept user payments in this currency. And the staff of Archive.org even started getting Bitcoin wages . Problems with the security of the system seem to be resolved, so this negative factor has ceased to influence the rate of cryptocurrency.
