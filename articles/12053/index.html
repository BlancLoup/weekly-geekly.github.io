The Chinese are developing a new technology of Internet search.

On the disgraces that are happening in the vast Chinese segment of the Network, already composed legends. Many have probably heard about the story of Google, and about the prohibitions of disagreeable government content, and about the most severe control of local Internet cafes. However, even these measures are probably not enough for the officials of the Celestial Empire. Now they, apparently, wanted to thoroughly learn what each Chinese Internet user is interested in. To make this government decided a very sophisticated way - to create a new search engine. But not simple, but intellectual, which will allow the user to find exclusively what he is interested in, without mixing ‚Äúgarbage‚Äù into the search results.

The development of a unique web search technology employed two scientists from the city of Taiyuan (Shanxi Province). The search engine created by them, in contrast to all the others, does not simply give the user all to one degree or another relevant results, but only those that fully correspond to the query. For example, at the request of "apple", the result will contain pages exclusively with information about the fruit, and no Steves Jobs and iPods. In any case, so the scientists themselves assure. Such a search engine is not easy to work: after entering a request, the engine is not only guided by the words entered, but also analyzes the user's personal data (this will be the local security officers!), Such as his occupation, age, gender, interests, etc. In addition, with each subsequent search query, the system takes into account which link the user clicked on last time, and returns results with the appropriate amendments. Chinese inventors claim that all this technology works perfectly and will allow users to significantly find the information they need.

In general, the leadership of China can be congratulated with another excellent method of control of local residents, and the latter, respectively, sincerely sympathize.
