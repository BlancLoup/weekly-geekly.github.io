Twitter will create a news service of the most talked tweets.

Until the end of 2015, Twitter will launch the Lightning service, announcing the most discussed topics of the resource. The application will be available to all Twitter users. For this, it is not necessary to go through the authorization procedure. The feature will also be supported through third-party sites.

A special button in the mobile application will allow you to get to the page with the most discussed topics on Twitter. Past events, urgent news, current events, or upcoming events can be discussed. It is planned to select 7-10 such events daily.

Lightning will allow you to get quick access to photos and video images that are associated with a particular event.
Each topic will be assigned a collection of tweets, which will be formed by special moderators, BuzzFeed reports . Twitter leads a set of experts for moderating content.

The Techcrunch edition reported that on June 16, a feature of automatic video and GIF files appeared on Twitter. If the content is uploaded directly to Twitter, it will be launched while viewing the feed. The feature is currently available for Twitter.com users and iOS apps. In the future, the developers promise to launch it for Twitter on Android devices.
