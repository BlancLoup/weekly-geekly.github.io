Webinar "Integrated IT infrastructure from Fujitsu"

July 16, 2014, we offer to take part in a webinar which will address the issues of choosing the best IT infrastructure.

Today, the IT infrastructure is the foundation on which all services provided for business are built. When choosing an infrastructure, a lot of questions arise:
  • How to manage equipment from different manufacturers? Can I manage existing equipment?
  • Is it difficult to manage the infrastructure? High management costs? How to reduce them?
  • Does management need to increase IT security?
  • The business requires a quick to market (time to market)
  • Thinking how to build a "cloud"?

We will tell you how Fujitsu approaches these issues and how our decisions change the future!
