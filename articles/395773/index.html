Mover
After you have reached the place, you need to park the camper and start preparing for the rest. Parking with the help of a car is still fun, so all caravaners have an unspoken rule: ‚Äúwhen a new camper arrives, everyone gathers and goes to help push the camper manually.‚Äù With a car, it is difficult to withstand accuracy of tens of centimeters, so often the trailer in the parking lot is placed manually by strong men. Everything would be fine, but when there is a bump or a small bias, pushing a camper weighing 1.5 tons becomes very problematic. This is where the mover comes to the rescue.



These are two electric motors, which at the time of parking are pulled up to the wheels and rotate them. There are motors from a 12V battery, which is installed in the camper. Everything is controlled from the radio control, so you can be on either side of the camper. The engine consumption is up to 120A, and the speed is 2 km / h. Therefore it would be good to have a charged and capacious battery inside. This is partly a toy, but a toy that makes life much easier. But toys for men differ from boys' toys only by the price. Such a mover costs up to 1,500 euros. Expensive, but very convenient!

Internet and Local Area Network
It may sound strange, but it turned out that even on vacation you need a good and high-quality connection. Someone shared photos through instant messengers, someone checked mail. In the evening we watched clips and films in an improvised cinema on the river bank, and during the day there were working moments when good telephone communication was required. Based on my experience, I decided to mount a Zyxel Keenetic Giga III router in a camper, and added the Keenetic Plus DECT adapter to my kit and picked up the first available handset of the DECT standard. The result was such a set:



The Yota modem was used as an Internet source, inserting it into a free USB slot. The second slot occupied the DECT-adapter. Two minutes of the router settings and now you can call from the usual Moscow number, being on the river bank, away from the capital. By the way, when the DECT adapter was inserted, the router itself offered to install the required package, after which it remained to enter the settings of the SIP provider. It is good that in the place of our parking there was a 4G network from Yota, but you can plug a modem into a 3G router and also distribute the Internet throughout the camp.
It was experimentally found that the walls of the camper very well shielded the signal, so to improve communication with the Internet provider, it was decided to move the modem to the roof. The two-meter USB extension cable solved the speed problem, and the whole company forgot about the mobile Internet and roaming!
Ethernet was connected to the router: a network drive, an IP camera, an MAP Dominator inverter, and a laptop was periodically connected to download video and photos from cameras and cameras. On the local network, the speed is gigabit and, when downloading video from cameras, they used a wired connection. The Wi-Fi adapter works at 2.4 and 5 GHz, so if there are many wireless networks around, you can switch to a free frequency and work much faster.



Another feature of the router: since we still have an autonomous power station, sometimes every Watt of energy is counted. A feature of the Zyxel Keenetic GigaIII router is the disabling of unused ports, which means lower power consumption and heat. The latter was especially true because during the day the air conditioner worked almost without stopping for us - the camper heats up pretty quickly.

Network storage for camera and movie storage
When I went to rest, I understood that every second person would take a camera, I would take a camera, there would be a quadcopter and a security camera that writes around the clock. I wanted to collect all the data in one place. I have to say, in total, we shot more than 100 Gigabytes.
Since I have had experience with QNAP appliances for quite some time, I trust their quality. In addition, I like the convenience of the firmware and the whole process of user-tech interaction. Therefore, I quickly decided on the requirements: you need a single-disk network drive with good performance and the possibility of a multimedia player. The latter was especially true because I wanted to arrange a cinema in nature. Such a drive was found and here it is - QNAP TAS-168 . By the way, there is a two-disk version of this NAS, but the doubled amount of data so far is unnecessary.



The remote control is really included in the delivery package, and we need it for this: the NAS itself has two OSes on board: QTS and Android. The first is to organize the work of the network drive, all services and applications. And the second is available when you connect a display or projector to the HDMI port. We get a media player based on Android with all the features: installing third-party programs, games, and more. Most importantly, access to the repository is provided, which means that you can play movies directly from the drive. It remains to put the screen and take the best places. Aerobaton
I must say that this is an extremely pleasant thing to relax. I often see on the forums the cries of sofa experts that we have not been able to produce anything. Therefore, I even brought this device into a separate paragraph.



Aerobaton is a comfortable and enjoyable lounger. Someone may argue that this is a copy of the western LAMZAC, but no - these devices differ fundamentally: the development and distinctive feature of a Russian manufacturer from Omsk is that the outer fabric cover is independent of the inner film sleeve. And if an American lounger is accidentally pierced, then it will have to be either very expensive to repair or to throw out. And with the Russian Aerobaton everything is much simpler: the puncture site is sealed from the inside with a good adhesive tape, for example, 3M. The second repair option is even simpler - the entire inner film sleeve is replaced. And the replacement of this sleeve will cost only 350 rubles!
