HTC Rhyme: stylish and elegant

Natural intuitive design in the development of HTC phones always comes to the fore. The functionality and usage scenarios of the smartphone are worked out to the smallest detail, but with HTC Rhyme we decided to go further!

At the heart of creating Rhyme is a completely different approach: there is a huge group of consumers who do not focus on technology, but want the phone to be more personalized and organically fit into the lifestyle. We have developed a line of unique accessories that will help in this!


HTC Rhyme is a stylish smartphone with a unibody- aluminum case, a 3.7 " SLCD display with a resolution of 480 x 800 pixels, a 5MPx camera with LED flash and built-in stereo speakers. Rhyme is based on the Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM8255 platform with a 1GHz processor core and also has 768MB RAM and 1GB of memory for installing applications.

Rhyme has an updated version of HTC Sense , which is a continuation of the visual design language of the case and includes a completely new initial screen, offering a simple and concise way to interact with interactive widgets that provide instant access to the most important.

Very often, people use their phones in the bedroom: set the alarm or set the phone to charge while waiting for the morning call from their boss. With the new docking station for HTC Rhyme, you will forget about the wires and be able to wake up with your favorite songs that will be heard from the built-in Bluetooth speakers of the dock. A docking station is included with the phone.

A smartphone can easily be lost in a backpack or wide jeans pocket. It is for this case that we invented the HTC Rhyme keychain indicator , which is mounted in a prominent place and connected to the device's 3.5-jack. As soon as a new call or message arrives on the smartphone, the keyfob indicator will inform you about this with a light signal. Keychain included in the delivery of the phone.

HTC Rhyme headphones are made of special material that prevents them from becoming tangled. In addition, the reliability of the cord is much higher, which will only benefit sports fans.


In addition, you can also purchase the HTC Rhyme Sports ArmBand over -the-shoulder case, a Bluetooth headset and a car bluetooth speaker .
