One day from our life

Good news, everyone! ASUS is holding a contest ‚ÄúMe and My ASUS: One Day in Our Life‚Äù within the framework of the AIESEC International Congress in Russia.

The International Congress is the largest annual conference of the AIESEC organization, which is held in Russia for the first time. About 1000 leaders of national offices from 110 countries of the world will come together to discuss current trends in social and economic spheres and the participation of youth in solving global problems.

A number of external events will be organized within the framework of the International Congress: the Festival of Cultures on August 17 and the Voice of Youth conference on August 17-21.

But, actually, about the competition itself.





To participate, you must create a video, photo or collage, which will show how you apply our devices in your life.

For example, it can be a usual weekday morning, where you watch your favorite program on our netbook while having a cup of aromatic coffee. Or it could be a video about how you first saw something incredible and captured it on the video camera of our tablet. Or maybe it will be a super-creative collage with photos of our laptop.

The number of works - one per participant. The materials can use ASUS devices: a laptop, tablet computer or netbook.

We will award the author of the best work with a tablet computer ASUS Transformer Pad Infinity TF700T, as well as other valuable prizes.

To participate in the Contest, you must be a registered participant in Vkontakte.ru and be a member of the AIESEC International Congress group in Russia .

Send your work to ic2012@aiesec.net . Do not forget to provide contact details.

Evaluate the work will be a special jury consisting of employees of the company ASUS. The jury will determine the most creative and interesting work. Detailed contest rules in this thread .

The competition is held until August 17.
