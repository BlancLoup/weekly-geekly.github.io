Sony Magic Link - the first device on the OS Magic Cap

The history of the Magic Cap operating system itself is very interesting. Its developers are Bill Atkinson and Andy Hertzfeld , as well as Andy Rubin , founder of Android Inc.

About the operating system itself there is a wonderful article ‚ÄúThe Amazing History of Magic Cap‚Äù . We should also note that Magic Cap is not a graphical shell, but a full-fledged operating system for which the scripting programming language TeleScript was also developed .
I want to tell you more about the first product based on this operating system - Sony Magic Link communicator.

The Sony Magic Link PIC-1000 device was introduced to the market in October 1994 as a personal intelligent communicator with a price of $ 995.95 as standard.

Specifications:
  • Touchscreen (resistive) screen size 4.8 x3, 2 inches, displaying 4 gradations of gray
  • 16-MHz Motorola ‚ÄúDragon‚Äù 68349 CPU

  • Powered by Sony NP-500 battery and external power supply
