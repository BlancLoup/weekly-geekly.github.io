Yahoo Messenger online version released

In March, Google released the enhanced online version of GTalk. Not wanting to lag behind Google, Yahoo has released an online version of its messenger - Yahoo Messenger.

Like the Google Talk gadget, Yahoo messenger is made on Adobe Flash, for each conversation a separate tab opens inside one window, you can search for contacts and also see their status. Yahoo's instant messenger keeps logs of all your conversations and makes them searchable from the application interface. Google keeps conversations regardless of the client used, but the story is only available from GMail.


While the Google Talk gadget is compact and can be used inside a personalized iGoogle page, in a new window, or even in a browser sidebar, the new online Yahoo Messenger takes up the entire page.
