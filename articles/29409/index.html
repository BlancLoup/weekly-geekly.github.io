As a matter of fact, the forum was devoted to cluster policy, the development of concrete steps to introduce a cluster approach in the development of the economy both in individual regions and in the whole of Russia. At the same time, cluster policy is considered as an effective tool for supporting national business in global competition.

The importance of the chosen topic was confirmed in a speech by the President of the Russian Federation at a meeting of the State Council in February 2008 on the development strategy of Russia until 2020. Among the main priorities is the increase in the rate of innovation development of the national economy. The innovation system will be formed on the basis of cooperation and interaction of state and private institutions, openness of the dialogue between business and government, and effective regional policy.
