We get a bank card while abroad



There are several issues that freelancers face when they realize that their lifestyle no longer binds them to any particular point on the map. One of the most important is a means of storing money and payments. Cards of Russian banks for a number of reasons become inconvenient for a long stay abroad - for example, in case of loss, blockage and the need to recover. Or, for example, if you need to start a new account in another currency - you will not fly specifically to Russia for this? Recently added political risks. But in the end, it all comes down to a purely logistic question: how to get a bank card while being outside the country of origin.

The only Russian bank card in the review. And at the same time - one of the most convenient options for freelancers: with its use of Yandex-money, you can pay wherever you accept cards without losing commission on money withdrawal. In addition, the first three years of card service are free, which makes the offer even more profitable. You only need to pay for the issue and delivery of the card: 149 r across the Russian Federation, 199 - worldwide. Emit card "Tinkov Credit Systems".

The Yandex Money card clearly shows how a Russian bank remains convenient for its customers even when they live abroad. Actually, the secret of success is simple: worldwide delivery and low tariffs. Webmoney


Did you know that Webmoney also allows you to issue cards linked to your e-wallet? And the choice of payment systems is not limited to "Visa" and "Mastercard" - there also includes Payoneer and Union Pay.

A detailed analysis of tariffs and conditions can be found in the material on the site credit-card.ru , and here I just give some excerpts from it.

Webmoney site is divided into many independent services, to use each of them you need to go through the authorization procedure. Due to the intricate links between individual parts of the system, many users often cannot understand all the possibilities of webmans. In this case, we are interested in the possibility of ordering a debit card using WebMoney.Cards service and replenishing it from a dollar or euro WM wallet. Withdrawal of WME (Euro) is possible only on PaySpark EUR MasterCard, and WMZ - on all others.

A minimum of documents is required for registration: a copy of a Russian passport (from the 2nd to the 5th page); for T24 PayVault Visa, you will also need a copy of the utility bill that contains the address information. Documents are submitted remotely through the Center for Attestation, at the time of application, they should already be checked.

However, for this convenience will have to pay. The sum of all fees depends on a number of factors: the type of certificate, the number of card transactions, the value of one transaction, etc. Naturally, all costs are borne by the client. And if at the time of the next debit of funds in the account is not enough, when replenishing the card, the bank will write off all payments due to it plus the amount of loss incurred due to lack of money. This item is registered in the terms of the card on the WebMoney site.

Another very important point. Since the contract is formally concluded in Cyprus, then all disputes that the parties cannot decide on their own are resolved in accordance with the laws of the republic. By the way, about the essence of the contract itself. If you open the offer, you will see that the title says "Securities Sale Agreement". The fact is that all money transfer transactions involving the WebMoney system are regarded as transactions in the securities market.
