LinuXPC: another Linux based desktop

Shuttle offers miniature (SFF - Small Form Factor) LinuXPC computers based on SUSE Linux .



The proposed models SD3002W (from 400 euros) and SD3002Q (from 430 euros) are based on Intel processors. The configuration of computers can be changed over a wide range (RAM from 512 MB to 2 GB, hard disk from 80 to 750 GB, body color: black or silver) before ordering. External graphics is not provided, but you can buy it yourself.
