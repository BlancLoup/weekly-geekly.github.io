The first screenshots of Gatineau: Microsoft's response to Google Analytics

The first screenshots of the MS Gatineau web analytics system appeared on the Internet. With the functions of segmentation of website visitors by sex and age, it should be a worthy alternative to the product of Google Analytics, which gradually occupies a leading position in the accounting and control of global Internet traffic. Naturally, Microsoft can not yield to a competitor in such an important matter.

Screenshots made and published by a blogger who managed to penetrate the corporate briefing Microsoft adChamps in London.

Microsoft has already responded to this information leak. They said that they themselves were going to make an announcement soon, and the official beta version of Gatineau will be released at the end of the summer. We add that this is the first official information about Gatineau, the rumors about the development of which go from January.
