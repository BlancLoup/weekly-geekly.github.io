ICO Telegram collected $ 850 million from 81 investors



Pavel Durov, the creator of the Telegram messenger, reported on the progress of the ICO, which was announced earlier. As it turned out, investors invested more than $ 850 million. This amount was provided to the development team of the messenger by 81 investors. The ICO report was sent to the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). The report identifies two companies related to ICO, both registered in the British Virgin Islands: Telegram Group Inc. and TON Issuer Inc. Both companies are managed by Pavel and Nikolai Durov.

Payment from the first investor was received by the company on January 29, 2018. All funds raised will be used to develop the Telegram Open Network (TON) blockchain platform. The means of payment between the participants of the platform is its own token.

The document provided by the Durovs in the SEC is Form D, which is usually used for ‚Äúnotification of an exempt offer of securities,‚Äù as reported by Vedomosti. According to experts, this form is used by small companies to attract money from venture investors.

Investors are not named in the document itself, but they managed to find out about some of them. In particular, the founder of Qiwi, Sergey Solonin himself, said that he had invested $ 17 million in Telegram. David Yakobashvili, founder of Wimm-bill-dann, provided another $ 10 million. In addition, it was not without the participation of Roman Abramovich - several sources told about his participation in the project, who are familiar with the details of the transaction. Moreover, the amount of investment provided by Abramovich is much higher than all other investors and amounts to about $ 300 million.

True, the representative of Abramovich John Mann said that the information about the participation of the entrepreneur in the ICO Telegram does not correspond to reality. Anyway, there were much more people who wanted to participate in the ICO Telegram than those who were admitted to the tokensale (and that was not a tokensail in the direct sense of the term). About the rush around Telegram can be judged by the number of applications for the provision of investments - they were filed at $ 3.8 billion . A part of small investors gathered in groups for joint participation in ICO Telegram. Sources say that some members of the tokensale managed to invest only $ 50,000 - $ 100,000, and not several million, as required by the organizers of the tokensale. The leadership of the tokensale had to refuse a number of investment proposals, including Russian ones.
