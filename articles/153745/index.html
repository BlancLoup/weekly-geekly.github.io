CSS3 browser support

Although CSS3 is not yet a W3C standard , recent browsers have partial support for it. You can find examples that implement certain properties of CSS3. But along with the delight of many, the feeling of fear also leaves. This is due primarily to the fact that there is no clear understanding of the behavior of the browser when processing these properties. Under the cat assembled a table which compares the supported CSS3 properties in all major browsers. In addition, the browser version and prefix for encoding are indicated. The crib is in the form of an image, so it is convenient to download it to your computer or mobile device for further use.
