Future news

There is an idea to make a social news service that will get news from the future.


I proceed from the following premises:
  1. Not anything can happen - the future is discrete .
  2. Unverified fact that will have to take on faith. The film Waking Life talked about a control group that was given to solve crossword puzzles. The study showed that if they solved a crossword yesterday (the one that already solved more people), the number of unraveled words jumped sharply in contrast to a crossword puzzle that no one has yet decided. This suggests that there is a single information field for all.
  3. Stochastic resonance. A properly configured system can detect subliminal pulses .
Now, if we create a social network in which each participant will post ideas that come to him, and compare them with each other, we can create news that will happen in the near future.

There are a lot of examples of this effect, even here , and it probably happened to everyone that the ideas that came came to life very soon.

For the purity of the experiment is necessary:
  1. Exclude feedback. That is, to prevent FuturesNews from influencing the occurrence of co-existence.
  2. Qualitatively develop a methodology for comparing published ideas.
  3. Competent calibration of each member of the community
  4. Something that I did not see :)
Gradually, this will reveal people who can see further in time ... In principle, profits and financials can be a lot of ideas.
