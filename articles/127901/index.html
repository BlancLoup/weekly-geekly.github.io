AppProfessionals - search for mobile developers

This week, a new portal, AppProfessionals.ru, was launched on Windows Phone Camp, a tool for choosing a reliable partner for application development.

This site from the Proactivity Group allows Russian companies wishing to create an application for Windows Phone to find reliable and highly professional developers.

Recently, the largest Internet companies, agencies, companies of medium and small businesses that want to create mobile applications and are looking for competent specialists are turning to us several times a week. On AppProfessionals.ru, they can find the developers they need , either manually or through the organization of a tender.

If you have experience in developing for various mobile platforms (WP7, iOS, Android, Symbian, Blackberry, Bada ...), register on the site, specify information about your applications and your contact information.

Now, during the launch of Windows Phone in Russia, attention to mobile developers will only increase.
