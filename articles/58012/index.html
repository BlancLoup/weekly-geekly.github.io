Internet Restrictions II: Soon in Russia

Following the European counterparts, amendments to restrict the Internet are being prepared in Russia and will be submitted for approval by the end of spring.
Deputy Minister of Communications and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation, Alexander Zharov, explained the position of the state in the fight against illegal users of Internet content.

He is about amendments:


At the last meeting of our council, a bill was proposed, which amends the 1st and 4th parts of the Civil Code, the Law on Information, Information Technologies and Information Protection and the Law on Mass Media, which protect legal content owners on the Internet and, first in total, television companies and producers of feature films, producers of music from the pirated use of their products. Currently, these amendments to the legislation are under analysis. I predict that by the end of spring we will submit them for approval to the interested ministries and departments, and we will see further.
