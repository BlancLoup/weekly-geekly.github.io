Dropbox - we get an additional 9 GB in one day

I started using Dropbox recently. By the way, when I tried, for a long time I wondered why I started using it just now, although I heard about it for a long time - everything is very convenient.

As you know, after registering for a free account, a space of 2 GB is available. This space can be increased to 11 GB in various ways. Next, I will tell you a little more about all the known methods, as well as a small life hack.

So, after registering for the service, we have 2 GB. Begin to increase.

1) Go to the Get Started page, perform various actions on the items there (it may be out of order), after completing 6 of the 7 actions, we get 250mb. Total we have 2.25 GB.

2) Then we go to the special Free page, there we can get as many as 640 free megabytes. To do this, just follow a few simple steps:
  • Connect to your Twitter account
  • Connecting to your Facebook account
  • Follow @Dropbox on Twitter
  • Tell your friends about what you love for Dropbox;)
  • Tell your friends about Dropbox on Twitter
For each action we get 128MB. Total 2.89 GB.

3) We get registration by your referral link. For each registration and installation of the application (installation is required), we get 250 MB. Maximum 8GB. Your referral link can be obtained on this page below.

And here the most interesting begins: either we can distribute our link to friends, acquaintances and other Internet sites (and this will take a lot of time and is ineffective), or use a little trick. ;)

For tricks we will use contextual advertising. I preferred Google AdWords because I had money there. I think any system will do here. Created one ad, used one keyword "dropbox".

The campaign lasted three days. I decided to start carefully, with a budget of 50 rubles per day. The second day was 100 rubles / day, the third - 150 rubles / day. I also first put an option so that the ads were shown at maximum speed, but it seemed to me ineffective, and in the middle I returned the default value - show evenly.

So, at the end of the third day I had an additional 8 GB from referral registrations. True, I did not have time to stop the campaign in time and received some big expenses due to idle transitions / registrations.

Total we have 10.9 GB.
