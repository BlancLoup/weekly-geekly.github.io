Secret fighting robot MAI



There are only 5 days left for the BRONEBOT 2016 competition! February 21-23 in the Olympic will begin grand battles of robots. How can you not look into the MAI workshop? Here, the FAUST team as a part of graduate student Stepan Ignatiev and the head of NICH-7 Vitaly Polyansky is preparing his robot for battle.

The first thing that attracts attention is the half of the MIG-23 combat fighter disassembled into parts right in the middle of the workshop. From the cockpit, they want to assemble an aviation simulator. And from its aviation parts almost all the elements of the robot are assembled and these are good details, because as they explained to me, the MAI deals only with combat aircraft and their department is exactly the destruction of airplanes from different types of weapons.



That is why FAUST decided not to disclose many parameters and not to demonstrate the body of their robot:



The concept partially migrated from the previous robot, there were also strike weapons, but we refused secondary weapons so as not to waste the power of the system on it. We also changed the drive of the weapon from spring to pneumatic in order to increase the force of the blows. Pneumatics are stronger than everyone expects, we removed it from the AKU-170 - such special mounts for bombs.



The ax will also be heavy and very sharp. With his help, we will be able to roll over if we suddenly find ourselves on the back. The percussion mechanism is assembled from pneumatics, the ax itself is fastened with the help of the handle of the MIG-23 steering wheel.
