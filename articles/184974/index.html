I will not hide, I am a fan of this project (it is convenient and beautiful) and I advise it to all. I also think that the introduction of the ruble symbol will help this project to become even more popular among Russian developers.

I also pursue one "mercenary" goal. I am working on a project where the design provides a symbol of our favorite currency and I would like to resolve this issue with the help of the updated Font Awesome.
