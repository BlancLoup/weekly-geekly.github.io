Thanks to the Universal Power Over Ethernet (UPOE) technology, the monitor is connected to the virtualized server via a network cable, which will reduce client workstations to just one monitor that displays the working environment.

Of course, the estate of the NC220 is the corporate sector, which can now be organized incomparably simpler and more economical. The entire infrastructure, hitherto numbering hundreds and thousands of computers, can now be a single server.
