Disney World inside Google, Google inside Disney World

Dreamland Factory Walt Disney Co has created a three-dimensional map of its giant Disney World in Florida for Google Earth. Apparently, the company thought that fairy tales are fairy tales, and guests who get lost in the breathtaking expanses are, in this age of the Internet in any pocket, simply indecent.

A virtual copy of the cartoon-cinema city includes all the objects that a visitor may need: from the ticket office to the huge rides to the benches, from restaurants and snack bars to the routes of horses and ponies. Based on their own statistics, Disney says that 9 out of 10 people planning a trip to the park do it online. Now there is nothing easier than to plan your time so that you have time to look as much interesting as possible.

The studio did not fail to notice that the work carried out by it, in the course of which more than 100,000 photographs were taken, summarized in a model of 1,500 objects, became the most serious campaign of private collaboration with Google Earth. Taking an example from it would definitely be worth it to other holders of large areas of entertainment and entertainment. Although you should not overdo it - having seen all the beauties on the Web, a lazy Internet user may refuse to see their real prototypes.
