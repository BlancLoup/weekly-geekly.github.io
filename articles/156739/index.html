The Windows Store apps I use everyday, part 2

best application reference guide

This is the second part of my Windows Store app recommendation. I recommend that you familiarize yourself with the first part , which reviewed more than 50 applications available for installation. In the first part, the most popular applications for working with social networks, applications supplied by Microsoft, news and media applications were reviewed.

In this part, I will talk about the best in my opinion applications for working with data, reference resources, editors of various content and other utilities.

Onenote




OneNote is without a doubt the best electronic notebook for Windows 8 today. Synchronization with the cloud, support for pen input, a radial menu unique to Windows 8 with controls. Highly recommend.

Bing cards




Bing cards , oddly enough for today the most functional map client for the Windows Store. Thanks to the cooperation with Nokia Maps, the maps already have everything you need: Russian names, house numbers, traffic jams, driving directions, turn-by-turn navigation.

Analogs: Yandex.Maps - the first release that can be considered a demonstration, while only traffic jams and address search are available. We are waiting for updates.

Maps Pro is a map client for a variety of systems: MapBox, Nokia, Stamen, ESRI, Bing, Nokia Mobile, Open Street Map. Many options for maps, overlays.

G Maps is an unofficial client for Google Maps.





Poster




Poster - the official client of the popular service afisha.ru. The latest information on events in your city.

ESPN FC




ESPN FC - no kidding the best client for football fans. Choose and track your favorite teams, watch dozens of football leagues and championships, read the news. Must have!

Dailymotion




DailyMotion is the official client for the popular video service. You can find videos for every taste.

Trello




Trello is the official client for the productivity service from the company Fog Creek Software of the famous Joel Spolsky. Create your own boards with tasks, share them with the team, track the implementation.

Analogs: flow.timer is a very sophisticated time-tracking and project management service.



Techdays




TechDays is the official client for the video portal of technical reports on the Microsoft platform. Hundreds of video reports from experts and from the best conferences in Russian.

Merriam-Webster Dictionary




Merriam-Webster Dictionary is one of the oldest and most popular explanatory dictionaries of the English language. Pronunciation by native speakers in stock.

Analogues: Advanced English Dictionary is another huge dictionary, with pronunciation available for both American English and British.

Dictionary.com is another decent dictionary.
