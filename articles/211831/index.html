NYPD is testing Google Glass


Well, what, another 5-10 years, and a fantastic film will become a familiar reality

Generally speaking, devices like Google Glass can be not only an interesting toy, a new-fashioned gadget, but also a serious working tool. There are a lot of spheres where Google glasses can be used. These are presentations, both scientific and marketing, and data centers , and the service sector, and, of course, the police and military forces.

As for the police, the department of New York is already trying this gadget in their daily work.

Even if you do not pay attention to applications such as NameTag , designed for face recognition in real time, the police can use the "glasses" to view calls, view current tasks, to communicate with the dispatcher ... But you never know, there are a lot to come up with.

By the way, here is a video with a demonstration of the work of the very NameTag application:



Currently, the police in New York in the presence of a few copies of Google Glass, with whom work is being done. Most likely, law enforcement officers received this gadget as part of the Glass Explorer program, since Google does not cooperate with the police on this issue.

So far, the police have not shared the results of testing the device, however, after a while the experiment will be over, and it is likely that some data will appear on the web. Well, we will wait for the results.
