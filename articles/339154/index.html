Motivated installations from the USA (Android) - review of options, statistics, opinion

Preface: I am the most common individual developer of Android applications, I have already published one of my creations quite a while on Google Play, after which I worked hard on it. At some point, I decided - "Why not try to promote the application?". For this, I began to diligently read various literature on this subject. Later came the idea to try to buy a motivated installation (about what it read below). It was decided to move specifically in the United States. Actually further - the fact that I got that I tried.

Chapter 1. Motivated installations


Motivated installations - ordered installation of your application by other users in order to increase positions in the category.

It's not a secret for anyone - the more people download an application, the higher it will be when viewed in a category. The ideal is to achieve 1-3 places, because they account for the largest part of installations and views (almost no one will scroll through the list of categories up to 300 applications).

This method is intended for "artificial" promotion of a position in a category. For this to happen, judging by various sources, you need to get as many installations as possible in 2-3 days for Google Play, I don‚Äôt know how Apple is doing, so I won‚Äôt lie.

In this way, alongside the obvious advantages, there are also disadvantages. After entering the top category, if the application is not massive, it can just as easily fly out from there, so you need to choose this method wisely.
