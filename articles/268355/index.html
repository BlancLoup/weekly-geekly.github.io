DEV Labs 2015. Online conference for .NET developers. 17 October

We are pleased to announce that the registration for the online conference for .NET developers of the DEV Labs series has started. The fourth event dedicated to this software platform will be held on Saturday, October 17th. By tradition, we have compiled a program of four reports that will be of interest to both beginner and advanced developers.

In the conference program:

  • Our permanent leader in the direction of .NET - Sergey Teplyakov will talk about the architecture of the Roslyn platform, and on the fly will write some simple analyzers for the C # language.
  • Andrei Gordienkov will talk about transitive dependencies - about how they are dangerous, how they can lead to the collapse of the project, and how, and most importantly with what they can be controlled.
  • Andrey Akinshin , within the framework of his speech, will teach how to accurately and accurately measure time when it comes to milliseconds, microseconds, or even nanoseconds.
  • Alena Denisenko will talk about innovative tools for distributed programming - F #, MBrace and Azure and will show what they can do with live examples.

Detailed descriptions of the reports, the exact schedule of the event, the registration form, videos of past conferences and much more interesting can be found on the event website . Free admission. You can connect to the conference both from a personal computer and from mobile devices.

PS: This time we are forced to move the beginning of the conference to a later date due to the fact that some of the speakers will come from the United States. Sorry for any inconvenience.
