Hackathon Google Cast
Recently, the Chromecast and Android TV platforms received a common Google Cast framework for streaming media content to a TV. At the event, you can have fun with Chromecast and Android TV and there will be time to think about ideas.
Due to the relative complexity of the technology, the implementation of prototypes will be homework. After some time, another meeting will be announced (most likely in the Google office) where the results will be presented. The best team will get Chromecast for everyone and posting on the Google Russia blog.
It is very desirable that someone from the team should be present at the event at the time of registration of teams and ideas, although online registration will be available for some time afterwards.
