In Russia in 2005, 947 thousand smartphones were sold

The number of smartphones sold in 2005 in the Russian market amounted to 947 thousand units worth $ 558 million, according to a report from the analytical department of the Division Group (Divizion trademark).

The leader in smartphone sales in 2005 was Nokia. Compared to 2004, its share increased from 76.3% to 81.4%. The sales of other vendors' smartphones were as follows: Sony Ericsson - 7.2%, Siemens - 6.4%, Motorola - 2.7% and Qtek - 1.7%.

The average retail price of a smartphone in 2005 was $ 589, which is 3.5 times more than the average price of a mobile phone in 2005, which was $ 165.
