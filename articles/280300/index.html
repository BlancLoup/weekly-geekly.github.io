Beeline Data School: Spring, Knowledge, New Course



Hi, Habr.

So, the third year of the Beeline Data School is nearing completion and we are recruiting a fourth.

We have 18 classes, 36 hours, all the main topics of machine learning and data analysis, a lot of practice, a lot of homework, two Kaggle competitions, presentations and workshops from partners, the possibility of placing Beeline in the BigData team for the best students, fellow students from various business areas, where machine learning is applied and much more.

You need to know Python at the high school level, linear algebra and mathematics at the 1st year level and the ability to allocate at least 8 hours a week for classes and homework for 9 weeks.

Classes in the center of Moscow, every Monday and Friday, from 19:00 to 21:00. Beginning of the course on April 4, places are few.

Sign up here . And here and here you can read the impressions of the participants of the current course.
