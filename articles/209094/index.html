Google Drive has a tape of user activity



Team work on files in the Google Drive cloud has received an improvement - a user activity ribbon appeared in the interface, allowing you to track a series of actions for a specific file.

The scheme works in this way: if the file needs to remember who worked with it and how, then it should be checked with a checkbox, click on the button - a list of users and operations that each of them performed, as shown in the picture above, will appear on the right. If you do not select a specific file, then a history of changes will be displayed at once for all of them.

The following operations are supported to track changes:

  • move and delete;
  • renaming;
  • loading;
  • the provision of public access and its prohibition;
  • editing and adding comments
