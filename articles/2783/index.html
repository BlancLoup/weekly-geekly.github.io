Interpol has created a worldwide database of counterfeit and counterfeit goods.

Interpol has created a worldwide database on international crimes in the field of intellectual property, the UN News Center reports. This was announced by Interpol Secretary General Ronald Noble at the Third Global Congress on Combating Piracy and Counterfeiting (Third Global Congress on Combating Counterfeiting and Piracy) in Geneva.

The new system will provide information on cases of piracy of law enforcement officers around the world, which will facilitate the investigation of such cases. He expressed the hope that the base will help raise international cooperation in the field of intellectual property protection to a new level.
