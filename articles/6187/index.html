Actualization of kiev.ua domains

Considering the numerous proposals of registrars and users to improve the level of service when delegating domain names in the KIEV.UA domain, the KIEV.UA domain administrators from March 15, 2007 put the KIEV.UA domain rules into effect and begin the procedure for updating information on domain names.

Information on domain names in the KIEV.UA domain is updated from March 15, 2007 to May 31, 2007, inclusive, funded by Hostmaster LLC and does not require additional payment from registrants and registrars.

All domain names that have passed the data update procedure from March 15, 2007 to March 31, 2007, inclusive, will work until July 31, 2008, inclusive, at no additional charge. In other words, the registrant or registrar who updated the information no later than March 31, 2007, receives as a reward two months of prepaid domain name validity in the domain KIEV.UA.

All domain names in the KIEV.UA domain that have undergone the data updating procedure from April 1, 2007 to April 30, 2007, inclusive, will work until June 30, 2008, inclusive, at no additional charge. In other words, the registrant or registrar, who updated the information no later than April 30, 2007, receives as a remuneration one month of prepaid domain name validity in the domain KIEV.UA.

Domain names in the KIEV.UA domain that have passed the data updating procedure from May 1, 2007 to May 31, 2007 inclusive, will be open until May 31, 2008 inclusive, at no additional charge.

Domain names in the KIEV.UA domain that did not pass the data updating procedure until May 31, 2007 inclusive, from June 1, 2007 are transferred to the HOLD state. For another two months, domain registrants will still be able to recover the registered domain name.

If the domain name information is not updated by the registrant by August 01, 2007, the delegation will be canceled and the domain will be free for new registration.

If your domain name is serviced by a registrar, then contact him with a request to update information on your domain name.

If you have registered the domain yourself, in order to update the data in the domain you need:

On the main page www.hostmaster.net.ua in the left column, select the link Search for Registrant Domains "
Enter your nic-handle and enter the password
A list of domains in which you are an administrative or technical contact will appear on the screen.
Select the domain, the data about which you want to update, and click "Update".
The screen will display current information about your domain and four buttons:
DELETE - to delete a domain. After clicking this button, a domain deletion request will be automatically generated.

According to the DELETE application, an email will be sent to the address of the administrative contact of the domain from the UANIC registration system with a request to confirm the deletion. According to the instructions contained in the letter, the administrative contact of the domain can either send a confirmation by e-mail, or enter a confirmation via the web form, the link to which is also indicated in the letter.

Application confirmation is expected within 5 business days.

If the removal request is not confirmed within 5 business days, the domain will not be deleted.
