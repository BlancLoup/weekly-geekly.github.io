The universe is infinite and flat



Scientists involved in the project - Baryon Oscillation Spectroscopic Survey (BOSS) measured with an accuracy of 1% of the distance between galaxies at a distance of more than six billion light years from us, which allowed them to draw conclusions about the shape of the universe.

To date, 90% of the data collected during the project has been processed, the data will be fully processed in June 2014. But even preliminary results simply cause admiration.
