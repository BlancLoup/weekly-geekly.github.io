My experience using Google AdWords Express

A few months ago, I returned to the provision of IT services in my small town. In order to loudly declare myself the first time I decided to try contextual advertising on the Internet. Since in our country, Yandex and all its services are blocked, the choice fell on Google and its AdWords Express service .


The system is very simple and beginners, even those not connected with the IT sphere, can quite figure out how to work with it. This article discusses the problems and shortcomings of the AdWords Express system that I encountered during use. Also referred to service is My Business, which is closely related to the subject.

So, the sequence of my actions was this: I first registered in the free My Business service, and then in AdWords Express . I received a nice bonus at the start - promotional code of the action, according to which I was supposed to 900 UAH. to the account subject to the consumption within 1.5 months 300 UAH. Problem # 1 - Ad Editing


Waiting for the "paper" letter with the code for confirming the home address I decided to prepare an advertisement in advance. In the course of filling in the forms of the ad creation wizard, I indicated a link to a personal site that is located on a third-party hosting. My site is completely useless, and My business service offers a simple website builder, on their domain business.site and hosting. Faced with the first problem, the hand reached for the help button (?) . Problem number 6 - the discrepancy of data on the state of the account in different parts of the system


As I wrote above, I chose the method of payment for prepaid advertising services. I considered it safer than tying a bank card for automatic debit. The AdWords Express system offers extensive account tracking capabilities. The amount of the remaining funds can be observed in three places:

  • Ad control panel -> Expenses block: the time schedule of expenses by day and total amount is displayed;
  • Payment section -> Payment Management tab: the balance is displayed, but knowing the amount of the deposit made, you can calculate the amount of expenses;
  • Payment section -> Promotional codes tab: a filling indicator of expenses and the current amount for participation in the action are displayed (spend 300 UAH and get 900 UAH )

And now "funny": in my case, after a week of using the service, all three amounts turned out to be different. Two values ‚Äã‚Äãdiffered approximately by several hryvnias, and the third differed (from the first two) by several tens of hryvnias. Ok, Google, what's the accuracy?
