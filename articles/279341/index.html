Preview Rambler.iOS # 6



March evening,
Sakura blooms,
Be Rambler.iOS.

Spring came, and we, the iOS development team of the Rambler & Co holding, are pleased to announce the next conference - Rambler.iOS 6. The conference will take place on March 30 in our office at Danilovskaya Manufactory.


A little bit about the speakers and their reports.


Feature toggles.


Every day we add new functionality to our applications at the request of the business, often forgetting about its encapsulation. Stanislav Tsyganov will talk about the approach ‚ÄúFeature toggles‚Äù, which allows you to easily and conveniently manage a set of ‚Äúfeatures‚Äù and their configurations.




AppDelegate - divide and conquer.


Many iOS developers have had to deal with the proliferation of the AppDelegate class. As a rule, notification processing, handoff, CoreSpotlight, and so on are in the same object, which violates the principle of uniqueness of responsibility. Vadim Smal will share the experience of researching this problem, as well as present a ready-made software component for its solution.




Do not growl at pbxproj.


Each of us at least once faced with conflicts in the file pbxproj. Obviously, the decision to revert changes and re-add / delete files to the project is not ideal. Dmitry Zhuravlenko will tell about the structure of the pbxproj file, as well as the causes of conflicts and an effective way to deal with them.




Pagination demystified.


In a set of client applications paginal data loading is implemented. This theme, which is rather simple in theory, can become complicated many times depending on the specifics of the project and the functionality of the server part. Egor Tolstoy will give an overview of various pagination techniques and describe the general approach to solving this problem at the design stage.

We are waiting for colleagues and all interested people to exchange experience and knowledge with each other. For guests will be offered pizza and drinks.

PS: As always, we will raffle the AppCode license!
