How to attract the best minds of the Runet to your startup?

Hi, habrachelovek!

We will give an answer to this question on June 29 at the BizSpark Camp conference on June 29 at the Extropolis conference center. The event is organized jointly by Microsoft and StartupPoint.
Everyone can take part. For this you need to register as a participant . If you do not have the opportunity to come, you can subscribe to the online broadcast .
Among the invited experts:
  • Igor Ashmanov - Managing Partner of Ashmanov and Partners
  • German Klimenko - the owner of "LiveInternet"
  • Dmitry Stepanov - executive director of the publishing house "Playbill"
  • Askar Tuganbaev - Director for Development of Internet Projects, CTC Media
  • Petr Didenko - Chief Specialist for Strategic Development of SKB Kontur
  • Arkady Moreynis - Head of GlavStart Holding
  • other.

What will happen?


In the framework of BizSpark Camp, ready-to-launch and successful startups will be presented. You can observe the reaction of investors and experts on the example of real projects.
You will be able to listen to the leading experts of IT business, talk with successful colleagues and draw investors' attention to your startup.
Up to 1000 people will participate in the conference online and offline. You have the opportunity to present your project to such a large audience. Apply for a presentation .

Why attend conference?


Do you have an idea, but you do not decide to run it? Do you need to soberly evaluate all the pros and cons, get qualified support, find the strengths of the idea and focus on them, without making any extra mistakes in the process?
Or have you already moved from idea to implementation, do you have a finished project? It remains the most difficult thing: to understand how this project can enter the market. What properties of the project need to be developed? What to focus on? What to do so that the project finds investment for further development?
To solve these tasks, we need not only and not so much money, but professional expertise and often - extensive experience in the market. It is not enough to listen to a lecture at a regular startup conference. New questions appear regularly, and answers are needed in a timely manner from a person who understands your project and is interested in its success. You can learn more about all this at BizSpark Camp. Register as a member

At BizSpark Camp, Arkady Moreynis (head of GlavStart) will tell for the first time about the scheme of work of the new holding GlavStart and about the new initiative for developing start-ups that is not yet represented in Russia but successfully used in the world.
