ThinkPad - a test of music and a jackhammer

ThinkPad notebooks have been tested with the Mil-SPEC * standard set and are able to withstand the most extreme weather conditions. What's so surprising, you ask? After all, we have previously told you about the six real survival stories of the ThinkPad . But can he survive with the connected power? For example, playing music during extreme trials? There is only one way to find out ...



* Mil-SPEC - a set of standards designed to test devices for durability, strength and reliability. These tests are based on extreme weather conditions, including temperature, pressure, dust, humidity and vibration resistance tests.

Without thinking twice, we went to the studio for the world beatbox champion Rahzel. Inspired by the strength of the ThinkPad, he recorded a new track especially for us. Krutotenyushka, right? As soon as the track was recorded - we just had to make a video for it.
