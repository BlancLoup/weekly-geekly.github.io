Bypassing the upcoming limit on international mail *

Somehow, the fire of the sci-fi places of users of Internet shopping after loud statements by those in power about the coming radical reduction of the duty-free limit on international mail (MPO) has died down. The Finance Ministry scared us by the imminent demise of virtual purchases abroad and went into the shadows. All stood waiting, in what size and, most importantly, when will the limits be officially approved and adopted by the customs officers?

A fresh forecast: Day D will be July 5, then the amount of the duty-free limit will be officially announced. However, on the sidelines, customs brokers bet that by the end of 2014. limits, even if approved, will not take effect ... Rumors are rumored, but one thing is obvious, sooner or later limits will take effect, and then the moneymakers from the Association of Internet Trading Companies will finally begin to cut the loot.

But for every tricky ass, as you know ...

While we were entertaining ourselves by voting at the ROI for the abolition of the introduction of the customs limit, people, whose business is directly connected with international postal items, were looking for workarounds.

Yes, the well-known forwarders (Shopfans, Banderolk, Mainbox, Polar Express) began to work out the options:
- delivery through Kazakhstan, Belarus with internal limits of the customs union were dismissed due to the rise in price of the kilogram of delivery.
- delivery through the European Union, in particular, sending of demand to Finland, was also problematic from a logistical point of view ...

And then the first burst Shopfans . Today, the company has issued a "press release" in which it announced the following: in one physical package for one addressee there may be goods for whatever number of recipients. At the same time, the customs limit for each recipient is separate.
Follow the logic: the parcel has an addressee - this is the person who will directly receive the physical parcel. But now, in addition to the addressee, the parcel also has recipients who carry their belongings in this parcel, but the courier does not need it when delivering the parcel; only one recipient is needed.

What does this give us:
If, say, on June 5, a $ 200 limit is introduced per person, then:
1) you can create a package in which there will be 10 Moto E pieces for $ 129 each.
2) At the same time, for each Moto E there will be a customs recipient (1 addressee and 9 recipients, totaling 10).
3) For each recipient, the customs limit does not exceed $ 200, despite the fact that the mail box of goods flies on $ 1290.
4) the cost of delivery of 10 phones is blurred at all, while no one pays a fee
5) profit

Now send the parcel to work with at least thirty identical goods within the customs limit is now easier than ever, only copies of your colleagues' passports are needed at the stage of filling out the declaration in your personal account.
