Legal withdrawal of money from oDesk in the Russian Federation

Hello!

Issues related to receiving money from foreign customers were repeatedly highlighted on various portals. We have been using oDesk for work for a long time, and colleagues from other companies have repeatedly asked about the details of the process of withdrawing funds from an oDesk account to an official bank account, be it LLC or IP. In this article I will describe the steps and publish the translation of the contract, which must be submitted to the bank.



Why do you need it:


  • Withdrawing money to a current account is the only legal way to work with oDesk in the Russian Federation
  • At high speeds, using the payoneer.com card becomes impossible.
  • With transactions from $ 2000 per week, the cost of money transfer in the Russian Federation is less, if you use wire transfer


By law, any legal entity working with foreign partners and receiving money from non-residents in USD is required to submit documents to the bank for passing currency control, otherwise the bank will not have the right to transfer them from the transit account to the current account. If you identify violations of the passage of currency control fin. supervision can punish you with a fine: 4,000 - 5,000 rubles for an individual entrepreneur, 40,000 - 50,000 rubles for an LLC, according to Article 15.25 of the Administrative Code, paragraph 6.

On the oDesk website, it is possible to specify the Wire Transfer Payment Method, into which the transit account data is entered. It is necessary to take into account that banks are obliged to provide information on the receipt of funds to a transit account, therefore it is advisable to prepare documents in advance. After the money from oDesk came to the transit account, you will have 15 days to pass the currency control.

The procedure for passing currency control


There are two ways to prepare and submit the documents necessary for the legal receipt of money.

  1. While the total amount of all transactions for all the time with oDesk has not exceeded 50,000 USD, it is possible not to issue a transaction passport, but to bring an agreement with oDesk to the bank, an act for each transaction and a certificate of currency transaction
  2. After the first 50 000 USD you need to issue a passport transaction


Now in order. The contract in English can be taken from the site: kb.odesk.com/questions/1386 It is necessary to bring a translation into Russian to the bank, and, only if asked, an English version. We decided to share our version of the translation (at the end of the article).

Usually, the act must be signed by both parties, and this creates some inconvenience, because getting a signature from an oDesk representative is problematic. As an alternative, we have chosen to work under a public offer contract (this is exactly what the above presented contract with oDesk is). You need to notify your bank and agree that an invoice will be provided for each transaction with the transaction number and the amount minus the cost of the transfer (at the moment wire transfer fee = 30 USD). An example of an invoice .

A certificate of a currency transaction can be completed by the bank for a small fee, but you can prepare them yourself. It is important to indicate the currency operation code in it. It will tell the bank. For example, 20200. The contract effective date is specified arbitrarily. This will probably be the date you started working with oDesk.

Transaction passport


If, according to a given contract with oDesk, you have passed through 50,000 USD, then you need to create a transaction passport (the bank itself will tell you about this, having previously blocked the funds). The easiest way is to ask the bank to issue a passport of the transaction for an additional commission, but you can also provide a ready one.

After the transaction passport is issued, the certificate of a currency transaction will change a little. Also, you will need to provide a certificate of supporting documents . It is also not complicated and is very similar to a certificate of a currency transaction - it is necessary to indicate in it the number and date of the same invoice that we provide the bank with each transaction.

After the bank has accepted all the documents, it will allow you to transfer money from the transit account to a current account and / or sell it in rubles.

All copies of documents for the last 5 years are best kept. In case of disputable situations that occur periodically in Fin. supervision, you will have the opportunity to remember the details of the transaction and resolve the issue.

Lastly, allow me to wish that the bureaucracy and formalities do not distract you from the real work!
