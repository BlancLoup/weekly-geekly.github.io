The first flash drive for 1 terabyte



At CES 2013, Kingston announced the world's first 1TB USB flash drive. It seems that progress in this area is especially fast: just a year ago no more than 256 GB of flash drives were sold, and over the year they grew fourfold. What can I say, not everyone has this kind of HDD ...

DataTraveler HyperX Predator 3.0 flash drive supports USB 3.0 interface, backward compatible with USB 2.0. Read speed 240 megabytes per second, write 160 MB / s. The size of a kind of bars: 7.2 x 2.7 x 2.1 centimeters.

The cost of a flash drive, of course, is not advertised , so as not to shock the public, just as once gigabyte flash drives cost more than a thousand dollars.



A bit oftopic, but an interesting fact: to promote its products, Kingston started its own girlish pop group, DreamGirls, and the girls themselves from the group last year formed the official Kingston PR department . That is, if a journalist is required to receive an official comment from the company, you need to contact them.





It is not surprising that with such personnel policy Kingston is among the hundred best companies in the world for employment, according to Fortune magazine.
