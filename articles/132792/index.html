Features of the LG Smart TV platform

We are almost certain that many people have already managed to get acquainted, if not with our Smart TV platform, then with a competitive one.

Briefly about the main thing - Smart TV - a platform (software) that turns your TV into a media center.

Naturally, the rest of the functionality of the TV is preserved - from playing analog and digital signals to 2D to 3D converter (in models with support for 3D mode) and playing files from external sources via HDMI and USB slots.

LG Smart TV is on both plasma and LED TVs of various diagonals (from 32 to 60 inches).
