Another Acer A1 Guglofon

Acer A1 smartphone is made on the basis of a Qualcomm 8250 processor with a clock frequency of 768 MHz.
The amount of RAM is 256 MB, the capacity of the integrated flash memory is 512 MB. User information is recorded on removable microSD cards.



The model is equipped with a 3.5-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of WVGA (800x480 pixels), a camera with a 5-megapixel matrix, wireless adapters Bluetooth 2.0 EDR and Wi-Fi, as well as a receiver of satellite navigation system GPS.

The communicator is designed for use in mobile networks GSM and HSPA / WCDMA.

The claimed battery life of the gadget from the battery is up to five hours in talk mode and up to 400 hours in standby mode.
The dimensions of the novelty are 115x63x13 mm, weight - 135 g.
