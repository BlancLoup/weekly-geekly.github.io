What do people actually do on the Internet?

More recently, Facebook said that its portal is visited by about 1.1 billion people every month. The figure is really amazing, but where are all the other Internet users? The statistics may surprise you a little. Despite the popularity of Instagram in Snapchat, it loads up to 100 million more pictures per day. And Yahoo Mail, as it turned out, is much more popular than Twitter. Well, MySpace overtook the number of Spotify users.



In order to understand at least a little bit how the Internet of 2013 looks like, a list of the largest Internet projects was compiled. All data is official, which means some will be slightly overstated by the creators and managers of the projects. However, the picture is still very clear.

The data below shows monthly statistics on the number of users of the foreign segment of the Internet.

Facebook is a social network founded in 2004. 1.1 billion visitors monthly.



YouTube is a video hosting service (hosting, processing and storage of video), founded in 2005, is a subsidiary of Google Inc. 1 billion visitors monthly.



QQ is an instant messaging service, extremely popular in China, supported by Tencent, founded in 1998. 700 million visitors monthly.



Weibo is a microblogging service of Chinese origin, created by Sina Corp in 2009. 503 million monthly visitors.



Google+ is a social network from Google, launched in 2011. 343 million monthly visitors.



WeChat is a mobile text and voice messaging service founded and already familiar to us by Tencent in 2011. 320 million visitors per month.



Shazam , a cross-platform project for identifying sounds and searching for information about them, was founded in 1999. 300 million visitors monthly.



Yahoo Mail - an email service from Yahoo, activated in 1997. 281 million monthly visitors.



Skype is a communications service (text, audio and video), founded in 2003. 280 million visitors a month.



Zynga is a developer of online games from the United States, founded in 2007 with 232 million monthly visitors.



WhatsApp is an instant messaging service for smartphones, created in 2009. Just over 200 million visitors per month.



Twitter is a social network and microblogging service, it has been operating since 2006. 200 million visitors monthly.



Soundcloud is a social network + digitized sound information distribution service, operating since 2008. 180 million visitors per month.



Pandora - an online radio service with the ability to listen to your favorite songs, began its existence in 2000. 175 million visitors monthly.



Badoo is a social network dating, founded in 2006. 172 million monthly visitors.



Tumblr - microblogging service with the ability to exchange messages, images, videos, links, etc. Founded in 2007. 170 million visitors per month.



LinkedIn is a social network for searching and establishing business contacts, it has been operating since 2003. 160 million visitors monthly.



Snapchat is a photo messaging application launched in 2011. 150 million downloaded photos per day (this data is comparable to the number of visitors per month).



Line - an instant messaging service using unique images and "smiles" (there is also a Line-camera, Line-games, etc.), founded in 2011. 150 million visitors monthly.



Instagram is an application for creating photos, processing and distributing them; it has been running since 2010. 100 million visitors monthly.



Flickr is a service for storing and further using digital photos and video files, launched in 2004. 87 million visitors per month.



Yelp is a search and evaluation service for various representatives of the services market (cafe, restaurant, hairdresser, etc.), established in 2004. 84 million visitors monthly.



Flipboard is a service for automatically collecting content from various social networks and presenting it in the form of a magazine for smartphone users. Founded in 2010. 53 million visitors per month.



Evernote - a set of software for archiving data and creating notes of various types (text, photos, videos, ved-page, etc.). Works since 2009. 50 million visitors monthly.



Pinterest is a social service for sharing photos and cataloging them. It was founded in 2010. 48.7 million monthly visitors.



Kik Messenger is an instant messaging application for mobile devices, created in 2009. 30 million visitors per month.



MySpace is a social network created in 2003. 25 million visitors monthly.



Spotify is a legal streaming service for music. Launched in 2008. 24 million visitors monthly.



Path is a social network for mobile devices, launched in 2010. 10 million visitors monthly.



Instapaper is a service for storing and further reading various articles and texts, founded in 2008. 2 million visitors monthly.
