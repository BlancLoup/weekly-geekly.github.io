[Moscow, 02.19.2015] Dmitry Lenev - Lock Managers in MySQL

Hello!

We have great luck! We were able to agree with Dmitry Lenev , a unique specialist, MySQL Server developer with 11 years of experience, about speaking at CodeFreeze. Muscovites, be sure to come!

So, on Thursday, February 19, at 20:00 in the Moscow office of Mail.Ru, a meeting of CodeFreeze with Dmitry Lenev, MySQL Server developer at Oracle, will take place. The report will review MySQL data lock managers (including metadata, table, and InnoDB locks). The purpose of each type and architecture of these managers will be discussed.





Special attention will be paid to what historical background and reasons led to the emergence of three separate subsystems and the choice of different approaches for solving seemingly similar tasks. Different approaches to dealing with deadlock (Deadlock Avoidance vs Deadlock Detection) and the use of a lock-free technician in the implementation of the lock manager will be reviewed.

The listener is not required to get acquainted with the internal device of MySQL Server, but familiarity with the main features and experience of its use are welcome. It's also great to have an idea about parallel programming.

Participation is free, prior registration is required. Register HERE .
