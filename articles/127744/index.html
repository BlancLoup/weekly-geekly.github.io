The Japanese are entering the game - first look at the Sony Tablet S and Sony Tablet P tablets

At the IFA 2011 conference, Sony introduced several new products. The huge stand of the company is filled with all sorts of devices. The main characters of the Japanese manufacturer are two Android Honeycomb tablets, which have already been written a lot. At first impressions, the devices work quickly, despite the fact that the OS is quite strongly changed. Details on the video:



Both gadgets have a rather specific case. The design of 9.4 inch Sony S is made in the form of a folded sheet, and it feels more like a real book or magazine. In addition, it has a port for a full-fledged SD card.

The Sony P tablet is designed to be portable, it has two 5.5-inch screens, which often use Sony's various services very successfully, for example, in PlayStation Certified games on one of the screens there will be controls and on the other the action of the game.



Both devices are powered by the Nvidia Tegra 2 dual-core processor running Android 3.2 Honeycomb . The software shell has several characteristic changes compared to the stock version, in addition, the tablets are well integrated with the services of Sony , among the pre-installed applications you can find a store of books , music , as well as a TV remote control.

The capacity of the battery is not officially announced, but the manufacturer promises 8 and 7 hours of work with the active use of wireless Internet and watching videos for the Sony S and Sony P, respectively.
