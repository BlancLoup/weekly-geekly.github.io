Study of salaries of project managers in Russia

The Project Management Institute last week published the results of a study on the salaries of project managers in 2013 .


The study is interesting.

  • Firstly, the data of the countries are interesting (who thinks about immigration?).
    It turned out that the largest median wages are in Australia: $ 134k, and the lowest in Egypt: $ 24k.
    In the US, the median salary is $ 108k.
    In the UK: $ 90k.
  • Secondly, the dependencies of salaries on gender, experience, position, size of projects and teams, education, availability of certificates, size and type of organization, etc., are even more interesting - and also in the context of the camp


The study is available free to all PMI members. The rest will have to pay money for access to the study.
However, there is a big fly in the ointment. Russia was once again not included in the study, because we have too few PMI members.

To correct this shortcoming in social networks in me profile groups, a survey was launched for project managers from Russia. Now there are a little more than a hundred answers and I want more.

The survey is completely anonymous.
I invite project managers who read Habr to come in and take part in a survey (it will take you 10-15 minutes) and share a link with your colleagues working in the field of project management.
