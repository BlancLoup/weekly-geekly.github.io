"Classmates" in RuNet has become more than a million

Internet service Odnoklassniki.Ru , designed to search for old friends and new acquaintances, reached an audience of 1 million users. The creators of the service claim that their project is the first community in Runet with such a number of registered participants. According to them, about 50,000 people are now registered weekly on the site. Recall Odnoklassniki.Ru service was launched in March 2006.
