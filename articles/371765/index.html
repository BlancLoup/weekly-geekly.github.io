How to create your game: education in geymdeve

The business of creating video games is gaining momentum all over the world. According to analysts at Digi-Capital, the total volume of the games market in 2016 will be $ 90 billion, and by 2020 these figures will grow to $ 115 billion! The revenues of individual companies have already reached several billion dollars, and the most profitable game in the world of League of Legends in 2015 alone earned $ 1.6 billion.

In such conditions, new companies develop game development and expands the staff of existing ones. But at the same time there are not so many highly qualified specialists in the game-dev market. In the conditions of a shortage of personnel in the gaming industry market, and also due to the lack of educational institutions of this profile, in 2014, the program of professional retraining ‚ÄúManagement of online gaming projects‚Äù (IIAI) was launched at the base of the Higher School of Business Informatics (VSHBI). supported by our company All Correct Group. Already 40 people have become students of this program, many of whom have managed to get jobs in large Russian game companies (for example, Mail.Ru Group, RJ Games and others) or even create their own indie projects.



IIPI actually became the first in Russia training program for the gaming industry, implemented on the basis of a higher educational institution. Upon completion of the 9-month course, a diploma of the established model of the Higher School of Economics on professional retraining is issued.

The composition of the areas in which they are trained is quite extensive and includes our favorite localization, game design, game project management and operation, community management, as well as player technical support, Internet marketing, game projects monetization, virtual reality and a number of other disciplines.

The classes are conducted by leading experts from large gaming companies, as well as by representatives of organizations from the field of information technologies .
