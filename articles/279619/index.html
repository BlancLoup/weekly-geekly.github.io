An exhibition of illustrations by Alexey Andreev, an artist from St. Petersburg, will be held for visitors and participants of PHDays. His work, made in the technique of digital painting, is very fond of visitors last year's forum. The artist again invites everyone to go on a journey to the fantasy world: objects and phenomena familiar to the eye appear in a new light, and it seems that everything that happens is true that this is the real life. Fantasy plots, charming heroes and incredible credibility - here, perhaps, are three main components of the paintings of Andreev.

This time the artist prepared a surprise - a series of paintings with augmented reality. Visitors to the exhibition will be able to "revive" the picture using a smartphone. To do this, you need to download a special application and scan the QR code of the picture you like.
