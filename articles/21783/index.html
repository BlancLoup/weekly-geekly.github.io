On the sixth of March, two thousand and one, a fiery fighter for the decoration of the world, our dear comrade, Epson Stylus Color 600 printer, died.
The Soviet people and all progressive humanity suffered a heavy loss. We all will miss Epsona Stylusa, for a long time we will remember its remarkable streak, giving us so many happy and happy moments.

Epson met his host on a cold winter evening in 1997, in the hero-city of Moscow, in a department store in the Three Station Square. Epson's work began early, and passed rapidly and fruitfully. On the day he distributed up to one and a half thousand sheets of copies in economy printing mode. He also performed well in the field of photo-quality color printing.
