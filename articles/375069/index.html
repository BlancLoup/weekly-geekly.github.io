About the work plan


Why at least one publication per week? I work according to the usual schedule from 9 to 18 and initially selected holidays for the experiment. Plus, it seemed to me (I can not prove it with numbers in my hands) that the publication on Sunday evening makes it easier for the post to go to the first page, where it is read many times more. The mechanism, it seems to me, is simple - there are fewer publications on Sunday evening, so a small positive rating at first is enough for the article to be on the first page at the peak of Monday morning. However, now, after budding GT from Habr, this knowledge is hardly very useful.
One publication takes approximately 12-15 hours of direct work. I have not timed up to a second, this is an approximate estimate. Usually the topic is chosen on weekdays, in free time material on the topic is searched, Saturday is almost always spent on leisure and household affairs, and the main work is carried out on Sunday.
