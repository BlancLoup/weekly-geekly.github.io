Dribbble Meetup 2014 at the Mail.Ru Group office



We welcome all designers and sympathizers. On May 24, Moscow will host the Dribbble Meetup 2014 conference, the official meeting of Dribbble participants in Russia. The meeting will be held at the Mail.Ru Group office, this will be the third Moscow Dribble conference. As in previous years, all the reports of the participants will be devoted only to practical tasks: the creation of modern websites, icons, illustrations, web services and mobile applications.

Last year, over 400 guests attended the conference, which were addressed by leading industry experts: SoftFacade, Vadim Shcherbakov, Pavel Proshin (Gipis), Phil Dunsky, Nikolay Verin, Dmitry Novikov (MacPaw), Mysmaksy, Dmitry Chut, Dmitry Zimin and others. More visitors are expected this year. For them, the conference speakers prepared many interesting reports on how the work on well-known projects proceeded, how to get the most out of the design tools, how the workflow was built in studios and grocery companies, what techniques and methods to use to create strong and modern design works.

Dribbble.com is currently one of the most popular designer social networks. It was created by Dan Cederholm, founder of the American design studio SimpleBits. Publish your work in this network is by invitation only. Getting it is not easy and, as a result, the site contains only high-quality works and the best designers from around the world.
