Sponsored accounts: the ability to get premium Evernote subscriptions for groups of any size

Now you can provide a premium Evernote account to anyone in your company, school or institution. We were often asked about this since the launch of Evernote two and a half years ago, probably because people are now loaded with information more than ever, and many of us can no longer draw a clear line between personal, family, professional and academic spheres of life. Evernote helps to streamline this chaos, and now we have made it easier to attach entire commands to services. This feature is sponsored Evernote accounts .



What are sponsored accounts?


Sponsored accounts are an easy way to pay and manage premium subscriptions for groups of any size. Any employee in your organization can take advantage of a premium subscription, and you get a single consolidated monthly bill. But that is not all.

Easy group management
When you create a sponsored group, you get the opportunity to customize the welcome page, the link to which you will send to anyone you want to provide with a premium account. You have the ability to approve and delete group members with a few mouse clicks.



Easy calculations
The cost of a subscription for one sponsored account is $ 5 per month. You can pay as many accounts as you want with a credit card and get a single bill for the month for the whole group of five or more people.

Priority technical support
Requests for support from the owner or administrator of the sponsored group receive the highest priority in the queue.

Who owns the accounts?


Only Evernote users own their accounts. Sponsorship does not give you ownership of their accounts, you simply pay them additional premium subscription options. If you decide to suspend payment for an individual user, then his account will simply return to the status that he had before the start of the sponsorship: free or premium account, paid from his own funds.

For users, joining a sponsored group does not mean losing any control over accounts. If your premium account Evernote paid for the company you left, then all your notes you take with you. The memories you save in Evernote remain with you forever.

Sponsorship and collaboration


Employers and other users who want to control the flow of information in their Evernote accounts can create their own corporate notebooks and give them access to team members as part of standard collaboration functionality. Currently shared notebooks are available in the web interface and Evernote beta for Mac, but this feature will soon be implemented on other platforms. We are also working to make this functionality more advanced and easier for sponsored groups. This will allow you to quickly open access to the notebook for your team.

Discounts for sponsored accounts for educational institutions


Sponsored accounts for students and teachers around the world are offered at a 50% discount on the regular price.

Example with an educational institution: Montclair Kimberly Academy
Montclair Kimberly Academy (MKA) is a private school in the American city of Montclair, New Jersey, whose experience can serve as an example of using the capabilities of sponsored Evernote accounts. This school year, MKA launched a new program, providing every high school student a MacBook.
