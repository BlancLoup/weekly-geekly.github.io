Toshiba TransMemory 32 GB



Toshiba America Electronic Components (TAEC) has expanded the Flash-based TransMemory family of USB flash drives with the new U2K series of Windows models that support the Windows ReadyBoost technology implemented in Microsoft Windows Vista.

At the initial stage, Toshiba TransMemory U2K drives will be available in sizes from 1 to 8 GB. These devices will hit the market in October. And closer to the end of the year, in December, the manufacturer promises to please the release of the drive TransMemory Limited Edition more impressive volume - 32 GB. The full list of new products is as follows: U2G-032GT - 32 GB capacity, U2K-008GT - 8 GB, U2K-004GT - 4 GB, U2K-002GT - 2 GB, U2K-001GT - 1 GB.

Among the most notable features of the U2K series of drives, in addition to ReadyBoost support, the company notes the presence of password data protection and a lanyard that secures the connector cap. Technical parameters of the products are as follows:
