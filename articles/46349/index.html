New Year's gifts from LiqPay.com and Roboxchange.com

Yesterday (December 6), the payment system of Ukrainian PrivatBank LiqPay.com, together with the exchange service Roboxchange.com, apparently did not seem to have decided to make a very generous early New Year's gift to all attentive users.

The fact is that for almost half a day a course of 1USD = 1RUR worked there. When choosing an exchange on, say, 10 USD LiqPay at 10 WMZ, after redirecting to LiqPay for payment, the kindest Ukrainian service set an amount for paying not 10 USD, but 10 rubles! After paying these 10 rubles, Robox issued a New Year's gift in the form of 10 WMZ.

Whose side was wrong? Most likely, all the same Robox passed the wrong currency code. In any case, responsible programmers will not be happy. And some resourceful citizens "earned" themselves on New Year's gifts.
