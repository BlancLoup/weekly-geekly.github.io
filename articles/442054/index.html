Joomla and e-commerce


In this section, we will once a month talk about any component for commerce on Joomla. The goal is to show a variety of solutions. Today we will tell about Hikashop.

Hikashop is a powerful and flexible online store for Joomla. The third most common online store for Joomla in Russia.

Hikashop is a whole set of extensions for Joomla, which includes:
  1. A free version of an online store where all the basic functions of the store are available.
  2. A full-fledged commercial version, which includes all possible extensions for an online store, unlike many other products, almost all solutions for an online store are included in the package.
  3. Expansion for the organization of auctions HikaAuction
  4. Expansion for the organization of multivendor stores .
  5. Extensions for selling serial numbers for programs or any other electronic products, as well as Hikaserial subscriptions.

There is a marketplace extensions .

On the developer forum and the forum of the Russian-speaking community Joomla there are sections dedicated to Hikashop: the Hikashop developers forum and a section on the forum of the Russian-speaking community Joomla .

Not so long ago, version 4 of Hikashop was released , which is expected to bring with it compatibility with the alpha version of Joomla 4 and many new features:
  • Compatibility with the Joomla 3.9 extension for the processing of personal data.
  • The administrative part was significantly changed.
  • SEO customization features have been added.

About the benefits of Hikashop


The qualitative difference of Hikashop is in the ability to customize the appearance of any part of the store, without the fear that these changes will disappear with the next update. System overrides Hikashop - one of the most convenient and practical.

By setting up the canonical addresses, Hikashop allows you to completely get rid of duplicate goods and other pages of an online store - duplicates are generally a sore subject of online stores.

Another unique feature of Hikashop is the ability to use mass actions. With the help of user-friendly actions in the admin panel of the store, you can perform batch processing of goods - for example, to make a massive update of prices or change the names of goods.

Work with categories or options of goods is available. You can change the parameters and other characteristics. The possibilities are wide enough, especially if one considers that mass actions can also be used during the import of goods.
