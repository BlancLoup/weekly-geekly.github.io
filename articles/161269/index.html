Portal oplatagosuslug.ru opened

Mobile payments as a way to pay for public services are gaining popularity - according to statistics from the Federal Tax Service, since the launch of the service for paying debts from a mobile phone in June 2012, more than 25 thousand citizens have used it. Therefore, we decided to expand the opportunities for those who are most suited to this method of paying for public services and launched the portal oplatagosuslug.ru .
Currently, mobile payments on the portal oplatagosuslug.ru can pay for traffic police services and fines: including the state duty for issuing vehicle registration marks, the state duty for issuing a vehicle passport, the state duty for issuing a vehicle registration certificate, the state fee for issuing a driver's license other. From the portal, you can also pay arrears in fees of court bailiffs of Russia (FSSP). The payment procedure is simple and does not require mandatory registration. Information about the payment made and the details of the operation will be sent to the payer's phone immediately after the payment process is completed. In addition, you can order a receipt with the stamp of the bank confirming the fact of payment. The service is available to subscribers of mobile operators Beeline, MegaFon, MTS.
The payment service of the portal oplatagosuslug.ru is integrated with the websites of government departments: nalog.ru, fssprus.ru, 78.gibdd.ru, gosuslugi.ru, will soon appear on the portal gibdd.ru. Thus, it is possible to pay debts to the state from a mobile phone account directly from the websites of government agencies.
At the beginning of 2013, a unique opportunity will appear on the showcase of the portal oplatagosuslug.ru - order a certificate for all existing debts to the Federal Tax Service, the traffic police and the FSSP, since the service has a gateway to the UNIPO system. In addition, the list of public services for payment on the portal will be expanded.
Despite the fact that the portal is now focused on accepting mobile payments, the company plans to expand the payment functionality, in particular, by the end of the year a new payment method will be implemented - using a bank card.
Services for paying for public services oplatagosuslug.ru were developed by Processing Technologies and implemented in cooperation with Settlement Systems LLC, Tavrichesky Bank and MOBI.Money system.
