Migration Digest

In the beginning was the word. And the word was “ It 's time to open the blog“ I Migrate “:-) ” Then this blog appeared.

And here I must say why. Recently, on Habré, more and more topics appear about how someone moved to an overseas country and lived a happy life there. The topic is very interesting and exciting, so you want all these stories to be collected in one blog.

It is expected that in their publications, happy migrants will tell about where they moved, what difficulties they encountered, how they settled, what fantastic creatures they saw and what dishes they tried overseas. Photos are welcome.

For starters, I collected links to comments on the post “ IT emigration to Cyprus: personal experience, ” in which a certain amount of impressions were gathered. I really hope that the authors of these comments will write detailed reports and scandalous details on this blog.

So, the pick
  • Bulgaria : "The overall impression is Good."
  • Montreal (Canada) : "The overall impression is super."
  • Helsinki (Finland) : “The overall impression is super.”
  • Oslo (Norway) : "The overall impression is quite good."
  • Goa State (India) : “The general impression is a relaxed atmosphere, you feel like your grandmother is in a village.”
  • Minsk (Belarus) : “The overall impression is a country of victorious socialism, clean and relatively calm.”
  • Moscow (from Minsk itself =)) : "In general, living is quite easy."
  • Kiev : "The general impression is a country of invincible socialism, fairly clean and often relatively calm."
  • Kiev : "The overall impression is not bad." And a comment here .
  • Kiev : “I've read two questionnaires about Kiev ... I will add a third, because the guys apparently do not live in the same Kiev, in which I live ... The general impression is VERY MUCH I REALLY WANT THAT I WERE BORN IN THIS GRAZY COUNTRY, that's just I cannot leave for personal reasons. ”
  • I will add Minsk, Kiev and Moscow :) : “The general impression is hard to say, I was born here.”
  • Denmark. I live in Copenhagen, myself from Kiev : “The general impression is super.”
  • Bangkok (Thailand) : “The overall impression is super.” The same lucky person wrote a separate post with photos “ IT emigration to the Land of Smiles, to Thailand ”.
  • Amersfoort (Netherlands) : “The overall impression is excellent.”
  • Zaandam (Netherlands) : “The overall impression is a social paradise for freeloaders. the rest are injected and kept. ”
  • Madrid (Spain), expirience more than 10 years : “The general impression is good for rest, there is not much incentive for work, real socialism, everyone is equal.”
  • Beijing (PRC) : “The general impression is that if you like culture, know the language and understand the mentality, then it's just a fairy tale.”
