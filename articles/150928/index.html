Autumn. It's time to school: a one-day student practical export school on Windows 8



September 15 in Moscow, in the Microsoft office in Krylatskoye, the first student express school on Windows 8. Registration is now open!

Very soon, the new OS from Microsoft - Windows 8 will become available to everyone. This system will combine the capabilities of various types of devices: laptops, tablets, and desktop computers. For Windows 8, it will be possible to create new colorful applications and distribute them through the Windows Store.

All those who are interested in trying themselves as a developer of a new type of application, we invite to the First Student Express School on Windows 8. We will talk about the basic principles of developing under Windows 8, talk about the possibility of monetizing applications on this platform and together go from idea to specific application. We invite you to come with a team of like-minded people and ready-made ideas to create a prototype of your application in one day, together with community experts and Microsoft.

A bit about the format of the event


The format of the event: "managed hackathon" with lectures. Registration is required .

You need to come with laptops with Windows 8 installed and development tools .

As a preparation, you can watch the recordings of reports on Windows 8 on TechDays and watch the online broadcast of Windows Camp , which will be held on September 7.

At an express school, you can listen to lectures, talk and ask questions to Microsoft experts: Konstantin Kichinsky ( kichik ), Dmitry Soshnikov ( shwars ) and Stas Pavlov ( stasus ).

Registration and participation


When : September 15
Where : Moscow, Krylatskoe, Microsoft office
Register : msevents.microsoft.com/CUI/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=1032524273&Culture=en-US&community=0
Participation : free, but you need to register
