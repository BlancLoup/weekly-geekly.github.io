ITSea-2011. Registration is open



The next conference of marine programmers, ITSea 2011, will be held on the Black Sea coast from 4 to 10 July in Balaclava . The base is the Listrigon Motel , which has a lot of flash panoramas with views on the website.

On the ITSea website you can familiarize yourself with the program of the event (it is still in the process), see the photo reports of the past ITSea, which we have been enjoying since 2008.

Now the registration for ITSea 2011 and the preliminary payment for accommodation are opened . Registration in manual mode - all the details you will find on the specified page.





Knowing how difficult it is to find a roomy enough hotel for our large and, importantly, noisy company, we booked a place in advance - we agreed last year. And now, in order to redeem the places that the owners of the motel hold for us, we need to pay them. This guarantees us that Listrigon will remain with us - those who paid for their stay in advance.
