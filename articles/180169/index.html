To the question about repost

The material is different. There are news, there is background information, there are reviews. And sometimes there is material to which we unwittingly come back again and again, as far as it affects us.



This material is often not tied to time, it is always relevant. Sometimes there is some truth in it that dissipates in everyday life, and we want it to come to our eyes again and again. Sometimes it's just someone's good idea, and it gives us pleasure to follow the train of thought. No matter.

If we stumble upon that which gives pleasure to having a good time, then we strive to experience it again and again.
That is why we subconsciously strive to regularly visit our favorite cafes, review some films, and we are going to play board games with the same company. And despite some recurring elements, each time we bring out something new.

Closer to the point

If we are dealing with the flow of information, then our task is to run as quickly as possible and take notes about what interests us. Our tools: RSS aggregator, memory and bookmarks.
If we need reference information - we need good resources, where there is a storehouse of reference information (the smaller, the better). Our tool is a search with custom morphology, categories and tags.
And if we want to have a good time discussing this and that, our task is to find a good company of like-minded people and a topic for discussion. It is desirable to have new themes each time. But since there is not always enough time to find out the truth, and the company is constantly changing, it would be nice sometimes to throw in old topics again and discuss the new ones.
