Steve Wozniak showed the contents of his backpack



Co-founder of Apple is famous for its fantastic love for gadgets. The photo above shows only rechargeable devices for recharging, the full list is much more extensive.

Steve carries in his backpack almost all the cables and USB-adapters from the photo, excluding the Belkin AC tee with double USB (suitcase), one USB-adapter Janet, his wife, a Canon charger (camera case), Firewire cable (suitcase), Verizon adapter 3G MiFi.

With the exception of two iPhone, iPod, iPad, owned by his wife, Steve carries with him all the devices from the photo.

Also in the backpack contains:

  • AirPort Express and MacBook Pro 85W adapters that are not in the frame;
  • Bluetooth mouse;
  • Binoculars;
  • Cables for the projector;
  • Several Gameboy Light, never sold in the United States, but only in Japan;
  • A bunch of multiple AC adapters;
  • Prismatic glasses for watching movies in bed;
  • Sunglasses;
  • Earplugs for concerts and earplugs Ultimate Ears UE18 for air travel;
  • Gameboy cable;
  • A bunch of pens, colored pens, sharpeners and pencils, the latter are often used for board games in the air;
  • Red, blue, violet, yellow and green laser pointers;
  • Bank cards, hotels, airlines, Hard Rock store, medical cards;
  • Other accessories, for example, Olloclip adapter for iPhone;
  • Square, bank card reader for iPhone;
  • Flash drives;
  • Flash card adapters;
  • Adapter for cigarette lighter for two USB;
  • Double-audio cable;
  • USB and SD adapters for iPad;
  • Toiletries;
  • Several types of batteries (AA, AAA, CR2, CR128 and several unusual types);
  • Eye drops;
  • Two dollar bills;
  • Puzzles;
  • Guides to places to visit;


How much does such a set weigh? Steve calls the figure of 50 pounds (about 22.68 kg), and jokes that with such a load it gradually becomes shorter. The security services at the airport have a hard time when checking Steve: the couple of times that they decided to ask to demonstrate all the electronics, it took seven baskets, five of which were filled to the brim.

And no device in the slow motion Wozniak set is unnecessary, although the Bluetooth mouse is close to that.
