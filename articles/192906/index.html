IFA 2013. New to Philips. Acoustics and TVs

This year I managed to get to the largest international exhibition IFA 2013 in Berlin. First of all, I visited the stand of Philips, which showed a lot of interesting new products. This is mainly acoustics for young people and cool TVs with 3D, Cloud TV, 4K and other newfangled technologies. Was on the stand and household appliances, for example, vacuum cleaners, coffee makers, razors and other things that are useful in the economy.



But let's get everything in order. Carefully, under the cut photos of gadgets and girls.

Philips held the presentation not at -1 day (like Sony and Samsung), but at 0, but this did not reduce the hype. The company shared its ideas for the future, talked about new products, and also brought one of the most famous DJs to events. The presentation was led by Peter Nota , CEO of Philips for LifeStyle products.

At the very beginning it was told about concepts. For example, a slow cooker with Wi-Fi. You connect to it from your tablet, find the desired recipe, read what you need for cooking. Then put the necessary products in the slow cooker, set the time on the tablet and wait. You do not need to follow her, all information will be on the tablet. Perhaps, there‚Äôs just a camera missing, which will show you how the food is prepared, and you will like it and upload it on Youtube.



Philips also told about the new Wi-Fi coffee makers that can brew you coffee. In general, Philips will now embed Wi-Fi in all of its household appliances, and in addition to Bluetooth, NFC will also be inserted into the acoustics. But more on that later. In addition to coffee makers and multicookers, Philips also has a room floor air purifier. Also with Wi-Fi, as you understand. Light can be adjusted, the power of the stream, in general, a handy thing for a "smart home", if anyone already has it.



The most important novelty that we were shown, of course, was the new Philips 9000 TV (TP Vision divisions) with 4K resolution and other newfangled accessories.



The TV, of course, is equipped with an Ambilight - a system that creates lighting around the display in the colors of the screen picture. This is convenient, since the perception is much softer and more pleasant for the eyes.



In addition, in addition to the usual Smart TV, Philips TV will also feature Cloud TV. This is a system that allows you to watch digital television. Of course, not all the channels familiar to the Russian user will be there. However, in private conversation representatives of the company promised in the coming months to fill the system with up to 60 channels. Of course, all this will not be free, there will be channels by subscription, there will be advertising, but some will be free. By the way, now Philips TVs will be connected with Dropbox and you can view photos on the big screen.



Philips TVs now allow you to transfer a picture from the screen to your tablet or smartphone. This is convenient if you are watching a movie and here you urgently need to escape to the kitchen. They pressed a couple of buttons, the film is now being broadcast to you on the iPad, and you calmly go to the kitchen to fry steaks and watch the show. Reverse broadcast is also possible. We were shown how to display an image from a phone on Android on a TV screen. Moreover, you can not only watch photos and movies, but even play games, although not without brakes, of course.

Perhaps one of the coolest new products is the M1X-DJ amateur DJ console (read as a DJ mix). This is a remote control with two turntables, various switches and buttons. In fact, they simply duplicate all the elements in the iPad application. By the way, the owners of the old iPad will not be able to use this remote, it is only for the Lightning connector.



By the way, it was created together with the famous DJ Armin Van Buuren, who introduced this novelty.



The device itself is quite interesting. Of course, the DJ panels on the bulk market. But this is the first in my memory amateur gadget at an affordable price. Oh yeah, it costs 16k rubles. In general, instead of another iPhone, you can buy such a thing and have parties at home. And if you buy 4 more batteries, then you can arrange raves and in nature.





In addition to this DJ station, Philips has introduced many acoustic novelties. Moreover, both desktop solutions and portable speakers. By the way, now portable speakers have already outgrown the form factor of small barrels that play disgustingly. Now it can even be a rather large box with speakers. In general, see for yourself.



I found the BR-1X column interesting. This is such a "brick" in which there are two dynamics. It can be used at home with a laptop or computer (if you do not have professional acoustics 7.1, of course), and on the street with a smartphone. Moreover, he has an interesting button that significantly increases the volume (quality loss can not be avoided) for the street. In general, a kind of boombox for gatherings with friends in the yard or in nature.



What is interesting, if your friends also have such a speaker, then you can connect them together and get an even louder and surround sound. It is unlikely, of course, that will happen, but anything is possible. Well and an important detail - the column is moisture-proof. You can not be afraid to put it in the rain, although you should not swim with it, it can not withstand a deep dive. As for NFC, almost all portable speakers now have this technology.
