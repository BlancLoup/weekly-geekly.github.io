The first race of the mobiles ended in an accident



In 2015, three organizations related to sports and electric vehicles entered into a partnership agreement. These are Formula E , the British Kinetik venture capital fund, led by Denis Sverdlov and Charge, a company dedicated to the development of electric vehicles. The purpose of the partnership is to demonstrate the capabilities of modern technologies by creating a world unmanned racing series of electric cars. The new championship was named Roborace . According to the plan, in this championship can take as major players from the automotive industry, and the leading technical universities. Ten teams can participate in races, each with two unmanned vehicles. An interesting point - all teams must have identical cars. Only the control software platform is different - a weak form of artificial intelligence and additional software.

Another Roborace mission is to play the role of an event to support the Formula E Championship, an auto racing class on electric vehicles with open wheels. It was decided to drive the UAVs with wheels before the Formula E races on the same tracks as the main championship. Now these races are held in ten cities of the world, including Beijing, Buenos Aires, Paris, Berlin, London, Moscow and others. This month, the first test race of the Roborace Championship took place in Buenos Aires. Not without accidents.
