WebHiTech and Web Standards Days Ceremony in Moscow on December 16


According to all forecasts , a double rainbow is expected in Moscow on December 16 . This is because we will not only show the world the best technological sites according to the version of the WebHiTech contest and award the best with valuable prizes and is simple about respect and respect , but also we will hold the traditional Web Standards Days meeting. And all this for free, and only for you.

Formally, Web Standards Days in its current capacity will come to Moscow for the first time. However, last year there was a WHT Party, in many respects similar to what we plan to hold this year, pay attention to the report and photos .

This year we decided to reduce the ceremonial part and devote more time to a full-fledged conference on client technologies. The program is expected to be the following:

See you at the meeting!
