Programming the human nervous system

7 years ago, while teaching Hustle (social pair dance), I ran into a problem in the older group of my students. We have already come to the study of complex supports, where the execution technique is important so as not to hurt the back and knees.

It was perfect to put the pair on the mirror perfectly, but during the dance all the beautiful and safe lines in the pair broke, I had to stop the music and put on the right technique again.

As a neurologist, I could not simply ignore the errors in the support technique, because every time my heart sank, seeing dangerous movements. One day, leafing through my notes on bodily psychotherapy, I came across a short phrase: "The Feldenkrais Method for Dancers, Actors ...". - Yeah! For dancers!

How does the proper programming of the nervous system work?

You can learn to turn your head correctly for years. In our culture there are a lot of semantic traps that lie in completely unexpected places. For example, the instruction: ‚Äúturn your head left and right.‚Äù Try turning your head and notice how easy and far you can do it.

What happened? Just by default, the instruction to turn the head is perceived as if the head and the neck should be involved in this turn, the rest of the body should be motionless.

From a biomechanical point of view, this is completely ineffective and leads to increased wear of the joints and intervertebral discs in the neck. You can tell as many times as you want to include the whole body in motion, but simply by stroking your cheek you will achieve more - one of the most basic reflexes turns on, the search reflex of the newborn and the whole body are automatically included in the search for the maternal breast.
