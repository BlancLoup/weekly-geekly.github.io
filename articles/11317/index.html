Wikimania 2007 will be held in Taiwan


The annual gathering of Wikipedia fans this year is held for the third time. Let's face it: in past years it was easier for our people to get there, because Wikimania 2005 and 2006 were held in Germany and the United Kingdom, and to participate in Wikimania 2007 you will have to fly to Taiwan.

Wikimania is both a scientific conference and a party. Here, the most active participants of all projects of the Wikimedia Foundation gather here, there is an exchange of ideas, acquaintances between the participants, presentations of projects and research.
