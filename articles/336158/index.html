How to remove ads from Android apps

Hi, Habr! I present to your attention the translation of the article How to Remove Ads from Android Apps by Owais Sultan .

Please note: the application described in this article belongs to third-party developers and requires root privileges on your device. Check it out before installing it.

If you are the owner of an Android device, you probably install many applications on it. But apps like MX Player show too much annoying ads. If you have a lot of such applications, and you often use them, then such advertising is very tiring.

Therefore, if you want to learn how to get rid of it, then you came to the address.

Today in this article, we will give you a complete step-by-step guide to using the Lucky Patcher application to remove all this intrusive advertising.

So let's start with what is this Lucky Patcher ? Then show in detail how to install it on your device? And at the end you will learn how to use it to remove Google ads from your device.

What is a Lucky Patcher?


With this utility, you can easily modify the applications you have installed. You can bypass the purchase of applications on Google Play . Using Lucky Patcher , you can remove system applications. But its best feature is to remove ads from your applications.
But before that, make sure your device is properly configured. This application will only work on devices with root.

Features Lucky Patcher


This application includes many interesting features, so before downloading and installing it, let's consider them:

  1. It can easily block Google ads from any application.
  2. With it, you can skip the license verification step.
  3. You can delete the applications pre-installed on your device.
  4. You can control application rights.
  5. With it, you can bypass the purchase of the application.
  6. With this application you can easily update your applications.
  7. You can also backup your data.
  8. You can freeze suspicious applications.

Basic requirements that your device must fulfill:

  1. First, your device does not allow downloading any applications from third-party or unknown sources. Therefore, you must first enable this setting.
    Click Settings > Security > Unknown Sources . Switch the slider on the parameter Unknown sources so that you can install the application from a non- Google Play store.
  2. The next thing you need to do is to check for the presence of root on your device, if not, then you must first unlock root on your device.

How to download and install Lucky Patcher on your Android device


  1. First of all, download Lucky Patcher Apk from the official site to your phone.
  2. Click Settings in the main menu, and then go to Security . There you can easily find the Unknown Sources option. You must enable this option to continue downloading.

  3. Now install the application and open it.

  4. That's all. You are ready to use it.

How to remove ads from the application using Lucky Patcher


  1. After you have successfully installed this Lucky Patcher on your device, open it.
  2. Now you need to click the Allow button to grant root access.

  3. You can see a complete list of all your applications on the Lucky Patcher home page.

  4. Now select the application from which you want to remove ads.

  5. Now click on the Open patch menu .
  6. You will see many options. There you have to click Remove Google Ads .

  7. Now you can see three options available on your device.
  8. There you have to click Patch to remove Ads from Google .

  9. Now click the Apply button. This may take a few seconds.

  10. You can see what the patching of this application will begin.

  11. That's all. You have successfully removed Google ads from your application.
  12. It is easy to check. Just open the app, and it will no longer show you any intrusive banners.
  13. You can perform the same actions for any other application in order to clear it from advertising too.

Final words


If you successfully completed the above steps, you will definitely get rid of these annoying advertisements.
