How to approach the issue of lead generation?

Today we will talk about where to start Lead generation?


When you start to offer (sell) your product / service, first of all you need to understand who your customers are and where they live? And only then, based on the answers to these two key questions, to think about how to present your product to it.

Further we answer where we will look for our client? Where does he live? When is the best time to contact him? The Internet space is huge and getting lost there is very simple.
Let's look at an example.

Example: You offer foreign language lessons for individuals.
The service is quite popular and you face the question of how to find customers? What to do, where to start?
And something has become lazy for you to think about how and where to attract them, and then you decide to go to a special agency so that they can do everything for you. This may be an advertising, marketing, lead generation agency. If the agency is professional, they will immediately start asking you many questions: what is the portrait of your client, what is your competitive advantage, what territory do you work in, etc. and as a result, if the agency is good, their services will cost you a pretty penny. So you can do a little time and effort to do everything yourself, or if you want everything to be done well - contact the agency .
