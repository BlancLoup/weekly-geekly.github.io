Startup Syndicate

Viewed recently new startups. There are more and more small, convenient services consisting of literally 3 pages. The century of super-duper portals has passed and this is natural. It is difficult and unprofitable to make a huge portal to one average start-up, even on a ready-made cms. A small online service may turn out to be a gold mine, and in case of failure, it will not be so painfully reflected on the startup.

The notorious problem with monetization pursues major projects, not talking about small ones. It was thought that if you form a syndicate of small promising startups. The idea has something in common with the Trade Union 2.0 , but it is more real.

Advantages:
  • Trade advertising will be easier. Whether you have one startup in the range, or a couple of dozen of them.
  • Promotion mutual, link exchange, exchange banners.
  • Power: D.
  • What else?
Problems:
  • Who will sell advertising?
  • Official status, taxes, etc. etc.
  • What else?
Maybe something like that already exists? It is interesting to listen to opinions, suggestions.
