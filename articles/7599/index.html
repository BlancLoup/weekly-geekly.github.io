Rumors of google investing in maxthon refuted

Recent rumors of a Google-Maxthon collaboration and a million-dollar investment have been refuted by Maxhon's founder, Jeff Chen (Bloodchen), on Chinese website SANA (in Chinese) . Nevertheless, Jeff talks about a possible business collaboration with Google in the future, although he has not yet disclosed the details of this collaboration.

From reliable sources it is also known that Maxthon receives more than $ 2500 per month from Baidu, for installing Baidu as the main search engine by default.
