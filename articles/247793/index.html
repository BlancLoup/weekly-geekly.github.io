Monitoring from Brazil - continue to conquer Latin America

HostTracker got a new point of monitoring sites in the New World. This time it's Brazil's turn. A little about the features of the Brazilian hosting, as well as another piece of information about our service - under the cut.




Is it easy to find hosting in Brazil?


As you know, Brazil is used in Portuguese. For me, the absence of an English version of the site for most Brazilian hosts did not come as a surprise. But here‚Äôs the absence of the Spanish version, in the context of the encirclement of Brazil by the Spanish-speaking countries and rather close economic ties with them, I was frankly surprised. So, in my personal experience, hosting in Brazil can be divided into three types: with the English / Spanish version of the site and the data center in the US; site only in Portuguese, data center in the US; site only in Portuguese, data center in Brazil. All of about two dozens of Brazilian hosting sites, which I reviewed in the process, fell into one of these categories. We needed a server without fail in Brazil, so the resellers from the first two categories did not fit. It was easy to calculate the first group, the problem was to distinguish the second from the third, using the simplified Spanish and Google Translator into Portuguese, as the support of the last two categories does not speak English. From the fact of the appearance of this article it is clear that a suitable hoster with a data center in Brazil was still found. If suddenly someone is relevant - I can share the experience.

Checks from around the world


As an example of using the service as a whole and the new point in particular, consider a part of the real log of checks of one site.
As you can see, the check from Argentina (in the red frame) returned an error, the site could not load and the request fell off by timeout:



This was immediately followed by a recheck, in order to determine whether the error was real. This is done to minimize false positives. As we see, in this case, 7 points take part in the re-examination (green frame), which includes servers from the USA, India, Africa, Russia and Brazil. And the whole world, inclusive of the neighboring Brazil to Argentina, returned a positive result. Thanks to the point from Brazil, we know that even from South America the site works and it really was an accident that can occur if the site being checked and the server for checking are located on different continents.

Our network is constantly growing, and if you have the opportunity and desire to join, it is very easy to do .
