Welcome to ElectroTestDrive June 6



The summer cycle season has already begun, and it's time to try on a new mode of transport, technologically and eco-friendly. But it is boring and long to drive in salons and shops, so we decided to arrange an exhibition and test drive of personal electric transport right in our 27-storey skyscraper!

This Saturday, June 6, in our Moscow office, you will be able to personally review and test in action more than 60 units of the latest technology: electric bikes, electric scooters, electric motorcycles and even monowheels. Unique models are waiting for you: a super-lightweight electric bike (weighing only 14 kg), a Russian development - an electric motorcycle LowRider 8000, an electric bike Giant Electron 2200W (incredible acceleration from 0 to 50 km / h in 4 seconds), an electric bike Achiever 500 with a central engine and a torxensor : it has the world's first integrated motor in a frame with a pressure sensor in the carriage, i.e. The speed of rotation of the motor depends on the effort.

The program of the event:

12.00 Start of the exhibition.

13.00 Performance of our guests: Arnaud Zhakar (silver medalist of the World Rally-Raid Championship, Re-Volt), Alexander Konevich (presenter of Science 2.0 programs, Experiences of the Amateur, Evolution). Start test drives.

17.00 Completion of the event.

Please note that you must pre- register . Be sure to take a passport or driver's license. Address of the event: Moscow, Mail.Ru Group office, Leningradsky Avenue 39, p. 79 (metro station Aeroport).

We are waiting for you on ElectroTestDrive!
