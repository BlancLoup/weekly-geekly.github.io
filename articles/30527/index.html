ULMART come

I recently wrote an epitaph for ULTRA Electronics . Many grieved and sincerely regretted the departure of this player from the market of electronic commerce.
Miracles happen. And we are talking about rebirth. As promised by the management of the company, they are essentially bringing the same network under a new brand - ULMART (Yulmart). The name comes from the intersection of Ultra and Walmart .
July 1 in St. Petersburg at Kondratievsky Prospect, 15, the company "KomBrig" opened a new store. The range of the store is constantly expanding, new descriptions appear.
According to the company representative, all the main services of Ultra will be present in Yulmart. Questions about the preservation of client privileges (discounts, etc.) will be addressed individually with department managers ( source ). The sale of large household appliances is not planned yet. Hopefully, once again debugging the work, the company will move to the regions, because there are always plans for expansion ( source ).
