The best videos are selected on YouTube

YouTube is launching the best video contest for 2006.

The nominees are presented in 7 categories, among which are the most creative video, the best series, the best comedy video, the best comment, etc. All of them are presented on the new youtube-channel - YTAwards (the list of nominations is at the bottom left). Of course, the users themselves will choose the best ones - by voting, which will last until March 25.

The Most Creative nomination presents a variety of videos in style: for example, I recommend comparing work using 3D effects and the recitative of a teenager ordering a sandwich . In the Most Adorable nomination, for example, a cat that likes to watch the water in the toilet , or an animated wingless bird that flew the first and last time in its life. And in the Best Series nomination, of course, you can find "the most famous cheat" lonelygirl15 and the series Ask A Ninja , as well as testing the strength of the iPod (which he could not stand). In general, see and evaluate!

PS According to comScore Media Metrix, 133.5 million users visited YouTube in January.
