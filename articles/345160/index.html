Win a trip to the Norwegian office of Vivaldi



Hello! Many are now planning their trips next year and we decided to offer you one interesting option.

2017 was very important for us. In July 2017, the community of users of the portal vivaldi.net has grown to 100 thousand participants . But this is only the beginning: at the beginning of 2018, this number will already be 200 thousand! Our community is growing very fast and we want to thank you for your help in creating the best browser in the world. Therefore, we decided to hold a small competition and invite the winner to visit our Norwegian office for a few days.

Grand Prize


So, the winner of the competition will receive a free flight ticket to Oslo and back, as well as a three-day stay near the company's office. You will have the opportunity to meet with the developers and explore the sights of Oslo with us. We will take care that these days will leave you a lot of good memories.

How to participate


Vivaldi browser is the most customizable among all the other popular browsers. You can make it individual both in appearance and in functionality. Therefore, we would be happy to know how you use the Vivaldi browser.

What features do you use? How does Vivaldi help you in your daily work? How individually does your browser look? In general, show us what it is - your browser Vivaldi!

You can tell us and other users in a variety of ways: publish a short story with screenshots, a video, publish a small article in a blog on vivaldi.net or in your own blog on another service - in general, in any way convenient for you. Then send a link to your work to support [at] vivaldi.com marked ‚ÄúTo the competition‚Äù until midnight on January 7, 2018.

Winner selection


On January 7, we will select the top 20 works and publish a blog post with links to all the works, as well as a vote for all community members - they will decide whose work will be the winner. Voting will end in a week, on January 14, and on January 15 we will announce the winner.

Incentive prizes


Also, each of the twenty nominees for the main prize will receive a set of souvenirs from the Vivaldi company, and all participants who submitted their works will receive a 20% discount in our Vivaldi store .

general information


The main prize includes flights to Oslo and back, as well as three-day accommodation (without meals) in apartments near the Vivaldi office.

You can take part in the competition if you are over 18 years old. You also bear all costs of obtaining a visa if necessary.
