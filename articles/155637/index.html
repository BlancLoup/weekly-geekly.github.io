Brazilian newspapers no longer work with Google News.



Brazil has not the warmest relationship with Google lately. That the head of the Brazilian branch of Google almost planted , now here are 154 newspapers of this country refused to cooperate with Google News. These newspapers have stopped exporting their news to this service. Representatives of the Brazilian National Association of Newspaper Publishers reported that the cooperation was discontinued for two reasons. First, Google refuses to pay associations for the provided news, and second, the Google News aggregator ‚Äútakes away‚Äù a portion of its readership.

It was suggested that readers interested in receiving news, get everything you need on Google News, and rarely go to the full version of the material, ie Do not visit the newspaper / magazine news site.

In general, the Brazilian newspapers had enough time to evaluate the effectiveness of working with Google News. The cooperation agreement was signed by the Newspaper Publishers Association of Brazil and Google in 2010. Since then, the number of visitors to the websites of newspapers has not increased, but rather, on the contrary, has decreased. As a result, both regional and national publications refused to cooperate with Google. The final decision was made by representatives of the association after Google refused to pay for the news provided by the newspapers.
