Fiksu: mobile marketing costs continue to grow


The price of acquiring a new user or keeping him in the game beats all records, and most likely this is not the limit! Such conclusions were presented by Fiksu based on its own data. Over the past April, growth was particularly significant - for iOS, the CPI (cost per install) increased by 39% to $ 2.13, and for Android - by 20% and reached $ 2.08. The CPL (launch price) index increased at a similar rate and for iOS increased by 34% over the month and amounted to $ 0.41, and for Android the growth is 44% and $ 0.34, respectively, per user.

For comparison, the total CPL for iOS for the year increased by 75%, and for Android - by 169%. The CPI index in the case of iOS grew by 54%, and with Android - by 59%, respectively. Last year, at the same time, it was quite tempting to buy traffic on Android, but today there is definitely no significant difference in cost, but to say that you can win on the volume of Android users compared to what you can it would be to buy on iOS, it is already impossible and profitable to invest in traffic on iOS, if there is a choice.

Fiksu analysts themselves are confident that the output in the redistribution of the budget. It is necessary to invest in more expensive, but more efficient means. If we talk about the advertising platform, then this is Facebook, and if about the format, then video advertising. It is also recommended that retargeting be used more actively to attract existing inactive users to the application. This will increase the number of IAPs and increase the profit generated by the application.
