VRToday - Everything about the virtual reality industry. Issue # 1

Virtual reality is the trend of 2016, it's hard to argue with that. Facebook, Samsung, Microsoft, Google, HTC / Valve, SONY, Apple, NVIDIA, and many more of the world's largest companies involved in the race are proof of that. Unfortunately, the awareness of end users and consumers of the VR topic in the world still leaves much to be desired. In Russia, everything is even more complicated. Large media are just beginning to be actively interested in the topic and often provide only the most superficial information for readers interested in the field of virtual reality.

After analyzing media resources, we came to the conclusion that now is the time to create a program that will help explain all the subtleties of virtual, augmented, mixed and other types of realities in an accessible and understandable way, provide reliable news information and get first-hand opinions of experts, entrepreneurs, analysts and evangelical technology.


So, we present the first release of the program on the virtual reality industry in Russia - VR Today (edition of February 6, 16).
