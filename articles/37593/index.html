CC Visitor Information 08

The festival will take place in 9 days, but now the following means of receiving information and communication are available to visitors and all interested:

Twitter
For the convenience of tracking the news by the festival organizers, it is supported (as far as possible and the strengths) of the CC tweeter: http://twitter.com/chaos_construct .

It is worth paying attention that the most efficient way to get reliable news about the festival is to subscribe to the RSS news channel: http://cc.org.ru/8/rss/news

Jabber room
On the jabber.ru server, the jabber room cc. Her full name is cc@conference.jabber.ru

You can join the room using any public jabber account.

IRC
A channel of the festival Chaos Constructions has been created in the RusNet network.

Details:

channel name: #chaos_constructions
network: RusNet
Connection server: irc.run.net or any of those listed on the official RusNet site .

If you are using a UTF-8 client, you should connect to the utf.rusnet.org.ru server on port 7770. For the rest of the encodings, read the MOTD server carefully.

WiFi
Visitors and participants of the festival are provided with free public Wi-Fi. Please note that the network is open - take care of your passwords!

Bbs
As many remember, before there was such a thing as a BBS. People called each other using modems, terminalki went to the BBS (Maximus, RA, SuperBBS, ProBoard, etc.) and chatted with the sysop, downloaded files, left messages. Sysops colorfully decorated their BBS - ASCII / ANSI screensavers, various pictures, etc.

So, at Chaos Constructions'2008 we plan to install two mini-PBXs (Panasonic 206), connect them and provide internal telephony with 6 numbers at each station.

Configure BBS, take modems (1200 without MNP :)) and go ahead. We shake the old days!
