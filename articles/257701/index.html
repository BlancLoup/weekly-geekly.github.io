Release of the new mobile 2GIS

The first to receive the update iOS and WP . Android on the way. In short: we completely redesigned the mobile 2GIS.


A little more precisely about the changes in our article.

Design
This is the first change that is immediately visible. To be more precise, we completely reworked the interface. The new 2GIS has a single style, but taking into account the features of each platform, of course.



If 2gis.ru we do for those who have time to choose a company in a relaxed atmosphere, then the mobile 2GIS is for those who use the application on the go. For those who need to quickly select and contact the company - call or reach. These scripts are now executed with a minimum of actions.

Search for travel by car
Now it is built taking into account traffic jams and real road load. Therefore, in the morning the route from A to B can be one, and in the evening - another. We show the best route, with possible transfers and exactly to the entrance.



The list of cities will grow, but today we already know about traffic jams in: Novosibirsk, Barnaul, Volgograd, Yekaterinburg, Irkutsk, Kazan, Kaliningrad, Kemerovo, Krasnoyarsk, Nizhny Novgorod, Novokuznetsk, Omsk, Perm, Ryazan, Samara, Saratov, Tolyatti, Tyumen , Ufa and Chelyabinsk.

Update on Windows Phone
For Windows Phone, the update turned out to be the most ambitious, since the previous version was with reduced features. And before continuing to tell what else is on iOS and will be on Android, I want to tell you specifically about WP.

Offline
As we said, we could not immediately release an offline application. With this update, honest offline-2GIS appeared on all platforms. Honestly, we ourselves are happy about this. And if someone thinks that offline is too much, then we invite you to read reviews about the old 2GIS, which worked on the API.

Besides
Search for public transport, 3D-objects, floor plans of 120 shopping centers in 16 cities and more than a million entrances to the organization - it turned out that all this was not. Now it is. And that's cool.

Let's return to the general changes on all mobile platforms.

Favorites
In the new mobile 2GIS favorites combined with 2gis.ru. You can choose companies or find places in a relaxed atmosphere on the big screen, and build a route right away on your smartphone.



Update
To use the latest data, they need to be updated periodically. In the previous version, this was a separate procedure. The update did not occur in the background and the application was required to work. Now cities can be updated not just in the background, but also automatically, in the presence of Wi-Fi.

The new mobile 2GIS for iOS works from version 8.0, for Windows Phone - from 8.1. For earlier versions of operating systems, the previous generation 2GIS application continues to be maintained and updated.
