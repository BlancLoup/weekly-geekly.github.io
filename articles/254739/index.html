Techno-Designed Alarm Clock - Arduino Based Desk Clock

In the last publication I described how to make an Arduino-based wrist watch using a 3D printer. The next step was logical - to make a desktop alarm clock from more or less standard modules. It took me one Sunday to assemble the alarm clock. The body turned out so-so - too simple. Paint blew out of spray cans, like it became better. Then redraw. Or redraw it yourself, in Sketchup for example.
But the sketch is very beautiful. With a sunset effect.
Anyway. The watch case is assembled from 4 plastic parts printed on this 3D printer .
