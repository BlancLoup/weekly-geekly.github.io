Facebook will be the biggest news reader?

A few days ago, an interesting message was published on Facebook's corporate blog calling for users to create their own news feeds, that is, to use Facebook as a standard aggregator for reading news. This is a very dangerous trend for Google News, Yahoo News and other news sites. Not to mention the RSS-aggregators, which have not received massive popularity.


more detailed statistics here

Experts warn that if this goes on like this, Facebook will easily become the most popular news site on the Internet. Already, many of the largest television channels and newspapers, including the New York Times and the Wall Street Journal, have opened their Facebook channels.
