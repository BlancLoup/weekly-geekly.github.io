C ++ Russia in St. Petersburg


Hello!

We finished processing videos from the C ++ Siberia 2015 conference and collected them in a youtube playlist . They are also available on the conference page.

We also have great news about the C ++ Russia 2016 conference in St. Petersburg. In short: everything will be very cool.




So, the conference will be held on February 26-27 in St. Petersburg (Park Inn by Radisson Baltic). We are still compiling a program and still accepting applications for reports.
