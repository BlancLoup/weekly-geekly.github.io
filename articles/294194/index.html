Online commerce in Ukraine legalized and regulated



Deputies of the Verkhovna Rada adopted in the second reading the law No. 0957 , which regulates online commerce, including online trading and working with electronic money. For the adoption of the law voted 235 deputies, writes AIN. In the first reading, this law was passed by the previous parliament before last year. If the law passed in the second reading is signed by the President, then Law No. 0957 will enter into force.

The author of the bill is the People's Deputy Olga Belkova. According to her, the main purpose of the bill is to give electronic transactions a legal status, similar to the status of transactions using written documents. The consequences of adopting a law are to simplify the interaction of online commerce representatives with the authorities.
