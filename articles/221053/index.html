Show Sound # 9 - Podcast about audio equipment, components, formats and technologies

Hello! With you, "Show Sound" is a podcast for those who like to live, work, relax and move around, listening to your favorite music, podcasts or audio books at home, at work, in the subway or other modes of transport. We speak in simple words about audio equipment and help you get acquainted with the world of devices and technologies.

In the 9th edition, together with Timofey Shikolenkov ( timshick ), we answer your questions on acoustic preparation of home rooms, talk about bi-wiring and bi-amping, touch on listening to 5.1 music and once again mention the difference between a sound card and an external DAC.
