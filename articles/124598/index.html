Design Competition RedsolutionCMS


Last fall, we wrote about the Django- based RedsolutionCMS developed by us and released under the GNU GPL v.3 license. Today we announce a contest for the best template for a site deployed with the help of Redsolution CMS. Participants need only css files and images for .html files (the archive can be downloaded on the site). All submitted works must be CC-BY licensed and must not include materials that contradict it. The number of works from one author is not limited.
The main goal of this competition is the development of the RedsolutionCMS project and the improvement of the appearance of the site, deployed with its help.

Prizes


It so happened that our company is strong programmers, but with the designers we somehow do not add up (for now;)). Therefore, we decided to turn to the community: help RedsolutionCMS to acquire a unique and beautiful design! In turn, the Redsolution company guarantees reward to the winners of the competition:
  • 1st place. 8,000 rubles;
  • 2nd place. 5000 rubles;
  • 3rd place. 2000 rubles.
But in general, we tend to think that monetary remuneration is more of a symbolic incentive. After all, the authors of the most interesting templates will leave their mark on our unique RedsolutionCMS, since their work will become part of the RedsolutionCMS modules, and the design of the winner will be used by default.
