WebAsyst Photos

We have released a new service for working with photos - WebAsyst Photos .
These are photo hosting, photo organizer and publishing tool :



Photo services and so much. Why did we do another one?

Initially, we planned to make WebAsyst Photo ‚Äúfor yourself‚Äù as an internal application of WebAsyst, in order to upload photos from various company events. The task was, first of all, to make a convenient and fast backend for organizing photos (we also had our own service for working with photos, but we didn‚Äôt like it at all). As a result, we decided that such a service would be needed not only by us. It turned out a product that, on the one hand, is a publicly accessible photo gallery , and on the other hand, a personal photo hosting service (quickly received a link, HTML or BBcode in the photo and copy-paste) with a fast backend.

This, of course, is not a replacement for photo hosting sites like Flickr, Yandex. Photos, iPicture, but just another product with its own features. For example, we use sharpen (sharpness filter) when creating thumbnails, access rights within your team can be assigned to albums, you can get HTML / BBcode / link directly to at least 100 selected photos, there are different types of photo publishing, etc.

Future plans: make a good design editor, many different themes of the public section (photo galleries) and an API for pulling photos from WebAsyst Photos anywhere (well, for adding photos to WebAsyst, of course).

WebAsyst Photos can be used as a web service or purchased as scripts (PHP / MySQL) and installed on your hosting. Works with Imagick and GD.
