New social grid - now on video plots

Youtube is like a drug. It was dawn, you shoveled a ton of commercials and think what else to look at? And one sucks ... Yes, man, you're not the only one. Unfortunately, Youtube is not perfect in terms of search, and with the Russian language there is not everything going smoothly. But let's unite and try to solve this problem.

How? Very simple. Absorb.tv - a new collective video blog with a social bias.

Any registered participant can add links to interesting video plots, as well as evaluate and comment on the links of other participants. The only request - no need to popsyatiny. Do not need Tokio Hotel, Timati, MuzTV, Comedy Club and other rubbish - it's all on the TV, which we despise :)

Despite the austerity of the site (standard Drupel skin) - the project quite successfully copes with its task. Already there is full of interesting commercials, carefully arranged in categories. Hour for two views just enough.

Let's create together a new TV that can be watched forever.
