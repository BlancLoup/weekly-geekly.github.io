Google has created a virtual museum of Chinese history and art.



In 2012, Google created a non-profit project Google Cultural Institute . This is a multimedia encyclopedia, and very extensive, where you can find a lot of materials, including historical photographs, videotapes, manuscripts and everything else.

At the very beginning of the project‚Äôs life, it was planned that the encyclopedia materials would cover the period from 1905 to our time. So, recently opened a virtual gallery-museum of Chinese history, which houses 1,400 exhibits. The gallery covers a period of 5,000 years.

All exhibits are scanned, photographed and posted on the site with relevant reference materials. In order to do all this, the authors of the project spent a lot of time and effort.
