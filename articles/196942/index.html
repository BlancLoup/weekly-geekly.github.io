MSI Slidebook S20 Ultrabook Transformer Video Review



MSI Slidebook S20 is a hybrid of a tablet and an ultrabook, made like a slider. The 11.6-inch display of the device based on the IPS-matrix has a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels and supports up to 10 touch points simultaneously. The slidebook works on low-voltage Intel Pentium Dual Core or Core i5 processors. The amount of RAM is from 4 to 8 GB (1 slot is available), and mSATA 128 GB solid-state drive is used for data storage.

SlideBook S20 runs on Windows 8, is equipped with two USB 3.0 ports, a mini HDMI port and is able to work for about 6 hours from the built-in battery.
