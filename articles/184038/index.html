Mobile application promotion: where to start

Frankly, to make a good mobile application is not so simple. And you will not find a ready-made recipe anywhere to help with this. But, to conduct an advertising campaign that will help get a lot of installations and even get to the top, it is quite possible.
It has its own features to promote Android and iOS applications. But in this post I want to touch on the general stages.

So where to start?



Promotion begins before the publication of the application. Before the application is published, it is necessary to prepare a site, a press release, a press kit, videos, communities in social networks. It is not necessary to use all of the above. But be sure to provide some kind of online presence (information about the application, the ability to contact the developer, information for journalists). At the same stage, contact the press and agree on the publication of the release or review of the application.

A little more detail on each item.

Site. If you think that the application is serious and will find its audience, then there should be no doubt about the creation of the site. The site is needed for technical support of users and for branding. Create it before the release of the application. What for?! First, collect email addresses of potential users. Secondly, to test the application to the target audience (you can conduct a survey on the site or give access to a closed version of the application). Thirdly, the presence of the site will allow you to establish primary contacts with the press (you can send them a pitch, a video, a link to the site before the application is launched).

Site availability is one of the key points of promotion.



Press release. Some might think that this is an old-school promotion method. But as practice shows, it is not. It is important not to forget that we are promoting a mobile application, so the press release is written by its own rules. Here is a great example that you can use when writing your own news.

In the release, do not forget to specify the unique features of the application, reliable facts (figures) and try to ensure that the news is not more than one page. Information for the release: the name of the application, key characteristics, general information that will interest the journalist and he will continue reading, introduction, screenshots, description, price, link to the application, information about the company, contact information.

An important addition. When you send a release to a journalist, you do not need to attach it to a separate file. Just add it to the end of the letter or give a link to the press kit.



Press kit It is needed so that the information can be easily accessed, so that a journalist can, if necessary, obtain it without wasting time. You can create a separate page for the press on your website. Here is a good example . The press kit contains an application icon in good quality, screenshots, a press release, links to applications, a site, social networks. It's great if you offer some kind of bonus to the press (install the application for free, tips inside the application, etc.). There is a special (free) service that will help you make a press kit in 30 minutes - http://dopresskit.com/ . Or the press kit can be in the form of an archived file.

Once you have prepared all the materials, you can begin your communication with the press and bloggers . These people are very busy, they receive a large number of requests every day, so it‚Äôs important that you remember it. And it is equally important that information about your application is published for free. Therefore, it is necessary to prepare a short letter with a personal appeal to the journalist (it is necessary to google, find the name of the person to whom you are applying, or you can send a message via LinkedIn / Facebook). You send an email in which you indicate the key points of your application. It turns out a short press release or pitch. It is necessary to indicate: the name, what the application does and what is its feature, price, link to the site (press kit), link to the application, screenshot, video (if any), description, contact information. A great pitch example for your application. No press release is required. If necessary, the journalist will request it or find it on his own in the press kit.

If you start communicating with a journalist before the release of the application, be sure to specify the release date in the letter. To the news appeared on the day of release.
