Speaktoit Assistant understands and speaks Russian

It seems, waited. Personal Mobile Assistant Speaktoit has learned Russian.

The assistant has a visual representation in the form of a character (it can be changed) and answers the questions asked to him in a natural language (text or voice). The assistant searches for information, performs tasks, and also recalls events using available user information. When searching for information on user requests and to perform tasks, the assistant interacts with external services.

It is free on Google Play.
In the AppStore, they want $ 0.99 for it.
Links to applications in stores are on the manufacturer's website .
Please note that both in Google Play and in the AppStore there are two separate applications - in Russian and in English.
