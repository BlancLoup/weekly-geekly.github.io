Improving self-efficacy: Yaware

Hi,% Habrayuser%!

It often happens that pathologically there is not enough time, or it is and should be used with maximum efficiency.

Many know how to properly motivate themselves to work and not give freebies, but even considering this, how can you increase your own productivity? Under the cut a little about the system of time tracking and improving the efficiency of Yaware.


Yaware is an automatic system for recording personal time, improving your own productivity.

How it works?




You have a client icon in the tray. At first, zeros are displayed there, but later the indication shows how much time you have worked productively, unproductively, neutrally / unknownly, and how much you have given to work.



This information is transmitted to the site of the system, where time use statistics are kept.

Below, I gave my illustrative schedule for using time per day. If you hover your mouse over time intervals, you will immediately see which programs you started and which sites you visited. Green shows the time effectively used, red is not effective, gray is not clear (then you can change it).



It is clear that you can manage the categorization and add your own categories if the system could not assign the program or site to a specific category.



You can also define performance for categories and then the time schedule will display your real activity.



You can see the total activity per day and activity by category





In the paid version, you can still see screenshots of the desktop and webcam photos, but this is more suitable for enterprises who are watching how employees spend time.

As for me, such time use control systems really discipline. It seems that he sat on YouTube for a couple of minutes, but in reality it turns out to be an hour.

After a few days, you can clearly see how you use your time, how inefficient it is and how you can improve yourself.
It also allows you to calculate the time spent on the task.

It's cool that the program after the flexible settings for itself more completely does not interfere with the workflow.
