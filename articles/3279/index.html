20 "Tuesday" patches

Microsoft on Tuesday published 20 security updates for its programs. 10 of them "close" critical errors.

8 patches are designed for programs from the MS Office suite: in particular, security issues are fixed in Word 2000. In addition, updates have been released for some versions of the Internet Explorer browser: they all fix critical vulnerabilities.

As always, all updates are available through the automatic update system and through Microsoft Update . Users of older versions of MS Office can use the Office Update site.
