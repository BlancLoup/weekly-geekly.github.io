Mobile Application Developers Forum Mobile People 2011

Hello colleagues

I would like to invite you to Kazan, to the Mobile People Developer Forum, which will be held on October 14, 2011 in the IT Park conference hall (52 Peterburgskaya Street). Beginning at 9 am (check-in), ending at 6 pm, with a lunch break.

Our official site is mobpeople.ru

Conceiving this event, we set as our goal to bring together people who are interested in and importantly what is happening at the moment in the mobile applications industry. Those who want to hear reports of their colleagues from other companies about the nuances of developing and promoting their projects in this area. To give an opportunity to ask the representatives of the leading company, the developers of the main mobile platforms, the pressing issues that are important for each of you.

Event format: 30-40 minutes of the report, 10 minutes - questions from the participants.

One of the important features of our Forum is that it is free for participants. However, a limited number of seats in the hall, albeit so large, forced us to introduce a pre- registration rule.

The program of the event ( teaser information - Microsoft, Google, Apple, Alawar, Game Insigt, i-Free, Paragon Software, Minister of Information and Communication of the Republic of Tajikistan and others ) and the rest of the information under the cut.
