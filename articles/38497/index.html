Red Alert 3 Beta

I was lucky to get on beta testing of the game Red Alert 3 from Electronic Arts. At one time, the second Red Alert and Yury's Revenge were covered from cover to cover, and the number of hours spent in multiplayer went off scale. Therefore, the third Red Alert awaits with great impatience. And I am very glad to present you the first screenshots and first impressions based on the beta version. Screenshots are presented on the basis of the Union against the Alliance party. The main focus on the Union (after all we are from there;))

What I would like to note in the first place - only multiplayer is available in the beta version. That is, you play with the same testers.
The game has three factions - the Union (with sickles, hammers and stars), the Alliance (with eagles) and some Asians do not understand what.
