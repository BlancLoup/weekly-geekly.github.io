Second PyCon Russia: Ekaterinburg, June 2-3

Pythonists, do not forget to include in your plans for the summer - PyCon Russia 2014 will be held in Yekaterinburg on June 2 and 3. Now we are actively looking for speakers (how to become them, read below). Even under the cut: a little from the history of the conference and our plans for this year.

Last year's foreign speakers in souvenir earflaps. From left to right: Jeff Lindsay, Dr. Russell Keith-Magee, Holger Krekel, Julia Gerasimovich, David Cramer, Amir Salihefendic


Spending PyCon in Russia is an old dream of many python developers. And we - IT-People - two years ago began to implement it little by little. In 2012, we gathered and held a small ekbpy conference for 130 pythonists. In 2013, we had the courage and held the first PyCon Russia in Russia , where 250 developers had already gathered (as it was, you can see here and here ).

Participants PyCon Russia 2013

This year we are hosting the second PyCon Russia and are planning to gather 300 participants. Join now!

How to take part in PyCon Russia?

1. We are looking for speakers with interesting experience with Python. If you are one of them, fill out an application for a report , there is still room in the program. Applications are accepted until April 15, after which the program committee will select the most useful ones.

2. Do you want to win the love and respect of python-developers and advertise your company among pythonists? Fine! You can become a sponsor or partner of the conference. Details here .

3. Come to participate! The site is already open registration . The cost of participation - from 8,000 rubles. The closer to the conference, the higher the price, then you can read the details.

The ticket price includes: participation in the conference, lunch 2.06, dinner 2.06, lunch 3.06, all coffee breaks, afterparty (performances, sauna, bowling), bus from the center of Yekaterinburg to the venue and back. Paid separately: accommodation (in the conference venue - from 2500 for a double room per day with breakfast), prepati and alcohol after the party.
