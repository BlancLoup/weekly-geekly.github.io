New Lenovo season: ThinkPad is more powerful, Yoga 3 is thinner and Yoga Tablet with a projector

The Chinese company Lenovo on the eve of London organized an annual review of new products, showing new generations of Yoga and ThinkPad Yoga laptops, as well as a new Tablet Yoga Tablet 2. I gathered a detailed description of the new products and several excellent photos in one review.


Lenovo Yoga 3 Pro can be recognized by the brilliant bracelet in the folding area

Lenovo ThinkPad Yoga 14


The new generation of workers, or as they are called, the Thinkpad business notebooks received a 14-inch display with a resolution of 1920x1080 - the minimum necessary with such a diagonal. The word Yoga in the title means that this is another Lenovo transformer with a 360-degree foldable keyboard. This is also one of the latest Lenovo laptops, which preserve the classic trackpoint, which is easy to see on any keyboard photos.



Under the hood is an Intel Core i5 processor with an integrated video card. The Nvidia GeForce 840M video card can be ordered as an option. The RAM is 8 gigs, the main one is a terabyte hard drive with a 16-gigabyte SSD cache (a terabyte is good, but generally the hard drive on the transformer looks weird).



From interfaces: card reader, two USB 3.0 and one more USB 2.0 ports. The weight of 1.9 kg has a ThinkPad at the heavy end of the ultrabook spectrum.

As a business notebook, ThinkPad Yoga 14 has an adapter to the docking station.

The cost of the laptop will be $ 1,199, and, as they say, Lenovo promises to freeze the dollar against the ruble for this book until the end of the year - obviously, in connection with the next political upheavals for the New Year holidays, it does not start as 3 average salaries.

Yoga Tablet 2 (Pro)


The Yoga Tablet comes in 3 versions: 8, 10, and 13 inches, plus two versions with Windows 8.1 instead of Android, available for 8-inch and 10-inch versions . The 13-inch version received the prefix Pro - and we will begin with it.

While the rumors around the large iPad remain speculation, Lenovo released the Yoga Tablet 2 Pro: 13.3 "screen resolution of QHD (2560x1440), weight 950 grams and up to 15 hours of battery life. This is almost a compact all-in-one, not a tablet. Surface has received improvements in the form of a cutout under the hook: the Yoga Tablet can be hung on the wall! Perhaps, it lacks only water resistance now - it would be useful in a bathroom.


But Lenovo thinks it's best to watch TV shows while cooking dinner.

The killer feature of the tablet is a projector embedded in a cylindrical thickening at the base. It can produce a picture up to 127 cm (50 inches) diagonally. As far as this is a necessary feature, you need to look, but the developers claim that thickening at the base is also needed for a more correct weight distribution.





The tablet has two video cameras: the rear 8 MP and the front 1.3 MP. The built-in memory is 32 GB, the slot for SD cards allows you to increase it by the volume of the card used (recommended up to 64 GB, but we know that there are cards and more compatible). Unfortunately, information about the processor could not be found - if it appears, I will make an update.

Yoga Tablet 2 Pro runs on Android 4.4.4 KitKat - it's not entirely clear why on the 13-inch Android tablet. Windows 8.1 can only be installed on a 10-inch version of the tablet (without pro). With a ten-inch modification on Windows, there is also a nice bluetooth keyboard included (for the version with Android, it is available for a fee).
