The competition for the best Russian photoblog has begun.

The joint efforts of the Photoblogs of Russia and Simple Tricks sites launched the project The Best Russian Photoblog. Registration of participants began yesterday, January 9, the debriefing will last from January 21 to January 24, and the results will be announced on January 25, in Tatiana's day.

All photoblogs located on a separate domain name of the second or third level, registered in the .RU zone, hosted on Russian hosting or Russian-speaking (photoblogs of Russian-speaking authors, including those living outside Russia) entered in the Photoblogs catalog, are automatically allowed to participate in the competition. org and Photoblogs.ru. When grading takes into account not only the skill of the photographer, but also the design of the site (design) and functionality.

The competition organizers call for adding worthy photographers to the list of participants. You can do this on the site Photoblogs.ru .
