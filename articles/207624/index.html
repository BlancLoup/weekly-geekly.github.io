After the publication of a review article about IPTV players , one of the comments to it led to the reasoning and comparison of Internet services for watching Russian TV online. The most popular by ear is the TV Picture service, but, unfortunately, it is fully paid. Therefore, I cannot say anything about the quality of the video, about the usability of it. But one advantage can be noted, the picture broadcasts not only through the Internet service, but also through the player, which can be downloaded from their website, and also sells its Set-Top-Box. Thus, they endeared themselves and fans to watch online, without any installations of various kinds of program, but also those who prefer to have software specially for themselves. Next, I will talk about free services that you can "touch" yourself.



The first, on request "tv online" in the google search engine, the Boar TV is displayed, so let's start.

Kaban.TV
Boar TV provides only Russian TV channels. In the arsenal of service 19 channels. The site has a TV program for each channel, you can view already broadcast live TV shows from the archive. The service is only aimed at showing the video. therefore, no additional features are provided. But with such a focus on the exceptional provision of video content, the picture quality could be better. In general, if it is important for you to watch some very important issue of the TV show and you are not picky about the interface or the quality of playback, feel free to go in and start watching.



Pro-tv.net
Compared with the previous service, Pro-tv.net provides not only Russian television, and in general has a wide variety of content for every taste. The site has more than 1000 online broadcasts of Internet and TV channels from around the world and their number is constantly increasing. Channels are divided by broadcasting countries and by category. The picture quality is better than the previous one, although not by much. A big plus is that the broadcast speed is indicated opposite each TV channel, in general it varies from 100 to 5000 kbit / s. These points are not taken into account in vain, on the website you can evaluate your speed and compare it with the one shown next to the TV channel. There is also a list of necessary players that need to be on the user's computer in order to watch TV, you can add TV channels to your bookmarks for easier navigation through the site, but in order to use it you need to register. In general, the service contains a large variety of content from different countries of the world and for the user on the site the most necessary points are sufficiently taken into account for ease of use.



Tvspectr.ru
They offer about 140 Russian-language channels, which are divided into categories. Good picture quality, user-friendly TV channel selection interface - the TV channel selection icon is located in the control panel. The important point is that you can choose the quality of playback, the service offers two options - HQ and LQ. In addition to watching TV online, the service offers to listen to the radio online and a separate online cinema. This service is very convenient, you can watch your favorite TV shows, separately download online your favorite movies and listen to your favorite radio wave using one service.
