Aluminum laptop Averatec N3400



The aluminum case is almost the most obvious sign of premium laptops. However, the company TriGem do not think so. Another confirmation of this is the Averatec N3400, a relatively inexpensive thin laptop in an aluminum case. The model is built on the Intel GM45 chipset, comes with an external DVD-drive, weighs only 1.7 kg and is selling for $ 800 in the USA.

Specifications:

Processor: Intel Pentium Dual Core T3400 2.16 GHz;
System: Windows Vista Home Premium;
Video: Intel GMA X4500HD;
Display: 13.3 inches;
RAM: 3 GB DDR2 (expands to 8 GB);
Winchester: SATA 250 GB;
Interfaces: Wi-Fi 802.11n, Bluetooth, 10/100/1000 Ethernet, VGA (D-Sub 15), three USB 2.0 ports, headphone and microphone jacks
Optional: 1.3 MP webcam, 4-in-1 card reader
Dimensions: 32.5x23x2.5 cm;
Weight: 1.7 kg.
