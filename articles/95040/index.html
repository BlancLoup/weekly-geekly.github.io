Infin.Online - Internet business management

The market for ERP systems is constantly growing. The Russian market of these systems shows a positive trend. Over time, there are new requirements for enterprise resource planning systems. Medium-level companies are starting to enter the market, which increase their competitiveness by developing more advanced systems.
One of the major players in this industry is Infin . This company is a developer of automated management systems for financial and economic activities of enterprises.
INFIN-Management is an information system for the integrated automation of enterprise management or a group of enterprises of various industries and fields of activity. This product is very popular among Russian enterprises.
Recently, the company Infin decided to launch the first ERP in Russia, operating under the SaaS scheme - Infin.Online

At the end of October 2009, the product Infin.online Business Management was presented at the exhibition ‚ÄúSoftool 2009‚Äù. A distinctive feature in comparison with other ERP systems is that this system is a web service and is provided to the client for rent. This approach is convenient because the system is always available from anywhere in the world.
The company's client list is quite solid: Lukoil, Tyumen Oil Company, Sibneft, Transaero, Ritek, Domodedovo Airlines, Volgograd Tractor Works, Mosgorteplo, Mosmetrostroy, ITAR-TASS, Izvestia, Moscow River Shipping Company, Mosgortrans, Russian Customs Academy and others.

Opportunities


The main functions of the system are sales, purchases, accounting, personnel, inventory control, customer relations, business analysis. There is also the possibility of preparing and printing standard and additional documents. The application is implemented using Flex technology, which is a very convenient option for building such systems.
The main sections of the program:
  • Trade
  • Finance
  • Staff
  • Business analysis
The ‚Äútrade‚Äù section allows you to manage operations on a product (receipt, return, completion, inventory, retirement of marriage), as well as everything related to accounts.
In the "Finance" tab, cash operations are managed (receipt, expense, loans, etc.).
All information on employees: personal data, various orders (appointment, dismissal, vacation) can be found in the "personnel" section.
Very useful is the last section "business analysis". Thanks to this tool, you can determine the best products or non-traded goods, find out the sales volume by managers, look at debtors, analyze the situation on employee leave and much more.

Security


The system is in its own data center. Security is provided using SSL. Regularly backed up data. The frequency of reservations is regulated by the tariff plan or is set according to the additionally ordered services. In case of emergency situations, malfunctions or erroneous deletion of data by the user, you can always restore the last backup copy of the data. You can also download them to your computer or order their recording on DVD.

Tariffs


Tariffs for services: the cost of rent per user - 350 rubles / month. A user is an employee of a client who has his login and password to enter the program. The total number of users is set and changed by the client in the personal account.
Clients are advised by email. For an additional fee it is possible to customize the software for the needs of the client.
