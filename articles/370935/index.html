Results presale and project news

A month ago, we announced the start of Presale: we talked about conditions, about the merits and risks of a financial model, about opportunities for new users. Some liked the project, others criticized it - but a month has passed and it is time to sum up.



On October 21, the first test sale of XRONOS cryptocurrency was completed. A total of 36,445.105 XPO were sold at a price of $ 2.15 per unit. At the time of completion, the presale collected 17.61 BTC. 106 unique users believed in the project.

Thanks to the work of XRSS, it was possible to bring the security of each XPO coin from $ 1.79 (80% of the purchase price) to $ 2.46. That is, the algorithm works not only on test data, but also on quite real money, and we are counting on a similar trend in November. It is XRSS that allows to gradually increase the price of coins through the wall of supporting orders.

In the process of sales, we received a lot of feedback: both positive and negative. We will listen to reasonable voices of crypto-community (which not only labeled, but also argued their point of view), correct the development strategy and product positioning so as to eliminate the ambiguity of perception of XRONOS by users. Thanks to everyone who supported us with criticism or bitcoin: with you we will become stronger and faster we will achieve our goal.

Project News


This week we plan to launch a new mobile wallet application: we have finally completed the development and have moderated the store for iOS. The web version of XRONOS WALLET will receive a tested two-factor authentication.

The work on the XRONOS software core did not stop; we will systematically test and release updates for the month before ICO. In particular, users are waiting for the expansion of the functionality of a personal account.

New exchanges and growth rate on ICO


We continue to develop the project towards decentralization and agreed with the new exchange platforms on partnerships and trade in the BTC / XPO pair on the eve of the second sale of coins, which will take place from November 21 to December 21, 2017.
