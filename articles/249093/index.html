Open Data Day February 21 will be held around the world



Worldwide February 21 will be Open Data Day - opendataday.org
This is a huge hakaton marathon and an event for everyone who works with open data or wants to learn more about them.

We, Information Culture and OKF Russia, are planning to hold an event according to open data in Moscow on this day.
In the near future we will write about how and in what format we will hold it.

If you plan to conduct a hackathon according to open data, then this day is very suitable for such an event.

If in Moscow, even if you will conduct the hackathon yourself, write, we will help the lecturers and materials and data to the hackathon. If you want to join the general event - write to us or comments to this post. In the near future, we will determine the platform and partners and announce the program.

And if you are planning to conduct a hackathon in your city - also write to us indicating the place, a brief description, links to the site (if any) and contacts of the organizers and we will help to spread on our sites and announce in the OKF blog.
