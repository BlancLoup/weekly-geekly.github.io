Selection of photo hosting sites





Flickr.com -The best photo storage service to date, which is paid for use (in the free version, the user can upload infinitely photos, but only the last 200 can see).

Picasaweb.google.com - The online photo service and computer program is owned by Google. To register with Picasa, you must be a Google account holder.

ImageShack.us - image hosting for quick posting.

MyPhotoAlbum.com - online services for storing and organizing photos. (unlimited space for uploading photos and videos.)

Fotki.com - Unlimited photo storage space (for users from Russia)

Smages - very simple and clear photo hosting. Maximum image size: 1500 kb. Image storage period: unlimited.


Fotki.Yandex.ru - Photos is a new Yandex service, where you can publish your photos, watch other photos, comment and choose the best ones, chat with their authors, and participate in contests.

SharePix.ru is a new free photo hosting.

iPicture.ru - the project allows you to quickly and easily publish your photos in forums, chats, blogs and on message boards.

Radikal.ru - photo hosting for forums and blogs.
