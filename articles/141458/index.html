Yandex.Disk - flew!

As you probably notice, life is accelerating more and more; Every year there is an increasing need for even simpler and even faster data transfer from person to person, and from device to device.

Information carriers change, communication means change: postcards and letters in pre-electronic, paper form, if we send them today, it is only from long-distance journeys. And friends who come to visit no longer bring with them disks and flash drives with a "collection of fresh photos."

Floppy disks are long gone, CDs are about to be the last, and flash drives will go after them.

Yandex will help to finally part with all these devices and step into the future. Today, a new name has appeared among our services - Yandex.Disk .

This service was created so that you can combine the library, documents and photos on your devices (phones and computers, at home and at work), securely stored them and easily shared with family, colleagues or friends.

You will get access to important documents uploaded to the Disc (for example, to a copy of your passport) from anywhere in the world where there is Internet.

The files change immediately on all devices, so you can start working on the report in the office, then put it in the Disk and continue working on the way home from the phone and, if you have the strength and desire, finish the report at dinner on your home computer. And the next morning, already in the office, go to Yandex.Disk and print the finished report.



Yandex.Disk is a free service. You'll get:

  • Up to 10 gigabytes of disk space;
  • access to the web version of the Disk (Files tab in Yandex.Mail);
  • Yandex.Disk program for Windows or Mac OS X;
  • Disk embedded in the Yandex.Mail mobile application for iOS and Android phones;
  • the ability to use the Disk in the development of its own and use of third-party applications and programs that provide for the WebDAV protocol .
