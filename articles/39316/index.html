How to buy Sony Reader PRS-505 for 8860 rubles with delivery to the apartment.



No, this is not advertising and not spam.

Just maybe someone will be interested in how you can buy a miracle device - Sony Reader with minimal money.

We go to the store B & H. As you can see, the price of the device is $ 294.95. Delivery (I had to Perm) - another $ 59.75 (International Express Mail). Total: $ 354.7 = 8860 rubles (slightly rounded).

How to pay:
  • The shop accepts visa and mastercard. My payment went with a simple visa electron.
  • When you first purchase you will be asked to scan a credit card and send a scan to the store. Do it as quickly as possible - the sending process will be greatly accelerated.

Delivery:
  • Only choose International Express Mail! Otherwise, there may be problems at customs. I remind you that we can buy once a week for up to 10,000 rubles. - Then do not take customs duty.
  • In Russia, the parcel will come to you via EMS-mail. I was taken straight to the apartment.
  • Delivery time (turned out for me) - 2 weeks, the state can be constantly tracked on the site using tracking numbers.

Problems: the reader is not originally Russified and does not understand fb2. The first is solved by the firmware, the second by the special program fb2 to lrf (the conversion is done in a couple of seconds).

Warranty - as if it is not, because The reader is not officially shipped to Russia. After reading the forum, I realized that it practically does not break, so perhaps this moment is not so important.

Taking into account the fact that the dollar has properties to fall almost to 24 rubles, and the prices in this store also sometimes change for the better, you may be able to buy this magic device even cheaper (from Russian Internet shops below 12,000 rubles) found).
