5 secrets of Google Buzz


Here are some secrets of Google Buzz that will significantly simplify the use of this service.

1. Formatting in the comment body


You can format your text by making it bold (* bold *), italic (_-tilted_), or strikethrough (-clotted-).

2. Posting messages via e-mail


You can post your messages to Buzz via mail. Just send from your e-mail address (where Buzz is activated), an email to buzz@gmail.com. The subject of the message will become the title, the text with the text of the content of the new record, but the text of the message will be ignored . Photos can be attached to the message, they will also be published.

3. Leave personal answers in the comments.


To leave a personal response in the discussion to the Buzz entry, just before the comment text, insert the nickname of the addressee with the "@" symbol (without quotes).


4. Report the new answer to the author of the post or comment.


Just use the above personalized answer option, but plus the nickname, add " @gmail.com " (without quotes).


5. Buzz Record Link


If you wish, you can get a link to any Buzz-record. Simply click on the link in the form of the publication date, or select the corresponding item in the drop-down menu of the record.
