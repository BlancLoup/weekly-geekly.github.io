HyperX updates Predator DDR3 and DDR4 memory modules

Hi, Giktayms! HyperX presents an updated line of Predator DDR3 and DDR4 RAM modules. New items are made in an aggressive design with a black aluminum heat sink and a black printed circuit board. They provide more efficient heat dissipation, increase system reliability, and are also ideally suited to the latest generation of computer components.



HyperX Predator DDR4 memory modules operate at higher frequencies (up to 3333 MHz) and have a low latency CL15-CL16: with them, gamers and enthusiasts can assemble the most productive systems. HyperX Predator DDR3 memory supports frequencies up to 2666 MHz with latency CL9-CL11 and allows you to significantly speed up your PC.



HyperX Predator DDR4 and DDR3 modules undergo complete factory testing for compliance with the declared speeds and are notable for their legendary reliability. All new items are covered by a lifetime warranty from the manufacturer with free technical support.
HyperX is a division of Kingston Technology, which specializes in creating high-performance products - DDR4 and DDR3 memory, solid-state and flash drives with USB connector, headsets, gaming accessories. The target audience for the brand were overclockers, PC modification enthusiasts and gamers. The HyperX brand is known worldwide as a synonym for quality, performance, and innovation. The company is actively involved in the field of e-sports and supports more than 30 teams around the world, as well as being the main sponsor of the Intel Extreme Masters team. HyperX products can be seen at many trade shows and conferences, including the Brasil Game Show, China Joy, DreamHack, gamescom and PAX.



Features and characteristics of new modules HyperX Predator DDR4:


  • New heat sink design: durable black aluminum heat sink ensures optimum heat dissipation and reliable performance over a long period of time. Both it and the printed circuit board are made in the corporate style of HyperX and are perfectly combined with the latest system components.
  • Extreme DDR4 performance: HyperX Predator DDR4 memory modules are compatible with 6-core and 8-core Intel processors and are therefore ideal for video editing, 3D modeling, AI processing and, of course, games. New DDR4 memory with a capacity of 4-16 GB supports a frequency of up to 3333 MHz and is available in sets with a total capacity of 8 to 64 GB. All modules are officially certified, tested for compatibility and compliance with the highest requirements.
  • Optimized for Intel 100 Series chipsets and X99 motherboards: HyperX Predator DDR4 memory modules are fully compatible with Intel Core i7 HEDT processors, as well as 100 Series chipsets and X99 motherboards. Users can easily overclock the system by manually setting up profiles in order to achieve higher performance and lower timings.
  • More efficient power consumption than DDR3: at a voltage of 1.35 volts, the HyperX Predator DDR4 consumes less power than DDR3 memory generation. Low voltage reduces heat generation and increases system reliability.
