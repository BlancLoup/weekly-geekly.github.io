ASUS showed the first Zenbook

ASUS has introduced a line of ultrabooks. The first device, the 11.6-inch Zenbook UX21E from ASUS, received a dual-core Intel Core i5 processor, 4 GB DDR3 ‚ÄúRAM‚Äù and a 128 GB SSD drive. The body is made of aluminum alloy.

So far, the entire Zenbook series consists of the UX21E and UX31E models. The presentation of both mobile PCs was held at the ASUS conference, held in New York, USA. The UX21E is the most compact ultrabook in the ASUS model range. It is a mobile computer in a very thin metal case (minimum thickness - 3 mm, and maximum - 17 mm). Even the keys of the Zenbuk keyboard are made of metal. The metal chassis did not affect the weight of the UX21E - it weighs a little less than a kilogram.
