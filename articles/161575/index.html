Tulpa - pocket schizophrenia for geeks or a real imaginary friend

What is characteristic, it is created quite consciously, by means of purposeful actions for a long time, and allows you to set the initial character and shape at your discretion.

Story

The history of the creation of a tulpa as an independent phenomenon is very interesting in itself. Have you heard of such a funny meme as fingerbox ? In short, it is an invented phenomenon or object, which in full seriousness and with all the details, and often even with photographs, is told to people. After that, enjoy a ton of questions Where to buy, How to build, How to use and so on.

So, tulpa at the very beginning of its existence was just such a fingerbox. Around the years 2008-2010, threads began to appear on foreign boards describing how people animated their favorite anime and movie characters in their own minds, telling that they replaced girls and boys with them. The idea was received with enthusiasm, on the one hand, by those who realized that it was a fingerbox, and in connection with this there were tons of questions and strange answers to them (well, this is called trolling and lulz). On the other hand, lonely zadrota people Tulpa seemed a great way to get a girlfriend, even if it is imaginary. At that time, the first part supporting the legend was engaged only in that (wrote messages and strongly supported the appearance of the reality of the pulp), and was not at all going to carry out simple instructions (correctly, who will collect the fingerboxes in all seriousness?), The second part, not such active, began to actively engage in forcing.
Then the unexpected happened. For some people, absolutely made-up instructions led to quite tangible results! This was somewhat unexpected, and even a number of people, including those who perceived the pulp only as a fingerbox, made an attempt to try it. Then the process went on increasing, within six months the first results were collected, and it became clear in which direction to move in order to obtain results with greater probability.
Somewhere in early 2012, the first manuals began to appear on Russian boards, first translated and then written. Currently, there is a very active Russian-speaking get-together, website, forum, several active blogs describing the process of creating your own tulip.

As a result, we came to the conclusion that, using certain practices, we can create a separate second person in our head, capable of perceiving the world around us (through our senses). What practices?

Process of creation

To create a pulp (or attempts to create), the classical and proven scheme is most often used.
  1. Character creation
  2. Creating a visual image
  3. Create wonderland
  4. Tulpa visualization and talk with her
  5. Tulpa transfer to the real world

Now for each of the items.
Character creation - you think about, write down on a piece of paper or remember the ‚Äúbackbone‚Äù of a character - a list of 5-10 (as you can remember without difficulty) personal qualities, pieces that make up character.
For example, "Compassion, curiosity, cheerfulness, loyalty, resourcefulness." Do not take it as an axiom in the following steps, this is a crib only for you.
Creating a visual image - Find a suitable image (if you can not draw, you'd better choose an existing image), stock up on pictures, photos and video materials. During the forcing you have to constantly maintain the image in your head, the easiest way to do this is by looking at the image, and then trying to restore it in memory. Already after the visualization of the image, you can refuse to reinforce the images. Keep in mind that when creating a visual image of an existing person you know, a tulpa is likely to get a part of his character, however much you want it.
Creating wonderland - you can do without this item, but it will be easier with it. At this step, using simple self-hypnosis and simple forms of meditation (there is nothing there that an ordinary person could not do) you create a piece of the virtual world. Your task is to learn how to get into it (and come back). The classical scheme is usually used - a ladder of a certain number of steps appears, on which you slowly descend, at each step more and more relaxing and plunging into a trance. Around the last step imagine the door, opening that, you find yourself in wonderland. However, you can think of your own way. One of the forsers got into it, wearing dark glasses.
Tulpa visualization and conversations with her - you must make your brain believe in the existence of a tulpa. In short, you start talking to emptiness, turning to the tulpe. Watch the movie - comment. Do the work - consult, ask, tell. Consider that the tulpa tell you, according to her character from the first paragraph. Gradually, if everything is done correctly, you will begin to receive ‚Äúresponses.‚Äù First, in the form of emotions - all of a sudden, for no reason at all, you will be given a wave of approval or interest. Then a voice will be added. You can do this simultaneously with the previous stage, then, most likely, to the appearance of an emotional response, you will receive a visual image in wonderland.
Transferring the pulp into the real world is a continuation of the previous stage. You practice in wonderland to communicate with tulpa, and in the real world you get an ever stronger response, and further, visualization.
In the links you will find several manuals in which this process is described in more detail.
