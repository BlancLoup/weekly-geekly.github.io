Trillian: Now completely free on all platforms


Today there was an official release of Trillian 5 for Windows, which included:
  • Completely redesigned interface.
  • Improved social integration.
  • Geolocation.
  • * Cloud-based chat history.

Also, there is a new feature " continuous client " that allows you to keep an open dialogue, switching between clients (from mobile to desktop, etc.)

From now on, all the usual Pro-functionality (the possibility of simultaneous input from several clients, themes, action history, etc.) becomes free. Pro-version will be replaced by Pro-service, which includes a cloud storage of the history of all correspondence.


Cerulean Studios plans to monetize the project through textual, unobtrusive advertising (during testing such banners were noticed, 100% long and two lines of text and organically separated from the correspondence) , which can be closed.

Also, ads will appear on mobile devices, on each platform it will appear to be different. The users who purchased Trillian for iPhone and Blackberry are not affected by the innovation until today, nor will they notice any banners or restrictions. In addition, support for purchased applications will not be stopped and developers promise free updates.

If you do not want to see advertising banners, you will have to buy a subscription to the Pro-service. The cost of the service is $ 1 per month and includes, in addition to the lack of advertising, cloud storage of stories throughout the entire period of using the service.

Let me remind you that Trillian is a multilingual, multi-protocol client for Windows, Mac, iPhone, Android, BlackBerry and Web. With support for typed and handwriting, minimalist design, built-in mail checking, the ability to create bookmarks in correspondence, create conferences in any network and other rich functionality.
