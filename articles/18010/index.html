US Army and Security

The US Army is looking more closely at the Macintosh in the face of increased security threats. After the hacker attacks on the Pentagon and on other organizations of the military-industrial complex, such as Boeing, Raytheon and Northrop Grumman, the army increased its defense against hackers, and uses Macintosh. According to the military, there are far fewer exploits for Mac OS X than for Windows. The US Army has about 700,000 computers and servers, 20,000 are manufactured by Apple.


For many years, the main obstacles in choosing were the high cost and compatibility of Apple products. Now the most important is the issue of security, in spite of the price difference between a Macintosh and a similar PC.
