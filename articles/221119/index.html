Home Connect - one app for all brands


Last Friday, BSH Bosch und Siemens Hausgerate announced the creation of an application that can monitor all household appliances in the house. Its name is Home Connect , and a distinctive feature will be that it will be able to control devices of different brands and companies. Bosch said this application is the first of its kind.

The main reason for the development of this application was that 90% of families have appliances made by more than one brand. The announcement was made at the IFA press conference in Belek, Turkey.

IFA is an annual event dedicated to consumer electronics and home appliances, held in Berlin in September. The Home Connect app debuts there, along with the first plug-in appliances from Bosch b Siemens.
