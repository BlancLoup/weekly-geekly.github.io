Cooking.


First you need to prepare all the components:
- non-working (or working) DVD-RW drive with a recording speed of 16x or higher;
- capacitors 100 pF and 100 mF;
- resistor 2-5 Ohm;
- Three AAA batteries;
- soldering iron and wires;
- collimator (or Chinese pointer);
- steel LED flashlight.

This is a necessary minimum for making a simple driver model. The driver is, in fact, a board that will output our laser diode to the necessary power. Connecting the power supply directly to the laser diode is not worth it - will fail. The laser diode must be powered by current, not voltage.

A collimator is, in fact, a module with a lens that reduces all radiation into a narrow beam. Ready collimators can be purchased at radio stores. In such, there is immediately a convenient place to install a laser diode, and the cost is 200-500 rubles.


You can use a collimator from a Chinese pointer, however, the laser diode will be difficult to fix, and the body of the collimator will probably be made of metallized plastic. So our diode will be badly cooled. But it is possible. This option can be viewed at the end of the article.

We do.


First you need to get the laser diode itself. This is a very fragile and small part of our DVD-RW drive - be careful. A powerful red laser diode is in the carriage of our drive. It can be distinguished from the weak by a radiator of a larger size than that of a conventional IR diode.

It is recommended to use an antistatic bracelet, since the laser diode is very sensitive to static voltage. If there is no bracelet, you can wrap the diode's leads with a thin wire while it waits for installation in the case.

According to this scheme, you need to solder the driver.

Do not confuse polarity! The laser diode will also fail instantly if the supply voltage is incorrect.

The diagram shows a 200 mF capacitor, however, 50-100 mF is enough for portability.

We try.


Before installing the laser diode and assembling everything in the case, check the performance of the driver. Connect another laser diode (idle or second from the drive) and measure the current with a multimeter. Depending on the speed characteristics, the current should be selected correctly. For 16x models, 300-350mA is quite suitable. For the fastest 22x, you can even submit 500mA, but by a completely different driver, the production of which I plan to describe in another article.


It looks awful, but it works!

Aesthetics.


The laser assembled on weight can only boast in front of the same crazy techno-maniacs, but for beauty and convenience it is better to assemble it into a convenient case. There is already better to choose yourself, as you like. I mounted the whole circuit in a conventional LED flashlight. Its dimensions do not exceed 10x4cm. However, I do not advise carrying it with you: you never know what claims the relevant authorities can make. And it is better to store in a special case, so that the sensitive lens is not dusty.
