Get acquainted, Instacode is like Instagram, only for the best fragments from life ... source code


Hello. Recently, on the Internet, I came across a very interesting thing, whose name is Instacode . What is interesting about this site - and the fact that it works on the principle of Instagram, except that images are generated from your source code. Do not believe? And you try. In general, anyone interested - you are welcome under the cat.

Well then. The site is a page with a field for entering the source code - any fragment of more than 10 lines. After you get a beautiful image of your character, putting on him various filters and effects.
You ask - why is it necessary. I also thought about this question, and then I realized that this is indeed a very useful thing. Imagine - you wrote Singleton, unlike any other - your own, perfect and beautiful Singleton. And what - he will sink into oblivion with the project - can not be allowed to happen. You copy the source code or, if you are an ardent opponent of copy-paste, then reprint it in Instacode - put filters and lights to taste - and voila - you get not just a code, but a work of art ready to recruit retweets and likes in popular social media today. networks.
Or maybe you want to print this masterpiece and hang it in a frame - please print how much you like. Or not. What if you take various requests to your database, process them on Instacode, and print them as wallpaper. Or instead of requests to use logs ... Or configs ... To glue the whole wall with these wallpapers to enjoy life. In short, as you wish. so use this service - I just wanted to tell you about this site so you can rest after a hard week and play around with your source code for a bit. Good luck to all coding.
