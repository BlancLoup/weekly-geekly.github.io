And now a little magic. Demonstration ( from 14 minutes on the record )

I have already spoken about the innovativeness of the EXOS operating system. Everything in it is pleasing to the eye - and the modular structure, and advanced use of Ethernet technologies, and much more. At our booth, you can see how EAPS native technology will show itself in practice. Let me remind you that EAPS is Ethernet automatic protection switching, a loop protection protocol with a convergence time of no more than 50 ms. Who else can boast such?

In our booth there is a ring of 4 switches, cameras are turned on in one of the switches (to complicate the task of the camera, let's turn it on not directly, but via the Extreme Networks access point), and we will receive a signal from the cameras through another switch.

The meaning and ‚Äúcoolness‚Äù of this protocol is that in a fast time, up to 50 milliseconds, it restores the topology. For example, we emulate a crash, the link disappears - no matter, pull out the cord.



You can be very critical, saying that it has been recovering for a very long time, but we see video transmission (which is critical for losses and delays). In this case, the simplest scheme of the EAPS protocol is implemented, with critical data, video and voice transmission, we can configure fast convergent mode - recovery to 2 milliseconds. So that you understand: when transmitting sound, the ear will not hear the loss, while transmitting the video, the eye will not see the loss. I find it cool.
