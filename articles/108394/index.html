Launched WebProfessionals.ru service to find partners for website customers

The company "Business Advertising" with the support of the general partner of the project of the company Microsoft, launched the WebProfessionals.ru service, intended for customers looking for partners to develop a website. The portal presents web studios recommended by Microsoft and hosting projects on the Windows Server platform.

The main difference of the WebProfessionals.ru project from other similar directories of web studios is that WebProfessionals.ru is officially promoted by Microsoft and the companies participating in the catalog, recommended by Microsoft as contractors for Microsoft customers and partners in Russia.

Any company that is professionally engaged in developing websites and hosting them on servers running Windows Server can participate in the project . The terms of participation in the catalog of partners are the quality of the final products and their publication on Windows Server.

Any web-studio can appear in the catalog - all you need to do is to start hosting websites on the Windows platform and submit an application for participation in the catalog. It is worth recalling that small web studios (up to 10 people) can receive software for development, testing and publishing websites for the WebsiteSpark program for three years and take advantage of special offers from partners - for example, Parking.ru offers discounts to WebsiteSpark members who placed the sites on Windows .

We encourage web developers to host websites on Windows and join the WebProfessionals.ru directory!
