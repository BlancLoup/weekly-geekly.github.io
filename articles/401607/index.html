Nokia 3310 Reincarnation



February 26, 2017 at the World Mobile Congress in Barcelona will be the official announcement of the Nokia 3310 - a modern version of the iconic mobile phone. It is not known what the modern reincarnation of the Nokia 3310 will look like, but Finnish engineers should approach this project with particular respect. Out of respect for the legendary device.

A durable, reliable phone with a battery life of several weeks is a good option as a backup device in addition to modern smartphones that only live 1 day without charging.

The rights to issue phones under the Nokia brand belong to the Finnish company HMD Global Oy, which was formed in May 2016 specifically for the purchase of part of the business of Microsoft Mobile. Veterans of Nokia work in HMD, they are engaged in the development of phones, smartphones and tablets for Android, and the production itself is given to the Foxconn FIH Mobile division. On the plans for the presentation on February 26, said an informed source . This is still unofficial information.

The Nokia 3310 went on sale in the IV quarter. 2000, replacing the Nokia 3210. This model has become one of the most successful for Nokia: for all the time about 126 million devices have been sold. Until 2003, the Nokia 3210 and Nokia 3310 are absolute world bestsellers in terms of sales, and to date, hardly anyone has managed to bypass these legendary models in popularity (the ratio of sales to the global smartphone market). Without a doubt, the Nokia 3210 and 3310 - is the number 1 in the history of mobile phones.

The phone stored up to seven ringtones and supported SMS dialing up to 459 characters.
