Microsoft Security Essentials is now available for small businesses.



Previously, the free Microsoft Security Essentials (MSE) antivirus could only be used at home for personal use. The license did not allow the use of this antivirus in enterprises and companies.

However, Microsoft is taking a step towards small businesses and announces a change in licensing rules . From now on, companies can install up to 10 copies of antivirus on their computers. For small firms this is enough.

Let me remind you that MSE won many awards for quality and received the highest marks when tested by independent companies. The beta testing of the second enhanced version of MSE has recently started .
