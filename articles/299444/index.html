Government experts: in the case of introducing measures to regulate OTT services, the RF budget will lose 10 billion rubles a year

The working group under the government of the Russian Federation discusses the issues of regulating the market for audio-visual and communication services on the Internet. These services include, for example, online cinemas and instant messengers. It was proposed to introduce a notification procedure for the operation of such companies, as well as to create a register of audiovisual services.

The ISS proposed to oblige online cinemas to show at least 80% of all products in Russian, as well as to introduce a mandatory quota for films and series produced in the Russian Federation in the amount of 30%.

In addition, the organizers of audiovisual services are required to provide access to all services throughout the Russian Federation.
