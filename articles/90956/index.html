24-hour call center in Russia

Several times over the last time there was a desire to hear the sympathetic voice of support, but there was a bummer, since he already had a non-working time. And on this occasion, it was thought that for large companies present in different regions of Russia, using a variety of time zones of the country and modern data transfer technologies, a round-the-clock call center could be implemented, whose employees would not have to work during the night and experience related problems . There would be only two offices in this call center. The first in the Moscow time zone (but not in Moscow, to save on wages). In it, the working time would be from 11 to 23 and the work would probably be conducted in 2 shifts - so as not to violate labor laws. And the second in the Magadan time zone (Kamchatka is now included there) with a working day from 7 to 19 and also in 2 shifts.
