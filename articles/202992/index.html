Meet the debit card Google wallet

Google asks to report that it is now not only a search line - but also your real debit card. Funds from Google wallet can now be spent using a physical card (works like a MasterCard). Daily limit purchases - $ 5000. They promise the absence of monthly or annual fees, the issue of the card is also free.

However, not everything is free : the fee for recharging the balance (from a card or bank account) and sending money to a card / bank account is 2.9% (minimum $ 0.3 per transaction).

While it is available only in the United States.

You can order a card from the Google Wallet app for Android (coming out this week), or from the Google Wallet site.
