Facebook Headquarters moves to former Sun Microsystems campus


Facebook Headquarters is moving from its current office complex in Palo Alto to the former Sun Microsystems campus in San Mateo.

Facebook sent invitations to a news conference on Tuesday, February 8, to a number of news blogs , where they will be told about the new headquarters. The event will take place at 1701 in Laurel Street in Menlo Park.

Facebook has been headquartered in Palo Alto since Zuckerberg moved there in 2004. The number of company employees is approaching two thousand, and very soon the place in the current headquarters will end.
