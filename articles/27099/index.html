Joint payment or "throw off and save."

Idea


Initially, a possible project was conceived as a place to unite people in order to jointly pay for money transfers, for which one person is expensive, and the translation itself is needed not by one person.

However, the idea with translations is rather narrow - hardly for every day.

Then the thought went further.
