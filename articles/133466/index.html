Indie Game Music Bundle

In addition to the expected discounts on games and gadgets, Black Friday in the US brought discounts on soundtracks to some indie toys. Meet the Indie Game Music Bundle : until 11 am on November 29 in Moscow you can buy 10 albums with music from indie toys, paying as much as you like (the minimum purchase amount is $ 1). If the amount exceeds $ 10, you will receive 11 more albums, as well as the ability to download music in FLAC format. Buy Music Bundle using PayPal or Google Checkout.

Indie Game Music Bundle was organized by several composers (anosou, Big Giant Circles, C418, danny B, Josh Whelchel, HyperDuckChris, Souleye, coda, surasshu, zircon) in order to acquaint more listeners with their music. They were inspired by the success of the Humble Bundle, which shows good fees each time.
