Conceit requires the husband to achieve universal acceptance of the theory of the omnipotent, because true. To recognize a scientist, you need Authority. Authority in the scientific world is acquired through publications.

Next action:

The scientist publishes an article describing the Theory and speaks at the conference.

Ten clever opponents are crushing the article to smithereens, and at the conference they mix the scientist with mud.

The scientist publishes ten more articles developing Theory, and speaks at ten conferences.
