The end of an era. Sony discontinues PlayStation 2

First, the game console stopped collecting in Japan, but now it became known that the release of the console stopped around the world. PlayStation 2 was released in March 2000. Since then, more than 150 million consoles have been sold worldwide. This console has become the most popular console in history.
Total number of games for PS2, there are about 11,000, the total sales of which exceed 1.5 billion copies.



Perhaps the production of consoles, produced since 2000, stopped due to the preparation of Sony to release the PlayStation 4. It is expected that the new console will be released before the end of 2013.
