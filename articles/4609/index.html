Full list of video services on the Internet

All video sites on the Internet can be divided into two types: video hosting platforms (well-known YouTube ); video portals with TV channels (ChannelChooser). Sometimes there are hybrids, such as Yahoo Video. The full list contains descriptions of all major video sites on the Internet.

Yahoo Video is a hybrid service that combines the properties of a video hosting platform and a video portal. To improve the quality of the video, Yahoo is trying to create its own content in partnership with Current TV .

Google Video service is more focused on video hosting. It supports a very modest functionality, including a breakdown by thematic categories and the possibility of buying protected material. There are no channel subscriptions.

Current TV combines user-generated content (about a third) and professional video. And they, and others have the quality of an order of magnitude higher than typical videos from YouTube .
