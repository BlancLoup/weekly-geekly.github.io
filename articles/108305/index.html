PixelBuzz.tv - experimenting with pixels



Modern cinema and television shows are solid multi-million dollar budgets, scandals, celebrities and crazy special effects. In PixelBuzz.tv , the opposite is true. We have 8 colors, 4 frames and a limited set of pixels. Everyone can become a director and make their own pixel video by sending it to a common channel. All videos are shuffled and randomly given to viewers.


You can watch videos on the PixelBuzz.tv website , in the screensaver, or embed our TV widget into your website or blog. Each random video has a permanent address (for example, a space invader , a submariner or an elk ), so, if you wish, you can easily show your creation to your friends.



Rollers can be added without registration, but post-moderation is valid.

All this was created as an experiment, so we are very interested to hear from you any feedback and suggestions. Is it worth developing the project further, and in what direction?
