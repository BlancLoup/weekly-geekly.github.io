At the end of April, I published the previous article ‚Äú Startup from scratch ... ‚Äù. To my great surprise, the number of pluses outweighed the number of minuses with a gap of 80 points. My search path partner STO does not look like a fairy tale. It is rather an action adventure comedy-dramatic action movie. Given the relevance of this topic, I hope that my experience will be useful for someone.


Hell

Hell began in the last article . He was quite a lot and in different guises. All this hell gave me invaluable experience. In this article I will tell you how I managed to dispose of this experience and eventually come to the happy side.


New understanding

Having filled the abundance of cones, I understood for myself an important point. I saw that there are globally two extremes between which a person can remain.

One extreme is when stability, current well-being is in the top priority in life. This extreme is comfortable when there are clear tasks, answers to the question ‚ÄúWhat should I do?‚Äù, When responsibility is minimal. If not, then even better. ‚ÄúWho is to blame?‚Äù - ‚ÄúNot me.‚Äù Uncertainty, lack of a source of instructions, increased responsibility cause pain, discomfort, fear, disgust, and someone has a rage.

The other extreme is when, on the contrary, any instruction from above enters into a rage. When they infuriate any encroachment on freedom of choice in any sphere of life Comfortably - this is complete responsibility for everything. ‚ÄúWho is responsible for all the failures and successes in my life?‚Äù - ‚ÄúOnly me.‚Äù The question prevails in my thoughts: ‚ÄúWhat do I need to change in myself, my actions in order to achieve the goal?‚Äù. If everything is known in advance, then immediately boring. Uncertainty, a challenge to faith and will for the sake of a big goal spark eyes and an interest in life.

Each of these extremes is useful for a particular type of activity.

Entrepreneurship, and especially startup in its initial stage, as the highest degree of uncertainty, brings happiness only to those who are closer to the second extreme. I will not say that I am completely at the second extreme, but somewhere between 68-73% I am exactly in it, and I want more.

I tried to stick people from the first extreme to a startup. I tried to stick a hired programmer, freelance, professional outsourcing and a partner on the s / n. At the exit - it was always a mockery of all participants in the process, leading to the finish lines of varying degrees of trouble.

Realizing this, I decided to change the conditions under which I would seek partnership with the future STO.


New conditions

The main new condition is no salary, only a fraction. This item immediately eliminated all those who are not ready to take full responsibility, swim in obscurity, to take the full risk.
The second condition is to do part of the work for a startup before the partnership. Such an item immediately extinguished the motivation of those who are in the business purely for the benefit of. Such an item attracted those who are really in love with the project, whom the process itself actually inserts. Who is ready to do it, simply because he likes.

On this occasion, I personally remembered and felt very deeply one moment. Through the mass of my past projects, I saw that in any business there are always periods when everything is difficult and long, although at first you believe that everything will be easy and fast. In any business there are periods when the project brings nothing but hard work: no profit, hopes and success. During such periods, the moral energy dries out very quickly, you can even dry out physically, get sick and hard.

But there is one essence, an exception that energizes in any conditions - it is the love of the project itself. For example, when it is hard for me once again, I turn on the music to my hellish mood with the help of my startup and charge myself with energy like an atomic reactor. I have a real love for the project. He solves my own problem of finding the flow of music in the mood. Sometimes you want to hug him for it.

I decided that the SRT should have the same thing, so that he would live happily ever after, like our cooperation.


New approach

In addition to the conditions, I also changed the approach to finding a partner.

First of all, instead of inspiring speeches, on the contrary, I talked about all the real complexities of a start-up life, which one has to give up and for what. How much risk, uncertainty and how much responsibility. In general, told the truth-womb. The enthusiasm of the trio of the majority, and the second conversation was no longer there, even if they said otherwise in the process.

The second thing I gave the real task of the project. You want, you can do. You do not want, you can not do. It's not obligatory. Those who did not want (‚Äúcould not‚Äù, ‚Äúdidn‚Äôt have time‚Äù, ‚Äúopportunities‚Äù ‚Äúthere were other more important matters‚Äù), he also stepped aside.
