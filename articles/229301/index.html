11 days to Odessa Innovation Week


Before the start of Odessa Innovation Week , a little less than 2 weeks remained and we have a lot of good news.

We have formed the conference program WebCamp 2014 , which you can see on our updated website: web-camp.org .

We have approved the final list of master classes:
  • WebCamp workshops: DesignersDay, July 22.
    • Delicious layout composition and the magic of owning color. Semyon Lobach.
    • Adam's rib or how cool logos are created from the air - without miracles and black magic. Alexander Tregub.
    Registration and all the details on the website at: bit.ly/1jdIkO0 .

  • Workshops from Ciklum Consulting / WebCamp: Project Management Day, July 24th.
    • Kanban pizza master class. Timofey Evgrashin and Mikhail Popchuk.
    • Tools workshop: the holy Jira + Agile family. Timofey Evgrashin and Mikhail Popchuk.
    Registration and all details on the website at: bit.ly/1xRIGgw .

  • Masterclass from E5 / WebCamp: Project Management Day, July 24th.
    • Management of requirements on the project: from idea to a working product. Prikhnich Alena and Sakharov Roman
    Registration and all the details on the website at: bit.ly/1xRIQoe .

  • CSM certification class with Nataly Trenina, July 24-25.
    Certified ScrumMaster is one of the certification levels of training for the international organization ScrumAlliance. SCRUMguides and Natalia Trenina invite you to receive a prestigious certificate, pleasure and benefit from a rich interactive training program.
    Registration and all the details on the website at: bit.ly/1zparPo .

  • Master class by Ruslan Shevchenko / WebCamp: Developer Day, July 25-26.
    • Introduction to the development of web applications on Play / Scala. Ruslan Shevchenko.
    Registration and all details on the website at: bit.ly/1oBkg5A .
