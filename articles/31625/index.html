Impressive home networks

Many have a computer at home, some even have a few. And there are those who have a data center at home.

We at Pingdom discussed the home network of one of our employees, who recently redid it and added a bunch of funny things to it. We thought his network was cool, until we found these networks.

Here is a list of our favorites:

Cute layout

This scheme looks great and it looks like the network covers every nook and cranny of the house.



Rack in the garage

Not everyone in the garage has 42 unit server racks and a high-speed VPN connection to the office.



Two providers and a bunch of computers

All the gadgets in this house are connected to cable and ADSL Internet.



Excellent floor-by-floor layout

Clear floor layout of all computers in the house.



The house that has everything

It seems that in this house everything is controlled through the network.



Wireless IP camera in the barn

If you have a barn, it should have a wireless IP camera.



Wires and servers

Some servers in the office are backed up.



Technowizard Network

These servers will cover the needs of a small company.



Two racks with servers

This is more like a hosting company, not a home network.



House filled with Apples

Any Apple hardware is allowed in this house.
