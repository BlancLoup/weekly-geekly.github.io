September 29, we invite you to view AzureCon and meet with experts at Microsoft Technology Center

Dear developers and IT specialists!

We invite you to the online conference AzureCon, which will be held on September 29 at 19:00 (MSK).



At the conference, you will learn about the latest innovations and simple Microsoft Azure solutions from leading architects and engineers.
You can register here .

If you are in Moscow, we have prepared for you an offline event, where an hour before the official start of the conference you will have the opportunity to hear local news from professionals in the field of Microsoft cloud technologies: Alexander Belotserkovsky, Zhanna Krivozubova, Sergey Nagorny, Andrey Vyavtkina - experts on Azure from Microsoft Russia. Then there will be a joint viewing of key-note lectures, and according to the results you will be able to exchange views with colleagues.

View held at Microsoft Technology Center, st. Lesnaya, 9, collection of guests at 18:00 (MSK).
Registration is required.

AzureCon is:

The opportunity to learn about the latest innovations and easily implemented Azure solutions from experts in this field. Listen to customers who take the stage to tell their stories. Take part in question and answer sessions live with architects and engineers who create the latest features. Choose from more than 50 technical sessions and accelerate your move to the cloud.

Access to experts. Get unimpeded access to Azure engineers and community members. Get the latest information from Scott Guthrie, Jason Zander, Bill Staples, Mark Russinovich, Scott Hanselman and others.

Customers go on stage. Listen to the stories of start-up companies and businesses that accelerate, differentiate and transform their business using the cloud. Find out what they are going to do with Azure. And also find out what you can do.

Customizable learning. Choose from more than 50 technical videos and learn about new features and existing Azure features. Whether you are just taking the first steps or you need a deep dive, you will find what you need.
