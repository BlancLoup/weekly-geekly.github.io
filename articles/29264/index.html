Alert system for shops and auctions

There is a series of projects proposed for the introduction of online stores, off-line stores, auctions and their developers, i.e. Actually implemented together with SMS. Traffic SMS services - notification of a change in the price of a product or when it is available, SMS notification of a change in the price of a product / lot. It seems to be useful for all participants services (if interesting, the description is given below), they do not oblige to anything - but there was a particular influx of people who wanted to connect them despite the placement of relevant information on specialized resources to Revolt.ru and Mobile content was not observed. I would like to know your opinion on the causes of this phenomenon.

SMS notification of changes in the price of the goods or the appearance of its sale. As is known, information on the product (photo) and its current value are placed on the Internet shop windows. Many users at the time of viewing are not ready to buy goods at current value, but they usually know that the price should decrease. This is especially true for the trade of mobile phones and computer equipment. The service is that the subscriber sends a paid SMS request with the code of the item of interest in the selected store, and when the price changes (or when it is available), he receives a message about it. The subscriber will be able to set a threshold for the price of the goods, upon reaching which he will receive a message. Also, the service will allow the customer to inform about the appearance in the sale of goods that he put on "waiting" in case of his temporary absence. It also means that the store will be able to place information in the off-line stores or on the website about a product that they currently do not have for sale at all, but which is popular and which they can search for. Placing next to him the offer to use this service, shops will be able to assess the demand for this product (ie, a kind of marketing), and return occasional visitors who searched for it if it is available for sale. Thus, the service will contribute to the preservation and return for the purchase of real customers, as well as allow you to study and take into account the expectations of potential buyers of stores.
