Playstation 4 will not be introduced in June

Despite the fact that representatives of some online resources say about the future June announcement of the Playstation 4, Wii U and next-generation XboX, Sony representatives think differently. Recently, the management of this company said that the Playstation 4 will not be announced, and indeed, this year's announcement of this gaming console should not be expected. PS3, according to Kazuo Hirai, will be relevant for a long time, since this game console is designed for about 10 years.

Experts believe that the PS4 is unlikely to be presented earlier than 2013, and many have doubts about this. Hirai also said that PS4 will not be presented in June with a 100% probability. Actually, this may be an ingenious course of this company, but it may well be that this is true, and then indeed, no one will see PS of a new generation in the next couple of years.

Previously, one of the online publications, Digitimes, reported that trial versions of the PS4 were ordered at the end of 2011. These consoles have a more productive configuration, plus a built-in controller, in addition to everything. Most likely, the new console will produce such well-known companies such as Pegatron and Foxconn. Sony, among other things, said that the new consoles will support both 3D and HD video, and more.

In general, it is rather difficult to say something based on the available information. But most likely, the new game console will not be presented yet. The output of PS4 is most likely in 2016, maybe in 2015 - but not earlier.
