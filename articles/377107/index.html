To blow or not to blow?

The essence of the experiment is simple as a door. In two identical glasses the same amount of water is poured, equal to the temperature. Two identical sensors (DS18B20) transmit data to a computer, where the temperature is plotted as graphs. Blowing one of the glasses - we fix the changes.
