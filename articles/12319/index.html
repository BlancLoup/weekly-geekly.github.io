Receive payments online.

We have already registered SPD, now we need to configure acceptance of payments from clients. The most popular in Ukraine are WebMoney, Internet.Money, bank transfer and of course credit cards.

Let's start with WebMoney. In order to receive automatic payments, you must obtain a personal certificate. The easiest way to do this is to meet with the attester. Before this, on the WebMoney site you must truthfully fill in information about yourself (passport data, identification code, etc.), select an attester and pay him 10 WMZ. You must fill out an application with the attester and bring in copies of the documents. Further Internet.Money. To connect this payment system you need to sign contracts and go through a non-trivial procedure. This payment system is not as popular as WebMoney, so I decided to abandon it and connect to RuPay. RuPay is a universal system that accepts 20 types of payments, including Internet.Money, Yandex.Money and many others. Just as with WebMoney, you need to pass certification, it is carried out in exactly the same way.

Further bank transfer. To do this, we use the previously opened bank account, now you just need to connect to the client-bank if you want to process incoming payments promptly. Of course, it is better to have an accountant, but you need to earn more.

And the last - credit cards. Acceptance of credit cards provides quality 2 offices: portmone.com.ua and privatbank.com.ua. PrivatBank makes the connection for free, the procedure is quite tricky: a bunch of documents, calls, correspondence. You can connect in 2 weeks, the previously opened account makes life easier. Technical implementation requires an SSL certificate. I tried to connect free - did not fit, then decided not to bother and buy the one that they offer for $ 20 a year. Not every hosting supports SSL, for example, on a very good mirohost.net package with SSL costs $ 322.84 per year, not very cheap. Therefore, I recommend FreeHost.com.ua to everyone, there are no problems with it yet and SSL support in the package for $ 27 !!! in year. The second option is portmone.com.ua. Connection costs - 500 UAH, duration - 3 weeks, the procedure is much simpler, no SSL is needed. I recommend this method.

All now we can safely receive money from customers and fulfill their obligations.
