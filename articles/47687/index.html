Domestic flash player

I have long used the player produced by http://flv-mp3.com/, which was distinguished by simplicity and elegance, but just now received a letter with the news about the release of a new version of their wonderful player. My description may seem too emotional, but this is only because the player is a bomb!

First of all, the demo site is http://uppod.info/ . After an hour of clashes with the settings, I can assure you that all the demosite chips are implemented by the player with a bang.


In numbers

  • 1 player for video and audio;
  • the weight of the player is about 70 kb;
  • more than 100 basic settings and options;

Player loses


Video standard Flash video (H.263) MPEG-4 (H.264 - HD)
Video formats Flv F4V, MP4, MOV, M4A, MP4V, 3GP and 3G2
audio AAC (HE, LC)
Video codecs On2 VP6, Sorenson Spark H.264
Audio codecs MP3 (11, 22, 44 kHz)
Flash Player Version any from 9.0.115.0

Main features

  • support for playlists of various configurations;
  • the ability to hide direct links to files using encoding;
  • settings and playlists separate files;
  • multilanguage interface;
  • ability to choose the composition of the control panel
  • (more than 20 buttons and indicators can be installed in any sequence);
  • custom tips;
  • full screen for video;
  • support for streaming and pseudo-continuous loading;
  • The interface does not have our advertising;
  • is free.

Features playlists (new feature)

  • location - inside, right, bottom row and column;
  • You can use a picture with a hint or just text;
  • You can set the size of elements and colors;
  • You can break the playlist into pages.
In addition, it is possible to communicate with the player via Javascript.

Distribution model


The player is distributed free of charge, however, it is possible to slightly expand its functionality by getting a pro account. I was pleased that you can pay absolutely any price, almost as it was when you bought the album In Rainbows from Radiohead.

So, register , collect your player, use!
