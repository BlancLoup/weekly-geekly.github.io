HP Netbook Trio



HP has added three new models to its line of netbooks - the Mini 1101, Mini 110 Mi and Mini 110 XP. By and large, these are three versions of the same model.

Mini 1101 - Win XP or Vista, 1 GB of memory and 160 GB or 32 GB SSD hard drive, no additional video accelerator;
Mini 110 Mi - Ubuntu Linux, the ability to expand up to 2 GB of brains and installation of the hard drive to 250 GB;
Mini 110 XP - Win XP, 1 GB of memory and 160 GB or 32 GB SSD hard drive.

The remaining characteristics are as follows:

Monitor: 10 inches, 1024x600 pixels, LED backlight;
Processor: 1.6 GHz Intel Atom N270 or 1.66GHz N280;
Interfaces: VGA-out, 802.11b / g, 3 USB-out, headphone jack;
Optional: 3G-modem and Broadcom Crystal HD Enhanced Video Accelerator (responsible for unpacking HD-video, including 1080p);

Interesting prices. In the States netbooks will be sold for

Mini 1101 - $ 329 - on sale from June 1;
Mini 110 Mi - $ 279 - on sale from June 10;
Mini 110 XP - $ 329 - on sale from June 10.

The glamorous pink and silver versions of netbooks will only be released on the market on July 8.
