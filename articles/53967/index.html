Timeline for the release of Diablo III

There is information about the timing of the release of Diablo III. True, the information is unofficial, but the source is close to Blizzard. So, the release date is December 24, 2009, and public beta testing will begin on September 11, 2009.

System requirements:
Operating system: Windows XP / Vista
Processor: 1.4GHz and higher
RAM: 1GB and higher
Video: 128MB video card that supports DirectX 9
Free disk space: 4-8GB

For Mac:
OS: MacOS X 10.3 (Panther) and better
Processor: 1.6GHz and higher
RAM: 1Gb and higher
Video: 128mb ATI or NVIDIA video card
Free disk space: 4-8 GGB.

Source in English and in Russian .
