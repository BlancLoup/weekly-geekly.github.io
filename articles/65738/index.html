Nikon Announces Four New Products

Today, Nikon announced four new products, two cameras and two lenses.

Nikon D300s

Nikon D3000

Nikon AF-S NIKKOR 70-200mm f / 2.8G ED VR II

Nikon AF-S DX NIKKOR 18-200mm f / 3.5-5.6G ED VR II


1. Professional camera - Nikon D300s at a suggested price of $ 1799.95 (will be available in stores, but unlikely in ours :), from August 2009). Already earned a mini site for the product, and you can also pre-order the camera on Amazon.com .

2. The entry-level camera - Nikon D3000 at a suggested price of $ 599.95 (also will be available in August 2009). Mini product website and pre-order page on Amazon.com .

3. Nikon AF-S NIKKOR 70-200mm f / 2.8G ED VR II lens - rec. The price is $ 2399.95 (November 2009).

4. Nikon AF-S DX NIKKOR 18-200mm f / 3.5-5.6 ED VR II lens - rec. price of $ 849.95 (September 2009).
