A little bit more about drift control:

Question: What is Drift Control and how does it affect accuracy?
Answer: Drift control is a prediction mechanism in mouse sensors that helps users build straight vertical / horizontal lines in graphics and work applications. These sensors predict when the straight line is deflected and correct the movement to compensate for the deviation, rather than precisely following the exact movement of the user's hand. This means that the deviation down by 5 degrees can be completely corrected to a horizontal line, as a result of which the player will not be able to capture the target.
