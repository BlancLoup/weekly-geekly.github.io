Lenovo invites you to a Formula 1 race car show in Moscow



On July 18, a unique Bavaria Moscow City Racing car show will take place in the very center of Moscow. It will be attended by the legendary team Vodafone McLaren Mercedes Formula 1, it will represent the current world champion Jenson Button. Since 2009, Lenovo has been the official technical partner of Vodafone McLaren Mercedes. Lenovo's laptops, desktops and powerful workstations are used by the team both on the racetrack and at headquarters, and are involved in all operations, from design and engineering to telemetry during races.

Lenovo invites everyone to take part in this unusual event. And those users who already have a Lenovo laptop or stationary PC can get one of four tickets to the show for free. To do this, just leave a photo of your Lenovo computer in the comments to this topic. You can also leave a review about your laptop or desktop computer: surely other users will be interested to know the opinion of the owner. Winners, authors of the most original and unusual photos, will be announced on Thursday, July 15th. Good luck!

UPD. Many thanks to all participants for photos and wonderful reviews. Winners, check your private messages please :)
