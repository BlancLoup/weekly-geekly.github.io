Rumors: Canon will introduce a new DSLR camera on January 24


The Swedish site Kamerabild, citing informed sources, reports that Canon is preparing to present a new DSLR model in anticipation of the PMA 08 trade show opening on January 31.

We can only assume in which line the new camera will be presented, but most likely it will be Digital Rebel XTi (EOS 400D) or EOS 5D because both these models have not changed for a long time.

If you have not had time to ask Santa Claus about the new camera, I think you should be patient and see what Canon gets out of its bins.
