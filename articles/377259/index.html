Google opens new spa-style office in Budapest



Google Inc., like some other major techno companies, is trying to shape its offices so that employees feel freer. Roughly speaking, the company tries to avoid the standard design of offices, where many people sit at the same tables, use the same technique and feel the same.

The original design (it is developed taking into account the wishes of specialists in ergonomics, doctors and other professionals) contributes to improving performance, improves the general mood. With all this, all the offices of Google - are different. This is the office, opened by a corporation in Budapest , it was issued in the style of a spa. Employees sit in the pool, and work - in massage rooms. The office design project was developed by Graphasel Design Studio .

Probably, this decision was made because Budapest is famous for its terms, baths, saunas and baths. Lovers of spa and water treatments here are just paradise.

In addition to design, materials are important - so, the condition of the corporation when creating this office was the use of recycled materials (obtained as a result of recycling, or simply remnants of various kinds of structures).
