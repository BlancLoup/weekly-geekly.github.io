C ++ User Group, meeting in Kazan May 28, 2016


Hello!

We have great news for C ++ developers of Tatarstan: on May 28, a meeting of the Russian community C ++ User Group Russia will take place in Innopolis

We have long dreamed of holding a meeting in Kazan, and we hope that this meeting will be the first and not the last. As always, we hope that this will be a wonderful occasion for developers from other cities to visit Kazan.

Who follows and participates in the life of the community is well aware that we always strive to form the program with reports from developers from different cities of Russia. Under the cut a brief about the speakers and reports.

Maxim Khizhinsky, Zodiac Interactive (St. Petersburg). Maxim is one of the most famous lock-free data structures in Russia. His posts on Habr√© (@khizmax), devoted to various sides of this topic, enjoy great attention and popularity. In Kazan, he will give a talk about iterators.

The report will consider iterators for various lock-free structures.

The second speaker of the program - Pavel Beloborodov - the developer of the Novosibirsk team Yandex. Browser.

In his report, we will discuss multithreading in applications. How to enter one river (function) twice and get off dry. Pavel will tell how to linearly implement scenarios with asynchronous operations and use resources rationally during the waiting period. And how to add to all this syntactic sugar.

Paul plans to present this library in June on Github and promises that it will be useful to developers who are faced with the need for asynchronous operations in their work.

As always, participants will have the opportunity to communicate with speakers and colleagues. After the presentations, we will discuss the need for the development of the C ++ developer community in Kazan. And for those who wish, there will be an excursion around the Technopolis Innopolis.

The program of the event.
11.30 Registration
12.00 Maxim Hizhinsky, Lock-free data structures: iterators
13.00 Pavel Beloborodov, Library for asynchronous programming
14.00 Informal communication and excursion in Innopolis Park (for those who wish).

Participation is free, but preliminary registration is required https://c-user-group-russia.timepad.ru/event/328752/

See you later!
