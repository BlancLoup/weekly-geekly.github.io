Subtleties of pronunciation: Linuh or Linax

Something got me long ago, that I write the name of this OS as “LinAx”. Affirms that it is necessary to say "Linuks". In my last post (a copy on Habré), the people even gave a proof of Linus' sound recording, where he explains how the name “Linax” should be pronounced. Comrades, it seems, are so coded that they no longer hear, but only believe. In general, I listened and it sounds like “LinAks”, not “Linux”. Listen for yourself: www.paul.sladen.org/pronunciation

If you still have doubts, compare how he says “U” in “Linus” (his name) and “A” in “LinAx”. Not even close. In general, here I am with Linus at the same time :-) In the end, Ipse Dixit!
