Master class "How not to make a mistake with the choice of IT-specialist"

And our InCube quietly and peacefully continues a series of educational and application activities open for both residents of the business incubator and for everyone. Only yesterday, the Winter School finished its work, in the framework of which the experts collaborating with us helped the projects to prepare for the selection in the SpringBoard. And here is a new initiative - a series of master classes by Sergey Boronin on the selection of IT specialists for the project, which will be useful for almost any manager or startup. The first meeting will be held on February 22. Registration is now open.

What will be discussed


- how to advertise vacancies to attract the right people and weed out office plankton;

- criteria for the selection of resumes, to pay attention;

- specifics of interviewing IT-specialists;

- multistage interviews;

- Tricks resorted by applicants;

- general hiring issues

About participation


Registration is required. For residents of the incubator participation is free, for other students the price is 1250 rubles.

About lecturer


Sergey has been working in the IT industry since 1995. The last 10 years - as a project manager and IT-director in large companies and integrators. Information security expert and consultant on project management, software design and development.

Time and place


The master class will be held on February 22, 2011 from 19:00 to 22:00 on the campus of the Russian Academy of National Economy and Public Administration under the President of the Russian Federation at the address: Yugo-Zapadnaya metro station, Vernadsky Ave. , Bldg. 1, audience 502.
