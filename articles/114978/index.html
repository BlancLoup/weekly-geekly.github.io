.Smallian pirates

Thousand devils! It turns out that in Somalia there are not only world-famous robber-pirates, but also their own domain zone .SO. Moreover, this zone was created for a long time - back in 1997, but due to the political situation in the Republic it was not used. It was even rumored that the domain could be given away to South Ossetia. But in 2009, IANA decided to transfer the domain zone .SO to the Ministry of Communications and Communications of Somalia.

April 1 (jokes aside!) At 3:00 Moscow time in the zone .SO, open registration will begin for everyone. REG.RU is already accepting preliminary applications for registration of the .SO domains from the most enterprising users. All applications will be sent for execution to the registry of the domain zone at the time of the start of open registration. As always, strictly observing the principle of the first application, REG.RU accepts only one application for one domain name and does not conduct auctions among users.

Why do we need another exotic domain, when there is a favorite and familiar .COM and .RU? Yes, if only because interesting semantic names can be registered in the .SO domain, as in transliteration - kole.so, mya.so, las.so, lit.so, and in English, for example, is- that-really.so.
The intelligent REG.Premium service will help you to verify the capabilities of the .SO zone and find a successful name (or even a few) thanks to its unique filter system and various subject headings.
