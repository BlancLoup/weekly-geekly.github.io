YouTube will appear paid view

Until now, YouTube was an absolutely free service, and 100% of all materials on it could be viewed for free. It was assumed that the profit will be obtained exclusively from advertising. It was a business model and a principled position. Now there is no.

Google has announced that it plans to develop a micropayment mechanism for the YouTube site so that it can show paid video with a pay-per-view payment model.

The president of the company, Eric Schmidt, announced this at a conference on summing up the financial results of Q1. 2009

Most likely, paid shows will be introduced primarily for feature films of high resolution.
