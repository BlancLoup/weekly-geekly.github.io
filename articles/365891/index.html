Of course, unreal, it's just a pretty model. Generally speaking, Fallout fans now have something to rejoice in: a new part of the game has been announced, plus a mobile version for the iPhone (here you can sigh for Android-device owners), plus now also Fallout Anthology.

id Software is hosting its annual Quakecon in Dallas, and now more interesting points are presented at the event than before. This was made possible thanks to the involvement of Bethesda.

"Bomb" will not cost so much - only $ 49.99. Sales will start from September 29, first in North America, and then in Europe, starting October 2. Inside the "bombs" will be placed in a slim DVD-packaging. Each DVD contains a separate part of the game. By the way, the bomb will emit a beep if you press on its "nose." All parts are PC only.

Fallout 3 and Fallout: New Vegas are the final versions (DLC, Game of the Year and Ultimate, respectively). There are Fallout, Fallout 2, and Fallout Tactics in the collection. Interestingly, Fallout 4 itself is not in the collection, but there is room for those who want to add this game to the entire collection.

All of these games will be sold on Steam, at a price of $ 41.75 per set. It turns out that the "bomb" costs only $ 8.25. Soon the opportunity to pre-order the "nuclear" version starts.

As always, all other details are available on bethesda.net .
