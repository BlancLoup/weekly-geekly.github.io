Fitness by mail


The world-famous sports brand Nike used unsophisticated but effective tactics to increase the focus on its Nike + share. The essence of the action is that each participant involved in running in the sneakers of the company can use a special miniature device that tracks the distance traveled and calories burned. Information can be transferred to the Nike + site, where the user will be available statistics on the races. E-mails confirming this operation contain direct links, a click on which will post the route and other race data on Twitter or Facebook.
