Instant bonus from QIWI Wallet and MasterCard

Like it or not, but it's time for New Year's shopping: Christmas trees, garlands, gifts. QIWI Wallet and MasterCard decided to enter this New Year's confusion with a pleasant gift to users: paying for purchases from 500 rubles with a MasterCard card tied to your Wallet you instantly receive 5% of the amount on your QIWI Wallet. The promotion is valid until 23:59 December 31, 2011, so there is still time to receive bonuses.

Thousands of stores and providers take part in the promotion. For obvious reasons, the purse replenishment operations, the purchase of QVV, Free payments, replenishment of payment systems, money transfers between users, and banking services are not involved in the promotion.

By the way, in some cases it turns out even a double benefit - for this promotion, MasterCard pays even those providers for which it was in ordinary life. A good example with the payment of utility services: in case of ordinary payment by card, the commission ranges from 0% to 0.75% (depending on the issuing bank). Under the terms of the promotion, firstly, this commission will not be, and secondly there will be a 5% bonus. OK!


Obviously, the larger the payment amount, the greater the bonus. You can even pay your rent to your office colleagues. You collect ten people who wish - from each you get 5% to your Wallet.
