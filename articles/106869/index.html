WiMax on MacBook

How realistic is it to use WiMax on a MacBook? Of course, you can carry a WiMax-WiFi router with you, but this is not very ergonomic. Another thing is USB modems. But are they compatible? This is what we decided to check by taking the two most compact USB modems from two different WiMax operators.



At the weekend I went to my friend who has a MacBook. He risked his laptop, and I his modems. As a result, not a single device was damaged, although I had to worry. We filmed the entire process of connecting a USB modem to a MacBook on the IPhone in HD quality.

I would be grateful for any suggestions for improving the test itself and videos.

PS: I have been using WiMax, 3G, CDMA for a long time. I have a lot of different modems from different operators. Ask me questions on this topic, something I can even protest specifically for habroys!
