May 19. Bit Byte - Festival of IT Professional Development in St. Petersburg

Good day, habrachiteli!



We are glad to remind you that the #BitByte festival will take place tomorrow on Saturday - May 19!

Racks registration of visitors will work all day from 9-30 am to 18 hours.

Choose interesting activities for yourself among IT-classes, job fairs, HR-performances and competitions from companies, and come at a convenient time for you!

In addition to the job fair where you can chat and get "access to the body and the brain" of technical managers of Livejournal, Mail.ru Group, Oracle, Motorola Mobility, EMC, EPAM, Motorola Solutions and other Top companies.
