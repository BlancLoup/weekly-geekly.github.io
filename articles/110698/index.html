Acer Liquid Metal. Tests stylish news. Video

In early 2011, the Acer Liquid Metal smartphone will be released in Russia.
A little later, it will appear officially in the stores of Ukraine. There is no information about other CIS countries yet.

What has changed since the first Liquid?
Outside:
  • Metal body parts
  • The best build quality (disappeared backlash)
  • New, Stricter Design
  • Moved speaker, and in its place there was a flash

Inside:
  • More powerful processor and graphics accelerator
  • Support for non-converted HD video and codecs
  • Dolby Digital Sound
  • Increased battery with a more economical processor
  • Updated touch sensing sensor

Communications:
  • Bluetouth 3.0
  • WiFi (b / g / n)


Anyone who wants to look at the work of the smartphone, welcome to the habrokat!


Multi-touch recognition technology is one of the most important in working with modern touch devices. Multi-touch in Acer Liquid Metal is made head and shoulders above its predecessor.


Gaming features Acer Liquid Metal on top. Despite the minimum lead over its predecessor based on benchmark results, this smartphone, without any brakes, displays any games on Android.


Quadrant Benchmark shows smartphone performance on various types of tasks (from 3D graphics to working with memory). The result of a non-overclocked smartphone differs from the first Liquid by only 120 points.


Convenience of working in the network is one of the important criteria for choosing a modern smartphone. How comfortable is it to sit in a browser and watch online video? I hope our video will give you a comprehensive answer!
