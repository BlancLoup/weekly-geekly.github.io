In the tincture of the display is found a curious item - MiraVision. This is a proprietary technology from MediaTek, the chipset developer on which Just5 Blaster is based. MiraVision increases the clarity of the image when displaying various multimedia content on the screen. This is achieved by adjusting the intensity of the backlight of the display depending on the brightness of the surrounding space and the color gamut of the content displayed on the display. This saves the battery charge of the device, plus the user's vision is less tired when watching movies or 3D gaming for a long time. For example, if the room is dark, then MiraVision technology will automatically reduce the backlight intensity. The light will hit less in the eye, and the entertainment of the game will not suffer from this.



However, MiraVision offers a set of tools to adjust the screen settings - sharpness, color temperature, and so on. All this is available in the "user" mode, while there are two options with fixed settings: "standard" and "bright."
