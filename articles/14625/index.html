Miranda IM 0.7

The new release of the multi-pager Miranda IM. A lot of various changes in all modules of the program. The most notable innovations: support for the Gadu-Gadu protocol, improved support for Unicode, now messages are stored in UTF-8, a centralized font manager, improved management of avatars, the ability to customize the menu ...

In my opinion, the most worthy replacement for all Internet paging systems put together. If you need minimalism, basic delivery without additional plug-ins is an ideal choice. If you want to build your own, completely unique messenger with a bunch of additional features and original appearance, then hundreds of plug-ins and skins are at your service.

In general: the program has a small distribution size, high speed, support for all popular Internet paging systems, a fully customizable interface and, most importantly, plug-in support, thanks to which you can significantly extend the functionality of the program.
