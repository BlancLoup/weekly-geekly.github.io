Is there a perfect hosting?

As I caught my host for the language ...


3 years ago, my internet activity somehow intensified and I needed a stable shared-hosting with a good support and a humane administrator who would not drink liters for every little thing. I found it. all 3 years I keep with him my own and client projects and am satisfied. this is an introduction.

In a semi-private conversation with the hoster I wanted to podnachit him. I say that the Germans who use the services of hosting companies are terrible people who frantically pay 5 Euros per month for packages in which safe mode is included. And in Russia, the hoster would have spat in such an immoral offer in person ... Word for word, the conversation took the following form: I decided to interview people interested in availability, various configurations and prices of the ideal hosting, and the hoster would implement such an offer. If the implementation takes place, I undertook to the hoster to translate its free page with proposals and documentation from German into Russian. An ideal hosting, by the way, is not at all obliged to have commercial success - this idea is rather an attempt to prove the possibility of offering an ideal service without losses.
