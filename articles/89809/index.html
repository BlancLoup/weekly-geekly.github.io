HTC has launched an internal release of firmware 2.1 for HTC hero and HTC magic

Today is April 2nd. As I wrote , the release of firmware 2.1 with the support of HTC Sense UI took place.
Yes, only those dates that leaked to the network did not mean public availability of the update, but, as I understand it, the receipt of the finished firmware by the regional offices of the company. About this tells in his twitter HTC_ru.

Now I want to understand what awaits us, and when the long-awaited update is finally available. Again, according to information from Twitter update will be implemented Over-the-Air (literally translated by air). This means that the phone will be flashed without using a computer and additional software. And of course before updating you will see a warning message.

Actually, in order for the firmware update to be painless, HTC takes time to prepare and test. Hopefully, with the exception of the actual firmware itself, everything else on HTC was ready in advance (FOTA servers are set up and tech support is trained :))

If this is indeed the case, then soon all those who are thirsty will receive a long-awaited update.
