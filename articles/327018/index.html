Welcome to VeloCPPed Meetup in Tensor


On April 27, 2017 at 19.00 we invite C ++ developers to the VeloCPPed Meetup at the head office of the Tenzor company in Yaroslavl on ul. Uglich 36/44

Let's talk about the industrial development of large C ++ applications - we will share the experience of managing a huge code base (2 million SLOC) and the process of its creation. We will understand how to speed up and reduce the cost of product development.

Meetup program



- Management of external dependencies in C ++ - Evgeny Boytsov, Tensor
A story full of pain and suffering about the techniques of connecting third-party libraries to C ++ projects, ensuring the unification of work under different platforms.
- Cloud in pants - Alexey Konovalov, Tensor
Experience in creating cross-platform mobile applications in C ++.
- how to make a mobile application with a single code base;
- how to generalize mobile application code with desktop or server solutions.
- Designer for adults - Alexander Konovalov, Tenzor
Experience creating modular desktop applications with a graphical interface.
- reuse code;
- use of web-technologies for the development of the graphical interface of "thick" applications;
- a generalization of the technological process of "cloud" and "desktop" development.

Gathering guests: 18:30
Start meetup: 19:00
End at about 9 pm

Registration for a meetup is right here . It is necessary for you not to be left without a place - do not neglect this formality.

Come early. You can relax after the road right in our office: several cafes, a restaurant, a lobby with sofas, billiards, a kicker, board games, and ping-pong. You can even take a walk in the garden on our roof.
Or look at our city and visit a couple of institutions (gastronomic, cultural or barbarian - choose yourself).

The road from Moscow will take about 3 hours by car. And the same on the train.
