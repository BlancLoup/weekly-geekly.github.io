MNP



MNP (Mobile Number Portability) is a number transfer service when the cellular operator changes, which has long existed in the developed cellular communication markets all over the world. Its prevalence on average is about 5 percent (1). In Russia, there are predictions that it will be from 1.5 to 6%.

Talk about the introduction of this service in Russia has been underway for more than a year, but what do we have today? At best, operators promise readiness to receive ‚Äúalien‚Äù subscribers from the middle of January. At the same time warn about the consequences - the connection may break, the phones explode, and the message does not reach. Do they have reasons to be so afraid of this law?

We believe there is. Serious battles unfold between the Big Three to cover the new generation LTE Internet networks. Rostelecom gives mobile assets under the management of Tele2 and together they plan to occupy 25% of the cellular communication market (2). Venture environment is preparing a draft of a free virtual operator Atlas on the model of freemium (3).

In the coming years, the mobile market is expected to make big changes, and we take on the task of making it as transparent and affordable for the subscriber as possible. How are we going to do this?

Dr. service Tariff



Our Android-based application analyzes data on the communication profile directly from the smartphone and selects the optimal tariff based on more than 2000 tariffs in all regions of the Russian Federation. The project received the approval and support of President Putin himself (4). In addition to the tariff optimizer, we will use the base of the transferred numbers to display the operator and the region during calls.
