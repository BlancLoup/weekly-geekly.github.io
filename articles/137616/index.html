List Lists

Thematic lists are a legendary feature of Wikipedia. What is there just not there: the list of dragons and the list of integrals , the list of scientific pairs and the list of solar eclipses visible from Ukraine .

There are thousands of these lists, some of them even lists of other lists. But now Wikipedia has come out but a new degree of enlightenment: a list of lists has appeared in the table of contents. What is great is that it contains not only different lists of lists, but also itself.



(The list of lists that do not contain themselves is maintained separately). It seems a little more - and Wikipedia will know nirvana.
