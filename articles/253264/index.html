General situation


The story is this: at the dawn of network development, we contacted these regions via satellite (in fact, even now a fairly large number of points work through orbital jumps). The satellite is not the fastest channel and the resource is rather limited. Therefore, with the development of 3G networks, new trunk lines were required. If the central part of Russia was covered by optics rather quickly, then it was here that the large traffic became commercially justified only in the last 5-6 years. And we just came with our rings to the region of the Far East.


The usual picture of the construction, the PTA on the reel on the orange car. The machine is called a "plow."

Inside, we began to build new lines, exchange our colleagues' lines, buy existing branches, enter into long-term lease agreements with other backbone providers. For example, in the region there are a lot of good lines of Rostelecom (here are the skinshots from their site):



When Rostelecom builds the line, as a rule, the entire big three operators follow the project with interest (lease negotiations are often under way).

Specific points of interest


From Krasnoyarsk to Irkutsk, we have a DWDM (dense wave compression, 88 channels per branch) - more than 100 Gbit / s per wavelength. The construction of the network continues from Irkutsk, we plan to finish it up to Khabarovsk this year. Technically, the line is ready to Chita, now is commissioning. In April, we plan to launch.


Once again, the map so as not to flip through

Further from Chita to Khabarovsk - under construction, we plan to finish in November-December of this year (if there are no surprises). Khabarovsk-Vladivostok - one line exists. We are planning the second backup line by summer: we are building our own, because now the backup channel is leased.

Now look at the section from Krasnoyarsk to Kansk, conditionally designated by two arcs. Physically, the two lines there are a few hundred kilometers of the highway (two physically separated routes).

Nearby you can see one more part of the ring on Bratsk, it is being built now, the delivery is planned in the summer.
