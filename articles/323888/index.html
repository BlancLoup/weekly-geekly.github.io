MBLT17: first speakers, latest early bird tickets



On April 25, the International Mobile Conference MBLT17 will be held in Moscow.

Over the six-year history, MBLT has become a landmark event in the Russian mobile. This year, we continue to acquaint conference participants with top representatives of the industry, talk about mobile trends and explore successful strategies.
Under the cut - details about the program and the latest early bird tickets!

Reports


More than a dozen speakers have already confirmed their participation in MBLT17.


Rudi de Veyle , futurologist, consultant and strategist in the field of innovation, will give a keynote address. Rudy predicts technological trends, analyzes ideas for achieving success in new economic conditions. His clients include BMW, IBM, Coca-Cola, Google, Intel, Louis Vuitton, and other global brands.

Klemen Chang , CEO and founder of Spiking, a Singapore-based startup, will talk about trends in Asian technology and share experiences in creating an application for trading.

How to achieve intensive business growth using mobile applications will tell Alexei Vvestsky from App Annie. The report will be devoted to the effective use of the mobile channel, together with Alexey we will consider the cases of world brands, whose strategies should be noted.

Artyom Keidunov , co-founder of a successful startup Statsbot, will tell you how a conversational UI changes our interaction with computers and why chat bots will first find their use in business, and only then will they enter the consumer segment.

Presentations will also be made by:
- Matt Jones, Manning Gottlieb,
- Maxim Evdokimov and Anna Mikhina, Tinkoff Bank,
- Andy Zmolek, Google,
- Dmitry Izmailov, Uber,
- Arseny Kosenko, Lifepay,
- Roman Kumar Vias, Qlean.

The main topics of MBLT17 will be:
- monetization strategies for mobile applications and mobile marketing,
- new and future IoT, VR, AI,
- the use of chat bots,
- trends in the design and design of mobile interfaces,
- successful mobile cases for different business areas.

Discussions


In the format of a lively discussion we will discuss the trends of fintech. The moderator of the round table, Igor Kostylev, will bring together representatives of banks to talk about trends in the creation of mobile financial products.
Another round table will be devoted to attracting users to mobile applications. What tools and trends have come to the fore in the past year, representatives of large mobile products will tell, Alexey Pisarevsky will be the moderator.

MBLT Talks


Last year, at the MBLT conference, we tried the format of an open interview, it came out lively, interesting and frank. This time we dig deeper. MBLT Talks presenter Ivan Kozlov from Aviasales will speak frankly with top managers of famous mobile products. No bright presentations and successful cases, just an honest conversation about achievements and mistakes, about industry and competition, about plans and doubts.

Networking


MBLT is a platform for effective networking. Tell about your projects, get acquainted with the leaders of the mobile industry and find like-minded people and partners. The conference annually brings together more than 700 participants: CEOs, product managers, marketers, designers, evangelists who work in mobile products, digital agencies, customized development, start-ups, all who already have their own mobile products or are just preparing to launch them.
We are looking forward to meeting old friends, meeting new ones, heated discussions and fans - after the official program, we traditionally wait for everyone on the grand after-party.

Until March 15, the ticket price is 3000 rubles. Register right now on the conference website .
