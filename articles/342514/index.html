How White Label helps in the development of services and products. 5 examples



Any partnership in business always has an individual character and its own prerequisites. Someone is trying to find new channels and ways of development, someone comes to integration with a partner not from a good life.

The ideal affiliate program is one that develops and complements sales. Building a business solely on a partnership is quite risky, since at the same time one of the companies can quickly become dependent on a partner and lose their goals and strategy. This article is about partnerships on the White Label model.

White Label is a relatively new concept. Will help to understand it 5 representatives of different businesses. These are services that have already implemented the White Label program and offer their partners mutually beneficial cooperation. Who and why can this be interesting? We understand together with the tour operator, SMM-service, anti-virus system and other products.

White Label is about mutual benefit.


White Label is a multi-company collaboration model. It implies the sale of services or products under the brand of the selling company and turns out to be the resources of another company. Today, such an interaction scheme is most often used by organizations with large client bases and a reputable brand in the market. In other cases, the model works less efficiently.

In Russian terminology, the term ‚Äú non-brand product ‚Äù or service is sometimes found. That is, the emphasis is again on the fact that the product is issued without a label and is offered to the retailer or distributor. The most common thing in business is the concept of White Label.

Why does business come to an affiliate program?


Everyone has their own reasons. In general, the prerequisites for using White Labeling are:

  • The company does not have a sufficient budget for marketing, and the product is worth it to be interesting to potential partners.
  • The company has a budget for marketing and there is time for that. But the sales potential has exhausted itself. The partnership in this case is a new breath, which does not change the direction of work, but perfectly complements and develops the business.
  • The company wants to enter a new market and beat the competition. Partners open up new opportunities and reach new audiences.
  • The company consists of developers and no one to do marketing and sales.

What are the benefits of White Label for partners?


Each White Label company offers its own conditions to partners. But more often, White Label implies the following benefits:

  • Quick start cooperation program. A partner can provide a new type of service in a short time and, thus, quickly increase competitiveness in the market. The time to develop and test a new product when using White Label is 2-5 times less than developing and testing your own solution.
  • Achieve new target audiences . The seller of the decision may receive additional income from the clients of the client and, thereby, enter new markets. The buyer can extend the functionality of the product and attract users to whom these features are important.
  • The possibility of integration . Often, off-the-shelf services can be easily adapted and embedded into existing partner platforms. For example, in billing or service delivery systems.
  • Ready infrastructure . A party acquiring a finished product does not need to ‚Äúinject‚Äù a budget into the initial stages of release and support, or pay for the work of additional specialists. The cost of self-development, as a rule, exceeds the purchase price of a turnkey solution by 2-7 times, depending on the region.
  • Additional support . Very often, partners are invited to support qualified professionals from the manufacturer. In the case of difficult situations, such an additional line will help to quickly provide an answer.
  • Reduced churn . Customer loyalty will increase if the service provider starts offering more of these same services and the customer does not need to look for other suppliers.
  • Branding The service acquired by the partner can be issued in its corporate style and can be installed on its domain names.
  • Flexible price . Typically, companies implementing White Label solutions offer flexible pricing and discounts. It depends on sales. The partner creates the final retail price independently for customers, and it is always negotiable.
  • The possibility of postpay . A partner can provide services and accept client payments, and after monthly pay for services upon use and with reference to the number of sales.
