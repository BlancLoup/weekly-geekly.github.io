Webinars from the Centos-admin.ru team



Hello, dear friends!

On behalf of the company Centos-admin.ru I announce the opening of our channel on Youtube and present you a cycle of webinars.

For more than eight years, we have accumulated experience in working with high-load projects, thousands of servers, and complex architectures, systematizing it.
Until now, only employees of our company had access to the accumulated knowledge.
Now we want to share this knowledge with colleagues and sympathizers.
Details under the cut.



The first cycle of webinars is scheduled for 5 lectures on the following topics:

  • Introductory lecture. About company. Configuration Management System. As we support a small number of more than a thousand cars.
  • Virtualization systems on personal experience.
  • Incremental backup. Backup as the basis of peace.
  • Methods of audit and acceleration of servers and projects.
  • Creating your own repository. Build packages.


Webinars are free. Duration about 30 minutes.
The level of training for students is arbitrary. If you saw the Linux console live and

We are waiting for you at our first webinar!
