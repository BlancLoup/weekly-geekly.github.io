Christmas app on Android

The New Year 2013 is approaching by leaps and bounds, and increasingly you want something snowy, New Year's on your mobile device. I hope that my application will satisfy these needs :)

Screenshots:


The purpose of the application - to arrange a snowfall on the screen of your device! It is possible to choose the amount of snow, the type of drawing (OGL / Canvas).

Anyone can download it on the XDA forum: forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?p=36067400 / Mediafire: www.mediafire.com/?c7j5e8xa5xpkwx1 , also, if someone is interested in publishing the code - the process of writing it.
