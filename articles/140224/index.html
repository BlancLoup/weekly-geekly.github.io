Antares mobile LTE network launched in test mode in Moscow (more than 80 megabits per second)

The Vedomosti newspaper in today's No. 48 (3062) reports that the Antares group of companies (owned by Yevgeny Roytman) launched its own LTE network in test mode in Moscow - and the Vedomosti correspondent was personally convinced that the access speed Internet access on this network exceeds eighty megabytes.

The network operates in the range of 1900-1920 MHz, which, according to the newspaper, is a novelty in Europe, although in the Chinese network "China Mobile" is quite common.

Antares promises to start commercial operation of the LTE network before the end of this year (2012).
