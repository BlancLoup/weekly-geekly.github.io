SchultzeWORKS: Romance of Recourse



Studio SchultzeWORKS introduced the concept of a personal computer Philco PC , which resembles the popular in the late 50s' epic TV Philco Predicta , whose models are still used in the gaming and film industries.



Under the cut screenshots and video


Philco Predicta PC is a rounded screen with an orange case, a keyboard with buttons stylized as an old typewriter, and a mouse resembling a pedal.



It remains to hope that this concept will go beyond the limits of graphic performance and will be implemented as a real device, even in the minimum, exclusive version. Since this product has already had its own audience for a long time, in the form of people who are bored with the classics in the new version, as well as people who prefer the post-apocalyptic rather than the high-tech style.





I, in turn, am confident that a significant part of the players in the fallout, will not spare money for the sake of owning such a PC, and in the event that this PC goes on sale, it will provoke for the purchase, restyling and visual modding of this device.
