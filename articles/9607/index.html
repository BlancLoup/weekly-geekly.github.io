Now ride me, Uncle Google

In addition to the interesting Street View feature, Google has a new one - now you can see the timetable and routes of public transport in selected (yet) cities of the world, this source tells us
The system is still severely curtailed, as it is based on public data on public transport provided in these cities, and does not always work as we would like. Nevertheless, in some cities you can see not only the traffic flow diagram, but also find out the schedule, which lines one or another bus stop serves, and so on. In addition, stops and stations can be found through the search line in Google Maps.
Another expected feature is the planning of land routes for local travelers.
