Bill Gates signed up on LinkedIn

A few days after the closure of his Facebook account, Bill Gates joined LinkedIn social network: it is the world's largest business social network, uniting 19 million people according to their business interests and professional experience (the Russian clone is My Circle ).

After registration, Bill Gates posted a question that was immediately posted to the LinkedIn Answers home page (along with the Windows Server 2008 advertising embedded in the message). Bill Gates asked how modern youth can be stimulated to do science and technology.

Now 22 hours have passed since the publication, and the number of responses has already exceeded 1,500 pieces. True, many users do not respond on the merits, but simply want to receive confirmation that they see the real Bill Gates before them, because there are more than a dozen people with such a name in any social network.

LinkedIn members have already begun to discuss how to get into friends with Bill Gates. He still has very few links on LinkedIn (it seems there are only three), so finding common contacts is almost impossible.
