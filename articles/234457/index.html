Voice recorder as inbox in GTD system

GTD is the most time-tested time management system. But there is one subtle place in it ...

GTD requires you to put everything in your inbox: thoughts, contacts, business, etc. ... People write down something more or less important, and they keep small things and information in mind. This is where the problems begin.

Why are people lazy to write everything down?

Most often this is not laziness, but simply uncomfortable tools. After all, what is usually used as an inbox? Either a smartphone or a small notebook.

However, sometimes it is inconvenient:

  • You are driving. A valuable thought comes to mind that you should write down. But what can you do? Do you really stop because of this?
  • Winter, frost minus 30. You walk down the street. Again you need to write something down. Will you get a smartphone? Yes, he may not even react to pressing ice fingers! Run to look for a warm shop?
  • Or you are a writer and some ‚Äúingenious‚Äù lines occurred to you. Where to put it all, if you're not at the computer? But such insights often happen away from the workplace. You get a notebook and start writing on your knee?


Of course, here you start to "be lazy"!

With a voice recorder, all these situations are solved simply:
  • Behind the wheel, you can easily dictate anything without being distracted from the road - your eyes are not needed!
  • In the cold you can dictate through a mitten. Checked - the sound quality does not suffer.
  • Long sentences? You dictate them in 5 seconds!
