Workshop in St. Petersburg: "Migrations and adaptation of applications to the Oracle platform"

Oracle Company welcomes you to take part in the workshop "Migration and adaptation of applications to the Oracle platform", which will be held on October 20, 2011 in St. Petersburg, in the hotel "Novotel".

Venue: Novotel Hotel Beginning at 10:00
Address: St. Petersburg, st. Mayakovsky, 3A.

The target audience:

* Administrators / Developers
* Team Leaders
* System architects


The event is held:

* Dmitry Nefedkin, Oracle Fusion Middleware Technical Consultant, Oracle Representative
* Alexei Selin, Technical Expert, Oracle Technology Center for development companies, FORS representative
* Andrey Tambovsky, Director of Partnership Programs, FORS-Development Center
* Alexander Potochkin, Oracle, Java team

At the workshop, we will present the possibilities of the Oracle technology center for development companies. The tasks of the center include:

* support for development partners in creating a new product based on Oracle technologies
* migration of solutions to the Oracle platform
* setting
* testing solutions of Russian developers

Requirements for technical equipment of participants: Participants come to the training with their laptops!

for the participants of the session with Alexei Selin: you need the ability to run VirtualBox virtual machines on your computer

for participants in the session with Dmitry Nefedkin: for your operating system, you must install Oracle DB Express Edition 10g or a newer version, the distribution kit is available at this link. You also need to install Jdeveloper 11.1.2, the distribution is available at this link.
