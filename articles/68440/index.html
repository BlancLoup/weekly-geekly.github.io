2. Excellent interface
Opera browsers have never looked so elegant. For Opera 10, we have developed a new interface with a convenient and logical arrangement of the menu and buttons for calling options.

3. Improved tabs
Opera remains a pioneer in the use of tabs. Opera 10 allows you to change their size by stretching the bottom border or by double-clicking the mouse. The new browser is capable of displaying thumbnails of open tabbed sites.

By default, Opera 10 tabs are rendered at the top of the screen, but they can be moved down or into the side panels, which is especially convenient when using widescreen monitors.
