YaC 2014: Yandex's main technology conference for those who really need it

Perhaps you remember that last year it became the biggest conference about hardcore technology in Europe. But this time we decided to do everything even more seriously. Hardcore and technology will remain, and they will even become more, but the people we call will be selected much more strictly (and they will be less).


We have previously tried not to miss any marketing bulshit from IT companies and now we are trying even more in this regard. YaC is a place where you can come and get new knowledge, and most importantly, share what you have gained over the past year. And it always worked very well. But the conference still got a lot of people who came just like that. For example, sometimes SEOs, which we overlooked, broke through, very young guys, students. The latter always really want to learn something new, but so far they can not give anything in return. Therefore, this year we decided to approach the selection of participants much stricter.

We have three mechanisms by which people this year fall on YaC. At first, we manually selected and invited people familiar to us, who are known for certain that they are terribly interesting and can be of great benefit to everyone at the conference. Now we have reached the second stage, when it is possible, as before, to fill in the questionnaire and submit an application. After registration closes on October 10 at 11:59 pm, people who can share new knowledge will be algorithmically selected from your questionnaires. And, yes, if you did not fill out the questionnaire, but only filed an application, then with very high probability you will not get anywhere.

If you are a person from the list below, this does not mean that you will definitely be interested in YaC:
  • Seoshnik . This conference is not even about searching, but about technology.
  • Webmaster . This is not an event about the creation of sites.
  • Journalist or blogger broad profile . It makes no sense to come to people who write about life in the city or fashion trends. And even beauty bloggers, despite the fact that they have recently been called Apple. If you write about technologies and understand that you will help your readers navigate the conference reports, understand what will be of interest to them, then watch the broadcast or come, having previously accredited the press service . If in the past years we tried not to limit them, then this year they are the same conference participants as everyone else. People who are not prepared, who poorly imagine the world of modern technologies, simply have nothing to do on YaC. Speaking directly, there is everything for programmers. And mathematicians. Previously, the conferences had such a ridiculous problem: the same person wanted to go simultaneously to several sections that went in parallel. We have now developed an intellectual scheme for visiting YaC, which allows a person to work out a route through the conference. Roughly speaking, we know which reports are most interesting for system administrators and people with a similar mindset. And the program for them includes some reports related to programming more than if they were sections only about administration. This is not just a separate room, it is a route, written as a schedule, according to which you can move between different rooms and always stay on the reports you are interested in.

We have developed six such schemes and want to set up an experiment. In general, we are just trying to make the conference as technological as possible. For example, in past years, they refused to hand over paper trinkets and other handouts, because this is a completely useless thing that accumulates among people in the garbage disposal.
