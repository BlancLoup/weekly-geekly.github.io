Both cash and back ...

Cash back service is becoming an increasingly popular service among Russian users. She came to me, but to be honest, somehow it was not clear how to use it. The main question is why my bank needs it. We are trying to figure it out.

Cash back is a concept meaning the return to the buyer's account (usually a card) of a part of the cost of a service or product. That is, the so-called bonus program, designed to attract customers and increase its loyalty. The first cash back-projects in Russia were launched quite recently - about a year ago. A similar idea came to us from the UK and the USA, where this model is already functioning and very successfully.
Cash back, as a relatively new phenomenon in trade and related areas, should be distinguished from a variety of promotions (for example, coupon or coupon), discount programs or all sorts of sales.

Cache backend mechanism


The cash back, in contrast to the traditional systems of discounts and bonuses, is represented by the following scheme with two main structural elements.
First, the client of the outlet (service point) pays the seller for the purchased goods (rendered service). Important in this sense is the preservation of all discounts and promotions provided by the seller.
Secondly, after the purchase and payment of the goods (services), part of the paid amount is returned to the client. The source of this return is the affiliate, that is, the entity that ensures the flow of buyers to the seller.
This scheme works on the basis of commissions, which are paid by the seller to its affiliate for the service of attracting each buyer. In turn, the affiliate uses part of the commission as payments to customers, thereby causing the promotion of purchases of goods and services through its portal in the future.
The money received on the card as a result of the cash back-up can be spent by the client, on the one hand, to pay for the next purchase, on the other hand, they can withdraw the indicated amount to their own bank card or to various payment systems.
Now banking services include the release of both credit and debit cards with the provided cash backing function.
