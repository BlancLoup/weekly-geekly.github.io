NormaSahar - a diabetic startup

Diabetes is the largest non-infectious epidemic of the 21st century. According to the World Health Organization, 8 to 12% of the human population has diabetes. We will not talk about hundreds of millions of patients - it is too difficult to visualize.
Let's just say easier. If we are talking about 8% of the population, then it is almost every twelfth. If about 12% - this is every eighth.

Now imagine that you are sitting in a cinema hall. A row before you; people in your row, to your right and to your left; the row is yours. So - just stretch your hand: forward, backward, right, left. And you will stumble upon a diabetic. This is what the penetration of diabetes into the human population means.

If diabetes is not treated - scrupulously, every day and hour, he will kill the patient with a probability of 100%. It can happen in five or twenty years after the onset of the disease - it does not matter. It is important that there are no exceptions to the rule.

Sad but true: diabetes cannot be cured today. But you can - compensate. And not to allow the disease to take away from the person those years of life that she will definitely take if he does not work on herself, each day achieving the necessary compensation, and let everything take its course and hope for the great Russian "maybe."
Diabetes mellitus is roughly divided into insulin-dependent and insulin-independent, or type 1 and type 2 diabetes; they also distinguish a special form, which today is called ‚Äúnot I and not II‚Äù. However, regardless of the causes and characteristics of diabetes mellitus, its compensation requires at least:
  • daily measurement of blood sugar levels at least 6 times a day and constant patient monitoring of a self-monitoring diary, which not only indicates blood sugar levels at certain hours, but also what was eaten, what exercise was, what insulin doses were administered;
  • regular injections of insulin in diabetics of type I and type "not I and not II";
  • specialized drug therapy in type II diabetics;
  • specialized diet therapy for all diabetics without exception;
  • constant regular (often daily) medical advice.

In actual conditions, the current state of Russian health care in sufficient quantities and quality of all components of the treatment process, and especially the one that we designated as number 5, unfortunately, is often not possible. Endocrinologists have an exorbitant workload, and in many cases they are only enough to prescribe patients for free insulin and test strips for blood glucose meters.
How to fix the situation? Now the impossible has become possible.

Now, diabetics have a very intelligent assistant in their daily care for their health. It is called NormaSahar .

NormaSahar is an automated online patient tracking system based on smartphones and web servers that allows not only monitoring blood glucose and semi-automatically recommending insulin doses needed to compensate for the disease, but also organizing remote medical consultations for patients through the Internet and phone.
Our methodical approach allows diabetics to keep their condition under control, not to start the disease and avoid the development of severe complications of diabetes, leading to disability and death. Speaking in human terms: we give the sick time to live a full life. And we provide the real quality of this life, and so, as without NormaSahar can hardly be achieved.
