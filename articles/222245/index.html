SMO subtleties. Do not be surprised that you do not have social traffic

80% of site owners are confident that their resources are optimized for social networks. As if not so. So we will understand where the dog is buried and what is the nature of this delusion.

All that is reflected in this note is the result of the introduction of the social buttons Grrow on several dozen sites of various subjects and observations, which content is given by primary sources to social networks.

So what we are faced with and understood.

On the one hand there is a site with good content, attendance, design, social buttons are, comments, and social traffic - the cat wept. And it will not.



Do you know why?

Many of us do not see the difference in the structure of content on the site and its reflection in social networks, and therefore the peculiarities of perception by the audience of the same content in different places.

This, from my point of view, is understandable. Who until recently was the main provider of traffic to any site? Of course search engines. And in the head of many webmasters hammered the standard of content delivery for search engines. Well, you yourself know them.

However, social networks straightened their shoulders, gained a huge audience and became a source of good traffic. And the standards for presenting information are completely different. Because people are far from robots, they are not driven by analysis and algorithms. People are driven by emotions.

So, how does a webmaster think, for example, a content site? Yeah, I wrote a good article, everything is SEO correct, I have social buttons at the beginning (this is a good tone) and at the end of the article (which is also not bad). The visitor will read, click the social button, zapropostit article on his wall and here he is social traffic in my pocket. It works, but weakly.

Why? Because the webmaster thinks like everyone else. Want more social traffic - think differently. Think one step further.
