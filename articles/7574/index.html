The self-learning neural network 20Q.net has already asked a billion questions

Experimental artificial intelligence 20Q.net overcame the mark of 50,000,000 tests and came close to the mark of a billion questions. This neural network is built on the game "Twenty Questions". A person makes a word, and the system guesses it, taking into account his age, gender and nationality in the process of communicating with a person.

Artificial intelligence is trained in humans. Receiving answers from us, he adds to his knowledge base. A billion answers means that now he is able to guess almost every thought of a person.
