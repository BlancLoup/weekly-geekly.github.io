Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology: the reception of applications to the online magistracy in modern combinatorics is ending

Until the end of the deadline for submitting documents for the online magistracy in modern combinatorics 1 week remained.
To apply, go to the page .
If you have any questions, write to omsc@phystech.edu or call +7 (499) 110 67 03.
To see the appeal of the program manager Andrei Raygorodsky click here .
We remind you that you can either pay the tuition yourself or apply for a grant.

At the moment we can say that the popularity of the program has exceeded our expectations: we received a lot of questions both by telephone and by mail from various cities and countries, the guys are actively taking exams and, although there are very few places left, there is still a chance for everyone who really wants and is ready to join Fiztehovskomu students.

Yes, it will be, as usual - you work under the guidance of a research supervisor based on one of the research institutes.
