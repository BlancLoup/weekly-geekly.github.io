The project 1C-Tools became the winner of the selection in U.M.NI.K.

Hello!
Finally, there was time to share the good news, my project became the winner in the competitive selection for the state program of U.M.NI.K.

Under the cut, I want to talk about the event and how I plan to dispose of an additional asset.


Brief information about the program itself


UMNIK - state program for supporting projects of young children ( up to 28 years ). Selection to the program is carried out within the framework of accredited events in many cities of Russia. The maximum amount of funding 400t.r for two years, in four tranches of 100t.r every six months. Money is transferred to the specified bank account of an individual (in other words, there is no need to register a legal entity and no taxes are paid on this money).

Event


I learned about the selection due to favorable circumstances. More than a year ago, I tried to knock on the yellow doors of 1C with the words "guys, see what I do, suddenly you will be interested," at that moment the doors were closed, but my Email somehow got on the mailing list for "selection participants theses "which are annually spent by firm" 1 ". It is thanks to this newsletter, I learned that on November 15, in the framework of the "Day 1C: Career" in Moscow will be selected in the program UMNIK.

The selection consisted of two stages, correspondence and full-time. For the correspondence stage, it was necessary to fill in a rather large-sized mold and attach a preliminary presentation. In total there were just over 50 participants in the absentee selection, 11 of them were invited to attend full-time participation in Moscow.

The first pleasant surprise for me was that 1C offered to settle the participants for free in their hotel.
The hotel was on the territory of their training center, everything was clean and tidy, the first day I lived alone in a double room, on the second I was hooked up by a guy from St. Petersburg.

The selection took place in the morning, before the main event dedicated to the career day, and the selection results were announced a couple of hours after the performances ended. I spoke sixth, the excitement was exactly before it was my turn to speak, because human nature is most tormented by the expectation of an event than the event itself =)

I asked a friend to record the speech, then to analyze. I will attach it at the end of the article, for those who are interested.
