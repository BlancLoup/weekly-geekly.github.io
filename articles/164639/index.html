What do they want in different countries? Google Interactive



Google Inc. decided to help its (and not only its) users to achieve their goals. According to Google employees, numerous researches of scientists have shown that people achieve their goals much more effectively if these goals are recorded somewhere. In general, there are already a large number of different kinds of services, users of which exchange their goals, and also show the level of goal achievement. Google created an interactive map where you can see what users from different countries want.

Such goals are grouped by color, and there is a legend to facilitate understanding of the map on the right. The points are mapped, so it is immediately clear which regions want love, which regions want health, and which regions have a successful career.

You can see the map itself here . Users, of course, can add their own goals and objectives (while the added entry becomes visible to other map visitors within 24-48 hours).

The Corporation of Good also has other projects that, according to the developers, also help to work effectively to achieve the goals. In a post on a Google blog , it is these services that are listed.
