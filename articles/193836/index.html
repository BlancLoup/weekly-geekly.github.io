10 interesting and free mobile applications for work



UPD: This post was added to favorites by 2 times more people than my post, which scored 86 votes FOR, and at the same time it is minus. Dear voters, be more objective in your assessments because The purpose of posts is to be useful to readers.



1. CloudOn allows you to run Microsoft Office on iPad and Android.
Support: Android and iOS



Support for editing documents and presentations. Sync with Box, Dropbox, Google Drive. The ability to send documents by mail.

2. Roambi Analytics visualizes the data.
Support: iOS



If you encounter large amounts of data, then Roambi Analytics can be useful to you. This application allows you to turn hard to read data into charts and graphs for easier perception.

3. CardMunch turns business cards into contacts on LinkedIn.
Support: iOS . Unavailable in Russian AppStore.



In this digital age, the exchange of paper business cards seems utterly insane, but we still do. Using this business card recognition application created by LinkedIn, you can convert business cards to your contact list on LinkedIn.

4. HootSuite is a dashboard for all your social media accounts.
Support: Android and iOS

Almost all of us use social networks for work including, HootSuite is an application to manage all our accounts. It can work with multiple Twitter and Facebook accounts, LinkedIn, Foursquare and many others. Teams can use it to manage corporate accounts in social networks.

5. Asana helps your team cope with tasks.
Support: Android and iOS . Trello Alternative



Asana is a common task list, a team management tool for projects. The mobile application allows you to see what your colleagues have done or transferred to you while you are not in the office. This allows you to be knowledgeable with all tasks. Attach files with Dropbox, place images in tasks. The application is synchronized in real time with the web version. The app is free for groups of up to 15 people.

6. LogMeIn allows you to connect to a computer remotely.
Support: iOS . Android fee: (TeamViewer Alternatives, 2X



Need a file that is on your computer at work? LogMeIn allows you to access your PC from a smartphone. The application allows you to remotely run many business applications, like Salesforce, SAP and Microsoft Office, and others.

7. Blue Jeans provides a video chat service.
Support: iOS



Blue Jeans is a service that allows companies to conduct video conferences, even if everyone uses different accounts: Skype, Polycom, Cisco, TelePresence LifeSize, Microsoft Lync, etc. Mobile application allows you to join the conference from your iPhone.

8. CamScanner turns your phone into a scanner.
Support: Android and iOS



CamScanner turns your phone into a scanner, so you can destroy even a ton of paper by transferring it all to your phone: receipts, notes, price tags, checks, business cards, and so on.

9. Hotspot Shield VPN protects you while using public WiFi.
Support: Android , iOS , Mac and Windows

When you work on public Wi-Fi, hackers can spy on you. To avoid this, use Hotspot Shield, the application provides mobile encryption over WiFi. The application can also help you to unblock sites that are sometimes blocked in public WiFi networks, for example, Vkontakte, YouTube, Facebook, Netflix and others.

10. TigerText - chat for work.
Support: Android and iOS



TigerText was originally conceived as a business application for corporate users. Mobile version allows you to send text messages, images or files that will automatically self-destruct.

The author of the post does not claim that these applications are the best in the world, but only that they are interesting, free and can be useful for Habr's readers, and tried for you by collecting all the links for downloading. Good luck to everyone optimizing workflows!
