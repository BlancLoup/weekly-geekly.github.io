the saga of 8 rubles

Matvey Ganapolsky:
On the screen flashed advertising the final transfer.
"In an orphanage, children live on 8 rubles a day"
And the corresponding picture.
That's what I want to say.
All those because of whom in the country in the orphanage children live on 8 rubles a day.
So that you are dead!

Suppose they die those whose fault children live on 8 rubles a day. But where is the guarantee that they will not come tomorrow like them? No one in this country gives guarantees, not even the guarantor. But I'm sure they will come. and for children everything will remain in its place. What is the difference for children who rule the country, who allocates these unfortunate 8 rubles to them, who passes laws according to which they (the children) must live on 8 rubles? It is absolutely not important for children. You can start agitating children, saying that Uncle P. is bad and that is why they live so poorly, but Uncle X. is good, and if they (children) come out for a rally for Uncle X., then tomorrow everything will be fine. So sometimes act. And the crowd will shout that it is necessary to catch the perpetrators and punish. They will catch, punish, show, trumpet all the media about the work done and forget it, but the situation will not change.
