Free Silverlight 4 Training Kit

At Chanel 9 they announced a free distribution of a training kit for Silverlight 4 (in English). The training package includes a technical description and a course of laboratory work that reveals the new features of Silverlight 4 and aims to build business applications.

The description says that the course includes eight modules with practical tasks and an explanatory video. The course tells about increasing the level of trust inside the browser, about the RichTextBox control, implicit use of styles, working with a webcam, drag and drop and multi touch capabilities, validation, authentication, MEF , WCF RIA Services, working with the right mouse button and much more.

Article on Chanel 9: channel9.msdn.com/learn/courses/Silverlight4
Download page: go.microsoft.com/?linkid=9709693
