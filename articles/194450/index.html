Officially announced the specifications of the first smartphone on Sailfish OS

A couple of months after the announcement, the exact technical description of the parameters of the first smartphone on Sailfish OS, which should appear on the shelves at the end of this year, appeared on Jolla's official Facebook page . As you can easily see, scrolling through the comments to the announcement, the screen is the most puzzling.

So, we are talking about the device with the following characteristics:
  • 4.5 "qHD screen Jolla Estrade with a resolution of 960 x 540 pixels
  • 1.4 GHz dual core Qualcomm Snapdragon processor
  • 1 GB of RAM
  • 16 GB of internal memory, SD cards are supported
  • A camera with a resolution of 8 megapixels, autofocus and LED flash are present; front camera - 2 MP, physical buttons for the camera will be
  • 2100 mAh battery


In addition to the above, among the "features" of the device is called wide support for gestures to control, previously announced compatibility with Android applications (for now, however, there will be no Google Play on Sailfish) and "The Other Half" smart removable panels.
