One-time business

Many people write about the creation of businesses, their promotion, the search for investment, competition and the intricacies of marketing.
I decided to raise the topic of one-time businesses.
These are businesses that do not live long and bring immediately a profit to their creators.
Their main quality is that they do not require significant investments during creation.

To create this topic, I was encouraged by a friend of mine who creates such one-time businesses all the time ‚Äî the main thing is not to do anything and pay money.

I will give examples of such real businesses, you see, and who come in handy.

EXAMPLE 1

MICROFINANCING



At first glance, this seems like a serious business and is available only to banks or pawnshops.
This is not true.

In reality, microloans are not issued, but a regular agreement between a physical person and an individual about a loan of funds is concluded, but not more than 50 tr.
Accordingly, no permissions are required.

The technology is simple:
- the cheapest office is removed, you can cooperate with someone;
- in the beginning you can not hire workers, you can handle it yourself;
- initial capital of 50-100 tr .;
- 8-10% per day on the security of property.

That's the whole business. Why is it a one-time ask you? Because during the crisis, banks stopped issuing loans, and people began to lose their jobs - and very few people would give money at the right moment.

Accordingly, the key moment is a pledge.
Many who are engaged in microfinance lend only on the security of real estate or cars. So far you will not leave.
It is necessary to give under any things having value.
Example, microwave for 8 tr. taken on bail with a loan of 500 rubles.
All things must be with the documents or you know what will be fraught with consequences.
Practice has shown that the average loan is equal to 3-10 tr. for 5-10 days.
For all the time of work, due to debt repayment, only a few things remained.
They take for different needs: to celebrate the wedding, on the day of the city, before the salary ...

People went on the first day as soon as a pillar was put on the street, and the flow is constantly increasing, because no need to mortgage an apartment or a car. Although they are also mortgaged (there were also plots of land) - we had to agree with the nearest natarius about a quick and out-of-turn arrangement.
You can't take gold, you need a bunch of papers and checks - but you yourself came from the pawnshop and offered to cooperate - we send them with gold, and they send us goods.

In general, while the crisis, this one-time business is relevant and brings good money.
