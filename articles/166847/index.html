5 days after the launch, file hosting Mega bypassed Rapidshare and Dropbox

According to Alexa.com , the traffic rank of Mega.co.nz has reached 141 today, leaving rapidshare.com and dropbox.com behind. Judging by the growth dynamics, Mega will have time to enter the first hundred of the largest sites in the world less than a week after launch.



In the first hour after opening, more than 100,000 people registered on the site. Even for a couple of days, the site was practically lying - it was almost impossible to download anything due to the rush of users. Now the site has already entered a normal track, the download speed is measured in megabytes per second, there are no delays.

A few days after launch, critical publications appeared that cast doubt on the security and privacy of Mega. In particular, the organization of client-side encryption using JavaScript and possible threats related to data deduplication on Mega servers were criticized. On January 22, in the official blog, the developers gave answers to most security questions, and today Kim Dotcom said on his Twitter account that in the near future Mega will hold a contest with cash prizes for finding vulnerabilities.
