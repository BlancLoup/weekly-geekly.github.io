Fight for a place in the ranking


It is very important for some people in this world to measure ... at least something. A vivid example was the story of five years ago, when the Russian-language section of Wikipedia, by the number of articles, came very close to the Portuguese. Some Portuguese users found this a cause for serious concern, and with the help of bots began to create many stub articles, the so-called nedabov . At first, ours did not succumb to temptation and did not use the same method. After some time, the Portuguese section took the 8th place in the ranking, after which the users calmed down. In general, over time, bots managed to rein in, and the mind prevailed. Our section in a natural way, not due to cheating, took the 8th place in the rating, which it now occupies.
