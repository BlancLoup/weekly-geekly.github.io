Bookshelf "Cave"


Multifunctional furniture is increasingly gaining momentum. Especially in our homeland. On the table, which does not fold out, and the sofa, which does not turn into a bed, look askance. We understand what this is connected with: few people can afford an apartment that can accommodate a sofa, a bed, and a lot of necessary furniture. How are things with the population and housing in other countries - a question for sociologists, and designers, meanwhile, work on this demand and come off in full.

Here, for example, designer Sakura Adachi (Sakura Adachi) came up with a thing that she called ‚ÄúThe Cave‚Äù (Cave). This is a large bookcase with built-in reading space. The seat has a very comfortable shape, and a light bulb is built into it. The rack is two-sided, so they can divide a large room into two parts.

In addition to saving space, this piece of furniture meets another human need associated with overpopulation: a person often wants to hide from everyone, to find his own personal space. The seat is located almost at floor level, which gives the feeling of such a shelter. Remember how you felt when you were hiding under the table as a child? The most shy can hang the curtains, the benefit of the outside light is not needed.

Yes, this rack really reminds childhood. By the way, there is also a smaller children's version. And even an option for pets: a shelf easier with a seat in the form of a doghouse. This will allow you to place all your pet's belongings, including books about caring for him, and give him a comfortable personal space that will visually decorate your home, rather than ruin it, as is sometimes the case with dog litter.
