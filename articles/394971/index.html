Yahoo!

It is interesting, but another IT giant, Yahoo!, Also sets itself to win the current German national team championship. The analytical service of the company based its forecast on the basis of the data set of the sports service and the Tumblr blog platform. The forecast, noted at Yahoo !, was compiled from an analysis of more than 20 billion messages, selected by the hashtags #football, #soccer, #BongDa, # Euro2016 and #UEFA. out of 24 million blogs from January 1 to May 31, 2016.

For the next stage of processing, 1.8 million relevant messages were selected with reference to teams and players. Then, using Opta Data ( Opta Sports ) technology, detailed match statistics for the last four years were analyzed for all participating teams in the current tournament. Further, using the available tournament grid data, Yahoo! calculated the average number of goals scored and missed by teams for the previous four years, the number of references to teams and players associated with Euro 2016 in Tumblr, and took into account the standard deviations.

If the calculation is Yahoo! confirmed, according to the forecast, the German national team will again win the final match of the European Championship.



NeuroBayes Michael Fyndt

While AI created in the laboratories of leading Internet corporations with surprising "unanimity" predicts Germany's victory, the brainchild of CERN (European Council of Nuclear Research) analyst Michael Findt - a neural network running the NeuroBayes algorithm, has its own opinion on this . NeuroBayes, originally created for analyzing the Big Data of the Large Hadron Collider in the process of studying and calculating collisions of charged particles, sees the French team in the final with a 34% probability.

For the analysis, NeuroBayes got at its disposal data on the results of international matches already played by the teams in the current main squad. Evaluating the chances for each team to win in Euro 2016, the neural network produced 94 billion possible results. This entire amount of data unimaginable for our understanding was ordered by the Monte Carlo random variable simulation method. As a result, a forecast was issued: France and Spain will meet in the final, and France will be the favorite of the match.

Bisnode

A version of the development of events at various stages and at the finish of the European Championship was voiced by the Swedish analytical company Bisnode , which is also directly related to Big Data analytics. As a material for analysis, information about historical matches, victories and defeats of teams, individual achievements of players was uploaded to the Bisnode neural network. The output was formed very detailed grid with the results of the projected matches.

According to Bisnode, the probability with which the Spanish national team will win in the final match was 21%. Almost the same chance - 20.2% of the neural network left the England team.
