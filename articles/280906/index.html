5th International Mobile Conference MBLT16 in a month



500 Startups, Facebook, Google, Alibaba.com, The Economist, Soundcloud and dozens of representatives of world companies will perform on April 28 in Moscow at the International Mobile Conference MBLT16 .

For the fifth time, MBLT will bring together leaders of the mobile industry on one platform to discuss trends, innovations and market prospects. The program will be successful cases, panel discussions and answers to sensitive questions. And after the official part - an unforgettable after-party from the acclaimed application for quick dating Pure .



You can familiarize yourself with the preliminary program of the conference, the full list of speakers, as well as purchase tickets on the official MBLT website . Until April 9, the cost of tickets will be 5000 rubles. When buying 2 or more tickets, a 30% discount is valid.

Subscribe to news conferences on social networks Facebook , Twitter .

Organizers: e-Legion and RAEC
General partners: Facebook, Aitarget
General partner of After-party: Pure
Partners: Rambler & Co, Aviasales, AppMetrica
