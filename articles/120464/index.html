Summer School of Apple Developers in St. Petersburg

From July 9 to September 1 in St. Petersburg, we will organize free courses for developers for the iPhone and iPad. In less than two months, it is expected to train a group of 8-10 people, the best of whom will later be offered a place in the company's staff.



For us, this is a great opportunity to prepare for the company qualified mobile development specialists who are so lacking in the market.


The teacher will be Stanislav Krasnoyarov , a system architect and lead programmer who develops applications for iPhone in his spare time. Stanislav is known for his performances on ADCSpb, where he talked about Cocos2D and errors when working with memory .



Classes will be held once a week on Saturdays at 16:00. Venue: Ingria Business Incubator .

Course program:
  • Objective c
  • Foundation and Memory Management
  • Views and View Controllers
  • Interface creation in Interface builder
  • Table view
  • Interface Development Principles (HIG)
  • Interaction with Web services server, JSON
  • Image view, Web view
  • Application Data Storage
  • Multithreading
  • Useful Utilities Instruments
Prerequisite is the experience of developing in C ++, java or C # . We all understand that if we learn to program from scratch, then out of 8 people in 7 weeks, two will come to a good level, God forbid. We want all 8 students after the courses to create applications without any problems and sell them in the App Store. Also, not mandatory, but the desirable condition is the presence of poppy or hakintosha for obvious reasons.


If you want to try, then:
  1. Perform a test task in C ++, Java or C #.
  2. Send it along with your resume to ios@e-legion.com.
  3. Pass an interview.
All information about the school link ios.e-legion.ru . Do not wait for tomorrow, while there is still space.
