Today is the next meeting of the organizers of WWW2006 Russia

This evening the next working meeting of the organizers of the conference WWW2006 Russia , the first conference in Russia for web developers, will take place. The meeting will be held in the restaurant "Bavarius" (Komsomolsky Prospect, 21/10 ; map ) at 19:00.

A wide range of topics will be discussed at the meeting, including the main one - why the conference site has not yet been launched, at which participants must register. Because of this, the scheduled dates for the conference on October 27-28 may be questionable.

Also discuss the conference schedule; indicative budget; report on venue options; reports on negotiations with universities; timeline for the preparation of the conference.
