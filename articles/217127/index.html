Up5 Alpha on Google Play

It did not happen quickly, but we published an alpha version of the application on Google Play. Now the owner of a smartphone running Android can put our application, try to create and share beautiful and interactive mobile business cards.

From interesting to other projects, details in the following posts:
  • The cloud service runs on Azure and it turned out to be convenient.
  • Beta testing organized via Googlu Play


First, I would like to remind who our application and service can be useful. And they can be useful to those who are on duty or by their nature are often forced to communicate with new people and make new contacts. In fact, it does not matter whether you are doing this online or offline. In addition, our user is not indifferent how it will look on a mobile device of a potential partner or client. Or just a good friend. We decided that it was not enough for such active people to transfer contacts in the VCF format or have a page on Facebook or Linkedin. They need beautiful and interactive mobile business cards. This, of course, is a hypothesis, and we must test it.
Yes, we adore printed business cards and very much hope that they will never leave our lives, as vinyl records have not. But the problem is that they need to be printed, they end at the most inopportune moment. In general, their real nature is big, I would even say, a huge plus and at the same time a minus.
Everyone whose interest is not lost, I suggest to see a short demo of our application .
Want to try? Forced to warn that this will require effort, and not everything will run smoothly. First is alpha. Secondly, testing on Google Play is arranged in such a way, to be honest, they have become too smart for me.
So here's what to do:
  • Join our closed Google community
  • Download and install the application

IMPORTANT! Join the community is necessary, otherwise Google will give 404 to try to download the application.
