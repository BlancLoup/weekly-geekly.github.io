Meet the Bug Detector and Wireless BagHunter Professional Camcorders
Radio detector bookmarks, hidden micro radio microphones and hidden wireless video cameras "BagHanter Profi" is a device that works on the basis of a microprocessor system. The bug detector finds, in principle, any radio transmitters: for example, analog and digital bugs, radio microphones, cell phones, bugs, wireless hidden cameras, etc.

Two antennas: internal and external.
Frequency range: 50 to 3000 MHz
Uniform detector sensitivity over the entire frequency range.
Dynamic range of the device 45 dB

The main features of this model:

In the search mode, the bug detector is used to examine the room, and both sound and light displays are present.

In security mode, the detector has an automatic sensitivity setting with low power consumption.

This mode is designed for continuous background work. The detector automatically adjusts itself to the specifics of the radio environment.

The acousto-binding mode allows you to most accurately detect by the characteristic "whistle" exactly where the analog scutes are installed. Search time is shortened.
