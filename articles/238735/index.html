Payoneer changes color. Our new brand

Many of you have probably noticed significant changes on our site: the gray color is no longer relevant for us, the Payoneer brand now presents the whole variety of colors and shades! We are proud of the launch of the new Payoneer brand and want to talk about the idea that formed the basis of the new brand.


The world is changing rapidly. Not so long ago, people could be associated exclusively with those people whom they could see. People lived, worked, raised children, and eventually died in the same villages or settlements. Over time, these settlements began to grow, and people found themselves associated with a large number of people living in a larger area.

Today, in the digital age, the traditional boundaries of time and space are gone. We can be associated with a person anywhere in the world. The new brand Payoneer reflects our goal - to connect businesses, professionals, countries and currencies, expanding the possibilities of global commerce. With our innovative international payment solutions, an engineer in Bangladesh, a translator in Australia, an online seller in China, and a landlord in Venezuela renting it out to tourists, can make international deals as easily as local ones. Leading companies in the USA and Europe can fully appreciate the talent of professionals from the countries of the former USSR, Asia and Latin America.

We see you, our clients, as our partners who work with us to create new products, technologies and services. We are always happy to hear your opinions and ideas, and now we have an identity that will lead us to our goal. The photos you see right now are businesses, people, countries, and currencies connected by the colorful arrows of Payoneer. We look forward to continuing the development of this idea.
