MBLT15: the fourth international mobile conference in Moscow

The fourth MBLT15 mobile conference will be held in Moscow on May 21.

Over the previous years, more than 2,000 people attended the conference, with foreign speakers from Google, Foursquare, Spotify, TechCrunch, TripAdvisor, Qiwi, Samsung, Rovio, VentureBeat, Facebook, Airbnb, Soundcloud and many other companies. You can read reports about past conferences here: # MBLT12 , # MBLT13 and # MBLT14 .



The MBLT15 program will be divided into thematic sections, which include reports, interviews and discussions from leading foreign and Russian companies. Representatives of Uber, Meduza.io, Metro UK, BlaBlaCar, Omate, Indiegogo, Jolla, Aviasales, Parallels, Kaspersky Lab, HotelTonight and many other companies will share a fresh look at the mobile industry.

The speakers will talk about how immobile companies survive in a rapidly changing world, about successful and unsuccessful crowdfunding campaigns, why the mobile community moves to Berlin, share their experiences in the field of travel, journalism and tell us what awaits us in the near future.

Until May 1, the ticket price is 7 000 rubles. After - 11 000 rubles. When buying 2 or more tickets, a 30% discount is valid.

More information about the conference - on the official conference website .
