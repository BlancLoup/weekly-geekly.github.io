Opera 9.6 Finale



So, the next final version of Opera 9.6 browser has been released. It would seem - well, what else can you think of a new one in order to make the browser even better, faster and more convenient? But no: as promised, the Norwegians are not going to yield to someone the laurels of browser innovators, again offering something that many people did not realize. Details, according to the press release, are:


Choose speed, performance and innovation with Opera 9.6
The new version of Opera offers improved compatibility and data transfer.

Oslo, Norway - October 8, 2008 - Today Opera Software announces the release of Opera 9.6, the latest version of the popular browser. Opera 9.6 is distinguished by improved performance and flexibility of the built-in mail client, as well as the addition of new features to the free synchronization service, Opera Link. Opera 9.6 is available for free download from the official website www.opera.com .
