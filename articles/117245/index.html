Dell Streak 7 tablet video review

Hello,
Earlier, we told you about the intersin 5-inch Dell Streak device. The diagonal of its screen was 5 inches and it was not quite clear how to properly name the device: a tablet or a smartphone. Today we will take a closer look at another novelty of the company, which, no doubt, is a tablet - meet Dell Streak 7 :



Like other Dell products, the tablet evokes the feeling of a quality and serious product without strong design delights. The body is a monoblock made of plastic. The battery at the device is not removable, the only cover that opens is designed for a memory card slot and a SIM card.

To cover the screen used glass of increased resistance to scratches and bumps made by technology Gorilla Glass . The main competitor of the Dell Streak 7 in the market is likely to be the Samsung Galaxy Tab , both tablets have their own advantages and disadvantages. The main advantage of the Dell device is the powerful dual-core platform Nvidia Tegra 2 .



Features Dell Streak 7:
  • Operating system : Android 2.2 (Froyo) + Stage UI 1.0
  • Processor : nVidia Tegra T20 - Dual Core @ 1 GHz
  • Memory : 512MB DDRII SDRAM, 16, 32 or 64GB on board; SD, MMC, SDHC expansion slot
  • Dimensions : 199.9 x 119.8 x 12.4 mm, weight 450 g
  • Battery : Capacity 2780 mAh
  • Display : 7 inches, capacitive, multitouch, WVGA resolution 800 √ó 480
  • Sound : 3.5 mm plug, stereo speakers
  • Cameras : rear 5 Mpx, frontal 1.2 Mpx
  • Data transmission : 2G (GSM), 3G (HSPA), WiFi 802.11b / g / n (2.4 GHz), Bluetooth 2.1 + EDR, built-in GPS receiver

During the conversation, the manufacturers promised to soon upgrade their device to Android 3.0 Honeycom b, as is known, the latest version of the system is aimed just at multi-core platforms. From the main drawbacks, I would like to note: poor viewing angles of the screen, a battery with a capacity of only 2780 mAH and dimensions (thickness 12.4 mm, weight 450 g).

WiFi version of the Dell Streak 7 will go on sale in May, and the Russian market will see the WiFi + 3G version only in June.
