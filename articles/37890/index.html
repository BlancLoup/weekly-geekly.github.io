STALKER: Clear Sky already on the shelves


So, gentlemen, waited. A game called Stalker: Clear Skies appeared on torrent store shelves!

The long-awaited continuation of the game (and to be more precise, the prehistory) STALKER: Shadow of the Chernobyl finally came out, and, despite all sorts of gossip and spam of detractors, did not suffer the same fate as the first part of the game (as you know, it was developed five years old). Well, let's start a review of the new game from GSC Game World.

Here is what the official sources say about this gaming product:

For the first time, a group of stalkers came close to the very heart of the Zone - the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant, provoking a cataclysm that almost led to a catastrophe. The grandiose Discharge of anomalous energy changes the Zone. There are no more tested and relatively safe roads. Whole levels disappear in the splashes of anomalies.



Stalkers and even expeditions are dying or trapped in the missing territories. New territories, about which nothing has been known since the appearance of the Zone, appear on the Zone map. Emissions continue to shake the zone. The zone is unstable. Anomalous activity is maximal.



Changes to the well-known stalkers map of the Zone upsets the fragile balance of power in the Zone. Enmity for new territories, fields of artifacts and spheres of influence flares up between the groups. No more old enemies or friends - now every man for himself. Between the factions began the war of factions.



The main character is a mercenary, who by the will of fate turned out to be at the very edge of the confrontation between the stalker factions, the Strelok and even the Zone itself. The main character will play a major role in the events that led the history of the Zone to the point where the original STALKER game begins.



What awaits stalkers in the opened depths of the Zone? What new dangers lurk in new territories? Why are emissions shaking the Zone? Why has the Zone changed? How to eliminate its instability? Which faction will prevail in the opposition groups? Why did the Shooter hit the death truck? What happened to Strelok before? Was there another choice? - Atmosphere of the Exclusion Zone.
- Studies of the new depths of the Zone - Red Forest, Limansk, Pripyat Undergrounds and others.
- Simulation Gang War.
- Ability to play and lead to the victory of any group.
- Modified life simulation system.
- Diverse behavior of stalkers-NPC in the simulation.
- A new level of entertainment plot scenes.
- Improved artificial intelligence of computer opponents.
- Bypassing dynamic obstacles computer characters.
- Use NPC grenades.
- Fast Travel.
- Improved concept of detectors, artifacts and anomalies.
- A new system of upgrades of weapons and armor.
- Repair of weapons and armor.
