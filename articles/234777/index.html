A cursory overview of the budget Chinese "six-core" Mijue M10

So I followed the other habravchanami and decided to try my luck and threw the net into the "Chinese Sea". Neod brought the Mijue M10 - a five-inch smartphone (or phablet, if you will) based on MTK 6592.


Caution - under the cut a fairly large number of images.


I purchased the phone in the store www.tinydeal.com at the price at the time of purchase of 5500 rubles. I chose the white version. Delivery of the phone was carried out including through the Russian Post and took exactly 1 month.

Unpack and review







The packaging was reliable enough to deliver the phone safe and sound, despite the most gentle handling of the Russian Post with the package.







Options modest, but more and is not required.







The reverse side of the small thickness of the body and its strength. It is better to remove the back cover with a special plastic tool in the form of a mediator.







The battery is already inside the phone. But its contacts are closed with a protective film. To turn on the phone, you need to remove the battery, remove the film and insert the battery back. Battery type - lithium polymer, capacity - 2200 milliampere * hour, voltage 3.7 volts (data taken from the label)











The phone supports dual SIM in dual standby mode. One of the SIM cards should be a standard size, the other - microsim. The phone works with microsd memory cards. The installation of SIM cards and memory cards is placed on the body under the cover.





The phone is equipped with two cameras. Rear - 13 megapixels (interpolation of 8) with autofocus, front - 5 megapixels (interpolation of 2)

WiFi and GPS antennas are located at the top of the case. 2G \ 3G antennas are located at the bottom. The speakerphone is also at the bottom.
