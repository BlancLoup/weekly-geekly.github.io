We invite you to the Russian final of the Imagine Cup: April 19 will be a holiday at the Mail.ru Group office!

Dear% USERNAME%!

We really want to invite you to a technological holiday: on April 19, 2014 , on Saturday, our most important event for this academic year for students, the Russian final of the Imagine Cup , will take place. It will feature the top 20 student teams of Russia, which have made their projects in the categories of games , innovations and social projects . To grow and select these teams, we conducted more than a dozen hackathons throughout the country, regional finals of the competitions were held in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Yekaterinburg, Tomsk, Kazan, Astrakhan, Voronezh, Saratov, Ulyanovsk and Perm. Come and see the unique student projects, listen to interesting reports (including about spherical robots in the cloud ), take part in self-promotion workshops (from a famous blogger) and on joining a large company. Read more about what awaits you - read below.



JOIN and come to the event in the very center of Moscow - in the innovative office of Mail.ru Group ( Leningradsky Prospect, 39 p.79 , m. Airport, SkyLight business center ). If you want to tell a friend about the event, then here is the VKontakte event , and here is a poster that can be printed and hung on the wall .

Not only student teams will perform in front of you on this day! Continuing the tradition of student days of technology , we have prepared for you interesting reports from the best experts from Microsoft and our partners. Here is what awaits you:
  • Performances of 20 teams in the categories of games , innovations and social projects from MIPT, HSE, MSTU. Bauman, ASTU, SSTU, PNIPU and other Russian universities. You will help us choose the best project in the category "Audience Award." Be sure to come to cheer for Moscow universities and bring friends!
  • Exhibition of student projects, during which you can get acquainted with all the teams personally. Make friends with the most active students of Russia who make up the intellectual and creative potential of our country!
  • Master classes on programming spherical robots and on effective marketing of mobile applications from Microsoft evangelists.
  • In addition, we recall what BizSpark is, and how to get free Microsoft software and resources in the Windows Azure cloud for more than 5000 rubles. per month if you are working on an interesting project.
  • Interactive sessions and trainings from the staff of the HR department and Microsoft PR and invited guests. You can learn the most secret secrets of successful interview!
  • Lectures from our sponsors and partners : Mail.ru Group, ABBYY.
  • Special activities from Microsoft partner students , including technology magic and community on-site ceremony

Of course, the main task of the event remains the selection of the best Russian teams in each category, one of which will represent Russia at the international final in Seattle. Students from Moscow and the surrounding area - be sure to register , come in person and invite your friends. Those who live far away - come to us to watch the broadcast on site imaginecup.ru .
