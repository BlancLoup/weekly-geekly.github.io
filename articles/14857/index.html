YouTube videos embedded in adsense

YouTube videos will be embedded in the global content network of Google AdSense, reports AP . Thus, amateur video clips can be shown by keywords on millions of partner sites (in principle, they are already broadcast, just for now without advertising and do not bring profit), and their authors, judging by everything, will receive a part of advertising payments, because traditionally Google gives partners from 60% to 80% of their income. Official confirmation of this news should come very soon.

As you know, Google bought YouTube a year ago and since then has never thought of a profitable business model for it. About a month ago, video clips from YouTube first appeared in Google search results, and now follows a logical continuation of this experiment.

Of course, the ad network will not immediately get all the videos that are stored on the site. This is only possible if the author of the video clip signs a special agreement and agrees to the advertising display.
