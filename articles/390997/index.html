9 drones that do not need registration



Suddenly, you still do not know, but all drones weighing more than 255 grams need to be registered.

The Federal Aviation Administration of the United States said that most of the drones that are currently being acquired are subject to mandatory registration, since their weight is greater than indicated above. For example, DJI Phantom 3 Standard weighs 1.2 kg, 3D Robotics Solo Drone 1.5 kg, and Iris + weighs almost 1.3 kg.


1. Aerius




Perhaps the Aerius drone is the smallest quadcopter in the world. Its weight is only 7 grams. This drone flies 5-7 minutes, charging time - 15 minutes. In addition, the drone has a 6-axis gyroscopic stabilizer, a 360-degree rotation in the horizontal plane and a programmed Trick-Mode for performing various tricks. Charging is via USB.


2. Cheerson CX-10




The weight of the Cheerson CX-10 mini-quadrocopter is only 12 grams, and the dimensions are 39 x 39 x 17 mm. The lightweight frame of the drone allows it to fly 4-8 minutes thanks to a 3.7 V lithium battery at 100 mAh.

In addition, the CX-10 6 axes and it is possible to install a gyroscopic stabilizer. Has 3 levels of speed. If you want to test it at night - no problem, the drone has a LED for this purpose.


3. Skeye Nano Drone




The Skeye Nano Drone nanodrone also weighs 12 grams, and its dimensions are 39 x 39 mm. Flies 7-8 minutes, charging time - 30 minutes.

The drone has three flight modes: for beginners, advanced users and professionals. Equipped with LEDs for flying with poor visibility.


4. FQ777




The FQ777 is a pocket-weight drone weighing 13 grams, and the 6-axis gyroscopic stabilizer provides flight stability both indoors and outdoors.

A unique feature of this drone is that it is able to absorb energy from a special battery, which is also a controller, its charging time is 20 minutes, the duration of uninterrupted flight is 5-6 minutes, the maximum distance is 50 meters.


5. Rolling Spider




The Parrot Mini Rolling Spider mini-rifle is designed for flying outdoors and indoors for 8 minutes in a row. The ultra-compact drone rotates 90 ¬∞ and 180 ¬∞ and can flip back and forth through 360 ¬∞.

The device weighs 55 grams, has removable wheels (65 grams with them), can move on the floor, walls and ceiling.

In addition, the drone is equipped with a vertical mini-camera and the Free Flight 3 application to control and monitor the drone via Bluetooth using a smartphone or tablet.


6. UDI U839




The 6-axis UDI U839 quadcopter weighs 35.7 grams, has 2.4 GHz anti-jamming technology.
UDI U839 flies 5-7 minutes without recharging and has 4 LEDs for flying in the dark.


7. YiZhan X4




The YiZhan X4 drone weighing 33 grams has simply amazing range of control - about 100 meters. The flight duration is about 6 minutes, it can perform 360 ¬∞ turns.


8. Estes 4606




Estes 4606 weighs 11 grams and flies for about 5 minutes on one charge. Suitable for indoor use only and is not equipped with a camera. However, it is a good device for beginners.


9. Eachine H8




The weight of eachine H8 6-axis drone does not reach 20 grams. Equipped with a gyroscopic system for flight stability and easy handling. Charges using USB for 20-25 minutes, and the flight time is about 5.5 minutes indoors and about 5 minutes on the street. Can make somersaults in all four directions. It has 4 LEDs.
