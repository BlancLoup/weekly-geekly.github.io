MUSICDEPO.RU - Community of Artists and Fans

Hello!

We did our best here and decided to make a community of musicians and their fans.

Have you ever indulged in writing music, or do you even enjoy it professionally? We have made a small step, which will allow, if it is given to you at all, to gain somewhat greater popularity.

Now everyone, even a novice musician, can lay out his tracks and begins to earn reward points that he gets from the audience.

We made several different ratings that allow us to subject each track to a comprehensive analysis (said, adjusting points), for example, to see the number of auditions, see the assessment of the hall, we even have a jury of several professional musicians who can express their own opinions. Also, according to a secret formula, we calculate such a vital thing as the popularity of the artist.

We tried to make the navigation as varied as possible: for example, you can view artists by genre, by country, and of course, write entries by any letter.

Of course, we have a general rating of artists, consisting of one hundred positions, sorted by an unbiased opinion of the audience and a biased opinion (joke!) Of the jury.

Also, you can write your own news that will appear on the main page, even more drawing attention to their creativity.

And, yes, you will not believe what we are going to do. Each artist will be able to buy a small promo for honestly earned reward points here, on the museum, as a line in the catalog of artists by genre, as well as a couple of other services, such as sending your data and tracks to special agencies around the world, which we carefully compiled. Of course, this is like a dream: you simply upload your tracks, which you may have forgotten 5 years ago, enjoy the reaction of the audience, and having gained popularity and reward points, spend them on the very only chance that we have prepared for you . Simply put - we figured out how to turn virtual popularity into real, acting as a producer for you.

It makes no sense to say that everything went for a long time to this - artists earn less and less, coming to the Internet, independently organizing VKontakte groups and earning just as much as they are talented.

Today, more than a thousand artists have registered for MuzDepo , and the number of listeners is close to five hundred, which makes us very happy: it means that we did a really useful thing.

We were pleasantly surprised to meet some well-known tracks. Remember, Coca-Cola, "Holiday comes to you"? So that same girl with the most wonderful voice.
