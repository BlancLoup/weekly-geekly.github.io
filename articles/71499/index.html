Apple protects its logo

Australian supermarket chain Woolworths may receive a lawsuit from Apple for the similarity of its new logo, which is presented in the form of a letter W stylized as a green apple.



Woolworths registered a new logo in August 2008.

A request regarding the similarity of the logos was sent to the Australian Department of Intellectual Property (IP Australia), which should recognize the similarity of the logos or refute. If the answer is yes, Apple will have the right to sue.
