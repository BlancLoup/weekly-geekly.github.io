Smart assistant


Smartphones work on a modified version of Android Nougat - the Google Assistant personal assistant is built into it by default, and this is the first device on the market with it on board. In essence, this is an improved version of Google Now: it is caused by holding the central on-screen button, it can search for information on the network, answer user questions, send a message to WhatsApp, book tables at restaurants, play music, and so on.

Assitant is such a Siri on Android, only smarter: for example, it can recognize the context. If you ask the assistant about who Sundar Pichai is, all additional questions about him can be asked using pronouns: artificial intelligence will understand that you are still interested in the identity of the top manager. Assitant in general can be called the leitmotif of the entire presentation - Google believes that the interfaces of the future should be tied to the use of AI.
