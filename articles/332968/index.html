Google Developer Days Comes to Europe


We like Google to meet and communicate with developers. This helps us better understand your needs and improve our products so that they are suitable for the different regions where you work. Developer events are a great way to do this. That is why we hold various events where we can communicate directly with developers. Today we launched a special website and open registration for the European part of our global series of events for developers - Google Developers Days (GDD).


Google Developers Days is an event where we‚Äôll tell you about the latest updates to our products and developer platforms that will help you continue to develop high-quality applications, develop your products, and maintain an active user base. GDD Europe will be held on September 5 and 6 in Krakow in Poland. Technical reports on a range of products, including Android, Mobile Web, Firebase, Cloud, Machine Learning and IoT, await you. In addition to the technical presentations, all participants will have the opportunity to join hands-on activities, one-on-one to chat with Google and the Google Developers Experts staff.



If you are interested and want to join us, then registration is already open. Now you can buy tickets at a discount, priced at $ 199 (full price $ 250). Please note that the number of tickets is limited.


If for some reason you cannot get to Krakow - no problem! All reports will be available live on the Google Developers YouTube channel , and later will be available as records. It's always great to watch such events in company with other developers, so GDD Extended will be held in different cities and regions. This is a format where developers gather to share and discuss the live broadcast. As a rule, Google Developers Groups in different cities organize such events. If someone in your area has already organized GDD Extended, then consider visiting it. Didn't find such a place nearby, but still want to gather colleagues in the workshop to discuss the latest news together? You can organize GDD Extended by yourself.


Regardless of whether you have the opportunity to join us in person or remotely, be aware of the latest news, follow the special hashtag - #GDDEurope on Twitter , Facebook and Google+ .


We look forward to seeing you soon in Europe!
