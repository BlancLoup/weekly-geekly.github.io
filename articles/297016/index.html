How to choose a site builder?

Choosing the right platform for creating an Internet site is the very first step you take. How not to make a mistake in choosing a site designer:



1. The first thing you should pay attention to is having a free catalog of templates for quick launch . This will significantly save your time and money.

2. The cost of subscription services - at the first steps in the business or testing niche, all costs matter. Some site designers have a test period of 14 days. This is not enough to familiarize yourself with the platform, and then you should pay.

3. Active support service . Each system has its own logic and must be dealt with. Friendly on-line support will help you to understand all the subtleties and advantages of your chosen platform.

4. Knowledge Base. Reference materials on work with the designer site. These are usually text articles and video instructions. They will help you quickly understand and deal with the designer.

5. Ability to integrate with third-party systems. There is nothing worse than a "closed" system. There is no system that would be perfect in everything. You should always be able to connect to your site your favorite CRM system or service for E-mail marketing.

6. Simple and easy site builder. You need to create a site without knowledge of programming and html layout. It should not be difficult and long. The site is just a sales tool, and you should not spend too much time on it.

7. Mobile version of the site. Now the popularity of mobile devices and tablets is growing. Sometimes you need to create a separate site. Remove all unnecessary and put a big button "Call me!".
