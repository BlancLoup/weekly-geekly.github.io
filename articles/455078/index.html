Where in the summer to talk about Apache Ignite and distributed systems

An Apache Ignite Meetup mitap will be held in St. Petersburg on June 14 : real cases . Let's listen to the guys who did it. The first case is IMDG for calculating the margins of trade contracts in Heineken. The second is the Gazpromneft industrial platform. Why open source and Ignite? Where is screwed? How and why does it work? Speakers will answer these and other questions at the mitap. Join us on Friday evening, June 14th. So that it was not stuffy, we even found a bigger room - registration .
