Blizzard Story

In the world there are many companies involved in the production of computer games. But only a few of them are considered legendary. One of these is the American Blizzard.



The company is famous for its hit series of games, such as Diablo, WarCraft and StarCraft. In addition, Blizzard is the best example of a company that has never compromised on the quality of a product for the sake of money or the speed of its intake. This story is about the real creators of the digital age.

First success


Alain Edham while still studying at the institute dreamed of creating computer games. The very process of programming brought him a lot of pleasure. At the same time, a young man, of course, would not mind linking his life with this craft. It was necessary to solve only one problem - the games were supposed to bring him money.

The solution to this problem, Alain took up while studying at the institute. Fortunately, by that time he was already a good programmer, and he had a good idea about the game industry, as he was in close contact with the founder of Interplay, Brian Fargo. In 1991, Alain Edham and his friend Mike Morheim opened their company. They gave her the name "Silicon & Synapse". The starting capital of the company amounted to 10 thousand dollars. In addition to the founders, there was another person in it - Frank Pierce, who became the first employee of the company.

I must say that the history of Blizzard is to some extent a typical history of a computer company. Companies of the XXI century. Why? At the time of its foundation, Alain Edhem was only 22 years old. Naturally, the founders had to lead a lifestyle of real startups. Thus, in the first two years of the company's operations, neither Alain Edham nor Mike Morheim paid any wages. They tried to invest all the money in the business.

Edham met Brian Fargo in vain. The first orders the S & S company received from Interplay. Moreover, a number of magazines even assigned S & R to the title of the best game designer of the year. This was just the beginning.


Despite such success in terms of creating good products, business is another. The company was slowly but surely plunging into debt. Her debt at that time was about 20 thousand dollars. Now the company is working on a new update, which should see the light in 2008-2009. I must say that the popularity of the world of WoW led to the fact that in the near future will begin shooting an epic film based on the universe of WarCraft.
