The Facebook Gifts service is closing

A couple of days ago it was announced that the Facebook Gifts service is closing.



From the prehistory.

Four years ago, on August 1, 2010, the gift service on Facebook was already closed. Then they explained it by the need to focus on other areas - games, news, likes and the like.

In fact, apparently, this service was not particularly popular with users, although it would seem that Chinese social networks earn well on gifts, and our Odnoklassniki also have the overwhelming majority of revenue from sales of virtual gifts .

Meanwhile, the IPO was nearing, and the closer Facebook came to this event, the sooner he needed to be shown what he was going to earn in the future. Apparently, therefore, in May 2012, Facebook acquired a startup, Karma, which specialized in sending gifts to the USA, and used data from Facebook for its work. At the same time, the startup essentially began work only in February 2012 and at that time he had 16 employees. The acquisition cost, as it was written in some blogs, 80 million dollars, and in September 2012, Facebook Gifts service was opened on the basis of Karma.
