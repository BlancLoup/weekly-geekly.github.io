Released official twitter client for android

Yesterday the official twitter client for android came out. Unfortunately, the client is available only for android 2.1 and higher. You can download it from the market - the first search rule on the word twitter or open this link in a mobile browser.



As can be seen from the screenshot above, it even loads previews of pictures from twitpic. Other client features: photo upload, url abbreviation, animated background, adding your location, notifications about new tweets, mentions and messages. From the currently unavailable features, you can select the video download through the application.

I was also pleased with the possibility of uploading several photos to one tweet, this is exactly what I lacked in all other clients:



In my opinion, the client turned out to be more than competitive: comfortable, beautiful, official, there are almost all the functions necessary for a full-fledged twitter-life :)
