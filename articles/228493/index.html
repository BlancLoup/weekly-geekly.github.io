Correspondence Programming Contest for MIPT applicants

For applicants MIPT conducted a school in applied mathematics and physics (more about it can be found on the official site ). On July 8, there will be a full-time Olympiad in mathematics and physics, but in computer science the Olympiad is by correspondence and is held at http://judge.mipt.ru . The Olympiad is held on the Kirov system on Ejudge. The results of this Olympiad will be taken into account at the interview at MIPT and the distribution of freshmen into groups in computer science. The winners will receive prizes from sponsors - the basic organizations of FUPM and FRTK MFTI - companies Parallels and Acronis.

The Olympics will end on July 7, 2014. All tasks are simple and are aimed more at ensuring that applicants once again realize their knowledge of languages, algorithms, and computer architecture, as well as become familiar with the testing system, with which very many of them will have to communicate during the entire first course.
