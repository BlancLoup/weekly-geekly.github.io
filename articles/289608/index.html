What prevents you from getting traffic for free?

Often, users of our service, solving the problem of increasing conversion , forget about the main thing. To convert visitors to customers, they must first be attracted. Therefore, we open a cycle of posts about high-quality channels to attract leads. Let's start with shareware - with search engine promotion.




Search traffic is still the most cost-effective sales channel for many sites. If you plan to use it for a new project - read the recommendations of experts on promotion. We learned from them the most significant mistakes, because of which the young project risks falling out of favor with users and search engines.

Head of Setup.ru

  • Pages are not sharpened by keywords: they forget to insert keys into the title, headings and text.
  • The promotion is focused on high-frequency queries, although for young sites it is necessary to work out the semantic core and start with low-frequency speakers.
  • Selected many keys for which the main page is assigned to the target (landing). It is not right. The page optimally advances in 1-2 requests, no more.

Michael commented on the main mistakes on the promotion:

After 2-3 months, search engines begin to take into account behavioral factors. We recommend to install Google Analytics and Yandex Metric.
  • The hunt for quick links. Many site owners are impatient and want to quickly get the fruits for their work, return and increase funds. They are trying to get as many links as possible. They do not care that the links may not be of high quality. In addition, the rate of growth of the reference mass is taken into account by search engine algorithms. You can not chase the number. Links must be of high quality. Everything should be done gradually and naturally, without arousing suspicion on the part of search engines. Link quality is measured by how often it links to your site.
  • Many project owners and webmasters do not see the picture as a whole. They create a site, focusing on search engines, not realizing that this is only a tool (intermediary) between the site and the user (potential buyer). Conducting resource optimization and the use of dark methods of promotion does not carry anything of value for the interests and convenience of the user on the site. At the same time, resource owners forget to add useful articles, videos, auxiliary elements. But it is the content that allows the user to get the necessary information. And there is a mistake here too. There is a savings in content: it is copied from other sites and do not think about why search engines should rank the new resource above that from which they have just borrowed content. SEO is a complex and time-consuming work. There are no quick tricks. This is real marketing, these are not toys.

Given the importance of texts for the site, we have compiled a minimum checklist for determining their quality.

First of all, the article should have been a complete response to the user's request. If a paragraph is deleted, and the essence of the text does not change from this - it means you need to throw it away. Squeeze the "water". If the article ‚Äúhow to make a roof with your own hands‚Äù does not allow the user to realize this, then the value of such a ‚Äúmaterial‚Äù is not high.

In addition to the benefits of the text, which the user needs in the first place, there is also the simplicity of perception of the material. How to provide it? In order not to be mistaken in the quality of texts, check them for these requirements:
  1. In two adjacent sentences there should not be repeated words. The so-called lexical repetitions. This is not to mention one sentence! A simple example. If the text is about "promotion" - this does not mean that in every sentence there should be the word "promotion". When it occasionally slips or is needed within the meaning, this is permissible. But pretty often you can't do that. From here also notorious "nausea" undertakes.
