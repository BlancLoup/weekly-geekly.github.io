We have become certified partners Magento 2



Magento platform appeared on the e-commerce market only five years ago, but during this time it managed to firmly consolidate its positions. This proves the popularity rating and statistics, which we have repeatedly written in our blog on the official website of the company.

To date, the development vector of Magento Inc. focuses on Magento 2 - the second version of the platform with many new features of the architecture and functionality.

On this occasion, we share with you great news - we have become certified partners of Magento 2 (Magento 2 trained solution partner).

Magento 2 certification process

To become a certified partner, a team of specialists must complete a serious 100-hour course. Training is conducted by Frontend and Backend developers.

The training consists of several stages:

  1. Fundamentals of Magento 2 Development for backend developers and Core principles for theming in Magento 2 for frontend developers;
  2. Development of modules for Magento 2 and final examination testing;
  3. Checking the quality of work by leading experts from Magento Inc. head office.

An important feature of the training is that only official partners of Magento have access to materials and programs on Magento 2, and only employees of partner companies can be certified to work with the system.

To date, 87 official Magento partners from around the world have completed a total of 11,000 study hours on Magento 2. We are the first Russian company to pass this certification.

Why is it important

It is worth noting that the demand for Magento 2 appeared even before the official release of the system. And then it will only grow, stimulating the growth of the development services market.

The status of a Magento 2 certified partner can be considered a guarantee of the quality and reliability of selected partners. This is exactly the criterion for selecting a development team to pay attention to.

We are proud to be among the first to be certified to work with the platform. This will help to reach a new level both for us and for the projects we create.

We will be happy to give you advice and answer all the necessary questions regarding Magento 2.
