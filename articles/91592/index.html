Separate line about the Mariinsky Theater

The Mariinsky Theater is a pioneer in introducing innovative methods of communication among the world's cultural institutions. It was the Mariinka who carried out in 1999 for the first time in history a direct online broadcast of an opera performance.

The partner of the Mariinsky Theater in organizing the world's first live television broadcast of the ballet in 3D is the Russian Corporation General Satellite. The signal will be transmitted in an open format, which will make it possible for any TV viewer with the necessary 3D equipment to view the world premiere.

As part of the celebration of the year of Russia in France, a gala evening was held at the residence of the Russian ambassador in Paris with a viewing of this gala concert. A special platform for viewing 3D broadcasts was also equipped in Moscow, which I actually visited.
