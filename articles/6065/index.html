Run BetsGoWild: go argue! :-)

Today, the public beta of the New York startup BetsGoWild , a community that makes different bets among themselves, was launched. The site uses its own virtual money, called Wildbucks.

Each user can make a bet, but not with one person, but with the whole community. So, in the settings for betting (it sounds unusual, right?), You naturally need to set a topic for the dispute, choose the side that you accept in this dispute (agree / disagree), place tags, put in a category (which is now eight + " other "), specify the date when the dispute is resolved and the date until which you can argue. You can indicate what the dispute will be about (you can choose either virtual money or some kind of gift). In each topic with a bet you can insert photos, audio and video.

Users can argue about different topic-questions. Initially (after registration), the user receives 1500 WildBucks, which, in fact, make (in most cases) a dispute. If you vote with the majority, then the amount of winnings is reduced, if you make a risky statement, then in case of victory you can take a decent sum. If you won, then in addition to increasing your virtual money (or getting some prize), you will raise your rating. Well, if you lose - stay with your nose :)
