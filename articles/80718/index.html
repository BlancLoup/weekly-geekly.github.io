In April of this year, iPhone 4G will appear; rumor has it that there will be support for fourth-generation cellular networks

The Korea Times published yesterday that the Korean mobile operator, called KT, plans to begin selling a new generation of iPhone mobile phones in April, which this time will be equipped with dual-core processors and powerful graphics co-processors (for video and photo processing), and screens on organic light emitting diodes (OLED), and support live video chats.

The AppleInsider website publishes another leak from Apple's internal sources : the new devices will be completed with ARM processors of the Cortex-A9 design, capable of overcoming the two-gighertz frequency limit. It also assumes the introduction of RFID technology , providing non-contact authentication.
