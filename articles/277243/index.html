Useful links for warranty verification

In the work of any service center, it is not uncommon for the customer to contact for support with equipment whose warranty is overdue. And the fact of delay becomes an unpleasant surprise for them.

This applies not only to laptops or PCs, but also servers, switches and other "heavy" IT hardware. We decided to record a brief video tutorial on how to view the warranty of the device, and, last but not least, its equipment / specification.

Check warranty is especially useful immediately after purchase or even better - before buying any IT equipment. Under the cat useful links and a couple of short videos on how to check the warranty devices from manufacturers such as HP, Dell, Cisco, APC, etc.
