Creating and sharing geographic information
Millions of Google Earth users add geographic information and exchange data every day using .KML, the Google Earth file format. In Google Earth 4, we have expanded this feature. You can now view travel photos and hiking trails downloaded from a GPS device, images overlaid on your own images, or large sets of geographic data. You can even add time stamps to the data to create animations that show how the world changes over time.
