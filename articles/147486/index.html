Ukrainian team QuadSquad won the Imagine Cup 2012


About a couple of hours ago, it became known that the QuadSquad team of Ukrainian developers beat 75 rivals and won the tenth annual Imagine Cup competition. The theme of this year was: "Imagine a world in which technology solves the most serious problems."

The second place was taken by the Coccolo team from Japan with the original energy saving project.

The third place went to the Portuguese team WI-GO for the development of shopping trolleys managed through Kinnect .

Next year the competition will be held in St. Petersburg.

Congratulations!
