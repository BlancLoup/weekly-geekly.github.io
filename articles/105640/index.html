Returning to the issue of emigration: the lottery from the US government to issue Green Cards has begun

My recently posted topic Would you like to emigrate? caused a heated discussion. In the comments, a war broke out between those who "want to leave" and "patriots of this country", occasionally diluted with messages from those who "have already sat on the tractor and left." The voting results showed that the overwhelming majority of those present here (74.24%) want to emigrate. I have good news for you: the US government has started the next lottery season, the winners of which will get the Green Cards.

Participation Instructions: travel.state.gov/pdf/DV_2012_Instructions_Russian.pdf
Application Form: www.dvlottery.state.gov/application.aspx
Checking photos for the form: www.dvlottery.state.gov/photo.aspx
