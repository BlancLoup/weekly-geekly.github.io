Creating your own "television" based on torrent-trackers

Just want to say that the word ‚Äútelevision‚Äù does not mean live broadcast.
I do not watch TV at all. But every day I can not choose what to watch either - there is no strength.

Therefore I want to have a folder (RSS feed, list, whatever name you like)
something new that I can see today.

I suggest such an idea for your critical discussion.

Let the computer have a folder with movies.

Every 3 days the content in this folder changes.
Every day, the folder contains - 3 foreign films, 2 old foreign films, 3 our old / new ones, 2 full-length cartoon films, 2 TV shows

How to download?

We first apply filters to all samples - not DVDScreener, size from 600 MB to 2.4GB, there are at least 10 seeds, in the .torrent file there is no more than one AVI / MKV. By title, we exclude those films that were watched during the last 12 months.
  • Foreign films - 3 random from the 1st page " Movies 2010-2011 ",
  • Old foreign - 2 random from the first 5 pages of " Movies 1991-2000 "
  • Our films - 3 random from the first 5 pages of " Our cinema ".
  • etc...

How to delete?

Every day, 20% of the films are randomly selected in the folder for deletion, which were downloaded more than 3 days ago. If, according to the films, Seed has a rating lower than 1, then they are moved to the folder (wait-for-seed) (It is deleted from it after 7 days).

Traffic consumption, places

Space - max 30 GB + 30 GB wait-for-seed.
Traffic - about 3 films are downloaded daily, 8 GB per day.

What you need to implement
  • Own database
  • Parser torrent tracker
  • API to manage torrent client
  • Crown run
