Handybank Integration

Good afternoon, habrovchane!

Today we will talk about the Internet Bank.
We sincerely believe that the less a client goes to the office, the better. And the more banking transactions it will be available through the Internet bank, the better. Saves time that a person can spend on more useful goals than a trip to the bank :) That is why we pay great attention to the development of our Internet banking. But there is no limit to perfection. There are no ideal systems and, to make the list of opportunities for our customers more extensive, we have integrated with the Handybank system.


This service has become an addition to its own, already running Internet bank . And, by the way, the system regularly takes the first places in ratings of banks, for example, the banki.ru portal and Expert magazine.

In fact, this is another intern-bank that our customers can use. In order to connect to it, it is enough just to issue a virtual HandyBank card in the Internet Bank for individuals of Bank24.ru and be registered in the System. Login to work in the HandyBank System is your cell phone number.

With the HandyBank System you can pay for various services in 60 cities of the Russian Federation:

1. View full information about your accounts

2. With the help of ready-made templates, you can pay for cellular services online and without commissions.

3. Utilities
4. Internet Service Providers
5. Products that are going to be purchased in online stores
6. Air and train tickets
7. Convert currency
