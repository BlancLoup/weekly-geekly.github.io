CIB Usability minicection

April 18, 12:00 - 13:30 in the framework of the Internet and Business (CIB) conference, a usability minicection will be held
Venue: Moscow, pension BOR , Mirror Hall.

The section assumes several reports in which the focus will be on successful examples of usability methods.

The following reports are planned.
* How and in what does web usability help?
Andrei Udalov, UKTs of the Group Makhaon, usable.ru
* Usability intranet systems
Konstantin Samoilov, HCI.ru, hci.ru
* Fast fixation of usage scenarios
Alexey Kopylov, UIDesign Group, uidesign.ru
* HolyGrail. Prototyping system for managers
Pavel Konoplitsky, www.amazedev.com
* Several illustrations to the problem of restructuring the website and tracking the effect of the work performed
Ivan Makarchuk, Miheeff.ru
* "Click Card"
Nadezhda Shipilova, Stat24.com, www.stat24.ru
* ‚ÄúSite Path Map‚Äù - graph of transitions
Anton Nikolaev, Alt-Media Web, www.tropinki.com
* Application of the method of recording eye movement in usability testing
Igor Zakharchenko, UsabilityLab, usabilitylab.ru

Duration of reports - 10 minutes.

The section will be of interest to managers of Internet projects, specialists, one way or another connected with the development and support of Internet projects.

Within the section, it is planned to discuss issues related to usability, the effectiveness of its use, and other issues of interest to visitors to the section.

Members of the RusCHI community will be active participants in the section.

RusCHI is the Russian branch of ACM SIGCHI, a specialized group on human interaction with the computer of the International Association for Computing Machinery Society. sigchi.ru/index_ru.htm
