15 sites in four countries have already joined PHDays Everywhere



Very soon, in Moscow, at the international forum Positive Hack Days, practical security experts, scientists, politicians and businessmen from around the world will gather. And for the third year in a row, visitors of the sites organized in other cities thanks to the PHDays Everywhere initiative will be able to join them ( how everything happened last year can be found on the forum blog).

On May 21 and 22, the hacker sites of different countries will open their doors. Abdu Dhabi (UAE), Bir-Zeit (Palestine), Kiev and Lviv, as well as Vladivostok, Vologda, Krasnodar, Moscow, Murmansk, Novosibirsk, Omsk, Samara, Saratov and Ufa have already joined PHDays Everywhere.

The PHDays program includes presentations and master classes by leading world experts, contests and online competitions, the PHDays CTF hacker battle, the Young School young scientists contest, and much more. Visitors to PHDays Everywhere will be able to watch all forum events in the live broadcast in HD format in English and Russian.

Competition


In addition to online contests, in which any Internet user can take part, separate competitions are organized for guests of PHDays Everywhere sites. They will be able to test their erudition during the PHDays Quiz quiz, participate in their own CTF and compete with teams from other hackspaces in a separate standings Online HackQuest, presented by the PentestIT laboratory. Memorable prizes from the organizers of PHDays will be received by a Twitter user, who described in more detail about PHDays in his city, as well as the author of the best published report from the scene. Detailed information on all competitions is presented in the PHDays blog.

Organizers of PHDays Everywhere will also hold separate competitions for visitors from other cities: their CTF will be held in Vladivostok, Novosibirsk and Omsk.

Do not miss the opportunity to talk with information security specialists in your city and take part in exciting competitions. Join PHDays Everywhere!

A complete list of PHDays Everywhere is available on the forum website. New members are added constantly, so if you have not yet found your city in the list - do not be discouraged, there is still time.
