Pre-ordered PS Vita in Russia

Good news! From December 25, 2011, a pre-order for the PS Vita will open in Russia, which can be issued before the console starts selling in late February - early March 2012. Making a prepayment of 999 rubles. During the promotion period, users will be able to receive a guaranteed discount on the PS Vita in the amount of the pre-order, as well as a set that includes special offers for the purchase of games, exclusive content and accessories, until April 30, 2012. The cost of the model PS Vita, equipped only with Wi-Fi module, will be 12,490 rubles, the price of the model with 3G support and Wi-Fi will be announced later.

Users who have pre-ordered any PS Vita model will receive:
  • The right of preferential purchase of the console with a discount of 999 rubles.
  • Branded PlayStation headphones blue.
  • The right of early access to the application Frobisher Says !, which will allow you to experience all the revolutionary features of PS Vita in a series of unusual mini-games - alone or with friends.
  • Voucher for a discount of 170 rubles. on PlayStation Store for one of the following games - Madhouse in Your Pocket (Little Deviants), Super Star Dust Delta, Hustle Kings or Escape Plan - each of which in a new way reveals the unique features of PSVita.
  • A set of virtual items from the PS Vita series for the PlayStation Home service: T-shirt, headphones, digital glasses, sneakers and bag.
  • Exclusive PS VITA avatar for PlayStation Network.
Make a pre-order for PS Vita and get an exclusive kit will be available in a number of M.Video, White Wind Digital, Time to Play, GAMEZONE, GamePark, Kay, 1C Interest, Republic of Games stores, Sony Center and the Sony online store (http://store.sony.ru/).
