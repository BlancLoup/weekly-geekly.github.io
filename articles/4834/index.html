Doctors have studied the "mouse rage syndrome"

Syndrome mouse rage (SMYA) - a phenomenon as ancient as the Internet, according to British scientists . In fact, this disease was generated by the Network itself.

Symptoms of SMYA are well studied: this is a rapid heartbeat, excessive sweating, anger and, and the most characteristic symptom is fierce clicks and hitting the computer mouse. In some, especially acute forms, the attack of SMYA is accompanied by screams towards the computer monitor. Mouse rage syndrome is an acute illness that is transmitted exclusively by network through the Internet.

The British Research Center for Social Problems conducted a large-scale study, which was attended by two and a half thousand Internet users. They found out that the syndrome of MSM negatively affects not only the heart, but also the nervous and immune systems of the body.

The main reason for the emergence of SMS is bad design, illogical navigation on websites on the Internet, slow loading of web pages, pop-up windows, unnecessary advertising and other elements that cause extreme irritation among users. One of the biggest irritants - the inaccessibility of the site.
