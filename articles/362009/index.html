Sensotrack Best: measurement of pressure, body temperature and oxygen in the blood "on the go"



On Kickstarter, an interesting project of a universal fitness tracker appeared, which can replace a number of devices. The device is capable of much: measuring blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen level in the blood, as well as counting calories expended.

All calculations and measurements the device keeps in real time, keeping a permanent record. In addition, Sensotrack Best helps to fulfill its goals, including, for example, the intention to take at least 4 kilometers a day.

Features Sensotrack Best





Sensotrack Best can keep records when performing various exercises and types of workouts, including walking, running, cycling, martial arts and more.

The main functions of the device:
  • Heart rate measurement;
  • Measurement of respiratory rate;
  • Measurement of oxygen in the blood;
  • Blood pressure measurement;
  • Counting the steps taken;
  • Determination of coordinates;
  • Activity tracker;
  • Tracker "good mood";
  • Personal trainer;
  • Workout log.


All data is transmitted to the application on the mobile device via a wireless connection. Data can be viewed at any time, monitoring is ongoing. Before use, you need to enter data such as body mass index, blood sugar level, weight and some other parameters.

As a personal trainer, the device helps:
  • Set different goals (take at least 5 thousand steps, for example);
  • Follow the implementation of goals;
  • Compare your results with those of other Sensotrack Best owners;
  • Demonstrate the results achieved by community members;
  • Rate the state of the team (football, cycling).


Software





The developers have provided a version of the program for mobile devices (Android, iOS), as well as a web version. The capabilities of the various applications are approximately equal:
  • General review. View analytical information on the results of training for the day, week or month. Convenient provision of information in the form of graphs;
  • Archives. Saving archive information in the cloud, where it can be viewed at any time;
  • Detailed review. The results of individual training, broken down by various factors and biometrics;
  • Advanced analytics. Recording and analysis of biometric and geographic information parameters (coordinates, distance, height above sea level).




The device is suitable for both individual users and the entire team. And the coach of the team, using the appropriate software, can monitor the results / performance of this team.



The gadget can work offline. To this end, the developers have provided an internal drive where the information is stored, if it is not possible to connect Sensotrack Best to a mobile device. The developers' plans are to build in an autonomous GPS module, which allows keeping a record of geo-information data without connecting the device to a mobile device.

What's inside?





  • Gyroscope / accelerometer;
  • Optical biosensor;
  • Micro-USB port;
  • The speaker, allowing the user to receive sound notifications about various events (a certain level of blood pressure, pulse, etc.);
  • Silicone earpiece;
  • A battery that allows the device to work for about a day without recharging.




All data received by SensoTRACK is transferred to the cloud, while all information is encrypted, third parties do not have access to the data.

Price



Pre-order the device in Russia can be done at Medgadgets, the tracker will go on sale in December 2014.
