The sale of UMI.CMS will change the site management systems market.

The deal resulted in the creation of a new independent company, Umisoft (www.umisoft.ru), which will develop new and sell already existing versions of the aforementioned CMS system. UMI name for the company and the product, it was decided to keep as a tribute to the developer and the popularity of the product on the market.

Sergey Zhuravlev, co-owner of the largest ad network of Runet AdMedia and its director, took the position of the executive director of umisoft.
