What to do if the habra search does not find?

I encountered such a problem a month ago when writing another article on HabraHabr.
So I decided to use a search engine to solve my problem. Since HabraHabr is very well optimized for the search engine, I typed on google: the search phrase site: habrahabr.ru and got a very good result, some articles on my topic quickly ‚Äúdug out‚Äù in this way.
