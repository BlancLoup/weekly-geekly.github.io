Oversan-Luna - the second data center Oversana

As many of you know, the first Oversana data center, Oversan-Mercury, was officially put into operation at the beginning of 2010. Some time ago, the launch of the second, smaller and budget data center - Oversan-Luna.

The data center "Oversan-Luna" is located near the "Oversan-Mercury", seven hundred meters away, in a departmental protected area.
