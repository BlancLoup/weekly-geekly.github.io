The election of promising developers BlackBerry Developer Community Awards 2012

There are only 2 weeks left until the start of BlackBerry Jam Americas , and now is the right time to vote for the most promising BlackBerry developer. After all, right now, RIM has announced the BlackBerry Developer Community Awards 2012 and is counting on you.

Presented six categories in which the most promising developers will be selected. Finalists will be selected on September 18 and further voting will begin. Winners will be announced on September 26th. The award ceremony will be held at the BlackBerry Jam Americas Community Dev Awards .

Category List:

  • The Most Read Developer on Twitter
  • Most visited BlackBerry development blog
  • The most popular promoter of BlackBerry development
  • The most useful member of the BlackBerry team on Twitter
  • The most useful member of the BlackBerry Developer Forum
  • Best open source project

Ways of nomination:

  • Via online form
  • Via Twitter (just tweet mentioning BlackBerryDev with #BBDevAwards tag and specify a category)

Election procedure:

  • Until September 16, the nomination of candidates
  • From September 18 to 25, voting
  • September 26 Winners Announcement at BlackBerry Jam Americas Community Dev Awards

News source: Inside BlackBerry Developer Blog
