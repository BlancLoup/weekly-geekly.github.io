Clear, bold and affordable. Highscreen Alpha R Review

When a modern person chooses a smartphone for himself - he first of all thinks about the platform (iOS, Android, WindowsPhone), then about the manufacturer, then - about the device stuffing. So, choosing "on a piece of paper" - you always risk running into a cat in a bag. And if you choose with your eyes? I do not mean the design of the device, it is of course important. I mean the display.

The Highscreen Alpha R smartphone with a display size of 5 inches has a resolution of 1080 * 1920 and a matrix made using IPS technology. So what does this mean? This means excellent picture quality and excellent image clarity. In addition, the Alpha R comes with two batteries. This means that you can forget the jokes about the outlet.
Well, let's start?

Operating system: Android 4.2
Number of SIM-cards: 2 (Mode of operation of several SIM-cards: alternate)
Weight with 4000 mA / h battery - 204g, with 2000 mA / h battery - 156g
Dimensions (WxHxT): 73x143.5x9.3 mm

Equipment


Traditional for Highscreen brown box with a description of the main characteristics of the device.

Inside the box is a charger, headset, USB cable, 2000 mAh battery, 4000 mAh battery, two covers (respectively), the smartphone itself.

Just want to talk about batteries. With a standard (2000 mAh) battery, the device looks very elegant and modern. The thickness of this 9.3 mm. It is pleasantly in the hand, and the weight of 156 grams is almost perfect (there is no feeling of a toy in the hand).

If, in pursuit of autonomy, to put a high-capacity battery, thereby increasing the thickness (up to 14 mm) and weight (up to 204 grams) of the smartphone - it turns into a fat one. However, while still well in the hand, and the resulting autonomy is worth it.


Here so the battery 4000th mAh sticks out.

The cover is made elegantly, smoothly beating the "hernia" from the doubled work time.

Of course, the elegance of the case is no longer the same, but to call Alpha R a brick - the language does not turn. A shovel that loves to eat. So: with a 2000 mAh battery, we have 3.5 hours of screen operation (2-2.5 days of work), and with a 4000 mAh battery, 5 hours (4 days of work!). For me it is so good! You can take two batteries on a business trip and not think about the outlet.

Separately, I want to note the unreasonable location of the power button (or charging connector?). No, I'm not saying that the microUSB connector is on the top. This is just the same already familiar. I mean that it is very close to the unlock button (on). When the smartphone is charging, then unlock it oh how awkward. Fortunately, it is often not necessary to charge it;)

On the left side - only the volume rocker. On the right side - generally empty. The graceful line of the case is a really good designer's delicacy

Just kidding, of course. But everything is very, very good. In addition, there is no air gap between the sensor and the display - which means that you are practically touching the screen. Very nice for eyes and fingers.
