Corporate communication in Telegram. Migration from WhatsApp at full speed

The company is engaged in wholesale and retail sales. A staff of ~ 300 people. Many departments with extremely diverse specifics of work:

  • Office managers
  • Sales managers
  • Regional representatives
  • Storekeepers
  • Drivers
  • Installers
  • Etc.

From software:

  • 1C UT
  • Bitrix24
  • Whatsapp
  • Some GoogleDocs

Why did you get the idea to switch to Telegram?

Chatting in WhatsApp spontaneously. Although their number reached 25 for the most involved employees, they sometimes discussed everything in special chat rooms. Important messages drowned in the comments.

The idea was this: to divide the chat rooms into information channels (one-way communication) for individual professional issues. Chats for shopping are divided not only by topic, as it was before (one chat - one topic), but also shopping (one chat - one topic - one shop).

Thus, the number of chats increased, but the number of messages in them decreased.

Nobody had a professional experience of using Telegram, but his possibilities were intriguing. By professional use I mean

  • the difference of devices
  • great importance of stability
  • variety of content types
  • a large number of chats, channels, groups and supergroups

In my messages I am going to share my experience, I want to get good advice from you. I will answer questions.
