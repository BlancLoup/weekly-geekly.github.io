Right holders urge to leave "VKontakte" in the list of "pirates"

According to the newspaper Vedomosti, right now the agency is collecting the opinions of all interested parties in order to form a list of countries for 2014, which are the largest markets for the distribution of piracy of music, films and other content. At the end of September of this year, a representative of the International Intellectual Property Alliance (IIPA) wrote to officials of the Trade Representation that Russia as a whole does not fulfill its obligations to protect copyright and related rights taken after joining the World Trade Organization (WTO) in 2012.
