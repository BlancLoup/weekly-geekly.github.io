The final release of PySide 1.0.0

The Qt blog has news about the release of PySide 1.0.0 with multiple bugfixes and support for all of Qt 4.7 goodies (Qt Quick, QML), as well as Qt Mobility.
It is also reported that support is planned for Python 3k and more (Pythonic API)

PySide - Python language binding to Qt toolkit, compatible at the API level with PyQt . Unlike PyQt, PySide is available for free use in both open and closed, particularly commercial, projects, since it is licensed under the LGPL.
The project arose from the reluctance of the creators of PyQt to change the licensing policy for their project. PySide saw the light in August 2009, when the first public version was released. The main developers of PySide are Nokia programmers.
