How to make money on vending machines: business and plans

Hi, habrachelovek.

So it's time for the next release of the project Vending them. Andorro , dedicated to the business of vending machines in Russia.

From the comments to past posts , as well as questions posed by habrakpochte and ICQ, it became clear that everyone cares about money - or rather, how to correctly calculate the profitability of the vending business and draw up a business plan.

In this connection, the financially-planned side of the psto business in the vending business.

Who needs it - welcome under cat.

Counting money


As expected, the main thing in drawing up a vending business plan, like any other business, is taking into account future imminent costs and expected incomes. The income from each of the vending machines consists of the following key parameters:
  • Cost of service for the consumer
  • Cost of service for the operator
  • Number of service users per day
  • The number of days in the month when the device is available
  • for users (for example, in the case of installation of the machine in a place where visitors are not daily - in business centers, through passage enterprises, etc.).

Costs
  • Rental price (fixed amount or percentage of income).
  • Electricity costs (per month)
  • Taxes (default is income tax (STS).

The main point


How can you significantly influence the magic of numbers in your business plan? By and large, there is only one way - and this is the main law of the vending business: a thoughtful choice of location. Two key factors for a successful choice are the search for synergy and concern for convenience for consumers.

Synergy

Some types of machines may be an additional service in relation to the installation site.

For example, Avtosberkassa provides instant payment services with the receipt of a bank receipt - this service will be useful in almost all state institutions or universities, or the Pit-Start mentioned above, a self-service for tire inflation, filling windshield fluid and cleaning the vehicle interior - for gas stations in a competitive place can be an advantage.

According to the experience of some operators, it is possible to even agree on the free placement of such machines, because they will also attract customers or serve as an additional factor in their retention for your landlord. Perhaps it does not mean necessarily, but a thoughtful choice of the place to install the machine is a key moment of the success of the entire business.

Convenience

Take the machines, designed to meet the needs of hurrying people, for example - for the sale of printed publications. Pay attention to where the devices are installed in the Moscow metro? Not always in sight, and meet the buyer last - when the newspaper, most likely, already purchased. You will not put them on the aisle either, since it will be inconvenient for the buyer to make a purchase. On the other hand, this problem has been solved by automatic machines for instant payments - they really stand at every turn and this does not prevent their active use. Therefore, in the case of a point that will stand in the way of hurrying people, it is necessary to think carefully about the location of the vending machine for selling newspapers.

Another factor is shyness or secrecy. You will never see a condom vending machine on the street or just hanging in public in a public place - for the same reason that this kind of vending generally exists and develops successfully: people sometimes hesitate to buy condoms - even in pharmacies, not to mention , to do it in plain sight. In this case, a large cross point for the machine - rather evil.
