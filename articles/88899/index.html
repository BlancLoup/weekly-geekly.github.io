DrupalCampKyiv 2010 - the official opening. The conference will be held May 20-22 in Kiev

Hello, dear Community!


Today is spring!
Somewhere it is cold, somewhere frosty, somewhere - real.


But the reality is - there are 2 months left before an interesting event that happens between spring and summer.


Before the conference DrupalCampKyiv 2010 there is nothing left.


But really - May 20-22 - less than 60 days and it will come.


What do we already have?


A lot of desire and experience.

Last year - there were more than 200 participants and everyone remembers - it was great.

Now, we are planning more than 500 participants and we are doing our best to make it work.



At the time of publication, there are already more than 430 participants on the site, we hope that by joint efforts we will be able to increase the number of registrations - at this stage it is very important.


By organization

Today, at the time of publication, we officially open the conference site and give you the opportunity to learn about it first.

Fortunately, the promise to open the site stretched a little in time - we decided to take an important step to improve the conference site - about this, too, a little later ...


We have an offer to media partners - we hope for your solidarity in placing promotional materials on your Internet projects, especially if you have a real interest in the development of the Drupal movement in our common region.


We have prepared a lot of pleasant surprises and rewards for especially active partners.


We also have offers for sponsors - with you the chances of success become much higher. For our part, we guarantee the effectiveness of investments in DrupalCampKyiv. The experience and results of 2009 are proof of that.


Program


During the conference three sessions of sessions will be held simultaneously (reports, discussions, master classes), in just three days of the conference approximately 50 sessions will occur.

1st day, Thursday, May 20


Four parallel daytime commercial trainings. The trainings are designed for people who want to get a knowledge base before the main stream of reports on the next day.

2nd day, Friday, May 21


Two parallel streams of presentations. The duration of one presentation is 30 minutes. - 1 hour. Subjects of presentations - review Drupal, how-to trainings, presentations designed for beginners. A party

3rd day, Saturday, May 22


Three parallel streams of presentations. Reports designed for professionals, case-studies, a round table on the theme ‚Äúthe integration of the countries of Eastern Europe into the global Drupal-community‚Äù.


In the program, depending on the sponsorship and your participation as speakers, there may be changes in the direction of increasing the number.


Need for help


- As already mentioned above - we need sponsorship and media support. If you are interested in cooperation and it is interesting to develop Drupal development, management, marketing - contact us, we will be glad to partnership.


- Support from the participants as HelpTeam technical support is very important to us - if you have a desire to participate in an interesting and loud event, to gain experience of the organization, contact the organizers and we will be glad to cooperate with you.

Come! We will be glad to each of you.
