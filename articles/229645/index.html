LG G Watch: a complete review of iFixit (and 9 out of 10 on the repair scale)


The guys from iFixit continue to disassemble new devices, literally, on cogs. Now they have disassembled smart watches from LG, LG G Watch.

Interestingly, such a small device turned out to be very rationally assembled, and in such a way that almost any workshop can repair the watch. A score of 9 out of 10 on the repairability scale (10 is the highest point) speaks for itself.

This is not Microsoft Surface Pro 2, which received one point out of 10.
