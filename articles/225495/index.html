DevCon 2014: how it was (+ a lot of photos)

At the end of May, DevCon 2014 conference was held in one of the natural resorts of the Moscow region, which gathered more than 900 software developers and testers on-site and more than 40,000 viewers online.



DevCon conference has been held for the fourth year in a row, each time offering a unique country participation format - all conference participants drop into a country holiday home and live there for two days of the conference, receiving all the pleasant advantages of a resort location.

This year DevCon 2014 offered participants the following format:

  • 2 days, from 28 to 29 May, on the basis of a natural resort;
