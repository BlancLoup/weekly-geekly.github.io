Nikon Coolpix S1000PJ: the first camera with a projector



Nikon today announced the Coolpix S1000PJ Digicompact. The main feature of the new camera is a built-in projector, thanks to which, you can view the captured photo and video on the big screen.

The diagonal of the image that the projector creates ranges from 4 to 40 inches (10 cm to 1 m). In addition to the projector, the usual TFT display is also kept. Its parameters: a resolution of 230 thousand pixels, a diagonal of 2.7 inches.

Other characteristics are standard for this class of cameras. The device has a 1 / 2.3-inch 12.1 megapixel CCD-matrix with a sensitivity of ISO 80-6400 aixed range (ISO 80-200, 80-400). The camera also boasts a Zoom-NIKKOR lens with a focal length of 28-140 mm (35 mm equivalent) and a focus range of 30-100 cm, as well as 5x optical zoom. The maximum video resolution is 640x480 pixels.

The device is equipped with optical and electronic stabilization systems, has 36 MB of internal memory and supports SD / SDHC cards. Also, the Nikon Coolpix S1000PJ has an autoplay feature that activates the desired mode itself, based on the results of the frame analysis.

The dimensions of the camera are 99.5x62.5x23 mm (without protruding parts), the weight is about 155 grams (without battery and memory card). Contact with the computer - via USB.

The camera is equipped with a remote control, with which you can control both the camera and the projector. In the sale of a new product from Nikon in a month. Estimated cost - 430 dollars.
