Census of the Russian and Russian-speaking community on open data



You all, for sure, see that we have been dealing with the topic of open data for many years, we organize events, contests, meetings and much more.

Over the years, an active community has developed in Russia and we know that many are engaged in open data, from participating in hackathons to organizing their own events, conducting research and more.

Somewhere we manage to interact with representatives of the state, somewhere not yet, but somewhere the officials themselves are the initiators of projects on open data.

And so, in order to understand someone happening in our big country, we will organize a census of the established community.

To participate, please fill out the form at https://infoculture.typeform.com/to/QXF6a6


The purpose of the census is to make a list:
- activists, project developers and mentors willing to participate in the community;
- those who are willing to participate in events and not miss the most interesting;
- those who need to know what is happening in the open data topic in Russia and in the world;

Just in case, let me remind you that what open data can be found here:
