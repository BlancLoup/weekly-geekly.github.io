Belarusian journalist Ksenia Avimova won an international blog competition held by Deutsche Welle radio station

For the first time, the international blog competition BEST OF THE BLOGS , conducted by Deutsche Welle, won a blog in Russian. The competition was attended by more than 7,000 blogs in 10 languages. The jury recognized the winner of the blog Photographer-like journalism and photographer Xenia Avimova from Minsk.

The best blog in Russian was found in /dev/karlson/mind.log by Kitty Carlson (Nikita Kozhekin).
