VexorCI - What's new?

Hi, Habr. With this post we start our Friday news releases, in which we will talk about new technologies, the support of which we have implemented in our project, as well as features that will make working with Vexor as productive as possible. Our team takes into account the wishes of users, and gradually we expand our functionality.
Recall that Vexor CI is a cloud-based continuous integration service for developers with unlimited resources and per-minute payment, which allows you to effectively test projects.

We added a bitbucket
We have repeatedly received questions when BitBucket support will finally appear, and we hasten to inform you that we have finally implemented it! Now you can easily add your projects and work with Vexor.



We are joined by Bookmate
The number of Vexor users is growing, and with it there are new reviews that we post on our website . By the way, this week the team of the well-known and large Bookmate service began using Vexor for testing.



Bookmate is a service for reading e-books by subscription for everyone who has a mobile phone. It is a leading book service in Russia with the largest library of books in Russian, as well as books in English, Spanish, Turkish, Swedish and other languages.

In the next releases, we will definitely tell you about how Bookmate was able to speed up its tests with Vexor.
