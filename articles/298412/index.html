ZAGG acquired Mophie for $ 100 million



Several large acquisitions have taken place in the mobile market today - in addition to the SwiftKey takeover by Microsoft, ZAGG bought Mophie, specializing in the production of accessories with additional batteries, as well as having increased protection. The amount of the transaction amounted to $ 100 million, taking into account possible adjustments after the announcement of the company's profits in the period from April 1, 2016 and March 31, 2017. As a result of the takeover, the company's total profit for 2015 was approximately $ 470 million.

In a statement, the head of ZAGG, Randy Hales, noted the positive impact of this transaction on the development of companies in the near future, opening up many opportunities. As part of the takeover of CEO Mophie, Daniel Juan, along with COO, Sean Dougherty will continue to fulfill his responsibilities, reporting directly to Randy Heiles.
