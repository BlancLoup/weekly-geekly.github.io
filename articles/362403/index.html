Test and comparison of robots-vacuum cleaners Neato, iClebo, Clever'n'Clean, Kitfort, LG

Robot vacuum cleaner - a dream of a lazy and zealous owner, who is sorry for the time for cleaning. In any case, the robot vacuum cleaner is the current trend, the number of models sold is huge, it‚Äôs not so easy to choose something, and there are very few independent tests from those who are not interested in promoting specific brands. For a month, my colleague and I used six models from popular manufacturers at home, and then tested them under the same conditions. Somewhere we could make mistakes or not notice something, but, in general, our results reflect the real experience of using real people. Test results and impressions - below. iRobot was taken to the test just now, the impressions on it a little later, beyond the general test.



Testing method
We prepared a small room and covered the floor with exhibition carpet so as not to create additional difficulties for vacuum cleaners. In the room we scatter a strictly defined amount of the prepared material that simulates pollution. This material is perfectly visible in the ultraviolet spectrum. In our garbage: paper pellets of different sizes, dust and the smallest remnants of fabric, scraps of threads. Real trash is a real challenge. We will not scatter rice, as others do, real garbage clings to the carpet, and rice can be removed with a broom, and it is useless to scatter food.





In vacuum cleaners in which this was possible, the method of moving a zigzag was established. After cleaning the vacuum cleaner, we turn on the UV lamp and look at what may not have removed the vacuum cleaner. To get closer to real conditions, we will put a chair, an obstacle of indefinite form and, with a separate test, simulate irregularities and completely accidentally forget on the floor of the wire. Before launching a new robot cleaner, carefully clean the room and fall asleep new trash. The last stage is the duration of one battery charge. We protected the technical area with our equipment with magnetic tapes and virtual walls from a set of vacuum cleaners.



For cleaning the device receives an estimate on a 100-point scale, depending on the percentage of the cleaned. The rest of the tests are 10 points, the scores are summarized to get the final one. Well, now let's take a closer look at the tested devices.

Participating models
1. YUJIN Robot iClebo Arte YCR-M05-20 Silver

2. Clever & lean Z-Series (White Moon)



The Clever & lean Z-Series manual states: "Never use a vacuum cleaner to clean wet surfaces / carpets." The real noise level in the room when the device is ~ 58 dB. The volume of the garbage container - 400 ml, the price is about 11,000 rubles

NEATO Botvac 85, the most powerful vacuum cleaner in the Botvac series. The device makes a map of the room based on the readings of the laser scanner, is controlled by the actual CPU and OS. Neato practically does not get confused and does not fall into the "trap". Some vacuum cleaners climb into the niches from which they cannot get out, but in difficult situations Botvac 85 tries to raise the case above the floor in order to reduce its area or overcome an obstacle. The main combined brush occupies almost the entire width of the vacuum cleaner. Restrict movement of the robot can be magnetic tape. Noise on average ~ 62 dB.
