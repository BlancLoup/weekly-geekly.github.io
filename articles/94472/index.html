Revenge Of The Titans Beta Released

Revenge Of The Titans is a casual RTS / Tower Defense game from the small company Puppy Games. The action takes place in a unique universe, created since the time of their first Ultratron game, as well as the subsequent Droid Assault and the ultra-popular Invasion remake - Titan Attacks!




I want to note that Revenge Of The Titans is one of the few games written in Java (!). Accordingly, among the available platforms there are Windows, Linux and MacOS X. Perhaps Habrahabr readers will recall some more interesting games from the world of Java gaming?

The first planet (Earth) is available in beta. The remaining several planets of the game Revenge Of The Titans will become available after payment of a preliminary order at a price of 10.66 euros. You can pay by PayPal or Visa credit card.
