Virtual reality around us. We invite to the MixAR conference

Friends! We would like to invite you to the wonderful mixAR2015 conference, which will be held on September 26, 2015 in the territory of the design factory FLACON in Moscow. The main topics of the conference are virtual and augmented reality, interactive technologies, digital art.



Considering that devices for entering virtual and augmented reality are now easily accessible to everyone (I‚Äôm talking about Cardboard , Fibrum- type helmets, etc.), for developers, the topic of creating corresponding applications becomes very relevant. For those who can not program, the conference will be no less interesting!

Microsoft is the general partner of the conference, the organizers are the Association of Augmented and Virtual Reality of Russia, the Russian Center for Youth Entrepreneurship Assistance and the 26dots digital studio. Mandatory free registration is here !

mixAR2015 is three floors of immersion in the newest technologies! On the ground floor, you can immerse yourself in another reality with the help of incredible gadgets, learn how to apply technology in your business. The lecture zone consists of two sectors: for developers, programmers and geeks in the morning and for b2b in the second. On the third floor, you can listen to lectures on the merging of digital art and interactive technologies, in the art zone you can try yourself in augmented reality and 3D workshops. In the final part of the event, as part of the after-party for informal communication, you will be able to see a digital performance from Tatiana Plakhova .

The full conference program is published on the website , the most interesting reports are described in more detail in the blog . For example, we recommend attending a lecture by Paula Monteiro from Wikitude on the subject of augmented reality.

We at the conference will tell you about the creation of virtual reality on the Windows 10 platform and about the future of virtual and augmented reality - Microsoft HoloLens . We also invite you to round tables, during which live discussions will be held with the participation of experts on the future of AR / VR technologies. In addition, we will try for the first time in Russia to show you one very impressive project from Microsoft Research on the topic of augmented reality. But until we reveal the cards, come and see for yourself!
