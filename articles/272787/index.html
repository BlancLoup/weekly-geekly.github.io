Juniper SRX lineup upgrade

At the end of December, Juniper announced updates to its SRX security line. Here is the line now:



Changes occurred in the entire junior line, so in May 2016 they will be discontinued from the production of the SRX100, SRX210, SRX240, SRX650. They will replace the SRX300 series, the SRX550 will be updated and the SRX1500 will be added (under the cat a brief overview of how new devices are positioned):



How new devices are positioned



The SRX300 should replace the SRX100, the SRX320 will replace the SRX110 / SRX210 / SRX220, the SRX340 will replace the SRX240. The SRX345 is stuck in to fill a niche that was between the SRX240 and the SRX550.

All new devices have an order of magnitude better performance, all of them have embedded SFP ports.



Juniper SRX300 is an ideal solution for small offices and offices, with a total staff of no more than 50 people. Onboard 8 ports - 6x10 / 100 / 1000Base-T and 2 SFP ports. The absence of an active cooling system makes it possible to use it in the desktop version.



Juniper SRX320 is also an excellent solution for small offices and offices, in addition to 8xGE, it has two slots for installing mini Pim cards. There is an option with PoE (802.3af, 802.3at) support with a 90W budget.



Juniper SRX300 differs from Juniper SRX320 in that there are 2 expansion slots in the sarshei version with PoE. The youngest has better power consumption and lack of acoustic noise.



Juniper SRX340 is an ideal solution for medium-sized offices and offices, with a total staff of no more than 100 people. On board 16 ports - 8x10 / 100 / 1000Base-T and 8 SFP ports, 4 slots for installing mini Pim cards. Compared to the SRX 240, the performance of the SRX340 is almost 2 times higher.



Juniper SRX345 SRX340 differs double performance and is designed for medium-sized offices and offices, with a total number of employees not exceeding 200 people. In both models there is a slot for an SSD drive.



The SRX550 was updated, the firewall performance increased from 5.5Gbps to 8Gbps on large packages and the number of competitive sessions, a slot for installing SSD appeared. So he replaced the old SRX550 and SRX650.



The SRX1500 came to replace the SRX1400, a 1RU device on board with 20 ports - 12x10 / 100 / 1000Base-T, 4 SFP ports and 4 SFP + ports. Additionally, there are two slots for installing PIM cards.



The SRX1500 is an ideal solution for large offices and regional offices, with a total staff of no more than 1,000 people. I want to remind you that in all the new devices, functional UTM, IPS, NGFW, Spotlight Secure, Sky Advanced Threat Prevention - Zero Day Threat Prevention is available.
