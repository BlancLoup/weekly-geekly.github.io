Mobile Report Opera Software. July 2008.

Published next mobile report of the company Opera Software for July 2008. According to the report, since last month, the number of Opera Mini users has increased by another 9.4% and amounted to a beautiful figure of 15.8 million users. At the same time, it is possible to note an increase in user activity by 15.9%: more than 3.7 billion pages were viewed in July, an average of 236 per user.

As for the data for Russia, we are still the most active users (according to Opera Software internal data, the number of Opera Mini users in Russia is an order of magnitude larger than in any other country). The list of the most visited websites has slightly changed:

1. vkontakte.ru
2. mail.ru
3. yandex.ru (previously 5)
4. odnoklassniki.ru (previously 3)
5. google.com (previously 4)
6. wapos.ru
7. yahoo.com (previously out of the list)
8. seclub.org
9. rambler.ru (previously 7)
10. dimonvideo.ru

The report also presents data for Latin America.
