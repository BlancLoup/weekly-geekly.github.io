Kyocera demonstrates a foldable mobile phone with a flexible OLED display



Kyocera (Kyoser) recently demonstrated a mobile phone that works on kinetic energy, which can fold like a wallet. Designed by industrial designer Susan McKinney (Susan McKinney), Kyocera EOS (ala " Canon EOS ", Eos ( Eos ) - in ancient Greek mythology, the goddess of dawn) is made of soft polymer leather, which embraces an OLED display. The memory of the form allows the buttons to pop up when the phone is in use and to hide back when the phone is in the standby mode.



Mobile phone can be used as in a semi-folded state to dial the number and call; or in widescreen mode for viewing. This phone is unique in that the source of its charge is communication with the user: by folding and unfolding the phone, tiny piezoelectric generators are activated.
