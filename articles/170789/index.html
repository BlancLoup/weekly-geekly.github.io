Refer A Friend: we pay for these friends

Our refer A Friend referral program has been running for several months, and we want to say thanks to all of its participants. More and more new users are registering through referral links. By the way, many of the residents of Habr are also active participants in the program Refer A Friend. This post is dedicated to what the Refer A Friend program is, as well as to the rules for participation in it.

The principle of the program Refer A Friend is very simple: any Payoneer card holder can register in the referral program and get links to invite friends via email, Facebook or Twitter. Refer A Friend is open to absolutely all Payoneer cardholders, including bloggers, forum moderators, website owners for freelancers and affiliates, in a word to all those who can use our service to get paid.
For each friend who registers on your link and earns at least $ 100, you get a bonus of $ 25. In addition, your referral also receives $ 25. Accruing bonuses to your Payoneer card can take up to 30 days, the number of people you can invite is unlimited.
Please note that in order to participate in the Payoneer referral program, you must have a valid account in our system. If you do not have a card, you can get it here .
To register in the program, visit the Refer A Friend page:


Enter your details and click "Submit". You will be offered three options: sending by email, links for posting on Facebook or Twitter.


Please note that you can only use these links. Do not copy the address of the page that opens on the link to send it to friends. The links you receive will look like this: share.payoneer-affiliates.com/a/clk/XXXXXX, where XXXXXX is your unique identifier.
If you have any questions about the program, do not hesitate to ask in the comments. Frequently asked questions about Refer A Friend are here , participation conditions are here .
