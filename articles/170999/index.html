OpenSignal - London


OpenSignal is an application that helps users get a good signal on the mobile, recognizes nearby free Wi-Fi access points, and also shows a coverage map with data on the signal strength of various mobile operators.

Tellyo - Helsinki


Tellyo is a startup that allows you to share interesting moments from the TV show on social networks and thus attract new viewers.

This mobile application allows you to find a parking space in a large city. And not only to find, but also to find out the exact number and location of available places, see photos, price and even pay for this parking via call / SMS or application.

The guys offer a new approach to photos, their application allows you to take pictures with sound, and the output is a JPEG file (the new format is already patented). When viewing such an image, the user in full colors can imagine the moment in which the picture was taken.
