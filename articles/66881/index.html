We solve the problem with the region of DVD

I bought in the American online store drives with the series. Got them with an opportunity. And I found out that stationary dvd-players (JVC and Philips) refuse to reproduce them, since the disks are intended for the 1st region, and Ukraine belongs to the 5th , under which the players are stitched.

The computer did not refuse to play, but offered to change the region for my DVD-drive, honestly warning that you can change it no more than 5 times.

The solution was the utility DVD43 , which allows on-the-fly circumvention of regional protection.
