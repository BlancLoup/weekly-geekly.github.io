Home sales of Android-smartphone HTC Tattoo in Russia


It seems with the start of a powerful marketing company Quietly Brilliant, HTC has decided to become even closer and friendlier to the final consumer. Less than a month from the start of sales of the smartphone HTC Tattoo , as HTC officially announced the start of sales of the communicator in Russia. The device will appear on the counters of authorized dealers of the company tomorrow (October 30) at a price of 16,990 rubles (~ 582 USD). What is the uniqueness of this device? HTC Tattoo has removable panels (apparently many remembered Nokia :)). HTC is positioning the smartphone as an accessory that will help highlight your personality. However, Tattoo is not a phone for blondes, it will appeal even to geeks, because it can boast with Sense UI brand chips and the Android 1.6 platform, which in the future is waiting for an upgrade to version 2.0.
