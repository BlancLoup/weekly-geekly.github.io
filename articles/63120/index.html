Creative Question: Project Manager vs Designer

Here you can encounter problems such as the timing that the PM puts on and which the designer violates, different visions of the graphic solution, and simply personal characteristics of each. But, the main problem we faced was neither deadlines nor characters, nor even a sense of beauty, but a creative component of this process. Namely - which of the two should deal with it.
