The announcement of the conference Mobius 2017: More mobile development, good and different

Hi Habr!

We started the announcement of the past Mobius by dealing with feedbacks and promising to make the conference better. If you believe the feedback from participants - we managed to achieve the goal:

‚Äú In general, I liked everything very much. Before that, I was 2 years ago and I will say that the conference has changed just at times. There were many interesting reports, there were no tedious retellings and promotions of their products. "

However, we do not stop at what has been achieved and continue the Mobius series. This time the conference will take place on April 21-22, 2017 in St. Petersburg . Yes, this time the conference will be held within two days: last time, participants often complained about excessively high loading by the end for, now due to the two-day format and reports will be more, and it will be easier to perceive them. The program will include not only the best speakers of past Mobius, but also new faces. In addition, a new format of reports and communication with speakers is planned.

Details under the cut.

Call for papers


If you want to talk about something hardcore and new Russian community of mobile development - give us a report !



If you have already spoken somewhere and have met a warm welcome from the organizers and the audience, show us the videotape of the report - this will significantly speed up the decision making by the program committee and increase your chances of performance. Class performances in the past is one of the important criteria that traditionally guides our program committee.

Beginners should not be afraid either - we will definitely communicate with everyone. Moreover, if your topic is fresh and interesting, we will do everything to hone the content of the report, the technique of performance, timings and presentation, in general, to prepare the perfect report with you, for this we even started a special section in the blog.

Community Talks




However, if you want to share experience, or your own work, and there is not enough information for a full report, we plan to make a special platform where any participant of the conference will be able to make short presentations.

Minimum moderation and almost complete freedom of speech - all this will be available to those who pre-register Community Talk at the conference. We have not yet decided on this format, so we would very much like to hear your opinion: Should we introduce such a format? Do you need such an opportunity to communicate the participants? Would you personally prepare such a short talk to discuss pressing issues with fellow developers?

Share your thoughts on this topic with us in the comments or email our program director: program@mobiusconf.com .
