Google Notebook unexpectedly exited beta

The Google Notebook online notebook, judging by the disappearance of the beta prefix in the logo and the interface change, waited for the official release.

The service is a notebook in which you can leave notes and subsequently access them from any computer connected to the Internet. Using the plugin for Firefox, you can easily save fragments of sites in it. You can publish your notes to the public or work together on a notebook, inviting specific users to this. The created notes can be easily exported to Google Docs.

You can work with the service through Internet Explorer and Firefox browsers. The final version of Google Notebook is available in Russian.
