Long-term hard work


To begin with, it was in the above mentioned posts. The most innocuous recommendation is vitamins . Almost completely agree. But there is one feature. Well-advertised today multivitamins, cause fierce disputes among specialists. There is an opinion based on proven facts that mixing a bunch of vitamins, or even together with minerals, in one pill is not good.

The fact is that interactions between vitamins and minerals occur both at the chemical and physiological levels. As a result of chemical interaction, the actual amount of vitamins or microelements in such a complex may be much less than that stated. In the interaction of vitamins and minerals in the human body, one component of a multivitamin complex can influence the effect of another in the desired or undesirable side (more here ).

Interesting fact. If you are a woman and are going to have a baby in the near future, then the use of multivitamins before pregnancy will increase the likelihood of having twins . This, of course, is not fatal, but it speaks in favor of the fact that any medicinal intervention in the body can bring quite unexpected results, unfortunately, not always pleasant. If you are fluent in English, see our multi-vitamin guidelines for patients.

I do not want to say that vitamins are evil. It's not like that at all. Just always, before deciding on the reception of a drug, you should consult with a competent specialist. But if you really want to buy any vitamins right now, pay attention to vitamin C. Yes, just vitamin C. Naturally, your body needs other vitamins, but with a rational and varied diet of vitamin deficiency you will not.

We turn to the nootropics . You may have been surprised that I did not mention them in the section on extreme methods of work (not to be confused with extreme programming), but these drugs will not give you an instant effect. Despite the fact that fenotropil, for example, has its effect already after a single application, the course of administration varies from 2 weeks to 2 months ( link ).

In addition, the effects of nootropics are not at all stimulating . That is, they will not give you the strength to the last spurt and will not be able to stimulate the body to work until the last breath. These drugs can help you, if you have already managed to bring yourself ‚Äúup to the handle‚Äù with continuous work, or they will allow you to slightly increase your efficiency during preventive use (I will give my opinion on the use of drugs by healthy people below). For your information, nootropics are a group of drugs whose effectiveness has not been proven. They are used mainly only in the CIS. For some reason, in Western countries they are not considered to be drugs.

Many times mentioned in the comments glycine. Glycine is a inhibitory neurotransmitter. He will not encourage you and will not give strength to work. The drug has a sedative (sedative) and mild tranquilizing (anti-anxiety) effect. Enhance mental performance occurs due to calming a person, due to the activation of inhibition processes in the brain and normalization of metabolism. The key word is normalization. Superman you will not.

There are also so-called adaptogens . Examples: Rhodiola rosea preparations, sandy immortelle, ginseng, Eleutherococcus, and others. A healthy person most likely will not have a pronounced effect, just you will become a little more resistant to stressful situations. But we should not forget that individual sensitivity is strongly pronounced for adaptogens: about 15‚Äì20% of people react to taking medications paradoxically - a decrease in efficiency, lethargy, and deterioration of mood. Adaptogens have more or less pronounced side effects: hypertension, hyperthermia, increased excitability of the nervous system, insomnia. Their use may lead to the manifestation of previously latent epilepsy. Therefore, even with the use of herbal remedies, medical supervision is required ( link ).

Now about dietary supplements. Supplements - not drugs. This means that a reasonable person should not be afraid of them. True and there are no guarantees of effectiveness. It is unlikely that they will help you with something.
