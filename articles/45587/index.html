Magnetic Flying Globe


I was looking for a birthday present and that was what I came across. A very funny and at the same time interesting performance of an ordinary globe - a smaller, simplified model of our Mother Earth.

Detailed description under the cut.



Magnetic Levitating World Globe.
According to the manufacturer :
This is an excellent model of the Earth, allowing you to globally explore some symbol of our planet.
The device itself consists of, in fact, a miniature globe and a stand with backlight (That is, even at night you can admire your native Earth)

Globe using a system of two magnets hangs in the air, and the backlight of the blue LEDs creates an absolutely cosmic impression.

The set comes: the globe itself, stand, instruction =), power adapter (for magnets and backlight), a spoon (apparently, to raise the Earth.)

A photo:


In my opinion, its main advantage over the usual "big" globes is that, firstly, it is miniaturized, but everything can be seen on it, and secondly, in fact, we can watch the planet practically in space, even with her circular movements.

I think that for the desktop is a very stylish little thing.
