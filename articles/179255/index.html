BlackBerry MiniJam in Kiev on May 28th

In Kiev, now the weather is good, it is no longer cold and the heat has not come yet, chestnuts and lilac are blooming. And if this is not enough to lure you to visit us, so here's another argument: on May 28th we will have a BlackBerry MiniJam. This is a conference dedicated to BlackBerry products - the BlackBerry 10 operating system and devices based on it.

Developer event - there will be few common words and many case reports (development for BlackBerry 10, native applications, Qt / Cascades, development on HTML5, porting Android and Adobe Air applications, enterprise).

There will be devices that can be twisted in the hands and people who will tell you what and how exactly they can be twisted. There will be a couple of coffee breaks and a full meal. The entrance is free.

Registration is here (same address and map).

10.00 - 10.30 - Keynote

Waiting for you.
