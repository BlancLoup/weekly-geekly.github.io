Assemble 2013

This weekend in Finland held the legendary computer festival Assembly 2013 . During AssemblyTV's 76-hour online broadcast, we were able to watch one of the largest computer festivals in the world, which is attended by more than 5,000 participants annually, and also more than 200 volunteers work on its organization.

Assemble this event for gamers, demos, and anyone interested in digital culture.

Broadcast is open to the entire Internet. With its help, we can follow the events in Hartwall Areena live, virtually attend interesting seminars, watch contests for the best demo and intro, watch graphic and music contests, participate in demos discussion in the StudioTV studio with famous personalities of the demos, watch interviews with sponsors and live concerts.

The main event of the Assembly traditionally are contests with a demonstration of visual and audio effects that are performed / calculated / reproduced by specially written programs in real time.
