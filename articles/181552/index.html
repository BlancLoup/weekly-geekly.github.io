Digest of upcoming IT events for June 2013

Presents to your attention the 5th edition of our (we are the portal Aiti-event. RF ) "Digest of the upcoming IT-events" for June 2013.



For ease of navigation, all events are divided into 5 categories:
  • I. Developer
  • Ii. Junior
  • Iii. Startups
  • Iv. Marketing
  • V. Miscellaneous


I. Developer


Meeting DrupalCamp Kyiv 2013
/ Kiev / 7.06.2013 / The cost of 150 - 250 UAH.
DrupalCamp Kyiv is an event that will unite in a joint circle of Drupal developers from around the world. This year the event will bring together about 400 professionals and enthusiasts to communicate, exchange experiences and ideas. It takes place in the two-day barcamp format, each day of which consists of four parallel streams of reports.

Meeting Web Standards Days
/ Saint Petersburg / 06.06.2013 / Participation is free
The main purpose of this meeting is a quality education and exchange of experience in the field of web development. Such a format does not imply the promotion of any particular commercial interests and technologies - we are united by the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãbuilding a single open web.

Conference IV International Conference of WebCamp Web Developers 2013
/ Odessa / June 12, 2013 / The cost of 400-600 UAH.
On the first day - reports in 4 main streams: web technologies and project management, usability and design, internet marketing, startups, as well as a Startup Crash Test and an alley of startups. The second day will be entirely devoted to conducting various master classes.

DevConf 2013 Conference
/ Moscow / 06/14/2013 / Cost of 4,000 - 8,500 rubles.
DevConf members are given a unique opportunity to access immediately all leading web development technologies by listening to reports from the founders of these technologies who came to Moscow from all over the world.

Conference J.Subbotnik in St. Petersburg
/ Saint Petersburg / 06/15/2013 / Participation is free
Speakers from the St. Petersburg, Moscow and Simferopol offices will share their knowledge and experience and discuss current issues in the field of web development. The organizers will be happy to see those who are interested in professional communication in a cozy and almost homely atmosphere.

JDay Lviv Conference
/ Lviv / 06.29.2013 / Cost $ 240
Speakers at the conference: Nikolai Krasko (JetBrains), a member of the Kotlin project team; Mikalai Alimenkou (XP Injection, Zoral); Philip Torchinsky (JetBrains), a technology expert at JetBrains; Russ Hertzberg (SoftServe Inc), vice president of Technology Solutions for SoftServe Inc. and many others.


Ii. Junior


Seminar Basics of CMS Drupal Development
/ Ekaterinburg / 2.06.2013 / Cost 9000 rub.
Participation in the seminar will allow you to: develop Internet projects using CMS Drupal; optimally select the concept of a project on CMS Drupal for its further development; qualify and qualify for an interesting job with the requirement of having knowledge of CMS Drupal.

Course Third School of Managers of Yandex
/ Moscow / 06/28/2013 / Participation is free
The school invites purposeful undergraduates and recent graduates who want to professionally manage projects on the Internet, but do not yet have sufficient experience and knowledge. For admission it is necessary to fill in the questionnaire with test tasks until June 9 inclusive.

Conference All-Russian educational marathon "Programmer"
/ Moscow / 06/29/2013 / Participation is free
The All-Russian Programming Marathon on Programming is intended to bring together thousands of active Internet users interested in modern information technologies in the framework of a common online event. The goal of the marathon is to give everyone who wants to master the key basic skills of the profession of a programmer.

Topics for the meeting: prototyping and design of mobile applications, game development, mobile marketing, presentations of startups. Invited startups.

First Hackathon in Saransk
/ Saransk / 08.06.2013 / Participation is free
On the first Hackathon in Mordovia, all amateur and software development professionals are invited. The program, in addition to coding, contests, games and other entertainment - but do not think that you have a lot of rest!


Iv. Marketing


Conference DEMO Europe (Russia)
/ Moscow / 06/03/2013 / The cost of 10 000 - 20 000 rubles.
DEMO is a conference dedicated to new technologies and innovations. The conference demonstrates promising developments and presents breakthrough ideas and projects in the field of information technology.

Conference Digitale 3
/ St. Petersburg / 06/03/2013 / Cost 4 000 rub.
The event is dedicated to the topic of innovation in marketing. Interactive lectures, going in one flow, will be devoted to such areas as guerrilla marketing, budgetless marketing, shopper marketing and marketing automation.

Forum MoCO and Mdday # MoCO 2013
/ Moscow / 06/04/2013 / Cost 3 500 - 27 000 rub.
This year, on Mdday # Moco, the organizers will introduce a new format for organizing space. The conference, which will consist of speeches by mobile vendors and open discussions, will be combined with the Developer Alley exhibition space, where the studios will show their work and negotiate with partners and customers. It will be noisy. It will be incredibly useful and effective.

Conference DIGITAL WITHOUT SILICONE
/ Moscow / 06/07/2013 / The cost of 15 000 rubles.
Digital without Silicone is a unique digital marketing conference where the best brand representatives share their experience and practical knowledge. The objective of the event is to enable marketers and brand representatives to share their experience in marketing communications.

White Nights Conference : Mobile Games Conference
/ Saint Petersburg / 06/27/2013 / Cost from $ 200
More than 800 people from leading companies are expected, including Supercell, Rovio, Wooga, Chillingo, Kabam, PlayFirst, Facebook, Microsoft, Google. Several reasons to attend the conference: a unique speaker structure; lectures and round tables on practical cases will be held in two streams in the afternoon. There will also be master classes; business contacts; networking, etc.


V. Miscellaneous


FriendWork FEST
/ St. Petersburg / 1.062013 / Cost 0 - 5 000 rub.
The event will be interesting to anyone who wants to find work in new and promising projects in various fields - from IT to PR. Do not miss your chance to change your life and find a really cool job at FriendWork FEST!

IT Paintball Cup 2013 tournament
/ Kharkov / 06/15/2013 / The cost is 2 400 UAH.
The most exciting event of the year: the battle of the strongest among programmers! On this day, all those who have long dreamed of breaking away from the backs of their chairs, monitors, endless tasks, kanban boards and inevitable deadlines will meet face to face. LAF Festival 2013
/ Kaluga region, the village of Petrovo / 06/29/2013 / Cost of 4,000 - 5,000 rubles.
LAF is the annual two-day festival of system analysts, which is held for the fourth time. The first day is a conference. This day will be devoted to traditional presentations and master classes. The second day - communication in a free format. Round tables in the open air, discussions, entertainment, cultural activities.
