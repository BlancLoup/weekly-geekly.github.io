E3 2011: Top 15 (games and hardware)

The exhibition E3, which will be held from 7 to 9 June, will be the most interesting in recent years.

And for good reason.




1.Batman: Arkham City - Expected October 18



There will be all the best that was in the previous parts, plus there will be more locations, secrets, puzzles, all sorts of gizmos and gizmos, riddles, fights ... Oh yes, there will be more Joker. He is back.

You can play on: PC, PS3, Xbox 360

2. Bioshock Infinite - Expected 2012



Want to fly around Steampunk Cloud City, the creator of which compares his brainchild with the Death Star? Irrational Games has prepared for you partly a first-person shooter, partly - a hallucinogenic and fabulous dystopia. It remains only to wait for 2012.

You can play on: PC, PS3, Xbox 360

3. Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 - Expected November 8



Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 does not look like a narrative about conflicts of modern times, but like an epic disaster film, compared to which Bruce Willis nervously smokes on the sidelines.

You can play on: PC, PS3, Xbox 360

4. Dark Souls - Released yet defined



Do you think it was hard to play Demon's Souls? Then try Dark Souls, a mix of action and RPG from the developers of Demon`s Souls.

You can play on: PS3, Xbox 360

5. The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - Expected November 11



The continuation of the Tamriel Saga will unfold on the snowy rocky lands of Skyrim (which looks like Iceland). You are waiting for flying monsters, saber-toothed tigers, mammoths and 16 square miles, where you can run from the heart. And all this - from November 11th.

You can play on: PC, PS3, Xbox 360.

6. Gears of War 3 - Expected September 20



Tactical 3rd person shooter is coming September 20th. The third part of the game promises to give answers to unresolved questions in the past.

You can play on: Xbox 360

7. Grand Theft Auto V - Release date not yet determined



Whether it will come out or not come out is probably not known, but since publishers have publicly spoken about continuing the series, the attention of fans has been riveted to it. Be that as it may, information leaks suggest that GTA V is to be.

8. Halo 4 - Released yet defined



What would you like more - to see the good old Halo, or to see Halo completely new, reworked and updated? Microsoft is forced to fend off the attacks of Nintendo, and Halo 4 may prove to be a worthy answer.

Something from the wiki page about the first part of the series: At the end of 2010, there was a rumor that Microsoft was going to release a remake of the game in November of 2011, and Saber Interactive studio, which had previously worked on the TimeShift series, would be involved in it.

9. Mass Effect 3 - Released yet.



In the first part of the game there was confusion on the part of RPG, in the second part almost all the flaws were eliminated. If the third part (finally) adds decent control, then it will become a worthy ending.
Ready to meet the Reapers?

You can play on: PC, PS3, Xbox 360

10. Metal Gear Solid Rising - Release date not yet determined



Already managed to check out the "Kung Fu Panda - 2"? Then imagine that instead of Panda - a cyborg with nuclear guns.

You can play on: PC, PS3, Xbox 360

11. Nintendo Project Cafe: New Console



In general, you can rename the upcoming E3 in the "debut of the new console from Nintendo." High-definition graphics are expected, a new controller, and a couple more buns that Nintendo is keeping secret for now. The cover of this mystery will fall on Tuesday.

12. Sony Next-Generation Portable



About the new successor to the PSP, and so much is known, but we still spend time on it. Sony needs to divert attention from its PlayStation Network fiasco.

13. The Last Guardian - Released yet defined



The game was postponed, and not once, but we hope to see it this year.

You can play on: PS3
