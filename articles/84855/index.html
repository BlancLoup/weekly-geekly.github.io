MSN Russia at Silverlight - Olympics, photo order ...


At the dawn of Silverlight, we were often blamed for the fact that Microsoft services themselves do not use new technologies. The situation was simple - it was about products launched before the advent of Silverlight. All new sites (of which Microsoft has very, very much almost every month) used our product.

Since then, the situation has changed dramatically in the portal part - to take at least the new Bing Maps , Bing Video and much more. It is pleasant to note that these trends also work in Russia.

This week a new service MSN Photo was launched, which allows you to quickly select and print photos from your computer and deliver them to the desired point. Convenient interface without registration - on Silverlight.

In addition, a special project Vancouver-2010 was launched specifically for the Olympics . In this section, you can, in particular, view the history of the Olympic Games in the form of a beautiful Deep Zoom- collection , as well as view all of our athletes in the form of a mosaic .

By the way, if you have not seen the opening of the Olympiad, look at the three-dimensional model of this vivid event using the service Photosynth.
