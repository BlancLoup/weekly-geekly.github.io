How to accept payments offline?

Long wanted to accept payments by card? Too lazy to look for an ATM to repay a friend? Is there a product or service for which you want to receive payment by cards, but do not want to buy a special POS-terminal and enter into an agreement with an acquirer for any reason? If at least one question you answered "yes", then you definitely should be interested.

In order to answer the above questions, you can order a mobile terminal for accepting payments made by PrivatBank. Website: https://pos.liqpay.com/ , payments are working on the technologies of their liqpay payment system.

It has long been heard from friends in Ukraine that PrivatBank is there ahead of all in technology and all kinds of it-features like convenient mobile Internet banking. Their mobile payment terminal is a bank card swiper that connects to iOS or Android through the headphone jack of smartphones. Then with the help of the application iPay, which can be installed from the market, you can use the device.



I am writing this text, and imagine how it would be convenient to pay with a card for delivery of flowers / pizza / rolls, pay for a taxi, throw off a gift to a colleague, and the like. Personally, I constantly do not have enough cash for such needs. Well, enough of dreams, let's see what this mobile terminal can do.

Application functionality


A mobile terminal can:
  • accept payments on the card
  • accept payments to the liqpay payment system account
