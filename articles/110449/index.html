Firefox 4 Beta 8 is available for download

Fortunately, despite the constant changes in the release calendar and release releases, Mozilla Corporation released the eighth beta version of the popular Mozilla Firefox 4 browser before the New Year, although some skeptics doubted this. It's nice to see that the developers have figured out with the addition of functionality, and now they are doing their best to fix errors and bring the browser into the proper form:
* Improved synchronization with mobile devices.
* Improved WebGL.
* Polished add-ons manager.
A complete list of changes can be viewed on the page of bug lists .
Downloads are available from repositories .
