Evernote surpasses 10 million users

Do you know what I like? When a new rank is added to a number. 100,000 miles, $ 1,000,000, 10:00. So you hear a click, with which a new number 1 appears on the left, and all the others are reset to zero. Awesome sensations ... We just heard such a click in Evernote: 10 000 000 users registered in the service!

Here are some statistics that show how we have progressed over the past five months:



Interestingly, most users use Evernote on several platforms. 75% of users work with two and a large number of platforms, and there are users who use up to ten platforms in working with the service. This is convenient because Evernote has both a web interface and software clients for Mac, Windows, iPhone, iPad, Android, BlackBerry, Windows Mobile and HP WebOS. In addition, clients for a number of other platforms are in development.



Ten million seemed like an unthinkable number when we were preparing to start the service in open beta testing less than three years ago. Well, that is, it was not unthinkable in the literal sense: we designated this stage in a business plan, presentations for investors, etc. Yes, we told people that we would get 10,000,000 users in three years, we had beautiful graphs with forecasts. But the reality turned out to be much more impressive than predictions.
