Report and materials of the conference MageConf 2016


December 10, 2016 in Kiev hosted the MageConf 2016 conference. The subject of the conference is the whole stack of technologies used in Magento. The conference was held in 2 streams: Backend and Frontend.

Under the cat you can find videos of all reports presented at the conference:

  • Dmitry Naumenko - “Profiling in PHP with XDebug, XHprof, Blackfire”
