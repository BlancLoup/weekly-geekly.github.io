Google released Android Dev Phone 2

Google has released Android Dev Phone 2 for developers based on HTC Magic (T-Mobile myTouch 3G). The cost is $ 399.



Differences from HTC Magic (T-Mobile myTouch 3G): it is not locked to the operator and fully open to change the operating system.

To purchase, you must be a registered developer at market.android.com (registration cost is $ 25).

Delivery is officially available for 18 countries: USA, Australia, Austria, Canada, Finland, France, Germany, Hungary, India, Japan, Holland, Poland, Singapore, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan and England.
Ukraine and Russia are not listed.
