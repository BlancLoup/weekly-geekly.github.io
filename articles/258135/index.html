Modified driver from ReactOS makes life easier on Windows on a Chromebook


Many owners of chromate books purchased them solely in order to use them with Windows. Installing this OS on the chroma book is fraught with difficulties, for example, with the inoperability of the built-in keyboard and touchpad. In chromabuds, Google's embedded controller is used , for which there are no drivers for other platforms.

A member of the Windows porting community on the Acer C720 ($ 168.99) , hiding under the nickname coolstar , found the original solution to this problem. He created the necessary driver based on the i8042prt source codes from ReactOS.
