Google invented a new kind of captcha (captcha)

A new kind of captcha forces users to rotate images to a standing position in order to prove their humanity.

The user needs to orient several images correctly ( upd: it is obvious that the correct positions of the images will not be displayed; see the real application examples below)

Warning: a lot of images under the cut.


Simplified working principle
