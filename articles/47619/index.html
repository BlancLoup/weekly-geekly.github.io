The first to suffer from the financial crisis were banks.

Then began the bankruptcy of enterprises.

Then the real estate markets, the car market froze ... Travel agencies are generally closed in batches. No one thinks about people: many are fired, and even more will be fired in the first half of 2009. The rest will cut wages. Experts of the Ministry of Economic Development believe that the first to be hit will be employees of the financial sector, trade, services and information technology.
