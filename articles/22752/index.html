boingo vs golden wifi

Many Muscovites with laptops are aware of the existence of golden wifi - the world's largest network of wifi access points, covering a large part of Moscow. 500 rubles per month provide unlimited access to this joy. But not everyone wants to gang up on the first sentence - especially tricky, like me, will look for alternatives, just in case

in fact, I found out about the alternative by chance. I read in LJ that there is a boingo company that rents its access point from Golden Telecom. I went to their site to read the details and was happy: for the same 20 bucks you get the same month of unlimited access, but worldwide! Moreover, in Moscow he knows more than 10 thousand points - even if these are not all golden wifi points, 10 thousand is enough for me :)

but inattention is still punishable. On the way to gaining unlimited wireless connectivity, I found two rakes. The first rake was that there is boingo wireless , and there is boingo mobile . The main disadvantage is that I found out about it after a subscription and payment of the usual boingo, which cannot be used on small devices. However, the kind support compensated for this minus in both senses - refund without problems :)
