And in order to give the selected candidates to the customer for consideration, you still need to print summaries and notes on the candidate, or copy the selected resumes into a separate folder for sending by email.

Ideal solutions are in principle, but they can be approached infinitely close. The project team hrbox.ru offered the original online solution, giving it to the world for free. After 30 seconds of registration, you get into your personal database of vacancies, candidates and their resumes. By creating a profiler profile in hrbox.ru, you have the opportunity to enter into it all the information about the applicant, including the summary file and any notes in chronological order. Applicants have the opportunity to record a short videoequette using a webcam and microphone and answering questions you have defined. Recorded video sheet becomes part of the profile of the applicant and is available for viewing directly to the customer.
