Captcha brotherhood

Many file hosting (rapidshare) have already switched to using recaptcha , breaking it is still a problem not solved, at least for the popular download JDownloader . When JD meets the recaptcha, the user is prompted to enter it within 30 seconds. It seems to be good, but at night such downloads will not leave.

The problem was solved by the Turkish programmer Selim, having founded the service Brotherhood Captcha . The principle is simple: you take out a loan for a while, solving other people's captchaes in a row, then the other fraternity members decide your captcha.

1) It is necessary to download a client under Windows , captcha solver, which during installation immediately connects to the installed JDownloader and takes recaptcha from it - you do not need to integrate them with your hands.

2) At startup, an account is registered on which credits will be accumulated. One user captcha added 8 points, captcha decided for the user removes 10 - a little more so that the system remains afloat.

3) Further, as personal need arises, the user gains credit by solving someone else's captcha (they now appear about once every half minute). Then the program is paused or closed.
