Big Las Vegas Sony: Xperia Z and Xperia ZL

At the CES 2013 technology exhibition in Las Vegas, Sony Mobile introduced two of its new flagships: the Xperia Z and Xperia ZL smartphones have a 5-inch display, a 4-core processor, and the Xperia Z is also in a waterproof case.


In the photo - Xperia Z in all possible colors

The design of both devices is designed in the new style of Omni Balance, which implies maximum symmetry; the body is made of durable tempered glass - including even the side panels.



Connector for HDMI-adapter is united in one MHL-port with microUSB. Connector for microSD is also brought out and hidden by a cap. Plugs are necessary because, like many other Xperia, model Z is waterproof and dustproof according to the standard IP 55 (protection against water jets from any direction) and IP 57 (short-term immersion to a depth of 1 m).
