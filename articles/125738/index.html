15-year-old girl won the Excel World Championship

Rebecca Rickwood, a British schoolgirl, became an Excel World Excellence Champion among schoolchildren and students aged 13-22. Yes, it turns out that such a competition has been held for ten years already in the Microsoft Office World Championship , and in the latter 228,000 participants from 57 countries have already declared. After the regional selection 78 of the best came out in the final, mainly from Asian countries.

Rebecca scored 100% in the Excel 2007 knowledge tests and won with a better time. On Wednesday in San Diego (California) the award ceremony was held, where the child prodigy was awarded the main prize of $ 5000.

In addition to Excel 2007 and 2010, the World Cup was also held in the disciplines of MS Word 2007 and 2010 and MS PowerPoint 2007.
