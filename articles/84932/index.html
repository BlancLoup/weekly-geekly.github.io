Imagine Cup: New contests - new prizes

As part of the student competition Imagine Cup , additional competitions (Awards) have been announced, in which you can participate regardless of the main categories or in addition to them.

New contests


  • Internet Explorer 8 Award
  • Next Generation Web Award

Internet Explorer 8 Award


Competition additions to Internet Explorer 8 is designed for teams of up to 4 people. As part of the competition you are invited to develop a web fragment, an accelerator and a visual search for Internet Explorer. (How to do this can be found on TechDays.ru .)

Application deadline is March 22. Read more - in the official rules of the competition .

The prize for first place is $ 5000. The second and third prizes are $ 4000 and $ 3000, respectively. Separately, prizes for the best accelerator, a web fragment and a visual search - for $ 2000 each.

Next Generation Web Award


A new generation of web programming contest is held between teams of students up to 4 people. You need to create an ASP.NET or Silverlight website that satisfies the topic of the competition and submit a screencast (no longer than 5 minutes) with a story about the project and a demonstration to the jury by March 15, 2010. To participate in the second stage, you must provide a link to an already working website.

Read more - in the official rules of the competition .

The prize for first place is $ 8,000 and a trip to Warsaw. The second and third prizes are $ 5,000 and $ 2,000, respectively.

Interoperability Award


The Interoperable Applications Competition is aimed at projects using Microsoft technologies along with open standards and technologies. The competition involves teams of up to 4 students or graduate students.

The deadline for submission is May 5, 2010. For more details, see the official rules of the competition .

The prize for first place is $ 8,000 and a trip to Warsaw. The second and third prizes are $ 4000 and $ 3000, respectively.
