Site vulnerability yota.ru fixed

Dear users, about the recent hacking of yota.ru, we inform you that a vulnerability was discovered on the site that allowed an attacker to inject malicious code on the main page. The breach was quickly resolved, but we did not stop there: this is the work being done to provide additional protection for the site.

Our experts have determined that the vulnerability was not localized in the CMS Bitrix system, through which the site was administered. This platform is isolated and not available via the Internet.

YOTA apologizes for what happened. All the consequences of hacking are eliminated, and now the site is not dangerous for your computer.
