Electricity and water from the air - we descend from heaven to earth

To my surprise, the topic " New technology for fully eco-friendly generation of electricity " has already gained more than fifty advantages. I believe that the method of extracting electricity and water described in the article is fantastic and has little to do with reality. Nevertheless, the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãgetting fresh water from the air by condensation seemed to me quite interesting. That's what I managed to google.

The company Eole Water has developed a wind generator that collects atmospheric moisture. The solution is quite simple - the electricity produced by the wind turbine is used to operate the refrigerator, through which the outside air is forced through the fan. Atmospheric moisture condenses and is collected for further use.



The wind turbine WMS1000 has a rotor with a diameter of 13 meters, a nominal electric power of 30 kW and is capable of collecting from 350 liters of water in desert conditions to 1,200 liters per coast per day. Since November 2011, the installation is being tested in the city of Mussafa (UAE).

Unlike the Aero Hydroelectric Station, this design does not use hydrogen airships at a height of two kilometers and water lines, nozzles and turbines capable of working with a water jet at 200 meters per second. In this case, the development and creation of the first prototype cost two million euros. So far, the installation of one such windmill costs 500 - 600 thousand euros.
