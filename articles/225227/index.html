1. Automated control system for electrical equipment of the power plant of its own needs.
Signal volume: 5500 I / O points

Link to project details

In one of the publications already briefly touched on this project. Its feature was the fact that it was from him that a subsystem of automatic generation of the project information base appeared on my basis based on structured tabular data from the TK (mainly the tables of signals, or lists of parameters).
The subsystem has proved itself very much more than once in subsequent implementations, and has been greatly refined to provide flexibility for adapting to any formats of TK documents that the developer receives as input to the project.

The system has been successfully operating for the 2nd year at the facility. Last year, it was successfully put into commercial operation.

5. SAU of Gas Turbine Unit (1.5 MW)
Signal volume: 1500 I / O points

Link to project details

This project appeared as a result of the exhibition, which was held in the spring of 2013, where the St. Petersburg company invited me to its stand. In one of the publications I have already covered this event. Representatives of a company that develops ACS automation systems for gas turbine units for auxiliary power stations approached me on the stand. According to their technical director, they have already tried many Western brands for this implementation, but all of them are not competitive due to the high cost of software. Therefore, they decided to try to execute this project on my scale. My prices are quite satisfied. Now we are jointly finalizing the project before carrying out the commissioning work at the site. All the algorithms and control logic of the unit are executed in the controller at the lower level, which works under the control of the CoDeSys system, my scada works on the AWS and is essentially a pure HMI for this solution. There are no serious control algorithms - only registration, processing (archiving and event logging), management and graphics.
