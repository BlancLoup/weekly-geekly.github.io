Bitcasa out of beta



Hello!

For the rest - a plan with an unlimited space costs $ 10 per month or $ 69 per year (limited offer)



Well, for the dedicated testers who participated in the test bitkasy, a gift: a one-year subscription costs $ 49 for the first year.



All the best.
