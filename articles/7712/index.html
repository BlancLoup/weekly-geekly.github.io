Talkonaut - VoIP and jabber client for mobile phone (J2ME)

During the total (as far as possible) transition to jabber I want to tell you about the jabber-client for Talkonaut mobile phone. In addition to communicating in jabber networks, it allows you to use the capabilities of Internet telephony to make calls.
The main feature of the program I consider the ability to work on several protocols (sorry not at the same time): jabber, icq, msn, gizmo not via jabber gates, but directly (although there is an opportunity to work through gates) and your account on talkonaut.com, which is Essentially the same jabber server.
When you start a talconaut, regardless of which network you are registered in, a voip service is automatically registered, and 10 US cents are credited to the account. The approximate cost of calls is 5 cents in Russia. So the relevance of calls is present when making calls over long distances.
In general, a very pleasant and original interface, speed, and rapid development of the program give it a chance to become the best client for the j2ME platform.
