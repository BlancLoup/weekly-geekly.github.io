application

To download the application, it is proposed to scan the QR code from the phone. He leads here on this page: lemonapp.mecare.cn . For known reasons , the Android application is not on Google Play, but simply lies in the form of an APK . For iOS, the application is posted in the official AppStore .

First you need to create a profile - enter the name, email address, password, as well as the date of birth and height. After that, we have three main cranes available: ‚ÄúMe‚Äù, ‚ÄúDiscover‚Äù and ‚ÄúMore‚Äù. Their purpose is not entirely obvious, so I will briefly describe what is what.

"Me" shows your current weight and tells you how healthy it is (body mass index - BMI)

To measure the weight, simply turn on the application, open this screen and stand on the scales. In the center - the nickname of the current user. On one device, you can create accounts for other family members.

In the upper right corner of the button with the image of the graph, you can see the history of the change in their weight over time.

Bugs


You should not have expected the perfect product for $ 12 :-) In the Android application, sometimes there is no weight recording in history. Not yet revealed what it is connected with.

Another application bug is the impossibility of binding to the QQ Health center . Binding simply does not happen, and so far it is not possible to share your dietary achievements with friends. Of course, for foreign users this loss is not great, because very few people use QQ in their smartphones outside of China.

Conclusion



I still have positive impressions. Extremely budget device in good technical performance, with a simple application, and not overloaded with a bunch of functions. Good old scales, which now have a drop of hi-tech. I think this is a good option for those who just started to surround themselves with smart technology, and decided to lose weight.

Pros :
  • Nice design of the device, quality materials
  • Normal design of the application, albeit with minor flaws
  • Price

Cons :
  • Over the stability of the application should work
  • Quite a large measurement error (see characteristics at the beginning)
  • No hope of support for Google Fit / Apple Healthbook, etc.
  • Overpriced at dealers.
