Conference WWW2006 Russia is scheduled for late October

As we reported earlier, WWW2006 Russia will be the first large-scale web developer conference in Russia. Up to 700 programmers from all over Russia will be able to visit it. Nothing of the kind has happened in our country so far: neither RIF nor CIB have ever claimed the role of an event for technical specialists, so the WWW 2006 Russia summit will be the first of its kind.

The organizational work on WWW2006 Russia takes place on a special wiki-forum , which anyone who has an access password can edit (you can ask the organizers in the comments to this text).

At the moment, the organizers have almost decided on the timing of the conference. The most auspicious date is called October 27-28 (Friday-Saturday). True, there are doubts that under such conditions it will be possible to rent a room. Because of this, the date can be changed.
