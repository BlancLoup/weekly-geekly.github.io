Mail.Ru Cloud on iPad



At the beginning of this year, we wrote about how our Mail.Ru Cloud service was created . Since its launch, we have released mobile clients and have included the service in the vulnerability scanner . Almost immediately, we started receiving questions from owners of iPads, when will the Clouds app for their tablets come out. And today we want to share one more news from Mail.Ru Cloud life - the long-awaited version has finally come out .
