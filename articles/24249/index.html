Sneakers with headlights


As stated in the title, the gadget, which will now be discussed, is a shoe with a bright backlight.

Practical waterproof and yet "breathing" sneakers with the name Pioneer, developed by designers Feijang Chen and Bin Zhao, look great and will allow you to save them when walking in the dark.

It's very simple - diode flashlights are inserted into the toe of the shoe, capable of illuminating the way in front of them for one and a half meters. Power is supplied to them from the battery located under the heel. The charge lasts for 12 hours of continuous work, but it is constantly replenished when walking.

The sole is made of dense rubber, so that you will be protected not only from the unevenness of the soil, but also from the accidental discharge of electricity.

Unfortunately, Pioneer is still only a concept and there is no information about how this system will be implemented. Judging by the photo, the charger is not a piezo element, then what?

Also unknown is the price of this novelty.
