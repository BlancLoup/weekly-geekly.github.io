Phone with out-of-the-box gaming system emulator



The company Fly on February 6 introduced a new phone MC100 . It is positioned as a music and video phone. Well, you know, there are many of them ;-) However, here‚Äôs another feature - it supports ROM files right out of the box from the NES ( Dendy ), SNES , GameBoy and GameBoy Color gaming consoles. Moreover, this is not a smartphone.

I once heard about the possibility of emulating on a PDA, but all the time it was preceded by dances with a tambourine. According to reviews of this phone, everything works very well, as expected and immediately. In addition, the phone is equipped with 2 buttons above the screen , especially for games.
