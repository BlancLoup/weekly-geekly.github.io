The project from the CIS became the winner of the annual battle of startups on TechCrunch


Photo: TechCrunch

Ukrainians Bogdan Suchik and Nikolai Lobanov with their project Mobalytics received the Disrupt Cup and a check for $ 50,000. It is more correct to treat the project as Ukrainian-Lebanese, because the third founder, Amin Issa, is from Lebanon.

Mobalytics is a kind of trainer for gamers. The project helps them to discover their weak points, correct weaknesses and start playing better. Beta-version of the application developers for the first time demonstrated at Disrupt. The project was highly appreciated by the representatives of the largest venture and technology companies - Founders Fund, Sequoia Capital, NextEv, YouTube, and also TechCrunch itself.

The first wards of the coach were the participants of the super-popular League of Legends game (LoL). Not every player can afford to hire a coach. Mobalytics in an accessible form demonstrates the individual statistics of each player, using visualization makes the language of numbers understandable for the user of any level of training.



The application takes into account all the necessary indicators - character skill points, magic power, level, maps, rivals, and so on. Thus, the player receives high-quality feedback, which motivates him to further growth.

In addition to LoL, Mobalytics plans to take Overwatch, DOTA 2 and Counter Strike players under guardianship.

A representative of the studio Riot Games said that during the month in the League of Legends plays, on average, more than 100 million people.
