Some will doubt the truth of the story told above. And it is absolutely in vain! When I showed my copy of the Ascend P7 to my friends, everyone expressed confidence that this is the Sony Xperia Z2. Immediately after getting access to the motherboard, we were alarmed by the design of its lower part, where the charging connector and microphone are located. User experience

The performance of the proprietary SoC Huawei is not a record. The shell works quickly, games of the Asphalt 8 level do not slow down - so what more could you wish for? But the hardware was very energy efficient. Even driving a smartphone to the tail and to the mane, I never managed to put the battery in a day by more than 50%. And this is not bad at all considering its unreliable capacity of 2460 mAh.



True, Huawei went on one trick. Diligently inspired by Sony's smartphones, the Stamina mode was dragged from P7 to it, for obvious reasons, abandoning the original name. Only in Sony you need to enable it yourself, and in P7 everything is included by default. And when the screen is off, synchronization with social networks and other energy-intensive processes do not work. I noticed this immediately and immediately turned it off. So, what is funny, it practically did not affect the time of work.

I really liked the screen. Right very much. High-quality 5-inch Full HD IPS-matrix gives a clear picture with eye pleasing colors.
