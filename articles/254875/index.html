Fresh sections on the popularity of CMS-systems, meters / systems analytics and online consultants

Hi, Habr. About 5 months have passed since the previous cut, and Ruward: Track updated the data for the first quarter of 2015. The study was based on an automatic crawler analysis of about 5 million domains of the .RU zone (according to typical service signatures).

Fresh cut on the popularity of CMS-systems





A number of experts argued that given the crisis, sales of commercial systems will fall, and developers will move to open-source solutions. While this does not happen - Bitrix and UMI are increasing their market share (as is Wordpress), and Joomla and Drupal are losing ground.

Distribution of popular meters and analytics systems





The shares of many participants have not changed, except for the most popular system - Yandex.Metrica. It grew by 3.66 percentage points. GA and Openstat slightly reduced their shares.

Increasingly, Yandex.Metrica began to be installed along with other systems. We associate this trend with the presence of Yandex.Metrica unique analytics tool - Webvisor.

Rating distribution of popular online consultants





The rating of the service is calculated based on the ratio of the amount of points of sites that use this solution to the amount of points of all analyzed sites. The score of each specific site is calculated using the formula score = e yandexRang , where yandexRang is the Yandex rank, ranging from 0 to 6. JivoSite is actively growing, LiveTex loses its position in the ranking, taking into account the rank of the sites.

Thank you for the work on the study to our colleagues from iTrack , and for the additional server capacity for analyzing the data of the company Status Telecom .
