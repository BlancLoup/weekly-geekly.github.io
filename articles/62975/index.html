Quick and large Sandisk SDHC card

Sandisk has announced the release of Extreme Extreme SDHC Cards, primarily intended for use in digital video cameras and DSLRs.


New items provide a data transfer rate of 30 MB per second (Class 10) and will be available in 4, 8, 16 and 32 GB.

According to the manufacturer, the new Extreme can operate in the temperature range from -25 to +85 degrees.
