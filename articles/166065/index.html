Several performance tests Sparkov R500 and R1000 from the MCST

By happy coincidence, I got into the hands of the console from two sparkless MCST - R500 and R1000. I admit I am rather skeptical about the projects of Russian electronics engineers, so I wanted to look at their performance. The results of which I hasten to share. As tests, I used the runs of GOST encryption algorithms - GOST R34.10-2001, GOST R34.11-94, GOST 28147-89 in one stream. Encryption and hash was run on 32k blocks. As can be seen from the graphs, the performance of the experimental R1000 800Mhz corresponds to ARM Cortex A9 1.2Ghz, which of course was pleasantly surprised.
