LG GX500 Review

This weekend, after the Androit smartphone LG GW 620 , they gave a new touch phone from LG to potestit. GX500 is a monoblock with a 3-inch display, the resolution of which is 400x240 pixels, without a keyboard. Dimensions of the novelty are 109x53x13 millimeters. A feature of the novelty is the support of two SIM cards simultaneously. And as far as I understand this is a budget version of the LG KS660

The number of such devices is growing and manufacturers pay attention to this direction. Mobile devices that can work simultaneously with two SIM cards are quite popular in the CIS countries.

Photo under the cut)


The first impression is a fairly solid monoblock, without creaks. High-quality plastic, with metal inserts, although it would be nicer if it were a newfangled SoftTach.


Support for microSD cards up to 8 gigabytes (The manufacturer says that the version is still inconclusive), accelerometer.


And pretty good, albeit only 3 megapixel camera. There is support for Wi-fi and a convenient browser. Just a bunch of social networking apps.


A 1500 mile battery gives you 3 days of phone operation with active use.


Sim cards are installed on top of each other, quite convenient and compact.

I got a prototype, with pretty raw software. But the phone was pretty smart. And perfectly adapted to the control of the fingers (the stylus is missing) The accelerometer works in several applications, the screen flips very quickly, without delays.
Synchronization with PC \ charging is carried out by MicroUSB.

The result, the phone is simple as a corner of the house, causes a feeling that you are holding a really reliable thing in your hands, with a pleasant user-friendly interface. Work with two SIM cards is organized very conveniently. The device turns on in 5-10 seconds.
Nothing is known about the price of the device. Sales starts in May)

Dimensions 109x53x13 mm
Weight 127 g
TFT display , 3 inches, 400x240 pixels, resistive
1500 mAh battery;
Memory 50 MB of internal memory (available to the user) plus expansion using micro SD cards.
GSM 850/900/1800/1900 MHz standard
3 MP camera , autofocus.
Communications Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 2.0 A2DP, micro USB (charging).

Advantages:
+ Excellent build
+ DualSim
+ Wi-Fi
+ Micro SD
+ Convenient design
+ Radio
+ Powerful battery

Disadvantages:
-It would be super if there was a sliding keyboard (for an amateur)
-Possibly in some applications there is not enough stylus
