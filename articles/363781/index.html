Install Android on your hand with Cicret



Cicret is a prototype bracelet with a pico-projector, also equipped with 8 long-range sensors and iron for full-fledged work of the Android OS. The fact that all this and, for example, the battery fits in such a compact case, is hard to believe. But the video was impressive, look.



There are two things you should know:

  1. Cicret does not raise money for a device using crowdfunding, but accepts donations through the site. This does not guarantee that you will get the device in your hands - no one promises that the device will be released at all.
  2. At the moment, the project looks very suspicious. The developers promised to start sales as soon as possible, but so far they have not even shown the final product. It is possible that the release of an unusual bracelet is actually not planned.


By the way, the creator of the prototype has an application, which is also called Cicret, but has nothing to do with the bracelet. This is an instant messenger for secure data transfer.
