Mnemonic: you can remember even numbers

In addition to the road of Cicero , through which you can memorize any number of words in the correct sequence, there are other ways to memorize a sequence of words. Unfortunately, they are not so successful, they require much more work and preparation, so I will not consider them. Let me just say that they are not needed. Palace of Memory (Cicero Road) is the best way to memorize lists.



But what about the numbers? If you tried to memorize a set of numbers using a memory palace, you would encounter some problems, for example:

  • The figures are dry, insensible. It is difficult to attach an association to them.
  • The numbers are repeated. Therefore, memories will overlap.
  • Most often you need to remember a lot of numbers (several phone numbers), which complicates the process.


Under the cut, I will introduce you to two ways to remember these soulless numbers. It is by numbers that annual mnemonic competitions are held, where thousands of numbers are memorized for a limited time!

Of all the methods that I studied, I found only two that were really worthwhile. Unfortunately, each of them requires preparation, but after that you can memorize phone numbers, house numbers, friends' passwords, and any sequence of numbers.

Alphanumeric alphabet


Description

The first way is to change the type of memorized information. Instead of numbers, memorize words. But how to translate numbers into letters? To do this, we need an alphabet, which we only need to memorize once, and then we need to associate numbers with letters using it.

It is very important to use only vowels or consonants in the alphabet. So as not to be confused, which letters were added to make up the word, and which are equivalent to numbers. It is best for everyone to come up with their own alphabet, something that first comes to mind at the mention of a number, but I will still write my own alphabet, which I use to memorize numbers:

  • Zero - the letters H or L. If you use vowels, you can the letter O.
  • One - F (first, as it happens) and the letter P (Once)
  • Two - letter D
  • Three - letter T, P
  • Four - the letter H or K
  • Five - letter P
  • Six - letter ,
  • Seven - the letter C
  • Eight - the letter B
  • Nine - the letter B (most), you can M (many)


The more letters you have attached to each digit, the easier it will be to make words, but it‚Äôs harder to remember what is attached to it.

Now, in order to memorize 178831, it is enough to come up with a road with associations associated with numbers. And then, bumping into each association, just remember the number. How to invent a road - read in the previous article.

I learned about this method from the book of Dominic O'Brien ‚ÄúHow to develop a perfect memory‚Äù. In it, the author makes preparation more, and memorization is easier. The author made associations for all numbers from 00 to 99, and he wrote down only people or characters. By the way, he wrote down the characters in alphanumeric alphabet. For example:

  • 04 = NK = Nicolas Cage
  • 24 = DK = Jim Carrey

and similarly the whole hundred.

In addition to this, for each character, he invented a unique action (it was never repeated by anyone), for example: he makes faces, plays baseball. Thus, in order to memorize 4 digits, he needed to come up with only one association with the action!
For example, 0424 is Nicolas Cage, who makes a face (action Jim Carrey).

But, of course, to achieve such results, long preparation is necessary, and I do not think that this is necessary for an ordinary person.
