How to make a project, the right investor?

Everyone knows: a project focused on the needs of a particular customer, at times increases its chances of being bought or receiving investments.

Recently, an increasing interest in start-ups is increasingly shown by large Internet companies.
And although the monsters of the IT market can afford to buy another large company along with all of its traffic or a running Internet business, they still prefer pre-projects.

Large Internet companies are always strategic investors. Buying other projects, they first of all solve the problems of their business, be it the audience, traffic or product properties of the project.

Why?
A strategic investor is more willing to invest in projects at the prototype stage, because:
- A prototype will be cheaper than a ready-made project that has been in the market for a long time.
- the prototype is easier to adapt to the needs of the investor
- a project just starting to enter the market is easier to integrate into the overall business process.



Startup Weekend is a place where everyone can make a project prototype from an idea and surround it with a team of developers.

- methods of studying user behavior on the Internet, advertising technologies.
