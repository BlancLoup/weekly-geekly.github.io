Tie with this augmented reality!

It would seem that ties exist in the world for a long time and it would be time for all men to learn how to tie them. But, as practice shows, not everyone can do it by memory. Tying a tie for many people remains a problem. Apparently, the complexity of this simple at first glance procedure is that you have to simultaneously look in the mirror at your reflection and manipulate with both hands, without confusing the left with the right.



It is especially difficult to do this in the morning, when the caffeine from the morning cup of coffee has not yet worked, and the time before the boss comes to the office, there is less and less. However, the torment will soon come to an end, the TieSight application looks after your actions through a web-camera and prompts you with your voice and on the screen what you need to do next.
