In the regiment of games from Valve arrived: Portal and Left 4 Dead 2 released in the status of Beta!

It seems that the release of Ubuntu 13.04 was the reason for Valve to uncork a couple of bottles of Burgundy with two new significant releases - the beta version of the first part of the Portal puzzle and the beta version of Left 4 Dead 2 - a survival horror shooter, which, in fact, started porting games at one time from Valve to Linux.

As usual, the game is available to those who bought it, without any gestures - on Steam there is a rule that buying a game for one platform automatically allows you to play it on all other platforms.
