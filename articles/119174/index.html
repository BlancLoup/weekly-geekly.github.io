MobileNews - a platform for creating news iOS and Android applications

The audience of iPhone and Android users is growing continuously. No one is surprised that the statistics on the confrontation of these platforms are presented as regular, monthly reports in business publications. We have been developing mobile applications for the local market for a long time and, seeing not only the crazy dynamics of growth in the number of iOS and Android devices, but also the ever-growing interest from media and advertisers, we decided to separate the solution for online media into a separate platform - Mobile News . With its help, a project, whether it be a news site, a TV channel, or a blog, can quickly solve the problem of creating an application for iPhone / iPad and Android.



The platform works in closed mode - we undertake all the necessary processes for building the application, eliminating the project from the need to understand technical issues. MobileNews allows you to customize the interface and design of the application in accordance with the style of the brand. The application displays photos in articles, allows you to view videos, share your favorite content on Twitter and Facebook, read the news offline.

The application can also be expanded with the help of additional features: a live broadcast, support for photo galleries or user comments. At the same time, the average time to create such an application is 1 week.

At the moment, based on MobileNews, we have developed iPhone and iPad applications for Podrobnosti.ua and Inter TV channel , Obozrevatel.ua , Pravinskaya Pravda , Android application UA Today ), etc ...

Soon it is planned to implement the banner system and the appearance of additional options for customizing the interface of the created applications.
