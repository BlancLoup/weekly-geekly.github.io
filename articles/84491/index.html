5 phrases that freelancers need to fear

If you are on the path of a civilian for more than a week, then I am sure you have met these phrases. Perhaps you can immediately select them from the text of letters and dialogues, and perhaps you are still a novice and want to find out which phrases you should pay attention to are most common. Really? You really can do this work yourself, but for some reason you want to give this pleasure to someone else, and even for much less money? Why? You do not have time? You do not like this kind of work? Yes, some of the civilians can actually hire someone to do some of the work, but I guarantee you will not hear these words from any of them.

If you help me out this time, then I will continue to work only with you.
