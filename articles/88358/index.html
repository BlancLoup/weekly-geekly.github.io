Review FullHD video camera Panasonic HDC-SD60

Good day,% username%!
I got a new toy - FullHD Panasonic HDC-SD60 video camera and I hasten to share my impressions and a small overview of the functionality.


Appearance and some numbers


The camera is rather small and light and sits well in the hand.

The set of buttons is minimal:
  • top - button toggle between Intelligent Auto Mode (iA) and Manual Mode (manual), Image Stabilization System (OIS) Mode Switch, Zoom Control Lever and Photo Snapshot Button
  • back - switching modes, playback, video, photo (IMHO is not a very convenient switch, but it is not often necessary to switch)
  • under the LCD screen - recording, scaling, menus, lighting control and a delete button
  • under the screen panel there are 3 interface connectors: HDMI, USB and AV / composite output, as well as an on / off button and a memory card slot

All the rest is managed through a very responsive touch screen (it will be difficult for people with thumbs to manage, but for them there is an unusual / unusual shape stylus)
Now about the characteristics:
Image sensor 1/4 "MOS
Lens aperture F1.8 (WIDE) / 3.3 (TELE)
Optical zoom 25x
Compression format MPEG4-AVC / H.264 (AVCHD standard compliant)
Image resolution 1980x1080, quality: HA (17Mbps / VBR), HG (13Mbps / VBR), HX (9Mbps / VBR), HE (5Mbps / VBR)
Recording media SD / SDHC / SDXC memory card
Weight (excluding batteries) 255 gr.
Overall dimensions (WxHxD) 51.5 x 65.5 x 112.0 mm

AF tracking


The essence is simple, poking on the screen at someone else and the camera will focus on this object, while if a person is selected, it will adjust the brightness / contrast so that the face can be seen as accurately as possible.


Automatic selection of shooting mode / scene


The essence is simple - the camera itself will choose the most appropriate shooting mode (auto, portrait, panorama, evening) depending on the factors: lighting, presence of faces, focusing range

Manual shooting mode


The mode is for those who do not trust AI or for conditions when this AI starts to go crazy. Here the choice is given:
  • auto / manual focus
  • aperture / shutter speed / white balance settings
  • lighting compensation
  • alignment of skin color
  • night mode (increased shutter speed)
  • macro shooting
