Meridian Mobile is already available for download.

Meridian is a music player that makes listening to music from the social network VKontakte pleasant, convenient and beautiful in visual terms. We already wrote about the version for Windows. And then we let slip that the application for Windows Phone 7 will be released soon. And now it happened!



Application features at the moment:
  • Listening to your audio recordings, albums, albums of groups and friends
  • Recommendations
  • Search by album and artist
  • Download album art and artist covers with Last.Fm, scrobbling
  • Ability to set the song to VK status (text, links are not in the API)
  • Sending a track to a friend's wall
  • Display information about the artist: photos, releases, related artists
  • Save photos of artists in the photo gallery
  • Radio
  • Live Tile


Now we are working on improving the functionality and correcting errors. In the near future an update is planned.
