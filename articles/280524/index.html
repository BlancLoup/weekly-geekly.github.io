QA: Conference. Many news, new reports, live



Our conference on testing software systems will begin very soon and, as is known, will be held in five cities of Russia, and the grid of reports on all cities is almost ready. The most important news:

  • We do online broadcasts from Moscow and St. Petersburg . All reports. For one ticket. With a record of events. Broadcast can watch visitors of any cities. The main thing - the presence of a ticket to your event
  • Max Shonichev . Center for Network Technology. He will tell about the automation of server-side testing and the UI in the Linux OS under the conditions of a rigid framework of system security settings. A lot of hardcore.
  • Arthur Orlov and Anastasia Lenshmidt . AutoTransInfo. They will talk about the problems of interaction between the departments of QA and Dev and their solutions
  • Jan Alekseenko and I will hold three workshops
  • Alexey Vinogradov is our special guest from Germany (he is one of the leading Radio QA and developers of Selenide). Alexey will introduce us to three simple and practical types of PageObject architecture - Static, Void and Fluent and demonstrate the use of code, compare the pros and cons of the approaches
  • Also from Germany - our guest Slava Schmidt is a Scala consultant. He will tell us about Property-Based Testing with API-First Approach
  • Daria Efremova from Performance Lab will talk about the methodology of Failure Modes & Effects Analysis - an approach to testing with a retrospective
  • And many, many other reports. Why do I need to rewrite them all? Visit us at the QAC conference site.
