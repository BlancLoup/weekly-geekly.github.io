Faraday Future has unveiled a 1050-strong electro-crossover



CES 2017, like almost any CES of previous years, gives a lot of surprises. And not only in electronics, but also in the automotive industry. It was here that was presented a powerful electric car from the company Faraday Future . This is an electric car FF 91, combining the features of a conventional electric car and a car class "superlux".

The developers showed a multimedia system with entertainment for passengers, demonstrated the navigation capabilities of the vehicle, as well as an automatic parking system. This function was demonstrated to the visitors of the exhibition, so that everyone could be convinced that the electro-crossover is able to park independently. The journalists who attended the event say that the electric car can compete with such well-known brands as Bentley, Ferrari and Tesla for a number of parameters.



By the way, ex-Tesla employees who have joined the company from Tesla Motors are taking part in the development of electric cars Faraday Future . One of them, a former engineer of this company, now holds the position of vice president. An electrocrosover can travel a distance of 700 kilometers on a single battery charge. Its length is 5.2 meters, width - 2.28 meters, height - 1.59 meters. The vehicle was built on a modular platform entirely created by the Faraday Future. There is here an adaptive suspension and a full control chassis. The speed of 96 kilometers per hour is gained by the electrocross for 2.39 seconds. This is faster than some Ferrari models.


The company's engineers have tried their best. In particular, they managed to reduce the drag coefficient to 0.25. Tesla Model S has the same figure of 0.24. According to the developers, even the pattern of the spokes of the rims plays a role here - everything is thought out to the smallest detail.

Power of 1050 horsepower car provides four electric motors. They derive energy from specialized lithium-ion batteries developed by the company in conjunction with LG Chem. Battery capacity is 130 kilowatt-hours. The speed of a full charge of the electrocross from the most powerful home charger is about 4.5 hours. Together with electric cars, you can order chargers for 110 or 240 V, 1.5, 10 and 15 kW. The company declares that cars can be charged from any electric gas stations. In other words, this elektrokrosover is universal, problems with it will not be anywhere.



As mentioned above, there is a car parking system. But the electric car can not only park, but also move independently along the road. For this, it is equipped with 10 cameras, 12 ultrasonic sensors, 13 radars and an LIDAR optical rangefinder. All this technical stuffing allows the FF 91 to identify the owner by face. By the way, at the last CES exhibition, the company presented only the concept of the electric supercar FFZERO1.


It was then only one seat, and the vehicle itself was more like a Batmobile than a real vehicle. That concept should have attracted the attention of the public and specialists to the products of the company itself, and it really worked.

Despite the fact that the electric car looks impressive, the company itself has financial problems. Thus, the construction of the factory is frozen due to delayed payments. Construction should cost Faraday Future in a substantial amount - about $ 320 million, if not more. According to management, the company has already invested more than $ 600 million in its projects.
