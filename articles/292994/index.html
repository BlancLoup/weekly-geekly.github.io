Tickets Cloud's Skolkovo resident received a $ 10,000 cloud grant from Amazon Web Services

Russian b2b platform for organizers and distributors of tickets Tickets Cloud received a grant from Amazon Web Services ( AWS ). AWS has allocated a startup $ 10 thousand to use cloud resources.

AWS will provide Tickets Cloud with services and the necessary infrastructure for the development of the project, Firrma reports . AWS also uses Pinterest , Spotify and Dropbox as part of a special program. It may involve startups that exist less than three years. In addition, the amount of investments attracted by the young project should not exceed $ 1.5 million.

Residents of the Skolkovo Foundation have had the opportunity to participate in the AWS program since 2014. Tickets Cloud became a resident in May of this year. The project was selected in the direction of "Processing and analysis of large data arrays." The 2be.lu Investment fund and the business angel Bogdan Yarovoy became investors of the startup, who in December 2014 invested $ 300,000 and received a share of 13.3%. Tickets Cloud is also a portfolio company of the Starta Capital Accessor Fund , which in July 2014 together with the Moscow Seed Fund invested 4.5 million rubles into the project.

Tickets Cloud helps companies enter the market or increase the number of sales and business contacts. All transactions and relationships are automated.

Amazon Web Services is an infrastructure for cloud web services platforms. She was represented by Amazon in early 2006. The operating income of the cloud unit compared with last year ($ 77 million) increased by 407% and reached $ 391 million. The division's revenue in April-June increased by 81% to $ 1.82 billion. In the Amazon Web Services over the past quarter, 350 new features and services.
