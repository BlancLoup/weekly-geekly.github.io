Informal economy. Anyone who wants to know more

Now it is obvious to everyone that everything in the economy has gone far under the most fun scenario of development. And when and how it will end no one knows, although it is important and interesting for everyone. Therefore, the need for fresh and high-quality food for the mind has increased dramatically with you and me, like any economically active person.

Since the official press deliberately distorts economic reality, and the informal one does not have competent analysts, an adequate analysis of the situation and events is easiest to find in the personal blogs of economists with few readers, but there is no censorship either.

True, many people are seriously writing about near-economic and near-crisis topics, but for the most part they publish either pseudoscientific shamanism or paranoid delusions of forerunners of the new world order, some are simply hysterical.

I want to share a small collection of Russian-speaking bloggers-economists selected by me (professionals and amateurs, traders and analysts), whose thoughts I consider reasonable and interesting. Some of them write blog entries solely for personal use, some are public and literary. But all "They are smart" :)
