Brainwashing by Evil Martians - Ruby on Rails development workshop May 19-20 in Moscow

Hi% username%!

Evil Martians will hold a master class on Ruby on Rails development for the third time on May 19 and 20: http://brainwashing.pro/rails .




The course will be only 18 students and 3 instructors from Evil Martians . This will allow instructors to conduct practical exercises, to pay maximum attention to each participant.

The course consists of eight blocks of topics, each of which consists of a theoretical and practical part. The participants of the course in two days will receive a baggage of new knowledge and will be able to start putting it into practice as early as Monday:
  • how to determine what is wrong with the application and how to fix it;
  • how to optimize and automate front-end;
  • how to organize work with the code;
  • How to prepare an application for high loads and solve the problems that arise.


Brainwashing will be useful to already experienced Ruby on Rails developers, architects and team leaders. Participation in the course is paid, 30 000 rubles. if paid before April 29.

By the way, here are photos from Brainwashing Rails, which took place on March 10 and 11 . You can ask us questions, chat with participants of past courses on our Facebook page .

Hurry up to submit an application to participate in the course , there are few places left.
