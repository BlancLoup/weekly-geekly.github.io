The new part of "Harry Potter" appeared in the torrents

Yesterday, five days before the start of sales, photos of each page of the seventh part of the Harry Potter novel appeared in torrents. Initially, two torrents appeared on Demonoid.com with photos of the American version of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows. However, after a while they were removed by the site administration.

Not surprisingly, both torrents quickly appeared on mininova and other torrent trackers (the torrent was soon deleted on mininova). One of the "strapped" torrents contained photographs of dubious quality only in the first 495 pages (approximately from 800), but the second included the entire book. Now the book can be found on almost all trekkers, including the notorious The Pirate Bay .

By the way, according to the Canadian newspaper The Globe And Mail, book publishers in Canada have publicly asked users who downloaded the book to keep the end of the book in secret. Naive?
