As we went out in the TOP-3 of the Ukrainian App Store



If you are an iOS developer, work for an iOS application development company or are in any other way connected with this sphere, then you know the approximate number of races needed to reach the top. The numbers vary from category to category, each uses its own strategy, so any experience is important. Below I want to share our experience. I hope it will be useful to readers. I want to warn you, the case is atypical. We went to the top Ukrainian App Store, and not Russian. Here you will not find the magical "thousand races with a zero budget for marketing." Day 1




Apple checked our app and it's time to publish. We didn‚Äôt have experience of getting to the top, however, many articles on this topic were read. In general, the advice is the same everywhere: a week before the release it‚Äôs worth preparing all the channels for attracting users for quick launch, write to all possible sources, and if possible get free publications.

Our team is quite small, so a week before the release we solved a kaleidoscope of other problems, most of which were technical. It's time to open the advertising valve to the full! Well, as a full, the budget is very limited, and the emphasis is on the Ukrainian App Store, which is much less frequently discussed Russian, and therefore need less downloads. Our application is geo-location, with information about institutions nearby (for more details you can watch and download for Android or iOS ), so we created a set of banners for each of the 5 largest cities separately.

A total of 3 advertising sites were used: vk, facebook (it also includes instagram), myTarget. Results: in vk - 210,000 hits CTR - 1.9; facebook / instagram - 44,000 impressions, 400 installations; myTarget - 7,000 impressions, CTR - 2.09. There was a small problem: myTarget did not quite correctly calculate the effective bid, as a result, the shows almost did not go. In addition, vk and myTarget refused to track the installation. Fortunately, analytics on the application can be viewed immediately in a dozen places, so the numbers are not lost. In general, for the 1st day we scored 1,900 installations for $ 0.07. Since we were still growing in the ranking, organics did not help us yet. But by the end of the day we were in 1st place in the Travel category. Hmm, that was pretty easy.
