Swift Books

Good afternoon, Habrovchane!

Enough time has passed, but books in Russian about Swift never appeared.
The Peter publishing house is interested in your opinion on what this book should be. Take a survey and help us choose the perfect book.

1. The book for beginners. For example such
Swift for Beginners: Develop and Design

Boise J. Pitre not only introduces you to the basics of Swift - variables, constants, types, arrays and dictionaries - it will show you how to use the unique Xcode environment that allows you to quickly and easily create applications for iOS and OS X. In this book you will find detailed instructions, tips to make it easy to create working applications, great examples of applications for Mac and iOS, and a quick reference guide for working with Xcode, Playground and REPL.

2. Classics from deytelov
Swift for Programmers (Deitel Developer Series)

Books of these authors do not need recommendations. In this book, as in the other books of Paul and Harvey Deytelov, you will find many examples of real-world programs that will allow you to smoothly move from the foundations of the language to creating your own applications.
