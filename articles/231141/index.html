Ruby on Rails 10 years old

The first public release of Rails 0.5.0 took place on July 24, 2004 , almost exactly ten years ago. This day is considered the official birthday, so yesterday it was possible to celebrate the anniversary.

Although Danish programmer David Heinemeier Hansson (dhh), the author of the popular framework, believes that it can be officially celebrated at the RailsConf conference.
