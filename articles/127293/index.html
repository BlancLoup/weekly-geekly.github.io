Google promotes HTML5 as a gaming platform.

Developers are releasing more and more games on HTML5 of quite decent quality (just mention, for example, Monster Dash , Angry Birds or SONAR ), and this is just the beginning. This breakthrough is expected when the Chrome 14 browser comes out with the Native Client , which will open the way for browser games of a completely new quality, with 3D and other bells and whistles.

Some are already seriously looking at HTML5 as a full-fledged gaming platform. The New Game conference was organized specifically for them - to all appearances, the world's first conference of HTML5 game developers. It will be held on November 1-2, 2011 in San Francisco, registration is already open. No wonder that one of the main sponsors of the conference was Google. She will also send there several speakers who will talk about WebGL. In addition to Google, representatives of Mozilla, Opera, Bocoup, GameSalad, Moblyng and others will read the reports on using and optimizing WebGL, Canvas and JavaScript.
