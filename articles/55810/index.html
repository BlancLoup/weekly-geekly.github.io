Two recent news on the development of client-side hardware for the Russian GLONASS navigation system.

The Research Institute of Space Instrument Engineering begins the construction of a special plant in Tatarstan , at which in the same (2009) year the Russian auto navigators Glospace will be mass produced, 350-400 thousand each year. The deficit will be eliminated by that, the rush demand will be eliminated, because of which, before Glospace, buyers dismantled the stores at a terrible speed .

It should be noted that GLONASS has long been criticized for the fact that navigational receivers are assembled from chips made not in our country, but in Korea. (The aforementioned Glospace, for example.) However, the news on the site of the State Unitary Enterprise NPC ELVIS teaches that these days are beginning to recede into the past: Zelenograd began production of three-core chips supporting GPS + GLONASS navigation, which can also act as communication coprocessors , providing hardware and software support for DSP (digital signal processing) and a number of communication procedures (Viterbi, turbo coding, correlation, etc.), IP telephony, image processing (IP cameras).
