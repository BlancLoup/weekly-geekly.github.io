Fastening the system unit to the tabletop

Very short post about workplace optimization. The goal is to remove the wires from the floor, hide the system unit, uninterruptible power supply unit and subwoofer, make room on the table.
The solution is very simple; we fasten the brackets to the tabletop (using simple handles) and fix the system unit with a tie strap (can be found in the car shop). The cost of the solution is about 250 rubles.
Under the cut a few pictures.

What happened




Since I fixed both the system unit and the subwoofer with the BBP - the price of the solution doubled.
PS On the Internet you can find a ready-made solution, at the price of 2 m., Looks much presentable. Actually this product was the source of the idea. Unfortunately, the price, delivery and its speed are not encouraging.
