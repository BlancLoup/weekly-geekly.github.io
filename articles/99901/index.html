Statistics of the wireless Internet of the two capitals

Recently, people understand the benefits of wireless Internet, which does not end near the exit from the cafe, but is all over the city, preferably not one by one. Now users have the choice of which telecom operator to use to get the Internet to their mobile phone or laptop. And the results were stunning. But better than any words, diagrams will say for me.


Sellers recommend modems of the following companies to their customers

The study was conducted by the Public Opinion Foundation at the end of June 2010 ( prooflink on the Public Opinion Foundation website). The survey involved sellers of 306 stores - 205 in Moscow and 101 in St. Petersburg - all these were stores of the M-Video, Svyaznoy and Euroset networks. Interviewers interviewed sellers under the guise of buyers - young people, active Internet users who want to use the Internet at home, at work and on vacation, mostly in the city.
