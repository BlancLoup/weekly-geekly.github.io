The South Korean corporation Samsung will produce chips for the new generation of smartphones from Apple, writes Bloomberg . So far, there is no official confirmation that Samsung will manufacture Apple A9 processors, since the agreement between corporations is still kept secret. Information about the new cooperation of companies in Bloomberg came from unnamed sources familiar with the course of negotiations.

The Apple A9 processors Samsung will manufacture at its plant in South Korea. Additional orders will be received by a partner of Samsung, the company Globalfoundries Inc.

It is worth recalling that in 2014, Samsung was ceded in the tender for the production of chips for Apple by Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing (TSMC). The volume of the semiconductor components market now reaches $ 300 billion, and Samsung and TSMC each year are nervous about the right to produce and supply chips for Apple and Qualcomm.
