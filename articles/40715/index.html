Yandex.Site

It seems to me that we have forgotten about this wonderful service of Yandex, which is in a state of beta according to web-based good tradition ...


According to Yandex, Yandex.Site is a search through the pages of your sites. Let me explain: the site is set to a form, the appearance of which you can slightly customize (the setting is mainly done by color). The form is installed on one of the pages of your site. When searching for information, the user will be sent to the Yandex page, in which there will be no advertising (perhaps this is just now), and you can also make the appearance of this page again when you add your site to the search on Yandex.Site. To make it clearer, I immediately place a screenshot on the resulting horror result.


To use search using Yandex.Site, you need to register your site on the Yandex.Site system and ... Well, after that, I think everything is clear.

PS This service seems to be under active development, which is good news. I note that Yandex.Site is located side by side with Yandex.Webmaster.
PPS I can not help reminding you about the auto-completion of the input field in Yandex. As a fan of Yandex services, I just jump for joy)
