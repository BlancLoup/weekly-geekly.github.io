Hackspace (technical creativity club) opened in Nizhnevartovsk



We will continue our excursions on hackspaces of the world, this time, from the electronics capital of Shenzhen, we will be transferred to the oil capital of Russia - to Nizhnevartovsk, yes, yes, it was here that enthusiasts recently opened a technical and robotic creativity club - hackspace .

Hackspace is a physical place where you can work on your idea in a comfortable work area with various machines and other equipment that can be used to work on projects.

This communication with like-minded people , experts in various fields, with whom you can consult or participate in a joint project. This is an opportunity to attract people to participate in their project or just to get the support of those who are really interested in the field of development. It is also possible to order or purchase on-site various unique components that can not be found in the regular sale.
