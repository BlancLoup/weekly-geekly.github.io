Transfer from Payoneer card to WebMoney system

We are pleased to announce the launch of a new service - transfer of funds from the Payoneer card to the WebMoney system. We always strive to provide our customers and partners with the best payment solutions: prepaid cards, US Payment Service , international and local bank transfers, checks, transfers to local e-wallets, various additional services and much more.
Seeing the huge interest of users in direct transfers from prepaid Payoneer MasterCard cards to WebMoney, Payoneer launched this service.

Transfers from Payoneer cards to WebMoney are currently in test mode. Gradually, more and more partners are connected to this service, and in the near future, the WebMoney output will be available to all interested partners.
You can transfer funds from your Payoneer MasterCard cards to your WMZ wallets of a formal certificate with verified data . To quickly verify your account in WebMoney, you can download a copy of your passport here .
