Do sites fall on holidays more often than usual? Myths, reality and how to protect yourself

New Year holidays are coming, and this is a special time for many areas of activity. For some, this is a low season, for others it is an opportunity to earn extra money. People whose work is closely connected with the work of sites and IT infrastructure in general is also not easy. On the one hand, I want to relax, but on the other - there is an irrational fear that the most carefully tuned system that has been working without interruption for many months will fall as soon as you turn away from it. Which is typical, it often happens. How can you afford to relax? One of the outputs is external monitoring of the availability of sites and servers .
