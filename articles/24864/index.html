TCPView - Monitor TCP / IP connections

TCPView is a small free program for monitoring TCP / IP connections in Windows, written by Mark Russinovich, a well-known security professional who is now working at Microsoft.


It shows real-time processes on your computer using the TCP protocol.
You can see who is listening to the ports to whom you are and who is connected to you.

I use it for a long time and often. The program is very convenient for catching viruses, detecting the network activity of various programs, and just to find out where and why you are connected. Directly from its interface, you can kill bad processes, or you can simply close their connections.
