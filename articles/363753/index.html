Santa Tracker has been updated - you can follow Santa and learn JavaScript



In connection with the inexorable approach of the holiday, Google has traditionally updated its Christmas project Santa Tracker , designed to track Santa Claus Santa and his reindeer Rudolph, to Catholic Christmas.

On the main page you can watch the countdown counter showing how many days are left before the holiday. They did not forget about the peculiar gamification of the process - every day a new level becomes available on the site, which is either a simple game, a video, or even JavaScript lessons (at the moment the latter are still blocked). If you click on the calendar icon in the upper right corner, you can go through the already open levels and make sure that the quests with the Learn icon (where JavaScript lessons are supposed to be available) are much smaller than the entertaining ones - play or watch the video.

For the project, there is Ukrainian localization, for some reason, there is no Russian for a mysterious reason. For those who want, mobile apps and extensions for Chrome are also available.
