Online premiere of the fourth open short film Tears Of Steel

In a few minutes, the online premiere of the fourth short film Tears Of Steel will begin. The short film is made exclusively on Blender open source software without the use of additional video editing tools, such as AfterEffects or Nuke. The shooting was carried out for 4 days, and the creation of special effects took 4 months. During the creation of the film in Blender, many new features were added, for example, full-fledged work with color spaces, green screens, a new mask editor and an improved 3d-tracking algorithm. All of these innovations will be included in the version of Blender 2.64, which will be released from day to day.
