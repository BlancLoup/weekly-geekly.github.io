iPhone 5 and new display



Most likely, the fifth iPhone will have a curved glass display, like the Samsung Nexus S, according to the latest rumors. For this, Apple purchased 300 glass processing machines.

A new type of display will mean that Apple really decided to redesign the device. It remains unknown whether the display will be curved inward, as in the Nexus, or will become convex.

The previous generation iPod Nano was also equipped with curved glass (but without a touch screen). Nexus S was positioned as the first smartphone with a curved screen, which "is more convenient to use, as it repeats the shape of the face."
