Google Voice snuck on iPhone

Despite the fierce opposition from Apple, Google Voice program still penetrated the iPhone, this time in the form of a web application. The user just needs to launch the browser, go to this address and enter their credentials.



Although the web program runs on HTML 5, it is almost as fast as the native application, which was banned by Apple.

In terms of functionality, it is also practically the same: here you can read call recordings after speech recognition, listen to messages from voice mail, send SMS, make a call via VoIP, etc.



Of course, mobile Google Voice works on any other smartphones.
