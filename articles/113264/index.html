Boot phone

You probably have a "regular cell phone", which can call, play music from a flash card and pretend to be on the occasion of a card reader when connected to a "big brother." Well, or was. Or have your other half / parents / children / grandparents / etc. Have you ever thought of using it in a more geeky way than just a means of communication? I do not mean modifying the firmware and getting console access to the phone itself, no - everything is much more banal and more interesting at the same time.



You will need:
  • Computer
  • Any cell phone with a USB cable, support for flash cards and the ability to connect to the computer as a regular "flash drive" (almost all modern Nokia, Motorola, Samsung, devices on Android, the list goes on)
  • Hiren's.BootCD.iso (or any similar boot image)
  • grub4dos
  • Card reader for the type of flash memory that is installed on your phone.
  • The desire to feel like a geek :)

To begin with, we will make our flash drive from the phone bootable, following the simplest instructions from the official website of Hiren's Boot CD: www.hiren.info/pages/bootcd-on-usb-disk
