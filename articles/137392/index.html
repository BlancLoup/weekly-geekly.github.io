Update the ranking of the best applications for Windows Phone 7

Two weeks ago, I wrote a habrotopic about the best applications for WP7 , having communicated to update it once a month. Given that more than 100 people have added it to their favorites, it seems to make sense to continue. So, the rating of the best applications for Windows Phone 7.5 received a monthly update.

Under the cat a more detailed description and links.

Social networks:


VKontakte : official client for WP7. Is free.


Mehdoh : Convenient Twitter client. Many like it more than the official. Is free.


Metrogram : unofficial client for Instagram. Alas, while you can only view the contents without the ability to upload photos. According to rumors, an official Instagram client is expected soon. Is free

Navigation:


City : a fusion of 2Gis and Google maps or Nokia Maps. Take the data from 2Gis and put it on the selected map. The result is a very functional mapping application. Is free.

Work with information:


SkyDrive : extends the ability to work with files lying in the cloud. Is free

Games:


Fragger : quite an interesting game, reminiscent of Angry Birds. Grenades are used as birds, terrorists are used as pigs. Price $ 3 .

Replacements:


KinoPoisk : replacing an informal client with an official one. Is free.



Shazam : replaces SoundHound as it understands songs in Russian better. In the free version allows you to recognize no more than 5 songs per month. Paid removes all restrictions, but costs $ 6 . So if you are listening to predominantly English-speaking music, it's easier to stop at SoundHound


Harbor Master : Flight Control replacement. Harbor Master is a bit more complicated and interesting: instead of airplanes, you control ships, and you need not only to get them into the dock, but to output them after loading. Price $ 3.
