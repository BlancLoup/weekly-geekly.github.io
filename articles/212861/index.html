The stone itself appeared in the photos taken by the rover, at 3537 sol, the stone was absent in the photo for 3536 sol. At the same time, scientists noted that one of the sides of the stone is dark with light inclusions, and the content of some elements (for example, Mn, S) is higher there than in other specimens of the rock analyzed. Geologists explained all this by the fact that manganese and sulfur gradually accumulated in the stone as the moisture dried up, in which there were a lot of dissolved salts.



According to scientists, all this happened relatively recently (recently with respect to geological time), either on or under the surface, and under the influence of erosion, the underground layer came to the surface.
