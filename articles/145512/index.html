Follow the European Football Championship on Yandex

Today the main sporting event of the first half of summer will open with matches in Wroclaw and Warsaw - the European Football Championship , so much expected by the fans. And if you are far away from Ukraine or Poland and will not be able to personally attend the stands, follow the news of Euro 2012 on Yandex.

You can watch the progress of each match live directly in the search results, including in the mobile version, by asking Yandex, for example, [ Russia Czech Republic ]. A special witcher will show the most relevant and detailed information about the events taking place in the championship at the moment. This is an account, and the authors of the heads, and links to live video and text broadcasts, as well as video clips of dangerous moments. Before the matches, the sorcerer will know the start time and the address of the broadcasts, and after that the result of the match and the necessary links to the statistics.



On a special project dedicated to Euro 2012 , you can see the results of current and past matches, group tables and calendar of meetings, news, photos, links to text and video broadcasts from our partners, information on the top scorers and goalkeepers of the tournament. You can follow the course of matches on the Yandex main page, as well as in @YandexSport .
