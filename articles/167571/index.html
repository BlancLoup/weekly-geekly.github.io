Epic Citadel on Unreal Engine 3 showed what Android is capable of


Very few Android games can be called a technical miracle. In general, the same can be said about iOS, but it at least has some technically impressive titles working on the Unreal Engine 3 from Epic Games, including the Epic Citadel demo. Now she also made a breakthrough for Android.

Epic Games has announced Epic Citadel, its Unreal Engine 3 techno-demo, for Android devices. In this version, we added a benchmark and refurbished the look. The benchmark will be useful to those who want to compare how the Unreal Engine 3 runs on different Android devices.



The release of Epic Citadel may be a hint that Epic and Chair can release Infinity Blade on Android. The series is extremely popular on iOS and will undoubtedly be warmly received on Android devices.

You can download Epic Citadel today both on Google Play and Amazon Apstore . The iOS version was also updated to include support for the iPhone 5, the fourth-generation iPad, and the fifth-generation iPod Touch.
