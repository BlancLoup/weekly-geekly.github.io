Computer - the engine of commerce or "Children's World" on the platform of Intel



The Detsky Mir store, located on Lubyanskaya Square in Moscow, was a branch of heaven on Earth for more than one generation of Soviet and then Russian children - his visit remained in the memory forever. After a large-scale reconstruction, Detsky Mir once again opened its doors on March 31 of this year to show how much it has changed. Intel also contributed to the transformation of the store. Video displays in the Children's World also exist, but not with ads, but with cute cartoons and clips. 55-inch TVs are connected to Intel NUC computers based on Intel i5 processors, powerful enough to show HD content and very small, which is good for editing.
