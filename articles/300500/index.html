I have laziness or procrastination?


It is very important to make the correct diagnosis. The psyche is a serious thing, and you should not act at random. Fortunately, the procedure of diagnosis is quite simple, the patient is quite able to perform it on their own. To do this, you need an office with a stove bench. Better if it is a comfortable bed. Eliminate extraneous noise: close the windows, escort from the premises of all uncomplicated and onlookers. Dim the lights: turn off the lights, cover the blinds and curtains on the windows. Now lie down. If it is cool, it is advisable to take cover with a blanket or blanket. For complete immersion in the procedure, you can close your eyes. All is ready? Now you need to do the following. Think of some important business that you need to do, but you somehow take it easy. Think about how you need to do it, what exactly your actions will be. Try to be relaxed. Let thoughts flow freely and at ease. After 15 minutes, analyze what happened to you during the procedure.

So, we make a diagnosis. If during the entire procedure you experienced nervousness, accompanied by increased sweating and rapid heartbeat - most likely you have procrastination. And if after 15 minutes you find yourself sleeping peacefully - probably you are just lazy. In the case, if you prefer to use more rational actions, such as throwing at the office, pulling out hair, punching the wall with your head, etc., you should know that you are not our patient. But in any case, do not worry and do not panic, dial 103 on the phone and try to articulate the essence of the case from which you take a shirk.
