Yamichat - customizable chat bot and online consultant in one platform

The chat bot communicates with visitors to your site, collects contact information himself or transfers the dialogue to a free operator. The creators of Yamichat are the Moscow team, led by Oleg Ratunin. These processes include the intersecting pattern of questions that customers contact daily.



Many of the questions are not too different from each other, for example: customers want to hear information about guarantees, differences and the cost of a product or service, to receive information about the delivery and conditions of the promotion, to learn how the site functionality works.

How it works?


With the help of the widget, scripts of dialogue with visitors of your site are created and configured. Just train a chat bot and he can easily answer the most common questions from customers.



Who is this service for?


Now the generation of Performance is growing up, which likes to compare, test and not overpay for bulky functionality. It is for such entrepreneurs that the Yamichat service, affordable and at the same time performing the main task of the conversion widget, was created.

The main target audience of the project is:

  • Internet services and portals with capacious FAQ;
  • Sites offering various services;
