Counting money

Introduction


To begin with, I am an employee of Finobox . I would like to share my personal experience in financial accounting, but as an ordinary user of the service.

Besides the fact that I participate in the project, I have my own small business connected directly with IT.
I am an individual entrepreneur and apply the simplified taxation system (USN). That is, I have a fairly standard situation and buying an accounting program in a yellow box is like a cannon on a sparrow. And it is still necessary to keep records.

I have some one-time projects, as well as subscription services for certain services. Story


And our project comes to the rescue - www.finobox.ru
At first, I just fixed all the income and expenses. And also I got the assets that are called: current account, plastic card, WMR, deposit.

Since the system allows you to create separate tabs based on payment filters, I chose for myself just this way of structuring income and expenses.

Payments can be recurring: in my case it is the payment of permanent services, staff salaries, office and apartment rent.

For example, I have one major client (let's call it Bigkash LLC) for which a large amount of various work has been performed for a long time and in addition to the income from this client, it is necessary to see overhead costs. Quite important and large expenses for me are renting an apartment and an office.
