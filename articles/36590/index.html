Solar table will feed energy

In the United States began selling the table, the cover of which is replaced by a solar battery, reports Engadget. The table is called SunTable and costs 2.2 thousand dollars.

The surface of the table is covered with solar panels that charge the nickel-metal hydride battery installed in the table. The latter can serve as a power source for various electronics. For its full charge enough four hours to find the table in the sun.



The energy of a fully charged battery installed in SunTable is enough to watch TV, or work with a laptop, for four hours. As a power source for the incandescent lamp SunTable can work six hours.

SunTable weighs 20.4 kilograms. The size of the table is 45.7x78.7x137.2 centimeters. Thanks to the waterproof coating, you can use the SunTable in all weather conditions.
