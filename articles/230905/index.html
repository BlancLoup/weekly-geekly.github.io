New statistics Facebook: 1.32 billion users, 30% come only from the phone



Facebook reported for the second quarter of this year, and one fact is becoming more and more obvious: Facebook has become a mobile company, writes The Verge. The social network receives 62% of advertising revenue from mobile users - a rather drastic change for a company that did not have mobile advertising at all two years ago.

At the same time, out of 1.32 billion people who use Facebook every month, 399 million - about a third - come only from phones. It is worth noting that the monthly Facebook user base has grown by 14% compared to last year, while the mobile user base has grown even more - by 31%. According to Mark Zuckerberg, American users spend more than 40 minutes a day on Facebook.

In the second quarter, Facebook announced $ 2.91 billion in revenue and profit of 42 cents per share, ahead of Wall Street expectations (2.81 billion in revenue and 32 cents per share).
