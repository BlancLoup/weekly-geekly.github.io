Seven rules for admin

Hello.

If you are my regular reader, then you know that by and large I am writing for people who are creators and administrators of Internet projects, for those who want to become them or are simply interested in web site collisions. And along with purely technical problems, any administrator will sooner or later face problems of moral and personal plans.

I want to give some tips to beginners, received once by me from smart people and on my own sad experience.

Similar rules for the administrator.

First, I have already spoken about this in one of the past articles and repeated it repeatedly - it is not worth personalizing. On the administrator must be a mask of inaccessibility. It is difficult to do at the beginning when the site is just developing, who, if not the administrator, will lead the first users and will help beginners to deal with the system, glitches and whims. Ideally, you should gradually move away from communication with users, but this does not work out, because users become your buddies and friends. And friends take time, everyone needs to explain and tell the next action, because everyone considers himself "special, approximate."

Based on this first rule - the administrator must be unavailable. Let the machine answer for it.

But without friends, users can not be. Who else but alfatesters will be innovations, at the moment when it is still emerging in the head. But when you have faithful assistants who are ready to listen and prompt, the chance to make a mistake is reduced.

Because the second rule - find yourself friends on the site.
