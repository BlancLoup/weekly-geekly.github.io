Toaster. Enhanced tag subscription: Receive instant email notifications of all new issues for any tag.

We introduce another way to deliver questions on the "Toaster" : using the "Enhanced Subscription" tags. If you strongly subscribe to the tag, you will receive all new questions on this tag to your email. You can cancel this subscription at any time directly from the next notification letter.

Such an enhanced subscription is very useful for highly specialized tags, for which there are few questions on the ‚ÄúToaster‚Äù, but at the same time, this topic interests you a lot and you don‚Äôt want to miss a single rare question about it.

In order to strongly subscribe to a tag, you need to go to its page, subscribe to it, after which an additional button will appear to the right of the subscription button, clicking on which you can put the necessary tick.



Why we introduce the feature "Enhanced tag subscription"


On the Toaster, the questioners and the respondents communicate with each other through tags: when publishing, the question is tied to the tags and gets into the zone of attention of those who are subscribed to these tags.

So far, we have had two ways of delivering questions to those who can potentially answer it:
  • Through the "My Ribbon" user, where the question gets along with other questions tied to tags that the user is subscribed to.
  • Through a weekly email digest , if the question is of interest to someone else, except the author, and dials a sufficient number of subscribers.

Obviously, both of these methods have their limitations. All questions on user tags get to My Ribbon, and if the latter is subscribed to a lot of active tags, then some of the questions can easily be missed because of the large number of them. Far not all questions get to the digest, but only the most popular ones - questions on narrow-specific tags are unlikely to get here.

A new delivery method is designed to remove both of these restrictions.
