nokia.ru - but Lebedev was better off ...

Not so long ago, we all witnessed the "mutual love" of Comrade Lebedev and Nokia. Lebedev officially wrote and quoted: “ Lebedev Studio terminates the contract with Nokia. Hesitated . However, representatives of Nokia also said that in fact Lebedev Studio did not cope with the tender and the development of the site fell on the back of a certain E-Development, which had previously been involved in the Nokia in Ukraine project.

I am not a big fan of such clashes, in the end, then, probably, both sides got what they wanted to get. However, today I spent the whole night picking up the new site nokia.ru and ... I somehow was not at all sure that Nokia took the right step by giving the right to work on the site of another studio ...
Immediately make a reservation! This is by no means an advertising post for anything. Nokia was picking up the site to “catch” official guides for the third mobile project. Seen, was very shocked ...

I started at about one in the morning, I thought in an hour or two I would already have a script for automatic collection of manuals from nokia.ru. But I was wrong =)
After several hours of working with the layout of nokia.ru, I realized that either:
a) the site was typed by hand
b) the engines of the site ruin the layout so that people like me suffer

Point b) rather paranoid than true. As for point a) I want to say that such typesetters need to take their hands off.
