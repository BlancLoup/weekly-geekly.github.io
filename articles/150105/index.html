Unique Samsung Store opened in the center of Sydney



According to The Sydney Morning Herald, today a new Samsung mobile device store has opened on George Street in Sydney, designed from scratch by two designers of a famous Korean company. Visitors who already come to the store for Samsung's new products are greeted by a showroom in bright colors with large posters of the company's latest products on the walls. The novelties themselves are located on two rows of tables in the center of the hall and on racks on its sides. Smiling managers in blue T-shirts are always ready to help customers.



The opening of the store also marks the beginning of sales of the new Galaxy Note 10.1 tablet with a stylus. Its younger version, with 16 gigabytes of onboard memory and a Wi-Fi module, is priced at 589 local dollars.
