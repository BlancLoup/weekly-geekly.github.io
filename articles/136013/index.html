Reddit website closes January 18 from 8:00 to 20:00 in protest against SOPA

Reddit's social news site continues the war against SOPA, in which, surprisingly, wins victory after victory. The organized boycott against GoDaddy was successful and forced the world's largest registrar of domain names to withdraw its signature on the bill. Activity on the / r / sopa branch spawned several related websites against SOPA and attracted media attention.
