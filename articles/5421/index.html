Skype 3.1.0.244

The developers of the Skype Internet phone presented a version of Skype 3.1 to the general public, in which, in addition to correcting errors, there were two significant innovations:
  1. SkypeFind is a system of reviews about companies and institutions, tied to their location. Each member of the Skype network can leave their opinion, for example, about the restaurant visited or read the recommendations of other participants. In the future, it is planned that the Skype Find system along with recommendations will be supplemented by the popularity ratings of the telephone numbers of the companies under discussion.
  2. Skype Prime is a handy tool for professionals involved in consulting and technical support. Allows you to set a time-based or fixed fee for incoming calls to you. Skype takes up 30% of the proceeds; the rest goes to your Paypal account.
