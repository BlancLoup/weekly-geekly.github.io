6 best tools to create a great site on Joomla



Developers, and those who are not, use content management systems to work with sites. There are quite a few of these systems, but three are especially popular among them: WordPress, Drupal and Joomla.

Joomla is better suited for developing websites, harder than WordPress blogs, but simpler than Drupal. One of the advantages of Joomla is the number of extensions. They help to expand and customize the look and functionality of the site.

Jsitemap


A sitemap is a list of important pages that helps search engines like Google find and index important content. JSitemap is one of the best map generators for Joomla, which offers an innovative way to work with site maps. According to the creators, this extension increases the visibility of the site by search engines by 400%.

After installing JSitemap, you don‚Äôt have to wait until the search engine searches your entire site - instead, all the information will immediately go to the search engines database.

JM Slideshow


Many users nowadays visit websites from completely different devices with different screen sizes. It has become very important to make websites automatically adjusting to the screen of the current device.

JM Slideshow is a great tool for creating responsive slideshows. The slideshow is optimized for search engines and done using Bootstrap. Among the settings - 5 different transition effects, navigation control, and much more. The application can be downloaded for free, it is compatible with versions 2.5 and 3.

Perfect Ajax Popup Contact Form


Many webmasters concentrate on the appearance of the site and forget to set up a feedback form properly. This can lead to the loss of potential customers. The Perfect Ajax Popup Contact Form application helps integrate feedback forms using AJAX. The form offers spam protection, unlimited fields and more.

It has several settings, and it allows you to change colors and make the inclusion of several forms on the page.

Social Media Links Genius


Profile in social networks is an effective tool for brand promotion. And it will be very good if you link your profile in social networks with your business website. Unfortunately, many site owners do not pay enough attention to this subtlety.

Social Media Links Genius is a useful extension for including profiles from social networks on your website. On the site there are icons of social networks that link to different profiles. Icons can be customized. Compatible with Joomla 2.5 and 3.

Vertical Accordion Menu


For the visited site it is very important to make a good and smooth navigation. Your navigation menu should be simple, clear and easy to use.

Nextend's Accordion Menu helps you create customizable menus. The extension is easy to use and easy to configure for various tasks. You can change colors, add animation, etc.
