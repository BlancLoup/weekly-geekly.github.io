Meeting in the Ministry of Finance with the developers on the subject of open data - June 16

Friends, on June 16 we will organize the next meeting of colleagues from the Ministry of Finance of Russia publishing open data with developers who already use or plan to use this data.

There will be interesting reports and the opportunity to ask the staff of the Ministry of Finance of Russia about what new open data are published and how best to publish those that are already published.

We will discuss:
  • how the Russian Ministry of Finance publishes data and what it may publish in the future
  • contests for developers like past BudgetApps
  • historical data such as data on the budgets of the Russian Empire and the USSR from 1865 to 1991

From myself I can say that today the Ministry of Finance is the only agency conducting such meetings on a regular basis.

Since in the ministry the passport system I ask everyone to register on the website of the event and come with a passport.

If someone is not able to come, but really wants to ask questions to colleagues - write them to me at ibegtin@infoculture.ru or here in the comments, questions will be asked at the meeting.

Personally, I have not managed to convince the rest of the ministries, services and agencies to conduct them regularly. However, I do not lose hope to convince them to start doing it.
.
A day before, on June 15 there will be an open data advice and I ask everyone to take part in the survey attached to this post. If it turns out that the format of meetings is convenient for everyone or it turns out that I want completely different formats of communication, then I will definitely talk about this on the board.
