Welcome to CocoaHeads Moscow October 31



This week is rich at a conference for mobile application developers, but nothing replaces warm communication on Friday CocoaHeads in the Mail.Ru Group office - come and discuss YaC, MBLTDev and everything that happened this month. And, of course, listen to the reports of our speakers. And we will have three speakers:

The first to continue the topic of efficiency, which was started last time by Oleg Ovechkin, Konstantin Zabelin from Zababako company, telling the report entitled ‚Äú How to write more code in Xcode or a little more about efficiency ‚Äù. Konstantin plans to talk about how to write more code using a couple of tweaks of your favorite and system input methods. He will also share several recipes to simplify the lives of developers.

After Egor Taflanidi , the leading developer of RedMadRobot , recognized as the best in mobile application development by Tagline , will tell you how to evaluate projects and their architecture in order to use code from past projects in the report " Effective code reuse in iOS applications ."

For high-quality and fast production of applications, you need to create your ecosystem. The ecosystem of the RedMadRobot team is based on reuse, that is, repeated use.

Egor will tell about the reuse at all stages of the application:
  • Documentation
  • Cool architecture
  • Convenient structure
  • UI practices
  • Testing

Raise issues of effective reuse of code in iOS projects, and share popular pitfalls.

And finally, in the report " Do not be afraid of Auto Layout ", our regular guest, Alexander Zimin , who built the application for 24 hours on the BattleHack hackathon without a single indication of the UIView coordinates, tells us about Auto Layout, which you simply must go to with new iPhones 6/6 +, if not done so before.

Why you should not hardkodit? Is it possible to do without constraints in the code? When it makes sense to add them in the code, and when to just redraw the frame. Hk Alexander will answer all these questions and show them with atypical examples.

After the reports, we will continue communication for hot tea and coffee. Spend the last day of October in the right company, come to CocoaHeads!

The meeting will begin at 19.00 at Leningradsky Avenue 39, p. 79. As usual, do not forget to take your passport with you. To participate, you must register . Waiting for you! There will also be an online broadcast of the event, and you will have the opportunity to ask questions and hear answers from the speakers - just send them to @CocoaHeadsMSK . Video recordings of the reports will be added to the youtube-channel of the meeting.
