VMmanager 6: a new tool for creating virtual machines from ISPsystem



On February 5, we opened beta testing of the cloud version of VMmanager 6, a new virtualization panel. We invite the Habra community (everyone who is faced with the creation and management of virtual machines) to see it and leave their feedback.

The first version of VMmanager 6 is a cloud version. It does not need to download and install on the server. At the same time, virtual machines, operating systems and user images are stored on your servers. VMmanager 6 On-premise will be released later.

The product can be tested now, go to my.saasvm.com for this. For testing, you need a dedicated server with a white ip-address and CentOS 7.

Free test server

If there is no free server, use the free one from our partner Selectel. It is possible to use the equipment of the provider within two days. To get the server, email us via my.ispsystem.com or chat on the site .

Read more about VMmanager in the news on our website.
