Corbina subscribers will get Golden WiFi

From May 1, 2007, Moscow home Internet users from Corbina Telecom for 250 rubles per month will be able to connect to Golden WiFi (Golden Telecom service) throughout the network coverage area in the capital. Currently, Golden WiFi consists of about 7.5 thousand access points and covers about 80 thousand households, open space in the city center and the most popular public places.

For this amount - 250 rubles - Corbina subscribers are provided with unlimited access to Golden WiFi. At the same time, subscribers have the right to choose - they will be able to use both WiFi and the Corbina 100-megabit connection.

The Golden WiFi option can be activated on any Corbina Telecom home broadband channel in Moscow with a valid ruble tariff plan.
