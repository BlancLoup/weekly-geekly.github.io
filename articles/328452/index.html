United Dev Conf 2017: results and useful materials

On April 6-7, a multi-profile conference United Dev Conf .





This is the first large-scale experience for the Belarusian IT-community, when the conference combines 4 areas and already familiar projects: Highload Dev Conf , Frontend Dev Conf , Mobicode , Marketing and Sales.

Conference audience : mobile application developers (iOS, Android, cross-platform development), Frontend and HighLoad devs, UI / UX designers, marketing & Sales specialists, PM and PdM, business analytics and CEO of IT companies.

The purpose of this experiment is to create a network, which will involve all units working in teams on the project.
