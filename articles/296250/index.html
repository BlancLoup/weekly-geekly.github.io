16 types of content



Video Content


When reading, only about 20% of all information is remembered. When viewing video content - about 80%.

About 65% of all people are visuals. They perceive visual information much better than dry text, especially if it is without pictures.

Convincing facts?

Video content is a great alternative to standard texts. You can do:
- Reviews of their products / services / projects.
- instructions on the use of your products / services
- viral advertisements that will increase awareness
- webinars
- recording of performances at events
- and other.

Any kind of content can be easily integrated into the blog. Yes, in general, you can stick it in any place on the site, if you really want.

Individual attention deserve video interview with famous people who are popular in your field of activity. Firstly, such interviews often contain a lot of useful information, insiders for your audience. Secondly, as a rule, the person being interviewed shares it on his channel. And thanks to this, the reach of the audience, which previously could not know anything about you at all, is expanding.

By the way, besides the fact that videos give an influx to an audience that does not like to read, they are also very well ranked in search engines.

Video content can then be easily converted to text format and podcasts. This further increases the value of this type of content.

Full manuals in the style of "How to ..." (How to)



In my opinion, this is one of the best types of content.

Imagine the situation that the user has a question in the style of "How to do ...". He scores it on Google. He is issued 10 articles, he opens the first of them and finds a complete answer to his question. This is what the correct ‚ÄúHow To‚Äù guide should be.

Free e-books



Free e-books are one of the best ways to lead generation. For example, on the website Boosta.ru I offer a free e-book ‚ÄúA / B testing: from theory to practice‚Äù in exchange for a postal address. And site visitors leave their mail. Because they see this as value for themselves. And I give what they need. As a rule, in such books information is structured and consistent. This is very convenient, as a quality e-book eliminates the subsequent searches for additional information in Google.



But the most important thing is that such books are very easy to make. You do not need to write everything from scratch. If you constantly run a blog of your company and write on a particular topic, then you already have a huge amount of necessary information. The only thing that remains to be done is to put the whole thing together, organize it and convert it into a PDF format.

This can be done directly in the Word, collecting all the information first, and then save it as a PDF. The cost of creating this type of content is minimal. Most often, this is just your time. And the result is very good.

Webinars



Webinars do not quite relate to the types of content, but nevertheless this is another way of conveying information / communication with the target audience. And a very effective way.

If you are well-versed in your field, you have a lot of ready-made content and presentations, then you can easily hold webinars once a week / month, communicate with the target audience, train and increase its loyalty (at the same time your image as an expert).

Webinars allow you to diversify ordinary text articles very well and attract an additional audience, especially if you can share them well.

Many Western companies every week conduct webinars on their topics. Thanks to this, they generate leads and increase their expertise. Moreover, systematic work is very important here. If you want to start conducting webinars, then you need to make a plan in advance, select the frequency and follow the chosen strategy. At the first webinar there may be very few people. But over time, with a consistent strategy, the number of listeners will definitely increase (of course, if you tell great information).



A good example of using this type of content can be found at WebPromoExperts Academy of Internet Marketing. Every month, about 10 webinars on various topics on internet marketing. On average, each webinar is attended by over 100 people. By the way, I once spoke there and talked about A / B testing - 140 interested listeners gathered. You can watch the recording here.

If you want to make something more unique and interesting, then the podcast can be in the format of an interview, as does Taisiya Kudashkina from Websarafan .
