Dangerous Lace Cupcake

Why dangerous? Yes, because this cupcake is 5 minutes away from you!



What do you need:


  • 1 mug (safe for microwave)
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 4 S.L. Sahara
  • 2 S.L. cocoa
  • 1 egg
  • 3 S.L. of milk
  • 3 S.L. vegetable oil
  • Pinch of vanilla
  • 3 S.L. chocolate chips, it is desirable


What to do:


Add dry ingredients to the mug, mix with a fork. Add the egg and mix everything again. Pour in milk, butter, vanilla, make a thorough final stirring, add chocolate chips (optional). Now feel free to put the mug in the microwave for 3 minutes, at a power of 1000W. Do not worry if the cupcake will rise above the mug - this is normal. Pour the finished cupcake with whipped cream / syrup / garnish with cherries / or just eat with a fork straight from the mug.
Enjoy your meal!
