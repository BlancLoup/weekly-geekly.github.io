YubNub: Internet command line

We recently released Ubiquity , but for some reason, few people know that there has long been a more powerful tool (albeit less linguistic).

yubnub.org

Type:
“En2ru things” - you will be taken to yandex.lingvo with the translation;
“Split yahoo.com google.com ” - both of them will open in 50% frames;
“G habr” - google “habr”;
"Wiki habr" - Search the wiki "habr";
“Flicc google” - Search Flickr Creative Commons;
"Site2pdf google.com " - guess.

In general, no matter what service you use - type its name + something else? most likely to happen what you expected. It integrates perfectly with all browsers . There is an opportunity to create your own teams and lists of popular ones . There is even an opportunity to scrape web and code .

About everything in Russian:

Installation


IE7: Here , URL: " yubnub.org/parser/parse?command=TEST " (without quotes), Name: YubNub

IE6: Here is the registry file, click execute. In the address bar, click "y en2ru hello"

FireFox: Here , choose YubNub, “OK.” Next, select the YubNub search by default (or when needed), click "en2ru hello".

Opera: Home , right-click on the search field, “Create search”, enter “y” in the Keyword. Done, now on the command line you can write "y en2ru hi!"

Integration with Safari, Google Desktop, bash, emacs, gnome, slickrun and yubnub.exe to look at of. site .

Creating a new team


Open here or execute the “create” command.
In the "Command": wiki
In the URL box: ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%s [use _ for spaces]
[use _ for spaces] - special command replaces spaces with underscores in% s.
In the following field: “Wiki Habrahabr 2” click “Test”, if you hit the desired page - click “Create”.
