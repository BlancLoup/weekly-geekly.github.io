Stickr.com - 1 year with you

The whole year is over. I can not believe that the time flew by so quickly. We decided to summarize our work, remember what it was, what we wanted to do, and what the project has become now. After all, 1 year is not so little. Plus we have prepared gifts for you!
We will tell about gifts just below.

Let's go back and remember how it all began.

Each year, the amount of information on the network doubles - you can not argue with statistics. You sit like that at work, and there is not even an extra minute to view articles in your favorite blog. You need to save the link somewhere, add a bookmark to the browser. And then you will come home and you will remember that the bookmarks remained at work.
And here we are thinking, but to make the process of storing information as simple and clear as possible. Where do people usually leave notes? Of course on the stickers. The truth is, before you had to go to the store, spend your time and money, and then look for a pen and a place to stick this piece, and not lose it ... No, it is difficult.
So the idea was born to create a portal for friends, where you can quickly make a note on a web page, and then read in any place where there is Internet.
The project Yousticker began its journey. The main goals that we have implemented in our startup are cross-browser compatibility, quick registration, easy interface and flexible functionality. So that you do not need to be distracted by such trifles as saving information, but concentrate on the process of its search.
Very soon the project received additional investments, and rapid development began. A small start-up YouSticker.com turned into Stickr.com :

Gradually, we added more and more new features, and the project went beyond the usual resource for web notes. Stickr.com has become an independent social network where people regularly come to just chat.

What else can Stickr do?

  • You can add not only a comment to the sticker, but also a picture, quote, flash, audio and video content.
  • Stickers have become public and private. You can add bookmarks only for yourself, no one will see them.
  • There were several types of groups - public, private and personal. Now you and your friends can unite in a group and paste your webpage with stickers.
  • We also became friends with Twitter, Friendfeed, Evernote, Facebook and Livejournal. Your stickers automatically fall on the walls of these services.
  • We came up with browser plugins , with which you can even add a screenshot to the sticker! Now with Stickr you can test your website, take a screenshot of the problem and send the link to the developer.
  • You can subscribe to stickers, read them in your feed, write messages to other users and much more.

All this you can see on our website. Registration takes only a couple of seconds. And in order to stick your first sticker, you will need exactly 3 seconds.
