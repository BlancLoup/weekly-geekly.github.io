Listen to music from Vkontakte on HP TouchPad

A beta version of the application called VKMusic for the webOS platform has been released. Using your tablet HP TouchPad, allows you to search, listen and download your favorite audio recordings.



The current version displays 100 records and downloads music to the / media / internal / audio folder.

In the near future:
  • interface improvement
  • Advanced settings
  • user authorization
  • upload custom playlists
  • batch uploads playlists
