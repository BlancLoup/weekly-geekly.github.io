Home inventory by Yandex.Market

Probably many, like me, occasionally encounter a problem when you need to urgently find any information about your home device (characteristics, instructions, date of purchase, driver, etc.). But in memory there is not only this information, but the very name of the model has long been forgotten. Everything is aggravated if the device is no longer with you, but, for example, donated to parents, and there is no way to go and see the name. And it happens that in a private apartment, access to the name is very difficult (for example, the name of the 50-inch and 50-kilogram plasma on the back side of it).
In order not to get up every time, if necessary, to look at the model of my iron or vacuum cleaner I did this.

I solved this problem by creating a shopping list with the name ‚ÄúAlready bought‚Äù in the Yandex.Market account. In this list, I move the cards I bought goods. The date of the listing is saved. According to her, I find out whether the warranty on this product has been released. Most of the characteristics I find in the card itself, I look for the missing ones on the Internet. I don‚Äôt even install HWinfo now, because I recognize the insides of all my home computers, not getting up from my chair, sitting at my main working computer.

Thus, we get a database of home inventory in fact in the Yandex cloud.

As a development idea, I would suggest Yandex to supplement the service with the ability to add their comments to the product cards (visible only by themselves), and also attach files (drivers, instructions, etc.) to them, so as not to look for them every time on the Internet.
