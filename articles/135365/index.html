Ice Cream Sandwich launched on Kindle Fire

Extra-popular and the most successful product on Amazon, which, according to the company itself, is being bought up like hotcakes and can easily compete with the iPad, and running on customized Android 2.3, it seems that thanks to enthusiasts it will be able to work on the latest version of the mobile platform Google.

The developer JackpotClavin has released its early Android 4.0 build for Kindle Fire, which is based on CyanogenMod 9 and which, of course, requires revision. The main problem - enthusiasts have already connected to its solution - JackpotClavin calls some problems when reading / writing data to an SD card.

The video shows how, without noticeable lags, Android 4.0 is already running on Kindle Fire.



It is safe to say that the latest version of Android 4.0, which refused to update its top-end Samsung tablets, together with an attractive price on the Kindle Fire, can really make a device more than a successful product.
