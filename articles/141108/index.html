Many reviews begin with the unpacking process. Honestly, I do not understand the pleasure of contemplating the details of each stage, I think that the photos of the device in the case will be enough.


WEXLER. Flex ONE unusual form factor: a thin screen with a significant thickening on the one hand.
In this thickening all electronic stuffing is hidden: processor, battery, buttons, connectors, etc.


The photos show that I got a copy of a glamorous pink color. But they promised that the first batch, which will go on sale with a black backdrop. Of course, there is a temptation to bend the device harder. As far as I know, in the process of developing several copies died the death of the brave, and the motherboard did not stand, and not the screen.



As I already wrote, screens for WEXLER. Flex ONE is manufactured by LG. With the exception of flexibility, these are already familiar screens made using e-ink technology. The contrast is very good, I will not say how many percent of whiteness, just attach a picture with the magazine MAXIM.

It is convenient to hold the book in your hands, just due to the thickening. Screen orientation can be changed.
