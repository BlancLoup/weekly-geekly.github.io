Nook - e-reader from booksellers

The publishing company Barnes & Noble, which owns the largest network of bookstores to the west of Moscow, has long promised, and finally launched its e-reader called Nook for sale. Yes, this is another alternative to the Amazon Kindle.

The Nook reader seems to be quite convenient - it has two screens, one of which is the touch scroll bar at the bottom, the ability to go to the online store of the same Barnes & Noble and order books there.

The screen for reading (e-paper) is black and white and, it seems, without illumination, the touch scroll bar is color. View and download books via Wi-Fi. In the absence of such, the user can access (provided by an AT & T operator) virtual 3G bookshelves. While reading, you can make notes in a book, save them, you can read several books at the same time, switching between them.

There are some limitations. Barnes & Noble provides an opportunity to ‚Äúshare‚Äù a purchased book with a friend. But only once every two weeks. According to William Lynch (William Lynch), President of Barnes & Noble.com, all owners of the iPhone, BlackBerry smartphones and Mac & PC desktops can read books from Barnes & Noble.

The stated price of the Nook is $ 259, which is comparable to the cost of the Kindle, the price of one virtual book will be equal to ~ $ 9.
