Unboxing NetApp E2700



We continue to acquaint you with the equipment as it is: large, from all sides and without a wrapper.

In this article, we will take a detailed look at the representative of block storage systems NetApp E2700 , who came to us a week ago for testing. The main areas of application of these systems are: full motion video, media content, seismic data processing, video surveillance, high-performance computing clusters (HPC), D2D backup, geological exploration, etc.

Who is interested - attention! We begin)

Consider in detail the rear view.

Four FRU (Field Replaceable Unit) - easily replaceable modules that support ‚Äúhot‚Äù replacement in case of failure, are grouped in pairs: 2 storage controllers and 2 power supplies. Controllers on top :-)



Replaced quite easily and quickly.



E2700 controllers are equipped with SAS 12Gb / s ports in the base, and support expansion with Host Interface Cards (HIC) with FC 16G, iSCSI 10G ports, as well as additional SAS 12G ports.

  • 6 Gb SAS Disk Expansion Ports

The E2700 series supports the ability to combine different types of disks in one storage system (SAS, NL-SAS and SSD). You can increase storage volumes using disk shelves for 12, 24 and 60 disks. In addition, a maximum of 7 shelves can be connected to the E2724.

The maximum number of disks is up to 192 pieces with expansion shelves and 120 solid-state drives with expansion modules for 24 drives. The maximum ‚Äúraw‚Äù capacity is 28.8TB, with the possibility of expansion - up to 700.8TB (168 disks with a capacity of 4TB in additional shelves + 24 disks of 1.2TB in a basket with controllers).

The 12-disk shelf is ideal for organizations with a limited budget that require productive and at the same time capacious drives. A 24-disk shelf allows for maximum performance per unit while reducing power consumption to a minimum. A 60-disk shelf increases storage density and allows you to store up to 240 TB of data in 4U racks, which is especially important for environments with a high need for disk space.

Supported disk shelf expansion:
  • DE6600 (4U, 60 disks): 2.5 "10,000 rpm SAS 1.2 TB / 900 GB / 600 GB (with full encryption / with incomplete encryption); 3.5" 7200 rpm 4/3 / 2 TB (with full encryption / incomplete encryption); 800GB SSD drives
  • DE5600 (2U, 24 disks): 2.5 "10,000 rpm SAS 1.2 TB / 900 GB / 600 GB; 800GB SSD drives
  • DE1600 (2U, 12 disks): 7200 rpm SAS 4/3/2 TB
