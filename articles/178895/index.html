Helvetica World, Competition and Event for Designers

On May 9, 1957, Max Midinger gave the world the font Helvetica, which is still popular and is not going to give up its position, neither among consumers nor designers. About the event:

About contest

Anyone can take part in the competition regardless of the city and country of residence.
To participate, it is enough to create a design, on a free theme, in any style from minimalism and photography, to modern and 3D. The key rule is the use of fonts and combinations in operation: Helvetica, Akzidens Grotesk and Neue Haas Grotesk
The work must be sent to contest@helveticaworld.com before May 8, inclusive, by submitting it as an A3 or A4 poster.
