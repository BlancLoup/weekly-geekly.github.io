Check your nickname in popular services

I found a service as simple as 5 kopecks: www.usernamecheck.com .
As the name implies, it checks any nickname for employment on popular gourmet sites (Digg, Livejournal, Linkedin, Picasa and the list goes on).

It can be useful in cases when you need to check your nickname for registration or just to be glad about how many places you have left to trace. Enjoy
