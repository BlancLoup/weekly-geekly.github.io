Development of mobile applications for business. Does business need a mobile app?


I would really like to answer this question and help small firms that are still in the process of finding the answer. In most cases, when I communicated with colleagues on the shop floor (mobile application development), I always asked them - And why did this company order a mobile application from you? - because I really did not understand why to spend such money?



In 90% of cases of our communication, the answers were as follows:
To increase loyalty and image for the company, other companies do not have such applications, etc.
It became interesting enough for me, because whatever tools were in business, everything must be done to increase sales. It sounds rather rude, but I'm talking a little about something else. A good quality product also boosts sales (a dress), and not just scripts and sales experience.
