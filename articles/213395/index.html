Announcement! DevCon 2014 becomes an international conference


Hi, dear software developers and testers. We continue to work on our DevCon 2014 conference and today we want to share the next details about its format.

We are pleased to inform you that since 2014, the most resort and most interesting DevCon conference has also become international! This status means that DevCon goes beyond the borders of Russia and the Russian-speaking audience and extends its influence further to the whole interested world.

Let's look at which of the features of DevCon 2014 help make the conference international.

International track reports


Becoming an international conference, DevCon 2014 should offer relevant content in English. And we are pleased to inform you that within the framework of the conference, an international English-language track of reports has been formed with all the other tracks that the other tracks have: recording each report and broadcasting live performances to the whole world. At the same time, we will organize simultaneous video broadcast in two languages: original English and with a live translation into Russian.

Of course, the remarkable opening of the conference, the plenary report, will also be broadcast in two languages, with translation into English for our international audience.

The international track of the reports will not only allow world experts on a variety of technologies to speak, but also build a unified history of the latest trends, announcements, and Microsoft technologies for software developers.

International speakers


To organize an interesting conference for the whole world, it is necessary to invite world-class experts, well-known speakers and specialists to the community. In further news posts we will reveal details about each of the speakers, and today I would like to inform you that at DevCon 2014, such experts as:

Larry Lieberman - the lead product manager on the Windows Phone team will visit us after the Build conference and give a number of presentations on the latest innovations in the mobile platform. Larry is an experienced speaker and is already familiar to the Russian public from speeches at our conferences, as well as at the largest Build and TechEd conferences.

Announcement! Tim Heuer is the lead product manager for the Windows team who develops a platform and tools for Windows developers. Tim is a software development veteran, an active speaker, a participant in many conferences, including Build and TechEd. Tim will come to us with reports and the latest news from the Build conference.

Announcement! Andrew Bragdon is a product manager for the Visual Studio Ultimate team that is currently working on the newest Application Insights tool. At DevCon 2014, Andrew will share his experience, the main news and the most important details of the latest and most interesting products from the Visual Studio line.

Announcement! Alexey Khalyako is a Microsoft Program Manager with more than 13 years of experience. He has been an employee of the Customer Advisory Team for the last 5 years, which focuses on building complex corporate solutions in such areas as well-known European telecommunications companies, air carriers and other industries. At DevCon 2014, Alexey will share with the audience his experience in developing complex corporate solutions based on the latest Microsoft server tools, such as SQL Server 2014 and the Windows Azure cloud platform.

Under these links, you can find the recorded presentations of these experts and get acquainted with their past performances. Soon we will share in more detail about each of the speakers of the international track, share the details of their performances. Follow the news!

International community


To consolidate the status of an international conference, we decided to invite not only world-class experts, but also representatives of the international developer community to take part in the conference.

We are very pleased to announce that dozens of European experts in various fields and technologies who wished to take part in DevCon 2014 as speakers have responded to our call to participate in the conference. We have yet to make a difficult choice, but soon we will introduce you to specific members of the community who will take part in the international track of the conference.

In addition, as part of the promotion of the conference, using our channels, we distribute tickets to the conference among the European developer community and expect the participation of a number of colleagues in the conference as regular participants. So do not be surprised that at DevCon 2014 you can meet guys from abroad who do not speak Russian. We are building DevCon 2014 also as a place where a community from different countries of the world can come together for communication. Get ready to share your experiences with your English-speaking colleagues.

Conference site and promotion


We have already started, and soon we will significantly increase the promotion of the DevCon brand in the international arena. Our goal is to declare DevCon 2014 as an important international event with excellent content and uniting the technical audience of many countries in Europe and around the world.

Of course, in order to promote the conference and broadcast its reports to an English-speaking audience, we need an entry point in English - the official international website. And it was prepared by us and is already working at http://www.msdevcon.ru/en/ .

Thanks to this site, we plan to carry out ambitious plans to broadcast the conference content to tens of thousands of people who will connect to the conference online during both days of its holding.

Welcome to the site! Study, register and get acquainted with the details of DevCon 2014! The site will be constantly updated.

See you at DevCon 2014!


We work hard to make the next DevCon 2014 even better than the previous ones. Our team prepares for you an excellent technical and entertainment program. We have chosen the perfect venue for the conference. And in the list of our speakers , the most interesting experts with unique experience and content have already been announced. And it will be updated weekly with new faces, so stay tuned!

Last year, tickets for our holiday ended three months before the start. So do not waste time and register today !

See you at DevCon 2014 - the first international - in May of this year!
