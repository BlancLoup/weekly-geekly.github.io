Productivity Test by Seth Godin

Seth Godin, with his usual bluntness, created a semblance of a test for an office worker, in order to reveal whether there is room for increasing the productivity of the latter. And added that they say, you can always find someone who will teach, if that.

Can you take a screenshot of the screen and paste it into Word or PowerPoint?
Do you have a blog?
Do you know how to follow the link that you received in the email?
Do you read more than five blogs per day?
Do you have a signature template in email?
Do you use Rss-reader?
Can you convert a Word document to .pdf?
Can you create a Google document and share access to it?
Do you have a combination of quick access to the addresses of six employees whom you most often write to?
Often you can find what you are looking for in Google?
Do you know how to download files from the Internet?
You use backup?
Do you have electronic contact tracking?
Do you use antivirus?
Are you addicted to chained forwarding of "jokes" that are being sent to you by email?
Have you ever bought something that was advertised in spam?
