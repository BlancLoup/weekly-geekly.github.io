Creating a CMS, suitable for search engine optimization.

Unfortunately, almost every time I undertake to promote a site on a CMS (more often it is critical for self-written, paid systems of various under- design studios) I face the problem of its unsuitability for initial optimization. And, unfortunately, errors are absolutely simple, such as the inability to specify meta tags or duplicate header (h1) with a title. As the name implies, focus your attention on the basics and not very much in the field of SEO, I will not, just write a few mandatory things that are really necessary for future promotion.

The main errors of various CMS:
  • The inability to set your meta tags.
    Among SEOs, there is a perception that the role of meta tags has moved to the second (or further) plan. This is partly true, you can do without them, but this will be more likely a mistake. First of all, it is easier for SERP to create a snippet using descriptions. Secondly, though not significant weight from keywords and descriptions is transferred, checked personally on a small experience (I can‚Äôt say 100%, only 6 sites were involved in the experience).
  • Duplicate H1 header (or any other) directly to title
    There is a dual error, the search engine may impose a pessimization for search spam (we will call it that way), and the second mistake is that the names can often be the same and / or not much different. Copying information in any form will never benefit.
  • Forming a title like {nodename | sitename}
    In continuation of work with the title of the page, I would like to remind you that search engines perceive far from the entire title / title / meta tag, cutting off its surplus. This number varies for different PS, but 70 characters will fit just right. So it is worth reconsidering the formation of the title in full.

Conclusions and solutions:
In the field with the creation of the document you just need to add fields such as: keywords, descriptions and page title.

Glory to programmers that other errors are much less common, but, of course, there are a number of suggestions that would also help speed up the optimization process.
