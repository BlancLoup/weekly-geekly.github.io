8 rules of innovation from Tyler

No one wants to produce bored goods all day long in an aimless race for a nonexistent consumer. Well, it goes without saying that you personally do not want this, do you?
So why do we think that breaking the routine and starting to do something fundamentally new is so difficult? Because it is really difficult: we are required to change priorities, change our plans and destroy a comfortable life. It is almost the same as becoming a different person.

"Fight Club" - a film about how to live the life you want, and what a thorny path you need to go to make it possible. Tyler helps the nameless hero of the film take the path of enlightenment. Perhaps Tyler's words will be able to help some of us.
