Mixcloud - mixes in the cloud

I want to share a review of the service I have been waiting for.
Service to listen to music - Mixcloud .


Key benefits:
  1. More than 7,500 quality mixes and their number is growing.
  2. Adequate rating system - the number of likes and listeners online
  3. Registration is optional , only for nice bonuses
  4. Clear interface and application for iPhone
  5. Excellent policy towards DJs - the key to the success of the service

I like to listen to mixes, since online radio does not guarantee that what I would like to play now, VKontakte lists are fragmented tracks of different quality, and last.fm is no longer a cake.

So, the main Mixcloud page for an unauthorized user:


Right from this page, by clicking on the green arrow next to the name of the mix, you can start listening. In the upper right corner shows the number of listeners at the moment.

By the way, in the screenshot mix from the leading developer of last.fm.

Two important things about this service:
  1. Digging a DJ (and getting alerts about his new mixes)
  2. Add a like mix (and add it to your favorite list)


The same search on the site, which all users want, but implement a few.

By the way, besides the basic 24 categories on Mixcloud, tags are actively used, thanks to which you can find mixes with your favorite style or subdirection.

The service is very caring not only for listeners, but also for DJs (no restrictions, free of charge, import from other popular services). Competitions are regularly held between performers . Thanks to this mood, the best artists join Mixcloud.


I use Mixcloud every day for many months and even tied it to my Facebook account. In my opinion, this is a service with revolutionary openness and I would like others to try and appreciate it.
