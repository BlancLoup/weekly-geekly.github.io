Participation in the WebsiteSpark program is completely free.

The Microsoft WebsiteSpark program allows web developers and web designers to obtain modern development tools from Microsoft, as well as to obtain licenses for the development, testing and publication of works.

Now the program has become completely free - the mandatory fee after three years of participation in the program has been canceled.

Due to the high interest in the WebsiteSpark program from web developers (in Russia, about 1,200 small web studios and freelancers are already enjoying the benefits of the program), Microsoft decided to cancel the one-hundred dollar contribution that companies should have pay after three years of participation in the program.

Participants already registered in the program under the old conditions will also not have to pay anything - the fee is canceled for everyone.
