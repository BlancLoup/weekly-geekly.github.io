PocketBook A10: geek reader

Leaving much to be desired

Epic fail happened on the second day of use. Before him, he spontaneously rebooted a couple of times. But I forgave: Android, Taiwan, stress test ...

And here evening, dinner, I peacefully read PDF. Suddenly the screen goes blank. And that's all. Brick. It does not react to charging, or to attempts to turn it on. Reset micro buttons are not provided. It's scary to take off the back cover: it sits firmly, but it will not take the broken one back.

They did it for me at the service center, advising me to "pick up a plastic card." The master solved the reboot problem by inserting and pulling out the battery. The device came to life. Already in the process of writing the article, it turned out that the reset can be started by holding the power key for more than 15 seconds. Neither in the brief nor in the expanded manuals does not say this.

The next day, he decided to reboot while trying to turn on Wi-Fi by clicking on the corresponding widget. However, after a couple of such sabotage, he took up his mind and so far has not rioted.
