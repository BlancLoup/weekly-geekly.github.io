Opening 9 virtual offices



Our country is huge and immense, and even such a giant as IBM is not able to cover all major cities and rapidly developing regions of Russia with its representative offices. But there is such a need - almost every corner of the country produces unique goods in its own way or extracts what is not available in other territories, which means that there is a business everywhere that is interested in optimization methods offered by IBM.

For this reason, we decided to open virtual offices in nine cities of Russia. Until now, local offices were located only in the largest cities: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Kazan, Rostov, Samara, Yekaterinburg, Novosibirsk and Krasnoyarsk.

Now, anyone can get basic information about the services provided by IBM in the regions, get information about new offers and initiatives of the company, as well as consult with a specialist on a specific issue. Virtual offices located in Ufa, Chelyabinsk, Irkutsk, Nizhny Novgorod, Krasnodar, Vladivostok, Omsk and Khabarovsk will allow us to communicate without obstacles, moving to a reasonable business in any, even the most remote, corner of the country.

Our experience in opening the first virtual offices suggests that the person who contacts the regional office, it is important that his problem was considered at the highest level - the capital team from the Moscow office of IBM. Therefore, in all regions, customers can be sure that they will be provided with full information on any subject of interest at the technical and organizational level of Moscow.
