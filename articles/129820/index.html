Free QtQuick and QtComponents trainings from FRUCT and Nokia

Hello!

Not so long ago, the FRUCT university collaboration community, with our support, conducted a three-day Qt training course for novice programmers in Moscow. It was an interesting experience for both us and the developers who participated, so we decided to repeat the Qt-trainings. This time they will be devoted to QtQuick and QtComponents, and will be held not only in Moscow, but also in St. Petersburg and Yaroslavl. Like last time, it will be free three-day classes.

Under Habrakat you will find a detailed description of the new training, useful information for taking part in it, as well as videos from the previous Qt-training .

The new series of trainings will focus on the principles of design, graphic elements in applications and animation, the creation of simple interfaces. Work with multimedia files, components for working with GPS and network interfaces will be described. During the training, all new knowledge will be examined with examples: hello world, business card editor, RSS reader, simple media player, browser, and several other applications.

After passing this three-day training, you will get all the necessary information and, most importantly, practical application development skills using QtQuick technologies, including QML.
