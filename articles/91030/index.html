Google follow finder

This morning, Google announced the launch of a new experimental service Google Follow Finder . The service is a simple search for interesting users of the popular Twitter service.


After searching, a button for quickly adding a user to your list appears next to each result. Follow Finder also searches among those who are subscribed to by users who have similar message content to you.
While using the service, I received many interesting messages from users who were not on my list before. I think that the service will be useful for many.
