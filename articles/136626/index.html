Collect donations with donateMe (Part II)

In the previous post, I began my story about another service from PaySto - donateMe , a donation service on the Internet. Let me remind you that this system works extremely simply, and the developers emphasize the convenience and ease of use by all participants in the process.


DonateMe service is easy to integrate into websites and web pages, and through it, it is also easy to make payments to those who want to do a good deed.


Donations with the help of PaySto can be collected both by individuals (for the realization of a dream or a plan), and groups - organizations or teams (for holding actions, organized help to the needy, launching projects). In the first case, donateMe is used, in the second, the ContributeWork variant.

Those who plan to raise funds, choose a method of receiving money from the proposed PaySto. Among them are bank transfers (both to a current account and to a personal account, as well as to a bank card) and money transfers within the borders of Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan and Moldova (through the Unistream or CONTACT system). Finally, charitable contributions can be received in the form of electronic money - WebMoney, MoneyMail, RBK.Money and Yandex.Money.

Again, the convenience and advantage of using donateMe / ContributeWork is that regardless of the currency in which the donation was made, the amount is withdrawn from PaySto in the currency that is convenient for the recipient. The amount of funds receivable in this case is not limited.

To use the services you need to register with PaySto and create your personal account. It will store all data on transactions that a user can view at any time, only having access to the Internet. Thanks to constant updates of information in the system, control over transfers can be implemented in real time.

If the user / organization collecting donations has a website, the form of depositing funds is integrated directly into it, in a prominent place. To do this, the code of the donation form is inserted into the site code, which is generated in the PaySto system. Those who do not have their own sites can share with those who wish to donate a link to a specially generated page that has only a form for depositing money. These pages on the Internet have their own short addresses - donateMe.net/ <code> or funding.to/ <code> (depending on the type of collection - individual or group).


The actual process of the implementation of the contribution is as follows: the payer who wants to make a donation, follows the link to the form of payment. There he selects the amount of the donation and the method of payment. When the money goes into the PaySto system, the payer is automatically sent again to the collector‚Äôs page. The latter receives from PaySto a notification about the input of donated funds and can withdraw them from the system at any time and in any way convenient to him.


Regarding the cost of using the donateMe / ContributeWork service, PaySto offers the following conditions to its customers. Registration in the system does not cost anything. The minimum transfer amount is 1 ruble, the upper limit of subsidies does not exist. The client pays the withdrawal of funds from the system (100 rubles), the commission for using the service (up to 7.5% of the payment amount) and currency exchange in the system at its internal rate (if necessary).

Obviously, working with these services from PaySto will greatly simplify the collection of donations on the Internet, because it will be implemented in a single system, and the client receiving charitable contributions will not need to use several ways to transfer funds (bank accounts, cards, settlement accounts, etc.) ). Having set a certain account for receiving financial injections, where funds will be transferred from the system, the PaySto client will only control the process of their transfer.
