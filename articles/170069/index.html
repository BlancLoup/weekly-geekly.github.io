HTC One - Impressions from London

So, HTC, finally, showed its new smart. It is called, contrary to all rumors, not M7 (it would be really strange), but One - without any prefixes and postfixes. Here is a "one of a kind."



It seems to many (and we are not an exception in NOMOBILE) that lately there are no highlights in HTC gadgets. It seems that everything is within the framework of trends: gigahertz, permissions, core ... But there was nothing like that, why I would like to buy HTC for myself. Model One seems to have broken stereotypes.



First, no matter what anyone says, the design really turned out to be fresh, although recognizable. They did not go after super-thinness, but they made a long-proven move - the edges of the smartphone turned out to be rather thin - only 4 mm. And in the ‚Äúthickest‚Äù part of the gadget is 9.3 mm.

Regarding iron rumors are fully confirmed. The Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 chipset was chosen as a hardware platform, which includes 4 cores operating at 1.7 GHz, as well as a couple of gigabytes of RAM and 32 GB of main memory. Apparently, there is no expansion slot, but most likely the epic with 20 GB of free space on Dropbox will continue.



It follows from the above that the device (well, or rather, Snapdragon 600) supports ALL new wireless standards. Naturally, there is a 3G module, but along with it is LTE, and the frequencies that are used in Russia are also supported. In addition, the smartphone can work with new Wi-Fi routers that support the new IEEE 802.11 ac. In addition to this, Bluetooth fourth version and NFC are worth noting.

Now about the screen. Looks like a record! 468 dots per inch ... I will not say that it will be very useful to the user, but no one will stick to the quality of the picture. On the diagonal of 4.7 inches fits the resolution of 1920x1080.

But the main features of the device are cool sound and camera. Especially for this business even came up with a new brand Boomsound. The user is a bit confused by this name, but in practice it resulted in two powerful speakers on the front panel. An amplifier is also present. Well, Beats Audio with all these equalizers has not gone anywhere. And yet declared noise reduction system Sense Voice.
