ePayService + eBayToday = Free Mail Forwading

It is known to any habrayuzer that the prices and assortment of various gadgets and good clothes in Western online stores are mostly much better than in their native spaces. Therefore, such names as eBay, Amazon, Zappos no longer seem alien to us. And this is good, because now there is a reason to get acquainted with online shopping even closer - all users of the ePayService payment system have the opportunity to take advantage of a special offer from the online shopping service eBayToday: free Mail Forwading.

Does the seller refuse to send parcels to your country? Not a problem, the guys from eBayToday know how to fix this.

Mail Forwarding is your virtual address abroad.
