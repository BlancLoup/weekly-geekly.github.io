Duracell and Powermat show off new charging system



Not so long ago, Duracell and Powermat formed the Duracell Powermat alliance, the goal of which is to develop wireless chargers (how to make such a device yourself - here ). The results of this alliance are already visible - this is a new charging system that combines several useful products for the buyer.

First, the charger itself is included. Secondly, there is a case for the iPhone, thanks to which it is possible to charge this device through wireless charging from the alliance. And thirdly, the kit also includes an additional battery, a battery, with microUSB and Apple connectors.
