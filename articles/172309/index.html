BAT: a wireless flying mouse that can prevent carpal tunnel syndrome



Omsk designer Vadim Kibardin ( Kibardin Design ) has developed a flying BAT wireless mouse for people who spend most of their lives at the computer. Not only does this mouse hang in the air, it also helps to cure a neurological disease , manifested by prolonged pain in the fingers.



The invention consists of a rug and a mouse with a floating ring magnet.



The gadget has a stylish design and two different colors: black and white.



The mouse hovers at a height of 40 mm by itself or at a height of 10 mm under the weight of the arm.



Development is still at the testing stage, so it is not yet available for sale.
