LG G Watch and Samsung Gear Live smart watches are available for pre-order

Today on MobileFun , the first smart watches on the new Android Wear platform, namely the LG G Watch, have become available for pre-order.

Advertise the platform Android Wear

The price of the LG G Watch is $ 254.99, the estimated shipping date is set for July 7.
It is expected that the official release will be announced on Google I / O 2014, which will begin in a few hours.
So devices have become available for ordering on Google Play.
