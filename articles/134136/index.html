10 billion downloads from the Android Market and super-cheap games

Last weekend was marked for the Android mobile platform by another achievement - the number of downloads of applications from their Android Market reached 10 billion, and the growth in the number of downloads added 1 billion a month during 2011. Probably, the search giant decided not to wait for the end of the year and started celebrating right now.



In this regard, Google offers a number of popular games from its store at a price of 10 cents, and this list will be expanded during all holidays. The top ten looks like this: Asphalt 6 HD , Color & Draw for Kids , Endomondo Sports Tracker Pro , Fieldrunners HD , Great Little War Game , Minecraft , Paper Camera , Sketchbook Mobile , Soundhound Infinity , SwiftKey X.


UPD: follow the rest of the proposals from Google is offered here - I found this news in the official blog of Android Developers. The zee browser also offers this page.
