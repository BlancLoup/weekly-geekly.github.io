Intensive announcement on ASP.NET Core at the Microsoft DevCon 2016 conference

I am pleased to announce the holding of an intensive topic on ASP.NET Core at the Microsoft DevCon 2016 conference, which will be held May 25-26 in the Moscow region.


Intensives are a new format at the conference that will offer participants a full day of deep immersion in a specific topic with mandatory practical participation.

We are very happy to announce the speakers and organizers of the ASP.NET Intensive: John Galloway - senior technology evangelist, Microsoft USA, Sergey Pugachev - technical solutions consultant, Google, Igor Sychev - prime engineer of development support, Microsoft Russia, Vladimir Yunev - senior technology evangelist , Microsoft Russia.

More information about the content of the intensive below.

The ASP.NET Core Intensive format includes several sections, each of which contains a deep theoretical part with demonstrations and practical experience of the participants in the form of laboratory work or a separate task under the guidance.

Intensive ASP.NET consists of the following sections:

Immersion in the interior of the platform ASP.NET Core (Level 300)

The first section is entirely devoted to the internal structure of the new version of ASP.NET Core 1.0, new routing mechanisms, work with views, security, modularity of the framework, request processing pipeline, work with databases, tools.

The speaker and head of the first section is our guest from Redmond - John Galloway.

Integration of ASP.NET Core and AngularJS 2.0, as well as work on OSX, Linux platforms (Level 300)

The second section will focus on the integration of ASP.NET Core and Google's AngularJS. As well as working with the platform on OSX / Linux operating systems using Visual Studio Code.

The second part of the intensive will be held by a specially invited star - Google employee - Sergey Pugachev , who has deep expertise in application development and is an experienced speaker and regular participant in DevCon.

Continuous deployment of ASP.NET applications: local (Linux and Windows), in the cloud, in containers (Level 300)

The third intensive section will focus entirely on the deployment of ASP.NET Core applications in a variety of environments, including Linux and Windows locally, in the cloud, based on Docker containers and using the continuous deployment process through Visual Studio Team Services.

The main speaker of the third section is Igor Sychev , an experienced Microsoft engineer who helps Microsoft partners and customers around the world create and maintain complex applications.

Preparation for intensive


We look forward to intensively trained participants who are already familiar with ASP.NET and .NET technologies and want to gain in-depth knowledge about the new ASP.NET Core.

DevCon 2016

We are conducting it for the sixth time and traditionally try to gather those who are not indifferent to the Microsoft platform outside the city limits, away from the city bustle, in order to discuss current technologies together and try out the latest versions of products in practice.
