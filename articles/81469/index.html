The number of downloads Tetris exceeded 100 million

More than 25 years after its creation, Tetris remains the most popular mobile game in history. The brilliant Russian invention continues to conquer the world, and recently the number of mobile downloads of Tetris exceeded 100 million (meaning only paid downloads, for which royalties were paid to copyright holders).

Currently, the rights to sell the most successful game belong to the corporation Electronic Arts. She received a license from Blue Planet Software. Today, these two companies are planning to organize a solemn event in Montreal (Canada), dedicated to the anniversary.

EA does not report what the total revenue came from the game. For example, for iPhone, it is sold for $ 5.

As you know, Tetris was coined by the Soviet programmer Alexey Pajitnov in 1984, and popularity came to him on the platform of the Nintendo Game Boy in 1989. After that, Tetris came to all platforms and is popular among all age groups and cultures.
