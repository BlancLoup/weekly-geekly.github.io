1. My motorcycle. Why exactly off-road?


Motorcycles are a passion and a dream of a fairly large number of people, so I will not dwell on why motorcycles became my current hobby. But why off-road motorcycles (enduro)? For myself, I noted a number of advantages over other types of motorcycles:
  • The first point I note that all motorcycles are cool :) in their own way. All classes have at the same time ... m ... hard :). After all, there are always various restrictions: money, garage footage. But there are more or less universal bikes - enduro, supermotors! The choice of a reasonable egoist.
  • You can not ride on asphalt (and I'm very afraid to smudge on it :)).
  • Much more diverse set of tricks that you can perform.
  • Falls are more or less harmless, more mistakes are forgiven. But do not bump into trees, crush mushroom pickers and tumble off rocks.
  • You can spend a lot of time in nature - beaches, forests, fields ...
  • It is easier to look at the device and design, less whimsical to care (at first glance).
  • You can ride enduro all year round and not close the season.
  • Universality - asphalt, curbs, steps, primer, sand, dirt ... in the winter - ice and snow. Total: one bike for a hundred different adventures.


And, of course, cons:
  1. Not all girls like it :)
  2. Does not fly, does not float (almost)
