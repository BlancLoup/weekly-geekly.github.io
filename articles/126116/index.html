inTaxi from VentureBrothers - order a taxi in the modern world

Hello.

We are a young company that is very interested in doing what makes our daily life better. We are delighted when we see services and services appear, which it was impossible to even think about three or five or ten years ago.

At the end of 2010, we gathered around the idea to do this everyday change: to make a real service for taxi passengers, to give people the opportunity to order a taxi without unnecessary movements, quickly and comfortably, and be confident in the quality of services provided.

Russian taxi. Private traders and taxi companies.


Taxi problems in Russia (although in the first place, I speak, of course, about Moscow) are so well known, perhaps, to everyone, that they can be simply listed without going into argument.
  • The feed rate of the machine and the guarantee that it will generally be filed.
  • Comfort and safety of the car, the adequacy of the driver.
  • Difficult and often expensive fares.
  • Already not always, but often a low level of automation and comfort in providing services: it happens that you have to hang on your phone for more than 10 minutes until you can finally place an order.

Our product, as we decided, should, first of all, strive to solve these problems through technology. We have to automate everything that can be automated. Protect the passenger where he can be protected. Save all participants in the process from unnecessary actions. But the main thing - to transfer control into the hands of the passenger. To provide him with all the information, to give him a choice, to serve him in time in the process of transportation.

Perhaps the first dilemma that has arisen is with whom to work, who will carry passengers. The inner idealist told us: ‚ÄúWe need to reduce drivers and passengers. Now is the age of social networks, peer-to-peer technologies, total mobilization and continuous barter. Hooray!". Distribute to each driver and passenger according to the application and withdraw.

Unfortunately, idealism does not work. The passenger service itself is so nontrivial that there are special organizations, most of whose activities are reduced to the management of taxi drivers, drivers, cars. Problems of quality control, safety, comfort, logistics, tariffs, mutual settlements, all these organizations assume. The name of them - taxis.

When we realized that, firstly, we didn‚Äôt want to do this, and secondly, that there are already people who can do it perfectly, we decided: let the taxi stations take up transportation, and we will deal with passengers. We will look for them, help them make a choice, it is convenient to report on the process of order execution. We will control taxis in terms of quality of service. We will develop a competitive environment, thus reducing the cost of travel. And most importantly, we will form a social environment in which taxi stations and passengers will be able to influence each other.

We work with legal carriers, and we focus on taxi companies that manage their fleet: they monitor the cleanliness of cars and the quality of drivers, and take responsibility for passengers.

inTaxi - our service and our products


The inTaxi system works as follows:
  1. The passenger places an order: indicates where and where he wants to go, what requirements and wishes he has and when he would like to do it.
  2. The order appears in the systems of taxis
  3. Taxis, interested in the implementation of this order make their proposals: indicate the tariff (or a fixed price) and the time in which they can submit the car.
  4. The passenger for a limited period of time (now it is 5 minutes), examines the received offers and selects the one he likes most. This taxi company becomes the full owner of the order.
  5. At a certain point in time, a taxi company assigns a specific vehicle to fulfill an order. The passenger receives information about his model and color, license plate, telephone number and driver's name (for special cases).
  6. When the car arrives at the starting point, the passenger is notified of this. We also remind him of the free time limit.
  7. Upon completion of the trip, the passenger is informed of the exact cost of the trip.
