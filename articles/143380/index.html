Kerbal Space Program

Recall that there are games that not only take time, but also teach something useful, and make it interesting! Kerbal Space Program is a game whose goal is to design a rocket yourself and successfully launch it!

An excellent spacecraft editor allows you to appreciate the advanced physical model of the game, you also need to monitor the level of fuel, and engine temperature. In the end, after a few trial and error, you can still escape beyond the atmosphere, but what about going into orbit or flying to the moon?
