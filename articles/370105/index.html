Dynakit (Dynaco ST-70) - DIY audio constructor


This revolutionary product, for its time, was developed by American sound engineer David Hafler. In 1954, Huffler, with another famous hi-fi father, designer Ed Laurent, founded the company Dynaco in Philadelphia. By 1959, the serial production of the ST 70 tube amplifier was set up, which, in a cheaper version (‚ÄúDynakit‚Äù), was intended for self-assembly.



The uncomplicated and efficient circuit design of the audiophile designer assumed the use of four EL34 lamps, one GZ34 / 5AR4 and two 7199.
