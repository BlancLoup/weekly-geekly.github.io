German telecom operators are the most profitable

The total revenue of telecom operators in the world in 2006 will amount to $ 1.4 trillion, and capital expenditures will reach $ 225 billion. This is reported in the report of the analytical company Ovum. Last year, according to various analytical data, the global income of telecom operators amounted to about $ 1.2 trillion, capital expenditures - about $ 200 billion, reports PRIME-TASS .

In addition, the Ovum report provides projections of which global operators will be the largest in terms of revenue:
  • Deutsche Telecom (Germany) - $ 93 billion
    Verizon (US) - $ 90 billion.
    AT & T (USA) - $ 80 billion
    NTT (Japan) - $ 77 billion
    Vodafone (UK) - $ 68 billion
    France Telecom (France) - $ 64 billion
    Telefonica (Spain) - $ 60 billion
    Sprint Nextel (USA) - $ 41 billion
    BellSouth (USA) - $ 39 billion
    Telecom Italia (Italy) - $ 38 billion

    In Russia, for the current year, the aggregate income of operators from telecommunication services, according to preliminary data, will amount to 745.6 billion rubles (about $ 28 billion).
