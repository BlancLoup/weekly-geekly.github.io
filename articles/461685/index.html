LED therapy is a slightly different story. The history of the study has been going on for more than half a century, and during this time, low-level LED therapy received essentially two uses, depending on the wavelength.

The most effective waves are 830 nm, which are recommended today for the active treatment of inflammatory processes and pain relief. In particular, there are a number of studies that confirm that with LED therapy of the near infrared range there is a significant reduction in pain in professional athletes. A similar effect is confirmed by comparative placebo tests.

Effective pain management attracted increased attention to light therapy, and therefore studies continued: some of them confirmed the hypothesis that LED therapy can be used not only to relieve symptoms and pain, but also to regenerate cells and bones.

The regeneration effect was confirmed in animal tests:


Changes in the inflamed cells associated with dermatitis after irradiation with 830 nm LEDs in rats (A: control sample, B: sample irradiated with LED). A marked decrease in inflammatory infiltration.

And when working with people:


Improvement of the patient's condition (24-year-old woman) with post-chemical contact dermatitis resistant to treatment, causing skin irritation, noted above at the beginning of the study and less than 10 days after the LED sessions with a wavelength of 830 nm, with an interval of 3 days 20 minutes per session .

Such tests and experiments quite unequivocally lead the authors of review articles to conclusions like this:

Currently, published literature strongly recommends 830 nm for all aspects of wound healing, pain, anti-inflammatory treatment and skin rejuvenation, with a combination of 415 nm and 633 nm for the treatment of only mild active inflammation of acne vulgaris. If the wavelength is incorrect, optimal absorption will not occur, and, as the first law of photobiology says, the Grott-Draper law, there can be no reaction without absorption.

Actually, the time has just come to say that size matters . And if 830 nm waves look like a panacea in most cases, then 630 ¬± waves (on the basis of which LED Magic works) is just more about cosmetology and beauty.


Penetration of waves of different lengths

The effectiveness of 630 nm waves was also confirmed during healing, pain relief, but in combination with 830 nm waves. Separately, today they are used both in clinical practice and in home cosmetology as a means against acne , superficial skin cleansing , smoothing wrinkles.



A wavelength of 630 nm in a number of articles is called a safe strategy for enhancing collagen, which is just directed against skin aging, increasing elasticity / elasticity, etc.
