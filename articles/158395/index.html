Building partner networks based on BILLmanager



Developing a successful, ever-growing business is impossible without interaction with companies operating in areas related to your company, without developing partnerships. Such relationships allow companies to combine their strongest sides and direct these forces to achieve maximum results in their activities. BILLmanager provides the opportunity to develop several options for partnerships. Among them - various referral and reseller programs.

Referral programs are a fairly cheap way to popularize your services with a company by attracting your own clients. It looks like this: a company that provides certain services gives its customers special referral links to their website or a set of promotional codes. Customers of this company distribute the received links and promo codes among their customers, readers, friends and acquaintances in various ways - be it mail, a link to a website, a banner, social networks or something else.

For example: a certain online music and film store sells media content. And the owners of this store offer thematic blogs, journalists, and other people associated with this topic (and even their own customers!) To participate in the store's referral program. These people are registered in the program and receive a special link, or a promotional code containing a unique identifier. Then they are required to make so that as many people as possible register with this link or code. They post them on their website, on various forums, blogs, send them to friends and acquaintances, etc., thus bringing new customers to the store.

Visitors registering on such links and codes can receive certain predetermined discounts during their first purchases. And those people whose links came from these clients will be charged a certain percentage of the money spent by the new client. Thus, the referral program is a mutually beneficial offer for a company seeking to popularize its products or services, as well as for current customers of this company who would like to receive bonuses and discounts, and perhaps even have additional profits by attracting new customers .

Sometimes your customers turn to the sales department asking about the possibility of renting a dedicated server.
