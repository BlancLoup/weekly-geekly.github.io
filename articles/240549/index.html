Barcelona tried to seize the domain in the .email zone

FC Barcelona has been refused a request to suspend the operation of the FCB.email domain as part of the URS procedure (quick suspension of the domain upon request of the copyright holder).

The representative of "Barcelona" said that the club has a logo with the letters FCB, which in 2004 was registered as an iconic trademark. In his opinion, the defendant, using a combination of signs "FCB", misleads many fans of "Barcelona", attracting them to your site. He demanded a suspension of the domain due to the illegal use of the club‚Äôs trademark.


The defendant objected that the FCB.email domain was created for fans of the German football team FC Bayern Monachium so that they can register their mailing addresses on it. Therefore, it could not have been created to extract commercial benefits from the use of the Barcelona brand.

Also, according to the URS rules, in order to suspend a domain on the basis of intellectual property rights, a claimant must be the owner of a verbal, not a graphic, trademark. Moreover, the abbreviation FCB itself is not a hallmark of any brand. For example, there is a Danish company FCB, registered in 1998, and several other organizations in other countries.

Therefore, the commission did not find sufficient grounds for suspending the FCB.email domain and decided to refuse FC Barcelona in its demand.
