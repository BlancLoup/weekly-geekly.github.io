Report on the First Habravstrebe in Almaty

Hello everyone, finally got to my laptop and you can write a report on our event. Habrabstrecha in Almaty was held for the first time and gathered more than 50 people !!!



Anyone who is interested to see how it happened we ask under the cat. (CAUTION TRAFFIC)

The meeting was held on March 29 at 19:00 in the semi-dark basement of the Cafeteria. More than 50 people attended, even though the people were constantly arriving. The meeting lasted about 3 hours.

We had 7 reports in the program.
  • Team Reporterr ( ratstraper ) - System for testing mobile applications
  • Andrey Andreev (Microsoft) - Windows Phone Future Platform
  • Timur Garifzyanov (Petersburg, video bridge) - WP7: Freedom in Motion
  • Rustem Bekmukhametov (KBTU) Etzoockee - Application Development for Windows Phone
  • Rahim Davletkaliev freetonik - Responsive Web Design and PhoneGap
  • Sergey Beer (Crystal spring) - Interface and distinctive features of the ATM Point application
  • Dmitry Nazarov - First Geosocial Service in Kazakhstan


There was an interesting discussion during the reports, since so many developers gathered whose programs are popular on various platforms.
