September 24, we invite you to the conference MIXAR 2016 - non-classical conference on the latest technologies


We invite you to take part in the conference MIXAR 2016 . Key topics this year: 3D, virtual and augmented reality, interactive technologies and their application in art, science and business This year the conference will welcome guests at the ZIL Cultural Center in Moscow on September 24, where throughout the day 3000 square meters will be available. m technology.

Free registration required.

MIXAR2016


MIXAR - at the moment it is no longer just a conference, it is an ecosystem of events dedicated to these technologies - it is a real community of VAMR (AR & VR specialists).

The purpose of the conference remains the same - the popularization of technology, but an even more important task is to explain how a multitude of technologies should be correctly used and implemented in our lives. That is why the theme of the conference was chosen PHYGITAL TRANFORMATION.

PHYGITAL TRANSFORMATION


What is Phygital? For example, these are practically all Microsoft products - which work at the interface of the digital and physical worlds. Kinect, Hololense, etc. The concept of Phygital unites a lot of areas of full-scale technology development and the transition of a person to a new stage of interaction with the environment, from entertainment and marketing to research and data visualization.

Development of AR & VR technologies in Russia


2016 has become an indicator of the pace of development of augmented and virtual reality technologies in Russia. The result of this development is noticeable not only to professional developers, the general awareness and knowledge of VAMR-industry issues has increased significantly in broad industries such as advertising, education, marketing and gamedev. The global trends and trends created by individual solutions using ARVR, as well as intra-Russian infrastructure activities, such as integrating vendors, large companies, start-ups and geeks into the professional community (VAMR.ru, ARVR in Gamedev), numerous events and common interest from side agencies and direct customers. For example, VAMR has more than 1000 companies engaged in solutions in the field of AR & VR

MIXAR2015


A year ago, the first non-classical conference on the latest technologies MIXAR2015 was held, which gathered in one day a record number of visitors in the field of augmented and virtual reality, as well as developers, start-ups, established companies and business guests. Microsoft is the general partner of this conference. Throughout the day, lectures and panel discussions were held in lecture halls devoted to art, science and business with the participation of Russian and foreign market representatives and related industries, as well as leaders and evangelists of well-known world companies.

MITAP


Throughout the year, after the organizers of MIXAR2015, a series of ecosystem mitaps was held, bringing together experts from the fields of architecture, the venture industry, and other areas. The events were held in the same relaxed and friendly atmosphere, gathering the best representatives of the market and novice developers, as well as customers and investors. In addition, during the same year, a series of workshops on ARVR development from the VAMR Academy was launched and conducted, designed for developers of different levels of knowledge. The goal of all events is to raise awareness and promote technologies related to virtual and augmented reality.

Haqaton


Also, with the general partnership of Microsoft and Skolkovo, as well as the organizers of MIXAR, the first hackathon on virtual reality in Russia was held, gathering about 40 teams. More than 30 teams reached the finals, some of them have already received investments for the development of their own products, designed on the hackathon.
