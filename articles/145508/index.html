How to simplify work with the printing device

Most KYOCERA MFPs are equipped with a color touch control panel. A menu is displayed on a wide screen with easy-to-read icons and precise descriptions of functions. The panel is designed in such a way that it is possible to adjust to individual working conditions and to obtain the optimal viewing angle under various lighting conditions.




Thanks to the platform on the basis of which the KYOCERA MFPs are developed, individual settings of the touch panel are possible. This platform is called HyPAS.
HyPAS is an abbreviation for the phrase ‚ÄúHybrid Platform for Advanced Solutions‚Äù. The platform is based on Java technology and Web Service. The HyPAS platform reduces complex multistage processes to a few keystrokes. Thanks to it, productivity increases, process of operation is simplified.

The HyPAS platform can be supplemented with any applications through Java technologies and a web service that will help maintain high employee productivity even during peak periods.

For example, these are applications specifically designed for the HyPAS platform: PanelPlus is a tool for quickly creating personalized scanning interfaces; KYOcapture to customize the distribution of workflow;

Language Select for fast switching of different language profiles; Fax Connect to optimize fax / scanner performance; and Color Control to control color printing and reduce costs.







The undoubted advantage of KYOCERA devices is the similarity (by default) of user interfaces. The control panels of KYOCERA devices are designed according to one scheme, and if the user is familiar with one KYOCERA device, then he will not have any difficulties in working with others.

Another feature is especially appreciated by network administrators. All KYOCERA devices are controlled using the same network tool for both printers and MFPs - KMnet Viewer.



KMnet Viewer is a free software company KYOCERA for managing network devices of small and medium-sized companies. Meter readings, status report, configuration for remote access, collection of statistics on all network devices in one place and ease of use are just some of the functions of such software.
