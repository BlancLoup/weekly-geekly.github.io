TDU2: massive open online races

Companies Atari and Eden Games officially presented the second part of the popular racing game Test Drive Unlimited. There is little detail yet, but it is already known that TDU2 will be the ideological heir to the first part of the undertakings and will be upgraded to MOOR - Massively Open Online Racing (mass open online races).



The main task of the developers is to create a single world for the players. And this means an instant invisible transition from singles races to competitions with other players, the ability to create teams and puzomerka achievements.

From the new-expected: an even more realistic behavior of the machines and, most importantly, an advanced damage system.

The project release is scheduled for autumn 2010. Test Drive Unlimited 2 will be published on PC, Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3.
