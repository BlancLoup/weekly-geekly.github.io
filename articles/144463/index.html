DigiTimes: Google will launch its tablet in June

Reputable resource DigiTimes reports that in June of this year, the search giant will present on the market a batch of 7-inch tablets in the amount of 600,000 pieces based on Android (whether this will be Android 5.0 is unclear). According to previously published information , Google will be engaged in the implementation of devices on their own, and their production is engaged in Asustek. Previously it was claimed that the tablet would be ready in May, however, the design and price of the devices did not meet expectations, and it was sent for revision.

In total, in 2012, Google plans to implement from two to two and a half million tablets. The price of the device is not reported, but we can assume that, given the serious competition in the market for 7-inch tablets from the Kindle Fire and rumors about the release of the iPad Mini also with a diagonal of 7 inches, the price of the tablet from Google will not exceed $ 200.
