Sony Xperia X smartphone is available for pre-order

Smartphone Sony Xperia X today has become available for pre-order. This is the first model in the new line, which also includes Xperia X Performance and Xperia XA. As a bonus for pre-ordering before the end of June, you will receive six free months of the Google Play Music service and twelve films from the Privilege Plus library.



Let me remind you that the built-in camera Xperia X has the technology of predictive hybrid autofocus, which predicts the movement of objects. Simply put, the device itself determines the trajectory of the displacement of a person, a car, an animal - anything that came into view. Sensor resolution in the main camera is 23 Mp. The front camera of 13 megapixels has a highly sensitive matrix (with ISO up to 6400 units), so you can not worry about the quality of video calls and selfies in poor light.

The filling of the smartphone consists of a 64-bit Qualcomm Snapdragon 650 processor, 3 GB of RAM and 32/64 GB of internal memory. There is also a slot for microSD cards up to 200 GB. The role of the operating system is Android 6.0. The battery capacity is 2620 mAh, which will be enough for two days of work without recharging. And thanks to technology Qualcomm Quick Charge 2.0 ten-minute charge will allow the smartphone to work for five and a half hours.

"Iron" is packaged in a case with a metal back panel and a glass front, the screen diagonal is exactly 5 inches, and the resolution is 1080x1920 pixels.

The cost of Sony Xperia X is 39 990 rubles in the partner stores Svyaznoy, Eldorado, MVideo, Know-How, Beeline, Megafon, as well as in the company's online store Store.Sony.Ru.
