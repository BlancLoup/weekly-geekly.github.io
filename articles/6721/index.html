Loser Zune will have younger brothers

The Zune player, which never became an iPod killer, this year will have two younger brothers - Zune 2.0, which, like its predecessor, will be created on the basis of a hard disk (though more), as well as a flash memory player - Flash Zune. The latter should, according to the developers, resemble the size of the iPod Nano, as well as have in its equipment a WiFi-module.

New devices will be manufactured at a factory in China (apparently, Microsoft decided to abandon the practice of purchasing players from Toshiba).
