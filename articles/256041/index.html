Do you remember how it all started

Intercomp was incorporated in the United States in 1994, its birthplace is Houston, Texas. At that time, the company's founders had been working in the post-Soviet space for four years and saw great potential in an actively developing market. Initially, the main activity was payroll. Shareholder expectations of high growth rates were confirmed in practice, after which all efforts were fully concentrated in the CIS. After 19 years, the business was bought out by the Russian investment fund Elbrus Capital , as already proven to be successful.

Initially, a small team of Intercomp developed rapidly and now has more than 750 specialists. Our units operate in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Ryazan, Nizhny Novgorod, Kiev, Astana, Almaty and Baku, but the main services are available remotely and in other cities of Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan and Azerbaijan.

Intercomp is considered to be the recognized Russian leader in the accounting function outsourcing market. Since the appearance in 2009 of the corresponding ranking of the Expert rating agency, our company has been steadily leading it.

In 2012, a subsidiary was created to provide services to the public sector. As you know, accounting in such organizations has significantly different specifics, so it was decided to divide the specialists into teams: commercial and budget.
