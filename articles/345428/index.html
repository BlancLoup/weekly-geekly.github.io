Six reasons to buy Hamster Marketplace tokens

Those who follow the cryptocurrency world know that in addition to the rapid growth of the Bitcoin rate, the recognition of cryptocurrencies in some countries and the general positive trend, the whole sphere is rather unstable. The sharp jumps in the Bitcoin exchange rate , speculation and the ever-changing moods of regulators make cryptocurrencies a risky investment. Slightly apart from the usual cryptocurrency, there are tokens - cryptocurrency, produced by specific projects for specific goals to attract primary funding. This tool, despite the large number of different crowdsales, is more attractive for long investments .



HMT Token is a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain, developed for the trading platform of unique gadgets and electronics Hamster Marketplace. Tokenization of the Hamster Marketplace, along with the use of the blockchain and AI elements, is one of the first and key elements of the future infrastructure of a self-managed decentralized marketplace. From the very beginning of the site, HMT tokens will be applied:

  • to pay for the service fee of the Hamster Marketplace platform;
  • for sales without commission platform;
  • to pay for internal promo on the Hamster Marketplace;
  • as a pledge to ensure compliance with all requirements for a verified vendor;
  • for discounts on purchases on the Hamster Marketplace platform when paying with tokens;
  • to access additional features of the Hamster Marketplace.

The total number of HMT tokens produced will be 20 million, of which 14 million will be released in January 2018 for open sale at $ 2 per coin, at the end of which the unsold and undistributed surplus tokens will be destroyed.

Users who have passed the personal identification procedure (KYC) will automatically be able to not only store, but also send and receive HMT tokens both on the hmstr.io web wallet and on their essential wallets. The HMT token will be added to the cryptobirth listings.

After launching the beta of the Hamster Marketplace trading platform, the manufacturers represented there will be able to pay for platform services with tokens along with other types of cryptocurrencies and Fiat, as well as receive discounts. The internal rate of the HMT token used in calculating the number of tokens for paying commissions, internal promo and accessing additional functions of the platform at the time of launching the public beta will be set at $ 10.

The market price of the HMT token at the time of the operation will be used to pay for the goods, receive discounts and as collateral. If the value of the token in the free market rises to 80% of the nominal value, the internal rate of the token on the Hamster Marketplace will be adjusted upwards. Thus, the owners of HMT tokens will be able to count on a discount on the services of the site at least 20% in comparison with any other payment methods.

HMT token owner privileges


1. Ability to pay Hamster Marketplace HMT tokens
