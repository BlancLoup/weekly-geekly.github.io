National Communications Regulatory Commission fights against illegal providers in Ukraine

The head of the National Communications Regulatory Commission, Vladimir Oleinik, believes that by the end of this year, there will be practically no illegal home networks and Internet providers left on the market.

As you can understand, even legal communication providers that work with violations can be excluded from the registry. In the case of continuing work on an illegal basis, such activity will be considered by the legislators as a criminal offense.

It is worth noting that companies do not need to obtain a license to work as an Internet provider.
