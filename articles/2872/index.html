OQO - Double 2



The site Engadget has provided a practical video review of the OQO Model 02 portable PC. Do not be confused by the name, which is a bit unusual for a Russian user, since the handicraft has an interesting set of characteristics. The equipment includes a processor with a clock speed of 1.5 GHz, 1 GB DDR2 RAM, 5-inch TFT screen, 60 GB hard drive, EV-DO, Bluetooth 2.0, HDMI, and 802.11b / g Wi-Fi.

The screen has the ability to work in resolution modes and 1000x600 and 1200x720. Another nice thing is that the OQO Model 02 can be freely connected both to a DVD burner and to a monitor. For this you can use any installed data transfer technologies. Also, the novelty has USB 2.0 and 3.5 mm input for standard headphones. Video inside.
