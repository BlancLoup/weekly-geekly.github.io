Acceleration factors


Google more than other search engines loves fast sites, especially on mobile devices. The behavioral factors of the visitor, in particular, the bounce rate depends on the speed of loading the page. The key to success of SEO is an optimized server, and if you have not optimized it, your film will be black and white.
