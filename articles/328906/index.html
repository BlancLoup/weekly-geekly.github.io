Lua in Moscow for the first time at DevConf 2017


We invite you to a meeting with presentations of Lua in Moscow on June 17th at the DEVCONF conference of developer communities .

At the mitap, you will have a chance to listen to reports on the design and application of Lua and LuaJIT and to exchange experience with community members.

Lua is a unique programming language that is used not only in the computer games industry, but also as an embedded extension language in web-based programming tools such as Redis, nginx, Tarantool, OpenResty . Lua is also used to analyze big data, scientific calculations. Many routers, printers and other similar devices have Lua.

Lua in Moscow is a national meeting on the Lua programming language.

Even if you are not yet writing in Lua, come, perhaps the mitap will open your eyes to something new and interesting!

Join us at Telegram: @luainmoscow

A visit to the mitap is free for everyone, you just need to register

If you already have a ticket to DEVCONF - you are lucky, you are already a potential participant.

See you at Lua in Moscow on June 17!
