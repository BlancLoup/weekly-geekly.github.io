Googlephone, Part 2: The Return (Update)

About a year after the release of the Android operating system, Google, apparently, is going to release its own smartphone with its own system. On sale in North America, the smartphone should appear in January .

Google employees got smartphones for testing. The Wall Street Journal calls the device "Nexus One" (development name).

HTC received an order for production: a company that has long been developing and producing smartphones with Android, and, I must say, very successfully. Nexus will be sold immediately with the latest version of the system 2.1.

It is reported that Google will sell the smartphone itself, directly, without a contractual agreement with a mobile operator and sim lock, which is usual for Western countries, which significantly distinguishes it from Apple's competitor, iPhone.

Update Features:
1 GHz Snapdragon processor
3.7 "capacitive OLED touchscreen
5 mp 720p camera
2 microphones for noise removal
Trackball
3D accelerometer
proximity / light sensor
Wi-Fi / Bluetooth / FM (802.11n support will be possible!)
compass

Estimated price: $ 199 , which is significantly lower than the price for any comparable device.

The exact technical details are still unknown, but it is assumed that the smartphone will be technically similar to the HTC Bravo / Passion.
