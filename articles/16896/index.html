MicroSD card + USB card reader = Silicon Power Combo Slim Drive

The product range offered by Silicon Power has a new product called Combo Slim Drive , which is a very tiny card reader with a built-in USB connector, equipped with a 1GB or 2GB microSD card.


The reader itself has dimensions of 30 x 12.2 x 2.25 mm and looks very much like a portable flash drive. At the same time, it can be easily attached to a cell phone or used as a key fob. Thus, according to the developers, the owner of a mobile phone equipped with a microSD slot can always have at hand a functional tool for sending documents, photos, audio tracks or video clips recorded on a memory card to any computer.

The only disappointment is that it is not yet known exactly when the Silicon Power Combo Slim Drive will be on sale and at what price.
