Favorite client from Russian Post

Hello % Habrauser :)
I noticed an interesting promotion when I visited the nearest post office. Russian Post offers to get a plastic card "Favorite Customer".

Under the cut of pictures on ~ 3.5mb

For a start it was required to fill out such a form.

The very meaning of the service is somewhat like a connected club card. For the use of mail, points are given. For points, it is proposed to buy hotels :) Unfortunately, this is most likely not to apply to packages purchased through services such as ebay. As I was told in the mail, you need the presence of cash on delivery or something like that.
The card is given immediately. It remains only to rewrite her number in the form.

It looks so special. Plastic with numbers on the back of the bar code. Together with the card they presented a booklet with prices for gifts and basic conditions for which points will be given.

Basic information here. I do not think that it is worth rewriting all this in a post.
