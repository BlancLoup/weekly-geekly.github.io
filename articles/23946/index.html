MSI showed off her killer Eee PC

Tells Lenta.ru



Micro-Star International introduced a new line of laptops Wind, which will become competitors of super-cheap laptops Eee PC, which produces Asustek, writes Digitimes.

Wind laptops will be equipped with displays with a diagonal of 8.9 and 10 inches and a resolution of 1024 by 600 pixels. The capacity of the Wind hard drive is 80 gigabytes. Wind will preinstall Windows XP.

The device weighs about a kilogram. Expected price ranges from 299 to 699 euros.

The screen diagonal of the first generation Eee PC is only 7 inches, and the resolution is 800 by 600 pixels. A nine-inch display with a resolution of 1024 by 600 pixels appeared only in the second generation of notebooks, the Eee PC 900. The new Eee PC is not equipped with a hard disk, and its volumes are smaller than Wind volumes. The weight of these devices, as well as the price are comparable with Wind.

MSI Wind is not the first competitor to the Eee PC. Prior to that, the intention to release such a computer was reported by Hewlett Packard. According to the characteristics and price of competing can also be attributed to Intel Classmate and some other extra-budget laptops.
