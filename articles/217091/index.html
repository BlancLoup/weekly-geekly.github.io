HTC One M8 Announced



Today, March 25, the Taiwanese company HTC introduced its new flagship One series - M8. As reported by HTC, the device has become 20% more aluminum (70% in the past One, and 90% in the present). Works with Android 4.4 KitKat, and with the internal shell of Sense 6. It has a five-inch screen with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels (440 ppi), a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 processor with a frequency of 2.36 GHz,
16/32 GB of internal memory (of which 10/24 GB, respectively, will be given to the user), which can be expanded with a MicroSD slot, and 2 GB of RAM. By the way, it works with nanoSIM cards. The thickness of the device is 9.35 mm and the weight is 160 grams (weighing 17 grams).



On the back of the smartphone are two sensors, one of which is a depth sensor, measuring the distance between objects in the frame. And this is what it looks like:



Camera


A 4.1-pixel main photo module is integrated into the main camera, the pixel size is 2.0 micrometers, the sensor is 1/3 inch, f2.0 aperture, 20-millimeter lenses, double illumination, video recording in 1080p format. Now you can shoot HD video in SlowMotion mode (slow effect). The front camera has become five-megapixel, which is pretty good for selfies.

Battery


Battery capacity HTC One (M8) - 2600 mAh. According to the company, the smartphone will work 40% longer than the previous One. Also, the company followed in the footsteps of Samsung, and introduced a super-saving mode into its smartphone.



HTC One (M8) is equipped with advanced stereo speakers on the front panel, running a specialized audio profile. As a result, the device sounds 25% louder than its predecessor.
