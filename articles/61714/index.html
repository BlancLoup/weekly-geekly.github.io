Manage multiple google accounts

Google invites everyone to take part in the next webinar for Internet marketing specialists dedicated to working with the Client Center tool for managing multiple Google accounts (google.ru/websiteoptimizer).

The Google AdWords Advertising Professionals Center ( https://adwords.google.com/select/ProfessionalWelcome ) makes it easy to manage advertising campaigns in multiple accounts, get the official status of an advertising professional, use the AdWords API to create your own applications, and use a common credit line for all accounts.

The seminar will be held by Igor Protsenko, Google advertising strategy specialist (Dublin, Ireland). Igor will talk about the creation of the Client Center and the consolidation of accounts under one umbrella, the advantages of the advertising professional certificate, as well as the intricacies of the certification process and the possibility of obtaining a credit line.

The online seminar will be held at 4 pm Moscow time on June 11, 2009. To participate and get more information, please go to http://www.google.com/adwords/webinars/ , select the Manage Multiple Accounts workshop, and follow the registration instructions.
