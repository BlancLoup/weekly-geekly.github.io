Sending SMS Via Jabber

What is it and How to Start?


  1. This service allows you to send SMS from your Google Talk IM client , Gmail client or other Jabber clients, for example Gajim .
  2. It is assumed that you already have a Google Talk ID (for example, test gmail .com) or another Jabber ID (for example, inf@letinet.ru).
  3. Now, register an account on this site using your google name (test gmail. Com - including gmail. Com ) or another jabber name as your username , but use a different password . You will receive a free 0.3 cent per account to check the system.
  4. Add a new contact to the roster to which sms will be sent. (see example below).
  5. For example, if your friend‚Äôs number is +12345678900, then you should add 12345678900@sms.messagingbay.com as a new contact. Note: Do not use the '0' or '+' prefix for a cell phone number .

  7. When the SMS is sent, you will receive a notification about the status of the account. And if the SMS will reach the subscriber, then you will see a delivery report.
  8. If you want your mobile number to be displayed to the recipient, please send your phone number and username to support@messagingbay.com and we will be able to give you this opportunity.
