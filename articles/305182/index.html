Visualization of financial data. Mitap Ministry of Finance with developers, journalists and designers



Shot from the movie "Operation" Y "and other adventures of Shurik. Film Studio "Mosfilm". The second creative association

On July 12, a meeting of the Ministry of Finance of Russia with developers, journalists and designers will take place in Moscow at the Boiling Point from 10:00 to 15:00.

At the meeting, participants will be able to ask questions and find out what work the Ministry of Finance is doing in organizing the openness of public financial data, what data sets it has, what is planned to be done in this area in 2016, how to make infographics on open data. Also at the meeting, you can get advice from mentors on how to participate in a project competition based on open public financial data www.budgetapps.ru .

Participants:
  • Elena Chernyakova, Head of the Department of Information Technologies in the Sphere of State and Municipal Finance Management and Information Support of the Budget Process of the Ministry of Finance of the Russian Federation,
  • Ivan Begtin, an expert on state open data, director of ANO Information Culture,
  • Sergey Usachev, leading consultant at Teradata

The meeting starts at 10.00, ending at 15.00.

To participate you must register by the link.


The organizer of the meeting ANO "Information Culture".
