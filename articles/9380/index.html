Wikipedia will search: a contract with AskMeNow

AskMeNow Inc. today announced a preliminary agreement with the Wikiedia Foundation to create a search service for the famous Wikipedia encyclopedia. Also, the company plans to launch a search service for mobile phones.

The beta version of the site will allow users to ask questions, provide feedback, change the content of the portal, and help AskMeNow create the world's largest user database.

The site system, according to the creators, will give users various types of search for more convenience and accuracy of the information received. Visitors to the service will have the opportunity to ask a specific question and get an answer directly from the Wikiedia database.

If the service works well and is popular, AskMeNow and the Wikiedia Foundation will also launch Mobile Wikipedia for mobile phones and other portable devices.
