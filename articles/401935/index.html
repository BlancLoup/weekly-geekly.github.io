Mozilla Acquired Pocket Deferred Reading Service



Mozilla Corporation completed the acquisition of the company Read It Later, known primarily for the service of the same name, deferred reading, later renamed Pocket .

During the last year, Pocket was integrated into the Firefox browser and was a deferred reading service by default. Now, as the general director of the service, Neith Weiner, says, they are hoping for closer cooperation.

The Pocket team will continue to work as a subsidiary of Mozilla Corporation.
