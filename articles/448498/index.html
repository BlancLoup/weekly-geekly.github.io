"Russia 404": How much is left to live free Internet

We made the site " Russia 404 ", dedicated to the law on the isolation of the Internet - a timer, counting down the time before the entry into force of the law.


The law on the isolation of the Russian segment of the Internet will be effective from November 1, 2019: starting from this day, the Russian authorities may oblige Internet providers to block all foreign traffic.

The counter shows how much we have left to live on the free, uncontrolled Internet; it can also be treated as a visual deadline for emigration.
- Nick McFly, Evgeny Kudashev, authors of the project

The project is accompanied by a story - a fictional letter, written on April 17, 2020, which describes a new reality, in which there are families living in different countries and isolated from each other by a digital border.
