We connect the monitor to Thecus N5200 without soldering (lifehack)



In ancient times, the Thecus N5200 NAS money was bought for 36,000 rubles . He attracted the opportunity to combine nas and the router in one device. On board, there was a wan port and 4 lan ports + the ability to assemble a raid of 5 disks. The device itself was not well justified in terms of the nas function, it does not see the hard drives at 3 Tb, the slow speed of work with several streams (less than 10 MB / s). And it was decided to put ubuntu on it. It seems the process is not complicated, opening the case shows that inside a normal PC, the presence of two usb ports allows you to plug in a usb-cdrom and a keyboard. The main bad luck is that there is no video output, and how to get around this I will tell in this article.
