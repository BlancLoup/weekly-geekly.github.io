Do not miss SharePoint Conference 2011 Russia



We invite habrayusers to visit SharePoint Conference 2011 Russia is the main event in Russia and the CIS dedicated to SharePoint products and technologies. The conference will be held this year for the second time. Last time more than 300 participants attended the event. As a result, we received a large number of positive reviews and decided to hold a conference regularly.

In 2011, the conference will be held on June 20, in Moscow, in the conference center of the Holiday Inn Lesnaya hotel . Participation in the conference is free of charge, subject to prior registration . For those who wish to take part in the conference, who do not get to attend the event in person, an online broadcast will be organized.

The main purpose of this event is to get conference participants new knowledge on SharePoint products and technologies and related technologies, acquaintance and communication of participants, search for specialists, presentation of their own solutions of partners.

The program is divided into three parallel threads. In the two main streams, participants will be able to hear what the upcoming update pack will bring, what SharePoint Online is and why it is needed and why it is important not only for end customers, but also for decision makers, they will learn interesting details about corporate search, the subtleties of placing SharePoint on the Internet and much more. The main focus of the reports will be on practical experience and best practices for using SharePoint. The third stream will be presentations by partners of their own decisions in the extra-section. At the end of the conference, a round table will be organized with the participation of invited experts to discuss the most pressing issues of developing and administering SharePoint.

The conference is organized by the Russian SharePoint User Group. The general partner of the conference is Microsoft.
