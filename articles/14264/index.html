32GB Panasonic Memory Card

Matsushita Electric Industrial, which manufactures products under the Panasonic brand, recently introduced a 32-gigabyte version of a P2 flash drive. The USB flash drive, which provides data transfer rates of up to 640 Mbps, will be delivered as an ExpressCard module.

It is noteworthy that this P2 flash card (weighing only 45 grams), received an AJ-P2C032RG index, will be packed in an impact-resistant case that can withstand loads up to 1500 G and constant vibration up to 15 G. In addition, the new product is resistant to adverse impact and is able to work at extreme temperatures (from -20 to +60 degrees Celsius in operating mode and from -40 to +80 degrees Celsius during storage). But the cost of a transcendental memory card is $ 1,650. Deliveries of P2 32 GB will begin in November.
