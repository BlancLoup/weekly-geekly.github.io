Adom is alive


Somehow very quiet and useless, not so long ago, the site of one of the most complex and deepest roguelike games came to life again - ADOM . In the roguelike world, time flows nonlinearly, against the background of the deepest refinement and testing, breakthroughs occur.

In particular, you can finally play natively ADOM on Mac ( download ). Hurray-hurray, this is what we have been waiting for the last 5 years, this is what we clicked on Amazon banners to help the author. It happened, in general :)

Secondly, Thomas again took up the JADE - java-engine for ADOM. Actually, as for me, it would be better if JADE always stayed in deep alpha. Because, without a doubt, the outcome of the world is predetermined, and the Universe will uniquely collapse as a JADE-subset when the last end and the subglacial point are set.

Here are the news. Until we meet in ten years, let's talk about the new version of ADOM, we will laugh at the development process of JADE :) It is nice that there are such repulsive points in the world.

Yes ... time in the roguelike-world flows nonlinearly ... And very slowly - turn-based.
