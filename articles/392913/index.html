Canon's most sensitive camera in the world, the ME20F-SH, went on sale



A Canon ME20F-SH camera with an incredible sensitivity of 4560000 ISO (four and a half million), or on a logarithmic scale, 75 dB, appeared on the market . The record figure is achieved due to the large size of the pixels on the photosensitive matrix. The cost of the camera is $ 20,000 (about 1 million 350 thousand rubles at the current exchange rate).

The pixel size is 19 microns, which is 2-4 times the pixels of the average camera. In this regard, the resolution of the image is not more than 1080p. But at the same time the camera has the ability to shoot video up to 60 frames per second.


One of the first reviews

However, for the specified amount, the buyer receives only the camera itself - not only without a lens, but also without a recording device, viewfinder, etc. Just a box with a sensor. In this regard, the assembly designer, necessary for work, will bring the photographer extraordinary pleasure every time before shooting.


Management and video outputs

The camera is designed not only for shooting in the dark, and according to reviews, it gives excellent pictures for both photos and video.
