Crocodile - a game of association



Dear users!

I want to present you my startup for the game of Crocodile (for those who do not know what it is - see the article in Wikipedia or on our website ). In short: Crocodile is a word game built on associations.

In Crocodile, we play with friends for a long time. First they played in real life, then they created a community of VKontakte. But VKontakte was inconvenient to play, and the game quickly stalled. Then I had the idea to make a special site dedicated to the Crocodile. Site requirements - sharpened for a specific version of the rules of the Crocodile, a convenient interface for the game and the players communicate with each other (without communication, much of the fun is lost). In addition, a simple interface was needed so that players could create the games themselves. Exactly in a month the site was made (from idea to launch).

The CodeIgniter framework was chosen as the site engine as the most convenient and scalable. Writing modules on it is a pleasure. JQuery is responsible for testing the site on the client side. The basis of the user base is the PhpBB3 forum, which has been integrated with CodeIgniter.

The site is already two months old, during which time he has managed to acquire a small regular audience. Also many bugs were removed, and some changes were made to the rules.
