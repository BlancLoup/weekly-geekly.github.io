Developed by CraftStudio on MonoGame , the beta release took place on April 4th of this year.

The interface, unlike most similar designers, is intuitive and extremely simple. When you first start, not even the sophisticated can start making your HalfLife 3 with solitaire and princesses. But, first of all, the positioning of the constructor as a means for collective development stands out. There is an internal chat, distribution of roles, the ability for other users to monitor the progress of development.


In addition, an interesting fact is the presence of multiplayer games created with ‚Äúone click‚Äù launching a local server on your machine.

All development is carried out inside the editor, there is pixel texturing, modeling, texture mapping, animation, creating scenes, creating your own fonts, etc.


For writing scripts Lua language is used, as an alternative - a visual code designer.



The result: a simple game editor with no "problems" and graphic delights. It will help beginners not only in learning how to program games, but also in teamwork.
