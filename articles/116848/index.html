CPlase.com - the evolution of information dissemination on the Internet

Hello!

On the 1st of April the 1st article about the CPlase.com project was published.

For those who for some reason missed it, briefly about the main thing.

CPlase is a service designed to store, as well as effectively distribute, copyright and licensed content, with the possibility of receiving remuneration.

Simply put, it allows you not only to create and store, but also legally distribute and sell copyright and licensed content on the Internet, on any website!

For those who are interested - the project was created on Windows Azure.

How it works? Read on



Three Whales CPlase

1. Authorship

On CPlase, you can publish only author's content, or content for which there is a right to distribute. Thus, in the event of a dispute, using CPlase it is always possible to confirm authorship using the user agreement.

Now on CPlase, you can create text publications and image publications, but
The ability to create audio publications and video publications will be added soon!

For whom CPlase is created:
For authors of textual and graphic content, publishing houses, content resellers, news agencies, magazines, creators of music and cinema, labels, any content distributors (‚Äúcopy-paste‚Äù) and for many, many others.

The author of the above content can be anyone - from the student to the publisher. The main thing is interest and quality!

2. Widget

This is a JS application that displays any publication created on CPlase on any website on the Internet. 3. Finance

Publications are of two types:
Free

Paid (paid)


What is called, find 10 differences

Free-publishing - this can be used by authors who want not only to distribute their content, but also to keep their name next to the story;)

Paid publication - has a Free description (free description), several free pages (optional) and a paid part, which is available only after purchasing the publication.

Some of you may ask: ‚ÄúWill the author lose his audience?‚Äù
No, it will not lose, because In the free part of any paid publication, you can insert basic information that the author wants to convey to the user, and in the paid part - any additional information, including any supporting materials and files. In this situation, the entire audience will be satisfied (the displeased will still be - where without them), and the most interested can buy the full version of the publication.
And you can do the opposite - in the free part, arrange interesting information and cut it off in half, and leave all the most delicious in the paid part. This is for those who are brave and who knows for sure that his information is worth the money.

Soon there will be another type of paid publications, which quench the thirst of the afflicted, but this is a topic for the next. articles.

The author can set the price of his publication, starting with 1c USD and above.
Why so? So that anyone could feel bought by the author!

To whom and how do we pay?
We pay the author directly to his account in the CPlase system. CPlase holds only 10% of the total remuneration of the author.
Pay using paypal. Soon we will connect other systems of input / output of funds.

And now about how you can earn, not being the author:
Any CPlase user, without any permissions, can insert a widget on any resource, and if the publication is paid - the user who inserted the widget will receive a portion of the amount from the sale of the post through this widget. Such a user is called a reseller. Reseller can be every user.
The author himself establishes how much resellers receive from his publications. In this case, the reseller's pseudonym is registered in the url of the widget inserted by him and the author of the publication knows who distributes its content. Thus, the symbiosis of the author and ‚Äúcopy-paste‚Äù in CPlase is limitless.
