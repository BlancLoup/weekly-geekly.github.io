Landing pages and traffic


The online sales system is simple. At the initial stage, you have 2 main tools, these are landing pages (or as they are called one-page pages or Landing Page) and traffic. Everything! Nothing more is needed. We need to understand how these tools work and think a little. The main goal is to create a working system, and here I will tell you how to do it.


Why do you need a one-page?


A one-page, in the usual sense, is a site that consists of one page and offers one specific product, service, application or service. This can be a physically perceived product, like bricks and flowers, or a digital product - courses, discs, trainings. Any services, from plumbers, photographers, education, courses, and ending with catering and industrial construction. Or a site that is used to collect subscribers. This may be the site on which the traffic from the email list is landing. There is a huge amount of applications, in the ideal case you can use the one-page system to solve your business problems. On the one hand, it does not matter what is on the site, but it is important that this is one real problem that the client has and you can solve it. To make it more convenient, we will call the goods (physical and digital), services and everything else - just a product.


Why odnostranichniki work many times better than ordinary sites?


A one-page has one specific, clearly defined goal. Solve one customer problem that is well packaged visually. Therefore, it works better because the user is offered one specific action. He can either do it or not. A one-page page simplifies the perception of information, since you yourself give the visitor answers to all his questions. He does not need to make an effort, to look for anything. You just bring him everything ready on a silver platter. You fully focus the attention of the person on the product / service, show the benefits that he will receive and solving his problem. At the same time, you close most of his objections and tell why he ‚Äúwants‚Äù to buy here, now and from you.

A one-page has no such problems as a large site, when the user forgets where and why he came, completely losing concentration on the product. A large percentage of sales are lost at the stage of the basket and the multi-step application.

We estimate the budget and charge a positive.


Before starting any business, you first need to estimate how much money you need to spend in order to test something and what the possible profit will be.

It is necessary to understand well how to calculate the costs of advertising channels in order to know what to expect and set realistic goals, and not to be disappointed later. Always draw your conclusions on the numbers. Figures unlike opinions do not lie.

Some definitions on which we will rely further to speak the same language:

The visitor is the person who came to your site.
Lead is a potential buyer who has contacted you, filed an application for a purchase, consultation, ordered a call, or pressed the buy button.
Conversion (CTR) - the number of committed actions / divided by the total number of potential visitors who could have committed such an action. For example, 100 people went to the site, ordered 1, then CTR = 1%, ordered 10 people, bought 1 CTR = 10%.
The client is the one who bought something from you.
The average check is the amount that one order brings. Not to be confused with the profit and margin on the product. If one order for 1 rub, and the second for 9 rub. Average bill - 5 rubles.
Revenue - how much money you earned.
Gross profit - how much net you earned and can put in your pocket or spend on vacation, hobby, dream.
Business Efficiency (ROI) - if more than 100%, then profitable, less than 100% - unprofitable. Calculated as the ratio of revenue to cost.

The cost of a lead is determined by the conversion of the Landing Page, which is why everyone is fighting for this indicator. The higher the CTR of the page, the lower the cost of the lead, therefore, it turns out more margins and profits, with the same advertising costs.

To roughly estimate the cost of a lead, you need to estimate the cost of one click and estimate the conversion for the page. This is all very approximate, but somehow makes it clear the future picture. Take the average cost per click in your subject (for example, 30 rubles) and the conversion value (for example, 2%). Because if you go through these tough conditions, and the cost per click will be less, and the conversion is more, then there will be joy and a lot of money.

Calculate how much the lead will cost.
For example, 30 p. / 0.02 = 1 500 rub. This means that in order to receive one application, you need to spend 1,500 rubles. From here, calculate how much you have to earn from one sale in order to make a profit. If you sell honey at 500 rubles, then of course this scheme is not at all profitable. And if you are engaged in project documentation, where you can earn more than 1,000,000 rubles from one order, then this is a completely different matter. It all depends on the specific business.

To lower the price for a lead, you can increase your conversion or find a cheaper source of traffic. Or add virality and recommendations.
