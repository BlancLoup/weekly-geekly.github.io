Vladimir Putin presented a fighting robot avatar

The robot avatar with a remote control made a circle on the quad and hit the target with a pistol with five shots. The operator was responsible for the actions of the robot with the help of sensors fixed on the limbs. The President said that it is precisely on such promising, powerful projects and relatively inexpensive projects that Russia relies.



The combat robot was developed by the NGO "Android Technology" and the Advanced Research Foundation and is represented by employees of the Central Research Institute of Precision Engineering . The robot android in camouflage is controlled remotely using a copy-type setting device: the robot repeats the actions for the operator.



Among the products of the NGO "Android Technology" is the anthropomorphic robotic system SAR-400 , developed by order of Roskosmos. This torso-robot was planned to be sent to the ISS last year, but they did not. In 2017, the NGO will start the development of SAR-401, which can go to the ISS in 2020.
