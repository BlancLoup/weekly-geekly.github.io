Introducing the Meizu MX2!

Welcome, Habraludi! November 27 in Beijing, the presentation of our new smartphone - Meizu MX2. The novelty will be a logical development of the MX line and, we hope, will appeal to users. And now let's take a look at the characteristics of the Meizu MX2 and talk about the most important information for users from Russia - the cost of a smartphone and the timing of the start of sales.




Appearance


As many users expected to follow the news on the Meizu MX2, the new smartphone retained the external features of the previous model, so you could call the Meizu MX and MX2 twins. But there are differences. The frame around the screen in MX2 is minimal, because the diagonal of the display and its resolution in the smartphone are increased relative to the MX, and in size the smartphone has only been enlarged in height, the width has remained almost unchanged. The lid in the new smartphone, just like in the Meizu MX, is made using double casting technology: the first layer is made of matte white plastic, the second, which is also external, is made of transparent.




Specifications


  • Operating system: Android 4.1 with FlymeOS 2.0 shell
  • Communication: GSM / EDGE 850/900/1800/1900 MHz, UMTS / HSDPA 900/2100 MHz
  • Screen: 4.4 "1280x800), touch, capacitive type, aspect ratio 16:10, ppi 347
  • Platform: Samsung Exynos 4412, 1.6 GHz quad-core processor
  • RAM: 2 GB
  • Memory for data storage: 16/32/64 GB
  • Camera: 8 MP with autofocus, f2.0 (main), LED flash, 1.3 megapixel front, video recording in FullHD for the main camera and 720p for the front camera
  • Interfaces: Wi-Fi (802.11 a / b / g / n), Bluetooth 4.0, microUSB (USB OTG support), 3.5 mm for headphones and headsets
  • Navigation: GPS, aGPS, Glonass
  • Optional: light sensor, proximity sensor, accelerometer
  • Battery: 1800 mAh
  • Dimensions: 124.9 x 64.9 x 10.2 mm
  • Weight: 142 grams
