New way to transfer funds to QIWI Wallet: by email

One of the basic "chips" of the Wallet has always been an instant transfer from Wallet to Wallet without commission. Now a new function has been added to it, which allows anonymously, instantly and without commission to transfer funds from one purse to another. Knowing only the recipient's e-mail .



The new service can be convenient, among others, to freelancers who often do not want to ‚Äúshine‚Äù their phone number.

Let's see how it looks in practice. And so, you need:
- Log in to your QIWI Wallet;
-select in the section "Translation" -> "Translation by mail address";
-Fill in the form fields: amount, email address, comment;
-Press "Pay."



The recipient receives an e-mail from QIWI Wallet, which indicates the amount and comment on the payment, as well as a unique link, clicking on which the user sees this page:



Here you just need to click "Next", and then go through authorization. As can be seen from the screen, even if the user does not yet have his own Wallet, then it is enough for him to simply register it, and he will still receive his transfer.
