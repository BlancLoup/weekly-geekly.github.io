How it all works


Everything works very clear . If you can teach someone something, help rearrange the bookcase, put your grandmother across the road, then write about it on the website, and soon people who need your help will respond to the announcement. And vice versa: if you need help, write about it, and those who are ready to help respond to the ad. In principle, everything.

But, despite all the simplicity, this mechanism allows you to perform several tasks: to encourage people to think about others, find help, get people out of their homes and help them find new friends. Depending on this, people use hlpme for various reasons:
  • I can help
  • I want help

About promotion


I began to create ads myself, attract friends . After some time we scored the first hundred participants. It was like a holiday. Then I decided to advertise on Layfhakera (this was the only ad placed on the network during the entire existence of the project). This allowed us to get another 100 users. And then I learned Zen.

As if mad, I rushed through meetings, conferences, forums of people to whom my project might have seemed interesting. Conferences of social projects, freemarkets, meetings with interesting people, I did not miss a single opportunity to talk about my project, and it always turned out that I found someone interesting, acquaintance with whom I strongly influenced me. Even became the operator.

Once I met at one of these meetings with a man who lived in the next doorway. He worked as a journalist in a newspaper, and soon he wrote an article about us.
