What if you have a regular Pebble Watch?


If you have one of the first generations of watches, when they were not collapsible (i.e. on glue), then you will have to sweat thoroughly, for it is quite difficult to disassemble them without damage. You can watch the video guides on YouTube - there users simply remove the top glass, and then they mold (in the literal sense of the word) it from scrap materials.

If on screws, then everything is much simpler. Unscrew these, install the gasket (I used the foam double-sided tape) between the battery and the back cover and the problem disappears.
