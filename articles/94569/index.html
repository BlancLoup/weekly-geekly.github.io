Nigma learned to draw graphics

Not all chemists have beautiful formulas to paint, and the holiday has come for mathematicians.
Now Nigma draws beautiful graphics of functions and even conducts their research .

Investigating Nigma Function
  1. finds the range of valid values;
  2. finds the intersection with the axes of the abscissa and ordinate;
  3. checks the function for evenness / oddness;
  4. checks for periodicity and finds the period;
  5. finds the limits at the points of discontinuity of the function, the limits on the left and right at the point, the behavior of the function at infinity, and the oblique asymptotes of the function;
  6. calculates the derivative, derivative roots, intervals of increasing / decreasing function;
  7. finds the global maximum / minimum function;
