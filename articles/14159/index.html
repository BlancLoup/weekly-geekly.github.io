Miro player developers are asking for money for democracy

One of the most promising and technologically advanced projects in the field of Internet TV Miro (formerly Democracy Player) turned for help to all users. Developers from the nonprofit organization PCF admitted that their financial resources are coming to an end. They set a goal of collecting $ 50,000 in September. The money will go to pay for the services of talented hackers who will make regular software updates under Windows, Linux and Mac. In the very near future, the first official release of the program will be released, which, incidentally, still works fine in version 0.9.9.1 ( download ).
