Serious science

PostScience has opened an English-language website about Serious Science ( http://serious-science.org/ ), which will work on the same principle as PostScience itself - scientists talk about their research, as well as theories and problems of their scientific disciplines.



The project was the result of a trip to Boston, where we shot scientists from MIT, Boston University and Harvard University. While the site will work in the light mode and will be updated once a day. One day - one lecture.


Filming at the MIT Media Lab, Cesar Hidalgo, Assistant Professor at the MIT Media Lab

Now we will constantly travel to the USA and Europe and film scientists there. By the way, if you have any wishes or maybe even familiar scientists at some Western universities - share in the comments.


Filming at MIT, Seth Lloyd, Professor of Mechanical Engineering

Now on Serious Science there are several videos, including the commercial of the Nobel laureate in economics, Eric Maskin.
The site is more focused on natural science topics, but over time we will balance everything.


This is the first video that we have mounted in the new project. Rudolf Jaenisch talks about induced pluripotent stem cells in his lab at MIT.

So welcome! It's time to get serious! serious-science.org
