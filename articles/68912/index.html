Unlimited Internet from MegaFon and Opera



MegaFon subscribers in the Moscow region will get unlimited access to the mobile Internet using the Opera Mini browser and a special package of services

September 7, 2009, Oslo, Norway - Opera Software today announced that MegaFon, a mobile operator, has chosen Opera Mini as the basis for providing unlimited access to the Internet using GPRS / EDGE and IMT-2000 / UMTS technologies to its subscribers in the Moscow region.

Starting today, by downloading the Opera Mini browser to their mobile phones, MegaFon subscribers in the metropolitan region can activate the Unlimited Internet with Opera Mini service and view any sites for 7 rubles per day without taking into account the amount of information received and transmitted around the clock.

Note: when viewing any Internet resources through Opera Mini browser versions 2.X 3.X and 4.X Download files and peer-to-peer traffic is paid according to the tariff plan

More than half of the company's customers are users of data services using GPRS / EDGE and IMT-2000 / UMTS technologies. The number of MegaFon subscribers is growing at a faster pace than the market, thanks also to the innovative Internet-based services that the company offers.
