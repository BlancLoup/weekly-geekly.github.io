Google AdSense or wood chopping - chips fly

I have several projects on which I have placed Yandex.Direct units (as a bonus to the main income). After the story with the discharge of data about the supporters of Navalny, I decided to replace the advertisement with Google AdSense.

For some reason it seemed to me that relations with a large global company would be more reliable and honest. In the beginning, everything was like in a fairy tale: there were a lot of clicks, they were all quite expensive (that is, they brought more than Direct). A few days ago (just before the first payment was due) a letter arrived:
