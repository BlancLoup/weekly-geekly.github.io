Google raises reward for bugs up to $ 3133.7

Immediately after Mozilla , Google has announced about the increase in payment for the found vulnerabilities in the browser. From now on, Chromium will pay $ 3,133.7 for the most dangerous holes in Chromium.

At the same time, the basic payment for ordinary bugs remains $ 500. However, Google now promises to pay more often not at base rates, but at higher rates. Factors that rank the bug as qualitative, apart from immediate danger and critical vulnerability status, now include a productive discussion of the problem, a qualitative analysis of the cause, rigorous testing, etc.
