Mdday # MoCO - conference for mobile developers

On June 7-8, Mdday # MoCO , a conference for mobile application developers, will be held in Radisson Slavyanskaya in Moscow.



Mdday # MoCO is an alternative program of the World of Mobile Content forum. This year the conference is entirely devoted to content applications. For two days, guests will enjoy individual presentations by leading representatives of the mobile industry.

More about the event on moco.mdday.ru

The main topics of Mdday # MoCO 2012:
  • video and audio content in mobile applications;
  • mobile applications for media and magazines;
  • mobile stores, retail in mobile applications;
  • B2B content - applications as a tool for management and sales;
  • AR (augmented reality).

At the end of the conference, on June 9, usability guru workshops Dmitry Satin and an expert in the field of mobile marketing Leonid Bugaev will take place. Program master classes can be found here .
