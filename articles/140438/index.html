Payment from mobile phones

Now it became even easier to pay for Parking.ru services, you can top up your balance anywhere from your mobile phone account. Today, the following operators are available: MTS, Megafon, Beeline (commission of telecom operators: MTS - 2%, Megafon - 2%, Beeline - no more than 5%).

Payment through mobile payments is carried out through the CyberPlat system. The procedure is simple and no different from paying by card.

In order to make a payment from the account of your mobile phone, in CyberPlat you need to select the item Mobile Commerce and then the desired Operator. Then you will be prompted to enter a phone number and click "Pay." After that, an SMS will be sent to the specified number with a request to confirm the withdrawal from the subscriber account of the amount of the order you have generated.

If you have any difficulties, please contact our support team - we are always happy to help!
