Chocolate headphones

Everything good in life ends. But, my acquaintance with Fischer Audio headphones is a pleasant exception to this rule.

I hope, they will like not only me.

Appearance




Headphones look quality made, plastic is quite pleasant to the touch. Chocolate color gives a special charm and individuality, while gold and black emphasize beauty. The size is regulated by metal gold-plated arcs, which only complement the unique design of the FA-004. The headband is soft and almost not felt on the head. The ear cushions are quite deep and wide, so the ear completely fits into them, it is a pity that they are non-removable and there is no replacement in the kit, since I would like the size to be a little smaller. In general, the headphones look very stylish and fit the notorious fashionistas who love high-quality sound.

High-quality materials finishing headphones provide excellent comfort during prolonged wear, absolutely no pressure, only the ears sweat a little, but this is a flaw in a closed design. The gold-plated 3.5 mm jack and 6.3 mm jack adapter allow you to connect headphones to almost all types of sound reproducing equipment.

I have headphones with a straight cable, but there are also variants with twisted ones. This is already everyone chooses for himself.
For me, the wire is a bit short, but, in principle, you can get used to. A little upset that you can not change the wire here, but in the end, this is not a professional headphones from Fischer.



The two halves of the headphones are connected by a wire in a rag braid, which provides the best protection against chafing. By the way, the arms are made in a very original way: metal, gilding and black, similar to stone, plastic.
