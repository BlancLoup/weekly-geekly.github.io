Firefox changes default search to Yandex and Yahoo

Yahoo will replace Google in the US, Yandex - in Russia


The Mozilla Foundation is a non-profit organization that pursues many goals that are useful to humanity. Despite all the lofty intentions, its existence is associated with banal financial problems.

Therefore, to embed a default search engine in your browser, Mozilla makes agreements with private companies. A post published by the executive director says that Mozilla and Yahoo have entered into an agreement for the next 5 years. No data on the financial side of the contract was announced.
