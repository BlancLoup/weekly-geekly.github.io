20 years of the company Blizzard Entertainment

Once upon a time there were three guys - graduates of the University of California, Los Angeles. In 1991, they decided to create their own company for the production of computer games. The main thing, they said, was for the games to be super and let them be a joy.

Twenty years have passed, and the beginner studio has become a world famous company. Her games are known and loved by millions of people all over the world.

I believe that novice programmers who want to start developing games need to take an example from Blizzard. Always try to do only good things for people, not for money.

Videos, interviews, photos and key events in the life of the company will soon be available on the page dedicated to the anniversary .
