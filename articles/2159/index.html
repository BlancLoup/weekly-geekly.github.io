Online advertising revenues exceed $ 4 billion

Since 1996, employees of the Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) have been carefully calculating the revenues generated by online advertising and reporting this to the world.

These bureaus are highly accurate because they are provided directly by companies engaged in advertising on the Internet (this includes ads on websites, commercial online services, etc.). In the third quarter of this year, IAB is in a hurry to share with us , revenues from web advertising amounted to $ 4.2 billion, which is 33% more compared to the same period last year and 2% more than in the second quarter of this year.
