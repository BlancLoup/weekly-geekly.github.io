Dmitry Kochnev resigned as managing director of Rambler

The post of managing director of the project "Rambler" left Dmitry Kochnev, reports Roem.ru. An anonymous source claims that Kochnev was fired because of the low rates of the Rambler in the first half of the year. However, no one has officially confirmed this information.

His place will be Marina Rossinskaya. She has been working in the holding since March 2015 as product director. Before her arrival, such a position in the staff did not exist. From May 2013 to August 2014, Rossinskaya worked at Maxfield Capital . Prior to that, she was involved in entertainment resources in the RBC media holding and worked in the TMT Investments business incubator.

Dmitry Kochnev has been in charge of the Rambler portal and the Avtoambergler projects, Quto.ru and Letidor.ru since September 2013. In addition to the portal, the Rambler project includes Real Estate , Auto (Avtoumberbler and Quto), Finance , News and some other units.

In 2010-2013, Kochnev held the position of Operations Director of the online game publisher Syncopate . Prior to that, he was vice president of business development for Mail.Ru.

On June 24, the former marketing director Anastasia Babkina became the managing director of the Rambler portal.
