The two-day course from Arie van Bennekum January 30-31, 2015 for the first time in Kiev

On January 30-31, 2015, Ciklum Consulting in collaboration with Cegeka invites you to the Agile Project Management & Facilitation course for the first time in Kiev.


Day 1 (January 30, 2015) - Agile Project Management *
Day 2 (January 31, 2015) - Facilitation *

* the opportunity to choose one of the proposed days for training or a fully two-day course.

Arie van Bennekum (co-author of Agile Manifesto) and Andrey Baglay (Ciklum Project Management Consultant) will present a two-day practical project management class, starting with the first contact with the client, organizing the development, implementation and final delivery of the product.
