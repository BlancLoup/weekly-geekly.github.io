Rambler & Co launches online travel services portal with offline customer support.

Launched a new online platform "Rambler.Travel" . Users will be offered tours of 20 popular destinations, both abroad and within Russia. While the project is in beta. Therefore, now only 50 tours are available.

The main advantage of Rambler. Travel, compared with similar online projects, will be professional offline customer support.
