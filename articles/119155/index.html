Students may be placed for 30 years for posting fake coupon codes for 4chan and Zoklet



Students at the Rochester Institute of Technology can be seated for a very long time for publishing fake discount coupons on sites such as 4chan and Zoklet. As a result, according to government officials, companies suffered losses of hundreds of thousands of dollars. Well, the student received nothing but, perhaps, a long prison term. Coupons that were placed by the student were not limited to one or two companies, the range was very wide - from discounts on Tide powder to Sony PlayStation. Law enforcers have already reported that the total loss of retailers due to the actions of a student (his name is Lucas Henderson) left hundreds of thousands of dollars.

The student is now 22 years old, as if the beginning of life, and he already faces 30 years in prison. By the way, the young man studied in one of the IT specialties, and he knew that what he was doing was a criminal offense. Lucas tried to cover his own tracks using Tor, proxies and other tricks.
