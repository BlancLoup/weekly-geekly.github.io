Choosing a CMS for an online store



If you are currently at the stage of choosing the engine for an online store or plan to do it later, perhaps this short review will help you. In the near future, I'm going to do e-commerce, namely the opening of an online store. With a successful scenario, I may be able to please the habrasoobshchestvo interesting articles on this topic, but for now, I suggest you a small overview of the engines and topic for discussion.


Requirements that we formulated for the engine:
  • Flexible extensibility
  • SEO (CNC, the ability to prescribe meta tags for each product, etc.)
  • Convenient admin panel
  • Multilingual (multilingual content)
  • System of promotions and discounts
  • Ability to compare products ( not critical, but very desirable )
  • Payment system support ( not critical, but desirable )

Further, having analyzed the n-th number of engines, we stopped our choice, for a more detailed analysis, on the following:
  • Drupal + Ubercat
  • WebAsyst Shop-Script
  • Opencart
