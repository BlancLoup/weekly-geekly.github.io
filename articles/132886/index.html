Three innovations in Evernote for Android

Today's update on the Android platform contains just three notable innovations. We significantly improved the sharing of notebooks, more closely integrated Skitch with Evernote and created a separate application under the widget for those who could not use it before. All details below.

Download Evernote for Android

Shared notebooks


Now you can open access to your notebooks directly from Evernote for Android, and your Android phone or tablet becomes a very convenient device for collaboration with colleagues or fellow students.



To open access, go to the list of notebooks, and then press and hold your finger on the desired notebook. Then select the Sharing Settings item in the list. There you can provide access to your content. Any of your notebooks can be opened for individual users, and for everyone in the world. Premium users can also give others the right to edit your notes. Similarly, you can close the shared access to the notebook.

Please note that an Internet connection is required to open notebook access.

Now Evernote for Android contains all the necessary collaboration functionality. You can open access to notebooks and individual notes, as well as accept invitations to view and edit notebooks opened for you by other users and to access them directly within the application.

More features from Skitch


Our Skitch image annotation application is becoming more popular. In less than three months, more than one and a half million people have loaded it. This means that this update will appeal to a lot of people, because now it‚Äôs easier to use Skitch from Evernote than ever:
  • If a picture is displayed in a note, press and hold your finger on the picture, then select Edit in Skitch ; If the picture is displayed in slideshow mode, simply click on the Skitch icon in the tools menu at the bottom of the screen.
  • Make the necessary marks on the image in Skitch.
  • Click on the elephant icon in the tools menu to return the image to Evernote.



After returning to Evernote, you will be asked if you want to replace the old image or add a new one next to the old one (in the second case, the annotated image will be added to the end of the note).

Evernote Widget


Many people enjoy using the Evernote widget, but there are plenty of users who have never had the opportunity to use it. The fact is that if Evernote is installed on the SD card, the widget will not be available (this is, unfortunately, a limitation of the operating system itself). Therefore, we have made a separate application for the widget, which works regardless of where Evernote was installed. We advise you to download Evernote Widget to all users.



The task of the widget is to provide quick access to the key features of Evernote. You can create text, audio recordings and photographs, as well as go directly to the search. If you selected a large widget in the settings, you can also view recent notes. To install a widget, tap and hold your finger on the home screen, and then select Evernote Widget from the list that appears.
