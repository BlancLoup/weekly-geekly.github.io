Traffic monetization: make more money online!

In the era of the Internet, only the lazy one did not ask: how to earn extra money without leaving home? Right! We need someone who will pay us for our work on the Internet, namely advertisers who are interested in receiving an unlimited number of visitors to their site, and ultimately - the buyers of their product.

What are they willing to pay for?





Of course, for each unique visitor, who will then necessarily become their client, preferably permanent.

As you know, demand creates supply, and therefore, over the past few years, several models of working on the Internet with such advertisers have appeared:

‚Ä¢ CPM (Cost Per Thousand Impressions) - pay per 1000 impressions;
‚Ä¢ CPC (Cost Per Click) - payment for the transition to the advertiser's site;
‚Ä¢ CPA (Cost Per Action) - payment for an action that was performed by the user who has switched to the advertiser's site.

Recently, advertising in RuNet, almost every advertiser has determined for himself and realized that paying for real customers is much more profitable and more promising (CPA) than paying for users who can see (CPM) or even want to go to his product site (CPC ).

In this regard, over the past couple of years, new and new affiliate networks with a pay per action (Affiliate Networks) appear on the Internet at an incredible speed.

They are an intermediary service between advertisers and webmasters (publishers), and offer the latter several affiliate programs (offer) for traffic monetization.

Now, for each novice publisher, in principle, as well as for the experienced, the questions arise: where to get traffic for monetization? And how, in fact, to monetize it?

How to do?

In order to gradually transfer your traffic from one category to another, you need a lot of time, experimentation and effort. But everything is possible.

For each type of traffic will be their own ways to improve quality and increase conversion.

In this article I will start with teaser traffic and share my experiences and numbers. We will go from the opposite and take the 4th category for the value of our traffic at the initial stage, i.e. we have low quality traffic with low conversion.

Let's start with what is a teaser?

Teaser (eng. Teaser "teaser, lure") - an advertising message, built as a riddle, which contains some of the product information, but the product itself is not shown.

The teaser is a text-graphic block with a bright picture of the relevant subject of the advertised product and intriguing text, which must motivate the user not only to read the teaser text, but also click on it.



You can use teaser ads in different ways, for example, place them on your own resources or buy advertising spots in teaser and social networks.

By choosing one or several offers in any affiliate network, you should start with the empirical method of trial and error, but still some can be avoided at the first attempts to earn money on teaser traffic.

So, create a teaser ad and place it on your traffic, a relatively small amount of time is needed to understand the CTR of your ad.

The easiest way to identify the best conversion is to create multiple ads at once. Then, analyzing the results, it will be possible to disable one or more ads, and at the most advantageous to increase the amount of traffic.
