Reducing staff at Maxis Studios

Publisher Electronic Arts has officially confirmed that Maxis studio staff has been reduced. How many developers sent to the free bread is not reported. According to representatives of EA, the reason is completely out of money. This is a common situation in the gaming industry, when the studio expands and decreases, it all depends on the current market situation. There will be no changes in Maxis project releases. Exit Spore Hero for Wii and Spore Hero Arena for DS is not postponed for a day and will take place on October 9.

Recall that in the spring of this year Maxis was left by its founder and legendary game designer Will Wright. He went to the company Stupid Fun Club, which he himself founded in 2001, where he is engaged in producing websites and television shows.

Turbine has entangled Atari in litigation

In the gaming industry, there are two great ways to get rich: make an amazing game and drag someone to court. And you can, without losing time, create a game and immediately start with someone to sue her.

Today, for example, it became known that the Massachusetts (USA) studio Turbine sued the publisher Atari. Do not share the MMO game Dungeons & Dragons Online and the issue price is very high - $ 30 million.

The situation was as follows: Turbine studio developed Dungeons & Dragons Online, the publisher was Atari, which has exclusive rights to the Dungeons & Dragons series.

According to the plaintiff, the publisher is trying to get rid of the previously concluded agreement on post-sales support of the project in favor of another own development, which it is going to launch. The studios have to independently support the project at their own expense, which is contrary to the contract and greatly offends Turbine‚Äôs management.

As compensation, developers want to get 30 million green rubles.

Final Fantasy XIII: Release Date Set in USA

The latest "last endless fantasy" Final Fantasy XIII has found the exact release date in North America. A significant event for fans of the series will be held June 21, 2010.
