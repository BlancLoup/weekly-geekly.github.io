The history of the development of Pocket FALLOUT



I want to tell the story of the development of the project Pocket FALLOUT , about which I already wrote earlier . I will say straight away that I have nothing to do with the project, I just decided to tell the public about its development, since the previous post was received very well.

How well it all began ...


The project grew and developed, and version 1.2 was released by April 1. Since the project lives at the expense of the enthusiasm of a small number of people, a system for selling mods has been introduced. Mods could be anything. From new weapons to the whole city. Those who wish could give their free, which happened in most cases. Since the author of the project itself, the person with the nickname maskin , deals only with the engine, the development of the game world is completely in the hands of the modders:
