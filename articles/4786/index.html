How many gadgets in the Moscow subway?

Almost 10% of Moscow subway passengers are active users of mobile electronics, according to researchers at SmartMarketing . Music lovers among them are most of all - 80.6%, while on the road, prefer to listen to music. The second largest "community" - mobile gamers, of which 34.8%.

The result is the identification of a number of unobvious trends. So experiences SmartMarketing showed that a significant part of digital music lovers prefer to listen to it using mobile phones and smartphones.

The study accepted for granted the hypothesis of 100% cellular penetration, but information on mobile phones is not presented in the report. As for other mobile devices, the prevalence of audio players has been established, of which 63% in the total volume of mobile electronics. 43% are smartphones, PDAs and communicators (53.5%, 34.9% and 11.6%, respectively).

Shares of portable gaming consoles, laptops, video players and e-books in the aggregate amounted to no more than 0.3%. According to Svetlana Zavyalova, the study dispels the myth of the popularity of portable game consoles. Regarding e-books, she noted that for the most part people are still reading from handheld computers. The total amount of devices exceeded 100%, since passengers could have several gadgets at once.

In the group of game consoles, Sony PSP (83%) and Nintendo DS (13%) are mainly recorded.
