The scheme is simple: take part in a limited edition of tokens, and later sell them on the stock exchange several times more expensive. Because of the thirst for easy money, many take a "blind" part in each new project that appears, forgetting all the risks. As a result, the ICO market was flooded with fraudsters, and Declouds are just the tip of the iceberg.

Methods of fraud are varied: from elementary fake projects that disappear after a successful ending to phishing tricks and hacking.

For example, during ICO Aragon a fake Twitter account was created. Some users mistakenly took it for real and for a false payment information transferred funds. Fraudsters collected about $ 10,000.

There are quite arrogant guys: for example, the creators of the Razormind project allegedly created a whole operating system based on the blockchain, actively promoting themselves in the media. It came to contacting Coinidol , who, when collecting information, found many inconsistencies and six months later laid out a full exposure of the ICO owner. How to protect yourself?


Initially, you need to understand - ICO is a high risk. But it's a good idea to follow a few simple rules when choosing an ICO, so as not to run into outright fraudsters:

Always look at the design of the project site.

Attention to detail is very important. ICO is, first of all, a competent presentation of a product that needs to interest an investor. And if you want to close the tab in horror from the site of the next project, then the probability of its success is ‚Äúextremely small.‚Äù See if the logo is stolen, whether licensed or stolen illustrations are used, and so on.

Assess the feasibility of the project

Any project in which money is invested should be real today, and not in the distant future. The hero of the occasion should be not only innovative, but also useful in the interests of a large number of modern people. Someone will use it. Do not invest in projects like SkyWay, which promise to create overhead vehicles capable of developing up to 500 km / h. And all this joy is 7 times cheaper than an ordinary intercity train. Miracles!



The project team is very, very important.

Often, fraudsters do not write about themselves, but about fake people who will help the founders to remain anonymous. The key to a successful ICO is experienced professionals who can easily be found on Facebook or Linkedin. And best of all when the team is assembled from famous people who were previously involved in any cool projects.

Check the legal entity

If you google the legal address of the project, and you are highlighted the address of the kindergarten in the Zyablikovo district, then it is not advisable to invest your money there. Any contact numbers and postal numbers must be active.

Tokens

ICO should have its own monetary system with exceptional benefits for its owner. The released token should not only be sold more expensive, but also have a weighty rationale for its further development within the project. Be sure to read the whitepaper section, which describes this item in detail.
