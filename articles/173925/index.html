3 years ago, being IT people with a good background, we got sick with running and bicycles. We quickly joined the sports crowd and realized what was happening. Once (as is usually the case), while running in the forest, we thought about it - what if we make our own national sports tracker? Suppose there are a million of them in the west, but there is no one of their own, but a big country ...

Having a sufficiently powerful IT resource and a desire to thank the sport for a new quality of life that it gave us, we decided to start. The pleasure (fuel) from the creation was the same as from a workout on a summer day on a bicycle in the aerobic zone (from there and the name, by the way).

Taking into account such serious and no longer young competitors (one million people) like Nike +, Endomondo, Sport Tracker, RunKeeper, Strava and another +100, we thought about how expedient it would be to go with them to the frontal one.

Empty niche is not the best sign! The situation in Russia is very bad with a massive, even aerobic sport, and no one wanted to get involved in a laborious, expensive and very long development.
