New service of monitoring and analytics of the Russian hosting market from REG.RU

REG.RU recently announced an update to the analytical portal StatOnline.ru , also known as Stat.reg.ru. Now, besides the detailed statistics of domain zones .RU / .SU / ., the statistics of the hosting industry are also available on StatOnline.ru.

The new section "Metrics" on the site StatOnline.ru includes seven subsections, each of which contains monthly statistics on one of the directions of development of the Russian hosting market. Below we describe in more detail about each subsection.
The service was launched quite recently and so far it is possible to familiarize yourself with the statistics for April and May 2013 with the help of it.

The sub-section ‚ÄúDomain use‚Äù contains summary statistics on the distribution of domains in .RU / .SU / . zones by type of use (hosting, parking, web forwarding, lack of IP-address, non-delegated domains or used only for mail).
The .RU zone is traditionally the most actively used - here the smallest among all zones is the number of non-delegated domains (8.5%) and domains used for web forwarding (5.1%).



In the subsection "Hosting domains" is the current TOP-20 hosting providers by the number of hosting sites.



In the same subsection, you can familiarize yourself with the statistics of distribution by providers of domains located in the parking area, as well as with statistical data on domains hosted by several providers at once (multihosting).
On the site StatOnline.ru there are also very curious, worthy entries in the Guinness Book of Records, information. Site developers will certainly be interested in the content of the Site Analysis subsection. This subsection contains six tabs, after studying which, you can find out which CMS, language encodings or web analytics scripts are most often used in .RU / .SU / . zones.
For example, Joomla is in the lead in zones. RF and .SU, whereas in the zone. RU the victory is behind Wordpress. The utf-8 encoding is naturally the most popular for all Russian national domain zones.
