Vacancy and project today (August 1-7): a PHP programmer is needed in the StarIndex project (Moscow).


StarIndex

StarIndex is a professional service designed for concert organizers, promoters, artists management, music media. The main task is to help make the concerts take place at the sites that were filled to capacity, and it was impossible to buy tickets for these concerts a week before it was held. Represents services for the preparation of ratings of artists based on data on their listening and popularity in the network, as well as a lot of opportunities for advertising concert with fine-tuning, allowing more efficient use of the budget, and to promote the artists themselves on the Internet.

Who is needed?

PHP programmer (with growth to head of development)

Description of tasks:

Modernization and development of new web service interface functionality
Crosbrowser layout on ready-made design layouts
Refinement and maintenance of web service performance
Preparation of software documentation
Testing the results of the new functionality
Communication and management of developers through outsourcing

Requirements for a candidate:
Experience from 3 years in web programming and web-interface development
Excellent knowledge of HTML / XHTML and CSS, features of their display in various browsers, as well as an understanding of the principles of usability
Confident knowledge of javascript, ajax, jquery
Excellent knowledge of PHP, SQL, ability to write competent queries (good design experience and work with MySQL)
Confident knowledge of XML
Confident work with various CMS (joomla, wp, bitrix, etc.), experience in upgrading and developing projects for these systems
Knowledge of English is desirable (minimum technical)

Personal qualities:
Responsibility, punctuality, accuracy, attention to detail
Independence and organization
Teamwork skills
Ability to work quickly in multitasking
The ability to interact with different people (project managers, programmers), quickly respond to requests and change situations
The ability to make decisions and take responsibility, sociability, diligence

Working conditions:

Office in the metro station Park Kultury (possibly at a remote location, but within the Moscow Ring Road)
Bonuses, awards for work
Working hours from 10 to 19 (discussed)
Cafe, restaurant with business lunch, canteens in the office area
Tea, coffee in the workplace
