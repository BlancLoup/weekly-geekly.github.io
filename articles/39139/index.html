Who to be?


The choice of a future profession, even more likely a field of activity, very often arises in front of young specialists. They say that work is most effective when you do what you like. Hmm, then it would seem there is nothing easier! One has only to make a hobby his work.

Do not rush, in fact, everything is not as clear as you think. A hobby is an activity you like to do in your free time, and notice that you never recycle when you do your hobby. Set goals for yourself. Yes, exactly, a person without a goal is a person with no future. In choosing a profession (path), do not proceed from the desire to do any work, but set yourself the goals that you want to achieve, analyze, choose the ways of their implementation.

Do you like cooking and do you want to open a cafe by the sea? Well, to stand for 12 hours at the bar or stove up to 40 years would not be the best way.

Set priorities. Do you have a lot of hobbies, like me? Well, then prioritize. Being a universal, and being able to do everything at once at the same time is not possible, because everything takes time, and to develop skills and abilities, too.
