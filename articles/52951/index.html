12 tips on optimizing the TITLE tag

The title tag is the most important part of the page of any site, so you should pay a lot of attention to how you make it. Creating headlines is like art and not everyone can make it the right way the first time; it requires knowing some subtleties. By applying the 12 tips for using and optimizing the title tag to your sites, you will avoid many problems in the future.

1. Title should reflect the essence of the page.

2. Include your brand in the title pages.

3. Use different titles on different pages.

4. The title tag should contain keywords.

5. Do not include too many keywords in the title.

6. Do not use stop words in the title.

7. Each word should not be repeated more than twice.

8. Do not use page headers that are too long.

9. Make the title a maximum of 8 words.

10. Put the most significant keys at the beginning of the title.

11. Use patterns if necessary to include several keys in the header.

12. Do not use special characters.
