We test p2p video for overload

Good day to all.

I want to test a new video broadcasting technology that does not require additional installations by the user. No plugins or add-ons. For the first time I test this technology and I would like to test it on HabraEfect. I have no relation to its development or advertising.

The essence of the technology is quite simple, there is video on demand or a live stream (in my case VOD with the Wowza Streaming Engine) and the website of the streamroot company. Our video on demand comes to their player and is then distributed to users using p2p technology, where each user gives the video further. That should help to avoid overloading the Internet channel and the technical capabilities of the server.

In this experiment, the server is my home PC, so if the video falls, do not kick much.
