So, from this year, companies and entrepreneurs engaged in software development and development, creating embedded and wearable devices, projects in the field of robotics, the Internet of things, IT security, the team are invited to participate in the competition along with Internet projects and solutions for mobile devices. , creating new interfaces for working with computers using gestures, voice and vision, and others.
