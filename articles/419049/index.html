GeekBrains launches free online educational marathon "Find Yourself in Digital"


From August 6 to 25, the educational project GeekBrains will hold the first free online marathon consisting of 15 webinars in three areas - programming, design and marketing. Participants will receive a special certificate of passage, the possibility of free training and an invitation to the final, which will be held at the Mail.Ru Group office on August 25.


Within a month, specialists from three industries will give real-life examples of how to get a job everyone dreams of, and also analyze the most sensitive and money topics for programmers, designers and marketers. Experts will share life hacking, how not to get lost in the abundance of design software, how to create a game on the Unity engine in an hour, how to promote business in social networks and create effective native advertising.
