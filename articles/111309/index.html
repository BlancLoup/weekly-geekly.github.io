Sony Ericsson XPeria Arc - the most stylish Android smartphone

In a series of dizzying announcements that were held at the CES electronics exhibition in Las Vegas, the appearance of a new flagship of Sony Ericsson remained completely unnoticed. XPeria Arc is a stylish 4.2 "FWVGA touchscreen with Android Gingerbread on board. XPeria Arc is based on System-On-Chip MSM7230 , a single-core Qualcomm Scorpion Application Processor running at 1 GHz, and the use of the new Qualcomm Adreno 205 graphics core gave the ability to compete in performance with the Galaxy S line.



XPeria Arc is particularly proud of its slim design: the minimum thickness of a smartphone is only 8.7 mm. Also, the smartphone has the most advanced at the moment the photo module from Sony with support for Exmor R technology. The 8.1 MPs XPeria Arc camera has f / 2.4 light power, 2.46x zoom, automatic image stabilization, and a built-in noise reduction mechanism. Coupled with LED-flash, touch-focus and support for recording 720P HD-video, this smartphone can easily compete with soap boxes of the average price range.

4.2 "capacitive display device has a resolution FWVGA (854 x 480) and is made by technology Sony Mobile Bravia Engine. Mobile Bravia Engine - the next generation of S-LCD displays from Sony, which have greater brightness and clarity due to the high PPI.



Traditionally for devices based on Qualcomm chips, Arc is equipped with 512 MB of ROM and a microSD slot. XPeria Arc will hit the shelves in the first quarter of 2011 and will be available in two colors: Midnight Blue and Misty Silver. The price in Russia is 26000-28000 rubles.
