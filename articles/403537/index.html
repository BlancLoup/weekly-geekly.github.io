Yachts with smart home on board. Made in Russia

Update: added integrators comments to their projects

Here we have a little marketing materials and that's it. Not yet reached good budgets for this business.



And suddenly two integrators immediately send us cool videos, in which a whole new market opens up for us with complete surprise. These are the watercrafts (yacht and houseboat), on which they made automation using iRidium. The truth is unexpected and I want to use these videos as use cases, show customers. The problem is that you need to choose one main one, and we can not decide which one is better from the point of view of potential buyers.

See for yourself:

Smart yacht Ak-Bars from the company "SM Tech" . Glass with adjustable transparency, motorized furniture and AV control. Flash quickly, but they are.



Description from the integrator:
How the task sounded:
Integration of a complete automation system on board a private yacht

Description:
The company SM-TECH implemented a project to integrate a complete automation system onboard a private three-deck yacht 35.6 m long and 6.9 m wide. Within the project, an individual interface was developed on the iRidium mobile platform. The developed interface allows you to conveniently and comfortably manage various functions of engineering and multimedia systems of the vessel, namely: integrated audio and video management of multiroom yacht systems, smart glass management, various motorized controls (curtain drives, table lifting mechanisms, TV), lighting system control. The logical scenarios inherent in the project have reduced the number of switches, reduced energy consumption and, in general, improved the comfortable atmosphere. A feature of the project was the integration of the geolocation system, which made it possible to quickly accurately obtain information about the current position of the vessel on the satellite map.

Difficulties:
The main difficulties in working on the project were the remoteness of the object (Kazan) and extremely tight deadlines.
