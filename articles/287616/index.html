Five ways to promote online media: numbers, cases, download, free

Somehow it happened that there are few good articles on the Internet about the experience of integrated promotion of Internet media. Especially regional. Especially business. We decided to slightly correct the situation and wrote our article on the integrated promotion of the regional business media on the example of our company's project - the business portal newsib.ru and analysis of other regional and federal Internet media. Whether it turned out good - to judge you.

In order to avoid excessive dryness and an abundance of numbers alone, we decided to compare the promotion of an Internet project with a game of chess - we must win a visitor.




Terms



Math - visitor attraction, result;

the black king is the visitor;

white queen - complex promotion;

white rook - banner and text advertising, direct promotion;

white elephant - promotion in search engines and news aggregators, promotion diagonally;

white horse - promotion through the formation of a community.
