In reality, it is impossible to break a wall of a skyscraper in a concrete part, especially on a technical floor, without using extremely powerful and highly visible special equipment. What to say about the modern ultra-high, which are designed for resistance to shock overload and progressive collapse?
As our chief engineer, Sergey Nikiforov, notes: "... over the past 50 years, the strength of structural steel has increased by 40%, reinforcing bars - by 50%, and concrete - by almost 100%." And to destroy this high-strength reinforcement and concrete without noise and dust, by minimal means? ...

If, nevertheless, the wall somehow managed to be broken through quietly and imperceptibly, for example, having borrowed a laser sword on the next set, then it still does not converge further. In any super-high buildings there is a system for monitoring structures. When the wall is destroyed, the sensors will transmit an emergency signal about it. Such a system can be arranged differently in different buildings, but its presence and actuation when a structural element is destroyed is a constant. Carbon dioxide and fire suppression


Yes, it does, but in limited cases.


The tower with people is not related to cases. Shot from the official movie trailer

CO2 in certain quantities is dangerous to humans: at 5% concentration, symptoms of intoxication occur, at 7-10% - death. The effect of fire extinguishing is achieved at a concentration of about 30% carbon dioxide in the room. So gas fire extinguishing - only after complete evacuation.

With regard to the climax of the film, even gas masks on the heroes would not have been able to make its fantasy realism. The essence of gas fire extinguishing is a closed volume, allowing to achieve the desired concentration of gas. ‚ÄúIf at least the window is open, such a fire extinguishing will be meaningless,‚Äù explains Alexander Smirnov, an engineer at the PB and OT Lakhta Center. So the final shots, when CO2 comes from sprinklers (for some reason in the form of a clear liquid), the fire goes out in front of the heroes to the accompaniment of a wind blowing out of the breaches in the floor and walls is an absolute reality unclouded by reality.
