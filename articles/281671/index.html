Details of the completion of the Stack Overflow beta period in Russian

A detailed story about how Stack Overflow in Russian came out of beta status appeared a few days ago in a company blog.

By the time the certification was completed, the statistics were as follows:

  • 115.7 questions asked per day
  • 82% of questions answered
  • 1,499 permanent users and 33,678 users in total
  • 1.7 average answer to a question
  • 42,464 visits per day

Besides:

  • More than 2,000 members per month join the community.
  • The site is most popular (descending) in: Moscow, Kiev, St. Petersburg, Minsk and Kharkov.
  • More than 3,000 participants per month get the right answers.
  • To get as much attention as on Stack Overflow in Russian, you would have to hire 23,000 full-time developers.

The launch of the beta version of SO in Russian took place in 2015, following the acquisition of the HashCode site. Thus, the process of becoming an equal member of the Stack Exchange network took less than eight months for the service.
