The fourth generation of clones was born in Japan

The fourth generation of clones was born July 23, 2007 in Japan. All newborns are exact copies of copies obtained from copies, which, in turn, were copied from clones born in April 2004, that is, they all have exactly the same DNA. This proves that the clones themselves are quite capable of playing the role of "master copies" and generate a new race of their own kind. Thus, if, as a result of a special selection, some particularly successful creature is obtained, then it will be possible to reproduce it in unlimited quantities by making the second, third, and fourth copies.

So far we are not talking about people, but only about pigs, but from the point of view of physiology, the difference between people and pigs, as we know, is minimal, so these experiments are very close to human cloning. In addition, many pig organs and biological materials are suitable for human transplantation, especially if you clone pigs using human genes (such experiments are conducted by BIOS Inc. ).

In addition to pigs, up to the sixth copy of the mouse is already cloned and to the second copy - the cow.
