"Telesystems" are invited to visit

The recent topic about the world's smallest voice recorders , produced by the Zelenograd company Telesystems , has aroused great interest and a lot of questions to the company, the existence of which, as it turned out, very few people on Habr√© knew before.

In Zelenograd, such a reaction was pleasantly surprised, and decided to provide an opportunity for everyone to see with their own eyes what it is doing, how it works, and how one of the few Russian companies producing a competitive high-tech product sees its future.

So, on June 10 at 14:00 the Telesystems company will open its doors and hold Habrahabr Day for everyone.

UPD For all guests of the event, the sale of Telesystem products will be open on this day with a substantial discount. So, if you have already looked after yourself something from the company's range, a visit on June 10 will be a good opportunity to save money.

Founded in 1992, and beginning with the production of AON, the Telesystems company now produces a wide range of diverse electronics - and the world's smallest dictaphones in the Guinness Book of World Records are just the tip of the iceberg. There are lots of topics for reviews on Habrahabr, but it's better to see once than hear a hundred times. And reviews will follow.

What is going to be there


Technical specialists and managers of the company will show their guests a department of development and innovation, assembly departments and production, a showroom, answer questions and share plans for the future, tell interesting stories from their practice. They really have something to tell: for example, about the use of Telesystems' technology in NASA or how it can be used to catch drug lords in Colombian forests. Share plans: the company releases a new product every week. And, of course, tea with coffee and cookies :)

How to get


The open door will start at 14:00 and last up to 16-17 hours. The event is open and the entrance is completely free.
