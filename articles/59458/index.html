Diesel DZ9044 - watch without dial



In fact, the Diesel DZ9044 model has as many as four dials - it is assumed that they can show time in four time zones. Here are just the arrows on the sides of the case, while the front surface is a pristine clean steel sheet.

It is not very convenient to track time on such a watch, but what difference does it make? Anyway, the chronometer now is more an accessory than a really necessary thing in the household.

The case diameter is 38 mm, the water resistance is 100 meters, the warranty is two years. Watches for sale here for $ 365.
