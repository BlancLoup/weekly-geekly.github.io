Microsoft Surface RT will be available in Russia



Microsoft is expanding the geography of sales of its Surface Series computers.

According to a press release , official sales of Surface RT will begin in Russia , Japan, Mexico, New Zealand, Taiwan, and Singapore at the end of March. Unfortunately, there is no Russia in the list of countries where Surface Pro deliveries will begin in the coming months (but there is the United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, France, Germany, China and Hong Kong).

At the moment, Surface RT is available in the USA, Canada, China, most European countries and Australia. The device is based on the Nvidia Tegra 3 ARM processor, equipped with 2 GB of operational and 32 or 64 GB of permanent memory (+ the ability to use SDXC-cards), has a 10.6-inch screen with a resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels and is running Windows RT.
