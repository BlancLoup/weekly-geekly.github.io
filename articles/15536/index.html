WikipediaVision - another time killer

WikipediaVision is a small but interesting project by the Romanian Laszlo Kozma ( László Kozma ). It is a map of the world, which shows in real time who, what and from where edits in the Wikipedia online encyclopedia, or rather in its English version.

As the author himself admits, the creation of his site was inspired by the service Flickrvision , which, as many believe, can be watched endlessly, just like fire, water and the work of others.
