BitTorrent seeks volunteers to test peer-to-peer broadcasts.

BitTorrent company in its official blog dedicated to BitTorrent Live, asked everyone to help in testing the peer-to-peer broadcast technology.

In essence, the help will be as follows: if a volunteer has a channel on Ustream or justin.tv, as well as a webcam, he is asked to install the application (before that he will have to answer the questions in the questionnaire) and thereby take part in testing BitTorrent Live.

The benefits from the introduction of technology are obvious: in fact, it will be possible to organize a video broadcast in real time using only your connection channel and without any infrastructure.
