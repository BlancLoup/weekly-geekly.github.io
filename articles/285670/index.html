IT-emigration of Ukrainians to Minsk

After reading the article about emigration to Minsk, the thought of moving began to creep into my head, especially since there were certain circumstances that influenced my decision. My story is different, not entirely logical, but it was forced. First he moved, and only then he looked for work.
I am a front-end developer, quite average in skill and experience (maybe for some it will be important).

Now, when I'm here, when many of my friends from the IT industry are interested in Minsk, if not the most difficult option to go somewhere, when I got a job and went through a very sad procedure of paperwork, I have something to tell.

Documents


The ideal version is:
Entering Belarus, you have already decided on a place of work (you have an offer, there is a special permit for work) and with a place of residence. If not, the steps are as follows:

Removal of housing with the obligatory conclusion of a contract of employment with the landlord in the housing and utilities infrastructure of the area for a period of one year.
Necessary clarification on the terms of this contract:

- You can be a Ukrainian in Belarus without registration for 30 days;
- The contract of renting an apartment for 1 year makes it possible to obtain registration for temporary residence for 1 year subject to the availability of relevant documents (see below).

Documents required for obtaining registration for a temporary stay for a period of 3 months:

- The contract of renting housing with the landlord;
- Passport and migration card;
- Application for registration of temporary residence (previously taken from the inspector without a queue and filled);
- Receipt of payment of state duty in the bank (0.5 base value).

Registration for a temporary stay for a period of 3 months is made out on the same day at the Citizenship and Migration Department of the district where the contract for renting an apartment is issued.

Documents required for obtaining registration for temporary residence for a period of 1 year.
- passport + copy of the passport;
- A copy of the contract for renting an apartment for a period of 1 year;
- special permission to engage in employment + copy. Done and paid by the organization that takes you to work. Registration time - 2 weeks;
- labor contract + copy. It is done after receiving a special permit for work within 1-2 weeks;
- petition of the organization that takes you to work;
- A copy of the registration for a temporary stay (if you did not have time to collect all these documents within 30 days);
- application for registration for temporary residence , taken from the inspector;
- payment receipt (3 basic values).

Registration for temporary residence for 1 year - 2 weeks.

PS There may be some discrepancies and inaccuracies. Some nuances may vary, something may just change.
