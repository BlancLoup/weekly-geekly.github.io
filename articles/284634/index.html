Dear Habravchane,

There was a reason to finish working days with a good mood and a sense of accomplishment.

Yesterday it became known that the State Duma in the third reading adopted an amendment to the Federal Law, which everyone had been waiting for. We are talking about reducing the threshold for the number of personnel of IT companies required to pay insurance premiums at preferential rates - from 50 to 30 people.


Prehistory


Exactly one year ago, the IT public was indignant: the State Duma made the last amendments to Federal Law No. 212- ‚ÄúOn Insurance Contributions to the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation, the Social Insurance Fund of the Russian Federation, the Federal Fund for Mandatory Medical Insurance and Territorial Funds for Mandatory Medical Insurance‚Äù.

According to the latest changes as of July 18, 2011, only those development companies that had been accredited had the right to benefits. In order to get under the exemption, it was necessary to observe two conditions:

  1. Have at least 90% of income directly from the main activity.
  2. Have a staff of at least 50 people.

We even made an open letter to the President , asking to somehow lower the bar, which small businesses in the field of web development were clearly unable to overcome.
