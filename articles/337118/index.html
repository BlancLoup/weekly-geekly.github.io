Avito Automation meetup - slides, video, reviews, photos

August 26 was the first Avito Automation meetup. We talked about test management issues, vectors for the development of test automation systems, the effectiveness of each test, tools for testing iOS applications and running tests in Continuous Integration. Under the cut - video and slides reports, feedback from listeners and a link to the photo report.



Problems of test management, or what prevents to create cheap and useful tests in the presence of a sincere desire to do it


To create a quality product, it must be checked several times. So, create a series of useful, fast, cheap and effective tests. But, as always, something hinders us. How to recognize the symptoms of problems and their causes, what losses are possible and how to find solutions - Fedor Zvolinsky from Yandex tells about all this together with Deadpool.
