3000 ships clashed in battle in EVE Online

Last weekend in the online game EVE Online in the Asakai system there was a large-scale battle involving 3161 warships.



According to the players in their blogs, the error of the pilot of the ship of the Titan class, which, instead of providing the Allied fleet with teleportation into its own system, accidentally produced a hyper-jump into enemy space, led to this battle. And since this jump did not go unnoticed, players from other factions began to arrive at the scene of the battle.

Game developers have provided a schedule of losses depending on the class of ships:



Results : 222 ships destroyed. Total losses, approximately, amounted to 700 billion in game currency, or 24 thousand dollars.

In fact, the random click of a single player caused the largest battle in the history of EVE Online.
