Google AdSense and WebMoney

It turns out that Google collects ideas from publishers of AdSense until December 15 . In particular, one of the ideas is support for payments in WebMoney.

The official message from Google. (at the bottom)

You can support the idea here .

Other ideas include CSS, low prices, filters ...

The ability to set a minimum CPC for the displayed ads.
Ignoring random clicks by the publisher on their ads.
Filter ads by keywords.
Allow CSS ad blocking.
Payments are at the end of the current month, not the next.
The ability to exclude the same ad on the page.

And many others (ingles).
