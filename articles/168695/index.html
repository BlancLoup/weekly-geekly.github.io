IOS 6.1.1 beta released

It has only been a week since iOS 6.1 became available, and Apple has already released iOS 6.1.1 beta for developers.



Build number 10B311.

New beta became available 48 hours after the release of iOS 6.1 Evasi0n jailbreak. It is not clear yet if Apple has closed the vulnerability that is used in jailbreaking, but Musclenerd on its Twitter urged jailbreakers to beware of iOS 6.1.1 beta.

The main difference between iOS 6.1.1 and iOS 6.1 is the improvement of Apple Maps for Japan.
