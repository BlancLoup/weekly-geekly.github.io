Applications open for Summer Research Microsoft Research


The traditional annual Microsoft Research Summer School opens registration on February 6, 2014. This year, training seminars for young scientists will be held from July 30 to August 6. The school is organized by a division of Microsoft - Microsoft Research, with the support of Yandex and the Moscow State University named after M.V. Lomonosov. The goal of the Summer School this year will be to teach the new generation of researchers the skills to create cloud tools and systems needed in the age of big data.

Applicants for participation must complete an application by March 15, 2014, inclusive. The system is already open for registration, submission of applications will start on February 6, 2014.

For more information about Microsoft Research Summer School, please visit cloud2014.cs.msu.ru/en/ab/about . You can apply for participation at cmt.research.microsoft.com/msrcloud2014
