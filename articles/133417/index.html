Hello! My name is Roman Novikov, I am the project manager of Platforma@Mail.Ru. Today we will talk about what constitutes our platform today, what has happened interesting lately and what is planned for the future.



Let's imagine what the Platform is, based on how the ancient people represented this world: imagine a turtle that flies in space. The turtle is our API through which we give out information about users. It has three large elephants. The first is social products for sites: like buttons, widgets and authorization for sites. Another elephant is external clients and applications: APIs for a variety of mobile platforms, thanks to which mobile clients can use social networking features. The biggest elephant is, of course, a platform for social games. And on these three elephants all applications that use Mail.Ru resources are kept.



Frankly speaking, our API is not a turtle at all, but a smart hare. It allows you to process thousands of requests per second, and we are going to make it more productive and faster this year. We are constantly improving the API and introducing advanced technical solutions.





Social products



One of the main events of the year is the integration of our social networks My World and Odnoklassniki. One of the fruits of this integration was the general block of buttons "Like". Using one simple block of code, you can insert two buttons on your website at once, with the help of which users will be able to share the content of your website with friends on social networks Odnoklassniki and My World.



As the statistics showed, the CTR of such a unit grew by 15%. The shows of the new block already account for more than 40 million hits a day. Over the last month, the increase in the number of "ball" was 60%. With these buttons you get access to the audience of the largest social networks. We specifically worked for several months to ensure that you do not need to customize when installing the buttons. Copied the code - and it all worked! We will do the rest of the work for you. ‚ÄúSharing‚Äù in one click, no pop-ups, the user does not leave your site, and the information enters the social network instantly. Everything is fast, easy and convenient.



If we talk about common figures, our social plugins are shown more than 250 million times. in a day!



By clicking on the balls, 5 million 800 thousand users from our networks return to your sites daily!



We also have solutions for authorization on sites. Today, those who write their own authorization do wrong. Use social networking solutions! I think 99% of those sitting in this room have social media accounts. I was right: -) Draw conclusions - do not want to torment users: use our authorization. Our statistics show that the user is gradually learning this and is no longer afraid of logging in to third-party sites using the Mail.Ru account. In addition, you can receive additional information about the authorized user and his friends through our API.



Ten thousand sites have already established authorization. New sites are added very quickly. Join and you!



Our main goal this year is to take the first place in the coverage of the Like buttons on the Runet.



The goal is ambitious, complex and aggressive. But feasible. And we will do everything to fulfill it.

In addition, we want to make a single authorization using Mail.Ru and Odnoklassniki accounts. For you, this will open access to a huge audience that can log in to your site using our social networks accounts.



External applications and clients



Now about external applications. We fully supported the OAuth 2.0 protocol, provided the core API functionality for implementing mobile clients, and supported third-party libraries.



A vivid example of a mobile client using social networking features is Agent@Mail.Ru application for iPhone and Android, which allows you to quickly publish photos on a social network. An interesting frame was caught, and right there your friends in My World see it.



What are our goals? We will focus more on the mobile direction - for us it is a strategic task.

So that developers can make full-featured clients, we will try to maximize coverage of the social networking API. And besides that, the platform will provide full support for developers of third-party libraries and the SDK.



If you have any ideas - write to us, we will definitely help you.



Social games



Now the most interesting part is social games. This is one of the most powerful drivers for the development of our platform.



On the day, 3 million users play social games in My World, and 7 million users in Odnoklassniki. The average check that a user pays in My World is 141 rubles a day. And many pay more.

Social apps are serious business. If you earn big money in this business, then you are already working in My World and Odnoklassniki. If you want to start earning them - come to us.



How have we achieved such success?

We have launched a new application catalog, completely changing it. Top applications are now built on the basis of ARPU, turnover and daily audience. And we will soon add retention to this formula.



We have altered the section "New". Now they are sorted not just by the release date, but also taking into account the time spent in the catalog, and it works very well.
