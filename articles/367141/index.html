Kibiro - cute robot friend



Every day, the possibilities of robotics surprise us more and more, which proves once again: there really is no limit to the development of the industry. We hurry to tell about one more amazing development of the Japanese - Kibiro .



Kibiro has a humanoid body with moving limbs and a head, equipped with a camera, a speaker and a microphone. The robot recognizes the voice of the host, can not just keep the conversation going, but also gather important information from it. Due to the presence of artificial intelligence, the machine can systematize your tastes in order to give necessary advice if necessary.

The task of the robot is to always know what the owner likes and what does not. All figures from the memory of the car are placed in the catalog, these data allow you to choose a good institution for dinner or a movie that fits the mood of a person.



And Kibiro can send emails to the host and communicate with him on social networks.

The creators of UBIC and Vstone made a robot that dissolves in human life in order to decorate its everyday life. Communicating with the owner, Kibiro remembers information about his affairs and preferences, so that at the right moment he can give a hint about what he might like. As a result, a person learns to be surprised at himself.



The device weighs only eight hundred grams, and its height is about thirty centimeters. Such compact dimensions allow you to hold the structure on your hands or set it on the table.

Emotions and states are transmitted via LED indicators, and communication with other devices is possible using connections via Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, HDMI and USB.

Commercial and public organizations will become owners of such androids already at the beginning of 2016, and in the middle of the year, sales of robots will start for everyone who can purchase it at a price not higher than $ 1.2 thousand.
