European VAT: what awaits developers in Europe

From January 1, 2015, new rules governing the determination and payment of VAT in the countries of the European Union come into force. The VAT rate for all companies selling B2C sales will not depend on the place of registration of the seller, but on the location of the consumer. That is, when selling the same virtual item, the tax rate will be different for players from different EU countries.
