Founder's advice. Anton Gladkoborodov, CEO Coub

On the "Megamind" again "Founder's Tips" . This time we talked with Anton Gladkoborodov - one of the creators of the Coub service.

Coub was founded in April 2012 by the brothers Anton and Igor Gladkoborodov and Mikhail Tabunov. The name of the service comes from the word "Cobb" - the protagonist of the film "Inception". In 2013, the company's office moved to New York. Funding to date is $ 3.5 million, the monthly attendance of more than 50 million unique users.

Coub allows users to publish short looped videos lasting up to 10 seconds (koubas).
