Opera's micro-blog at Runet Blog 2010



Another contest started in Runet, the purpose of which is to determine the most popular Russian-language blog. I think Opera users will be interested to know that one of the nominees of this competition was the official Russian-language Opera micro-blog , posted on the popular Twitter service. This channel, which already has almost a thousand subscribers, reports the latest information on Opera browsers and the process of their development, and also often publishes insider information from Opera Software employees. Everyone is welcome to vote for this micro-blog on the contest page .
