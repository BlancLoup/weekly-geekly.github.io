Myanimelist - Social network for anime artists

The other day I decided to create a list of scanned anime, I was offered to help one site myanimelist.net .
By registering, I found a nice interface, somewhat reminiscent of Vkontakte.
The sheet itself consists of several summary tables:
1 watching;
2 comleted;
3 on-hold;
4 droped.
My example: myanimelist.net/animelist/glamball
And the same for manga.

Adding is done through a search, or you can click add in someone's sheet.
It is also possible to add friends, keep a blog, be in clubs ...
Huh ... I do not know what else to say ... YA!
