Google glass still alive



Google Glass was widely presented to the public in 2012 and aroused considerable interest, including as one of Google‚Äôs products. Later, a group of selected participants of the Explorer Edition program had at their disposal for testing instances of points for the price of $ 1500.

On the past, messages began to appear on the Internet (probably not for nothing) that the Google Glass project did not leave the Explorer Edition stage and its fate was generally not enviable. Not only because the number of news about the project has declined, but also for a number of specific reasons: for example, project founder Babak Parviz (Babak Parviz) left Google and now works at Amazon, technical observers at Reuters claim that many developers have abandoned projects for Glass (including Twitter), and Glass Collective , a joint initiative to create applications, has quietly disappeared altogether.
