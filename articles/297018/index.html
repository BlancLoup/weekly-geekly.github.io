Business model


We initially assumed that it was not very correct to give into the inept hands of a newbie partner rather complicated technological solution without further sales support. We are interested in our cloud PBX being sold and not looking at the world through the eyes of another half-forgotten landing page. We are interested in the market reaction to the product and we want to develop it on the basis of a live picture, and not just roadmaps born in the depths of the analytical department.
