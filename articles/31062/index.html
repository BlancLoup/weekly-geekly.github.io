Web 2.0 in the office - a social table


We do not get tired of looking for stunning and amazing solutions in all branches of human life. Now touch work. Who among us boasts a comfortable workplace? Of course, over time, we all try to make it as convenient as possible, but in this news we would like to offer a fundamentally new look at your space from the Bouroullec brothers

Of course, you could call it a table or even a furniture system, but rather what you see is the name of the office platform.

Instead of a multitude of closed rooms, isolated from each other by partitions (which were taken so far - approx. Translators), the brothers offered a workplace where all colleagues sit next to each other at tables that are assembled on a modular basis. This opens up new opportunities for communication, discussion of problems and interaction with colleagues in general.
