Selection of podcasts and video sites for content marketing, interface design and usability

Last week we talked about books that could be useful for designers and content marketers. Today we decided to see additional sources on the topic in audio and video format.


Photo francois schnell CC / Flickr


Podcasts


1. Social Media Happy Hour
Two specialists each week talk about the intricacies, approaches and strategies of content marketing, as well as discuss mistakes in the field of the same name.

2. Growth Byte
This series of podcasts will help to create an image of your potential client and understand how to interact with it and with the help of which letters. Especially interesting may be questions of targeting, conversion and the ability to understand the train of thought of your client.

3. WP Curve
You will be told about the basics of working in Wordpress, will explain in detail the subtleties of using the platform and will tell you about common mistakes made by both novice and advanced users.

4. The McMethod
The leading and invited experts - entrepreneurs, talk about the nuances of work in the field of e-mail marketing. Here are just a few popular topics for discussion: attracting customers, creating quality mailings, increasing sales, etc.

5. Art Version
Mailing lists, content marketing, interface design, optimization issues, etc. - a small part of an impressive list of topics that the moderator tells in an accessible form.

6. ‚ÄúExperienced Way‚Äù
A Russian-speaking host will talk about the business as a whole, about advertising, as well as about all phases of work in the field of email marketing.

7. Amy Porterfield
This podcast focuses on attracting and retaining users. Many issues are focused precisely on how to develop your business, taking care of the comfort and experience of customers.

8. UX Podcast
The two presenters talk about the pros and cons of different approaches to improving user experience and the corresponding improvement tools.

9. UXpod
A series of podcasts about creating content and improving user experience, interacting with the user, studying the audience and its needs, developing design strategies.

10. UX Radio
A transfer that emphasizes the importance of customer participation in the development of UX. The subject of the airs is generally limited to the study of customer needs and the ability to distinguish good UX from bad. The author often argues on topical issues: a reassessment of values ‚Äã‚Äãin society, leading to a change in needs (and what designers and marketers should do about it).

11. UI Breakfast
Presenters and guests of the program consider such issues as: direct influence of the interface on site traffic, gamification and interactive, user motivation, psychological principles of persuasive design technology, etc.

12. User Interface Engineering
These podcasts are divided into two topics. 1. Basic theoretical questions of design and improvement of user experience (for example, how to achieve the responsive design effect for your page?); 2. Practical advice on creating literate texts and page design, including links to the necessary software, tools, books, etc.

13. The Web Ahead
The presenter talks about news in the field of user interfaces and the needs of the audience and makes assumptions about how they will change in the future.

14. "Metric"
The podcasts of this show cover a variety of topics, the main ones: design and storytelling issues in the UX, technological trends and platforms, organization and motivation in work, design and strategic component.
