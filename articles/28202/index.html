Windows Live Writer



Hello again friends. I will try in this article to tell you about such a convenient thing as Windows Live Writer.

That's what Live.com says to us (the program can be downloaded from there)

Windows Live Writer is a desktop application that makes it easy to publish a variety of content on a blog.

Posting entries in leading blog services
Supported services are Windows Live Spaces, SharePoint, Wordpress, Blogger and many others. Still no blog? Writer helps you create it!

Create a great blog without any effort
Instantly insert photos and videos, tags and a huge amount of other great content. Powerful editing features include spreadsheets, spell checker and quick hyperlinks.
View posts before posting to the blog.
Writer shows you how a blog entry will look like before it is published, using templates and blog formatting.

Create your records offline
Publish when connected to the network.

And you know, I agree with him, this is really a very convenient and free program. I myself was surprised when I saw. Let's take a look at the interface:



Consider the most interesting commands:

Print - you can print your work

Preview - or you can view the article before publishing.

Post to blog - automatically publishes the developed article in the blog.

With the help of the program, you can without any problems insert hyperlinks, tags, tables, images, as well as video to the article. A special feature is the insert card.

Windows Live Writer has very good help as well as a community forum where you can always get help with questions.
