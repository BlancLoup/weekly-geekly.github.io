Innovations YouSticker


Analyzing the users' stickers, we came to the conclusion that most of them are devoted to various kinds of media information. Without thinking twice, we introduced the ability to attach pictures, video and audio, and quotes directly to the stickers.
This functionality is very simple to use. If you decide to add any picture or flash drive, you need to load a sticker, hover your mouse over the picture and the YS icon will appear above the picture below.



To create quotes it is still easier - you just need to select the desired text and the same YS icon will appear next to the cursor.

Widget yousticker


A small js-code installed inside the pages of your site will show a stylish YouSticker slider. With it, your users get the opportunity to leave stickers on pages and view other people's stickers. You do not need to have a YouSticker account.

Why a widget can be useful for webmasters?
Elementary, it will allow to receive fast feedback from users. We have tried to give an opportunity to any visitor of your site to easily comment on the resource pages. You, as an administrator, get full control over your site‚Äôs stickers. It is also an opportunity to get additional traffic;)
