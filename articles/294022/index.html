Operators are asked to introduce building codes to house equipment with several Internet providers.

The Ministry of Communications and representatives of operators discussed changes in building codes and regulations (SNiP). According to telecom operators, when designing new homes, it is necessary to provide infrastructure for the work of three or four Internet providers.

Communications must be adapted to accommodate Internet and television equipment in a special room, as well as networks of different standards - Ethernet , coaxial, optical.

The new rules could remove an additional barrier to access to services, expand the choice of their suppliers, believes Dmitry Petrov, director of relations with the legislative and executive authorities of MegaFon . The introduction of new standards is also supported by "Akado" and "VimpelCom" , say their representatives.

Now the SNiPs regulate the deployment of only one operator, and that fixed one, notes Dmitry Solodovnikov, MTS representative. The new SNiPs can also provide for the placement of equipment of several cellular operators. The representative of the Ministry of Construction claims that the position of the Ministry of Communications is "not sufficiently reasoned." Innovations will increase the cost of construction, he believes.
