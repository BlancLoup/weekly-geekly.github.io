AgileCamp 2013 Report

If you want to read about the contents of the Agile Camp and the marshmallow challenge, the simulation of Scrum, Business Model Canvas and people, welcome to the cat.

KDPV






Getting to AgileCamp 2013, which ScrumTrek spent in Taganrog, was not so easy. I had to fly through Rostov and get from Rostov by train with the working class. But when the camp began, I never regretted the time spent on the road. The opening was unnoticed, as should be the opening of events. And everyone went their tracks. I stayed on the process track. I could probably do the engineering track myself. The track began with a fun "seating". It was very difficult to decide between "smart" and "beautiful" - a physicator and a generator of ideas. As a result, at each table as part of each team, there was one facilitator and an idea generator.
The first exercise was the presentation. It was necessary to interview a neighbor. Ask the name, surname, company and three facts about the person, and then present it to the team. A similar exercise was at Induction training at AT Consulting.

Marshmallow challenge




After dating - immediately into battle. Known in narrow circles marshmellow challenge was new to me. The essence of the marshmellow challenge is that the team must build a tower of several macoronones, scotch, strings and marshmallows, so that it would be higher than the other teams and stand as long as possible. It was difficult to convince the team to try and build a small tower first, as early as possible, and then improve it. The fact that it was necessary to do just that coach and told after the challenge. The guys from my team tried to read when measuring the height of the tower, but they failed.
