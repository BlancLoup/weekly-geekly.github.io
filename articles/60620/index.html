Skype 4.1 beta: Screen Sharing now for Windows

In pursuit of Skype 4.0, Skype 4.1 for Windows is in a hurry. It added the function of screen sharing, which many have asked since it was introduced in Skype 2.8 Beta for Mac, and some other innovations.

Screen sharing

Have you ever tried to help your friend deal with his computer? Or show a colleague a presentation? Or to help a fellow student with his essay? Or show some tricky trick in the game? With the screen sharing feature in Skype, it becomes very simple. Just click on the Share menu icon in the Skype conversation window.

Using this beta version, you can show what is happening on your screen to any Skype user on a Windows, Mac or Linux platform. You can show the whole screen, or only a part of it, and everything is completely free.
