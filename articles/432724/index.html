Shining 3D EinScan Pro 2X Plus 3D Scanner Review



Shining 3D has introduced a new generation of multi-functional portable 3D scanners EinScan Pro 2X Plus . According to the manufacturers, the device received significant improvements in scanning speed, accuracy and functionality, compared with the previous model of the line.

About scanner


Shining 3D EinScan Pro 2X Plus

  • Operating systems: Win 7, Win 8, Win 10, 64 bit
  • Weight: 1.13 kg
  • File Formats: OBJ, STL, ASC, PLY, P3
  • Ability to capture textures: yes (with color scanning module)
  • Scan time: <0.5 s

Description


Compact size and low weight allow you to take the device with you and easily connect it to a laptop to scan the necessary object even in the field.



The weight of a 3D scanner is a little over a kilogram. EinScan Pro 2X Plus is able to scan and process up to 1.5 million points at 30 frames per second every second - the speed is one and a half times faster than its predecessor. Data transfer is carried out via high-speed USB 3.0 interface.



The 3D scanner supports several scanning modes: fast manual, manual high-precision, automatic - using a rotary table, and stationary. Also, the developers have provided several alignment modes: automatic, by markers, by marks on the turntable and manual.



The scanning accuracy of the EinScan Pro 2X Plus in automatic and stationary modes is 0.04 mm, and when using markers in the manual scanning mode, it is 0.05 mm + 0.3 mm / m. The minimum distance between the scanned points is 0.2 mm. This allows you to receive data with high detail, even if the model contains very small elements. The working distance when working with a 3D scanner is 510 mm.



For EinScan Pro 2X Plus there are several useful options that help to improve the quality of scanning in many ways. Among the available kits is a color scan module with an optional Color Pack camera.



Industrial set with turntable and tripod Industrial Pack.



HD Prime Pack is another additional camera to increase accuracy and capture details.



The standard set with a 3D scanner includes: a calibration panel, markers, a cable for connecting to a computer, a power supply unit, and a USB flash drive with the latest software.



Production and reverse engineering, 3D modeling of personalized products, art and restoration, design, healthcare, research and education, virtual visualization - the EinScan Pro 2X Plus scanner will find application in many areas.



To work with the device, you need a computer or laptop with Windows 7 or newer operating system, at least 16 GB of RAM, and an NVIDIA GTX770 video card with 4 GB of video memory. The minimum processor requirements are Intel Core i7.

Total


The EinScan Pro 2X Plus scanner is a professional scanner that can be used in education, reverse engineering, metrological control, the energy industry, people scanning and architecture.
