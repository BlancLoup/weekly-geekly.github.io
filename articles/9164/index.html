You do not want to constantly carry with you a heavy laptop, and the screen of your smartphone is not enough for comfortable viewing of mail and web pages? Then the new Palm Foleo is just what you need.

A 10-inch diagonal screen, a full-sized keyboard and instant device power-on are perhaps the most interesting features of the new Palm Foleo, recently announced on the company's website.

This gadget is positioned as an extension to the smartphone, but can be used separately.

The device will allow you to connect to WiFi networks, use your phone / smartphone via Bluetooth as a modem, check / synchronize mail, visit websites, create and edit documents (Word, Excel, PP), view PDFs and pictures.

Unfortunately, the detailed specifications of the device are not yet known.
