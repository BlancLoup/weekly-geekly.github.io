New parts lived less original, needed a permanent repair, but for good parts just did not have the desire to spend money, it's still a budget bike. It was decided to buy a new one with more serious attachments for more specific tasks - to drive around the city in comfort. They became Fuji Crosstown 1.1, but this post is not about him.
