Salaries of "top" IT companies again hit the top



A study by the recruiting agency GlobalCareer shows that the salaries of top managers in IT companies remain among the highest in comparison with other industries. The researchers analyzed compensation packages for top managers and senior executives in the 60 largest Moscow companies from the IT and telecommunications sectors.

They considered companies with an annual turnover of at least 500 million rubles, both with headquarters in Russia and representative offices of foreign companies (there were 13% of those surveyed).

The total amount of remuneration of the general director for one month is 1.14-2.66 million rubles. This is due to the size of the annual bonus. : it averages 90% of annual income. In addition to the size of the business, the main factors affecting the amount of compensation for the CEO, GlobalCareer experts include the number of managed divisions.
Executive directors' salaries range from 450,000 to 1 million rubles per month, and the annual bonus is on average 40% of the fixed payments per year. The CIO earns an average of 400,000 to 900,000 rubles a month, and its annual bonus averages 70% of the basic salary.
Salaries in the amount of 1 million rubles are comparable to the income of the vice-presidents of companies in the oil and gas industry. In the automotive industry, such salaries are held by some managers of large corporations, but this is still not a common phenomenon in this industry, Yury Kuzmenko, director for work with key clients of Ancor FinTech, told Vedomosti.

The GlobalCareer data looks plausible, says Mark Rozin, managing partner of Ecopsy Consulting. Elevation to the West




The US News & World Report published a ranking of the most promising professions in the USA in 2017.

IT manager is located in this ranking at the 13th position with a salary of $ 132 thousand.

Top 10 best technological professions, according to the US News & World Report looks like this:

  1. Computer systems analyst, salary - $ 86 thousand, 2.4% of the unemployed.
  2. Developer, $ 98 thousand, 2%.
  3. IT manager, $ 131 thousand, 1.9%.

  6. Database administrator, $ 82 thousand, 1.0%.
  7. Security analyst, $ 90 thousand, 3.9%.

Historically


The salaries of American top managers are 130 times higher than the average salary of ordinary employees. This was discussed at the World Economic Forum, reports RNS.
Since the 1970s, payments to top management in America have grown exponentially. Since then, they have grown by 1000%. Payments to staff and workers increased by 11% over the same period (adjusted for inflation).
20 years ago, a top manager received 45 times more cash than an employee, now the gap has increased to 130. Statistics refers to large public companies.

PS Statistics show that, on the whole, average wage rates for the European Union are 15 percent lower than those in the United States.
