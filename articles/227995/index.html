ASUS fonepad 7 LTE tablet

Over the past few years, tablets have firmly entered our lives. The most compact and popular Android tablets have a screen of 7 ". Such tablets are produced a lot, and it is difficult to surprise with something new in this format. Therefore, ASUS has just combined the best of seven-inch cards with its new fonepad 7 LTE model (ME372CL). tablets, and added something else.



The main features of this model are stereo speakers on the front panel and support for LTE. There is a similar model without LTE: fonepad 7 (ME372CG) .

Specifications


Operating system: Android 4.3;
Processor: Intel Atom Z2560 (1.6 GHz, 2 cores, HyperThreading);
Video accelerator: PowerVR SGX544MP2;
Memory: 1 GB RAM, 16 GB flash;
Screen: 7 ", 1280x800, IPS, capacitive MultiTouch, oleophobic coating ;
Connectivity: GSM / GPRS / EDGE (850/900/1800/1900 MHz), 3G / WCDMA / DC-HSPA + (850/900/2100 MHz), 4G / LTE (800/900/1800/2600 MHz), Wi- Fi 802.11 a / b / g / n, Bluetooth 4.0, Miracast;
Camera: main 5 megapixel without flash, front: 1.2 megapixel;
Optional: GPS / AGPS / GLONASS, G-sensor, compass, hall sensor, proximity sensor, light sensor, stereo speakers on the front panel;
Interfaces: Micro USB with OTG support, 3.5 mm audio jack (headphones + microphone), MicroSD, MicroSim;
Battery: Li-polymer 15 Wh (4000 mAh);
Dimensions and weight: 198x120x10,5 mm, 333 gr.

Design and scope of delivery


In the box are a tablet, power adapter (5.2 V, 1.35 A), USB-MicoUSB cable, instruction.



The screen glass has an oleophobic coating that makes it difficult for fingerprints to appear. Along the edges of the screen are two stereo speakers. ASUS FonePad 7 is one of the few tablets that allows you to listen to music and watch videos with full stereo sound without connecting headphones. Above (when the portrait orientation of the tablet) is the front camera, proximity sensors and light.

MicroUSB connector is on the left at the bottom. There is also a microphone hole.



At the top there is an audio jack.



On the left side - MicroSIM socket, on the right - MicroSD socket, power and volume buttons.



On the back side is the main camera, protruding 1.2 mm (taking into account the camera, the thickness of the tablet is almost 12 mm). The case is glossy and gets dirty very quickly.
