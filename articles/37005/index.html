I want to suggest options for keeping your thoughts:
  • Voice recorder on cell phone. Just dictate and save to your phone;
  • Write an SMS and save it as a draft;
  • Write an e-mail from your PDA / Communicator and send it to your working E-mail;
  • Write text on a piece of paper and take a picture of the camera on the phone (do not forget to turn on the macro mode for a clearer picture), and then destroy the leaflet (tear it up or burn it);
  • Call your home and leave a voice recording on your answering machine;

If none of the 5 options turned out to be suitable for you - take a piece of paper with a pen, but do not forget to write down where you removed the leaf with a brilliant idea ;-)
