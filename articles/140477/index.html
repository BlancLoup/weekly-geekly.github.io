2GIS Online updated once again! The March update introduces several useful changes.

Firstly, the company‚Äôs operating hours in the directory are now displayed in a simpler and more convenient form. Previously, users were given a table with the organization‚Äôs work schedule for the entire week, even if this schedule was the same on all days. The new view allowed us to abandon it, except for the most difficult cases. According to the statistics of 2GIS, companies working daily on the same schedule - 30%, and companies having a lunch break - 9%. Almost 95% of organizations have no more than three modes of operation (Mon-Fri, Sat, Sun). Thus, in most cases, the new form of presentation is more convenient than the old schedule.
