DataTalks # 3: Online Surveys

The third meeting of the DataTalks community will take place on July 23.

This time we will talk about how to correctly collect data using online surveys. We will discuss the possibilities and limitations of this method, as well as share the secrets and trends from the research of user preferences.
The representatives of the companies Wargaming, Studio Sorokina and Kulinkovich, as well as the Deputy Dean for Science of the National Research University Higher School of Economics, will share the secrets of conducting and processing the results of online surveys.
