Break the iPhone in this case - get a new one for free


This is the slogan of the new Cellhelmet protective campaign advertising campaign from Cellpig. It all started as a project on Kickstarter`e, but now the product has gone into mass production and is available in stores .

Mike Caine, head of Cellhelmet:

- In general, we wanted to make the most unkillable of all non-killed covers. In the development process, we realized that it turns out just another hefty bulky case. Legion them. So we dropped the idea. And then we decided that it would be better if we made an aesthetic thin case, but provided support for the product in the form of a promotion - break the iPhone in our case and get a new one for free. Or you will repair this for free.



I waited a long time that he would attach the phone well to the video. Or at least drop it. But no.

What is the result? The only case on the market, when buying a case, the warranty covers not only the case itself, but also the phone.

If the phone in the case broke due to damage (here is a small lyrical digression - the damage due to water is not covered by the warranty), the company will give you another one. The phone will be replaced or repaired within three days from the date of appeal. That is, for $ 50 (the cost of the cover), you get 1 year of such a guarantee.

Apple still offers AppleCare +, the insurance rate looks like this - $ 99 per year for the program itself, $ 49 for each repair after damage. Well, 90 days of technical support by phone. So, Cellhelmet is quite able to win over to itself all those who have ended the warranty AppleCare + (and who do not want to renew it), as well as the owners of jailbroken devices.

Cover case - dense rubber and polyurethane.

- unlimited repairs during the warranty period
- one replacement during the warranty period
- original spare parts
- US & Canada only
