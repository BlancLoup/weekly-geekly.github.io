open production - arguments for skeptics

We continue to consider the subject of open production . The first feedback appears, questions, doubts, assumptions. A part can already be grouped together - ‚Äúdoubts about the feasibility‚Äù, and so, especially for skeptics who consider the idea to be utopian, I begin to give examples of realizations of the ideas of open production .
In order not to mix different things (DIY, peer-to-peer, custom-made production) the main requirements for projects that are suitable for examples:
1. Disclosure of intellectual property (open).
2. Belonging of a product to objects of the real world (metaphorically - not bits, but atoms)
3. Practical value, competitiveness, expediency.

1. Development of equipment with open architecture Open Source Hardware (OSHW). The topic on this topic has already been posted on Habr√© Developers of "free iron", earned more than $ 1 million .

There are areas that are fundamentally suitable for the selected criteria, but apparently due to the tradition of not being isolated as an open source, you can find a lot of house projects in the network that can be implemented, including by subcontracting organizations. Here the separation of information and material component has developed historically. Architects and builders worked without a hard link within the same enterprise, effective architectural solutions were instantiated many times.

Highlighting the common denominator between the above open source development directions, we can definitely say that the greatest results will be achieved in areas where the cost of instantiating a single product differs little from mass production.
