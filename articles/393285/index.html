Huawei Technologies. Great combination of price / quality

To press the leaders of the mobile market, introducing new technologies, making high-quality smartphones at affordable prices, while using the best practices of world designers - possible! And all this is the Chinese manufacturers of Huawei.



A bit of history from Huawei Technologies


Huawei Technologies supplies new generation telecommunication networks, cooperates with the majority of world-wide operators, was founded back in 1988, and has its headquarters in Shenzhen. The company is engaged in the development of technology for wireless networks LTE, CDMA, GPRS, GSM. In addition, Huawei specializes in the production of network devices - routers, modems, switches. For our market, the company is better known as a manufacturer of inexpensive, but almost in no way inferior in quality to top brands and smartphones.

In February 2009, at the World Mobile Congress (Mobile World Congress) in Spain, Huawei presents its first smartphone on the Android platform and announced a partnership with T-mobile, whose goal was to promote the smartphone on the market. An interesting fact - in 2010, Huawei's supply volume exceeded 100 million mobile broadband access devices, and took up almost 50% of the global market. Huawei occupies a leading position in the market of mobile broadband access devices.

In 2010 , at the International Exhibition of Household Appliances and Electronics (IFA) in Berlin, Huawei Technologies introduced IDEOS, the world's first smartphone on the Android 2.2 platform of Google. This device is a "guglofon" entry-level, mainly focused on the younger generation, users who only took the first steps in mastering the Android operating system, but did not want to immediately purchase an expensive device.
