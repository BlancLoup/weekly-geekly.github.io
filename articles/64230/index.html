iWallet - wallet with memory


What do you have in your wallet? Well, except for money, of course ... Although by today's standards, the phrase "other than money" sounds especially mocking. :)
Surely many in their wallet is a photo of a loved one, child, or pet. Often, after some time the photo bothers or loses its relevance and I want to update it. Having found the necessary picture in the archives of digital photos, you have to print it first, and only then put it in your wallet. For owners of iWallet this problem does not exist. The fact is that iWallet is a wallet with an integrated photo viewer. The image is displayed on a small LCD monitor. The device has the ability to connect to USB. Images can be accompanied by audio descriptions.

Characteristics and completeness of iWallet:

* Black leather wallet
* Removable multimedia viewer
* USB support
* Memory 16 MB
* Built-in rechargeable battery
* USB cable and installation software
* Dimensions: Height - 12.0 cm Width - 9.0 cm Thickness - 2.0 cm
* Weight: 0.1Kg
