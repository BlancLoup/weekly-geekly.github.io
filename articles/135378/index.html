Well, a very secure USB flash drive IStorage datAshur



On Habr√© more than once and not two already published news about flash drives, the data on which are in relative safety. There is also a hardware, so to speak, protection ( flash-cryptex example), and software, in the form of AES encryption standard. In general, it is difficult to come up with something else, besides what has already been invented in terms of protecting USB flash drives. But iStorage recently offered another interesting solution.

The new model of protected flash drive is called IStorage datAshur. The information here is protected, first of all, by the encryption standard that is also used by the military - this is 256-bit AES. In addition, in order to access the information stored on this carrier, you must enter the PIN correctly (alphanumeric, from 7 to 15 characters). The flash drive also supports several access levels, including root and guest access, so to speak.



An interesting feature is the ability to instantly destroy all user data. Manufacturers have provided protection against brute force combinations, i.e. The flash drive is protected from Brute Force. According to the manufacturers, the device is compatible with all popular operating systems, and additional drivers for operating systems are not required.



Of course, the flash drive is also protected from accidental damage, it is shockproof and waterproof. Interestingly, the cost of the carrier is not particularly high, as one would expect. The IStorage datAshur lineup includes three models, with 4, 8 and 16 GB of memory. The cost of the carrier, respectively, is 39, 59 and 79 euros.
