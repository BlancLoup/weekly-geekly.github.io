The general principle - the more experience and material, the more attention to the details of the type of name.

The speaker is not a servant of the audience.

The first task of the speaker is to read the report in the best way (it depends only on him), the second is that the report is accepted by the audience (here a fair amount of luck, because we are talking about the first speeches). One way to depend less on the mood of the audience: to tell one person in the hall. Quickly choose a victim and speak to her, it is better to put such a person on board.

None of the professional speakers will change the technical / professional report if they have not reached the audience. It is better to read a report well past the audience than a bad reading past the audience.
