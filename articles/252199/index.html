Zend Certification. Not so devil

I would like to share with a respected community my experience in obtaining the coveted Zend certificate.

It's no secret that numerous certifications, and ZCPE (Zend Certified PHP Engineer), in particular, are treated differently: someone collects them and hangs them on the walls for narcissism, someone considers it a waste of time and money .

Do not forget that the test is mostly artificial and theoretical, and you can pass it without serious professional experience, and therefore the main value of certification for you personally consists in conscientious full-fledged study of theoretical material during preparation.

But soon it was time to take hold of the mind. After answering all the questions, I had 35 minutes to review and double-check all the tasks. Thanks to this, several errors were found. The program for testing twice asked if I really wanted to complete the test, since I still had time, and I was persistent. A second of waiting when impressionable people should not look at the screen. We open our eyes, look at the screen and become resigned to fate.
