Free conference broadcast on the Internet of Things InoThings ++



Tomorrow, on January 30, our first conference on the Internet of Things InoThings ++ will take place and there will be an open broadcast on our Youtube channel :

The full schedule is available on the website , and below we will tell you about the most interesting reports.

The conference will begin with a presentation by Andrei Tsislav (CPT), in which he promises to tell about the development of IoT devices what is not written in the books . For example, the importance of writing API at all levels and the benefits of universal subscriber devices and base stations.

And further on the rise: technological issues, real cases, legal aspects.

LPWAN Rival Technology


Hall number 1, 16:00

Understanding the strengths and weaknesses of each of the competing technologies of LPWAN networks becomes critical for the successful implementation of specific IoT projects. Oleg Artamonov (Unwired Devices LLC) in his report will discuss both technical features and the following functional limitations of the main technologies, as well as examples of projects. such as: street lighting control, remote metering of testimony in the housing and utilities sector, monitoring in agriculture and animal husbandry, indicating the reasons for choosing one technology or another.

Antipattern software development for the Internet of Things


Hall number 1, 13:00

Sergey Aksyonov, on behalf of Strizh Telematic, wants to share with other developers experience, warn against pitfalls and tell how the company has overcome the difficulties associated with growth and development:

  • why the data storage and processing systems, standard for other areas, do not work;
  • how not to test the device before sending it to the series;
  • why waste time searching and researching ready-made solutions;
  • for which it is necessary to minimize the number of used platforms and technologies;
  • which means the letter "S" in the abbreviation "IoT".

How IoT hacks our legal system: dismantling the age-old legal foundations in three cases


Hall number 2, 17:00

Kirill Mityagin from Nevsky IP Law proposes to disassemble the three main areas that IoT has encountered in legal practice , and which are of value from a practical point of view. To make legal material easier to understand, there will be examples for everything.

Samsung IoT Academy - what can we give to the market?


Hall number 2, 18:00

Tatiana Volkova from Samsung will talk about the Samsung IoT Academy Academy educational project , the tasks of teaching technical technical students (the project is already working at MIPT and MIREA), and how industry representatives can participate in it.
