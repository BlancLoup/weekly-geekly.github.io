Excessive demand for Belarusian rubles (WMB)

In just the last couple of days, the revenues of Belarusian IT specialists who earn WMZ fell sharply by 20%. The reason is Russian bookmakers and new Russian laws.

From July 2 to July 7, the dollar exchange rate on the stock exchange fell from 2920-2930 to 2500 Belarusian rubles (WMB), today the exchange rate has partially won back the positions to 2650. The list of applications contains amounts in tens of thousands of dollars, although earlier daily turnover for this pair of currencies did not exceed three or four thousand.

Who collapsed the dollar in Belarus? Only now it became clear that blame ... the bookmakers, which since this month, the Russian authorities have blocked oxygen. Here, read the news of July 2 on the website of the largest Russian bookmaker's office "Marathon".



Let us hope that the demand for Belarusian rubles will soon run out, and the rate will return to the usual 2,920 rubles. Most likely, this will happen during the week, when bookmakers will be able to arrange acceptance of rates in the WMZ currency, which is more familiar to Russians. Well, some Belarusians will be able to earn good money this week by buying dollars at an unusually low exchange rate.
