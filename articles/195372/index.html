364 days for the iPhone - a fun calendar for every day

Dear friends!



Autumn has come and I want to add fun and adventure to my life. Together with Arriba! creative agency we made a wonderful free iPhone especially for you - a calendar that will allow you to start every morning with a smile and perform funny tasks during the day!

And now a little more detail.

Every day - a new task


The traditional calendar is sad. We are sure that every day of the year is an opportunity to make your life brighter, more fun, more unusual! Therefore, we have come up with an endless calendar with 364 tasks for each day, which are guaranteed to cheer up, will be of interest to people of any gender, age and occupation, can be performed at home, at work, in public places, alone or in the company of friends.






Ranking position


We are very proud that in the two days of our presence in the Ukrainian AppStore, we hit the first place in Lifestyle and the sixth in the general category of free applications. 6 days after the release, we ranked the Top-1 in the Store (free). Perhaps this is not the greatest achievement for the project, but we, as beginners, are proud of them and believe that with time we will be able to overcome the Russian store.

Project development


Now we are working on a bug-fix release and add sharing in the cozy Vkontaktik (completely forgot about it). Soon we will begin work on creating a new, much more advanced version of the calendar, in which users will have the opportunity to share the results of completed tasks and look at how they are performed by other users. And at the beginning of 2014, the appearance of the application will take on a completely different look.

Joy and warmth to you!
