Announcement of the Apple Tablet Event Audio Broadcast



Caution! If you do not like Apple products, I advise you not to read Habrahabr, Twitter and news blogs in the next few days. Tonight at 21:00 Moscow time and at 20:00 in Kiev and Minsk , an Apple Event will take place under the slogan: "Come see our latest creation" (come and see our new creation). Presumably there will present a new package iLife'10, firmware 4.0 for the iPhone and one more thing ... the long-awaited tablet from Apple.

To listen


On this occasion, Dean , Arina and I decided to conduct an audio broadcast , especially since we already have similar experience in the past. We do not pretend to an expert opinion, but we will cover this event from the standpoint of ordinary makovodov. Come, it will be very interesting. For those who do not have time to broadcast, we are going to record the broadcast.

It is worth saying in advance that none of us are present at the event itself. We will translate live what foreign journalists write who are directly in the Yerba Buena building and cover the event, and add their comments.

Audio broadcast will begin 15-30 minutes before the official event. Re- broadcasts of official broadcasting are also planned.
