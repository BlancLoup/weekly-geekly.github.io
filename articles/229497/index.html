Welcome to GeekFest Night July 26



Hello! We have news for Irkutsk people and other Siberians: on the night of July 26-27, in Irkutsk, in the KwakInn cafe, the third annual GeekFest Night will be held. At this night event, everyone will be able to meet like-minded people, assemble a spontaneous team and develop a project for everyone to envy. Each developer and programmer dreams of the implementation of certain ideas. Only this is usually no time. So, on the last weekend of July, you will have a great opportunity to realize your ideas, try new technologies for yourself, or just look for your soul.

In the morning results will be summed up and ready projects will be presented, from which the participants themselves will select the best. Winners will receive approval, respect and something inexpensive, but enjoyable. There are several honorary nominations that the organizers are still silent about. But the rewards will definitely be, and not only from us. :)
