Google service to check the display of sites on mobile devices

The search giant today in the framework of the Google Initiative introduced a new tool Go Mo , whose purpose is to analyze the "friendliness" (friendly) of the site for display on mobile devices.

The developers of the service claim that about 60% of users will not return to the site, which for one reason or another is displayed incorrectly on the screen of a smartphone or tablet, and considering the spread of these devices in the world, then at least part of this audience will be deprived of at least careless .

In this regard, the service can evaluate the following site parameters: download speed, reading text without zooming, the ability to search the site and several others. According to the results, the developer can get a report with a specific description of the deficiencies and tips on how to fix them.
