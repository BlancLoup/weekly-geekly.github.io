Rent a phone with unlimited internet in Hong Kong and Singapore


When traveling, especially business travel, it is important to be in touch. But there is one important drawback - predatory roaming of domestic operators. That is why, when traveling, many people choose a place for lunch using Wi-Fi here stickers, periodically rush into the hotel to check if an important letter has arrived. And if in the tariffs for some European countries you can still find more or less reasonable rates. For example, in Asia or America, not everything is so beautiful.

When planning another trip to Hong Kong in October last year, I accidentally stumbled upon a company that rents phones with unlimited internet for rent.

Service handy appeared recently, in October 2012. The company has two booths in the arrival hall of the Hong Kong Airport in which you can rent a Google Nexus or Samsung Galaxy Note. It comes with a proprietary protective case, extra battery, charging with a wire and a bag for accessories.



The second battery is very useful, Note sits quite actively. Also, just in case, I carried with me both charging and, if possible, recharged the battery.

Calls

The second tariff without the included calls, apparently entered recently, at the request of users. I used my included calls only twice. One, when they did not find my reservation at the hotel counter and had to hang on the phone from booking.com for 10 minutes while they called the same hotel and found out what was happening (they all found it later). And the second when I had to bail out a compatriot who happened to meet by chance, who found himself in the middle of the city without language and documents, who remained in a taxi and were looking for an embassy.

Unlimited calls from Hong Kong are available in the following countries: Australia, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Italy, Japan, Netherlands, New Zealand, Singapore, South Korea, Spain, Taiwan, Thailand, United Kingdom, United States.

Russia, as you can see in the list is not, but almost everywhere in Hong Kong is the perfect Internet so you can call via Skype via 3G and HDSPA.

Tetering

If you have an intolerance of phones on android, or you want to continue to use only your device in Hong Kong, you will not be able to rearrange the SIM card. She is locked on a handy phone. But there is a tethering function. You can share the rental phone as a modem, and forget about it by connecting your phone / tablet / laptop to a Wi-Fi point. It remains only to change the battery.
In truth, I was going to use it. But in the end, all the applications that require simple authorization, like Skype or Instagram, I used on the rental Galaxy Note.

System

Rental phones have onboard Android modification. On the main page is the most important, according to the developers, information for the tourist, namely, a guide and coupons.



In October, there was quite a bit of useful information, but they promised to improve everything and add ticket sales to various events, horse racing and amusement parks right from the phone. May have already added. It would be extremely convenient.
Many system settings are locked, so that you yourself can not reflash your handy. However, you can download any applications from Google Play, so that twitter, instagram and other applications will be with you. For me it was an entertaining experience, I have never used an android before. (torrent right on the phone is extremely convenient).
