Google car scared horse



How many years the service of Google Street View works, but people continue to find the amusing photos made by the panoramic camera of the car. These photos can restore the chronology of events. We have already seen how the Street View car hit Bambi , and in 2013 Google had to prove that it did not kill the donkey in Botswana . Another funny incident has now been discovered, when a car on a rural road unexpectedly met a horse with a rider. The meeting was not too successful for the latter.

Photos were taken in October 2009 in northern Esterbotin (Finland).

Driving along a dirt road, the Google Street View car caught up with a horse and rider .



But as the car approached, the animal got a little scared.



It seems that the rider lost control, and the horse decided to run away to the field, a safer place.



It was a bad decision. As can be seen in the next photo, the rider lies on the ground helplessly, and the horse runs away .



Perhaps, Google cars appear very rarely in the Finnish countryside, and these cars look rather strange, so the reaction of the horse can be understood.
