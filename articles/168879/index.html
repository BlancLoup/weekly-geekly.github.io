To connect to Megafonovsky LTE, I had to go through a small quest.

What you really need to do to connect to Megafon LTE *


  1. You need to get a special SIM card called U-SIM. Your SIM card should be changed for free on a U-SIM at any subscriber service office.
  2. You need to ask you to connect the Internet L 4G, Internet XL 4G or Internet XXL 4G tariff option. more about tariffs
  3. And turn on LTE .

* If you already have a working LTE device with a Megaphone SIM card, this does not concern you

Grunt

Theoretically, this can be done independently, through the Service Guide . But in practice, LTE will not work, because there is some option that allows you to register on the Megafon LTE network, but only Megaphone employees at the service office or call center operators can turn it on. Practice has shown that few people understand this issue in the subscriber service. As a result, after I received a U-SIM and connected the Internet XXL tariff option through a service guide, I was assured in the call center that 4G should work for me, and I fought with drivers, Watchers and AT for a whole day without success. -commands. In the evening, when I arrived at the service office, I shared my problem and asked me to demonstrate that my SIM card can connect to LTE with at least some device. But an employee of the operator took my passport, went into the settings of my account in a megaphone, sighed sadly, said that I had not turned on that very magic option of LTE, which I myself could not turn on. After that, everything worked fine for me.

What you need to do to connect to Yota


  1. You need to flash the modem with this thing: Yota firmware for MC7710 .
  2. Go to where they sell the equipment Yota (suitable stand in the mall), and get a Yota SIM card.
  3. To persuade Yota employees to make the initial registration of a SIM card on the Yota network. (for this you need to go to the Internet through this sim card from the official Yota device)
