Beauty Gadgets - Serious Business



Beauty gadgets have long remained an unloved stepchild in the world of consumer technology. Everything changed in the new century: more and more companies are dealing with the topic seriously. They create functional and new inventions. Among the innovators is the Swedish company Foreo, which produces cult devices for skin and oral care.

3G, graphene and silicone


The Foreo company was founded in 2013 by the Swede Philip Sedic. He was born in Bosnia, but this is not the most interesting moment in his biography. The fact is that he likes to invent different things, and at the moment he has registered more than 200 patents. What did you invent? From an eye massager to a Kegel simulator, from a finger-ring massager, to a light skin cleaning method.


The person who invents what you are afraid to even think

Sedicz actively applied his inventive talents professionally. At one time he headed one of the first digital marketing agencies in Sweden, he worked on 3G technology at Ericsson and on graphene condoms at LELO.

In 2012, Sedich's wife, Ivana, bought herself a brush for the face of a famous brand. As is often the case, the skin on the face of a woman has become worse due to the brush. Problem device fell into the hands of a meticulous husband. He concluded that it is better to wash your hands than to rub your face with such a thing: the nylon bristles and the removable brush head were probably infested with bacteria, and the material was too hard.

Surprisingly, a more gentle and hygienic alternative did not exist. Therefore, an enterprising Swede took matters into his own hands. This is how the first Foreo product - LUNA face brush. Hard nylon is replaced with a gentle medical silicone that does not irritate the skin. Silicone bristles were set in motion by sound pulsations. In the process of creating a prototype, Sedic actively consulted with his wife.


First generation of brushes: Luna mini on the left, LUNA on the right

Foreo started with a couple of people, but the company grew quickly. In 2014, the team assembled by Sedich developed its own version of an electric sonic toothbrush, called the ISSA. It, compared with competing products, cleaned the teeth delicately, without sacrificing the quality of cleaning.

In the following years, several iterations of both brushes came out, and hygienic products appeared for use with them. In 2018, the company launched a new product: the ‚Äúsmart mask‚Äù of the UFO, a replacement for Korean cloth masks. The device became another hit and as a result took part in London Fashion Week. The clever mask refreshed the skin of models that were on the podium during the Victoria Beckham show. Either the cunning inventor managed to circle the finger of the beauty guru, or a small gadget that looks like a glowing puck really works.

Electric brushes LUNA instantly became a hit, once they go on sale. The company relied on word of mouth and social network. The approach is fully justified, especially the popularity of new brushes contributed to Instagram.

This is not to say that LUNA had no competitors. The American company Clarisonic, founded in 2000 in Seattle, has been selling its own electric sonic brushes since 2004. Apparently, this is exactly what Sedic's wife used, but this is not certain. These priborchiki have replaceable nozzles, and they need to be changed every three months - because of the "fatigue" of the bristles and for hygienic reasons. On the face there are many bacteria and microscopic creatures such as skin mites, but it is dangerous to replenish the "zoo" with vaginal staphylococcus. Now Clarisonic belongs to the concern L'Oreal.


On the left - Foreo LUNA mini, on the right - Clarisonic brush

LUNA does not look like a competing product. The case of the device is more like a large sponge or washcloth. The above-mentioned medical silicone, from which the entire surface of the brush is made, is devoid of pores and therefore does not overgrow with colonies of bacteria. It is enough to rinse the brush under water - and it is again as new.

To drive on the face you need to part, where are the silicone bristles. It is necessary to use the brush together with the means for washing, which is whipped into the foam with the help of vibrations. These vibrations contribute to the fact that the remnants of cosmetics, sebum and dead cells are actively removed from the skin surface. In general, it is faster and more efficient than washing with a sponge or with hands. A massage with bristles additionally gives the skin elasticity, as blood flows to it.

UFO smart masks


Even if you diligently bypass the topic of cosmetics, you probably have seen the ubiquitous bags of cloth masks in supermarkets. It is the most famous part of the Asian skin care system. They owe their fame to ease of use. The mask, soaked with a special serum, is applied to the face for 20 minutes. Depending on the composition of the impregnation, the mask operates in different ways: it softens, moisturizes, cleans and evens the skin tone. Of the minuses - a universal cut, because of which the masks are not suitable for every person, and the large time costs of the procedure. To show the result, once to put on a mask is not enough. They are used regularly.

Serum applied to fabric "mask". Yes, they have not gone away, but have decreased to the size of a large cotton disk. This consumable is fixed on the gadget ring. There are several types of masks: day, night, for oily skin, for moisturizing, for lightening, for the skin around the eyes, and collagen (anti-aging).

ISSA Toothbrushes



Toothbrushes Foreo ISSA

In 2014, Foreo broke into this new market with a serious offer - the ISSA series of sonic toothbrushes. Their heads can be changed once a year, and the brushes themselves charged every six months. Long-lived heads were made of silicone, so beloved by the company. Silicone bristles do not harm the gums, and due to the absence of pores, colonies of bacteria do not breed in them.

The Swedes first presented the gadget at the CES 2014 exhibition of innovative technologies, and then launched a crowdfunding campaign at Indiegogo. The gadget has collected about $ 80,000, which is very good for a toothbrush.

The second generation has changed somewhat, since the main heads have become hybrid. Along the edges of the brush are silicone bristles that do not harm the gums. In the middle, instead of them, bristles of rigid PBT-polymer are inserted for more effective cleaning of the deposit. But the service time was reduced by half: these heads recommend changing every six months. Silicone heads are still sold as they are well suited for sensitive teeth.


Foreo ISSA 2

The ISSA 2 brush comes with a hybrid head. Her work time, according to the manufacturer, is up to a year on a single charge. It depends on the intensity and frequency of cleansing; To save battery, the brush turns itself off after 3 minutes.
