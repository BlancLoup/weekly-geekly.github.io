Appearance


I got a white-white laptop for review, although it is also available in dark blue, red and silver.









In fact, the first thing you notice is not the appearance, but the weight. The laptop is incredibly light, only 980 grams. The device is very compact, thin, moreover, instead of the hard drive, a 32 GB Flash-memory module is used for data storage, which gives considerable savings in weight and size.

But back to appearance.

The lid is made of glossy plastic with barely noticeable sparkles, shimmering beautifully at the slightest movement. Unfortunately, in the photo I could not pass it.



The bottom and the inner surface of the laptop are made of matte rough plastic, pleasant to the touch and "grip", which is important for the gadget, which involves very high mobility.

The battery is non-removable, on the bottom there are only small grids of stereo speakers.





On the left side is a power connector, card reader, HDMI port and audio jack.



On the right side there are only two USB 2.0 ports.



In front you can see three small indicators: power, charging and CapsLock. In the closed state, the laptop is only 17.5 mm thick.



There are two small plastic protrusions on the back of the cover. They serve as a kind of legs, on which the laptop begins to lean, when the lid opens more than 92-93 degrees.



It opens, by the way, widely, which adds comfort when working with a laptop while lying on the sofa and leaning it against legs bent at the knees.



The screen has a glossy surface, on the sides a plastic matte frame with a textured surface, 14.5 mm wide. Above is a webcam lens, a work indicator and a microphone hole.

Display


Here an 11.6-inch TN matrix with a resolution of 1366 x 768 is applied. The image is very smooth, but the viewing angles are small. The coating is glossy, but since the display is not touch-sensitive, there will be no problems with the constant fingerprinting.

Works laptop running Windows 8.1. And it works very fast, not like devices 5-6 years old, grunting under Windows 7. Performance and work experience


The laptop is equipped with an Intel Quad Core Z3735F processor in the Bay Trail-T family, operating at 1.33 GHz. The manufacturer itself is positioned as a solution for the most affordable devices. As the video card is used built-in Intel HD with 1 GB of memory.

In my opinion, driving benchmarks on such devices is pointless. Laptop performance is enough for comfortable work of office applications, surfing with a dozen bookmarks in the browser, viewing HD-video and casual games. Actually, all that for which a laptop of this class is needed. Although in the "parrots" notebook power looks very modest.
