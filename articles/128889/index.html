Manufacturing ATM skimmers using 3D printing

Many people know about the device ATM-skimmer , which can be found in the first ATM on the street. Usually a skimmer consists of a patch keyboard or camera, as well as a magnetic tape scanner. Previously, these parts were made by hand (see the catalog of skimmers ), carefully adjusting the size of the device to the dimensions of the details of a particular ATM.

But now the criminals have mastered more technological methods of production. The South Texas court published documents ( 1 , 2 ) about the process of four criminals who stole more than $ 400 thousand from ATMs, and bought a 3D printer for making skimmers. Thus, the criminals were able to make perfect copies of the original receivers in the ATM, with very high accuracy.


3D-model details skimmer

It seems that this is not an isolated case. Last year, Materialize reported on the receipt of an order for the manufacture of a skimmer part (illustrated) in an online 3D printing service.
