STOR.E ART4: with USB 3.0 and beyond

Toshiba is not only first-class laptops. Toshiba's line of portable drives is also constantly being updated. Toshiba recently unveiled new STOR.E ART4 external hard drives with USB 3.0 support, which is slowly but surely becoming the accepted standard in the computer market.




From the point of view of design, external hard drives STOR.E ART4 are flawless: small size, rubberized edges, weight less than 190 g. STOR.E ART4 are made in the form factor 2.5-inch. They are convenient to use both in the workplace, and if a large amount of data is often required on trips. There are models in a glossy black case with a red, blue and white pattern. The discs come with a protective case that protects the device from accidental damage during operation. The lineup includes models with a capacity of 500, 750 GB and 1 TB.

STOR.E ART4 support 256-bit encryption function with password access, to protect private data.
