A year or two ago, the author of QIP (as is well known, this is Ilham Zulkorney, who now transferred the role of the main developer of the client to Sergey Galezdinov - SegaZero ), sharply raised the question of the future fate of the project. The project had a site, the site was more than visited, the site was on the server, it was necessary to pay for the server. Not at all, I had the choice to either score on the project or find partners and give it a further life. I chose the latter, because the former would not suit anyone, especially QIP users. I am not familiar with the entire RBC company, but only with its part and would say that it is a very democratic and promising company so that the media would not talk about it there.

So, at the moment, MediaMir, which is part of the structure of the RBC group of companies, has an option to buy part of a QIP project (not owning it!). RBC pays for servers hosting the qip.ru website, the jabber server webim.qip.ru, the forum forum.qip.ru, the storage.qip.ru settings database (see below), as well as additional services. And the main stumbling block is their imposition and integration into the program.

The first thing is to understand: this is quite understandable condition of RBC. Moreover, it is even in some forced condition. Zulkorneev, when he was planning an agreement, had a tough condition: no advertising in the client . Therefore, in order for the project to pay off, you need hits to make advertising on services. Otherwise, there will be no salaries for the staff servicing the servers, there will be no iron upgrade for them ... eventually the project will be bent, and it will be bad for everyone that Ilham did not want. And now let us remember that the overwhelming majority of the historically established QIP audience needs only ICQ, they never would have gone anywhere in their lives, some do not understand in principle why the authors of the free program are not obliged to immediately fulfill their requirements immediately.
