What is what?

Settings


By clicking on the clock in the lower right corner, a quick settings panel pops up, from which you can immediately go to the main menu by clicking on the inscription Settings.







A long press on the screen brings up another batch of settings relating to the desktop. From there, you can drag and drop widgets, application shortcuts or change the background to one of the five screens.









Programs


The list of programs appears by clicking on the Apps button (surprisingly, right? :), located in the upper right corner. The list is traditionally divided into all installed applications and those that were downloaded from the Market.
