Success story of a sixteen-year-old Russian boy

By the age of 20, he got married, divorced, sat in prison and was the owner of 3 firms.

At 16 he decided to start his own business. That was in 1994, when I was in the business of ‚ÄúTurnkey company registration‚Äù. The young man convinced me to sell him a company for half the price. How did he do it?


Scam 1: Directory of Tver banks


He came to my office, said that he was publishing a directory of Tver banks, but he needed a firm, and only enough money for half the price. And he made me a super offer: placing my ad for free and selling another ad in my own pocket. The price of each advertisement was announced as for the whole company, so the offer looked quite attractive. I agreed.

When we went to register the transfer of the enterprise, he managed to talk his teeth to a lawyer, and he missed that the boy was still 16 years old, and he simply did not have the right to own his enterprise.

Then he borrowed a computer, an inkjet printer and a scanner. Then he took a loan office. Then he printed 400 copies of the directory (instead of the promised 10,000). Nobody checked, he distributed to advertisers several pieces, they were satisfied.

I then earned it: I sold two of my advertising space. Although in fact I became an accomplice in the scam - the circulation was false.


Scam 2: Fake Russian Lotto Tickets


After some time a lotto ticket seller came to the young man. He said that they would be scattered with terrible force, and proposed to print fake ones. The degree of protection was really low. Tickets have been printed and sold out.

It is usually difficult to win the lottery, but then they were lucky: they printed winning tickets. In jail, they first put the seller and then our hero. He spent 8 months and received a conditional. He sat in a separate chamber for economic crimes and made good connections there.

Scam 6: Design of the stand of the Tver region


At the end of 2006, the company I worked for was contacted from the development department of the Tver region. Representatives of the department complained that the contractors did to them what the hell, and the governor is extremely displeased. Who made the design? Of course, "Maps of Russia". We in a terrible rush made a good design, at the same time booklets and presentation.
