Selling as an acquaintance with a girl

Just want to note that this is not the guide "How to seduce a girl . " This article on the example of a normal life situation shows that salesman skills are in all of us. Information can be useful to doubters who want to try themselves in this area, as well as managers who are looking for ways to give confidence to newcomers.

Sale - exchange of goods for money, confirmed by the contract. In other words, you get what you want in exchange for money or another equivalent that the other party finds acceptable.

Few people thought that this term accompanies us from our first steps to the very end. We begin to make deals in the nursery, where for good behavior or porridge eaten with appetite we get the desired toy. With age, the needs and desires grow, and now, one toy is not enough, we want more. Namely - there is that sweet, beautiful girl in the cafe.

Greeting

The effect of a first impression is an opinion about you, which is formed by the subject in the first minutes of communication. As a rule, it is almost impossible to change it in the future. To our happiness with you, following only a few rules, the chances of moving to the next stage grow significantly.

Smile
It is not necessary to advertise your dentist and smile in all 32 teeth! A light, sincere smile will be quite enough.
Make eye contact
Welcoming the person - do not hesitate to look into his eyes. Just do not play, because too long glance can be mistaken for aggression. We do not want to be mistaken for a maniac?

These should not always be shoes from LOUIS VUITTON. Enough to look neat, and a t-shirt with slices of last year's sandwich left at home.

Identifying needs

In short, this is getting the information you need. Experienced sold, before the meeting will prepare these same questions, because he knows that what information he needs to extract from the client.

There are three basic needs : time, quality and cost. If you delve into this question, then of course they are much more, but they all boil down to the three factors listed above. The main types of questions are also of three types.

Closed questions - questions for which we get a short answer (yes / no).
Guiding questions - questions through which we push the client to a favorable answer for us.
Open questions - questions that get a detailed answer.

Also, before you identify needs, you need to thoroughly examine your product. Knowing all the pros and cons, you can make maneuvers and move away from uncomfortable questions or topics. In this case, it will be easier, because no one knows about the person, better than himself.

Presentation

If you have reached this point, then everything is going well. The interlocutor is interested in your campaign, otherwise he would have already stopped the dialogue.
Having identified the need - start selling. Only not the product or service itself, but the benefit that the buyer will receive. After all, no one needs a non-working TV.
