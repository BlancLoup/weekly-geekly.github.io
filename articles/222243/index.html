NASA announced the collection of projects / ideas for the expedition to Europe, the satellite of Jupiter



Many scientists and ordinary people are convinced that extraterrestrial life exists, and not somewhere there, with distant stars, but in the Solar System, on other planets and their satellites. Among the potentially habitable satellites, scientists are attracted by Europe - the sixth satellite of Jupiter. It has been practically proved that under the many kilometers of ice (water, by the way) Europe, there is an ocean in which living creatures (microorganisms or multicellular ones) can dwell.

NASA called on engineers, scientists and all people who are interested in this subject to send their projects on organizing an expedition to Europe, as well as methods for studying this satellite of Jupiter.

Of course, the expedition has limitations and goals, and the ideas sent must correspond to all of this.

So, the cost of the expedition should not exceed $ 1 billion . This should include all equipment, all technological and scientific tools. That is, you need to think about how to organize an expedition, from scratch, for $ 1 billion.

In addition, the expedition has five main tasks:
  • determining the size of the oceans of Europe;
  • determination of the influence of the oceans on the processes of relief formation and in general, the "life" of this satellite;
  • determining the composition of the oceans;
  • create a map of the surface of Europe, with a maximum resolution;
