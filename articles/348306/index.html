Registration for NeoQUEST-2018 is open: steampunk expedition to Atlantis


Registration for the NeoQUEST-2018 annual cybersecurity competition is open !

Individual CTF will last from March 5 to March 16 , and this year the NeoQUEST team will send you on an expedition for the treasures of Atlantis! Welcome under the cut: there we will open the secret about the subject of tasks and tell you what's new will be at the "face-to-face"!

Who else does not know what NeoQUEST is?


NeoQUEST - an annual cybersecurity event hailing from the banks of the Neva! At one time, NeoQUEST was only a competition for information security specialists, but for the past few years, competition (hackquest) has been only part of an exciting program.

NeoQUEST includes 2 stages:

  1. The online stage is a competition for all comers, during which participants registered on the site for several days remotely complete tasks from various areas of cybersecurity;
  2. "Face to face" - an event of the conference format, which includes:

  • interesting reports from information security practices;
  • simulation of the most significant and sensational cyber attacks;
  • demonstration of ways to protect against cyber attacks;
  • workshops and workshops that teach working with modern security mechanisms;
  • Twitter- quiz "Exam on IB";
  • hackquest - the final of the competition for the best participants of the online stage: 8 hours of real-time task completion, and at the end of the day the winner is announced who won the main prize - a trip to one of the international conferences on cybersecurity!

Online stage NeoQUEST-2018


This year, the online stage will be held from March 5 to 16 , and anyone can take part! Some tasks are even possible for beginners, and some are submitted only to experienced specialists. The only condition is individual , not team passing of tasks!
