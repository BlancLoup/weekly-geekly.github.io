Kraken


Founded in 2011 in San Francisco, Kraken is currently the largest Bitcoin platform in the world. Trades are held in US dollars, euros, as well as in Canadian dollars, British pounds and Japanese yen. Kraken is recognized by independent media as the best and most secure trading platform for cryptocurrency exchange. Kraken was the first bitcoin platform, whose prices and trading volumes were published on the Bloomberg Terminal, trusted by the Japanese Government and the German financial regulator.

Bitstamp


Bitstamp is a bitcoin platform in the UK. Since September 2014, the second largest turnover has been achieved. Has permission to trade between USD, EUR, GBP and Bitcoin. It was founded in 2011 in Slovenia, but changed its jurisdiction to the UK in April 2013.

Bitfinex is a trading platform in beta testing phase. Created with the assistance of iFinex Inc. (Bvi). At the moment, passed all stages of licensing. He has a staff of experts who have come from different banks and companies: analysts, developers, and marketers.
