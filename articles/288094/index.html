Content and communication are now the same thing.

What do people do on the Internet? If you believe the study of Online Publishers Association and Neilsen // NetRatings, then they almost all the time now goes on reading texts (blogs, social networks) and watching videos. In general, Internet users now spend almost half of their time on content consumption (47%). For comparison, four years ago, this figure was 33%.
