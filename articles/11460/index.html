Established award for the best bloggers of Belarus

July 15, 2007 in the Minsk club "Rock-House cafe" (Nemiga, 12), participants of the Belarusian segment of the Live Journal will gather. The main event will be the first LJ-Prize ceremony of the Belarusian segment .

The organizers of the event rogach , aquamber and kapodaster77 promise "dancing and universal jubilation, youtube-jokes, Internet entourage, raffles, lotteries, WiFi." The awards will be presented by TV and Liver Stars, including Tamara Lissitzkaya .

Entry is by invitation only (or by LJBY community cards).

“I admit honestly, I did not carefully watch what Rykov and Parker did, ” said one of the organizers of the LJ-User Prize, kapodaster77, “ We initially did not want any plagiarism. Therefore, we sat down and began to think out how to do it ourselves. So it was decided to make a beautiful presentation, introduce the symbol of the award - Zhezhebai and so on. And only after that we saw how best to organize a vote :). We have a series of LJ-parties scheduled, the Prize is only the first one. And we very much hope that it will be successful . "

A total of ten nominations are planned: “Literature”, “Best Press LJ”, “Lytdybr” (the finest nonsense), “Best music or star blogger” (story about music and domestic show business), “Aggregator” (best entertainment content), “Politeg and Parshcheg” (politics, society), “Hellish photoblog” (best photos “Livejournal”), “Flooder vseya BelZhZHZh” (to the most active commentator).

Claim the award can only bloggers living in Belarus.
