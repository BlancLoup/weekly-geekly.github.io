Study: the closure of Megaupload could have a negative impact on the box office of many films

The closure of the largest file sharing service Megaupload about a year ago had a controversial effect on the box office movies. A study of the impact of this event on the collections of 1344 films in 49 countries, conducted by German and Danish scientists, revealed that only high-budget blockbusters, reaching more than 500 screens, benefited from the closure of Megaupload. Movies with an average and small budget are more likely to lose - their box office has only decreased, although not very significantly.

The researchers suggest that this effect may be due to the fact that blockbusters get enough promotion with centralized means - through traditional advertising.
