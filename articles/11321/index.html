Sonic turned 16 years old

Here 3dnews.ru ( http://www.3dnews.ru/software-news/soniku_ispolnilos_16_let-265538/ ) writes:

In particular, recently it became known that this character will be the hero of the RPG for the portable platform Nintendo DS. By the way, BioWare will develop the project. In addition, together with the well-known competitor plumber Mario (Mario), the Dream Interpretation will appear in a project dedicated to the upcoming Olympics.



However, modern virtual adventures of the famous character seem to us not so fascinating as 16 or 10 years ago. Maybe when Sonic comes out of adolescence, hurricane drive will return to him again.
