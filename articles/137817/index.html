Lightpack 6 is available for pre-order.

Already today the 6th version of Lightpack has become available for pre-order .
In the new revision of the device improved color reproduction in the range of colors with low brightness. The new board received acrylic protection, which covered all the connectors on the board. According to the developers, this is the most convenient and best solution for this type of device. Lightpack 6 received a square case with convenient connectors, now you do not have to clean the wiring and fasten them with a screwdriver. Soon the video will appear.



Lightpack is an open-source monitor backlight device. Depending on the chosen settings, you can either enhance the presence effect for movies and games, or keep your eyes if you often have to work at the computer in the dark. Anyway, Lightpack in less than an hour and a half is mounted on the back wall of your monitor, TV or laptop and highlights the surface behind it with actual colors.
The software analyzes the image on your monitor and via USB transfers data about it to the board, which, in turn, with the help of RGB-LEDs illuminates the wall with the appropriate light. Most of all, the effect resembles the illumination of Philips Ambilight TVs. The whole principle of operation is described in detail in the project wiki .
