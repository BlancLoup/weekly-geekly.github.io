4 applications that help me save on basic expenses

Over time, I have formed the main items of expenses for which I try to save - food home, business lunches and dinners in cafes, hotels, clothes. To do this, I use 4 applications on a mobile phone, each of which helps to save in a certain category of spending.

Booking


I often use the Booking application for three reasons: business trips, recycling, and divorced bridges. A wide choice of hotels allows you to find the nearest one on the map and immediately leave a request. This is especially true when business trips are unplanned - conferences, unscheduled maintenance, or personal presence is simply required. For example, completing the first meeting, I already understand how many nights I spend on a business trip and can immediately book a room for a specific number of days.


Due to the fact that I work in an international company, sometimes requests for maintenance come from colleagues from the east coast of the United States. The time difference makes itself felt: the task director is now 11:00, we already have 21:00, and the solution may take 5 hours. It turns out that by the end of the working day we already have one in the morning, the metro is closed, and the bridges are about to be reconstructed. And in order not to waste money on a taxi, not to steal from myself for an hour and a half of sleep and to go to work on time, I look for a cheap hostel or hotel in advance using Booking.


City life


To visit the cafe and cinema, I use the City Life card, with which you can get discounts and cashback in various stores and establishments. The card works including the network Ginza, Grizzly. In addition to restaurants, laundries, garages, recreation centers, and pastry shops are connected. There is Uber in the mobile application of the card, so you can pay for a taxi ride with accumulated cashback, which is convenient.

I visit restaurants not so often, so I can allow myself some freedom of choice: the average check in such a place is large, taking into account the discount and a 5% cashback, I get 100-200 rubles from an order that I can spend on paying at any other restaurant.

It is also interesting that City Life works in 50 cities of Russia. Arriving in Yekaterinburg, for example, I choose where to go for lunch on the site, this greatly helps save time for choosing places to rest in a new city.


I buy clothes online at Lamoda, Proskater, Adidas, Nike, so as not to waste time going to the mall. Online stores of well-known brands are connected to City Life, so you can get cashback from online purchases. It is also convenient that the Uber service is integrated into City Life (and with the latest changes, Yandex.Taxi).



Purchase conditions remain the same as without City Life. Cashback - partner bonus from the fact that cashback service brings more customers to the store. The money saved is withdrawn from the account of the personal account, taken into account at a discount in a restaurant or when paying for Uber.

Spender


Personal and family budgets require a separate simple application that will help control costs and reduce debit and credit. I spend all the expenses in Trangire, an application that takes into account expenses on purchases in a few simple steps. The interface is intuitively simple, so filling out expenses is not like full-fledged accounting with a bunch of positions:

  • chose a category
  • entered amount
  • confirmed


For each day, I can look at my expenses to plan a budget for the next day. It also helps to track when I paid for the car MOT - it is convenient for me.


And, of course, I can view the entire expense and balance for the month down to the individual categories.

An extended version will be useful for a family with an average salary, a mortgage and a small child. The functional monitors all budget changes and helps to identify unnecessary costs.

Conclusion


Often, applications clutter up the memory of a smartphone and, over time, only the most necessary and convenient ones will remain. They reduce the time for each of the daily tasks of working with the budget. Thanks to this set, I spend less than a minute on finding a hotel, less than 10 seconds on accounting for one cost, 2 minutes on finding a restaurant and calling a taxi.
