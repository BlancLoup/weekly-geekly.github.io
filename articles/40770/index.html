All have become accustomed to, that around the domain system, and in particular, around the domain registration, turns a lot of different scams. A warning about the appearance of scammers came from the organization IEDR - the national registry of the domain zone of Ireland IE. The scheme used by fraudsters to deceive people is extremely simple. The attackers, on behalf of Internet Register Ireland, are sending organizations, by fax and mail, an offer to register a domain in IE zone for just ... 958 euros. Needless to say, people who have bought this letter get nothing. And this is not surprising. A registrar with this name does not exist.

But, apparently, it works well, because in different countries it has been used more than once. Therefore, if you are going to buy a domain for yourself, do not rely only on yourself, contact a specialist. Or at least do it with large, well-established registrars.
