New version of free tender b2b platform



Hello, dear users and readers of Habr!

Recently, I wrote about how erumpo services organize information on commercial tenders in an easy-to-use form, as well as some accumulated statistics. This time I want to share important news: the erumpo team has updated the version of the portal, and is ready to offer all its services for free.

For those who hear about erumpo for the first time

Erumpo is a website that provides suppliers of goods and services with information on all tenders and purchases available on the Russian market today, aggregating this data from more than 80 government and commercial sites (among them zakupki.gov.ru, sberbank-ast, roseltorg, b2b- energo, telecom companies, banking sector, etc.).

In addition to tenders taken from third-party sites, erumpo provides a convenient tool for finding competent suppliers and contractors for the implementation of projects of any orientation and degree of complexity, with any budget (through the mechanism of ‚Äúfast‚Äù 3-5 day tenders that do not require large expenditures on preliminary training). At this time, the portal has an extensive and reliable database of suppliers, the list of which continues to grow. Along with complete information on the supplier companies, information is provided on the goods and services provided by them.

Back to the news

In addition to free access to erumpo services, several important features have been added:
  • New categories of tenders and supplier companies are included: Construction and repair, Administrative and household goods and services, Transport and transportation. Soon it is planned to launch two more categories - Business and Insurance.
  • Suppliers opportunities are added: each registered user of the site can create tenders, search for suppliers of different areas of activity, as well as participate in tenders himself.

We hope the new services will be useful for you!
