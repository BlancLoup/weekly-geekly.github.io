CoMindWork - Teamwork Online

Our vision COMINDWORK
In the pursuit of efficiency, modern companies go their own ways and try different approaches. The old model of the hierarchical power does not work for teams that are engaged in innovative activities, teams where everyone can make decisions - and execute them. How to make all employees aware of other people's decisions? How to motivate people to share their findings, discuss ideas - and so that the results of the discussions would not disappear, not be forgotten? The task is complicated by the fact that modern communications are not limited to only mail and personal communication at meetings. ICQ, video conferences, e-mail - how to put together all the information flows with a specific client or with your team?

Such tasks are in front of any leader. Collecting information from different sources, making decisions, communicating decisions to each interested person. These three steps should be made as soon as possible. Most of the information at the same time - in the minds of people, and this will not go anywhere. And people can not only make mistakes, but also forget.

Consider the classic top three: people - processes - technology. Developing the qualities of any component increases the effectiveness of actions, but where to start? The simplest option for the task of preserving and distributing information is the introduction of new communication technologies.
