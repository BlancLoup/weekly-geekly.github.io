SE Xperia X10i: video review and bug-report

Editorial NOMOBILE recently prepared a Sony Ericsson Xperia X10i video review.



I want to add it a little.

A lot of speaker boom - they say, the interlocutor is not audible during a conversation. Honestly, I do not know where it came from - in my sample with the speaker everything is in order. Well, anything can happen.

Nevertheless, I can not resist: here are three things that annoy me the most in SE Xperia X10i (not counting, of course, the weak battery, the ancient version of the platform with all the consequences, the lack of multitouch and flash in the browser)

1. Timescape. The idea is good, but the implementation - unfortunately! - just put out the light. It turns out that it is impossible to use one of the main features of the smartphone.



It looks beautiful, but:

- If you put the Timescape widget on the main screen (logical, right?), It will show the last event. In the case of SMS it may be stupidly pale. And not only in the case of SMS.

- Opens with special effects, but very long. Every time to wait, while on the screen translucent rectangles fly - well, at no gate.

- During the update (especially when it sucks them from three sources: Twitter, Facebook and Vkontakte) Timescape begins to slow down the entire system.

- Bug with tweets: if they are written in Cyrillic, Timescape does not show spaces between words.

- If the text does not fit in the field (and even the maximum length tweet does not fit there), it is completely suggested to view it in a third-party application (by default, in the browser). Rave.

- When you update the status - it is updated immediately in all networks in which you are logged in. That is, a tweet, a post on Facebook and a Facebook account status are published simultaneously. Photo attach, of course, impossible.

- Avatars, which Timescape automatically loads, interfere with reading the text.
