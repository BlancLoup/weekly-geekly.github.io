History reference
The first studies began in the 50s, people with radar equipment started having health problems, had occupational diseases, which gave an impetus to the start of research.
The second stage - the end of the 60s and the beginning of the 70s, when powerful radar centers appeared. Then hygienic standards were developed, together with the Americans a commission was created, the purpose of which was to develop common standards. San. Norms were issued in the 2000s, but they are based on the program that was in the mid-80s, i.e. does not take into account the specifics of cell phones. By 2005, it was planned to completely replace the hygienic standardization, but so far this has not happened.
Another interesting point: it turns out, the gap in san.normas between the United States and Russia is gigantic, two orders of magnitude, and mobile phones are manufactured in accordance with American standards.
