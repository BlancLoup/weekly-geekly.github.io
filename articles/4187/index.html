Edward Shenderovich: "Our business is memories"

The main difference between Dandelife, for example, from MySpace is in the reliability of the information. If MySpace users can impersonate anyone and these fantasies are even encouraged by the very logic of the system, then everything is based on the documentary nature of the events: the truth of every fact, every name, every tag.
