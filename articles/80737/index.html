ATI Radeon HD 5670 - the first budget graphics card with DirectX 11



Despite the fact that game makers cannot yet fully understand DirectX 10, AMD continues to implant the cult of DirectX 11. Today, a new psychological height was taken - the budget ATI Radeon HD 5670 graphics card in the budget version costs less than $ 100. In principle, even if there is absolutely no faith in the prospects of DirectX 11 - for such a price, it is worthwhile to think, or maybe take it?

The card is made on 40nm technology, has 400 stream processors and a core clock frequency of 775 MHz. GDDR5 memory size is 512MB or 1GB.

For power consumption, the ATI Radeon HD 5670 is a very modest model. Typical power consumption 61 W in operation and 14 W during idle time. Powered by a single PCI-Express slot. In the list of outputs DisplayPort, DVI and HDMI.

The card supports ATI EyefinityTechnology technology, which allows you to simultaneously connect three monitors with a resolution of up to 2560x1600 pixels each.

According to the test results, the ATI Radeon HD 5670 was 10% faster than the NVIDIA GeForce GT 240.

Estimated price of 99-150 dollars.
