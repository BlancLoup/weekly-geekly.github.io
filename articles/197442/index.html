Replenishment of Yandex.Money: any account from any (almost) card

A few months ago, an opportunity to replenish an account from a bank card appeared in Yandex.Money:

  • instantly;
  • with a commission of 49 rubles, regardless of the amount of replenishment (for a single operation - from 10 to 15,000 rubles);
  • with limits of 30,000 rubles per day and 100,000 rubles per month (for any account and any card);
  • with PCI DSS recharge page protection;
  • Only with Visa and MasterCard, protected by 3-D Secure technology, issued in Russia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Israel, Kazakhstan, Turkey, Ukraine or Estonia.
