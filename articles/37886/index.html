New PIM communities Gimao.com

I would like to introduce to the general public a wonderful service, to the creation of which I have a relationship (I am on the board of directors of the Jimao company).
We provide the first in the internet service of the Semantic Web Web 3.0 - a Group Information Management (GIM) service.
The company is located in Israel, the board of directors includes various cool people from the Aichi industry.

The meaning of the service is very simple in Russian: you download a ICQ-like client to your computer, create a group / community through it, in which there is not just a messenger and further blog, but also a convenient PIM Database with a tree structure, through which you can define absolutely any data type to manage.

All members of your group can, together with their permissions, jointly manage this Community PIM Database.
And for this you do not need to be any programmer; the interface is extremely user friendly and does not imply any tables, keys, and so on.
The base is very similar to the file system, the client is entirely reminiscent of Outluk.
