Review: SLA DLP in Russian. How to respond to foreign monsters?

Dear amateurs and professionals of 3D printing, in this post I want to talk about the decision in the field of printing by photopolymers, created by Russian specialists in the Russian city called St. Petersburg. Obtained after growing models can be used for direct pouring, or removal of cold or hot rubber, as well as possible as a ready-made solution.



It will be a question of a 3D SLA DLP printer called Starlight and its younger Russian DLP model, now rev.7. This project has existed for more than a year, but it's time to tell more about it. Over the past period, the main assemblies were refined, the photopolymer was improved (several materials of different composition were developed - about this in a separate article) and field tests were carried out by the first owners.

The junior model (personal, desktop, - underline the right one) is a DLP 3D printer that prints high-precision objects using SLA technology (stereolithography).
