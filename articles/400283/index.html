Mod the Future looked into the future: behind the scenes hackathon in New York

What do 53 developers, 18 entrepreneurs, three designers, one surgeon and a dog named Goose have in common? All of them gathered at the 24-hour hackathon Mod the Future in New York, the purpose of which is to create the best Moto Mod concept for the Moto Z smartphone.



The result was truly amazing Moto Mods projects. We saw ideas for video event recognition systems, video paging, a modification for retail purchases of the Smart Cart, and an alarm button function. Also it became known about the concept of measuring the level of insulin and a tester for water pollution. All these projects speak about the basic principles of Moto Mod - the development of innovations and endless possibilities for buyers. This is the future for the stagnant mobile industry.

Teams and developers from five countries came to the hackathon: the USA, Canada, Brazil, Mexico and Latvia. All ideas were unique, just like the participants themselves.
