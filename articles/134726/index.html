About sound





The feature of the device is not a powerful technical specifications or interesting design. No, mainly attention is attracted by the conceptually modified approach to sound reproduction. ASUS, together with specialists from Bang & Olufsen, managed to break an established stereotype and present a laptop in which the sound is comparable in quality to external audio systems. Of course, attempts have been made before. The ASUS N71JA had 2.1 sound, and the subwoofer was located on the bottom of the case. This approach improved the overall sound picture, but the hand still stretched to connect at least a weak one ... and a plastic one ... and a small, but external column.

So, ASUS N55SF uses advanced technology ASUS SonicMaster, which is a complex of hardware and software tools for improving sound quality. SonicMaster technology brings the sound quality of laptops to a whole new level, providing a wide dynamic range, deep bass, clear vocal reproduction. Naturally, to achieve perfect sound exclusively in the case of the laptop at this stage of development of science and technology is impossible. Therefore, for a more powerful and expressive transmission of low frequencies, an external subwoofer is included with the notebook.



The subwoofer is connected to a special audio jack. The subwoofer is lightweight, weighs only 150 grams.
