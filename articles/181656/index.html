Hello!

Legalization of software at the present time is not only a thorny way to eliminate karma, but also a sure way to avoid disagreements with the laws of the Russian Federation, which we honor and respect.

And no matter how joked Windows users are about the number of programs on Mac OS X, however, software is written, and often quality and convenient. But these programs, used in unlicensed mode, can lead to serious problems with the Criminal Code.

I note that due to the low prevalence of Macs on the territory of the Russian Federation, even despite the rapid growth of their population, the percentage of unlicensed software is used much less than on Windows.

What is the reason for this?

Software out of the box

Traditionally, Apple provides its computers with a sufficient set of software so that you can buy a computer, personalize it and immediately start working on it (one of the commercials of the 'Get A Mac' advertising series is even devoted to this quality):


Out of the box on Macs the operating system itself and the three multimedia programs iPhoto, iMovie and GarageBand are preinstalled. At least, the Macbook Air purchased at the beginning of 2013 was delivered with such a set of software.

Thus, buying a Mac, you are at least a non-pirate, because the operating system is legal! And even if you have an old Mac and you need to buy an operating system, you will lay out 649 r for Mountain Lion, compare it with price tags on windows.

Mac App Store

In the wake of the success of iTunes Store and the App Store, on January 6, 2011, Apple opened its own online software store for Macs - the Mac App Store (MAS), which currently has more than 10,000 items. If there is a corresponding desire, MAS is a convenient tool for the voluntary purchase of software for each day.

Of course, in Windows systems the possibility is similar, but the clear advantage of Apple and MAS is the savings when installing the same programs on several Macs. Of course, Apple does not block the ability to install the program under an account on unauthorized computers - this is the conscience of the owner of Apple ID.
