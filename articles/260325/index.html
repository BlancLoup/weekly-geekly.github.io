How to get an invite to Dribbble with a 100% guarantee



Recently, I have often been asked to invite (invitation) to Dribbble. I want to tell those who are interested in web design to Habrahabr readers about how, with almost a 100% guarantee, get an invite to this popular service among designers. Just want to note that this article is suitable for designers only with a good portfolio; this article does not help the designers with bad work.

For starters, those who are not in the subject, I would like to tell you about what Dribbble is and why everyone needs invitations to this site so much.

Dribbble is a global social network for designers. By the principle of operation, Dribbble is very similar to Twitter, only instead of text messages 140 characters long, users post pictures of 400 * 300 pixels. Like Twitter, Dribbble has become very popular in a short time among designers. The whole situation is heated by the fact that you can get there by invitation, which is not as much as we would like.

In order to get to Dribbble, you need to invite from a person who is already registered on the site. On average, one person has 2 invitations. Invitations are given when the designer purchases a pro account for a year, so the number of invitations is limited.

In order to get the cherished invite, you need to perform 4 simple steps.

  • 1. Register on the site
  • 2. Find a suitable shot.
  • 3. Fulfill stated conditions
  • 4. Write a confirmation email

1. Register on the site


In order to get an invite, you must be registered on the site as a prospect, that is, you get the opportunity to keep likes, but you are still not allowed to post work.



When registering, I recommend filling in all the information about myself, since no one likes to communicate with faceless bots and even more so to help them.



2. Find a suitable shot

Shot is the job you post on the site.

Next you need to find a suitable shot, for this you will need to enter in the search "Dribbble Invite Giveaway".



Next you need to change the tab from popular to latest. On this page you can see the latest works of all designers who give out invitations.



I recommend responding to a large number of works in order not to wait a long time until someone gives invite.

3. Fulfill stated conditions

Before giving you an invitation, designers usually ask you to complete various conditions.



4. Write a confirmation email

After you fulfill all the conditions, I advise you to write a confirmation letter to the designer so that all the work will not be in vain.



Additional recommendations

The rule of good tone is to make a thank-you shot for the designer who will give you a invite. The first shot must be cool. If it is appreciated by other users, it will give you the opportunity to quickly start on this site.
