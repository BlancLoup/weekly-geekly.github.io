Internship at HP for students

The theme of the program for graduates , which Boris Yastrebov and I touched on in one of the previous posts, has found its continuation. We opened vacancies for interns, and Boris wanted to tell about them separately.

In 2009, trainee positions in the HP office in Moscow were opened in the Sales Support Department and the Software Development Department. A detailed job description is available here: Sales support.doc , Software developer.doc .

By participating in the HP Internship Program , you can:

  • gain experience in an international company that is a world leader in the field of information technology;
  • plan your development and career, go through an individual training program;
  • take part in the Program for young professionals ;
  • work in part-time mode according to a flexible schedule: the number of working hours is determined; hiring manager in consultation with a future intern.


The company offers interns a competitive reward and benefits package . The duration of the internship is determined individually, the contract with the intern is usually for a period until the end of the university.

We invite to participate in the competitive selection of students and graduate students of the penultimate year of study (2010 year of release):
  • With specialization in information technology, technical disciplines or economics
  • Good English speaking
  • Possessing analytical skills
  • With excellent communication skills
  • Able to work in a team, who want to work in a dynamic and multi-functional environment
  • Capable of proposing new ideas for solving business problems
  • Ready to acquire professional knowledge and experience, seeking professional and personal growth


Selection of candidates for the Program includes the following steps:

  • Call for applications: by e-mail hp_staffing@hp.com marked Internship_Hport , as well as on the HP website
  • Telephone interview with a specialist of the recruitment department
  • Assessment Center or / and individual interview


At Hewlett-Packard, your career is in your hands. But at each stage of professional growth you will be ready to provide support!
