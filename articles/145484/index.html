How to find an email marketing team



Intelligent personnel is not enough. We are often asked where to find an intelligent email marketing team. How to find someone who can handle mailings? What qualities will he need? We decided to summarize our experience in this post.

So, looking for an internet marketer or mailing list manager. This material can also be useful for assessing the suitability of specialists working for you.

Required skills
Paradoxically, an intelligent marketer working with mailing lists should know not only the market, analytics. He should speak the language better than a copywriter and fumble at least a little in html. These skills will be needed because he will work in a team with these people, organizing their work. It is difficult to organize or monitor the correct implementation of what you do not understand. Sometimes corrections will be made in the course of work: it is useful to sit down with a coder next to and check the work done, making edits and modifying the newsletter. And to understand that people who are connected with you by a common goal are doing is necessary. Just as it is necessary to convey to them the concept of this common goal.

The ability to be friends with the sales department
Your marketer or mailing list manager should have good communication skills. To increase the chances of a good return on mailings, he will need to establish a reliable communication channel with the sales department: sales people should be aware of what is selling and when, and the marketer must clearly know what to sell and when. Flexibility
It is no secret that in marketing mailing lists, you need to collect as much data as possible, analyze them, and then build a further strategy based on the data obtained. Sometimes you have to give up the strategy that you really liked, because it does not work. If a person lacks such flexibility, nothing will work out with mailings.

Work speed
Not once had to see the marketing departments, where everything goes quietly, sleepily and calmly. Until the marketer bursts in with the fiery god of maran, he will not distribute the magic pendals to everyone and the work will not be accelerated for some time, in order to wallow again in the sleepy depths. A familiar picture, yes? And not only in marketing it can be observed. So, such a mailing list manager is not suitable for us. Marketing is war. Here strategy and tactics are important, decisive throws "at the last moment" here are more likely to fail.
