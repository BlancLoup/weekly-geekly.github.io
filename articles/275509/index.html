The price factor is not the only reason that IT companies use second-hand servers. Where to buy and what to use?


It is also important to choose a company that sells used equipment. Warranty - the main sign of the legitimacy of the reseller. Find out information about the company. Can you provide recommendations from clients with whom you have already made deals?


As for the tasks for which used servers are used, then the tasks for them, in principle, can be solved the same as on the new servers. We have not yet encountered a lower rate of complaints in the Russian market of used servers.


Why buy used servers? Sometimes - for renting or resale. Hosting is a typical application.
