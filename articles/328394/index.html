Google I / O Extended 2017 in 20 cities of Russia

Hi, Habr! On May 17-20, 2017 in Mountain View, California, the eleventh Google I / O conference will take place, dedicated to the most current cutting-edge trends in technology and software from Google.
Those wishing to go there a dime a dozen, but not everyone has such an opportunity, take and fly to California. Therefore, as in the past year, almost everyone will be able to visit Google I / O without leaving your city!

Google I / O Extended is a unique opportunity to become a member of one of the largest gatherings of Google-technology lovers. Everything will happen online, so you can watch the annual presentation of the coolest new products in the IT world with GDG Mountain View representatives! Last year, 600+ I / O Extended events were held all over the planet simultaneously, this year there will be more!


Together with millions of other people, you will see Keynote, including a review of key conference trends and a forecast of new products for the coming year, take part in a lively discussion of new technologies with other representatives of the community and invited experts, see how I / O Extended is going on in other GDG communities around the world .

How to get to I / O Extended


Google I / O Extended 2017 events will be held on May 17 in 20 cities of Russia (there were 15 in 2016), in some cities preliminary registration is required (site conditions, passes, all business), but the event itself is free of charge:
