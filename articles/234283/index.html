Presentation of personal account Payler. Attention poll!

Friends!

We try to keep you informed of all our current news, but this summer was hot and full of endless hours of refinement of our product. And here we are ready to present you some new items from us. We would also like to ask you some questions at the end of this post, please note.

But first of all, we want to show you a small video that we made in order to briefly outline the main advantages of Payler for new users. If you have not heard about Payler, this video will answer all your questions.



Under the cut a detailed story about our improved personal account! Stay in touch.


So, we recently launched an expanded personal account and added new important features to it. First of all, it affected the types of display of the dynamics of payments made. The total amount of payments can be ranked by day, week and month. You can view statistics for the selected period:



It is also possible to display on individual payments. You can divide payments into clusters by region.



Regions are also conveniently displayed on a geographic map; this type of monitoring can be enabled at any time:



You can also rank payments by a range of amounts. For example, see all the latest purchases from 1000 to 5000 rubles. Doing so is worthwhile in order to assess consumer behavior in your store. In the era of Big Data, such assessments sometimes help to make important business steps: to purchase more of those products that diverge most quickly, to offer special conditions to the group of customers that you have identified. Such a system will work especially effectively if you have already implemented loyalty or lead generation programs.

Also, the Payler interface allows you to create different profiles for each payment. The profile is assigned to the payment automatically, you can gradually supplement all other information for personal reporting. If a customer makes a purchase in your store not for the first time, Payler automatically identifies it. In this part, there is also the possibility of ranking on the most successful payments returned.



Each payment can be viewed separately, with all the information that is on it. This can be conveniently used if there are several sellers in your store and you want to track the performance of each seller separately. In the payment profile, you can leave notes - any information that you want to highlight on this payment.



Each client is also assigned a separate profile with similar editing functions. In principle, already this information will be enough for you to apply bonus programs to the payer.



These features will soon be available in test mode, so you can try them yourself. We will inform on it in the blog.

Additional optimization

Payler also offers front-end customization of the look and feel of the payment gateway for your site. Adaptation is also possible under the design of the mobile application.



Mobile client

Mobile Payler client is fully adapted for all smartphones based on iOS and Android. For tablets assumed mobile version of the site. The application is primarily designed for monitoring and chat with customer support.
