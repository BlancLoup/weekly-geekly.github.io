Intel 0x7E3 summer internship awaits its students

According to the ancient tradition, for many years now, the Intel office in Nizhny Novgorod has been holding a summer internship for students and graduate students of Russian universities. In July-August of this year, the next group of lucky people will have the opportunity not only to listen to lectures from the coolest Intel developers, but also to join the real projects of the company and contribute to the development of its products.



In total this year, the choice of students is offered more than 30 tasks in various directions. At the same time, leading scientists of various scientific schools and areas, teachers at Russian universities and leading staff of Intel will hold a series of special lectures, trainings and seminars.

The following categories of citizens are admitted to the summer school:

  • 2nd year and older students
  • undergraduates and graduate students,
  • graduates of Russian universities of 2016-18.

Must have the citizenship of the Russian Federation.

During the internship, a monetary reward is paid, Intel pays for travel and accommodation for nonresident students, each is provided with a workplace and means of production. The best students who have successfully completed the internship within this program will have the advantage of working at Intel during their studies as interns and after graduation as full-time employees.

In order to get to the Intel 2019 summer internship, you must fill in the questionnaire on the academic project website by May 4th . First you need to familiarize yourself with the list of tasks proposed for solving this year, choose the most suitable one for yourself and think about it. From May 5 to June 3, a competitive selection of applicants is held, which includes, if necessary, an additional interview or questions. Final lists of internship participants will be compiled before June 4th .

In conclusion, we give for example a few tasks from the list - this year they are more interesting than ever.

  • Search for efficient compression methods for convolutional neural networks
  • Using Intel RealSense cameras in real-time systems for industrial automation
  • Development of an algorithm using deep learning to solve practical problems
  • Improving QR Code Detection Algorithms
  • Optimization of machine learning algorithm for GPU

These and many other tasks you can do in the summer internship of Intel - under the guidance of experienced benevolent mentors, in a creative setting and with undoubted benefit for themselves. We are waiting for you to visit our cozy white and blue office.
