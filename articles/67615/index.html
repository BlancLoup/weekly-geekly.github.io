Obvious next

It would seem that the obvious solutions are floating in the air, you just need to create a concept. Today Novate.ru has pleased a couple of simple and even obvious solutions for everyday things.


For example, a flashlight. Everyday and ordinary thing. and in fact it is often not very convenient to use it in everyday life, when you need to light up the workplace and at the same time two hands are occupied, you have to get out and come up with solutions to the problem that has just appeared. And the solution is pretty obvious - a tripod! Yes, yes, tripod, why only the camera is fixed on it? After all, it is really convenient to attach a flashlight to a tripod. And, yes, such a device really is, it is called a gorillatorch and costs $ 30 . On offsite , there are original and, most importantly, very usable (at least in appearance) tripods for iPhon and cameras, gorillamobile and gorillapod, respectively.



We will continue to unload hands :). The next item of daily use, to which the playful hands of designers have reached, is an umbrella. The same situation - absolutely unfairly takes one (and only two of them) hand during the whole time of use. We will treat the same way.
