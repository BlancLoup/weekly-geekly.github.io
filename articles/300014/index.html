Invitation to the conference RIF + KIB 2016

On April 13, we invite everyone to the opening of the twentieth, anniversary Internet forum RIF + KIB 2016 . The forum will be held in the format of a three-day off-site Moscow event, consisting of a multi-threaded conference, exhibition and various non-program sections. The main conference and exhibition venue will be the Lesnie Dali boarding house near Moscow.

According to the estimates of the Organizing Committee of the 20th RIF + CIB, it will become one of the most popular and interesting spring events in RuNet in the entire history of its holding: over 3 days of work (April 13-15) more than 8000 participants will take part in it, about 600 speakers, more than 100 round tables, sections and master classes. The program RIF + CIB 2016 includes sections with market experts, an exhibition of key eCommerce participants, an entertainment program and traditional surprises from our international processing company PayOnline .



The three-day conference program of the Russian Internet Forum, which will be held in 7 parallel halls, covers the main topics relevant to all participants of Internet business: trends of innovative IT / Telecom solutions, Internet + Finance, SEO, marketing, usability, social media, instant messengers, security , international technology standards, CRM, loyalty, customer service, Big & Fast Data Solutions, mobile communications, web analytics and much more. A complete list of topics and speakers is presented on the official website of the event .

The exhibition will feature the stands of partner organizations of the conference, as well as other exhibitors - leaders of the Russian high-tech industry. The total exhibition area is over 1000 sq. M., More than 50 Russian and foreign companies will present their achievements. The exhibition platform RIF + KIB 2016 is a great place for lively communication with market participants, conducting negotiations, establishing business relations, new acquaintances and business connections. Also at the exhibition will work Press and Media Center, charge areas, recreation and entertainment.
