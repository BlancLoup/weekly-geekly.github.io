Wood shower - the concept of modern barrels for washing



I am sure that this post will not collect a million "pluses", but I could not post it. Too vivid example of the notorious isolation of some artists from life.

In fact, this 12-liter cube is nothing more than a portable shower concept for a dacha. You hang it on a tree, you pull out a retractable hose - and you water. The pressure is created thanks to a small pump that runs on a battery.

Unfortunately, the author of the project - designer Peter Hempl (Petr Hampl) does not explain how to pour water into the tank. However, he also does not explain how to adjust the pressure and heat the water. According to the mind, 12 liters is enough except to wash the cat. In this regard, it seems that if you run with a bucket, then the presented concept is not very different in functionality from the usual barrel with a hose.
