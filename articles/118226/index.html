Dingoo A320 vs Gemei A330 - unboxing

And finally, I received the long-awaited Dingoo A320 and Gemei A330, which are vigorously discussed in the comments to the previous post . As promised to everyone, I post many photos (they are all clickable) and my first impressions.

The parcel box is slightly crumpled, and our valiant DHL tried and knocked out more ‚Äúcustoms clearance‚Äù money from me, but still they are here:



Inside the box there are: two Dingoo A320 , one Gemei A330 , a charger for the A330, a scattering of adapters for sockets (there are a total of 5 shots in different places), a crumpled console instruction on a dozen A4 sheets, and most importantly - plastic polyethylene, rolled up in the lump in the corner of the box! ;)



Discarding all unnecessary, we open two boxes:



Each of them has: the game console itself in a bag, some paper for Chinese fans, a miniUSB cable and headphones.
The A320 still has its own charger and cable for connecting to a TV, while the A330 comes with a protective film on the screen and an adapter microSD-> miniSD.
Near the console look like this:






To the touch, the A320 is assembled much more qualitatively - nothing creaks, everything is done very nicely. It looks more expensive than its value. The A330 squeaks slightly at the case, but for China the quality is very good, the fours plus.
But the screen A330 looks more juicy and it has much wider viewing angles. In the photo LCD panel look awful, but in real life, both do not cause any complaints.






Today is not too sunny day, so checking outside did not bring clarity - the image on both screens in sunlight is approximately the same, glossy screens, can be used as a mirror;)

Another label was attached to the A330, which shows the production date:


This unboxing is complete. The A320 successfully got Dingux out of here , only the miniSD card had to be formatted in a card reader.
After that, Dingux local pack v035 was installed, which I am now scouting.

I wish a speedy arrival to all those who are still waiting for their parcel! Both consoles are on my table, so I am ready to try to answer any questions that may arise.

Update. As a source of reference information, I recommend a320.emulate.su - an excellent Russian-language resource about this console.

And a couple of good reviews of the A330 model - on emulate-su.livejournal.com and on gbatemp.net (English)

Update 2. It seems that this is the Gemei A330 Fashion - it does not support multiplayer between two consoles.
