Asus G55VW laptop video review



Asus G55VW is a new gaming laptop from the gaming line of Asus - Republic Of Gamers.

The novelty works on the Intel Core i7 3610QM processor (there are modifications on the i7-3720QM and i5-3210M) with the support of up to 16 gigabytes of DDR3 RAM. The 15.6-inch display with a matte finish and a resolution of 1920x1080 (1366x768 as an option) is responsible for the image, and some modifications also support the three-dimensional image thanks to Nvidia 3D Vision 2 technology.

For graphics processing, the Nvidia GeForce GTX 660M graphics card with two gigabytes of GDDR5 memory is used.

As a data storage in various modifications of the Asus G55VW can be used as traditional hard drives (5400 or 7200 revolutions per minute), and solid-state drives.
