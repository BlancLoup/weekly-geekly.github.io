Motorola will present a new Droid Turbo on October 28

In the family of "droids" is expected to soon replenishment. Motorola and US mobile operator Verizon will introduce a new Droid Turbo on October 28. There was no official statement about this, but yesterday Verizon sent invitations to the specialized press, and on its website DroidDoes.com posted a screensaver with a countdown to the presentation.



The fact that Verizon will present exactly Turbo, which is already called the most powerful smartphone in the line of "droids", has also not been mentioned anywhere. But, given the data leaks and rumors in the press, it will be him.

Droid Turbo, which will be available in two colors - red and black metallic - is recognized by specialized media as the most anticipated smartphone of 2014. Earlier it was reported that its 3.9 mAh battery will fully charge from a wireless charger in just 15 minutes and withstand 8 hours in active use (48 hours standby).
