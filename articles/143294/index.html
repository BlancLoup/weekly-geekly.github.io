The Elder Scrolls Online

GameInformer reports that the first details about the upcoming IMO world TES are waiting for us in the June issue of the magazine.



The theme of the June issue of Game Informer will be a journey of editors all over Tamriel, from Elsweyr to Skyrim and everywhere in between.

Developed by Zenimax Online Studios, TES Online combines the unsurpassed world exploration experience that TES series is famous for and the social component of multiplayer online games. Players will open a completely new chapter in the history of the world The Elder Scrolls. TES Online takes place about a thousand years before the events of TES V: Skyrim, when the Prince of the Daedra Molag Bal tried to send the whole of Tamriel to his demonic world.
