Top 100 ZX Spectrum Games for iOS

Today, when the Sinclair ZX Spectrum is 30 years old , the British studio Elite Systems has released an application for iOS, representing the top 100 games for the ZX Spectrum.



Appendix 100 Greatest Hits gives you access to 25 packs of 4 games each (such unfading classics like Chuckie Egg, Saboteur !, Manic Miner, Barbarian, etc.) and another classic fighting game Bruce Lee. All games are available in one application.

The games on the new mobile platform were restored almost in the original - the emulator retained not only the appearance of the old games, but also the sound, and the authors tried to simplify management as much as possible and added original box art and instructions for authenticity.

The iPhone version costs $ 8.99: itunes.apple.com/ru/app/id500523067

IPad Version - $ 12.99: itunes.apple.com/ru/app/id514592906
