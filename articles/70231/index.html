Meet the Kyuubi!


Hello! It so happened that the .by zone is for me native. On the one hand, it is still quite green, and many of the necessary sites have to be searched on the neighboring Internet, and on the other hand, it is already mature enough to develop independently. Today I am PR and want to acquaint habraradey with my brainchild - his name is Kyuubi, and he is the first of its kind Internet assistant Belarusian spill.

Kyuubi almost as alive: he knows how to move his eyes and perform about eight more useful actions. I'll tell you about them in order: first, I taught Kyubika to shorten links and send notes . From any long and ugly links, he can make a concise and beautiful (the name, by the way, you can choose yourself), on which you can go with a preview or without. And when shortened references are collected enough, they can be written onto a sticker, put in a lock and told Kyuby to burn this record after reading.

Also, in order to have something to reduce, I added a search based on Yandex.XML, and then also an IP identifier and SEO tools . SEO tools are able to determine Yandex CY and Google PR sites, and the IP identifier is so far limited to an IP address.

In addition to the above, Kyuubi has a small online file hosting service that stores single megabyte files for up to 24 hours, does not ask to enter captcha and, after registration, allows you to upload as many as 5Mb at a time. In addition to four additional megabytes, registration gives access to the RSS-aggregator , which, moreover, is the first and only one in Bynet today. At the moment, the aggregator is in test mode, so that when using it, unexpected bugs may occur, which I ask to report here or in feedback .

For a snack, I want to offer to the court of the Harabrasocial community a broadcast of the Belarusian twitter- sphere broadcasting 24/7 twitter status from all over Belarus. That, in fact, has resulted in three weeks of hard work, stretched over four months of real time. The fruits of labor are available to all comers, and users of the largest Belarusian Internet provider also by guest connection.

We welcome your opinions, comments and, perhaps, praise,
Yegor and Kyuubi. ;)
