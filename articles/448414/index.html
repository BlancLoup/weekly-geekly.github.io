Let's start with a simple - test strips

It may not surprise anyone, but a huge amount of them to detect a variety of diseases, including cardiovascular, oncological, ophthalmic, STDs, etc.

All such strips have the appropriate medical certificates, have clinical accuracy and are often used by emergency doctors for urgent understanding of what is happening to the patient at the time of the call. So the question of trust as such should not be raised.

What is important to note in our opinion. Test strips are a diagnostic tool, but at home they should not become a guide to action. They can serve for the early detection of any problems, but for further diagnosis and conclusions you still need to see a doctor!

Let's try to enumerate for a start what is actually possible to reveal at home.

Analysis of urine

There are test strips for urine analysis. They can be both for general analysis and for identifying individual parameters: acidity, protein, glucose, etc. Prostor is huge. It seems to us that it makes sense to immediately consider a general analysis.

Infections

Here we mean tests that can be done at home to identify various infections. There are, for example, tests for determining Helicobacter pylori, tests for identifying various STDs from syphilis to HIV, tests for tuberculosis at home, hepatitis and other infectious diseases.

Oncology tests



There are home tests that allow you to quickly identify occult blood in the feces or more particular ones, for example, to detect alpha-fetoprotein in the blood, which may be an indirect indication of ovarian tumor; prostate-specific antigen (prostate cancer); cancer and embryonic antigen (lung tumors, gastrointestinal tract).

The cardiovascular system

Such tests in particular can help determine a heart attack within 2-4 hours after the first symptoms. There are tests for the determination of cardiac troponin, the determination of creatine kinase-MV, myoglobin.

Ophthalmic

Ophthalmologic test strips are divided into two types, as it were: one of them is not diagnostic: it allows you to simply understand how well the lenses are installed. There are also test strips for detecting corneal epithelium injuries (eg, Rose Bengal) or for the diagnosis of dry eye syndrome.

Separately, say a few words that there are test strips for the definition of drugs and alcohol.
