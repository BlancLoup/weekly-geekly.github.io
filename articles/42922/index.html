Video tours on Infocom2008

We ( VZOchat ) have figured out how to massively test our mobile client. Namely, the communicators (AnyData, CDMA, WM) were distributed to the girls at the Skylink stand, taught (I hope) to use the mobile client and asked them to answer calls. And so, from the page http://vzochat.com/ru/InfoCom2008.aspx you can try to call directly to the exhibition and ask to show how it is there. Usually girls sit on a stand, but you can ask them to walk, walk up to a stand, etc. (may refuse :)

Understandably, you need to call either a VZOchat client or a widget (the plugin in the browser is put on its own if there is no client in about half a minute). You may not have cameras, just a microphone and speakers (a headset is better). Registration is not required, but is desirable. The client and the widget work only on PC and Windows (IE, FF, Opera). Makovtsam is a separate hello, the version for them is not ready yet, but soon.
