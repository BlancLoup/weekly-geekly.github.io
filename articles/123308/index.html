The ideal desk for daily work at the computer

All the best of the day, I want to boast a good habra audience with my future order. So, recently I went into the office in the office and from one employee of our company I saw such an interesting table:





(this employee is not in the photo , taking the opportunity that he was on a business trip, asked a colleague to take part in a photo shoot)

So, the table has a little non-standard design, the main function is to quickly change the height of the table top, at any time and without any additional manipulations with the contents of the table. Those. tired of sitting - quickly raised the tabletop with all the contents and work standing up. Tired to stand, reverse order :). Everything is very easy and fast. The table top has dimensions of 130 cm (length) by 60 cm (width)


It rises easily - a principle like office chairs.
Height varies (up or down) in the range from 76 cm (sitting) to 101 cm (standing).


On the ruler, you can remember and re-establish a comfortable height.


Sockets are always nearby and even with a raised table top the length of the wires is enough.
Wires are hiding in a box under the tabletop.


It looks like a table with a monitor on the rack.
Stand under the monitor has a height of 40 cm (ie, the center of the display is at a height of 40 cm from the table top). This option is suitable for monitors that have 4 mounting holes on the back of the bracket (type VESA).


Wide sliding tabletop, and placed the keyboard and mouse.


The table rests on the front legs, and on the rollers at the same time, it is at the same time a stable and, if necessary, mobile option.
