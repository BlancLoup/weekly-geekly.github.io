USB set as a gift

If you need to make a gift to any of your friends or acquaintances, then you should not pass by the cute and pleasant USB trinkets, which are in abundance on the virtual shelves of the Brando store. New Brando - USB-kits for men and for women .


I personally would have a USB gift set, which includes a mini-vacuum cleaner with a brush for cleaning the keyboard and a lighted pen stand with a USB hub for 4 ports bought is unlikely. In the sense of personally and for personal use. But to get it as a gift would not mind. In addition to a vacuum cleaner and a stand, a desk lamp and a cup heater are included in the men's kit. In the women's heater, the mirror is replaced, and the desk lamp is replaced with a small fan. All components of the set are made in the same color scheme. Men's trinkets carefully painted in silver, female - in pink. The price of the sets is only $ 30.
