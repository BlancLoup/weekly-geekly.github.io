Choosing the best innovative project in 2010

Innovation Project Competition


In the past year, the Innovators Club held 15 meetings, at which many interesting projects were presented. We believe that the time has come to choose the best of the best and declare open the ‚ÄúCompetition of Innovative Projects‚Äù. According to the results of the competition, the best innovative projects of 2010 according to the Innovators Club will be named. The winners will receive prizes from a company whose founder is confident that innovation is what distinguishes the leader from the catch-up.

Participation in the competition


Authors, managers and coordinators of innovative projects are invited to participate in the competition. Stage of development of the project and its focus does not matter. The main criteria are innovation and prospects for commercialization. We also evaluate the level of your presentation. Following all the presentations, the expert council will select the best projects that will receive diplomas and valuable prizes. The prize for the best project is not a lot - PocketBook Pro 902.

Our competition is held in conjunction with the organizing committees of two international conferences (X Conference on High Technologies and MES-Conference 2010), as well as in cooperation with partner organizations of the Club. This allowed us to attract leading scientists and professionals in the field of innovation management to the expert council.

The competition will be held in St. Petersburg, but will be available to all. Authors of projects from other cities can send a video clip (500 seconds long) with a project presentation. And viewers will be able to watch everything happening through the online broadcast and comment on the projects being performed via twitter.

At the meeting


In addition to the competitive program, everyone who comes will have a lot of new information, delicious coffee-break pies and free expert advice. So we invite everyone who wants to usefully spend the Sabbath day to us for the final Competition of the Year.

Time and place


The competition will be held on December 11, 2010, 2:00 pm in St. Petersburg, at ITC ITMO, Exchange Line, 14-16 ATTENTION: due to the large number of registrations, we decided to change the venue, the new address is two steps from the previous one: Exchange Line , d.4 (council of rectors) . The building has a pass system, so you must first register to participate in the competition.
