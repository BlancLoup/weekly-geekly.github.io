The principles of the brain. Synergistic approach to brain activity, behavior and cognitive activity


Principles of Brain Functioning: Brian Activity, Behavior and Cognition


Publisher: PER SE, 2001
Hardcover, 352 pp.
ISBN 5-9292-0047-5
Circulation: 5000 copies

Hermann Hacken is an eminent German scientist, well known in Russia as one of the pioneers of the term "synergy" and the synergistic approach to science and interdisciplinary research. His books "Synergetic", "The hierarchy of forms of instability in non-equilibrium structures", "Self-organization and information" were translated into Russian and were most favorably met by the Russian scientific community.



The proposed book, published in Germany in 1996 and first translated into Russian, is an excellent example of a synergistic approach to the study of the natural science and philosophical problems of society and man on the example of such a complex process as the functioning of the brain, behavior and the cognitive process of man.

The book is equipped with an extensive scientific apparatus, a large number of color and black-and-white illustrations and is addressed primarily to professionals involved in this topic, but can also be used to teach students of relevant specialties in universities.
