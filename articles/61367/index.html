iCamp / StartCamp 2009 - the most informal conference

From July 24 to 26, the Informal Conference of Entrepreneurs iCamp / StartCamp 2009 will be held in the cultural and educational center ‚ÄúEtnomir‚Äù.

iCamp / StartCamp 2009 takes place outside the city in tents and yurts, filled with fresh air and modern technology. She has almost no people in shirts, and absolutely for sure - in ties. And it removes many barriers to communication, which are filled with big cities and expensive offices.


The main idea of iCamp / StartCamp 2009 is to enable entrepreneurs who have already started or are just planning to start their own business, get the necessary knowledge and experience, establish connections and speak out loudly about themselves.

iCamp / StartCamp 2009 is a fair of projects at early stages (startups). Anyone can come and take part in it. The conference program is based on the open principle, when each participant of the event can declare his topic, gather like-minded people and hold a seminar, master class, open workshop, competition or presentation.

The conference will be lecture, presentation and interactive sessions. And most importantly! Every entrepreneur will have the opportunity to present his project (even if he is now at the idea level) to professional investors and experts, to get feedback and, perhaps, an invitation for further negotiations on cooperation.
Registration fee is missing. The road to the event, participants pay for accommodation and meals on their own. The level of your stay - from your own tent to a yurt or a comfortable hotel room - you choose.

The iCamp / StartCamp 2009 is carried out by the Social Design Corporation. The organizational partner is ROCIT .

The partners of iCamp / StartCamp are the AddVenture Foundation, ABRT Venture Fund , Trinity Technopark , Datigra , Business Incubator of the HSE , the Start in Garage educational program.
