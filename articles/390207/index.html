In Nizhny Novgorod began testing the Russian exoskeleton for people with disabilities

In Nizhny Novgorod, an exoskeleton from Russian developers is tested on a healthy person. At the next stage, researchers will attract volunteers with diseases of the lower extremities. The main difference from competitors is the neuro-computer control interface.

The cost of the device will be no more than a million rubles, doctors hope to add it to the list of state-subsidized rehabilitation tools.
