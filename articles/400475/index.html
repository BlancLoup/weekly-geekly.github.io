Toothbrushes. Why they quickly become useless

Introduction


When I was small, I often noticed that my parents have toothbrushes very quickly becoming the same as in the picture above. From my such did not happen. And this concerned not only toothbrushes, but all the brushes in the house: for cleaning the carpet (a usual household brush), for cleaning shoes, even for an old broom made from rods. I did not attach any importance to this, because there are things that are much more interesting than thinking about brushes. Then I just decided that these are bad brushes.

Why it happens


Almost any brush is designed to clean the surface. But how does she do it? I am an artist so-so, but painted so that it was clear.

That is, you drive bristles over the surface, they spread their tips into small gaps and sweep debris from the surface. Some hard, sticky particles are scraped off due to the fact that many bristles in a short period of time stumble over them and gradually loosen.

Here the saddest thing is even a toothbrush not spoiled in 2 weeks, but the fact that the quality of tooth brushing is fading away. With the same success, you can rub them with not crumpled exercise book.

How to fix?


It's simple, you just need to press on the brush only slightly. On any brush. You can press hard on a rag, on a sponge, on some other means for cleaning surfaces, but not on brushes.
