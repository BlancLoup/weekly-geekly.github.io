Arsonists new buildings calculated through FindFace


Bullies did not know that there is such an application FindFace

The facial recognition service FindFace on the profile in the social network "Vkontakte" has recently become very popular. Many have fun, recognizing bystanders in the subway or porn actresses . And for the first time for FindFace, they have found really useful applications. With its help, Internet activists have identified the vandals, who staged an arson in the St. Petersburg new building.

It all started with a complaint on the website of the St. Petersburg portal Fontanka "( Google cache ).

Weekdays, two in the morning.

While the firefighters arrived, the fire was eliminated by the security forces of the neighboring corps.

The house is occupied by a third, no one knows anyone yet and, I repeat, the time is two in the morning.
