Owners of Wi-Fi equipment are charged with GLONASS

Owners of routers, laptops, smartphones and other devices working in Wi-Fi networks can lose their property at almost any moment and be fined. In any case, officials of the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media insist on the obligatory obtaining by users of such equipment of a license, permission to allocate frequencies, etc. In addition, according to the order of the liaison department that came into force on October 10, all wireless access points should be equipped with the GLONASS satellite navigation system.

The Information and Public Relations Department of the Ministry of Communications and Media has unequivocally confirmed that they [routers, laptops, smartphones and other devices operating in the 802.11 standard] refer to equipment that is to be equipped with GLONASS or GLONASS / GPS satellite navigation equipment! In this case, the owner of such an access point requires licenses and permits from Roskomnadzor.
