We introduce a system of achievements on the Toaster

Hello everyone from the Toaster team!

In this post we want to introduce you to the system of achievements that we introduce on the Toaster. First of all, I would like to note that it was created in order to mark certain merit of the user to the resource, be it good questions, answers or, for example, editing someone else's content.



For all the details welcome under cat.

Types of achievements


In total there are 2 types of achievements: recurring and unique. At the moment, you can get 7 types of achievements, of which 2 recurring and 5 unique.

Duplicate achievements have 3 levels. Depending on the fulfillment of the conditions, you earn a badge of achievement of one of three levels, and at the same time you can have badges of all three levels. The badges of these achievements are issued 24 hours after the creation of the content. If the content has been deleted before the lapse of 24 hours, the achievement is not assigned. They can be obtained an unlimited number of times.



Unique achievements also have 3 levels. The achievement badge is issued only once, when the minimum condition is met. In the future, when the condition for obtaining the top-level badge is met, the previously received badge is replaced with a higher-level badge. Badges of these achievements are issued when the condition is met.



Achievement badges are displayed in the user profile in the "Information" section,



and cards with brief information - in the section "Achievements":



I would like to note that we have already given badges to those users who have fulfilled the conditions of achievements in the past. You can check their availability in your profile. And in order not to miss the new icons, enable the corresponding e-mail notification of achievement.
