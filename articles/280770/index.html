Mobile OCR. How it all began

The transfer of OCR technologies (optical recognition technologies) from a PC to mobile devices has been discussed, perhaps, from the moment they appeared. Back in the late 90s, when smart devices could be counted on the fingers, we thought about creating a program that extracts data from a business card, the Business Card Reader, or BCR for short.

On the second day of the conference, Vice President Scott Guthrie said: Xamarin cross-platform solutions will be integrated into Visual Studio and will be free, even in Community Edition.
