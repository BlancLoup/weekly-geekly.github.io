Mail.Ru Group has acquired the service RuTwit.ru



Domestic microblogging service RuTwit.ru, it seems, is really a good analogue of Twitter. In any case, it was this service that Mail.Ru Group paid attention to. in fact, ‚Äúpaid attention‚Äù - an incorrect expression in this case, since the group simply bought out RuTwit.ru. According to the management of Mail.Ru Group, the service will receive a new life in a new company, and will be able to become even more interesting for users, therefore - it will be more popular than it is now.
