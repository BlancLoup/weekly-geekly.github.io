We open registration on Bynet 2.4

Preparation for Bynet 2.4 is in full swing. This year there will be a lot of new things - both in detail and in the concept itself.

What remains the same?

The conference is still free. As before, the organizers are Artem Gerasimovich, director of the portal it-job.by and Sergey Nazaruk, director of the Internet advertising agency Singular Advertising. We are still open for cooperation.

Opportunity to listen to authoritative speakers, meet colleagues, discuss current affairs on the sidelines ... We clearly realize how important such events are for the Bynet. Therefore, we will welcome any feedback and suggestions that will help make the conference better.

What's new? To begin with, this year the conference is divided into sections. In past years, we faced a problem - people registered for both days at once only in order to visit several reports that were most interesting to them. This year we have divided the conference program into sections that are interesting for various groups of audiences.
Well and the important moment - the number of places is limited : 250 people.

Section Business on the Web - a section for heads of marketing departments, marketers and company executives, entrepreneurs. In this section we are talking about internet marketing. That is relevant, what works today and what will work tomorrow. That does not work, did not work and will not work. How to really solve marketing problems on the web.

Web Development Section . This year we will not dwell on narrow topics. We want to discuss the key points - the organization of business processes in a web studio. Today, for most Belarusian developers, the issues of optimizing internal processes are relevant. Today in Belarus, both developers and managers are forced to fill the same bumps, make the same mistakes, independently go through the whole path of formation. There are very few examples of successful web studios, it is almost impossible to learn from the experience of colleagues and learn from the mistakes of others. We will try to consider and discuss the most important and fundamental aspects in setting up web development processes.
