Passed DConf 2013 - Conference on the programming language D

A little more than a week ago, ended a three-day conference on the programming language D.
About him have repeatedly written on Habrahabr, and the conference itself was devoted to a separate post where you can see the full list of reports.

In short:
  • She passed from 1 to 3 May 2013
  • Venue - Facebook Headquarters in California
  • Presentations were made by the developers of the compiler and the standard language library, its creator Walter Bright, as well as representatives of companies that use D in the development
  • The funds for the conference were raised by the Kickstarter community.

Report directions:
  • Techniques and tools for effective development on D
  • Experience of using D in solving applied problems: writing compilers, research and development of games
  • Language Interiors: Metaprogramming, Specific Design Patterns, SIMD
  • Discussions of the future language between its developers
