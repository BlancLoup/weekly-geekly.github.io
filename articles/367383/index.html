Alfa Bank changes SMS to USSD messages

Alfa-Bank continues to redo Alfa-Click: now there is a warning for users that soon one-time passwords will be sent to users not by SMS, but transmitted via USSD.



There is also a reasoning for the innovation:

  • USSD is a more secure channel for sending one-time passwords than SMS.
  • USSD message with a one-time password come almost instantly.
  • The USSD message will immediately appear on the screen of your phone - there is no need to go to the SMS section.
  • A USSD message with a one-time password is not stored in the phone and, accordingly, does not ‚Äúlitter‚Äù your important SMS history from Alfa-Bank.

They honestly warn about the disadvantages of the USSD:

  • If you close, then the message will disappear permanently (not saved anywhere) - you will need to re-request the password.
  • On the phone with the IOS operating system, which is in "sleep mode", the USSD message will not come. You will need to request a password again. It is better to keep the phone active at the time of receiving a one-time password.
  • Depending on the phone model and operating system installed, a USSD message may come without sound. It can not be configured.
  • Currently, one-time USSD passwords will be sent only to Beeline, MTS, Megafon subscribers. For subscribers of other operators one-time passwords will come in the form of SMS-messages.
  • When you re-request a password, it will also come in an SMS message.
