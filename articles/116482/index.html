The Large Hadron Collider is 20 years old

In 1991, the game Another World was released about the scientist Lester Chaikin, who as a result of an experiment on the acceleration of particles fell into another world.

In childhood, this game was perceived as something amazing thanks to a thoughtful plot, excellent vector graphics, video sequences, music, and drawing technology using rotoscoping. Among other things, all this miracle was placed on a 5-inch diskette with a capacity of 1.2MB.

I remember we played it for hours and re-passed all levels several times. Many dangers and traps, as well as riddles that could not be solved right away, made this game not only a means of rocking virtual muscles, but also food for the mind.

It was almost complete immersion. No descriptions of where you went and what will happen in the end. The atmosphere of loneliness and tension.

A few years later, I learned that the game has a sequel and was looking for it for a very long time. As a result, Heart of Alien was very disappointed. From the original gameplay of Eric Shayi, almost nothing is left, and play for almost all the time accounted for humanoid friend Lester. But the most annoying - at the end of the game Lester Chaikin dies.

By chance I came across a video about creating a game that I propose to habrovchanam. There are many points in it that should be learned by the developers of modern games.
