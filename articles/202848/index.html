Havok holds a competition for mobile game developers.


Do you develop mobile platform games and want to reach a new level? Havok, one of the leaders in the global gaming industry, gives you this opportunity. In addition, each participant in the competition who submitted his development to the intermediate finish - February 1, 2014 - can count on the advice of experienced Havok specialists aimed at improving the product. The final version must be submitted to the jury until May 31.
