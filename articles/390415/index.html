I want to be an entrepreneur: 8 good chances to start



What money to develop the project? How to enter the international market? Where to train? For any novice entrepreneur, these are extremely burning questions. If you are planning to create your own project , you have really unlimited possibilities . The most interesting of them are described below.


1. International Conference in California Startup Conference


One of the most serious events of Silicon Valley will be held on May 19 with the participation of start-up entrepreneurs from around the world. Here they are waiting for those who wish to present their projects, find allies, show themselves to investors and media representatives, learn a lot of new things at lectures and seminars.

The price of pleasure is two hundred dollars. Applications can be submitted almost to the day of the meeting. For participants in the competition for the best startup, the last day for submitting an application is April 15.


2. Grants from Microsoft


The IT giant provides many new opportunities for development in the form of webinars, conferences, grants and scholarships, software, current publications and links to sources, access to the "clouds", discounts for startups. They are also given free Microsoft BizSpark content.

Participate in the program can companies that develop replicated software or services based on it. Deadlines may vary.


3. Grant Exist


Students and graduates who wish to transform an innovative business idea into a profitable project can participate . Funds can be up to three thousand euros, deadlines are missing.


4. Grant for engineering projects


This European program is designed for a six-month period. To participate in it, the organizers are looking for those who are dreaming of their own project, but so far have only an idea. Here you will be helped to find someone who wants to invest in you. Reception of applications ends on December 31.


5. Russian-Finnish Foundation for Entrepreneurs


Contributes to the development of small firms in terms of science and technology, about fifteen million rubles is allocated for financing. Winners are determined ninety days after receiving the documents, which ends February 26 .


6. Startup Tour from Skolkovo


The largest Russian competition for those who are interested in information technology. Its initiators will visit eleven Russian cities (Almaty and Minsk are also included in the tour program), having appraised for all time about one and a half thousand teams. The winners will be able to show themselves at Startup Village, sharing the prize of 100 million rubles. Reception of applications ends on December 31.


7. Google Developers Launchpad


The program should be of interest to Google Developers Launchpad startups and application developers , for whom the sum of one hundred thousand dollars is allocated. It has a division into two stages. The initial implies the development and development of products in the early stages, and at the next level the project is scaled. Applications are accepted until December 31.


8. Summer school for startups


To be held in Sussex (UK) on September 16-18. Here you can present your own projects, learn the intricacies of creating a business plan, learn how to avoid mistakes by starting your activities. You can register for this school right now on the site .
