Tagsrating.com - directory of sites, distributed by tag



Users often complain that they can not find something new or, on the contrary, similar to what they read. Search using a conventional search engine is not always able to give the desired results. To solve this problem, we created Tagsrating , a resource aimed at facilitating search on the Internet. Our database contains more than 500,000 websites and more than 30,000 tags in all languages. You can search for tags by getting a list of suitable sites with brief information about them: tags, space taken and monthly number of visitors in the US and around the world. Related tags are shown on the right, which can also facilitate searches or interest you.



By selecting the domain of interest, the user receives a lot of information: site rating by tags, description, domain age, daily audience reach, a list of similar sites read by other users, the monthly number of unique visitors, demographic profiles, search engine details and many more relevant information. From here, the user can go to any of the sites or tags, or update the report.



The "Top 1000" page contains a list of thousands of the most popular sites, and "Recently visited" - the last 10 domains and tags viewed by the user.
Having found something interesting, you can immediately share it with everyone: Twitter, Facebook, MySpace, Google, and many more social services bookmarks are at your service. To speed up the search and ease of navigation in the presence of toolbars to popular browsers Mozilla Firefox and Internet Explorer.

Tagsrating is available in three languages: Russian, English and Ukrainian.

From additional details - the domain information page can be saved in pdf and printed without advertising, upper, left blocks and other tinsel.

Fox Toolbar is available at: addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/161818

Website address: http://tagsrating.com
