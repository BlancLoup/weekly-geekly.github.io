Giving robots for the New Year

The new year is left to wait for long, and many have already planned their holidays. On the other hand, we often start thinking about gifts only in the last New Year's Eve days. For those who buy gifts in advance or just want to prepare an unusual gift, this review article has been prepared. I recommend everyone to save the article in the bookmarks so that the numbers of the 28th suddenly remember it, open it, and, without puzzling, run to the store. As you already understood from the title, we will choose exclusively from robots, and, perhaps, a couple of radio-controlled models.

You can pay attention to the possibly impractical, but interesting and cute butterfly in the bank.


It is enough to knock on the lid of the jar as the butterfly begins to flutter inside the jar. What do housewives regularly do? That's right - clean the apartment. Want to please your favorite, then save her from the dreary of cleaning. It is enough to buy a robot vacuum cleaner, the benefit of models and manufacturers is already enough on the market to have something to choose from.

We will not dwell on all models, consider the 3 most interesting and different in price.
The most inexpensive model from iRobot (we will not consider Chinese junk, such as QQ-02) is the Roomba 521 model worth 12,800 rubles.



Workhorse no frills. The price is relatively low due to the lack of additional accessories included. There are no additional brushes, there are no virtual walls and the more so beacon coordinators. In addition, the robot is not programmed by the day of the week and removes no more than 3 rooms on one charge. On the other hand, Roomba 521 is no worse than older brothers in cleaning quality. The result will be about the same. The same sensors of distance, height, side brush for cleaning in the corners. Just press the button and you can do other nice things.



More advanced, but also more expensive model - Neato cost from 19,500 rubles. A fairly well-known model in the foreign market and less popular in the Russian.



A unique feature is the presence of a laser scanning system for the room, thanks to which the robot vacuum cleaner can construct a room map for itself. Moving on this map, Neato will more effectively pass the sites. The vacuum cleaner is also programmed for the week ahead. You can set the cleaning time tomorrow at 15-00 and forget the household chores. Come home from work - the robot removed everything and got back to the base for recharging.

More and more expensive models from iRobot differ mainly in the presence of many accessories, increased dust collector and a more capacious battery.



The above robots vacuum cleaners do not know how to wash the floors, only dry cleaning. For washing you will need to purchase a Scooba model.



Due to the four-stage model of cleaning, the robot very qualitatively launders tile, tile, laminate, linoleum and other types of floors. Model iRobot Scooba 385 removes up to 85 squares per charge.
