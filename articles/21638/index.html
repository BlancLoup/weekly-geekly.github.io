Question for Neo

This is not news, not a question, not a presentation, it is a discussion on the topic.

Here you are, people who went to this site and read this post - who are you? Not the first time in the network, having been on more than one dozen sites, expressing their opinions, reading someone else's, earning their bread (to whom with butter, to whom with caviar) using the same network. Who you are?

The network was originally planned as an information repository, as a storehouse of knowledge - but humanity did not limit itself to wikipedia and the digitization of the Great Soviet Encyclopedia. The network went further. Fido, chat rooms, social networks (I do not want to talk about what will happen next) what unites them?
Communication and entertainment.

You wondered when was the last time you were on the net looking for something to live off the net? Is the time that you spend on the net commensurate with how much information you have found for living in real life?
