Microsoft Hyper-V Server 2008 is available for free download.

Microsoft Hyper-V Server 2008 is available for free download, as promised a month ago. Let me remind you that only 64-bit processors with virtualization technology are supported.
Find out if your processor supports this technology:
Intel :
In Processor Spec Finder, select your processor family and filter by the presence of EMT64 and Intel Virtualization Technology
AMD :
Check the AMD Virtualization option for your processor here.
This product, apparently, will become a serious competitor to both paid VMWare and free VirtualBox , Xen and others.
