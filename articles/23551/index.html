"Routes of Moscow" bypass the river bridges



The route network of Moscow is very well developed, but despite this, metropolitan transport can not always take you directly to home. Sometimes it is an extra reason to walk along the bridge and enjoy the beautiful views. And sometimes there is no bridge and the obstacle can be overcome only by swimming. How to be?

The Moscow Routes transport service has updated the route search algorithm and now takes into account the bridges and rivers of the capital. Now in the issue should not appear routes in which it will be proposed to cross the river where there is no bridge.

The river is considered insurmountable on foot, if the distance to the nearest bridge is greater than a third of the distance between the points in a straight line.

Calculation of rivers and bridges from today is available in test mode. Developers will thank users for information about any inaccuracies found.
