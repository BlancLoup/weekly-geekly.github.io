MODX Revolution Test Sites



I present to you my service for a close acquaintance with the MODx Revolution. No registrations, sms and other nudyatiny.

Absolutely free, for a day you get a freshly installed site on the Selectel cloud with admin rights.
It is like a demo site, only for one person and with the possibility of full-fledged work. You will get access within 10 - 15 minutes after some time, for Habraeffect.

After 24 hours, this site will be automatically deleted.

The main task of the service is to give quietly acquainted with an excellent system, as well as to at least a little dispel the myths of "inhibition" and "complexity."

Tellingly, the management of these services is also made a component for MODx Revo.

At the moment, the service has the following features:


  • Selecting the right packages during installation.
  • The ability to download the site dump during the day after removal.
  • Selecting an installation template. So far, only one has done - shop on miniShop.


There are limitations, but minor


  • 24 hour limit of site life
  • Sending mail from the server is disabled (but you can use third-party SMTP)
  • 100Mb of disk space for each site
  • Only 50 sites per day for everyone
  • PHP memory_limit = 32M
  • PHP max_execution_time = 10
  • PHP enabled open_basedir
  • PHP enabled disable_functions

In general, such that I do not fly into the payment of hosting and not be banned by all sites on the Internet.
