Flash drive cartridge

Companies Crooks & Castels and Mojo Laboratories presented an interesting concept flash drive. As usual business in design. In spite of every minimalist tendencies, colorful ugliness (flash drives in the form of food or animals, for example) and glamor, their USB flash drive is made in the form of a cartridge.


The unnamed so far (and what to call it, the cartridge as the cartridge), the concept is a metal case, a chain for wearing around the neck, a built-in USB connector that hides under a screw cap (bullet) and 1 GB of volume for military lovers.


The only pity is that so far there is no data on the potential commercialization of the concept.
