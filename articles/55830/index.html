Railways: Pleasant surprises ...

The other day there was a need to purchase train tickets. There is an interesting service on the Russian Railways website - buying tickets via the Internet. I will briefly describe my actions:
  1. Registered on the site - a standard data set.
  2. Logged into the site under your account information.
  3. I issued tickets for myself and my children according to passport and birth certificates.
  4. Entered credit card details for payment (If within 10 minutes after the receipt of the order, it is not paid, then the order is canceled).
  5. Printed out two sheets with order data and barcode.
  6. I arrived at Kazansky railway station, stood in the appropriate window and prepared to wait for the end of the technological break, but then an employee came to a pleasant appearance and took me to the self-service terminals. (It turns out I lost 15 minutes for nothing)
  7. He brought the barcode to the scanner and entered the series and passport number without spaces.
  8. I pressed the "Print" button and got my tickets.
  9. Pleasantly surprised went home.
