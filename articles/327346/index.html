10 most profitable markets for launching a mobile game

In 2017, we tried to answer 2 main questions of mobile game developers: ‚ÄúIn which country should I release a game?‚Äù And ‚ÄúHow much can I earn at the same time?‚Äù. We analyzed almost all available research on this topic and ranked the profitability of mobile gaming markets. Here we collected data on the 10 largest mobile gaming markets and talked about some regional features.



At the beginning of 2017, several reports were released on the state of the mobile games market for 2016 and forecasts of its growth in the near future.

We took Statista's data as the basis of our own rating (as, on average, the most conservative of all known). It is worth noting that in some countries, data from other sources, for example, NewZOO, may differ significantly. However, how exactly all these companies obtained their data remains unknown.



Statista provides data on 49 countries. The revenue of the mobile games market for 2016 in them amounted to 27 billion dollars. As a rule, in such studies, countries are united by region (North America, Asia, Europe, etc.), and it is for the regions as a whole that the revenue of the mobile games market is calculated. We considered each country separately.

In addition, in our rating we tried to take into account the costs for entering each market. We calculated the cost of installing the application by all users and removed it from the total revenue in a particular country. So we obtained data on the marginal profit from the market.
