Region codes and vehicle characteristics in the search results

Motorists always have a lot of questions for Yandex. And if Maps and Navigator best deal with the construction of routes, other questions are better addressed to the search. Now on the search results page there are direct answers to some of them.

Any motorist is interested in what region an unknown number belongs to (especially if the owner cut you). Everything is clear with my city and neighboring regions, but there are many regions and territories in Russia, and some of them have several codes. For example, surely not everyone knows which region the code 91 belongs to . Now the search will easily answer this question.



The wizard works in the opposite direction - if you know the city, but do not know the code .

Ask, for example, [ auto codes ] - and the search results will be a convenient form in which you can enter regions or their numbers.



In addition, now immediately on the search results page you can get answers to other questions of motorists - for example, how much Citroen C4 eats and what is the clearance of Opel Corsa .



The search data is taken from Yandex.Avto , and you can go directly from the witcher to the service in order to learn more about all the technical characteristics of the car.
