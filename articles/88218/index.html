Opolis.eu correspondence new generation


A long time ago I wanted to write on Harbrabrabre. But everything was postponed. There was no serious topic.

Here I have the honor to introduce to you a startup with Austrian roots O! Polis , which is designed to encrypt and protect email.

So at the heart of the idea were several

1) The Power to the Sender - the sender of the message should have the right to control the letter. And he has all the ultimatum rights to control letters and attachments.
2) I Protect - all letters are immediately encrypted / encrypted and can only be decrypted and read by an authorized letter recipient and no one else. Oh! Polis uses Public-Private Key technology, and iTrust as a public key registrar for Oh-polis users. The developers guarantee that the letters can not be manipulated or falsified.
3) I Decide - the sender decides in advance when sending whether the recipient can copy, print, reply, or redirect the letter to another O! Polis-a user or not.
4) I Control - the sender can see the status of the letter actively and live.

The technology is inherently very interesting and innovative and in my opinion in the near future it will be very active to use different companies and individuals for whom the privacy of correspondence between them is important.
Now the service is completely free, but I think in a year and a half some restrictions will be imposed, and paid accounts according to the developers will be very affordable.
The service so far has an English interface. I spoke with the developers and I was told that the Russian version is on the way. So I will actively follow this startup and will be with the eyes and eyes of habrakhabrovtsev.
