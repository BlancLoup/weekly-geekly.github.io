Magento Community Edition - 1.4.1.1 released

While we were all sleeping peacefully here, a fresh release 1.4.1.1 of the Magento Community Edition e-commerce platform was released at the height of a sunny American day (that is, the free version of this open-source system).

The previous release 1.4.1.0 was released a month ago in June, and therefore the frequency of updates is quite pleasant. The main focus of the new release was on fixes for problems and errors found by users of the system, so only the youngest digit changed in the version.

Before updating, do not forget to make a full backup, clear the internal cache of the system and disable its compiler in the admin panel. The update itself can be done through Magento Connect Manager or by rolling the system from scratch on your data.
