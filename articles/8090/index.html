Comstar-Direct will launch the cheapest anlim in Russia

Internet access in Moscow has been cheaper for a long time than in the regions. Comstar Direct will be the champion in the capital the day after tomorrow, which will launch the Start tariff.

According to the terms of the tariff, for $ 8 users will get unlimited access at a speed of 128 kilobits per second, as well as 17 TV channels. And then a few pitfalls emerge: you can watch television for only four months, and in addition, you need to buy a decoder ($ 152) or rent it ($ 3 a month), and also purchase a smart card for $ 20.

But, whatever they say, the Comstar-Direct proposal will be the most profitable. Just below it in this improvised rating will be Komkor-TV, which offers Internet access at a speed of 120 kilobits per second and 22 channels for $ 9.3 per month.
