Make a start-up possible in your life?

Note: a little emotional, so let us forgive me in advance.

So, I suggest everyone reading this to ask themselves the question: is a startup possible in his life? The problem is that we are going and talking about the Internet business, about hi tec, about google, about anything, we all dream to repeat their success and are looking for a way to it. But can we stand still? Yes. For most it is.

We are looking for business ideas, day by day, some are trying to make money on
the same google, ebay, adsense and others, but guys, don't you see that making big money is more difficult? In fact, the amount of effort for earning $ 1000 and $ 1,000,000 is the same. Just in the first case, you will do what you have done before (for example, write CODE), and in the second case, what no one else has done. This insignificant difference in approach and increases the remuneration by orders of magnitude.
