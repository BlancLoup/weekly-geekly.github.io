A tale about how we printed a t-shirt

As we already found out in the post about the sublimation printing, everyone wants to be different . As one of the examples of how this can be achieved, we cited the Topshop trade network, in which anyone can order a print of a print on a T-shirt, simply by choosing a picture from the collection or by uploading their own. After that, the order goes to the store's specialists, who print on a special carrier, carefully transfer the imprint to the T-shirt and generally do a lot of routine work due to the peculiarities of the organization of sublimation printing.

With the help of specialists from our Moscow demozal, we decided to personally familiarize ourselves with the process of printing a T-shirt on an Epson SureColor SC-F2000 textile printer , which we recently talked about in one of the posts.

The printer allows you to perform direct textile printing on fabric, which we did not fail to use and removed a small report specifically for Habr!



Interesting Facts


  • The entire printing process, from downloading a file in the program to opening a thermal press, took about 5 minutes.
