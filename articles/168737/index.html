Moscow State Technical University im.Bauman launches discipline "Operating Systems" based on ReactOS

  A small step for one person is big for all mankind.

The text of this topic will be slightly longer than the message on Twitter, but I think the news itself is of great importance.

At the January meeting of ReactOS developers, the protocol of which was recently published on the official website, besides discussing participation in the upcoming CLT2013, launching a new website, extending the fundraising campaign and implementing the UDF file system, Alexey Bragin made an important announcement. Alexey announced that he had accepted the invitation of Bauman Moscow State Technical University from 2013 to become a teacher at this university and conduct a course on operating systems with ReactOS as a subject and an example for study .

All sympathizers and those who wish are invited to leave their suggestions and wishes for the content of the training course here in the comments or in Alexey's personal messages.
