23 questions that you ask at the interview at Apple

Apple is a company that is known not only for unique technological solutions, but also brainwashing questions at interviews. What and share.




1. “Tell us something about what you have done in your life and what you are especially proud of”
Interview for Software Engineering Manager position.


2. “Tell us about your mistakes - what did they teach you? „
Interview for Software Manager position.


3. “Describe an interesting problem that you have to solve”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


4. “Explain the function of the router / modem to the eight-year-old child”
Interview for At-Home Advisor.


5. “Why did you come here today?”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


6. “You have 100 coins. They lie on the table, each has two sides. 10 of them lie face-side up, and 90 - rear. You cannot feel, see or somehow recognize which side is where. You need to divide all the coins into two parts so that each of them has an equal number of coins that are face up. ”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


7. “What would you like to do in 5 years?”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


8. “Why do you want to be part of Apple and will you miss your current job?”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


9. “Tell me about yourself, what delights you?”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


10. “How would you test a toaster?”
Interview for Software QA Engineer position.


11. “If we hire you, what would you like to work on?”
Interview for the position of Senior Software Engineer.


12. “There are three boxes. Only in one apples, in the other only oranges, and in the third and oranges and apples. All boxes are labeled incorrectly (messed up). Having opened only one small box and took only one fruit, how can you immediately say which box contains what? ”
Interview for Software QA Engineer position.

Have you already gone offline interview at Microsoft ?

13. “Have you ever disagreed with a manager’s decision, how did you line up your arguments? Give a specific example and explain how you decided this dispute, what was the result of the decision, how does it describe you personally? ”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


14. “Why do we need to hire you?”
Interview for the position of Senior Software Engineer.


15. “Are you creative? Tell us about your own creative idea? ”
Interview for Software Engineer position.


16. “Describe not the most pleasant experience in your life (even humiliating)”
Interview for the Apple Retail Specialist position.


17. “What is more important: solving customer problems or positive customer experience when using the product?”
Interview for Apple At Home Advisor


18. "You seem rather positive, but what can upset you?"
Interview for the position of Family Room Specialist.


19. "Show me how you will demonstrate to the client the desire to help, using only a voice"
Interview for a College At-Home Advisor position.

20. “What was the best day for you in the last 4 years? And the worst?
Interview on the Engineering Project Manager.


21. "How many children on a planet are born every day?"
Interview for Global Supply Manager position.


22. “When you enter the Apple Store as a customer, what do you feel?”
Interview for Specialist position.


23. “If you have 2 eggs, and you need to find out from what maximum height you can throw an egg without breaking it, how will you do it / check? What is the best solution? ”
Interview for Software Engineer position.
