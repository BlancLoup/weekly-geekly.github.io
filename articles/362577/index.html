Parrot Minidron Overview



Gone are the days when a toy machine that wraps backwards from a click caused delight in children. Modern toys are more than just radio-controlled cars, but their distribution is still limited by the large number of numbers on the price tag. Well-known manufacturer of drones - the company Parrot - has introduced several new products to the market, which can be attributed to the niche of available mini-robots.

Parrot Jumping Sumo, in our opinion, is not only entertainment for children, but also a timekiller for their bored parents. Managed via Wi-Fi (using a smartphone or tablet), this device is capable of jumping to a height of over 70 cm and moving, instantly turning 90 or 180 degrees.

Parrot Rolling Spider - minidron suitable for the first acquaintance with the world of quadrocopters.

We will start from the earthly but jumping into the sky Jumping Sumo.

Control




Jumping Sumo connects to the phone or tablet via Wi-Fi - to do this, select the appropriate network from the list of wireless networks. Control is carried out through the application Free Flight, running Android, IOS and Windows Phone. Jumping Sumo has a maximum range of 50 meters (if there are no obstacles on the way), but the battery life from a fully charged battery will be a maximum of 20 minutes.

Tests




The big problem of all robots, small and large, is to overcome obstacles. A gadget that successfully chases a domestic cat across a carpet becomes almost helpless on an uneven surface. Parrot Jumping Sumo comes to the solution of the issue fully armed. Equipped with retractable wheels for greater stability, built-in gyroscope and accelerometer, the robot is capable of sharp turns and high jumps from the starting position.



The main feature of the gadget is fascinating jumps from a place. By squeezing the two springs, and then releasing them, the drone launches itself forward or upward by 80 centimeters. Climbing the stairs, jumping on a bed or a table is really fun.

The machine will accelerate to 7 km / h, which is convenient when you need to properly kick a piece or slip through a fleecy carpet. You can only move in a straight line, turning is possible at 90 and 180 degrees.

Wheels that provide good protection against falls, quickly wear out from driving over rough terrain outside the home / office. Movement on the grass is extremely difficult - Jumping Sumo does not have enough weight, the wheels slide. The conclusion is simple: the device is not intended for use outdoors.

Summing up


Minidrony - a new generation of gadgets designed to kill time. Not many toys can boast jumping like a Jumping Sumo. The compact dimensions of the device will allow you to explore the inaccessible (or captured) territory of your own apartment.
