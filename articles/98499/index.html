Opening of free access and new services in Synchronet

As part of this post, I would like to inform the habrasoobshchestvo about innovations in our service and tell those who do not know about us, what www.synchronet.ru can be useful for you. Of course, we are waiting for your constructive criticism.

So, the innovations are:

Added cross-platform file synchronization client on JAVA (Beta)
Disable the lifetime limit of the free version
Improved data access system
Fixed known bugs and errors

Why and what are we doing?

We want our users to conveniently store files, contacts, calendars, and other data of their computers, cell phones, and other devices in a single online repository, while having the opportunity to update this data on each of their devices as soon as possible. transfer them to another user online, community, or simply in open access. At the moment, in our opinion, we have implemented it in the service www.synchronet.ru . Did it happen - to judge you.

Such services already have you say, what distinguishes you? Here's what:

- Sync files to mobile devices

- Supports a large number of different platforms as well as third-party data synchronization applications.

- Sharing of files, groups of contacts, calendars with different access parameters for users, communities and simply open access

Our service contains free and commercial versions. Due to the existence of a commercial version, we hope to fully concentrate our efforts on the usability and functionality of our project, without trying to delay the user to view the advertisement or to force it with any other inconvenience.

As far as we know, we are one of the few Russian companies that has implemented a project of similar functionality and scale on Adobe Flex technology, except for the true homepage, help and registration pages.

If you have time and desire for feedback, we are very interested in your opinion about our project:

1. What does not like in our project?
2. How do you like our Flex interface?
3.Social functions, is it necessary?
4. Any other aspects of our product that you want to pay attention to.
