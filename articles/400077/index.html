Pharaoh 3D Printer - Mass Portal Pharao



3D printer Mass Portal Pharao - the differences between different modifications and our experience in repairing it.
Who is he - Pharaoh?

From the darkness of ages


Since the days of the Union, we all remember the quality Baltic electronics - transistor receivers, portable televisions and other high-tech products that were considered the best in the country. Those times are over, the plants have died, the glory has faded and, it would seem, everything is bad in the land of delicious sprats.

That was five years ago, but something has changed.


Mass Portal is a privately owned company that develops, manufactures and markets desktop 3D printers, founded in April 2012 and located in Riga, Latvia. The company took as an example the best samples of existing 3D printers and, having improved their characteristics, developed their own devices.


Pharaoh's ascent


In April 2014, Mass Portal unveiled the second generation of its main product - desktop 3D printers based on delta bots technology - the Mass Portal Pharaoh series. Mass Portal 3D printers are faster, quieter, and less prone to vibration than traditional 3D printers. Made in a laconic industrial design and with a concern for safety, these machines are most suitable for use in educational institutions and offices.



According to several manufacturers of independent materials (not intended for printers of a particular brand), as of mid-2015, Pharaoh was one of those providing the highest quality 3D printing.



Pharaoh's reign


The quality of the Mass Portal 3D printers, both the quality of their assembly, which ensures reliability and uninterrupted operation, and the quality and accuracy of work, which gives the best result when printed, enabled them to spread over several continents and attract deserved attention.
