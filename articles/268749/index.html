Benefits of Managed Security Service



In a corporate environment, cyber threats are becoming more and more sophisticated, security standards are more complex, and budgets are less and less.

The technological world at work is no longer limited to servers, workstations or mail accounts, and now we need to take into account also mobile devices and the principle of using our own devices in the BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) workplace. In addition, we need to be aware of the problems that may arise within the framework of new trends (such as social media ) and the extent to which they affect the security of our corporate networks.

All this leads to serious stress in enterprises when it comes to monitoring and managing information security.

What are managed security services?

Currently on the market there are new series products that position themselves as Managed Security Services. They offer typical services such as antivirus, firewalls, intrusion detection, updates, content filters and security audits, as well as other services designed to meet new needs that businesses may have. Such solutions are based on services operated by third-party companies that take responsibility for their operation and monitoring at any time.

A study conducted last year in the United States, Britain, Canada and Germany showed that 74% of enterprises still used self-managed security systems, but 82% of IT professionals worked in companies (or thought about working in such companies) that provided security management services.

Moreover, since many of them have the opportunity to work closely with law enforcement agencies, this means that they have additional advantages when it comes to performing an expert analysis in the case of a court.

Adaptive Defense as a managed security service

Bearing in mind everything mentioned above, Panda Security decided to offer its self-managed security solution Adaptive Defense . Thanks to the most advanced cloud computing technologies (based on machine learning techniques and Big Data) developed by Panda, Adaptive Defense is able to automatically classify all the running applications in the system without the need for any user intervention.

Adaptive Defense is also a solution that has zero impact on enterprise infrastructure, since is a centrally managed service via a web console that allows you to ensure the security of Windows workstations, servers, mobile phones and remote offices.
