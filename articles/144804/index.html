Twig. IPhone Multifunction Cable



Meet Twig (Prut) - cable tripod for iPhone, developed by former WIMM engineer Jason Hillborn.

The cable can be transformed into a stand for the phone, used as a tripod, save the headphone cable from the natural formation of sea nodes, in general, the use of this thing is limited only by your imagination.

While the project is collecting donations ( Kickstarter ), the guys need $ 50,000 (5700 collected at the time of writing the topic).

If you donate the amount of $ 18, then get one of the first devices that came off the assembly line.
