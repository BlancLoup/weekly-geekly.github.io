Action Camera SJ4000 based on Novatek NT96650 processor

Not so long ago, an interesting, in my opinion, action camera called the SJ4000, which outwardly copies the famous cameras from GoPro, appeared on the market. It is clear that this model is incorrect to compare with GoPro in terms of the quality of materials and iron, since the price difference is fivefold. Accordingly, the quality is different. But all the same, it is interesting to see what the manufacturers of the famous brand SJ4000 have spotted good. In addition, the accessory kit for this camera is very rich and partially compatible with GoPro. In order to show the full potential of this model as objectively as possible, he made more diverse surveys at different times of the day. But first things first.



> Technical Specifications


Matrix: CMOS, 12Mp;
Lens: 170 ¬∞ A + HD wide angle;
Processor: Novatek NT96650;
LCD display: 1.5 inches (4: 3);
Video resolution: 1920 * 1080/1080 * 720/848 * 480/640 * 480
Record format: MOV;
Video codec: H.264;

Photo format: JPG, 12 MP, 7 MP, 5 MP;

Additional options: single frame, delayed shooting (2s / 5s / 10s), face detection and smile mode, white balance adjustment, start recording video when the camera is turned on, charging from USB, serial and interval photography, cyclic video recording;

> Iron


The Novatek NT96650 processor installed in the camera easily processes video with a resolution of 1920 * 1080 at a frequency of 30fps without failures and overheating.
This processor was released in 2013, and at that time was the best in terms of price and quality. Many sales hits among DVRs are equipped with this processor.

The camera can shoot:
1) Video mode: 1080p_30k / s, 720p_60k / s, 480p_60k / s (hardware camera only supports 30fps, and 60fps in the video is obtained by software duplication of frames: two identical frames instead of one).
2) Photos with a resolution of 5Mp to 12Mp.

> Interface and software


The camera interface is simplified to minimalism: front - button on / mode switching; top - OK start / end of shooting; on the side - the volume-zoom-transition menu. All buttons are intuitive and easily searched in the blind.



When connected to a PC, the camera asks for an operation mode, either as a USB flash drive or as a webcam.
I will note some operating modes and functions: 1) Mode of cyclic video recording of 3/5/10 minutes and rewriting; 2) Photo timer 2s / 5s / 10s; 3) CAR DV mode (recorder in the car: the camera can start recording when the charger is connected and stop after); 4) Photos while recording.



> Shooting in various modes

SJ1000 can take photos with a resolution of 12 megapixels. The images are of medium quality, with some spatial distortion and a decrease in sharpness at the edges.

> Conclusion:


SJ4000 made a positive impression on me. For its price, this model deserves the highest praise.

Pros:
1. Shooting FullHD 1080p 30fps (clear picture with good illumination of objects)
2. 1.5 "color viewfinder
3. Micro-HDMI interface for video output
4. Recorder mode in auto: cyclic video recording (3, 5, 10 min.), Image rotation, video recording while charging, motion sensor
5. Compact size and light weight (59 g)
6. Waterproof protective box (up to 30m) and a rich set of accessories in the package
7. Compatibility with accessories for GoPro
8. Webcam mode when connected to computer
9. LED charging indicator, shooting mode

Minuses:
1. Lack of support for 60fps (Novatek NT96650 processor hardware limitations)
2. Date and time get lost after changing the battery.
3. When shooting in the dark, the picture loses clarity.
