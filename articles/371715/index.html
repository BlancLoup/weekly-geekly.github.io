The principle of operation of the REDMOND Smart Plug is quite simple. In addition, the outlet can be controlled via the Internet at any distance, even from another country. To do this, you need a gateway - any gadget on Android 4.3 with the free R4S Gateway program installed, which will receive signals from your main gadget over the Internet and transmit commands to the Redmond smart technology via Bluetooth.
