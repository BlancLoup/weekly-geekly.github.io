ICAgile class in Odessa October 31- November 2

Become an ICAgile Certified Professional! The first in Ukraine open certification class in Odessa, organized by Ciklum.
The first open class confirming the experience and understanding of the worldview and practices of Agile development, certified by Agile Manifesto creator Alistair Cobern. We are waiting for everyone in the sunny Odessa October 31 - November 2.




ICAgile Certified Professional class
The goal of the ICAgile course is to help participants understand that Agile is primarily a philosophy based on values ‚Äã‚Äãand principles, and not just another methodology . Who is the class for
Top and mid-level managers (team leader, project), senior programmers, lead testers, analysts - anyone who needs to work closely within Agile projects or with Agile teams.
What do you get
You will gain a deep understanding of how and why agile development methods work in practice, implemented both in one project and at the whole organizational level. Practical exercises and small group discussions will prepare you for working with Agile teams.
Detailed program of the link: icagile-odessa-october-2013.ticketforevent.com/ru

A wide Agile worldview gives you the opportunity to adopt and use various agile development practices in accordance with the needs of your project instead of blindly applying the book Scum to any project, whether it is suitable or not.
Instead of basic practices and generic processes, ICAgile provides a multifaceted overview of various agile development practices, an understanding of the scope and uses of them.
Upon completion of this course, participants will receive a certificate and the title of ICAgile Professional from the International Agile Consortium (ICAgile) , which confirms their knowledge of flexible software development.
You will also receive 21 PDU units required for PMI obtaining a PMP certificate from the Project Management Institute.
