The paradox of the low iPhone interface


It seems to be the usual situation - the phone is dead, there is no charge, you need to pull out the SIM card and insert it into another device. But here the iPhone presents a riddle.

Phones are usually turned off before removing the SIM. Usually, the iPhone is turned off using a red slider. But in this situation it turns out that the energy in the battery is so low that the device does not even display this interface.

It turns out the situation: "Turn on the phone to turn it off!" .

In the forehead, the problem is solved in the following way: by repeatedly calling up a picture of a discharged battery, you can finally discharge this battery. Then the iPhone will be completely cut out.

There are opinions that in the iPhone, you can change the SIM card "on hot." But experience, the son of sad mistakes,
prompts - with complex electronic devices in this matter it is better not to get excited.
