Sunset Mobile Flash: Android 4.1 does not receive Adobe certification


The era of Flash ends on mobile platforms.

After Adobe's announcement about the refusal of Flash mobile development last fall, the next blow was struck by Google, which turned off Flash in Chrome for Android.
Now, however, it was completely announced the termination of support for Flash in Android 4.1.

Moreover, Adobe warns of possible incorrect behavior of Flash on devices updated to Android 4.1 and recommends that you remove Flash Player on updated devices.

The point of no return will be passed on August 15, when Adobe Flash Player disappears from the Google Play store.
It is worth noting that support and updates will continue for those devices where Flash was preinstalled before August 15, in order to improve system security.
