Gingerbread for Sony Ericsson Xperia X10

Good news for all owners of Sony Ericsson Xperia X10 - the company has released an update to the version of Android 2.3; it is already available for download in Russia.

What does this mean:

- users will have access to applications from the Android Market, requiring versions of Android 2.2 or 2.3
- Multitouch capabilities will expand to work with the menu, browser, etc.
- the camera interface remains the same as in the current version of Android 2.1 - this means the preservation of such functions as facial recognition, "capture smiles", etc.
- the menu interface became identical to the models of 2011: the principle of 5 desktops was preserved, but a horizontal fixed bar of icons was added at the bottom of the menu (taskbar), a proprietary media preview widget and an equalizer in the media player
- Moxier organizer will be replaced by native Gingerbread functionality
- Mediascape will be replaced by media videos and music player from the 2011 line.
- DRM-keys of the protected content will be lost during the upgrade process
- the proprietary backup and recovery system will no longer exist either.
