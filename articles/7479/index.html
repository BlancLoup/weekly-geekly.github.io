CGEvent'07

Last weekend I attended a conference organized by the 3D community and other
designers / computer artists hanging out on CGtalk.ru

The overall impression is as follows:

1. Before this conference, I believed that I had a strong medium level in design.
After the conference, I think that I overestimated my skill, but I think that all the same
I am down to low level.

2. There is a good field for 3D activities not only for people.
with artistic skills, but also for programmers. There is a need
in writing scripts and plug-ins, simplifying the exchange of data between
various three-dimensional packages.

Now my impression is on several of the reports heard.


Best game at KRI-2007 - Disciples3 (.dat) Secrets and technology. Anry, Nikolay Kovalev, Maxim Tkachev.
The most important thing noted in this report is the preliminary development of images,
Reasonableness of each game character. The guys did not save time on the details
went to museums, studied heraldry.
The second favorite feature of their work was using Zbrush to create shaders,
which allowed the characters to make a low poly, and all the small details to make the shaders.
Nevertheless, all the characters in the game looked flawless, the detailing was at the highest level.

3D trends and a little bit about the 3D future. Sergey Cyptsyn
Sergey is right - any universal thing, including universal 3D packages,
does almost everything, but does everything badly. Professionals choose highly specialized
programs, and 3D platforms like Maya or Max are used to convert
and matching the data.
In general, it is worth noting that this report was extremely useful to me for general development:
before that, I thought that only Maya and Max exist, but it turned out that there is at least
4 more software packages of this level (XSI, Houdini, Lightwave, Cinema4D).
