Simpla 1.1 online store engine

Thanks to responsive users and your constructive comments , the Simpla online store script has become even better.

Today, a new version of Simpla 1.1 has been released, and at this solemn moment I would like to thank you, as well as my colleagues boriss (layout designer) and timokhin (programmer) for their invaluable contribution to the development of this modest product.

And also thanks to Annne Hiddekel for the colorful illustrations for my posts (and for the design of the CD, which will be released soon, only tsss).



Payment through Robokassa


Robokassa makes it possible to pay for goods in a variety of ways: Webmoney, Yandex.Money, Moneymail, RBK Money RUR, EasyPay, Wallet One, LiqPay, WebCreds, PAYEXPRESS as well as SMS, Payment terminals and credit cards.

When you pay for an order through the robocash, as well as through most other systems, the goods are automatically deducted from the warehouse, and digital goods immediately come to the user on his computer.
Registering a store in Robokassa is completely free and takes literally several hours. Thank you guys for great service!

Payment via Assist.ru



Assist.ru allows you to pay for goods with VISA, MC, DCL, JCB, AMEX bank cards, as well as WebMoney, Yandex.Money, e-port and Kredit Pilot.
