Results of a uniform rating of web studios 2013

Hi, Habr! Ruward project under my (Terekhova Andrew) sensitive management published the results of the first single rating of web studios . It is not a secret for anyone that a car and a small truck of various ratings already exist on the market - all with different methodologies and nuances, which are often completely opaque to the casual observer. That is why we decided to go the other way - and make a common top, which would aggregate the data on all these existing projects. And we kind of turned out.

Our charts are based on 11 different niche ratings published during 2012. The results are based on the aggregation of a large number of tops - and this made it possible to exclude from the top hundred all random companies that sometimes fall into local ratings due to the shortcomings of their construction methodology. The law of large numbers in action. The methodology for constructing a single rating is absolutely transparent, and anyone can check the correctness of the calculation of points for each of the companies.



Here is the first five of our rating :

  • Lebedev Studio . The focus of attention of Studio Number One has long since shifted towards environmental design and industrial design, but the super-popularity of the brand makes it possible to take gold in almost every local top.

  • Actis Wunderman . One of the largest agencies on the market with a cool client pool and the worldwide WPP advertising network.

  • ADV / web-engineering . Very strong web production (still, by the way, not bought by a single grid / integrator), whose recent bias has shifted towards heavy MS-based solutions.

  • Red Keds . Red Sneakers are actively forming their own network, buying shares in small local studios, as well as actively developing market infrastructure (they make cool IKR and Digital without Silicone, have created the portal Cossa.ru, etc.).

  • Creative People . The guys are like a steam locomotive, winning a huge number of contests and festivals. Despite the fact that this is a very young company, they managed to get through to large customers and gain an excellent reputation in the market.
