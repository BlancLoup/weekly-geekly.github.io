Announcement of Shop-Script 5

Many habrip users are familiar with the Shop-Script product ‚Äî a script for creating online stores that our company is developing. You can have a different attitude towards Shop-Script products that were available until today. The product had its own problems and difficulties, but now is not about that.

Today we announced a new version of the product - Shop-Script 5 .





With the help of Shop-Script 5, we plan to solve problems with which previous versions of the product handled with difficulty.

We developed Shop-Script 5 from a clean slate and spent a lot of time observing how real online stores work on the basis of our products, which takes the time of operators when processing orders, what data and reports are missing, which of these tasks can be solved means of the script, and most importantly - how to make a product to help online stores to earn more.



Flexibility of adaptation


The two main problems of previous generations of Shop-Script that most of our customers have encountered are the difficulty of customizing the logic of the scripts and a rigid tabular layout (which was, that was ...). Since each store has its own characteristics, and everyone needs its own beautiful storefront, it was impossible to move on without solving the problem of flexibility.

In Shop-Script 5 we solved this task, having previously developed a whole PHP-framework and created a new Shop-Script based on it. The second problem was solved by itself after modifying the design templates from scratch. We had to sacrifice the possibility of automatically transferring design from previous generations of products, however, this allowed us to make the design editor truly flexible in customizing templates.

(You can see the layout examples on the example of the Blog and Photo apps ; for the new Shop-Script, the design templates will be published along with the official release of the product, and they will be built in the same way.)

Printing forms for orders


Experience shows that online stores (especially small ones) operating in Russia spend an incredible amount of time processing orders for paperwork: preparing invoices, waybills, and especially filling out forms with Russian Post when sending orders for parcels. After you send a few parcels in the nearest post office, it becomes clear that the function of automated form filling (directly over GIF-images ready for printing) is not just a cool feature, but a necessary thing.

We still receive many grateful comments from users of previous versions of Shop-Script for this seemingly simple function.

Internal API of Webasyst framework


In the product description, you can fully use Smarty and use the Webasyst internal API to integrate Shop-Script with other installed applications. In the future, for Shop-Script 5 and the Webasyst framework, an application store will be opened, through which you can install new themes, plug-ins and modules directly through the browser with one click (the framework downloads the scripts from the server and installs them).
