Improved mobile version


Three months ago, a mobile version of WorkStrek was announced . However, after some time by experience it turned out that it was completely inconvenient to use it. In addition, there is not enough functionality to work with the list of cases and events. This time we tried to correct this defect. So, we present an improved mobile version of the work.

At first, it was decided to increase the cells of the table of pinarians: let them fit less, but it would be possible to hit the touchscreen of the phone with a finger.

It was: It became:

Mobile calendar and schedule


The new mobile version has a calendar for the current month. In the cells of the calendar is displayed a pushpin , if a list of to-do is scheduled for the relevant day, and hours , if any event is scheduled for the day.

When you click on a cell, you can go to the schedule for the corresponding day. A schedule consists of a to-do list and a list of events. Cases can be marked and edited on the spot. Events can be edited by clicking on the name.

In the header icons are added to go to the pinarian, the schedule for the current day and the calendar.
