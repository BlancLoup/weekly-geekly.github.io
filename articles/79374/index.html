Letter to Santa Claus

Dear Grandfather Frost, in the past year I behaved badly and did not deserve gifts. The reasons for my bad behavior and failures in many issues were:
  • Laziness
  • Preconceived notion
  • Inability to listen
  • Inability to get things done
  • Lack of concentration
  • Cowardice
  • Unwillingness, and possibly inability to work in a team
  • The habit of postponing everything for the last moment
  • Neglect to trifles
  • Inability and unwillingness to avenge those people who betrayed me
  • Envy
I realized my behavior and I want to ask you in the coming year to teach me how to concentrate . For me it will be the best gift.
