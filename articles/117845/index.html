We invite you to participate in DEVCONF 2011 on June 4 (PHP, PERL, PYTHON, RUBY, .NET, JS)

DevConf 2011 is a professional conference dedicated to leading programming and web development technologies.
In 2010, she gathered more than 1,200 web developers.

DevConf members are provided with a unique opportunity to access immediately all leading web development technologies by listening to reports from the founders of these technologies who came to Moscow from around the world.

As part of DevConf, on June 4, 2011, five conferences will be held:

DevConf :: PHP ()
DevConf :: PERL ()
DevConf :: PYTHON ()
DevConf :: RUBY ()
DevConf :: ASP.NET ()

The DevConf conference format provides for the first conference day and the second day (June 5) of master classes. The second day is optional and requires separate registration.

For whom DevConf?

The conference is aimed at professional web developers and those who want to become them. It unites ALL the most common languages, with each one having its own stream (hall).

It will be possible to immerse yourself in the technology that is of the greatest interest. At the same time the level of any of the areas is the most professional. In each section, reports will be presented by 1-2 direct authors of the language, as well as recognized experts in the field.

Who are the DevConf speakers?

For the organization of each of the areas we involve the community of a language / technology. They invite recognized experts of both Russian and international level.

Who conducts DevConf?

DevConf is the result of a huge number of professionals. Respected people in the respective community take part in the organization of each stream.

We invite speakers in the main streams, as well as master classes.

We are open to cooperation and will be happy to discuss any ways to interact with sponsors, information, technical and other partners - 2011@devconf.ru
