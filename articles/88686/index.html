Nimbuzz 1.4 released

A new version of Nimbuzz 1.4 has been released for the Google Android platform.
Nimbuzz is a free mobile instant messenger aggregator, representing instant messaging, location display and VoIP services. Wikipedia ¬©


The main innovations of Nimbuzz 1.4 for Android:

  • Navigating between chats is a new, useful way to view all open chats, just slide your finger over the chat window to see other open windows.
  • Autostart - never miss a chat, now you can choose to open Nimbuzz automatically every time you launch a mobile
  • Flashing LED - will notify you of each new chat or missed calls. Very useful when your mobile is in silent mode.
  • Accelerated registration - this should be noticeable from the 3rd login
  • UI optimization for for Nexus One & Motorola Droid / Milestone
  • 9 new languages ‚Äã‚Äã- Russian, Chinese, Portuguese, Polish, Italian, German, French, Danish and Spanish


Nimbuzz will change the language of the application in accordance with the default language in your mobile phone. So if you want to have Russian, Italian, German, Dutch or any of the nine available languages, make sure that this is the default language on your Android mobile.

In addition, the quality and stability of the application has been improved.
