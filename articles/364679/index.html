Amazon delivers orders within one hour

The world's largest online hyper-pier, Amazon launched the high-speed package delivery service Prime Now .

So far, the new service is available only to Prime subscribers who live in New York in areas with 10001, 10016, 10017, 10018, 10019, 10020, 10022, 10024, 10025, 10036 postal codes. Over time, they plan to expand the geography of the service. Order must be made through a special application (FireOS, Android, iOS), through it you can track the path of the parcel in real time. Delivery in 60 minutes will cost $ 7.99, while two-hour delivery will not add an extra cent to the cost of the order.
