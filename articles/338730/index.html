IT events digest for October

The end of the month is approaching again and it is time to fill out the diary and agree on travel allowances. Let's see what kind of educational and entertainment program awaits programmers from the capital and regions in the next few weeks. Increased activity in the areas of IoT and blockchain, hakatons gradually returning from vacation and some decline in courses and trainings after the September fever - this is our forecast for October.



All-Russian Forum "Blockchain: Formula for the Future"

When: October 1-3
Where: Ufa, st. Mendeleev, 158, VDNH-Expo
Participation conditions: free, registration required

The Ufa 5-in-1 forum on the blockchain is distinguished by a variety of formats: here, in parallel with the usual lectures and the exhibition of fintech products, there will also be a hackathon and two start-up competitions. A rich three-day program allows those who wish to keep up everywhere - and compete for cash prizes, listen to expert speeches, and evaluate exhibits.

Course "Basics of building an automated testing framework on HP UFT"

When: October 7
Where: Moscow, 1st Volokolamsky passage, 10, p. 3
Conditions of participation: Moscow - 14,200 rubles., St. Petersburg - 12,780 rubles. Omsk - 10,650 rubles.

If you are a QA engineer and you are reading this, it may be worth your while to master HP UFT for two working days. The short course is entirely devoted to the art of writing easily followed, accessible, reusable automated tests. This is achieved by examining in detail the VBS synatkis with all the basic commands, the principles of working with objects and the use of classes in UFT, the basics of VBScript, the parameterization and recognition of objects and other intricacies. Upon completion, you can add another certificate to your collection.

HackCV

When: October 7
Where: St. Petersburg, Bolshoy Sampsonievsky Avenue, 61
Participation conditions: free

Hackathon, where you can realize the dream of an army of science fiction writers and breathe life into the car with the help of Computer Vision technology. The organizers will offer teams to familiarize themselves with theoretical materials on neural networks, work with libraries of computer vision and safety-critical embedded system-specific frameworks and jointly teach the car to drive so that in case of an uprising of machines, it can easily get right. Requirements for candidates include knowledge of C ++ and good mathematical training, the experience of participation in such projects is optional.
