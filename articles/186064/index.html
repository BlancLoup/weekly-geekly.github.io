Tizen App Challenge App Competition

Tizen, an open source operating system supported by Intel and Samsung among others, is entering its battle for developers. The Tizen App Challenge contest offers us a total of over 4 million dollars.

According to the results of the competition, this amount will be divided between 54 winners - the main prize is $ 200,000, and in addition, for example, the top 10 HTML5 applications will receive 50,000 each.

The end of the competition is scheduled for November 1, 2013, and application acceptance will begin in a few days. To participate, you only need an application or applications hosted in the Tizen Store - you can submit as many applications as you want.



Official site: http://tizenappchallenge.com
