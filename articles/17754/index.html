DJs where are you?


Recently I found out about a new startup www.top-dj.ru . The organizers promise 1,000,000 rubles to the best DJ! I did not manage to find out anything about awards in individual nominations, but it is known that the rating will be determined by the new system, which eliminates cheating and not honest ways. A web-interface and a lot of services for clubbers like me are expected. According to the developers, the usual clubbers will have the opportunity to use all the information services of the resource for free and vote for their favorite DJs. I hope that this award will bring clarity to the Russian club life, and there have long been such awards abroad. Now while DJs are being registered to participate in the prize, we are waiting for January 1!
