Surface RT comes to Russia. Start of sales - April 4th!



Microsoft Surface RT tablet finally got to Russia! The start of sales of Surface RT is scheduled for this Thursday, April 4, and it will take place in one of the M.Video stores in the center of Moscow. At 18.00 in the shopping center "Neglinnaya Plaza" (Trubnaya Square, 2) waiting for the "Brigades U" show, an incendiary concert of Vera Brezhneva, acquaintance with the novelty and the opportunity to purchase Surface among the first customers, having received a special gift, all those who came.

Surface RT can be described as a tablet with the capabilities of a laptop, which perfectly combines performance and mobility. It allows you to easily switch between various tasks: work, play and chat with friends and colleagues.
