Fast encryption of your messages

We often send different messages over the network - in forums, social networks, on ICQ, by mail, etc.
And often you want to hide the content from prying eyes (yes, in mail services and in social networks they can read your messages if it falls into the internal search results by message).
So here. To set up some kind of encryption software for this is tedious, difficult, and for the most part just lazy.
And here a very useful bookmarklet Encipher.it appears in the arena .
It represents the usual Javascript. It is put elementary simply - save it in the "Favorites", bring it to the main browser panel and use it. Or simply via drag-and-drop move to the bookmarks bar.


For example, in order to encrypt a message, simply select it, click on the ‚ÄúEncipher.it‚Äù icon that appeared on the browser panel, enter the password in the window that appears and your message will be encrypted immediately. Everything is decoded in the same way - select the encrypted text, click on the bookmarklet, enter the password and decrypt the message.




It remains only to agree with those who are going to correspond, on a common password. Naturally, the transfer of the password you need to use a different communication channel other than where you are going to correspond.

Encryption / decryption occurs entirely on the client side.
