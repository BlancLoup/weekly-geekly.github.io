Kharkov office

Today we want to invite all Khabrovtsev to visit our Kharkov office. Get comfortable and feel at home.

As it should be, the guests are warmly welcomed by HRs and office managers. Of course, you can stay at the entrance, fascinated by employees, but perhaps we will go forward.

The mood is set immediately from the entrance. Bright interior in the colors of orange and milk, diluted with the freshness of green, does not leave anyone indifferent. Want, you can sit on the cozy sofas.





Or you can go to the kitchen to warm up with a cup of aromatic coffee. On Friday evening, she reincarnated from a place of food in a small Las Vegas. All gather in a friendly and gambling atmosphere, to again and again determine the most skilled poker player and expose the Mafia.



Not far from the kitchen is located HR room. It is from here that calls are heard with news of an acceptance to our friendly family and letters are sent about gay corporate events and educational events.



And now let's look at the holy-saints, a place where ideas go into thousands of lines of code, and become high-quality software for various industries.
