Advise a good domain registrar in the zone. Ru

Good time of day, habra people!

I was faced with the need to register a domain in the .ru zone for a small non-commercial project. However, having stumbled upon a whole bunch of registrars, doubts began to torment me - is this or that registrar the best choice. My wishes are as follows:
  • The registrar should provide convenient opportunities for remote management and payment of the domain. Because I am not a resident or a citizen of the Russian Federation; I cannot solve issues directly;
  • Have a fairly good reputation. Those. do not engage in cybersquatting;
  • Provide domains at a reasonable price, including the facilities listed above.

I rely on your experience and answers.
Thanks in advance.
