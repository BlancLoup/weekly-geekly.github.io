"Mamba" comes to mobile phones

Dating service "Mamba" announced the launch of a new project Mamba Mobile.

The strategic partner for this project is WapStart , it will develop, support and promote the project, including the partner program for mobile sites and portals.
The starting point for the development of the mobile direction was the wap-version of Mamba, developed by WapStart. Today the project is located at wap.mamba.ru, and is in beta testing. Based on the collected statistical and analytical information, corrections and additions will be made to the wap-site. And this fall, the second version of the project will be presented.

Mobile "Mamba" has a single database with a web version. Mobile phone users can get acquainted and communicate via a WAP-browser (view profiles, vote, send and receive messages); and the owners of mobile resources connected to the affiliate program, earn money.
