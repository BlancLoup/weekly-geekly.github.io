Cost accounting


Hello!

I want to bring to your attention (propiarit) the first release of my work, namely, the program for the Android platform for accounting for finance.

I created this application with thoughts about how to simplify the process of financial accounting as much as possible and spend a minimum of time on it, because for me personally this process is quite boring, and I don‚Äôt always have time and desire to record expenses. And the need to analyze the spent money sometimes arises. It would be ideal if expenses were automatically recorded for later analysis if the need arose. Unfortunately, it is impossible to achieve 100% automation, but to strive for excellence is real. For this was implemented support for SMS messages from a mobile bank. The program parses incoming SMS for analysis and if the analysis is successful, then an entry is made about the costs without your direct participation. Thus, if you set up an application, you may not be completely distracted by the task of cost accounting. In many cases, this is quite enough to control your family budget.

I think my product will be interesting to those people who do not want to bother much on the account of each step, but want to coordinate their expenses. Right or wrong, of course, the market itself will show.
