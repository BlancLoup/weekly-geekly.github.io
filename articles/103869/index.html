Estonian Information System



In this topic, I would like to talk about how the information system is organized in Estonia and what amenities it provides to citizens. This topic has already been covered in this topic - habrahabr.ru/blogs/the_future_is_here/60862 . But I am sure that I have something to surprise not the citizens of this country.

Under the cut description of various popular services in Estonia.

There is a lot of traffic under the cut.


City portal eesti.ee


Full size picture.

This is the largest and most important portal about which every citizen of Estonia must know (But not everyone knows about it). Through this portal, you can view any of your data up to the logs of your transfers from class to class.


Full size picture.

You can enter the city portal using an ID card (which any citizen who has reached 15 years of age has), via a mobile phone (Mobiil-ID technology - Read more - here - id.ee/?id=10356 ). However, you must first activate this service with a mobile operator.

They were not even too lazy and shot a small video clip about how the Mobile-ID system works.


Login can also be made via the Internet bank. You can read more about the ID card in Wikipedia - ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/Estonian_ID-card or from the link I gave at the beginning of the topic.

I prefer to enter through the ID card as for me it is the fastest and most convenient way to enter. All that is required of me is to insert my ID card into the reader (Reader cost up to $ 10, in banks is often issued free of charge), connect it to the computer, and enter a PIN code which consists of 4 digits and which was issued together with the ID card. There are drivers for both Windows, Mac and Linux, which is good news. (Under Linux, the truth does not rise without dancing with a tambourine). After installing the software, you can use the ID card to enter the state portal and other services which will be discussed below, the software is compatible with Windows with IE and Firefox, with Mac - Safari and Firefox, with Linux with Firefox.

E-school. (http://ekool.ee)

This system was established in 2002 and most schools are currently connected to the so-called e-school system or (eKool), this system is a complete replacement of paper journals where all your omissions and evaluations were noted. In this system, a student can see all his grades, see upcoming quizzes or homework, and the teacher can conveniently add grades, assignments, comments, etc. There is also access for parents to monitor the progress of their children.

Digital signing of documents

Imagine the situation, you need to write a business letter, but bad luck, the interlocutor on the other end can not be sure that you are the one for whom you are posing. This problem is intended to solve the digital signing of documents.

Digital signing of documents is as follows. For Windows, special software is downloaded - id.ee/?id=11147 .

For Linux and Mac, the DigiDoc Web service is available where you can download a document and get a signed document or download a signed document and be able to read the decrypted document. - digidoc.sk.ee/?f=chg_lang&lang=en

In short, the recipient gets the opportunity to see that the document was indeed sent from such and such a person, a sort of confirmation of identity.

Buying tickets for public transport

No less interesting is the sale of tickets for public transport through the portal www.pilet.ee/cgi-bin/splususer/splususer.cgi?lang=ru .

Through it, you can buy tickets for public transport and check its operation, the service is notable because it automatically checks your eligibility through a single database, in case you do not have the privilege, it will sell you a ticket anyway, but an inscription appears that the right to benefit is missing.

When controllers stop the bus, all you need is just to give it an ID card. The verification process takes a few seconds. If the right to a benefit is not found, and a ticket has been purchased for a discount, the controller has the right to ask for a paper document confirming the availability of the benefit.

This is the interface of the purchased tickets.

People who do not really trust high-tech can still use paper tickets or also pay ID-tickets at any kiosk where the seller will issue a ticket for you in a minute.
