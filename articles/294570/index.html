Conference LeanKanban 2015



There are not many days left until the moment when one of the most serious Moscow conferences for managers will open the doors of their site. It will happen on October 2 and 3, on the territory of Digital October, where dozens of reports will be read in three tracks of the conference and several trainings with invited Western gurus in the field of Lean-methodologies and techniques will be organized.

To begin with, what exactly is the Modern Management Methods conference that we organize. First of all, it is the assemblage point of all interested industry professionals, an opportunity to get to know the faces of colleagues and get to know them individually, listening to cutting-edge information from foreign speakers. All of them come to Moscow with one goal: to share a history of solving real business problems, formulating a pragmatic and effective plan for changes in the company or division, based primarily on facts.

And after 2 weeks


Let's go back now. The 2015 conference in numbers is as follows.

  • More than 300 managers and business owners
  • More than 30 reports of invited experts will be read.
  • Key foreign speakers
  • Workshops and trainings before and after the main conference tracks

In general, Modern Management Methods remains a conference on management processes and the quality of managerial decision-making under conditions of high uncertainty. Attending the event will be primarily interesting for those who make decisions in difficult conditions and, as a result, manage risks. Practical benefits from reports and master classes will be made not only by top managers and unit managers, but also by trainers / consultants in project and product management.

When the situation in a business changes with great speed, and the degree of uncertainty is always high - traditional management approaches often turn out to be ineffective. Budgets, KPIs and work plans for several years ahead, consistent with months, become irrelevant, as much as possible, by the time they are approved.
