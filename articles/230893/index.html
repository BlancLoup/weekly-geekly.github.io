WebCamp 2014 Live Webcast: Developer Day


From July 21 to July 24, the V Annual WebCamp 2014 Web Developers Conference will be held as part of Odessa Innovation Week in the following areas: development, project management, design, internet marketing and mobile technologies.

The organizing committee team organizes a test online broadcast of the conference for the first time. Today will be a day of web development . In three parallel threads, participants will discuss current issues in such areas as: Front-End, Back-End and Frameworks / Architecture.

Program of the day: webcamp.in.ua/developers-day.html .
Live translations: geekslab.co/WebCamp2014Stream . (The beginning of the broadcast at 10.00 in Kiev).
