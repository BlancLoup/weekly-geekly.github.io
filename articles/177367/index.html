Information about the lecturer:
Vitaliy Stepanovich Mitchenko - Honored Worker of Ukraine, Associate Professor, Lecturer at the Department of Graphic Arts at NAOMA (over 15 years), Winner and Winner of numerous exhibitions and competitions in calligraphy, typography and book art.

The main objective of the course: the study and understanding of the font culture, the development of basic font tools.
