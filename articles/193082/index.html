Bitcoin exchangers will be installed in Vancouver



Bitcoin cryptocurrency is pleasant to many, above all, by the anonymity of transactions. In addition, the possibility of mining is also a serious means of earning for many (at Habr√© miners there are more than one hundred, probably). But still, Bitcoin still does not enter into life, such as, for example, poison, Webmoney or PayPal. Perhaps the installation of Bitcoin exchangers in large cities will help the cryptocurrency to gradually come into our lives. So, five Bitcoin exchangers will be installed soon in Vancouver, Canada.

These devices are called RoboCoin. To buy a cryptocurrency, you do not need anything, except for knowing your wallet number in the system, and cash. As far as one can understand, withdrawing cash by selling a little bitcoin is also easy. At the same time, the commission is minimal - so far only money is charged for the transaction.

In general, acquiring Bitcoin is now not so simple to a person who is not very familiar with this system, but would like to know more about it. But real-money cryptocurrency exchangers are able to rectify the situation. By the way, these devices are not the first, since previously exchangers, created by two US entrepreneurs, were announced.

True, RoboCoin is more expensive than the American exchangers. The price of one device is 20 thousand US dollars. The American equivalent costs only five thousand.

The first RoboCoin exchanger will be installed in October. The remaining devices will follow later - around December this year. It should be noted that, externally and functionally, RoboCoin exchangers are similar to ordinary ATMs, although they are not.
