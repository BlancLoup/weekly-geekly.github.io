Mightier or self designer



How often have you scolded the game for the design of locations? And how often for character design? Ratloop's Mightier is a game in which there is no one to scold for character design or locations. Here everyone is his own designer.




Being a simple arcade with puzzle elements, Mightier does not make you look at the screen all the time. After printing a riddle on a printer, it must be solved - with a pencil on paper. The webcam will send the solution to the game and in a couple of moments the orbital satellite will change the terrain relief according to your sketch. The same technique can be done with the character - it can be drawn. If you suddenly run out of paper in the printer or simply do not have a webcam, you can use the in-game editor - you can play, but not so interesting.
