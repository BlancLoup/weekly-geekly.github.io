Comparison of Russian cloud service operators

I waited a long time and asked the author of the 2014 study to repeat it . Did not wait, despite regular requests. Apparently, imagining what a titanic work lies ahead, the author postponed this idea. But it so happened that not only me, but also my friend, needed cloud services for her company, and she offered me her help in collecting information. So the crazy idea was born to repeat the article. The author did not have any objections to the previous review, so we set to work, using his calculation method as a basis.
This time, 36 companies were included in the review, and the joint work on collecting and analyzing information was delayed for as many as 5 months. We took the configuration, based on the previous comparison, adjusted for the requirements of the company to my friend.


So, we sent the request to the following companies:

We reduced the number of questions to company representatives and asked 17 questions:


  1. Used hypervisors?
  2. What processors and servers are used? The name of the manufacturer and the series.
  3. What storage system is used? Manufacturer, model?
  4. What is the level of TIER data center?
  5. What is data center? Where is it located?
  6. The possibility of placing on 2 sites? Possibility of accommodation abroad?
  7. Possibility of hourly billing? (Does the price change from this?)
  8. The possibility of postpay?
  9. What SLA at no extra cost do you provide?
  10. What is the guarantee of resources CPU, RAM, HDD, Network?
  11. What is included in the basic support?
  12. Is 24x7 support free?
  13. Whether staffing technologies like VMware High Availability are applied (automatic restart in case of server failure, ~ 5-10 minutes to start the virtual machine)
  14. How many telecom operators are used for fault tolerance AS?
  15. Have a test access? Many days?
  16. How soon is it possible to provide test access?
  17. Is it possible to test access to the resources requested above?

You were


  • Megaphone was ready to offer only cloud telephony from the requested services.
  • DepoCloud reported that they are closing their cloud business and are not ready to provide services.
  • Orange, despite all our requests, did not make a preliminary calculation, since they must first conduct a technical inspection of the object, while the description of the necessary services is quite enough for the calculation.
  • Clodo can still offer infrastructure only on Linux.
  • But Cloud One never provided a calculation, despite our most persistent requests, calls and letters, arguing that our questions look "suspicious."

The evaluation was based on the number of pros, cons, and total cost. As a rule, it is most convenient for the customer to order services and work with one performer, therefore companies that did not have the requested services received a minus.
