Hacker has published personal data of 20 thousand FBI agents

While the Super Bowl was being watched in the US, an anonymous hacker promised to publish the names, email addresses and phone numbers of 20,000 FBI agents and 9,000 employees of the American MVB. And he fulfilled his promise in 24 hours.



The attackers hacked into the US Department of Justice servers and stole two hundred gigabytes of data. Information includes names, titles, phone numbers of employees of the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the FBI. The criminals operate under the slogan "Freedom of Palestine" - #FreePalestine.

The attacker compromised the email of one of the employees of the Ministry of Justice who have access to the internal infrastructure and obtained access to the data.

The FBI has not commented on the situation MotherBoard . Circumstances are being investigated.
