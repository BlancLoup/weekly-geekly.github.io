TOP 7 - for 7 days. Marketing iPad apps on the Apple Store

I will start not with the methods of promotion, but with the preparation, which is no less important than the promotion and promotion of the application itself. At once I will make a reservation that I cite as an example only paid applications and the Russian Apple Store.



You just can not imagine how important the icon is 72 * 72 pixels, which is located in the program catalog. I believe that it is better to have an attractive icon and screenshots, but the limited functionality of the application itself than the fancy functionality of the application and the nondescript icon. A user who wanders through the AppStore, in search of something, can be attracted only by an icon and nothing more. Make a very noticeable icon to stand out from the background with others (fewer small details), let it be contrasting colors with a bright frame or a beautiful illustration, also do not forget about the HD icon if your application is for iPad. For an example, look at the picture with the apple and the screenshots - that's what I'm talking about.

After the user is interested in the icon and he has visited the product itself, a large-sized icon and screenshots immediately catches the eye. I think this is one of the key points, if the user did not like the screenshots, he will not go into details, such as: description, iOS requirements, size, etc. Only after that the user begins to view the details of the product itself. At this stage, the description of the product becomes important, try to indicate all the advantages of your application in 214 characters.

Before publishing an application on the AppStore, be sure to work on the following: keywords, sections (main and additional), try to specify all sections that are suitable for your application, do not save on sections, because in additional sections there is a chance to get into New & Noteworthy.
