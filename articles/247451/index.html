And this is a safe, opened by the power of thought in the quest in reality, "Cyberpunk." There are four valves, each with its own level of complexity. The first can be opened without concentrating almost, and the last is already a challenge for the skill of concentration.

Both devices can be tested in St. Petersburg: NeuroKran - in the FabLab of the Polytechnic University, and safe - in Lostroom , on Nevsky Prospect, 61.



These devices were made on the basis of Neurosky MindWave by the NeuroLab team, which in 2013 began developing applications for mobile non-invasive brain-computer interfaces (brain-computer interface, BCI).

Both devices are essentially a neurotrener in the form of a game: with the regularity of classes, the increasing complexity of tasks (pouring a full glass for 60, 50, 40 seconds ...), visualization on the indicator of the magnitude of brain activity - the effect of biological feedback appears.
