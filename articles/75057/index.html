Picsa.ru - hosting for pictures


Recently launched a new hosting for storing and publishing images - Picsa.ru . I want to tell you about this service, as one of the developers of its interface.



Another photohosting, devoid of any hope?


Creating Piks , we did not set out to surpass the giants of the media industry. At first, we were faced with the task of optimizing work with images for our services. What turned out to be interesting to us, and we decided to make the photo hosting site public.

We knew something about the visitors of our services: they are mostly people for whom the Internet began with social networks. They love to communicate on sites, love gadgets and amaze with the ability to quickly find and master all new ways of communication. They were the center of attention when we filled this project with life.

Ease of use and reliability of the technical "stuffing" have become the development motto for us. A little later they added external austerity and a number of experiments in the field of interface.

Warranties and Filling


Most of us have blogs. The volume of the media resource sent daily to the network is growing rapidly. A little about future plans


Whether this project will have a future is basically up to you. In short, I will talk about what we still want to implement in the near future.
We want to completely get away from the modes that are still present in the interface, and provide the user with a single canvas space on which all the albums will be presented at the same time. We want to add simple group actions and make the interface scalable.
For the first version, we also prepared an introduction to the service elements of "socialization". For example, in the work on the compilation of albums and collages was supposed to introduce friends. A natural continuation of the start will also be the addition of add-ons to popular browsers, simplifying the process of downloading images.
