We invite you to the first Zabbix conference in Russia

From August 23 to August 24, the first Russian conference Zabbix will be held in Moscow - an event aimed at sharing experiences and in-depth study of the possibilities of the universal open monitoring system Zabbix.




Now we are actively working on the program of the event - applications from the speakers are accepted until July 5, inclusive. The holiday in honor of the opening of the Zabbix office in Russia showed how many impressive cases were implemented by local users. If you also have something to tell and show to the Russian community Zabbix, become one of the speakers !

Themes of speeches


At the moment, several topics have been approved by the Zabbix technical team:

  • Monitoring without borders: what awaits us in Zabbix 4.4 (presentation by Alexey Vladyshev)
  • How does Zabbix server work (Zabbix Internals)
  • Zabbix in the cloud (Azzure, AWS, Digital Ocean, Docker)
  • Monitoring Kubernetes with Zabbix
  • How to make a template in Zabbix? Useful and harmful tips

The full schedule of the conference will be published here soon after the deadline for accepting applications from the speakers. Follow the news!

Not just lectures


In the continuation of a successful tradition, a practical workshop (included in the price) will be organized during the conference, as well as the opportunity to pass certification by passing an examination at a reduced price. Please note that we have planned trainings at the ZCS, ZCP and ZCE levels immediately before and after the conference so that during one trip you can significantly increase the level of knowledge and gain skills for professional work with Zabbix. As usual, a rich entertainment program will be organized.

An early booking offer is valid until July 1, so if you plan to attend a conference, we recommend not to delay registration.
