Artificial Intelligence - a question will be asked for each answer.

Artificial intelligence in speech expert systems is developing in the direction of chat bots. Undoubtedly, this is clear for all the direction of development. Certainly, there are a lot of problems with its formation, it can be seen from the presentations of developers . Let's look at all these problems with information theory. Information - information perceived by a person and (or) special devices as a reflection of the facts of the material or spiritual world in the process of communication (GOST 7.0-99).

The main functions when working with information:

  1. Search for new information;
  2. Saving information;
  3. Transfer of information.

And the choice of source is determined only by the costs of the user.

Tasks that cannot be solved by the user on the basis of local information can be attributed to the external (upper) level, that is, to a level that has a dimension higher than that available to the user.
