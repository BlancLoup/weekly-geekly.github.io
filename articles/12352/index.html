Convenient service to save videos from popular video services

Many were puzzled by the question of how to save the video you liked to yourself is not a computer. With 0download.ru nothing is easier. 12 popular services are supported: video.mail.ru, vision.rambler.ru, rutube.ru, youtube.com, myvi.ru, news.ntv.ru, loadup.ru, video.privet.ru, .liveinternet.ru, videoradar.ru, nashe.ru. Enter the address of the page with a roller and get a direct link to the file!

For download convenience, special domains 0rutube.ru, 0mail.ru, 0rambler.ru, 000youtube.com, helloyoutube.com have been created. Using these domains is easy.
Suppose you want to download the file www.youtube.com/watch?v=cQ25-glGRzI
It is enough to add to the beginning of the domain 000 or hello - and you will receive a link to the file to download! Link will look like this
www.helloyoutube.com/watch?v=cQ25-glGRzI or
www.000youtube.com/watch?v=cQ25-glGRzI

For Russian services:

rutube.ru -> 0rutube.ru
video.mail.ru -> video.0mail.ru
vision.rambler.ru -> vision.0rambler.ru
