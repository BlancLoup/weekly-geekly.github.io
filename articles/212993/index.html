Cutting down money is not a business.



Here I will mention such a project as 2druga.com , a classical pyramid, a lot of such people appeared in 2012 and you could really make money in them. Create a project when you need it.



When I became interested in web development seven years ago, practically the first project I created was a rental property website without intermediaries in my hometown. Analyze the market to be sure that your product is needed!

As a result, I came to the conclusion that you can create long-term projects that will not take much time, their creation and maintenance will not require a lot of money, but at the same time each of them will be the embodiment of some kind of my idea and, most importantly, will bring I have money!

The basic principles of success in this field are simple and now I just repeat:
- not every project will even be self-sufficient, but it is always an experience;
- assess the complexity of the project at the design stage, maybe it will just be inconvenient or not cost-effective;
- study the market, make sure that the people you need really think up;
- if the project does not imply serious software development, then it is easier to hire a designer, a programmer than to try to implement your own idea with someone else in the stake;
- make a bet on long-term projects, a project that is able to self-regulate and develop with a lower monthly income is actually more profitable than a knowingly temporary one.
