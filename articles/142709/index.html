According to the results of the forum DEPO Computers "Formula DEPO: the race of IT leaders"

On April 10, DEPO Computers Forum - “Formula DEPO: IT Leadership Race” took place in Moscow.

The “Formula DEPO” brought together more than 400 Russian information technology specialists (including many Habrazhiteli) to the Radisson Royal. Using the terms of a real “Formula 1” - the clashes of the best riders in the world, IT specialists, experts from DEPO Computers, Microsoft, Intel and APC talked about tools for survival and leadership in IT through new approaches and solutions available in DEPO engineering projects.

We offer you a mini-review and photo report from the scene.



In red, the DEPO team: experts and IT specialists from DEPO Computers.





Particular emphasis in the Formula DEPO was made on the victories of Russian specialists: CIOs from large, medium and small companies told about projects to develop information systems and infrastructures, the introduction of a cloud approach and virtualization, the first for Russia to experience integrated protection of a virtual computing environment and aligning the IT infrastructure with the current requirements of the Law on Personal Data.





The whole day at the forum was an exhibition of the latest products and solutions by DEPO and the company's strategic partners - Intel, Microsoft, APC, VMware.



In the “Infrastructure and Data Center” zone, new models of servers and storage DEPO, micro and mini data center form factors were demonstrated.





Test drive of unique ready-made DEPO solutions was available in the exhibition areas "Cloud Solutions and Virtualization" and "Information Security"





In accordance with the traditions of the real "Formula 1" in the finals of the "Formula DEPO" the winners in the nominations "Grand Prix" and "Designers' Cup" were announced.



The winners of the Formula DEPO were IT specialists who achieved victories in projects through successful and competent implementations, as well as well-coordinated teamwork.

Among them, pilots of the DEPO team are customer companies in which projects were successfully implemented using DEPO solutions and with the participation of DEPO Computers.

CJSC IC "Investments and Finance" - nomination "The best project for the protection of personal data"
GK "Demidov" - nomination "The best project to build a fault-tolerant cluster"
INTERCOM LLC - nomination “The best project for building a reliable power supply system for the data center”
FKB "ENERGOTRANSBANK" - the nomination "The best virtualization project on DEPO Storm 3300V2 servers"
CJSC "Active-soft" - nomination "The best project for the security of virtual infrastructure"
BSC “RUSSKOLLECTOR” - nomination “The best infrastructure audit project under the requirements of the Federal Law 152
NPO Microgen - nomination "The best project for virtualization DEPO Cloud 1000"
Publishing House "Economic Newspaper" - nomination "The best project for the comprehensive modernization of the IT infrastructure to ensure the intensive work of remote and mobile users"



And in the end, of course, a grand reception!
