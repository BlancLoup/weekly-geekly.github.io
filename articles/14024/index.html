Ashmanov & Partners Launched Search Engine Analyzer

Andrey Ivanov from Ashmanov and Partners announced the launch of a new analytical project called Analyze This . The system automatically analyzes the most important parameters of Russian search engines: the frequency and strength of updates, the quality of navigation search, the quality of thematic search, the level of SEO pressure, the number of spam in search results. The entire archive of measurements is available for viewing, comments are supported.

Nine largest search engines are exposed to the analysis: Yandex, Google, Rambler, Gogo, Live.com, Aport, Webalta, Liveinternet, Yahoo (for some unknown reason, Nigma.ru, Quintura.ru and others are missing).
