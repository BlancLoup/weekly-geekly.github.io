West Game Development Forum: Create, Share, Improve

Wargaming Company invites you to take part in the largest conference of this spring - West Game Development Forum 2016, which will be held on April 22-23, 2016 in St. Petersburg!

The West Game Development Forum is a major international conference dedicated to game development and everything related to it. It will be held in St. Petersburg and will bring together professional developers from different countries and companies, both gamedev veterans and young professionals.



The purpose of the conference is to build an ideal platform for the exchange of experience and views and to present the modern gaming industry in all its diversity using the example of various world practices. In the conditions of the record pace of development of this sphere, it is the unification of our professional community that will help everyone to more accurately determine the main trends of the present time and outline a further course.
Our audience is game designers, programmers, designers, artists, sound engineers and other game developers. All developers who want to grow professionally and develop in game dev.
The program includes dozens of lectures, seminars and round tables on the most pressing issues of the development of modern games. Take part in discussions with world-class experts!

You can register and find out more on the official website of the event www.wgdevforum.net
