Laptop for 7000 ?, for women

At CeBIT, a laptop for 7000 ?, for women was presented.
"Ego" - this is the name of the laptop - in the closed state is very reminiscent of a handbag covered with leather.

The main technical characteristics of the laptop:
160GB hard drive, 2GB RAM
three usb-outs, one mini-usb, ps / 2; 4-in-1 card reader, DVI
Wlan, bluetooth
DVD Writer, 1,3mp webcam
12 special keys in the top row for quick access to programs
Windows vista
