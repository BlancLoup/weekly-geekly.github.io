SAP on Twitter - an enterprise level social experiment



The ESME project is a corporate analogue of Twitter on the SAP platform, the world leader among ERP systems. It was created to take business communications to a new level. It is based on the Scala / Lift web framework with a client on Adobe Flex / Air. The project is created with the active participation of SAP, Adobe & Siemens (where it is being tested). One of the project consultants is David Pollack, who also works with Twitter guys on Scala-related issues.
