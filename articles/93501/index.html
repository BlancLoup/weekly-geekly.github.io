Panasonic Lumix DMC-TZ10 - compact with GPS

In the regiment of compact arrived - Panasonic released its compact camera TZ10, quite attractive in terms of parameters.

Lumix DMC-TZ10

According to the manufacturer, LUMIX TZ10 is well suited to people who prefer outdoor activities and travel.
There are three main advantages for him:
- intellectual 16x zoom
- GPS
- Ability to shoot HD-video
Now more.


Main characteristics:

The total number of pixels: 14.5 million
Focal length: 25 - 300 mm
Aperture: F3.3 - F4.9
Manual setting of shutter speed and aperture: yes
LCD screen: 460,000 pixels, 3 inches
Size: 103x60x33 mm
Optical Zoom: 12x
GPS: yes
Weight: 218 g with battery.

Intellectual zoom. The Lumix TZ10 has an optical 12x smart zoom and can increase it up to 16x with a digital zoom. The lens of the TZ10 (Leica) has a large diameter, but in addition, it also has a good focal range - from 25 mm to 300 mm.

About GPS. The TZ10 has a built-in system that not only enters into the snapshot metadata coordinates that it receives from GPS, but also recognizes the name of the settlement, which is located in these coordinates. This is a unique opportunity today.

HD video. High-definition video from this compact turns out to be of high quality and can be viewed on the big screen (by the way, the LUMIX DMC-TZ10 has connectors for connecting it directly to a TV). LUMIX DMC-TZ10 saves video recordings in the AVCHD Lite format, which is currently the most progressive format for compressing video data, and is used in new-generation technology. The video has three levels of quality - SH (17 Mbps), H (13) and D (9).

The price for this model ranges from 14 to 17 thousand rubles.



It seems to me that manufacturers very correctly characterized this model as a camera for travelers. Due to its size and very good quality pictures and videos, it really is a universal camera for any occasion.
