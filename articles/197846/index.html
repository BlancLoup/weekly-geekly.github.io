Luxoft launches a series of free IT webinars with famous speakers

Luxoft launches a series of free webinars with famous speakers from the IT world.

Luxoft Technology Series (LTS) is an online format event for information technology professionals who strive for continuous professional development. According to the value of the information presented in the webinars, they are similar to the best IT conferences in the world, with the only difference that participation in them is absolutely free. The number of participants in the webinars is not limited.
A series of free IT webinars will begin October 30, 2013
The first webinar, scheduled for October 30, will be hosted by Shai Almog, CEO and Co-founder of Codename One, which allows Java developers to create mobile applications for any device. Shai Almog will tell the participants in detail about the cross-platform development of mobile applications with a focus on Java, as well as the principle of operation of these technologies. " "The focus of the presentation by Mr. Almog will be on the following topics:
  • Environment and history of mobile application development
  • Cross-platform or native development in practice - choosing the best approach
  • Understanding native platforms
  • Cross-platform approaches and conditions - HTML, Lightweight, Heavyweight, programs for processing, translation and cross-compilers
  • Java in low-level tools of modern devices: XMLVM, J2ObjC, RoboVM, Avian and Dalvik
  • Cross platform or WORA
  • Creating a simple application
  • Bits and bytes, how platform abstraction works - what is created in native conditions
You can register for a free webinar and find out more information at www.luxoft.com/lts
