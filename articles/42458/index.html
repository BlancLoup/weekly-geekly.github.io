Further worse. Bank Globex imposes a ban on withdrawing deposits

Here we are, the Bank "Globex" temporarily banned the issuance of funds from deposits in case of early closure. On such measures, one of the largest Russian banks had to go because of the rush demand of depositors, who began to urgently withdraw their funds from the accounts.

According to the internal order of the management, it became impossible to close the deposit in the bank ahead of schedule. Already yesterday, it was impossible to withdraw money from Globex payroll cards. The bank also did not make payments in the Western Union system, which was explained by technical problems in Globex.

The management of the bank explained these measures by a significant outflow of funds, observed since September. According to bank vice-president Emil Aliyev, if on September 1, the volume of deposits was 27.1 billion rubles, then on October 1, it was already 23.5 billion rubles, and by October 14 it was reduced to 20 billion rubles (15%). ).
