According to the characteristics, I got the gadget, I think almost no one will have any serious questions, except, perhaps, the class of protection .

In this case, IP67 (Ingress Protection) is a system for classifying the degrees of protection of the electrical equipment shell against the penetration of solid objects and water in accordance with the international standard IEC 60529 (DIN 40050, GOST 14254-96). Please note that this characteristic is only a probe, the device itself does not have this degree of protection.

The marking is carried out with the help of an international protection mark (IP) and two digits, the first of which means protection against ingress of solid objects (dust), the second - against the ingress of water.

The maximum protection for this classification is IP69: a dustproof device that can withstand prolonged immersion in water under pressure.

In our case - IP67: a dustproof device capable of operating during short-term immersion in water, while the water does not get inside in quantities that disrupt the operation of the device. Permanent operation of such a device in the submerged mode is not expected.

According to the video specifications, you can see that the device supports both NTSC and PAL. The main advantages of the first are good compatibility with black and white images, low cross-distortion of brightness and color signals, as well as good noise immunity, while the transmission channel is used most efficiently from all existing systems, allowing for its relative narrowness to transmit an image with good color clarity.
