With the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãimplementing this gadget, I decided to become a resident of our innovative Mecca - I went to conquer Skolkovo. It is enough to register on the site sk.ru and fill out an application for obtaining the status of a Skolkovo member. The application passed the formal verification stage in 1 day and went for substantive examination.

The substantive examination is a meeting of the Expert Board on the issue of whether your project meets the following criteria. Following the meeting, experts vote. At the exit you get a verdict on the passage / not passing the project, an assessment of 5 points and abstracts from the experts.

5 points of voting. The project is considered quality if for each of the 5 points scored more than 50% of the possible points.

  1. The created product and (or) technology and (or) the expected result of applied research (that is, scientific research, the commercialization of the results of which is expected no later than five years after the Applicant submits an application for assigning the Project Participant status (an application for the preliminary examination of projects); - Applied Research) have potential competitive advantages over world peers;
  2. The created product and (or) technology and (or) the expected result of Applied Research has a significant potential for commercialization, at least in the Russian, and in the future - in the global market;
  3. The project is theoretically realizable and does not contradict the fundamental scientific principles;
  4. Key researchers, developers and project managers (hereinafter referred to as the Project Team) possess the necessary knowledge and experience for the successful implementation of the project and (or) the implementation of Applied Research;
  5. The project team has a specialist or several specialists with international experience in the field of research, development and (or) commercialization of their results.
