Qiwi Visa version 2.0


Today, a new service, QVC (Qiwi Visa Card), has become available to all Qiwi-wallets users in a personal account. This product is similar in purpose to Qiwi Visa Virtual. The latter by the way a lot of scolded and scolded. First, they blamed for the fact that the Qiwi Visa Virtual card was not replenished - it was fixed, until now they are scolded because of the high commission, the maximum payment limit, the short validity period, the extra gestures to get something, but product, Qiwi Visa Card seems designed to correct these shortcomings. So let's take a closer look at what kind of beast it is.



There are several advantages to this card :

The commission has been reduced from 3.5% to 1.5%, which is pretty good.
The card has been extended, now the card is issued for 1 year - a sensible decision, and every quarter a new card was issued and linked to PayPal.
You no longer need to replenish the card before you buy, the money is automatically deducted from the balance. Therefore, there is no need to go into your personal account at all. It will save a lot of time, because on Visa Virtual I still did not keep money for the future (they would become frozen) and each time I replenished the card with the necessary amount for the purchase.
Quite an interesting moment with the disclosure of personality. After all, this was the stumbling block, why it was impossible to buy something more expensive than 15,000 rubles. via Qiwi Visa Virtual. At least, the impersonality of the card was the only reason for such a limitation in the mouth of one of the employees of Qiwi. Comparison with other payment instruments


Of course, it is not entirely correct to compare physical cards with virtual ones. But people still use them for the same thing, so it is impossible not to mention the Svyaznoy Bank and Kukuru cards . These cards are physical, but they are obtained in 10-30 minutes in the communication shops of Svyaznoy and Euroset, respectively. They, of course, have a rather small network of card replenishment and, of course, it is incomparable with the Qiwi multimillion payment terminal network, but, unlike QVC, when using, for example, Svyaznoy Bank cards, the commission is not only not charged, but even interest is paid on the balance, but the subscription fee is added. For me personally, 1.5% is not critical and still I will not keep money on such cards, so it makes no sense for me to buy them now. Although I confess, just a few days ago, before the release of QVC, I was thinking of buying such a card for the reason sounded above (HTC) :)
