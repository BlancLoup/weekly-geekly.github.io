MSI Wind in Russia in July


MSI Wind will be available in two versions: with the operating system Windows XP Home and 1 GB of RAM and with the operating system Linux and 512 MB of RAM. The first version of the laptop (with Windows XP) will appear at the end of July 2008, the second option (with Linux SUSE) - in mid-August. The cost of laptops will be 11999 rubles. and 9999 rub. respectively.
