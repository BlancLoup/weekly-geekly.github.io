Gosbook.ru - a social network for Russian officials has opened registration for everyone



Russian officials have their own social network, Goskuk (gosbook.ru). The project cost 1 million rubles, received in the form of a grant. Experts are sure that such a project is needed, but in order for it to be a real tool, state support is needed. And given the fact that the project was developed with the participation of Medvedev will support.



The main screen of the system is quite simple, which resembles the existing social networks, which, incidentally, is not bad, as it will help the user to quickly get used to the new environment. It is assumed that the new network will be officials who care about the future of our country



By the way, the text of the Roskomnadzor order has already been published there.
Can we discuss? Maybe they will notice all the absurdity of their initiative?



Moreover, the registration process is more than simple. And now passes in a free mode.

In order to become a member of the expert network, several different ways are proposed:
  • You can receive an invitation to become a member of the project by e-mail from any user or from the site administration;
  • You can request a registration by filling in all the fields of the registration form below. Your application will be reviewed and within 3 days a representative of the project administration will contact you to clarify and confirm the data indicated in the questionnaire. The administration of the project reserves the right not to register the participant if the data of his questionnaire does not correspond to reality or is not fully completed.
