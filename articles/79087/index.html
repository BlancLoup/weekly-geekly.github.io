Ford cars - hotspots on wheels

Ford announced that almost all new car models will be equipped with WiFi routers.



Right on the dashboard of the machine will be a USB port for connecting a wireless 3G modem.

Developed in conjunction with Microsoft, SYNC technology is supported in models Focus, Fusion, Taurus, Explorer, etc., a full list of the link. So when entering the salon, all passengers immediately enter the hotspot zone and can surf the Internet from any handheld device.

This is a very convenient feature, because now you do not need to buy 3G modems for each device and for each family member separately. I started the car and the Internet appeared at once. Moreover, it is cheaper, because there is no binding to a specific operator, and you yourself can choose any standard tariff from any operator.

Ford cars run over a secure connection (WiFi Protected Access 2 or WPA2), so passing motorcyclists will not be able to steal your signal.

The equipment of WiFi-routers of stock cars will begin during 2010.
