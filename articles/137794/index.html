Miss Gamer 2 - to the delight of gamers in Russia!

We have already informed you about the launch of the second season of the beauty contest among gamers Miss GAMER 2, organized by the social network for gamers Gamer.ru.

Girls with maniacal zeal rushed to fight for the right to represent their favorite game in the competition. Although the qualifying rounds are coming to an end, the number of participants who are ready to try on the game crown is growing every day.
