The Monopoly building model competition Hasbro: Hasbro, the author of the Monopoly City Streets game , has just announced a competition that will allow users to create models of buildings that can appear in the game. Until October 6, create your building model using Google SketchUp , and then upload it to Google 3D Warehouse . Within 48 hours, your model will appear in the official Building Competition Collection. The more buildings you create, the higher the chances of winning. Make sure your buildings are shipped before October 6; and the winners will be announced on October 13th.
