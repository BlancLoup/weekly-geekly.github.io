Will the interface Cascades in Blackberry 10?

Speech by RIM in Orlando at the global conference of the name itself leaves me with different emotions. Canadians showed great new features on the video that will appear in the Blackberry 10, primarily related to typing and post-processing photos. But the "live" device, distributed to developers, is different from what was in the commercial.



Personally, I was expecting first of all some chips from Cascade: an interface developed by the Swedish design company The Astonishing Tribe (TAT), which RIM bought several years ago. Why they were not even in the commercial? It is unlikely that RIM abandoned this direction, such as the development is not frozen, and they are trying to implement it in Blackberry 10.

Why is the interface - this is still completely QNX-menu from the Playbook? New transitions, layers and other special effects are not found ...



So while RIM showed the same Blackberry Playbook, only a small one: both externally and on the filling, the gadgets are identical, the only difference is in adding a slot for a micro SIM card.
