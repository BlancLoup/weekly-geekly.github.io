Review of the desktop cradle and holder in the car for the HTC Sensation

I present you a review of pleasant things that will make the life of the owner of the HTC Sensation more convenient. This is a desktop cradle for phone and car holder. Both devices can charge the phone. Both came to me from Hong Kong this morning. I liked both

HTC Sensation I bought after using the iPhone for a while. I used to, that for any use there are dozens of different gadgets of different companies. Stands, cradles, holders in the car, etc. When I started to look for the same for HTC, I was shocked by the fact that there are practically no accessories. I was looking for a holder in the car and docking station, which could immediately charge the phone. Inserted and forgotten. No need to connect any additional wiring. And somewhere on the enemy sites I came across KIDIGI products. I looked at their site and was pleasantly surprised - all I needed was there.

I ordered everything on their official online store and after 3 weeks the package was already knocking on my door :-). Unfortunately, she stayed because in the beginning of October, the Chinese had some kind of a holiday, and until the 10th no one practically worked.

Cradle is pleasant to the touch. It looks "expensive", and not as a cheap Chinese fake (not cheap, by the way, never :-)).

Behind the cradle is a microUSB connector. In the place where the phone has a volume button, there is a notch on the cradle. When you insert the phone the volume is not pressed. The holder kit is standard: cradle, holder with suction cupboard, disk for mounting on the panel, cord, charging, micro instruction. Everything was packed in sachets, and the glossy panels were sealed with film. Everything came neat, new, without scuffs and scratches.



Cradle and holder look and feel nice, but the cable and charging - so-so.
The holder is good, without bending parts. Everything is fixed with clamps. From experience I know that this will not tremble in the car.

Cradle with microUSB plug under Sensation. The phone is inserted into it from top to bottom and fixed on top by a latch. On the latch and on the side of the cradle - gum, so that the phone is not scratched. Behind the cradle is a microUSB connector for charging and wire holders. If you hold the wire through them, it will not accidentally pull out of the connector. Trifle, but nice.



In the car, everything fell without problems.



The phone is inserted as easily as in the desktop cradle. The latch on top snaps easily and holds securely.

The latch holds the phone well, so that it can be used in an upright position. I like this option more because The main screen and the call screen in Sense does not rotate.



Conclusions: I liked the device. Well made, 100% fit the phone, normal equipment. Cons: expensive ($ 28 for the cradle and $ 37 for the holder in the car).
