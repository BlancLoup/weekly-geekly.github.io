Who to entrust your business? Part 2 - legal support

Sooner or later, an entrepreneur will need the help of an intelligent lawyer who will give advice and prepare beautiful documents for a deal. Solving a legal problem is a dime a dozen. In order to better understand the market in which our company operates, we conducted a small study: we introduced the variety of accounting services in the previous article , postpone the proposals of secretaries and business assistants to the next one, and today tell you what to do when a business stumbles about legal issues .



The legal services market in Moscow is similar to what accounting offers. You can easily find a couple of web services , a bunch of reference and legal systems, get a one-time consultation, hundreds of outsourcing companies will take care of everything, and there is no shortage in staff resumes and remote employees. Our goal is to choose an option that is suitable for the price and level of service ranging from micro to medium-sized businesses.

Reference systems


Sophisticated habrayuser easily find the answer to any question, saddling Google or Yandex, but you will have to deal with conflicting information from disparate sources. Even before the advent of popular search engines, there were reference and legal systems for searching legal information. Many of them survived the millennium, reached our days, prettier in some places, and sometimes remind of those immemorial times when sharp-nosed shoes still tread the ground and tinted gelenwagen of people in crimson jackets - the forerunners of modern Russian business.

The undoubted advantages of such systems over the usual search engines: accuracy, completeness and constant updating of data, the abundance of author comments that translate information from the bird's language into Russian. In addition to legislative articles (including archival articles), legal acts, judicial practices, these systems contain typical forms and templates for legal documents.

Some of the information is available to everyone, but if you want to get an interpretation of the laws, extended reference books, legal advice and assistance in solving legal issues, you will have to share with developers a couple (or a couple dozen) thousand rubles. With the help of some ATP, you can order the preparation of documents. Most of these systems are installed locally, but those that keep up with the times work in the browser and even release mobile applications.



To suit
In the hands of an intelligent lawyer, they will become an effective tool for navigation in the turbulent waters of the legal ocean, while an entrepreneur will be helped to independently understand uncomplicated situations, but they are unlikely to contribute to solving a serious problem, not to mention predicting and preventing it.

Online legal exchanges


Systematic attempts to independently understand all the peripetia of the legislation are fraught with migraines and dysfunction of the digestive system, and the content of a specially trained person in the state can lead to depletion of the wallet. In this situation, the beginning entrepreneur will come to the exchange of lawyers, where you can quickly and inexpensively get an answer to almost any question.

The most interesting solution in this category is Pravoved.RU . Most of all, it looks like a kind of Stackoverflow: you can search for an answer among a heap of previously created topics, or immediately ask the community of professional lawyers. However, lawyers, unlike IT specialists, do not want to share their competencies on a voluntary basis, so each question must be backed up with a monetary rate. Sensing the sound of coins, knowledgeable people will run into the comments, they will give a little jolt, come to a consensus and give an answer. You will need to choose one or several people who will get your money.

The service team checks the lawyers, constantly improves the ranking mechanisms, monitors the answers and returns the money if the answer was incorrect or incomplete. For a fee, you can talk with a live lawyer by phone (which is especially useful when you do not want to make your case publicly censured), and also order the preparation of documents (contracts, powers of attorney, claims).

The liveexpert.ru service is similar to a lawyer on the principle of work, but does not specialize in legal matters, therefore it is unlikely to provide a similar level of advice.



To suit
For individuals and aspiring entrepreneurs. Lawyers will not be immersed in the specifics of the business, but this is not required when solving typical issues of small business.

Disposable Services


A slightly more intimate approach for relatively little money can be obtained by using one-time services of law firms or freelance lawyers . You will not have to search for a long time: Moscow law firms are fighting to the death for positions in the search results, and the addresses of portals for searching for civilian specialists are already well known to every habrovchanin.

If we did not have our own lawyers, we would prefer to apply to a law firm. Competent people work in such offices, whose competence has been confirmed at least during the interview when applying for a job, and this is already more than what a freelancer can boast :)

To the same category, conditionally, we refer the company-registrars , whose ads (among other exciting works of folk art) are generously plastered with all free advertising space (posts and fences). These highly specialized offices will prepare documents for registering a company with any economic and legal form (IP, LLC, CJSC, JSC), or even take up the whole process, including going to the tax and Pension Fund, printing and opening a bank account .

It is possible to prepare documents for registration independently, using web services like Elba or My Business , but if you want to spend your time on more pleasant and useful things, it is better to immediately turn to professionals. Moscow, to put it mildly, does not feel a lack of registrar firms . Selecting the best among them is not an easy task (after all, Button does not provide such services :), but we cooperate with Express Registrar and, at least, are convinced of its reliability.



To suit
Anyone who needs a one-time consultation, preparing a package of documents and those who are just about to register their business. In addition, you can test a law firm once before entrusting your business to it for months and years :)

Legal support (outsourcing)


If questions of a legal nature arise constantly, one-time consultations can cost a pretty penny. As in the case of accounting, you can get full support without inflating your own staff. The same ‚Äúone-time‚Äù law firms will gladly take on serving a business of any size on an ongoing basis. In addition to advice and consultation, law firms will undertake the preparation and analysis of documents, transaction support, pre-trial settlement, participation in negotiations, representation of interests in government bodies and even in court. The cost of service is usually included a certain number of hours (10 and more), and additional time is charged separately. The circle of duties that the law firm will undertake also depends on the amount of money the entrepreneur is willing to part with, which makes it difficult to determine the final cost.
