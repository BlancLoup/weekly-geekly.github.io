Gmail opens MS Word attachments in Google Docs

Now, instead of saving MS Word files attached to a letter to your local computer, you can directly import them into Google Docs with just one mouse click. Viewing in the form of HTML is saved, but it is logical to use it only for quick familiarization with the document.

Unfortunately, complex documents and documents with graphics are not always displayed correctly. However, the process of importing and editing is quite simple and convenient.
As for other formats, on November 2, 2006, the import of MS Excel files into Google Spreadsheets became available. And it can be expected that the next will be a PDF.
