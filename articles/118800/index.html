de: coded: 72 hour computer festival


A few hours left to the opening of one of the most anticipated and significant computer festivals - DE: CODED 2011, which will be held in Ukraine, the city of Lviv, the Palace of Arts on May 6-8.

de: coded is organized by students, members of the BEST community (Board of European Students of Technology), which has offices in 29 European countries. The festival will be held within 72 hours, starting from 9 am 05/06/2011 to 9:00 05/05/2011. It can be there around the clock.

de: coded is held annually, starting in 2006, but now it has become even more ambitious, you can safely talk about entering the international level. The expected number of participants is about 2000.
Program, plan and more under habrakat
Personally, I am not the official representative of the festival, but I am a potential participant, and I want to inform the people about such a wonderful event.

This time, the entire Palace will be available: the organizers will be on the 1st floor, an exhibition will be set up on the 2nd floor, and the participants with their computers will be located on the 3rd floor. It should be noted that with the move of the hall with computers two floors above, the network also underwent some changes. Now it is designed for 550 participants simultaneously.

The main stage will be located on the 2nd floor in the exhibition area directly opposite the screen. An additional stage will be located on the 3rd floor in the participants hall and in the conference room. The conference hall is designed for 200 seats, it will host all the celebrations, seminars and presentations. For those who want to be in several places at the same time - live broadcasts from one hall to another :)

Seminars



1) Eugene Perepelitsa , Adobe Certified Instructor, will talk about the unique features of the fundamentally new product of the creative line of Adobe CS 5 Flash Catalyst and its integration with the traditional vector drawing tool Illustrator.

2) Ostap Korkuna Will Talk About Innovation And Scale AT Facebook. What are the scalability of them? He said he was in the field of infrastructure development.

3) The theme of the seminar from Google will be the Internet and its future. The speaker will be Dmitry Shilyuk , Industry manager, Google Ukraine.

4) Roman Seniv with SoftServe will tell us about Agile, or rather, why it is fashionable. It will be about why there are fewer problems with the use of Agile'y rules, and then Roman will share with us an analysis and assessment of the latest trends based on statistical data.
