Yoga Tablet 10 HD +


On the very first day, the MWC presented a new incarnation of the Yoga Tablet 10 . The novelty received a display of the same diagonal 10.1 inch, but its resolution increased from 1280x800 to 1920x1200. The changes were made and iron - HD + will be released with Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 1.6 GHz and 2 GB of RAM on board. The operating system is Android 4.3. The tablet received two awards - Android Authority and Expert Reviews.

All of the above perfectly complements the stated work time without recharging - 18 hours. And the stand has become a bit more, which should make the tablet more convenient.

In the US, the tablet will go on sale in April at a price of $ 349, and a keyboard for it (with an additional battery) will cost $ 60.







SHAREit App


The SHAREit Android app has won the Tom's Guide award. It allows you to transfer files of any format without using the Internet "at a speed 40 times faster than Bluetooth." I downloaded this application on two smartphones (both are HTC One X) and transferred 176 megabytes of photos - it turned out 80 seconds, 2.2 megabytes per second. Wi-Fi is used for transmission: the application creates a temporary access point.
