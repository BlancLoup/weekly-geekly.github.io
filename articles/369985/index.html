Fast Shot [1000 FPS]

I suggest a little distraction from the microscope and together watch a small collection of slow motion video.

The story began with the fact that about a year ago I purchased a Sony RX100 IV camera . She turned out to be an unusually good thing (and my friend, who understands this technique, does not advise the bad) and became the main video recording tool both in the garage-laboratory and on trips around the world.

A distinctive feature of this camera is that it can produce accelerated shooting, the so-called slow motion at a speed of 1000 frames per second. Shot video looks 40 times slower, and this is very useful for popular science videos.

Slow motion video I combined into a playlist, which is called - 1000 FPS .

Gull


Let's start with the graceful flight of the seagull. I shot this video from the ferry, which moored to the island of Gotland . At that moment, when the side engines of the ship are working, fish are thrown to the surface of the water, which gets into them. For her and the birds hunt.



Squirrel


Waking up one morning and sitting down for breakfast, I saw a squirrel outside the window. My camera is always at hand. Of course, I did not have time to adjust all the parameters of the video, but the squirrel decided to jump at the right moment. Never before have I seen a squirrel very much like a cat.



Dragonfly


Dragonfly is a very fast insect, it flies at a speed of up to 50 km / h, and also takes off very quickly. But this instance has decided to repeatedly pose for me.



Waxwings


Another video about birds, but from a different angle and in Russia.



Waterman


These insects are also very, very fast. Especially well they get an acceleration from 0 to working speed, and all sorts of turns. Some of their maneuvers managed to shoot in their own pond.



Water and stone


Change the subject and move on to inanimate objects. That's what happens if you throw a stone at the Baltic Sea.
