Pornhub began to remove fake celebrity videos generated by the neural network.


FakeApp program

On January 12, 2018, a Reddit user named Deepfakes released FakeApp , an application for replacing faces in videos. Since that time, several new versions have been released (the current version of FakeApp 2.1 was released on February 4) - and the program has become terribly popular. The number of downloads has long exceeded 100,000. Especially she liked the strawberry lovers, who have long wanted to see their favorite actress or some other chaste celebrity in an interesting position.

Fake celebrity videos were so widespread that popular video hosting had to take action. The first ban on fake videos was introduced by the famous site Pornhub - the global porn giant with an attendance of 75 million people a day.
