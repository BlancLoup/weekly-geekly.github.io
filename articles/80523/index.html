ACM-R5 is a snake robot, with improved protection against dust, waterproofing and rigid frame, able to function in a dry and dusty environment, water and other liquids. It consists of several modules, with small passive wheels, which allow the robot to move smoothly on a flat or rough surface.

ACM-R5 is able to swim in underwater environments. The front module is equipped with a wireless camera mounted on a special mechanism that constantly maintains the orientation of the lens. Designed for underwater research or work in remote areas. The official presentation of the robot: video , pdf . Developer: HiBot Corp.
