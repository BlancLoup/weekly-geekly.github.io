20 techno sounds of the past

I never thought that someday I would listen with tenderness to the sound recordings with which the dial-up modem establishes a connection with the provider. I will never forget what problem it was to connect via the Saratov analog PBX, and then hold on for at least fifteen minutes. If it was raining outside, the connection process stretched into a clock. And this annoying sound ... And then I turned it on - and at once I dropped it like 15 years. So many memories and associations surfaced - my homage.

And it turns out that there are quite a few such sounds, which are inherently unpleasant, harsh, but evoking some magical memories. Some good people have already collected 20 pieces . True, some of them are quite specific, and our man is practically unfamiliar, but the majority still causes everyone who is over 25, if not a tear, to have a thoughtful expression on his face and a desire to call an old friend whom he has not heard for many years.

It is even interesting what sounds will excite us, of the current, 10-15 years from now.
