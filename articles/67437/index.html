A little bit about BlizzCon 2009

BlizzCon is a gaming salon dedicated to Warcraft, StarCraft and Diablo. This is a key event for all Blizzard fans. The two-day program includes "round tables" with the participation of developers and other promotions during which players can chat with game creators. Visitors will also have booths with demo versions of their favorite games.
This year, BlizzCon will be held on August 21, Friday, at the exhibition complex of the city of Anaheim, California.
Also this year, BlizzCon will be broadcasted live for the first time over the Internet (in the form of paid streaming video). 18 hours on the air include the most important announcements about new games from Blizzard Entertainment, exclusive interviews with developers, reports from tournaments in which the world's strongest professional players will meet, a spectacular rock concert by Ozzy Osborne at the closing ceremony and more. The package also includes an exclusive in-game satellite: Murloc Infantry Postrel.

According to Kompyulenta, tickets worth $ 125 were sold in two stages: on May 16 and 30 and distributed through the online store. All tickets to the exhibition BlizzCon 2009 went for 8 minutes, despite their impressive number as much as 20,000. Original sources: the official site of Blizzard and Kompyulenta
