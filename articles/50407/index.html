How to get to the QIWI terminal in two days?

OSMP Company is a leader in the payment terminals market. Over 80,000 units have already been installed throughout Russia. I think that many people are interested in the opportunity to have at their disposal so many cash desks accepting payment 24 hours a day for your orders! For comparison, Sberbank has about 20,000 branches all over the country.

Well, how to get there?

There is a path number 1 - a contract with OSMP and a button in the terminal. But it does not go for 2 days.

There is another way - it is a system for receiving payments through the billing system from the payment system QIWI Wallet .

How it works?


Interaction with the payment system and the online store site takes place via the xml protocol in on-line mode. An example of implementation can be found on the website of the charity fund .

The protocol is configured individually, based on the needs of a store. It is possible to automatically register a user in the payment system after the invoice is issued, select the lifetime of the account, and so on.

The protocol and all instructions are available after registration in the system .

The scheme of work "nominal wallet" does not imply the conclusion of the contract. That is, you can accept payments immediately after approval by the Security Council, that is, 1-2 days after registration.
