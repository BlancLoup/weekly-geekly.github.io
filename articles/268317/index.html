We invite you to RAD Studio 10 Seattle webinars and slides for performances in Moscow and Almaty

Friends!

We all know that on August 31, 2015, Embarcadero released a new release of its flagship product, RAD Studio 10 Seattle (Delphi, C ++ Builder). The new release of RAD Studio 10 Seattle has the greatest capabilities for the rapid development of cross-platform applications using Object Pascal and C ++.

We have already conducted a review webinar for you, now events are taking place as part of the RAD Studio world tour to various cities. Recently, presentations of RAD Studio 10 Seattle took place in Moscow and Almaty. Links to the slides to the speeches will be published at the end of this post.

We are starting a series of webinars in support of the release of RAD Studio 10 Seattle .
We divided the webinars into several episodes by topic, registration for the first episode is already available. And as always, the webinar recording and slides to it will be available on our resources.
