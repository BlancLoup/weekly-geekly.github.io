We update Nexus 4, 7, 10 to Android 4.4 with the new interface

Everything is very simple!
1. Take the Nexus device (4, 7, 10)
2. In Settings / Applications / All find the Google Services Framweork application.
3. Stop it, erase the data and clear the cache.
4. Go to Settings / About phone / Update the system , see the Last update from 197 0. Tap it Check now and update.
5. After updating the system, we install a fresh launchep from Google , which for unknown reasons is not included in the OTA build.

We get Android 4.4 with the right interface, like the Nexus 5!

Tested on Nexus 4 and Nexus 7 in person.
