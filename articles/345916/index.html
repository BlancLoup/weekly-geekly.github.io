In 2017, 11,637 publications were created, of which 10,965 were published. In 2016, there were slightly more publications - 13,094 were created, but only 12,255 were published. For various reasons (registered, randomly placed, with violations, etc.) in 672 publications turned out to be draft copies, and last year - 839. This is approximately 5% of the total, which, in fact, is also quite a lot. But in general, users have become kinder - the number of pluses has increased, and the number of minuses has decreased.

Of all the published materials, 1,148 publications received the mark ‚ÄúTutorial‚Äù (in 2016, 1,208).

Despite numerous requests to add markdown markup (which we added at the beginning of 2016), for 2017 the year only 1 921 publications were written, that is, only 16.5% of the total (1,322 in 2016). We hope that in 2018 we will correct this alignment in favor of the WYSIWYG editor, for which we have big plans.

With a decrease in the number of publications in 2017, their total length was 152,738,122 characters (in 2016 this figure was 150,051,882). This is approximately 84,854 A4 sheets, that is, almost 170 packs (500 sheets each) or a volume 8.84 meters thick (based on the thickness of a sheet of A4 paper 0.104 mm). Until the football field still do not reach, right?

If we calculate the average number of characters per publication, then in 2017 the volume of material averaged 13,125 characters, and in 2016 - 11,459 characters, that is, 7.3 and 6.4 sheets, respectively.
