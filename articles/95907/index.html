Brainstorming Rules


1. The main thing - the number of ideas. Do not make any restrictions. In the comments you write your ideas. The bigger, the better. Anything that comes to mind.

2. A total ban on criticism, including positive. You do not criticize other people's ideas. They may be bad, absurd, but you do not criticize them. Like - put a plus. Do not like it - ignore. There are ideas for the development of someone else's ideas - write. But do not criticize.

3. Unusual and even absurd ideas are welcome. Yes, it is important for the development of fantasy. The idea may be completely absurd, but it may lead other participants to productive ideas. Therefore boldly express anything.

4. Combine and improve any ideas. If you like someone's idea, but you would like to supplement it a little, or shorten it, or change it, or combine it with another idea - write, do not hesitate.
