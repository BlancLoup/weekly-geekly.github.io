Conference "SAM-2011" live

Software asset management is the theme of the fifth annual conference SAM 2011 . It takes place on April 20, 2011 in Moscow and is organized by Softline with the general partnership of Microsoft. The conference is addressed to all who are interested in the latest global developments in this area and to those who intend to systematically evaluate the effectiveness of the company's software.

How to buy software cheaper? How to reduce legal and administrative risks? Finally, how to form a long-term strategy for using and updating software? These tasks are vital for any serious company. Buying software is not only one of the most important, but also one of the most expensive items of expenditure. And at the same time any violation, even out of ignorance, often entails punishment - up to the criminal one. The threat to the business reputation and the very activity of the company - what could be more serious?

Global software management experts and leading Softline experts will help to avoid problems, effectively apply software assets and manage them. Among them is the director of SAM Microsoft Corporation Raul Bandeira and the chairman of the Board of Directors of Softline Igor Borovikov. Live broadcast of the conference can be seen on the site SLTV.RU.
