New problems and unexpected solutions


All anything, but it turned out that it is quite difficult to make many different levels. My friend, again, undertook to help my friend, and already to the fortieth level, there was a very acute problem not only that it‚Äôs hard to invent levels, but also because, due to some linguistic features of human perception, we too often chose certain letters and patterns, stubbornly ignoring others.
