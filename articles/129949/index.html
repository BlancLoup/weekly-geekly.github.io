Stream interactive


Service category VoD (Video on Demand): the desired position is selected from the list and viewed. Streaming video has a standard resolution. The constantly expanding range of "Stream Interactive" includes various television programs and reports, documentaries and Russian TV shows. Convenient interface and availability descriptions are attached. This service is a logical addition to conventional television, all the more, all content is provided free of charge.

You can search either within the specific channel of the holding (Russia 1, Russia 2, Russia 24, Science 2.0, My Planet are available), or by global categories: news, serials, documentaries, programs to children. As the name implies, this application allows you to access an extensive music collection (the application says more than 700,000 tracks, and a million are already mentioned on the Yota website).
