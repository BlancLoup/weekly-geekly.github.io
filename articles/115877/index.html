SVG Open 2011 Annual Conference



The annual SVG Open Days conference has been held since 2002. It focuses on the use of vector graphics, specifically SVG technology, in particular the use of SVG on the web.

In 2011, the conference will be organized by Microsoft and will be held at the new research center in Cambridge. The event will be held from October 17 to 20 and the organizers are already accepting applications from those who wish to participate in the event, both as visitors and as speakers and course organizers.

In 2011, SVG Open Days is held under the slogan of SVG meets the Web , which indicates the focus of the event on the use of SVG technologies on the web.
