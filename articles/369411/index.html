What is the result


Everything went as I had planned - I immediately let the amount received to complete the repair, soon repaying a microloan.

Now, actually, a few numbers. So, I was approved a loan of 12,000 rubles - at the first appeal it is hardly possible to get more, as I understand it. According to my calculations, the daily commission for the use of the loan was about 140 rubles.

I redeemed it ahead of time and in full (I did not see the point in partial early repayment), taking a total of 16 days. It is easy to calculate that I had to return 2240 rubles more, that is, 14240 rubles and another 300 rubles was the commission for the transfer to the card.

What can be said about the result of this whole story?
