List of professions for immigration to Canada

A new list of professions suitable for a professional immigration program to Canada has been approved.

In short: programmers have entered!

0211 Engineering managers
1112 Financial and investment analysts
2113 Geoscientists and oceanographers
2131 Civil engineers
2132 Mechanical engineers
2134 Chemical engineers
2143 Mining engineers
2144 Geological engineers
2145 Petroleum engineers
2146 Aerospace engineers
2147 Computer engineers (except software engineers / designers)
2154 Land surveyors

2174 Computer programmers and interactive media developers

2243 Industrial instrument technicians and mechanics
2263 Inspectors in public and environmental health and occupational health

Good luck, guys!

PS Quote from the blog : Let me remind you that in the old days up to 100-120 thousand immigration visas were issued under this program. For example, in 2010 a quota of 20 thousand applications for this program was allocated, with a quota of 1 thousand applications for each specialty. Now the quota is only 5 thousand applications, with a limit of 300 applications in each specialty.
...
It should be borne in mind that the vast majority of specialties in the list of permitted professions are subject to licensing in Canada, and sometimes it takes several years to obtain licenses for foreigners. In the case of such a specialty as Computer programmers and interactive media developers, which do not require licensing, the quota is only 300 applications, and it is obvious that it will be selected within a few days after opening the program, if not the first two or three hours.
