The main flow of visitors went, of course, on the day of the post - the 23rd. Analysis



As you can see, the failure rate was very high. And the percentage of failures on the first day of the action was much higher than the average . In fact, 3/4 of new visitors closed the site, casting one look at it. Why?
I see only one answer - design. The article with which the transition to the site took place, honestly and in detail explained to the user where he was going.
