Intel invites to Droidcon. Moscow, September 25-27


September 25-27, for the second time in Moscow, the largest in the Old World conference of Android developers Droidcon will be held. The conference program includes reports by leading Russian experts on Android and, of course, many, many conversations on interests in various formats.

Intel is the gold partner of the Droidcon conference - we invite you to visit our events within its framework.
  • On Saturday at 12:10, we will introduce a new tool for developing cross-platform Java applications on the Intel Multi OS engine .
  • A Sunday at the Intel Developer Zone will take place at 11:30 on Sunday , where we will talk about the news of the Intel Developer Zone for Android program and hold a master class on Intel XDK.

On Saturday and Sunday at the conference will be held an exhibition of Android achievements. At the Intel booth, you will be able to see Android devices based on Intel Atom x3 (codename SoFIA), devices with RealSense cameras. There will be demonstrations of the Multi OS engine and XDK, other Intel tools, as well as contests with original prizes.

A separate ad is addressed to pretty ladies. Intel is sponsoring the participation of female developers of Android applications in Droidcon conferences throughout Europe. To participate in the Intel sponsorship program, you must complete the form .
