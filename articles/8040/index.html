Youtube faces 15 years in prison

Thailand is going to sue YouTube for clips that it considers offensive to King Bhumibol Adulyadej.

The charges against Thailand are insults - insults to the monarchy, which in Thailand are regarded as serious crimes for which punishment is envisaged - 15 years in prison.

These videos actually represent a slide show with a photo of the monarch, painted (apparently in the point) with strokes of beginning computer artists. Sometimes there are tie-ins of his face into specially selected photographs of animals and mostly naked people.
