Dokkur - the first PaaS from Russia

Hi, Habrayuzer!
We are a small development team of four people, and we want to present our PaaS, an analogue of Heroku, to your court.

Working for quite a long time on the web development market, at some point we were faced with the problem of competent application management. Setting up our servers turned out to be not a very quick solution for us, and the variety of technologies created an additional load.

Having tried Heroku as a solution, we understood what quality and convenience are. The only thing that bit is the price. Therefore, about 4 months ago we set a goal to make our own platform. And we made it. Meet Dokkur - the first full-fledged PaaS from Russia!




What is Dokkur?


This is a cheap but quality PaaS for websites and applications. The main management components at the moment are a web-based office , console tool and iOS application . Starting from the simple, we tried to make the most convenient and practical software complex for deploying applications for small projects.

One of its features is the use of buildpacks (who do not know, see the information here ), which allows you to cover Ruby, Node.js, Clojure, Python, Java, Gradle, Grails, Scala, Play, PHP, Go, and also custom Docker image.
Supported DBMS: CouchDB, Elasticsearch, MariaDB, Memcached, Mongo, MySQL, Nats, Postgres, RabbitMQ, Redis, RethinkDB.

Of course, if your application is a complex microservice system of a million components or if you use a complex custom front-server configuration, balancing, and you also want to have your own amusement park with blackjack and whores cluster, then it is more profitable and easier for you to rent an individual server space and hire good specialist. Dokkur is primarily focused on small applications that need good service: from fast deployment to operational service.
