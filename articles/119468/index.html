Game consoles: an extra reason for nostalgia

Console games of the last century had the opportunity to "breathe" a second time after the development of emulation on computers, and after a while on smartphones. But after the rapid development of the gaming industry on the PC, the interest of "gamers" to console games of the last century began to fall gradually, although some of them still remained popular, mainly due to smartphones. But‚Ä¶. no wonder they say that Apple shoots rarely, but accurately.
