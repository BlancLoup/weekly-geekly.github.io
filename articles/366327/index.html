Suitcase with an electric scooter will help to catch the plane

Suitcase Briefcase Electric Scooter is equipped with a built - in electric scooter that can travel at a speed of up to 20 km / h for about ten kilometers with a fully charged lithium-ion battery. The weight of the device is about eight kilograms, and the size allows you to carry it into the plane as hand luggage.



A smart suitcase that can weigh itself is, of course, a good thing. But what's the point in a bunch of bloat if you can be late for the flight? Then the Briefcase Electric Scooter comes to the rescue . A suitcase with a rigid case, if necessary, folds out and turns into a scooter that can reach speeds of up to 20 km / hour and travel 10 kilometers on one charge. If you are confused by the slanting glances of others because you are riding the airport on a scooter, you can use the gadget as a personal porter .

A brushless motor device is hidden in the rear wheel. It works from hidden lithium-ion batteries. The gadget without your baggage weighs 7.9 kilograms.

The cost of the gadget is $ 599, and for the version with solar panels you will have to pay another hundred.





For those who need a smaller suitcase, you can recommend a Movpack backpack - this gadget is equipped with an electric skateboard. Control is exercised from the panel, the maximum speed - 24 km / h. The weight of the backpack is 7.7 kilograms.
