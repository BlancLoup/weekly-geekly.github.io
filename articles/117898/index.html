In fact, the Frodio design concept begins where traditional broadcasting ends. He lacks the very interactivity and sociality that listeners want from him.

Listeners and Broadcasters (whether you are a radio host, DJ or station owner) are met in one place, with a full range of social functions.

The basis of everything: Frodio Social Radio System, abbreviated as SRS, which integrates existing radio stations. In the near future, we will also provide the opportunity to create our own station with us - specifically for this, we built Frodio on the basis of the cloud infrastructure of Selektel in order to give you complete freedom of action.

We offer listeners 42 best streams in real time, each of which will be appreciated by a habrauser. All of them are selected by community members, and in the future we will give everyone the opportunity to independently select and evaluate the best radio stations.

Everyone decides for himself which stations to evaluate so that friends on the network find out about it. Frodio is integrated with such music services as: Last.fm and Musicbrainz, not to mention the most popular social networks: Facebook, Twitter, VK. In the end, we can search for information about the performers on Wikipedia - who is not picky?

But besides the listeners, we also appreciate those who create the radio. Presenters, DJs of all stripes, and podcasting fans - this is for you!
