Updates from Habra. Browser Extension

I am glad to present to your attention the next version (1.5) of the extension for habr (the previous topic ), which has learned to accept and show the subscription, the tape and mentions , and also work with the user profile on the habr.
Also now shows your karma, rating and balance of the voting charge .




User Profiles


Now on any page of the habr, if you hover the mouse over the user's nickname, the extension will show you in a small block information about this user - avatar, name, nickname, karma, rating, registration, when it was the last time, a note.
Here in the block, you can immediately vote for the user, subscribe / unsubscribe to it, change the note, and also write a private message.
You can also change the group in which the user is located (for expansion).
Information about the user is cached for the time configured in the extension.



Subscription, feed and mentions


Accepts updates from these channels. Shows them on the corresponding tabs: two little men; orange rss icon; green ribbon.



As elsewhere, you can work with this information - immediately respond , vote, see all the answers and comments in QA , etc.

Filters have been added to the subscription and feed - you can configure it to see only the events of the desired type.

Charge and settings


Now you can see your karma, rating, and the remaining charge for voting in the right-above in the extension.
After any voting inside the extension, these values ‚Äã‚Äãare instantly updated.

Quick settings for sound and pop-ups transferred to the usual settings. In fast there was only "silent mode".
Settings for new channels are in the tabs "tracker" and "tape".

Also accelerated the launch + works without the Internet , because it now remembers the user and does not wait for the response of the habra to the login every time it starts.

After the installation, do not forget to include all the checkboxes and, if necessary, add zeros to the page load limiters. For the default settings are set to save traffic and the extension almost does not make requests to the Internet.
