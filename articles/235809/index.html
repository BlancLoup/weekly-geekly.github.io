OnePlus One - Chinese Beast

Today we will talk about a very interesting device from a young Chinese manufacturer OnePlus - OnePlus One (abbreviated as OPO).

I found out about this device quite by accident (I looked at the review from Rozetked), then several reviews were published on various resources and I began to read, search for information, and so on. But to buy the device was not as easy as we would like. I will explain why.


At the moment there are 2 versions of the smartphone:
1) International
2) For the domestic (Chinese) market
Several differences. The smartphone for the Chinese market does not support US LTE networks; He goes to the Color OS, and not to Cyanogen and, accordingly, on the back panel there is no Cyanogen inscription.

The version for the Chinese market can be bought in China, but the international version is distributed through the invitation system, which is connected with the ideology of the company. Good products should be distributed in a narrow circle of family.
