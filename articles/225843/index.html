FAB PICNIC at Polytechnic

This weekend (June 7-8), an open-air festival of scientific and technical creativity called FAB PICNIC is held in St. Petersburg. On the official page of VKontakte it is stated that exhibitions of technical and design projects, 3D printers, robots, quadcopters, board games, as well as hackathons and lecture halls with real scientists are expected.



For some reason, this news passed by Habr. On the first day of the festival, I got quite by accident: the night before I was walking near the Polytech and saw the unfolding objects. I decided to go and, as it turned out, for good reason.

Under the cut I will describe some details, share photos and received promotional codes.



The beginning was scheduled for 12:00, but already at 11:30 most of the booths were deployed, the 3D printers printed something green, and the robots cheerfully drove around the tables. Entry is absolutely free for everyone.

Robotics



TRIK

The first stand I reached was made by robots with TRIK inscription.



Project engineers have developed a controller of the same name. The controller can analyze data from the connected external sensors, including the ability to monitor the object through an external camera (one of the robots had a shield from the Arduino).



The controller is programmed through its TRIK Studio IDE. Included you can purchase a designer with all the necessary strapping.
Anyone could steer a tracked all-terrain vehicle using a tablet. 15% discount on promotional code # FP2014

Scratchduino

The project has approximately the same objectives as the previous one: an entertaining robot platform for schoolchildren / students.



Designer based on the Arduino with a box-set of various sensors and a visual IDE.
