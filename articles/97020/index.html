The first ever Russian-Iranian agreement in the field of IT has been concluded: the MMORTS Elements of War will be published in the Islamic Republic

Playnatic Entertainment and Sina Data Co. announced the signing of an agreement for the publication, localization and operation of the Elements of War project in the territory of the Islamic Republic of Iran. It should be noted that this is the first ever Russian-Iranian agreement in the field of information technology.



According to the Persian comrades from Sina Data Co. in Iran today there are about 5 million gamers. Given that the entire population of Iran is 74 million people, the figure is very, very rather big. The growth prospects of the market are also evident: Iran is a young country, 2/3 of its inhabitants are people no older than 35 years.



The agreement was signed at the Iranian Embassy in the presence of the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Islamic Republic, Mr. Reza Sajadi, who noted the high significance of the signed agreement for the development of interstate and cultural ties between Russia and Iran.
