Blender new release released

A new release of the Blender program has been released.



Today on the official website of the Blender 3D-package its new release of version 2.64 was published ...


As previously reported in this post, the support of this 3D package is handled by the Mango team, which has been updated and improved by this program.

On the Blender releaser, the buildbot has stopped at R51027 so far.

For those who do not know, let me remind you that the Blender program is distributed under the GPL for artists license . This product has all the necessary tools (I will not be afraid of these words, since I myself work with pleasure in this program) for 3D creativity:

- modeling and animation of complex models;
- compositing;
- video editing;
- camera tracking;
- non-linear installation;
- work with sound;
- the dynamics of the fabric;
- solid-state dynamics;
- dynamics of soft bodies;
- dynamic drawing;
- sculpture;
- fluid generator;
- smoke generator;
- built-in game engine;
- writing scripts in Python;
- physical ray tracing;

etc.
