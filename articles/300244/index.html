Steve Wozniak, Richard Branson and Nicola Mendelsohn will select the best startups at the Talent Unleashed Awards



Large companies remain interested in acquiring promising start-ups and continue to use all available tools to support them. Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak, along with billionaire Virgin Richard Branson, as well as Facebook Vice President Nicola Mendelson, became the jury members of the Talent Unleashed Awards .

They will take part in several categories of the Talent Unleashed Awards: the best startup that influenced society, the best startup in the field of innovation, the best startup SME, the most effective CIO / CTO, and the best startup that raised investments.

Startups and entrepreneurs from Australia, New Zealand, Asia, Europe and the UK can take part in the Talent Unleashed Awards. The Talent Unleashed Awards Final will be held in Sydney in August, and the finalists will also have the opportunity to meet with Steve Wozniak and travel to Silicon Valley.
