Al Jazeera releases videos and photos of protests in Egypt under Creative Commons licenses



Qatar News Service Al Jazeera and Creative Commons have a long relationship. In 2009, Al Jazeera released a high-quality video of the war in Gaza under the license Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 on a specially created for this site cc.aljazeera.net .

Now Al Jazeera continues to delight us . The video of protests in Egypt is licensed under a free license Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported on the same website http://cc.aljazeera.net , and the photos are posted on Flickr under the unfortunately non-free license Creative Commons Attribution-NoDerivs 2.0 , but this is better than nothing

Well done! What else can you say ...
