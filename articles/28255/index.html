The Yandex advertising network begins to conclude contracts with individuals directly

Now any site owner - an individual - a resident of the Russian Federation, can conclude with Yandex a direct cooperation agreement on the Yandex Advertising Network program. In this case, he will receive the earned partnership reward to the account in Sberbank of the Russian Federation.

Partner's share is 45% of the cost of advertising on the site. Of the accrued amount, Yandex holds and transfers to the budget of the Russian Federation the tax on personal income (13%).

In order to start working with Yandex directly, an individual partner needs to sign an agreement with Yandex.

If the partner already has a site that participates in the Yandex Advertising Network, then you need to create a new account on Yandex and resubmit your application in it.
