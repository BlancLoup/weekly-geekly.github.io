Creating your company. Mission declaration

Good afternoon, dear readers of "Megamind"!

I have long thought what unites successful companies, and whether their success is a regularity, and not an accident. And every day I approached the answer, to the solution of an important question - what is characteristic of all successful firms? Yes, this is a mission, or rather, its presence.

As I wrote in my article, the mission should be defined at the first stage of development of the company. But he did not specify that the mission should be declared in this case - that is, it was announced (published) to the public.
Having defined the mission of your company, you acquire an unlimited amount of funds for sale. But from words to deeds - I will give examples of real firms, and in the end I will tell about my own.

The mission of my company

I have long enjoyed helping people. But I always understood that not everyone needs my help. And recently, I realized that I can create a company (and have already created), which in its mission puts
Make people more comfortable, more interesting and safer.

I like it when people satisfy their desires. But it is also more pleasant for me when they do it not to the detriment of themselves and others.

What allows me to declare my mission? The fact that I can engage in completely different activities: create a convenient application planner, open a sushi studio, establish an advertising agency, run a design studio. All my future (and current) directions are united by one thing: I try to make the life of every person more comfortable, more interesting and safer.

Comfortable - it will be convenient for you to work with me; more interesting - I really appreciate your desires and make every effort so that they come true. Safer - I guarantee the integrity of work with the client, the quality of their services and their legitimacy.

I hope that my article will help determine the undecided (what they want from their company) and adjust the plans for those who have almost achieved success.

PS: By the way, with a clear mission in my head, it is much easier to get funding.
