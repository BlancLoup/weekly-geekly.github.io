Reporting

Every 1-5 day of the month, following the end of the quarter, it is necessary to submit reports on income and tax paid to the tax (at the beginning of the year a report for the last year is submitted).
All this is quite easy to do via the Internet, with the help of a special program. By the time it takes about 10 minutes. Well, plus the time to calculate the profit for the quarter (smiley with dark glasses)
Every March 1-31 it is necessary to submit reports to the Pension Fund - INDAN.

Bank

Immediately after opening your business, you must open a bank account. The choice is yours, there are different procedures everywhere.
I chose a SMALL bank, it was important to me that it was close to home, the availability of Internet banking. Tariffs, too, look at the banks.

In large banks it is possible to open everything much faster, but then there will not be so much individual attention. For example, I was allowed to bring documents after operations, received applications by telephone, a diary was presented for the New Year :)
