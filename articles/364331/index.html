The court ruled: the removal of DRM-protection is not a violation of copyright

Denise Cote, the federal judge of New York, ruled on the case where the two publishing companies were the plaintiffs and the seller, Abbey House Media, was the defendant that spreading information about the possibilities of removing DRM protection from electronic books is not a copyright violation. This is reported by EFF .

Such a lawsuit was filed after the seller, closing his online store, warned buyers that they would not be able to further adjust the DRM protection of the sold books to new devices for reading. And in this regard, I advised to use the program to remove the protection Caliber.
