Fool Solitaire!


Opened a way to cheat the statistics of positive outcomes of the game in solitaire "Free Cell" ( "FreeCell" ). I do not know who this may be useful for, but the error looks interesting. Tested and works in Windows 7 beta (build 7000).

1. Start the game and play until you realize that " one more turn and solitaire will work ." For example, so (it remains to transfer 10 Hearts to Jack Cresti ):

Close and save the game on exit.
2. Start the game again, agree to continue the saved game. Make the last move, enjoy the beautiful screen saver of falling cards :)
3. And here is the most interesting. Notice on the final table the statistics of win / loss:

Click "Exit"
4. GOTO 2 :) Start the game again, agree again to resume the game ... And again our saved game will open! You can again shift the last card ... And so on.

Look at the game statistics (F4) and make sure that the total number, the number of games won and the percentage of win / loss increases, while the number of lost games remains unchanged :)
