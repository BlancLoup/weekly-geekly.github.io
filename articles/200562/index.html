HiConversion - beta test invitation

Hello!

We have released our first open version of a social media advertising automation system. We offer those who wish to experience it in action.

Why do we need this system:


With the help of the system, you can accurately determine which ads lead orders to you.
When it comes to one ad, statistics are not hard to see manually through Google Analytics. But one ad, very little! I want to tell you how you can work with hundreds of ads. And more importantly, get a good CPA from it.


How it works:


For example, we sell women's dresses through Vkontakte.
We have two cool images of dresses that we want to advertise.



We need to understand which image often leads to clicks on the ad, and then to the sale.
In addition, the conversion affects not only the image, but the text that we use.
Here, too, not everything is obvious. Here are three examples of text that I could come up with:



But in each region of Russia, texts and images can work in different ways. It may be that in the capital it is more important to use the delivery information. Whereas in the region, it is more important to report the price.

So it turns out that we have to test 2 images, 3 texts and 4 cities where we deliver.
The result is 2 * 3 * 4 = 24 ads.
Some of them will work well. Some worse. The difference in the number of sales between the best and worst ads can be several times.

HiConversion allows you to test all combinations and find the best ad. Ads can be created one by one through the interface. But if the ads are not 24, but closer to 100, then it will be more convenient to use Excel like this: hiconversion.ru/excel-example.xlsx
Here each line corresponds to one ad. It is convenient to copy lines and change one of the parameters. This way you can create hundreds, even thousands of ads.

Statistics:


After creating ads, we can integrate HiConversion with Google Analytics and collect information from the site. Which ad gave us the most sales.

Other functions:


In addition to the mass creation and counting of transactions, the system has a number of functions, which are best written briefly:
  • Automatic tactics. You can drive the CPA you need and the system will manage the rates to achieve the CPA.
  • Mass operations. You can copy, edit, run, stop immediately ad groups. It's comfortable.
  • Filters, shortcuts and sorting that make it easier to work with arrays of ads.
