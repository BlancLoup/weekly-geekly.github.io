Three budget quadcopters with cameras in the historical museum "Kolomenskoye"

Recently, I described the most budget quadcopters in the world for $ 20 without a camera and for $ 35 with a video camera , but today I have prepared a material about three very different quadcopters:
1. One is a bit clumsy, of modest size, with a weak camera, but very budgetary - Top Selling X6 .
2. The second one has a more significant size, has a decent enough camera on board, but still fits into the category of budget quadcopters for $ 30-60 - SYMA X5C .
3. The third one hears not the first year and has already managed to recommend itself, this is the most budget version from DJI - Phantom 1, although its budget immediately increases the gap from the second 10 times.

All these 3 quadcopters went to fly to the former royal residence and patrimony, and now - the State Art Historical-Architectural and Natural Landscape Museum-Reserve "Kolomenskoye".



Spring came and quadrocopters flew.

I wanted to compare quadcopters of different price levels in order to understand what makes them different and for which we overpay or underpay.

Findings:
DJI Phantom 1 is definitely still good. Smooth movements, stable hang-up on a point using GPS satellites, precise and easy operation and of course the ability to carry gopro on board is an unshakable advantage, since the quality of the shooting is simply not comparable with the built-in cameras.

His battery showed quite a decent result, having lasted about 7-10 minutes, and considering that the batteries of this model are relatively affordable ($ 22.99), then you can have a few pieces in your arsenal enough for a full-fledged survey. But in general, the price of such a drone is justified only if you really need it.

X6 is certainly the most brisk quadrocopter, even with the protection of screws, it is not so easy to plant on trees, as it easily flies between any branches, and due to its low weight, even from high altitude it threatens to fall only with fear. However, it is possible to break it, since it accelerates very quickly and if it encounters a pillar on its way, the hull can crack and the screws get lost. Perhaps that is why in China " crash sets " with new cases and screws are actively sold.

At the same time, he has very small screws and can only be a bit beaten about your fingers, and with the protection of the blades you can fly even next to the children.

In any case, for its agility and safety, the X6 is No. 1 for home indulgence .



SYMA X5C - this quadrocopter has become a real discovery for me. I really haven't had so much fun with a quadrocopter for quite a while.
