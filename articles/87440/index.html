Simpler! Simpler! Even easier! How to get out when not enough hands

Tonight there was nothing to be done to solder a couple of transistors. Starting with regret, I found out that the sight was already not that ...
The lamps were not at hand, and the flashlight in his teeth is only convenient to hold in the cinema. Made a quick highlighting. On so much simple that I could not not share with the community.

The ingenious simplicity of design ideas and the elegant curves of the luminaire body make the audience tremble. Ridiculously simple and convenient.

So we need:
  • White LED (suitable from unnecessary more flashlight)
  • a piece of wire, such as twisted pair .
  • any 3v battery



The wire can be bitten from unused pairs in the network cable of your computer , but I would not recommend this to inexperienced users. Well, if you decide to google beforehand, ‚ÄúI lost the Internet, what to do,‚Äù and save the results. You will need them.

The tool to your taste: the wire can be broken by repeated bending, and the LED can be simply screwed. I had a soldering iron on hand, a suitable battery and 5 minutes of time too.



So, let's begin.

From a piece of wire of suitable length we bend the stand. The shape, wire color and size of any - feel free to fantasize, but it is better to make it stable without the weight of the battery, then your lamp will be not only the easiest but also convenient.



Customize contacts.



After checking the polarity we connect the LED (if it is not lit, switch the legs in places or turn the battery over if you have already soldered).



Check the system assembly.



And in the work.



After enjoying the form, we bend the wire as it is more convenient for us, we direct the light to the darkest corners, keeping our hands free. Production time 5-10 minutes depending on the desire to enjoy the process.
