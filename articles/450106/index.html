The project of the organization of construction and reconstruction in cramped conditions in SPDS Construction site

We invite you to take part in the free webinar "The project of the organization of construction and reconstruction in cramped conditions at SPDS Construction Site".

REGISTER



On May 29, a webinar on the Project for the Organization of Construction and Reconstruction in Constrained Conditions at the Construction and Construction Projects Building Site will be held, within which unique methods for creating a construction plan will be shown in the program for construction and construction projects Construction site, when the building is being built under dense construction and developed infrastructure.

The purpose of the webinar is to educate users on the practical use of SPDS construction site tools. At the webinar you will learn:

  • how to put construction sites, various types of buildings, storage sites, temporary nets, fences, temporary roads and areas for reversal on a construction general plan;
  • how to select construction equipment from the program database;
  • how to work with automatic hazardous areas and edit their contour;
  • how to insert safety signs;
  • how to create automatic explications, specifications and legend table.

The webinar is focused on specialists of the POS, CPD, construction departments, IT-services departments, as well as all those interested in the capabilities of the SPDS Construction Site program.

Moderator - Olga Artemyeva, Head of Software Development Department.
Start date and time: May 29, 2019, at 11:00 Moscow time.
Duration: 30 minutes.
Participation in the webinar is free, the number of places is limited.
