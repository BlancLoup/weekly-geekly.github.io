SmartQ V7 Review


A few weeks ago, the Chinese company SmartDevices began selling a new line of MIDs - SmartQ V, which consists of SmartQ V5 and V7. The devices differ in screen size (4.3 "vs. 7") and battery capacity. The V series is based on the Telechips TCC8900 chipset, which can play video up to 1080p.

  • Device Screen: 7 ", 800x480 pixels, resistive
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth 2.0, Wi-Fi 802.11b / g, can connect via 3G USB modem, USB Host / OTG
  • Chipset Telechips TCC8900 with TCC8901 processor operating at a frequency of 600 megahertz, 256MB DDRII, 2GB of non-volatile memory
  • HDMI, composite, 3.5mm outputs, built-in microphone, SDHC card reader
  • Li-Pol 4500mAH battery
  • Dimensions: 200x120x24, 480 grams
