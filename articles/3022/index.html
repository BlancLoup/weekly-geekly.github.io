Kodak imprints its name into the history of digital printing

And without that, already renowned for its achievements in the development and popularization of photography, Kodak seems to have made another breakthrough in the industry.

At the presentation, held on Tuesday in New York, several models of printers were presented, as well as ink for them, created using the latest revolutionary technology.

These inks, as Kodak promises, will allow you to print photos that will not lose appearance for as many as 100 years! In addition, they are two times cheaper than counterparts from the current market leaders - HP, Epson and Canon. And this is confirmed by independent testing.

The company has released an update for the EasyShare line of large, full-featured printers for the first time in 5 years. And they, too, are surprised by their performance: a very fast printing of color images at a relatively low price will surely bring them deserved popularity.
