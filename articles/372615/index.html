VKontakte plans to launch an internal money transfer system



In the social network "VKontakte" intend to introduce a system of internal payments between users, the newspaper "Izvestia" , citing sources close to the leadership of the resource. The planned functionality will most likely be repeated in Mail.ru, the Odnoklassniki social network.

The transfer of money will be between the personal pages of users. And for the transfer of funds details of the recipient will not be needed, just enough attached to the profile of a bank card. The size of the commission for such transfers is also unknown.

Such a step may be due to the fact that the social network plans to capture market share of cashless transfers. According to statistics from Yandex.Money, the number of transfers through the system in the first seven months of this year increased by 74%, and direct transfers from card to card doubled.

In the wake of the Russians' refusal of cash payments, VKontakte and their owners, Mail.ru Group, are making a successful step to consolidate in this fast-growing market. The huge user base and the success of this functionality in another project of the company, Odnoklassniki, shows that it will be popular in VKontakte too.

According to the head of the press service of social services. Odnoklassniki networks of Anastasia Zhbanova, OK users favorably accepted the transfer system and began to actively use it (the average amount to transfer is 4000 RUB), and then, at their own request, the ability to transfer funds abroad was added.

The functionality of internal payments was launched in Odnoklassniki more recently - in February of this year. It is possible that the less popular social network Mail.ru was used as a huge test platform before introducing the system to VKontakte.
