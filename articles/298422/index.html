Reducing prices for vacancies in the regions by 20-25%, making prices fair

We are glad to inform you that since February 4, we are reducing prices for vacancies in the regions.



For Moscow and St. Petersburg prices remain the same - 3,000 rubles. And in million-plus cities the monthly placement of a vacancy will now cost 2,000 rubles. (and not 2500 rubles), in other cities of Russia, Ukraine, Belarus and Kazakhstan - 1500 rubles. (not 2000 rubles.).

Why we lowered prices


We have accumulated sufficient statistics, according to which it is clear that, on average, one and a half to two times more applicants respond to capital vacancies than regional vacancies. Therefore, we decided to reduce the prices for regional vacancies so that they are more fair.
