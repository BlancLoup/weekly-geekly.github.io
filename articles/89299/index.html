Early build of Windows Phone 7 Series launched on HTC HD2


On the western resource HTCpedia flashed photos, and even a couple of videos, which captured the HTC HD2, running the new Windows Phone 7 S. And it looks like this is not just a picture in the photo gallery on full screen.
Under the cut photos and videos.

The team of young men from Russia, who provided the materials, declares that all the functions of the device, including bluetooth, Wi-Fi and even GPS, are stable, the video driver turned out to be the only problem, because of the lack of which the system runs not so fast and smoothly as we would like .

Previously, such claims appeared on the network, but so far any rumors seemed rather doubtful against the background of an official statement by Microsoft representatives that HD2 is hardware incompatible with new windows .
But our masters not only declare the success and dedication of their undertaking, but even promise to release a ready-to-install and use build system before the official release .
