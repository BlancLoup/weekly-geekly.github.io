Initially, the purpose of the purchase was: to facilitate the morning awakening because I do not regularly go to bed and roll for 10-20 minutes before getting up.
Before buying it, I chose it among several devices, including the Sleeptracker. In choosing factors were taken into account: price, compatibility, comfort in use, an online service for monitoring sleep (but as it turned out later, this is a complete garbage)
Also deceived bribed me the following comparison plate with competitors
Ie wakemate allegedly ahead of all competitors in terms of comfort, portability, the ability to automatically download data, exhaustive means for analyzing sleep, the duration of the "window" during which the device will try to wake up.
Colleagues at work expressed their opinion about the device as follows: it is not reliable or the battery will sit down or the bluetooth connection with the phone will break. I did not listen to them and decided to take a chance ...

Principle of operation
The wakemate version for iOS and Android is on sale, i.e. use the same bracelet with two operating systems will not work. The device consists of the iron bracelet itself with a battery and 2 LEDs (the LED is lit continuously green - there is a bluetooth connection between the bracelet and the phone, it flashes green - there is no connection or it is lost and the red charge indicator LED) and software. With the google market (play) or appstore, the free wakemate program is downloaded. When you first start the program, pairing of two devices via Bluetooth occurs (the default code, if I'm not mistaken, is 1234). Developers on the site assure that the battery lasts for 24 hours or 2-3 nights. In fact, my battery died after 20-23 hours (and this is because I forgot to turn off the bracelet after waking up)
Purchase and delivery
I bought a bracelet for Anroid in May last year for $ 60. The first problem is that wakemate is delivered only to Canada, the USA and Europe, since I live in Kazakhstan, I ordered it via Shipito mail forwarding service.

Equipment
The bracelet itself, the battery (it is necessary to insert the bracelet into it), a leaflet with brief instructions and that's it.

Experience of use
On the bracelet was the inscription wakemate (photo no), a week later it was worn out.

In total, the bracelet was used continuously for about 1.5-2 weeks. Suppose I have to get up at 7.00, the alarm clock will try to wake me up from 6.40 to 7.00, and this interval of 20 minutes cannot be changed for the android version (for ipad, you can). For all the time of use, the bracelet always woke me up at the latest time of the 20-minute interval, i.e. at 7.00. And the first week of use, I woke up myself 20-30 minutes before waking up. I put it on my left hand (on the site it is indicated that it is necessary to wear on a non-dominant hand) Often (about 30% of the total time of use) in the morning there was an error "There seems to be a problem with reconnecting to the Wakemate.Please plug the device ‚ÄúThere was an error when connecting to the bracelet, please plug in the bracelet to charge, turn it on and off and click Continue to restore the sleep data.‚Äù Naturally, in the morning, when you get woken up, it breaks very much to practice these dances with a tambourine, especially to look for a charger. After clicking Try again, nothing happened, in general, an endless loop :)
