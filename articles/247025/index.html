GDG DevFest Voronezh 2014: photo report and impressions

On December 13 and 14, the third annual GDG DevFest Voronezh 2014 conference was held in Voronezh, organized by Google, Voronezh State University and DataArt. The event gathered about two hundred developers, designers and just Google-enthusiasts.




December 13, theory day


The first day of the conference was hosted by the Voronezh State University. We took a great assembly hall of VSU and added a little Google holiday to it.

December 14, the day of practice


On the second day, the first Design Sprint in the city was scheduled. The participants decided not to divide into teams, but to try by joint efforts to create a really cool application for smart watches. Technical experts from DataArt and SurfStudio helped them: Aleksey Rybakov and Vadim Kotov.

In the course of heated discussions and many hours of fruitful work, an application for travelers was designed, allowing you to make audio, text and video notes and save on the map the place where they were taken. Simple, useful and convenient - what else is needed for a good smartwatch application?
