Mobile Internet lifecycle

Historically, I always went online via a cell phone, connecting it as a modem to my computer.
There were times when cellular operators for providing access to the Internet demanded besides, actually, paying for traffic (with corresponding rounding), also a monthly fee, and also the connection / disconnection of GPRS services was paid.
Nevertheless, the ability to be connected to the Network for 20 hours gave an indisputable benefit compared to dial-up access.
Oh, what were the times ...
The question of saving money was then most acute.
I had to use the shutdown of scripts, graphics, traffic compression, and even artificially lower the speed of the modem (I usually set 9600 bps).
