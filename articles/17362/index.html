Russian World of Warcraft will be released in 2008

Blizzard decided to please the Russian (by the way, all Russian-speaking) fans of World of Warcraft and announced that in 2008 it would fully translate its most popular MMORPG into Russian.

It is interesting that not one of the Russian companies will be involved in the translation of the game, but a special department of Blizzard, which will be formed in the French and Irish offices of Blizzard Europe.

Although there is no exact information on the timing of the release of localization, this will probably happen at the same time as the appearance of the next World of Warcraft add-on called Wrath of the Lich King.

Simultaneously with the release of the Russified version of WoW, the Russian-language site of this game will also work.
