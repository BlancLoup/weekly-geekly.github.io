Analysis and synthesis of survival models

Here is the article I have promised with a summary of the experience of the 3 stages of survival. It has long been in a closed state, but is now available to subscribers of the Survival blog. And now the final article in my series.

Survival is an activity in difficult and dangerous conditions, when inaction or incorrect behavior can lead to death.

For many of us, the word "Survival" is associated with some abstract difficulties and hardships. In the process of training for survival training, when we can plan a situation, there are often disputes about what is honest and what is not fair. We would have those or other resources in a real situation. What to take, and what not to take. All this happens because the workout does not have a clear model. There is no clear picture of the situation, available resources, conditions and dangers. Someone thinks about life after a plane crash, others are preparing for a nuclear winter, and others - make a fire. Often you have to see arguments that seem to be combined into one situation, but not really existing simultaneously in life. The purpose of this article is to try to systematize possible hypothetical situations and describe how to proceed, based on the circumstances of the available resources and what bonuses can be expected. In other words - we will try to build different models that do not contradict possible situations.

It is given: a desert island, hot tropics, a wrecked ship drowned, of the things - only clothes, there‚Äôs no help waiting.

When you find water, start looking for food. You might find it all at once.
The bonuses of this model: when you find a stable source of water and food - you are unlimited in time to acquire everything else: fire, housing, tools of work, and time is a valuable resource.
