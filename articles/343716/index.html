Experiment to promote the game on Google Play. Part 1

Hello! In my free time, I am an indie developer of android games. At the moment I am trying to promote the game on Google Play. I think there will be those who will be interested in this experience. Perhaps the advice received from you will help me avoid unnecessary expenses.

* The author of the publication does not claim to be a professional in the field of promotion and development. The experiment is conducted to gain experience - positive / sad.

Why do I need it?

I do not develop the game, I can say abandoned. Despite this, it generates $ 3-6 per day. I wanted to run the game for a foreign audience, but the specifics of the game (Guess the song by smiles) does not allow.
