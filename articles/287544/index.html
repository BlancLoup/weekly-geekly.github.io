PC Magazine Magazine Goes Online

Since February, PC Computer Magazine, a well-known computer magazine, has completely ceased publication on paper . Only online edition will remain.

Founded in 1982, PC Magazine was one of the most successful IT publications in the world. One of the indicators of its success is the amount of advertising. So, sometimes it was such a huge amount that the American edition came out on 500 and even 600 pages. However, the time of glory is over, although now the PCMag brand is profitable and generates income of several tens of millions of dollars , taking into account localized versions that have bred around the world, including the Russian PC Magazine / RE (by the way, this magazine, published since 1991, will continue its existence: an exclusive comment by the chief editor Oleg Lebedev (see under the habrakat).

Now almost the entire potential audience of the magazine receives information from the Internet. Even the magazine itself already receives about 70% of the profits from a network of affiliated sites . The continuation of the printing business is clearly unpromising.

The farewell release of American PC Magazine will be published in January. Almost all 140 employees, however, will save work and will work on the online version of PCMag.com .
