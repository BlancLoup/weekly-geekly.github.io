CodeCamp. Developer Section

Developers, do not miss iCamp - the main Internet event of the summer!
On the thematic section of CodeSamp, we will discuss development technologies, languages, frameworks and servers. Let's talk about the technologies that will replace the existing ones and how they will change the society and users.

The program section is formed by the participants themselves iCamp-2008, because they are all considered the organizers! Just register and create an event on the iCamp-2008 website.

Here are some of the upcoming events:

Future Technologies / WEB 3.0

The round table for those who are interested in the future of the Internet, is invited to discuss the most pressing issues:
- how should the search be carried out?
- How should the information be related to each other?
- what should the interface be?
- How much power should PCs and servers need to handle requests?

Useful rallies, HOW-TO

Presentation + Discussion. How to make a meeting enjoyable and useful? A small presentation based on the principles of retrospectives in Scrum. A lot of rallies are tedious, and this must be fought. How? There are several practices, let's discuss :)

3D web & viz

The mini-section is devoted to the three-dimensional Internet and information visualizations, the main topics:
- Browser three-dimensional web projects (based on flash, Java, etc.);
- secondlife and other virtual worlds;
- augmented reality and 3d scanning;
-visualization of information: from mainmap and conceptual - to interactive three-dimensional graphs.

Drupal - create a project and not get bogged down

The Internet is the place where your project is needed yesterday. And the prototype last year.

We really need something that allows us to manage to do Internet projects and not wallow in them for years. We have no extra time!

Drupal is the ideal system for many projects. In our section we want to touch on the problems:
- Creation of information and social services and their prototypes based on Drupal
- Prototyping without a single line of code!
- Drupal scaling and configuration
- Development of the Drupalera movement in Russia

Our audience:
- Web developers and near-project managers of Internet projects
- Potential startups
- Drupal ninja (as guest gurus)
- Translators, fans of PHP, GPL and all interested.

Let's share experiences and promote Drupal in Russia!
