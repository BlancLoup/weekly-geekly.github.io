Video: neural network "repaints" the movie under Van Gogh (Munch, Picasso, Kandinsky, rock art)

Google has already demonstrated how the neural network creates pictures in the style of Van Gogh and Picasso, but this method is not suitable for video: the result of a frame-by-frame change of the film will be difficult to glue. German scientists coped with this problem - their neural network recognizes objects in the frame, remembers them and uses the same style for drawing when they appear again.


Processing frames from the movie "Ice Age" in the style of "Starry Night" by Van Gogh

Neural networks are taught using a large number of images. Each such network consists of a series of layers - from two to 30 - with a different level of abstraction. A photo or drawing for the end result passes through all these layers. In July 2015, Google demonstrated how the neural network creates works of art from random noise.
