HTML 5 picnic



October 11 in Kiev will be HTML5 Picnic. I previously wrote about HTML5 Camp, which was also held in Kiev in the spring. And this is the second event of this kind, organized by Microsoft within the UXNext community.

They promise to meet in the form of a day of selfless immersion in the code and eating barbecue outside the city. The day should pass in discussions of user behavior, design patterns, design of metrics, methods of collecting requirements and talking about the future of web technologies. Admission is free and strictly by registration .

Official hash tag: #UXNext
