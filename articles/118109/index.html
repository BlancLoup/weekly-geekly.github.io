Google changed its mind to delete Google Video content


The week before last, Google sent letters to users of the Google Video service stating that on April 29, the videos on Google Video would become inaccessible for viewing, until May 13 they can be downloaded, and after that they will be completely deleted.

Although the possibility of adding new videos to Google Video was closed back in 2009, the site still contains almost 3 million videos. Many users, in response to a message about the closure of the service, expressed dissatisfaction with the fact that Google did not make it easy to transfer the video to YouTube.

Now Google Video users can be sure that they will not lose their videos - the deadline of April 29 is canceled . Google will work to automatically transfer Google Video content to YouTube. At the same time, a video posted on Google Video will remain available and existing links will still work.

In addition, users can transfer videos to YouTube on their own, using the corresponding new feature on the page with their uploaded videos . If users prefer to just download their videos from Google Video, then this feature is still available.
