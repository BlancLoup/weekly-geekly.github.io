Google Mobile App for iPhone / iPod touch released

The day before the start of sales of the iPhone 3G, the search giant released its first application for this platform - the Google Mobile App. As they say in the official blog of Google Mobile , the new application combines all the power of Google services, while having a simple and convenient interface.



The Google Mobile App has an input string that allows you to search the web and in contacts. As you type, the application helps the user by guessing the typed text and offering suitable web pages or contacts. And also have the opportunity to quickly get to earlier search results.

In addition, the Google Mobile App eventually remembers the most frequent user requests and displays them above the rest. The application also supports the My Location function to determine the current location and search for nearby cafes, pizzerias, and the like.



You can install the Google Mobile App by clicking on the App Store icon on your iPhone, or through iTunes, by searching for 'Google Mobile'. The App Store will be available on the iPhone only after upgrading to the latest firmware version. In addition, the application is available so far only for US residents, and other countries will add in the near future.
