Ultra-compact laptops have been doing for a very long time, since the 80s. During this time, there was a long way from monochrome typewriters with a full replacement for a desktop PC. Over the years, the concepts of super-compactness and performance also changed, but such devices never lose their charm.
By the end of the 90s, manufacturers managed to form an image of a universal mobile computer and began their mass production. Laptops of the VAIO C1 series, launched in 1998, were among the first ones that were closest in concept to modern counterparts. About one of these subnotebooks and will be discussed in the article. Under the cut a lot of pictures.
