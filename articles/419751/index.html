That ended another year. Many innovations have appeared in the design world. But what awaits us in the future? The team of the online magazine Mindsparkle Mag is closely following the news in the design world and publishes the most interesting things on its blog every day. After analyzing the information for 2017, Mindsparkle Mag specialists tried to predict how the design sphere will develop in the future. Without long introductions, we present the result of their work - the 20 most likely trends in the field of design in 2018.

Over the past two years, more and more projects in the field of 3D-design began to appear. Watching the success of leading 3D artists, for example, Peter Tarka and the guys from MVSM studio, we can safely assume that in 2018 3D still lifes will become even more popular. This trend is particularly evident in the collaborations of various companies for the creation of logos and in product placement.
