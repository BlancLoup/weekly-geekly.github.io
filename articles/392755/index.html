Roskomnadzor blocked Noname Club

Now in the Russian Federation for life the three most popular torrent trackers in the country are blocked.

One of the most popular torrent trackers in Russia today was blocked by Roskomnadzor. We are talking about Noname Club, and the reason for blocking is a violation of the rights to the films of Mosfilm, writes RBC. It is interesting that two decisions on the security lock of this resource were made by the Moscow City Court back in 2013 . At the same time, the site agreed with Roskomnadzor to remove prohibited resources from the registry in exchange for the removal of pirated distributions.

Now Noname Club was blocked by the decision of the same Moscow City Court. The case was initiated by Mosfilm, complaining about the placement of such films as Afonya, White Sun of the Desert, Two Captains, The Munchhausen, Seven Old Men and One Girl, Little Tragedies, "Viy", "Effigy" and "Departure is delayed." Separately, it can be noted that all these films are available for free viewing on the Mosfilm online platforms; they are also available for free on many legal online cinemas.
