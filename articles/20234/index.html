Issuu: the first normal program to publish PDF magazines

Danish startup Issuu has released an interesting program for displaying PDF files on the Internet so that they can be viewed as closely as possible through the process of paging through a paper magazine or book. According to TechCrunch , this is the best program of its kind. And indeed, tools for viewing PDF files online are a dime a dozen , but until now there has not been a truly intuitive and beautiful "reader" of magazines and books.

The Issuu program does exactly that. You upload a PDF file to their website, enter the file name and its description, as well as your e-mail, where the link will come after the conversion process is completed. Under this link you already see your finished document. Of course, a widget for viewing a book or magazine can be easily implemented on your blog or corporate website.



Zoom with one click of the mouse, moving the page around the screen without any clicks at all, just by moving the cursor (in full screen mode). Probably easier and more convenient to do is impossible.
