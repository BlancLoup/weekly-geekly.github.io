Comic Contest: Dream Society. What is it?



In the modern world, social networks, LiveJournal, twitter and other means for communicating with people have become very important. Previously, there were no such advantages, and accordingly there were no problems associated with them.

Undoubtedly, all these services help us quickly communicate with people, be in the know and so on. But there is another side to the question. Didn't follow a friend on twitter? Consider it the beginning of the end of your friendship. Put the status of how the client insulted you at work? Do not avoid a serious conversation with the chef. Noticed the problem in the city / country? Rather, write about this in the LJ to the President of Russia.
Yes, the era of digital innovation gave us a lot of opportunities, but in it we became hostages of the technological achievements of civilization.

How has our world and communication with society changed? What will it be in 10-20 years? How will people communicate in the future? Maybe all the thoughts and conversations of people will be merged into a single mind? What remains of the current social order?

In general, there are a lot of questions, but we offer you the answer. (Details of the contest under the cut)

As everyone probably already guessed, the theme of this contest is the Society of Dreams .



For this contest, we give you the tool of the future - Comic Editor 2.0
Since communication is increasingly taking place in a digital environment, then entries should also look as futuristic as possible. You have three frames, a certain number of words (if you use words), and a huge number of objects. Using various objects and backgrounds (or not using them at all - this is not excluded), try to tell us about the Society of the Future.

Those. Your entry is a fun, interesting, possibly parodic, mind-stirring comic made in the Editor.

The deadline is August 2, 2010 23:59 Moscow time. More detailed details

By the way, we already announced Comic Editor 2.0, but the comics were minted, so now we have decided to offer you to come up with your own. And for the best and give prizes more. May wits and interesting ideas win!

There are no clear rules to win the competition, but we advise you to adhere to some criteria:
- The basis of any comic should be primarily an idea, not your ability to build graphic objects.

- We will pay great attention to the works, to the world of which you immediately want to move.

- The number of works from one author is not limited, but if you have drawn a long comic in fifteen parts with one idea throughout all releases, only one will be evaluated (the best in our opinion).

- The dream society may not seem perfect, but it should look logical


I hope you will like it. Good luck! Draw. And of course, post comics in the comments.
