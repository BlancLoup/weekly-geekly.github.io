Photoshop CC hacked in just one day



Yes, some programs are hacked very quickly, no matter how reliable the developers themselves consider the protection of their software. For example, only lazy did not speak about the security of the new Photoshop CC. And so, it took only a couple of days, and quite a working pirated copy has already appeared on torrents. It should be noted that a multilingual version has appeared on the Web, with a Russian and Ukrainian interface.

As far as is known, Photoshop CC, when installed, requires a connection to the Web to verify the license. If the check is passed, and everything is fine, connection to the network will be necessary only after 30 days. The hackers somehow managed to bypass the Photoshop CC protection system, so the pirate works and is quite normal.

After the announcement of the program, it was believed that hacking it is unlikely to come out, because a connection to the Adobe cloud is required. Of course, since Adobe Photoshop is one of the most popular programs in the world, many hack teams are working on its hacking, among which there are high-class specialists.
