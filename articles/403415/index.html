Do not call me



It seems we almost beat SMS spam. It's time to fight advertising calls! The solution should be simpler than RosSpam, we decided.

The application RosSpam for complaints was a good solution, but:

  1. Not everyone wanted to specify in the application their personal data (although they are nowhere, except for FAS and were not transmitted);
  2. For many, a complaint is not the best way to resolve the issue.

Of course, the application recently we added the ability to complain about advertising calls. But there is a better solution.

In Western countries for quite a long time there are registries of those who do not want to receive advertising calls - the so-called Do Not Call Registry .

It is quite simple: you do not want to receive promotional calls - add your number to the registry. No personal data such as your full name is needed - just your phone number. The one who makes advertising calls must exclude phones from the registry from their call lists.

But we have the Federal Law on Advertising , Article 18 of which prohibits both the sending of SMS spam and the implementation of promotional calls. For SMS-spam is punished with a fine from 100 to 500 thousand rubles (see examples in the RosSpam twitter ). For calls - a similar punishment.
