The fourth galaxy is here

Good afternoon, Habr!

Two weeks ago, Samsung unveiled its new flagship smartphone GALAXY S4. This event was preceded by a large-scale advertising campaign aimed at ensuring that everyone and everyone knew about the announcement of the novelty, except perhaps for the dead of the lazy. Many assumptions and speculations, rumors and real leaks about the gadget passed through the media over the past six months, reaching a peak in the last couple of days before the announcement. And, finally, on March 17, the presentation with fanfare and fanfare took place at Radio City Hall, which in New York was a grandiose show with actors, orchestra, surprises, and large-scale visual effects. In general, everything is as it should be.

It should be recognized that we were somewhat late with the announcement of the new flagship on Habr√©, but, to date, the network still does not have a single detailed review of the device (not counting the blitz reviews from the exhibition itself). Now, when, finally, all passions have subsided, the excitement and all announcements have died down, we would like to soberly and balancedly describe the new smartphone, as well as draw parallels, track the continuity and compare the capabilities of Samsung's predecessor GALAXY S III and its follower - GALAXY S4.



‚ñå DESIGN


Samsung GALAXY S3 was so successful in terms of the appearance of the device that Samsung decided not to deviate much from the given conceptual direction, while working on the design of the GALAXY S4. Any user will know the rounded edges of his body and the lone ‚ÄúHome‚Äù button at the bottom of the smartphone instantly.



The texture has changed a bit, the edge around the screen has diminished, the edges are decorated a little differently ... There is nothing more to say here. ;) On the screen we will stop separately and in more detail. The 3rd generation of the Galaxy possessed a glossy plastic casing, and in the 4th it was replaced by textured polycarbonate.

‚ñå DIMENSIONS





One of the key points in the novelty is the size of the device, which not only did not increase (despite the fact that the iron in the smartphone is much more powerful), but on the contrary: the case became thinner by almost a millimeter (it was 8.6 mm, it became 7.9 mm) and lighter (it was 133 g, it became 130 g) than its predecessor.

‚ñå SCREEN





As for the screen, then Samsung has fully met the expectations of the audience: GALAXY S4 has a 5-inch Super AMOLED display with a resolution of Full HD (1080x1920). GALAXY S3 could boast of a 4.8 inch Super AMOLED screen with a resolution of HD (720x1280). The density of the screen of the new flagship is also impressive - 441ppi. The frame around the screen has become even thinner - and this is noticeable even with the naked eye. Other advantages: black-black color (no glow in the dark), maximum viewing angles, very bright and juicy colors. At the same time, this screen is 25% more economical than conventional Super AMOLED matrices. It is also worth noting that GALAXY S4 was the first to acquire a new protective coating for the screen from Corning - Gorilla Glass 3. The main advantage of the updated version is the improvements at the molecular level, which are called Native Damage Resistance (NDR). Compared to its predecessor, the latest version of Gorilla Glass managed to achieve a three-fold increase in protection against external influences, but even if a scratch does appear, it will be 40% less noticeable. Also, Gorilla Glass 3 is two times thinner than its previous version. Well, another no less pleasant "bun" is the ability to work with the screen in gloves.

‚ñå CAMERAS





Cameras are an important and integral part of any modern smartphone: many, many users of mobile devices prefer their individual compact cameras. Samsung GALAXY S4 will please the main 13-megapixel camera, complete with autofocus and LED flash. On the front is a 2 megapixel camera. Both cameras have the function of recording Full HD-video at a frequency of 30 frames per second. GALAXY S3 was equipped with weaker cameras: 8-megapixel rear and 1.9-megapixel front.

‚ñå PROCESSOR





The international version of the Samsung GALAXY S4 will be equipped with the Samsung Exynos 5 Octa 5410 processor, which integrates four Cortex-A15 cores with a clock frequency of 1.6 GHz, four energy-efficient Cortex-A7 cores with a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz, and One of the best graphics accelerators on the market for mobile devices is PowerVR SGX 544MP3. For some markets, a different smartphone version will be released, built on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 quad-core chip with a clock frequency of 1.9 GHz and Adreno 320 graphics. The S3 predecessor "thought" with a 1.4-GHz quad-core Exynos 4412 processor.

‚ñå MAIN TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS





The new smartphone has onboard 2 GB of RAM, from 16 to 64 GB of flash memory and a slot for microSD cards, Wi-Fi 802.11 a / b / g / n / ac, NFC and Bluetooth 4.0, GSM, HSPA + and LTE (all frequency), GPS / GLONASS. The battery capacity is 2600 mAh, which is 500mAh more than it was in the GALAXY S3. This resource should be enough for an enlarged screen, a powerful processor and a more voracious 4G for a day or more. The battery itself is removable, so you can carry a spare with you if you are thinking of traveling for a long time. In addition to the usual accelerometer, light and proximity sensors, compass and gyroscope, Samsung has equipped its new flagship with a variety of different sensors, including an atmospheric pressure, temperature and humidity sensor. It is also worth noting the infrared port that turns the smartphone into a universal remote control and the ability to connect external displays via MHL 2.0.
