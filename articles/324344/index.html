3CX PBX Express - own cloud PBX in 5 minutes


This week we presented a new service PBX Express !


PBX Express is the 3CX PBX Configuration Wizard hosted on our web site. It allows you to create PBXs that are ready for work in 8 simple steps and automatically place them on OVH hosting, Amazon , Google or another cloud service with support for Openstack VPS technology.


Our new service will be especially interesting for small companies, users who want to test 3CX and IT integrators.


Small companies get a fully functional PBX in the cloud in minutes. In this case, you yourself choose hosting, SIP operators, IP phones, VoIP gateways, in a word, you own your PBX. This is fundamentally different from the usual offers of "cloud" PBX, where you, in fact, use only the service for the selected tariff plan, but can not affect either the stability of the service, or the safety of their own data. Imagine that instead of placing a PBX in your own office, you place it in Google or OVH. Only the location changes, but all possibilities remain: 3CX clients for computers and smartphones, presence indication, chat, video conferencing for 5 participants, 8 simultaneous conversations and an unlimited number of extensions (this is a free version of 3CX). By the way, hosting PBX on OVH costs only 3 Euro per month !


If you want to test 3CX, you do not even need to pay for hosting. We provide you with free hosting from 3CX from the selected provider for 7 days. This time is enough to explore the possibilities of PBX and check the quality of communication. Then you can make a backup and transfer it to your own hosting or to a local server.



If you are an IT integrator or 3CX partner, use PBX Express to install a PBX for a client in a few minutes - without investing in your own hardware and hosting. Focus on the sale and maintenance of PBX, VoIP equipment and related services. And an international operator will take care of a reliable hosting! It is worth noting that you, as an integrator, retain control over the client‚Äôs system, and do not act as an intermediary between the client and any ‚Äúcloudy‚Äù PBX. If the intermediary can be abandoned at any time, then the integrator, who really manages the communication system, works with the client on a completely different level. You just need to create your own account on the hosting OVH, Amazon, Google, etc. and create a PBX for customers under this account. Of course, you can transfer a client's PBX from a local server to hosting or vice versa, start with hosting, and then transfer 3CX to a local client server (or on your own hosting if the integrator supports Openstack technology). When setting up a PBX in the PBX Express service, specify your reseller ID (Partner ID) , and this 3CX installation will be automatically associated with your reseller account. This will not only simplify the accrual of partner points, but will also secure the user (lead). It also ensures that the lead will not be transferred to another partner.


The PBX Express service guarantees the quality and stability of the service, a competitive price and the widest possible settings. They are provided by the largest international cloud operators, the 3CX brand and a wide network of IT integrators .
