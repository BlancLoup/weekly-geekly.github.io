Tax officers did not understand Lotus Notes

The competition of the Federal Tax Service (FTS) for the purchase of antivirus software was held in violation of the law. This conclusion was reached by the Commission of the Federal Antimonopoly Service (FAS) , considering the complaint of one of the participants of this competition - Leta-IT Company , reports the Prime-Tass agency . This company offered the lowest cost of the contract - 47 million rubles, but was disqualified by the competition commission. As a result, Systematica won, with a proposed contract price of 58 million rubles. The third place in the competition was taken by Kaspersky Lab .
