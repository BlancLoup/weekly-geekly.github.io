A set of converters for Livestreet (from CMS Cogear, DLE and WordPress)

A set are three plugins:
  • Cogear Converter
  • Converter from DLE
  • WP Converter

Plugins will allow you to quickly transfer the site to LiveStreet from CMS Cogear, DLE and WordPress (yes, so far only three, but not all at once), if your site is not overgrown with modifications and various plugins. And in the case of a sufficiently developed site with a large set of different extensions, the use of the converter will save time on developing the converter for you.


Migrated:
- users (additionally created personal blogs);
- topics;
- comments;
- tags;
- static pages (with activated LS Static Page plugin);
- images of avatars of profiles, images in the topics and comments

It is possible to transfer both from the same database (the tables of two CMS are in the same database) and from different (the tables of both CMS are in different databases but from the same user).
After the transfer, on the new site, users will have to do password recovery.

The converter launch page (regardless of the CMS source) is located at _ your_sayt / conv

Converters tested in working with versions LiveStreet 0.5.1 and 1.0 and the latest releases of CMS Cogear, DLE, WordPress

Plans:
- converters from other cms;
- make the transfer of private messages;
- make a mailing of new passwords.
