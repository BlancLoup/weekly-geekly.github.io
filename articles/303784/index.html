What digital strategies are effective? Why use personalization in online advertising? How to act in new circumstances to keep your business afloat and continue the development of the company? The main trends of the IT sphere, according to Alexey, are deep learning and neural networks of deep learning, which are now represented everywhere:

Andrey Mayboroda , sales organization expert, director of the Green Bussiness company (St. Petersburg), talked about how to build effective sales with a human face for IT companies. In his opinion, the future lies in the maximum rejection of all kinds of manipulative techniques. When selling IT products, it is important to help the client solve his problem. He also mentioned the importance of social networks:
This is a smart tool for sales, it simplifies our life: it becomes easier to find a person, to enter into direct contact with him, to have a direct dialogue - and this is the beginning of sales.

Also, modern IT companies need to show "flexibility" in service and be competent in their field in order to find concrete solutions for businesses, without leaving customers at the crossroads of a choice.
