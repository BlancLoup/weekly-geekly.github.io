Logorama (best short animated film)

Recently, the 82nd ceremony of the well-known Oscar. You have probably already seen all the award-winning films. Does the short cartoon "Logorama" tell you about anything? I did not talk about anything until a friend told me.



About the cartoon


"Logoram" was drawn by the French studio H5, specializing mainly in video clips, and represents a kind of civilization where the characters live, exclusively from advertising labels and logos (they are said to be 2500). They personify the current society with violence and crime, many cartoon characters are consistent with advertising.

The clown Ronald MacDonald in the cartoon is represented by a terrorist, animals from different logos sit in the zoo. It seems that the cartoon carries in itself a hidden meaning about the corruption warriors of advertising campaigns.

The cartoon won an Oscar, and also won the prestigious Kodak Discovery Award at the Cannes Film Festival.
