Android "domesticate"

MIPS Technologies and Sigma Designs are working on adapting the Android OS for use in consumer electronics. The first steps have already been taken - on the Digitimes Tech Forum in Taipei, a demonstration of new OS features was successfully held, in particular, support for video in Full HD 1080p resolution.

In addition, AllGo Systems demonstrated a media player that runs on the Android OS.

What can I say, given the other segments of the market, the transition (or, at least, plans for the transition) to the operating system from Google begins to take on a massive scale.
