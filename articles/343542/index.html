Event digest for HR specialists in the IT field for December 2017



Even when everyone is preparing for the New Year, HR specialists continue to receive new knowledge, share experiences, and sum up the year. There will be few events in December, but they are worth it to find time to visit them in the pre-holiday rush.


HR TECH WORLD 2017 & HR Russia 2018


When: December 1, 10:00
Where: Moscow, Leningrad Highway, 39A c2, Kaspersky Lab
Conditions of participation: ticket price 1 490 rubles
Organizer: Atsearch Crowd Recruitment

The program of the event:
  • Seven mistakes when starting on-line training. Oksana Kukharchuk, Head of MTS Corporate University, Speaker from Russia at HR TECH WORLD 2017
  • How to talk to the business in terms of numbers. Elena Morozova, HR Dashboard partner
  • Vera, a robot recruiter. Alexander Uraksin, co-founder and commercial director of Stafory
  • Panel discussion: HR Tech World Amsterdam 2017. What changes are waiting for HR in 2018. Trends, technology, people.

Intensive IT recruitment (webinar)


When: December 13 and 15, 13:00
Where: webinar
Conditions of participation: ticket price 3 000 rubles (2 500 rubles until December 11)
