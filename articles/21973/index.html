Help with gift selection

After all the winter and spring holidays, I thought: how much time does it take to choose gifts: parents, girlfriend, friends, and sometimes almost strangers. And in 90% of cases, the preferences of man are little known.

Therefore, invented a service to search for gifts . Can:

1. write what kind of gift you would like to yourself (this is to fill the base),
2. search for a gift on the basis of the collected,
3. find out what gift the person you are interested in is getting.

I understand that the result of the second point depends on the first.
