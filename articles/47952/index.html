Packard bell

Immediately decided to write a little about the company, as in Russia it is little known. Packard Bell is currently a subsidiary of Acer. The company itself was founded in 1926 as a manufacturer of radio equipment. In 1978 was acquired by Teledyne. In 1986, Packard Bell began producing computers. The company has pretty well advanced in this direction in the United States, has become one of the leaders of the European market. In 2008 was purchased by Acer. By the way, the first coming of the company to Russia was before the 1998 crisis.
