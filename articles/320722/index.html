Creating a quest tour of Berlin - step by step instructions

Berlin is an incredibly beautiful city. After World War II, the city lay in ruins at ground level. Modern Berlin rebuilt, so lovers of ancient buildings there may not like it, as, for example, in Prague. But for those who appreciate the cleanliness, the German style and the bustle of the big city in Berlin should be enjoyed.

How to make so that your trip was not muddled? First, think about what places you would like to visit. Use our designer Surprise Me, do your own quests, quests and go to conquer the new city!

In the constructor, you can set the time or place to start the tasks, provide the route with additional information about streets, places of interest and much more with the help of photo and audio files. Thus, the application will become your information partner in the walk.

For example, we at Surprise Me offer you this route:



Start from Alexanderplatz Square, one of the central places of Berlin. There are various festivals, shows, and just going to young people who play music or just dancing to music. Near the square is a huge mall "Alex", in the galleries of which you can buy anything.

Alexanderplatz is a very social place. Here you can see a variety of people: from urban madmen to fashionable artists and sellers of shaverma, which is called doner here. This is the square in its old sense - a place where people meet, walk and do everything they want.


Alexanderplatz

After Alexanderplatz heading for the Berlin TV tower. The structure is very similar to Chupa-Chups or an olive impaled on a skewer. If you have a desire, then go up to the observation deck, it has a spherical shape, so you may experience a slight panic from the fact that under you almost 400 meters of emptiness. But these are Germans, these guys are safe. From 368 meters you will get a gorgeous view of Berlin. We recommend climbing the tower in the evening when the capital of Germany begins to glow with all its lights.



Not far from the TV tower is the church of St. Mary. As already mentioned, Berlin was almost completely destroyed during the Second World War. This small church is the little that remains of the old city.


Kirche St. Mary

Next to the church built a chic fountain "Neptune". Seals, tridents, women with jugs - this is all in the composition of the fountain.


Fountain "Neptune"
