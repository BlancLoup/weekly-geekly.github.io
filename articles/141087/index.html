This year we have worked not only on the technical stuff, but also on the design.

Our LG Cinema 3D TVs have become bestsellers, and it is thanks to them that we:
  • became global # 2 on the flat-panel TV market, displacing two competitors in 2011;
  • strengthened as # 2 on the Russian market, for the first time in 3 years we grew not due to the fact that we were pushing out European and Japanese brands, but because we were squeezing our competitor friend;
  • We became No. 1 in sales of 3D TVs in several world markets (we are especially strong in South America, and from countries closer to us, we became No. 1 in Kazakhstan).


In 2011, everything was great - our 3D-TVs received the highest marks from professionals, we won litigation and proved that our TVs give Full HD images in any mode, both in 2D and in 3D.

The redesign touched not only TVs, but also 3D glasses. They are still easy, inexpensive, and most importantly, extremely comfortable, even when watching the longest films.

Design points for every taste:


If it speaks of technical innovations, then the 2012 LG Cinema3D is:



Brighter colors, both in 2D and in 3D, thanks to FPR technology, as well as Dynamic Color Enhancer technology, which calibrates and adjusts color reproduction in dynamics without external intervention.


Unprecedented image contrast, thanks to the uniform LED system and the Local Contrast Plus system, which programmatically enhances the brightness of LED diodes in places where it is needed (the technology is available on flagship models).



The Resolution Upscaler feature is a software option to increase video resolution to HD, even for content shot at a lower resolution.



Comfortable, light, inexpensive glasses that do not require recharging, do not cause headaches and eye fatigue. 3D image without flicker, split and headache.



High-quality conversion of 2D content into a 3D image.



Advanced 3D image management functionality. You can independently configure which type of 3D image is preferable - a deep picture or 3D "off-screen". All in your hands!



Unique LG Dual Play functionality. Special glasses allow you to play on the console on one TV, but at the same time each of the players will see their playing field in full screen. Ideal for racing and sports!

Remote control Magic Motion with motion sensors, working like a mouse. In 2012, we improved the ergonomics of the console and added voice control, including in Russian.
