Introducing Google Drive. Yes, yes it is true!

Sundar Pichai, Senior Vice President, Products (Chrome and Applications)

Today, we introduce Google Drive - a single space for storing your files and working with them. It allows you to work on documents simultaneously with other users - for example, prepare a joint project with a colleague, plan any events or keep records of expenses with partners. With the help of this service you can upload to the cloud and have constant access to any files, including videos, photos, PDF, text documents and many others - a total of 30 types.



Google Drive will allow you to:
  • Create files and collaborate on them. Now Google Docs has become part of Google Drive. Together with other users, you can edit documents, presentations and spreadsheets in real time. In addition, you can now leave comments and respond to them in files of any type (PDF, images, video, etc.). If other users comment on a shared document, you will receive a notification about it.
  • Protect your files and provide access to them from anywhere, including from a mobile device. All the necessary files of any format will always be at your fingertips - at home, in the office or on vacation, no matter what device you work from. Google Drive can be installed on a computer (PC and Mac), and for Android mobile devices you can download the Google Drive application. We are also working on an app for iOS devices. Visually impaired and blind users can work with Google Drive with the help of a special program that converts text to speech, or a screen reader.
  • Easily find the files you need . You can search by keywords and filter results by file type, owner, and other criteria.
