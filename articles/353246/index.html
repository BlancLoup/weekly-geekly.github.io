We invite you to a lecture evening on game design on April 18th at VSBI

On April 18, several lectures on game design will be held at the HSE Graduate School of Business Informatics in Moscow. Beginning of lectures from 19:00, gathering guests from 18:30.
Both the VSHB teachers and invited experts will be speaking, who will share their experience and knowledge on game design with the guests of the event.

Venue: st. Trifonovskaya, d.57, p. 1 (metro station Rizhskaya).

Admission is free, but by appointment. You can register on the event page . Under the cut details about the lecture evening itself.



How to get to work as a game designer

Alexey Trushkov, director of the Moscow office of Playrix, brand evangelist.

Alexey will tell you what game designers are, how they become and what you need to know, be able to and understand, in order to get a job as a game designer.

Profession game design: math and art

Sergey Gimelreich, game mechanic teacher at VSBI, game designer, game producer.

Empirics and mathematics perfectly coexist in games, creating bizarre forms of interactive interaction between the player and the gaming environment in the frame of dramatic stories, sensory experiences and new experiences. But what is primary all the same - Mathematics or Art in the context of the game designer's work on the game world? We will look at these and other issues that reveal the limits of the game designer profession in a new lecture by Sergey.

The event will be interesting:

  • Game developers who want to understand how to create their own successful game project.
  • People who are interested in games and are considering the opportunity to start working in the gaming industry.
  • Game designers and producers aimed at systematizing their own knowledge and studying new approaches to game development methodology.
