Features of promotion: how we built a partner channel. Errors and solutions



In this article we will talk about the difficulties and advantages of attracting customers in our field, what kind of promotion strategy we built in the end, what pitfalls we stumbled upon and what benefits marketing agencies and studios will get from this.

So that you understand our specifics, a couple of words about us:

We have a rather difficult product for the Russian Internet marketing sphere - the Carrot Quest service. In fact, this is a universal service for internet marketing, which combines 6 important tools:

  • Event Analytics. Collection of key information about each visitor on the site (his actions, sources of transition, contact information, etc.)
  • Sending automatic messages (chat, pop-ups, letters, webhook-i, js-scripts, calls, SMS);
  • Smart online chat, which is based on a modern model of communication in instant messengers ;
  • Email newsletter. Both manual and automatic with complex scenarios and various triggers;
  • Pop-up windows that are very finely tuned for each user and are shown when it is relevant for him;
  • Creating funnels for any user actions.





We are faced with the fact that users do not quite understand the possibilities of combining all these tools, because previously not encountered. Yes, some segments represent separate advantages, but in general there is no product.

Therefore, we can say that we are creating a new market - a market for such solutions in Russia. At the first stage of promotion, we did not use paid advertising at all, we simply created useful educational materials (articles, cases, interviews, instructions) that would show new opportunities in the market and the huge benefits that companies can get from the service in the context of modern Internet. marketing. Users read and come themselves.



In such training materials we talked about different solutions in the service:
  • Improving the efficiency of the site;
  • Increase first and repeat sales;
  • About the new model of user and customer support;
  • Hold and return users;
  • Personal work with leads and customers, their step-by-step heating and bringing to purchase;
  • Automate Internet marketing;
  • Increased customer loyalty, LTV and average bill;
  • Refusal of a zoo of services and association of all tools in one place for the sake of optimization of processes of work;
  • And etc.


The main problem:


The approach with the training materials gave a good effect, but our clients (whatever they were specialists in their companies) are mostly very conservative (or are loaded with other tasks) - they expect ready-made solutions from the service, so that without any efforts - ‚ÄúI installed the code - I got the benefit‚Äù, but in this case it does not happen.

There are two options:
  • the company has a project team that implements such solutions;
  • There are several marketers in the company who are already deep in their work and this additional task seems too heavy for them, which is why they are not activated and do not understand the service.

Guess which companies more?


Decision:


We began to work on the affiliate channel so that through marketing agencies we can go to companies that do not have their own marketing project teams, and the marketers themselves are busy doing other things.

That Carrot Quest is great for sale and companies are ready to use it with pleasure, but they need help in setting it up, we understood a long time ago, but ...

It turned out that similar tools are necessary for marketing agencies themselves . Not only because they will receive additional income by offering services to their own clients, but in general, the use of the service improved customer performance because of what he trusts the agency and orders more services.

Why? Let's figure it out.

It would seem that the majority of marketing agencies concentrate on a single direction of work (event-events, seo and contextual advertising, website development, etc.) and, logically, this should be their advantage.

In general, this is the case, when you focus on a single task, you rapidly develop in this direction and give the maximum result, and if you offer all kinds of services, the focus is blurred and the result leaves much to be desired.


But there is one important nuance in the work of agencies and studios.


Client companies evaluate your efficiency not only for the work you have done, but also with an eye on your main indicator - sales. You can very well set up contextual advertising and make an effective website, but poor processing of incoming leads and leading them to the purchase of a company at the crossroads of all your merits. It is unlikely that such a client will contact you again or will appreciate your work and will pay more. And all the profit of marketing agencies is often based on a long-term relationship with a client and a high LTV.

Today there is one such problematic place for which the agency never has enough time, but with an emphasis on which, they can easily raise the main results of the client, without investing much time and effort into it. Also, receive additional payments for this. What is it about? On increasing the main indicator of the client - sales. All the solutions in the Carrot Quest, which we have listed a little higher, are ultimately aimed at this indicator.

Quite a common situation: if using different tools to increase the conversion in the purchase in the company by only 0.5-1%, then the number of sales of the client increases by 2 times, and the profit in 3-4. Unfortunately, not everyone sees these bottlenecks. How do you think, how will the attitude of your client towards you change, if you raise his profit by 3-4 times, but do not spend a lot of time on this? Your main work in this case becomes many times more valuable for the client.

Results and first mistakes:
