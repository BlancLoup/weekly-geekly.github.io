The world's most popular fitness bracelet Xiaomi Mi Band will change the model with the display?



Last week, the company Xiaomi published the results of sales of the Xiaomi Mi Band fitness bracelet. It turned out that now this device, priced at only about $ 15 (in China), is the most sold device of this type in the world. At the moment , more than 6 million devices have already been sold .

The popularity of the device is easy to explain - the Mi Band is built very qualitatively, much more can be done, and even more expensive models (for example, a smart alarm clock) are capable of, plus it costs, by the standards of the wearable device market, a penny. Now the developer plans to release the second generation of bracelets.

Earlier, information appeared on the Web that the device would be equipped with an NFC chip, which would bring a social component to the bracelet owners (if they touch, the devices will exchange information). In addition, it is possible that the Xiaomi Mi Band 2 will display.

In any case, a render appeared on Weibo (unfortunately, so far only the render) Xiaomi Mi Band 2. If this is indeed the case, the capabilities of the Xiaomi Mi Band 2 are significantly expanded - the device can already be used almost autonomously, without the need to pair with a smartphone. Perhaps the developers will teach the gadget to respond to notifications coming to the smartphone (and the first model of the bracelet can also vibrate, blinking with an LED in response to an incoming phone call).



If the developers are able to keep the price the same (or at least raise it not too much), the success of this device can be considered predetermined. Many users (including me) are waiting for the release of the second version of the bracelet from Xiaomi with impatience.
