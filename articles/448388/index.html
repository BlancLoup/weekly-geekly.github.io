Digital Dictation will be held in Russia for the first time



The main goal of the joint initiative of ROCIT, RAEC and Microsoft is to give everyone the opportunity to test their digital literacy.


From 18 to 24 May, the Digital Dictation will be held for the first time, which will be the most extensive test of the knowledge of information technology in Russia. During this week, each user will be able to test their computer skills and other devices, as well as knowledge of network security rules and communication culture. Digital Dictation was organized by ROCIT, RAEC and Microsoft with the support of Avito, the Internet Coordination Center, the Russian Popular Front, HP Inc., Rossiyskaya Gazeta, and the Rambler Group. The number of participants, according to preliminary estimates, will be about 50 thousand people.

This article is in our blog .

On the first day, May 18, events in support of Digital Dictation will be held in many cities of Russia. Those who do not have the opportunity to visit them will be able to take the test during the week on the website of digital dictation . RF , as well as in a special application. The dictation consists of 30 questions designed for different age categories: for children (14-17 years old) and for adults (18+), and divided into 3 semantic blocks. The first block is devoted to the basics of computer literacy, namely, various devices and knowledge of basic programs. The second is working with the Internet, social networks and online applications. The third is cybersecurity, including the protection of personal data.
