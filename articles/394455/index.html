In the California mall began to watch the robot police



In the state of California, autonomous police robots took over law enforcement . Robots patrol shopping malls and other public places. They can not yet prevent crimes or detain criminals on their own, but they hang around the perimeter tirelessly and can capture an attempt to commit a crime or identify suspicious activity.

The creators of the robot from the company Knightscope say that the terrible tragedy at Sandy Hook school inspired them to develop this device when children were killed as a result of the shooting. Later, the investigation found that, if the police arrived just a minute earlier, they could save 12 lives.

For the first time about the robots from Knightscope became known in 2014 . The first tests were scheduled for 2015, but the development was a bit delayed. So far, robots are being run in real conditions.
