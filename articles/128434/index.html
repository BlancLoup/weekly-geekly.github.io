The government loved Wi-Fi. Gorky Park, metro, universities. Calculating the cost of the network at the university

Our country is amazing. Then we declare that we will make an incredible technological breakthrough with the help of nanotechnology or Skolkovo, then we rediscover the long-running technologies.
So, recently, Mayor Sobyanin wanted Wi-Fi at metro stations and in the park , the President wanted it at universities . It seems that the interest of officials is a sign that the technology has taken place.

12 years after the appearance of the first standard, Wi-Fi began to be taken for granted, and it is logical that it should appear on the bus, in the metro, and in the institutes. In order not to be unfounded, I will give a typical example of a project, of which our partners have already made hundreds. The building (institute, office, hotel - there is no difference from the point of view of radio coverage). 100-150 employees (residents, students) per floor. Estimate the cost of equipment and materials.

To fully cover the floor of a typical building (without taking into account the load on the network), 4-6 access points are enough. We do not take extreme cases when the building is very large in area (for example, MSU), or compact. For budget coverage you need:
