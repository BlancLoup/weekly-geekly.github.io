Opened the Internet project "Advertising"

WebMaster.SPb, with the informational support of the newspaper Delovoy Peterburg, presents a new Internet project , Advertising Hall - Advertising Store .

The project is a database of advertisements, the use of which makes it possible to track the advertising campaigns of its competitors. It is enough for a visitor to choose the source, category and period of publication of the advertisement, after which the system will present the name of the advertiser company, the release date, module size, publication format, as well as the content of the advertisement and its cost. In addition, the resource provides the ability to calculate the total amount spent on advertising of any company. The creators of the project hope that the site will become a convenient service for compiling reports and planning advertising campaigns.
