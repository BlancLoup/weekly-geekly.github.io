Extenders. Evolution

Sound, image, light, data ... Not always we can reach the right place by standard means or connect the required number of devices to one source.
Tee, extension cable, splitter, hub ... All this appears as soon as a new signal source appears. Round, square, beautiful, reliable, most of the characteristics remain the same, but there are some individuals that evolve in their development. I will tell you about the most interesting of them that I have met.

Immediately I warn you that I will not consider the UPS and network equipment as it is too serious, and I wanted to show the most interesting. We also exclude various wireless solutions from the review, otherwise no one will come to the end.



So:

Nomination "the most quirky."


Sometimes straight and strict lines do not contribute to the successful connection of all necessary devices, and here adaptability becomes an exclusively evolutionary step in development. Meet the Pivot Power




Nomination "the most cunning."


This nomination includes a whole class of devices for transmitting some types of signals via communications, which are not intended for this.
I think it is enough to list the types of such devices:
  • USB Extenders via Ethernet
  • Ethernet "extenders" (no need to find fault with this word in this place, please, because this is the essence of the matter) through the power network
  • PoE, i.e. electrical extension cables via Ethernet

All these devices differ in that they are paired and must be on both sides of the transmission medium.
Whether they are useful - certainly. I happened to try all three types of devices. When performing certain tasks without them just can not do.

Nomination "safest".


Electricity is an extremely dangerous thing, so any attempt to improve safety is great. Well, when it comes to throwing an extension cord into an aquarium and continuing to work, safely connecting and disconnecting electrical appliances, there are no words left ... Impressive.
It is hoped that with time these technologies will become truly universally used.
