Phone with a battery life of - 1 year


According to unconfirmed reports, the Chinese phone LionKing 800 can work without teasing, in standby mode for 365 days and in talk mode for 3-5 days.
The power supply of the phone is a 16.800mAh lithium-ion battery (this is about 2 times more capacious compared to the batteries of modern laptops and about 10 times more than the batteries of modern phones).
The phone works in GSM900 / DCS1800 networks, supports two active SIM cards, a 3.5 inch screen with a resolution of 320x240, Bluetooth 2.0, a slot for microSD cards, two cameras, the size of the phone is 120x65x20mm
