Royal Wii

The Queen of Great Britain received from THQ an unusual gift. This is a game console Nintendo Wii, made of gold. Apparently, the recent news that this console looked to the Queen of England, who with pleasure, despite her age, enjoyed herself for Wii Sports with her royal relatives, was the impetus for such a PR action.

Extravagant gift - an attempt to draw attention to the release of a collection of fun casual entertainment called BIG Family Games from the publisher THQ. Most likely, THQ decided that advertising a collection of family games through the royal family is very justified. And, considering that everyone who is not lazy is writing about this event, the decision was right. And this despite the fact that the present obviously cost a lot of money. Yes, and this console should not be playable - the controller of pure gold (if it is so) weighs very much.
