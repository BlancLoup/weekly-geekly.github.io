Smartphone Meizu MX2 is becoming more accessible!

Dear habrayuzer, today we have two great news at once! The opening of the first Meizu branded sales point in Russia, as well as the price cuts related to this event, for all the company's smartphones, including the Meizu MX2 model!




On July 10, the first Meizu branded sales point in Russia opened in Moscow. It is located in Moscow, in the Gagarinsky shopping center, near Leninsky Prospect metro station, on the first floor, literally 20 meters to the left of the main entrance. Here you can buy all the smartphones of the company, as well as accessories for them, including various covers for the Meizu MX2. The time of the company sales point from 9 to 22 hours, without lunch and weekends.

In honor of the opening, we announce a special offer: a discount on all versions of the Meizu MX2 smartphone! In the mymeizu.ru online store and at the point of sale you can buy the Meizu MX2 at special prices:

  • Meizu MX2 16 GB - 14,990 rubles
  • Meizu MX2 32 GB - 16 990 rubles
  • Meizu MX2 64 GB - 18 990 rubles

Also reduced prices for previous models:
  • Meizu MX 4-core 32 GB - 13,590 rubles
  • Meizu MX 4-core 64 GB - 14,990 rubles
  • Meizu M9 - 7 000 rubles
