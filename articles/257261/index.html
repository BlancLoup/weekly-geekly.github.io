Website under the microscope: a new analysis of search queries

Hi, Habr! Regardless of whether your website sells something, simply informs visitors or is an entertainment resource, information about how users find your website and how it is presented in Google Search can greatly help. Improve search engine optimization, deal with conversion and internal links, evaluate convenience for mobile devices ... in general, there are many uses for such a tool. Previously, such data could be viewed in the Search Queries report, which was one of the most popular features of our Webmaster Tools .



It is clear that there is a heightened demand for such a claimed report. In recent years, we have received many offers from site owners to expand existing capabilities. In general, you asked - we did. Meet: search query analysis - a new report in Webmaster Tools, which allows you to analyze search traffic in even more detail.

What's new?


Analysis of search queries offers you advanced capabilities for analyzing, organizing and displaying your site‚Äôs indicators in Google Search results. Now at your service a thoroughly expanded set of filters for analytics results.

For example, the updated report will allow you to compare mobile traffic before and after April 21 of this year, we changed the algorithm for ranking mobile web pages.



Multilingual: now a tool, not a punishment


In a special category of wishes can be distinguished work with multiple languages. Creators and owners of multilingual sites now have access to flexible configuration and filtering of analytics with geolocation ranking. For example, now you can easily find out which users from which countries are interested in your brand: to do this, select the report by show, apply a filter by the name of the company or product, and then combine the data by country.

Of course, the possibilities of the new report are not limited to these two examples. Analysis of search queries allows you to check the traffic sites in the smallest detail and on the basis of these data to improve the efficiency of your projects.
