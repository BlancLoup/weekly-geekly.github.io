ASUS Eee Pad EP121 Tablet
12 '' - inch tablet with multitouch support, built on the Intel Core 2 Duo CULV processor and running on Windows 7 Home Premium. Unlike the iPad, there is a built-in webcam, handwriting recognition and full support for high-quality video.
There will also be a 10 '' - inch version of the ASUS Eee Pad EP101 (weighing 675 grams and 12.2mm thick) running Windows Embedded Compact 7.
