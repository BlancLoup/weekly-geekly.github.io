Funny pictures from the competition for the logo and the name of the national payment card

The Bank of Russia and the National Payment Card System ( NSPK ) are holding a competition to develop a logo and the name of a bank card, which will have to become a competitor of Visa and Mastercard in Russia. Acceptance of works on the contest website will last until April 30, and a vote will be held from May 11 to May 22. The winner will be announced on May 28, the main prize - 150000 rubles.

All interested persons take part in the competition. At the moment, more than 800 work options have been submitted, which can be viewed on the competition website . Submitted works are moderated. Of all the works, the competent jury will select 20 options, for which visitors to the site will vote from May 11.

The need to introduce a national payment system in Russia started talking back in the early 90s. Then, on the wave of young capitalism, banks began to create "local" payment systems, where only cards of these banks were serviced. All the 2000s were spent in attempts to create a nationwide payment system, but at first this was hampered by the lack of a regulatory framework, and then simply the unwillingness of existing systems to part with their incomes.
