How much do Apple executives get?



Do you wonder how much the current head of Apple earned over the past year?
We all remember the annual salary of Steve Jobs $ 1. Interestingly, what has changed with the change of generation of managers in one of the most marginal companies in Silicon Valley?

Fortunately, it is not difficult to find out, because Apple is a public company, which means it publishes all financial reports that are available to anyone interested. This morning, information on salaries and bonuses was updated.

Tim Cook for 2014 replenished his bank account with $ 9.2 million: $ 1.7 million in direct salary (salary) and $ 6.7 million as non-share revenue (this means that the amount varies depending on which General indicators reaches Apple in one way or another of the parameters of production and sales).
