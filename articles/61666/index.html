Change the background image on the login screen in Windows 7

As previously announced, in the new Windows 7 operating system, you can easily change the background of the login window. You do not need to change the resources in executable files or perform any other special actions. The process of changing the image is quite simple and can be performed even without the involvement of additional tools. This functionality is available and works correctly since build 7057.
