Russia: created mutual fund for investment in online projects

Russian Internet projects have acquired a new source of funding. RAF Group in cooperation with the investment agency StartupIndex announced the creation of a closed- end mutual fund of venture investments, the main task of which will be financial support for Runet startups. Experts are quite pessimistic about the prospects for the initiative.

The management company RAF Group announced the creation of the Aurora Information Technologies mutual investment fund. This mutual fund is closed and will be created within three months - from September 20 to December 20, 2007. The size of the initial issue of the shares should be 26 million rubles, and the cost of one share at the time of placement - 1 thousand rubles. The focus of the fund is the most interesting: the venture fund Aurora - Information Technologies will be focused on financing Runet startups.
