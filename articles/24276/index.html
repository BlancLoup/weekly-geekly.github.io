Easter mac-eggs


The Makovod blog , in my person, heartily congratulates everyone on Christ's Resurrection - the most joyful holiday for Orthodox Christians.

Easter eggs are specially painted eggs that are given to each other for Easter. According to legend, the first Easter egg, Mary Magdalene, was presented to the Roman emperor Tiberius. When Mary came to Tiberius and announced the Resurrection of Christ, the emperor said that it was just as impossible as the fact that the hen's egg would be red, and after these words the hen's egg that he held turned red.

Especially for Easter, I found for you Easter mac-eggs, which you can, following the tradition, give to each other :)

Happy holiday!
