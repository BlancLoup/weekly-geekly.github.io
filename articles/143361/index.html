New fraud threatens Facebook users

Of course, you are smart, reasonable, but many foreign users can get caught. The attackers adopted the model, the infamous participants of the Russian social networks "V Kontakte" and "Classmates", and created for Facebook a special application Profile Visitor, which requests the user access to his wall, promising to show a list of those who visited his page. In fact, a picture is posted on the user's wall with a link to a fraudulent website.
