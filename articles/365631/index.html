Design and ergonomics


A digital pen is a seemingly ordinary pen, complete with a special base unit, which is installed at the top of the page and is designed to track the position of our pen.
On the base unit there is an LCD display showing the current battery status, the number of saved pages, as well as the mode of communication with the computer. On the case there is only one button. With a long press it turns on or off the device. With a short-term - completes the input of the current page and starts the next.
The base unit is mounted on the sheet using three oblique clamps

Not the most convenient solution - a rather small number of sheets are clamped. And in order to open all three hooks, you have to use your fingers to represent Kraken. But on the sheet, this design is held securely, and with proper skill, the base unit is rearranged from sheet to sheet in seconds.
The handle fits comfortably in the hand, pleasant to the touch, thick, but after all-metal pens it is a bit light for me, although it is a matter of habit. The inconvenience is caused by the button, which strives to get under the finger. But when writing from her no sense, so it does not lead to a catastrophe. The receiver is located three millimeters from the end of the rod and is hidden behind a translucent grid.

The rods are all-metal, 67mm long and 2mm in diameter, simply inserted into the hole in the handle and fixed there somehow. To pull them out, there is a clip in the cap of the handle.

In general - it looks decent and durable.

How it works


This instance is a typical pen. Along the edges of the base unit, two ultrasonic sonars are located behind the decorative grilles, which accurately determine the position of the handle. These sonars are clearly visible in the photo:

In the center of the base unit is an infrared photodetector. IR communication is necessary for the implementation of the power saving mode - the pen itself has two IR LEDs and, in active mode, sends presence signals to the base unit. Switching to active mode is performed by pressing the handle bar on the surface. If within a minute no more clicks were made, that is, the pen was not used, it goes into sleep mode and the LEDs go out. Accordingly, the base station only turns on the sonar when it receives a message from the pen via IR. This photo shows a barely glowing point:

Probably through the IR channel is feedback from the pen to the base unit.

Operating experience


The pen supports four different modes of operation - it is standalone mode, online mode, note mode and mouse mode. Each of them is interesting in its own way.
Offline mode

This is the most important pen mode, allowing you to conveniently record lectures, make quick notes, sketches. To work in this mode, simply install the base unit on a sheet, turn it on and use the usual way to record by hand. After the next mark is made, it is necessary to press the button on the base unit. The page count will increase by one, we can start a new entry. In offline mode, over 100 A4 pages can be stored in the device‚Äôs memory.
On-line mode (PC connection mode).
