The first release of Simply Linux - a new distribution for ordinary people has been released :)

Yesterday (111109) a new distribution kit, Simply Linux, was born. In general, the first question would seem: why do we need another distribution kit? How many of them already, all sorts of different. Many enthusiasts make their assemblies on different repositories. Nevertheless, the development of Simply has a powerful ideological basis - a focus on novice users, most of whom will never become "continuing", since their goal is not to dive into the depths of the computer, but to use it as a means to perform typical tasks.

And there is nothing bad about it, they just have other interests. There must be someone to heal, teach, build, and not everyone to be IT people :) Well, for such users, windows only?
What happens to most distros? They need to be penetrated in order to choose worthy and convenient programs for themselves, to penetrate in order to customize everything for themselves, to penetrate in order to at least choose a distribution kit. I don‚Äôt argue that many people like this process, but there is a large part of indifferent people who don‚Äôt care, they‚Äôre just not interested in what‚Äôs inside. It is not interesting to choose a computer, it is not interesting to choose a system, programs. ‚ÄúGive what works, show how I can carry out my tasks and can be free. And we need to do business. "


Simply Linux has a minimal package base. Unlike other distributions, the number of programs in which rolls over 1000, Simply includes only about 30 programs, according to the principle 1 task - 1 program. That is, one browser, one office suite, one player, etc. The programs are selected the most functional, the easiest, and fully Russified by the results of the author's analysis. Yes, 1000 programs are cool, a lot, and a lot is always better than a little, because a lot is a choice. But the opportunity is specific, as choosing one needs to analyze, try, learn and take a little risk. Who wants to spend time on it? The answer was in the previous paragraph: "... give ... show ... and we need to do business."
By the way, the entire distribution kit is placed on a CD.

What else? Distribution is easy. Not only because of the program staff, but also because of the shell (xfce), which does not require a lot of resources. This means not only that you can safely work on weak machines, but also the fact that more resources can be allocated ‚Äúfor the cause‚Äù, that is, for the work of the programs themselves. How much memory does downloading photos from a modern DSLR require? In general, whatever computer it is, it will load faster if ‚Äújust the system‚Äù does not take a large chunk of memory. Well, you can still remember the movies in HDTV.

In addition to the system itself, an important place in the project is the site. While he is not ready yet, because the idea is really serious. Make not just a promotional product site, but also a resource for those who have switched to Simply. There will be everything: from recommendations on additional software with detailed instructions on what to install, to detailed documentation, including answers to the most basic questions in the opinion of Linux users. The proposed programs for additional installation will be selected on the same principle as in the basic set - Russification, functionality, stability.

Well, the very design of the site should radiate warmth and comfort. :)
