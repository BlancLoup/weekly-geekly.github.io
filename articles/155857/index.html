Nokia introduced the Lumia 510 for $ 200



Nokia introduced its cheapest smartphone in the family Lumia - Lumia 510. The cost of the device should not exceed $ 200. The smartphone runs on the Windows Phone 7.5 Mango platform, but may later receive an update to 7.8.

It is not yet known whether it will be possible to purchase a device in Russia, sales will begin next month in India, China, South America and Asia.

Detailed specifications ( source ):

  • Display: 4 ", 800 x 480 pixels;
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon S1,800 MHz;
  • RAM: 256 MB;
  • ROM: 4 GB;
  • Camera: rear - 5 Mpx, VGA @ 30;
  • Wi-Fi: 802.11 b / g / n;
  • Bluetooth: 2.1 + EDR;
  • Navigation: GPS;
  • Sensors: accelerometer;
  • FM radio;
  • Connectors: microUSB 2.0, 3.5 mm. audio;
  • Battery: 1300 mAh, 6.2 hours of talk time / 38 hours of music.
  • Dimensions: 120.7 x 64.9 x 11.5 mm;
  • Weight: 129 g

The device is available in five different colors: red, black, white, blue and yellow. Buyers of the device will also receive an additional 7 GB in SkyDrive.
