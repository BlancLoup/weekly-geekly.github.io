On the eve of the European football champion (also known as Euro 2012 ), Google Play has quite a lot of various applications that will help fans not only keep up with all the latest sports news, but will also be useful to those who decide to go to live matches.

EURO official app


Category : Sport
Developer / Publisher : UEFA.com
Version : 2.0
Price : Free - Link in Google Play

The developers of the official EURO 2012 application primarily focused on a variety of multimedia content, without forgetting about news and reports, the ability to view tournament tables and all sorts of alerts. All users will be able directly from the application to watch the broadcast of matches, however, for this you need an Internet connection.



The main functionality of the application is available to users for free, but it also has paid features. For example, viewing the best moments. How this content will be distributed, and how much it will cost is still unknown.

Euro 2012 Football Meister


Category : Sport
Developer / Publisher : Green Orange Digital Marketing
Version : 3.1.1
Price : Free - Link in Google Play

One of the most important features of Euro 2012 Football Meister is the active interaction between users. You have ample opportunity to predict the results of upcoming matches, and in case of your victory you will get points. Earn the most points will receive an interesting title.



In addition, Euro 2012 Football Meister provides information about the schedule of upcoming games, their venue and the results of matches. The user can obtain data on the standings, as well as detailed information regarding the cities and stadiums where matches will be held.

Euro 2012 - Boar Veschun


Category : Entertainment
Developer / Publisher : Amberfog
Version : 1.0.1
Price : Free - Link in Google Play

In essence, Euro 2012 - Khryak Veshchun is a simple voting box where anyone can make their predictions for the upcoming football game. But the beauty of this simple application is that the opposite of your forecast will be the result of the match, which will predict a funny little animal - Boar Veschun (the official successor to the octopus Paul ).



For example, for the upcoming match Russia-Czech Republic, the animal predictor gives victory to the first team. Let's see if her prediction will come true. By the way, you can share your predictions with friends and acquaintances on Twitter or social networks Vkontakte and Facebook.
