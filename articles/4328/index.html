Centrohost again registered domain pizda.ru

Recently , Tsentrohost was trying to register a domain pizda.ru, but then the registration record was deleted as contradicting the Domain Name Registration Rules . Now the registrar "Centrohost" re-registered the same domain.

As you know, obscene domains contradict clause 4.4 of the Domain Name Registration Rules , according to which the registrar has the right to refuse to register a domain if words of obscene content are used as a domain name.

The problem is that very often domain names are written using transliteration and can be read in two ways. In addition, there is nowhere a list of words that are officially considered obscene and that are prohibited to be used as a domain name. Therefore, swearing in domains occurs very often.
