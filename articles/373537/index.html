As a programmer, I bought a car. Part III

In the two previous parts ( I , II ) of my story about the service for finding profitable cars, I detailed the technical side of the issue - setting the problem and its solution.

In this article I will focus on the results and capabilities of the service robasta.ru .

For those who have not read the previous articles and are not eager to do this:

Robasta.ru is a service for finding profitable cars (the price of which is lower than the market price) according to data from all major sites with ads for the sale of used cars in the Russian Federation.

Before buying a car, most people want to sell the previous one and do it quickly and efficiently - for this, there is a car assessment service on our service, using which you will receive a pdf-certificate , which will indicate the value of your car at the moment, the cost in the future (if enough statistics on your model) and more.
To save your time, a telegram informant is implemented on the site, which allows you to receive notifications about new advertisements of car models that interest you with the frequency you need.



Car search

The main functionality of the service, which is provided absolutely free, is the collection and analysis of data from all the main sites of the Russian Federation, aggregating ads for the sale of used cars. The analysis uses machine learning algorithms to identify profitable sales offers and evaluate the quantitative value of the gain in rubles from the acquisition for each ad. Under the "profitable" should be understood offers, the price of which is below the market.

For example: Audi Q5, Moscow, price up to 1.1 million rubles, does not require repair, only private owners .



Chart of distribution of ads at a profit facilitates the search and serves for visibility of the algorithm. As a result of the search ads are always sorted from profitable to unprofitable.

Telegram informant

For those who appreciate time and are not ready to spend it at the monitor in search of a decent and profitable car, I added the telegram-informant service . By saving the search for cars by the filters you are interested in, you can create a telegram newsletter with the frequency you need using @robastaBot .



Car rating

For those who do not want to sell cheap when selling their iron horse, I implemented the online car evaluation function. After analyzing ads from all popular portals of the Russian Federation in just a few seconds, you will be provided with a detailed report in pdf format . In the report, you will receive answers to questions regarding the market value of your car now, in the future (if statistical data on your model are enough) and a number of other interesting features.

API

If you want to be among those who already have access to the functionality of the service robasta.ru in automatic mode - use our API , which can be customized to your requirements upon request. The API also provides free trial access.

At last

Thus, the first service in Runet appeared that helps the user at all stages of buying a used car: from search to assessment and selection of models of interest to you in real time. Thanks to robasta.ru you will not go crazy when searching for a car on several sites at the same time, you will know the deviation from the market value for each of the cars sold, you will receive in the telegram new ads on the parameters you are interested in and will be able to estimate the market value of your car.

Of course, our service will not check the legal purity and technical condition of the car, but it will tell you exactly which cars and in what sequence you should go to watch, thereby saving your time, energy, nerves and money.
