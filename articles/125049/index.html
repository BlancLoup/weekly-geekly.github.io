Moscow Innovation Evening

Starting July 20, 2011, we have been holding Moscow Innovation Evening for entrepreneurs, experts and investors interested in leading companies to venture capital markets.

Meetings are held at 20:00 every third Wednesday of the month in the restaurant of Digital October at the address: Moscow, Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6

Innovative evenings are held in the following format: From 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm 3 teams make ten-minute presentations and answer the questions of our guests within ten minutes, after which there is a general discussion of the projects presented.

If your team wants to make a presentation of your project, please fill in the application form in advance at http://www.innovationlabs.net/ru/min-2/ .

A very good list of examples of presentations compiled by Roy Rodenstein is located at the following link https://bitly.com/bundles/royrod/2

View more information and vote for your favorite projects on the event page on Facebook , as well as on the Global Innovation Labs Facebook page.

The next meeting will be held on August 17, applications for performance on it will be accepted until the end of the day on July 31.
