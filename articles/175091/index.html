The browser manager will help you maintain full control over your settings.

On Habrahabr, the topic was often raised about not entirely bona fide ways of distributing programs and changing browser settings without the explicit consent of users. Today we are releasing a new product whose task is to prevent it.

Its name - Browser Manager - well describes what task it is intended to solve: control the browser settings and prevent them from being changed without your knowledge. We want to emphasize that he himself is absolutely neutral: he does not impose and does not even offer any default settings or anything like that and protects that search, default page and options that you chose.



We carefully monitored so that Browser Manager only helps users save their own settings. Honestly we admit that in part this program has become for us the work on our own mistakes.

As you know, we had a similar product before - Yandex.Zaschitnik. He defended the settings of those users who use the Elements of Yandex. But over time, we came to the conclusion that this is not quite correct technically and ideologically. As a result, the Browser Manager has a number of differences from its predecessor:

  • it is completely neutral: it does not install itself and does not offer to install it by default;
  • it can be removed through the standard Windows uninstall interface - as a standalone product without being tied to any other;
  • The manager is able, by your request, to help remove the most common programs that regularly change browser settings and at the same time do not explicitly ask about the desire to do this;
  • the program's task is to respond only to automatic attempts to change the settings and not take into account those that the user makes on their own;
  • in its settings, you can independently choose those types of changes that, in your opinion, require prevention (we highly recommend that you always leave the option to protect the hosts file enabled);
  • Sometimes the Manager can help to cope even with the fact that it is not easy to manually fix and technically advanced person;
  • Browser Manager is written from scratch for efficiency and less resource consumption.




We hope that with the new program we will be able to save a lot of time and nerves to you and your not very technically advanced acquaintances. After all, now they can cost only a few ticks, not removed in time when installing your favorite program.

Download Browser Manager by following the link bm.yandex.ru .
