All-Russian Meeting of System Administrators 2012

According to the good old tradition, on the last weekend of July, all system administrators in Russia, the CIS countries, and far and near abroad gladly leave their cold server rooms, put up a camping tent, a sleeping bag, stuff a tambourine into a backpack and go to the Kaluga region in unison connect with thousands of like-minded people like him, and take part in the 2012 ACD Celebration , timed to coincide with the celebration of the Day of the System Administrator.


This is not just a professional holiday - this is a kind of New Year in the world of system administrators, the opportunity to take stock of the year, discuss problems in an informal setting that have been worrying the entire IT community lately or just meet old friends living thousands of miles away and have a great time over a cup of cold refreshing beer. And what else does a system administrator need on a hot day in the middle of summer ?!

The meeting of the ACD has become an annual event, which is timed to the celebration of the Day of the System Administrator. The event has long won the love of many representatives of the IT-community in Russia, CIS countries, as well as system analysts from far and near abroad. Glory of the ACD is growing every year, and the number of its participants has long exceeded 5,000 people.
From the moment of the first Meeting in 2006, Softline has been the constant organizer and chief custodian of the traditions of the Meeting. The event is traditionally held in the hospitable Kaluga region, the ACH Meeting 2012 is no exception. This year, the organizers chose a new Glade of the Meeting, which was picturesquely spread out on the steep bank of the Oka River, and will hospitably receive all participants of the Meeting from July 27 to July 29, 2012. Registration is in full swing!
