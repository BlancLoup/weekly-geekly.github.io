I am the representative of the provincial branch of a small Moscow university. Preparing and analyzing the lists of applicants, I came to very interesting conclusions, which I want to talk about.

Already that year, the ministry has been experimenting with the procedure for enrollment in the USE. In our region, the USE reception has been going on for more than seven years (we are one of the first experimental regions), so many of the points are obvious to us and are fairly well studied.
