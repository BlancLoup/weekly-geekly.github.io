An example of creating a simple 2D game for Android using the Unity game engine

Introduction




First of all, I want to immediately note that I am not a professional developer. In this article I will try to explain my experience in creating the game ‚ÄúFeel Speed ‚Äã‚ÄãRacing‚Äù. This material will most likely not be of interest to those who already have extensive experience in game development, but I think it will be interesting for novice developers who have worked with Unity at least a little.

Design


The concept of the game is that the car must pass, as much as possible while on the road dynamically appear obstacles they need to go around otherwise the game is over, you still need to monitor the fuel scale and collect fuel tanks on the road, after which the game also stops .
