What's new in Delphi XE

Not so long ago, new versions of Delphi XE and RAD Studio XE development tools were released.



Pros have probably already learned everything, but we are sure that there will be those who will be interested to listen to the new joint online softkey seminar with Embarcadero Technologies.

The presentation by Vsevolod Leonov, Embarcadero Product Specialist, will be devoted to the very same Delphi XE and RAD Studio XE development tools updates.

About the seminar:

Date: November 25, 2010.
Time: from 11:00 to 12:00 (Moscow Time).
Location: virtual playground .
Registration: here .

In a programme:

During the online seminar, new features of Delphi XE and RAD Studio XE development tools will be considered, which are aimed primarily at improving the performance of individual developers and groups of programmers with the possibility of using Agile-techniques:

- integration with Subversion;
- profiling tools;
- automation of the project assembly;
- modeling tools.

There will also be considered the possibilities of RAD Studio in terms of the implementation of systems with a complex multi-tier architecture and the placement of modules in the cloud infrastructure.

Registration ends 25.11 at 10:00.
Participation in the seminar is free.

If you have problems registering for a webinar in Mozilla Firefox, use a different browser.
