How to increase organic traffic without generating tons of content

Did you know that the average Internet page, which is on the first page of Google search results, consists of at least 2,000 words? And this means that if you want to increase organic traffic, you need to generate lots and lots of content.

The concept of your site does not provide an abundance of text? You are doomed!
That's not true. There is always a solution. And before plunging into debriefing on this decision, let's try to dispel the myth that creating tons of content in order to build up organic traffic is the only correct decision.

Is the increase in the amount of content on the site is equal to the increase in the rate of organic traffic?
When you submit sites from the first page of search results, what resources come to mind first? Wikipedia, Habrahabr, Correspondent ... The reason for the good ranking is that these resources consist of thousands, if not millions, of unique content pages. Yes, trite - the more content you create, the more keywords are read by the search engine, which leads to a positive ranking of the site.

By the way, if you start to figure out which keywords these sites are ranked, most of them will turn out to be so-called long-tail keywords.

But does this mean that you have no chance to rank well without producing content? Let's try to figure it out in practice. A great example would be a website with multi-million traffic adme.ru , where texts reach a mark of about a hundred words. So how can you increase organic traffic without increasing content on the site?

You will be able to succeed in the attendance of your resource by performing the following steps:

1. Create a lot of pages on the site - regardless of whether they are filled with text on a string or there will be one line, you need to increase the number of pages. The more pages the site has, the more chances it has to be ranked in the future.

2. All your pages should be indexed - if Google does not like the quality of the content of your pages, you can fall under the Panda filter. In order to avoid this, any, even the smallest, text must be unique, stylistically and grammatically verified, to carry the meaning for the reader. You also need to pay attention to the content of the site, which is created by users in the form of reviews, reviews, comments.

3. Links to your site on information portals - this is quite time-consuming, but giving an excellent return on the task. To get a reference in a popular intern-edition requires effort, but links in those will bear fruit in the form of an increase in positions in search results. Meet journalists, write useful articles for portals with the target audience that interests you, ‚Äúbe friends with blogs‚Äù on a related topic.

4. Be proactive - use resources where your knowledge can be useful. For example, keep track of questions in Quora or Pressfeed, give answers to them on behalf of a member of the resource team - sooner or later your information will be in the ‚Äúten‚Äù and will be published, as well as the link to the site. Also, track the advisory lists of resources on your subject, contact the authors of those - convince them that your site should be among those indicated.

5. Finally, be patient - if your content is not detailed and not sufficiently informative, organic traffic should not be expected right now. In the near future, you will receive search traffic, but don‚Äôt hope for a daily miracle - everything will be, but not immediately.

So, this article does not reinvent the wheel, but is intended to once again draw the attention of the owners of successful and almost successful resources to building long-term relationships with search engines. And this can and should be done by creating a field of external links to the site from the trust resources of the network. In particular, if your site does not allow you to move through a large array of content.

And, of course, do not forget about the quality of the services and products that are presented on your site, because such giants as Amazon or Apple, if you look closely, do not really focus their attention on blogging.
Synergy of diligence in the promotion of the site and a good promoted product is not the easiest thing, but it will definitely lead you to success.
