Pillow that allows you to fall asleep anywhere and not notice others

This morning I caught the eye of an interesting device, which I would like to talk about.

We often want to sleep, but there are quite a few reasons that impede the fulfillment of this desire. This may be the distance from work, maybe the delivery of the project, deadlines, and many other household and work moments. Very often you want to sleep on the road, at the train station while waiting for your flight, even studying at lectures.

Designers from Kawamura-Ganjavian have developed a solution so that we all can finally sleep.



OSTRICH PILLOW (ostrich pillow) is an open air cushion for the arms, mouth and nose. The eyes are closed and the head is in a soft "suit", so you can safely lie down on the table or sit back in a chair on a bus or plane. Undoubtedly, you will look like an alien, but the fact that you do not see anything, allows you to ignore the gazes of others.



1. What is Ostrich pillow?

This product is designed to ensure a pleasant sleep anywhere, anytime. Its unique design allows you to create your own micro-space in which you will easily and simply relax. Its soothing and soft construction isolates the arms and head (mind and body) for a short break without having to leave the table, chair, chair or other place where you are at the moment.




2. The advantage of sleep power

You probably know that sleep for 20 minutes improves performance by more than 30%. Seeing how we spend a lot of time at work, in front of the monitor, on trips, the developers thought why not create a device that will help you disconnect from everything and rest for short periods of time and gain strength.



3. Developers.

Kawamura-Ganjavian Young Architecture and Design Studio is engaged in studying and understanding the needs of modern man. Sleep is considered to be one of the most important human needs. Last year, this product was developed and uploaded on its website. After they received a lot of positive feedback about their product, they decided to put it into production and even attracted investors.


By the way, this wonderful pillow costs $ 75
