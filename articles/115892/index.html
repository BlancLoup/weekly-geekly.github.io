From idea to prototype in two days: HackDay will arrive in Voronezh and Yaroslavl in early April



HackDay is preparing for an amazing spring trip. Almost simultaneously two HackDay meetings will take place in different cities.
On April 1-2, we will arrive for the first time in Voronezh, where DataArt will receive HackDay.
On April 2-3, Hak takes Yaroslavl State University for the second time.
What HackDay will help you in these wonderful cities:
- free the head from the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe project, which was long nurtured, making sure it is promising (well, or hopeless);
- assemble a team;
- chat with experienced programmers;
- explore new technologies.
We invite programmers, designers, developers, project managers. The goal of the events is to develop projects from idea to prototype in two days.

What will be provided: the opportunity to present your idea and say "Join us!"
Traditionally, you can come up with ready-made ideas, for the implementation of which you had no time at all. You can assemble a team directly at the event. You can also come and listen to great workshops.
And this time we are supported by remarkable sponsors - the company JOB.ru , which established for Voronezh Khak the prize for the most organized team, the companies Tenzor and Altius Plus in Yaroslavl.
On the evening of Sunday (Saturday for Voronezh) a demofest will be held - a presentation of the developed projects. The participants and the jury will be summed up by open voting, the authors of the best projects will be awarded.

Participation is free.

We have a lot of prizes for the winners, funny presenters and cool music on demofeste. If you have an idea, but no team, or you want to participate in an interesting project, then come to us.

Registering:
April 1-2 in Voronezh
April 2-3 in Yaroslavl

Voronezh: Voronezh State University, ch. building (University Square, 1)
Yaroslavl: 7th building of YarSU named after P.G.Demidov (ul.Soyuznaya, 144).
