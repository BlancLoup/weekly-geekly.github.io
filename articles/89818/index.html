New version of Rediska 0.4.0 - PHP client for Redis

Good news every one! We have worked hard to make this imperfect world a little better :). For those who are not familiar with the subject of conversation, you should first read my previous posts about the wonderful key-value base Redis and our handy PHP client Rediska (hereinafter referred to as Radish), as well as take a walk on its homepage .
Already half a year we successfully run Redis on our projects (on the most popular, on average, 130,000 unique visitors per day and 1,500,000 views) and cannot rejoice. In the course of project development, and frankly, in order not to lose face in front of you, my dear, we tried to bring Radish to mind.

What's New in Rediska 0.4.0


  • ReTwitter is a small clone (demo app) of twitter written in Zend Framework and Radish.
  • PEAR-package of Radishes , for convenient installation and updating
  • Symfony integration
  • Optimization of the Consistent Hashing algorithm (more than 10 times!)
  • Added commands: BGREWRITEAOF, ZINCRBY, ZREMRANGEBYSCORE, SLAVEOF, EXPIREAT
  • Added support for the WITHSCORES option for the ZRANGE command
  • Added timeout setting for connections
  • Optimized fromArray method for classes Rediska_Key_List, Rediska_Key_Set, Rediska_Key_SortedSet
  • New environment for unit tests
  • And more than ten improvements and fixes


In the near future, the implementation of cloud-based distribution of keys across servers and serious optimization (transfer of the most bottlenecks in PHP extensions).

In conclusion, before going to the bar, I want to wish you a relaxing weekend and stressful everyday life (the main thing is to bring pleasure and go to work). Be healthy.
