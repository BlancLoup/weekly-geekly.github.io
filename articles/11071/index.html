AT & T offers live video transmission service.

AT & T launched Video Share, the first service that allows callers to share live video between phones. But for this you need a 3G network and a Video Share compatible phone - at the moment it is only the LG CU500V.

However, Video Share provides only one-way video communication, that is, the recipient cannot send his own video at the same time. Thus, until AT & T does feedback (if it does, of course), this service can only be used to show interesting videos in real time.

It should be noted that the new service is now available only in three US cities: Atlanta, Dallas and San Antonio - in other cities, Video Share will appear at the end of summer. The cost of the service is $ 4.99 or $ 9.99 monthly, depending on the minutes included. There is also an option without subscription fees - in this case one minute of broadcasting will cost 35 cents.
