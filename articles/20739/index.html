How to come to a successful business model, abandoning most of their ideas. Experience creators RSS2email

Artem Kashekhlebov and Oleg Basha, leaders of Olevarty , talk about their projects Kanban , RSS2email , Seku . About how they saw the development direction from the beginning, and how reality corrected their vision.
Recently, there are enough startups in RuNet that differ in the use of any technology. But this difference often does not become a competitive advantage.
The creators of such startups (as well as those who make such a difference in their head) will be useful to familiarize themselves with the experience of the company, all of whose projects are based on RSS technology.
Difficult way of complex product
- From the interviews you have already given, it is known that you previously owned a web studio, then you saw the potential in the RSS technology and decided to create your own products based on it, and everything started with Kanban.
AK: Yes, that's right. The project is already 3 years old, initially it included all the same functions that are now distributed across different projects. It was very difficult for the audience. Then we began to isolate the functions, so RSS2email appeared - perhaps the most popular reader in RuNet. I don‚Äôt know what Yandex.Lends have, they don‚Äôt publish data, but we feel like that.
OB: The fact that everything is collected in one place turned out to be very difficult for the user. Therefore, we gradually divided the project into parts, and there are ideas that can be done yet. Initially, we wanted to do something similar to pusk.ru or Netvibes . Experience shows: what is difficult to configure - does not work.
. .: Pusk will ever get its audience, but it will not be massive ... If we consider the example of products from the mass market, we first unite simple products: for example, the camera combines both a camera and a player. But then it will probably again become very difficult, and then it will break back into simple products ... Such waviness.
OB: so about 2 years ago we began to narrow the functions of the service, highlighting them into separate projects. So, by the summer of that year, seku appeared. And Kanban became what we originally intended, until we started adding a lot of features to the project - a directory of blogs and news feeds.
And some things, like a subscription to comments on posts in RSS feeds, were generally deleted. For six months, we have accumulated 8 subscriptions to comments.

About simplicity and complexity
OB: RSS continues to be a complex technology, the term RSS itself knows 2-3% of users, and this figure will not increase over time. This is a technical term, unnecessary mass user detail. Focusing purely on RSS limited us.
- Is it right that with the direct use of technology RSS service will cease to be relevant, because it is an intermediary, not always necessary?
OB: Here we must understand what kind of user we are talking about. People who have more than 100 tapes in a subscription - less than a percent, while the remaining 99% have only a few of them.
. .: Another example - Newsgator , the world leader in the field of RSS aggregation, calls the main product the module that allows you to read RSS feeds in Outlook.
That is, RSS2email is not an intermediary, but an RSS-reader, where the interface is your favorite email program.

OB: It is better to focus on a mass audience, and not on the geeks who are demanding and capricious. And as soon as a new, more technological product appears, they will leave for it.
The same Netvibes is geek-oriented, and probably gathered all such an audience, but this is not the way for a project in RuNet.
