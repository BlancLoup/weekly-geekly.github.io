The association of small UAVs is calling under the wing



Anyone who has recently acquired a flying and buzzing drone, a large and small copter, helicopter or airplane, is dedicated. Robohunter wants to tell you about his new partner - the recently emerged initiative Association of small UAVs .

It was founded by a group of socially active fans of radio-controlled copters and professional helicopter pilots who want to continue to participate in the development of amateur flying equipment and to achieve legislative legalization of small UAV flights in the Russian Federation.

The guys from AMB are open to dialogue and gladly accept applications for entry from everyone associated with aircraft.

If you:

  • The operator or the owner of the drones of aircraft type, helicopters or copters.
  • Shop and retailer managed aircraft.
  • A company that wants to commercially master drones.
  • UAV manufacturer.



Then you definitely need to join this initiative group of people. Especially since being a member of the SBA , you get some nice privileges. For example, a discount in specialized stores, legal support or invitations to participate in thematic events.

Best regards, see you in the sky!
