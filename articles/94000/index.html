We manage the mini-airship directly from the iPad



The guys from the Breakfast NY team created a dirigible that was completely controlled from the Apple iPad. On the airship there is a camera that broadcasts the image on the iPad's display. The control is performed with an accelerometer, that is, in which direction you turn the tablet, the airship will fly there.

The thing is of great interest, so maybe soon we will see these toys for mobile devices already on store shelves.
