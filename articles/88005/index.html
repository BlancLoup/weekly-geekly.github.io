Every minute 24 hours video is uploaded to YouTube

YouTube has published the news that they have crossed the line at 20 o'clock video every minute. In March, the number of hours of video uploaded every 60 seconds to the server was 24. It turns out that every second users download 24 minutes of video.

On May 20 last year, YouTube reported on a per-minute download of 20 hours of video. According to this, over 10 months, the growth of downloads for video hosting has increased by 17%. This is impressive! More than two million minutes of video (34 thousand 650 hours) is being downloaded per day on YouTube now.

By the way, in May 2008, users downloaded only 12 hours of video per minute. In less than two years, YouTube doubled that figure and is not going to stop there.

The site also lists a graph of growth in the number of video hours downloaded per minute.
