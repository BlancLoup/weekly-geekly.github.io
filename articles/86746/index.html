Ultra-thin laptop LG X300

LG will present its new X300 laptop this month. The laptop is remarkable in that its thickness is only 17.5 mm.

Other features of the new laptop:
- 11.6 inch display;
- 970 grams;
- 2.0 GHz Intel Menlow;
- 2Gb RAM;
- 128Gb SSD;
- 1.3 Mpx camera;
- SRS TruSurround HD sound;
- 3G modem;
- Windows 7 Home Premium + LG Smart Pack;
- 7 hours of use without recharging.

Deliveries will begin in March in Asia, and later in other regions. At first glance, a very interesting and tasty model. The price is still unknown.
