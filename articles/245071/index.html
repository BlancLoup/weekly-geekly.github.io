Take your place in the ranking of Android-developers Intel

Do you want to gain recognition among Android developers, and along with it - Intel's financial and organizational assistance in promoting your product? Then this information is for you. This year, Intel has launched a special project , Intel Rating for Developers, in conjunction with the Apps4All developer community. Each quarter, the Intel jury selects several interesting applications for review on the site. The author of the best application receives the status of "Top Developer Intel" and all of the above buns.

  • Use the appthwack.com cloud service to test the performance of your application on devices with an Intel processor. Please note that software testing for the x86 mobile platform (and only for it) can be performed there absolutely free of charge! Examine the results of various tests on various devices - even screenshots can be viewed - and, if everything ended without errors and warnings, proceed to filling out the case.
  • Fill out the form on the Cases tab of the Intel community page on Apps4All: tell us about the unique functionality of your application and the experience of using Intel tools to create it. Be sure to add a link to the appthwack report.
  • All developers who submitted applications for participation in the NDK project will receive Android x86 devices for testing and optimizing their applications. The presence of x86 support in the Android NDK application is a necessary condition for participation in the project. You can also request a device for testing by filling in the appropriate field on the application form.


Interface appthwack.com

To qualify for the prize in the 4th quarter, you must send your case before December 20. Winners will be announced on December 26th.

The results of the previous quarter can be found here .

Applications will be evaluated on the following criteria:
  • for NDK applications - availability of support for 86 architectures;
  • performance;
  • interface quality;
  • innovation;
  • Using Intel tools and technologies ( C ++ compiler for Android , Intel GPA , Intel XDK , Intel INDE , etc.)

Read more about the project in the official rules .
