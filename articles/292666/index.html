Rostelecom intends to gain full control over the Russian search engine Sputnik

Rostelecom intends to consolidate 100% of Sputnik LLC, the developer of the search system of the same name. Now the operator owns 74.99%. The remaining 25.1% belong to Vyacheslav Rudnikov and Vasily Vasin. While the parties have not reached any agreements.

May 30, Sputnik Ltd. lost control over the domain sputnik.ru . Its new owner was Rostelecom. The operator also received the rights to the Sputnik trademark for a variety of goods and services, including e-mail, Internet access, and also radio telephone communication.
