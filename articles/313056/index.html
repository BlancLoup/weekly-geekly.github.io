This task is simpler: one person for every fifteen voters to be delivered, in order to win elections in a district with a population of a third million people and hold its deputy to Congress, in which it happens that bills are passed by a majority of just one vote ( this was, for example, before Pearl Harbor).


So, as you can see, in essence, it is not difficult to win the election. That is why volunteers can take power in the country, or in any part of it, and manage it as they see it right. Candidate Selection


Often the choice of candidates for the nomination is very limited. In our country, people who are ready to work for the good of society are not valued. Payment of this hard work is so ridiculous that an honest person, working in power, suffers a loss. In addition, to our universal shame, rotten eggs and rotten tomatoes fly into it much more often than words of praise for good work. The motives for such self-denial are the same as those of volunteers who, during a war, put their lives at risk in order to protect their country, but to wake this feeling in a person in peacetime is somewhat more complicated.


Most likely, at first your candidate will behave hesitantly, but will gladly give in to your convictions to run for the elections, because in his heart he believes that his efforts will not be wasted. Do not promise him anything, except that he will be allowed to personally nominate his candidacy in a closed meeting devoted to the election of candidates - caucus, unless, of course, he joins it.
