Company Registration in Hong Kong for IT business

Experience is a comb that we get after becoming bald. (c) Chinese proverb

At the request of readers in the continuation of a series of publications on incorporation abroad (see previous articles on Belize and Estonia ) I want to talk about the registration of a company in Hong Kong.

We were faced with the next task - to register a company in Hong Kong for a client and open a bank account for the SaaS project in the same jurisdiction.

Jurisdiction Overview


The legal system of Hong Kong is based on the norms of British law, which is certainly a plus (it's not hard for a lawyer to appreciate the advantages of this fact, in simple words: a convenient judicial system and the simplicity of concluding contracts).

Hong Kong is not an offshore from the point of view of the legislation of Ukraine and Belarus ,
From the point of view of the legislation of the Russian Federation - is.

A shareholder of a company can be both an individual and a legal entity. The minimum number is not set (it is logical that at least one).

Bearer shares are prohibited.

Share capital can be 1 dollar.

The minimum number of company directors has not been established, but at least one of them must be an individual.

Companies are required to keep financial statements and at the end of each financial year submit it to the auditor for review. (The price for audit and accounting with the number of transactions up to 300 per year is ~ 500 US $. But after registration, this procedure is carried out for the first time only after 18 months and costs about 1100 US $).

Geographically, reporting can be done anywhere, but it should be borne in mind that, upon the request of the Hong Kong tax, it may be required for verification.

Corporate tax rates (income tax) - 16.5% of the profit received in Hong Kong and 0% of the profit received for its territory.

It is better to explain in simple words. So, if you
- do not work in the domestic market,
- do not have customers \ buyers in Hong Kong,
- there is no real office and staff in Hong Kong

there is no need to pay tax.

It is necessary to have supporting documents for all transactions, since you will be obliged (through auditors) to prove that you can apply a 0% rate to your profit.

VAT is not provided.

The cost of registration (provided that the shareholder of the company is an individual) *

Bank account
If you need a PayPal business account , then you need to open an account in Hong Kong (the PayPal requirement - the bank and the company must be located in the same jurisdiction, and the card issued by the bank must be Visa).

Below is a list of banks with which you can work:

- HSBC (the most popular bank, opening an account is free, and, by the way, you can open a merchant here);
- Hang Seng;
- Standard Charter;
- Bank of China;
- China Trust;
