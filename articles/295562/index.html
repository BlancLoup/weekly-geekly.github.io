Why is it worth learning foreign languages?


  1. Work or business abroad, cooperation with foreign partners, etc. I would also include here the possibility of faster career growth due to the fact of knowledge of the language and access to foreign literature and other educational resources related to the specialty.
  2. Learning a language helps improve memory and better concentration. Move your brains now, and this can save you from cognitive impairment in old age, my friends!
