The release of the free antivirus Panda Cloud is available for free

Antivirus software, which has been offered as a public beta since April 2009, has received wider publicity in the press than regular free antivirus software, thanks to its cloud approach. Information from all computer systems that Cloud Panda Antivirus works with is automatically shared with all other users.



And version 1.0 introduces additional improvements over beta versions and regular antivirus software.

Panda Cloud Antivirus 1.0 represents a further improvement of the 100% cloud-based architecture of its protection model, which processes and blocks malware more efficiently than locally installed signature products, with minimal impact on PC performance. Version 1.0 already boasts how to improve throughput and impact efficiency over its beta versions. Traditional anti-virus solutions consume approximately 15% of computer resources (memory, disk space, etc.), and Panda Cloud Antivirus 1.0 consumes only 3%.
