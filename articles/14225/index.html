Will Google issue coupons?

Last week, Google registered or bought out nine domains, the names of which contain the words coupon (coupon) and, in fact, Google. This indicates that Google may be planning to issue custom coupons.

Will it be possible to pay these coupons at a local store or at Google Checkout? Maybe. But most likely, these coupons will need to be activated in mobile phones, and you will have to make purchases from local stores with them ... do they all remember the recent Google patent on mobile payments?

Here is a list of new domains:
  • CouponGoogle.com
  • Google-Coupons.com
  • Couponsgoogle.net
  • Google-Coupons.net
  • GoogleCoupon.net
  • GoogleCoupon.org
  • Coupongoogle.org
  • Couponsgoogle.org
  • Google-coupons.org
