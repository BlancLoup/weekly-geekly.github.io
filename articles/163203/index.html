The coolest program at Winter Nights

We published the program of the winter conference dedicated to mobile games. Such a composition of speakers has not been anywhere else in the world. If you want to listen to Supercell , Rovio , Wooga , Chillingo , Big Fish , FishLabs , PlayFirst , Facebook , Microsoft , Google and others, sign up sooner - on December 25th we raise prices.



In addition to the reports there will be interesting round tables.

- Representatives of the app stores Google, Microsoft and GetJar will talk about the possibilities of working with them.

- Facebook and Odnoklassniki.ru will talk about social networks as platforms for mobile games.

- A separate discussion devoted to where to take traffic. There will be a report on SEO optimization in the App Store (advice will be given by AppCodes) and expert opinions from InMobi, ChartBoost and PocketGamer.

- Director of HeadHunter North-West will give a summary of the salaries of specialists in the industry, after which there will be an open discussion on the topic of finding and hiring the right people. For example, Boomzap Ent. share experiences on how employees can live in different countries and create great games together.

We are in charge of organizing everything, Nevosoft, and the platinum sponsors of the event are i-Free and Big Fish.

If you're wondering how to succeed like Angry Birds, Clash of Clans, Diamond Dash, and other hits, then Winter Nights is an event not to be missed.

Now the ticket "Premium +", which includes a visit to all reports, coffee breaks, dinner and party, costs $ 300. There are also standard tickets (without dinner and party) worth $ 200. Sign up here .

The number of tickets is limited.
