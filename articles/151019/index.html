Windows 8 Camp - WinRT Pro, components and more

In recent days, news about Windows 8 is pouring out of the horn of plenty - these are new devices from Samsung and Nokia with Windows 8 on board, and the upcoming presentation of Visual Studio 2012, and the immediate availability of Windows 8 to users all over the world.

After such a flurry of events, it is especially important to use the opportunity to visit Windows 8 Camp , which will take place on September 7 in Moscow at the Digital October center - after all, hundreds of our colleagues will gather here with whom you can share your experience writing programs for Windows 8 and acquire new knowledge and new friends.

DevExpress Company will not stand aside either! Alexander Kravtsov (aka AlexKravtsov ) will give a presentation tomorrow on the development of WinRT components for Windows 8. You can listen to it in the hall for partner reports at 11.15, or join the online broadcast, which will be held on the Windows 8 Camp website.

If you are lucky enough to visit Digital October and see everything live, we will be happy to answer any questions you may have. We will be a few people and you can easily find us among all the participants in bright orange T-shirts with the slogan "DXv2 - Let's see what develops"

See you at the camp! #wincamp
