"42" - a conference and exhibition of startups, playing out tickets

May 29 in Moscow will host an international exhibition and conference of startups "42" . We gather representatives of European, American and Russian funds on the democratic platform, as well as specially selected startups for the exhibition.



All invited guests and ventures will be available for communication in person or through a business contact system that will be launched for the event. The official list on the site is now 96 venture capital funds , and closer to the event their number will grow.

We give a discount on a ticket for all registered with the promo code habracode. Instead of 12,000 ticket for you will cost 8,000 rubles. Also, every tenth registered with the specified code will be presented with a free ticket.

The event consists of two parallel parts: the exhibition and the conference. The exhibition of startups from the areas of: Internet, high tech, biotech, medicine. The conference program is designed to show the prospects of the Russian market to the world community.

Conference "42" will be held in the "Chocolate Workshop" of the Red October factory at the address: Bersenevsky Lane, 2, Building 1, opposite the entrance to Digital October.

Some of the confirmed participants are: Alexander Galitsky (Almaz Capital), Fadi Bishara (Blackbox), Max Niderhofer (Accel Partners), Igor Taber (Intel Capital), David Vorkvir (Mangrove Capital Partners).

Come. It will be interesting.
