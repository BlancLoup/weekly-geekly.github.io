Choosing a budget tablet computer on an Intel Core M processor

Last year, Intel was pleased with the new processors in the Intel Core M series, which allowed tablets to reach a new level in both performance and battery life. These processors heat much less, so many of them managed to get rid of the additional cooling, which, in turn, affected both the appearance and the duration of the work. On tablets from China, one can come across either Intel Core M-5Y10 or Intel Core M3-6Y30. The first modification is from the 5th generation of processors, and the second from the 6th, that is, the last.

I chose 5 tablets for comparison: 3 on Intel Core M-5Y10 processors, although they are a bit outdated, but they are much nicer in price and 2 of the most powerful on Intel Core M3-6Y30. By the way, in the Macbook 12, too, stands the Intel Core M 5th series, but about it today we will not.
