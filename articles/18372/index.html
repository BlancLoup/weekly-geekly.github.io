On the air: My home is always with me

When traveling to a picturesque corner, people often think about getting their own home there. But if the hunt for changing places does not allow you to enjoy the familiar look for a long time, one of the solutions is to buy a motorhome, at the same time a vehicle and a comfortable dwelling for rest. Traveling in motor homes is popular in Europe. In today's Russia, rest on wheels has not gained mass popularity, but as the necessary infrastructure appears, this gap can be filled in a short time.



Technique and fiction

The motorhome market is mainly represented by foreign models. Motorhomes produced on the chassis of well-known car brands (Mercedes Sprinter, Ford Transit, Fiat Duato, etc.). According to Andrei Leshin, the owner of Auto Travel, the entire list of manufacturers of motorhomes takes from 45 to 105 lines. European ones are Adria, Burstner, CI (Caravans Internasional), Chausson, Dethleffs, Eura Mobile, Fendt, Hobby, Hymer, Knaus, LMC, Rollerteam, Tabbert, and others. Statistics on the high demand for motorhomes: in 2005 Europe, they were sold 80,237.

In Russia, the figures are much lower. According to Evgenia Levinskaya, marketing director of the Hymer representative office in Russia, more than 100 autohouses have been sold since the company first appeared on the Russian market in 2003. The company "World of Kupava" noted that unit sales accounted for the motorhomes. Among the buyers are not only individuals, but also organizations.

For example, motor homes on the basis of "GAZelle" "Kupava-3780" acquired circus "Chapito", "Mosfilm", studio "TRITE" Nikita Mikhalkov. The production of motor homes was set up in Belarus, having purchased technology and equipment in Europe for these purposes, and the chassis from the Russian GAZ-3302.

In motor homes of integrated type from serial cars, only the engine, transmission and suspension are used. The cabin is combined with the living space of the motorhome. Integrated motorhomes have excellent maneuverability and speed characteristics, are distinguished by luxury in the interior decoration. In semi-integrated homes, a residential compartment is installed on the chassis of a production car. Due to the absence of an alcove in the residential building (a niche above the cabin where a berth is equipped), as provided for in the alcove motorhomes, the car has increased maneuverability and speed characteristics at lower fuel costs.

Produced autohouses also differ in size, gross weight, number of beds, engine size, capacity of drinking water tanks and other indicators. House for rent

But buying a motorhome is not necessary. It can be rented. Rental services in the capital alone are provided by several companies. As was reported at the representative office of Hymer, the fact that the rental of motorhomes was a turn this summer is indicative of the positive dynamics of demand for motorhomes in Russia, the entire rental fleet was in demand. According to Levinsky, only one metropolitan dealer of the Hymer company, who opened the rental of motorhomes, from April to May issued a lease for more than 200 people.

Rental rates are closely tied to the seasons and the lease term. For example, in the "Auto Travel" in the high season, falling on the summer months, renting a house on wheels, where four people can comfortably travel for 3-11 days, will be 180 euros per day. In the middle season (this is April - May and September), the rental day will cost 145 euros, and the lowest (from January to April and from October to January) - 125 euros. In the company "World of Kupava" you can rent a Russian autohouse, the rental conditions are discussed individually.

The minimum cost of one day rental at official dealers Hymer is 160 euros. For example, in the company "Caravan Center", the autohouse Hymer Exsis for four people for a period of three to seven days will cost 180 euros, and from 15 to 30 days - 160 euros per day.
