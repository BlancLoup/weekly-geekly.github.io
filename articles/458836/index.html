Index borscht. A systematic approach to assessing, comparing, determining the price / quality ratio

The main purpose of the BI borscht index (borsch index) is to form an unbiased, comprehensive system for the assessment and comparison of borscht in different places and institutions.

Stage one. Integral evaluation


To determine the BI borsch index at the first stage, it is necessary to carry out an integral assessment of borsch.

The integral assessment IA (integral assessment) is used for a comprehensive assessment of the taste and visual qualities of borscht.

The result of the integral assessment will be the assignment of a certain number of points to a borsch (a maximum of 500 points is possible). The greater the number of points scored borscht, so it is better for the totality of parameters.

Stage Two. Borscht index


At the second stage, knowing the integral estimation of borsch, we determine the borsch index.

6. Consider the borscht index

BI - borsch index (borsch index) is considered as the ratio of the cost of borsch to its integral assessment with inflation.

The mathematical meaning of the borscht index is the value in rubles of 1 point of the integral estimate adjusted for inflation.

You can also say that the borscht index is a digitized price / quality ratio.
