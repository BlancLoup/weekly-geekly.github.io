Asus announced a series of ultra-thin monitors Designo MS



Displays are distinguished by an elegant design. According to Asus Designo should be the face of the company in the market of monitors.

The line includes monitors with a diagonal of 20-23.6 inches. The thickness of the new products does not exceed 16.5 mm.
All displays have a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels, a brightness of 250 cd / m2 and a contrast of 50,000: 1.

The largest monitors - Asus MS246H / D and Asus MS236H / D have a diagonal of 23.6 inches and 23 inches respectively and a response time of 2 ms.

The devices are equipped with HDMI, D-Sub and headphone output.

The 21.5 inch Asus MS226H has less ports: HDMI, D-Sub and HDMI Audio Out.

Younger models of the line: 20-inch Asus MS202N / D 22-inch and Asus MS227N have no HDMI port, but they are equipped with DVI-D.

Also for a 20-inch monitor, the response time is 5 ms. Other specifications are similar to older models.
