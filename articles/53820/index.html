We cling healthy ammeters to a PC via Arduino

I hooked up a pair of healthy ammeters to the Arduino - unscrewed the shunts from them, and through the resistance on the PWM I hung the outputs.
Immediately I remembered 3 application options (use zero, one pampering :)):
- measurement of processor load (just now, 2-core processors have gone);
- displaying the speedometer and tachometer readings from racing games;
