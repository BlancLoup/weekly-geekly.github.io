For example, a Chinese who was stolen from a family many years ago was able to find his family thanks to the Google Maps map service.

The boy has stolen the hero of our news, taking about 2 thousand kilometers away from his home. He remembered practically nothing, neither the name of his native city, nor the name of the street where his house stood. But he remembered that it was a place with two bridges.

A stolen boy, 23 years later, tried to use a service whose goal was to search for lost families / family members. On the site, our hero laid out an approximate scheme of the place that he remembered. Lao Gong, that is the name of the hero, used Google Maps to search for his lost home in Guangan. And I found, after finding those two bridges. Now the young man has already met with his family, however, it is not yet clear what he will do next - whether he will live on his own where he lived before, or reunite with his family.
