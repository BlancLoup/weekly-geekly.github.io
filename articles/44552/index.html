Sprouts on the keyboard 2

Recently drew a logo on the theme of "electronic" vegetation. I was eager to grow and pofot live greens sticking out of a laptop. The network has already walked photos with sprouts in the keyboard. There, the dude poured the seeds between the keys and watered abundantly until the harvest came. But I still need the keyboard of the laptop, so I decided to plant a garden in places remote from electronics, and plant a laptop only for the duration of the shooting.

Seeds stole from a hamster. Then I will return in the form of a salad. :) In the feed was corn, sunflower seeds, millet, wheat, oats, came across undocumented as part of hemp and some other small grains. Made a selective selection and laid out soaked on a wet cloth. I decided to grow in a box of compact. Wonderful thing, great replaces the greenhouse.



Added land as roots climbed.



When the greens grew and began to rest against the ceiling, I changed the lid to a higher one.



By the way, the roots appeared on the second day almost simultaneously for all comrades, and the sprouts were first to see the millet and the corn (second day), then the sunflower leaves began to try to break free from the shell, and the oats were proklyunulsya.
