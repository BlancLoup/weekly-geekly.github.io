HackDay # 25 - Hackathon in Nizhny Novgorod

In Nizhny Novgorod on April 12-14 , with the active support of the portal ITG-Event , the HackDay # 25 will be held - a hackathon where participants will be able to translate the ideas of their projects into working prototypes within 48 hours. For two days you will be able to completely disconnect from everyday worries and head off into the development of the project.




Program


Hackathon starts at 18.00 on Friday and ends on Sunday at 21.00. Detailed program .

An educational program is scheduled for HackDay # 25


# Natalia Burina (mentor of the American accelerator 500 startups) will tell you what a customer-oriented product is and why consumer facing should be taken into account when developing a prototype;

# Mikhail Kechinov (co-founder of the accelerator Coffeelab.vc) will explain what a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) is and what it is worth focusing on when developing a prototype project;

# Sergey Mashkov (leading developer of Skype) will give practical knowledge that will be useful for a quick start of the project under iOS;

# Artem Vorobyov (Intel engineer) in practice will introduce participants to the Intel Perceptual Computing SDK for developing applications that use innovative methods of user interaction, such as recognition of gestures and voices.
