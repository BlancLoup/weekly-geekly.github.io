[Video] Reports from the iOS developer Red Hot Chili Apples


Last year ended with a regular meeting of Red Hot Chili Apples iOS developers. Under the cat you will find records of reports about the VIPER alternative, the basic principles of functional programming on iOS, as well as how to make a quality project with limited resources.


Mobility in Yandex iOS development


Alexander Denisov from Yandex talks about approaches that allow his team to maintain the speed and productivity of development, even on large projects. As examples - the search application Yandex and J. Browser for iOS.



Yet Another Architecture


An architecture is a set of agreements accepted in a team that help make the code clean, uniform, and testable. You probably heard a lot about MVC, MVVM, VIPER - but these approaches are not always convenient.


Ilya Tsarev tells why Alfa-Bank's iOS team abandoned the usual MVVM and how YARCH came into being.


In the second part of the report, Ilya Kuznetsov examines what YARCH is and how to develop YARCH modules.



Brave new Swift. Functor, Monad
