HP and Universal launched their own music service in Russia

In principle, something, music and video services is now enough. Of course, each has its own merits, its own shortcomings, but in general, with similar projects, the situation is quite good. Nevertheless, many companies open their own projects related to movies, music or books. This time, HP and Universal distinguished themselves by launching the HP Music Club music service.

HP in this case - the developer of the service, Universal - provides music, nothing unexpected. At the time of opening in the HP Music Club, there were already 320 thousand different songs. Over time, this number will only increase. The service will be available to everyone, but owners of computers and laptops from HP will have several additional functions. For example, only owners of HP computers will be able to download the tracks they like. Interestingly, there will be no restrictions on the number of downloaded tracks for owners of computers from HP.
