Pearl 3.0 update to 3.0.1

It was the most common weekday, July 17th. I needed to test the display of the site in different browsers, for which I launched Firefox 3 installed on my site (I also have Firefox 2.0.0.11 on my computer, also for debugging purposes). Lys immediately issued a message that a new version appeared - 3.0.1 and strongly suggested upgrading to it. I click "update", a connection is established, 2 megabytes of updates are downloaded. Firefox is being updated. Then, as usual - after the restart, it checks the compatibility of the add-ons, found only an incompatible Pencil, updated it. Finally, the familiar Fx treshka window opens with the title of the window ‚ÄúFirefox successfully updated‚Äù . But what do I see? The Mozilla page opens, in which in large letters it says " You are now using Firefox 2.0.0.11 "! Do I have to say that there was no limit to my surprise?
