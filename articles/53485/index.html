Up to $ 1,000,000 per startup

Recently, more and more often we have to read articles on the topic of weakening the activities of venture capital funds and business angels due to deteriorated financial conditions. As a result, the really worthwhile ideas do not find capital for their implementation. Therefore, I want to write a post about a project that started a couple of months ago - a venture fund Softline Venture Partners , which is pleased to consider promising projects for investment and is ready to provide startups with up to $ 1,000,000.

Venture Fund Softline Venture Partners specializes in investments in software development projects and web development, including soft-as-service, in Russia and the CIS countries. Investments are considered both in developers who have completed software products and in developers who do not yet have software prototypes. Venture fund is ready to allocate to finance one project - up to one million US dollars in case of a positive decision. At the same time it is allowed to consider interesting projects with the required investments and above this limit.
Cooperation with Softline Venture Partners gives an additional bonus - all funded projects have access to all Softline's marketing and software distribution opportunities on special terms (since the venture fund is a member of the Softline group of companies ).

Consideration of the project is as follows:

In making investment decisions, Softline Venture Partners Venture Fund is guided primarily by:
  • the presence of a professional development team;
  • the presence of a unique idea / product;
  • commercial value of the product.
Investments are made in the form of direct investments in capital. For a share in the project, the fund plans to receive a share of success based on the results of the project implementation - by subsequent sale at a higher price of the share held by Softline Venture Partners to other investors.
