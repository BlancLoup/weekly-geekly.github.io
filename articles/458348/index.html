"Samsung Ecosystem" - the results of the competition

We are pleased to announce that the competition has been summed up! Within the framework of the competition, 127 scenarios were received, of which 43 corresponding to the objectives of the competition and approved for evaluation were received.

Who is the lucky one who suggested the best idea? This is George from Moscow! His idea is "smart food storage and optimal nutrition." The essence of the idea is to scan the receipt of purchased products, based on the available products, their shelf life and the user's physical activity, recipes for cooking will be recommended. When approaching the end of the product's shelf life, the user receives a notification and recommendation of its early use, and then purchases in one of the online stores.

The jury noted the integrated approach in the stated idea and the structured presentation.
And he gets the main prize of our competition - the flagship smartphone Samsung Galaxy S10!

The second place was taken by a graduate of IT-school Vladimir from Novosibirsk. Vladimir suggested a similar idea for proper nutrition and recommendations of dishes based on the user's physical activity and the available products, as well as their shelf life.

And he will get the second prize - an innovative wearable device Galaxy Watch Active!
