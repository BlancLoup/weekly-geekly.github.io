iPhone 3G - July 11th!

The entire Internet is full of articles and notes about the imminent release of a new version of the already acclaimed iPhone 3G phone. The main differences between the new version of the phone and the old one are that the new products are equipped with fast 3G technologies, a built-in GPS receiver, as well as some new software.



Also, Apple, after the release of the first version of the iPhone, took into account all the drawbacks of both the phone software and the physical flaws of the device and eliminated them in the new version.

This morning, a letter came from Apple that the iPhone 3G will go on sale on July 11. The price of this unit will be: 8GB - $ 199, 16GB - $ 299. In my opinion a very good price. Whether the novelty will create the same excitement as its first version, we can only guess, and we will have to wait for feedback from the first users.
