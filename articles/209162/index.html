Fujitsu webinar "Portfolio of services for IT infrastructures"

On January 23, 2014 we offer to take part in a webinar which will consider services for IT infrastructures .

The portfolio of services for IT infrastructures (Managed Infrastructure Services) is designed to meet any customer needs for technical support and maintenance of IT solutions. The customer can extend the terms of the standard warranty or order services for launching and integrating solutions into the IT infrastructure. Regardless of whether the infrastructure belongs to the customer or is provided by Fujitsu, the solution may contain the following main elements:

  • Data Center Infrastructure - engineering systems, servers, data storage systems, network equipment.
  • Management services - administration and management of customer systems.
  • Workplaces are user devices that allow you to access and effectively exchange information.
  • Customer support is a single point of contact and responsibility for the interaction of users and service providers.
  • Fujitsu IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) and SaaS (Software as a Service) services - the ability to use and pay for IT resources according to the actual volume of services consumed.

Extended warranty, integration services, data center infrastructure management services and user support are the main topics of the upcoming webinar.


Date: January 23, 2014
Time: from 13:00 to 14:00 (Moscow time).
Our expert: Anatoly Tretyakov, Fujitsu service product marketing manager.
