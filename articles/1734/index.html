Google "fasten" WebMoney to AdSense

Google is in talks with WebMoney about the possibility of using an electronic payment system in Russia, the newspaper Vedomosti reports. If companies agree, the process of getting adsense advertisements from websites will be much easier.

Recall that a site that has placed contextual advertising from Google receives between 3 and 150 cents in one transition. Previously, the money earned from this advertisement was received by resource owners in the form of a bank check, but it could take months to cash in our country. With the transfer of calculations in WebMoney, this problem will be solved. In addition, the commission will be significantly reduced from the amount - in the electronic payment system, the sender deducts only 0.8% of the amount.

According to Webmoney executive director Victor Turskov, using the system will greatly simplify the process of receiving money from Google. However, it is not known yet whether this opportunity will be two-way - whether Google itself will accept payments from advertisers via WebMoney.
