Do we need a silicon valley? Or a review of Technopark of the Novosibirsk Academgorodok

Did you hear that? I think they did.

While in Russia there was a selection of the most suitable place for this wonder-zone of innovations, Novosibirsk took and built the first stage of Technopark, where in a month the most progressive companies of the city of Novosibirsk will be settled.
