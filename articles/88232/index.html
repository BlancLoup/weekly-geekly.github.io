The first Russian tests Intel Core i5 670 on liquid nitrogen

Today, members of the OCClub.ru team: Smoke, DeDaL and slamms tested the Intel Core i7 670 processor, cooling it with liquid nitrogen. The processor was able to overclock to a frequency of 6733 MHz.



Moreover, the task was to find the maximum frequency at which the processor will be able to perform the Super Pi1M test. Calculate the number of PI to one million decimal places managed at a frequency of 6719 MHz. This is the best result in Russia and 9th place in the world. The potential of the processor is still there, and a more detailed study and selection of settings for the EVGA P55 Classified motherboard will certainly improve the result.
