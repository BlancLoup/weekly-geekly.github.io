Requirements


At the very beginning of the search, I formulated the conditions that, in my opinion, should satisfy the ideal resource. Probably, the requirements are too high - now I understand that. But at an early stage I expected the following from the material provided (in order of decreasing importance):
  • Quality (for example, high resolution in video, layout for books (if I download a book for free , I immediately find several options and then choose the best of them: pay attention to the table of contents, vocabulary footnotes, etc. - in other words, readability))
  • Variability (for movies / TV shows this is the presence of sound tracks and subtitles, for books - different formats)
  • Quantity (the requested material must be on the same resource so that you do not have to register for additional ones)
  • Player (normal buffering, speed, no glitches)
  • Design of the site (in order to avoid bitter tears with each visit to the site)
  • Adequate administration (courtesy, timely responses and assistance)

Conclusion


Once it seemed to me that in some few years the problem of legal services in Russia would be resolved, that this was a matter of technology and everything would be just fine. However, copyright legislation iron sticks bound all available means to implement such an idea. In addition, at the moment legal services are forced to compete with pirated ones. Moreover, the struggle for users is not on an equal footing - pirates have long since managed to ‚Äúshape the minds‚Äù of their consumers: not only that people are used to downloading for free, but also because everyone likes to get what they want.
