Concept


Most products in this area are based on the good old principle of subscription. There is a free functionality (if any), and additional ‚Äúbuns‚Äù are shipped to premium users as a kind of tariff plan.

We approached the matter differently. The system is based on payment management: creation, systematization, structuring, summation, etc. We try to make these operations as convenient as possible. You can enter payments one at a time and in groups, correct the amounts and record the fact of payment - right in the table, without going to the payment editing page. All this - immediately and for free.
