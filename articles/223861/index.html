GOkey: data cable, battery, USB flash drive and key detector in one bottle



I do not even know why no one has invented such a gadget until now. For owners of constantly discharging gadgets and constantly losing keys, this is simply an indispensable device. Now GOkey is raising funds at Indiegogo, and of the planned 40 thousand, the team has already collected $ 372 thousand (as of this writing). Until the end of the campaign another 12 days, so that the campaign itself can be called very successful.

Yes, and this is the explanation. The fact is that the project team created a universal gadget that can charge smartphones, serve as a data cable, work as a flash drive, and also discover keys and smartphones themselves. And all this is in a miniature case, only slightly larger than the size of an average key fob (although there are more charms and more, to be honest).

If more, then the device can:

- Charge the smartphone for 2 hours of additional work;
- Serve as a data cable for devices on both Android and iOS;
- Show on the screen of the smartphone, where are currently the keys of the owner;
- Send a signal to the smartphone so that it beeps, so that it, the smartphone, is easier to detect;
- Work as a flash drive with a capacity of 8 to 32 GB.



In addition, the developers will add the ability to show the latest coordinates of the location of the keys (that is, there will also be a GPS module). This is if the campaign will reach 500 thousand dollars.

And if the campaign dials 750 thousand, then the interface of the flash drive will lead to the USB 3.0 standard (now USB 2.0).



As for the price, it also does not bite. So, for GOkey with 8 GB of flash-drive, the team asks $ 59, for 16 GB the model is 69 US dollars and for the 32 GB model - 79 US dollars.
