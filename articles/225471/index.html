Mover.io: service for cloud migrations



Now we have a serious range of various cloud storages in stock. In some you provide more space for your data, in others less. It often happens that information is distributed between two ++ different services. It would be nice to have a handy tool to bring these remote folders to the desired form without much time and traffic. I discovered this service here.

In short, and in fact, this service invites you to choose the Source and Destination from this list.



By connecting the connectors you have lists of files.
Having chosen the necessary folders in source, you need to specify where to place them at the destination.
Further, clicking the Run button will add a task to transfer your information.

Everything. After some time, the files will be moved. There is an opportunity to monitor the process. Transfer 15 GB took me about an hour.

Thank you for your attention, I hope that this service will be useful to many of you.

PS Small update.
Currently 22 connectors are supported.
Here you can vote in favor of the platform / features you want and soon support will be added. Now the closest candidate to add - MEGA. There will be a convenient opportunity to merge with various small storage in one 50-gigabyte.
