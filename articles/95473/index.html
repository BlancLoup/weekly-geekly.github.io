Fundamentally new developer conference will be held in Nizhny Tagil

On June 7, the day of the beginning of the international WWDC conference, a fundamentally new developer conference, BolgenOS Developer Connect 2010, will take place in Nizhny Tagil. Already now all technical media and forums are flooded with information about the upcoming announcement of the new and revolutionary BolgenOS 2.0, which, according to the main developer BolgenOS, Denis Popov, turn our understanding of personal computers.

According to rumors, actively discussed geeks on Gizmodo and TechCrunch, as well as on the popular profile blog BolgenInsider, the new BolgenOS will have the following fundamentally new features:
  • Download in 4 seconds.
  • An even more minimalistic antivirus without command line.
  • Support for a variety of devices based on memory programming of the processor .
  • Full support for the fundamentally new standard BOLGENML5 in the OgenBrowse browser.

Recall information about the new BolgenOS 2.0 surfaced in the media after a month ago, an unknown person accidentally left the BELL laptop with the early assembly of BolgenOS 2.0 installed in the toilet of the canteen of the Research Institute of Mechanical Engineering in Nizhny Tagil.

At the conference, in addition to the presentation of the revolutionary BolgenOS, the following reports are planned (both invited guests and current developers and top management of the Bolgen Corporation):
  • Denis Popov. Thought about Flash. Why BolgenOS won't have Flash about our relationship with Adobe.
  • Denis Lokhtin (Denis's classmate). What opportunities for developers provides a fundamentally new store Bolgen Store.
  • Artemy Lebedev. Graphic design using PIMP. Design wallpaper using DeviantArt.
  • Gregory Bakunov. Developed under BolgenOS using the bython language using bolgemacs.
  • Alex Exler. Using BolgenOS in the work with texts.
  • Peter Didenko. Why I was disappointed in Microsoft and moved to Bolgen Corporation.
  • Vasily Strelnikov. BolgenOS for podcasting.
  • Evgeny Umputun. The story about the programmer BolgenOS 2.0.

The conference, according to the program, promises to be hot. We have to hear serious reports, participate in vivid discussions, see the creators of BolgenOS live, talk on the sidelines with the most powerful experts in the development using the technologies of Bolgen BCode SDK.

Who will go?
