"Science and Life" and other popular science magazines

Remember, was such a magazine - "Science and Life"? Probably, someone thinks that he is no longer being released because his parents stopped subscribing to it? But no! He exists. Recently, in the subway, I noticed another issue in the hands of a foreign student. He gazed at it with undisguised interest.

This magazine is available to all. And do not think for advertising, because for someone, I am sure it will be a pleasant surprise, a memory:
- The site of the journal "Science and Life" with a small archive over the past 10 years.
- Buy the archive of the journal for 1990-2005. on DVD on the website of the Softkey shop (many payment options), or on the website of the magazine . Keeping a disk is very curious. The price is only from 300 to 400 rubles, depending on the method of payment and delivery - these are mere pennies for such valuable information.
