Registration for DEDF-2012, Moscow, March 30, 2012 is open.



Today, an invitation to take part in the DEDF-2012 electronics developer forum, which will be held on March 30, 2012 in Moscow, Digital October, has fallen into the mail. Forum topic: "Systems on a chip in multimedia and portable devices . " They promise to show projects using:
  • Linux and Android OS based on chips from world vendors, including Texas Instruments (TI)
  • Specialized technologies for the markets of multimedia, datacom, automotive and navigation electronics
  • Digital signal processing
  • JTAG testing
Participation is free . The planned number of participants is 180 people, so that those interested must hurry - registration for listeners is open only until March 16 .

Additional information on the forum site .
