Satya Nadella officially became the CEO of Microsoft. Bill Gates resigned as chairman of the board



Yes, the information announced earlier has been confirmed: Microsoft's employee has become an employee of the same company, Satya Nadella. An Indian has been working in a corporation for 22 years, reaching a peak at a relatively young age. He himself is now - 46 years old. Prior to his appointment as CEO Satya Nadella, he headed the department of cloud and corporate services, and also served as executive vice president.

It is worth noting that this is not the last personnel reshuffle in the corporation, the fact is that simultaneously with the appointment of Nadella, the general director, Bill Gates, who is known to us, left us as chairman.



He will remain in the company, but only as a technical adviser to Satya Nadella.



But the chairman of the board of directors was John Thompson.

At the moment, many expected Satya Nadella to become the company's general director, but few expected Bill Gates to leave the post of chairman of the board of directors. Despite the fact that he himself has already said this several times.

The new head of the company has already said that Microsoft has great prospects ahead, and in order to achieve them, the company must move faster and also change in the process of movement.
