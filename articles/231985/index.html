Perform the calculation of energy consumption for the room type "deaf corridor". Parameters: 6m * 2.5m * 2.8m (height). At the moment in the corridor there are 2 ceiling lights with 2 lamps in each. The number and power of the lamps will be chosen from the consideration of ‚Äúlight enough‚Äù.

Suppose the light in the corridor is on:
Mon-Fri from 7-30 to 9-00 and from 18-30 to 23-00
Sat-Sun from 10-00 to 23-00

Then we get that the work time per month (6 hours work.d. * 5 + 13h out * 2) * 4 = 224 hours of work.

We make a summary table at a cost of 1 kWh 2.3 rubles.

lamp set Power consumption Costs per month
4 SVD lamps, let it be 2 to 7 and 2 to 9 (7 * 2 + 9 * 2) * 224 = 7 kWh 7 * 2.3 = 16.1 rubles
3 lamps of 60 W each 3 * 60 * 224 = 40 kWh 40 * 2.3 = 92 rubles
2 gas lamps 30 * 2 * 224 = 20 kWh 20 * 2.3 = 46 rubles


We make the calculation taking into account the cost of the lamps and the assumption that the incandescent lamp needs to be changed every six months, and the SVD and gas once every 2 years.
We get that:

lamp set Average stand Costs for 2 years
