Most of all I am pleased that some people who looked at my pictures begin to re-read fairy tales-epic, learn a lot of new things and understand why Vasilisa the Beautiful has a doll in her bag, why Water Mom is on a soma, why Ilya Muromets has a sword in her hands, and not a mace, etc. This return to the sources through my humble project pleases me as the author most of all.
