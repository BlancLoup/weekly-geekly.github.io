HP iPaq 614 (610). Appearance to the people.


The representative of the newest line of GPS-communicators from HP came to light! What can interest this thing?
(Under the cut photo)

This model is a kind of debut for HP, as it had never before been presented to all-in-one GPS with a numeric keypad. The first thing that catches your eye is a classic and stylish design, serious and original. The second is the Smart Touch touch disk, located on the keyboard. In fact, it is an analogue of the iPod sensor, but how exactly it will be used in the PDA is not yet known.

What is worth noting from a slightly different point of view is the combination of a Marvell PXA27x processor with a frequency of 520 MHz and 128 MB of RAM. As a result, we have a processor that is absolutely identical to the Intel XScale 520 MHz processor installed on the Asus p535, which, according to mobile-review , is currently the most powerful communicator in the world. But it is worth considering that 64 MB of RAM is installed on the p535, and the HP iPaq communicator has twice as much.

The device has Windows Mobile 6 installed, and it supports almost all the latest communications standards - GSM; GPRS / EDGE Class 10; UMTS / HSDPA; Bluetooth 2.0 EDR Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g and GPS receiver with A-GPS support;
A microSD slot is announced, but most likely SDHC support is not available. TFT screen size - 2.8 inches, 240 x 320 pixels, 65,536 colors.
Also available - 3 MPc camera (in the version iPaq 614c, in the remaining 1 Mpix) with autofocus.

At the moment, the price in the US is $ 600, nothing is known about imports to Russia.
