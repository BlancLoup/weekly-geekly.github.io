Coworking and startup incubator in Novosibirsk. Any interest?

After a recent remarkable post with a report on the success of the Yekaterinburg coworking club "Tower" for the one and a half years of its existence, a logical question arose: why Novosibirsk, the third largest city in Russia, cannot boast such successes?

Being a committed freelancer, I know for sure that a freelancer often wants a human society during the working day, and sometimes the team spirit, and advice. Besides, thanks to my experience in an IT company, I know for sure what great ideas and projects can be born in the informal atmosphere of a programmer team.

And since there are certain opportunities in this direction, I decided to probe the soil and understand whether such a club is needed in Novosibirsk. So, we have:
  • Large room in a new office building in the center of Novosibirsk (for the ‚ÄúFlagman‚Äù)
  • The opportunity to equip a co-working club in this room (furniture, Internet, etc.) for 25 workplaces with comfortable furniture, a seating area and everything necessary for productive creative work.

The only such project in Novosibirsk that I managed to find, in my opinion, is too expensive - I personally, as a freelancer, would definitely not pay more than 7,000 a month for the warmth of human communication. So our idea is to make the membership in this club truly accessible to a freelancer engaged in any activity, and at the same time to create opportunities for the implementation of new projects by joint efforts.

The most important thing is that if such ideas of new projects are really born and realized (and I really want to believe in it, because in creative teams something new and often unique and very necessary is always born), then we have the opportunity to support them in every way, starting with product, legal and marketing consulting and ending with raising funds for the most promising projects. In addition, there are excellent marketing and organizational opportunities to bring these projects to the US market (a company is registered, there is an office, bank accounts, paypal and everything else - and, most importantly, the experience of creating and promoting startups on the western market).
