Adobe Shadow - make the development of mobile sites easier and faster.

Each of us, at least once in the development of mobile versions of the site, thought about how you can speed up the development process, debugging and quick viewing of changes. Someone raised a local server for this, someone put emulators in order not to reach out to the phone for viewing, someone made more crutch solutions. The guys from Adobe Lab took care of us and released great applications today for these needs. Drum roll, meet - Adobe Shadow!

Adobe Shadow is a completely new tool that helps front-end developers and designers to maximize the efficiency and speed of developing mobile versions of websites by optimizing the process of viewing changes and debugging on mobile devices. Shadow works independently of other products from Adobe and is completely free for now!


Today version 1.0.0 has been released, in which developers have focused on the following features:
  • Linking devices to each other;
  • Synchronized surfing and updating pages on your computer and mobile devices;
  • Remote debugging for instant display of HTML, CSS and JavaScript changes.

How to use all this business? Very simple!
To start, download the desktop version of Adobe Shadow ( Mac OS X , Windows , Linux most likely will not, this is Adobe ).
Then, we put the add- on in your browser, unfortunately, so far only Google Chrome is supported.
And finally, we put the application on a mobile device ( AppStore , Android Market ). We launch, connect our devices and enjoy the convenience!
