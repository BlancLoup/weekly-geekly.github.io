CodeFest 2015 Main Topics



By tradition, on the last weekend of March, we hold the largest IT conference outside the Urals. On March 28-29, we meet in the same place where last year and the year before, and the year before last - at the Novosibirsk Expo Center. The reasons are the same: to get acquainted with cool speakers, chat with colleagues in the industry and make some noise at afterparty.

There are seven tracks in the program: mobile-, frontend- and backend-development, testing, product and project management, design and the flow of homeowners - this is how we call the discussion panels.



The conference will be opened by keynote speaker and evangelist Dino Esposito. Dino will tell you why patterns, flexible architecture and auto-tests will not make a useful and popular product out of your software .



This year's trends : lambda architecture , CQRS , private clouds , reactive programming and microservices . We will also discuss ambitious projects: Jsquery in PostgreSQL, minimal CoreOS , C framework for PHP Phalcon . Be sure to analyze the architecture of high-loaded backends: Badoo mobile and Banki.ru service .



Frontend does not stand still and develops very quickly. Let's look at how things work with functional programming in the browser ; is it possible to render web pages using webgl . Let's talk about whether BEM is suitable for studio development. We recognize the pros and cons of one of the most popular frontend frameworks - AngularJS . And not only that, as you guess.



In the field of mobile development and gadgets, the nails of our program are Internet of Things , a functional programming approach and advanced A / B testing . In addition, consider how to work with OpenGL ES 2.0 and synchronize client data without pain.
