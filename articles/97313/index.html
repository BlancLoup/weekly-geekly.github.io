The charge for traffic in mobile Yandex.Maps for MegaFon subscribers is canceled.

From today, Yandex launches a program of cooperation with mobile operators, the purpose of which is to cancel payment for traffic for mobile Yandex.Maps. The first were lucky subscribers of MegaFon Moscow, the Moscow region, the Central region of Russia, Siberia, the Urals, the Caucasus and the Far East. Downloading a special version of the application ( link for phones), which is currently available for Windows Mobile, Symbian and Java, they can use Yandex.Maps absolutely free of charge today. In July subscribers of the Volga region and the North-West branch will join them.
