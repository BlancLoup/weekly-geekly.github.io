Welcome to Moscow PM 06/02

On February 6, for the first time this year, the Mail.Ru Group company will open the doors to the Moscow.pm community (a group of Moscow Perl programmers).

As part of this event, you have an exclusive opportunity to listen to the reports of the best specialists, to exchange experience.




Place and time: February 6 from 7.30 pm to 10 pm, meeting number 1 on the 2nd floor of the Mail.Ru Group office.

In the program you are waiting for reports:

"Development and optimization of the Perl program for Raspberry Pi", Ilya Chesnokov, Developer of the UK2 Group.



A short story about how Ilya programmed in Perl under Raspberry PI: he prepared the development environment, developed and optimized the program to search for relevant advertisements.

Raspberry PI is a credit card-sized computer based on an ARM processor with a clock frequency of 700 MHz, 512 MB of RAM. The operating system boots from the SD card.
