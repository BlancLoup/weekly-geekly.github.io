Russian Design Cup 2016

October 10th Odnoklassniki and Mail.Ru Group launch the fifth Russian Design Cup . This is the oldest and most famous tournament for Russian-speaking designers, which has been running since 2012 - last year we gathered 210 participants and more than 300 solutions.



RDC gives an excellent opportunity for beginners and more experienced designers to pump their skills on tasks close to reality. Format - three rounds, in which a variety of problems are solved for the currently relevant platforms and industries. Tasks in real-world constraints are much more interesting and useful for development. Moreover, they capture modern platforms (bots, virtual reality, smart watches, etc.) and skills (mobile design, animation, etc.).

As always in the competition strong prizes - MacPro for the first place and a pair of aces in the sleeve for the second and third. The best solution for each of the tasks receives an additional special prize from our partners - Mann Ivanov Ferber, ICQ , State Services and others.



And also - a powerful jury with five top domestic designers. Feedback is very important for the development of skills, and most decisions of the Russian Design Cup in addition to evaluating several parameters are also accompanied by a comment on strengths and weaknesses.



  • Anton Yarusov , art director Innova and teacher of the British Higher School of Design
  • Dmitry Karpov , curator of courses at the British High School of Design , Scream School and Bang! Bang! Education
  • Sergey Popkov , founder of AIC studio (No. 1 in the ranking of web studios Ruward 2016)
  • Leonid Ivakhov , Head of Odnoklassniki Design
  • Yuri Vetrov , head of the Mail.Ru Group portal design team


Now we accept registration, and then we will announce the task for the first selection round. Get connected .
