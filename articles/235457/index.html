Google and Bungie created the interactive world of Destiny using Street View



A very interesting idea occurred to the guys from Bungie - the company that created the game Destiny. Together with the developers of Google, an interactive world was created based on the Google Street View service.

With this development, you can go beyond Earth, and see what is happening on the Moon, Mars or Venus. Of course, everything that can be seen is related to the game world of Destiny: units, mobs, in-game objects - all this looks like the present.

You can get comprehensive information about each object by simply hovering the mouse over the object. In addition, due to the device and the game world, the relationship between the worlds of clans, etc. Actually, this is the first time that a game world, rather than a real world, has been applied to the Google service map.

Apparently, the guys from Bungie clearly know what to do to attract new people to the game world.
