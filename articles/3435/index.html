Google Reader - the most popular aggregator on the Internet

The Google Reader team has finally started publishing the number of subscribers for each RSS feed. Judging by the published figures, Google Reader aggregator is almost the most popular on the Internet, at least among geeks. True, here the Google developers are a little cunning.

Google Reader developers have disclosed information about the number of subscribers to each RSS-feed. The numbers are not yet shown in the Google Reader interface, but they are already in the crawler ‚Äúuser-agent‚Äù header.

According to the official FAQ , the ‚Äúuser-agent‚Äù header now contains four parameters: the name of the crawler (‚ÄúFeedFetcher-Google‚Äù), the feed URL, its unique 64-bit identifier and the number of subscribers. It looks like this.

If one feed is published to different URLs, the statistics take them into account individually, because different URLs will have different identifiers.

Starting from Saturday, the number of Google Reader subscribers is also taken into account in FeedBurner statistics and other analytical systems. This suggests that Google Reader after a short time after its creation ( interview with developers, video ) has already become one of the most popular RSS aggregators on the Internet.

True, there is a small trick. The fact is that the number of subscribers includes not only those who read the RSS feed through Google Reader, but also the owners of personalized pages of Google Personalized Homepage. But RSS feeds are integrated into other Google products. In the future, they will also be taken into account in the statistics.
