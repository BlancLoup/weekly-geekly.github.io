We are looking for the owner of a working Toshiba laptop of the very first series

So, we are looking for the owner of a working Toshiba laptop of the very first series. We will be happy (and, most importantly, publicly) to give the first person who gives the notebook of this series to our museum in working condition - an ultra-modern, powerful notebook from the premium Toshiba Qosmio line.

Also, we are forming a gallery of Toshiba laptops.
Send us modern photos of your laptops, with a description of the parameters, performance and brief information about when it was purchased, how long you used it, what you liked in your work. The most interesting stories will be published in the Toshiba Gallery.

For sweetness: among those who decide to transfer their faithful Toshiba from 1985 to 2005 to the museum, we will raffle off a laptop from the Satellite line.
Also publicly and with the same pleasure.
