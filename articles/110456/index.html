Startup Poster

In RuNet, there is a new service for tracking news in the world of startups. Such a startup about startups. Or rather, about sites, incubators, conferences, etc. The idea to collect information about all the sites in one place and provide a single entrance is very logical and is not something that was in the air, but just begged for implementation.

And now it has materialized in the form of a Startup Poster service .

Everything works very simply: go to the site and enter your email in a single window. And from that moment you begin to receive a weekly digest of upcoming events. Like good old times.

There is also a news feed (which you can subscribe to via RSS, as opposed to the main block).

The inability to subscribe via RSS to the main materials of the site is similar to a low start, and probably with time it will be corrected.

In general, in my opinion such a service has long been lacking. There are a lot of business incubators in Russia and the former CIS, including IT conferences, codefests, developers camps, etc.
