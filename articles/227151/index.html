How to do business with friends


Best of all - no way. Everyone probably heard about it. But I thought this: these are my friends. I am sure of them. If someone is unlucky, it does not mean that I, too, will be unlucky. How to choose mates / companions


You can find a smart, talented, responsible person who has something behind them. Such as you. This person will agree to work with you, get inspired by the idea and start working. And there will be fruit. Just consider one thing: such a person will have his own ideas, which will be more interesting to him, and which he will implement himself. Such a person will leave you at any time. Keep this in mind.

How to allocate shares in the project


By offering another person to work on the project, you will inevitably determine the shares in the project. If your project idea is yours, then always keep at least 70%. Then you will be able to reduce your share in favor of the companion, but only then, after the results. No 50/50, otherwise the situation may turn out when your companion worked first and then stopped, and still insists that he has a 50% share.

How to distribute shares in the project 2


Do not reduce your share in the project below 50%. Yes, you started working. Your companion works. It seems to work well. He promises that everything is cool and a lot of work. Who to start the project with?


With no one. On their own. Do it yourself. Make a damn prototype alone. Do not offer anything to anyone, do not involve anyone in the project.
