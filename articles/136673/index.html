Who is it useful?

IMHO - many of us!

I think that almost everyone is familiar with the situation when it is necessary to very quickly transfer information about the location: to tell where the meeting will take place and how best to get, reach, fly? Pinpoint allows you to mark and share a point on the map in 1-2 mouse clicks.

Realtors and retailers!

It is easy to place and store business objects on the map, organize and sort by subject, send the conditions, offers and photos of the object to the client in one link.



Architects and photographers!

Conveniently, when a professional portfolio is always at hand. It is enough to place the projects and locations on the map, attach photos or pictures to them, come up with short convenient names for links (URL) to project folders (sets). How to work with the service?


Everything is simple here. And it was strange if it were otherwise for public service. Registration, as it should be, takes a few seconds, and you immediately get down to work.

First, a convenient, understandable and familiar Flickr interface.
The scheme of work is elementary, with a maximum of three steps:
  1. Created a point (mark on the map or enter the address),
  2. Added a description and pictures, add tags and select the level of privacy.

From a technical point of view, Pinpoint is a platform for storing and organizing geodata and related information. The Web is just one of the possible interfaces for interacting with the service. The existing iphone application using the Pinpoint API allows you to fully use the service in any place, wherever you are, which is very important for geolocation services. The API allows you to embed Pinpoint functionality (primarily the sharing of geo-data) into other applications and create new services based on their combination. For example, using our API, you can embed an incident search function in your area or city, and there are a lot of similar applications in the news site. Development of the platform and the addition of new features (maybe even not presented on the site) is a priority for us.
