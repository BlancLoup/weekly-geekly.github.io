In the Moscow region will be able to watch the graves of relatives online

Municipalities of the Moscow region will offer relatives of the deceased to install video surveillance of the graves, Moscow writes 24 . The service will be paid, the price is not reported.

Video surveillance will be included in the commercial grave care complex, the results of which will be sent by the municipalities to customers. Remote observation of the graves has two functions: it will help in the fight against vandals and will allow you to monitor the state of burial when it is impossible to come by yourself.

In the Moscow region there are 1559 cemeteries, of which 1,133 cemeteries are open for burial. 15% of Muscovites who have passed away are buried in the Moscow cemeteries.
