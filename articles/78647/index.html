New Year's gifts from e-Style ISP

This year, e-Style ISP has opened its third own data center. A new generation facility that meets international standards of quality and reliability, a data center with a TIER 3+ reliability level - the highest category represented by data centers in Russia.
The power of the data center allows e-Style ISP to offer high quality services at reasonable prices. That in the post crisis situation is very important.

In connection with the New Year holidays, e-Style ISP has set up holiday tariffs for the service of collocation of servers in its own data center.
More information about the New Year collocation can be found on the website.
In addition to holiday tariffs for hosting servers, the company is working on the formation of new offers and other services, including data center services.

As an educational program for clients, e-Style ISP conducts excursions to the data center. That allows not only to learn, but to see how, where and in what conditions the equipment is located. What services the company offers, as well as ask all the questions of interest to leading experts.
I would like to note that the first excursions gave very interesting results.
And for those who cannot come in person, a virtual tour of the data center is presented on the company's website .

The change in tariffs is due to the change in demand for data center services.
In this connection, and for other services, the company plans to make interesting offers.
Services e-Style ISP:
Traditional communication services: Internet, telephony, rental of communication channels
Data center services: server hosting and rental, virtual servers, telecommunication cabinets rental
Hosting services: hosting sites, hosting services, domain registration.
Ready solutions for business based on licensed software.
Network integration services.
