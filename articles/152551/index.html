It is against this background that the player has never worn a string and will have to begin to establish dominance over the surrounding reality. However, the plot in this game is a purely secondary matter, a very pleasant graphical performance, subtly reminiscent of Heroes of Might and Magic II, and the gameplay itself are of much greater interest. Upon completion of manipulations with the choice of race, the player enters the capital management screen - his first and so far only city, representing a hexagonal field, the number of cells of which depends on the level of the City Hall , one of the two buildings initially available in the city. The second is the Gate , which - lo and behold - behave like the most ordinary gate, in an open state (to which it is necessary to live - for the initial opening with the solemn cutting of the red ribbon, it is necessary to study the corresponding science, which is far from the beginning of the research tree) to perform combat missions, send workers to slaughter in the villages, explore the territory, establish new cities, in a word, do everything that requires the creatures to leave the city walls. Initially, only a small part of the buildings is available for construction - for the construction of the rest it is required to carry out the relevant research. After the player has sufficiently developed the capital, has equipped it with settlements and opened the gates, you can begin to expand their areas of influence: searching, arranging and protecting from the encroachments of opponents of external possessions - mines, adits and gold veins, where the resource extraction rate is much higher rather than in cities, founding new cities, finding ruins and salt lakes, which is perhaps the main task - after all, the last two types of locations bring the same black pearls . The salt lake works like any other external possession - it brings a stable amount of black pearls in a certain period of time. Another way (except, of course, the purchase of black pearls for real currency), the extraction of the treasured resource is an auction, where lots of gold with the sale of PCs regularly appear.



Now about the most interesting - the possibility of displaying game currency in real currency. After registration, the player is given a promotional period, during which he decides for himself whether he wants to play for free, but without the possibility of withdrawing funds, or pay a subscription (including black pearls earned in the game) and play with a premium account, which provides quite convenient additional features such as creating a queue of research and construction of buildings and, most importantly, the ability to display game currency in real. Subsequently, the subscription can be stopped to pay and go to a free game scheme, but the transition from free mode to premium is not possible - the decision must be made immediately after the promotional period, otherwise you will have to start all over again. The withdrawal scheme is quite simple, Black Pearl from an account on a particular server is converted into a common internal currency ML $ (that is, you can play on different servers in parallel and withdraw accumulated BJ to a common in-game account), and already ML $ is transferred to a WMZ wallet from 5% commission.
