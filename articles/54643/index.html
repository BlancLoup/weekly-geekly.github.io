March 17 can be registered two million domain RU

At the beginning of the day on March 16th, there were ~ 1,985,800 domains in the RU domain, about 15,000 registrations remained before the registration of the two-million domain.

The daily growth of the RU zone is about 1500 domains. On weekends and Mondays, the ru domains are not removed, so the increase can reach 2500 domains per day.

It would seem that 15,000 registrations can take another week, but, as practice shows, in front of round numbers, user activity increases significantly. If it is the same as before registering a millionth domain, then we can expect an increase in the number of registrations by 6-8 times. On the day of registration, the million-plus increase amounted to 12,000 domains, at the usual rate for that for 1300 names. However, it is necessary to make a reservation that in 2007 the millionth domain hanti.ru was registered with a powerful jerk - a mass order from one domain company over 5,000 domains.

Another interesting point is that theoretically a two millionth domain can be registered twice. The deletion of domains ru is not carried out from Saturday to Monday, these are an additional several thousand domains that bring us closer to the cherished line. The jubilee domain can be registered in the afternoon of March 17, and in the evening several thousand names will be removed and the total number of registered domains ru will again fall below the two millionth bar.
