Flux: electric bikes line for all riding styles



From the very beginning of spring, projects, prototypes and already run-in models of the newest electric cars broke out from laboratories and workshops for the judgment of a sophisticated community. One of them is FLUX , which the creators call none other than Tesla S among bicycles. The project was successfully launched on the site Indiegogo, collecting for the month almost $ 500 thousand.



The attention of biker bikers presents three models of the family, designed for different riding styles. City electric bikes Roadster, for rough terrain - Trial, and for MTB fans - Attack.

The main elements in all models are identical, there are small variations in the design. In the Attack modification, a more powerful lithium-ion energy storage device is installed:



Bike design

All models are built on light aluminum frames, forks differ depending on the type of bike. The model for MTB has a rear shock absorber, plus, as already said, a more powerful 17 A battery (gives out 612 Wh). The models for the city and the trial have 13 A batteries (468 Wh).

As for the basic elements of construction and electronic equipment, they are the same for all members of the FLUX family:



Two main distinguishing points from fellow market: battery and the location of the electric motor. The source of energy in the FLUX line is the same as in the Ilona Mask cars - Samsung's lithium-ion storage cells. Without recharging the bike can drive 74 km with a maximum speed of 40 km / h.
