A note on how to sit in front of the computer.

Most of us, including you,% username%, spend a lot of time in a sitting position in front of the monitor. Many people at the same time do physical exercises and work out in the gym - this solves the problem, but only partially. Sitting at all is bad (unnatural) for a long time, but it is wrong to sit at all wrongly. Since most of us cannot give up long meetings, we will talk about how to sit and sit comfortably and properly, so that our eyes, back and arms do not hurt.

It is important to say that there is no definite prescription that would suit everyone without exception, and many questions are individual. The main thing is not to ignore them and not hush up - then you will achieve a result, otherwise you are probably waiting for:
1. Myopia.
2. Curvature of the spine.
3. Pain in the hands.

First, let's deal with the details of our seat, in the train of life. Then I will talk about some tricks and exercises.

Eye exercises


If you look into the distance all the time, then after some time you will stop seeing well up close. And if all the time to look at objects at close range, then you will stop seeing distant objects. This is because the muscles in our eyes are not a bit different from any others, and when the eye needs to focus - shrink or stretch - muscle strength is needed. When the muscles cease to tense regularly, they weaken, and the eye stops focusing correctly, hence a blurred image appears as in photographs, where objects are not in focus.
To avoid such problems, it is necessary to look into the distance, look over to close objects, and then again into the distance.
If you have already developed myopia / hyperopia, I can advise you to watch the lectures of Zhdanov V. G. or go to a session.

Do not sit at the computer in poor light and do not read!
Turn on the light first, then the monitor. Turn off the monitor first and then the light.
The fact is that our eyes catch the light with the help of the pupils, and they can narrow and expand. When there is a lot of light, they narrow, reducing their sensitivity, when there is little - they expand, capture more light and then it is better seen in the semi-darkness. After all, you noticed that if you turn off the light, you will first see badly, and then ‚Äúyour eyes will get used to the darkness‚Äù - this is just the pupils dilated. And when you get up in the morning, your eyes will first be cut from the light, this is because in the dream the pupils are dilated and too sensitive for daylight. It is worth noting that the expanded state of the pupil is natural, and in this position the eye rests at rest. Squeezing the pupil is an effort.
When you look at the monitor or read in the dark, the pupils are dilated and the light causes them great damage!

The monitor is better to put away from the face, for this you need a wide table. Also, I advise him to move during the day, when you want to move closer, when you want further.
