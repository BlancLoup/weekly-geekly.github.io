Contest jointly with Doctor Web

BitByBit and Doctor Web are organizing from November 9 to December 28, 2010, 3 competitions for developers for the best lesson, an article on programming and an article on data protection on file servers.
Anyone wishing to take part in the competition must register and add a publication on the website bitbybybit.ru. BitByBit is a dedicated portal for programmers. The material should correspond to the subject of the site. Publications about programming, development and protection of information will be considered.
The first competition for the best lesson will be held from November 9 to November 30, the second for the best article on programming from November 23 to December 14, and the competition for the best article on data protection on file servers, which will take place from December 7 to December 28, will end.
You can see and evaluate all the work on the site bitbybit.ru. Feedback and evaluation by site visitors posted within each competition are accepted until 23:59 hours on December 28, 2010.
For each competition, based on the ratings of visitors to the site bitbybit.ru, 5 best publications will be identified, the authors of which will choose one of the products for home users (licensed for 1 PC / 1 year) from Doctor Web as a prize. The list of winners of all three competitions will be published on biybybit.ru on December 29, 2010.
