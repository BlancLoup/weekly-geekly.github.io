DevCon 2014 conference program published


Dear software developers and testers, participants of DevCon 2014 conference. On behalf of the organizers, I am pleased to present you the program of the two days of the conference, which included reports in five parallel tracks, three exclusive workshops and an evening program with round tables and two hackathons

At http://www.msdevcon.ru/schedule you can find the current conference schedule.

Below I will talk about some details of the program DevCon 2014.

Main program



The main program of the conference includes a plenary report, keynotes and mini-sessions. In total, conference participants and online viewers will be able to choose from 55 very different reports .

Note! The entire main conference program will be recorded and broadcast online in real time! If you are unlucky, and you can not take part in DevCon 2014 personally, then visit the official website http://msdevcon.ru on May 28 and 29 to view the sessions online.


This year we have decided to make the conference program and reports more in-depth. Therefore, more than a third of the DevCon 2014 reports will be level 300 or higher.

The first day of DevCon 2014


The conference will be opened by a two-hour plenary report , within which, according to tradition, a general overview of all company news for developers over the last year since the last conference will be given.

On the first day, along with the plenary report, participants and online conference viewers will be able to attend 20 sessions on all software development topics: client and mobile application development, web and cloud application development and development, development tools, and corporate application development.

The second day of DevCon 2014


The main program of the second day of the conference will open with 30 minute interesting mini-reports. You can learn about a lot of interesting things from our partners and invited speakers.

The entire second day will be filled with technical reports. During 25 hour presentations, conference participants and live viewers will be able to learn a lot of interesting things about the most modern software development approaches and the latest tools.

The final of the conference will be the official closing ceremony, during which the results will be summed up, the winners of the competitions will be announced and memorable gifts will be given out.

Additional program


DevCon 2014 also includes an additional program, exclusive for participants who arrived at the venue in person. Reports and workshops of the program will not be recorded or broadcast online and can only be visited in person.
