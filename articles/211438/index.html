JetBrains Day in Yaroslavl

From time to time, our friends and colleagues call us to visit. It happens that we are ready to visit the next street, and sometimes - a few hundred kilometers from our office. In November 2013, there was just such a chance: friends from the company Akvelon invited us to Yaroslavl University. Since we happen to be in Yaroslavl infrequently, then the topics for conversation were typed for the whole day, which we called so: Day JetBrains .

The program was very dense, and there were reports designed for different levels of training: it all started with reports for students without serious industrial development experience, and after lunch, developers with experience would have to go massively. Akvelon even chartered a bus, which brought both developers and students from Ivanovo.
