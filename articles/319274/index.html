How we went through the UMNIK program for receiving a grant from beginning to end with the CNC machine tool design project

Hello! I would like to tell about the UMNIK program aimed at receiving a grant from the Assistance Fund, about our development of a safe and simple CNC machine tool FIGIRE and about where we are going.

A little bit about the program UMNIK



UMNIK is a competition aimed at receiving a grant from the Foundation for Assistance to Small Forms of an Enterprise. If your project passes, you will receive a grant of 500,000 rubles for 2 years to implement your idea. In the first year, they give 200,000 rubles, the next - 250,000 rubles, and at the end of the second year another 50,000 rubles for their own expenses. Your idea must in one way or another be scientific and innovative, and most importantly, it must be commercialized. Anyone between the ages of 18 and 30 inclusive can participate in the project. The competition itself is held in 2 stages. Semifinal and final. At the same time, the final is held in the regional center once a year. Semifinals are quite a lot. At the same time, they are not only full-time, but also in absentia. If your project did not pass the semi-final in one place, then you can safely go to another place to the next semi-final, and perform there. There are no restrictions in this regard.
