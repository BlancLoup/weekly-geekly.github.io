Combining two Internet channels into one.



Half a year tormented the familiar sysadmin with the question of combining two channels into the Internet. He was going to build a Linux server for this. Then I experimented with Asus 500 - there you can also configure Linux and start the services. In general, the canoe lasted a long time: (While I myself did not use the Internet to vent the issue.

And now: DI-LB604
Express EtherNetwork 4-port router with load balancing function and 2 WAN ports.

I hasten to share with you joy. For 3000 re you will have a great device that will help:
- merge two channels into intrnet
- balances the load on the channels
- in case of a fall of one of the channels, it automatically redistributes the load (i.e., the channels are reserved for each other)

Read about its other features on the manufacturer's website: www.dlink.ru DI-LB604 .
