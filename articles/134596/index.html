Rambler Cashier: now in QIWI Terminals

Today, Rambler Cashier began selling movie tickets at 120,000 QIWI Terminals - a service from the Internet went to the streets of cities.



In any terminal QIWI now there is a section "Movie tickets". Here you can see the schedule of films, choose a convenient session and a cinema. After that, the buyer is automatically offered tickets to the best available seats in the hall, which are as easy to pay as to replenish the balance of a mobile phone. After payment, the user is issued a check, which shows the name of the cinema, the date and time of the session, the film, and an SMS with the ticket code is sent to the mobile phone entered at the time of payment. With this code, tickets can be printed at the box office or at the cinema ticket terminals.
