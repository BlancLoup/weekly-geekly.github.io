Packages of games and applications on the App Store




With the release of iOS 8, developers have a new opportunity to sell products in the store via packages. Now you can see offers from ZeptoLab, Rovio and many other large companies. All packages are divided into three categories: application packages, game packages and application packages of the day. The main feature of the packages, which is obligatory, is a discount. By the way, there are not so many requirements for packages yet:

  • the package should be cheaper than all the applications included in it;
  • one publisher can post up to 10 packages at the same time;
  • one application can be simultaneously in no more than three packages;
  • if at least one of the applications included in the package is withdrawn from sale, then the package will also be withdrawn.

It is not yet clear whether it will be possible to distribute packages of free-to-play-applications, but this is a great opportunity to show customers the entire series of games at once and offer to purchase it at a discount. Packages of games will also be displayed in the tops of paid and cash applications.
