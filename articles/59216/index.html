For 44% of Runet users, Internet is more important than food and clothing.

The RU-metric project based on Rambler TOP100 rating data, the Rambler search engine itself and the results of its own online survey provided information on the impact of the crisis on the Russian segment of the Internet, namely on the dynamics of network resource attendance and entire Runet sectors, changes in Russians' interest in a crisis, Russians attitude to the Internet.

Willingness users to abandon the Internet




As it turned out, almost half of the users (44%) would not refuse the Internet even if they did not have enough money for food and clothing. Donate the Internet, if they have a shortage of funds to buy food, ready to 25% of users. And 19 percent of respondents say they are easily prepared to abandon the Internet without giving reasons.

The usefulness of the Internet




People are aware of the utility that the Internet gives them - only 5% of respondents do not see the utility in using the network. But the respondents presented a lot of options for benefiting from the network: these are savings on communication in general and phone calls in particular (21%), cost reduction for various purchases (16%) and the ability to download interested software, music and movies at any time (52% answered). Also, in a crisis, the network is rightfully considered one of the most economical leisure options.

Another study found that 69% use information obtained from the Internet in their work, and 7% earn money online.
