Hewlett-Packard opens a development center in St. Petersburg

Hewlett-Packard has seven development centers in different parts of the world - in Beijing (China), Bangalore (India), Bristol (United Kingdom), Haifa (Israel), Tokyo (Japan). Headquarters HP Labs - this is the common name of these centers - is located in Palo Alto (California, USA), and the last seventh center - in St. Petersburg. It opened just today, January 22.

The professional resource of the northern capital is claimed by world and domestic leaders of the IT industry.
