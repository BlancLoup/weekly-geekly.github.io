IcoFX - free icon editor



At work, it took to draw a couple of simple icons, and I began to look for a free program to create them (buying a pirated editor is a pity for money, a licensed one - especially, it is a pity for money).
Found. Thanks to the blog Free alternative. I am not a professional in creating icons, so I cannot express an enlightened opinion about this program. I will be glad to hear the opinion of the public, sophisticated in this matter.



In my opinion a very nice program. Allows you to create icons of all standard sizes and color schemes for Windows and Mac, import pictures of different formats, contains many popular effects and filters, etc. A pleasant moment was the presence of the Russian language in the interface settings.

You can download IcoFX here , and if you want to visit the program's website, go here .
