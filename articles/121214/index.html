Evernote for iOS has the opportunity to share notes on social networks and much more.

The Evernote for iOS update released this weekend has received several new interesting features. We also made a number of internal improvements and fixes. Read more about the updates read below.

Download Evernote for iOS (version 4.0.4) from the iTunes App Store

Share your notes via Twitter and Facebook on your iPhone, iPad or iPod


Evernote for iPhone or iPad allows you to instantly save worthwhile ideas and remember all the interesting things you encounter in everyday life. In addition to this, you now have the opportunity immediately, ‚Äúwithout departing from the ticket office,‚Äù to share your notes with friends in social services.



To share them, open the note and click on the corresponding icon. A list of options appears that includes Twitter, Facebook and email. Both Twitter and Facebook will require authorization with your account for the first time in order to allow Evernote publishing. Next, you will need to confirm the publication of the new record. The note to which you will open access will be available for viewing to anyone who follows the published link.

This is a significant step in the development of the social capabilities of our client for iOS. Soon it will be added the ability to open for other users and notebooks entirely.

New options for sorting notebooks and tags


If you are used to navigating through notes using notebook and tag lists, then you should like some new sorting options that will make working with the application more convenient. A new icon has appeared in the list of notebooks and tags in the upper left corner. Click on it to view the sorting options. Sorting alphabetically or by number of notes is available. Optionally, you can hide empty notebooks or tags.



View snippets of notes in iPhone and iPod landscape mode


Now you can view notes in fragments and in landscape mode. To do this, simply turn your phone to the side when you have a list of notes. You will see large thumbnails if there are images in the notes, as well as fragments of text from the notes. This mode allows you to display more data about the notes in the general list and makes viewing the notes more informative.



Many improvements


This update also includes a number of improvements that touched search speed, note caching, synchronization, improved overall stability, and much more. Many other interesting features for iOS are in our work. Follow the news.
