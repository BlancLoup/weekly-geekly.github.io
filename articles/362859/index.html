Belkin and Mr. Coffee will teach "smart" to make coffee

A really hot novelty was prepared by Belkin. This time, lucky coffee lovers, whose life should be easier with a smart coffee maker Mr. Coffee Smart Coffeemaker . The device costs $ 149.99 and makes coffee without direct human intervention. Nearly. The process is controlled using a smartphone and the WeMo application for iOS (7.0 and higher) and Android (4.0 and higher). You set the time when the drink will be ready - a notification about this will come as a push message - and get ready to gradually get out from under the blanket and go to the kitchen.



Using the application, you can schedule a coffee preparation schedule for the whole week, and if the plans have changed, make quick changes. What is convenient: thanks to push-notifications, you will immediately find out when you need to clean the coffee maker. True, this will have to do manually. There is no app for that yet.

The chrome-plated, double-walled, double-wall decanter with vacuum insulation contains up to 10 cups of hot drink. A full coffee pot will be ready in 8 minutes. According to the manufacturer, it is 20% faster than competitors' coffee makers. There is only one button on the panel and a Wi-Fi signal LED indicator, which simplifies interaction with the kitchen device.



Minus one: if you have lost the Internet, you have to be dragged into the kitchen in order not to be left without an invigorating drink.
