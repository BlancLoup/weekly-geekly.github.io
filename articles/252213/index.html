The Seventh Annual Microsoft Research Summer School in Machine Learning and Intelligence - Cooperation with ACM Europe

Hello!

As we wrote earlier , the annual Microsoft Research Summer School on machine learning will open for the seventh time on July 29 in St. Petersburg.
Usually, the post following the opening post is written when the speakers are known, but here I could not resist.



The school received support from ACM Europe! There will be a speaker from ACM, and all participants will receive the status of a professional member of the association (ACM Professional Membership) and access to the digital library (ACM Digital Library) for one year . In one of the evenings, the association organizes a party (beer party) for school participants. For us, this is a great joy - this happens for the first time, and the speaker from ACM, who everyone knows as the oldest IT organization, is a value for the audience.

We remind you that you still need to register here .
