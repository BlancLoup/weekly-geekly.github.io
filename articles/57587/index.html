Gyazo - the minimalist screenshot creator

One screenshot is worth a hundred words.
Very often, the developer / tester has to create screenshots - to illustrate a bug, point out an inaccuracy in the description, just quickly transfer a section of the screen to a friend with a funny quote from a blog or a cool match between the news and the banner.
How do non-advanced users? Print screen -> Paste to Photoshop / Paint -> Save as -> Upload to Imageshack -> get the link. Fuh.
Advanced use some kind of service, such as clip2net ( presentation on Habr√© ). It allows you to significantly save time on creating a screenshot, but you still need to click about ten times and go through several screens to get your link.
Decision
Gyazo is an extremely simple utility. One click - launch the application, a second click - select an area on the screen. Everything - after that, the program will save the screenshot on its server (in PNG), open the image in an open browser and store it in the clipboard.
There are versions of the distribution under win, and under linux, and under mac.
