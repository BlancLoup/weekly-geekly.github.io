What can you do in 2 days?

The list of things - big or not - everyone will have their own. And what can you do in 2 days with a whole team of like-minded people? Isn't it true, the range of affairs is increasing dramatically. And not only a circle of affairs, but also ideas - alone you will not even take up the implementation of a complex promising project, but if you are helped by equally positively charged people ...

Therefore, we propose to spend the weekend in a pleasant immersion in new ideas and the preparation of new projects with a team of such initiative and enterprising people as you. Communication, entrepreneurial spirit, working atmosphere - all this awaits you at the second Startup Weekend in Moscow , which will be held April 9-11 in MIRBIS.
