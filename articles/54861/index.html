"Agency of Special Orders"

I am a freelancer, so I am trying to maximize my freedom. Recently registered on porucheno.ru . I signed up for all kinds of alerts and orders began to arrive, and the third in a row even gave me away for execution. We had to buy a nice bouquet of 3 long fresh roses without stint and hand it to the girl at her home address. I liked the quest, especially to design a bouquet not for my money. =) I spent a little more than an hour on it, and earned 500 rubles. The cost of the bouquet and my payment I was transferred to a personal wallet, and later I brought them from Yandex money.
If this topic unwind in full it would be great. You can thus order the execution of any task in any city and even have a guarantor of the implementation of this business. The third party is what is usually lacking. Here the responsible manager monitors the quality and timeliness of implementation. They even called me a couple of times.
I do not know what kind of commission they charge for execution, but as far as I know more than similar services do not exist. Thank God that I have the right people in Moscow, otherwise I would have become a customer of orders myself. =)
These startups have a future. I mean startups that are closely related to real life and real money.
