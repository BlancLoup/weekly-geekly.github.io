Support for Deep Learning Researchers

Habr, we got an idea here ... At the moment, we have a certain off-season between different educational programs. We thought why our infrastructure should be idle when there are people who could do something cool on this infrastructure.

We decided to make a small contribution to the development of deep learning in Russia and to allocate 3 virtual servers with GPU to those who do something in this area. We decided to give 2 virtual girls to our graduates, and to give one virtual user to someone from outside.



The conditions are as follows:

- for 2 weeks we give you access to a virtual server with a single Tesla M60 GPU and all the necessary environments (Torch, TF, Keras, etc);
- according to the results of 2 weeks of work, we do with you a post on our blog on Habr√©, where you share your results with the community;
- Your project must necessarily be associated with deep learning.

Send your application with a description of the project to my mail apichugin@newprolab.com until Thursday, June 15, 23:59. On Friday, we will decide whose projects deserve our modest support more.

UPD. It is possible to extend the term for another 2 weeks, provided that in the first 2 weeks a good intermediate result was obtained, and the goals that can be achieved in the next term are clear.
