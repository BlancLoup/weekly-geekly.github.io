Where to look for work: chat rooms in Telegram, groups in FB and other resources

The scheme with creating a resume on one site and waiting for invitations for an interview does not work anymore. Social networks will help you find an excellent job, where your profile plays the role of a resume, and friends can become recruiters. For example, friends on Facebook can tag you for jobs, and LinkedIn can confirm your skills.

3. Specific people


Life hacking is for those who know which company they want to get into and that they have the right skills. Saw a vacancy somewhere and don't know how to get close to it? Add a company HR manager to your friends. You can find it like this:

1. Switch the language on Facebook to English.
2. Look for a recruiter or future leader for the team people who work at * company name *).

Add friends, introduce yourself, ask about vacancies - be polite, kind and professional.
