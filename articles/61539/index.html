ZAP Reader

Each of us wants to read faster, but not everyone succeeds. My attempts to master speed reading with the help of well-known programs led to the loss of the meaning of what I read quickly. And the faster I read, the more I lose. It was very reminiscent of the light fragrance of perfume that remained on the memory of a beloved woman ... And where is the deepest range of feelings? Distracted.
The desire to learn to read quickly, without losing the meaning of the read, did not leave me. As possible and you.
And then I went to the site and I had hope again.
This is a simulator that allows you not just stupidly to hone your ability to read quickly, but to read what you read daily, but with ever-increasing speed! If the advertisement on the site is not lying (and it usually exaggerates), then the speed of your reading should increase to 300%. But even 200% is not bad.
