DEV Labs 2015. Final online conference of the year. Java and javascript

The final this year, the online conference of the Soft Labs series will be held on December 19 and will be devoted to the development of Java and JavaScript! 4 current reports from our leading experts in a seven-hour online session. From us - interesting and useful cases, from you - tricky questions to speakers.

In the conference program:
  • New features of the JavaScript language: review of ES6 innovations, problems of ES6 support in browsers and the ability to broadcast from ES6. In addition, we will discuss further prospects for the development of the language. Speaker - Vladimir Sonkin.
  • Efficiency and code optimization in Java applications: what is efficiency and how to measure it correctly, performance myths, examples of effective and inefficient code, necessary and meaningless optimization. Speaker - Sergey Morenets.
  • Development of Maturity Level 3 REST services on Spring REST: maturity model of REST services, examples of implementation and documentation of REST services in Spring REST. Speaker - Gennady Kruglov.
  • Access to shared data in high-loaded applications: an overview of the various approaches for solving problems related to shared data access in a high-loaded multi-threaded environment. Speaker - Oleg Sopilnyak.

The conference starts on December 19 at 11:00 Moscow time . To participate and / or quickly access the video of the event you need to register . Participation is free.

Important innovation! Now we use an advanced platform for our events. In addition to excellent sound and picture quality, the new tool allows you to connect to events using mobile devices. Let's hope that now a children's matinee or a New Year's corporate party will not be an obstacle for participation in the conference.
