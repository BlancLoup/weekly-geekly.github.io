Conference "User Experience Russia 2007"

On November 8, at the Radisson SAS Slavyanskaya Hotel in Moscow, the first User Experience Russia conference will open, dedicated to the still unusual and usable topic of electronic usability. The date was not chosen by chance: for the third year in a row, the Association of Usability Professionals (UPA) celebrates World Usability Day , which by its very existence is intended to bring to society the knowledge of the ‚Äúneed to simplify access and improve the ease of use of products and services of particular importance to humanity ". This year, the emphasis is on health care.

In two days that the conference will go, prominent world experts in the field of design and ergonomics will speak at it, who will make several presentations on general topics of methodology and usability design features, as well as consider particular issues of creating interfaces for people with disabilities, for mobile devices and intranet systems, as well as cross-cultural perception of technology. The event is being organized by UsabilityLab and the edu-itonline training center under the auspices of the UN , UNESCO and the Ministry of Communications of Russia . Habrahabr acts as an information sponsor.

On the first day of the conference, the reports of foreign experts will be in the spotlight. Maria Stone of Google will talk about the company's goals in the field of usability. Eric Schaffer , founder and head of Human Factors International, will talk about the experience of his company, which is rightfully considered one of the world leaders in the industry. His colleague Apala Chavan will touch on the problems of adapting interfaces to the cultural characteristics of the user. Susan Fowler , founder of FAST Consulting and author of several classic works on GUI design, will address the transparency of government websites. Shailesh Manga from New Zealand-based Optimal Usability will talk about his vision of how to create world-class intranet interfaces. Victor Stenwick , a UI designer from IBM, will hold on November 10, together with Mrs. Fowler, an author's seminar on how to circumvent the most narrow and expensive places in the process of designing web applications.

The second day will include a round table "Efficiency of interaction between the state and society through the Internet", conducted by the Union of Webmasters of Russia , as well as reports and master classes of public experts. At the conclusion of the conference, foreign guests will hold separate seminars for professionals, at which strategies for developing interfaces of specific applications, such as search engines, as well as general issues, such as accepting attention to usability as a common practice in the life of an enterprise, will be considered in all its subtleties.

Details about each of the stages of the conference, including brief biographies of the speakers and summaries of their reports, can be found on the convenient official website . You can register as a member there. Consider that participation is paid and costs 7,000 rubles.
