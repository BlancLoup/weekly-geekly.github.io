Two months in a row electric cars topped the ranking of sales in Norway


Electric cars in the parking lot with a recharge in Oslo

In September and October of this year, Tesla Model S and Nissan Leaf, respectively, became the leaders of car sales in Norway. If Tesla electric car, which appeared on the Norwegian market in August, took the lead rather due to the fulfillment of pre-orders (in October, sales fell several times), then Nissan Leaf shows consistently high results. In 2013, Leaf ranked fourth in sales, with a share of 3.2%. In total, 925 electric vehicles were sold in Norway in October, which is 7.2% of the total number, which is more than twice as high as last year.
