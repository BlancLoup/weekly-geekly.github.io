Headquarters Touch Instinct. Tour of the St. Petersburg office

Hello! I'm Alice, a marketer at Touch Instinct. We make mobile applications for not indifferent brands. Today, a small tour of the headquarters in St. Petersburg, join.



Kolobovs House


The headquarters of the Touch Instinct is located in one of the most picturesque buildings on the Petrograd side - the Kolobov House. The patterned lattices on the balconies, the corner turrets, the Atlants, and the columns in the lobby - everything is preserved. Even antique stoves. We have these two.



Office with offices


The office employs 40 people. We do not like openspaces: the children in the offices work comfortably and quietly, no one is noisy. Lovers chat on the phone and skype sit separately.

We try to plant the project team in the same room, thus increasing productivity and team spirit. Designers and QA have individual cabinets. HR works closely with an accountant in a pink ottoman humanitarian zone.

Picture gallery


There are many cabinets, the corridor is long. Invented a useful tradition. After the release we hang a poster with the application icon on the wall. It lists all those who have made a tangible contribution to the development of the project. If we support the project for a long time and the team expands, we rework the frame.



Talk


Negotiation for clients, interviews and meetings. The designers did the table themselves: they bought boards, polished them, processed them. It turned out stylish and environmentally friendly.



In the center of the table is a brick. Our CEO Dmitry Kostin brought it from the garbage.



A board with badges was nailed to the wall. We constantly replenish with trophies - we carry badges from all conferences where we spoke or were just listeners.



On the wall we collect letters.



Artwork director Touch Instinct Alexander Yudin designed the door sticker. Based on the screens Tutu - Avia, one of the favorite projects. Invented several easter eggs for guests. The print hid the chicken, the cat and the Masonic badge.
