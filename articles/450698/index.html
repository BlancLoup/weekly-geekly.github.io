Gears in Box2D

Good afternoon, dear reader! This is the fourth article on Box2D. And here I will offer a turnkey solution for programmers. I myself needed gears for my project, and I made 4th, 6th, 12th and 24th gears. The code took 2115 lines, 4 days of work. And I want to share it with you so that you can save your time for really impressive projects on Box2D. All gears are scaled, you can set the angle of rotation, coordinates, availability, speed and engine power. In the code, everything is commented in detail.

If you need something like this (gif-animation will be after kata):


Figure 1. Gear system.

So this article is definitely for you!
