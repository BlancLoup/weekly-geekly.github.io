From the depths of the unnamed workshops of "Uncle Lee" in the expanses of online commerce appeared another creation - the mobile phone NokiPhone N3000i. As it is not difficult to guess from the name, the model is a hybrid of the two most popular models - iPhone and Nokia N95. The device is a double slider with a 3.2-inch touch screen. Probably not worth mentioning that the functionality of this phone with support for two SIM-cards does not correspond to the original models. However, for the cramped, but ambitious teenager can arrange such a compromise for $ 130.
