Android smartphone HTC Gratia officially presented

After the announcement of HTC Desire HD and HTC Desire Z, it became clear that, following the update of the flagships, HTC will present something new in the middle price category. This smartphone was the HTC Gratia, which is essentially a complete copy of the American HTC Aria in a new color design.



Technically, this smartphone is as close as possible to the HTC Legend: the SoC Qualcomm MSM7227 with a 600 MHz processor (ARMv6) acts as a platform. The smartphone is equipped with 512MB ROM, 384MB RAM, 5 MPks camera with LED flash, and 3.2 "LCD-screen with HVGA resolution. The novelty is running Android 2.2 Froyo.

HTC Gratia is aimed at undemanding users who need a full-featured smartphone of small size. Gratia occupies the price niche of its closest analog HTC HD mini: the recommended cost of a smartphone in Russia will be 17,990 rubles. In the Russian Federation smartphones of white, black and green colors will be delivered. Start of sales is scheduled for mid-November this year.



HTC Gratia fits perfectly into the company's product chain, taking place between Wildfire and Legend, which will also receive updates to Android 2.2 Froyo later this year.



The features of the new items include a fully removable back panel, which is made of a thick layer of soft-touch plastic. Like the HD mini, the Gratia battery compartment is colored yellow :)
