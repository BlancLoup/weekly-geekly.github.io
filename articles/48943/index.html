It's about navigation and interfaces. On the simplest solution to ease the user's life of any browser or interface that uses tabs or windows.

In view of the increase in the information consumed, the services used and some constantly open resources, it is becoming increasingly difficult to find a tab from which you literally just left. And the more difficult, the more tabs. This problem accompanies since acquaintance with Windows and its windows.
The essence of the problem is that as soon as you leave a tab, it immediately becomes absolutely the same as all other inactive tabs (or windows, in the case of Windows). And only one single active tab is highlighted.
