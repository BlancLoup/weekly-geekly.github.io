Half an hour before the final - Vivaldi 1.0.435.26

Hello!

While I was rapidly recovering health after the recent crash, my colleagues did not waste their time and baked new test versions like cakes, sometimes giving out several sets of assemblies per day. Following the recommendations of the doctors, I did not fuss and run online for every occasion, but slowly went down from the hospital bed and rewrote all the announcements into one. Here it is, and I bring to your attention.

So, what's new happened from my previous post. First, I was discharged from the hospital: the functioning of the heart was fully restored, even without the traditional for infarction scarring on the heart muscle. Thank you all so much for your support and kind words - everything could have been different without you. And secondly, my colleagues and I came close to the release of the first stable version of the browser. This explains the high rate of release of new assemblies (a total of four versions) - the process of eliminating the last subquality is underway.
