Corporate Google Buzz


Those companies that have already registered their corporate email in Gmail are most fortunate. Now, creating a post in Lenta, they can contact everyone with whom they corresponded.

In order not to spam everyone in a row, of course, you should use the privacy settings and make notes that are available only for certain groups. Corporate "Buzz" may include such groups as Employees, Customers, Partners, PR.

Thus, Google Buzz will be able to replace several channels of information distribution at once: a corporate blog, an internal corporate newsletter and correspondence, a newsletter with press releases for reporters.
