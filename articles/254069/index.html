Supercell 2014 Financial Report


The industry titan showed itself in numbers: according to the financial report, the company's revenue for 2014 amounted to $ 1.7 billion, which is about three times more than revenue for 2013.

Such a significant increase in revenue could have been influenced by the appearance of a new title in the past year - a number of games like Clash of Clans and Hay Day added to Boom Beach, as well as the amount of money spent on marketing and advertising. Supercell CEO Ilkka Paananen (Ilkka Paananen) said that $ 400 million was spent on promoting games last year.

It is noteworthy that analysts were expecting more from Supercell: Superdata estimated the company's revenue in excess of $ 1.8 billion.

IPad share in Russia fell by half




According to a study by J'son & Partners Consulting, in Russia, more and more preferences are given to Android tablets. So, in 2014, their market share rose to 89%, while the iPad share almost halved and dropped to 10%.

It is important to note that over the past two years, the average selling price of tablets has almost halved, which is below 10 thousand rubles. In this case, the highest sales growth, almost doubled, was shown by the price category of tablets up to 5 thousand rubles. This is reflected in the difficult financial situation in the country, and the constant increase in prices for Apple devices during 2014.

At the same time, the world share of the iPad fluctuates slightly, and according to the results of 2014, it was 28%, and Android tablets did not exceed 68%.
