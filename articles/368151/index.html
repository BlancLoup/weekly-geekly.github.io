First, we formalize the requirements. Some of them are very specific or boring, but most fathers will understand me. So:

  1. The backpack should be roomy. So that all things for a flight and a long walk, including 2-3 beach towels, should be put in there.
  2. All paternal gadgets should be placed in the backpack, and, moreover, they should be protected as much as possible. Usually I take a trip: a laptop, a charger for it, a smartphone, a power bank, a Kindle, a son's tablet, a notebook, pens, a pile of wires.
  3. The pocket for the laptop should be separate, providing quick access to it. As a rule, it is necessary at airports, because need to get a laptop for inspection. If the pocket is inside the main compartment, then it is very difficult to stuff a technique into it.
  4. The backpack should have an outer pocket for a water bottle. It is desirable two (if there are two children - it happens that it is undesirable for both to drink from the same bottle.   5. It should have a carrying handle - standing in line for inspection or for boarding a train / bus, it is much more convenient to hold it in your hand by the handle.
  6. It should have a pocket on top, where it is convenient to put a poverbank and a cord for charging. Why top? Because sitting on a chair it is convenient to put a backpack between your legs and connect a phone or tablet with a relatively short wire. Typically, such a pocket manufacturers positioned as a place for points. Why not from below? Downstairs, a power bank is much easier to be hit, and batteries are fragile and sometimes fire hazardous.
  7. It should have a convenient external pocket, with a bunch of pockets where it is easy to shove passports, Airbnb reservations and boarding for the whole family. However, it must be safe.
  8. And the last: it should be beautiful, but made of practical, waterproof material.
