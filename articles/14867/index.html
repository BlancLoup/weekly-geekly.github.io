45 W watches from AMD

AMD has released several new processors, characterized by increased energy efficiency. The 45-watt processors, according to the company, can be an excellent solution for manufacturers, opening up new opportunities for manufacturing PCs that are notable for high performance and, at the same time, low heat generation and noise. Therefore, among the main areas of application of these CPUs, AMD refers to computers for home and educational institutions.

In addition, according to the company's subjective assessment, PCs based on new processors must not only comply with EPA EnergyStar Version 4 standards, but also exceed them.

The list of 45-watt CPUs presented, with a reduced value, including a batch of 1000 units:

Athlon X2 BE-2400 2.3 GHz - $ 104
Athlon LE-1620 2.4 GHz - $ 53
Athlon LE-1600 2.2 GHz - $ 47
Sempron LE-1250 2.2 GHz - $ 53
Sempron LE-1200 2.1 GHz - $ 48
Sempron LE-1100 1.9 GHz - $ 37.
