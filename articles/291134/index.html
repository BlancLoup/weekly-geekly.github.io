40 million Russians use Viber: instant messenger doubled the audience in a year

Viber marketing director Mark Hardy spoke about increasing the audience of the Viber application in Russia to 40 million active users, Sostav.ru reports . In July 2014, 20 million Russians used the service.

In total, the number of users of the messenger in the world is 574 million - in the middle of 2014 this figure was 300 million. Russia by the number of users Viber is inferior to the USA and India.

According to TNS Russia, in February 2015, Viber entered the top ten most popular online resources for a mobile audience. Users at the age of 12-64 years old, living in cities with a population of 700 thousand people, Viber installed more than 6 million smartphones.
