GoogleEarth 5, Cyrillic Mapping Issue

All those who set a landflight of the 5th version know that the Cyrillic alphabet is displayed in small squares. Qt at google without nls flag chtoli compiled? In general, having thought, the idea arose - to translate Google Earth into system by creating symlinks.
At the same time get rid of a handful of localizations by the linguas-flag.

It turned out that it is possible to translate part of the google earths to the system, in particular from: net-misc / curl , sys-devel / gcc , dev-libs / icu , media-libs / mesa , x11-libs / qt-core-4 and sys -libs / zlib . Well, as a result - Cyrillic is displayed with a bang.

But the long-awaited ebuild (googleearth-5.0.11337.1968_pre1.tar.bz2)
Who has already put the 5th version, I advise you to rename the ebuild to googleearth-5.0.11337.1968_beta or copy GoogleEarth-5.0.11337.1968_beta.bin to GoogleEarth-5.0.11337.1968_pre1.bin in the distfiles directory.
