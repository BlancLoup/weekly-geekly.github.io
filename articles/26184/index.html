Kazakhstan Internet has accelerated.

The national telecommunications operator of Kazakhstan, Kazakhtelecom, has updated its tariff plans for broadband Internet access services under the Megaline trademark.

On the official website www.megaline.kz , new information on tariff plans is already available. Now for $ 33 you can get unlimited internet at a speed of 256Kbps. Tariff plan with a maximum speed of 1Mbps offered for the price of $ 49 with 2GB of prepaid traffic.
At the same time, for all tariffs, unlimited access is available at the speed of 512Kbit / s for domestic resources (including traffic between subscribers) of the national operator.
