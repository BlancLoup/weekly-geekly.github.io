These are dollar coins from the US Mint State Mint.



The trick is that coins are sold at par with free delivery within the United States.

Since the coins have been serving for more than 30 years, and the bills are only 21 months, the release of as many coins as possible into circulation is considered a state-important task in the United States, which is why the sale of coins for bills is sponsored by the government, writes Wall Street Journal.

The scheme is clear. You receive coins, and immediately deliver the bags to the bank and deposit into your account until you have overdraft and penalties on your credit card. And voila - you get a huge amount of free miles, without spending a penny (only the price of gasoline to drive a car to the bank).

Such is the bonus from the US government. The promotion of free delivery of coins is valid from June 2008. According to US Mint, during this time, $ 130 million coins have been sent to 40,000 customers. Of course, soon the airline or the mint will surely cover the shop for freebies, because WSJ wrote about this ingenious scam. But who has the opportunity - use, the scheme is still working. According to him, he ordered the coins several times and laid them out on different accounts in different banks, with sums of no more than $ 70,000.
