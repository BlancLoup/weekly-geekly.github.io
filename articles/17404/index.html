InternetUA magazine named the leaders of the Ukrainian Internet

A solemn ceremony of awarding the InternetUA Awards to the best Ukrainian Internet projects of 2007 took place today. The winners in 19 nominations were determined by the jury consisting of well-known and reputable professionals of the Ukrainian Internet market.

The following projects became laureates of the InternetUA Awards 2007.

1. Cable provider of the year: Will
2. Mobile Provider of the Year: MTS-Ukraine
3. Hosting Provider of the Year: Data Center Volya
4. Search Engine of the Year: META
5. Business news portal and web service of the year: Liga.net
6. Sports portal of the year: SportDisc
7. Portal and mail of the year: Ukr.net
8. Advertising Agency of the Year: AdWork
9. Payment system of the year: Privat 24
10. Trading system: Price.ua
11. Trading system: Nadavi.com.ua
12. Online store of the year: Bamboo
13. Gadget of the Year: Bambook Fusion E-book Reader
14. Community of the Year: Jeans Invaders
15. Thematic resource of the year: Proformula.ua
