Who loves porn more than anyone in the world

It is often said that pornography is to be thanked for the success of videotapes, cable television and the Internet. The argument with video players and cable is simple: at that time, many viewers could easily do watching movies in cinemas, but it is more convenient to watch porn at home without strangers. Others call pornography the main driving force behind the development of global network technologies. Even before the WWW era, users exchanged ASCII porn , and with the advent of websites, the pornography industry was rapidly mastering online payment technology, broadband Internet, video broadcasting, video chat and virtual reality helmets.

In cooperation with the The Next Web site, SimilarWeb conducted a study and published the results. The company's specialists wondered how different nations like to consume pornography, and the findings may surprise, although some may seem expected.

As of February 2015, 4.41 percent of all visits from desktop computers fall on adult sites. The 2012 ExtremeTech study found that pornographic websites receive 4.4 billion page views per month. For comparison: a large forum and social news site Reddit reaches only 2.8 billion impressions. Adult topics should be the seventh in the list of the most popular categories, falling just under computers and electronics. Pornographic sites are more popular than such strong categories as games and sports, gambling, beauty and fitness, travel, health and recreation. Adult sites are two times faster in traffic than the online finance industry.



Sites for adults are the fourth category in terms of traffic from banner advertising, trailing behind shopping only by some percentage. The top lines are gambling sites and video games.



According to SimilarWeb, the largest proportion of pornographic websites are in Iraq and Egypt, two fairly conservative countries that could hardly be expected to hit the top twenty. This is followed by European nations with inclusions of Asian Japan and Latin American Peru.
