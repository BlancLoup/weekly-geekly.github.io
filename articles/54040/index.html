Social networks are more popular than email

Five years ago, e-mail was the main occupation of Internet users, it was leading by a large margin, overtaking and searching for information, and reading the news, and regular surfing. Very quickly everything turned upside down.

Now, according to Nielsen , e-mail in the general list of the most popular classes on the PC has already dropped to fifth place, yielding to search, portals, and regular software. Moreover, now it is even inferior to social networks and blogs, whose active audience has grown to 67% of Internet users.

Most of all, Brazilians like to communicate on social networks (Orkut is the leader with 70% of the market).
