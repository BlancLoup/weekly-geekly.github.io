What would you do if you had everything, or thoughts of a Zeitgeist 2.

In the second part of the Zeitgeist film (addendum) there is a fragment dedicated to the Venus project, a resource-oriented economy, which removes the need for money and human labor. As I understand from this fragment, all the work will be done by robots, and the person will just enjoy life. Project ideologists believe that the idea that money motivates a person is wrong, and that a person will find something to do in the event that all his needs are met.

In this regard, I would like to ask the public, and what would you do if you had everything and no longer needed to work, would you not be bored in that case?
