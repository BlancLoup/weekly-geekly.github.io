Features of national registrars

This moment TrendVision pays close attention, although it would seem - what kind of "local features" can we talk about, what are the same bad Chinese or Korean models for our country? I'll start at least with batteries. Almost all Chinese devices simply do not start at a temperature of -10 ¬∞ C and below. For this reason, for example, experienced motorists prefer not to leave their cameras at night in the cabin - from the morning until the cabin warms up, switching on the device is extremely problematic. The Korean models have another extreme - due to the peculiarities of the legislation of South Korea, lithium-polymer batteries are not installed in them. Supercapacitors are used exclusively, which, although they have a number of indisputable advantages (about them below), are not able to offer long autonomous shooting and allow only to complete the recording correctly and save the current video.



In TrendVision models, this issue was resolved by an approach that no other registrar developer has yet repeated. Specialists have implemented a special super-capacitor (0.22 F) in the recorders along with the usual Li-Po battery, which allows the camera to start when it is freezing down to -30 ¬∞ C! In addition, in the case of a full discharge of the main battery, the supercapacitor can store date and time data for several days. Power and GPS "on the mind"


Special attention is given to the organization of power supply in TrendVision recorders. Which, in turn, is closely intertwined with the company's fundamental approach to the construction of a GPS receiver. It is unlikely that anyone will be surprised by the power supply of the recorder when energy is supplied through a connector in the bracket, from which, in turn, enters the chamber. This allows you not to insert wires into the recorder body, which means - quickly remove and install it back. It is no secret that in Russia they love to drive not only on left-hand drive cars, but also on right-hand drive cars. Therefore, when choosing the location of the power connector in the ‚Äúbox‚Äù models (TDR-708/718/200), TrendVision specialists took, and ... We installed two ports at once, on both sides of the mount.





In addition, a different inclination of the windshield was taken into account, in addition to the kit, such a competent adapter is attached.





In models with a GPS antenna, the corresponding receiver is external, and in fact is included in the power supply chain.



That is, the cable from the recorder mount is included in the unit with GPS, from which, in turn, the wire with the adapter is included in the cigarette lighter connector. This was done for the purpose of convenience - this is not how a ‚Äúhedgehog‚Äù is formed from a set of parallel wires.



In general, why did you need to make an external receiver, if you could embed it in the housing of the recorder or in the mount (the most popular option)? Analysis of a large number of competitors recorders led TrendVision engineers to the following conclusions. The most "beautiful" way out would be to embed a GPS antenna in the recorder's body, but the inevitable interference from the internal electronics would have a negative effect on the reception of the satellite signal. And the more sensitive the antenna, the more susceptible it is to interference. Therefore, the manufacturer may praise the GPS module in every way and not be guileless at all, but it is quite another thing to keep silent about the real operation of this component in the device.

The companies that decided to install a GPS receiver inside the recorder's body chose one of two ways to fix the problem. The first is the installation of a conventional GPS antenna, which solves the issue of a long search for satellites. However, instead of 12-15 satellites, the recorder begins to hold the signal on average from 7-9 sources. The second way is the shielding of the board with a metal frame, which actually eliminates interference, but creates a number of other problems. For example, natural ventilation is disturbed, which causes the processor and other components to heat up more, the recorder starts to hang more often.

In registrars of Russian and not only companies, brackets with a GPS receiver built into them are extremely popular, which is a very effective solution. The main thing is that in the end the distance between the antenna and the recorder should be at least 20 mm to eliminate the negative effect of the clock generator. It seems to be all great, but we are returning to the criminal plane - embedding the antenna makes the mount bulky and perfectly visible from the outside. The result is a sharp increase in the probability of finding the abandoned car uncovered, which is only confirmed by police reports.



Also, do not forget about the increasing popularity of athermal windshields, which are not radiotransparent and interfere with the operation of the GPS receiver. Of course, in such glasses there is usually a "radio-transparent window", the location of which is indicated in the instructions. The only problem is that this is the very place of the ‚Äúwindow‚Äù from the point of view of mounting the recorder is often extremely inconvenient. And on the contrary, the external GPS unit of TrendVision models allows you to install it anywhere on the windshield without being tied to the recorder.
