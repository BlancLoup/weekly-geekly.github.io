Best Work in the USA: Top 5 Most Attractive Specialties

Many specialists strive to work in the USA - after all, there are quite high salaries in this country, plus a good standard of living. When choosing a job in this country, you should pay attention to the list recently submitted by CNN : journalists have developed a list of professions with high pay and some other "buns".

In the list, most professions are related to IT, which is not surprising. Maybe someone is already working on one of these specialties (not necessarily in the US). It would be interesting to hear the comment of a person who is directly related to one of the mentioned professions.

Software Architect





  • Average salary (per year): 124 thousand US dollars;
  • Maximum: 169 thousand dollars;
  • Growth in 10 years: 23%.


As Vicki tells us, a software architect designs software architecture, i.e. making key design decisions regarding the internal structure of the software system and its technical interfaces.

Representatives of this profession usually direct the work of several other specialists so that all the developed elements of a system can be assembled into a single functional program.

Advantages of the profession: the constant study of new technologies, every day does not look like the previous one. A lot of work, but all of it is interesting.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to society: B; Remote work: A; Low stress: A.

Video game designer





  • Average salary (per year): 79900 US dollars;
  • Maximum: 115 thousand US dollars;
  • Growth for 10 years: 19%

Do not think that game designers are constantly playing, and in their profession, everything is very bright and fun. Like film directors, video game designers are responsible for the overall vision of the project, the representatives of this profession direct the work of programmers, designers and other specialists to implement a common goal.

Advantages of the profession: such work is relatively new, so there is the possibility of introducing innovations. Video game designer can manage the whole process of creating a game, which is certainly very exciting.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to the community: B; Low stress: A.

Landman





  • Average salary: 103 thousand US dollars;
  • Maximum: 160 thousand US dollars;
  • Growth in 10 years: 13%


Landman has several translation values. This, for example "surveyor." But in this case there is an employee of an oil producing company who deals with the owners (or tenants) of the site. Representatives of this profession are negotiating on behalf of the oil company with the owners of the land, where, for example, an oil field has been found.

Advantages of the profession: working with the project of oil production from the beginning to the end, bonuses for work.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to the community: A; Low stress: A.

Patent Attorney





  • Average salary: 126 thousand US dollars;
  • Maximum: 182 thousand;
  • Growth in 10 years: 13%.


Patent attorneys study inventions, and determine whether these or other technologies or objects can be patented.

Advantages of the profession: working with new inventions means that a person always sees new ideas, some of which can be called "ambitious". And the patent attorney chooses the best of the best in order to issue a patent to the creator of this invention.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to the community: A; Low stress: A.

Hospital administrator





  • Average salary: 114 thousand US dollars;
  • Maximum: 207 thousand;
  • Growth in 10 years: 23%.


The hospital administrator coordinates the work of the various departments of the hospital and the work of the staff so that all processes run smoothly. All this is similar to the work of watchmakers, which adjusts the work of individual mechanisms and parts so that the whole system works perfectly.

Advantages of the profession: constant changes, the dynamics of work, the high activity of the process itself. It is clear that such work is not suitable for everyone, but many people like this kind of activity.

Quality of work:
Personal satisfaction: A; Contribution to the community: A; Low stress: C.
