Google Goggles - Photo Search

Google has released Google Goggles for Android.



With its help, you can search in Google using the camera of your phone - you just need to take a picture of an object or object and the program will tell you what it is.

The program can recognize:

- attractions



- works of art



- books or discs



- wine



- logos



- shops, bars, restaurants



Another useful feature of the program is the recognition of business cards. Unfortunately, I do not have Ukrainian-speaking or Russian-speaking business cards at hand, but the English-speaking business cards are recognized perfectly by the program, offering to call (write) a person or bring him into contact.

Android OS version 1.6 or higher is required for its operation, the program is free and available through the Android Market.
