Google paid $ 700 million for the purchase of ITA Software

Internet giant Google has agreed to purchase for $ 700 million company ITA Software . The ITA service helps users find detailed information from the airlines on any flights, as well as airfares and the number of empty seats on the boards. Using the service, you can, for example, compare similar routes of two different airlines and choose the most budget or comfortable one. There is also a ticket booking.


Google promises to integrate this service into its search engine, which will allow the user to plan their own flight routes. At the same time, the Internet giant, in accordance with the obligations assumed by ITA Software , promises not to engage in the sale of tickets.
