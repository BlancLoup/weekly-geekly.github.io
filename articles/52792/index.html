Mini PC from ASUS Nova Lite and Essentio in Ukraine


The ASUS computers of the Nova lite and Essentio series are PCs that are several times smaller than the standard ones! But, despite the compactness, mini PCs Nova Lite and Essentio are reliable and easy to use.
In addition to the standard PC usage, these devices will successfully fit into the interior of the media system, and will serve as an excellent multimedia source for playing movies, music and games.
NOVA Lite EP20 is incredibly small. This mini PC can be put on a shelf, like a book, or placed on a table. The built-in speakers located on the front panel of NOVA Lite provide high-quality sound reproduction without the need to connect additional devices. The power consumption of NOVA Lite is 18 watts in operation mode and only 5 watts in standby mode. ASUS energy saving technology reduces power consumption while ensuring high performance.

Nova Lite PX24 is also equipped with a built-in HDMI / DVI interface.
