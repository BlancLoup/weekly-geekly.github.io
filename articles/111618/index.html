We - Glavstart - by and large, the embassy of investors in the country of startups. First, we are looking for interesting start-ups at the level of ideas or early prototypes for seed investments. Secondly, we are looking for startups that are useful and necessary for strategic investors.

So, what kind of startups and why are we looking for strategic investors?
