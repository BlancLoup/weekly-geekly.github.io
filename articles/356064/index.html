VKontakte refuses to block communities containing links to illegal online bookmakers



During the campaign of a number of government agencies to combat illegal gambling on the Vkontakte network, more than 200 groups were entered into the Unified Register of Banned Sites for publishing links to illegal online casinos, the Izvestia newspaper reports and stresses that communities have not been blocked yet.

Twitter and Facebook have deleted accounts of large offshore betting shops engaged in illegal gambling on the Internet. Among the deleted accounts were such companies as Fonbet, BetCity, PariMatch, Winlinebet.
The mobile applications of the bookmakers banned in Russia from Google Play and the App Store were also removed.

The absence of Vkontakte in this list is striking. But the main audience of online gambling is concentrated there.
