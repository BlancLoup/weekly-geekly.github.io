What is fraught with a mug



In the future, using a computer will become much more understandable and simpler than in our time - even children and owners of negative karma know this.

To effectively use computer technology, you no longer have to master specific skills, and computer technology will have an impact on the user's daily behavior. The simplest example is the use of a mug . True - not entirely for its intended purpose.

The concept of " The Cup " is based on knowledge about the behavior of people and their needs, in any place and at any time. Each person has several mugs at home; we see them in offices, cafes, shops, and many other places. This subject is so tightly and painlessly blended into our lives that we almost ceased to perceive it .

Observing such simple actions as picking up mugs, manipulating with them, transfusing water and much more - an innovative approach was proposed that considers the simplest mug as an information management tool .

The main task of the Circle is to simplify the interaction between the user and the computer . The device makes the application usage interface more friendly and intuitive (I would even say instinctive ). The ultimate goal is to make it so that absolutely anyone can use a computer as an object of daily use - a mug, without the need to master any special skills.

Potential users of the " Circle " - all of us . People who want to simplify the interaction with the PC. Each of us almost daily surfs the global network, listens to music and watches movies. For this reason, the desire to make routine operations as accessible, quick and clear as possible is understandable.

In particular - the device will appeal to those who support the restoration of culture and traditions . This does not mean a return to archaism - it means the upgrade of technology to the usual form for everyone. All this is explained very simply - high technology does not arouse interest from a wide audience. Partly because of its geekiness, partly because of the price, partly because of the complexity. But at the same time, there are things that attract almost every person - this is nostalgia and naturalness.

Using the " Circle " has some input threshold - a person must be able to use it. Not in terms of manufacturability, but in terms of dishes . This means that the project appeals to a huge niche of buyers - from children to pensioners . Anything can be an approach, every person can be a goal.
