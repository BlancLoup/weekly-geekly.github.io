REG.RU opens privileged club

Dear Habravchane!

We are pleased to announce that REG.RU is entering a new level of communication with regular customers and partners: for the first time in the Russian domain and hosting market, the registrar opens a privileged club for customers and partners. Each member of the Privilege Club receives one of three club cards - Silver, Gold or VIP, which make it possible to take advantage of privileged service, advice and assistance on legal, technical issues, and a discount of up to 15% on the company's services.

Say discounts and bonuses will surprise no one? Especially for Privilege Club REG.RU, on the basis of many years of experience in communicating with customers, has developed special client services and services that were not previously offered on the domain and hosting market.



Among the unique services and privileged services available to members of the Privilege Club :
- Loan for services
-Personal manager
-Help in the selection of domain names
- Service "Callback" at a convenient time for the client
- Assistance in negotiations on the purchase / sale of domain names
- Increased priority in the ticket system
- Priority completion of the company's automatic services upon the client's request
-Expert domain name valuation
- Assistance in the preparation of documents for transactions with domain names
-Free help on setting up a site on hosting
And other exclusive solutions and features.

Privilege Club is open to all clients without restrictions: the system automatically assigns status in accordance with the client's activity and individual history of work with REG.RU. At the start of the program, more than 5,000 customers and partners received privileged status and access to exclusive services.
