"RAEC / Media" proposes to bring down the market of "pirated" sites, depriving them of their sources of income (upd: expert comments)



Pirate sites must suffer a huge loss of profit. This is what the RAEC / Media cluster is counting on. He urged payment systems to work with them to cut off their cash flows. In addition, the organization urged advertisers and agencies to stop placing ads on pirated sites. These proposals "RAEC / Media" outlined in a statement.

The statement was signed by the Internet Video Association, the Association of Film and Television Producers, the Russian Producers Guild, the Russian Anti-Piracy Organization, the National Federation of the Music Industry and the Internet Copyright Protection Association.

Source: intermedia.ru

The problem of piracy concerns not only media companies whose content is used by pirates, but also advertisers, according to the cluster. "Placing advertisements on pirate sites is incompatible with the ethics of conducting responsible business," the statement said. In addition, the placement of such advertising has a negative effect on the reputation of payment systems, since the income of pirates is ‚Äúassociated with illegal activities,‚Äù the letter's authors believe.

In 2006, the payment systems Visa and MasterCard appealed to the banks included in its payment system with a request not to process payments for purchases in the online store allofmp3 . The site Allofmp3.com has long been the second most popular in Europe after iTunes. The tracks cost only $ 0.1, and the albums cost $ 1. In 2006, recording studios launched a campaign against the owners of this site. Moreover, they achieved that Russia's accession to the WTO depended, inter alia, on its closure. In 2007, the site was closed, although a Russian court acquitted its former owner.

Placing video ads on third-party sites is permissible only on sites with completely legal content, such as Kinopoisk , Afisha , Mail.ru , the statement said. And online cinemas should not place their players on pirated websites and ‚Äúshare advertising revenues with the owners of such pirated websites.‚Äù The authors of the statement believe that advertisers may be misled about the status of the sites that host its advertising messages.

In October, the Deputy Minister of Communications, Alexei Volin, accused large Western companies of advertising on sites with pirated video. After that, the advertising group VivaKi prohibited legal video services from displaying Internet advertising for their clients in the so-called partner networks, employees of several media companies told Vedomosti earlier. Other advertising groups have reduced such placements, but have not abandoned them completely. Because of this, the video advertising market this year will grow noticeably less.

To identify sites with illegal content, the RAEC / Media cluster used a number of special criteria, Vedomosti writes . The main feature of the pirate site was ‚Äúplacing copyright objects or information necessary to receive them, without appropriate licensing agreements or other permits from the copyright holder‚Äù .

The additional signs of the pirate site also include the lack of contact between the site owners, the contact form for rights holders and the system of digital fingerprints for content filtering. In addition, a site where copyrighted content is distributed through torrents by itself can be considered pirated, since ‚Äúa torrent is not, in principle, an established form of legal distribution of digital content‚Äù.

The copyright holders attributed the torrent trackers Rutracker.org and NNM-club.me , music projects zaycev.net and vk-sound.ru , as well as lib.rus.ec , lostfilm.tv , seasonvar.ru and other sites to pirate sites. In the near future, the cluster promised to publish a list of 100 sites that qualified as pirated.

In late April, "Megamind" wrote that the resource management with pirated video content Kinokrad.net and Bobfilm.net offered cooperation to the right holders. The offer was sent to WebKontrol . She is tracking illegal copies of films on the Internet. WebKontrol works with such film companies as Bazelevs , Our Cinema , Art Pictures , as well as with Hollywood copyright holders.

Owners of pirated sites reported that they are ready to cooperate with WebKontrol under any conditions. They promised, if necessary, to remove from the resources of any films. Moreover, they agreed to install a video player with licensed content.

Since May 1 of this year, a new edition of the anti-piracy law has entered into force. Right holders were able to block the pirate site forever, if they twice prove in court the fact of violation of rights. Now the law applies to other types of content: music, books, software, and so on.
