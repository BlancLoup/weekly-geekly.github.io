Sony introduced the PlayStation 4

Sony has finally introduced a new generation console - the PlayStation 4 (but did not bother to show the console itself at the presentation).



An interesting feature of this console is iron - Sony has moved away from the tradition of installing specific hardware stuffing like the Cell processor and installed the usual x86 processor in the PlayStation - an eight-core AMD made by AMD (details are being specified).

Unfortunately, with the transition from the custom platform to the x86 platform, the console of the new generation will lose the traditional backward compatibility - in this case with the PlayStation 3.




The video card is also a PC-class, and also produced by AMD. Accelerator demonstrates performance level of about 2 teraflops.
