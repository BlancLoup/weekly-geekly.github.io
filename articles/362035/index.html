C 26 years after Super Mario Bros 2 was released, a secret trick was discovered in the game.



It would seem, what secrets could remain unrevealed for the game, released in 1988, passed by the fans of the series along and across? But only 26 years later, a Japanese gamer under the nickname Games Haya was able to detect a bug feature.

The secret is hidden at level 5-2. If in a special place to force the mustache plumber to jump between the shells of turtles, then after a few jumps you can earn extra lives. This action can be done almost infinitely.



The release of the first part of the Super Mario Bros series took place in 1985. Super Mario Bros 2 went on sale in the United States in October 1988 and was a converted version of the game Doki Doki Panic.
