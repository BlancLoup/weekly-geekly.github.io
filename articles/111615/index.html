Pressure meter + iphone



The Paris-based company Withings, after staggering success with its wifi weights, received $ 3 million in investment from the Ventech Foundation. The guys spent money wisely and presented to the public their new innovative product - a pressure meter connected to the iPhone.

The project is terribly simple. Interest is only integration with the apple phone. How to use the gadget will understand even people who do not know English:



Using Withings API, other projects can extend their functionality by interacting with the platform through the API. For example, Withings WiFi Body Scale is already used in Google Health and Microsoft HealthVault .



Now in Withings there are only 20 people working. Thanks to generous investments in the plans - active development in Asian markets - in Japan, Korea and Taiwan. In the meantime, the main audience is this States and Europe.
