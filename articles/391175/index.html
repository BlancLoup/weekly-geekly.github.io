Falcon 9 launch canceled for third time


AP Photo / NASA / Bill Ingalls

The launch of the SpaceX Falcon 9 launch vehicle with a geostationary satellite on board has been postponed for the third time. The company claims in its Twitter account that the launch was halted due to technical reasons. Only a few seconds remained before the launch, as the control center gave the command to abort the launch.

It is also reported that the booster did not receive damage. The new launch date is still unknown, it should be announced later.
