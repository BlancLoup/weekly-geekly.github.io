Automatic box for context



The ability to automatically optimize contextual advertising based on call analytics provided their customers with Calltouch call tracking and the Origami context optimization platform.

Collaboration services will save time on context management and make it more efficient. The integration does not help manually, but automatically adjusts the rates based on the data about the sources of calls and the keywords for which the callers came to the site.

- Suppose a marketer is faced with the task of increasing the number of calls. Calltouch collects data on call statistics up to a key phrase, and Origami calculates and sets optimal rates for each keyword, based on the client's KPI, explained Alexey Avdeev, the principle of service interaction, at Calltouch sales.
