QIWI Wallet for iPhone Version 3.2

Today we sent a new version of the QIWI Wallet payment application for approval in the AppStore. Not everyone has an iPhone, but we think that a small review of innovations will be interesting to other users, since many features will appear in applications for other mobile platforms, for example, Android, and on the Wallet site.

What will be new in version 3.2:

1. The ability to specify a name for saving details in the favorites when filling out the form of payment;

2. Adding details to your favorites without the need for payment:


3. The possibility of making a favorite payment, even if the provider information is not in the current version of the mobile application;

4. The possibility of paying for utility services in Moscow both with and without insurance;

5. The ability to invite friends from the address book in QIWI wallet by e-mail:

6. The ability to replenish a mobile phone account from a QIWI wallet account via USSD, without going online;

7. Display user-defined name for a registered bank card;

8. Highlighting blank fields required in the form of payment;

9. Information exchange between two devices using BUMP technology:


10. Feedback form with technical support:


Do not forget about the already familiar, but such useful things as finding the nearest point of replenishment, whether it be a terminal, ATM or a communications salon:

QIWI Wallet version 3.2 will be available for free download in the AppStore, we hope, at the beginning of next week.
