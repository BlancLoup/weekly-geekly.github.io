What is a virtual (prepaid) plastic card?

Virtual or prepaid cards are debit cards accepted at most online stores and services, including PSN Store, PayPal, Ebay, Google Adwords, iTunes RU, Moneybookers, Amazon, Ozon, WOW RUS, LineAge, etc. .

Technically, virtual cards, on the forums they are also called ‚Äúvirtual cards‚Äù, are no different from ordinary plastic Visa Electron, Classic, and others.

The main differences between a virtual prepaid card and a simple plastic card are that:
1. Unlike a regular plastic card, for example, a mastercard, which has a hard peg to the data specified when it was issued in the bank, the virtual card does not have a peg to the address, first name and surname, which is the main advantage.
2. Virtual cards - one-time, without the possibility of replenishment.
3. The validity of such cards is 2 months.
