Universal Audio UAD-2 SOLO / Laptop for Laptop



In addition to the fact that the company Universal Audio from time to time produces amazing devices for project studios, sometimes fundamentally new products appear on the market for home studios as well. Such a product is the compact express card UAD-2 SOLO / Laptop based on the DSP accelerator.

With this card, you can transfer the analog audio emulations from Universal Audio to your laptop and use them in Cubase, Logic, Digital Performer and Pro Tools. The UAD-2 SOLO / Laptop card provides full access to the full-fledged versions of the UAD plug-ins and does not require power or cables to connect. Like any other equipment in the UAD-2 series, the SOLO / Laptop card supports the VST, AU and RTAS formats on Mac and PC.
