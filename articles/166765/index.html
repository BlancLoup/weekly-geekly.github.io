Video review of smartphones HTC Windows Phone 8X and HTC Windows Phone 8S



HTC Windows Phone 8X and HTC Windows Phone 8S are new HTC smartphones based on the Windows Phone 8 operating system. They run on Qualcomm S4 dual-core processors with a frequency of 1.5 GHz (8X) or 1 GHz (8S). The amount of RAM is 1024 or 512 MB, respectively.

For storage, the HTC WP 8X is allocated 16 GB of internal memory without the possibility of expansion, while the HTC WP 8S in addition to the built-in 4 GB, you can use removable micro-SD memory cards up to 32 GB.

The models use Super LCD displays 4.3 "(1280x720) and 4" (800x480), respectively.

As for cameras, HTC WP 8S has only one rear camera (5 MP), while HTC WP 8X boasts both rear (8 megapixel) and front (2.1 megapixel) cameras. In addition, the older model is equipped with an NFC-chip, an accelerometer and a compass.
