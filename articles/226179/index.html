Nest



Nest is a new generation thermostat or a learning thermostat, one of the projects of Nest Lab. The project was founded by people from Apple, Tony Fadell and Matt Rogers. The Google giant recently acquired this project at an impressive price of $ 3.2 billion.
The thermostat is a device in the form of a tablet with a diameter of 3.2 inches (81 mm), a thickness of 1.44 inches (36.6 mm) and a mass of 150 g. Temperature, humidity, movement and light sensors as well as a rechargeable battery are built into the case. Information is displayed on the device display, which occupies the entire front panel. The red color of the display indicates the heating mode of the room, blue indicates air conditioning, large numbers indicate the temperature, the image of green leaves indicates the energy saving mode. Control is carried out using an external rotary ring and a display button.
Installing a thermostat is very simple. First, the old thermostat is dismantled (Nest is compatible with the vast majority of existing climate systems). Then install the base panel. The base panel has a built-in bubble level that will help ensure that the panel will be installed correctly. There are additional mounting plates that will help hide any openings from previous thermostats. The next step is to connect the wires, and the following terminals can be connected: heating system (boiler), air conditioning system (air conditioning), heat pump, humidifiers and air dryers, fan. And finally, the device itself is installed with a display. Nest has a built-in Wi-Fi module that connects to your home network during installation.


By installing and connecting the thermostat, the owner starts to control it in manual mode - raise, lower the temperature, turn off at the exit from the house, set a comfortable temperature for the night, etc. After about a week of training, the thermostat remembers the habits and mode of the owner and begins to regulate the air condition in the house . The thermostat itself determines that the owner is not at home and includes an energy saving mode. Self-study does not negate the possibility of manual adjustment of parameters, in addition, the parameters can be changed remotely from a mobile device using an installed application or from a computer on the company's website. Also on the site you can see the history of energy consumption, the amount of energy saved through Nest and get useful recommendations.
In the US, the thermostat is sold for $ 249 with free shipping.
