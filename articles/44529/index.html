A feature of the RealMeters project is filtering ads by users; anyone can add a reseller number to the stop list and exclude an ad from the list. Thus, time-consuming work carried out by each agency is removed.

In the near future, a list of agencies capable of providing only legal services and the cost of their services will be published in the RealMeters project.
