Who should Microsoft customers switch to the Dynamics 365 version?

Dynamics 365 is a new integrated product from Microsoft that combines Microsoft ERP solutions (AX and Nav) and Dynamics CRM. And this is a product that caused quite a heated discussion and disputes even before its release, and most marketing materials spoke about technological advantages that are not so easy to translate into the language of business.

Let's try to make a small review for those industrial enterprises that have thought about changing their current system (it could be Axapta 3.0 or Microsoft Dynamics AX 4.0, 2009 or 2012 - our experience says that the entire product line is represented on the Russian market) on Microsoft Dynamics 365 Enterprise Edition.

The review concerns industrial enterprises and therefore is rather subjective. To make it more substantive, we analyze the main advantages of the new system, which are voiced in marketing materials, and we will assess whether our enterprises need them.
