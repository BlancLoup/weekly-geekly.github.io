March 1 - Teamlead Meetup: on employee assessment and motivation

Together with colleagues from Tinkoff.ru, AGIMA, CSSSR and DigitalHR, we gather in Moscow a timblid rally, where we discuss how to evaluate and motivate developers. It is easy to motivate when you have a small ideological startup, and we will talk about the work of the team leader in an agency or a large company. Come March 1 to Digital October, it will be interesting!



First, the leaders of the development of Tinkoff.ru, AGIMA and CSSSR will speak:

  • Andrei Ryzhkin, CTO AGIMA, will talk about how he is struggling with demotivating employees: what things enrage developers and how he levels the factors that prevent programmers from working normally.
  • Elena Alshevskaya, operational director of CSSSR, will share her experience in evaluating and supporting developers in a completely remote team.
  • Alexander Polomodov, head of development in the management of attracting Tinkoff.ru, will tell you how he manages to circumvent the difficulties of hiring a large company with a well-established brand and attracting the best.

After the reports, we will immediately turn to questions from the floor in a round table format: Katerina Gavrilova, CEO & Founder DigitalHR, and Ivan Mikheev, Deputy CTO AGIMA will join the discussion. And after that there will be time for networking and personal acquaintance with the speakers.

Mitap will be held this Friday, March 1, at the Digital October site at Red October. Register on the site .

We also have a Telegram-chat in which you can chat with colleagues, meet the speakers and ask them questions. They promise to answer everything during a speech or talk in person afterwards.
