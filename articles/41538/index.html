iTunes 8.0.1

An update for iTunes is available.

In iTunes 8.0.1, reliability and performance are improved, and several serious flaws are fixed and the following results are achieved.
  • Play the current song while creating a new Genius playlist.
  • Improved synchronization of voice menus with iPod nano.
  • Resolving the problem of removing HD transmissions at boot.
  • Improved check for updates from the App Store.
  • Improved compatibility with Windows.
  • Resolving Genius Result Sync Issues with iPod.
  • Solving the problem of "blue screen" on a computer running Windows Vista when iPod is connected to a computer.

Russian localization is also corrected.
