iPhone - Central Bank Courses

Good day to the habra people!
For me today he really became good).
Well, I hasten to share my joy and a little popiarity.
I would like to present you my first application. Exchange rates of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation. Yes, it's probably trite, but ...

And so, the functionality:


- display the list of currencies. In addition to courses set daily, there are also courses set monthly, and this is about a hundred currencies (Egyptian pounds, Israeli shekels, and many others);
- the ability to display only monthly exchange rates, only daily or both;
- ability to display only selected currencies;
- sorting the display of currencies;
- the ability to display the difference with the previous exchange rate in percent, just the previous value of the rate or the difference in rubles.

A bit of history


The application was written a long time ago, respectively, I used it myself. I, like all those infected with the gold rush of the AppStore, decided to start with a simple one, and for me the exchange rates seemed simple for me. But it was necessary to do better than the competition. It turned out better or not to judge now not only me ...
