Internet Explorer 9 launched in Russia with Mail.Ru, Yandex and a new game.

As you know, this week we released the final version of Internet Explorer 9 - just 11 months after the first public version. About the capabilities of the new version can be read in the official press release .

However, besides international news, we traditionally cooperate with the largest Russian companies so that users of the Runet can use our browser as conveniently as possible.
The largest Russian companies, Yandex and Mail.Ru , offer to download IE9 with their services embedded today.

In addition, one of the features of IE9 is the function of the attached sites, which allows you to work with your favorite site as with a regular desktop application right from the Windows 7 taskbar.
For example, read mail on Mail.Ru or Yandex, chat with friends on Odnoklassniki, Facebook and Twitter and even manage the viewing of music videos on IVI.ru.



Each of you can add such an opportunity to your own site - and participate in the competition of attached sites .

Of course, Internet Explorer 9 allows you to write modern applications in HTML5 and display them as quickly as possible to the user using hardware acceleration. Especially for the launch of IE9, JoyBits developers from St. Petersburg have released an online version of their popular mobile game Doodle God.



Each of you can test your own knowledge of web standards.
You can also see all the materials of the recently-launched HTML5 Camp in Moscow.

All information for users is on the Online Beauty site, for developers - on MSDN . Welcome to the modern, fast and beautiful Internet! :)
