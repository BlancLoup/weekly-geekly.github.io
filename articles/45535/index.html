TV program for your Mac

Watching your favorite TV shows on a poppy, I ran into a rather interesting problem - a TV program! The fact is that for macs there are not so many software for working with tuners, so it was not so easy to fasten a TV program to this software. However, a solution was found: EyeTV + XMTLV.

EyeTV is a program that works with various TV tuner models on Macintosh computers. It has a full range of modern functions, including watching TV channels, receiving radio stations, time shift mode, recording, scheduler and editing records.

XMLTV , a format based on eXtensible Markup Language, has been specifically designed for the distribution of programs on television channels that can be used in various applications.
