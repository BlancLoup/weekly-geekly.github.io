Parsing Heads-up Display from the Recon Instruments MOD Live ski mask. Attempting to fix ...

Dear ladies and gentlemen, do not repeat this method of parsing Heads-up display, since after, with a high probability, there will be only an Android device without the ability to display information.
I did not find a single article about the analysis before I began to disassemble, which is a pity! I will try to correct this defect.



For the brave and curious photos of the opening of the display, as well as more than a dozen images of the internal microdisplay under the cut ...



My acquaintance with the Heads-up Display technology did not begin with the points from the search giant, but with the MOD Live mask from the Recon Instrument display. Great was a gift from a friend for the thirtieth! It is a pity that I managed to break the display on the first trip to the mountains in this mask.



I will not paint marketing nonsense and obviousness about Heads-up Display its convenience and innovation ... You can find it all on the manufacturer's website and shops selling gadgets. Next you are waiting for photos of the opened display and only hardcore ...

The cause of the breakdown is trivial - trying to adjust the viewing angle of the display in a mask on a slope without disassembling it. The owners of MOD Live and, possibly Snow2, be careful !!! The protective metal ring supporting the display hinge has come off. The hinge broke into two parts and, continuing to rotate the display by inertia, I broke the train.

It was a shame, but the RF service centers Recon Instruments could not be found. None of the repairmen forums responded to the announcement. It remained to independently try to somehow replace the train.

Analysis of the main part of the device is very simple, you only need a small screwdriver with hexagonal nozzles. I once bought such a screwdriver and successfully repaired the audio plug in my first Asus P535 smartphone, and that's when the screwdriver came in handy.

Actually, in the MOD Live photo from the article the hinge is marked, it is also the place where I managed to break the train.
