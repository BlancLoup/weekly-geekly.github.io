A 60-day trial version of Microsoft Office 2013 has become available.

Very short news.
Not so long ago, a trial 60-day version of Microsoft Office Professional Plus 2013 from Microsoft became available.

Editorial Plus is the maximum, it includes all the relevant programs: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, Outlook, Access, Publisher and Lync.

A version with Russian localization is available for download , 32 and 64-bit systems are supported.

To obtain a trial key and download links, simply follow the link to enter your Microsoft account or register. Next, you need to confirm your personal data, select the desired 32x or 64x edition (do not forget to uncheck the checkboxes) and click continue.

As a result, we have the key for the 60 day version and the download link. Enjoy
