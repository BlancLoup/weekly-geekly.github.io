Protected USB flash drive for 350 rubles with your own hands

We are buying

For a start, I bought such a container in Alloy. It is made of aluminum and, supposedly, quite durable. The walls are a millimeter thick on the shiny side and a half millimeter with blue. It costs 55 rubles.

For sealing in the store Fix-Price, a 20-watt thermogun was bought, which, though low-powered and very fragile, is enough for our tasks. He cost me 37 rubles. On the inside, we disassembled a Transcend JetFlash T5 flash drive for 2 GB. Specially took not very big, so it was not a pity. For the connector, I also purchased a usb-plug for 20 with a compression on the cable.

As a result, I got 350 rubles.

Putting it all together



I apologize in advance for the pictures, but there was nothing near the phone.

For a start we deal with the flash drive. We disassemble it in any accessible way, it is all held up with glue. When you take it off the plate with a lever-button, be careful. It rests on gum. As a result, we have an almost monolithic construction in our hands:


These four contacts are a rather fragile design, I had a sad experience when they broke off, taking with them half of the flash drive. That is why we will strengthen them with a full usb-plug.


We take any four wiring (for example, two pairs from the power cord) and solder them on one side to the USB flash drive, on the other to the back of the usb-plug with glue to make something like this:


Then we warm the gun thoroughly and fill the entire sealed container with glue and quickly insert the USB flash drive into it. If something did not work with hot melt, then it can be melted even with a soldering iron. He stink and will not burn.



The flash drive is ready!
