Level 80 Watchmaker

One of Weil's notebooks in which he sketches his ideas. He has already accumulated more than 300 such books.











In this watch, the author uses gravity. When the wheel moves along the guide, the glass ball reaches the 6 o'clock position. This reminds the owner of the need to translate the dial.



The battery is suspended on wires, which are part of the watch's electrical circuit.
