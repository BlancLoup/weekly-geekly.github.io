The most popular topics of reports at DrupalCamp 2011 in Kiev

As of May 1, 2011, and according to the data on camp11.drupal.ua/ru/program, I received very interesting information about what people who will be on the Drupal camp in Kiev on June 3-4, 2011 wish.




Surprise!



I was surprised by the fact that most of all gathered votes on the topic of using Drupal for mobile devices ! The topic is new and interesting and it would be very cool to have reports on this issue.

The direction of the " Drupal-community " gained the least votes, but this is not surprising. Rather, this suggests that the majority in the Drupal community do not see the benefits and potential in putting their modules on drupal.org or otherwise interacting with the community. But we need to highlight this area, so we will have a parade of modules made by our developers. Moreover, the Ukrainian Drupal-Company continues to take shape and we all participate in its development.

The second most unfavorable direction is the " Drupal-build ". It is also quite expected result - the conference in Moscow showed that our developers still do not see the prospects for the creation and development of their own assemblies, although the experience of our western comrades suggests otherwise. I will not prove and explain this topic to you - let us leave it to those speakers who will take to reveal this direction. Very promising direction.

The distribution of the remaining directions is approximately uniform, except for the direction " Development and Deployment ". This direction has always enjoyed special success at all camps and conferences. The process of supporting large sites is not an easy task, which has to be solved in its own way, because there are no similar projects. I hope that we will hear a lot of interesting and new on this topic on the camp. It is also very important to use good methods to organize development. This will either enable the company to grow, or will inhibit its development. At the camp there will be a unique opportunity to listen to company executives who have already searched for and found their own development workflow solutions and successfully use them.
