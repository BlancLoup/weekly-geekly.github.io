From the "South-West" to Cambridge. Winter Business School from InCube

InCube starts cooperation with the springboard business accelerator in Cambridge! We want to give the opportunity to projects that will apply for participation in the program, better prepare, understand the strengths and weaknesses of their team and product. Therefore, we invite them to the Winter School - it will be held from February 15 to February 17 on the campus of the RANEPA. Three days of intensive, from morning to late evening, the work will be devoted to building brands around the business of students and working out the art of project presentation.

The winter school will be a preparation for the competition for the selection of Russian start-ups to participate in one of the best technological clusters in the world - Cambridge. According to its results, projects with international potential and the strongest teams will go on an internship where their business will be mentors and experts who have already created a name for themselves on both sides of the Atlantic.

Up to 25 people can join the InCube program, including residents of the incubator. They will have an excellent opportunity to make business contacts, get acquainted with the experience of colleagues and get feedback on their projects. And, of course, it is better than others to prepare for a public presentation - a key stage of the competition. Registration is already open - you can apply by filling out a form with a description of the project.

The students of the Winter School will be divided into small teams and under the guidance of mentors and InCube business incubator teams will work on their projects. Two working days will be devoted to basic tasks that face any project at an early stage of development: creating a brand (led by Sergey Mitrofanov from BrandFlight) and a public presentation (led by Dmitry Lazarev - worked with MegaFon, Philips, GfK, the largest Russian media).

And at the final stage of the program, everyone will have the opportunity to consolidate the skill of public speaking and present an updated concept of their business. The projects will be divided along industry lines: each group will have to make a presentation to the jury, which will include an industry expert and a venture capital investor.

Following the results of training, graduates of the Winter School InCube will be awarded diplomas. The cost of training for students whose projects are not residents of the ANE, will be 5 000 rubles for two days. You can ask questions and learn more about the program by writing to the team of the InCube business incubator RANEPA.
