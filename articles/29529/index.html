coDraw - weekly startup

Thanks to all those who took part in the discussion of the Two Sheets project a week ago.

Misha_kvakin and I took into account most of the comments and suggestions and are pleased to present the coDraw project - joint drawing .

We added everything that you lacked and corrected something that was not convenient. Presently:
  • One big field.
  • A set of necessary tools: pencil, line, rectangle .
  • Ability to upload images , add a link.
  • Adding to the drawing area a preview of any site, drawing with text .
  • Convenient chat. Now, not two people, but up to 5 (more - negotiated separately).
  • Preservation With watching all the action! It is better to see once: demo from merdanchik (thank you very much).
  • And all the pluses from the previous project, while no flash .
Comments, as usual, welcome any comments and suggestions.
