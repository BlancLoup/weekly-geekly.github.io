Luxury electric car for children for 1000 dollars

Meet this Henes Broon F870 - luxury electric car , whose length is about 120 centimeters. It is perfect in size for six year old children. A fully charged version of the electric car has two engines and a four-wheel drive, like the Tesla P85D, and is capable of driving up to 3000 miles on one charge.



The base model was named Broon F830. The basic package (are we talking about the toy?) Already includes a four-point safety belt to prevent injuries from driving fast. The kit includes a remote control, which makes this electric car one of the largest radio-controlled toys. The remote control works via Bluetooth 4.0 from a pair of AAA-batteries.

The top speed of the top model F870 is 10 miles per hour. The car is perfectly controlled thanks to differential steering and a short wheelbase. On one charge, the car will travel 3000 miles , according to Wired.



Body parts electric cars can be replaced to change the color or style of the car.



Front and rear lights and turn signals are fully functional.



The electric F870 is equipped with independent suspension.
