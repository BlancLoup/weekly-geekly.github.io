Pop stars under the lens of artificial intelligence

Intro


The image of pop stars attracts millions of audiences, it consists of the appearance, music, actions, and of course the lyrics of their songs.
What are popular artists singing about? At all times, the themes are the same: love, loneliness, sex, entertainment, eternal values.


However, surely you yourself felt that from each artist there is a deposit in the soul, a taste. And the words seem to be the same, but the shades are different. And each has its own.


The picture that the artist draws in his own words is unique and reflects their inner world, their psychological portraits.


In the article, we try to look behind the veil of words through the prism of AI and discern behind them the soul of such popular stars as Drake, Rihanna, Coldplay, Twenty One Pilots, Dua Lipa, The Chainsmokers and Katy Perry.
Found someone from your favorite artists and you want to know their hidden emotions and experiences?

In the right corner is the IBM Watson Personality Insights service.

A few words about him.

The service is designed to analyze a coherent conversational text that people communicate in social networks, letters and forums. Based on this text (it is desirable that the text length exceeds 500 words), the service provides 4 sets of characteristics:

  1. Psychological characteristics of the person according to the theory of "Big Five"
  2. Needs
  3. Values
  4. Consumer preferences

Yes, at once I will make a reservation that the service does not understand Russian, you can bypass this restriction by direct online translation into English, losing some of the original information, but keeping the context sufficient for automated application of the service. I drove through Personality Insights, translated via Google.Translate Borodino of Lermontov, and the overall impression is quite acceptable results.

A little about the research methodology.

For the analysis, the last most successful marketing albums of several top stars were selected. A complete set of poems from each album as a solid text was sent as a solid text for analysis to the Watson Personality Insights service provided by IBM.
