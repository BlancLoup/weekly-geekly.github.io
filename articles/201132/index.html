Test Labs 2013 Part II. November 16th Online conference for testers

We invite you to take part in this year's second online conference for testers and quality engineers - Test Labs. If in March our attention was focused on testing automation, this time we decided to expand the topics a bit and bring to your attention reports on the effectiveness of automated testing, as well as test management on complex projects.

Thematic areas of reports:

  • Creation of autonomous system of automatic regression testing;
  • Load testing in a short time;
  • Use of simple testing metrics in practice.

Test Labs 2013 format - one day, 4-6 reports, discussion sessions during breaks. The purpose of the event is to tell about the experience of solving non-trivial tasks and to hear the opinion of colleagues in the workshop. As with all the events of the Soft Labs series, at this conference we will try to focus on practically useful knowledge that would be useful in daily work, both for the beginner and for the advanced specialist.
The conference will be held on the Webex platform. To connect to the event, it is enough to have access to the Internet (you can connect from mobile devices).
To become a participant in the conference, you must register on the site . Participation is free.
