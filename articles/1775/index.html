Yandex.Market has become more convenient in the regions

The Yandex.Market service groups goods delivery in a new way: now users of Russian cities will first of all see offers from local online stores. And only then - the offers of other stores that deliver goods to a particular region.

Yandex is implementing this initiative as part of a program to support regional online stores. To participate in it, the Internet merchant needs to have a regional warehouse or to deliver goods in the city (region) declared. The program does not include Moscow and St. Petersburg.
