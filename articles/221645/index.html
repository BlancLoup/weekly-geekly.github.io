Design


The Marshall Major headphones bring us back to the heyday of rock'n'roll and the New Wave of British Heavy Metal. You look at them - and in your head starts to play something like Run To the Hills or Dirty Deeds Done Dirt Cheap. But it is optional and depends on your particular musical preferences.

The outer walls of the ear cushions are made of vinyl and decorated with the brand-new white Marshall logo, which is recognizable from afar.





Conveniently, the headphones can be folded. So they will save space in the bag.



Part of the wire is twisted by a spring, which prevents the possibility of spinning anywhere else. If necessary, you can stretch the wire to two meters, in a quiet state, it conveniently reaches the jacket pocket, where the mobile phone lies.



The steel spring prevents the wire from wrinkling.



The main colors of Marshall are black and gold, as well as the white logo. This is reflected in the design of the headphones.



Inside, the fasteners are decorated with embossed Marshall logos on a gold background, the jack and the spring are also gold.



The painting of Jim Marshall, the company's founder, is printed on the inside of the rim.



The microphone remote is equipped with one button. On Android, one click starts or stops the music, double-click - and you call the last number you dialed.

Materials


The first thing you notice when you hold the headphones in your hands is high-quality materials and a perfect assembly.



The main materials of the headphones are soft-touch plastic, vinyl, leather, eco-leather and steel. The arc is covered with natural leather, while the cushions are made of soft eco-leather, which is difficult to distinguish from the real one. Even by smell. There is a minus in this - in summer it can be hot. The outer walls of the ear cushions are made of vinyl. Ear pads attached to the arc with steel wire. Headphone cups are made of vinyl - the same material used in Marshall amplifiers. It's nice to know that I pulled the speakers of such a manufacturer over my ears. The wire is thick rubber.
