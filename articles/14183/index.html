Google Docs - now and presentations!

Google has finally added the long-awaited and expected feature of working with presentations.
Now users of Google Docs (or Google Docs, as this service is officially called in Russian localization) have the opportunity to create presentations from scratch or download or edit PowerPoint presentations.

Available basic functions for the design of presentations, several design templates. It is quite natural that there is a collaboration function.
The finished presentation can be published as a slide show, while the document gets its own unique link, and viewers who have joined the viewer can communicate using the chat built into the sidebar. Example.


The appearance of the new application:


In general, web-editors of presentations are not a novelty by themselves, many online office suites (for example, Zoho) already have this functionality.

PS It seems that news sites have noticed the appearance of the presentation editor before the guys from Google made an official statement on their blog.
