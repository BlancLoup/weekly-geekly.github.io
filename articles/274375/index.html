The world's first delivery of SIM-cards by drones




In September of this year, delivery by drones was the first mass experimental project in the framework of the Yota FutureProof. The experiment on the delivery of SIM cards by drones started on September 2 at four points in Moscow: on the territory of the Strelka Institute, in the Museon and Sokolniki parks, and also in the Pavilion of the Polytechnic Museum at the Exhibition of Economic Achievements. The goal of the experiment # yota delivers to honestly and openly test in real conditions an unusual way of delivery and share the results with startups, young scientists and creators of projects in the field of IT.

  • In just one month, 964 SIM cards were delivered.
  • The total distance that the drones have flown is more than 578 km. This is approximately equal to the distance from Moscow to Voronezh.
  • The price of one air delivery in Moscow was 488.5 rubles, which is slightly lower than the average cost of delivery by courier (from 500 rubles within the Moscow Ring Road).
  • The total charging time for quadcopter batteries exceeded 96 hours, it took 6.5 kW of electricity.
  • Each flight of the drone cost 4 kopecks.
  • The average delivery time for a quadcopter was 90 seconds.

According to the results of the experiment, we concluded that delivery by quadrocopters can now be used as an alternative distribution channel for goods. But the project will be as effective as possible for those types of businesses where it is possible to eliminate the work of personnel at the points, which will save about a third of the budget and reduce the cost of delivery. The project, on average, pays off in 5-6 months.

Experiment with drones was appreciated not only by clients, but also by experts. Yota became one of the winners of the 2015 Innovation Time award.

Updated Yota Many Router




We started sales of the updated Yota Many router, which has a higher speed of receiving and transmitting data, as well as a more stable connection. In intensive use mode, the router works without recharging for more than 12 hours, and the standby time is 60 hours. The updated Yota Many model charges in just one hour from 0 to 70% - twice as fast as the previous model.

Mobile router Yota Many still allows you to connect up to eight devices, works in two modes (via USB or WiFi), suitable for smartphones, tablets, readers, laptops, personal computers, TVs, gaming consoles. Yota micro-SIM cards are already installed in the routers. Yota Many is compatible with Windows, Mac OS, Linux and other operating systems.
