Intel Xeon E3 v5 - Skylake for server

Just six months ago, we announced the appearance of the first sixth generation Intel Core processors (Skylake). And now, very quickly, given the scale of the development process, Skylake for servers in the face of the younger Intel Xeon series - E3 appeared. A detailed study of the entire line of E3 v5 can be studied on the Intel ARC website, it is quite extensive, so that the v4 turned out to be, one can say, a passing stage. The main differences compared to its predecessors:
  • increased system bus frequency and speed of data exchange with memory;
  • DDR4 support;
  • support for Intel Software Guard Extensions (Intel SGX) and Intel Memory Protection Extensions (Intel MPX).

To date, there are already at least several servers for Intel Xeon E3 v5, for example, I can quote HPE ProLiant DL20 Generation9 . So get a little Skylake in your backend you can now.
