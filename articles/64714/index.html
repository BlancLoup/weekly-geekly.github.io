Poken - your new business card



What do you think it is,% username%? Trinket? Flash drive? No, this is your electronic business card. This little animal will allow you to share the necessary information when meeting new people. Both at seminars and conferences, and at parties and concerts you can always find a person with this wonderful device and exchange contact information with him just by touching the poken legs.





This will save you from having to dictate a phone number or email to a new friend, look for new friends in social networks. Poken will always tell you a lot about your friends: what social networks your friends use, how their phone numbers and instant messengers have changed.

How to do it? Very simple. You just need to chock up: attach the paws to each other and they will exchange business cards. If everything worked out, the paws will flash green.



And after that, at home you will be able to connect the poken to your computer and use the website doyoupoken.com to look at the contacts of your new friends. To do this, run the Start_Poken.html file on your poken.


Each poken can store up to 64 contacts until they are transferred to the computer and warns the owner when it is full.
