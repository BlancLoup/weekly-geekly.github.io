Dell Technologies Webinars: all the details about our tutorial

Hello friends! Today's post will not be long, but hopefully useful for many. The fact is that for quite a long time already, Dell Tehnologies has been holding webinars on products and brand solutions. We want to talk about them in brief today, and also ask the respected Habr audience to share their opinion on this matter. Immediately an important remark: this story is about learning, not about selling.

By topic, all webinars are divided into 7 categories: storage systems, cloud solutions, data protection, convergent (and hyper-convergent) infrastructure, servers and networks, client equipment. The seventh category is called "professional services." If everything else is clear by name, here, perhaps, a little explanation is needed. These webinars are not about technology, but about the services that Dell Technologies provides to its customers: warranty services, service support, deployment services, upgrades, and so on.

Also, these 7 categories can be divided into two directions. Six of these are fully within the purview of Dell EMC solutions. Most webinars last about an hour, and the estimated duration is always stated in advance, so that viewers can plan their time. They are led by Dell Technologies staff members. Sometimes, when it comes to launching products and solutions based on hardware of partners, representatives of partners can also act as speakers. For example, relatively recently it was with Microsoft and VMware.

Speakers are not marketers and sales managers, but direct product specialists or even system engineers who are deeply involved in the topic and can answer almost any questions from the audience.
