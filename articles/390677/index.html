There are 7 words on this screen from ‚ÄúSouth Park‚Äù. 6 of them are familiar to almost everyone learning English. 4. Build a bike


The task is reduced to the search for complex words in the text that need translation.

The basic idea is that you can analyze ooooochen many texts in English, count statistics on the use of words and understand that some words are used much less frequently than others. 5. We go on a built bike


I drove through a program of 3-4 dozen sabs, estimated the values ‚Äã‚Äãof the metrics that the analyzer issued. I tried to watch movies with what happened. Showed to friends, acquaintances and site visitors.

To evaluate the results, I used two classic metrics for machine learning tasks:
  • Precision (precision) - the ability to correctly classify a word
  • Fullness (recall) - the ability to find all the words that require translation

It turned out that metric values ‚Äã‚Äãtend to jump from movie to movie. On some films, the completeness and accuracy showed 85% -90% of the desired, and on the other - around 55%. Having rummaged in a problem, I found the reason - I collected most of the data for statistical analysis from art books over the past 300 years and some words are found more often in them than are found in modern English. For example, the word bayonet (bayonet) in those days was much more common than now, but our classifier considers this word not so rare.

Although Colin, my friend from Britain, laughed for a long time and said that the expression ‚Äúmy meat bayonet‚Äù ( beef bayonet ) is now very often found among the military, but we will not consider this case.

I decided to roll back to the old version of the classifier, which I used several months ago. A classifier with a smaller but more varied amount of data turned out to be an order of magnitude better than a classifier built only on English fiction. The word recognition algorithm has become much less common.

The result obtained is generally consistent with the goal, but the algorithm still provides subs that are suitable for a person who has solid experience in using English. You need to have a certain skill in speech recognition and a tangible vocabulary of several thousand basic words. In this case, the subs will stand in good stead in improving English.
