Travel Charges for iPod, iPhone

Are you going on a trip and are not sure that your apple devices will have enough power? We'll have to look at the market for the relevant periphery. Take, for example, new items from Kensington. The company introduced two portable chargers with support for Apple iPod and iPhone. A capacious battery weighing 100 g will prolong the life of the iPhone by 100/21/6 hours in the mode of listening to music, watching videos and talking, respectively. The less bulky Mini Battery Pack and Charger weighs even less. Of course, its power reserve is losing compared to the full-size version - it will extend the use of devices by 30/3 hours in music and talk mode. Kensington will sell chargers for $ 70 and $ 50 (for the US market).
