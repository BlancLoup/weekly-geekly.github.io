Automatic news gathering on Twitter feed

Have a nice day, everyone! It just so happened that now my work is related to information, and I often have to look for news concerning certain topics. In addition to the already classic solutions in the form of popular news resources and official sites, an aggregator was needed, which quickly and constantly collects news on various resources in one place. It is desirable so that their receipt can be automated and as simple as possible. And you know, such a resource was found!




I do not know who created this thing, why, and why it was not spun. Maybe one of the creators of this service is on Habr√©, and he will answer, but this is not the point. This site collects all information feeds from various news resources in Russian, forms twitter feed on a separate account from them, and it can be easily (and most importantly - free and convenient) obtained in any usual way. In my case - tweetdeck with customized sheets and columns, which constantly pulls in more and more new data that can be used in my work.

This service works very simply: we take the company or person we are interested in, we enter the first letters of the name / name on the site . If it is in the database, a hint appears in which we select the object we are looking for.



For example, you can take the most popular companies: ASUS , Intel , AMD , Samsung , Google , Oracle , Apple .

We get links to the created twitter tapes, then we follow them or add them to the sheets, we use them as needed in any client or from the twitter site. This is so simple, without any manipulation, tedious manual search - everything is done for us.

I hope this site will develop, and later it will be possible to select a whole group of news at once (for example, mobile devices, or medicine) or news, screened out by geographic location - find out what's going on in your city or in a particular region.

This thing helped me a lot in my work and saved a lot of time, I hope, it will be useful for you too.
