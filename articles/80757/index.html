Release MS Office 2010 can take place in April


According to unofficial information, Microsoft plans to complete testing of Microsoft Office 2010 in the spring, after which (probably in April) it will go into production. On sale new Office , presumably, will appear in June.

During CES 2010, members of the Microsoft Office 2010 office suite team reported that the number of downloads of the Microsoft Office 2010 beta reached 2 million . Thus, since the release of the beta version of the program in the public domain, the program was downloaded approximately 40 thousand times every day. In this case, 9 out of 10 users called the new version of Microsoft Office much better than the previous one.
