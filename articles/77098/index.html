The blogindex - a new look at the blogosphere represents the Topblogi.ru blog rating



Our project, topblogi.ru , is more than just a rating of Runet blogs .

The goal of our project was to make not just a heartless rating - a copy of Yandex, but to create an interesting analytical element of the Runet Blogosphere , which will be interesting and useful to everyone.



First , in the ranking there is a new element of the analysis of the blogosphere - BLOGOINDEX .

This is our own invention and I will describe below what is in fact its uniqueness and benefit for anyone interested in events taking place in the blogosphere.

The blog index is a chart derived from the content analysis of the blogosphere. This is a new element of blogosphere analytics . On the topblogi.ru website , such an analysis is carried out for all posts from the top 50 of the Runet blogosphere and is displayed in two versions:

1. General Blog Index - words that, by and large, are most often found in the best posts in the last 10 minutes.

2. Local Blog Index - for each post from the rating, an analysis is carried out and a diagram is displayed according to the words that the author of the post most often used. ( still in development, but soon to be completed )

All people write in different ways , but All have elements that they most often use in the text. Someone often writes the words parasites, and someone often uses in the text - "I".

General Blog Index - shows which words Bloggers used in those posts that are now at the top of their popularity. Some people may not seem informative, however - this is actual data and we do not give them an estimate. How to evaluate this data each person decides individually. The fact that these particular words are used most often. In any case, the element in our opinion is necessary , although not sufficient for the analytics of the Blogosphere. In the future, the project will receive a more serious development and we will provide a more detailed and accented color of the Runet Blogosphere . I am sure that soon indexes like topblogi.ru will appear, however, a start has been made by us .

Also, of course, there is a standard set of features for the submission of rankings and RSS feed top.

Now every blogger who gets to the top will be able to quickly see which words he most often used in his post.

Secondly , it is possible to view the post - right from the main page - without switching to the blog.





Also, it is possible to enlarge the image in the post title with one click, without switching to the blog.
