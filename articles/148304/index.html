Regional legislation and judicial practice in the ATP "Pravo.Ru" for Android

In March 2012, we presented to your attention a review of new mobile applications of the Pravo.Ru reference and legal system. Today we have good news for users of mobile devices on the Android platform:
Regional legislation has been added to the application !


In addition, an opportunity has appeared in related documents to filter court practice by instances and courts, as well as to choose the editors of the document under study.
Quick navigation

Now by clicking on the search result immediately opens the part of the document where the search query is found. At the same time the application selects the desired text.


Regional legislation

You can go to the laws, orders and other official documents of regional significance from the search results. Indicate the rule of law or legal problem of interest, and the application will display all relevant documents. Just select the regions. In the next version, regional legislation will be available from the main page of the application.


Arbitrage practice

Working with court practice has become even more convenient. In related documents, you can open a list of judicial acts of specific courts and instances. To do this, select the article of interest and check the courts in the panel that appears.


Editorial document

Now, using the application, you can explore not only the current, but also any existing version of the document. When working with text, open the list of revisions and select the desired one.
