XEL-1 Organic LED TV with a screen thickness of about 3 millimeters

The Japanese again get everything before us.


Currently, OLED displays are used in digital cameras, mobile phones and other portable electronics, but they have not been used to create televisions. The XEL-1 is only 11 inches diagonal.


Thanks to the organic light-emitting diodes (OLED), the new TV boasts excellent image quality: good black depth, accurate color reproduction and correct display of fast moving objects, in general, everything LCD lacked (contrast ratio of 1,000,000: 1). The resource of this OLED-screen is 30 thousand hours - 10 years of operation at 8 hours per day with 45 watts of power consumption.


This miracle will appear on sale in Japan on December 1 of this year at a price of "approximately" 1,740 dollars.
