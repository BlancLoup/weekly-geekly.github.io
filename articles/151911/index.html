We change the design of the online store depending on the season, holiday and even mood

A person always wants something better, and the owner of an online store is no exception. Previously, its design seemed the height of beauty and laconicism, and now the range is different, autumn has come and in general - the mood has changed.

The new release of the AdVantShop.NET online store engine Ultimate is equipped with a design transformer - OnTheFly . It literally from the air - without entering the admin panel and without a programmer - allows you to change the design of the store and bring it closer to the ideal ... even if the standard itself changes several times a week.



Today's client wants the online store to live and change with him: he has a new year - even if there is a Christmas tree, a snowman and tangerines on the site.

With the OnTheFly design transformer you can always surprise the buyer: the online store gains flexibility and reacts quickly to any changes in reality - be it a holiday coming, a change of season or a simple desire to bring a fresh stream to your business. By September 1, for example, we created a school-autumn design.



With OnTheFly you can create by yourself or use ready-made solutions - the product offers about 300 design options.

There are 12 topics for different branches of business and seasonal holidays. 11 color schemes. 13 options for wallpaper. And we will only increase the base of design elements.



The design with OnTheFly changes once or twice: one time - open the interactive window of the transformer, two - create the unique face of the online store.

Transformer not only changes the design, but also constantly updated. When we release new themes - they can be activated immediately - through the admin area or manually by copying the required file from the product server to the desired folder.
