The course "Introduction to technology entrepreneurship" in ITMO

March 11, 2009 begins a new cycle of classes "Introduction to technological entrepreneurship."

Post in any case is not advertising, but a way to bring to those who want to attend courses.

Quote from the QD project website:
Interuniversity Innovation Studio QD starts a new cycle of the course ‚ÄúIntroduction to Technological Entrepreneurship‚Äù. The course is designed specifically for young entrepreneurs - innovators who want to bring to the market a new technological product, but do not yet know how.


You can learn about the pilot cycle from previous posts on my blog.

Here are the topics of 10 classes of which the course consists:

1) Introduction to technology entrepreneurship
technology as a competitive advantage / methodology for searching and validating ideas

2) Work with a business idea
brainstorming techniques / methods of preparing and conducting presentations / practice;

3) Basics of marketing
consumer problem: identification, classification, decision / definition of the target market / segmentation and resegmentation / practice

4) Value chain and business model
market positioning / business model design

5) Customer Development Methodology
step-by-step instruction on the development and promotion of the claimed product

6) Sales
sales process / sales and purchase cycles / sales organization

7) Team building and project management
time management techniques / team building rules / project management tools

8) Venture investments: raising capital
venture capital / stock types / venture business stages / writing executive summary / investor negotiation tactics

9) Venture investments: investor entry and exit from business
tactics of negotiating with the investor / drawing up Term Sheet / getting out of the investor from the business: IPO, direct sales, etc.

10) Conclusion and testing
practical testing / presentation of projects to investors of the QD fund

Also invited lecturers are expected.
