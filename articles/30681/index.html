I am rich

I Am Rich is a new application for the iPhone OS, which the author Armin Heinrich offers to purchase on the App Store for $ 999.99.



Description of this program:
A red icon on the screen of your iPhone or iPod every day will remind you (as well as everyone you show it to) that you can afford it.

This is a work of art without any hidden functionality.

If you press the information button on the main screen of the application, you will see the secret mantra. It will help you stay rich, healthy and successful.

UPD: $ 999.99 is the maximum price that a developer can charge for a single application on the App Store.
