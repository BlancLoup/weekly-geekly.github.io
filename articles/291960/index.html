Setting up an automatic series of letters in MailChimp on the example of an online store

Similar scenarios and principles can be applied to any of your projects. For example, you can set up a series of letters for subscribers; those who have registered for the trial; for clients.

The goal of all these letter manipulations is simple - to increase sales without any special expenses. Now we will step by step describe what emails need to be sent and how to set it all up in Mailchimp using the example of an online store. The principle of setting is this: once done, and then everything should work itself. Already in the process of work we analyze, improve, rework, etc.





Some things without developers or in your current store will not work. In this case, just remember that there is still something to strive for and, perhaps, it is time to update the platform. If anything, let's us :)

So, we have two scenarios for getting subscribers to the list and corresponding goals.


1. When you lured the subscriber by any bonus or useful information through a subscription on the site . 2. With a new order . For simplicity, we will consider only the first customer order. We need to make these people buy more, become more loyal and participate in the development of your store.

These are two different scenarios, since the buyer could not be a subscriber prior to his purchase. Came and bought, then we sign it to the newsletter. And those who simply subscribe buy and then follow the second mailing scenario - for customers. The third scenario is a general newsletter, which is useful to all.

Consider all these scenarios in detail and configure them in MailChimp.
