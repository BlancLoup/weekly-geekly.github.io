ZTE V880E smartphone review

Hi, Habr!

We are pleased to announce today another smartphone based on the Android OS. A feature of this smartphone is to work with two SIM-cards. We hope that for many of you ZTE V880E can become a reliable tool for all occasions. Next, a little about the smartphone itself.


ZTE V880E

Design


The appearance of the smartphone is minimalistic - nothing superfluous: a touchscreen candy bar with a calm design.
Size 122.5 x 64.6 x 11 mm, weight about 130 grams.



The ZTE logo is located on the back of the smartphone, the lid fits snugly, but it can be removed easily, there is a plain pattern on the lid.



Rear camera lens and flash. 5 megapixel camera resolution, autofocus.
On the right side - the volume rocker.



At the top there is a power button and a 3.5 mm jack for connecting a headset or headphones.



On the left side of the smartphone is placed connector microUSB.



Below is the microphone point.



Equipment


In a compact case, in addition to the smartphone itself, packed:
1. Wired stereo headset
2. Charger unit
3. USB data cable and charging
4. User Instructions

Screen


The screen diagonal is 4 inches, the resolution is 480 x 800 pixels - a decent screen resolution for a device of this class, if desired, you can start the game and watch the movie.

Work with two SIM-cards


Under the battery is a compartment for two SIM-cards. One slot is labeled WCDMA / GSM, the other is just GSM, if you need to use 3G for communication, install a SIM card in the compartment labeled WCDMA, because there is no such possibility in the second one. At the same time, both SIM cards are available in standby mode and the names of two mobile operators are immediately displayed on the screen, in the status line you will see the signal level of each of the SIM cards. When you call, you need to choose which card you need to use - one or two call buttons are displayed depending on the number of SIM cards inserted.
If you are talking using one SIM card, the second number will be unavailable at this time. Accordingly, in order not to miss important calls, you can contact your service provider and activate services such as call forwarding, voice mail or voice mail.

When sending an SMS message, you can also choose which SIM card to use for this, it is convenient for those who connect packages of the corresponding services.
