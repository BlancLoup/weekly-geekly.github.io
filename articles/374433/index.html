The phone received a large 6.2 inch display with an aspect ratio of 19: 9, a Qualcomm Snapdragon 636 processor, a camera with four-axis optical stabilization, Google Pay, and face recognition. Design


When creating a body ASUS Zenfone 5 used glass and aluminum. The latter is sandblasted using a zirconia-based abrasive. The front and back panels of the smartphone are covered with 2.5-D glass with curved edges, and the back panel has received an additional layer of lacquer, thanks to which concentric circles have acquired a rich sheen. The phone looks really cool, especially when the lighting is falling right on it.



The process of creating Zenfone 5 consists of more than 200 different technological steps. In the manufacture of the smartphone was taken into account every little thing. For example, its metal keys received a thermoplastic polyurethane seal that provides a soft, pleasant response.



Thanks to the combination of materials and well-thought-out manufacturing procedure, ASUS Zenfone 5 looks spectacular from any side. Its front panel stands out due to the large 6,2-dyumovy frameless screen, and the reverse side due to concentric circles that diverge from the fingerprint scanner.



There is also a dual camera module and a dual LED flash at the back. At the bottom of the smartphone is a USB Type-C connector, next to which is the calling speaker and microphone





On the top panel there is nothing remarkable except the second microphone, which is useful for the noise cancellation system. The SIM card tray is also located in its usual place, on the left side of the smartphone. It allows you to use either two SIM cards or one SIM card and a memory card. In the first case, both SIM cards will provide the ability to receive and make calls, but only one SIM card will be responsible for data transfer. Which one depends on the choice of the user.

Screen


One of the main distinguishing features of ASUS Zenfone 5 is its screen. The display with the Super IPS + matrix occupies 90% of the front panel area, the resolution is 2246x1080 pixels, and the aspect ratio is 19: 9. This rare resolution - 2246x1080 - is due to two reasons. Firstly, the screen got rounded corners instead of straight lines, and secondly, it has a small cutout in the upper part.



Such cuts have become a new trend, and they are found mainly in expensive flagship devices. In the resulting recess was placed front camera, earpiece and sensors. The rest of the area is given under a large display.



Disputes about the benefits of a notch display, also called a mono-brow, have not subsided for several months. Some of the users like it, and some, on the contrary, are against this approach. Judging by the size of the cutout at the Zenfone 5 screen, the engineers tried to make it as inconspicuous as possible. Therefore, the ‚Äúmonobrow‚Äù turned out to be small and will not hide the Android notification panel.

Cameras


Single-camera smartphones on the back have long been supplanted by dual-camera solutions. This is the module installed in ASUS Zenfone 5. The main rear camera has a 6-element lens with a viewing angle of 83 degrees, optical stabilization and a f / 1.8 aperture. The basis of this camera is a Sony IMX363 matrix with a resolution of 12 megapixels. The second rear camera boasts a lens with a viewing angle of 120 degrees, thanks to which you can use it to get widescreen shots. Also, both cameras received a common, dual LED flash, and switching between them happens instantly.

Also, the rear cameras received a manual shooting mode in which you can change the shutter speed, exposure, ISO, white balance and focusing distance. Well, in auto mode, you can manually open or close the aperture. Of course, the mechanical aperture of the Zenfone 5 is not, however, with the most open electronic aperture, the background behind the subject (and in front of it) will be blurred nicely.





The quality of the photo from the main camera can be called good. In many ways, it will depend on the external lighting. The biggest enemy of the Zenfone 5 camera is the same as that of other cameras - a small amount of light, or none at all. However, in a simple ‚Äúfive‚Äù the camera is noticeably better than in the light version, which liked to create lighted areas. Here it is minimized, and the light on the photo is unlikely. And if you choose the right focusing place, you will not meet them for sure.

Among the improvements to the camera is also worth noting automatic recognition of the shooting scenario. In total, the camera has 16 profiles, which is enough for all occasions. The developers even provided separate modes for shooting cats and dogs. Well, if you are a fan of sharing lunches and dinners on Instagram, then the world will know what you eat, in all colors!

Soft


ASUS Zenfone 5 is running Android 8.0 Oreo, which is complemented by a beautiful and lightweight branded shell ASUS ZenUI 5.0. In addition to her, he received many other "bonuses". This is a contactless payment system Android Pay, live Emoji ZENIMOJI and support for heterogeneous computing systems, reminiscent of artificial intelligence systems. In any case, the smartphone can learn from the user.



Emoji that has come to life, which can replace the user's face during online broadcasts and video calls, is another pleasant innovation. This option is found in single smartphones. ZENIMOJI technology was developed exclusively for ASUS smartphones, and it copes with its task remarkably.



Another useful feature, which can not be silent, is the automatic control of the ringtone volume. If this option is activated, Zenfone 5 will evaluate the volume level of the environment, and automatically adjust the volume of the ringtone, according to external conditions. Also, Zenfone 5 has the function of active noise cancellation, which is useful during calls from noisy places.

To protect Zenfone 5 from unauthorized access, a fingerprint scanner and face recognition technology is provided. Up to 5 ‚Äúfingers‚Äù can be stored in the scanner‚Äôs memory, and the front-facing camera can recognize the owner of the Zenfone 5 even in a dark room.
