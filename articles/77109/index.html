Mobile developers for the Google Android platform are not satisfied with the work of the online store Android Market , according to a study by Skyhook Wireless analysts, who surveyed 30 Android developers in October.
  • 57% are not satisfied with the income.
  • 90% reported that the number of downloads of their applications does not exceed 10,000.
