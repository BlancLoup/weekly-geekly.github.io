Facebook has made all 2 trillion messages available for search.



About a year ago, through the Facebook search box, it became possible not only to search for other users, but also to search for messages from friends . From today, Facebook has taken another step forward and has opened a full-text search on all 2 trillion posts on the social network , published since its launch.

‚ÄúWith 1.5 billion searches per day and more than 2 trillion posts in our index, search is an important long-term strategy for Facebook,‚Äù the official blog said.

With the expansion of the entire social network, the search acquires a completely new meaning. If before you could see the reaction of friends and relatives, now it is a complete search engine for current news. They no longer need to follow Twitter.



Simultaneously with the expansion of the search base, the work of search prompts was improved while typing a query, with the display of queries on current topics right now. This makes it easy to find news. For example, if you type "water", then immediately prompted "water on Mars" (in English). However, the results for all will be different, because the search is personalized and customized for each according to their interests.

Search results from friends and the rest of the public are separated from each other. For public posts, statistics are updated in real time, how many people have over-pledged this information. Apparently, the search in all content on Facebook is currently only in English, that is, if the profile is set to English (US) as the main one.
