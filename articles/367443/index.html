LETV X3-55 is the flagship model of the line with the largest diagonal of 55 "and powerful stuffing. So, the TV runs on a powerful 4-core Cortex-A17 processor with a clock frequency of 1.8 GHz and has a 4K screen with a viewing angle of 178 degrees.


The screen frames are quite thin - 6.9 mm on the sides, 11.5 mm on top and 13.8 mm on the bottom. The working area is thus 54.64 inches. The very same TV, like all models of the line, is made of plastic with aluminum elements. In this case, the front of the TV is made of aluminum. The thickness of the panel is 15.5 mm, and the maximum thickness given the block with the filling on the back of the TV does not exceed 46 mm.


The LETV X3-55 is also notable for an advanced stereo system that includes six 20W speakers, as well as Dolby Audio and DTS Premium Sound systems. All this is reinforced by fiber optic audio output.

LETV X3-50, while retaining all the features described above, is inferior to the previous model in terms of diagonal size and processor clock frequency. However, there is support for 3D and a 178-degree viewing angle along with 4K resolution here is just the way. Branded pair of 3D-glasses is included. Speaking of equipment, all models except LETV X3-50 include the same set of components: a remote control, 3-in-1 connecting cable, a power cord, and a stand.


In the case of the LETV X3-50, the stand has an arcuate appearance and this can be considered the hallmark of powerful models (the LETV X3-55 comes with a similar stand). All models also support VESA mounts.


The design of the LETV X3-50 is exactly the same as the 55-inch model. There is a similar thickness of the frame, and the design of the TV itself is designed in such a way that the panel is visually separated from the unit with the hardware and the ports as if they were two separate devices. Such a decision is justified in the sense that when viewed even from a large angle, the user can hardly notice anything other than a thin panel.

The LETV X3-43 is not inferior in performance to the two older models, but is simpler. This is manifested primarily in the diagonal reduced to 43 inches, the absence of 3D and 4K resolution. There is also a more simplified sound system, including two speakers of 8 watts each. As for the LETV X3-40 - this is the younger model of the line, which differs from the LETV X3-43 exclusively reduced to 39.56-inch panel and lower price tag.


In addition to a significantly lower price in relation to the senior members of the line, it is worth noting reduced dimensions: the panel thickness is 12.5 mm (maximum 38.5 mm), the thickness of the frames at the top, left and right is 6.9 mm, at the bottom - 11.5 mm. Instead, the metal parts here are all made of plastic, and the arcuate stand is replaced by a more minimalist simple form.


The design of LETV X3-40 and LETV X3-43 is made in a solid form factor, and the block with connectors is not as veiled as in older models. In general, this is a more classic type of TV for those who are not ready to keep a futuristic thing in their living room.
