The fastest processor will launch IBM

The processor at a clock frequency of 4.7 GHz will release the company IBM. Representatives of the company called the development of "the fastest processor in the world."

Compared to the Power5 chip, the new version doubles the clock speed and 4 times increases the cache (8 MB).



IBM says that Power6 will focus on attracting customers who use HP, Dell and Sun servers with Intel Itanium and Sun Sparc processors.
