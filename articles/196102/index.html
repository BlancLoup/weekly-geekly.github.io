Now you can link Google Play Developer Console to your Google Analytics account.

Hello,

Today I saw in Google Analytics the ability to link Google Play Developer Console account
(I watched Conversions - Google Play - Transition Map - I didn‚Äôt see such a tab before).
What it gives us - now it will be possible to see not only how many new users have downloaded the application,
but also the number of page views of the application on Google Play , which was promised more on Google IO.



I myself Analytics offered to do it, for the rest it seems to be done somehow like this:

  • Go to the Administration.
  • There we select the account of your mobile application (resource).
  • Resource Settings.
  • Advanced Settings.
  • Links to applications from Google Play.
  • On!
  • Put a tick in front of your application.
  • Save.


It seems to be all.

Since after enabling this analytics option, I still have very little - I cannot do a more detailed review so far.
