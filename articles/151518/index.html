And that's all. That's all. Read more here: www.ripe.net/internet-coordination/ipv4-exhaustion . And PI-addresses (provider-independent) are no longer issued. Now.

Only the reserves of the LIRs themselves will remain (it is clear that in recent months all the hamsters have done nothing to themselves, but how long will this last?).
