YouTube introduced Trends Map: visualizing user preferences on a map.



True, so far only for the United States, but that is also the case - after all, now you can see which video, for example, is preferred by retirees from North Dakota. For some, a new statistical tool will only be a means to entertain, and someone that may well be a candidate in psychology will be protected on the basis of the Trends Map. In addition to the geography of user preferences, the developers have provided some more tools.

So, you can watch YouTube trends for 12/24 hours. And also you can view the preferences of people of different ages / gender. So really, to find out what videos like, for example, women of balzakovskogo age from the state of Nevada, now is not a problem.

There is also an opportunity to see in which regions and among which groups of people this or that video is popular. According to the developers, so far this is an experimental service that only works in the United States. But there is a high probability that the global trend service YouTube will be presented soon. And then, indeed, many will become more interesting to live.
