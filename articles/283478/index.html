Communication 2016: what's interesting?

Hello!



While the public is actively discussing the non-opening doors of the UAZ, in Moscow, Krasnaya Presnya, the Svyaz-2016 exhibition is once again held. When I visited the exhibition two years ago, it took up more space. At least one pavilion. Now occupies 4 rooms in two pavilions. This is not very much, but if we consider that now the members of the big three are not participating (and they used to occupy a significant part), it is not that small. There were practically no operators, there were more manufacturers and developers, and this is good news.

Under the cut a short story about interesting products, companies and trends.
Of course, with photos, wherever possible.

Welcome!

One hall was fully occupied by manufacturers of copper and optical cable. Another - manufacturers of navigation devices Glonass / GPS (albeit, goes under the flag of Navitech). One - almost completely given to Chinese manufacturers. Well, two more halls - the main pavilion.

Official website of the exhibition: www.sviaz-expo.ru

See also the guide . It has a full list of exhibiting companies.

Russian switches

What first caught my eye was the abundance of Russian switches. This is eltex, Angstrom Telecom, Natex. Each has its own line.



And not only fast and gigabit ethernet. Eltex showed new products - aggregation switches with 24/48 ports 10G and 40-gigabit uplinks . Made on the Broadcom chipset, but the cisco-like control is its own, samopisny, native (I believe eltex representatives with the word). Eltex never ceases to amaze, actively expanding its lineup with cool solutions.



Chinese manufacturers, of course, were also represented in large quantities.

TS Computers

In general, the exhibition clearly shifted the focus towards the development of special devices: for military applications, special services, and even space. For example, laptops in non-killed version with a core i7 on board. The body is made of milled piece of aluminum. Outwardly, something reminded macbook 13, but it is heavier per kilogram, and it will be even thicker.



Another novelty: a tablet that also works in a 20-degree frost. Pretty normal sizes, especially considering its purpose.



It is clear that the stuffing (boards, chips, displays) is imported. But the undestructible construction is ours,

Russian LPWAN

At the booth Skolkovo solution was presented Strizh. It basically has LPWAN technology.
Here it is - the Russian IoT in action. This is already an end-user solution with marked prices.

LPWAN bitrate is extremely small (hundreds / thousands of bits per second) and is intended for telemetry. Manufacturers claim a distance of 10 kilometers (!) teltonika

Lithuanian contract manufacturer surprised with its power - hundreds of thousands of devices per month! The guys claim that now they are very competitive against the backdrop of increased prices from Chinese manufacturers. In this situation, it is easier for European companies to come to Vilnius with an order than to China. But how important is European production for Russian companies is a big question for me.

Yes, even these guys are expanding and want to go out to a million devices per month. Impressive.

They showed their IoT devices as manufactured devices .

Official website: www.teltonika.lt

FOD

The recognized leader among manufacturers of reflectometers introduced the mini-OTDR 7330 , which works in both automatic and manual mode. And also supports the connection of microscopes.
Very compact size, two wavelengths, automation.



Responsive resistive touch interface and device start in 1 second (!). I even took a video:

Conclusion
