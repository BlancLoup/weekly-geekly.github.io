Sea shell


Our digest is opened by Duke Ellington Stafford Hunter orchestra musician, who in addition to the classic trombone chose a seashell. Hunter himself says that the seashell is a trombone made by nature.



Vegetable Orchestra


For years, these guys from Austria have been invited to various talk shows and concerts. They are interviewed, and the group conquers the audience with their resourcefulness. The team consists of 10 musicians, one chef and one sound engineer. The peculiarity of the orchestra, as the name implies, is that all musical instruments are made of vegetables.

Bikrophone


This tool is built on the basis of a bicycle frame. Sounds are extracted from bass strings, bells and other elements of the structure. You can play with the bow, and just fingers. The peculiarity of the design is that the microphone is connected to the recording system, which loops the extracted sounds, and the effect is superimposed. It sounds mysterious and even a little scary.
