Evernote for iOS update: shared notebooks, simplified access to offline notebooks and more

Today's Evernote update for iOS (version 4.1.7) will allow you to open access to entire notebooks for other users. The update also includes a number of improvements aimed at improving stability and simplifying access to offline notebooks. Read more about the new features below.

Download Evernote for iOS ¬ª

Sharing notebooks


The ability to share notebooks entirely is very useful when working in a team. Evernote for iOS already allows you to share individual notes, and now you can share your notebooks with others. This is done like this:

  • iPhone: On the Notebooks screen, click on the ‚ÄúEdit‚Äù button, then on the desired notebook, and select Share this notebook .
  • iPad: on the notepad screen, click on the ‚ÄúEdit‚Äù button, select the notepad and click on the share icon.


After that, the sharing settings screen will appear, where you can choose whether to open a notebook for the whole world or only for individual users. You can enter email addresses of people you want to open access to manually or select them from the contact list by clicking on the plus icon.

In addition to new sharing features, premium account owners can also allow other users to edit the contents of their notebooks. These settings can be specified in the field Recipients are allowed:.

If the notebook is open to everyone, you can post a link to it on Twitter or Facebook from the sharing settings screen. You can also copy the link and send it by mail or chat.

Sharing control
From the sharing screen, you can also open access to the notebook to new people, cancel access to selected users, configure access rights or close the notebook completely.


Easy access to offline notebooks


Premium account owners have the opportunity to store the contents of their notebooks entirely on the device and have access to them anytime and anywhere, regardless of the Internet connection. In this update, we have simplified the transition to offline notebooks, which are now grouped at the top of the list of notebooks. Go to it to view your offline notebooks or click on the gear icon to add new notebooks, delete old ones, or make all notebooks available offline.

And not only...


This update also implements a lot of seemingly unnoticeable improvements, in particular, the application will now be much more responsive in the process of editing a note. We continue to work on new features, so stay tuned.
