VKontakte, Facebook - the main social networks in the world



Overview of the annual growth of the mobile Internet in the Opera mobile report
Oslo, Norway - January 20, 2008
Data
VKontakte and Facebook social networks are the most visited Opera Mini users according to Opera‚Äôs mobile report published today. The number of unique users of Opera Mini Facebook has grown by 693% in 2008. Due to the huge popularity of Opera Mini in Russian-speaking countries, VKontakte is the most popular social network. The user base of the site has grown by almost 910% in 2008. The full report is available at www.opera.com/smw .

Opera Mini users viewed more than 6.4 billion pages in December. Since November, the number of pages viewed has increased by 13.0%.
Opera Mini users have used more than 93.8 million megabytes of operator traffic from around the world. Since November, the amount of traffic used has increased by 13.2%. Data in Opera Mini is compressed by an average of 90%. If data were not compressed, Opera Mini users would use 938 million megabytes of traffic in December.
Note: In November, during the scheduled verification of Opera Mini statistics, we discovered technical problems associated with counting unique users. The problem was solved before the data for December appeared. All data until October 2008 are approximate. We will update the charts as soon as we correct the data. This has affected the information on unique users, however, data on the level of growth, page views and traffic are reliable. Indeed, the number of pages viewed per user per month increased by almost 360.
