What do domain zone coordinators want?

In order to register a domain, you need the following data:


  • Domain owner;
  • Administrator;
  • Technical contact;
  • Contact for invoices;
  • Group nameservers.

But some coordinators of domain zones are very boring and, for fun, they invent puzzles for domain owners and developers. I'll tell you about additional data for registering a domain that need to be added to an extension block.

Domains: .cat, .scot, .gal, .eus


Text field: intendedUse - to describe the purpose of using the domain. You never know what!


Domains: .nz


Text field: authCode
If you want to register hobbit.nz and there is already a domain hobbit.co.nz , then please enter the authorization code, otherwise you will receive ... you will not receive anything (clarified, the requirement has now been removed, but left the point as an example).

Domains: .vote, .voto


Checkboxes: vote Acceptance and voto Acceptance
When registering a domain name, you confirm that the registrar of this domain complies with the registration policy as described by the link: get.vote/vote_registration_policy

Domain: .bank


Checkbox: bankAcceptance
You confirm that the registrar has the right to register the required domain and you are aware that the money will not be returned if the registration is unsuccessful. And all the contacts of the domain (owner, technical, etc.) will be checked by Symantec. They will call, punch through various databases of information about banks, etc ...

Domain .organic


Checkbox: organicEligibilityAcceptance
You confirm that the registrar has the right to register this domain. Qualification requirements: get.organic/registration_policy
Checkbox: organicPolicyAcceptance
You acknowledge that the registrar is aware of and acts in accordance with the domain registration policy: get.organic/direct-download/1636
Text field: sendVerificationEmail
Email, which must be verified by the registrar within 24 hours.

Domain .ngo, .ong


Checkbox: ngoOngEligibilityAcceptance
You confirm that the registrar has the right to register this domain. Qualification requirements: pir.org/what-are-the-eligibility-requirements-for-registering-ngo-and-ong-for-nonprofits-and-ngos
Checkbox: ngoOngPolicyAcceptance
You acknowledge that the registrar is aware of and acts in accordance with the domain registration policy: pir.org/policies/ngo-ong-policies/registration-policies-for-ngo-ong
Text field: sendVerificationEmail
Email, which must be verified within 24 hours.
Note: registering the domain omg.ngo , domain omg.ong you get for free and vice versa. All operations on each of the domains will be mirrored on the second.

Domain: .hiv


You do not need to send anything here, but $ 125 of the cost of the domain will go to the HIV charity foundation.

Domain: .cat (my favorite)


Array: domainNameVariants

Explanation. The fact is that in Catalonia, there are many characters with squiggles on top, and so that there is no confusion, registering domains containing such characters, you still receive a maximum of 10 options as a gift. Each option after the 10th will cost about $ 1.5.

Domain: .jobs


Text field: website
The correct address of the organization's website
Attention: but the subtlety here is that this field should be in the data of the site owner, and not in the data of the domain itself.

Domain: .us


Field: nexusAppPurpose
Here you need to choose one of the options:
P1: Business use for profit;
P2: Non-profit business, club, association, religious organization, etc .;
P3: Personal use;
P4: Education purposes;
P5: Government purposes;
The field must also be in the data of the site owner.
Field: typeOfRegistrant
And here are the options:
C11: A natural person, who is a United States citizen.
It is a person who is a permanent resident.
C21: US Organization incorporated within one of the 50 states or a US Territory.
C31: Assignment of an organization or organization for-profit relations in the United States).
C32: United States of America.
And this field must be in the data of the site owner.

Domain: .lotto


Text fields:
verificationCode - verification code;
membershipId is a certain Id.
Data for these two fields must be obtained from approved standardization bodies (the WLA, NASPL, EL, ALA, CIBELAE, or APLA).

Array: ruAccreditationFields , for this domain, all additional data should be stored in the data of the site owner.
taxPayerNumber - TIN;
mobilePhoneNumber - necessarily verified phone number;
Fields for organization:
companyNameCyrillic - the name of the company in Cyrillic;
companyNameLatin - the name of the company in Latin;
companyPostalAddress - mailing address of the organization;

lastNameLatin - last name in Latin;
