Wolfenstein 3D - now in the browser



Before the iconic Quake and Doom appeared, there was one game that amazed the players of those times. This is Wolfenstein 3D, a game introduced in 1992. Probably, many habra people played this toy, even more - just heard about it. There are, of course, such gamers who have not played and have not even heard of Wolfenstein 3D. Nevertheless, this is a cult game, and now there is an opportunity to play it directly in the browser.

Bethesda Softworks introduced the browser version of the toy, and made it completely free. The game is supported by all major modern browsers, including Google Chrome 16, Internet Explorer 9, Firefox 11, and Apple Safari 5. For some reason, there is no mention of Opera browser, but probably this browser is also supported.

Bethesda Softworks bought iD Software in 2009, and released a browser version for the 20th anniversary of the toy. Imagine - 20 years have passed! By the way, it was not the first 3D shooter, Hovertank and Catacomb 3D first appeared. At the same time, this is the first toy that creates the illusion of a completely three-dimensional world (it is built from 2D sprites).

In general, you can play by going to the game's website at this link. In addition, you can watch the video, podcast with the participation of the legendary representative of the game world John Carmack.
