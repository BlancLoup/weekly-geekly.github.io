In addition to the work of laboratories and departments, ITMO University also supports other projects in the field of information security. The project fixes the program's calls to the system kernel to detect potentially harmful activity. ‚ÄúOn these applications to the core of the system, we build a computer model that allows us to distinguish one process from another.
