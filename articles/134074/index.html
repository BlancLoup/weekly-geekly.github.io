New York, New York

Another important note. It may be tempting to open an account somewhere closer. For example - in New York. And the flight is shorter, and more flights, and cheaper tickets. Grace? Not.
Remember, in the last article addressed the issue of tax residence of the company? Bank accounts - one of the principles for determining residency. If you open an account in a bank in another state, be kind enough to register your corporation there as foreign, get up for local tax accounting and pay taxes to the local budget. We don't need that, that's why I chose a bank by location - in Nevada.
