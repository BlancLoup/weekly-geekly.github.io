Electric car Tesla Model S


Tesla Motors company never ceases to amaze with its electric vehicles.
Let me remind you - this young company has made a lot of noise with its Tesla Roadster .
And recently the Tesla Model S family sedan was demonstrated.

  • Acceleration to 100 km / h in 5.6 seconds.
  • Maximum speed - 193 km / h.
  • Power reserve - 480 km.
  • Battery charging time is 45 minutes.

And all this in the form factor of a large family sedan.
In the cargo compartment, you can install 2 extra seats, which makes the car an excellent choice for large families.
And selling this miracle will appear in 2011 at a price of $ 49,900.
