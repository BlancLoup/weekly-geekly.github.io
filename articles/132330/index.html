The first industry conference on email marketing: results



Exactly one month ago, the first industry conference dedicated to e-mail marketing - Mailing Conference was held in the Baltic. The event was organized by DirectList with sponsorship from UniSender and was held aboard the ferry "Princess Anastasia".

Speakers from the projects www.epochta.ru , usabilitylab.ru, www.e-xecutive.ru , Mail.ru , unisender.com , www.begun went to the three-day swimming-conference on the route St. Petersburg - Tallinn - Stockholm . ru , www.directlist.ru , www.mailersoft.ru , brandflight.com, Mailigen.com, www.plantoengage.com and others, listeners and journalists.

A small report on this event and upcoming events - under the cut.
