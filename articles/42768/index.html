Second psp concept

Most likely, Sony will sooner or later release the second incarnation of the portable console Play Station Portable. Rumors of this circulate constantly. What will it be? According to designer Tay Chiem, the PSP 2 will be equipped with a flexible OLED display.



In addition, the portable console will have a pair of stereo speakers and a camera.


The designer has not provided any technical characteristics yet. This is just a concept that exists only in the form of illustrations.


It is unlikely that Sony is thinking in the same direction as Chiem. Since, despite the cool appearance of the concept, with ergonomics and usability this option is not very.
