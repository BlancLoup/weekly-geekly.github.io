New firmware ABC 516

Congratulations to all current and future users of the ABC 516 reader. On July 13, a new firmware for ABC 516 was released. The most important fixes are doc format support. In the new firmware, bugs were fixed, thanks to which the device will work much more stable - in reality, the reader began to work as a calculator.


Tip: make the firmware not through "reinstall", but through your USB port. So at the same time you clean the memory of the book - all bad sectors will be closed. The loader checks the bad blocks, cleans them up, and replaces them before writing.
