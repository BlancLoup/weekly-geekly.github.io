If the country was ruled by IT specialists

Let's fantasize and imagine what would happen to the country if it was managed exclusively by IT specialists.

Thus, each voter can be sure that his vote is counted correctly, or rather, it is counted in general.

2. Personal voting for all legislative initiatives.


Now our interests in the state are represented by popularly elected deputies who put forward legislative initiatives and vote for their adoption. Before the advent of digital technology, there was no other way, the people needed to nominate one candidate, since technical ability to collect the opinion of all was not. Now technologies allow us to express our opinion personally regarding the laws put forward by initiative groups.

In this case, it would be possible to dissolve the dumas of all levels from the state to the municipal, and to save the funds for the maintenance of all these civil servants to bring to the masses the usefulness and significance of a particular bill.

3. Karma for civil servants


Why not make karma similar to many online resources for officials? He put forward a good idea and most importantly implemented it - get a plus in karma. He promised a lot and did nothing, said something without thinking, podboshiril on the plane, tarnished his reputation - get a minus in karma. Got a lot of advantages - increase, minuses - resignation.
