hosting.ua - did not expect such impudence

Good morning to all who still use the services of hosting.ua

Before the fire, they hosted their own photoblog and had no particular problems. When the server was unavailable for several days, I ordered hosting from another provider and restored the site from the backup.

After a couple of weeks, they restored something and the site started working. One domain still points to it and someone even goes there, but very few. Swearing with them and demanding money (a small amount) did not have any desire and decided to leave it as it is, suddenly come in handy.

And last night I receive the following letter from them:

Dear Mikhail Benkovich,

We are sorry to announce the blocking of your account:
Login: mikhail
Account Number: # 526575_0
Plan: Start (Start)

Your account has been suspended for a reason.
server overload



This surprised me a lot, and I asked to send logs. In response, the following came:

[05/17/2010 21:13:13]
A: Please, explain why it is caused by the fact that a connection to the server 188.72.80. * Was running from your account in several threads, clogging up the processor by 100%

Do you have scripts that use this server?


I wrote that there are no such scripts and in general there are no scripts that themselves establish a connection to another server.

And lo and behold, a letter arrived this morning.

Dear Mikhail Benkovich,

We are pleased to inform you about unlocking your account:
Login: mikhail
Account Number: # 526575_0
Plan: Start (Start)


Of course, I immediately went to watch the logs (FTP access to this was also closed) and did not find anything there. The average processor load of about 1-2% of the mentioned IP in access_log is not at all, the scripts have not changed since April.

I wrote them the following:

I repeat my question. What compensation can I get for a period of inactivity for two weeks after the fire in the data center and for the time that my site was unavailable yesterday and today due to your fault without any reason or justification?

Are you willing to voluntarily return the money and compensation when you close your account?


Waiting for an answer.
