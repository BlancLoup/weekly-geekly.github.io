AWS: Semifinalists Startup Contest

Hello!

A couple of months ago, I wrote the AWS Global Start-Up Challenge . This event gained momentum and gathered applications from representatives from 84 countries from all the inhabited continents of our planet.

So, the semi-finalists were selected in 4 nominations by topic:
  • Big Data & High Performance Computing
  • Business Applications
  • Consumer applications
  • Gaming



And now about each semi-finalist a little bit of information separately.
