Quick search for terminal errors in Google

How many times did you have to copy, paste, and then google the incomprehensible error that the terminal gave you? Surely a lot.

TualatriX, the developer of the Ubuntu Tweak program, added Google search support to the GNOME Terminal. Now, when you see something incomprehensible, you can simply select the desired fragment with the right mouse button and find it in Google.



The PPA of GNOME Terminal with Google search is included in the Ubuntu Tweak. Just open Ubuntu Tweak, go to the Source Center, check the box next to Google Terminal with Google search support and click Refresh.

Code here: code.launchpad.net/~tualatrix/ibentu/gnome-terminal
