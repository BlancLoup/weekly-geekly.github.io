Million from Apple.

On September 24, a certain Dongmei Li sued Apple, Steve Jobs and AT & T for suing the price of an iPhone by $ 200.

Dongmei Li on June 29 stood behind the phone in a long line. When it came time to buy an iPhone, it turned out that there was no 8 GB left, and she bought an iPhone with 4 GB of memory. Dongmei Li said that Apple had no reason to lower the price of the iPhone: the sales of the phone went well, and the company's shares rose in price. She also did not like the fact that Apple gave out $ 100 on iTunes only to those iPhone buyers who bought it no earlier than two weeks before the price was cut (she was offended that she did not get it) .

The most interesting thing is that Dongmei Li wants to sue Apple a million dollars. This money will cover the costs of the case and the lawyer and compensate for moral damage.

It is even funny when buyers sue the seller for price reduction ...
