The program of the meeting with the developers of Evernote Dev Meetup in Moscow


As you may have heard, this Friday, April 27, we are meeting with developers in Moscow. It will be held at the Digital October center and will be devoted to the issues of integrating applications and services with Evernote, a review of our Evernote API and partner product promotion channels. In addition, there we will answer all questions about the participation in the competition developers Evernote Devcup .

We offer all registered participants, as well as those who are considering the opportunity to attend a meeting, to familiarize themselves with the program.

Where: Digital October Center, Moscow, Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6, audience 4
When: April 27, 2012, 2:00 p.m.

Reports


Dmitry Stavisky , vice president of international development for Evernote .
Evernote Ecosystem and channels to promote affiliate projects. Gallery Evernote.

Igor Afanasyev , manager of the Russian-language service Evernote .
Overview of features Evernote API, examples of implementation and interesting products of partners.

Gleb Tarasov , independent developer, author of the iGuides application.
Export information to Evernote from an iOS application using ShareKit.

Nina Grigorieva , co-founder of News360

Pavel Vakhitov , yaM
yaM and Evernote

  • ideas of applications and user-requested functionality;
  • Answers to questions about the Evernote API and the ecosystem of applications;
  • Announcement of the developer competition Evernote Devcup with a prize fund of more than $ 100,000.
