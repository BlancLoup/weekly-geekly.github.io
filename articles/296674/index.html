World University Rankings: Focus on IT

Let us take this news as a reason to look at the latest ratings and other ways to assess the attractiveness, quality of education and level of research in universities in not only Britain, but the whole world.

Just to remind that there are ratings of universities of individual states, universities of groups of states and global ratings, in which universities of many countries are compared on a single scale. In this post I will try to mention the most notable ratings. In this case, I will focus on English-speaking countries and EU countries, since it is the universities of these countries that are most often attractive for training part of the citizens of the countries of the former USSR.

findings


Were briefly considered the ratings of universities in the field of training and research work in the world and in the UK. If we confine ourselves to the universities of Great Britain, then it can be noted that the leaders of both computer science and research work in the field of computer science are the same universities of Oxford, University College London, Imperial College London.

That is, we see the expected - where scientific activity is strong, the quality of education is higher (and vice versa). The universities of Oxford, University College London, Imperial College London - are widely known universities, the cost of education in which is often high. The cost of living in London and Oxford is also higher than in other cities in the UK. However, here in the ranking of scientific activity at a sufficiently high level are the universities of the cities of Manchester, Shefield and Warwick, the cost of living and education in which are lower. If we start from the assumption that a high level of scientific activity contributes to a better education and gaining valuable experience for future work by students, then the universities of Manchester, Shefield and Warwick look quite attractive to students.

For researchers (postdocs) the most attractive, of course, will be all universities - leaders. But the cost of living in the cities of Manchester, Sheffield and Warwick is lower, and, therefore, the possibility of accumulating money in your account will be higher.
