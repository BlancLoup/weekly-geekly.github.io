Winstar M4 USB Server



Everyone knows that the Ethernet port is far from being found in all devices that I would like to plug into the network (for example, only advanced printer models have it). Winstar M4 will allow you to connect virtually any USB device to your work as well as home network, be it a scanner, printer, external drive, speakers, or webcam. With this gadget your device will be online.
PSIf you want to connect multiple devices, you need to connect a USB hub.
It is open for sale only from March 14, 2009, but now you can order here for $ 33. Cheaper by the way =).

Allows you to bring up to four devices on the network using the hub.
Gives access to a flash drive or removable hard disk, etc. inside your network
Accessing a webcam, printer, scanner, fax or MFP device ...

Supports USB 2.0 and 1.1
