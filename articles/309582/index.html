Opinion Habr: disable or not a large site owed 450 000 rubles?


On the evening of the 8th of September, the first site of the Runet about cultural and club life geometria.ru was disconnected from the world wide web. The reason for the disconnection was the debt of half a million rubles to another well-deserved organization - the Selectel network of data centers.

Under the cut there will not be a tearful story about who is right and who is to blame. I just want to describe the situation as a whole, to hear the opinion of habrazhiteli about it, as well as what way out of this situation you would seem most correct if you come across a similar one, regardless of the side of the barricades.

Disclaimer


The author is in no way connected with any of the parties in the events described, and his interest is exclusively sporting, since The situation is quite controversial for both parties. You can hear about what happened, for example, in the news on the radio . There is no claim to objectivity in the text, but only a personal opinion based on rumors, but I tried to describe it from the most neutral position.

And what actually happened


So, there is a fairly large site with a 14-year-old life history, spread over several racks and leasing communication channels, both to the outside world and between data centers. Resource media, deserved, with all sorts of ratings and regularly organizing various events.

Due to internal squabbles, the site has arrears in paying for the services provided for several months before the data center for a pretty decent amount - 450,000 rubles.

According to representatives of geometria.ru, when it became known about the debt, they immediately contacted Selectel to explain the reasons for the current situation and even allegedly provided guarantees of payment, but the negotiations did not lead to a positive result and access to the site was terminated, which is legally perfectly correct .

And here we come to the main topic, for the sake of which this topic was started - is it worth it to completely disconnect the connection from major sites or is it better to influence them somehow differently?

Perhaps it is enough just to limit their access speed to uncomfortable? Or maybe it is better to first restrict access to servers and go to arbitration, using servers as a guarantee of payment?

How generally to measure the criticality of debt levels for projects of this level? Indeed, it is one thing when a hipster server brings a unit server to you, and then they end up with a smoothie, and they disappear into nowhere, and quite another, when one resource rents several racks with communication channels at once, and it definitely will not disappear.

The following question arises from this: how to deal with customer orientation and at the same time not drive ourselves into the endless waiting for payments on debts? Interruptions in the receipt of money occur at all, but how long to wait for payment? Or, again, to what amount to lend?

You can dig a little deeper - the Supreme Arbitration Court somehow decided that during disputes between operators it is impossible to disturb subscribers' access to communication services. Of course, this is not 100% about this situation, but you can see from a slight angle. Selectel is undoubtedly a licensee and a telecom operator providing services to the client for 150,000 a month. Geometry - a resource with a huge attendance, reputation and well-deserved name. Not a licensee, but in fact, they provide telematics services to a large number of citizens, i.e. access to servers, to the site and its services. It turns out that legally, even though they are not an operator at all, in fact, disabling such a resource for a non-zero number of people may be equal to disconnecting from communication services. In the situation with the portal Geometriya.ru, we are talking exclusively about the repayment of arisen debts - any other interpretations cause us only bewilderment and regret.
