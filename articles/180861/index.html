Google Glass and ridiculous view from the side

Google Glass is a very convenient and wonderful thing for a person, but despite this, many say that a person with such an assembly on his head will look not only strange, but even suspicious. We really need to take a step back and seriously think about how new technologies will affect our lives. Of course, having the functions of navigation and face recognition, which will always be accessible "at hand", life becomes much more pleasant and exciting. But if you are already annoyed by people using smartphones and tablets, cameras, imagine a world where everything is constantly being shot with their glasses.



www.youtube.com/watch?v=qGxLkaCdpLc

From sticking their faces to food plates, to showing on Instagram what you eat, or looking at the horizon, for a few hours, like stupid zombies to take an accelerated survey and much more. We hope that people will be more modest and restrained.
