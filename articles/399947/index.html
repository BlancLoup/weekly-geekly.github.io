Street Fighter II: The World Warrior


In 1990, Capcom released the sequel to Street Fighter, the first part of which appeared in 1987. First, the company sold 60,000 Street Fighter II slot machines, and in 1991 consolidated its success with the help of the even more popular Street Fighter II: Champion Edition. Only in Japan, slot machines with this version of the game sold out in the amount of 140 thousand pieces. By 1992, 60% of all coins in slot machines in the UK were provided by Street Fighter II, about 260 million pounds per year.

For 1995 in the United States, the games The World Warrior and Champion Edition earned together 2.3 billion dollars. Taking into account inflation for 2017, it is about $ 3.7 billion.

With the game Street Fighter II is the emergence of the concept of "combo". Players accidentally discovered the ability to combine multiple attacks, which the opponent could not block with the desired sequence of execution. The developers did not envisage such a possibility (probably), but later combo became commonplace for fighting games. And not only for fighting games.

Based on the game in 1994, the film ‚ÄúStreet Fighter‚Äù was shot, in which the main role was played by Jean-Claude Van Damme, and the role of Cammy went to Kylie Minogue .




Mortal kombat


The success of Street Fighter has led to the emergence of new games. In Killer Instinct, for example, for the first time at the game level they introduced combo. But the synonym for fighting game so far is Mortal Kombat, first released in 1992

In the game, the characters were no longer drawn. Mortal Kombat shows the digitized actions of the actors. The master of martial arts and at that time employee of Midway Daniel Pesina played Johnny Cage, Scorpio, Sub-Zero and Reptile. Actor and, again, martial arts master Park Ho Sung became the prototype of Liu Kang and Shang Tsung.

Juggling appears in Mortal Kombat. You throw the enemy in the air with a blow and continue to beat him until he falls. Translated this "juggling".

The Mortal Kombat franchise has become one of the most successful and influential fighting games in the entire history of video games. It is distinguished by a high level of violence, which is perfectly visible in terms of the number of Fatality options - finishing the opponent in dozens of different ways, one more sophisticated than the other.




Myst


In 1993, Czechoslovakia fell to the Czech Republic and Slovakia, tanks stormed the White House in Moscow, and the TV-6 channel began broadcasting. Meanwhile, in the world of computer games appeared the graphic quest Myst.

Myst became the best-selling game for personal computers, until its records broke the Sims and a few more games released five or more years later. By 2003, Myst had sold 6 million copies, and sales of all games in the franchise reached 12 million copies, of which 8.03 million fell on the very first game of 1993. Myst received four sequels, three remakes and a number of offshoots, including the MMORPG Uru Live.

In Myst, the player moved through the interactive world with a first-person view, interacting with objects and moving between screens with the mouse. To complete the game, you need to solve puzzles and visit all the worlds, collecting pages for two books - red and blue, which have become prisons for two characters capable of creating books-worlds. Workers of the game were inspired by the novel "The Mysterious Island" by Jules Verne.

Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness


Since 1995, it has been leading the E3 Electronic Entertainment Exhibition, in August of that year, Windows 95 was released, and in November, Nintendo presented a working prototype of the Nintendo Ultra 64 with a pair of games - Kirby Ball 64 and Super Mario 64.

Blizzard Entertainment in December 1995 released the second in a series of strategy Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness. The game initially worked under DOS and Windows 95, was the version under Mac OS. In 1997, Warcraft II was ported to the Sega Saturn and Sony PlayStation.

Three months after the launch of Warcraft II, Blizzard sold half a million copies, and in 2016 the game got into the corresponding edition of the Guinness Book of Records with 4.21 million sales of games for personal computers.

Gran turismo


In 1997, a criminal code began to operate in Russia with an article ‚Äúfor violation of copyright and related rights‚Äù, Bill Clinton took over the presidency of the United States for a second term, and Intel officially introduced the Pentium 2 microprocessor.

This year, a few years after the release of the first game, Need For Speed, a new racing franchise appeared on the scene. Gran Turismo for Sony PlayStation in the company Polyphony Digital worked for five years, although the team was very small - up to 15 people at the best of times. The game of 1997 was far from competitors in terms of realism and complexity, it had an interesting career mode and the possibility of tuning cars.

A total of 76.8 million copies of all games of the Gran Turismo series were sold, of which 10.85 million accounted for the first part of the game for the PlayStation. The rating of the first game on Metacritic is 96 points out of 100 possible. This is the case when critics praised the game and players voted for it with their money.





Half life


Released in 1998, a first-person shooter with a laconic main character, red mount and the lack of video savers was an important milestone in the history of the genre. Gordon Freeman is so fond of the players that they bought 9.3 million copies of Half-Life. The second part was also successful - sales of the continuation of 2004 amounted to 9.4 million units. But the third part, we, apparently, never wait, because Valve brings a good income Dota, Counter Strike and the game distribution service Steam.

Half-Life was, one might say, fan-made. Before the creation of the game, Gabe Newell and his partner Mike Harrington looked into id Software - one of the coolest companies at the time, which released several games of the Doom and Quake series. In this company, they received the source code for the Quake engine, for which they hired mod developers and game fans. In addition, Newell and Harrington took several industry veterans from 3D Realms, Shiny Entertainment and Microsoft, from where the founders of Valve, an unknown studio at that time, came from.

The game significantly influenced the management in shooters . At the time of the release of Half-Life, there were a lot of different keyboard layouts: AShiftZ, WADX, ESDF, ASDX. One of the creators of Quake, John Carmack, added the WASD configuration to the second part of the game, but for the first time it was used by default in 1998's Half-Life.




Counter strike


In 1999, fans release a modification for Half-Life called Counter-Strike. The modification, noted by Valve, then turned into an independent game and received extensions of Condition Zero, Source and Global Offensive. In the past ten years, CS remains one of the most popular online games, Global Offensive - in first places on Steam.

Counter-Strike - team shooter on the confrontation of terrorists and special forces. The goal of the game is to complete the task or destroy all opponents. At one time, Counter-Strike 1.6 began to decline due to the emergence of powerful personal computers and much more developed games in terms of graphics, but by 2008 there was a reverse process - CS had become a tournament discipline.

Today, eSports is a spectacular event of various levels with a wide variety of prize pools. In this industry with pleasure to invest. For example, Alisher Usmanov in 2016 announced that he intends to invest more than 100 million US dollars in eSports, namely, his USM Holdings will invest in the project Virtus.pro. The project includes teams in key disciplines, including StarCraft 2, FIFA, League of Legends and, of course, Counter-Strike.




In the 1990s, three generations of gaming systems changed - after the 16-bit Sega Mega Drive and Super Nintendo came the Nintendo 64, Sega Saturn and Sony PlayStation, and at the end of the decade the era of the sixth generation began with DreamCast, XboX and PlayStation 2. Game consoles from cartridges began to move to the disks. Processors for graphics processing have become more powerful, and as a standard for creating video games, true three-dimensional graphics are widely used.

An important milestone for the gaming industry was the advent of the Internet, so that in the second half of the 1990s online games appeared. E-sports has reached a new level.

In the US, sales of games have overtaken sales of another entertainment industry in the country - cinema.
