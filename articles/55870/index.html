Questions for interviews with Program Manager in the division of Connected Systems Yevgeny Osovetsky

We are still preparing to publish interviews with Alex Mogilevsky and Ruslan Yakushev, and we offer to ask questions to Eugene Osovetsky, who works as a Program Manager in the Connected Systems division.

Now Eugene is responsible for supporting the work with web services in Silverlight. Prior to that, he was involved in integrating ASP.NET AJAX and JSON into the Windows Communication Foundation as part of the .NET Framework 3.5, essentially turning WCF into a powerful platform for supporting Web 2.0 applications.
In addition, Eugene was responsible for all aspects of working with data (serialization, XML processing, XML schemas, and more) in WCF in releases for .NET 3.0 and 3.5.

You have the opportunity to ask Eugene questions in the comments to this post. We will collect the questions and ask them during the recording of the interview in a week.
