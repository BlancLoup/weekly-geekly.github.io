New Google Maps

At the Where 2.0 conference, Google announced a new feature in Google Maps - viewing city streets in a three-dimensional form. Thus, now each of us will be able to visit the virtual space of any point of the city, however, their number is now limited. However, it is already possible to look at this function in New York and San Jose .


Google also introduced Mapplets at this conference - layers on top of a map that display, for example, gasoline prices, or weather. Layers can include HTML, Javascript and Flash. At the same time, you can view several such layers.
