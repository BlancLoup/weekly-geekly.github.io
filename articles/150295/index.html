40 million installations of Web Of Trust

Web Of Trust (WOT) - reached a new milestone in the number of installations - 40 million . The company's technology is based on processing user opinions about sites and helps them find reliable sites. The turn was also achieved thanks to the recent integration of the WOT extension into the browser of the largest Internet company, Mail.ru.



WOT is a free browser extension designed by two students from the University of Technology in Tampere, Finland. In August 2008, the number of installations reached 1 million, and already in March 2010 the 10 million bar was overcome. In 2011 - 20 million, and 30 million in January 2012. The 40 million milestone was taken only 7 months later.

About WOT Services
WOT Services Ltd. ( www.mywot.com ), founded in 2006, specializes in developing and providing software and services to protect the online community from malicious content and fraudulent activity.
The uniqueness of the protection provided by WOT is that users themselves evaluate sites, adding to the WOT database. WOT is a free add-on to all popular browsers that warns the user about potentially insecure web pages when searching for information or making purchases.
