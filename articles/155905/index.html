Learning English with LinguaLeo is now possible on Android!

It happened! We are ready to present the long-awaited application for Android. So far, only 200 people have seen him - journalists, participants in beta testing, the project team. The time has come for the official release!

The Android application LinguaLeo allows you to learn English words and phrases, workouts and see progress in real time. How exactly a little robot can help you learn English with LinguaLeo, read on ...



UPD: Hooray, we have released a new version! Now the application can be transferred to a memory card and it takes up less space.



How to use the Android version?


Download the free app and log in to your account using the email and password that you use for English classes on the LinguaLeo web service . After logging in, your personal dictionary is synchronized with the web service itself, so you can be sure that all your words are available for study. If you do not have an account on LinguaLeo, create it directly in the application.



Leo - a conductor in the jungle of the English language - will meet you and tell you what to do next. First, he will offer to find 10 unfamiliar words in the thematic glossaries and add them to the dictionary.

Inside the application, you will see three main sections: "Topics", "Dictionary" and "Training".

Topics




"Sport", "Family Members", "Irregular Verbs", "Business meetings and presentations" ... - this is not a complete list of available topics (at the moment there are more than 24 topics and more than 3,000 necessary words in the application).

Vocabulary


Sent "to study" words and phrases from the "Theme" will fall into your personal "Dictionary" and the "Training". Before training it is useful to get acquainted with the words, look through them, listen to the pronunciation, see the picture associations and translations.



Search with autocompletion is the ability to quickly find the right words.

Get training on fixing words everywhere: whether it is public transport or a boring lecture at the university, a long lunch at work or an endless queue to the doctor. You can learn new English words at any time!

Watch real-time progress and have fun.


Each studied English word or phrase, the training passed and the subject learned give experience points for your pet Leo. You can set up your Leo's daily ration to feed him with new words every day, get training sessions and learn English with optimal intensity.



Accumulate experience points and get new levels and titles!
