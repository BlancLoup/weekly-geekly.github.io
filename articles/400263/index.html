MegaFon will buy a controlling stake in the Mail.Ru Group from Usmanov for $ 740 million



Today, MegaFon announced its intention to acquire 63.8% of the voting shares of Mail.Ru Group from the USM group of companies, founded by Alisher Usmanov. For the controlling stake, MegaFon will pay $ 640 million at the time of closing the transaction and another $ 100 million in a year.

In a press release, MegaFon explains the need for a deal by simplifying joint projects of companies and favorable conditions for active cooperation between telecom operators and Internet companies:

The deal is aimed at obtaining significant synergistic effects for both companies, including expanding the range and ways of promoting MegaFon‚Äôs digital services, launching the VKmobile special offer for users of the VKontakte social network and other possible initiatives.
