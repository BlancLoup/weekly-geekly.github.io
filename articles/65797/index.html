E-commerce thrives in crisis

As you know, during the crisis, sales fell at all. And even sales of mobile phones in mobile phone stores fell by as much as 65%. But online stores, it turns out, were not completely affected by the crisis, but quite the contrary, they noticeably increased their turnover.

The newspaper "Kommersant" publishes amazing statistics on online shopping mobile phones. For example, the Euroset online business grew by 40% (it has 29 online stores), and Svyaznoy (1 store) - by 15%. Inspired by, cellular retailers began selling even televisions and musical instruments over the Internet.

It is not known how things are at other online stores. For example, about 200 Internet sites sell only phones in RuNet.
