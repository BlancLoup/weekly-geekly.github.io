Start of official sales of iPad 2 in Ukraine on May 27



Unexpected news for residents of Ukraine. According to the UkrainianiPhone.com blog, already on May 27, at 13:00 Kyiv time, official sales of iPad 2 will start in Ukraine in the networks of MOYO, iStore, Comfy and Foxtrot stores.

The pricing policy for tablets is quite loyal. The minimum grade iPad 2 16Gb Wi-Fi will cost about $ 665, and the oldest - Pad 2 Wi-Fi 3G 64GB - $ 1115. It is even cheaper than in Russia .

All prices for official iPad 2 in Ukraine:

iPad 2 Wi-Fi 16GB - 5299 UAH ($ 665)

iPad 2 Wi-Fi 32GB - 6399 UAH ($ 800)

iPad 2 Wi-Fi 64GB - 7499 UAH ($ 940)

iPad 2 Wi-Fi + 3G 16GB - 6699 UAH ($ 840)

iPad 2 Wi-Fi + 3G 32GB - 7799 UAH ($ 975)

iPad 2 Wi-Fi + 3G 64GB (black) - 8899 UAH ($ 1115)

Pre-orders for the iPad 2 can already be done on reseller sites: istore.ua , moyo.ua , comfy.ua , foxtrot.com.ua .
