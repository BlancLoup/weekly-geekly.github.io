How to get PMP in 2 months and meet in 24000r

Today I did a short presentation at the professional development + certification work and immediately after the presentation I understood what I did not do. I forgot to ask 2 simple questions for participants:
  • Are you satisfied with your position and salary?
  • And what are you doing that they do not do the rest to raise / increase your salary?

Certification can be the answer to the second question.

So, in short, how to get PMP ( Project Management Professional degree, one of the most well-known and prestigious project management degrees worldwide) in 2 months and spend up to $ 24,000.
  1. We check compliance with the requirements for higher education + 36 calendar months of experience in project management + 4500 hours of project management + good knowledge of English (or according to the second scheme without higher education - see pmi.org). If it is not, we turn around and leave.
  2. We register at pmi.org , get a membership and pay $ 139 for the first year of membership.
  3. We download the PMBOK edition in English from pmi.org 3 - we will not read it, but let it be.
  4. We register at http://insite.velociteach.com/ and pay $ 99 per month. Within a month we recruit 35 PDU on the site.
  5. Fill out an application for PMP (it will take up to several days) and ship. We are waiting for confirmation, after confirmation we pay $ 405 and schedule an exam in 1-1.5 months.
  6. On pmi.ru we write an announcement that we will buy Rita Mulcahy - PMP Exam Prep, 5th edition , we buy for about 1000-1500 rubles.
  7. We read PMP Exam Prep 3 (three) times.
  8. Come take the exam!

As a result, you will spend $ 139 + $ 99 + $ 405 + 1000 rub <24 000 rub. Upon delivery of PMP according to this plan, you will need about 2 months, during which it is necessary to allocate about 200 hours for self-preparation. The standard pmp delivery procedure that pmexpert, for example, offers, even now with their huge discounts, costs more than 50,000 rubles.
According to pmi statistics, less than half of candidates pass the exam the first time (although the passing score seems to be only 60%), but with Rita your chances are significantly higher.

Attention! From July 1, 2009, the PMP exam is held according to the new version of PMBOK. So the recipe above only comes up to this time.
