Mark Zuckerberg, Bill Gates and Jack Dorsey: Anyone can program at any age

Mark Zuckerberg, Bill Gates, Jack Dorsey and others starred in the commercial of the non-profit foundation CodeOrg to inspire children to start programming. In it, the founders of well-known IT companies, such as Facebook, Microsoft, Twitter, Dropbox, Zappos, Valve, talk about how they learned to program, what programmers companies are looking for now, and also what fate awaits IT people in the future. The purpose of the video is to increase the prestige of programming and collect signatures so that in every school you can learn the basics of coding.
