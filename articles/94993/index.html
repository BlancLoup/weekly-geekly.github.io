Programmer's lunch

Hello!

Today, once again, I want to boast a hand-made case for a flash drive. This time, in the form of a dumpling. Some may have the impression that I am always hungry, but this is not so (although you can feed me something tasty) - just polymer clay of such delicious colors, and the size of the flash drive so well with the size of different goodies ...




Dumpling is an excellent choice for starting clay modeling, because each of us at least once in our life has made dumplings, which means there is experience. The clay is not very different from the dough, only it is more plastic, holds its shape better, does not stick, and can be * smoothed * and * polished * from various kinds of defects. Therefore, as you can see, nothing complicated - the main thing is to give the body the most appetizing shape as possible.

So ... First, knead the dough (i.e. polymer clay) - white + yellow in the proportion of about 50: 1 - add a little, until the color is the same as the color of the dough for dumplings.



Then rolls the cake ... (it is convenient to make a glass bottle)



Now (although it would be more correct to do this in the first step) you need to make a dummy flash drive, a pig, which we wrap in clay and bake with it. I made it from the original case and carton:



Next, make a dumpling and bake it. Until it is cold and the clay is a bit plastic, we cut it into the body and the cover.
We put a flash drive into the ready cooled dumpling, and glue it to the edge with some * superglue *. In * the lid of the dumpling - paste the original lid from the flash drive (I did not fit, and therefore the half of the cap was inserted. Cutting it is not difficult, of course, if the body was not metal))).

Next - delicacies - we make sour cream: more PVA + yellow and white gouache. True white was not enough, and I added white acrylic. To sour cream flowed naturally - and it must be applied naturally - I had it as in the picture:



Well, it remains to stick the inscription * without GMO *. It's so important in our time!





Nakleochek * non-GMO * got a few, so without modifications was my computer, mouse, and fish =)



PS: A few obvious things that can be overlooked when working with plastics:
1. Make an even cut a high probability when the clay is half hard - it should be either frozen or cut hot after baking.
2. To make something similar to the present, it needs to be done like the present.
3. The more accurate the dummy is, the less the hatter needs to suffer.

Bon appetit!
