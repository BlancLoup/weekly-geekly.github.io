Sony Xperia Z5 Premium comes in pink

Gone are the days when smart phones were all black, gray and occasionally white. The Sony Xperia line has long been green, gold, orange, yellow and other bright colors. Today, the flagship Z5 Premium, which remained the most conservative in terms of color, is officially presented in a pink case.




The shade is bright, but there will be no associations with barbie tones. The color was slightly different compared to the pink version of the Xperia Z5. According to Sony designer Murey-san, the Xperia Z5 Premium shade is deeper and richer, and the flagship model is exactly what you need.



Recall, Xperia Z5 Premium - the first smartphone from Sony with a screen resolution of 4K (3840x2160). The screen features to match the main camera - it is 23 megapixel here, and allows you to shoot video in 4K. Inside, an eight-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 810 processor, 3 GB of RAM, 32 GB of internal memory and support for microSD-cards up to 200 GB, a 3430 mAh battery is installed. And thanks to the Quick Charge 2.0 technology, the battery can be charged from zero to sixty percent in just half an hour. IP 65/68 protection against moisture and dust is also available. In general, we already had a review of the Xperia Z5 Premium .

Sales of the pink version will begin in May.
