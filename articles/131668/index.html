LED light bulbs. Practice



Introduction


Like many people, I quite liked the post about the internal structure of LED light bulbs . The only thing missing was seeing how it all shines in life. Over the past six months, a dozen Chinese LED bulbs have accumulated and some experience that I want to share.

Update 02/11 23:00 - New test, new light bulbs.


Light bulbs


A pair of LED bulbs was chosen as a replacement for the usual 60W (different in price and quality). For comparison, I took a couple of ordinary and fluorescent light bulbs:



1) Normal 60W Philips (720Lm / 2700K)
2) Normal 40W Philips (415Lm / 2700K)
3) Fluorescent coil in a 13W Navigator ball (780 Lm / 2700K)
4) Fluorescent Spiral 15W Shine (900Lm / 2700K)
5) LED 9W China (cheap) (declared 800 Lm / 3000K, turned out to be dimmer and more than 5000K)
6) LED 7W China (expensive) (declared 630Lm / 3015K, subjectively corresponds)

Test


- Room 3x4 meters.
- The lamp is approximately in the center at a height of about 70 cm from the floor.
- Shutter speed, aperture, ISO, white balance of the camera are fixed.
