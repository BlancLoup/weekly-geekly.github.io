Yandex research - true or fiction?

Good day friends.

We recently came across a curious study conducted by Yandex regarding the distribution of shares in the market of Payment aggregators and payment systems. To be honest, we are in a slight bewilderment, because the data in this study, to put it mildly, differ from ours. We, of course, are incomparably smaller than Yandex, and operate with disproportionately smaller statistical data. But they used to think that our figures still have a certain representativeness. And if Yandex is to be believed, then our numbers do not reflect reality at all.

Therefore, we decided to conduct a survey in the professional community and understand for ourselves, is it really the way it was written in our research by Yandex?

As we are an interested person, we will not raise the issue of the distribution of market shares between payment aggregators (and in order not to look bad for beginners, against the background of more experienced colleagues), and will conduct a survey on the distribution of popularity among payment methods investigated by Yandex.

We hope our initiative will not be perceived as a pathetic attempt at self-praise, and together we will conduct an interesting survey with reliable results.
