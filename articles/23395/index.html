Watch - mobile phone


The company Van Der Led has released a watch-mobile phone. Similar devices appeared before (for example, from CECT and General Mobile GM500 ), but this watch looks quite nice and would not be ashamed to wear them =)

The device is designed to work in networks GSM / GPRS 850/900/1800/1900 MHz and equipped with a 1.3-inch display that displays 262 thousand colors and a Bluetooth module. The manufacturer managed to find a place for a 1.3 megapixel camera and a USB connector. The amount of internal memory is 1 GB. It is worth noting the good performance of the built-in battery, one charge of which is enough for 5 hours of conversation or for 240 hours in standby mode.
The price is not the most democratic - $ 471
