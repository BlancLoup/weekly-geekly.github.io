Fujitsu HandyDrive Family Additions

Fujitsu, or rather its European branch, announced the release of new portable drives HandyDrive series. The volume of these 2.5-inch hard drives, made in a nice package is (depending on the model) from 120 to 300 GB.

Among the advantages of the new HandyDrive can be identified low weight (less than 250 grams), data transfer speed of 60 MB / s, USB 2.0 interface and built-in data protection system from mechanical damage, shock and vibration.

At the moment, drives of 120, 160 and 300 GB are priced at 90, 120, 200 euros, respectively. In the near future, Fujitsu will organize deliveries of two more modifications - 200-gigabyte and 250-gigabyte. You can buy them the other day at a price of 150 and 175 euros.
