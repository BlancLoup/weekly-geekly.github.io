RealTimeBoard - a new way to collaborate

Recently I discovered a new interesting and extremely useful collaboration service for the Russian RealtimeBoard project - endless virtual boards on which you can work with any visual content (attach pictures, draw charts and graphs, create collages, etc.) individually or with a team.
At this stage, the service is in open beta testing, but this does not prevent us from working with it now and solving a wide range of tasks.


What tasks can be solved using RealTimeBoard?


1) In RealTimeBoard it is very convenient to visualize information using both template images and from the Internet or from your own collection.


2) RealTimeBoard is great for collaboration. Comment system - mini-chat allows you to leave notes around any element. So it is very convenient to discuss advertising modules, interfaces, posters with the team, customers and partners.


3) Thanks to integration with Google Drive, you can conveniently and visually work with documents, edit them and scroll pages.

4) The service also has a set of templates, among which there are boards for Agile-management and a template of the Osterwalder business model. Library of avtofigur and virtual stickers help in working with boards.




5) The results of the work can be saved as a picture or PDF file. The function of saving the board in the form of a presentation is also implemented.

The RealtimeBoard team is not going to stop there - the future is planned to develop visual thinking tools.
