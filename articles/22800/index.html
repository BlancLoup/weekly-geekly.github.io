How much water has flowed?

Faucet Buddy is one of the rare devices whose purpose is clear in the photo. It is placed on the faucet. The top line of the fixture shows the number of liters. Lower - water temperature. Especially important in Europe, where, according to the latest calls of environmentalists, water should be strictly conserved. In general, Faucet Buddy is so ingenious that it is absolutely incomprehensible why in this technological age this thing is not yet a standard part of any mixer. Perhaps not to be frightened by huge numbers of those who like to take baths for hours.
