New Ukraine on Yandex.Maps

Today we have updated maps of Ukraine. Not a particular city, but the country as a whole. We made a lot of corrections and clarifications thanks to error messages from our users. In particular, on the map of Odessa you can find the new Lergeron ZRK , and in Donetsk - the Sky City business center .

Tell about what is most noticeable.

Item number one. Meet on Maps 25 new cities. Here is the city where Gogol studied , and the legendary resort , and the former capital of the Crimean Khanate . However, each of them is worthy of a separate mention: Alexandria , Bakhchisarai , Belaya Tserkov , Berdichev , Berdyansk , Vasilkov , Drogobych , Enakievo , Izmail , Kalush , Kanev , Kolomyya , Korosten , Melitopol , Mirgorod , Morshin , Mukachevo , Nezhin , Obukhov , Pavlograd , Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky , Truskavets , Uman , Fastov , Shostka .

There are now 82 detailed maps of cities in Ukraine on the maps.

Item number two. Euro 2012 is approaching, and we want our Maps to help fans from other cities and countries easily navigate the cities where competitions are held.

We updated the maps of the territories near the stadiums and airports in these cities (recall that the Ukrainian part of the Euro will be held in Kiev , Donetsk , Lviv and Kharkov ). Now you will see on the maps new buildings and terminals (the latter are signed, so they are easy to find), parking places and other useful objects.

Item number three. The design of the card has been updated - but it's better to look at it with your own eyes .

PS And one more thing: maybe not everyone knows about it, but a few weeks ago, for users from Ukraine, we launched maps in the local domain UA with the option of choosing the interface language - Ukrainian or Russian.
