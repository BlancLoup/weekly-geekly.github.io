Belarusian Father Frost congratulates over the Internet

Letter from Santa Claus - the real letter in the mail in the envelope :-)

There are things that we always want to believe.
One of them is a New Year's fairy tale. And the tale is not that magic can be found here and now, but the fact that a fairy tale is warmth and kindness, attention and care.

It does not matter whether we believe in Santa Claus or not, it is important that we expect someone to know our desires and fulfill them no matter how we behaved this year.

We sometimes do not think about how important it is to present a Christmas card to a person whom we value and respect.

Once upon a time we were children, and with a sinking heart waited for gifts from Grandfather Frost, wrote him letters resembling lists of gifts. Sometimes he brought them, but never answered letters.
Of course, many children are waiting for a response from Santa Claus.


Now imagine - you open the mailbox - and there is a real letter with New Year's greetings from Santa Claus:

‚ÄúDear, Sasha!
Happy New Year. You behaved well this year, and therefore I give you the "Spy Kit" so that you can become a real scout!
Listen to your parents and until next New Year.
Santa Claus"

The letter, of course, can be much longer and more pleasant. But the main thing is the warmth and memory of him for life. And, of course, the happy eyes of a child.

I really wanted to give everyone the opportunity to receive a real letter from Grandfather Frost.
To do this, we created a project www.iMoroz.by , thanks to which everyone can send real letters from Santa Claus to their near and dear ones, with ready or own text.
And when the letter comes - that miracle will come true!

The month of December is already outside, a year has passed quite unnoticed, but the winter has not yet begun. Although it has become colder, there is no snow yet.

What will happen on New Year's Eve? Will it snow? Will there be patterns on the windows?

So far, we do not know that. But we are sure: the house will definitely have a tree and an olive!

And under the tree - good grandfather Frost will surely put gifts and respond to the letter!
