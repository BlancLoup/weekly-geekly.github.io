Microsoft Student Day 2011 - Don't Miss October 14th at Digital October!

Did you think student day only happens in January? We decided to change this tradition, and for the second year we have been holding Microsoft student day in the fall. Last year, the head of Microsoft, Steve Ballmer, spoke at the MSU student day. This year we are holding for all Russian students an equally interesting Student Day 2011 - October 14 at Digital October   (Moscow, Bersenevskaya nab., 6). To participate you must register . Those who want to stay at home, or who do not want to come to Moscow, will be able to watch the online broadcast (will be available on the day of the event).



This year, Student Day will not be just a set of lectures - we want to organize an unreal movement throughout the day. You can visit different zones and dive into a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãinteresting activities, including:
  • Fascinating presentations and a story about the future of IT from Microsoft Russia President Nikolai Pryanishnikov
  • Master Classes: Time Management by Gleb Arkhangelsky
  • The story about building your business by Arkady Moreynis
  • Career advice from HeadHunter.ru
  • BootCamp for developing applications for Windows Phone 7 and Kinect , for creating prototypes of SketchFlow applications and websites
  • Booths Windows Phone 7 / Xbox 360 / Windows 7 / Office 2010
  • Performance winners Imagine Cup

In two parallel halls, lectures will be held almost continuously, in addition to this, master classes, bootcamps, workshops and seminars will be held in four rooms, and you can also visit Windows, Office, and career corner stands. We are planning so many interesting activities in one day that you do not have time to get bored! The secondary schedule of the event will be available on the website a little later, however, you must register now, while there are still free places!

Coming to the event is better with a laptop, with friends and in a good mood! On the spot, you decide where to go: learn how to create games for Windows Phone 7 and Kinect, improve your public speaking skills, or optimize your resume along with career counselors. We believe that this day will be able to change your life for the better !

The most active participants of the event - both offline and online - will be able to win prizes: a laptop , Windows Phone 7 phone , etc. To participate in the online, use the hashtag #studay .

Register , invite friends (on Facebook , VKontakte , post a poster at a university ), come in person or join us online on October 14 at 10:00 Moscow time.



See you soon on the student day!
