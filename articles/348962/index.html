English version of Habr: to be or not to be?

Motivation


In recent days I have been thinking about new material for Habr, at the same time I want to publish it in English, in order to refer to articles in the English-speaking segment of the Internet.

To achieve a similar result with Habr, you can write an article on Medium and post a link to Hacker News .

But in addition to development, Medium publishes anything and therefore the audience there is very different, there is no possibility to broadcast only to developers, as is the case with Habr, and only those who are likely to search for it in a search engine will read the article. Cons can be listed further.

Hacker News (in general, like Medium) is more like a forum about everything than a serious resource where you can discuss a publication that will be relevant for many years after the article is published.

The topic of searching for an analogue of Habr in English has been raised several times ( here , here , here and here ) and I think there are many more different options, but there isn‚Äôt, in my opinion, a standard that Habr is considered to be in the Russian segment.

Apparently the topic is of interest not only to me. Therefore, in my opinion, a logical proposal arose that I would like to submit for public discussion.

What does the community and the TM team think about launching the English version of Habr?
