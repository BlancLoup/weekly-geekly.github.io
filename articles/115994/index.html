Free test call abroad



My post is dedicated to people like me. Those whose relatives, friends or loved ones live abroad. For those who uncontrollably miss them and are looking for opportunities to call more often, cheaper and without much hassle.
From this point of view, my most favorite achievement of science and technology is ip-telephony. And if earlier I had a very vague idea about this, then when my mother moved to America (4 years ago), I had to look for options.
At first (of inexperience, of course) we used traditional telephony, we were terribly ruined, we tried to call less often, to speak less. By the end of the second month, our conversations began to resemble telegrams:
- Mom, everything is fine with us, Igor was promoted, Lenka got married, and now I am a legal specialist in public relations.
- Convey my congratulations to all! We, too, everything is fine, but I missed buckwheat, in stores it is not found.
Then we half went to Skype with grief. But mom has a complicated relationship with computers. She is my musician, you see. Harpist No science, no technology in the head, some notes. And, frankly, the quality of communication via Skype at that time was far from being at the highest level.
It was during that period of my life that I learned a lot about ip-telephony. It's funny that after 3 years I got a job at E1tele.com (ip-telephony operator on the Russian market). Now my mother and I are actively using my official position and both are very satisfied: the quality is excellent, it is indecently cheap, with no problems with special programs.
I suggest you do the impossible - to call abroad for free . Our company provides users with the possibility of a test call to another country . To do this, do not even need to register.
Just in case, I enclose instructions.
  1. First you need to determine the place of your current stay.
  2. Then enter your mobile phone number in a special form.
  3. Then an SMS message with an access code will be sent to your phone. Enter it in a special form.
  4. Enter the number to be called.
  5. Your phone will receive a call to which you need to answer. Subscribers are connected using callback technology. The essence of this technology lies in the fact that E1tele.com first dials to one subscriber, then to the second, and then connects them to each other. After you pick up the phone, two phone numbers are connected and the conversation begins.


As you can see, making a call is very simple. If everything goes smoothly and you appreciate all the benefits of ip-telephony, which is offered by the company E1tele.com , then I am sure we will become good friends.
PS If you really want to call abroad, but absolutely no one, then call my mom. Still, she is bored there, across the ocean ...
