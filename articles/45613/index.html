Wikipoogle

At a special event, which took place on November 25 in the Moscow office of Google, the company officially announced the Wikipoisk service (SearchWiki) - an individual setting of search results. Actually, a week ago, this service was discovered and described by the habrechelok k48 . A little later, habrachelovek zakalka copied the news Cnews about it. And much earlier, as many have already forgotten, Google experiments noticed kossnocorp . Now we know everything exactly - it is not just an experiment.

Yes, now every logged Google user will be able to rank links in the output at his own discretion - to raise some to the very top, and to exclude some from it altogether. These estimates will only affect the issue, which is shown to the user himself, and he can roll back any of his estimates.

If the search query Google did not show the page that seems to be the most appropriate user, he can add a link to it in a special form at the bottom of the page. The added link will automatically take the first place.


Also now you can add a comment to each link in the issue.

Comments and votes on the links for any request from all users can be viewed on a special page.
