STORY Station with USB 3.0

The device is made on the basis of 3.5 "hard drives, with a capacity of 1 or 2 TB, as the name implies, the connection to the computer occurs via high-speed USB 3.0 interface, which is backward compatible with USB 2.0 and USB 1.1.

USB 3.0 is characterized by a data transfer rate of 5 gigabits per second, while USB 2.0 offers only 480 megabytes per second, which allows you to transfer a 4Mb music file or image in just 0.02 minutes, and full HD video in 2 ,3 minutes. Also, Samsung STORY Station 3.0 has three energy-saving modes: standard, standby and sleep mode.

This model comes with software such as: SafetyKey (password protection), Samsung Auto Backup (backup) and SecretZone (encryption). The product line has been in existence since last year at the end of April, and has already passed the test of time. On the product extends branded 3-year warranty.

The aluminum case promises durability and lightness of this external drive. The body material complies with the environmental standards of RoHS, and thanks to an improved ventilation system, the disk works smoothly and does not overheat.

Already in June of this year, this model should appear on the shelves of Russian stores.
