Celestia



In the light of the heightened interest in astronautics and astronomy on Habr√© (thanks to NASA and Curiosity), I would like to tell you about the wonderful free planetarium created by Chris Laurel - Celestia (or rather, about the extended build of Celestia Extended Pack).
By the way, it was with the help of Celestia that the screensaver was created for the popularly favorite series The Big Bang Theory :)

About Celestia


Celestia is a free astronomical program for three-dimensional modeling of the Universe, using OpenGL technology, created in the C ++ programming language for Microsoft Windows, Linux and Mac OS X platforms and available under the terms of the GNU General Public License. Celestia displays more than 120,000 stars from the Hipparcos catalog, allows you to navigate the virtual universe, to simulate various events of any time, both past and future. With the presence of connected 3D-models of space objects, you can create realistic pictures of what is happening, for example, the traveling of spacecraft, eclipses, and much more. Celestia displays the atmospheres of planets and satellites, moving clouds, shadows from eclipses, sunsets and sunrises, detailed surface structures of objects, planetary rings, night light of cities on Earth, constellation lines, their boundaries and illustrations. The current version is 1.6.1 from June 2011 and the program is slowly developing.



For any object, basic information is displayed about its size, distance from the central object of the system (if any), temperature, as well as the current distance from it. The names of important surface objects can be displayed on planets and satellites. You can create your own scripts, tours, take screenshots and record videos (which, in fact, the creators of the screen saver for TBV used ).

For each object, you can turn on the display of orbits, coordinate grids for planets and satellites.

Celestia Extended Pack


Frankly speaking, the standard delivery of Celestia looks rather dull and specially optimized for weak computers and dialaps. More advanced users should add nice textures, models, etc. themselves. But, the world is not without good people, one good person created an extended build of the program, which initially included a large number of nishtyakov - Celestia Extended Pack v1.1
