Virtual assistant


Stylish, fashionable, youth outsourcing has penetrated into all areas, the topic of this post is not an exception. For relatively little money, you can get a virtual secretary or a whole front office that will work with correspondence, receive calls, search and structure the necessary information and carry out simple mechanical orders without occupying your office spaces (if any).

Slightly more complex tasks will be able to take on the services of virtual assistants, such as YourBees or Assistant . These guys will book a hotel, order flowers for their beloved woman, arrange the event, help them find employees, suppliers, contractors and even real estate. It was not possible to grope a clear border of competencies of the virtual assistant - he will undertake any legal tasks that do not require going beyond the office. If you can not cope himself, help find a contractor.

Another type of virtual assistant is concierge services . In essence, this is a virtual assistant of the premium level, which will be 146% more courteous, polite and diligent. Such services, as a rule, go to the appendage to elite brands like Vertu , Visa cards of Gold level and higher, or to membership in closed luxury clubs , so not everyone will be able to participate. We have never faced face to face with similar phenomena, but they say that the quality is top notch. Maybe you had this experience?



To suit
Virtual secretaries and assistants will save you from daily struggle with routine, free up time for thinking about taking over the world. The entrepreneur does not have to think about who will entrust this or that task. Disadvantages: you will have to work with different employees (although there are assistants for the dedicated and elite levels) who will not dive into the specifics of the business. But such an assistant will never get sick and will not give up everything for a trip to an exotic country.

It is incredibly difficult to select new epithets for the category that you describe for the third time. One of the advantages is a more personal approach than that of virtual assistants and a lower cost in comparison with a full-time employee. The disadvantages are also obvious: not the highest motivation, the impossibility of physical impact. Yes, and tea will not bring.

On the other hand, the secretarial profession does not require the same level of knowledge and skills that an accountant and a lawyer need. You can hire a friend, a neighbor's daughter or a second great-nephew. The main thing is responsibility and discipline. It is advisable to conclude a contract of civil nature in order to avoid legal battles.



To suit
For those who need a personal approach. Theoretically, when a business has an office with a reception, the remote employee will be able to get the level-up to full-time. In this case, the entrepreneur does not have to re-acquaint the person with the internal cuisine of his company.
