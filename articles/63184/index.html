What keeps www.asp.net?

While surfing the Internet, I accidentally came across this post, in the comments to which the notorious ScottGu revealed some statistics on the ASP.NET main site.

So, www.asp.net :

2 web servers and 2 database servers. On one database server there are blogs and forums, on the second - everything else.

2 web servers serve:
  • 120 million page views per month
  • 2400 forum posts daily
  • Over 500 blogs with over 1 million RSS subscriptions


Maintaining a full range of sites on the Microsoft web platform (www.asp.net, www.iis.net , www.silverlight.net , www.windowsclient.net ) costs more than a million dollars a year, with most of the costs for suppliers content (original articles, tutorials, screenshots and various video content). In addition, technical costs (development, support for wiki / blogs / forums, 24/7 job security, design work), and traffic payment (several terabytes per day).

Such is the kitchen.
