Secure Payments with ActivateTo Codes (Part II)

The first part of the description of the ActivateTo service was devoted to the general principles of its work, who can become its user, and what benefits ActivateTo brings to the entrepreneurs who chose it. Let me remind you that thanks to this service from PaySto, sellers of web services and products can receive payment in a safe environment, with a guarantee and maximum profit for themselves.


Payment through ActivateTo is realized using any electronic currency (whether it is Yandex.Money, WebMoney or other types of digital means), bank transfers, via payment terminals, as well as using plastic bank cards and even private money transfers. As in the case of other PaySto services, when working with ActivateTo, currency is converted within the system. The money is withdrawn by the recipient already in the form and in the currency that is most convenient for him.

In addition to the ActivateTo secure payment service itself, PaySto also provides sellers with a special, only for this purpose, created, generator of payment activation codes - Keys.ly.


This is a convenient service, especially for those providers of goods and services that are far from information technology and programming, or simply wish to focus as much as possible on their products.

This is a simple process consisting of several stages. First you need to create a template in the ActivateTo system that will be used in Keys.ly. This is implemented in the user's personal account. In the form of creating a template, you will need to specify the name of the template, its width and face value in USD, as well as the mandatory return URL , which guarantees the correct operation of the form.


Next, the merchant registers in Keys.ly his own form of activation codes , through which payment will be made for his products or services. In the form of payment on Keys.ly the seller pays the cost of registering the form (about 3 rubles.).


When payment is received, an email with a code confirming the payment of the form and a PIN code is sent to the merchant's email address. These codes must be entered in the form generator to tell Keys.ly about the payment. The template ID is also indicated here (it should be viewed in the corresponding template in the Personal Account) and the merchant's e-mail address. The next step is to confirm the purchase of the form via the link in the email received from PaySto. When you click on the link, the seller activates the form of receiving payments from their customers. Now, notifications about each payment made by customers will be sent to the merchant's email.

When the consumer of the services or products of the user ActivateTo decides to pay for his purchase , he can use the simple form of activation of the purchased code through the form on Keys.ly. To do this, it follows the corresponding link generated by the system, after creating an activation template.


Clicking on the link, the buyer enters the form of generating codes ActivateTo. By specifying your e-mail and payment method, it activates the generation process. When the payment by the method chosen by the buyer is implemented, he will receive a code and a pin confirming the payment in an e-mail.


This data needs to be copied and entered in the code generation system.


As a result, the seller will receive an email to his email address stating that payment for his item has been made. All transferred funds are sent to the merchant's account with PaySto and can be withdrawn at any time.

Convenience and profitability of the ActivateTo service is obvious. The seller saves money and manpower, not creating independent forms of accepting payment for his goods, and the buyer chooses the only convenient payment method from the wide range of methods PaySto works with. In this case, both parties are fully protected from theft of confidential information and funds, because the payment takes place in a closed, secure environment.
