Nirvana

The workplace of a sysadmin, a php programmer, a PR specialist, advertising, and much more than what you have to do as the manager of your own small regional portal is very sensitive to convenience, but completely insensitive to the operating system. Computers, operating systems (xp, vista, windows7, ubuntu, mac os) change easily and naturally, and the browser-mail-ssh-icq-phpide software continues to work without causing every time brain cancer with its innovations.

Experimenting with thin clients, monitors, keyboards, reached nirvana :)



mac book pro MB990 with memory increased to 4GB, stand Rain Design mStand, monitor 24 'in my case, viewsonic vg2427wm and racially-correct keyboard and mouse.

After a series of rb, hp, acer, asus, two months of pleasure from working in a new environment. Although, of course, if we forget about the cost of this software and hardware complex. But if you forget about the price, for example, my three-year-old daughter passed through a closed laptop and he honestly passed this test with dignity, I think this is a very good plus of the mb pro performance.

Well, this post was not created with the goal of being KO, but with the goal of conveying a simple truth that it is not always worth trying to bend the changeable world :) You get tired, sometimes you get tired of setting up the environment for yourself, you just want to work comfortably.
