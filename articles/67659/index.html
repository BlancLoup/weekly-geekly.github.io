The American version of the center of treatment of Internet-addiction. Expensive

Americans followed in the footsteps of the Chinese and recently opened a rehabilitation center for Internet addiction near Redmond (the city where Microsoft headquarters is located).

For 45 days of the reStart rehabilitation program at the Heavensfield Retreat Center, the lodger will have to pay "only" for $ 14,500. The kit includes the suppression of thirst for online games, social networks and even writing SMS.

So far, the Heavensfield Retreat Center has only one patient - a 19-year-old gamer, whose parents seem to be so preoccupied that they have not been greedy and have paid a considerable amount.

By the way, there are about 200 such clinics in China.
