Romo: pet

Designers, Lego, radio-controlled cars and helicopters, computer games ... The industry produces a lot of goods for children of any age, but let's be honest: the purchase and subsequent games for dads are not less exciting (and sometimes much more) than for their beloved children.

Recently I received a parcel with another toy for my son (yeah, I have nothing to do with it!) - robot Romo:

(iPhone not included)

I want to acquaint all readers with this wonderful, not afraid of the word, pet. We in the office have already played enough of it .

History of creation


Romo is a bright representative of a successful KickStarter project. The guys from Romotive acted wisely and first (in the fall of 2011) presented a simple project to the public, received about $ 115 thousand of investments (out of the requested 32 thousand) in 45 days and created the first version of Romo:


The robot turned out to be rather clumsy, the interface of communication with the phone through the headphone jack caused a lot of questions, but the idea turned out to be fruitful and was accepted by users favorably: there were many orders and the guys had to assemble their own hands in four months and mail 2000 devices.

It is not surprising that after some time (in the fall of 2012) the guys launched the second project on a kickstarter and collected 170 thousand dollars in 30 days (of the requested 100 thousand). Users were asked to finance the production of the new version of Romo: more advanced and much prettier (although to whom, let 's remember Bender's pron ):

Scope of delivery and specifications




Romo comes in a beautiful box in which you will find the robot itself, a mini-USB cable and a small instruction manual.

Inside the robot there is a 2000 mAh NiMH battery, which is enough, according to the manufacturer, for two hours of continuous play. The battery is charged using the included mini-USB cable, while the iPhone connected to Romo will also be charged, and when the cable is connected to the computer, the iPhone can synchronize with it.

The device body, its design and build quality are excellent.
