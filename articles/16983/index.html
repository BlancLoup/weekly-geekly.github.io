Top 100 games of all time. IGN version

The editors of the famous resource IGN Entertainment took up the difficult task of revising the list of hundreds of the best games in history. To be honest, even the first ten raises some questions. So, along with such mega-hits as Super Mario Bros, Tetris, Civilization II and Final Fantasy III hit, for example, Resident Evil 4 (10th place), ahead of many great games in this rating.

In fact, the final verdict on the charts has not yet passed. First, registered IGN users can contribute to the vote by opting for one game or another. And, secondly, the decision of the editorial board can be challenged in a specially created for this forum thread .

Ten best games according to IGN :
1. Super Mario Bros
2. Tetris
3. Civilization II
4. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
5. Super Mario 64
6. Half-Life 2
7. Super Metroid
8. The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past
9. Final Fantasy III (US)
10. Resident Evil 4
