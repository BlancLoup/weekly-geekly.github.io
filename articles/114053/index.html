Design


Optimus has ceased to be a budget tube. The connectors and buttons, in my opinion, are located exactly where needed - nothing interferes with manipulations, and there is no need to reach far too far. The 2x widescreen display is one of the largest on the market - four inches. Plus, several special hardware solutions for three-dimensional graphics (an eight-core ULP GeForce chip), as well as video and audio encoding and decoding.
