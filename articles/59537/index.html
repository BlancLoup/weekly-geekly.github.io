Ubuntu Media Player

Portable media players running Ubuntu Linux began to produce a Chinese company SmartDevices .
The 4.3-inch touchscreen models are SmartQ 5 MID ($ 150), and the 7-inch model is SmartQ 7 ($ 190).



Characteristics of media players:
  • CPUSamsung S3C6410 (ARM11) 667MHz;
  • RAM 128MB DDR RAM, Flash 1GB;
  • Slot for microSD cards.
  • 4.3- or 7-inch touchscreen, both with a resolution of 800 X 480;
  • WiFi 802.11b / g;
  • Bluetooth;
  • USB 2.0 Host port;
  • Size: 120 x 74 x 14 mm for the SmartQ 5 model; SmartQ 7 dimensions are not announced;
  • Weight 150 gr. for model SmartQ 5 and 250 gr. for SmartQ 7;
  • 2000mAH (SmartQ 5) or 4500mAH (SmartQ 7) battery.

Both models are almost identical, both have Bluetooth and WiFi, 128 MB of RAM and 1GB of flash memory. Both models are distributed with the same Ubuntu Linux distributions.
