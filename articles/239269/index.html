Welcome to Office 365 Summit October 23-24



We are pleased to invite you to take part in the Office 365 Summit conference, which will be held in Moscow on October 23-24, 2014 at the Congress Park of the Radisson Royal Hotel Moscow at Kutuzovsky Avenue, Moscow, 2/1.



The Office 365 Summit is a two-day technical conference for IT professionals, developers and Microsoft partners at the world roadshow , during which you will have a unique opportunity to learn about the latest technical innovations of Office, Office 365, Exchange, SharePoint, Lync and other products.

You are expected by more than 60 sessions with content level 300-400.

You will be able to talk with leading experts of Microsoft, to gain knowledge first-hand, to take part in practical classes, as well as get certified.

Event languages: English with simultaneous translation and Russian.

Participation in the event is free .
Online translation is not provided.
