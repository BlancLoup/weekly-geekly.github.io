Samsung Series 7 Slate PC

At an exhibition in Germany, Samsung presented a tablet based on Windows 7. First of all, the hardware component attracts attention: Core i5 and 4Gb Ram make this tablet an order of magnitude more attractive than current x86.



Specifications

CPU : Intel i5 2467M 1.6GHz
RAM : 4 Gb
HDD : 64 or 128 Gb SSD
Screen : 11.6 "1366 x 768, capacitive
Camera : 2 megapixel front, 3 megapixel rear
Connectivity : 802.11 b / g / n WiFi, 3G
Ports : USB 2.0, micro HDMI
Accessories : dock, keyboard, Wacom pen
Price : $ 1,099 - $ 1,199
Weight : 920 grams
Thickness : 13mm

Minuses

The price is depressing, but with such hardware characteristics, we can talk about a full replacement laptop.
The dock position is fixed and the slope cannot be changed.
Air vents hint at heat and gluttony.
The battery life is not specified, but I rate it as 4-6 hours.
Third-party software about the tablets did not hear, and even more so.

pros

Characteristics of a full-fledged laptop.
Weight. It is quite possible to keep the mouse-keyboard charging at home and in the office and carry less weight with it.
Capacitive screen.
All the usual software at hand.
Branded shell, sharpened by the fingers.

Conclusion

On sale Series 7 Slate PC should arrive on October 2.
If it really costs about 1000 bucks, then I would prefer a tablet to a laptop.
