Windows Camp 3: how it was + new courses on MVA

Just a couple of weeks ago, on September 12, we had a third (already traditional) Windows Camp , which gathered more than 500 participants on the spot and more than 25,000 online. Many thanks to everyone who listened to us and watched, talked with us and supported us in social networks!



The very first Windows Camp was dedicated to the first preview versions of Windows 8, on the second we shared key information on the eve of the release of the final versions of Windows 8 and Windows Phone 8 (this was a year ago).

The third campaign has grown both in content and on topics: 4 tracks, more than 20 reports, added Windows Azure and, as always, fresh information that should help you keep up with the latest market trends and get ready for the final version of Windows in a timely manner. 8.1. We also used the experience of our partner summit events (for example, Windows 8 Summit) and added panel discussions in which our partners shared their development experience for Microsoft platforms.
