Blacklist sites can benefit the Internet

Today the law on the black list of sites came into force. His harm is obvious.

But our world is not black and white. Let's try to take advantage of the new law.

For the first time in the legislation appeared the rules of blocking sites. Moreover, without a court decision, only access to child pornography, drug propaganda, and suicide calls may be restricted.

Previously, the prosecutor's office, the police, the FSB, and anyone else could have demanded the closure of the site! Providers often went with them.

Let's all together make the law apply.

Write in the comments or in a personal about blocking sites without making them in the registry .

For each fact, I will send an official appeal to the prosecutor's office on behalf of the organization in which I work (I will not name it, habr is not for politics).

Together we can stop at least illegal censorship on the Internet.

In my opinion, this is not so little.

UPD:
I do not support the blacklist. But if he gives any opportunities, they must be used.
