Game Exhibitions 2015

Hi Habr!

In light of the imminent release of our project (already this summer), our team decided to explore what kind of game events will be held in 2015 and which ones we should pay attention to. As soon as this list was compiled, we carefully studied it and selected the events of interest to us. We suggest you also get acquainted with this list of more than 30 events.




Let's start with the bad news. Unfortunately, as you could already know , the GDC in 2015 will not take place. But there are still a couple of good events worth a look, if not as a participant, then as a visitor - necessarily.

CIS events


OGIC
Peter, dates are specified on the site

OGIC is an exhibition of advances in computer technology and graphics. The event brings together developers of computer games, distributors and publishers, service providers, services and entertainment equipment, representatives of educational centers and schools, producers, production centers and other representatives of the industry. The exhibition includes e-sports competitions in various disciplines, such as Dota 2, StarCraft 2, Counter Strike, League of Legends, etc.
http://ogicon.ru/



Devgamm
Moscow 05/16/2015

DevGAMM conference is:

  • conference for game developers and publishers
  • the most productive event of the gaming industry
  • 90% content about game development and only 10% about marketing.

http://devgamm.com/moscow2015



White nights
Peter 06/26/2015


WN Mobile Games Conference is an international conference dedicated to the development and promotion of games for mobile devices. It takes place twice a year (in February - Winter Nights and in June - White Nights), and each time it gathers in St. Petersburg an increasing number of professionals who influence the development of the mobile gaming industry.
http://wnconf.com/



Igromir
Moscow 10/01/2015

IgroMir is the first full-scale exhibition in Russia for all lovers of interactive entertainment: computer games, games for consoles, mobile phones and other platforms. This is a fascinating tour of the virtual worlds and real emotions, the opportunity to evaluate the latest gaming news and see for yourself those who make them before others.
http://igromir-expo.ru/



NextCastle
Peter 10/30/2015


NextCastle Party is a unique non-commercial gaming festival, which will be held in St. Petersburg. The main focus is on independent developers and retro games.
The festival combines contests for game developers, competitions among players, a retro exhibition where you can easily use all the exhibits, look at the exhibition of self-made games and play them, and, of course, some lectures that will lift the veil of game development secrets.
Among the goals of the festival, I especially want to highlight a couple of main ones: to give a second life to the old, good games, on which more than one generation has grown, to support independent game developers.
http://2015.nextcastle.ru/

Tokyo Game Show
Tokyo 09/19/2015

The Tokyo Game Show, commonly known as TGS, is a game exhibition / convention traditionally held in Tokyo, Japan. The show is represented by the association CESA and Nikkei Business Publications, Inc. The show focuses on Japanese games, but some international video game developers use it to demonstrate upcoming releases or game-related hardware. Like Gamescom in Germany, the Tokyo Game Show allows the general public to attend the show during the last two days.
