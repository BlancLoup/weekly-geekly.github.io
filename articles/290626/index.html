General information about studying

  • Study in Poland for students begins on October 1
  • Winter session begins after the New Year holidays, summer in early-late June.
  • Duration of training:
    Bachelor's degree - 6 semesters
    Engineering degree - 7 months
    Master's degree - 4 months
  • Education in Polish is cheaper than an English-language program.
  • Universities are divided into: private and public

I will not write specifically about the university, since each university has its pros and cons.

Student path

I did not have the opportunity to study at the State University. For them you need a level of knowledge of the language - B1 (Polish, English). It was possible to spend 1 year and go to general classes and at the same time study the language at the university. Since I clarified that I came without knowledge of Polish, I chose a private university, with a very international audience.

Since the Faculty of Computer Science is Engineering, I have correspondingly basic subjects. All expectations about fashionable frameworks and foreign teachers slightly dispelled. I would like to see advanced teachers of programming, and not those people who ended their careers 15 years ago and did not write lines of code except for classes.

Regarding state universities such as: the University of Warsaw and the Warsaw Polytechnic University.
From workmates, I hear much more positive reviews and I see that the knowledge base is much higher. Not to mention the fact that you can always do something extra. Speech only about the university program.

Work searches

During my studies, I began to look for work and visited Targi Pracy (meetings with employers).



Before I started looking, I accordingly resolved the issue of a work permit.
For me, the way out was to get Karty Pobytu (the type of document allowing the arrival of a foreigner, with access to the labor market).

The first meeting was not entirely successful. I got the impression that I looked better than some HRs. Of course, I was unprepared for everything compared to subsequent meetings, but for that I had the motivation and thought how to use my potential next time and what to pay more attention to and of course coffee, donuts, Playstation and interesting speeches and presentations of companies and their activities, as well as new technologies - their eyes diverged.
