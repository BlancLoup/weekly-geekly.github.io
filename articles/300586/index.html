What is the most important thing for IT vacancies for you, without which you will not respond to it?

A week ago, we published our small study , which confirmed the hypothesis that IT vacancies that contain wages are more likely to respond. And the size of the specified wage does not matter - the main thing is that it should just be immediately indicated.

We decided not to dwell on the statistics we collected on the vacancies posted on My Circle and went further: we conducted a sociological survey among IT professionals from the Habr community. We asked them a question: ‚ÄúWhat is the most important thing for you in a vacancy, without which you will not respond to it?‚Äù.

At the moment, more than 400 people took part in the survey, and it is hardly likely that the results will change dramatically with the increase in the number of respondents. So show them to you!



  • The absolute majority, 83% of the respondents, said that they would not respond to a job that did not include a salary .   • For the majority, 55% of the respondents, the description of the technologies used in the work is extremely important. The technologies used on My Circle are indicated not only in the job description, but also in the required skill tags. The latter is also extremely important so that we can recommend the most qualified specialists for you.

  • For almost half, 47% of respondents, it is important to know the type of business of the company .
