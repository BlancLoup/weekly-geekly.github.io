Amateur radio community

The number of fans poking around with a soldering iron and radio details is growing and, moreover, the number of people who want to share knowledge also comes. And about half a year ago, having noticed a sharp increase in the number of articles sent to easyelectronics , we decided to sip a multiplayer community dedicated to radio electronics. With a focus on learning information on different topics from the basics and analog technology to monster-like systems on microcontrollers and FPGAs. The result was a project:

we.easyelectronics.ru

We also organized a continuous cyclical competition for the greatest contribution to community development, where, by beating in a hundred posts, we distribute to authors who score the most points (credited for articles), any useful small things - digital oscilloscopes, soldering stations, logic analyzers and other things that are cute to any ironworks. Those who did not have enough points for a little one, in the next iteration of the contest have every chance to snatch a toy fatter, because the points scored are partially saved, providing the author with a head start on this circle.

And if flux hiss, the brilliance of solder and the friendly blinking of the LED you like, then we are waiting for you in our community!
