Ordered a rare phone in a foreign online store? Get a subpoena

According to Kommersant, a resident of the Ulyanovsk region, who ordered a Motorola Moto G phone in a German online store, was brought into administrative business for the illegal importation of a phone containing encryption tools.



In May of this year, Yevgeny Yarutkin, who lives in the Ulyanovsk region, ordered a smartphone on the German site. In Russia, this model is not for sale, and this is the most important nuance in this story. At the post office, after the parcel arrived, they refused to give the phone to Eugene, because He came with custom notice. Customs officers also could not issue it, because there is no notification for this model, which is issued by the FSB. Having skirted a month, Eugene waved his hand - they say, let the parcel go backwards. The parcel went away (and to the buyer, according to various sources, it seems like they even got the money back), but then they called the customs and said that they were inciting an administrative case to the buyer. Now Eugene faces a fine of 1.5-2.5 thousand rubles.
