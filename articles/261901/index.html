Yes, even at the level of large software systems and complex information systems, this terminology can be applied if we talk about the exchange of hyperlinks at a high organizational level. The main thing is that all communications are professional.

The fact that any designer periodically needs to refer to the normative-technical documentation, GOST, SNIP, taxiways and other important regulatory materials is a fact. And not only to the designer, but in general to any engineer or normocontroller - actual regulatory documents are needed by all, regardless of the field in which the specialist works. Even to me, the humble author of this material, in order to determine how well the prepared text complies with the standards, before you submit it for proofreading, you often have to turn to professional sources for an answer. But, first of all, such a need is, of course, among engineers of a different profile.

Understanding the importance of this functionality, we agreed in February with the company ASCON, the developer of the popular Compass-3D system, about combining our integration efforts. The result of these agreements was the creation of an intelligent panel right in the working environment of this CAD system ‚ÄúCompass-3D‚Äù, which will allow you to place complex hyperlinks between CAD systems and regulatory documents in professional help systems ‚ÄúTechExpert‚Äù. What is the point?

Imagine you are creating three-dimensional models or working with drawings in Compass-3D. To solve engineering problems, you urgently need to go to an important GOST, without being distracted from the project. Our smart panel allows you to quickly navigate to the desired document in the system, open the TechExpert without leaving Compass-3D, and also place hyperlinks to all regulatory documents in your drawing. The same works for multi-page projects - in this case, the specialist can use the automatic placement of hyperlinks, which will reduce the time of his work on combining the necessary graphics with the relevant regulatory documentation by a hundred times. We also provided for the case when it is necessary to place links only to selected documents - now this can be done manually.
