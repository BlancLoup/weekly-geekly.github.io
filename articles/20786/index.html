Coworking office from an internet cafe? How realistic is it?

I have in mind a nice Internet cafe in the center of the city, the windows overlook the fountain, trees and benches around the fountain.

I want to offer this internet cafe to change its profile in a coworking office. Pluses for freelancers, in my opinion, are obvious:
  1. You do not need to deal with the registration of a tenant office - the company is already there!
  2. There is no need for start-up capital for the first payment of rent and collateral - the owner of the cafe already has the room and money!
  3. It does not take long and tedious (and probably also expensive) to work through legal issues - Internet caf√© has a very similar business model , which needs to be slightly corrected.
  4. Money for the internal arrangement will be spent less - in the cafe already have repairs, tables, chairs and the Internet

By the way, this cafe is located in the center of Moscow, from the metro station within walking distance. The list however can be applied to any Internet cafes in any city. Residents of the capital can add a convenient location to the above list.

So, the list of advantages for a freelancer can be continued, but what to say to the owner? What advantages will he have? Of course, the emphasis should be placed on the money they earn on this, but I am not in the economy at all booming. I can only guess that Internet cafes are beginning to transform into a simple caf√© due to a drop in demand, because in Moscow home anlim is very cheap.

How can it look and work? It may look like a simple Internet cafe, only there people will work: no documents and advance payments, they always came, paid for 1/3/5 hours of stay, sat down with his laptop and worked. Availability can be pre-specified by phone (no places - stay at home). IMHO, this is easier for both the visitor and the owner, but it is harder to build a community.

I propose to conduct a brainstorm, the result of which will be a list of commercial benefits from the conversion of Internet cafes to a coworking office. Well, or as an option, you can come to the conviction that you will not do business on this and as a result - you will have to deal with these freelancers themselves on a voluntary basis.
