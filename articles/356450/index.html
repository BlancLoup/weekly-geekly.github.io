The number of users of electronic public services is growing by more than 1 million per month

Nikolai Nikiforov, Minister of Communications, presented a regular report to the government with indicators of the functioning of e-government infrastructure. The growth for the month exceeded one million people, in February only through the State services and the application received 12.7 million federal services, in some regions of Russia the number of users registered on the portal exceeded 40% of the population.



In the Unified Identification and Authentication System (ESIA), 24.5 million people are registered, and in February 2016, the increase was 1.1 million. You can register and confirm your identity in ESIA at 10.8 thousand points in the country. Penetration of services in 30 regions of Russia is above 20%. At the same time, in the Primorsky Krai, the Khanty-Mansiysk Autonomous Okrug, the Tyumen and Kaliningrad Oblasts, more than 40% of the population is registered in the system.

The indicator is below 10% in the Nenets and Chukotka Autonomous Regions, Trans-Baikal Territory, Dagestan, Ingushetia, Kabardino-Balkaria and the Crimea, Sevastopol.

49 subjects of the Russian Federation are integrated with the System of interdepartmental electronic interaction. They sent the system 58 million federal and 7.2 million regional information for February 2016.

On the Single portal of public services created an interactive form of applications for the provision of services for the reception and issuance of documents on registration of the conclusion and dissolution of marriage, birth, adoption or adoption and death. Pay the fees for the conclusion and dissolution of the marriage can be immediately on the portal. For those who want to marry or adopt a child, the portal offers a joint application from two private offices.

At the meeting of the Subcommittee on the use of information technologies in the provision of state and municipal services of the Government Commission on the use of information technologies to improve the quality of life and business conditions, they also discussed the work of the Multifunctional Centers: their network covers 95.3% of the population and provides 42 public services.
