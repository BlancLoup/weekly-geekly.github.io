Conceptually, the ComfortWay RSP platform is a technological platform that unites mobile operators, users and service partners.

The main value of the platform in the possibility of "seamless" integration into the products of partners of mobile operators tariffs. The set of technology components included in the platform will ensure the integration of platform capabilities for:

  • Manufacturers of any wearable device.

Example You are a virtual operator. In order to expand the offer for our subscribers with local tariffs in 140 countries of the world, it is enough to install our software (java applet) on the operator's SIM card and integrate with the ComfortWay RSP platform.

Technology

The platform implements the concept of ABC - always best connected. The choice and connection to the operator is implemented via a SIM card and an array of profiles or via a partner's SIM card onto which ComfortWay software is integrated.

SIM management is possible, either through a custom application, or from platform servers.
CwSim is the client part of the platforms in the form of a carrier-agnostic e-SIM (SIM) card, on which the Remote SIM Provisioning specification is implemented. The uniqueness of the card is that it is not tied to any one operator and a profile (subscription) of one of the mobile operators can be loaded into it on demand at any time.
