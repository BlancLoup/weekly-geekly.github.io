Updated Evernote extension for Chrome: parallel search in Yandex



Our new version of the Google Chrome browser extension should appeal to Russian-speaking users of Evernote. Now, when searching in Yandex, the search for your notes in Evernote will be automatically performed. This will allow you to quickly find out that you have once stored the required information in Evernote. You might be surprised how often this happens.

Download the Evernote extension here . If it is already installed, the browser will update it automatically.

Parallel search in Yandex


If you have not enabled parallel search, you will need to manually activate this feature. This can be done in two ways - by enabling the corresponding checkbox in the extension settings or in its authorization window. To work parallel search, you must be logged in to Evernote. So if you‚Äôve been looking for something and you probably know that there are relevant notes in your Evernote account, while not a single result is shown on the extension icon, then most likely you‚Äôve just not logged in to your account (this can be done, by clicking on the extension icon).

After enabling the parallel search function on the search results page in Yandex, the number of found notes matching the query in your Evernote account will be shown under the search bar. The same number will be shown as an indicator on the extension icon in Chrome. To view the found notes, just click on the green tab in the expansion window.

Personal search


Parallel search is useful in a variety of situations. For example, if you plan to please your loved ones with a delicious dish, the first thing you need is a recipe. Enter the corresponding request in Yandex, and suddenly it turns out that once you have already prepared it and even saved the recipe in Evernote. You can quickly open it, and at the same time look in Yandex search results for other cooking options and compare them with your own.

Or you need to find a sample of a contract. It is very likely that once you have already saved it in Evernote for the future, but out of habit decided to find a sample in Yandex. The extension will remind you that the contract already exists in your account. However, the search results are also worth a look: suddenly the standards for processing such documents and legislation have already changed.

In general, with Evernote for Chrome, your search in Yandex becomes truly personalized and more functional.

Recall that, along with Yandex, parallel search works in Google, Bing and Yahoo.
