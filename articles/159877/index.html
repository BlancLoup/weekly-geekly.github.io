Behind the gate of the school


For the daily work of teachers and student achievement lies another, no less important task - the control of the educational process and interaction with educational authorities.

At the end of each quarter, Anna Vladimirovna, Head of the School of Grammar School No. 0 (head teacher), sends reports on the school‚Äôs activities to the municipal education authority. Before the transition to electronic document management, this process took a lot of time from her and her colleagues (80% of reports are requests from educational authorities and their subordinate organizations, very often duplicating statistical reports and federal monitoring data).

To see the necessary report today, the head teacher opens the Dnevnik.ru administrative interface and selects the necessary report - the Passport of the educational institution.



This is one of many reports that Administrator of Grammar School ‚Ññ0 can generate:
  • Attendance reports within a school, class or student.
  • Statistics of grades in each of the classes.
  • Statistics of electronic journals and diaries.
  • Regional report on progress, which is based on the final grades of all students in each of the subjects.

For cases when additional manipulations with data are necessary, each of the reports can be downloaded in Excel format.

Everyday education authorities


The changes also affected the education authorities. In Dnevnik.ru, regional reports are collected from the entire municipality, formed on the basis of the activities of the staff of each school.

For the convenience of interaction, each incoming report has the status:
  • Not submitted - requests for educational institutions that have not yet sent their reports
  • Unverified - received reports that require verification
  • Sent for revision - reports for which there were comments or necessary revisions
  • Checked and approved - immaculate reports from educational institutions
