Why we send the parcels before you pay for them



Last year, we opened a sorting center in Moscow and launched the auto-send function for parcels using our delivery methods Express and Air .

After packing, your parcels will immediately fly to Moscow for further shipment, and will not wait for payment in a warehouse in the USA. This makes the storage of your goods completely free.

How much do these changes increase the speed of delivery and optimize the warehouse operation, is the money lost with this method and how many packages remain unclaimed? In the post we tried to answer all the questions.



Why we decided that it is necessary


Wait until you pay for delivery from overseas of an already packaged parcel, it is not at all necessary, on the contrary, you need to send it as soon as possible. This will reduce the storage time of parcels in a warehouse in the US and save money.




How it works and how much it speeds up delivery.


After your goods arrived at our warehouse in the United States and were packed, we immediately send them to our warehouse in Moscow. There your parcels are waiting for payment and sent by courier services around the country. But to be precise, 90% of the packages do not expect anything at all. Customers pay them when they fly over the ocean.

This helped to increase the speed of delivery by 2-3 days . First of all, our clients from Moscow noticed a reduction in expectations.




How much do we lose on this form of delivery?


No, all the packages that customers have packed, they will definitely take. Someone earlier, and someone later. This is the specifics of shopping through the MOF. LiteMF users are, first of all, advanced buyers who know how much they have to pay for delivering their package. And if a person simply forgot that he has a package in stock, we will always remind him.




Who throws goods in stock


Often these are inexperienced buyers. For example, a customer bought a gun trigger. He did not know that any parts of the weapon are prohibited from being shipped. We offer him to return the goods to the seller. But the seller does not want to pay for shipping across the country. The result: the cost of the trigger - 3 bucks, the cost of delivery in the country - 5 bucks. The client says that he will buy such a trigger at home, and with it do what you want. We dispose of this product, and the customer is transformed from an inexperienced customer into an advanced one.




Fortunately, in most cases, all the goods can be returned. This is largely due to the fact that we have no additional fees for returning goods.

Good luck, and be careful when choosing a product!
