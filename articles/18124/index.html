Nigma on ice

Not only the developers of Habrakhabr celebrate the New Year , the developers of Nigma.ru also have fun to the fullest :). I decided to post pictures from our corporate party.
This time we decided to go to the Sports Palace in Sokolniki. It turns out that there is an evening skating rink, which is open from Friday to Sunday from 20:00 to 00:00 (information here: www.sokolniki.info ). By the way, a great place for corporate parties, I recommend!

At first, of course, it was scary - most of us went to the rink for the last time in early childhood; there were those who skated for the first time. I, for example, preparing for this joyful event, decided to buy myself skates. It turned out to be not so simple: first I had to stand in the kilometer queue at the store, and then another 1.5 hours in line to sharpen skates (if you decide to go on the ice, do not forget to sharpen the skates :).

It turned out funny - those who ride better, drove behind them those who are worse. But after half an hour, everyone settled down and it turned out very fun. What can I say - let the pictures speak for themselves:
