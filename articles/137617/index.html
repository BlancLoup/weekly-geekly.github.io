Daz Productions distributes free licenses for Bryce 7 Pro, Daz Studio and Hexagon

Great news for amateurs and professionals in the field of three-dimensional graphics and animation! Digital Art Zone , also known as Daz Productions, distributes licenses for three of its flagship products for free as part of a limited promotion: Bryce 7 Pro, Daz Studio 4 and Hexagon 2. The total cost of licenses for all three products exceeds $ 800, but now for a limited time, we can get a full license for all three products for free!

Who cares, go under the cat, sit down comfortably.

These products are not leading in the market; among three-dimensional graphics specialists and designers, the products fall into the budget category. Nevertheless, these products are widely used in the industry, and very well known. Probably, the company thus wants to expand its community and thus also its presence in the market.

Briefly about software packages


Bryce 7 Pro

Its main purpose is landscape modeling, but the package is not limited to this. How to get a license?


To obtain a license, you must register as a buyer on the Daz Productions website , go to the store and "buy" a license for each of these products. You do not need to enter your account details, just provide the name and email address.

After the "purchase" go to your account. From the 'Available Downloads' section, download the installation files, the serial numbers are available in the 'Available Serial Numbers' section. All three packages are available for Windows and Mac OS X.
