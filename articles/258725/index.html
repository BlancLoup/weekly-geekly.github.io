Valera began with his favorite question: ‚ÄúWhat is object-oriented programming and why did they come up with it?‚Äù Yerzhan was surprised at the simplicity of the question and immediately replied: ‚ÄúEvery student knows this. The time has come for Valera: ‚ÄúWhat you talked about is true. But this is not the main thing. In fact, the main goal of the PLO is to combat complexity. Let me explain what I mean. In addition, the system will be convenient to develop a team of programmers, distributing independent components between them.
