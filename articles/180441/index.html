Unity for iOS and Android is now free for indie developers

Good news for indie game developers for mobile platforms. The ability to publish from Unity for iOS and Android is now free.

It is worthwhile to clarify that only the base license is given free of charge, which cost $ 400 for each platform. If you are interested in additional features, such as dynamic shadows, rendering in texture, post process and some others, then you still have to buy a Pro license, which costs $ 1500.

You can take advantage of this opportunity if you are an individual developer or a small studio with last year's income not exceeding $ 100,000.
Also, this news is useful to those who received free licenses for iOS and Android for Unity 3.5 under the action and could not switch to the fourth version due to the need to buy them for the new version.
Also, Unity CEO David Helgason promises to add in the near future to the list of supported platforms Blackberry 10 and Windows Phone 8 and also absolutely free.
In the last 30 days, representatives of Unity promise to contact those who bought these licenses in order to smooth out a negative deposit and offer discounts on future purchases.
Unity once again emphasizes its indie focus and significantly reduces the threshold of entry into mobile development for beginners.
News on the Unity website.
