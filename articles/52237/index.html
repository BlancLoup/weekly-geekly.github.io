Search from Yandex.Answer: the power of search algorithms plus the mass character of UGC

Recently, Yandex launched the Yandex.Answer service http://ask.yandex.ru . At first glance, the classic question-answer service. However, if you look deeper, the Yandex web-based ideas presented us with an interesting concept of a new search engine.

Many of us faced problems in discussing various topics on blogs and forums. Indeed, it is possible to get the right answer to the question asked, but this is very difficult. People do not know how to read and respond not to what is being said in the question, people answer without understanding the topic, people are engaged in advertising. I myself tried to ask a question on the Yandex.Answer site (I deliberately asked the question in such a not very detailed form), the result is obvious - I never received an answer.

So, the main advantage that Yandex.Answer provides is a search for already prepared answers. Why ask something that someone has already asked before us? Why waste your time on comments to incorrect answers, if you can take advantage of the fact that people have already done it before and just use the fruits of their work?

If we evaluate Yandex.Answers from the point of view of search technology, then Yandex presented a rather innovative solution:
