Features of loyalty programs and accruals of aviamil in Russian banks

Hi Habr!

Today we want to talk about such a complex product as a loyalty program. Many IT people often fly on business trips and on vacation, accumulating airline miles. Let's see what programs are profitable, how miles are credited, but first things first.




Many banks have become popular card issued with a special offer - the accumulation of bonus miles. In the future, these miles can be compensated for the purchase of air tickets. The essence of loyalty programs in all banks is the same - to attract new customers and give them the opportunity, with active use, to fly about 1 time a year on a plane absolutely free of charge. Below we tell how the bank actually earns miles, how to spend them correctly and what difficulties and illusions are found in these bonuses.



Banks and cards with such offers are a very large number.
For the example, the cards were selected - All Airlines from Tinkoff Bank Platinum Credit Systems (hereinafter AA), Aeroflot MasterCard Gold from Alfa Bank and Gold Travel VISA from Raiffeisen Bank.

These banks were not chosen by chance: they are very popular with IT audiences.

How much is the loyalty program

As such, the loyalty program is not paid for separately by the client and, according to the Bank, it is free. In fact, most cards with a loyalty program imply a high, and sometimes even a premium status of a card, which may indicate a relatively high cost of service. Thus, switching to a loyalty program with airline miles, the client begins to overpay for annual service 3-4 times more.

How do I earn miles

Each bank has its own rules for calculating bonuses. As a rule, for some purchases, a percentage of the purchase price is charged on a ‚Äúmile‚Äù account. Everywhere he is different and may even differ from the category of purchase. An example of this is AllAirlines. 2% of the cost of all purchases on the card, 1% for transactions made via the Internet Bank or Mobile Application, 3% for purchases of air tickets and increased rates for special offers up to 30% of the purchase price.

At Alfa Bank and Raiffeisen, everything is much simpler. In the first, for every 30 rubles that you spend, 1.25 miles will ‚Äúfall‚Äù into the account .
Well, Raiffeisen earns 1 mile for every 40 rubles spent from the card. There is also an additional partner iGlobe.ru , where for every 48 rubles you can get up to 15 miles when booking services on your card. This amount immediately makes you think that for forty-eight rubles? .. After reading the small print, we learn that this charge will occur for every euro spent on the site (Miles are calculated based on the order value in euros. The number of miles may vary depending on the ruble to the euro.)

The last two cards immediately raised a question. Not all numbers are divided by 30 or 40 without a residue. After reading more details with these loyalty programs, we can single out a similar principle for earning miles. The purchase amount is divided by 30 or 40, the result is multiplied by the coefficient of accrual miles.

There is a separate "but" - rounding the value of miles to a whole. This always happens according to the rules of the bank. A good example is the purchase on the Alfa-Bank card in the amount of 187.20 rubles. Mathematically, the sum of miles for an accrual of 7.8, but after rounding, only 7 miles will be credited.

Waiting for settlement
187.20 / 30 = 6.24; 6.24 * 1.25 = 7.8
Real action
  1. 187.20 / 30 = 6.24
  2. 6.24 * 1.25 = 7.8
  3. 7.8 = 7
  4. Accrued 7 miles.


At first glance, the order of crediting miles on the Tinkoff Credit Systems is significantly different. But this decision was wrong. The detailed terms of the action describe the process of ‚Äúreal counting‚Äù.

If we translate into the ‚Äúlanguage‚Äù of other examples, then for every 100 rubles a client will receive the corresponding coefficient of miles. A distinctive feature is the rounding process. When buying in a store for 299 rubles, I should get 5 miles (by analogy with other examples), but in fact only 4 miles will be credited to the account.
The sum is divided by one hundred, rounded down to a whole number, and only then multiplied by a factor.

Peculiarity of rounding in TCS Bank:
  1. 299/100 = 2.99
  2. 2.99 = 2
  3. 2 * 2 = 4
  4. 4 miles earned

This is the kind of mathematics that banks use.
