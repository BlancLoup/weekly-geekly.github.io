Sony Ericsson Xperia Neo video review

Official sales of the Sony Ericsson Xperia Neo smartphone started in Russia yesterday. The device is very interesting because of its compact design and filling, which is almost identical to the SE Xperia Arc . The difference lies in the diagonal of the screen - 3.7 inches, as well as in the presence of the front camera.



Despite the smaller size of the display, its resolution is 854x480 pixels. Also on board are a Qualcomm processor with a frequency of 1 GHz, an 8.1-megapixel camera with an Exmor R matrix, an HDMI connector and a 1500 mAh battery. Manages the work of all this electronics Android 2.3 .

Another advantage of Neo in comparison with other representatives of the Xperia line is a better location and execution of the buttons. They are pressed much more conveniently than on Arc or Play smartphones. The design of the case is not satisfactory. The device looks stylish, criticism of someone can only cause that shiny plastic.



Summarizing, we can say that the Xperia Neo is a choice for those who want to enjoy the advantages of a productive filling, but at the same time is not ready to carry a 4-inch "brick" with them. Moreover, even the initial price of a smartphone is very attractive - 17,490 p.
