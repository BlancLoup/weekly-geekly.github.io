GiiNii Movit Mini - the first TabletPC running Android

At CES in the United States, GiiNii promises to present a prototype of its device running the Android operating system. Namely, it is a TabletPC (Tablet PC) called Movit Mini .


The device has a 4.3 inch display. You can work as a stylus or with your fingers. Bluetooth, wifi. 256 MB internal memory. Supports microSD flash cards.


More photos can be found here .
To summarize the currently known technical characteristics:
  • 4.3 inch LCD display (480x272px)
  • wifi
  • bluetooth 2.0
  • 256 MB internal memory
  • microSD memory card support
  • camera on the front panel for video calls
  • built-in lithium battery
  • stylus
  • 3.5 headphone jack
  • built-in microphone and speaker
  • pre-installed skype
  • easy access to social networks such as facebook, twitter, myspace, etc.
Other characteristics (for example, about the processor), unfortunately, have not yet been found.

In the near future (namely, by the middle of this year), GiiNii plans to launch Movit Maxx on the market, which will have a 7-inch display.
