I invite everyone to the cinema!

Hello, dear users! I really like going to the cinema and I believe that many of you also often or not very much attend cinemas. For a long time I did not have enough convenient, and most importantly, a quick search for the nearest sessions, both in time and location. In this regard, I decided to make my own website with a schedule of sessions that meets these requirements - kinogeo.ru

The site has the ability to filter the schedule for several films, choose the time and place. Almost all films can immediately watch the trailer. To some available description and reviews from Twitter.

We should also say about the card. It serves as the main filter of the schedule - only those cinemas that are visible there are shown. Using geolocation via the browser or manually, you can specify your current location, then the cinemas will be sorted by distance. Sorting of films is always based on the total number of sessions (in my opinion the most convenient option is the newest and most promoted ones always top).

Currently, the schedule is available for two cities - Moscow and St. Petersburg. The city nearest you is automatically determined by ip. Soon it is planned to add many other cities, as well as a mobile version and the possibility of booking tickets.

Use on health! Ready to answer technical questions on the implementation of both server and client side.
