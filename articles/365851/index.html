Vacuum cleaner manufacturer Hoover has uploaded to the Network 3D models of parts for its devices



Many household devices are thrown away by people after any minor detail has broken. For example, in some models of vacuum cleaners breakage of latches for fastening of filters is sufficient for failure. In general, the failure of a good device due to minor damage occurs quite often.

Hoover, a well-known manufacturer of vacuum cleaners in the home appliance industry, is trying to solve this problem. According to company representatives, a good option here could be the provision of 3D models of various parts for their vacuum cleaners.

These models are available in the public domain, on the Thingiverse website. True, so far there are only models of accessories, and not important details. We are talking about various mounts for batteries, miniature models of vacuum cleaners, holders for flashlights. Solutions from owners of Hoover vacuum cleaners began to appear, these are already more serious and practical spare parts.

Over time, new models will appear in the catalog. The start is very good, and it would be nice if other companies could create their own catalogs of 3D models for accessories and spare parts for their products.
