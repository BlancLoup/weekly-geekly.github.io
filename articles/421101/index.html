Why read books


Recall all your experience in software development and testing. If there is little or no experience, the book will seem too complicated. It will be confusing and you will need time to recover. If you have experience, feel free to open the book and compare it with your projects.
Preface to Lessons Learned In Software Testing (Cem Kaner, 2001)

So with the books. As long as you have no work experience, books will not help you. The knowledge of them will simply have nothing to connect - there is no experience yet. And for some reason, recipes from books will not work. Colleagues will laugh at you, and you will be disappointed in the books, because they have a solid "naked theory", "inapplicable in practice." I still want to read, but I do not have the time and lack of motivation


Time and motives can be found.


Read in transport
20-30 minutes on the bus, while you are going to work - this is surprisingly much, a good book can be mastered in a few weeks.


Audiobooks
Books for managers can not read, but listen, because they usually have little diagrams and many words. For example, driving a bicycle / car.


Read in the morning 10‚Äì15 minutes
In the morning, no one has distracted. Take a book, tea and read 15 minutes. It's a lot.


Do not read boring, stupid and uninteresting
If the book does not go, then why torture yourself?


Read long
Reading a good smart book of the year is normal. Postpone the book and return to it later - why not?


Work with the list of references
Have a list of books to read? It is not necessary to take the first one from the list. You can view the list and take what is interesting now.


Authoritative opinion
Who do you think is cool? Want to be like him? Find out what books he reads and read the same. There will be a reason to talk with someone who you think is cool.


I also want my colleagues to read more.

In our corporate social network, people write reviews on articles and books. At the end of the recall, a call is sent to three more colleagues. It is very difficult to ignore such a challenge, and again you have to read!


Book clubs
Read is not easy. And if you have a life besides work, then it is more difficult. We do not forget about laziness, permanent deadlines and ‚ÄúI will write down my list now, then I will read it‚Äù. Yeah. This is all so we are too. Therefore, we suggest making a small book club:


  1. You take a few colleagues.
  2. Choose a book.
  3. You book a periodic meeting in the calendar for 30 minutes for each week.
  4. Agree with colleagues on how much you read (‚Äúread one chapter per week‚Äù).
  5. At each meeting, discuss what you read. Sometimes the discussion will move away from the book - you need to follow this and return to the topic of the meeting.

It helps not to be lazy, because you undertake to ‚Äúread this piece before September 30‚Äù. For example, my colleagues and I read Lessons Learned In Software Testing. Out of one and a half years.


You can also ...


Make a library


For this you need:


  1. Think of how to keep records of books. Who took the book, from whom on hand, who passed.
    Options: board in trillo, guglotablet or any other convenient tool.
    This is how our library looked in Trello:

    "


  2. Come up with the rules.
    We have these: the book can be taken for 2 months, then renewed for one more (by writing to the librarian). Books you can take as much as you can carry. To take - you need to be noted on a special sheet in the library. To hand over - put the book in a special place, stick a name sticker on it.





  3. Agree on payment of books at the expense of the company. Most likely, with a manager or office manager.

    Answer the questions: how much will you spend, do you need to keep records, how will you keep track of books. We order up to 8 books per month, it takes up to 5 thousand rubles. We order in large Russian online stores directly to the office.

    To achieve answers to the questions: at whose expense will the books be and if yours, how will they be compensated.


  4. Agree with the authorities that you will spend time on the library (if necessary).


  5. Find a good place in sight so that people walk past. Ideal if people often stand or fit the place (cooler, stand-up area).


  6. Make a list of 10 books you want to order in the near future.
    How to add to the list: listen to reports at conferences, chat with colleagues, google ‚Äútop 100 books for testers‚Äù.

    An example of a list with beautiful books will be below.


  7. Order books, wait, put on the shelf.


  8. Write somewhere an announcement that came a new book, come and take it.
