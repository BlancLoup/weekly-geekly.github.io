Celebration programmer's day



On September 13, a meeting of the Moscow User Group will be held, under which Microsoft will congratulate programmers on their professional holiday. There will also be legendary speakers from the Patterns & Practices team who came to Moscow at the P & P Summit . The team really wants to talk with the local community and is ready to meet with us and discuss key issues of application architecture.


Event Plan:
  • Announcements (19:00 - 19:15)
  • What is Microsoft Patterns & Practices? (7:15 pm - 7:30 pm)
  • Microsoft Enterprise Library (7:30 pm - 7:50 pm)
  • Designing scalable cloud applications (19:50 - 20:10)
  • Creating flexible, modular applications with Prism (20:10 - 20:30)
  • Questions and Answers (20:30 - 20:45)
  • Tea party (20: 45-21: 15)

Speakers:
  • Gregory Melnik, PhD, Senior Program Manager at Microsoft Patterns & Practices
  • Euhinjo Pace, Senior Program Manager at Microsoft Patterns & Practices
  • Chris Kaiser, Program Manager at Microsoft Patterns & Practices
