The first photos of Ice Cream Sandwich



Yesterday, the world's first photos of the new OS Ice Cream Sandwich appeared on two sites at once (Android Police and RootzWiki).
Among the notable changes in the interface:
- Android changed the color of the UI, from green to blue.
- For the first time since Eclair, the notification panel has been redesigned.
- The camera has a mode "Panorama".
- Changed the design of the application GMail, to fit the new design of Ice Cream Sandwich. I understand GMail, too, "turn blue."
- Fully redone launcher and a list of installed programs. One of the photos shows something similar to the task manager.
- In general, the guys with Android Police and RootzWiki note that the changes mainly affected the UI and therefore there is an assumption that ICS will be available for older phones.
