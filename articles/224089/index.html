DevConf 2014: Piracy and other threats for Android applications. How to defend?


The problem of piracy is one of the topics discussed at DevConf :: Mobi .

Almost anyone who laid out applications on various markets, especially if the application is paid or contains In-App purchases, probably faced with the fact that his application was broken, unscrewed the licensing system, made clones and inserted their ads, or even collected malware based on it .



This topic is devoted to the report of Ivan Kinash - co-founder of the company Licel.
The report will tell what threats are relevant at the moment, and what they lead to.
The existing methods of protection will be analyzed and we will find out how good they are.
From the experience of the speaker: there are many tips that need to be taken into account when developing applications for Android.
