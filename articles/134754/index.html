Google Android Development Tutorial


Wanting to attract even more developers, Google decided to organize independently a special program for teaching programming under its mobile platform.

The tutorial is called Android Training and is already available to those who wish. I must say that the range of program themes is quite different: starting from the basics, such as working with Layout - then such topics as working with audio, photos, battery performance optimization are considered - ending with the issues of monetization of the application. Articles, of course, are accompanied by infographics and code examples, and the resource itself, as the Android Team promises, will be developed and supplemented.

It seems that the translation of the Android Training program into Russian can become one of the best tutorials for developing for Android.
