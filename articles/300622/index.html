Zynga mobile gaming profits continue to grow - App Store main source of revenue

The report Zynga mentioned that Facebook has ceased to be the main platform for the company's projects - now the place of the social network is occupied by the App Store. The total daily active audience of players has grown by 7% over the last quarter.
