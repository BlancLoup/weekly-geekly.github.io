The court for the second time blocked the library "Flibusta"

Once a little


The lawyers of the Association for the Protection of Rights on the Internet (AZAPI) have hard work. In June 2015, they secured the blockade of the non-commercial online library Flibusta.net through Roskomnadzor. Now they repeated the procedure and, according to the anti-piracy law, have already achieved a life-long blocking of the resource . On the decision today announced the press service of the court.

The Roskomnadzor blocking is not technically different from the eternal blocking under the anti-piracy law - except that it can be removed legally, AZAPI CEO Maxim Ryabyko, who represented the interests of the plaintiff-copyright owner - Exmo publishing house, explained .

Now we have to complete the case with the Flibusta.is and Flibusta.me mirrors (they have decided to block them for the first time), then figure out how to remove the Flibusta chatbot in Telegram (Ryabyko says that he convinced Telegram developers to solve the problem). Then repeat the same procedure with hundreds of other libraries on the Internet.

In general, the work is enough for a long time. Rights holders do not particularly care that access to blocked sites is available through Tor and other means.
