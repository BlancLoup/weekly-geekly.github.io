Spb Day in St. Petersburg



If you are an owner of the Pocket PC and Smartphone and are actively using it, then you may be interested in a free conference in the format of round tables, which will be held on August 9, 2008 under the auspices of Spb Software House .

I think this is a great opportunity to meet like-minded people, find partners, and together with the developers of ‚ÄúSpb ...‚Äù discuss ideas for new programs and offer them interesting and unrealized functions for old applications. Who knows?..

The conference will be held near the city center at the metro station Kirovsky Zavod, ul. Revival d 4.


Description of the round tables:

1. System utilities
This round table will discuss various system utilities that improve and correct the basic functionality of Pocket PC and Smartphone. Recommended for users of Spb Pocket Plus, Battery Pack, WisBar, VITO ButtonMapper and other system programs. This round table will be held by the Spb Pocket Plus team.

2. Mobile Internet
This round table will discuss the use of the Internet on a Pocket PC and Smartphone (mobile TV, radio, reading mail, browsing websites, online games, IM, etc.). This roundtable will feature a new product from Spb Software House.

3. Usability programs for mobile devices
There will be a discussion on the ease of use of programs for Windows Mobile, and the interface of the platform itself. This round table will be held by the Spb Mobile Shell team.

4. Mobile payments
This roundtable will discuss the possibility of making purchases using a Pocket PC and Smartphone. Representatives of Spb Software House will talk about the current activities of the company in this direction.

5. Mobile TV
At this round table will be discussed applications for watching television. Representatives of Spb Software House will tell about some new projects of the company. We are interested to know your opinion and your experience of using Windows Mobile devices for viewing TV channels.

6. Navigation, Travel
This roundtable will discuss applications for travelers and tourists. The round table will be conducted by developers of programs such as Spb Traveler, Spb Weather and Spb Time.

7. Windows Mobile vs other platforms
This roundtable will discuss the pros and cons of the Windows Mobile platform and its comparison with other platforms.

8. Games on Pocket PC
This roundtable will discuss games on Pocket PC and Smartphone. Representatives of Spb Software House will tell about the new game project of the company and demonstrate the latest developments in this direction. Also in the course of this round table the championship in online games on Pocket PC will be held.

9. "Open Topic"
Open discussion of the ideas of various programs for Pocket PC and Smartphone. All that is not included in other topics.

All the details and registration on the website of the organizer .
