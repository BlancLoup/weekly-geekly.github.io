Non-Commercial Projects Support Program - Voting

On November 15, the Clodo.ru project announced a campaign to support social and non-commercial projects . We received 46 grant applications. After reviewing the provided questionnaires, we selected three projects that fully comply with the conditions of the competition.

Until December 20 will be held a public vote. According to the results of the voting, a project will be identified that will be able to use the services of the hosting project clodo.ru at no cost for one year.

There are three projects at the finish line:

AgiliaLinux is a freeware operating system, a distribution of the Linux operating system. Developed by the MOPSLinux community after the discontinuation of the distribution by the Network Research and Production Association, which has the rights to MOPSLinux. Distributed under the GNU license. The main principles that guide us are the ease of installation and mastering of the system as well as the selection of the most stable programs.

phpMyEngine is a free open source website engine distributed under the GPL v.3 license. The project is written in PHP version 5.3, for the separation of the presentation used Smarty template engine. For data storage, the default is MongoDB, but thanks to the modular architecture, it is possible to use other NoSQL solutions without changing the source code. phpMyEngine is a great tool for creating corporate websites, online newspapers, social networks and other websites.
