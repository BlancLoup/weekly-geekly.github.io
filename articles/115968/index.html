Domainer dominates domains

We did what every webmaster lacks - the replacement of text files with information on domains!
Domener from Tikno allows you to store all the necessary information on domains, admin panels and databases in one place under a strong password. Is free.




We announced Domaerner beforehand, but we delayed the release - we wanted to add the necessary features and achieve work without bugs. A stable beta version is now available for download. The program is actively being finalized and supplemented with new features.

We tried to make everything as simple and convenient as possible:

A small but positive manual at the start.

After entering the domain name, the domainer immediately loads the information when the domain expires. For 2 weeks, he will notify you about the need to extend the domain.

You can enter information on FTP, databases, admin panels, hosting and registrar.

It is possible to copy in one click any field or a whole block. Blocks can be cloned and renamed, and also can be deleted.

The program can be zaparolit by clicking on the lock. A few minutes after the inactivity of the domainer will be blocked. Each time you start or open after blocking, the domainer will ask for a password.

In general, everything. Hope you enjoy it ...
