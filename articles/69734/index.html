Karma indicator



Designer Ahmad Bittar focused not on form, but on content. In his Gaia Phone concept, the main thing is the built-in puzomerka. It is assumed that the owner of Gaia is a social network user. The higher the number of received-sent messages, posts and kamentov, the brighter the backlight of the phone.

Well, actually, what is bad. Carelessly placed on everyone's mind, the indicator of karma, blinking like a Christmas tree, will highlight the status, authority, and also the young age of the owner.
