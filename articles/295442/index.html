How professionals work. Mikhail Sokolov, CEO OneTwoTrip

The heading "How Professionals Work" continues to acquaint the readers of "Megamind" with the working habits of various specialists in the IT market.

Mikhail Sokolov is the CEO of the OneTwoTrip Travel Angecy LLP group of companies, or simply OneTwoTrip .

Mikhail joined the company in August 2013 as a managing director, but very soon, in September 2014, he began to act as the operations director responsible for business processes and development.

Actually, before the change of the vector in a tourist startup, Mikhail worked for 7 years in investment banks in Moscow and London, including Deutsche Bank and Barclays, having passed a career path from analyst to director, managed to participate in more than 20 successful transactions, from IPO to M & A .

Mikhail did not abandon the investment even after the end of his banking career, he is quite an active angel investor (early stages), providing financial and expert support to young companies. Michael is focused on finding projects for investment in retail, e-commerce and, directly, information technology (in which the last word is more important than the first).

Mikhail - a graduate of the MEO GUU them. Ordzhonikidze (Honors) and the New York Columbia Business School. In his free time, Mikhail travels and plays sports: tennis, swimming and windsurfing.
