Sony is going to put into operation the Playstation Network and Qriocity by July 6

Sony has emerged with the promise of putting into service the Playstation Network and Qriocity services. Interestingly, the latest access to these services will be the Japanese. Until now, services have been disabled, as previously reported by Sony representatives, the disconnection was made for security reasons, after unknown hackers launched a series of attacks. As a result of these attacks, hackers got a "trophy" in the form of a database of 100 million users of the Playstation Network.
