Bang Bang - all eggs in one toaster



Designer Lo Chi Di when creating the Bang Bang cooker was inspired by toasters and sea sponges from the cartoon about Nemo fish. It turned out cool. Judging by the pictures, the cooked breakfast jumps out of the nests to a tee, like natural toasts.

The water required for cooking is poured into a special pallet stand. The regulator allows you to set the time - from 1 to 15 minutes. And in order not to burn the fingers, getting a hot egg out of the toaster, special tweezers are provided.

In general, IMHO, extremely friendly concept.
