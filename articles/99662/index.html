League of Legends game review or logical continuation of DotA

Already created many multiplayer online games. There are simulators, strategies, RPGs, and ... The list is endless!

But a game that would not take a lot of time, which would allow gathering friends on Skype and an interesting exercise to argue on pressing topics that would fascinate with its rapidly changing world and, finally, in which there would be an interesting and exciting gameplay - this game is not It was until LoL or League of Legends appeared from the creators of DotA.



Description


The creators did not become strongly sprayed, and why, if Dot was so popular, and created an improved version of the famous map for Warcraft. Now there are 2 options for the game - for a team of 3 people and 5.

By accessing the client, you will be in control of your account, where you can analyze game statistics, monitor your level and chat with friends. It is worth mentioning that the game is free, but for money you can buy skins for your favorite characters, that is, the availability of money does not affect the balance of the game, which is very important.

Your account can and should be upgraded. For each game you get experience for each level of skill points and the ability to buy a rune. About this in more detail:

Skills consist of 3 branches and affect the parameters of your future hero. Depending on the skill leveling, your future hero will receive various advantages to his parameters and additions to abilities.



Runes also affect the parameters of your hero. The more of them, the better you are. But let's not dwell on it - you can easily figure it out.

Character selection


So you decided to play. You automatically get into the queue for the game, and as soon as 2 teams with similar levels are selected, you will be taken to the hero selection menu.



Your team is on the left, there you will see the choice of your party members, and you can choose from the heroes that are in the middle of the screen.

After choosing a hero, you need to decide on additional spells that you take with you:



There is also a chat room where you can discuss tactics with the team and give them your opinion on the chosen heroes.

Gameplay


You start at the healing fountain in the best traditions of DotA. There you spend your first savings on clothes. The system of things here is much better thought out than in the same DotA.



After shopping, you choose one of the aisles to the enemy‚Äôs castle and go to war. As you already understood, your goal is to destroy the castle of the second side. In case you are killed during the battle, then you will have to wait a certain time and resurrect again at the fountain at your base.



On the way to the enemy's castle, you will encounter towers that are very painful and are a serious obstacle to victory. And to destroy these towers will help you minions (mobs), which after a certain period of time appear at your base and run towards the enemy's castle, destroying everything in its path.



The graphics, in spite of its cartoon character, are very pleasant. So do not look at the pictures, but go in, download a client for yourself, and try to play this game only once and you are guaranteed pleasure.
