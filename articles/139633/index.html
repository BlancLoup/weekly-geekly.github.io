New game controller will add interactivity in the game



Since the first gaming controller appeared, a lot of time has passed, respectively, a huge number of changes have been made to the controller design. Now such high-tech gaming controllers are being released that one only has to be surprised at the quickness of the developers. Analog controllers at one time created a furor. Then there were all sorts of "gadgets" that added even more interactivity to the gameplay. Developers from the University of Utah have created another conceptual controller, which, according to them, is even more interesting than the devices that existed until now.

The fact is that this device, in addition to the already usual vibration, is capable of stretching the skin on the hands, more precisely, on the fingertips. The idea, of course, unusual, but the developers are confident in its success. They have already expressed the hope that such devices will be used in game consoles of the new generation.
