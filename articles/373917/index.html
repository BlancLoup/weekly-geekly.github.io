Top 3D Shop invites video bloggers



As part of the development of its channel on YouTube, Top 3D Shop announces a set of video bloggers for ongoing cooperation. Our future partners - people who understand 3D printing, 3D scanning or CNC machines, can make interesting videos and want to share knowledge with the audience.

In addition to payment, Top 3D Shop bloggers receive free consumables for reviews. If you live in Moscow or St. Petersburg, then you will have the opportunity to personally explore the latest equipment, make a video about this video, share your impressions with the audience and get money for it - up to 20,000 rubles per video clip.

For video bloggers from other cities it is planned to send consumables, and in the case of stable long-term cooperation - and equipment too.

Examples of successful work:






This is a great chance to share useful knowledge with a larger audience than usual, and to get financial incentive to grow and develop in technical video blogging with us.

If you have a portfolio of successful videos related to YouTube, contact us and get started.

If you do not have a portfolio, but there is a great desire to cooperate - we offer a test task: remove a new interesting video review on any 3D equipment or materials, put it on your YouTube channel and send us.

This should be an original video shot specifically for Top 3D Shop, with unique content and title. In the description of the video should be mentioned Top 3D Shop, hashtags # 3dbloger and # top3dshop, as well as a link to the site top3dshop.ru . The language of all the titles, descriptions of the video and the video itself is exclusively Russian (except for the official names of brands and models).

Successful videos whose authors will become our partners will be paid.
Join now!
You can send your videos to 3d@top3dshop.ru
