Thermal Imager Seek Thermal Compact

Add a review about my little assistant - a mobile thermal imager-prefix Seek Thermal Compact. A thermal imager for smartphones and tablets will help detect and repair heat or cold leaks, notice problems with electrical wiring, see local heating or overheating areas, find prey while hunting, and so on. Seek Thermal managed to create an inexpensive and affordable compact device that connects to a smartphone or tablet, and has the full functionality of ‚Äúadult‚Äù professional models.



The Seek Thermal Compact mobile imager is a small external camera attachment for a smartphone, which includes a micro-bolometric matrix with a special LWIR lens. The imager is designed in the USA and, unlike Chinese fakes, has excellent characteristics.



Main characteristics:
Brand: Seek Thermal
Model: Compact
Thermal sensor resolution: 206 x 156 pixels
FOV viewing angle: 36 degrees
Detection range: Up to 300 meters
Sweep frequency: 9 Hz
Autofocus: Yes



The thermal imager works in the LWIR-range (infrared). Ordinary lenses do not transmit LWIR radiation, so for thermal imaging devices they are produced from germanium (Ge). The microbolometer sensor is a matrix of miniature thin-film thermistors. The infrared radiation collected and focused on the sensor by the lens of the thermal imager heats the elements of the matrix in accordance with the temperature distribution of the observed object. With each switch-on and during continuous operation, regular recalibration occurs with a characteristic click.



Included is a special case for carrying and storing the device. The case protects not only from shocks and falls, but also from moisture.



Inside there is a soft insert that protects the imager from shocks and other effects.



The case is compact, there is a ring for hanging on the key chain. Easy to fit in your pocket.



To adjust the image there is a ring on the lens.



To connect to a smartphone or tablet is a MicroUSB connector (or Lightning for Apple devices).



For smartphones with Type-C, conventional adapters are suitable. I recommend not to save and take the adapter more expensive, because due to the bounce of contacts in cheap models, the thermal imager periodically turns off. Better "perebdet."



To work with the device, you need to download and install the special application ‚ÄúSeek Thermal‚Äù from the App Store or Google Play



Next, we connect Seek Thermal directly to a smartphone or tablet, or through an adapter.



We start the application, give permission to access the camera, select and configure modes for ourselves.



The application has a number of settings, markers, palettes to choose from to increase the contrast of the image, as well as the ability to shoot video.

Application scenarios for the Seek Thermal Compact thermal imager

The problem of home insulation has been taking humanity since the time when our ancestors settled in the caves. We can protect the premises from the effects of cold with our own hands and on the eyes. But how to check how well we did it? You can walk with a candle, study the directions of drafts or fix frost on the walls. And you can conduct thermal imaging inspection of the premises using a mobile thermal imager Seek Thermal, getting a complete picture of the temperature difference in the study area.
