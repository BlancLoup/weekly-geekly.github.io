Open lecture on the basics of Swift from MasterUp



We are glad to announce that on the sixth of October we will start our second Swift course!

This summer we did not sit idly by, but carefully analyzed the experience of teaching a course on objective C for the iOS platform. The first open lecture will be held on October 6 from 19:00 to 21:00 (Kyiv time) in the webinar mode.

In this lecture, we will examine the basics of syntax and write the code that will select from the participants one lucky person who will get access to the subscription block for free!
