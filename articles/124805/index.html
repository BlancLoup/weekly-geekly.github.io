Service for viewing captured images

I have long wanted to write some simple service, tied to the materials published on the site. Yesterday, a rather simple idea came to my mind - to display popular pictures from comments. So, meet: habhab.ru - service viewing zahabrennyh images.

Shows pictures from publications over the past three days. The pictures are sorted by the rating of the comment in which they are present. Pictures from the articles themselves are not shown. with them would simply dump images. But comments are quite another thing - usually a picture in a comment is placed in order to present your thought in an original and effective way. In fact, most of the pictures are used in a humorous context. The site interface does not make sense to describe, I hope, it is understandable without words. The site provides an RSS feed. Everything. Interesting to you.
