RoverBook Neo U100



On the Internet flew the news about the imminent start of sales of ultra-compact laptop RoverBook Neo U100 domestic company Rover Computers . There is no more specific information about this on the company's website, but, for example, quite authoritative Tom's Hardware Guide states that sales started already on the 1st of August, last Friday.

Main characteristics of the device:
- Intel Atom 1.6 GHz processor;
- Intel 945GSE chipset;
- 1 GB of RAM standard DDR2
- hard disk capacity from 80 GB
- a 10.2 "widescreen matrix with a resolution of 1024x600 pixels is used as a screen
- dimensions 260x180x19 mm
- weight - 1.12 kg

The expected price of new items is, again, according to thg.ru, 13,500 rubles.
