40 unusual questions asked at the interview at Apple

Apple is one of the most prestigious companies in the world, it is obvious that getting a job in it is very difficult. Among the questions asked by candidates at the interview, there are both technical and stunning puzzles.



And when it comes to working in Apple stores, applicants are asked a lot of questions, which in most cases are related to the smoothing of conflicts. Some issues require tricky math problems, while others seem simple at first glance, but in reality this is not the case.

  • Interview for the position of Product Design Engineer:


“We have a cup of hot coffee and a small packet of cold milk from the fridge. The temperature in the room is equal to the average temperature of the two liquids. When do you need to add milk to coffee to get the lowest temperature as early as possible (at the beginning, in the middle or at the end)? ”
