Blabnote: the first pure voice social network

Experts agree that the next breakthrough in the social networking market will occur on a mobile platform. At the same time, it is clear that typing on mobile phones is very difficult, and it is unlikely that this problem will ever be solved. Keyboards are inconvenient in nature: they have no place in the future, as even Bill Gates says .

The future belongs to the interfaces of a new type. The most natural is the voice interface. In this sense, the new English startup Blabnote looks like an exceptionally futuristic project, which is far ahead of its time.

Blabnote (currently closed beta) is the world's first voice-only social network, which is operated via a mobile phone and only by voice. To log in to the service, you just need to call a special number from your phone. The system recognizes the number and logs in automatically.



All teams in the service are entered by voice, including the creation of new collective blogs (communities), the addition and removal of friends, reading new messages and much more - an almost complete range of standard social network. To recognize a command set, a fantastic speech recognition system is not required at all. Quite enough of those developments that are available today. Recognizing a voice for translation into text is also not required, because messages can be stored simply in sound files.
