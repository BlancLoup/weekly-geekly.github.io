Electric Vacuum Food Marinator
Based on the name, it is not difficult to understand what it is for. This kitchen appliance * produces marinating products for a short period of time, which allows you to save your precious time at times. In our market, this unit appeared relatively recently, but has already become quite popular.



The principle of operation of such a device is simple: due to the vacuum created inside the container, the fibers and pores in the products open, and very quickly begin to absorb the marinade. At the same time, the container rotates, thereby constantly redistributing products, facilitating their mixing. Marinator base is electrically driven, making the container rotate. Meat for kebabs can be marinated in 2-4 cycles of 9 minutes. For vegetables, one such cycle will suffice.



Basically, the marinator is designed to feed a juicy barbecue (and not only) a company of 4-6 people. It is also suitable for preparing a delicious dish of mushrooms, vegetables, fish, poultry, beef, pork, will allow in a short time to prepare Korean dishes with the addition of carrots, tomatoes, zucchini, corn. For impatient and not willing to wait, I think the thing will become indispensable and useful in any case. By the way, due to its property to create a vacuum, the device is ideal for making blanks for the winter.

Portable Solar Grill


This amazing device uses only solar energy, allows you to cook almost anything, from meat to cookies using the sun, while without making fires, without using propane, liquid fuel and other things. Such a device is completely safe, does not cause fires that occur when using gas cylinders and fires. Food on this grill does not burn.

This device is simple, it has curved reflectors polished to a mirror surface, which redirect sunlight to the surface for frying. The food that is being cooked in it is enclosed in a glass vacuum cylinder, which makes it possible to preserve the moisture content of the product and isolate the heat inside the system itself, while the outer side of the container remains cold during cooking. The temperature inside the cooking chamber can reach 200 degrees, even when there is no sunlight.



The portable sunny grill * is specially designed for travelers and for those who like to have picnics in nature.
