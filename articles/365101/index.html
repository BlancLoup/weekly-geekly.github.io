Windows 10 Technical Preview has been launched for Windows Phone-based smartphones.

Today, the Windows 10 Technical Preview has been launched for some Windows Phone-based smartphones.





Update available through Windows Insider for Nokia: Lumia 630 , Lumia 635 , Lumia 636 , Lumia 638 , Lumia 730 , Lumia 830
It was also released a video from Joe Belfiore, where he talks about Windows 10 for smartphones:


The main innovations in the story of Joe Belfiore:


  1. New personalization mode with the installation of images instead of a black background.
  2. Changes in the program list (new sorting options added).
  3. Notification Center Updates: More Switches.
  4. Interactive notifications.
  5. Keyboard and text-to-speech updates.
  6. Brand new universal Office, which will be pre-installed on smartphones and small tablets. Is free.
  7. New mail application.
  8. New calendar application.
  9. Spartan Browser.
  10. New photo app.
  11. New maps application.
  12. New Cortana features.

Microsoft warns:


  • Do not use your primary phone for this. If you have a spare phone, this is an excellent option for installing Technical Preview;
  • Unexpected phone crashes can damage or even delete files, so you should keep backup copies of all data on your computer. Some equipment, such as Bluetooth speakers, may not work, and the software may not be installed or work properly. There may also be problems connecting to your home or corporate network.

The remaining smartphones on Windows Phone 8.X will be able to install Windows 10 TP later, the exact date is not reported.
