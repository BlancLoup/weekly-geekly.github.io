Commodore USA Announces Atom-Based C64 Replica

Commodore USA, LLC and Commodore Licensing BV have released a press release about reaching an agreement that Commodore USA, LLC will release a full line of new keyboard computers "AIO" (All In One).



In particular, the Commodore C64 exact replica has already been announced, which will include an Intel Atom 525 processor with NVIDIA Ion2 chipset, 4 Gigabyte DDR3 RAM, a terabyte hard disk, HDMI, a DVD / CD drive (Blu-ray optional), DVI Dual-link , six USB ports, 802.11n Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and a 6-in-1 card reader.

It is worth noting that the Commodore C64 is still the sales leader among all computer models.
