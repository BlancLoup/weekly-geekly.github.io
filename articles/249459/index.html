Raspberry Pi 2: 4 cores, gigabytes of RAM, six times the performance

Perhaps the price will remain the same - $ 35


Without much preparation, the head of the Raspberry Pi Foundation, Iben Upton, told The Register about the new Raspberry Pi. According to his statement, now it is a relatively full-fledged computer, looking at the characteristics of which do not need to be repeated, "it costs only $ 35."

The main changes of the new model called the Raspberry Pi 2 Model B: Broadcom BCM2836 single-chip system with four ARMv7 Cortex-A7 cores at 900 MHz and Broadcom VideoCore IV 250 MHz, 1 GB of RAM, at least six times more performance than B +, and free windows 10 . Here are the results of issuing the uname -a command for the new board .

Prior to this, since the launch of the Raspberry Pi in February 2012, the BCM2835 chip has always been on all models, which had one single ARM1176JZF-S core with a frequency of 700 MHz. The second revision of Model B and Model B + 512 MB of RAM, all others cost only 256. In the new model, only the processor and memory have changed, the full-sized HDMI, 10/100 Ethernet, CSI and DSI ports, power over MicroUSB and the same remain the same , the form factor is slightly larger than a bank card.

The figure of performance improvement with respect to Model B + obtained using the multi-core benchmark sysbench , that is, using only one stream of the program will not increase productivity as much. But even with the Sun Spider run, the performance increased by a factor of three compared with B +. New power can be useful in embedded systems for image processing, says Upton. There are many enthusiasts who want to create computer vision based on OpenCV. Of course, you can greatly optimize OpenCV to run on the DSP of the first Raspberry Pi, but in the end, the microcomputer simply does not have enough power.
