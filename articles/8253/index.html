Mozilla is going to release a mobile version of Firefox

It seems that the most favorite browser of habranaleniya will soon become available for mobile phones. This was told to the APC magazine by the head of Mozilla , Mitchell Baker.

Dan Warne, an APC journalist, asked Mitchell Baker whether Mozilla plans to release a mobile version of his browser, just like the Norwegian company Opera Software did. The head of Mozilla said that the company is going to release a mobile version of Firefox, but this will not happen in the near future.

The whole difficulty lies in the fact that developers have set themselves a difficult task: the ability of the mobile browser to support all extensions and plug-ins created for the full version of Firefox.
