The NSA will extend its audition program for another 180 days.



If you thought that the US Congress could put an end to the secret programs of listening to everything and everything from the NSA, then you are mistaken. FISC (Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court) continued this program for another 180 days, while the interception program will be related to the collection of information from mobile phones. Moreover, it is possible to continue the program further. In the meantime, the NSA will wiretap mobile phones until the end of November .

It is worth recalling that the US Congress banned the NSA from continuing its audition program, missing the USA Freedom Act , which was signed by Barack Obama on June 2.
