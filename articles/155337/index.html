IDCEE 2012 - the first day

The main scene of the conference was the very relax zone. It was dark in the hall, beautiful gears swirled around the walls, various reports and panels with the participation of investors, businessmen and start-ups alternately took place. Then I got lost in the stands of startups. I will list those with whom I managed to communicate:
