Reports from the past JiraMania: about updates, dependencies and deadlocks


In August, Yandex.Money held a Jiramania meeting . On it, developers and administrators told about the master keys and know-how to work with Jira.


In addition to unhurried conversations over coffee and croissants, Tsymzhitov Racer from ITIVITI shared pepper pain relief patches from the Jira update. After that Vadim Gauzyak from SberTech came on the scene with the results of his research of the deadlock in the agile plugin Jira 7.


But enough intrigue - under the cut you will find both reports.


How to upgrade Jira without pain


The report has a whole encyclopedia of pain points in the Jira update process, including the transition to a new major version. In addition, the driver stops on the incompatibility of add-ons with the new version and on the issues of Jira-tuning.



Dedlock in the agile plugin Jira 7


After discussing the advantages of upgrading to a fresh version of Jira, Vadim Gauzyak came out, spoke about the other side of the process and the sad experience of his team. They just switched to Jira 7.1.1 in the system of 60,000 users and then faced its regular crashes. The report examines in detail the compatibility issues of plug-ins, as well as the sad floating bug of some versions of Jira. If you do not want to step into the deadlock with any update - be sure to look.
