Master IBS Company

In the light of the latter, I would like to write about a positive example: the magistracy of the IBS company, in which I study in the second year. The master prepares IT specialists in several areas of system integration.

There is a lot of information, so I am summarizing it briefly. Those interested can follow the links provided.

About IBS

IBS is one of the largest IT companies in Russia (5th place among IT companies, 1st place among consulting companies, rated Expert RA). Founded in 1992. Main activities: management consulting, business applications, IT outsourcing, IT infrastructure. The holding also includes the software development company Luxsoft. Read more on ITPedia .

Master IBS

The IBS company decided to independently train personnel for themselves in ‚Äúscarce‚Äù IT specialties. The project started in 2006, in 2007 the first intake to the magistracy took place. Training is conducted on the basis of 2 leading technical universities - MIPT and MISiS . Both universities recently received a higher status - a national research university. Each university has a corresponding faculty and departments for specialties.
Education - paid . In 2008, 2 years in Fiztekh cost 210000r. I have no more recent information. IBS provides assistance in obtaining an educational loan for this amount. If the graduate decides to remain in the company after the diploma, he is fully compensated for the loan payments (with interest), i.e. you will simply receive a wage premium for 3 years and slowly pay the loan.
A hostel in Dolgoprudny is provided , the building was handed over last year. He himself did not live there, but the reviews are positive.

Studying proccess

Training is provided by streaming lectures and seminars for groups. Classes are 4 pairs a day from 10:00 to 17:00. Sometimes there are 5 couples or classes on Saturday. Basically, all classes are held in the building of MIPT on m. Tretyakoskaya. We had 1 day a week of classes in the city of Dolgoprudnom in the main buildings of the Physics and Technology Institute. Some lectures were at MISiS on Oktyabrskaya metro station.
Subjects are taught by professors from MEPhI, MSU, GUU, MIREA and other universities, leading (no kidding) IBS specialists. Master classes are held with invited "gurus" not from the company.

My point of view

Most of the courses were very interesting, greatly expanded the IT outlook and accustomed to a systems approach. It is relatively difficult to study, but this is probably because I have already got used to freezing in the 3rd and 4th year :) Alas, I cannot say that I am a very diligent student. At the end of the 1st, 2nd and 3rd semesters, there was a rather stressful research effort.
Master IBS looks very advantageous compared to a regular high school. This is especially true for consultants. Now the institutes have neither material base, nor courses, nor teachers to read, for example, ERP-systems. However, like any Windows Server or Cisco.
It will be especially interesting to study here for those who, having received a bachelor's degree or specialist, have not yet mastered the IT profession and do not know where to go next.

- You need to take a big loan or pay hard.
