Mobile Professional Days in Kharkov

November 19, Kharkov will host the Mobile Professional Days conference dedicated to the creation of modern mobile applications.



Mobile Professional Days is a specialized conference for those who are professionally engaged in the development of mobile applications, for beginners who are only planning to develop in this field, as well as for all those who are interested in modern developments for mobile devices.


About what?


The conference program participants will try to consider the most relevant topics in the field of mobile development. The invited speakers will talk about the features of developing applications for Android, iOS, WP7 and Bada.

Special attention will be paid to cross-platform technologies. They will not forget about innovative development and testing methodologies, usability, 2D and 3D technologies.

For whom?


The event is designed both for practitioners and those who are beginning to be interested in the creation of modern mobile applications. Mobile Professional Days opens its doors to:
  • programmers;
  • project managers;
  • designers and usability specialists;
  • QA Specialists;
  • students and teachers.
To participate in the conference you need to register .
The cost of participation is 200 UAH. (~ 800 rub .), For students 100 UAH. (~ 400 rub .).
