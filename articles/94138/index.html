Mobile solar charger


Leafing through the articles of Habr once again, I found a topic about the autonomous charger for the phone habrahabr.ru/blogs/DIY/94121 , and immediately thought that my friends had presented a great device for the last birthday: Solar Mobile Charger P1100F for a mobile phone, digital camera or mp3 player. I didn‚Äôt have to do any device reviews, but I really liked it, so I decided to tell the public about it.
It is designed to convert solar energy into electrical energy, which accumulates in the built-in lithium-ion battery with a capacity of 1500-2600mA / h.
P1100F requires only the presence of sunlight, and for cloudy days a power adapter is included in the kit, which allows you to charge the built-in battery from the mains at any time of the day.


PACKAGE


1. The box is small, 15x10 cm, and even closes with small magnets)



2. Instruction

Made on one small sheet (half A4, folded vertical - 105x297mm), on which information in English and on the reverse side in Chinese is compactly placed.

3. A set of adapters

With their help, you can connect various devices. The manual says that it can be a mobile phone, digital camera, mp3 player, mp4 player and other digital products.


I do not know the connectors of all the devices, but among the available ones I found out for sony ericson, nokia of the old sample. For his old siemens cx-75 and camera pentax s12 did not find suitable adapters. I would like to believe that they can be obtained at retail in some specialized store, but so far it has not been possible to tackle this issue.

4. The main functional component - the panel of collecting solar energy

The main part, of course, is occupied by photocells. The body is made of high quality, made of thin metal. The size of the panel 120x73x10mm



To monitor the status there is an indicator, and two connectors for connection: 5V power supply and output - 5.5V. Output current: 500-800mA.
During the charge of the internal battery, the indicator lights up and lights up green, regardless of the source of charge: the sun, USB or the AC adapter.
When the device was connected and its charge, the display did not react at all, and after the process was completed, it lights up red.


The declared time of a full charge of the built-in battery from the sun: 10-14 hours. Approximately these figures correspond to reality and, I think that everything depends on the amount of light falling on the panel. I used the houses on the windowsill, but the direct sunlight almost did not reach because of the large number of trees.

5. Network adapter and USB cable

Most liked the fact that the internal battery can be charged from the network or from the USB port of the computer, and the accumulated charge to use when it is convenient. If you look at the image of the cable, you can see that, on the one hand, it is USB, and on the other, a mini-USB connector. mini-USB connects to the input panel for charging, and USB can be connected to a network adapter, or directly to a computer port. As it turned out, the cable can be used to directly connect my 6300 to a laptop, but it will work only for data transfer - the phone will not charge! This flexibility is very convenient and allows you to get rid of unnecessary wires and adapters in the bag, and the device itself acts as a buffer for charging many modern devices.
