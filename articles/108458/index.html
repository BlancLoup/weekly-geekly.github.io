Penguins Without Borders: How the Character was Created for an Internet Provider

The Internet in Vladivostok is strongly associated with the three largest local telecommunications companies: Dalsvyaz, Alliance Telecom and Vladlink. For fifteen years of development of services, Internet providers have managed to master, it seems, all areas of the city. And the struggle for new subscribers has grown into competition for already connected.

The methods are used not only pricing and service, but also advertising. Dalsvyaz, for example, created and powerfully pumped the Disly bear cub. At some point, it was found that this character is present almost everywhere. ‚ÄúAlliance‚Äù contrasts advertising budgets of Dalsvyaz with ingenious advertising messages and a kind of graphic creatif.

Vladlink was lagging behind its competitors in this respect. But this summer, the company turned to the Black Fox studio. To us that is.
Initially, the order was received for the redesign of the internal Internet subscriber service system (CCIS). Developing a design concept, we proposed a series of character icons, a different one for each section of ICS. At first they were little funny robots.
The customer liked the idea. We even managed to draw two dozen sketches and paint some. And suddenly ... the customer changes the task.



So, the task was set - to develop an image of the character of the company. And it must be a penguin.
Characteristics have been designated such:
  • Agile and emotional
  • Not fat, but not thin.
  • Painted in corporate colors (orange and blue)
  • Unlike the Linux Mascot - Tux Penguin
It was proposed to take emperor penguins as a basis.
