Reading Wikipedia offline

Because of this, it is necessary not to close the browser. Naturally, this is inconvenient, it is impossible to see which articles I have already read, to follow the update, etc. As well as the opportunity to open / read the article for the second and subsequent times. Do you understand? How convenient it would be to load a list of words of interest into the program and go about your business while the client is downloading articles.
