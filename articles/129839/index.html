Steve Jobs is dead

The media reported on the death of Steve Jobs, former CEO of Apple. You can have different attitudes towards Apple and Steve, but you cannot deny that this is a significant figure in the IT industry that has created many trends. Requiescat in pace, Steve.
Let me give you the translation of the message on the official Apple website:
Apple lost its fantastic and inventive genius, and the world lost a wonderful man. Those of us who had the good fortune to know and work closely with Steve lost a dear friend and an inspired mentor. Steve left behind a company that only he could create, so his spirit will forever remain in the heart of the company.
The source is Apple .
In Apple stores around the world, all Apple logos have not been highlighted.

Those who want to share their empathy can send their thoughts and condolences to rememberingsteve@apple.com . Perhaps some will be transferred to Steve's family.
A good video from CNET, which briefly visually retells Steve 's career.
The memories of his colleagues in the IT-shop below:

Larry Page, the CEO of Google, offered his condolences to Steve's family and friends, and admitted that Steve had always been a source of inspiration for him.
Sergey Brin, co-founder of Google, agrees with his friend Larry that Steve became a source of inspiration for him and offered condolences to his family and friends.
Microsoft co-founder Bill Gates also offered his condolences from himself and his wife Melinda and said that he would miss Steve a lot.
Mark Zuckerberg thanked Steve for being a mentor to Mark and becoming a friend.
Robert Scoble, one of the leaders of Rackspace, arrived at Apple Headquarters, from where he leads a photo report. Even the sky itself over Apple HQ mourns Steve's care:

Barack Obama and his wife Michelle offered their condolences to Steve's family. The US President noted that Steve is one of the greatest American innovators.
Meg Whitman, who replaced Leo Apotheker as CEO of HP, said that Steve will remain in people's memory for his innovations, as well as for the inspiration given to the world.
