Results of the NORD industrial design competition

In October of this year, JSC "NORD" , a manufacturer of complex household appliances,
announced an industrial design competition in 2 categories (refrigerator and a free-standing stove)
with the main prize - a Mac Pro Cinema HD Display workstation.
91 participants from Ukraine, Russia, Belarus, Moldova and Uzbekistan are registered in the competition.
27 participants submitted competition projects in one or two categories at once.
Almost all projects were completed at a very high level and already at the stage of formation


the shortlist (link) the jury was faced with the difficulty of the choice.
November 18 announced the winners.
Participants who received the first prize, prepared a comprehensive draft of the refrigerator and stove.
By awarding a prize in one of the categories, the jury gives high marks to both projects.

Category "Refrigerator"



winner - Andrey Bondarenko, Nikolaev, Ukraine.



second prize - Roman Kovbasyuk, Sumy, Ukraine.


second prize - Ilya Melekhov, Moscow, Russian Federation.



Category "Cooker"



winner - Stepanov Oleg, Odessa, Ukraine.


Second prize - Denis Belmas, Lugansk, Ukraine.


Second prize - Andrey Vostrikov, Moscow, Russian Federation.



The jury of the competition - representatives of the Research Institute of Domestic Engineering UkrNIIBytMash, practicing designers. Chairman of the jury - Member of the Union of Designers of Ukraine, DonNASA teacher Scherbak N.G.

You can see the works of the short-list and winners at www.nord.biz.ua.
Congratulations to the winners and we wish victories and achievements of new heights to all participants!
Organizers:
JSC NORD, Donetsk, Ukraine
in collaboration with Design Bureau KARANDASH, Kiev
