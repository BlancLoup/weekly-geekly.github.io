IT Championship "Racing Heroes", or the first project LANIT on the military ground "Alabino"

Quite by chance we in LANIT found out about the IT Championship of the Heroes Race at the Alabino military training ground and decided to participate in it. In our team of ten fighters, only three had previously participated in such competitions. The rest had little idea what it was. Some, as subsequent events showed, were very, very poorly aware of what was awaiting them!

An exhausting race with a bunch of difficult obstacles. Dirt to the waist, cold water, high fences, rukohody, tanks, barbed wire ... Injuries (two people had a leg cramped, one beat off a knee) and a sharp decline of strength ... It all happened - but it didn't matter on the track: we did not stop, breathed in the back of competitors and "ate" them one by one!
