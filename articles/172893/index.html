Webinar "Testing Applications with Microsoft Visual Studio Test Professional 2012 and Team Foundation Server 2012"

March 20, 2012 at 11:00 Moscow time, we invite everyone to take part in the webinar: "Testing applications with Microsoft Visual Studio Test Professional 2012 and Team Foundation Server 2012" .

The webinar is aimed at students who are interested in building an effective and transparent application testing process, are at the stage of choosing a methodology or tool for interacting with the team.

In addition, the event will be interesting for managers who are already working with VS Test Professional and Team Foundation Sever (TFS) and want to learn more about the capabilities of products and approaches to their use.

During the presentation the following questions will be raised:
- Overview of the Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) platform.
- TFS and Test Professional interaction.
- Planning tests.
- Manual testing, testing by script.
- Research tests.
- Record new test scenarios.
- Autodocumentation and error tracking.

Visual Studio Test Professional 2012 is a product for test engineers, business analysts, product managers and other stakeholders who need the tools to work together from different groups, but do not need a full-fledged integrated development environment.

Team members can use integrated testing and product management tools to ensure quality throughout the development process. Visual Studio Test Professional 2012 also supports easy requirements definitions and continuous customer feedback, allowing you to track across these processes.

Team Foundation Server 2012 is the interoperability platform underlying Microsoft's application lifecycle management (ALM) solution.

With TFS, your team can use tools to track participants' actions and work items such as requirements, tasks, errors, source code, builds, and test results.

The webinar is conducted by Alexander Yakovlev, an expert on the organization of development processes, Microsoft.

The duration of the webinar is 1 hour.
