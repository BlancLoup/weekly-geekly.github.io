MATR–ÅSHKA - modular unmanned commercial vehicles



Just recently , the concept of modular electric transport was presented by the Russian company Volgabus , which is engaged in the production of buses. To be more precise, the development is a subsidiary of BMG (Bakulin Motors Group) created by the owner of Volgabus Alexey Bakulin. But despite the status of "concept", the development of the project is in full swing and tests are already underway on the territory of Skolkovo.
Let's explore this concept together.


The modular concept offers us three options for its use:



But the passenger should go first to the series, apparently as the most theoretically claimed.
The transport consists of three modules, the extreme ones contain all the stuffing, from batteries with electric motors to autopilot, yes, yes, you read everything right, everything is in the trend of this season. The central module performs only a cargo function.



This layout allows you to replace the modules in a very short time, for example, to replace the module with dead batteries and to send the transport as soon as possible to the next route.

Autopilot suggests the possibility of round-the-clock use, as well as safety and autonomy. At the same time, dispatch centers will be created to monitor the movement of vehicles, the health of its work, as well as communication with passengers.
