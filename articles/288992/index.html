How does the online store of chandeliers, LEDs and lamps

This application article will allow you to get an idea about the work of online stores of lighting and lighting equipment. It is aimed at startups who are interested in this line of business from the point of view of business logic and implementation techniques. The article is written based on the experience of developing online stores, which started in 2008, and are now among the leaders in terms of visibility of sites in Yandex search results and presence in contextual advertising in Moscow and Moscow Region.

- Individuals who buy light to the apartment or to the country;
- Legal entities for the office;

Average check, margin and cost of goods


All calculations are in rubles at 2014 prices. The margin is 30-60%, minus logistics, accounting and marketing. The average price of a check is 10 thousand rubles, this includes the lamp itself, including related consumables (energy-saving light bulbs, ceiling lamps) and delivery. Orders may be 100-200 thousand rubles. So maybe, for example, if you need lamps, poles, lights to illuminate the entire perimeter. In this case, the volume of the order will be the whole car, plus the light on 30-40 thousand. The order may be on several chandeliers, but expensive.

Seasonal business model


Light is a seasonal commodity. In autumn there is a peak, from September to the end of December, there is an active sales season. There are many wholesale and retail deals. Even with a small advertising budget, you can get a good profit. From January to March there is a slight rise, then a time of stagnation. Thus, it turns out that in the fall a huge company is needed, which should ensure large volumes of sales. The team should have a sufficient number of logisticians, more precisely couriers, managers, an expanded marketing department, which will increase all kinds of advertising. It turns out that in one period of time we need a huge department, and at another stage we need a small department. I think many are faced with such problems. This situation is solved by dismissal. The main thing - it does not inflate the state, if you inflated the state, get too big expenses. It is always necessary to keep the staff at a minimum, and only in the case when employees are overloaded by 200%, then hire additional people.

Seasonal personnel policy


To neutralize seasonal fluctuations, large companies usually increase their staff by the end of the summer, and reduce it in the spring. As a rule, this applies to the departments of marketing and logistics. It is necessary to draw up an employment contract at the stage of recruitment in order to reduce the number of employees by the time of a decline in demand. When applying for a job, it is better to immediately agree on piecework wages, and on urgent labor relations.

Company structure for a startup


With a turnover of 2 million or 6 orders a day, a team of 4 people is required:
- The head, who controls all, is looking for reliable suppliers, negotiates discounts, monitors SEO and container manager;
- The content worker who places the new product, works with the base, finds errors, etc., updates the balances and prices. Balances change every day, prices also change every day due to the dollar rate;
- Well-savvy manager who will answer calls, control the purchase of products and logistics;
- Logistics department, 4 drivers are required here, but third-party organization can also be used.

From the outside it may seem simple, but maybe the situation is that one driver is sick, the other is missing with money, the third is returning the marriage, etc. It happens that there are a lot of cases, my head is spinning, but it is quite possible to cope.



Medium company structure


Major players have up to 120 orders per day:
- CEO. Agrees with suppliers about discounts, is responsible for sales and financial performance, if there is no financial director. All other key employees are subordinate to him;
- Marketer. Supervises website developers, contextual and display advertising, SEO-company or full-time employee, copywriters, content managers, etc .;
- Accountant. Responsible for the absence of tax claims;
- Head of Logistics Department. Prepares a plan for shipment of goods from suppliers and delivery to customers;
- Client manager. Accepts orders from customers and informs customers about the delivery time of orders, and also works with reclamations.

Suppliers and cross docking


Cross-docking (eng. Cross - directly, cross, eng. Dock - dock, loading platform, docking) - the process of receiving and shipment of goods and cargo through the warehouse directly, without placing in the zone of long-term storage. Cross-docking is a set of logistic operations within the supply chain, due to which shipment from the warehouse and delivery of goods as accurately as possible are coordinated in time. As a result, products are delivered in the shortest possible time.


There are several main suppliers from whom the goods are usually purchased. They unload the balances, and the prices, once in one or several days, we put them on our website. As soon as an order is formed with us, we reserve the goods from these suppliers, and ship them, or they ship them directly, to the final consumer. The scheme bought-sold, that is, it is not necessary to immediately have a lot of money in order to start selling lamps. In the case when we first buy the goods from the supplier ourselves, we arrive and pay for the goods we want to buy. Suppliers may have a minimum balance on their account, say 30 thousand, and some 100 thousand, and money is already written off from this balance. But, the majority of suppliers work in the usual way, that is, he arrived, gave his order and paid.
