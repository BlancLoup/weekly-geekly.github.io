Publishing an application to Android Market

Good or bad, but for Android applications there are a large number of different sales markets. On the one hand, we have to publish in several, on the other - the coverage is provided wider. When I thought that it would be time to use alternative markets for my application , I did not find any sensible information. Once again I want to warn you that the post is not for the bigwigs of the market, but only for those who, like me, timidly opens the world of Android applications from the opposite side of the normal user.

Forum w3bsit3-dns.com , dedicated to Android software and devices is the most popular resource in the CIS about Android. Publishing a topic is insanely simple. Go to the general (not thematic, moderators themselves will transfer) section or about the games, or about the programs and click "New Topic". There is an available template that a housewife can fill in.
On the one hand, you can say that this is just a forum, but in fact, if you distribute a free application, then posting it on w3bsit3-dns.com, you will almost automatically receive publications on dozens of other websites dedicated to Android. Of course, for the most part these are scarcely visited resources, but this is really free placement, for which you practically have nothing to do. Recently I was asked how to get to the top in Google Play (my application is in the top of free social). I replied that high marks plus thousands of downloads themselves raise. But before the publication of this article, I thought that perhaps the frequent mentions on the net also had their effect.
The theme was created in early February 2012. For 4 months, the number of downloads from the forum did not even reach a hundred. At the same time, the attendance of the resource after publication has doubled (from 100 unique to 200).
