Project "Solar Eclipse"

Background ...


Have a project idea. Which originated this year, thanks to my memories from childhood, and some changes in my life in the past few months. I was very fond of astronomy, my elder cousin and I were studying it, writing notebooks with information about constellations, planets, stars; they made observations on maps, remembering where any constellations are located, lay on the roof at night, observing the night sky space. It was a good time ...

Now at the expense of the project ...


The main idea is a blog that will bring together a community of people interested in astronomy, and not only. Initially, it was meant that the purposefulness would only be traveling to other planets, but in the end it turned out that the people around me helped to look in a new way, and decided to do something generalized. There will be articles on various topics regarding space. Those. blog site, similar to habr.ru, but at the same time with its subtleties and capabilities, which will certainly increase over time.
