Mistake 2. Use machine translation tools and ignore dictionaries.


Only after you have understood the topic and created your own list of professional terms, you can start writing the text. Do not write the text first in Russian, and even more so do not translate it using machine translation services - prefer dictionaries to them. Otherwise your article will suffer from literalism.

In a phrase composed by a human translator, the noun iOS stands in a preposition and acts as an adjective.
