Lecture 2 of 10. Seven kinds of hypotheses



For rapid growth, Internet companies need to test hypotheses. To systematize experiments and associate team actions with analytical data, you can use hopox . However, he himself does not come up with a hypothesis.

Below we analyze seven types of hypotheses so that it is clear which direction to conduct the experiments.

Today there will be a lot of cases. Immediately to the point.

Experiments can be with attraction channels, sales funnels, monetization, user behavior, product recommendation methods, active customer retention mechanisms, resurrection of abandoned customers, etc.

You can divide the field of experimentation with the help of Dave McClure's Pirate Metrics: Attraction, Activation, Hold, Virality, Income. These five steps describe the path of the user who is able to give maximum value to the business.

1. Hypotheses of attraction

- hypotheses, testing channels of attraction to the product.
We are constantly changing something in the channels of attraction. However, now all actions are tied to the results in numbers. Depending on the channel, actions can vary greatly, but the key metrics for success are the number of impressions, audience reach and number of clicks to the site.
