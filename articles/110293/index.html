The Settlers: Now Online

I want to please fans of the game The Settlers , which is already more than 10 years old!



UbiSoft, together with BlueByte, created the online flash version of The Settlers. She is currently in Beta testing and is free to register.

Of the benefits :
1. She is on a flash: he set up a sawmill at home, progredil her friend, came to work and sold ...
2. Very beautiful graphics
3. Many types of buildings, upgrades and features
4. Full social. network: clans of users, guilds, different chat modes, etc.

Of the minuses :
1. From the checkbox to the checkbox, the transfer of resources at the time of 12/19/2010 is not yet implemented.
2. They have not yet translated into Russian, but those who played Settlers earlier will immediately understand the purpose of each building and what the settler does there. (UPD: There is no Russian translation. And English. The game is only in German .)

So I recommend to go and try it yourself.
