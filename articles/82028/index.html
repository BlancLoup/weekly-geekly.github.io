Meeting Startupers StartupPoint in Moscow (31.Janv), St. Petersburg and in your city

This Sunday (January 31) StartupPoint will host the 15th meeting of startups and investors - a meeting where initiative developers can present the best aspects of their projects, get expert evaluation and even funding (as received by Minibanda, EasyFinance, Turometr and others). Registration is open.

The second good news is that we are starting to organize meetings in the regions of our country. We invite all regional developers to speak out for holding the next meeting in your city. If you want to participate or help with the organization of the meeting, please write in the comments the name of your city.
