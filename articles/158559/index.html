How do you have forbidden content on your site and not get into the registry of locks?



Inside this article there are reflections on how to completely circumvent the new law on the prohibition of information and not get the whole site to the list of blocked resources.

Many people know about such an anonymous network as I2P, content inside it cannot be moderated or banned.

Please note, I do not propose to transfer the entire site to the I2P network and eliminate the usual one, before throwing a tomato at me to criticize my opinion, please read the article.

I repeat, but I will mention:

The main advantages and disadvantages of I2P:

+ Fault tolerance
+ High anonymity (the network was created taking into account the fact that all nodes are under the control of the government)
+ Inability to determine the physical location of the site
+ Advanced onion routing (dopilenny version of the TOR)
+ Using different encryption methods and their periodic alternation

- There is only one access to the large Internet - through a network node located in Germany
- Slow network performance *

And what can be done to ensure that your site does not get into the registry and continue to function normally and at the same time not remove content from it that does not please the government?

The answer is simple - transfer part of the site or the entire site to the I2P network.

At this point, you may object and say - "Tor is better, he also has concealment services!"
- Yes, there is, but the Tor network was originally created as a chain of proxy servers and not the hidden Internet, the principle of networks and the place of their use is different.

How to transfer parts of articles that are deleted under the threat of blocking

To transfer a site to an I2P network, you will need to install an official network client from http://www.i2p2.de/index_en.html

Then you will have to create a tunnel for the site and allow the web server to communicate locally.

Please note that the I2P client can not be installed on the same server where your site is located.
Example: When entering this address, you can access lurkmore through the tunnel I created from my computer

And with the help of this service, you can get access to the I2P network resources via the usual Internet: http://i2p.in ( Open )
If the address is not available - the computer is turned off or the home channel is clogged, it is not rubber, unfortunately

To share access (for regular Internet / I2P) you can make a trigger in PHP (or in the server language you use) that you can only access blocked content from a specific IP address (if the client is installed locally for I2P, then 127.0.0.1)

Thus, you will get two advantages:
- Your site will not be blocked on the territory of the Russian Federation
- Content on your site will be available through the I2P network
And those people who want to read it, and I am sure there will be a lot of them, they will be able to download an I2P client and access the resource, or use the i2p.in service to view the content.

Now let's talk about the last item on the list of minuses - Slow I2P network performance

When creating a tunnel, you will be asked to choose the number of "hops" before your service and the fewer hops, the less anonymity and you will be easier to detect but faster to work.
