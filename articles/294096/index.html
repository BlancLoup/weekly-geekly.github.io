New partners of Innopolis are Acronis and Kazan IT Park

The young city Innopolis continues to attract partners. ‚ÄúWe set ourselves the task for Innopolis to become one of the drivers of the development of the high-tech industry and IT in Russia,‚Äù said city mayor Yegor Ivanov in an interview . The city should become an effective platform for interaction between science, education and business.

Russian developer of cloud services Acronis will open a cloud research and development center for data storage on the territory of Innopolis. The company entered into a bilateral agreement with JSC "SEZ" Innopolis. "

The agreement will allow Acronis to take a new step in working on a system with geo-distributed storage, optimized mobile access and data protection. The company began working on the system before the negotiations on cooperation.

On August 28, Acronis division appeared in Kazan IT Park.

In the first half of 2015, the company's total ruble revenue from sales in all areas increased by 51% compared with the same period in 2014, according to the company. The highest growth was shown by sales of cloud products and virtualization solutions. Acronis revenue from sales of cloud products increased by 573%, and revenue from virtualization solutions increased by 337%. The maximum volume of Russian sales (18%) falls on the telecommunications industry, reports Firrma.

IT Park launched a business incubator and its own educational programs at Innopolis University. Students will be able to learn technologies for developing startups. They will receive infrastructure support from IT Park. Training will be built as a cycle of practical workshops for introducing into the methodology and working with mentors. In addition, students will be able to receive individual recommendations from IT industry experts, reports Firrma.

In Kazan, the IT business incubator has existed since 2011. The residents of the business incubator are 60 projects - 27 in Kazan and 33 in Naberezhnye Chelny. The total amount of investments attracted by startups over four years exceeded 500 million rubles, according to the company.
