Moscow: in public institutions incandescent bulbs will be replaced with energy-saving

After the cessation of the sale of ordinary light bulbs in Europe, such a policy was decided to be resorted to in Moscow, the truth began with the state. institutions.
In the state institutions of Moscow, all incandescent bulbs will gradually be replaced with energy saving ones. The corresponding order has already been signed by Mayor Yury Luzhkov. From October 1, 2009, state institutions and state-financed organizations should purchase only energy-saving lamps and lighting fixtures where these lamps can be used.
