Joker 2016 Release Notes: 100 technical sessions in 2 days

On October 14 and 15, a Joker 2016 Java conference will take place in St. Petersburg. It is already clear that the conference will change notably: not only in scale and form, but also in content.



The most important thing here with all the innovations to keep the atmosphere of Joker. An atmosphere of enthusiasm and benevolent fanaticism, productive holivars and backstage discussions.

Details about how it will all take place this time - under the cut.

100 sessions in 2 days


What is important in the conference? Right, reports. And it is with them that our new challenge is connected, which, perhaps, will eclipse all the past: this time we will hold 50 thematic, purely technical sessions per day .

The plan is as follows:
  • 4 Keynote performances;
  • 48 reports;
  • 48 thematic areas for discussion.

However, the conference is growing up, and more and more often the participants ask us where the reports and case studies from the developer‚Äôs real life are.
