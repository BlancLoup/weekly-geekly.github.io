Deciphered the mechanism of karma

By the end of the experiment, the administration of Habrahabr blocked 7 characters, but the nature and scale of the experiment, it seems, did not solve. In this connection, I apologize to the administration in advance and look forward to understanding: if worlds are being created, then isn't it to investigate them?
