Shares of the company "Yandex" have risen in price after the actions of FAS against Google



At the close of trading on the MICEX exchange, the shares of the domestic company Yandex rose immediately to 6.45%, to 809 rubles per share. The growth driver was the news about the recognition of Google by the FAS as a violator of antitrust laws, Lenta reports .

As for the Nasdaq exchange, here, Yandex shares rose immediately by 7% by the time of opening. The cost of one share on this exchange is now $ 12.09. Google's securities have lost a bit in price - by about 0.64% of the value of the stock at the time of the opening of the exchange. Now the price of one share of Google is 651 dollars.

Recall that today the FAS found the US corporation Google guilty of violating the law "On the protection of competition."
