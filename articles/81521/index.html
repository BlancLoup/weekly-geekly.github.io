Seesmic Look - a new cool twitter client

The well-known company Seesmic , which makes the eponymous popular twitter client, has released Look, a client for the simplest users, that has a beautiful, rich interface, unlike any existing client at present. In addition to the usual functions of Twitter, Look contains links to channels, trends and various interests, so that an unprepared user can click on groups and read the news feed that interests him.



I am convinced that many will find the level of "beautiful" going off-scale. But this is to someone like. As observers have noticed, the appearance of the Seesmic Look and its Playback Mode feature are ideal for displaying pictures on TV.

Seesmic Look with its beautiful features is only available on Windows and uses the full power of Windows 7. Download from here .
