March 30 - 31, SIBUR CHALLENGE in Nizhny Novgorod

Hello!

In a couple of weeks, on March 30-31, we will hold a hackathon in Nizhny Novgorod dedicated to data analysis. The selection of teams will go until March 30, the tasks will need to be solved not abstract, but quite lively - we will provide real company data for this.


Here are specialties whose representatives can take part:

  • Data engineer
  • Data Architect
  • Data scientist
  • Solution architect
  • Front-end developer
  • Back-end developer
  • UX / UI Designer
  • Product owner
  • Scrum master

More information about the tasks and stages - under the cut.

The first stage is already underway right now, from March 1 to March 30, this is a free online educational course, after registering you will receive links to tasks, you can accumulate points and in the process meet other participants if you have not chosen a team yet. Yes, it is important to choose a team, because it is the teams that participate (from 2 to 5 people in each).

We developed the educational program together with AI Today specialists, the tasks are already available in the telegram-bot @siburchallenge_bot . By the way, in the bot you can also check your current balance of bonus points (they can then be exchanged for useful merchandise, additional features (like an extra mentoring hour), or participate in an auction for a super prize.

Points are awarded for registration on the hackathon itself (previously registered = got more points), for completing the entire program, for the data left, and much more.

Full list
  • Up to 500 - for registering on the hakaton site (the earlier the registration date, the more points).
  • Up to 500 - for the registration team (in the same dependence on the date).
  • 100 - for the presentation of #siburchallenge participants in the chat and the information about yourself.
  • 100 - for sending a resume.
  • 100 - for each correct answer after video lessons, and in case of successful completion (75% of correct answers) of the entire educational program - additional points.
  • 100 - for passing the first lesson in the bot.
  • Up to 1500 - for passing the entire program (at least 75% of correct answers) before a certain date: the earlier, the more points.
  • 500 - for participation in the referral program.
  • Up to 300 - for announcements and reviews in social networks.
  • Up to 500 - for attending additional events in front of the hakaton.
  • 100 for feedback.
  • 200 - for found bug or error.


The second stage, March 29, mitap . Here you can already join the right team, if you have not already done so. Communication with company representatives (IT, HR, business units).

The third stage, until March 30, the selection of teams . If in the first two stages you have not joined the teams, then this is the last chance. Either you form a team yourself, or join the existing ones according to the profile you need. Here there will be a number of activities for which they will give points - you need to collect the required number.

The fourth stage, March 30-31, is the hackathon itself. Here your team will need to develop a solution for the problem. You can consult with our experts in the process.

And about the tasks


First, there will be a task about the distribution of vouchers. In a large organization, this is still a big date with a bunch of parameters.

From our side:

  1. Dataset 19 000 applications of employees for the provision of vouchers with analysts for work experience, awards and personal data for receiving benefits, the number of rooms of the sanatorium, the criteria for awarding employees with vouchers.
  2. A business manager of a process that will tell and show everything.

From your side:
A comprehensive solution that will allow a labor decision maker to quickly make decisions on the distribution of these vouchers among employees who have applied for vouchers, to propose solutions for the distribution of vouchers among enterprises and a number fund.

The solution should consist of two parts:

  1. Algorithm based on data analysis.
  2. Interface with data visualization and results of the algorithm and any additional data.

Secondly, the task of the adviser on the production of butadiene (about this we wrote a little here ).
