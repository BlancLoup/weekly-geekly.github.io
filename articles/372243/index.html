In Telegram, you can now change messages after sending

On May 15, 2016 a new version of Telegram 3.9.0 messenger was released, which is used by 52% of Geektimes audience .

In version 3.9.0 for Android and iOS several functions are implemented. First, you can now edit messages after sending (within two days), so that typos and drunken revelations will be less. Editing works in all chat rooms, including group.

To edit on a mobile phone, you need to click on the message and hold it, and then click the "Edit" button. On the desktop - just arrow up.

Edited messages are labeled "Edited".



Another new feature - the interlocutor mentions in a group chat, with the character set @ . Now you can mention any member of the chat, even if he does not have a nickname . When a person or group is mentioned, he / they receive a notification.



The developers added bots to the attachment menu (the most frequently used ones are above) and implemented the list of the most popular interlocutors in the search.



Small changes in the interface have been made: more understandable notifications from messages with stickers and quick sharing buttons to sent messages from bots, from channels and groups.

The client for iOS now remembers the scrolling position after closing the chat and shows the number of unread messages.
