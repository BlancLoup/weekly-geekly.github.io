Another grumble about Yandex Market

This is the second grunt about Yandex Market. The first was inspired by a terrible interface and incomprehensible instructions for connecting.

This grumble concerns the inflexibility of the program and the human factor. I will form it from the correspondence with the market support service. I will not comment on anything. From the conversation, I think everything will be clear.

Letter one. Formulation of the problem.

Situation: I have two stores. One on the domain natropinkah.ru, the other on the subdomain sumki.natropinkah.ru. Legally, they work with one Individual Entrepreneur. They have different price lists.

Your system does not allow you to place a second store, saying that the same company is behind it. This is true, but I do not see any crime in this. These are two different brands, but legally one firm.

But I can‚Äôt specify two price lists and two domains for one store in your system. And this, too, by the way, is somehow wrong. One store can quite easily have two sites, for example, for selling the same goods to different categories of buyers.

But I will not argue - you programmed as programmed. You will not think about everything at the development stage. And after development it is usually lazy to finish something.

Question: But what should I do? Is there a way to get around the system and somehow advertise both stores in it?

UPD: Possible solution under the cut.



Answer:

The assortment of your stores intersects in the "Bags" section. Please remove all possible intersections on the assortment on the sites of your stores, as well as in the downloadable price lists. After that, let us know about it and we will consider the possibility of placing two stores.

My letter:

Removed all the intersections that I could find.

Answer:

On the site natropinkah.ru remained section bags. Placing two stores is impossible.

My letter:

I do not understand which section is being discussed. Can I link to confirm?

Answer:

We are talking about the section "Backpacks and bags": natropinkah.ru/shop/125 .
You can transfer all backpacks and bags from the natropinkah.ru store to the sumki.natropinkah.ru store.

My letter:

Transfer unrealistic. In tourism goods, people should be able to buy hiking backpacks and hunting backpacks, cartridge belts and duffel bags.

But the goods in the stores are different and do not overlap.

There are hiking and tourist backpacks and bags of the same brands in the shop on the Paths, and bags in the store. On the paths there are backpacks and city-type bags, handbags and wallets of completely different manufacturers. These are different and completely non-overlapping products.
