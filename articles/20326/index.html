VeniVidi - fellow travelers


And so today launched the old old service "Fellow Passengers." Why old? Because this section is on VeniVidi from the very beginning. Why new? Because now it has been completely redesigned, it has become easier and faster, and I hope it‚Äôs clearer.

The new concept of the section is based on the understanding that, on average, people search for companions a couple of times a year. Except for backpackers.

Therefore, the service should be as simple as possible without registering a person with registrations, the need for contacts only with internal messages on the site and long forms to fill out.

The service should act as an imperceptible "switchman," connecting the searching people directly with each other. Directly implies that people will communicate through their blogs, ICQ, Skype, phone, etc., but not sit on VeniVidi, pressing f5. Contacts are indicated in free form.

Perhaps, I will not describe here the interface and some moments of working with the service - everything is very simple (I hope). I will only note that everything has been done on the basis of Google Maps, which has recently been considered a bad form, but in such a matter as searching for people traveling, I consider it a very correct decision.
