On the Russian advertising market only Internet grows

In the first quarter of 2015, the advertising market in the country fell by 17% compared with the same period in 2014, writes Vedomosti . The Internet has become the only growing segment of the advertising market in Russia.

The advertising market in Russia in the first quarter of 2015 amounted to 63.5 billion rubles, as calculated by the Association of Switching Agencies of Russia .

The revenues of the publishers of newspapers and magazines fell by a third to 5 billion rubles, radio stations - by a quarter to 2.5 billion, outdoor advertising - by a quarter to 7.3 billion. Television advertising declined by 22% to 30.6 billion.

Internet advertising grew by 9% to 18.1 billion. At the same time, display advertising was reduced by 18%, and growth was due to an increase in spending on contextual advertising by 16%.
