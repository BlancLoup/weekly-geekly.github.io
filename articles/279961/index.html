UNetLab 1.0. A serious competitor for GNS3

Hello colleagues! I do not know why this news has not yet appeared on Habr√©, but recently a release of a new version of UNetLab 1.0 took place.

The new version contains many improvements especially in the part of the web interface. I will not describe what UNetLab is and what it is for, it was already done before me and quite well.

This is a very young project that in such a short time managed to make a big rustle in the whole community of networkers. The product itself is developed by a very small team, which does not prevent them from conquering their audience at a tremendous pace. If you want to know my opinion, then I am deeply convinced that within two to three years, UNetLab will become the standard in the field of network technology training. In doing so, Cisco Packet Tracer will prevail for primary education, while GNS3 will slowly go into oblivion (unless they change their approach to development). But this is only my subjective opinion, it may well be wrong.
