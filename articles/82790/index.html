New way to purchase premium



Evernote is free. You can use Evernote all your life without paying a penny, and this suits us perfectly. To some, such an approach may seem crazy, but thanks to the premium service Evernote, this is a really working business model (see Freemium Model ). However, until now, those wishing to receive expanded paid features of Evernote had to resort to the services of Google Checkout and PayPal, which are not very actively used in Russia. But now it's over.

Today we are announcing the appearance of another payment method for a premium account in addition to the existing ones. Now we support payments from Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, Diners Club and JCB plastic cards!

Get a premium account ¬ª

Why go for a premium?

Vast reserves of space for notes
The monthly available disk space for your notes will be increased from 40 to 500 megabytes. Every month you will have 500 megabytes ready.

Add and synchronize files of any type
As a user of the free service, you can create notes with text, audio recordings, pictures and PDF files. As soon as you switch to a premium subscription, you have the opportunity to attach arbitrary files to your records, including Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, Photoshop files, and any others. Along with the rest, these files will be synchronized, and it will be possible to access them wherever you are. The only limitation is that the size of one note cannot exceed 25 megabytes.

Offline notebooks on the iPhone and iPod Touch
Get offline access to any of your notebooks or all at once. This will not only make access to notes faster, but also allow you to view and search for your records in the absence of an Internet connection.

Search PDF Files
When a premium user places a scanned PDF document into Evernote, our servers recognize the text in it and make it searchable.

Teamwork
You can open your notebooks for viewing by individual users or the whole world using the Evernote web interface. Premium users can give others rights to both read and edit their notes, turning Evernote into a great collaboration tool.
