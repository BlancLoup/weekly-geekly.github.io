iOS 5.1.1 available for download


Today, unexpectedly for iOS users, firmware 5.1.1 has been released. The update does not contain any "new products", past problems are fixed, and there are improvements.

Updates


  • Improving the reliability of using the HDR option for photos taken using the shortcut icon
  • Correction of errors that could prevent the switching of a new iPad between 2G and 3G networks
  • Correction of errors under certain circumstances affecting video playback using AirPlay
  • Improved synchronization reliability of Safari bookmarks and Reading List
  • Correction of the error that could lead to displaying the warning "Could not buy", despite the successful purchase
