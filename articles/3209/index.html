Launchy - small, but very useful!

Launchy called by Alt + Space (changeable) makes it quite easy to run any of your applications (and indeed any files, depending on the settings). Already in the first letters, she tries to guess what you are looking for and, like Google Suggestions, offers a choice.





Maybe someone she will seem useless pribluda, but to me personally, she made life easier. Everything has become even a bit simpler - but isn't this the goal of all this computer chaos?

And yes, it is open source and free of charge accordingly.
