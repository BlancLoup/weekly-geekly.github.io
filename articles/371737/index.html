Once again about the storage of cyanoacrylate


How much CA glue disappears in vain from improper storage! Do not discard desiccant sachets (silica gel), which can be found in some purchases. Put the tube of glue with silica gel in a transparent bag with a clasp (in which small parts are sold). Then put it all in the fridge, the zipped bag with glue is also not so annoying to family members. Keep out of the reach of children!

Problems


  • Print speed (it is not clear what).
  • Poisonous atmosphere a la "all serious".
  • The complexity of storing consumables (a barrel of cosmofen, they say, dries out in 8 months).
  • Difficulty of printing in living conditions (the air is humid).
  • Heating the workpiece (exothermic polymerization reaction).
  • The destruction of the parts at 80..100 degrees Celsius.
  • Delamination of formwork, cooling shrinkage.

Probably, there are still many problems :)

Benefits


  • Powerful adhesion of parts with the "table" of the printer.
  • The larger dimensions of the printed products.
  • Products of complex shape, with easily detachable formwork.
  • Relative availability of consumables.
