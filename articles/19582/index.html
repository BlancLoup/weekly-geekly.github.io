RBC acquired online digital content store Fidel

RBC acquired a 51% stake in Fidel with the right to increase its controlling stake to 75% within three years. The Fidel.ru service was created by the Fidel Solutions company in 2005. and is currently one of the largest online digital content legal stores in RuNet. In the sale - music, audio books, mobile content and computer games.

In accordance with the purchase agreement, the amount of the transaction, taking into account the commitments on investments in the project in the next three years, was $ 8.5 million. Fidel will be developed by a subsidiary of RBC, Media World, which specializes in mass Internet services.
