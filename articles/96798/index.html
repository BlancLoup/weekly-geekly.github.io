Opera will answer for everything



As many may have noticed, recently Opera has constantly surprised the well-educated public: either the Unite revolution starts, then suddenly, for no reason at all, it dumbfounded by a tenfold (or already more?) Increase in the speed of the browser, and only about any innovations in interface or in support of future web standards can be mentioned a little weekly. There are also surprises with a negative effect, after which users get tired of turning the mouse wheel, looking at the lists of fixed bugs in the next weekly build. In general, every sensible person has a reasonable question: damn it, what's going on in them in this Norway ?!

The question is, of course, interesting. But it would be much more interesting to find out the answer to it - all of a sudden it will help preserve a million or other nerve cells or just make the general direction understandable - where the thoughts and dreams of Opera browser developers are directed. Well, this problem is solved. And this topic serves as a kind of announcement of the new initiative - "Opera Habragost" . In this case, the questions will be answered by people who occupy not the last place in the staffing table - up to the director of the company or even the chief financial officer. Of course, they will not tell everything (there must be some mystery in this company), but I am sure that, for example, communication with one of the co-founders of Opera Software will not be boring. However, to a greater extent, everything will depend on the quality of the questions. Therefore, some time is allocated for their preparation - suppose, a week. Quite a sufficient time.
