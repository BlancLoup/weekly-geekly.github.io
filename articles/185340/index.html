Firefox OS smartphones go on sale



The first devices officially presented under the operating system Firefox OS are the Alcatel One Touch Fire and ZTE Open models from the operators Deutsche Telekom (Germany) and Telefonica (Spain), respectively. ZTE Open sales began today, July 2, 2013.

ZTE Open with a 1 GHz Snapdragon processor, 256 MB of RAM, 4 GB of flash memory, a 3.5-inch display, HSDPA, a 3.2-megapixel camera, Bluetooth, A-GPS and Wi-Fi from a Movistar operator ( Telefonica) is sold without a contract for 69 euros , while 30 euros are put on the account of the subscriber.

The device Alcatel One Touch Fire is about the same characteristics as the ZTE Open, but it works on a Qualcomm processor.



Firefox OS is the first open source operating system on the Linux kernel to natively launch HTML5 applications. Each program (calls, SMS, mail) on the phone is a web application. In April of this year, a limited batch of experimental phones running Firefox OS for web developers was sold out in a few hours , so this platform has quite good prospects.

Firefox OS is positioned as a more friendly alternative to Android, which does not require payment of license fees to Microsoft . Even small payments of 5-10 dollars for each device result in a significant percentage of the cost of an entry-level smartphone.

18 mobile operators around the world, including two Russian operators, have already expressed their desire to sell HTML5 smartphones. Mozilla counts on the markets of Central and Eastern Europe, Asia and Latin America, where inexpensive Android devices are very popular.

About the development and production of "more than five reference devices" under Firefox OS announced the world's largest consumer electronics company, the company Foxconn, in the framework of strategic cooperation with Mozilla. The Taiwanese giant has already started recruiting 2000-3000 programmers "with experience in developing HTML5, operating systems, mobile applications and cloud services." This is a relatively small department, given the Foxconn staff of 1.23 million employees.
