Python Junior Meetup # 2

On April 13, at 19:00, the Python Junior Meetup # 2 will take place. This is a meta for beginner Python programmers. The meeting is organized by the Python.ru team. Mitap will be held at the Rambler & Co office, a ten minute walk from the Tulskaya metro station.

At the meeting we will hear three reports:

Grigory Petrov (Voximplant) will tell how to learn to read any code in 30 minutes.

As simply as possible I will talk about how the program text looks in any programming language from the point of view of the programming language itself. You will learn about expressions and statements, about the terrible thing to evaluate, about the fact that you will have to learn English, and many other interesting things.

Nikita Sobolev (wemake.services) will tell you how to learn programming, not Python.

It is possible to study, as bequeathed to the great ones, all my life. Especially programming. In my report, I will try to clarify the learning process. Answer questions that often arise from those who learn programming and learn it. And also to talk on the topic of "knowledge" and "not knowledge" in general.

Oleg Churkin (Rambler & Co) will tell you about the path from a novice to a professional.

A story about how a developer can plan his professional growth: how to develop technically and personally, how to make important career decisions and what qualities and skills employers are waiting for from really cool specialists.

To participate you must register
