Vanilla - Web 2.0 or Classic?

So we got to the new review article. So, we continue to discuss the Vanilla forum.
Judging by past comments, many people are very interested in the description of Vanilla add-ons, what kind of assemblies and why such a division of one forum into 2 different directions. Will try .
As mentioned earlier , the main advantage of Vanilla is its modularity.
Depending on your needs, you can build exactly the kind of forum you need. Moreover, in this case, some modules are functional enough to call them plug-ins in the usual sense of the word.
We are building Vanilla in 2 most general directions - a classic forum and a web2.0 version . We will not describe all the additions included in the assemblies - each of them is described in the community forum, and here we give a comparative description of the assemblies.
