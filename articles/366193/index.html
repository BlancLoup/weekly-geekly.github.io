Microsoft gives free 100 Gb in the cloud storage to everyone



To reward users of the Bing search service, Microsoft awards bonuses to everyone who joins the Bing Rewards program. The most interesting gift should be considered 100 pounds Gb of additional free (for two years) space in the cloud storage Microsoft OneDrive.

The default for all new users is 15 Gb of space. But if you sign up for the Bing Rewards program, your storage will be 115 Gb. In addition to registration, nothing more needs to be done. If you already have an account on OneDrive, you can still take advantage of this offer.

There are also more gifts for using bing - having accumulated points for each use of a search engine, you can spend them on gift cards, participate in prize draws or send them to charity. The only disadvantage of the proposal is that it seems to be available only to users with an ip address from the United States. But this few people have to stop.

This week, astrologers clearly announced a week of cloudy generosity, as Google recently announced a 2 Gb gift to your Google Drive for anyone who passes the account's security settings.
