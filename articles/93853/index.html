As for the filling

GSM

It works in all four ranges (other models supported mainly three). But the level of reception is much lower than on other phones. Sometimes, where Nokia took with two or three divisions, there is no connection to Sunno at all. But there are practically no problems with coverage in the city, so the moment is uncritical.
A much more unpleasant problem with incoming calls. Periodically, the phone does not respond to them. On the other side, you can hear beeps, dialing, and nothing on the phone. Similarly, with SMS - does not reach. Until I dial from him to any number myself, the device does not wake up. It happens not so often, because I talk a lot, and since the phone is not the main one, you can put up with it.
There is a problem with SMS: it is not possible to send new SMS, but the responses normally leave. What is the problem, I have not figured it out yet. Makes either the radio module (hardware or firmware) or WM6.5 itself
