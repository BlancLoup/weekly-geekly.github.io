ICANN approved the Yandex application for the top-level domain zone .yandex

Today it has become known that ICANN , an international non-profit organization that regulates issues related to domain names on the Internet, has tentatively approved a Yandex application for the first-level domain zone .yandex.

Especially for Habr, we asked the most basic questions about .yandex to the head of NOC Yandex, Vladimir ivlad Ivanov, and the deputy head of the operation department, Tatyana tvt Bakharevskaya.

What does this ICANN decision really mean?

Tatyana : It means that we have passed the stage of Initial Evaluation . Now Yandex needs to sign the Registry Agreement, which will define our relationship with ICANN.

But this document is not ready yet, it is being discussed by the community:
www.icann.org/en/news/public-comment/base-agreement-29apr13-en.htm

Delegation of this zone will be possible after the signing of this document.

Are there other companies in Russia that have already received the zone?

Tatyana: At the moment, the Initial Evaluation stage has passed the domains: Moscow, children, Rus. But applications are constantly reviewed, so maybe the list has increased during this time.

What did we do to get the zone? Anyone can do this?

Tatyana: It was a great job of the financial and legal department, and Safenames British firm helped us to apply, which has long helped us with domain registrations.
The application is quite expensive - 185 thousand dollars, so, apparently, not everyone.
