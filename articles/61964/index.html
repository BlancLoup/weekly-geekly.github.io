MyNotifier is offered / for sale, an informer for freelancers

Hello, Habrahabr!

A year and a half ago, I made a website for freelancers , which collected information about orders from the six largest freelance sites and delivered hot to the desktop Windows users hot. The service did not spin up and never brought me money. In a year and a half, I reworked the site several times, he changed servers; I was thrown twice with a new version of the Windows client; I constantly leveled the traffic on the server in order not to overpay ... The service brought me many familiar and new customers.

About the service is written here , and here about the history of its creation.

Since its inception, much water has flowed. I almost do not visit freelancing sites - they are overgrown with work and regular customers. There were services similar to my own, for example alllance .

On June 2, the site Free-lance.ru changed the design, as a result, the parsers that collect information from it stopped working.   • at the same time, the program was launched by an average of 30 people (after a loose free-lance - 10);
