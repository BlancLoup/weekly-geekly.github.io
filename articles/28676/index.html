Black and flat basic tab

Whether we like it or not, but the output of the iPhone with its touchscreen and user interface set the tone for the further development of mobile gadgets. Therefore, there is nothing strange in the fact that designers, developing their own concepts, follow the path of Apple's chief designer.

That designer Jaren Goa, who presented his concept phone called the Basic Tab, is also not far from the iPhone.

What he, in fact, does not hide, stating that he took from the mobile "hit" soft rounded shapes and a thin body. The rest turned out more than good.

It makes no sense to describe (the designer mentions only a 3.2-megapixel camera from the technical characteristics) is better to just look at the photo.

By the way, the name of the concept source is quoted as Basic Tab. But one of the photos clearly shows that the Black Tab is written on the front panel of this phone.
