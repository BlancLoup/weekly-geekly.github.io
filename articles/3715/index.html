Microsoft is trying to promote a new sales model

Microsoft has been beginning to promote pay-as-you-go software sales in South Africa, Mexico and Romania. The meaning of this model is that computer users pay a certain rent for using the programs installed on the computer. For example, access to all functions of MS Office 2003 costs $ 15.

Previously, Microsoft launched the FlexGo initiative, offering to purchase computers with its own production programs installed on them at a reduced price, and then access them using prepaid cards. The venture did not receive recognition anywhere, except perhaps Brazil.
