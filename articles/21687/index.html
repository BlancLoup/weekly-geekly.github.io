Asahi Beer Bot. When too lazy to stomp to the fridge

Japanese engineers recently showed a somewhat controversial development. Robot Asahi Beer Bot, looks like R2D2 from "Star Wars" and is designed to make it easier for particularly lazy beer lovers to consume this noblest drink.

This piece of iron can work both as a refrigerator and as a "bartender". Inside the Beer Bot breaks six beer cans (it is a pity that only cans) and a couple of mugs. In order to "update", you just need to click on the button on the front panel of the robot and attach the mug to one of his "hands". Cooled to the desired temperature, the beer will be neatly poured into a mug (how does he cope with foam, I wonder?). What the robot immediately and inform the owner.
