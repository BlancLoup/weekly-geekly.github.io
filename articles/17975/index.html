We decorate the computer for the New Year: 4 great ideas

A few days left before the New Year. People buy gifts, set and decorate Christmas trees. In general, they prepare in full and stock up on a festive mood. And what about the more computerized habranele of the country? ;) All the same, but everything else we can still decorate your computer!

You can decorate absolutely everything, from the banal desktop wallpaper and ending with the program interface. And the best part is that all this can be done absolutely free! What will help us create a festive atmosphere? I will gladly share the answer to this question ...

... with you, dear habrachiteli!

To create a festive New Year's Eve atmosphere will help us a few free programs:

Snowfalls and Baubles


Since we want to get a festive mood, so why not let a couple of programs of exclusively entertaining nature go to your computer? :) For example, on the desktop you can plant a beautiful Christmas tree:

Such a Christmas tree can count down the time until the New Year or Christmas and notify you of the approaching holiday every half hour with a cheerful jingle bell (if you wish, of course). In addition, the program does not limit your choice to one tree - you can choose any of 11 favorite skins. Christmas tree can be downloaded on the developer's site .

Yes, and what a New Year without snow? Especially for the organization of local snowfall created several programs. All the programs I found were written several years ago and do not have their own pages on the Internet. Unfortunately, I did not meet something new. From the "snow" programs, I would recommend Snow 1.0 , WinSnow98 and KSnow . Try searching by names in search engines and software archives.

Wallpaper


Not decorated was the desktop - we will find for him beautiful New Year wallpapers. And so that the picture does not bother you can download the program for the automatic change of wallpaper and each time to observe a new Christmas picture on the desktop.
You can organize automatic wallpaper changes in 1000 and 1 ways, so I will not recommend something specific, but rather share one interesting idea :) Not so long ago, I talked about the Kalleidos Wallpaper program - this is also a wallpaper change, but much more interesting! The program creates a wallpaper based on the effect of a kaleidoscope and if you give it the same Christmas pictures as the source data, you can get at least the original wallpaper :) Worth trying.

Screensavers


Apparently, developers like this type of software more - it is much easier to find New Year's screensavers than desktop decorations. The choice of a screensaver is a purely individual matter and here I would not interfere with my advice about specific programs :)
The only pity is that I hardly see the screensaver ... Like the desktop, by the way. Most of the time in front of my eyes is the interface of any program. But if the program supports skins, then it can be decorated!

Skins


Of the programs I easily skimmed and frequently used, I will call Firefox and Winamp. Perhaps your favorites are different, but they most likely support interchangeable skins. Finding a beautiful New Year's skin on the net is a matter of five minutes, especially if the program is popular.

Anything else?


Well, now our computer is fully charged and you are ready to celebrate the New Year in a great mood! I did not miss anything? Maybe you know more ways to decorate the computer for the new year? Share impressions and tips in the comments!

PS: If after performing all the above actions, your working mood will evaporate somewhere and only happy holiday will remain - the author is not to blame;) With Coming!
