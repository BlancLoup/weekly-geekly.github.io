Giving the book "Harry Potter and Rational Thinking Methods" to olympiadians



On July 16, we launched a nonprofit crowdfunding for the Harry Potter and Rational Thinking Techniques stamp. What is it all about?


The book popularizes the ideas of critical thinking and effective altruism - and we decided that it is worth spreading it among talented people who, thanks to GMPM, will be able to bring the greatest benefit to the world.

Alexander Panchin tells about the book
The author of this book is an expert in artificial intelligence Eliezer Yudkovsky. He created the community of Less Wrong , whose members are trying to think rationally, discussing complex philosophical and scientific issues. Yudkovsky wrote a Harry Potter fanfiction in which the wizard‚Äôs world becomes more logical, and the motivation of the characters is understandable. Smart Harry Potter faces magic, ponders how it works, why it breaks the usual laws of physics, and uses the scientific method to figure it out and subdue it. All the characters here are different from those described by JK Rowling, but Harry himself has changed the most. True, one of its quality remained unchanged - it is still the same upstart.

At least this novel is very well written - I could not tear myself away from it.
