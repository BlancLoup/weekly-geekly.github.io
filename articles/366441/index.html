Aether Cone allows you to create multi-room



For Aether Cone there was an update that will allow him to play music on multiple devices.

Aether Cone makes it easy to listen to audio. For example, you can control your device with your voice, or simply twist its disk to access podcasts and music. There is no need to use a special application for working with the gadget, which makes the speaker an excellent choice for a party.

The speaker with its cone-shaped design, of course, attracts attention. Thanks to it, you can create a multiroom without the need to connect to Wi-Fi, which makes the device easy to use.

Aether says that the owners of the gadget will use it about two hours a day. The released update gives the gadget additional functionality without having to pay extra for it. Speaker users will receive the update automatically when the device is not in use.

Ather Cone price on the manufacturer's website - $ 399
