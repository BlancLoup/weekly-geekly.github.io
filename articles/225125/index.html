Devices and buns for Unity-developers for Windows and Windows Phone



If you are an active developer at Unity, you have probably heard that Microsoft and Unity Technologies are actively collaborating to help game developers port their creations to Windows and Windows Phone, and even better to create something new with an eye to modern Microsoft platforms. In particular, one of the results of this collaboration was that the Unity extension for publishing in the Windows Store is available for free .

You may have heard about this program before, but you could not participate in it due to geographical restrictions. We are pleased to announce that the program is now also expanded to Russia and Ukraine!

Apply for participation here : unity3d.com/en/pages/windows/offer

Competition




We also want to remind you that we are continuing the global competition for the best games on Unity, made under Windows and / or Windows Phone with good prizes. Hurry up to participate with your new or existing games until July 20. (Please note that by this time your game should already be published in the app store!)

Contest page : unity3d.com/contest/windows
