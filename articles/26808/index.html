Cloud sites



SitesCloud or ‚ÄúCloud sites‚Äù is such an odd job , which in fact does not bring much benefit by its existence, but it demonstrates the possibility of an alternative way to catalog sites, display and submit them.

It's simple: you add a site, and link it to a specific word that most closely matches the subject of the site. Then it appears in the general cloud. If another site has already been assigned to the specified word, then in order to crowd out a competitor, the site will need to be pumped through a special link . This same pumping is also needed so that your site cannot be forced out of the cloud if someone else wants to take your word with its site. In other words, every word in the cloud refers to the site that scored the most clicks on a special link for this site.

The most pumped word-site pairs have the largest font size in the cloud.

One site can be associated with only one word. One word can be associated with any number of sites, but in the cloud it refers to the one that scored the most clicks for pumping.

SitesCloud works on the CodeIgniter platform.
