Minimalistic Mobile Layout

In our work, there are often tasks on the layout of mobile versions of sites. It can be very difficult to start the layout of the mobile version without a prototype of the application. And it happens that the mobile version is needed, but the budget for it is not provided. But it is better to have at least some kind of mobile site than nothing at all. In such cases, miml - Minimalistic Mobile Layout comes to the rescue .

miml is just 2 images, 4 styles and 1 html template file.

Download: github.com/masted/miml/archive/v1.0.zip
HTML: github.com/masted/miml/tree/v1.0
Demo: masted.ru/blog/miml/index.html
