The choice fell on Atmel microcontrollers, because a comrade understood them quite well and, if anything, had someone to ask. The first thing I did was assemble a programmer (clone STK500) and set about my first project: blinking LEDs. And since it was nearing February 14, I decided to combine business with pleasure and to please my beloved spouse.
