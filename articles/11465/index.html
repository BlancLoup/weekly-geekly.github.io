Iogear Mouse - with a biometric authentication system

Iogear decided to provide all possible assistance to system administrators, exhausted by a long and unpromising struggle with users, always leaving passwords in the most prominent place, and launched the Personal Security Mouse optical manipulator with a biometric authentication system that eliminates the need to enter passwords when logging into the system.



The proposed solution is most suitable for computers shared between several users, since the mouse can store the fingerprints of five different people. An additional feature of the new security system is the creation of files and folders on the disk, accessible only after passing through biometric authentication.

The new product, like the Germ Free Wireless Laser Mouse, released last year, is coated with an antimicrobial nano-coating made of titanium dioxide and silver particles, and is part of the Iogear Nano family of peripheral devices.

Personal Security Mouse is available from the day of announcement, the recommended price is 89.95 US dollars.
