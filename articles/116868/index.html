# 404fest: Section Design

At the last 404 Festival I ruled the design section. The most interesting reports of the section were recorded on video. I bring to your attention a selection of the most interesting design video from the Festival.

The entire design section was on the second day, but the most useful designer report opened the Festival on the first day. Bill Buxton, a designer with a capital letter, spoke to us. What is there a designer - a scientist, a specialist in the interaction of man and computer, a world-wide personality.

Bill Buxton (Microsoft Research)
Design, websites and new standards

It all started in 1993 with Mosaic browser, then our ideas about websites were made up of GUI traditions, which in turn were dictated by personal computers and laptops. But since then, when smartphones began to be used to access the Internet, it became clear that the traditional model is not perfect. In my presentation, I will look at the era of mobile devices and talk about the digital eco-system in which we will live very soon, as well as about the different forms of life that populate this system. The current problems with the web and mobile Internet will fade in comparison with the upcoming changes. Browsers of the future will be different from the present, as now the Internet is different from newspapers and magazines.
