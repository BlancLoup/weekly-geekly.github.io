What reports can I create?


Diagnostic . All data coming from the device is added to the track table, and unloaded as it is in this report, with the ability to select fields.

Equipment operation . While the equipment is working on the vehicle - GPS chip, GPRS chip, fuel level sensor, etc., various malfunctions can occur, for example, the lack of a GPS signal, all these errors can be analyzed in this report depending on the zone or object.

Drivers. On-line monitoring users can attach drivers to cars for a specific time. And find the people who drove transport at a certain time.

Trips The report is repelled from the main parameters of the sensors on the trip. If the car did not move or stopped, the data are not taken into account. In particular, in this report you can get information on the length of the route, speeds, equipment work during the trip, fuel level before and after the trip, etc.

Parking / Stops . data is taken into account only when the car stopped or stood. In this report, you can find plums. There is also data as in trips, only those that were recorded during stops or stops.

Zones report and Objects report . This report takes into account the tracks that fall into the user-defined areas in the system. If the ‚ÄúWarehouse‚Äù polygon was marked, the average speed of the fuel level and other information that was obtained while the car was in this zone will be displayed.
