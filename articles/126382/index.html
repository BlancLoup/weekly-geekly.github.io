The second season of the Lift project starts - a reality show to find investors in a startup

i-Lift.TV is a multimedia project for TV, Internet and mobile, which in real time shows the ecosystem of start-up environments, venture capitalists and business angels, and most importantly - allows you to find real investments of professional investors, private businessmen in innovative technology.

The point is simple - you need to shoot a video to your Elevator Pitch and send it to the site. Your application will pass a double casting (open and closed voting - from social media and experts, respectively) and then, if everything is cool, you get to the real shooting of the Lift project, where you will be able to sell your project in real time to investors.

This is not the first launch; in the 2nd season, the shooting will take place in Moscow, Vienna, Silicon Valley and San Francisco: 20 well-known businessmen and foundations will be looking for start-ups for potential investments for 3 months.

List of investors : Igor Agamirzyan (RVK), Yuri Milner (DST), Dmitry Korobkov (ADG Group), David Yan (ABBYY), Nikolay Pryanishnikov, Pavel Cherkashin (Microsoft Russia), Vladimir Dolgov (Google Russia), Oleg Tinkov (Tinkoff) , Mikhail Gerchuk (MTS), Vadim Dymov (Dymov), Sergey Vykhodtsev (Bystrov), Alexander Galitsky (Almaz Capital), Aidar Kaliev (VTB Capital), Yevgeny Zaytsev (Helix Ventures), Alexander Turkot (Skolkovo), Edward Shenderovich (Kite Ventures), Pasha Romanovsky (Intersol), Igor Taber (Intel Capital), Mikhail Prokhorov (ONEXIM).
