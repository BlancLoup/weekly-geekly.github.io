Habra Informer

Like many of those who constantly visit habrahabr, I often check the main page for new articles, until one fine moment I got the idea to create an informer that would hang on the tray, whether my browser was launched or not, and I checked the update on the main page. page, if there is one, then I opened the program in the tray and watched which articles are currently on the main page. Next, clicking on the desired link - immediately got into the browser to the desired page.


As a result, I received a QT program that performs all this.
