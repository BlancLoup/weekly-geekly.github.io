One of the media headlines: Elbrus became free. Download links
The developer of the line of domestic processors "Elbrus" updated on its website a section on specialized software. The Elbrus OS for standard x86 processors is freely available for download. Soon the developers are planning to open its source code.
Another headline of the same news: Elbrus operating system can already be downloaded!

Yes, it really could be a very important milestone in the development of Elbrus OS. It could be, but unfortunately, it has not yet become (I hope that the key word will be the word for now )
