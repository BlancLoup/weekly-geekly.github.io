Correspondence Olympiad on sports programming for schoolchildren from NUST MISiS and Cognitive Technologies

Hi, Habr!

This fall for students will be held the correspondence Olympiad on sports programming from MISiS and the company Cognitive Technologies. Pupils of 9-11 grades from any Russian schools are invited.


How to participate


The Olympiad is free. In order to participate, you need to register on the site acm.misis.ru before October 15. The very same Olympiad will be held on October 20, on Sunday, according to the rules of ACM ICPC.

Terms and conditions


In total, each participant will have 10 tasks that need to be solved in 5 hours. Task conditions will be published on the website acm.misis.ru on the day of the Olympiad.
It is planned to determine about 40 winners of the Olympics. Winners and prize winners during the winter holidays from January 3 to January 10, 2014. will be invited to the Winter School of Programming. The second round of the Olympiad (full-time) will take place there, where valuable prizes such as laptops, tablet computers, etc. will be raffled off. Accommodation and meals in the Winter School will be free. Independently you will only need to pay transportation costs to the place and back.
More details can be found here .

We wish you success! We hope that you will enjoy participation in the Olympiad!
