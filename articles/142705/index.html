Certification by Yandex.Direct. Want to become an expert?

The certificate of a specialist in the Directive can finally be obtained by anyone who has enough knowledge and experience! From today, certification is open to advertisers who lead campaigns in a professional interface for six months and know how to manage budgets from 9000 rubles.

Certification takes place in the form of testing on the Yandex.Expert service. Log in using the login under which you manage advertising campaigns in Direct. After that, you can immediately go to the certification or take a test to get acquainted with the interface. Do not neglect the test test, it will help you get comfortable in the system and as a result do not waste valuable time searching for the right button.

Specialists who successfully pass the test, we will send a personal certificate.

Immediately after the first certification, a page with a history of the received certificates appears in the interface of the Expert. You can share a link to it in your blog or on the website, as well as add to the electronic version of your resume, and potential employers can verify its authenticity. By the way, the printed version of the certificate can also be checked by finding it by the number and name of the specialist using a special form on the service.

Learn more about the conditions of access and the rules for passing the test can be in the Help Yandex. Expert.
