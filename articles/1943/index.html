Internet in Georgia - for fun

The Internet audience in Georgia is 17.7% of the population, and the average user age is 25-34 years old, reports Day.Az, the conclusion of a local consulting group that polled 4,500 respondents in the country.

In addition, they found out that 52.6% of Internet users use the Internet for entertainment - the audience of the Network in Georgia, as everywhere, is young. People aged 18-24 make up 29.1% of all users. 31.1% of the audience are people of the age group 25-34. 56.3% of Internet users have email, which is accessed daily - 33.2%

21.4% of respondents chose not to answer the question of income, the rest of the earnings are 300-1000 GEL per month. 48% of respondents use the Internet at home, 36.4% at work, 15.6% at an Internet cafe.
