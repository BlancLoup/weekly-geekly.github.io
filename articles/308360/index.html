British Higher School of Design and MBLTdev will present a track for designers

November 17 in Moscow at the conference MBLTdev 16 will host a track for mobile application designers. Designing interactive interfaces, learning technologies and tools, and of course, experimenting with code in lectures and workshops throughout the day.



Lectures and practical knowledge will be shared by leading instructors of mobile application design course at the British Higher School of Design - specialists from well-known companies with significant experience in the field of UX / UI service applications and digital products, mobile development and marketing.

Already confirmed their participation:

  • Leonid Bugaev, British High School of Design
  • Alexander Burdin, British High School of Design
  • Nikita Stepanov, Yandex
  • Daria Zolotukhina, Yandex.Taxi
