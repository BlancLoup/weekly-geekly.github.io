What are smart lists?


A smart list is an additional audience for remarketing in Google Analytics, allowing you to increase the number of conversions. Considering the statistics of already completed conversions, Google identifies new users who are most likely to make a conversion. In the future, when managing bids, Google prefers these particular people.
For learning Google uses such signals as: devices, browsers, location, transition URL, page depth, information about the time spent on the site and so on.

How to set up smart lists in Google Analytics?


In the Administrator tab of your account, go to the Remarketing section and create a new audience.


Choose a ready-made audience " Smart List ", enter a name for the list and set the Participation Term


Create an advertising campaign in Google Adwords to target this audience.

Try smart lists - now

You will not need to keep track of this list on a daily basis, as Google takes over all the work, will only periodically change ads, testing advertising messages and measure the effectiveness of this campaign in Google Analytics.
