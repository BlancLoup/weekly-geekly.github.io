Microsoft updates Zune client

Microsoft has announced the release of a software update client, designed to "communicate" the Zune player and personal computer, reports Zune Insider . Zune is now compatible with the Vista operating system, and various glitches are fixed in the program.

Another good news: independent developers have released a program for downloading and uploading audio and video podcasts to the player. You can download the program for subscribing to podcasts from the site feedyourzune.com , and update the client itself - through the automatic update system or on the Microsoft website .
