Marketing and Cloud: What are the opportunities here?

Surprisingly, one of the main areas of activity that cloud technologies enrich and strengthen is the marketing. Let's see what exactly a modern approach to working with IT infrastructure can give to those who are engaged in the promotion of products and services.


/ photo by Yoel Ben-Avraham CC

Forrester and Gartner analysts say that cloud technologies have become an integral part of business processes in almost any organization. If we talk about specific numbers, here we are talking about the fact that within a year more than a third of all digital content will be stored in the cloud.

This thread reinforces technological progress in relation to the design of the data centers themselves. In this area, there are all new breakthroughs and improvements. Experts expect a significant increase in the efficiency of data storage technologies, which will allow to restructure the data center structure and make their services more accessible. Here you can mention such technology as 3D Xpoint.
