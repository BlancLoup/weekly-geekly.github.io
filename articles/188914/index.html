Freedom Hosting founder arrested in Ireland and awaiting extradition to US

The founder of Tor Freedom Hosting, 28-year-old Eric Owen Marquez, was arrested in Ireland. The US authorities are seeking his extradition, he was presented to the court as "the largest mediator of the distribution of child pornography on the planet."

Marquez, who has dual citizenship in Ireland and the United States, was arrested on the basis of a warrant of the state of Maryland, in which he is accused of distributing and promoting child pornography on the Internet. There is evidence that he allowed content providers to continue to post prohibited photos on their servers despite having received warnings repeatedly. The FBI took a year to establish his whereabouts.
