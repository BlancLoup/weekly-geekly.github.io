ConfeT & QA Testers Online Conference

What will the usual tester do in 20 minutes?

Drink two cups of coffee? Eat a box of chocolates? Find another bug?

Now in 20 minutes you can get new knowledge in the field of software testing, find out the answers to the questions that have long tormented you and see simple solutions to complex problems.

Meet: ConfeT & QA Online 2011 - International Software Testing Conference right at your home or in your office!
ConfeT & QA Online 2011 is
  • 15 motivational reports lasting 20 minutes on the most relevant topics in the field of testing
  • questions and answers live
  • discussions in a closed forum and offline versions of reports
  • real examples, megabytes of code and additional materials
  • discussions, holivary, the creation and debunking of myths
  • 5 days in a row, from October 17 to October 21, 2011, from 5 to 7 pm (Moscow time)
  • and you don't even have to get up from the couch!

The conference program is already fully staffed - within 5 days 15 reports will be presented on various aspects of testing. Every day you will be able to learn about three useful things that you can implement the very next day, increasing the impact of the test.

We started active work with speakers, there are many discussions and even rehearsals ahead. Over the next month they will have to work hard to perform perfectly. Because the best speaker, according to the results of the voting of listeners, is awaited by a wonderful prize - the Xbox 360 game console + Kinect sensor!

But the participants will not be left without prizes either! The most active participant will receive as a gift the tablet computer Archos 7 Home Tablet. What you need to do to win a prize? You need to ask interesting questions to speakers, both during online sessions and in the forum. A week after the conference, each speaker will select some of the best questions, based on this information the winner of the competition will be determined.

Details about the program and conditions of participation - on the conference website confetqa.ru
