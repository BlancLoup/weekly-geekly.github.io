Open interview with Vasily Voropaev (Free-Lance.ru) [UPD]

The news did not pass even popular publications like Lenta.ru.

Having read the entire discussion thread in the Free-lance.ru topic, I went crazy, I decided to accumulate the most asked questions and, in the form of an open interview, send them to the site administration, hoping to get answers.
