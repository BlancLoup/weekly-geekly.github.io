MyGrid Wireless Charger from Duracell

Electricity without wires is now being rather actively discussed, for example, take a recent demonstration from Eric Hiller .
Duracell also keeps pace with the times, so in the near future, a device should appear on the market, with a wireless principle of power supply.

The device has already received the name MyGrid. With it, you can recharge communicators and mobile phones Apple, Blackberry, Motorola and Nokia. The device consists of a charging pad and a set of adapters made in the form of linings and covers.
Available in October of this year, the price of the device - $ 80. Additional adapters will be available for $ 35.
