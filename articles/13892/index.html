OpenKofe.ru - open club for those who are at the start!


OpenKofe.ru starts - an open club for startups, investors and everyone who is somehow connected with the topic of startups.

OpenKofe exists in order to:
  • discuss ideas, share, pronounce. Understand whether to implement your idea at all or not. Very often, in order to give the idea a first push, it must be spoken out loud with a sane interlocutor. (This is the main meaning of OpenKofe; the rest is bonuses!);
  • find \ advise those who can implement the idea - designers, programmers, etc. (Connections are our everything, as is known.);
  • find an investor for your startup;
  • and for an investor - to find an idea in which to invest.


OpenKofe.ru was created based on www.opencoffeeclub.org - a large international start-up community. Almost all major cities (not Russia, though) are OpenFire clubs. Everything is as easy and free as possible: any quiet coffee shop with Wi-Fi is chosen as a meeting place, people come on certain days and talk. According to Openkofe clubs experience in Europe and America, such a free-style scheme is best. Read about the ideology Openkoffe here and here (carefully, English).
