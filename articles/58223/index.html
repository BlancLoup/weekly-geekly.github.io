Installing Ubuntu 9.04 and a bug in ubiquity

When installing Ubuntu 9.04 I encountered a bug in ubiquity (installer ubunt).

The installer crashed to 88% - at the moment when the settings were imported from other operating systems. Although I did not choose anything, and there was no window with a choice, he (the installer) tried to import something from somewhere.
After a long search, I found the root of the problem. It turned out to be installed next to Crunchbang . And accordingly import from it something unintelligible.

All this is treated by adding the kernel boot parameters (the string is called on F6) of the following:

ubiquity --no-migration-assistant
