Motivated traffic (motivational traffic) is a tool to attract users in the digital industry, based on user rewards for actions performed.

The essence of motivated traffic is to reward the user for any actions regarding your product, service or website, for example:

User traffic is the most modern kind of motivational traffic, the demand for which has arisen in connection with the development of analytics and methods for controlling spam on the Internet.
