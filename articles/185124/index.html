mySQLgame

Tired of monotonous browser games, which are just different interfaces to databases? Meet mySQLgame - a game that is just a database.

Feel the overwhelming feeling of joy that you insert your own row in the rows table!

With a reasonable choice of SQL queries, you will soon receive a three- or even four-digit number in one of the fields of your string! In this case, know: in the game there is a real war. In the new requests you can use these numbers to subtract from the rows of other players - while the numbers in your own line will be even higher!

Perhaps sooner or later you will find that you have already inserted more than one line into the game - maybe even a few of them! All you have to do is just login and start the game.

For those interested in it, see the wiki game guide and discussion group .

Link to the game: mySQLgame .

PS Be careful. It is possible that under all this is hiding a really interesting game.
