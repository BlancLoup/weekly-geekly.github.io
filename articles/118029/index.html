HBO Go - HBO series on Android and iOS

Taking advantage of the uproar around the premiere of the series Game of Thrones , the American cable channel HBO has once again announced the service of providing video content in the streaming mode HBO Go .



The service is designed to play videos on tablets and smartphones, so it is planned to launch an application for iPhone, iPad and Android. The launch of the service is scheduled for May 2, 2011, unfortunately at first the service will be available only to Americans .

HBO promises more than 1,400 programs available, including its mini-series, by which the channel became famous throughout the world. Ability to plan a list of favorite programs, subscribe to them and allow the application to automatically download them as new materials arrive.

I wonder if they will still open access to the rest of the world. Soaps produced by HBO have already proven themselves. And the hit "Broadwalk Empire" (Underground Empire), "The Sopranos" (Clan Soprano), "Rome" (Rome) and the first episode of "Game of Thrones" (Game of Thrones) say that "HBO" is only gaining momentum and will delight the audience for a long time. In the meantime, it remains to rejoice in their work taking them from torrent trackers.
