Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine makes a new site for $ 100,000 - a development period of 2 months!

The staff of the Verkhovna Rada intends to create a new site for the Rada, as well as websites of the chairman of parliament and parliamentary committees.

This was said by the head of the computerized systems management department of the Rada office, Serhiy Mironchenko, at a meeting on the development of new sites, Ukrainian News reported.

According to the results of the tender, which took place in August, KI-Technology (Lviv) will deal with the development of the sites.

On their creation provides 493 thousand hryvnia.

Mironchenko noted that technologically new web portals will be created.

He stressed that the current Rada site has many shortcomings, in particular, the site requires improvements in terms of optimizing the search system and creating user feedback.

In addition, a significant drawback of the site Rada is that it may fail during resonant events, therefore we are not talking about upgrading the existing site Rada.

According to programmer-developer KI-Technology Sergey Kravets, the company will be ready to provide a test version of the sites by the beginning of February 2009.

As reported, in the summer, the Rada apparatus reconstructed the parliament hall and updated the Rada-3 electronic voting system, establishing mechanisms for individual voting of deputies.
