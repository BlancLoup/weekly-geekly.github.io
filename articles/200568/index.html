Conference GDG DevFest 2013 will meet in Moscow on November 4

Next Monday (November 4th), the annual Digital Free Conference will be held at the Digital October center site, hosted by the Google Developers Group (GDG) community worldwide, GDG DevFest .



The GDG DevFest Moscow 2013 conference is organized by the Moscow community of the Google Developers Group with the support of Google and is intended for both beginners and experienced developers interested in modern technologies and topics such as Android , Google Chrome , App Engine , App Script , Google Glass , AngularJS .

Activists of GDG and Google Developer Experts, will speak to an audience and answer all questions of interest to developers. A total of 8 reports are scheduled. Detailed schedule of the conference can be found at the link .

Conference speakers will share reports and:
  • Get acquainted with several interesting tricks when working in Google Apps Script. They will tell how you can use GAS to develop Glassware.
  • They will explain (including a simple and practical example) how Angular makes the HTML5 + Javascript development process easier.
  • They will tell you what is happening with the compiled C / C ++ code outside the Android NDK, and why it happened that the NDK is noticeably different not only from the ‚Äútraditions of C ++ coding‚Äù, but also from the real environment on the devices.
  • Demonstrate how AdMob is better for building larger business applications than other ad networks and more.

Venue: Digital October Center, located at Bersenevskaya Embankment, 6 .
Conditions of participation: Participation in GDG DevFest Moscow 2013 is free. You can register for the conference here .

Sign up for the conference and follow the official hashtags #gdgmsk and #devfest on Twitter.

ps In Russia, DevFest besides Moscow will also be held in Omsk (2-3 November) and Voronezh (6 November).
