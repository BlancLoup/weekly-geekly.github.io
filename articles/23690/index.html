How to develop ideas

Hello!

I came across a very interesting checklist that helps to structure the approach to the development of ideas a little bit. I wanted to make a transfer first, but then it turned out that I really want to know about your approach ...



1. Be a good observer.
Keep your eyes open and your ear will look around.

2. Play with ideas.
Let ideas run absurdly, jump and tumble in your head.

3. Do not be critical during the formation of ideas.
Never discard ideas immediately.

4. Combine ideas.
Listen to others, use their ideas to generate your own.

5. Be free: the more the better.
Remember, the more ideas you generate, the more likely one of them will be just super!

6. Be unbiased.
Do not allow limited thinking to stop developing your ideas.

7. Dream.
Take time out in the middle of the day to practice your imagination with a dream.

8. Know when to relax.
When ideas lose their power, stop thinking about them. Allow yourself to relax playing tennis, cycling, reading, etc.

9. Keep a notebook of ideas.
In order not to forget ideas, write them down in a small convenient notebook.

10. Revise old ideas.
Invent new ideas based on old ones: add, remove, exaggerate, diminish, turn upside down, tear.

11. Accept the call.
Do not be afraid to accept danger, following new ideas and a new course.

12. Be inquisitive.
Discovering something interesting for yourself, do not be afraid to be curious.
