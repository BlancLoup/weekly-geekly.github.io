The software package ViPNet Client for Android is certified by the Federal Security Service of Russia

We are pleased to announce that the ViPNet Client for Android software package is certified by the Federal Security Service of Russia as a network means of cryptographic protection of KC1 class information. The obtained certificate of conformity SF / 124-2226 confirms that ViPNet Client for Android meets the requirements of GOST 28147-89, GOST R 34.11-94 and the requirements of the Federal Security Service of Russia for encryption (cryptographic) means of class KC1 and can be used for cryptographic protection of information that does not contain constituting a state secret.

ViPNet Client for Android is the first certified VPN client for mobile devices on the Android operating system on the Russian market with encryption using the GOST 28147-89 algorithm, intended for use as part of secure ViPNet networks. Encryption is carried out in a transparent mode, which allows you to ensure the protection of information exchange for any applications that work with corporate resources: email, terminal clients, workflow systems, ERP / CRM, corporate video conferencing or IP telephony.

The product provides protection of the device against network attacks and allows access to resources of the corporate network using a VPN tunnel protected by ViPNet technologies. As a data network, both corporate and public Wi-Fi networks can be used, as well as 4G / 3G / EDGE communication networks.

The certificate is issued based on the results of tests conducted by Certification Research Center LLC.
