"Mail of Russia" revealed a gang of fraudsters, who sent bricks instead of electronics



If earlier for the purchase of bricks at the cost of the phone and the cash in hand it was necessary to be in a dark doorway, now it is enough to use an unverified online store.

Pochta Rossii has published an entry in which it reports on the disclosure of a gang of fraudsters who have sent various items to customers instead of expensive equipment: from glasses to bricks. The investigation was conducted together with law enforcement.

The scheme worked simply. Through fake online stores, fraudsters "sold" various goods to the citizens of the Russian Federation, mainly expensive equipment, such as smartphones or tablets. After a cash on delivery, the victim was sent a package with a brick fragment, a glass, a bottle of water, or another object of a suitable mass.

Back in 2016, a series of reports of thefts on sorting points or in post offices swept across Runet. Deceived buyers actively shared their stories on the Internet, most often in social networks or on the entertainment resource Pikabu, where these stories often came out in the top rankings. All parcels were sent without an inventory of the contents. The scheme worked, as many do not inspect / open the package immediately in the department, but they do it at home.
