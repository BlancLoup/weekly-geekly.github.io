You can activate the Black-and-White Lists service, which allows you to block access to the services provided by content providers at your request. Special options
Beeline offers special options that can be enabled to protect against spam and paid SMS
  • Blocking commercial SMS (paid) - prohibits the reception of paid SMS from commercial providers. Free SMS (for example, from a bank) will be sent
  • Block all SMS - disable the ability to receive any (paid, promotional, etc.) SMS
  • Turning off the Chameleon service - the Chameleon service allows Beeline partners to send free promotional SMS. It is connected automatically to a new SIM card and when changing the SIM card. Disabling it can get rid of advertising spam via SMS
