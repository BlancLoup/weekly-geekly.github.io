Become a part of Davos 2010


On December 9, in the Swiss city of Geneva, Youtube, together with the leadership of the International Economic Forum Davos 2010, decided to open the doors for us to Davos and offer a unique opportunity for any person to appeal to the world community.

The video contest, entitled ‚ÄúYour Pitch to the World‚Äù, is designed to find a person who can best illuminate one of the problems of humanity that should be discussed at the next International Economic Forum, which will be held from January 27 to January 31 in Davos-Klosters, Switzerland.
