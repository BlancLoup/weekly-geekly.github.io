The 30-minute video clearly shows how, where and for what you can connect USB HUB, keyboard, mouse, flash drives, external hard drives, joysticks and other useful home accessories to your smartphone. The video is in English, so below we have prepared a description of all lighted devices.



1) USB Host. The Samsung GALAXY S III has a cable for removable media, through which you can connect external devices via USB Host. Thanks to this adapter, you can easily pick up a card reader, USB flash drive, digital camera, even a keyboard or mouse. Finally, from a flash drive, you can fully transfer music, photos, videos and other content that is dear to your heart to your smartphone, but you can also not transfer it: view information directly from the carrier.

2) Ability to connect an external hard drive. It is likely that the information will be loaded a little longer than in normal conditions, due to the large amount of content, but after the smartphone determines the media, the video from it is played without problems / brakes.

3) USB HUB. This is a handy device with multiple ports. In the video clip, they picked up: an external hard disk of 500 GB, a 32 GB flash drive and a wireless keyboard with a built-in touchpad, which allows you to take full control of your smartphone.

4) USB Audio and external speaker allow you to listen to music loudly, clearly and in decent quality.

5) Connect the PS3 controller! Pleasant moment: after connection it is determined automatically. The video shows how using the controller and the FPse emulator program (the Sony PlayStation One game console emulator), you can comfortably plunge into games such as Tekken, for example.

6) Next, connect the XBoX 360 controller. It is not automatically detected, but on the Android market there is a USB / BT Joystick Center application that scans and finds the controller, after which it can be used to the full.

7) MHL micro USB BM adapter (HDMI interface cable). With it, you can display photos and videos in HD or any other content on a high-definition TV screen. Just insert the power cable with a micro-USB connector into the adapter, pick up a standard HDMI cable and voila: HD-picture on the big screen. You can also use the camera of the smartphone while it is connected to the TV and hack into your favorite games.
