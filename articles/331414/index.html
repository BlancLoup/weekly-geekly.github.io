Seminar "Trifle, but nice: 20 little things that will make the work in the server really comfortable", July 4, Moscow



On July 4, we invite you to a free seminar where we will talk about simple and useful things for convenient operation of the server and data center.

What shall we talk about:

  • Markings and designations
  • Instructions and diagrams
  • Auxiliary premises
  • Tools and equipment
  • Wi-Fi and telephony
