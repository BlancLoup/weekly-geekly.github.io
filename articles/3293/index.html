Estari 2-VU Model DC15 - a laptop with two displays

The company Estari has released another laptop with two displays in its line of 2-VU. Laptop PC 2-VU Model DC15 is not equipped with a familiar keyboard, it took its place and took the second display. The user obviously has a choice - either use a virtual keyboard (both screens are touchscreens), or connect an external one.



Both displays are 15 "diagonal in size and have a real resolution of 1024 x 768 pixels (XGA), the graphics adapter is Intel GMA 950 integrated into the Intel 945GM chipset. 2-VU Model DC15 is based on an Intel Core Duo processor with an operating frequency of 1.83 GHz and comes with 1 GB of RAM, the capacity of which can be increased to 2 GB. The capacity of the hard disk is 60 GB or 100 GB, at the option of the buyer.

The network capabilities of the novelty are represented by an Ethernet adapter and a built-in Wi-Fi antenna (802.11b / g), and switching ones - by two USB 2.0 ports. Optional Wi-Fi adapter (Intel / Pro Wireless 3945, 802.11a / b / g), external DVD-drive and drive are available.

With Windows XP Professional preinstalled, the price for a 2-VU Model DC15 is $ 4,350.
