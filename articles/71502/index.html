Shared hosting justhost.com

Today in comments, I saw a link to the virtual hosting of justhost.com .

I looked at the description of reviews once and twice , I recently recalled friends asked to find hosting for mini business card sites. I tried to register and pay, entered all the data and payment cards, it turned out that the balance was not enough to pay. Checked, really ... I thought I will replenish the other day I will come back again. In 10 minutes a letter arrived at the post office:

Free translation:
We found from the records that today you were trying to place an order, but for some reason your card could not be processed and the registration procedure was interrupted.

We apologize for the problems that you have to face, here is a special link to receive 50% of the normal cost of our services.

This link will work 24 hours.


It turns out that when registering, you can enter the details of the empty card for the first time, get a link and within 24 hours pay Katra with the balance on the account and get hosting for 3 (!) Years for only $ 100. Cheap and angry.

ps Not advertising and not an attempt to transplant everyone to this hosting. Just a note, like life hacking.
