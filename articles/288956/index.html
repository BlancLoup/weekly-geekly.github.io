The law on registration of blogs is adopted + FAQ by law

Vote against the law can be here www.roi.ru/11762




Yesterday I already wrote ( More than three will not be collected: Blogs will need to be registered ) about the excellent amendment of the State Duma that was supposed to equate a personal blog with the media.

Today, the State Duma officially adopted the amendment in the second reading.

What exactly did you accept, what to fear, what to do?
Please note, the law enters into force on August 1, 2014. there is still time

In the last post, many doubted that the law would be adopted, but it was quite expected that it was adopted.

What changed?


- Created a new group of Internet users - bloggers.

Who is a blogger?


The owner of the site or pages of the site on the Internet, on which is placed publicly available information and access to which during the day is more than three thousand users of the Internet

Important: It does not matter which page, your business card, blog, VKontakte page, twitter, YouTube channel, or something else, any site with an audience of more than 3000 per day and which can be called ‚Äúpersonal‚Äù can be attributed to the operation of this law.
(Simply put - any site)

I have over 3000 visitors, what to do ?!


Be prepared to get into the registry, there is an alternative - en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Index_freedom_press

What does getting into the registry?


Ironically, you will not be blocked, your duties:
Entering the registry will oblige blog owners to verify the accuracy of the information posted, follow the rules of election campaigning, do not disseminate information about the private life of citizens, do not distribute extremist materials, and indicate the age limit for users.

Simply put:
1) At the entrance to the site you must have a notice - the site is intended for an audience of 21+
2) If you are going to publish: privacy information, prohibited materials, extremist or deliberately false (WHAT ?!), then you can do this by referring to another resource - on a darknet to choose from Tor / I2P or even MAGNET if it is just download files

In more detail what is forbidden
1. The owner of the site or pages of the site on the Internet, which host publicly available information and access to which during the day is more than three thousand Internet users (hereinafter referred to as the blogger) when posting and using this information, including posting such information on a given website or website page by other users of the Internet, is obliged to ensure compliance with the legislation of the Russian Federation, in particular:
1) not to allow the use of a website or a website page on the Internet for the purpose of committing criminal offenses, for disclosing information constituting a state secret or other secret protected by law for disseminating materials containing public calls for terrorist activities or publicly justifying terrorism, other extremist materials, as well as materials promoting pornography, the cult of violence and cruelty, and materials containing foul language;
2) check the accuracy of the publicly available information prior to its placement and immediately remove the false information placed;
3) to prevent the dissemination of information about the private life of a citizen in violation of civil law;
4) comply with the prohibitions and restrictions provided for by the legislation of the Russian Federation on referendum and the legislation of the Russian Federation on elections;
5) comply with the requirements of the legislation of the Russian Federation governing the procedure for the dissemination of mass information;
6) to respect the rights and legitimate interests of citizens and organizations, including the honor, dignity and business reputation of citizens, the business reputation of organizations.
2. When posting information on a website or a website page on the Internet, it is not allowed:
1) use of the site or page of the site on the Internet for the purpose of concealing or falsifying socially significant information, disseminating deliberately false information under the guise of reliable messages;
2) dissemination of information in order to discredit a citizen or certain categories of citizens on the basis of gender, age, racial or national identity, language, attitude to religion, profession, place of residence and work, as well as in connection with their political beliefs.
