Details of the secret deal Opera Software

August 26, the company Opera Software revealed some details of the "secret" agreement, which became known on July 1. The Norwegians then informed about the new agreement, according to which Opera Software will receive license fees, as well as provide for a paid basis technical support and support for their software products, but at that time they could not name the name of the partner. Yesterday, finally, this information was presented to the general public: NVIDIA Corporation became the partner of Opera Software, which chose Opera 9.5 Software Development Kit (SDK) as the main software development platform for its future models of new-generation chipsets. It is likely that one of the world leaders in the development of graphics chipsets decided to prepare in advance for themselves a place in the market of mobile devices and various television and game consoles.
