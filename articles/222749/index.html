Stylus for large smartphones

The number of processors, RAM, and, most importantly, the size of displays is growing in smartphones. Sometimes the use of a gadget with a large diagonal looks ridiculous, but this is not the main problem: using one-hand five-inch phone is hard. But this is not the largest screen size at the moment.

The Japanese, as always, offered another fun solution from latex. This is a stylus-finger for a sapper shovel of a smartphone.




The scheme of the device is simple: you just need to put the device on your finger. So you lengthen your finger by one and a half centimeters and be able to reach any point on the screen - say the experts from the company Thanko.

In addition to lengthening the finger, the gadget is positioned as a means of low responsiveness of displays.



Specifications


Dimensions: 49x25 mm
Hole diameter: 20mm
Finger magnification: 15 mm
Material: silicone
Weight: 11 g
Cost: $ 15 (so far only in Japan)
