Recessed superstition

When the time comes, the witch is here and chooses some beautiful girl. She says: "This girl should become the wife of the river god." And the preparation for the engagement ceremony begins.
After 10 days, the girl is put on jewelry and ordered to lie down on the mat. Mat is thrown into the river. She swims a few miles, and then with the girl goes under the water to the river god.
