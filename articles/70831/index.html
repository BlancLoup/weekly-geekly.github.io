Russian team "Selenokhod" joined the fight for Google Lunar X Prize



On September 21, the first Russian team, Selenohod , joined Google Lunar X Prize.

About Lunar X Prize


Let me remind you that Google Lunar X Prize is a competition with a prize fund of $ 30 million, the participants of which must safely deliver and land an unmanned lunar rover on the lunar surface.

Another interesting feature of the competition is that participating teams must be financed from private sources of at least 90%. The first prize can be awarded no later than December 31st of 2012.
