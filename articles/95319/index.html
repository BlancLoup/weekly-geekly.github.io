Why is our time - time of self-education? We have internet. ‚ÄúHe promised to share an idea, but he himself bears some kind of evidence‚Äù - you will think and you will be right. Actually, the idea.

Site for self-education with self-stimulation. Regular text and video courses online (self-education), for passing the exams for which you must pay yourself (self-stimulation). This is short. Next in steps.


I want to start learning

You register on the site, choose the course you need. Each course has a ‚Äúcost‚Äù. Starting the chosen course, this cost is deducted from your account (a somewhat incorrect word is ‚Äúwritten off‚Äù, but this will become clear later). You yourself can choose the cost of the course, but there is a minimum.

What is the course today?

Courses are divided into stages, at the end of each stage - a small test. The test may not be a test as such, it all depends on the type of training (more on that again later). Anyway, for the successful completion of each test at your expense (oh, a miracle!) Returns a portion of the "cost" of the course. Thus, if you want to return almost the entire cost of the course, you need to pass all exams.

What kind of fool would pay then?

A fool who does not want to be such anymore. I do not undertake to discuss the psychological aspects of such a scheme of education at a professional level, but for some reason it seems to me that there are quite a few people who are ready to learn this way.

And the money in my account?

Money can not be withdrawn (with this one can argue, I am not strong in monetization). They can be used as collateral (here is a more appropriate word instead of ‚Äúcost‚Äù) for other courses, after which it can be returned again with a successful surrender and mortgage again. When everyone is tired, they can be purchased, for example, useful literature on the topic of the course, or, at worst, a certificate of completion of the course. Well, or something like that.

Oh well. Where is the profit?

A small part of the cost of the course immediately goes to the owners of the service. Part of this part is the teachers (about them below). In addition, the option "say thank you" is available, which allows you to transfer the entire current account to the owners of the service.

Something said about the type of training ...

The student chooses who he will learn from before starting the course. There are 3 types of teachers:
  • Professional - a specialist in his courses, selected by the owner of the service, he has a higher minimum amount of collateral and a higher percentage of tuition fees.
  • Amateur - standard pledge and interest, but the competence may be lower. Although it may actually be higher, there is a risk here.
  • Newbie - works for free to become a Lover. Pledge standard, the lowest percentage.

And what do they do? Professionals can communicate quite tightly with a student during their studies, and instead of tests, they can offer written exams or practical tasks. Lovers, in general, can also. The main duty of all teachers is to ensure that the student does not miss something important. This means checking the test results, pointing out weak points and advising what and where to read / listen / see. In general, to guide the true path.

Any site member who has successfully completed several courses in his specialty (for example, web programming), has trained a number of students on the site and has passed the Professional test can become an amateur. There is another incentive: after some time you can start earning. Not so much money, but nice.

To become a Newbie, you just have to complete your course and test Amateur.

We impose a system of reviews / rating / karma for self-regulation and self-purification for all this nonsense, you can still spice it up with trendy social features, but everything else is to your taste.
