New debug board Radxa Rock based on SoC Rockchip RK3188

More and more, debugging boards based on the Chinese SoC appear in the world. Many previous boards were based on SoC AllWiner. New debug board Radxa Rock built on SoC Rockchip RK3188. About the possibilities of SoC Rockchip RK3188, you can read in the article Tronsmart MK908 - a new generation of mini-computers on a 4-core Rockchip RK3188 chip . The board exists in two versions:
  • Radxa rock
  • Radxa Rock Lite

Radxa is a startup that was created in Shenzhen, China, 2013, by a group of young people who love computers and high technology. According to the description, Tom Kubi (by Cubieboard ) is on the development team.

Radxa Rock debug board based on SoC Rockchip RK3188


Specification:
Processor: SoC - Rockchip RK3188 ARM Cortex-A9 quad-core @ 1.6Ghz + Mali-400 MP4 GPU
RAM: 2GB DDR3 @ 800Mhz (1GB DDR3 @ 800Mhz in the Lite version )
User memory: 8GB Nand Flash (4GB Nand Flash in Lite version ) + micro-SD SDXC up to 128GB
Video: HDMI 1.4 to 1080p @ 60hz, and AV output (tulip)
Ethernet: 10 / 100M Ethernet port Connectivity: WIFI 150Mbps 802.11b / g / n with antenna
Bluetooth: Bluetooth 4.0 ( in the Lite version is not available )
Sound: Audio S / PDIF, headphone jack
USB: 2x USB 2.0 host port, micro USB OTG
Debugging: Serial Console
Miscellaneous: IR sensor, power button, restore button, reset button, 3 LEDs, RTC (real time clock)
Expansions Header: 80-pins (pins) including GPIO, I2C, SPI, Line in, USB 2.0, PWM, ADC, LCD, GPS, etc.
Size: 10x8cm, about 1.2 cm in height.

Block diagram of the Radxa Rock debug board

The debug board supports Android and Linux. Video acceleration will not be supported in Linux until a solution is found (maybe libhybris?). Schemes in PDF format for download are available by reference.
About 100 engineering sample boards are now available, which are available at half the initial cost. Mass production of boards will begin soon.

The cost should be $ 89 for Radxa Rock, and $ 69 for Radxa Rock Lite.
You can pre-order on the official website radxa.com .
