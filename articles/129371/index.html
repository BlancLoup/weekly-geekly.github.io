The Humble Frozen Synapse Bundle

The continuation of the grand sale of games from independent developers.



The idea is that you can purchase Frozen Synapse at any price.

Plus, if you pay more than the average amount paid by other members, you get access to the Humble Bundle Frozenbyte. This collection includes the following games:
  • Trine
  • Shadowgrounds: Survivor
  • Shadowgrounds
  • Splot
  • Jack claw

The last two games are in development. One game is presented in the form of a prototype, another organizers promise to add to the kit later.
