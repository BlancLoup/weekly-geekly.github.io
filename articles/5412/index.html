Want to sell a website?

If you replenish your site or keep a blog for several months, but do not know what to do next (priorities have changed, interest has disappeared)?

Despite the reason why you are ready to abandon your site, there are always people on the Internet who are ready to buy your blog (the only question is the price with which you agree or disagree).

Before looking for a place to sell or organize an auction for your project, consider a few important points:

1. Customers always want to see site statistics and server logs (unique visitors, number of page views, visitor geography, indicators on Alexa). You can either immediately give this information to interested persons by filling in the appropriate boxes when registering at the auction, or provide it at the request of potential buyers.

2. If you use Adsense, Amazon Affiliates or another advertising system for profit, be prepared to share financial indicators. Potential buyers will use them as key when making a purchase decision.
