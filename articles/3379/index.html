US Presidential Candidate Launches Second Life Campaign

Blogger Steve O'Hir, in his journal on ZDNet, said that John Edwards, one of the presidential candidates in the United States, had begun his election campaign in the virtual world of Second Life.



The representative of Edwards in Second Life is Jeremy Richie (Jerimee Richir), and his character is called Jose Rote (Jose Rote). According to Richier, the virtual presidential project is his initiative, but the Edwards team knows about it and fully supports it.
