Solving the problem of synchronizing RSS-subscriptions on multiple computers



While the entire Internet community is testing ReadAir , a desktop application for working with Google Reader , I discovered a great alternative that solves my old wish list .

An alternative is to use a bundle from the Newsgator service and a desktop client for it - FeedDemon .

As a result of this symbiosis, all RSS subscriptions added to the client, or an online service, are synchronized with each other, allowing you to easily access news channels of interest to us from any computer on which FeedDemon is installed, or through a browser.

You can register for the service directly at newsgator.com , or during the installation of the client on the computer.

The useful detail can also be attributed to the ability to import a list of subscriptions from various online services and formats, including OPML , through which I imported my subscriptions from Opera .
