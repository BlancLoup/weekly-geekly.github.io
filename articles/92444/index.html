The share of Internet Explorer 6 fell below Opera Mini. Don't you test your sites in it yet?

Today, the share of Internet Explorer 6 is about 7-8% and continues to fall. However, the popularity of this browser is still large enough to deny its users the ability to use your sites. Most developers still test each function for compatibility with this browser, some even struggle with pixel-by-pixel layout. But for some reason, many people forget about another browser that is gaining popularity. It's about Opera Mini. With the development of mobile technology, people are increasingly accessing the Internet from their mobile. If you believe liveinternet.ru, Opera Mini is used to surf more often than the notorious IE6! Maybe let's test our sites in the most popular mobile browser? Moreover, for this you do not need a mobile phone .
