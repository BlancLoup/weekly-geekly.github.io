Finalists of the Technovations 2012 Cup

Two days later, the Technical Innovations Cup Final will be held. It reached 13 projects selected by experts from more than 200 from across the country. The winner will receive a cup and a cash prize, and all the finalists will be given the opportunity to participate in closed investment sessions of venture capital funds-partners of the competition and much more.
I propose to get acquainted with the projects finalists:

Cytodel
Insiderpharm.com

The project team is developing a device for loading anticancer drugs into red blood cells. The need for this is due to the fact that traditional anticancer treatment is carried out with highly toxic drugs that cause irreversible negative changes throughout the body. The use of the device will reduce side effects during the course of treatment, while improving the quality of life and well-being of patients.

DendriVax
Medvio.ru/dendrivax

The developed DendriVax vaccine is designed to treat various types of cancer, primarily prostate cancer (2nd place in the structure of cancer mortality, more than 25,000 patients annually). Also, the vaccine is able to effectively deal with diseases such as breast cancer, stomach cancer, lung cancer, colorectal cancer, etc. An important feature of DendriVax is the possibility of use in advanced cases. The basic technology has already shown effectiveness in pilot clinical trials.

Kiparis 3d
Skb-kiparis.ru

The project team develops fast 3D prototyping devices (3D printer), and also promotes 3D printing and 3D modeling technologies. The main innovations are a convenient and intuitive interface, almost inertialess work mechanics.

Labicom.net
labicom.net

The project creates a set of software tools that allow the owner of a remote or virtual laboratory to make it accessible via the Internet. The system allows the laboratory to earn money by selling the time spent on working with equipment to interested parties.

Lactocore
Lactocore.com

The project develops an innovative nutritional supplement for feeding newborns, which contributes to the harmonious development of the child‚Äôs nervous system. Doing with food, the product compensates for the lack of bioregulators.

MD Voice
The project team is developing a mobile solution to identify trends in the vocal tract. It will solve the existing problem with the definition of diseases of the throat in the early stages, for example, cancer of the larynx. The solution can be used as a separate application, as well as possible to integrate into existing software, for example, Skype.

Asynchronous motor with combined windings
YouTube video
A new design of the electric motor has been developed, which makes it possible to achieve improvements in key operating parameters, for example, efficiency.

Multiphase flow meter
A multiphase flow meter is a device for non-separation measurement of the flow rate of a multi-phase product from an oil and gas production well. The novelty of the project lies in the synthesis of hydrodynamics and acoustics of multiphase fluid and mathematical methods of signal processing. The solution allows you to get information about the flow rate of each well online.

Microwave oscillator based on spin electronics
The project is developing a powerful, miniature, easily customizable microwave oscillator with the ability to integrate with other devices. According to the calculations, it will have improved performance in terms of power and relative level of stability than existing analogues.

Development of technology for producing semiconductor heterostructures with quantum dots by the method of ion-beam deposition for the needs of solar energy
As the name implies, the project team is working to create a technology for producing semiconductor heterostructures for the needs of solar energy. Solar cells created on the basis of this technology will have high efficiency (about 40%) and low cost.

Energy efficient heating control
The system improves heating efficiency by using the weather forecast. The system integrates with existing climate control equipment.

Effective three-layer monolithic products with a nanostructured transition layer
Layered wall fencing products with improved properties and energy-saving technology of their accelerated production have been developed. The use of this technology significantly reduces the cost of products.
