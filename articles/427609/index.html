Solving an equation with integer division without enumeration

On the same day, they all drank, and at VT decided to continue, but there was no more money left. Then they handed over the bottles, added yesterday's surrender, and re-stocked everything, just like yesterday. The same was done on Wed and Thu. And in PT money was exactly one bottle.
