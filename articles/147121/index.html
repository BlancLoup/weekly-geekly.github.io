Vacancy of the administrator / business analyst MS Dynamics Navision

Good afternoon, colleagues!
Our company ( Cardiff Insurance Company, part of the international banking group BNP Paribas) has opened a vacancy for the administrator / business analyst of the internal Navision system.

The system has implemented financial and operational units, the latter is very strongly customized. Now the system has about 30 users. The system is not integrated with anything, data is obtained from it for building reports in MS Reporting Services (reports are handled by an individual).

At the moment we are attracting two companies to support the system. One will be engaged in strategic development, the second operational support.

The person who takes the vacant position should:
1) Maintain end users
2) Administer the system (including users, roles, security, updates, etc.)
3) Configure the system when possible.
4) Set tasks and control outsourcers
5) Monitor the relevance of the documentation (it will be written by outsourcers).

Conditions: salary 50-80K rubles (white), you can discuss + LCA after the probationary period.

Requirements:
* relevant experience (3-5 years)
* the ability to document the system (notation, etc.)
* knowledge of the subject area - finance
* Experience in the insurance market is a big plus
* experience in western companies - plus
* advanced communication skills

The office is located in Moscow, metro Dmitrovskaya.
