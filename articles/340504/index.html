Issue price - 10 million. Softline Venture Partners accepts applications in the IT business accelerator

Hi, Habr. The Softline investment department together with several vendors and development institutions decided to distribute some money (up to 10 million for each startup) to young companies for a share in the projects. Where, if not here, to talk about this venture? If you create (or know where to find) projects in the areas of cloud, mobile solutions, cybersecurity, IoT, lead generation technologies, legaltech and Fintech, machine learning and artificial intelligence, we need to seriously talk.



Why take part in the accelerator?


If you are a young IT project with a prototype or product (MVP) and would like to refine it, pump skills, adjust business processes and find investors both within ‚ÄúSUPPORT OF RUSSIA‚Äù, among interested state structures and large corporations, and beyond then send a request for the selected destination.

In which areas can I apply?


Cloud technologies, mobility, information security, Internet of things (IoT) (this direction is supervised by IBM), digital technologies (digital), legal technologies (LegalTech) (supervised by the Skolkovo Foundation), Financial technologies (FinTech, including blockchain), machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI).

What is the deadline for submitting applications?


Call for applications is open from October 4 to December 1, 2017 inclusive on the site. Acceleration of projects will take place from December to February. In March, at its completion, there will be a demo day, where you can see the achievements of projects. The platform for the accelerator events will be the Skolkovo Innovation Center.

What is the result?


According to the results of acceleration, the teams that showed the most convincing results of development will be able to receive investments in the amount of 10 million rubles.

Who are the coaches?


Accelerator participants will learn from the best business leaders, will have access to extensive opportunities to refine and test products and technological solutions. Accelerator experts will be the leading experts of OPORA RUSSIA, Softline Venture Partners, IBM and Skolkovo, and accelerator partners.

Additional bonuses


The opportunity to join the startup program of the IBM Global Entrepreneur program , access the IBM Bluemix platform, and also become a Skolkovo resident (when following established procedures ).

Leave an application and learn more details on the site . Participation is free.
