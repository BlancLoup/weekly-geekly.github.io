Remote work, is it good or bad?

Hello. In this article I would like to share my thoughts about remote work. Yes, yes, yes, there are already hundreds of these materials, again another garbage ... , but I think that my thoughts will not take up too much space on the Habr servers, and the decision about the time spent and efforts is made voluntarily.

I worked remotely for quite a long time (4 years), and had a lot of work in offices (about 3 years), here I would like to talk about the pros and cons of remote work for a programmer.

I think that the stereotype of a remote player lying on the beach with a laptop has long been debunked, but still I would like to talk with you about why remote work is good / bad.



The first topic I would like to talk about (probably one of the most important) is the market.


At first glance, the market plays into the hands of office work, but is it all so simple? Yes, of course, for residents of Moscow, St. Petersburg, Yekaterinburg, Minsk, Kiev, Kharkov, and other (not all) millionaires, the choice of work is quite large. Especially programmers of the two capitals of Russia can sense an abundance of vacancies and companies. But residents of regional cities with a population of 500 thousand, no longer have to grasp the long lists of vacancies from local companies. There is already either move or work remotely, or look for something in your city. And if you look at My Circle , then on January 14 you can see the total number of vacancies in 941 pieces, and with the mark ‚ÄúCan be remotely‚Äù 384 , which is 40.81 % of the total number of vacancies. Pretty good, isn't it?

It would seem that for lovers of work from home 40.81% is a good indicator. But it is understood that there are several factors to consider.

For example:

  1. It is logical to assume that the competition for jobs with a remote potentially higher.
    • Most of the programmers working in the office are ready to consider the option of remote work.
    • Due to the lack of attachment to the location, the number of candidates is increasing.

  2. It is believed that the lead is difficult to find a remote job. I did not come across this, but as for Senior, for such a level of developers, I constantly observe vacancies.
  3. Companies "Giants" can afford to choose the best personnel and transport them to him. Accordingly, if you want to work in Google, then most likely, the remote is not for you.
    Although in one podcast (I don‚Äôt remember where exactly), I heard about a Go-developer working remotely at Yandex. So maybe not so simple.
  4. On the remote is less salary. There is such an opinion. I will not argue or try to refute it. Difficult question on which, everyone has their own opinion.

Development


I heard polar opinions about the speed of development of the technical level of a specialist remotely and in the office. But if there is a goal to become a good specialist, what if not self-development and self-education will affect raising the qualifications of a specialist? Is remote work good or bad?


Of course, everyone will determine for himself, and the question itself is incorrect. I love remote work and I enjoy working at home sometimes. I consider my remote work experience as good and glad that I have it.

Jobs work discord, I think it is obvious that a specialist can work well in an office in one company, and work poorly at home in another, and vice versa. However, if I decided to move to the remote work entirely (for example: I would move to a small city), then I would take care of a few things:

Need to take care of your workplace


If working from home is the only source of income and this perspective is seen in the next few years, or even the whole life. First of all, you need a comfortable workplace. Investing in a computer, a comfortable chair, and even a separate office is an excellent and right decision.

Of course, not everyone can easily get out of his pocket $ 3000 to buy a super-powerful PC, multiple monitors and an expensive headset. But striving for the most comfortable working conditions is necessary. This will contribute to high performance and enjoyment of work.

Nobody says that the absence of an employee behind the workplace for a short period of time is a crime. Everyone may need to retire for various reasons.
