Malta as a new direction for IT professionals

If you thought about the sun, the sea and yachts - then you understood correctly.



The sun shines here most of the year, and even in the winter, which is sometimes rainy, often the clouds evaporate and all your problems melt away in the sun. At least with me it is.

The climate is suitable for all lovers of sun and sea. Almost always sunny, even in winter. Although once in 3 years the winter is very rainy, and at a temperature of +14 and the wind can be quite cool. But the island blooms with greenery!

The Republic of Malta is located in the Mediterranean Sea, in the Maltese archipelago 93 km south of the island of Sicily. It consists of two inhabited islands of Malta and Gozo and one little inhabited island of Comino (from wiki).

The main island is Malta. There is the capital Valletta and the whole business. Gozo is more for living away from the noise, and Comino is known for a magnificent blue lagoon.


Blue lagoon

I live on the island of Malta. Although it is the largest of the 3 islands, its length is no more than 30 km, and its width is 15 km. But it is not felt at all. When you live some time on the island, 5 km seem a great distance.

The best way to get to know the island is to take hop on hop off tours. So you can visit the most interesting places, and although there are not very many of them here, some of them are amazing. Work in IT


Due to low taxes (relative to other EU countries), Malta is very attractive for foreign companies, and especially for iGaming companies. Perhaps the largest foreign company in Malta - Betsson Group has about 1,000 employees. Most of its IT office is also located on the island.

Low taxes and a warm climate are also attractive for start-ups, in connection with which there are too many of them and companies such as startupmalta gradually emerge. But the startup sphere is still in the early stages of its development and therefore not interested in recruiters. Further in the article I will tell about this in more detail.

I myself came to Malta 3 years ago through a recruiter who offered a job in a German company in the field of horse betting. At that time I only heard about Malta, but knew nothing. I was curious and so I found myself on the island.

As a rule, we need senior and middle level specialists. Front-end, Backend, Fullstack, DevOps, QA, Product owner. Medical care, in my opinion, is quite a good level. Central Hospital Mater Dei is well equipped. Of the shortcomings of local medical care, and I would single out long lines. Rental of property


Rent 2k, 3k apartments in good condition in popular cities will cost ‚Ç¨ 700-1200. 1k and 2k apartments are less common and the price is not much lower. Most IT companies are concentrated in the cities of St Julian's and Sliema, respectively, the cost of renting housing there is one of the highest.
