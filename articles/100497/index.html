All at ISDEF'2010

We invite all habrayusers to the annual " Independent Software Developer Forum " (ISDEF).

The ninth autumn conference ISDEF'2010 will be held September 30 - October 3, 2010 in Moscow (Holiday Inn Vinogradovo).

The event will be devoted to analyzing the situation in the international software market and the development of companies operating in the field of IT. The conference will be attended by owners, top managers and leading specialists of IT and Internet companies, representatives of Western and Russian investment funds, marketing specialists, representatives of PR services and journalists.

The main topics of the conference:
  • marketing and software sales technology;
  • legal aspects of software companies;
  • financial investments in the IT market;
  • personnel management and personnel issues;
  • the work of a PR service in an IT company;
  • business growth and development issues.

Participation in the conference is paid - $ 200. For members of the Association ISDEF - free.

You can register for ISDEF'2010 on the conference website.
