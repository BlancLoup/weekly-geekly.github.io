Do not miss the virtual hackathon from Microsoft with the support of Forbes



Microsoft, with the support of Forbes, presents the Virtual Hackathon !

Together with our partners, we give young developers a chance to test their strength and gain valuable experience:
  • skills in solving real business cases relevant to Russian business;
  • unique industry insights from the project partners - leading Russian companies;
  • networking and mentoring from large companies;
  • wide coverage of the project and mentioning the winners at the leading thematic sites, in the Microsoft communities and on the pages of the Forbes portal;
  • prizes from the partners of the Hackathon and the possibility of continuing to work with them after the end of the project.

Thanks to the Microsoft BizSpark start-up support program, Hackathon members will receive free access to a set of modern Microsoft software, including Microsoft Azure development tools and cloud resources that support Open Source technologies.

Virtual Hackathon will last until May 30. All this time, experts from Microsoft and partners of the project will work with the participants: Alfa-Bank , Beeline , VKontakte , FRIA and Sports.ru . In addition, Beeline Data School will act as a special expert for teams working with Big Data and help them develop the most powerful solutions using Machine Learning technologies.
