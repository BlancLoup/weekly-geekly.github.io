Philco Radio - home media center in retrostyle



Philco Radio is another good example of how you can give a second life to old things. Inside the classic stationary receiver of the 50s of the last century is built Mac mini. A touch screen is added as a navigation blog and the multimedia home media center is working again, khe-khe, just like 60 years ago. You can play music, you can watch a movie.
