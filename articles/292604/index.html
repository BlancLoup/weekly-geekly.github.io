State Duma deputies propose to charge a fee from foreign aggregators for using news from Russian media

A new initiative appeared in the State Duma: a proposal to oblige foreign aggregators to pay for the news of domestic media. The project can be implemented by amending the law, which will allow a foreign news aggregator to ban the use of information published by the Russian media for free, writes RBC. The proposal itself was made by State Duma deputy from the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia Vadim Dengin.
