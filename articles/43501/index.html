Does Motorola reduce staff and completely switch to Android?

Not so long ago, information appeared that Motorola is expanding its Android department right up to 350 people. Now it became clear that this is part of the company 's anti-crisis strategy , which includes a serious restructuring of the mobile phone business to reduce costs (now this business only brings losses). As part of the new strategy, a massive staff reduction is planned, as well as the transition to the Android platform.

Sanjay Jha, a new co-director of the company, initiated the reforms. He came to the company in August and immediately launched a stormy activity. Details of his plans and the exact number of dismissed Mr. Jha will report tomorrow at a corporate meeting. It should be noted that over the past two years, Motorola has already reduced about 10,000 people.

What can I add? If Android allows you to reduce production costs and significantly reduce the cost of mobile devices, then we can welcome similar actions from all manufacturers. It is a pity that they are capable of decisive actions only during a crisis.
