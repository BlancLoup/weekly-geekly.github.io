Comstar-UTS and MTS organize WLAN-roaming between their Wi-Fi networks

Comstar-United TeleSystems and Mobile TeleSystems announced the launch of Russia's first sms-authentication service, which allows the user to pay for Wi-Fi services from his subscriber account with a mobile operator. In the framework of the same project, WLAN roaming between the Comstar-UTS hot spot networks and MTS is organized. Now you can pay for Wi-Fi services from your personal account at MTS in the hot-spots of Comstar-UTS and MTS.

The sms-authentication service assumes that the subscriber receives a login and password for accessing the Internet on the territory of any Wi-Fi-MTS hot spot, for which the MTS subscriber sends an SMS message to 1106 or 1107 short number. As a result of the transaction from his personal account $ 5 or $ 10 is charged, which are required respectively for a one-hour or two-hour Internet access session via the Wi-Fi-MTS network.
