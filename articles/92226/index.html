GameDev: where to go to work?

Recently, games and applications have become very popular in social networks. In addition to increasing the time spent by visitors on the site, applications bring a good income to their creators and owners of social. networks. Social games brought last year a total revenue of about $ 30 million (of course on the Russian market). As a result, many companies rushed into the development of social games. Now large holdings and small studios are engaged in this, but as in the entire IT industry, companies engaged in a relatively new direction feel the need for experienced employees.

I made a small overview of vacancies in firms of developers of games for social networks (collecting them by company). Especially for those who are looking for work in the game development industry. The list of companies under consideration includes companies from Top-10 for April compiled by socialplay.ru , as well as several companies that are still developing.
