The company's accounting department was desperate to receive accounting data accurately and on time, because ‚Äúall sorts of things‚Äù in the office already had about a thousand units spinning, try to count and figure out what is what on a piece of paper.

Scene One: "TZ"

There was a task to automate the accounting of the OS, the guys connected with us, looked at the description of our software products on the data-mobile.ru website, got in touch with each other , talked. Automation of barcode accounting always starts with labeling. We recommended the TSC TX200 label printer . The printer is designed for thermal transfer printing, which is important for the inventory, because the labels must be properly printed and retain their characteristics for a long time, do not fade and not be erased.

To automate the labeling process, we used 1C processing, which allowed

Scene Three: ‚ÄúMobile Inventory Device‚Äù


You could even use a phone with DataMobile software to read barcodes , but there was a question of promptness; after all, the amount of work was not small and nobody wanted to scan before the second coming. Without hesitation, they decided to purchase a mobile data collection terminal . TSD scans barcodes incomparably faster and is intended for corporate use. The device picked up a budget and fresh - then he just appeared on the market - Honeywell EDA50 . Despite the low price, stuffing it allows you to quickly and easily process a large amount of information. TSA EDA50 runs on Android OS, has a large touch screen and a protected case.
In the office of the radio station there is no single WiFi network, there are various remote premises, soundproofing studios, etc. However, this is not a problem for our software, it does not need a WiFi network everywhere. The TSD loads data into itself and performs an inventory, and upon subsequent connection to the network or PC unloads it. Everything is simple and convenient.



Scene Four, Typical: ‚ÄúWe here remembered that we forgot‚Äù


The customer purchased the necessary equipment and software license, delivered our processing for 1C, marked the property. Dealing with DataMobile Invent for kids was not difficult. The software is intuitive for the user and ready to work, all instructions are available, integration into basic configurations takes a matter of minutes. For additional questions we have an online help-desk . In short, everything went like clockwork.

But about a month later, their IT specialist contacts us and says that new radio accounts have appeared in the radio accounting department. The company is still registered with low-value fixed assets (computer mice, keyboards, etc.). Usually, they are already labeled with factory barcodes, and how to get them into the contour of the inventory process with DataMobile Invent was not entirely clear. The low estimate is taken into account quite differently, it has its own bar codes, it implies the input of the quantity (and this is the specificity of the commodity accounting), in the accounting department for it are completely different accounting documents.


Scene Five: "Any whim for your money"


It was necessary to adapt the processing of the exchange with 1c for marking ‚Äúlow prices‚Äù and loading the result into a regular inventory document. It also required the program to understand such positions and process it differently. Our developers quickly implemented this refinement in the base program.

In the software package, a revision was implemented, in which operating systems with certain attributes (for example, ‚Äúlow-value ones‚Äù) were distributed to separate directories. When uploading a document for recalculation on the TSD , the refinement in DataMobile Invent software made it possible to understand that if this is an object with a ‚Äúlow score‚Äù attribute, then the quantity input for this item is available. If the item was from the list of normal operating systems, then the input quantity in the mobile application remained inactive.
After the inventory, when uploading the results to 1C, the revision by 1C automatically distributed everything to the relevant documents in accordance with the type of OS:

  • filling in various documents of the inventory of the type of OS
  • relocation or withdrawal documents, depending on the type of OS

A week later, with the help of DataMobile Invent, the client was able to start working with "low-value" OS. The entire accounting of goods and materials became possible to maintain centrally with the help of one program, and load the result into the necessary accounting documents. Just magic!

Epilogue


As a result, the customer received a means of operational accounting of fixed assets, including low-value ones, using a single software tool. The term of launching a fully automated inventory process from scratch, taking into account all the necessary improvements to the needs of the client - 1 month.
