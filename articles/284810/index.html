Internal rating system in anarchy mode


In the first part, I already talked about the differences between the regime of Anarchy and the Monarchy when creating a forum.

But let's take a closer look at the Anarchy mode.
In this mode, there are no administrators and moderators, the forum is moderated on the basis of a general vote.
Each user has an internal rating (analogue of karma). If the rating is high, the user‚Äôs trust is high, and if it‚Äôs low, no one sees the user‚Äôs edit.

The principle of wikipedia is preserved here, everyone can edit other people's posts and even change the structure of the forum! (add and delete sections, topics, forums, under the forums)

But any editing can be both accepted and canceled by means of voting.

There is a Bitcoin network rule here - if at least 51% of users are sane and vote for correct edits and not for vandalism, the forum will live.

Integration of Osiris and I2P


You can integrate these two networks in two ways:
1) Make an Isis gate on the i2p network
2) Configure proxy access to the Osiris node on the I2P network

Moreover, in both cases you will have up-to-date data, because the i2p network has access to the external Internet, then the changes will be synchronized.
