Work mechanics:

  2. Upon the provision of the service to the client, manually or automatically, an SMS message is sent with a request to evaluate the quality of work.

  4. Depending on the settings, the client is sent a final SMS with thanks, a discount code, etc.
  5. The obtained customer ratings are available in the form of reports (see below) or can be automatically downloaded to the external system.
