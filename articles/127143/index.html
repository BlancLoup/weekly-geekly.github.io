Nokia Announced New Symbian Belle Smartphones - Nokia 600, Nokia 700 and Nokia 701


On Habr√©, the notice of three new Nokia smartphones, which took place on August 24 in Hong Kong, passed unnoticed. Despite a radical change in the global strategy, Nokia continues to work on Symbian and releases both software updates and new devices on Symbian. In my opinion, smartphones have turned out very interesting and can compete with competitors in their price segment. As a software developer for Symbian, I was very pleased with the new smartphones.
All smartphones are based on a single-core ARM 11 processor with a frequency of 1 GHz, contain 512 MB of RAM. According to the manufacturer, the GPU in new smartphones is four times faster than the GPU on the models of the current generation (Nokia N8, E7, etc.). All smartphones have a built-in NFC chip, and the new version of Symbian, Symbian Belle, is able to work perfectly with it. The screen resolution remains traditional for Symbian - 360x640. Also, each smartphone line can shoot video in HD, has a built-in FM-receiver, GPS, Bluetooth 3.0, WiFi b / g / n, USB host. From the bad - in all smartphones of the ruler, a camera without autofocus (again ...).

Nokia 600


Nokia claims it is the loudest smartphone they have ever created. In addition, this is the most budget model in the line.


Features:
  • 111 x 53 x 13-14.25 mm, 100 g
  • Display 3.2 ", 360x640, TFT
  • 5 megapixel camera with LED flash
  • 1200 mAh battery
  • Built-in 2 GB memory, microSD support (up to 32 GB)
  • FM transmitter



Nokia 700


Nokia claims it is the smallest smartphone they have ever created.


Features:
  • 110 x 50 x 9.7 mm, 96 g
  • Display 3.2 ", 360x640, AMOLED
  • Gorilla glass
  • 5 megapixel camera with LED flash
  • 1080 mAh battery
  • Built-in 2 GB memory, microSD support (up to 32 GB)



Nokia 701


Nokia claims it is the brightest smartphone on the market. This smartphone has a high-quality display and uses the proprietary Clear Black Display technology to improve image quality.


Features:
  • 117.2 x 56.8 x 11 mm, 131 g
  • Display 3.5 ", 360x640, IPS-LCD
  • 8 megapixel camera with dual LED flash
  • Clear black display
  • Gorilla glass
  • Front camera for video calls
  • 1300 mAh battery
  • Built-in 8 GB memory, microSD support (up to 32 GB)
  • FM transmitter



Price


The most interesting thing in my opinion is the prices. Smartphones will be available in the third quarter at the following prices:
Nokia 600 - 200 Euro
Nokia 700 - 270 euro
Nokia 701 - 290 Euro
