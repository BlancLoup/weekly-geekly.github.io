Firefox has shifted IE from first place in Europe

In December, a significant event for the browser world took place: Internet Explorer lost its first place in Europe. According to StatCounter for December, the number of Firefox users was 38.11%, and IE - 37.52%.

As stated by the head of StatCounter Edan Cullen (Aodhan Cullen), the reason for this was not the growing popularity of Firefox, but a decrease in the number of IE users. Including due to the transition of some of them to Chrome.

Chrome still occupies the 3rd position in Europe, but has a good growth rate. From 5.06% in December 2009 to 14.58% in December 2010.
