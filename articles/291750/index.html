We will sort out the large online stores and do the same on a cloud platform

Megamind, hello! The owners of Megamind helped our platform to create Digistr.ru stores - they gave us a Startup tariff so that we could share information about the development of our project. This is what we are going to do now. If the first pancake is lumpy, do not judge strictly, we will quickly correct it in the process of work.

For dating, to demonstrate the current level of development, we decided to make a demonstration online store that will show the maximum capabilities of our platform. In addition, we will describe in detail what and how it works in similar stores on the example of other well-known projects, as well as how it is implemented in our country.

Under the cut I will tell you more about the development methodology.



We studied the TOP20 online stores of Russia according to Forbes , made a list of the capabilities of these online stores and implemented them in our platform Digistr.ru .

Every week we will publish a report with a detailed study of the capabilities of top stores and how they are made with us. For example, we are implementing this design . By possibilities, it turned out to be most similar to the top stores. We will implement missing and specific modules in it.

We make the maximum of opportunities that can be designed as you like, which is required from a serious shopping platform. The material turned out to be about 150-200 pages (there are many photos, and also text), so we divided it into several sections:

  1. home page and storefront layouts ;
  2. catalog and card product ;
  3. checkout and personal account;
  4. services, automation, integration;
  5. SEO opportunities for stores;
  6. information sections;
  7. store management and refinement.

We will publish in parts, otherwise it will not be possible to study. The purpose of this series of publications is to tell the most comprehensive and detailed about the development of a serious online store, show everything with real examples and demonstrate this development on a cloud platform for online stores.

  1. to make a clear task for the developers of your store with examples and descriptions of what should work and how (of course, competent developers will correct you if suddenly something inappropriate for your store is chosen);
