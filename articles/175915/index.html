Some theory

In this case, there is a need for at least several instructions, more precisely, ‚Äústandard operating procedures‚Äù (SOP, English version - SOP).

1. The procedure for initiating a review of the implementation of the task. There may be a lot of ideas. Too much. Mad people enough.
