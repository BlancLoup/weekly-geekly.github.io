SAPE

You probably already know what happened at sape.ru. If you are an optimizer or webmaster, you certainly know. For those who are not in the subject, let me remind you: sape.ru is the largest exchange in RuNet for buying and selling links. With the help of this exchange sell links more than 160 000 sites.
A week ago, the administration of the resource changed the conditions of work with the exchange. Now all calculations are made in rubles, all participants must re-register and conclude an offer agreement with two CSCs (Partner Service Center, each of which is a separate legal entity) - they enter money through the first, and withdraw through the second. In this regard, significantly reduced the profits of webmasters.


The situation with Sape, I think, will be interesting not only to optimizers and webmasters, because many services (freelance exchanges, copywriters exchanges, Blogun, banner networks, etc.) carry out financial relationships with users just like sape until October 1 . In this case, it is not so important that the exchange sells links. Interesting financial moments.
