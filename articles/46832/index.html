For those who wanted to see what was inside

As promised , we made a clone of the Buyer and put it on test.pokupator.ru so that those who wanted to see what was inside could do it without making fake requests on the combat server. You can not only try to place your test application, but also yourself for it (and for test applications of other users) and bargain on behalf of one of the stores.

To place a request, an invite is not needed. After you have placed an application, our staff will process it, tie it to the item card, if necessary, and take it to the auction. Calls from the call center will not be. Also, if you specify a specific product model, it will be indicated that you allowed the stores to offer not only this model, but also models similar in characteristics (on the battle server you can prohibit doing this in a conversation with the call center operator).

You can bargain for your and other people's applications by logging in under the store here . We have made for this 3 stores with the following logins to enter the system:
