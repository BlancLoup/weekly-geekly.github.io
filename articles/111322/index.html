Mouse from Microsoft. Arc touch


Recently, such a beast fell into my hands. New mouse from Microsoft - Arc Touch. It is interesting that instead of the central scroll wheel there is a touchback with reverse feedback.

Compare the old and new model



The new model is significantly different from the previous one (Arc Mouse). First of all, the part on which the palm lies is made of matte plastic, pleasant to the touch.

To turn off the Arc Mouse mouse, it had to be folded.


Mouse Arc Touch, on the contrary you need to straighten


IMHO, quite an interesting solution, and when folded / opened there is a click.
