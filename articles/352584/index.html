Do the world need technical certificates?

Some IT specialists and HR recruiters believe that the level of knowledge gained in a Russian or foreign university, as well as project experience, is more important than a certificate. There is some truth in this. Students and specialists who graduated from our universities brilliantly know how to solve business problems. However, it is no secret to anyone that the movement of the Russian IT market is highly susceptible to events in large global markets.



In this regard, obtaining a certificate indicates an understanding of global processes and awareness of potential new niches that open up new business prospects. And the Russian market shows a growing interest in such knowledge. Preparation options


Depending on which exam you are going to take, Microsoft will offer you the most effective training scheme. On the page of each exam , all the recommended resources, practical test tasks and now such a plan of preparation are already collected:



Authorized Courses at Microsoft Learning Centers


And, actually, item number one in the recommended training options.

There are several authorized training centers in Russia (below are detailed information) where you can receive training in preparation for the exam:

  • Offline in class.
  • Remotely.
  • In the format of open training (you can choose the pace of classes).

At the end of the course, you will receive in addition an official certificate from Microsoft: about the successful completion of the training.

So, the advantages of this method of preparation:

  • Resources, teaching methods - all prepared by Microsoft. Always the most current information.
  • In authorized centers courses are taught by certified teachers. You get the opportunity to ask a question, deal with difficulties.
  • For practical work, you have everything you need: virtual machines are pre-configured, there are special stands, demo environments.
  • Group classes. The effect of the team does its work: common themes, new acquaintances, inspirational examples.

Cons :

  • Pleasure is not cheap.
  • Duration of training is 5 days (40 academic hours).

Official Microsoft Online Courses and MS Press Books


For self-study, Microsoft offers online courses on the Open EDx platform. This is the most current online content that Microsoft releases. Courses are updated every few months.

For those who like to page through and replenish the library - paper editions. They can be purchased at the official Microsoft Press store. Not cheap, but I must say that this is the most comprehensive source of information for training.

So, the advantages of self-training:

  • Resources, for the most part, absolutely free.
  • You are not bound by the attendance of courses and you can choose the pace you want.

Cons :

  • You should be a disciplined enough person not to stretch your exam preparation for a long time.
  • The most current technology content in English only.

Language preparation and delivery


As we wrote above, you can prepare for the exam in Russian and in English. On the one hand, the Russian language is more familiar and familiar - but as applied to exams it can play a cruel joke. Firstly, not all terms in the translation version have the same obvious clarity, as in English. Sometimes the questions in the translated version look much more complicated and flatter than in the original version. Secondly, all the latest product documentation first appears in English and only then is localized into Russian - i.e. initially focusing on studying the material in English, you expand the range of resources available for training.

Well, in conclusion - if you take certification as a step towards an international career - then in international projects, English will come in handy for you a lot more! But, for the sake of justice, let us say that it is possible to take in Russian, and the flow of those who donate significant.
