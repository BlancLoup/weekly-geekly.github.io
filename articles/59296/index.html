Muzicons audio player site has ceased to be audio hosting

We have to admit that the situation with musical widgets has worsened compared with the February analysis of our colleague Bukasa .

This is what happened: the same Muzicons sound player site, which suffered a disaster in January and collected money at Habrahabr (and which even a little from me then), apparently still suffered some disaster in the end.

Now on the front page, Muzicons no longer offers to upload MP3s to the site. In addition, I personally managed to notice that all the MP3s I downloaded there before were no longer available, and the corresponding muzicons suddenly stopped working.

Now they offer to specify only the URL of the external MP3-file. This means that the Muzicons site, formerly hosting sound files, has now become a simple sound translator from other hosts to its sound players. Now you can use it, at best, based on the attractiveness of the appearance of its sound players, but not the need to place an MP3 file on the Internet.

It is sad.
