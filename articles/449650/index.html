From forum to video - the way of the graphoman

Good day everyone. If you like to draw letters on paper, or pixels on the screen, and especially when it adds up to some complete thoughts, you will come to the decision to share it with others around you. The reasons can be very different from the banal curiosity to professional interest. And if you are in addition to a graphomania like me, then you generally have no choice.


I went through all the stages from an anonymous blog to writing professional articles, and recently a cherry appeared on this layer cake. Now I can say with confidence that I have tried all the ways of self-expression that modern technologies provide. What I want to talk about.



Forums


The simplest and most initial stage. There is nothing easier than a mood to participate in any discussion on hundreds of pages. And the number of users and the administrative hierarchy add different tastes to this dish. You can choose any role from an expert on growing chrysanthemums to a zealous opponent of the forum administration.


My active participation in the public forums took place in the years 2000-2008. I even managed to become the administrator of the people in the forum of 100 thousand people, where the children of spree, intrigue, and all the orgy created by young people with unlimited access to a computer were going on. It got to the point that I had an animated avatar, although the rules that I lobbied, being appointed to admins, it was strictly forbidden.


Then I founded and for a long time admin a highly specialized forum, which is still alive today. I regret to say that the forums are dying. Life is warming in some specific areas where the forum is convenient, as a platform, but now there are no interesting forums of general orientation.


The forums are still a great sandbox to hone their skills. The forums give remarkable anonymity. In the end, the forum you can always throw, but the memory will remain for centuries, especially if you wrote something sensible.


Anonymous Personal Blog


Personal blog becomes the next stage. I opened my first blog in 2003 in LiveJournal, and kept another one on the forum. Looking back, it's hard to say why I needed two blogs. But that was, that was. Blogs are the next stage of socialization, here you have friends by interests. If you maintain proper digital hygiene, you can not be recognized all the time of use.


The problem lies precisely in anonymity, if you want fame, even in narrow circles - this is not your option. Sooner or later, de-anonymization will occur, which may have a negative impact on you, because you run an anonymous blog. The consequences of this can be very different. And, of course, the range of your topics will be extremely limited, for example, you can hardly write on professional topics. If this is what interests you, the next section will help you.


Admittedly, personal blogs on known platforms are dying , along with their platforms. Now there are practically no convenient social networks for periodic blogging. Yes, LJ fits perfectly, but he died. And Vkontakte with Facebook, yet somewhat for another.


Last year, after 15 years, I closed my anonymous blog.


Professional articles to order


If you are professional enough, sooner or later you will be asked to write an article on a given topic. This is a qualitative step forward. The problem is that you are strictly limited to the topic. And the editor who made the plan can understand your specifics very vaguely. Then you will write about employee tracking programs. But if your material is interesting or of high quality, you can be given some freedom in covering the topic .


Typically, such tasks arrive with extremely tight deadlines and specific volume requirements. For example, 9000 characters in 2 weeks. As you understand, they do not pay for it. But, if you want to follow the path of an evangelist in your area, a person who promotes and promotes certain areas, this will be a good start. Sooner or later you will be able to become a specialized PR manager and opinion leader . For example, in the field of information security, Alex Lukatsky passed this way.

Corporate presentations at conferences


This is the next step, when you do not just express your thoughts in writing or are in a loyal audience, as with learning, but convey what you need to a wide audience. This is a classic of public speaking, to go or not to go, in which everyone must decide independently. Usually the employer sends such speeches, less often the vendor.


Each speaker at the very beginning must solve the following tasks:


  • An interesting topic (at least not banal);
  • Slides This is a separate art, which you can talk about long and enthusiastically .
  • Speech text. It is necessary to know it at least in general terms, so as not to read without notes.
  • Timing The unpleasant fact that it is possible and not to have time in the allotted 10-15 minutes to bring the necessary thought, or vice versa quickly shoot back and the remaining time to wait for questions from the audience, which usually does not happen.
  • Appearance. Public performances are quite demanding on appearance, although now there is a tendency to simplify, you can read even in a sweatshirt.
