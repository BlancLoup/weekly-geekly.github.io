Divide means multiply

Dear friends! You are present at a historical event in the world of information technology. Today, November 1, 2015, Hewlett-Packard has become two new companies: Hewlett Packard Enterprise and HP Inc.



Some details under the cut.

Activities


Hewlett Packard Enterprise deals with corporate IT systems. Meg Whitman, who took over as president and CEO of Hewlett Packard Enterprise, says that over the past three years, HP, which she managed until now, has revolutionized corporate offerings.

Namely, we are talking about such innovative solutions as Apollo, Gen 9 and Moonshot servers, and 3PAR storage systems. Next is the HP OneView management platform, HP Helion cloud and a wide range of software and services in the areas of security, data analysis and application conversion.

Hewlett Packard Enterprise has a portfolio of "corporate" developments HP. As a result, the company's customers can take advantage of all the capabilities of cloud technologies, big data management solutions, security features, and mobile systems. All this will allow them to fully implement the strategy of New Style of IT.

The second company, HP Inc., will be headed by Dion Weisler, who was formerly HP's vice president of Printing and Personal Systems. HP Inc., which grew from this division. deals with printers, laptops, tablet PCs, specialized solutions for the organization of mobile and stationary workplaces. Among the development portfolio that HP Inc. has received are 3D printing technologies and much more.

New logo


HP Inc. retained the well-known HP logo. But Hewlett Packard Enterprise has acquired something completely new. Speaking about the logo, Meg Whitman revealed some secrets of the new style, spoke about his connection with the company's mission.


Hewlett Packard Enterprise Logo

Every detail of the new logo makes sense. The strict black sans-serif font on a white background is a symbol of the desire for simplicity and clarity in the way HP did business. This course will continue in Hewlett Packard Enterprise.
The green rectangle is a symbol of the window to the future. He is a sign of opportunity and concern for the environment.

About division and multiplication


Today's high-tech business is inconceivable without a close relationship of companies with the outside world, without speed of decision making and willingness for continuous improvement, for constant search and implementation of innovations. This is called the "economy of ideas." Clients choose those companies that are able to more quickly translate ideas into reality and work towards achieving results.

One of the reasons for the emergence of Hewlett Packard Enterprise and HP Inc. - This is the desire to comply with the current state of affairs.

Splitting HP into Hewlett Packard Enterprise and HP Inc. - This step, which can be called part of the movement to increase the opportunities and results for all those associated with Hewlett-Packard: for customers, investors, employees, shareholders and partners.
