What are all the actual problems?


For the online store Adverti , which sells corporate souvenirs and gifts, it is very important to focus on the application. This is when you print your identity on a T-shirt, pen, calendar, etc.



If you look at the current version of the T-shirt product card , a number of problems pop up:

  • the application functional is hidden so far that its value is not obvious to the client (read: the client does not see)
  • for some products there are many options available that are hidden in drop-down lists and require a lot of extra clicks
