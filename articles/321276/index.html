What we come from


Brought YouGile project management system . There is no super innovation. For almost 10 years of my own experience, there is an understanding that project management systems are very poorly integrated into the business processes of companies and even worse in the daily work of people ( reviewed this topic ). As a rule, an ordinary employee does not look into the planning system once again and does not know in detail what the company does and why it works at all. If you take a team of more than 50 people, then this problem becomes the main one.

The task seemed to be solved with long-term system work. The point is not about any particular function, but about the general construction of the application, with the emphasis on the fact that it should drag out the user. If using YouGile in the company will be 10% more people who understand how business processes are arranged, then our goal is achieved.

If more detailed about YouGile then:

  • We make so that Agile boards of one system could be used by any department. The whole company must plan and communicate in one place, according to the same rules. And not in five different systems, as now many. There is a rumor that 83 different CRMs are used in Rostelecom.
  • We combine communications and planning processes. Each task in YouGile is a chat. On the phone, the workflow is similar to whatsapp correspondence. Now we can already say for sure that it is convenient, the first 500 active users liked it.
  • Based on the data of communication and viewing of tasks, we build real pictures on what the team was focused on. Not a special report for the head, but we show the team where her time really went.
  • We do powerful API. Do you want the task to fall into the first column, turn red and tell Vasya that it is time to do it urgently, if there is number 13 there, we can do that. Any fantasy about how the process should be implemented in the information system is realizable here. But at the same time there are several fundamental principles that leave uniformity.

A couple of specific tips if you are going to have an interview at YCombinator:


  • Train 100 times with the whole team, it will stay with you regardless of the result. On the net you can find a complete list of questions that may be asked. Only a few really important matters matter: what are you doing ?, why is it for people ?, why will it grow to a billion-dollar company?
  • Try to be completely honest, open and very clear. You can embellish artistically, but there are very insightful guys sitting on the jury. Each of them has raised a company for $ 100 million and they understand a little more than you expect.
  • No marketing. Everything should be short and easy to understand. With this approach, it is easier to get to the bottom of it and it is very much appreciated.
