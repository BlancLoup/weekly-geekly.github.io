What are the public procurement and what corruption schemes are used in them


Federal Law No. 94 establishes the following types of procurement:
  • Closed auction
  • Closed competition
  • Request for quotations
  • Notification of interest in the competition
  • Open auction
  • Open auction in electronic form
  • Open competition
  • Preliminary selection

Most often used today:
  • request for quotations (if the amount of the state contract is up to 500t.r.)
  • open tender or open auction (if the amount of the state contract is more than 500t.r.)


What is a request for quotations?


The state customer publishes on the federal or regional website the tender documentation which specifies the initial maximum contract price, deadlines for the performance of work and other conditions. Contractors send quotes to the customer indicating the price at which they undertake to perform this scope of work.

What is an open auction?


The state customer publishes on the federal or regional website the tender documentation which specifies the initial maximum contract price, deadlines for the performance of work and other conditions. Contractors send applications to the customer to participate in the auction, the commission of the state customer on a certain day considers the applications and issues a decision on admission or non-admission of contractors to the auction.
In contrast to the request for quotations, in open auctions there is almost always a requirement until the submission of tender documentation to pay for the bid security (most often 5% of the initial maximum contract price, the amount is returned after the auction). Also, there is often a requirement for ensuring the fulfillment of a government contract - the winning contractor must provide 30% of the state contract value, most often with an insurance contract for this amount or by transferring this amount to the customer‚Äôs account.
On the appointed day and time, the contractors come to the state customer and the auction for a lower price begins. The auction increment is usually 5%.
