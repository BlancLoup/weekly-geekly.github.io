10 popular games and applications for Ubuntu in May 2012 (repository UALinux)

I would like to offer to get away from watching the football championship and study new games and applications that, according to statistics on how to access the repository, scored the most points in the last month of spring.

From the statistics readings, like last time, the games and applications mentioned earlier in the announcements were excluded:
here: 10 popular games and applications for Ubuntu in April 2012
here: 10 popular games and applications for Ubuntu in March 2012
and here: The 10 most popular games for Ubuntu in February 2012
because of the mass entry from Habr statistics is not entirely accurate.

I pay attention that the repository is already available also for owners of Ubuntu 12.04 .

And so, here are the results of May:

Popular games:
1. Urban Terror - multiplayer shooter
2. Enemy Territory: Quake Wars - strategic online shooter
3. The Ur-Quan Masters - Intergalactic Adventures
4. The Babylon Project - Space Battles
5. Oolite - a cosmo simulator of commerce and battles
6. Tile Racer - 3D stunt racing
7. Spring - real-time strategy engine
8. Extreme Tux Racer - 3D racing with Tux
9. FreeOrion - exploring the cosmos and conquering the galaxy
10. Sonic Robo Blast 2 - 3D implementation of the game Sonic by Sega

Popular applications:
1. GNOME ALSA Mixer - Installing a Mixer
2. Rainlendar2 - Customizable desktop calendar
3. KernelCheck - build the kernel for the lazy
4. TeamViewer - Remote Access and Online Support
5. WINE - allows you to run Microsoft Windows applications
6. Google Earth - three-dimensional model of the Earth
7. Picasa - Image Management
8. Real-time Sunlight Wallpaper - a planet on the desktop in real time
9. Grub Customizer - settings manager for GRUB2
10. Linux MultiMedia Studio - a virtual studio for creating music.
