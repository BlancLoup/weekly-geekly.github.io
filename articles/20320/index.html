We digest News2.ru

Nobody has to explain the importance of social networks. To direct visitors to your website / blog, to get a large number of natural links, or to launch a viral advertising campaign - in this you can not do without social networks. In this particular case, I will consider the social news site - news2.ru .

With an average attendance of 6.5 thousand people a day, a news item or article from the main page can lead to your site several thousand of them. If you hang the news regularly, this number will increase many times.

So how to bring your news to the first page of news2.ru? If you simply sign up for the service and post your news, your chances will be very slim. Only top users from the top thirty (hundreds at best) can effectively and effectively advertise themselves. First you have to sweat, gaining credibility, leaving just interesting news for the community. How to do it?

When is it better to hang the news?


Most of the Internet users live in the western part of Russia, and the most active in Moscow. So on Moscow and need to focus. The best time is from 8.00 to 22.00 Moscow time.

And why is it so important to hang the news during high user activity? The fact is that the more time has passed since the appearance of your news, the more difficult it is to get to the first main page.

Multiple accounts - is there any point?


Sometimes there is a desire to make 5-6 auxiliary accounts and pump their news with free voices. The effect of this will be rather negative.
1. If for the most part you and your bots voted for the news, then under it will appear a big red inscription: ‚ÄúThe probability is high that the votes are twisted‚Äù. After this, the first page you just do not shine.
2. It is quite difficult to pump several profiles at once, and the voices of your ‚Äúcopies‚Äù will have very little weight. One more or less authoritative user who simply likes your news will bring you much more.

The only useful application of bots is to add your comments. For them, no cheating is not displayed, and other users, seeing a powerful positive reaction to your comment, will also tend to give you a plus. Thus, you will form the image of a respected commentator. And if the comments are credible, then the news too.

Small tips in bulk

  • Do not post the news immediately after registration, wait a couple of days, get up to date. It is rather difficult to immediately determine if the news you want to add is already hanging (searching does not always help). Nothing good will happen if the first few of your news are buried.
  • Immediately add the top 30 users as a friend. It is possible that some of them will add you as a friend in response.
  • Add new users to your friends and vote for their news. The intended effect is similar to the previous one.
    Get rid of the inscription "new member" as soon as possible. Chances news beginner get to the top tend to a minus of infinity. Hanging out two news stories a day, in 3-4 days you will become like a regular community member.
  • Do not fight with the top users - it will be more expensive. If one of them buried your news, do not make a "terrible revenge" and do not bury his news in return. Your voice is unlikely to seriously affect, but you can very badly ruin your life. Even if your high-quality news regularly buries one or several giants, it is better to go into the shade for a couple of days and not post the news. It is possible that they will forget about you and leave you alone.
  • Follow the movement of your news from the profile. There they are gathered together, the number of votes is also noted. To update one page from time to time is much more convenient than jumping on different pages.
