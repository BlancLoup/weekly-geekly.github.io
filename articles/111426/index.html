Community-SSU for Maemo 5

It's no secret that Nokia has finally abandoned Maemo, and transferred all its strength to the development of Meego. And most likely that PR1.3 was the last of. update from Nokia. But since Maemo has a lot of fans, a group of people decided to create a community-ssu , that is, to continue supporting Maemo and releasing updates for it without the participation of Nokia. So far, they have not yet moved to public, so is testing, but plans to provide the community with frequent and most important stable updates, where the emphasis is on bugfixes. At the moment, is testing neof. updates through the repository. They already include patches and bugfixes, for example for the same hildon, which were not included by Nokia in PR1.3. Of course, they can be installed separately, the descriptions and code of which can be found on TMO , but the idea is to make it as convenient as possible for ordinary users. For example, one of the last was the update hildon for the portrait menu from MohammadAG:



It was also discussed with Nokia about opening the code of some blobs in Maemo and drivers (or specs) for the N900, but unfortunately, the majority remained closed. Therefore, some of these people are also developing to replace the closed UI Maemo elements, those that Nokia did not agree to open. Since some of them contain many bugs, and Nokia is not in a hurry to fix them or even marked it as wontfix. In addition, these people want to ensure that Maemo is a completely open project. First you plan to rewrite MediaPlayer to Qt. At the moment, the design is one-on-one like that of. Player, only added support for portrait mode, as in the plans to make the most familiar to users design. But in the future innovations will be added (as for example the equalizer).



PS I advise you not to bury Maemo ahead of time and follow the development. If someone knows English well and thinks that he can somehow help the project, you are welcome.
