Application Developer Days - Programmer Days

We invite you to take part in the conference of professional programmers of Application Developer Days , which will be held on September 23-24, 2010 in Yaroslavl.



This unique event, implemented by experts in the field of software development, will bring together individual professionals and entire IT communities on one platform. The conference will not be limited to any particular technology or platform. Specialists who use a variety of software platforms and tools in their work will be able to tell colleagues about their projects, share experiences, learn about new approaches and opportunities.

Programmers are the very people that make applications come into being. And it was they who for a long time turned out to be in the minority at industry conferences. The development industry has grown, and project managers, testers, business analysts have pushed programmers out of the scope of activities. Only in the last year did the tendency to return to true technical conferences. Application Developer Days 2010 will open the season, and this promises to be really interesting.

The conference is a qualitative continuation of last year's event of the Software Engineering Forum, which was held in Minsk in May 2009 and gathered a large number of positive responses. The conference has gained the deserved authority of a leading event in the CIS in software engineering. The list of last year‚Äôs speakers includes such names as Sergey Arkhipenkov, Alexander Orlov, Stanislav Fomin, Alexander Alexandrov, Askhat Urazbaev, Andrey Bibichev, Alexander Baikin, Denis Petelin, Yury Shilyaev, Dmitry Lobasev and others. The conference is supported by high-tech industry leaders, as well as national IT associations.

In 2010, within the framework of Application Developer Days, there will be a discussion of a whole range of issues related to software development, choice of programming languages, consideration of successful architectural solutions and recommendations for their creation, consideration of the most popular technologies, products of well-known vendors and Open Source solutions.

In addition to programmers themselves, the conference will be of interest to all those who are involved in the process of creating software products, who want to understand what developers live with, to look at everything from the point of view of a programmer.

Conference format and program


The conference consists of three parallel sections.
The duration of the conference is 2 days.
