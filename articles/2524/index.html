Webalta will open a new data center

In the next three months, Webalta plans to complete the creation of a new data center and place in it 250 racks of equipment storing and processing its search engine data. It is reported that the datacenter resource will allow to place about 5000 servers in it. Now the park Webalta has 150 servers.

The data center will be located next to the business center "Nagatinsky", where the headquarters of Webalta is located, the company said. The premises will be equipped with modern uninterruptible power supply systems, automatic fire extinguishing, microclimate support and security.

In the future, Webalta is going to provide server hosting services to third-party companies.
