Whoever concerns the infrastructure of digital signage systems, I think, even readers of the Intel blog are not fully aware of its versatility and complex structure. In the end, only one, small, part of this system faces the audience - this is the screen that displays the final picture. Meanwhile, the issue of security and power consumption is no less acute here than in many other network and infrastructure solutions. At the hardware level, Intel has developed technology vPro, which allows you to effectively manage the delivery of multimedia materials remotely, using secure protocols, in energy saving mode. Naturally, all this reduces the cost of ownership of the system and the cost of maintenance. At the software level, Intel uses the Intel AIM Suite anonymous video analytics technology, which can be integrated into modern digital display systems based on second-generation Intel Core processors. The AIM Suite recognizes people who are in contact with a digital screen by gender and age, but does not identify the individual, and therefore does not use the personal data of visitors.
