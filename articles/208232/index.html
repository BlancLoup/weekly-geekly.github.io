Olympiad FUPM MIPT for schoolchildren

Many of us at least once participated in various programming contests.
Now on the server of the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology the correspondence Olympiad for schoolchildren in programming is being held.
At the moment there are 9 problems in the contest and new ones are gradually being added.
The contest will be available until the end of January .

The results will be taken into account at the interview at the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology and in the distribution of first-year students in computer science.
The Olympiad is held according to the Kirov system (that is, points bring even a solution that passes only part of the tests) to Ejudge. Questions on a condition are set through the checking system to judges.

There will be tasks of different levels from the simplest to the most complex, so that everyone is interested. The winners will receive prizes and souvenirs from the faculty of management and applied mathematics and sponsors.
The contest is compiled by trainers and part of the teams of MIPT Waterogers, ACM ICPC gold medalists from 2011-2012 and MIPT Lambda, ACM ICPC 2012-2013 finalists. All of them are graduate students and graduates of the Faculty of Management and Applied Mathematics of MIPT.

The winner of the last Olympiad FUPM MIPT found out about her through Habr. Now we are looking for a new winner. We invite all schoolchildren to take part and wish them success!
