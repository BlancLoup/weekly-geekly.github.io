New ARM processors in mobiles by the end of the year

The headquarters of ARM have long been talking about the upcoming Hi-End mobile processors Mali-200 and Mali-400, but only today from an official - Remi Pedersen (Remi Pedersen), product manager for graphic development, received information about The appearance of new stones in mobile devices. At best, this will happen at the end of 2009.

Despite the fact that not a single word was said about the mobile phones themselves with the new processors, Remy made several unsubstantiated, so far, statements about the performance of the new hardware. So, according to him, Mali-200 and Mali-400 are able to run OpenGL ES 2.0, allegedly producing 16 million triangles / sec and 275 million pixels / sec. The difference between the two processors is that the more powerful 400 series is multi-core (up to 4 cores at 300 MHz) and supports resolutions up to 1080p, which makes it possible to view full-fledged HD video on a mobile phone.

It remains to wait only for the presence of new processors in the devices on the shelves in order to check the conformity of statements and facts.
