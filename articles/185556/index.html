Open program for developers from Thalmic Labs (MYO)

On July 2, Thalmic Labs sent out a notice about opening a registration to a program for developers. The company Thalmic Labs is known primarily for its MYO bracelet ( Habr√© news ), which uses the electromyography method to record the electrical activity of the arm muscles and transmits via Bluetooth in minute details about the movement that the user made. Thus, we create a wearable control interface for any device that supports Bluetooth integration.

Translation of information distributed by Thalmic Labs:
We are pleased to announce that the program for developers Thalmic Labs is open for applications. We will review applications, and provide access to our APIs, documentation, and other resources gradually over the coming months. Participation in the program will depend on a number of factors, including compatibility with early versions of our APIs and details of your project idea. Apply to join now at developer.thalmic.com/apply !

Need early access to MYO?
We will distribute a limited number of products for pre-orders in the program for developers. In order to have a chance to get a device, apply for participation in the program for developers. Applications from developers who have already placed pre-orders for MYO will be considered first.
