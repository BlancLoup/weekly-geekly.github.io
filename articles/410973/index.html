What drones are used in world cinema



Amateur drones can shoot great shots, but their payloads are low, so they are not suitable for professional use. Drones for filming are often inferior to their light counterparts in flight duration (usually it is no more than 15-20 minutes), but the developers create models that can hold out in the air for up to half an hour.

Professional drones can buy anyone, the prices of them you will find in this post. But for large film projects, quadcopters do not buy or rent separately - there are special companies that provide shooting services along with the work of a team of operators. Each drone has its own specifics and requires the ability to handle it.

Learn more about the popular drones for filming below. Quadcopters are placed in a sequence from the most expensive to simpler models.

Aerigon MK II



Price: $ 42,000

The world's first 6-frame serial unmanned aerial vehicle designed for professional use in the movies. Designed to work with heavy industrial cameras and lenses. He participated in the filming of many Hollywood films , including “Spectrum”, “The Avengers: the Age of Ultron”, “The Farther Into the Forest”, “Mission Impossible: The Outcast Tribe”, “Hunter”.

Features hexakoptera:

  • Maximum take-off weight - 23 kg;
  • Overall diameter - 1510 mm;
  • Weight without batteries - 5.5 kg, with batteries - 11 kg;
  • The minimum load is 6 kg, maximum 13.4 kg.

How does the industry of professional filming from the air


The use of drones brought the film industry to a new level. In essence, Hollywood itself can order the development and manufacture of new models of devices that they need for camera work, but this is not necessary: ​​there are already many companies that provide services without any problems. The cost of one day of renting a professional quadcopter with a team of operators is about $ 15,000, and given the fact that aerial surveying services are usually not needed for the entire shooting, the amount does not affect the total budget of the film - Vladimir Bely told this in an article for Forbes. .

Vladimir Bely is the owner of one of the Russian companies that build and rent professional drones. His company Alpha Smart Systems produces about 150 drones per year, the cost of each of them varies from 1-1.5 million rubles, and the cost is about 700,000 rubles. Individual development of the drone is more expensive, up to 30 million rubles. Such a drone is designed for about a month and takes another three days to assemble.

At the same time, the company does not receive the main revenue from the development of drones and related products, but from renting them out with pilot support. One shooting day in Russia costs 120,000 rubles.

Most Russian companies use ready-made drones for the film industry, and only a few develop their own professional drones.

A drone on the set can replace the crane’s work for the camera crew and even a helicopter, and the quality of the shooting will be higher.
