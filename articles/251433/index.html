Started voting for the new features of the browser Vivaldi

Hello!

A few days ago, we gathered from blogs and forums the wishes of users to add new features to the Vivaldi browser or to improve existing ones. The list was huge - more than 90 features and improvements. We have removed from it what has already been implemented or is in the process of implementation, and also for the time being have removed from the list functions that cannot be implemented in the near future for various reasons. The final list includes 48 points, they are presented on the voting page .

The voting rules are simple: you can mark out three most important functions in your opinion that Vivaldi really lacks. In this way, developers will be able to add them to their plans, depending on their priority. Also remember that this voting is the first, but not the last - in the future, the remaining functions in the list will be added to new polls that are planned to be conducted after each important release of the Vivaldi browser.

Thank you for your support and welcome to the survey page !
