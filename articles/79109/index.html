Wishmart.ru - wishlist.

Briefly about the features of our wishlist and the tasks that he solves.

Wishmart will help:

  • Choose a gift and tell your friends what to give to you for your birthday (convenient notification functionality);
  • Find out what gifts your friends want;
  • Organization of joint gifts - to give guests the opportunity to cooperate to buy 1 expensive gift (underway);
  • Bookmark yourself on "my dreams, goals" (you can not show them to other users).
  • Organization of wedding gifts (exclude repeated gifts, give guests the opportunity to easily choose the right gift for the newlyweds in their pocket);

    In the nearest future realization of original and convenient functions for searching and adding gifts. Now gifts are added manually + there is a small catalog of interesting gifts and original ideas, from which you can also add a desire for yourself.

    The project in the beta version is growing and improving daily. It is being tested in Fire Fox 3.5.5., Opera 9.6, Safari 4.04, Chrome (under mac and windows) - if there are comments, please indicate the browser used.
