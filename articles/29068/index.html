New version of PROMODJ

The most popular Russian website / social network for DJs (now and not only, but more on that below) has recently been completely updated for the first time in 2 years.

Strangely enough, they never use the numbers "2.0" anywhere, despite the fact that the changes are very noticeable. :)


There are quite a few extras. At first, a lighter and web-two design is striking.
There is a new internal rating PROMORANK. It works on the basis of polls and downloads of a DJ track from the site.

There was a live rating TOP 100, the first rating of DJs in runet, which changes not once a year, but constantly.

Also now there is a wiretap of material right on the site, in a flash player. They promise to increase the download speed - one of the weaknesses of PromoDJ.

A new section has opened - Market. He collects offers for the sale of equipment for DJs from large sites. A DJ and a musician can make a list of their desires and publish them on their page.

The online radio site was divided into three channels - Channel N5 , Deep channel and 300 km / h channel .

All material with a quality of 320kbps is allocated in a separate section.

There is a new release section. Something reminded me similar to dj.ru.

Updated forum section about events. New forum and control panel.

And, finally, one of the major, in my opinion, changes. Now there are new groups on the site - Listener / Clubber , VJ, Promoter, Art Director. Now the average listener can actively participate in the life of the portal.

Now on PROMODJ it has become much more convenient and more pleasantly located. Surely, such radical changes will make this famous project even more popular. :)

More information about the changes can be found at the promodj .
