Wrong honey: French beekeeper has learned to make hemp honey


Facial expression of a person who achieved hemp honey

A 39-year-old French beekeeper named Nicholas, who calls himself "Bee Trainer", managed to train the bees to produce hemp honey . At the same time, the bees do not have any problems with cannabinoids. The resulting product, many people call the first edible natural product with marijuana.

Nicholas says that at school he was not successful because of his hyperactivity, and did not even finish his studies. Then he discovered that marijuana helps him deal with his psychological problem. Since then, he has become a cannabis lover, and also a beekeeper, mechanic and artist.



He managed to train his bees for various unusual actions - for example, to get sugar from fruits, not flowers. At some point, he set out to teach them to make honey based on cannabinoid gum.
