Following Apple, Samsung has raised prices for mobile devices in Russia.

On Friday, September 11, Samsung has increased the purchase prices for flagship and some other smartphones in Russian stores. RBC sources estimate the rise in prices at 8-20%.

According to the interlocutor of RBC, the Samsung Galaxy S6 Edge model with a 64GB storage capacity went up most of all in M.Video - immediately by 9,000 rubles (or 22.5%), now the device costs 48,990 rubles. Other manufacturers' smartphones increased in price by 1-3 thousand rubles, or an average of 5-6%: for example, the Samsung Galaxy S6 Duos went up by 5.2%, to 41,990 rubles, and the Samsung Galaxy S6 SS - by 8.6 % up to 37 990 rubles.
