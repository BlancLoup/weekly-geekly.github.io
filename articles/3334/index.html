Adobe released Flash Lite 3

Adobe has announced a new version of Flash Lite 3, a mobile version of Flash Player, at 3GSM in Barcelona. The main innovation of Flash Lite 3 has become a full support for video on mobile phones, writes Cybersecurity.ru . In the spring of this year, phone manufacturers will receive licenses for the new product, and devices that support Flash Lite 3 will appear in the autumn.

The new version supports the function of saving video on a mobile phone, and also has special extensions for receiving data from streaming servers, in addition, Flash Lite 3 will allow you to create screensavers for smartphones and mobile handsets. True, the video for new mobile phones will have to be compressed in size, since it will be impossible to display a full-scale picture on small screens.

By the way, last year about 70 million phones were sold with pre-installed versions of Flash Lite 2 or Flash Cast. This year their number should double.
