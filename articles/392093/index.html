Sound through the bones. SoundAround Headset Overview

Most recently, I wrote about a device that can act as external speakers for a tablet or smartphone. Then it was about listening to music by the company. I decided to see what is new in the field of individual listening.

I first met unusual sound transmission technology at the age of 7. Then I first saw an army walkie-talkie and learned what a laryngo phone is . It seemed something incredible: you can speak in a whisper and you will be perfectly audible.
A couple of decades passed and I felt deja vu, I again got acquainted with the new technology of sound transmission: you can listen to music individually and with open ears. Sound vibrations are transmitted through the bones, and the audibility is the same as when using conventional headphones. I decided to study in detail the possibilities of these headphones and wore them for a week, taking them off only for the night. I will describe my observations below.





Headphones based on bone conduction in the form of a commercial product have recently entered the market. But in the military industry, they are used a little longer, and the laryngophones mentioned by me have gained popularity there. Although, if you pay attention to the Russian military equipment "Warrior", you can see that now uses a regular earpiece. I think this is done so that in the rumble of the battle, at least with one ear, you can hear the commands transmitted by the radio.



Well, we live in a peaceful environment, so we have slightly different requirements for sound transmission. According to my observations, I would divide all people who use headphones in everyday life (street, work, gym) into two categories:
1. I want to withdraw into myself so that no one distracts
2. I need rhythm, music and talks on the phone with free hands

And if the first category of people chooses gags or full closed headphones, then the second tends to minimalism so that you can remove them in the shortest possible time to hear what is happening around. The SoundAround headset (or Aftershokz Bluez 2 ) is intended for the second category of users.

Packing and packaging
Headphones come in a beautiful big box that would fit several pairs. That is, the manufacturer did not stint on the packaging. But they look presentable, so the headset can serve as a spectacular gift.



Unfolding the box, it is difficult to miss the first instructions for the use of headphones. The picture clearly demonstrates that no need to close the ears.



The package includes the headset itself, a snap-on elastic band to reduce the bezel, a fabric case, a USB-microUSB cable for charging the device, original instructions and translation of instructions from the Dadzhet company. In principle, more is needed.

First look
The headset comes in three different colors: black, neon green and red. The inner part, which is in contact with the head, is painted in neon color, and the coating itself is pleasant to the touch - soft touch.



If you look at the headset from the bottom, you can see the controls and the microUSB connector, which is covered with a rubber stopper, to charge the internal battery.



And although this is not a completely waterproof solution, it will help protect the connector from rain drops. Nearby are the volume buttons and the on / off button of the device. There is also an LED indicator for charging and operation.



The inner surface of the points adjacent to the temporal region, rubberized and has pronounced projections. First start
To first turn on, you need to press the power button and hold for about 10 seconds. Then besides the greeting you will hear the pleasant female voice ‚ÄúPairing‚Äù. This means that the device is searchable. At this moment, we start the search for Bluetooth devices from a smartphone, phone or computer and select AfterShokz BLUEZ 2. The headset rests on the back of its head with its bow, but if this is not enough, then you can use the rubber strap included in the package, which forms a smaller handle. Thus, you can customize the device for any user.



We use!
Connected, conveniently located, included on the smartphone music. Incredibly, the sound is almost the same as when using ordinary headphones. Yes, this headset has small holes and the sound is partially perceived by the eardrums, but it is enough to plug the ears from external noise, as it becomes clear that the sound is indeed transmitted differently. With the ears closed, by the way, the sound acquires a chic bass.
No wonder I always say "headset", because this gadget is equipped with microphones. They are located on the right "ear", and on the left is a multi-function button. She is responsible for answering calls, pausing for playing music, and double clicking switches the melody to the next one in the list. The sensitivity of the microphone is sufficient to talk in the normal mode, but it is worth understanding that the microphone located at the ear requires a certain volume when talking. The voice is somewhat distorted, compared to the usual conversation on the phone. Due to the presence of microphones, wireless noise suppression technology has been introduced into our wireless devices . Its peculiarity lies in the fact that external noise perceived by the microphone is released in the form of sound in antiphase when transmitted to headphones. More often this applies to closed headphones to create the highest quality sound with minimal exposure to external noise.

The functionality of the universal button was mentioned above. Now let's say that the headset is equipped with four different sound settings - an equalizer. Switching between modes is performed by simultaneously pressing the + and - volume. In complete silence or with ears plugged, the difference in sound will be noticeable, but not in the street or in noisy traffic.

How is it in operation?
After three hours of operation, my ears are out of habit. It was once and all subsequent days, I did not notice the headset, so that the ergonomics at the level. I am glad that the single standard of connectors (microUSB) has not bypassed this device, so it‚Äôs not necessary to look for an additional charging cable. With daily use of the gadget, I listened to music for several hours, and the rest of the time it was on standby or served for negotiations. The latter should be discussed in more detail: headphones that allow you to enjoy music and socialize are especially useful for those who are used to live an active life and often communicate. At the same time for a live conversation is not required to pull out the headset from the ear, because you continue to hear the surrounding space, as before. This is passive safety: when playing sports on a bicycle, roller skates or skating, it is important to hear what is happening around. But music helps keep the rhythm alive. Moreover, with this use, the ears do not sweat, which inevitably occurs when using any other type of headphones.
As for the charging frequency, it is worth noting the low power consumption of Bluetooth 3.0 technology. With regular listening to music and talking on a bluetoth headset , I charged the device no more than once every three to four days.
With this method of listening there are downsides: in the subway, this headset is useless, and with active driving at speeds above 100 km / h in a car with standard noise isolation, you have to speak loudly so that the other person can hear you. In the subway you can make a funny feint with your ears: it is enough to use earplugs and then the sound literally surrounds, thanks to good bone conduction. At the same time, the main advantage of these headphones is lost - you practically stop hearing all the surrounding sounds.
