The main theses of Oleg Tinkov in an interview with "The Secret of the Company"

An updated edition of The Secret of the Firm last week published an interview with the most controversial entrepreneur in Russia - Oleg Tinkoff.

The conversation turned out to be emotional and interesting, capturing the timeline from the beginning of the nineties to the current moment.

"Megamind" leads the most interesting quotes from the interview.

About motivation and crisis


And we, businessmen, have survived a bit, we can count them on the fingers of our hands. I mean real businessmen, not oligarchs, who muddle something with the state. If to compare with the 1990s, now there is no entrepreneurship in the country at all. But this is a normal situation. We were hungry and poor, we had to do something, and now everyone is well off. I speak 3-4 times a year in front of the students - MSU, Ph.D. and others. I have rational goals - to recruit the best people. And I see that the students are very well dressed, the girls are beautiful. But they have no motive. Like my children, there will never be a motive to work just like me. My son is friends with the son of Misha Friedman. They even have some kind of joint business on the Internet, but this is all ridiculous and naive. This is a kindergarten. Because you need a powerful motive.

But so far there is no particular crisis. Everything worsened only in comparison with fat years. I do not see much suffering. Just think, the ruble fell twice - in Ukraine, the currency fell four times! Maybe, by the way, there will be a business boom in the coming years. And we will buy less boots. Not five pairs will be a student, but two. Nothing tragic happens. We have over 5 million customers. Everyone pays, there is money.

About banking and business management


Any business is cyclical. And especially banking retail business is cyclical. We are at the very beginning of a bad cycle. The next two years will be bad. Yes, now we do not give out 100‚Äì150 thousand cards per month, as before, we give out only 40‚Äì50 thousand. Risks began to grow two years ago, and jumped strongly a year ago. All that matters is whether you make money or lose it. All the rest is details. In 2013, we earned $ 180 million in net profit, in 2014 - $ 90 million, and in 2015 we will be profitable. And something tells me that this year we will be the only non-state profitable retail bank in Russia. I wonder when a business earns and pays dividends. I personally received $ 30 million last year. I understand this, business.

I am not ready to invest in anything at all, I have never done it and I do not intend to. I do not know how to be a partner. I can only buy 100% of the company and offer the founders a job. Sometimes it is useful to work as an entrepreneur to become a good manager. Recently, for example, was going to buy Ubank and "traffic police fines." But they wanted too much money. In vain. An important point - I do not want to disperse. Because the focus is very important. Write big so that everyone understands. Here is an example of Nogotkov.
