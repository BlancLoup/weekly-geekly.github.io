Contest of Russian Interactive Literature 2011


Since the topic of text games is not indifferent to me, I publish the news here. I hope someone will be interested.

The Contest of Russian Interactive Literature 2011 has started.

What is this competition?



This is a game contest.
Are you the author of the game or want to become one? This competition is for you and for everyone in whom the creative streak beats, who likes to invent plots and who writes or wants to write a game in the genre of interactive literature. It can be a text game, an adventure, a quest, RPG, a game book or a visual novel. Write your own game, and we will take it to the competition.
Do you have a translator talent and a desire to share a foreign game with the rest? Translate it into Russian (with the consent of the author) - and also participate in KRIL!

Anyone can take part, show everyone his creation and win the competition.
The size of the game is not important - it can be either a small sketch for a few minutes of gameplay, or a game with a huge, developed interactive world and lots of side quests. At KRIL, everyone has equal chances, the main thing is to invest something special in your creation. The platform is not important (game engine) - you can use existing ones or even write your own. Everything else is limited only by your imagination.

How is the winner determined?
If the game complies with the rules and is accepted for the contest, then the national vote under the close supervision of the organizer decides everything. To vote, you must register on the main forum of the community of interactive literature.

And the prizes will be?
Nomination " best game ":
1st place - cash and clothing prize from our general sponsors.
2nd place - a valuable prize.
3rd place - a valuable prize clothing.
The honorable last place is a priceless prize from our best independent sponsor.

Nomination " best translation ":
The winner in the nomination for the best translation of a foreign game according to tradition will receive a game on Steam as a prize.
Plus, each participant will receive a prize with the symbols of the competition!

All prizes are provided on a voluntary basis by our sponsors (which, by the way, you can become if you want to support us).

What is the timeline?
The deadline for submitting the games to the competition is November 1, 2011 Moscow time (after this deadline, no modifications / corrections / changes to the games are accepted);
The voting period is December 30, 2011 Moscow time;
Summing up - until January 1, 2012.

Rules and other details can be read here - kril.ifiction.ru
And what interactive literature can be found from here - ifwiki.ru/FAQ
