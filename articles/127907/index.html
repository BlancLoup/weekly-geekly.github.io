The discovery of the "Ideal System" by the esoteric method of knowledge

Not for nothing in fundamental science there are no inventions or creations. There are discoveries. That is, everything in the world already exists, you just need to open another secret. The whole essence of things is contained in them. Hence, the secret of the Ideal System cannot be invented, it can only be opened. It is logical that an imperfect creator has imperfect creations. Someone is better, someone is worse, but it is always very far from being perfect.

Yes, man is not perfect, but there is an Ideal Creator (You can call him whatever you want - God, Vishnu or the Supreme Cosmic Mind, this does not change the essence). And if the Creator is perfect, then his creations are perfect. Consequently, we already see examples of Ideal Systems around us. It remains only to make a discovery.

There are many systems to the creation of which man is not involved. As an example of an Ideal System, I will take the most obvious one ‚Äî a living being. For special clarity, let the example of the Biological System be Man. For comparison, choose Technical and Information Systems created by man. As a technical system ( TS ), we take the car. And as an Information System ( IS ) - an Internet resource, site.

Imaginary contradiction


Probably, someone has already paid attention to the apparent contradiction in the given statements. If the Creator is perfect, then his creations are perfect. Man is imperfect, and his creations are likewise imperfect. But man is created by the Creator, which means he must be perfect, but the opposite is asserted. On the face of a contradiction. But this is only if you look superficially and not reach a subtle understanding. Man is made perfect in its essence. He is like the Creator. But it is an autonomous, self-developing system, which independently acquires new qualities, developing or degrading. What a person is and what he is is two different things. I do not want to go into these subtleties, and I can not probably. I just want to emphasize that there really is no contradiction.

What is the ideal system?


The ideal system consists of three main parts. Surely everything is much more complicated, but so far I have not discovered more.

Part 1. Interface


An interface is a part of a system that determines the characteristics of its physical or informational interaction with other systems. That is, using the interface, we can manage the system or collect information about it.

Interface: Technical System

Interfaces of the car are its controls - steering wheel, pedals, levers. It is obvious. There are still sensors and signal lights. This is a human interface. But most of the interface is built by itself. By the appearance and sound of the car, we can determine its approximate weight (inertia in driving, center of gravity), driving performance (in what road conditions this car can be operated), approximate power, age, and a lot of information.

Interface: Information System

The situation is about the same as with the vehicle. But there is a difference. In the Technical System, part of the interface is built independently, as it is connected with a cause-effect chain (the suspension is broken - a knock is audible). But in the IP interface is not built by the system itself, and the whole is created by man. Therefore, almost all IP interfaces are far from perfect. This is a big problem for all users.

Interface: Biological System

The human interface is very rich. There is an incredible number of ways to influence it. Just by the way he walks, talks, reacts, looks, and so on, you can collect a huge amount of information about him. Unlike IP, everything here is honest - everything is as it is, everything is justified. If we eliminate the imperfection of our perception of information and analyze it correctly, then the conclusions can always be drawn correctly. Because the BS interface is perfect, not created by man.

Summary:
The interface is important, but not the most important part of the system. A reasonable person will not buy a car just because of the color, and we do not choose the circle of friends because of the height or color of the eyes. The interface is important at the time of the first acquaintance with a new object, when we have no information about it. It is also needed to analyze the current state of the system and interact with it. But the interface is only an access guide to the most important thing. It is important, but not primary.

The functional part is an important component of the system. For people smarter than those who choose a car by its appearance, this part of the system is more important.

The human body is perfection itself. Just think how much work it does. It removes oxygen from the air, splits food, regulates temperature, fights infections, regenerates tissues. Everything?


So, we have considered two components of the systems: functional and interface. With regards to IP and TC, anyone will say that this is enough. Yes, the machine works, the site too.
