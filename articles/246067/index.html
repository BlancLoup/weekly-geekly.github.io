Viber messenger launched its gaming platform on iOS and Android


Today, Viber Games is available in Belarus, Ukraine, Israel, Singapore and Malaysia, and in Russia the service will be launched on December 20. In other countries, it should appear in early 2015.

The first games on Viber Games were Viber Candy Mania and Viber Pop from the studio Storm8 and Wild Luck Casino from the team Playtika. The main characters in them are recognizable characters from the sticker store Viber Sticker Market. The games have a rather active social component: competitions with friends, high score tables, the ability to send virtual gifts and much more.

The company is not yet the names of studios that will cooperate with it, but with such an incredible DAU, like Viber, the projects are doomed to success.


Kabam leaves the browser market


Kabam began to appear quite often in our news bulletin. This time the news is rather ambiguous. Kabam sells most of its browser projects, including those that support mobile versions of games, for example, Kingdoms of Camelot. There are only games developed by third-party studios and posted on the site.

The buyer was RockYou, the developer and publisher of social games.
According to Kent Wakeford (Kent Wakeford), COO Kabam, the company intends to focus on mobile games and at the same time provide support to players of browser projects.

If all is true, then this is more good news. Companies focus on what they do best, and this should improve the quality of the games themselves, and, equally important, the level of support in the games.

Monkop Alternative TestFlight and Hockeyapp for Android


Monkop will help developers test their applications on a variety of Android devices. To do this, you need to upload the apk-file to the site, and after a while you will receive detailed statistics on compatibility, security and user experience in your inbox.

According to the description of the project on the company's website, Monkop employees work in the laboratory, where they have access to smartphones and Android tablets with almost all versions of the operating system, various screen sizes and performance.

The system not only deals with the detection of errors or shortcomings of the application, but also offers developers various ways to solve them. While the project is in beta testing, and in December, the service can be used for free.

But imagine that all sorts of applications and games with various functions and interfaces will be sent for testing. In fact, their testing differs radically. In this case, it is expected that Monkop will only be able to automatically verify that all possible screens are supported and the application is installed on each device, but no more. It will be difficult to make sure of the quality of the test or to check whether the work is really done on all the specified devices. But for sure the company will not bear responsibility for its tests.
