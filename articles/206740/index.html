Registration for the DevCon 2014 conference is open




Dear friends! We are pleased to inform you that on May 28-29, 2014, Microsoft's main technology conference, DevCon, will be held. You can already register on the site.

The format of the conference remains unchanged - this is a country-day two-day event at a natural resort in the near Moscow region, and everything is included in the ticket price for the conference:

  • Food and hotel accommodation;
  • Participation in the main program;
  • Attend master classes;
  • Participation in round tables and focus groups;
  • Transfer from Moscow and back;
  • Entertaining and sports program;
  • Evening program.

What we will tell you (and show)


Six months before the event, but we have already formed a team that is responsible for the substantive part of the conference:

  • Dmitry Andreev dmandreev - conference content
  • Konstantin Kichinsky kichik - responsible for the most important report - plenary session.
  • Roman Zdebsky zdebskiy - track "Development and design of mobile applications"
  • Stas Pavlov stasus - track "Tools, development methodologies and programming languages"
  • Natalia Efimtseva inatale - track "Cloud computing and the web"
  • Alexey Shulenin alexejs - track "Corporate development, data processing"
  • Vladimir Yunev XaocCPS - the international part of the conference, English-speaking reports

We pay a lot of attention to the quality of the program and what you can learn. You will have a unique opportunity to talk with Microsoft representatives directly involved in the development and creation of products, as well as with employees of other companies who have deep practical experience in using our technologies. The main conference grid will include about fifty reports on the most relevant topics. The main program will include four areas:

DEVELOPING CLIENT APPLICATIONS FOR WINDOWS PLATFORM


Development of modern applications for Windows 8 and Windows Phone 8 using the unique capabilities of the platform. Development, monetization and promotion of applications for app stores. Design of modern applications on the Microsoft platform.

CLOUD CALCULATIONS AND WEB



Development of cloud applications and services for Windows Azure , as well as the use of the cloud platform as a backend for mobile and other applications.

TOOLS, METHODOLOGIES FOR DEVELOPMENT AND LANGUAGES OF PROGRAMMING



Methodology and use of Visual Studio development tools and software lifecycle management. Use current features and discuss the future of .NET, C #, JavaScript, XAML, development technologies

CORPORATE DEVELOPMENT, DATA PROCESSING



Work with data, business analysis tools and Microsoft office platform. Development of corporate applications on the .NET platform, SharePoint and Office.

Want to speak at DevCon 2014?


DevCon is not only an opportunity to get current knowledge on Microsoft products and technologies, but also a unique chance to speak at the main technology development conference. If you want to share your knowledge, you can submit an application to the organizers. Write a little about yourself and the topic with which you would like to speak to e-mail ms@devcon2014.ru .
