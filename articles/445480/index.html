TWIX method


Disassembled in the previous article, the Twix method has a long history. Francois Philidor , the Frenchman who laid the foundations of the modern understanding of chess, came to the maneuver of a horse in the shape of the letter W as early as the 18th century. In the next century and a half, various authors of chess monographs paid matte to an elephant and a knight a certain amount of attention, but this scattered knowledge was not a complete methodology.

At the beginning of the 20th century, the Polish grandmaster David Pshapyurka systematized all the available information and since then this methodology has remained unchanged to this day. Practically in any book where attention is paid to this ending, this method is described.


After another century, the children's chess trainer Yar Sanych (in the world, Yaroslav Aleksandrovich Prizant) introduced the successful name TWIX, so that the kids with more pleasure would learn this mat. The well-known pedagogical method - children learn new material better if it is associated with something fun, pleasant, familiar.

Knowledge of the TWIX method allows, by and large, not to bother much, pushing the frown king into the corner. It is enough to simply advance and, limiting the free space, to drive it into any, and if necessary, on the edge of the board, transfer exactly to the one you need. ‚ÄúTwix‚Äù is well suited for blitz: the name of the method is mnemonic and allows you to quickly recall what its essence is, even if the player has not trained in this mat for a long time the edge of the board is in the form of a capital vowel I , and an elephant, if not standing on the edge, is always controlled by two crossed diagonals in the form of the letter X ).

Deletana Triangles Method


The strongest side in general does not allow the opponent to hide in a safe corner, and immediately sends it to the matte corner with precise moves.

The first draft of this method dates back to 1780, which is somewhat later than the time when Philidor told in his book about the zigzag movements of a horse. In the February issue of the French chess magazine "La Strat√©gie" of 1923, an article entitled "Mating with an Elephant and a Horse" was published by Daniel Deletan , a Frenchman who lived in Argentina. The article provided an exhaustive analysis of the end.

The method did not receive special distribution, for the following reasons:

  1. Requires quite a more accurate calculation than the Twix method. Especially it concerns the maneuvers with an elephant and a horse - you need to weigh each turn, otherwise the king will get out of the matte web and you will have to start all over again.
  2. The opponent has a big chance to immediately whisk into a safe corner. From him, just in the course of the game, the matted king may be too close, and the enemy figures are too far away. The strongest side simply does not have time to build the desired structure. Then on the application of the method, by and large, can not speak.
  3. Longer than Twix. Yes, yes, not only easier, but also faster to drive into a safe corner, and then overtake to a matte corner than to drive them to a matte corner with verified maneuvers.
  4. There is no time for implementation. In the pool / blitz / rapid, in fast chess, just in time trouble - especially there is no time to count. But the TWIX method allows you to lightly checkmate on hanging flags, while not going into much detail about the position.
  5. There is no power to implement. In a serious tournament game at such a late stage (deep endgame), it is possible that there is time for analysis - if time control has passed. Main steps


For simplicity, we assume that the strongest side is white, their elephant is light-squared. White pushes the black king into the upper left corner. Of course, the alignment of colors and angles can be any other, but in order not to confuse the explanations, we‚Äôll dwell on this version.


Matted king is kept within three rectangular triangles, nested into each other. We call them as external, medium and small triangles. The total right angle is in the field of the same color as the elephant.

From larger triangles, crowding into smaller ones occurs. First, the king is transferred from the outer triangle to the middle one (this is the most difficult stage), then from the middle one to the small one (after which the checkmate is put).

To keep the opponent inside the triangle, it is necessary to control the black-and-white cells in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hypotenuse of the figure. The elephant controls half of these fields, almost all the rest are usually a horse, and the king, if necessary, remains.
Of this trinity, almost all activity lies with the king, who almost alone is engaged in repression. The horse and the elephant for the most part of the action still stand in their positions, putting an irresistible barrier in front of their rival, while being completely safe.

Clipping from safe angle


Before building any triangles, it is necessary to make sure that the king in question does not dive into one of two safe corners (these are angles whose color is opposite to the color controlled by the elephant). Sometimes it is required to find literally an etude solution in order to block this possibility for the weaker side.

It is not always possible to do it in time, and if the lonely king managed to temporarily slip away, then he would have to smoke out of a safe corner by the TWIX method.

Outer triangle



This is the widest triangle, you have to use both the elephant and the horse and the king to control the hypotenuse.

The task of the elephant and the horse is to control its sections and, at the first opportunity, reorganize by moving from the outer to the middle Deletana triangle.

The task of the king - controlling his plot, pushing the enemy into a dull corner.

The diagram shows the so-called ideal location of the elephant and the horse. In principle, these figures can stand differently and at the same time also put an insurmountable wall in front of the enemy king. However, if the horse and the elephant are too far apart, the task of pushing it into a corner becomes quite difficult.

Islands of security


If the elephant and the knight are unsuccessful, then for the black king may appear areas on the edge of the board where he will feel calm.

Summary algorithm


In view of the foregoing, it is possible to summarize the optimal order of actions that allows you to mate with an elephant and a knight using the Deletan triangles method.

  1. Clipping the lone king from a safe angle. Such an opportunity is not always the case if the mated king is much closer to a safe angle than the figures of the strongest side.
  2. Construction of the outer Deletana triangle. If the isolation from the saving corner is successful, the strongest side builds the first version of the outer triangle as quickly as possible in order to fence the opponent into the desired area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe board.
  3. Transfer of a horse and an elephant to ideal positions in the outer triangle of Deletana. Most likely, the position of the elephant and the horse will not be the best, so these two figures are regrouping in the right way. The process is backed by a king, who controls the cells through which a fouled king could break out of a triangle.
  4. Pushing the lone king from the hypotenuse of the outer triangle to the edge. The horse and the elephant stand on their ideal sites, and their monarch bravely goes to meet his opponent.
  5. The transition from the outer triangle to the middle. When the mated king moved away from the center, the elephant gets the opportunity to change the diagonal.
  6. Transfer of a horse and an elephant to ideal positions in the middle Deletana triangle. Again, you need to transfer the horse and the elephant to the ideal position. During the transition period, the king monitors the cells of the hypotenuse, from which control is temporarily removed.
  7. Pushing the lone king into a frosted corner. A horse and an elephant hold the perimeter from their ideal positions, their king advances, taking space from his counterpart.
  8. The transition from medium to small triangle. After the frown king is in the very corner, the elephant moves to a new diagonal.
  9. Matting The elephant and the king do not allow the weak side to leave the corner, the horse for the mat is brought.
