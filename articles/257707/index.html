Rating of sites by the number of referring IP addresses

There are quite a few ratings of sites in terms of citation.
However, we decided to create another one that will help to easily and visually evaluate the performance of sites.

The basis of the new rating was chosen the number of referring IP-addresses. The main advantages of this metric are:
- ease of analysis and verification
- quick bonding of more than 97% of subdomains and affiliated projects

The rating is based on the global index of external links MegaIndex.com and is constantly updated.

All domains rating:
https://www.megaindex.ru/index.php?tab=siteRating




Example filter for domains in RU zone
https://www.megaindex.ru/index.php?tab=siteRating&filter=.ru



Example filter by domain mask:
https://www.megaindex.ru/index.php?tab=siteRating&filter=wikimart.ru
