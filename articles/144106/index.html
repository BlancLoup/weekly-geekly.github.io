Home Screen Chameleon for Android



Chameleon, an alternative to the home screen for Android 3.2 / 4.0, changes depending on the current time of day, the wifi network nearby and the user's current location.

The user will be able to customize different screens for work in the office (the tablet will recognize with the GPS that you are in the office and immediately turn on the initial screen with business applications), for home, for traveling - in general, what your heart desires.

Manual switching between these screens is carried out with a standard gesture.

So far, the application has the status of a project on Kickstarter , its creators are announcing the release of the application on the Google Play Store in September. Those who donate $ 5 or more to the project will be able to expect to receive the application earlier - in August.
