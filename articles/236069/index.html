Samsung opens the world of mobile virtual reality with innovative glasses VR Gear

Good afternoon, Habr!

Samsung Electronics has unveiled a new generation of high-tech wearable devices - Samsung Gear VR Innovator's Edition's first virtual reality glasses. The gadget was created in 1.5 years, built on the basis of the development of a virtual technology leader, the company Oculus, and is compatible with the new GALAXY Note 4 smartphone. Gear VR for the first time will allow a wide circle of users to immerse themselves in virtual reality for movie viewing or games.



GALAXY Note 4 plays a key role in a bundle of mobile devices, thanks to the 5.7-inch high-resolution screen Quad HD Super AMOLED (2560 x 1440), which is equipped with a new flagship. Wearing a Gear VR Innovator's Edition, the user is immediately immersed in virtual reality, providing a 360-degree view and video with a 3D effect. Right in the glasses, the functions of receiving a call and viewing messages are available so that each time you do not take off the smartphone with an incoming call or SMS.



Gear VR glasses provide a viewing angle of 96 ¬∞, while the virtual display remains in front of the user's eyes when rotated 360 ¬∞. The device is equipped with air vents, two lenses, an proximity sensor, a wheel for changing the focal length, volume buttons, a touchpad on the side of the case, a return button and a USB connector. The dimensions are 198 x 116 x 90 mm.



Samsung Gear VR is made of a lightweight material with a soft lining, taking the shape of the head and face. The design of the device is completely wireless, which allows the user to be fully involved in the virtual world, without a lot of wires coming from the computer. In the near future, Samsung will provide a large selection of content optimized for viewing on this device: a wide range of movies, games, 360 ¬∞ video will appear in the arsenal of user points, including experimental videos and educational content videos from leaders in the consumer content market.



The cost of glasses of virtual reality Samsung Gear VR in the United States will be $ 199, and on sale gadget will appear before the end of this year.
