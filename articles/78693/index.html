Railroad and internet - another step towards


According to the passenger service of the Belarusian Railways, by the end of this year, it is planned to start trial operation of the help system, which will allow Internet users to receive information on the availability of seats, a better train schedule and fare.
In 2010, the sale of travel documents for the train via the internet will begin. At the first stage, it is planned to pay for travel documents through the Internet banking system of JSB Belarusbank.
At the moment, the terms of reference should be agreed with the International Business Alliance CJSC (simple IBA) for the development of this system.
It is worth recalling that already on the road booking tickets via the Internet, with the issuance of travel documents at the stations of all regional cities of Belarus.
