What if you need to brand not only the content of the display, but also the appearance of the phone itself? For such cases, Snom offers three so-called customization packages:

Custom Logo Package . The cheapest version of out-of-the-box branding is that you get phones with your own logo printed above the display (instead of the Snom logo). The menu will be decorated with a standard color scheme. The minimum order for such a package is 50 phones.



Custom Printing Package . In this case, the manufacturer will supply you with phones not only with your logo, but with other signatures of all keys (at your discretion). You can add additional labels and logos in other places on the front of the phone. And also you can name the phone models as you want. The minimum order for this package is 1500 phones.



Custom Design Package . All of the above + you can order the manufacture of plastic parts of the body and tube of the phone in any other colors. The minimum order for such a package is 3000 phones.
