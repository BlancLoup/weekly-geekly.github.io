UPA 2008 Conference: Reviewers are invited
The organizing committee of the UPA 2008 conference invites reviewers to cooperate. Participation in the review is voluntary. Reviewers will receive from three to five papers for review in mid-October 2007.
Mikhail Elfimov. About the book Information Dashboard Design: The Effective Visual Communication of Data
The book "Information Dashboard Design", which tells about the design of interfaces, more precisely about the design of the screen. Not about the design process, not about visual design, not about usability, not about the information architecture and not about the user's tasks. In other words, this book is about exactly what a designer needs.
Mark Johnstone. Usability bugs
On the Internet today you can see many sites that have problems with usability. Constantly ignoring this important element in the creation of the site leads to the fact that visitors to the site are lost on it, are confused and disappointed. The time during which the visitor makes a decision to stay and explore the site or leave it, is 0.4 seconds. Attracting visitors to the site is a very difficult task - it is to quickly establish a connection with them and save it.
Shaun Medero. Paper prototypes
As interfaces become more and more universal, and the specifications describing them becoming shorter, it may be useful to replace user interface modeling with something simpler. All you need is paper, pen, scissors and your imagination.
Elena Bocharova. Professional education program "Usability Engineering"
The Usability Engineering Seminar (St. Petersburg, September 20-21, 2007) is an important milestone in shaping the fundamental professional usability of education in Russia. The new program is based on many years of experience in introducing usability in Russian companies and international experience, systematized as part of the Certified Usability Analyst (Human Factors International) certification program. This combination provides an international level of learning, taking into account the usability maturity stage in Russia.
