PHDays online contests: battle for invites



Only a month remains before PHDays VI , but already now there is a great opportunity to warm up before the forum and try yourself in several hacker contests, which will start on April 11 and 13. At stake is the opportunity to get free on PHDays!

Hackquest


ONsec company is preparing a traditional HackQuest competition, in which participants will be able to try their hand at solving various tasks on information security. The assignments will be based on real-life cases that ONsec had to work with during security audit projects in 2015 and 2016. The solution of each assignment will push for research and help develop new practical skills. Organizers promise a lot of web! Different, good and, most importantly, new. At the end of the competition, participants will receive souvenirs and invites to the forum. The contest will run from April 13 to 19.

Best Reverser


In order for fans (and professionals!) Of picking up the code to fully enjoy the PHDays conference, we decided to hold the Best Reverser contest a little earlier. Terms of assignments will be quite common for such contests. The winner will get a valuable prize and an invite to the conference. The competition will be held from 11 to 17 April.

Information about the start of competitions will be published soon. Follow the news and get ready - it will be fun!
