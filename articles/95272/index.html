Devil's contest "Usable Web Project"

All categorical habra. If you stumbled upon our blog earlier, you could not help but notice how biased our company is in matters of web design and usability. Over the past eight thousand years of work in the domestic SEO market, we have seen enough of all. A steel grip and an eagle eye have appeared, allowing, without a heat map and other lighttracking, to determine how usable this or that site.

It is difficult to surprise us. But boredom and spleen - the flip side of experience and professionalism. You start to feel like Mephistopheles.



So we decided to offer you a contest. We will evaluate the usability of young sites, startups and online stores for free , and the winners will receive a completely free SEO book and a completely free SEO audit from our company.

Conditions for participation (in small print, optional reading, just put a signature):

1) the site should not be more than a year,
2) applications are accepted in the comments to this or this entry, as well as by mail: smm@webprojects.ru,
3) the deadline for applications is July 12,
4) ALL contest participants will receive short comments on the usability of their sites,
5) the first five sites in our ranking will receive the SEO-devil book by Ivan Sevostyanov "Search engine optimization" , released this year by the publishing house "Peter",



6) the first three in our rating will receive free usability audits with specific recommendations,
7) and, finally, the owner of the most convenient and user-friendly site user will win a full-scale SEO audit from our company.

Evaluate sites will be the collective intelligence of the company WebProjects. We will announce the results after July 12 in our blog.
