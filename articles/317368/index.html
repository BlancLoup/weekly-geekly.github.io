Firsthand about software lifecycle management products

The HPE product portfolio for managing application development is very extensive: it covers solutions for the full cycle of software development, fast market launch and application quality control for different technologies and form factor options. Of course, we not only help our customers to implement the most effective approaches to the delivery of applications, but we also apply what we profess. Therefore, we want to give you the opportunity to learn how we develop our products ourselves, as well as to influence what our decisions will be able to do in the future.



At the ADM SIG event, dedicated to the release of the new version of Application Lifecycle Management, ALM Octane, we examined in detail how our products support Agile and DevOps, integrate with modern development tools, build and test applications from both the Open Source world and commercial ones. In these videos, HPE development center managers tell us how we build the new product development process in our company, how we moved from a waterfall development model to a continuous model with nightly builds and test automation, and also share experience in building CI / CD processes in software development .

How HPE is working to simplify the transition to a new development paradigm and application lifecycle within ALM Octane , says ALM / ALMOctane CTO Andrew Flick.
