5 important life lessons from Kung Fu Panda

Below is the translation of the article " 5 Critical Life Lessons You Can Learn From Kung Fu Panda ", in which the author tells us that he learned from the Kung Fu Panda cartoon .

In a rather classic plot of the Kung Fu Panda cartoon, there are several interesting life lessons, and the learning process is made in the form of a DreamWorks story. Although we first watched a cartoon a week ago, my children are still discussing it.
In the story, the panda, whose name is Po, is elected by the old wise turtle Ogway to become the Dragon Warrior and defeat the enemy. Unfortunately, fat and clumsy Po is far from being the best candidate for this position, and both Shifu, a martial artist and the Furious Five: Tigress, Monkey, Praying Mantis, Viper and Crane doubt his victory.

In my opinion, the denouement of the cartoon is closely related to the numerous streams of wisdom and quotes expressed by various characters. Wisdom can be found everywhere, all you need is to carefully watch and listen.

Here are five wise sayings I have chosen:
  1. Live the present
  2. From fate you can not escape
  3. There is a reason for everything
  4. Our passion gives us strength
  5. At first you can be the most unpromising.

This is what can be understood from the Kung Fu Panda cartoon: act, follow your fate, even if at first glance it seems to you that you cannot . You only need to believe that you are the Chosen One. And, following your Path, you can find out how outstanding you are, or how attractive you are - who you really are. And there is no price!
