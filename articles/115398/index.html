Do it yourself: register Ltd.

Not so long ago, we posted a podcast in which Anton Shayakhov told the world how to quickly and accurately register an IE. Completely unfairly, we ignored a more complex process. Correct.

It is given: the activity is conducted as an individual entrepreneur, there is Internet access, the level of intelligence is above average.
Task: in the shortest possible time and with minimal costs to close the IE and register an LLC.

Why LLC?


First of all, we will try to understand why it is necessary at all to break away from the private space of an entrepreneur and organize a legal entity. In general, in terms of accounting, legal entities get more than IP - accounting is more complicated, more reporting, higher rates, ask more strictly. It also discourages higher entry barriers and registration difficulties.

What then motivates entrepreneurs to choose such a legal form as an LLC? As a rule, it is warm to everyone that the founders of an LLC, unlike an individual entrepreneur, are not liable for debt obligations with their own property. Indeed, the LLC risks only the authorized capital, i.e., the probability that the founders will be left without pants at the broken trough decreases.

There is also a popular belief that a legal entity is a more serious organizational and legal form. This is not always true, but the fact remains that our companies trust more willingly. Along with this, legal entities are more willing to give out bank loans for business development. Getting a loan, being an IP is not so easy.

So, the pros still outweighed the cons, and you decided to stop the activity as an IP, but you do not know where to run.

Close the shop


Termination of activities as an IP is a separate process, if you are not an IP - feel free to scroll to the next heading:
  1.   2. You pay the state fee for closing the IP in any branch of Sberbank (160 r), a receipt can be taken in the same place.

  3. There you will write a statement, the FIU officer will check your contributions and, if all is well, will issue you a certificate.

  4. Submit a declaration on the taxes you paid to the inspection.

  5. If there is a checking account, close it. Notify the tax and funds. If there is a cash register, remove it from the registration.

  6. Bring to the tax office a statement P26001, a certificate from the pension fund and the original receipt for the state fee with a note about payment.

  7. 5 days later (if lucky), get a tax certificate of termination.
