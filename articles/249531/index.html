Tracker emotional stress Emvio. Video of the working prototype

Good evening everyone!



About two weeks have passed since my first post , in which the emotional stress tracker Emvio created by our team was described. Many thanks for the comments, for the most part they were very constructive and positive. The topic caused a keen interest, so before moving the topic to Geeltimes I decided to publish another post here.

I can not disagree with one of the comments - in the first post would not prevent the photo and (it would be quite good) video of the working prototype. To shoot a video project on the Kickstarter platform, we collected a working and decent looking prototype and shot a small video.

I suggest everyone to watch it on Youtube or just run it in the window below.



The original video was quite large (about 10 minutes), to save the live interest and save the time of the viewer it was reduced to 80 seconds. It was originally planned to make a mix, removing the device in the hands of each of the project participants. But after the last person was filmed, it turned out that due to the failure, all the videos were not preserved. This fact explains the increase in stress up to 5 points in the subject (up to this regrettable moment was 3). Due to the fact that by this time the team had safely gone home, only one person was captured.

The post has turned out small, I hope that it is not scary. For brevity is not only the sister of talent, but also, undoubtedly, the friend of any busy person. As promised, I am preparing information on the principle of operation of the device, it will soon be published most likely already at Geektimes.

See you soon!
Sincerely, Darta Systems Team.
