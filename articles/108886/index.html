Ariadn - SaaS personal recommendations
Leader: Mikhail Fedorov

A distinctive feature of the algorithm is the speed of its work, which allows you to process the data of millions and tens of millions of users. From the very beginning of its development, Ariadn focuses on serving users of social networks, media portals, search services, etc.
