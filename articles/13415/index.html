MyProgress.com

People love to watch their progress. In anything: in design, in study, in earnings. But in order to monitor progress, you need to remember where to start. A person is a forgetful creature, so he often writes down things to remember. And in the days of Web 2.0, a notebook and pen , even a PDA, is not that. Give us access from anywhere in the world, the ability to show off with the help of widgets. All this is available in the service MyProgress.com .


MyProgress allows you to keep track of your finances, your skills and affairs, property and health. All this in a nice interface.

During registration, you are invited to choose your profession and currency, and among the bourgeois yen and francs there is the native of most readers Ruble. After this ritual, we get to the page of finance . Separately, there are revenues, expenses, loans and investments (useful for starters who have bred). All this variety is decorating graphics (common to all finances and separate for each category). It is possible to add one-time (freelance, large purchase) and periodic (salary, loan payment) payments and expenses. Future revenue forecast available.

The next tab is the knowledge page . Behind this monumental name is a tool for tracking progress in learning and skills. For example, you code for n hours every day, sometimes you work overtime. At the end of the year, I suddenly wanted to know how many months of pure time you spent in tweaking lines of code. Thanks to MyProgress it is easier to do this (counting man-months).

Once you have worked, you can admire your possessions on the property page . The property includes real estate, bank deposits, animals, etc. Everything shows the cost ( purple speckled chinchilla costs a lot of money ) and in each category the total cost is highlighted ( and a lot of chinchillas - even more ). All again decorated with graphics. If you believe the service, its richest user has possessions of $ 104,835,100.

There are three more pages: health, widgets and a general log. The first page is in development, the second - allows you to insert fashionable news widgets from the knowledge page (finances and property - strictly personal information).
