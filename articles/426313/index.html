New Microsoft Learn

We are pleased to announce the launch of the new Microsoft Learn study site, a convenient resource for learning Microsoft technologies. Look under the cat for details!



Microsoft Learn Tasks


Our mission is to open new opportunities for each developer, architect and IT administrator with any level of training and in any organization. Staying abreast of the latest changes in our industry is not an easy task, even for those professionals who always keep abreast. And to constantly keep track of innovations, combining this with work and personal life, is almost impossible. That is why we created Microsoft Learn - a single convenient site where you can explore all the products and services available on our platform at an arbitrary rate using step-by-step instructions.

In 2016, we launched docs.microsoft.com, a modern portal where everyone can get the information necessary for professional development. Today, technical documentation is available on the site, covering the entire range of Microsoft solutions - from HoloLens to Azure. In the coming months, even more valuable technical information will appear here.

In the documentation, individual topics are covered in the smallest technical details. But in order to become a professional, you need to understand how links are established between such separate topics to form an integrated system. After the launch of the site, we held many conferences and meetings with developers who shared their ideas about the ideal learning environment. As a result, we have identified the following requirements:

  • Disclosure of the purpose of individual technical functions as part of a common solution.
  • Unified training location with the most current content.
  • The educational process, as close as possible to the real environment of technology.
  • Ability to independently determine the pace and schedule of classes.
  • Maximum practice.
  • And it would be great if the training was free.

Welcome to Microsoft Learn!


Today we publish more than 80 hours of training materials on Azure, Dynamics 365, PowerApps, Microsoft Flow, and Power BI. In addition, there are training materials to help developers, administrators, and solution architects prepare for certification.

  • All materials are grouped according to training schemes that form a complete picture of the application of the technology being studied and allow you to master all the necessary skills.
  • Training schemes consist of walkthroughs with interactive exercises on working with code. To study and complete them, you get free temporary access to Azure resources. However, you do not need to enter credit card information for authentication.
  • On the website, you can track the progress of your training, test knowledge, test deployments, as well as earn points, overcome levels, receive certificates and awards.



As we have said, the main task of Microsoft Learn is to promote the development of all IT professionals with any level of training. This will help continuously expanding knowledge base Microsoft Learn.

We will constantly add new products, specializations, training schemes and features to the website. We are pleased to contribute to your development.

Waiting for your feedback!


We hope you enjoy Microsoft Learn. Write to us what other educational materials and features would be worth adding to the website.
