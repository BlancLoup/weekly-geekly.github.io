Hyundai T7 Tablet - Samsung Exynos 4 Core + Built-in GPS


We all are used to the fact that Chinese tablets use Allwinner, Rockchip, Amlogic, and occasionally Mediatek chips. And if there are tablets with a Samsung Exynos chip, then they are quite expensive.
Hyundai tried to correct this injustice, and installed the Samsung Exynos chip in its new T7 model, and also a GPS module as a bonus. And all this at a price close to $ 150.

It should be immediately noted that the automaker Hyundai is not related to the production of this tablet. Hyundai Digital is manufactured by Hyundai Digital , which is located in China.

Specifications


7-inch IPS screen with a resolution of 1280x800
Four-row video accelerator Mali 400MP4
Quad-core Samsung Exynos 4412 processor on Cortex A9 architecture
1 GB of RAM
Android 4.0.4 operating system (upgrade to Android 4.2.2)
8 GB user memory + microSD card slot
2 cameras: rear 2 megapixel and front 0.3 megapixel
Connectivity: Wi-Fi (802.11 b / g / n), Bluetooth, GPS
Optional: USB OTG
3300 mAh battery
Dimensions, weight: 196x118x10.9 mm, 296 grams
