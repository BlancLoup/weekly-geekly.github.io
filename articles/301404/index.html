All you need to know about DevCon 2016



DevCon 2016 conference will be held on May 25 and 26, which means that in 2 days the largest event for developers from Microsoft will take place! We have collected all the announcements and necessary links that may be needed so that, regardless of the location, you can follow the conference and attend reports of interest remotely.

Join the live stream

You will be able to join the online broadcast via the link at 9:30 am on May 25th. The broadcast of the conference program will begin at 10:00 am from the opening on the main stage of the event and continue until the very end - May 26th. Do not miss reports on Windows 10, Azure, Office, reports from community representatives, industry experts and a dedicated startup track with representatives from IIDF, Skolkovo, GVA LaunchGurus, Microsoft Ventures and Microsoft Russia. On the broadcast page, you can download a reminder to yourself in the calendar, so as not to miss the reports of interest.

We recommend you to visit the conference site to study the program presented this year.

Download the conference application

We collected all the necessary information about the DevCon 2016 conference in a mobile application available for iOS , Android and Windows 10 . You can see the entire list of reports, information about speakers, get a link to the online broadcast and other additional information. However, the application will be useful to you even after the end of the conference, because all the largest events for Microsoft developers in Russia will appear in it.

Join the conference and share your impressions on Twitter, Instagram, Facebook or Vkontakte using the official hashtag #msdevcon .
