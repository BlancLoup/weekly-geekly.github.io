If we are inconsistent and contradictory - our teams will be the same. If we never seem to leave the office, then our employees will also wonder if they should do the same.

We must constantly learn


Our world is changing with unimaginable speed. What is left for us to keep up with the times? Right! Constantly monitor these changes, learn something new.

Components of a daily 20-mile march


Our daily march consists of two main components: the morning and evening.

Morning
In the morning we need to focus and orient ourselves on making the right decisions and making the right choice.
  • What is my mission / goal?
  • What matters most today?
  • What should I be grateful for today?
  • What morning habits set me up for success?

End of the day
In the evening, we focus on the results of the day - what we did, what decisions we took, what opportunities we discovered.
  • What successes have I achieved today?
  • What is new learned / learned? How will I develop further?
  • Who am I on the way? Who do i need
  • What evening habits will help me to relax, sleep well and prepare for the next day?

Even if it seems to you that the morning component is more important, do not ignore the evening rules - without following them you will lead your team to stagnation.
