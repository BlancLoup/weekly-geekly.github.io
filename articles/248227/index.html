Pivotal stops developing Groovy & Grails from March 31



Bad news everyone!

The company Pivotal, which sponsored the development of Groovy & Grails in recent years, announced the termination of sponsoring projects from March 31.

Groovy 2.4 and Grails 3.0 releases will be the latest releases under the wing of Pivotal.

Does this mean that the future of Groovy & Grails is over? Of course not! Both projects have already begun to look for new development sponsors. They will continue to develop as Open Source projects, as it was before, but now they need our help in finding new development sponsors.

How can we, the developer community, help them? First and foremost, spread the word. Let every company that uses Groovy & Grails in its development, learn that these projects are looking for a new sponsor, and perhaps it is your company that will become their new home.
