See you at DevCon 2014!

Tomorrow, May 28, the largest Microsoft software development and testing conference - DevCon 2014 will begin in the Moscow region!

DevExpress Company traditionally acts as a partner of the conference, participating in the efforts of Microsoft to develop and support the IT community in Russia.

DevCon 2014 is a unique opportunity to learn about where the Microsoft platform is heading and to hear about the first results of the introduction and use of new technologies. The conference is held in the open air, which allows you to escape from the bustle of the city and concentrate on full immersion in technology.

If you also participate in DevCon 2014, be sure to visit the DevExpress company booth! Here you can talk live with the developers of our products, get useful and pleasant gifts, and also find out what is written on the back of the samovar :-)



If you do not have the opportunity to visit DevCon 2014 in person, we invite you to join the online broadcast on May 28 from 11:00 (Moscow time) and on May 29 from 9:00 (Moscow time).

On the official conference website, you can visit more than 50 technical reports online on 4 key topics:
  • Development of client applications for the Windows platform and Windows Phone;
  • Cloud computing and the Web;
  • Tools, development methodologies and programming languages;
  • Corporate development, data processing.

A detailed schedule of the conference can be found at http://www.msdevcon.ru/schedule .



See you at DevCon 2014!
