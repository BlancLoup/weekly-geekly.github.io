About the site metro29.ru

Hello. Today I want to tell you about who and why made the site metro29.ru .
The fact is that many on the Internet were puzzled by the question of where and why it was done.
The site was clearly and clearly written for what purposes it was created. Anyway, most of the people did not believe. Well, the people do not believe that there are people in our country who do something not for the sake of profit, but for help for.

Created this site kzotov and boston , the server for the site provided mic . We all live in Chelyabinsk and learned about the terrorist attack from Twitter. Then we tried to find out at least something from the media, but almost all sites were inaccessible. Attempts to find lists of victims, to make sure that no friends and acquaintances were among them were in vain , Twitter was full of unverified information and made the situation more intense. Similarly, others could not see. Actually, for this we took the courage to make this site. Only two static pages, which gave nginx, did not exert any load on the server, and anyone could get the necessary information. Now the media sites are working as usual, so the site metro29.ru is not updated and only contains links to official sources.
