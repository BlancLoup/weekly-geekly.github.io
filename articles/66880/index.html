Verbatim USB flash drive

Verbatim has released the original TUFF-CLIP flash drive, the body of which is made in the form of a carbine. Actually, the value of such a solution is the ability to securely attach the device anywhere. So losing TUFF-CLIP is quite difficult.


TUFF-CLIP weighs 57 grams (impact-resistant ABS plastic case), comes in 4 and 8 gigabytes of memory on board. The connector extends using the slider. Works with all current OC.

Cost depending on the volume is $ 30 and $ 50.
