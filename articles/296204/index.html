"Innopolis" ate money allocated for the project of the Russian satellite Internet for the "people"

In 2012-2013, 4.7 billion rubles were allocated from the federal budget through the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media for the construction of the Innopolis Innovation Center in Tatarstan. The decision to build was taken in the summer of 2012 after the appeal of the President of Tatarstan Rustam Minnikhanov to the President of Russia Vladimir Putin. The project was actively promoted by Nikolai Nikiforov, who in the same year moved from the chair of the Minister of Communications of Tatarstan to the post of Minister of Communications of Russia.

However, the federal budget of 2012 did not initially include funds for the construction of Innopolis. The Ministry of Communications and Roskomnadzor have found a total of 2.7 billion rubles from other budget items. Roskomnadzor managed to save 540 million rubles in 2012 on expenses for the conversion of the radio frequency spectrum. For its part, the Ministry of Communications and Mass Media has saved 2.2 billion dollars on the implementation of projects approved by the Commission under the President of Russia on modernization and technological development of the economy.
