VKontakte and commercial application development

I am glad that the administration of the social network VKontakte turned to face the application developers. But I am interested in one moment. that I just ran into again.

The problem is the following: the administration can block the application at any time and without warning.

Even despite the fact that they had previously written a letter asking them to evaluate the riskiness of the application in relation to the blocking.

Look here. I'll take a certain amount of money. I will invest in the designer. And God forbid, I'm also not a programmer. And I will take another programmer or two to write an application. Maybe even invest in the merchants. If it is a bank card, then it will take more jur. face. And if the money is not its own. How then to explain that the bust - the application is blocked?

Especially when it is very popular among users and was blocked at the time of explosive growth.

This morning I discovered that my application, which in one day increased its audience from 900 people to 4000. And the growth rate increased. Since the saturation point was much higher. The essence of the application was in the logging of friends added and deleted by the user. Even the fee was not taken.

What is criminal in it? I cite a link to the blocked application so that you can be sure of the numbers. This is not PR, but material evidence - vkontakte.ru/app545981 .

I want to note that in the new version of the API applications, officially appeared the function of obtaining a list of friends of the user, if he gave his consent.

And now plans for the implementation of other applications collapse? Or is there still a way to civilized relationships?

Thank you for your attention and hope for the support of colleagues.

UPDATE 04/16/2009 06:15 The application was unlocked by the administration. Thank!
