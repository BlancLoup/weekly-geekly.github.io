Apple temporarily closes the Search Ads service for Russian developers because of the "tax on Google"



From December 12 of this year, application developers from Russia are losing the opportunity to use the Search Ads service, which serves to advertise their application in the App Store search. About this corporation notified its partners by letter. Already running campaigns will be suspended until special notice.

Unfortunately, it is not yet clear when the service can be used again - it is closed indefinitely. The Corporation explains its actions by changes in the tax legislation of Russia, which will become relevant from January 1, 2019.

On January 1, those foreign companies that cooperate with Russian partners must register with the Federal Tax Service, independently calculating and paying VAT on sales in Russia. At the moment it is the responsibility of customers.
