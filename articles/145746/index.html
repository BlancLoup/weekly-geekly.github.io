ICANN opens a list of new top-level domains

As ICANN promised after the transfer of big May news to June, today it became known which new first-level domain zones were accepted earlier this spring.



This morning, for the promotion of the holiday, the following general figures were laid out :

  • Total number of applications: 1930 = 1846 "standard" + 84 "community"
  • 66 applications were submitted for geographical names
  • 116 names were not taken in the Latin alphabet, i.e. IDN names
  • Requests came from 60 countries
  • From North America: 911
  • From Europe: 675
  • From Asia and Oceania: 303
  • From South and Central America: 24
  • From Africa: 17
  • More than one application was submitted for 230 domains (together, 751 applications were received)
