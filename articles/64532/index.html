Nakazator - betray the righteous judgment of the enemy of the people!


I want to present to the respected world community a new entertainment project - Nakazator ( http://nakazator.ru/ ). Surely many of us have ever wanted to punish someone who deserves it, especially if this someone is a public person. known to the general public. Nakazator allows you to give the people's court literally anyone, whether it be a politician, a star, or even just a boss at work. The result entirely depends on the users. According to the total number of votes, a court decision is made - to justify the defendant, or to punish. The method of execution is determined by the user who added the defendant; in addition, he can leave the choice for the majority. Among the variants of executions, both banal (shooting, hanging), and more perverted (forced eating cigarette butts, sealing in the trash can, heating in the toilet, etc.) At the same time, for users, of course, complete anonymity is observed.

In addition, the site has a system of personal messages, user profiles, RSS-feed, and other necessary attributes.

Despite the fact that the resource was originally planned as an entertainment, over time it may become a kind of monitor of public opinion on many issues related to the people discussed there.

In the plans:

- visualization of the process of execution itself (comic video illustrating the process of execution);

- the ability to send a notice of the results to the defendant himself (if, for example, the defendant is the boss at work), the opportunity for the defendant to speak in his own defense;

- increase in the weight of the voice for active and "trust" users (now everyone can leave only one vote in one discussion);
