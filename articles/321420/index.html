Algorithm-Driven Design - How Artificial Intelligence Is Changing Design

Smashing Magazine has an updated and structured version of an article on algorithmic design. It contains examples of tools that will help simplify the construction of the interface, the preparation of graphics and content, as well as the personalization of the product. In 2016, the technological basis for such tools became more accessible - the design community also began to look closely at algorithms, neural networks, and artificial intelligence. So it's time to rethink the modern role of the designer. Also updated the Russian version .

Tiny bought Dribbble

A conglomerate of design companies Tiny bought Dribbble. They now have a rather interesting portfolio - the Designer News community, the SMM service Buffer, the design studios MetaLab and Pixel Union, Task Manager Flow and another bundle of related services. Owners are planning to continue to buy near-design projects .



UX-Environment AMA: Clay (ex-SFCD)

Session AMA from UX-Wednesday and vc.ru with the Clay design team, founded by the SFCD / SoftFacade studio team: Anton Zykin, Oleg Turbaba, Dmitry Tsozik and Alexander Khmelevsky answered questions. Originally launched in St. Petersburg, SoftFacade later opened offices in New York and San Francisco. And recently, the backbone of the team launched a new brand Clay. This is one of the brightest design studios with domestic roots, which was noted for work for large brands and has long gone beyond the icons - the portfolio has many interesting interface works, and the list of awards includes the majority of large international awards.
