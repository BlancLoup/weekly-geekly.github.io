Russians on IGF!

The other day, indie gaming festival Independent Games Festival summed up the results in the Student Showcase nomination.
Last year, our country was represented by the acclaimed HammerFall (HammerFight) in the west, authored by Konstantin Koshutin , who had already gone on sale and many players won the laurels of the best domestic indie :)



But this year it was not without surprises, I mean the Russians. The experimental game of Konstantin Gominyuk ( website ) under the uncomplicated name Mi ( download ) was nominated for the Student Showcase , but, to my great regret, could not stand the pressure of the strongest competitors, among which there is also a well-known in the ASCIIp0rtal community .

But nevertheless, Mrs. Fortune did not bypass our compatriot (to hell with the framework, Ukraine and Russia are fraternal countries) by party - the game Mi was mentioned by the festival jury as exemplary and made the list of recommendations. At previous festivals, indie industry monsters like Portal and flOw were included in this list.

There is a reason for joy, I think :)
