Airnergy - we are charging along the way from WiFi

At the last (passing) consumer electronics exhibition in Las Vegas, RCA presented its new development - Airnergy. This is a small, handheld device that will help you charge your gadget without wires. Details and link to the photo gallery below.

Development data is very scarce. But here the news itself is more important than the technical component. So, the device allows you to charge your mobile devices on the road. The most important thing is that you are in the coverage area of ‚Äã‚Äãat least one WiFi network. Obviously, the same method of induction is used as in the recently popular wireless charging, but much more clearly tuned and more sensitive. Again, there is no exact data on the technology.

The device is compact enough to put in a backpack or jacket pocket. At this stage, has a miniUSB to connect the "rechargeable". Data on charging speed no.

Next year, the company is going to integrate its development directly into batteries for popular / modern mobile phones. This will theoretically lead to no need to connect the charger to the mobile phone at all, since WiFi networks in cities are developing at an incredible speed, even in the Russian Federation.

Airnergy will be available this summer for purchase at a price of $ 39. But this, of course, in the United States. When and what prices we will have in Russia, no one predicts. But the device is undoubtedly worth the attention of constantly moving city geeks.
