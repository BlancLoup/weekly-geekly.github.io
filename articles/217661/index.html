Science fiction technology: Warp (Star Trek)



In this series of posts, we will talk about many Star Trek technologies, such as warp, light engines, and similar sci-fi and technology-like achievements today.

Wrap, also known as ‚Äúwarping,‚Äù is a technology of space-time curvature using plasma and multilayer curvature coils, which can be found in different variations in different races.

Each race with warp technology and above is officially recognized as being sufficiently developed to go out into space and collaborate openly with all other races, at least according to the rules of the alpha quadrant.

Warp itself is not an engine, it does not move the ship, it shifts the space-time inside the zone around the ship, relative to the space-time outside it. It is not explainable how ships can catch up with each other on the warp, because for this it is not enough that we need the most accurate warp generators (gondolas, rings, etc.), so also the sensors must be superluminal and without distortion. As for the sensors, they use a subspace in which all signals reach from point to point instantly, which does not allow to find out the distance to the target, only the direction.



Also, warp is the source of almost all space-time distortions in serials, where ships, reaching 10 warp on the old scale, begin to move with infinite speed and find themselves at all points of time and space, which has been achieved more than once, but not explained, as a fabric of space time it carries and regulates such interventions.

Warp is layered on the zone around the ship, each layer requires more and more energy, but also allows you to bend space-time even more, which gives priority to speed. Warp is also a fairly flexible field, which is shown in different numbers and shapes of emitters. The simplest type of warp emitters is a ring around a ship. These are used by the Vulcans and the XCV330 was built with such a system.



Another option is 1 gondola bottom, top or through the central axis of the ship.
Also very often, almost all races use a variation with 2 gondolas, usually on the sides of the ship.
However, this is not all, there are modifications that can be considered transitional from a full ring (it has the smallest cost of applying each new field) to a single-gondola type, which can be detected using 3 or more gondolas, sometimes with different configurations, to make the warp field cheaper and resilient. But this affects the energy consumption at speeds of less than 5 warp, which is why such structures are usually used on the fastest ships and ships where you need to minimize the cost of superluminal movement and transfer energy to shields and weapons.

Such ships often become military dreadnoughts and quick-response cruisers. Almost every ship in Star Trek has an analogue with 3-4 or even 5 gondolas, which also represents a smooth transition between different modifications and new ship hulls.



Among other things, in Star Trek, the warp field does not create space-time gaps between the objects in front and behind, which translates into the need to repel space debris and dust from the ship in front so that it does not hit it at superluminal speed by making a hole in the hull.

It also shows that ships cannot pass through stars and planets, as if pushing their space-time, which also forces us to route the route not directly, but taking into account all oncoming large objects.

Warp technologies have great advantages, but also disadvantages, such as the need for constant feeding of high-power plasma flows to each ring, as well as space-time requirements and the presence of a subspace, which collectively results in a Cochrane number other than 0.

The Cochrane number is the coefficient by which the entire power of the warp field is multiplied, which gives the real power of the warp field. If it is 0, warp is impossible, it also means that the subspace fabric is burned in this space-time, which usually occurs when omega particles explode. Accordingly, the Federation and some other cosmic powers by all means prevent the creation of new omega molecules, which, in addition to a huge explosion, can create a complete rupture of subspace within a pair of solar systems.
If the Cochrane number in the limit is from 0 to 1, then warp is possible, although not at full capacity.
