New feature in Gmail Labs - improved video chat

Google announced a new lab: Improved video chat. This lab allows you to see new improvements and features before they are included for everyone. To enable it, you need to go to the Gmail Labs tab in Settings . Enable "Video chat enhancements".
Here is what it looks like:


What can be seen now - the resolution has improved and the size of the video window has increased.
