Yahoo and Microsoft have signed a search agreement

On Friday, Yahoo and Microsoft finalized a collaboration agreement on search and online advertising, according to a preliminary statement released in July. The process dragged on until December, including because of the work of highly paid lawyers, who, as they say , for a very long time read and coordinated every letter in the contract.
