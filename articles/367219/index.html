Standard alarms are not selective. The sound of some of them awakens not only the husband, wife, children, but all the neighbors (this happens even if the alarm is relatively quiet). But what to do if today someone needs to get up on a call, and someone can lie in bed a little longer?

Then the alarm clock Wakƒì comes to the rescue, which is designed to wake up just one person sleeping on the bed (husband, wife, child and cat as a bonus can sleep together). The alarm clock is very selective, and not so much with sound, as with a narrowly directed beam of light.



How does this work?


The device is mounted on the wall above the headboard. The alarm clock is equipped with a Bluetooth wireless communication module, through which it communicates with the native application installed on the user's smartphone.

Thus, the natural environmental conditions (sunrise and the awakening of nature) are modeled, so that the awakening is easier than with a normal alarm clock. As soon as a person wakes up, the system understands this and stops acting.



The main advantages of Wakƒì are :

  • Wake-up only one person, the second (and third) continues to sleep;
  • Sunrise modeling with more effective awakening;
  • No need to press the "disable" button. As soon as a person wakes up, the system shuts down by itself;
  • If a person continues to sleep, the system continues to work;
  • If a person got up earlier, before the alarm, the system detects it, and the alarm does not work;
  • A firmware upgrade is possible;
  • The battery lasts for several months;
  • The system tracks a person by thermal radiation, no photos or videos.
