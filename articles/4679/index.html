Internet Ukrainian or Kiev?

Company SputnikMedia.net. published the next monthly statistics report of the Ukrainian Internet.
At the end of the month, the audience of the Ukrainian Internet amounted to 3 445 636 people. The average weekly audience is 1,595,034 people, the daily number is 610,972 people.
Ukrainians spent an average of 1.11 sessions per day, generating 602,425,403 hits and spending 1,503,971,475 minutes on the network, 3.27 minutes on the site.
As before, most of the Ukrainian Internet users are residents of Kiev, according to the authors of the study, their share is a surprisingly round number - exactly 53.54%. At the same time, they produce 66.78% of all hits.
According to SputnikMedia, 86% of internet users use Windows XP and the browser Internet Explorer 6.x (although Yandex thinks that they use the browser)
Interestingly, 58.89% of Ukrainian users prefer Google and only 14.97% prefer Yandex, and as many as 12 users per month used search from search.com.ua.
Favorite requests of Ukrainians "abstracts", "work" and "weather", unlike, for example, from Russia, where "sex", "porn" and "download" are more popular.
