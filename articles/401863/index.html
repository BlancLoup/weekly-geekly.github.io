Google closes its Messenger Spaces


Google closes its Messenger Spaces just ten months after launch. The service will be completely stopped on April 17, 2017, according to the Google support page.

From March 3, 2017, Spaces will be available only in read mode, most likely, so that users have time to save important information somewhere else.

In the period before April 17, users can:

  • view, edit and delete content;
  • delete all created Spaces chat rooms or exit them, and delete contacts;
  • report support to rule violation and block users.

After this date, the Spaces project and all data will be deleted.
