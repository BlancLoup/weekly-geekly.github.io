Couponators are dead. Long live BonJoin

The peak of popularity of coupon sites that sometimes offered unbelievable discounts on goods and services has long passed. There were many reasons: the bad faith of partners, the greed of the couponers themselves, and the artificial nature of this business. And yet, it was the couponers that gave birth to the new trend - the active search by the consumer for the optimal offer, ideally balanced in price and quality. This idea was used by the creators of BonJoin.

In the conditions of the market, the client and the performer are constantly looking for a friend. This is an endless process, it is exhausting, it often brings disappointment - either the price is high, the terms are not satisfied, the quality is lame, it is impossible to pay comfortably. Here both, the client and the performer, begin to lose patience. And in order to save nerves, money and time, and the process of interaction between the client and the contractor is conveniently simplified, we created BonJoin .

The search for an artist on the site is fast - within 30 minutes, 80% of orders receive more than 3 offers, for this it will take 2 minutes to place a request on BonJoin website for what you need. Since several executors respond to a request at once, bidding for an order occurs: how competing performers are accepted at an auction to reduce the price for their services so that the customer chooses them. Rating artists, reflecting their reliability, helps to make a choice. Today, about 85,000 partners are registered in the BonJoin system.

Working with BonJoin is easy. The user searches for a service and visits the site where he first selects a category ‚Äî for example, insurance services, real estate, tourism, medical services, etc., and then fills out a form. Each category has its own form with several fields that should be filled out. The more detailed the form, the easier it will be for partners to make an adequate offer. In case of something unusual in a given category, there is a field for arbitrary filling.

After the user has sent a request, the system notifies all partners working in this category about the receipt of a new order. They are potential performers of the order. By accessing the BonJoin interface and responding to a request, they give their price and offer a brief description of how they see the solution to the task.

BonJoin is a unique playground. By registering here only one account, the user can act in three roles at once and freely switch between them. That is, on the one hand, he can order some services, and on the other - act as a private individual or company. Each role has its own set of features.

BonJoin uses Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) for organizing email transmission within the system - this infrastructure seemed to be the most convenient from the point of view of sending a huge number of emails. The interface itself is created using the Yii framework. This choice is due to the high performance of the framework and quite powerful features for working with forms - and for the BonJoin service this factor has significant weight. When developing the interface, the service team proceeded from the simplicity and convenience of users.

To register a user, only one e-mail is sufficient - the user can fill in all the other necessary information in his profile, based on the selected roles and the need to work in the system. Registration via social networks is also possible.



Application alerts are set up and managed using icons to the left of the application name in the list of user orders. You can select an alert (SMS or e-mail), view the details, copy and delete the application.
