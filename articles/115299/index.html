What to do?


The problem is solved by creating a hybrid office and cafe. Many come to work in the cafe for a free wifi and normal food. However, the cafe owners are hardly excited about such visits. And visitors do not contribute to focus on work. The solution is simple - we make a cafe, which is primarily for the freelancer. We supply part of the tables with partitions, and we make part of them completely isolated (cabins). Each place is equipped with high-speed Internet, there are cabinets (turnkey), desk lamp, mouse pad and similar trifles. Of course there is a wardrobe and other amenities. In the cafe, of course, anyone can come, but the requirement for guests is to observe silence, the lack of booze and the like.

The main visitors are freelancers, they rent tables, that is, they work for a certain period at the same place, setting it up for themselves. Minimum rent - let's say a month. Rent is not very high, because entering into a contract, the freelancer negotiates the menu (which can be changed only by prior notice) and subscribes to other bonuses. Such as visiting the gym or swimming pool. Freelancer can rent a computer, if you do not want to carry or work with a laptop. Additional buns, such as a coffee machine or meeting room, are also available for subscription. The number of options can be anything: we take any office and adopt what is good. And we offer the freelancer to use it for the minimum price at the time of rental. As mentioned above - the tables are normal, with partitions and VIP - completely isolated, with air conditioning and a sofa, for example. Yes, even with a personal shower, although it is in any way common.

Cafe owner - receives a set of guaranteed visitors, with a predefined menu that will minimize costs. The owner, depending on the number of subscribers - concludes a partnership with the gym or visiting coach, who conducts group classes. Also, partners will want to become distributors of food machines, ATMs and terminals (everything will depend on the volume). In general, in order not to list the obvious paths of development, it will suffice to say that this will be essentially an office without a boss. No bosses, but there is a team. True, everyone is busy with his own, but will you refuse to help your neighbor with whom you work side by side?

Freelancer also solves his main problems. He motivates his need to work out the rent and the general staff, he eats according to the fresh products regime, no one distracts him like at home, he has a free schedule, all the charms of work in the office are present, but there is no bad boss and he will not. And the coach reminds that it's time for training. Isn't it a dream? :)

Of course, additional costs. But given that the freelancer will be the consumer of this cafe, the rent can be made minimal. Let them be minimal, the main thing is that they are encouraged not to skip work all the time. Well, the main incentive for a truant: money is dripping for rent, you have already paid for lunch.
