Today is the third, the official birthday of the FreshSMO social marketing studio !

What do we plan to do?

Our studio will realize its potential in the market of social marketing and social optimization services.

In short - we will help our customers to use the potential of social networks, blogs and Russian-speaking Internet communities for the benefit of business.

What is SMO and SMM?

SMO (Social Media Optimization) - site optimization for social media.

It is necessary to optimize the site for the needs of the audience, make it convenient for users and push them to the actions we need. It is possible to encourage a site visitor to quote the article in their blog, subscribe to updates or invite friends to the site with the words ‚Äúwow! how cool! ‚ÄùSocial optimization of the site helps to solve all these problems.

SMM (Social Media Marketing) - social media marketing. This is an active activity in attracting visitors to the site from social services. The marketing component of SMM is the integration of the advertising message into the communicative environment. The more organically integrated advertising message, the better it meets the demands put forward by the community - the more potential customers will come to your site, the more loyal the audience will be, the better they will relate to your brand.

  • Creation and promotion of corporate blogs "turnkey".

  • Organization of advertising campaigns in the blogosphere.
  • Creating and promoting your own projects.
