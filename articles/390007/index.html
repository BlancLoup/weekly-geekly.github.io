A good project from the Kickstarter site - LuMini lamp with a light alarm clock

March 12, 2014 on Kickstarter started a company to raise funds to finance the project LuMini . Less than in the month of April 1, the necessary amount for the start of production was collected, and by the end of the company the amount of fees was $ 76,240 dollars. The company involved 1 171 people. In principle, for the start of sales it was enough to collect $ 25,000, but with an increase in the budget, additional bonuses were announced in the form of releasing lamps for different sockets. In addition to the widespread E26 and E27, when the threshold of $ 37,000 was reached, an E14 base was added (a very popular format among small bedside and table lamps), and a slightly more specific and compact E12 was added when the threshold of $ 50,000 was reached. In principle, the E14 format with a small adapter for E27 turned out to be a winning option, it was in this form that I got this dj.





Here is the packaging of the lamp. Very decent look. Some kind of cardboard is very dense, and inside the base on which the lamp is held is generally made of cardboard, as if wooden. So the inner holder holds the lamp very well, in the center, not letting it lean against the walls of the package. This is true for transportation, especially since the body of the lamp is ceramic.



Yes, yes, no cheap plastic painted with silverfish for aluminum, only rub ceramics.



Together with the lamp inside the package lies the instruction and the adapter base for chambered E27. That's basically all the equipment.

For the initial tests, I have a detail from an old floor lamp, there is just an E14 base, and we will check all lamp modes on it.

There are also modes for creating mood:

Choose a music track in the lamp app and the lamp adjusts to your track. Playing comes from the phone. If you use a smartphone from Apple, it is very convenient to use Airplay here and send sound to external speakers. Get a full party.

Flicker.




In this mode, the lamp just blinks all the colors of the rainbow at a certain interval. At first it lights up to the full in one color, then it gradually fades out and the next one turns on.

Relaxing light




In this mode, the light alternates in different colors in warm shades: red, orange, yellow, pink, etc. Under such soft lighting it will be pleasant to spend time at a romantic dinner, the lamp will work almost like a fireplace, pleasantly lighting the space.
