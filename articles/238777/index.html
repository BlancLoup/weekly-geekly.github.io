Epson Contest Reminder for Android Developers

Dear habravchane,

We remind you that from September 1 to November 30, 2014, there is an Epson Moverio BT-200 video contest developer competition in three categories: the best AR application, the best non AR application, the best game (AR or non AR does not matter).

Prizes in each of the three categories (yes, that is, three pieces each!):

  • 1st place: Epson Moverio BT-200 video glasses
  • 2nd place: Epson EH-TW490 home projector
  • 3rd place: Epson L355 Print Factory

Where are you, developers?

To participate in the competition it is not necessary to develop an application from scratch ! You can port an existing one, upload it to the Moverio Apps Market and inform us about it. And you will automatically become a participant in the competition and get a real chance to win one of the prizes.

No seriously! We want to distribute the prizes to the participants of the competition, but you cannot see something.
