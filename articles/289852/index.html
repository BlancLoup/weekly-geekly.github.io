TNS Russia: Runet audience growth slows down

Based on a recent TNS Russia study , the number of Runet users ceases to grow. Even on the most popular Internet sites, a significant increase in the number of users was not found. And what's more, in some of them the audience has shrunk.

Earlier, the audience of the Runet increased as the residents of the regions and the age population connected.

Some experts cite the slowdown in economic growth in the country and, therefore, the slow connection of remote regions of Russia, as well as the stagnation of investments in mobile communications in hard-to-reach areas, as a reason for the slowdown in the connection of new subscribers to the network.


Source: TNS Russia

Also, they are not ready to save on products, medical goods, clothing and footwear. But to reduce the cost of jewelry, furniture and goods for repair, compatriots "consider it possible."
