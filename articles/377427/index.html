Coursera has opened effective training courses.



Since March 1, an online course has been launched at the University of California at San Diego that will tell you how to study with high performance. All the mistakes of the students, all the techniques and techniques of effective study will be considered at this training. More than 100 thousand people are willing to participate.

The author of the course, Barbara Oakley, will explain the latest research in cognitive psychology and neurology. He will tell about his practical experience of teaching particularly complicated courses at the university. In general, the goal of the course is to teach how to learn correctly, to explain how the process of memorizing new information, its assimilation and consolidation takes place.

Barbara Oakley herself, now being a doctor in the field of system design, according to her, cannot boast of success in her student life. Especially - in mathematics. Nevertheless, the personal experience of Barbara Oakley gave her to develop their own methods of improving the effectiveness of training. And she outlined these techniques in her book, a brief squeeze from which we give below.
