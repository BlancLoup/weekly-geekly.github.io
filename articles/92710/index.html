ASP.NET flow program on DevConf 2010 - May 17


Formed the final program of the ASP.NET report flow at the DevConf conference. The program looks great, there is a specially invited well-known guest and many speakers with interesting topics.

Stephen Walther - is a Senior Program Manager at Microsoft. Engaged in creating content related to ASP.NET and ASP.NET MVC technologies. Author of well-known books on the development of ASP.NET.

In addition, please note that another Microsoft employee, Gaidar Magdanurov, will speak on the topic of Dynamic Data technology.

Below is a shortened version of the program (full version can be found on the website):
devconf.ru/asp.net/page/programm

9: 00-10: 00 Registration of participants. Presentation of accompanying materials.

10: 00-10: 15 Opening remarks

10: 15-11: 00 Alexander Nevolin: Application of Visual-ORM technology in the development of web applications in the context of frequently changing requirements

11: 00-11: 15 Coffee break

11: 15-12: 00 (translation hall) Stephen Walther: What's New in ASP.NET 4 Web Forms

11: 15-12: 00 Alexey Barantsev: Why does testing take so much time?

12: 00-12: 15 Break | Flipchart Session:

12: 15-13: 00 Andrey Mayorov: Using XSLT to develop websites (on ASP.NET)

13: 00-13: 15 Break

13: 15-14: 00 Gaidar Magdanurov: Using Dynamic Data in practice

14: 00-15: 00 Lunch

15: 00-15: 45 (translation hall) Stephen Walther: What's New in ASP.NET MVC 2

15: 00-15: 45 Layer Dmitry and Wolfson Boris: Agile MixFight on the rule of Win-Win

15: 45-16: 45 Lyubko Aleksey: Development of mobile sites on ASP.NET MVC 2 in Visual Studio 2010

16: 45-17: 00 Coffee Break

17: 00-17: 45 Vladimir Yunev: Managed Extensibility Framework: introduction and use with ASP.NET MVC

17: 45-18: 15 Andrei Ignatov: Rapid development of the site on ASP.Net without using databases

18: 15-18: 30 Solemn closing of DEVCONF :: ASP.NET 2010, drawing of prizes, presentation of participant certificates.

Let me remind you that the conference will be held May 17-18 in Moscow in Crocus Expo. During the conference, there will be at the same time 5 streams of reports on technologies: ASP.NET, Python, Ruby, Perl, PHP. Each conference participant will receive free access to any report of any stream. Thus, you can visit exactly those reports that you are interested in in many different directions at once.
