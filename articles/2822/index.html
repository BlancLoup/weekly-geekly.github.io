An online brain fitness center opens in California

California-based Dakim has opened the world's first brain fitness center on the Internet and the world. In a fairly playful manner, a visitor to the site Dakim.com will be able to improve their long-term and short-term memory, raise the level of critical thinking, computational abilities, visual-spatial orientation and language.

To stimulate mental activity, a variety of techniques are used in which old films and television commercials are involved. Multimedia content is updated once a day or two days, so that customers can not remember a particular task with which to cope. For different ages, visitors have separate programs; in addition, you can hire a personal trainer, writes Market Wire .
