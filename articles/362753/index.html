Former member of the Pirate Bay team is free again

Former member of the Pirate Bay team and computer expert Peter Sunde was released from the insane asylum . He spent five months there. Prior to that, he had been hiding from Interpol for two years, since the court found him guilty on charges of piracy.

Peter lived for a long time in Berlin, but periodically visited his family in Sweden, where he was captured by law enforcement officers.

After his release, Peter should recover a little, and then he will again take up the business of his life - improving the Internet.

Although the crime was not a criminal act, he was detained in a high-security prison. According to him, it was very difficult there. In particular, they did not provide him with a vegan diet. As a result of this, as well as a completely understandable depression, Peter lost 15 kilograms.
