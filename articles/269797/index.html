November 10-17, we invite you to take part in the action for mobile developers and users - Russian AppFest

The campaign will be attended by IT companies of Russia, as well as communities, developers, users and all those who care about the application industry.

From 10 to 17 November on the Russian AppFest website you can find profitable offers and bonuses for applications and services, current announcements and industry events.

To participate in the promotion, 3 types of content are accepted:
1) Discounts, special offers for applications and services;
2) Events related to applications, services associated with them, and mobile development;
3) Announcements from the companies - developers.

There are no restrictions on the platforms, we welcome, if your offer will apply to all mobile platforms.

If you want to take part in the action or get more information, leave a request on the site www.russianappfest.com .
