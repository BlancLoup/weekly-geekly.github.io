Donor blood.rf - quick search for blood donor in emergency situations


Hello!
How often do you receive messages in ICQ and social news that a child needs a blood donor and someone else needs a blood donor and more and more? And all of them are not from my city and often I have the wrong blood type and rhesus does not fit. Those. even with a great desire to help them with my blood, I could not. Over time, such messages began to be perceived as spam, although it would seem that people have trouble.

Last week, after the next message, I thought, what if you make a website where everyone can leave their data: blood group, Rh factor, e-mail and city of residence, and everyone in need can write a message that only those who receive can really help?

The idea is formulated in one sentence. Implementation is also not an extremely difficult task. You just have to sit down and do it! I bought a domain right away. Began to do immediately. Then he deleted everything and rewrote it overnight. Yesterday morning I put everything in public.
What remains to be done: add the script for the e-mail distribution, add the function of collecting statistics on the read messages.
