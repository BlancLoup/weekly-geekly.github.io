New model of the reader ABC n618

On Thursday, the Interfax introduced a new e-reader model, the ABC n618.

This is where the official part ends, and the unofficial one begins with a photo review of the device.
Let's start with the box. The box turned out beautiful, as our designer said.

But the main thing is not the box, but what is inside.

Here's a look.
Open up. Here is such an accessory to the sofa, kitchen or subway.

View from the end - the power button, it is also the power button of the Wi-Fi. Connector for microSD, headphones and miniUSSB.

Immediately about the main thing. What's new:
The screen has become larger - 6 inches, as well as whiter and more contrast.

The most important technical innovation is the wacom touch screen. The screen, if it does not touch the stylus, falls asleep, so as not to eat the battery, and when you press the first time - it wakes up, and the second time you press on what you need. The touch screen extends the control of this device - many buttons are displayed on the screen.

The stylus, by the way, snaps into the case and does not fall out anywhere.

Wi-Fi module. There is a built-in Opera - it automatically scales sites for mobile devices. For example, if there is a mobile version of the site, then it will automatically load it. You can see the mail, not only see, but also respond to letters. You can also edit the text immediately.
