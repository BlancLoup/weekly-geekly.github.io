Pando will expand the capabilities of Skype

Skype and Pando Networks have announced an agreement that will allow Skype users to share large files, according to Mashable.com .

Pando Networks is a developer of a p2p client for sharing large files. Integration with email clients, email services, instant messengers and social sites allows users to share files up to 1 GB in size. Skype has built-in support for file sharing, however, using Pando Extra, you can send files to offline contacts, multiple users at once, post files to MySpace, etc.
