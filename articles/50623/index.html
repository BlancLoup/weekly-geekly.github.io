Looking at the overseas market for creating websites, the eyes of a number of large and small-scale design studios are diverging. One rating on www.thefwa.com makes you wonder how much money and customers abroad. But in fact, the market abroad is more transparent than in Russia, more mature and more interesting.

Few people know that there are not so many large agencies. If we talk about Internet solutions for large multinational companies, there are several hundred agencies in which they are serviced. While the design studios and web-production agencies are several thousand.

And I call agencies really large companies that are engaged in strategic planning on the Internet, creativity and development (on their own, or by contractors). And even the sophisticated designer will be mistaken if he starts calling North Kingdom, 2Advanced, Firstborn Multimedia among these agencies.
