Award to outstanding IT journalists!

Friends!

We are glad to inform you that Softkey and the public organization ROCIT have established an award in the field of IT journalism . We invite authors of journalistic works on IT, e-commerce, Internet development in Russia to participate.



Competitive materials are accepted from October 25 .

The results will be summed up on the eve of the next RIF + CIB 2011 event, during which the winners will be awarded.

The competition can send journalistic online and offline work , reflecting the objective state of the Russian IT market, as well as the field of e-commerce. Publications should be targeted to the general reader. In addition, they should not be customized.

The jury of the competition includes representatives of companies from the Russian IT market and journalists from major socio-political and specialized publications. First of all, the work will be evaluated on such criteria as the relevance of the topic; literacy and competence of the author; practical use of the publication.

Under the terms of the competition, three finalists will be awarded cash prizes in the amount of 150,000 rubles, 100,000 rubles and 50,000 rubles.

After registering on the contest page, participants can place the material on their own in the attached form or download it as a scan or a screenshot. In addition, the publication can be sent to the address of the organizers. Offered for consideration of work should be published no earlier than October 25, 2010 - that is, the date of the official start of the competition. The founders of the award reserve the right to supplement the base of competitive works by posting publications from open sources.
