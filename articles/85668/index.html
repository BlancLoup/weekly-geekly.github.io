Closing WebMoney automatic interfaces

Hello.
Today I received a letter from the WebMoney administration because I am the owner of the certificate of the seller:
Dear friends,

You have received this email because you are the owner of the WebMoney Seller Certificate.
In accordance with our rules, WebMoney automated reception interfaces and Vendor Certificate are issued only to businesses registered in the Megastock.ru catalog.
We ask you to go through the registration procedure in the catalog here: www.megastock.ru/AddResource.aspx . The term of consideration of the application is three working days.
For those who for some reason do not want to be publicly visible in the catalog, we have provided hidden registrations, as well as hidden categories of goods and services. When submitting an application, you can choose a hidden registration and indicate the reasons for this.
After March 1, all Certificates of WMID vendors who have not submitted an application for registration in Megastock will be turned into personal, and automatic interfaces will be disabled . If you do not agree with the above conditions, you can continue accepting WebMoney in manual mode, no other consequences than turning off automation will not result.
Note: if you have several WMIDs attached to the same certificate, take care of registering them all in Megastock.


It is good that I quickly responded and applied for registration in the catalog. But everyone confuses me with two points:
- why they warned so late;
- and whether they can in one working day and two weekends - have time to process all applications and will not turn off the reception of money in my stores, whether I lose customers and money.

What do you think about this?
