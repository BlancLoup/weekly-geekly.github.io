How to remember all that you have learned?


Use the Supermemo model.



Let's say you learn Chinese. You've learned a new word and remembered it. Without practice, after a while, it will be harder to remember. You can even calculate how long you will forget it completely, and ideally you should be reminded of it just when the word is about to be erased from memory. The more often you will be reminded of a word, the more likely you are to remember it for a long time.

This training program is called SuperMemo, it was developed by the Polish researcher Peter Wozniak. Having learned something, you need to remind yourself about it at the following time intervals: after 1 day, after 10 days, after a month and two months.

How to deal with the dilemma?
