Feedback? Without need

First, a small entry.

One of my personal creative projects is a site with a set of short and often seemingly plotless phrases that appeared as side effects of mental activity (it happens when you think hard, something else comes to mind). There is also a play on words, funny verbal constructions heard or seen somewhere, self-made aphorisms, a splash of emotions and so on. The project is clearly not for a wide audience, but, no less, a long-standing (more than 4 years old) and carefully supported. Works on a standard engine and handwritten theme. No advertising links are there, never have been and never will be.

And so, the other day, quite accidentally, it turned out that the site had stopped being indexed by Yandex.

This is not to say that this project had, is or is supposed to have some tangible influx of visitors from search engines, but was lost from the index.

In the course of the correspondence with the support service, I received two letters on standard templates, with which they, apparently, are responsible for the owners of spam and junk sites. With links to rules (about seo-links, search spam, advertising, dishonest receptions, pop-up banners, etc.), which I, of course, have never violated.

After analyzing and classifying the pages of your site, our algorithms decided not to include it in the search. <...> At the moment, your site will not be returned to the search, as it does not fully correspond to our understanding of a quality site. <...> We do not enter into correspondence regarding specific technical methods on the site or addresses of poor-quality pages.
