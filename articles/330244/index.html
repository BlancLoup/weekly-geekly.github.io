The fee for publishing the game via Steam Direct is set at $ 100



Valve has made a decision about the contribution for developers who plan to publish their games in the Steam catalog. These fees will be charged after the current Greenlight system is replaced with Steam Direct. The fee will be refundable and, in principle, for developers, almost nothing changes, because in the current system the size of the refund for publishing the game is the same .

According to Valve representatives, the decision was not so simple, the team members proposed to change the size of contributions, and the amounts from $ 100 to $ 5000 were considered (no, zero is not superfluous here). After discussion, it was decided to stop at $ 500, but the opinion of the community itself was decisive - Valve listened to it and decided not to change the fee to allow developers to publish their games without any problems.

It was decided to replace the Steam Greenlight system in February, in order for developers to have a more direct and simple way to publish their games. Actually, Valve's intentions are reflected in the very name of the new service - Steam Direct. To work, you need to register, after which make money, as mentioned above. "We will ask new developers to work with documents, having passed the verification procedure and adding tax information, it all looks like opening a bank account," explained in Valve in February.

After all the formalities have been completed and the payment for the game has been made (when publishing a new title, the fee will have to be paid again), the developer can begin the adding procedure. However, so far there is no information on how the developer can get his refundable fee back, although Valve representatives told one of the online media that the developer will receive money to achieve a sales level of $ 1000. Also in Valve modified tools that allow curators easier to manage their recommendations. The curators have also made the task easier when displaying content for other platforms, including YouTube.



They got the opportunity to create a personal list of games. According to Valve, this will allow curators to provide specific advice on how to purchase games for the current sale. Valve promises that work is already underway to create a system that makes it easier for the game creators to find curators.
