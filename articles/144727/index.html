ITU has approved an ultra-high resolution TV standard (UHDTV)

The International Telecommunication Union (ITU) has announced recommendations for an ultra-high-resolution television standard UHDTV with a sound system 22.2.

Two screen sizes are recommended:
  • 3840 x 2160 (4K system)
  • 7680 x 4320 (8K system)

It is assumed that in Japan, such TVs will appear in 2013-2014.

The pictures for clarity show the ratio of these sizes with a standard TV (SDTV) and the current HDTV.
