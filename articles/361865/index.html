Omate Lutetia: smart watches that girls will definitely like



Samsung, Pebble, LG and other companies produce smart watches with wide functionality that can be called stylish. But still most of the models of smart watches are suitable for male hands. The same Moto 360 is a large device, and it looks a bit strange on an elegant female hand.

Omate decided to release a watch that is positioned as a smart device for women. This model of smart watches is called Omate Lutetia.

The design of the watch really looks like a woman, if you can put it that way: a small round display, a metal gold-plated bracelet - these watches are perfect for women. It is worth noting that the clock display (LCD) is covered with durable sapphire crystal.



The clock is based on MediaTek Aster MT2502, software shell - Nucleus RTOS. According to the developers, offline watches can work for about a week. Pairing is possible with mobile devices based on Android and iOS.

What can Omate Lutetia:

  • Pedometer;
  • Manage player on your smartphone;
  • Display weather forecast;
  • Stopwatch;
  • Voice control;
  • Getting notifications from the smartphone (messages, calls, events).


According to representatives of Omate, these watches are not competitors to devices from Pebble, Samsung and Apple. Rather, they are positioned as an analogue of women's fashion watches from Michael Kors, Calvin Klein, Guess and D & G.



It is necessary to separately indicate the price of hours: they are planned to be sold only for $ 169. Omate Lutetia will be available from December this year. A unique code will be engraved on the first thousand copies.
