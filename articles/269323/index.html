Introduce the anti-spam tool in personal messages on My Circle

On "My Circle" we have introduced the ability to send complaints to messages from personal correspondence. All complaints are sent to a separate moderator interface, in which each user keeps a record of all complaints received. If the violation is serious, the moderator immediately blocks the offender. If the violation is not very serious, then the moderator blocks the profile of the offender when a certain number of complaints accumulate.



We go in correspondence and in the upper right corner click on the icon-dot. From the drop-down menu, select "Report".



The pop-up opens, in which you need to select the cause of the complaint - spam, insult or other - and click on the "Send Complaint" button.



Do not leave violations without attention, send complaints! So you will help the service to be a cozy and useful place.
