Which IT specialists are now in demand in Germany (performance report)

I am writing a report based on the last 5 months of active project development.

The report consists of 3 parts:
1. What IT specialists are needed now (on the example of Germany). I think that this also applies to all of Europe, but so far everyone is settling in Germany.
2. What important points I noticed in the process of working with candidates
3. Announcement of the project's functionality (it will be useful for those who are not going to go abroad yet)

1. Trends.
What experts are needed most often

If a candidate demonstrates a high level of technical knowledge and knows English well, then he should not cling to any particular company. Such a candidate will find a job with a 90% guarantee.

Geography offers
So far, Germany (Berlin and Frankfurt). This is due to the fact that the growth rate of the IT industry in Germany exceeds all expectations.

2. Important points for candidates
Underestimation of their knowledge and capabilities
Very often, candidates underestimate their level - indicate Middle instead of Senior. This is despite the fact that the level of knowledge of basic technologies tends to 100%. In the process of clarifying the reasons, I found out that most often the candidates believe that the Senior requires a team management experience. Yes and no at the same time. It depends on what the employer puts into this concept. It is for this reason that I advise you to rely on your technical knowledge in determining the level of qualification. The fact is that employers often look at the headlines in a resume and look for a senior there. All the rest are immediately discarded and no one even knows how talented guys they have just missed.
