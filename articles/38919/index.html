Chrome and Google Bookmarks

Friends, judging by the comments in almost all the posts about Chrome, many use the service of bookmarks from Google. Among them is me. Therefore, I want to tell you how to get to them (bookmarks in Google). Although it is not very convenient, but there is at least some option for those who really need it.

So,
  1. Here the Help Center tells us how you can add a button in Lame to add bookmarks to Google Bookmarks. Namely, you need:
    • open the bookmarks panel with the keyboard shortcut ctrl + b;
    • drag the proposed button to this panel.
  2. But the trouble is - how then to use them? There are a couple of options:
    • after the bookmark is saved, Lamer undertakes, when entering the first characters of the saved link in the address bar, to offer you its full address;
    • You can go to the bookmarks page itself. Fortunately, without additional plug-ins in the new tab, the most visited pages can be displayed, in which there can be a link to Google Bookmarks;
    • Also in Google Notebook there is a block of tags, which is essentially a block of your bookmarks.
But, of course, we are all looking forward to the full support of "their own" bookmarks from the developers :)
