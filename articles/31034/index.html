Original bookmark for books



It is possible that, despite the presence of such progressive devices as the Sony Reader , you continue to read books with the only chip inside - protection from thieves. And certainly comparing paper books with e-books you thought about how to save the page on which you finished reading without these annoying bookmarks that are easy to forget. In addition, they need to shift each time and usually a wide bookmark overlaps part of the text. But in our age of high technology and this problem has been solved.



A new bookmark from Atilina from Brazil offers a simple, cheap and surprisingly practical way to solve the problem of finding the right page in the book. At first glance, this tab is completely normal, but its feature is in a flexible and transparent part, which remains on the necessary page automatically. Now you do not need to be distracted from reading, every time you turn the page, the bookmark jumps to the next one.





To use a bookmark, all you need is to follow the video instructions (just below). The product was developed by Brazilian designer Lesandra Martins Pereira (Pandora), a patent application has already been filed, but the bookmark still does not have a name and is not sold in stores. The company hopes to soon sell its development to publishers.
