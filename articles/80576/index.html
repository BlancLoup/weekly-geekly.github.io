Air umbrella



An umbrella is a rather conservative thing. Of course, for the many hundreds of years that have passed since its invention, steel and nylon have been used instead of bamboo and paper, but the design has not changed in principle - only materials. Designer Je Sung Park offers an umbrella to mechanize and improve. The meaning is this: we refuse from the dome altogether, and in the handle we embed a powerful compressor that will blow away the falling drops.
It remains only to invent a battery on which all this happiness will work for any reasonable time.
