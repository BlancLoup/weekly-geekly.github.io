IBM Opens Bluemix Garage in Toronto



IBM is opening Bluemix Garage centers, where IBM staff provide advice to startups on IBM products that can improve their start-up projects. The company has now opened another consultation center in Toronto with the goal of telling the developers of the Bluemix program who have not worked with this product about the benefits of the Bluemix program.

In addition, the company plans to launch similar centers in Australia, the United States (New York), France (Nice) and Sweden. Bluemix Garage in Toronto will be located on the territory of the DMZ of Ryerson University (Digital Media Zone, Canada‚Äôs largest business incubator). Here, company experts will help independent developers and companies understand the capabilities of next-generation cloud applications based on Bluemix. In addition, here enterprises of any size can get acquainted with the developers, as well as get feedback from customers and other companies.
