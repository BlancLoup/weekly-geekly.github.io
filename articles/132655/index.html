Sony Ericsson has confirmed the upgrade of the entire line of Xperia to Android 4.0



Sony Ericsson has announced that the upgrade to Ice Cream Sandwich will get all the phones in the 2011 Xperia line. This became possible due to the fact that all Xperia models this year were released on a single powerful platform that allows you to install a new version of Android.

The full list of smartphones with Ice Cream Sandwich owners is as follows: Xperia arc and arc S, Xperia PLAY , Xperia neo and neo V, Xperia mini and mini pro , Xperia pro, Xperia active , Xperia ray and Sony Ericsson Live with Walkman .
