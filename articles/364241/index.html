Chipsets for tablets and chipsets for smartphones: what are the differences?

To begin with, what is meant by the word "platform" only one chip in our time is fundamentally wrong. The differences between them are as follows. MediaTek actively applies the concept of ‚Äúcomplete platform solution‚Äù.
