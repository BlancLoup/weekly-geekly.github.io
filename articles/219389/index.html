Epson Connect - printing from anywhere in the world

It is hard to imagine a modern high-tech device that would somehow not use in its work wireless networks and the Internet. And even such familiar tasks as printing on a printer or scanning can be simplified, giving owners the opportunity to solve such problems over the network or from anywhere in the world via the Internet.

We at Epson understand this and are trying to keep up with the times, so all modern Epson printers and MFPs provide networking using the Epson Connect service.

What is Epson Connect


Without unnecessary introductions, I suggest to watch two videos about the principles of work:





Those. Epson Connect consists of several components, each of which is designed to solve a wide range of tasks:

Epson iPrint


An application that allows you to control the device on the network via WiFi: send documents and photos in PDF and JPEG format from Android and iOS devices, as well as scan documents and photos with image transfer to a mobile device.
Using the application you can print:
  • Photos stored on mobile device
  • Documents from file storage box.net, Dropbox and Evernote
  • From iOS devices ‚Äî documents in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint formats
  • Web pages

You can download the application for iOS and Android .

Epson Email Print



A feature that allows you to send documents and photos to print by e-mail. For the function to work, it is enough to install the Epson E-print application on the computer and register with it the printing devices on the special portal www.epsonconnect.com . Each printing device will be assigned a unique e-mail address to which it will be possible to send documents and photos for printing.

Thanks to this function, it is possible, without installing a driver and without connecting a printer, to print, even from a mobile phone or any other device connected to the Internet, from anywhere in the world.


Airprint


AirPrint is a technology developed by Apple that allows you to print documents and photos inside a local network. In particular, it is actively used in devices running iOS, as it allows you to send documents to print via WiFi.


Google Cloud Print


A feature that allows you to send documents and photos to print by e-mail. In fact, it is very similar to Epson Email Print, with the difference that printing is carried out through the Gmail mail service.

What is the result?


As a result, it is enough to turn on the printer in the socket and connect it to the network (you can also use WiFi). Everything!
You can both print and scan over the network, using both popular wireless printing standards and specially designed Epson solutions.
