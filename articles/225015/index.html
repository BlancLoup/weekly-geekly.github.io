Google launched indexing applications in Russia


May 30, Google launched the indexing of mobile applications in Russia. Previously, this option was only in English-speaking countries. Users now have the opportunity to decide whether they want to go to your website or open your Android application on their device and see the content there.

The updated Googlebot indexes content in Android applications in the same way as on sites. The webmaster only needs to specify the desired content using a Sitemap and webmaster tools.



Learn more about how to mark the desired content for indexing, read here . And here you can send an application to participate in the application indexing program.

It is unlikely that this solution is suitable for games, but, for example, for news and other content applications - this is a very convenient and useful thing.
