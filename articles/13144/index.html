My tudushnik

I always liked paper. It is more pleasant for me to write on paper ( quite non-caligraphic), it is more pleasant for me to read, holding a hundred or two sheets. And I don‚Äôt understand people who use programs to create fashionable ToDo lists. Comfortable tudushniki can only be on handheld computers, and not on the colossus weighing more than 2 kilos.

I conduct my work on paper, more precisely in a notebook bought for 6 hryvnias (~ $ 1.2). My keyboard and mouse is ... PEN! Convenient, practical and cheap. And the appearance of the tudushnik can be very widely changed. For example, I stuck a ubuntu sticker on my stylish black-and-silver notebook.


My personal ToDo-list supports tree structure and wiki-like markup. For example, the films that you want (want) to see, I tick / movie /, themes for blog entries. Tags can be combined. For example, in order to remember to write about a movie, you can write </ movie />. Fulfilled item - crossed out. Even a no brainer. And in order not to forget the "tags" you can list them on the cover.

Also, pictures and lyrics can be attached to a notebook notebook with a regular end attachment. Now you do not have to wait two or three minutes while the computer starts up to show your friends photos from the trip! Just open tudushnik, and we get a photo. And still the notebook can be used as an archive for sketching new templates, new logos, new programs ... You never know what can be put on paper.

And do not be afraid that your heaped laptop or PDA, in which you take notes, will be stolen. The tudushnik offered by me costs a penny and will be affordable even for schoolchildren and starving students.
