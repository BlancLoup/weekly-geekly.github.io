A selection of interesting accessories for Nokia smartphones

Hi, Habr!
It's no secret that Nokia has always been distinguished by extraordinary solutions both in their devices and in accessories for them. In our blog on Habrahabr, we repeatedly told about many branded add-ons for Nokia smartphones.



However, not only we can surprise with interesting solutions that can improve and beautify our devices. In this post we want to highlight the accessories of third-party manufacturers and some of our new products.

Charging device


In a recent review of new Nokia chargers, we talked about a rather unusual Nokia wireless charger DC-50. In addition to the built-in 2400 mAh battery, its main advantage is the support of wireless charging based on Qi technology.



A good alternative, especially for those who are not so picky about the size and design of chargers, can be Mugenizer N11 from the Japanese company Mugen Power. It also supports the possibility of induction charging, which is suitable for smartphones such as the Lumia 920, 1520 and 930. For the rest, you can connect via a standard microUSB / USB cable.



The Mugenizer N11 has a battery capacity of 4800 mAh. On the case LED status indication is provided. Weight is 230 g, dimensions - 75x137x14 mm. The cost of the memory in the official store is about 2 500 rubles, but in this case it will be possible to receive the device only by mail.



Motorists who often use Lumia on the road can use our next device. Nokia CR-201 car charger holder helps to simplify and secure the process of using a smartphone while driving.



CR-201, like most ordinary mounts, is mounted on the windshield of the car and, thanks to the swivel mechanism, allows you to install the smartphone both horizontally and vertically. The device itself is securely fastened in the holder with adjustable side "legs", equipped with rubber pads.



The charger connects to a standard cigarette lighter and, like the above devices, allows you to recharge your Lumia with QI wireless induction technology.

In addition, the Nokia CR-201 supports NFC technology, which in this case activates the driver mode: a special smartphone menu with up-to-date information and quick access to three applications (HERE Drive +, Nokia Music and a quick call are installed by default, but the user can set their favorite applications).



The size of the accessory is 117.4 x 74.7 x 101.6 mm; its weight is 165 g. You can purchase it at the Nokia official store for 3,490 rubles.



Audio


For those who appreciate the headphones, not only sound, but also the appearance, we suggest to pay attention to the new Coloud Boom headphones for Nokia.



Distinctive advantages of the novelty: minimalistic, but bright design, as well as good sound with deep low and clear mid and high frequencies. In this case, the cost of new items - from 1 190 rubles, which is quite nice for headphones of this type.



Among the pleasant things: a sensitive microphone with a player control button and a non-tangling flat cable with a Zound Lasso mount that allows you to quickly fix the coiled wire.

Those who like to keep a smartphone in a bag or pocket, and listen to music via Bluetooth, we recommend the stylish brick Nokia BH-121.



The device case 393912 mm in size (which can be presented in blue, red, yellow or black color) is the dream of a minimalist and perfectionist. On it you can see only the clip mounting, control buttons, volume control, battery status LED indicator, USB port and a standard 3.5 mm audio jack.



Supported Bluetooth profiles include: HFP / HSP and A2DP / AVRCP. In addition, the device supports NFC technology for synchronization with a compatible smartphone with one touch.

Music playback time is 6 hours, and in offline mode, the Nokia BH-121 can spend up to 7 days without recharging.

The Nokia BH-121 comes bundled with Nokia WH-208 headphones. The cost of the official store is 2490 rubles.

The drone!


Yes Yes. Among the available accessories for Nokia, and more specifically for the Lumia 1020, there is even a drone. Produces his reputable company for the production of drones - Lehmann Aviation.



The LA300 model is designed for aerial photography and the Nokia Lumia 1020 should serve as the main working tool: it is mounted under the fuselage of the aircraft. The management of drone is extremely simple and does not require any special skills. The user sets the flight path by drawing it on the tablet (based on Windows 8) and launches the drone into the air from his hand.



The device is able to fly a distance of 15 km, developing a speed of 20-80 km / h. The only price LA300 really bites: 3990 euros only for the device itself. You can purchase on the official website of the manufacturer .
