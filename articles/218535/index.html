Utility StorTrends iDATA for intelligent analysis of bottlenecks in IT infrastructure

The company AMI (American Megatrends Inc.) is familiar to everyone in the distant past and can present the present-time logo in the upper left corner of the monitor when you turn on a computer or laptop.



Announcing the release of the StorTrends IDATA utility (Intelligent Data Analysis Tracking Application) for intelligent analysis of IT infrastructure bottlenecks , this is a free software tool designed to determine accurate estimates of IT infrastructure performance, capacity, and bandwidth requirements. This useful tool will help prevent infrastructure sore points before they become a headache, providing detailed reports for making necessary and informed decisions based on the future.




StorTrends IDATA software runs in the background in the existing IT environment for 7 days, collecting information and analyzing it. After collecting the data, the tool generates a report with important indicators:
  • Capacity utilization
  • IOPS Testing
  • Comparing read / write performance for volumes.
  • Network performance and bandwidth
  • Server statistics and information about applications

With these indicators, companies can develop the right strategy for the development of IT infrastructure within the enterprise.
