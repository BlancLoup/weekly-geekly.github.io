SQA Days-17: with the best memories - to the next conference

On May 29-30, SQA Days was held in Minsk - the International Conference on Software Quality. She was already the seventeenth in a row and gathered more than 600 visitors from different countries. The organizers, the Testing Laboratory, did not disappoint the regular guests of the conference, nor those who visited it for the first time.




In just two days, 67 speakers from the USA, Great Britain, Czech Republic, Russia, Ukraine and Belarus spoke at the conference. They shared their experience gained on a large number of diverse projects, including banking software and enterprise systems, social networks and video services, mobile applications and websites, and even a platform for making decisions of national importance.
