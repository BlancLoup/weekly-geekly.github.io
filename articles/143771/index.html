HTC One S - powerful thin-case stuffing

This time, the HTC One S came under scrutiny. This may not be the flagship of the current lineup, but One S will not disappoint users with its characteristics. In addition, it was in its production that a number of technological innovations were involved, which makes this device truly unique.




So, with each case produced for the HTC One S, plasma electrolytic oxidation is performed. It consists in passing a high voltage electric current through the metal, which makes it possible to obtain a much more durable material. As a basis for the One S case, a unibody aluminum billet is taken, which is subjected to a current treatment with a voltage of 10,000 volts! In addition to giving unsurpassed strength, five times superior to traditional aluminum, the bodies that have undergone this procedure are painted in a unique and rich shade of black.





Thus, you can not worry about the state of the body of the HTC One S in normal operating conditions. Of course, do not prick them with nuts or hammer nails, no smartphone can withstand such treatment. But if you accidentally put the device in one pocket with the keys - do not worry, nothing terrible will happen.





Possessing a 4.3-inch (11 cm) display, the device was able to maintain dimensions that were acceptable to most users. In addition to the record strength of the main material, the novelty also boasts a very thin body, the thickness of which is only 7.8 mm. The width of the device: 65 mm, and the length is 131 mm with a total weight of 119 g. Thus, it will not be difficult to put it in a shirt pocket, not to mention the inner pocket of a jacket or preferred wearing in trousers.



The screen resolution of the HTC One S is 960x540 pixels (qHD), and it is made using Super AMOLED technology, which allows you to enjoy the natural black color, as well as see the rich and high-quality picture under any lighting conditions. If the back wall of the device is made of aluminum and is resistant to mechanical stress, then its facade can boast a certain strength, because the protective glass of the display is made using Gorilla Glass technology and is not afraid of scratches. Capacitive display with multi-touch is distinguished by responsiveness and good readability in the sun, and automatic brightness control, as a rule, correctly selects the desired value and does not force you to turn to the settings, peering at menu items.
