Guests in the new Japanese hotel will be met and served by robots

In the Japanese hotel Henn-na, the main part of the work will be done by robots: they will meet at the reception desk, take the luggage to the room and clean it. Other technologies will increase the efficiency of the hotel - the door will be opened by the facial recognition system, the temperature in the room will be adjusted automatically, and this inexpensive hotel will save on electricity using solar energy.



For meeting guests and registration will be responsible actroids - robots that mimic the appearance and behavior of a person, capable of engaging in meaningful dialogue. The rest of the robots will fulfill the duties of other service personnel - they will carry the luggage as a porter and carry them to the room. The third category of robots will keep it clean and tidy. In addition to the eleven robots in the hotel will work ten living staff.

One of the options for the actroid from the company Kokoro


The hotel belongs to the category of low-cost, but it is equipped with the latest technology to increase efficiency. The number will open after the facial recognition system recognizes the guest. In the rooms - a minimum of amenities, but everything you need can be ordered from the tablet. Instead of the usual conditioner, the system, detecting the heat of a person in the room, adjusts the temperature.

The first building of the hotel for seventy-two rooms will open in July 2015. Rooms can already be booked .
