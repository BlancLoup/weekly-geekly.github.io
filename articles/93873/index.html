Intellecom is the second Mobile WiMAX operator in Kiev

Yesterday, the second mobile WiMAX operator (frequency 2.3 GHz), Intellecom, was launched in Kiev. The first was FreshTel .

Tariffs:
- For 90 hryvnia 850 Kbit / s
- For 150 hryvnia to 20 Mbit / s

Starting promotions:
- When you connect, you have free internet access at maximum speed until the end of the current month.
- When signing additional. agreements for the year modem cost - 1 hryvnia. When connecting without a contract price is 199 hryvnia.

A little poor coverage ...

Surprisingly, they did not give any press releases about the launch!
