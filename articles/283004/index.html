How to build a competent testing system? Insights from QA experts on May 19 in St. Petersburg



What cloud testing tools are used in Yandex? How does Selenium testing in Badoo work? What is Wrike's automated frontend testing system?

On May 19, a testing meeting will be held in the St. Petersburg office of Wrike , where we will discuss pressing, eternal and, at first glance, almost insoluble QA problems in large (and not so) projects.



On the eve of SQA Days, our Wrike TechClub organizes a meeting of testing specialists . Together with experts from Badoo, Yandex and Wrike, we will discuss successful practices and tools, analyze specific cases, talk about building an effective testing system in the company, give advice and recommendations.

The meeting is scheduled informal; the main goal is for each participant to gain practical benefits, therefore, in addition to reports, live communication is assumed. We invite all experts in the field of automated testing and technically savvy "handbrake".
