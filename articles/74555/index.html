Myka Bittorrent a year later

Probably many of you remember about the startup Myka Bittorrent , which was announced over a year ago. At that time, the heart of Myka was to become System-On-Chip with Broadmedia multimedia capabilities. However, it was obvious that the lack of funding was preventing the guys from developing.

However, the solution was found: from now on, Myka migrates to the ION platform. The cost of new items will be $ 379. Equipped with Myka ION is a dual-core Atom 330 CPU at 1.6 GHz, 10 USB ports, and VGA / DVI / HDMI connectors.
