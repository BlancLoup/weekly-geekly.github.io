AdSense interface - on any site

Published AdSense APIs make it possible to integrate the advertising service interface on any website. Through program interfaces, functions such as account registration, account management, creating and editing advertising modules and advertising links, viewing detailed reports and financial statistics become available. All this is completely free for registered developers. The beta testing allowed site owners with attendance of at least 100,000 hits per month.

When implementing the AdSense API, you can enforce certain parameters of advertising modules. For example, the format of advertising and color scheme. In fact, thanks to SOAP and WSDL support, a talented developer can tailor the AdSense API for a wide variety of tasks.

For example, the AdSense API is ideal for implementation by companies that manage web content: these are hosting companies, publishing houses, the media, blogging platforms, social networks. One of the first beta testers of the AdSense API was Blogger , the largest site on the Internet for free hosting of blogs (see case study with screenshots ).

For these types of customers, implementing the AdSense APIs can be a very profitable business, thanks to a Google partner program. The firm promises to pay the developer a percentage of the income of all users who register through its web interface. Plus, he will receive a bonus of $ 100 for each major customer (a large customer is one who earns at least $ 100 from advertising within six months).

Thus, hosting companies, blogger platforms and other companies can make good money as intermediaries on the sale of advertising. It is possible that many paid services (the same hosting) in the future will become cheaper or even completely free for end users.

Among other things, by opening software interfaces, Google is trying to make the relationship between advertisers and sites that advertise more comfortable. The AdSense API programming interfaces can be configured so that the advertiser can easily and quickly buy ads on the site without leaving this site. The AdSense service acts here simply as a technological intermediary and provides financial settlements between the parties (the payment system from Google promises to be very popular).
