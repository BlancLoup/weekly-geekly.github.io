Competition for students and supervisors: "Microsoft UniApps Challenge"



Dear students and academic leaders, we are pleased to announce a new competition held by Softline Venture Partners , with which you can win prizes using modern technologies in the educational process!

Student work can take part in the competition (both undergraduate and graduate and laboratory work), carried out with the participation of the supervisor, during which an application was created and placed in the Windows Store or Windows Phone Store .

The creators and scientific leaders of the three best works with Windows Store applications and one best work with the Windows Phone Store application will be awarded with laptops, phones and other valuable prizes from Microsoft and partners of the competition. The best theoretical papers will be recommended for extraordinary publication in subject journals from the VAK list.

Applications for participation are accepted until May 15, 2013.

You have a defined theme of work, but the application is not ready yet? We are happy to help you complete it!

We will be happy to help you complete the application by providing participants who submitted an application before February 1, 2013. expert support and useful materials for the preparation of the work. Also, among the participants who submitted an application before February 1, a special prize will be drawn!

Do not miss the unique opportunity in the framework of the educational process in practice to get acquainted with the latest technologies, to replenish your portfolio, publish your work in a specialized scientific publication and win a valuable prize! And participation in the contest will help prepare for the filing of an application for the Imagine Cup .

You can familiarize yourself with the full rules of the competition and submit an application on the website of the competition organizer: softlinevp.com/ru/ms.php .

Attention! Only work performed in universities and colleges of the Russian Federation are accepted.
