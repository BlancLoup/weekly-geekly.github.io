Special offer from Marmalade Technologies Ltd for Tizen application developers

Good morning, Habr!

If you are not yet in the know, we are in a hurry to announce that Tizen application developers have a unique opportunity to get a license for Marmalade SDK for a period of six months from the partner company Tizen Association - Marmalade Technologies Ltd , as well as the opportunity to receive a cash reward from appbackr .



Participation conditions are as follows:

  1. By December 31, 2013 sign up by the link .
  2. Download the latest version of Marmalade SDK with support for the Tizen platform (to work, you must have Visual Studio 2010+ installed, including Express or XCode 4.1+.).
  3. Design or port your application to the Tizen platform using the Marmalade SDK.
  4. (In addition to this offer) Send your completed (or close to completion) application to appbackr and earn points for your application.
  5. When the application is fully ready, register it before January 31, 2014 and pass the certification at the Tizen Store . If the application was previously sent to appbackr, do not forget to add "appbackr" in the "Comments to Validation Team" field.
  6. Get a Marmalade SDK license for six months and a cash reward from appbackr (up to $ 10,000 via PayPal).
  7. The best apps will get the opportunity to promote from Tizen Store and appbackr.

Details of the action: www.madewithmarmalade.com/offers/tizen
