Mouse "Alien" and other work in the category "Modification of peripheral devices" competition "King of Modding 2009"



The time has come for the next report on the recently held King of Modding competition (a kind of Modding-Oscar) , a professional award for specialists in the field of modifying the appearance and functionality of PC components and peripherals, organized by www.modding.ru , www.occlub .ru . Today we continue to highlight its results in the "Modding Peripherals" category. Often, novice modders choose a mouse as their first test subject. The competition gathered not only entry-level works, but also labor-intensive mice!
