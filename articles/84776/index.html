Association of Internet Developers Announces Accepting Applications for Project Funding

The Investment and Expertise Committee of the Association of Internet Developers announces the acceptance of applications for project financing. AIR acts as an intermediary between investors: venture funds, business angels and Internet developers.

We have established contacts with a significant circle of professional investors, the amount of funding at the seed and startup stages can be up to 3 million rubles per project. We are interested in interesting Internet projects: projects that work only online, projects in conjunction online + offline.

In order to receive funding, you must fill out an application on our website :
When filling out an application, make sure that there are answers to the following questions:
- What is unique about your project, why users will use it, and not analog sites if they exist, and how many of those users.
- If the project involves large volumes of traffic - describe where visitors will come from, and how much will have to be spent on advertising, if you are sure that information about your project will be distributed on the word of mouth and even for free, then describe why you are sure of it.
- Where do the revenues come from in the project and why are there so many of them.
- At what stage is the project now (the idea, there are some developments, an operating business).
- What are the fixed costs needed for ongoing project support?
- How much was invested and how much an investor should invest in a project.

Since we work with a significant number of investors, we have the opportunity to thoroughly and carefully consider all applications received to us.
Please note that the standard time for consideration of an application is 2-3 weeks, the outcome of consideration may be: an invitation to an interview, an invitation to an informal meeting, a list of additional questions.
