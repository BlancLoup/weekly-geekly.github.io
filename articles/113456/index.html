Open testing of the Polk Audio Surroundbar 6000 IHT soundbar was held



On February 5, 2011, the Polk Audio Surroundbar 6000 IHT soundbar was openly tested.

The event was held by the distributor company Polk in the Russian Federation by Inforkom.

The invitation was sent to several authoritative communities in the network, including Habr.

Testing took place successfully. About the testing, its prerequisites and its results - in this message.

The reason for holding such an event is obvious - the low level of trust in acoustic systems such as the soundbar, which was also manifested in the discussion of the piece of iron on a Habr√©, is due to logical prerequisites:

1) The concept of "column in front, and the sound from behind" is a priori doubtful, since it contradicts everyday experience and the natural course of things.
