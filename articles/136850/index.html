Welcome to our office

This is a tour of the FSF office for people who can not get here, to visit us.

This is the reception room - when you arrive at the FSF, it will be the first thing you see.



Here is John, our executive director.



This room is a team of system analysts - Pibo, Bernie and Ward. At any time of the day or night there are spare parts from computers everywhere.



It's a kitchen. Even the one who has gone into free software sometimes needs a snack.



Over the years we have won some prizes ... stop for a minute to take a look at some of them and look through our scrapbooks.



The meeting room (behind the glass with the Emacs logo) is the place where we meet, chat on the phone and have lunch together every day.



This is a view of the department of system analysts on the other hand - hence the GPL preamble on the glass is visible.



If you order something from our store, we pack it here ...



... And this is our huge stock of T-shirts and other goods with GNU emblems.



In the back room there are a lot of non-plastered brick walls filled with construction company messages that cannot be deciphered.



We have not figured out what the company is actually doing on the other side of the street, but they seem to never notice the people who are walking around our office, pulling at the huge heads of GNU, so there must be something interesting.
