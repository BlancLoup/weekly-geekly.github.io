Ministry of Finance refused taxes for Apple and Google

The Presidential Administration's initiative to tax large IT companies has not found support in the Government. The Ministry of Finance will continue to consider this issue with the participation of the business community, and Google and Apple will still not pay tax on applications, music and video purchased by Russians in the AppStore, iTunes and Google Play stores.



In March 2015, it was reported that Assistant to the President of Russia Igor Schegolev held a meeting on the subject of additional taxation of foreign Internet companies. First of all, it was about Apple and Google. According to the participants of the meeting, IT giants underpay taxes: in particular, VAT is not subject to purchases in the AppStore, iTunes and Google Play. The reason for the meeting was the complaint of "Yandex" to the Federal Antimonopoly Service.
