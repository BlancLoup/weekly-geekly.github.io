Mail.Ru launches video hosting

Mail.Ru has entered a promising video hosting market by launching Video@Mail.Ru . The Rambler-Vision service will be the closest competitor of the service, but the Rambler service was launched back in 2004 and has a regular monthly audience of 760 thousand people.

On the site Video@Mail.Ru plan to post both amateur and licensed content. But the custom video will dominate. Over time, the company will begin to include advertising in it, and potentially it could be the main source of income from the service. In addition, traditional display advertising will bring in money. The service interface is made similar to the Photo@Mail.Ru interface.
