Confirmation when publishing a new post



Hello!

It seems to me that it would be nice to add confirmation before posting. In my opinion, the current placement of buttons in drafts , publish and preview is not the most successful.

For example, I write articles at night, by this time the attention is already dissipating, and you are doing some operations purely mechanically. And with the current placement, there is a very high chance of missing and not randomly publishing the finished article, but, as you know, you cannot delete an already published article.

A quick search showed that this problem was not only disturbing me, it was mentioned here and here , and I am sure many more times.

I think that such a change should not somehow complicate the lives of habusers, because the time taken to confirm the publication will be incomparably less time to write the article itself.

PS If this topic has already been discussed, I apologize, but I could not find this discussion.
PPS As a solution without confirmation, you can spread these buttons so that they are not nearby.
