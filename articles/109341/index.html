And now, many games with these consoles cause not only a sense of nostalgia, but also aesthetic pleasure, so to speak. About a year ago, Dune 2 passed on the emulator, along with Rock-n-roll Racing. After that, for some reason I wanted to choose a console for the house, and decided to choose the PS2 - because I can't play a lot, and it makes no sense to take the Xbox 360 or PS3. In general, I play from time to time. And today I came across interesting news that shows that, like no one, Sony understands the feelings of gamers. Why? About this - in the sequel.

In general, the company has released an interesting hybrid, which is a system of 22-inch HD TV and the very Playstation 2, built-in TV. In the UK, the cost of such a device is only $ 300, which, as always, causes a feeling of envy - after all, such a system will cost a lot more. I think if such a device gets on the shelves of the CIS shops, then it will cost $ 500-600, no less.

The model is called Sony Bravia KDL-22PX300. The device configuration includes 4 HDMI ports, 3 USB ports, two of which correspond to the USB 1.1 standard. There are also two Ethernet connectors that allow you to connect the system to the network. The native resolution of the device is 1,366x768, which automatically means the impossibility of displaying HD video as 1080p.

As you know, PS2 allows you to play old games with PS, there are also emulators for Dendy and Sega for the same device.
