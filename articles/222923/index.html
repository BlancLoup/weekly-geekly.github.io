Yandex Money: "It's not about you, it's about me, I'm sorry, we can not be together"

The other day, we received a letter from the Yandex Money administration stating that on May 16, amendments to laws No. 115- and No. 161- come into force. They will affect all e-money operators in runet. For users who do not pass identification, there will be more restrictions.



Letter text literally:
It will happen on May 16
Hello Ivan Ivanov.

What will remain as before
You can:
1. Make purchases at Russian online stores that accept Yandex.Money.
2. Pay for any bills and receipts in the City payments.
3. To replenish the account by any means - in cash (through terminals),
from a bank card or from an account at any bank in the world.

What will change
The law says that now some operations require personal data. Without them you can not:
1. Pay at non-Russian stores - for example, the App Store or Skype;
2. Send and receive transfers from one account to another ;
3. Transfer money to Visa or MasterCard.

If your passport is at hand, fill out a few fields on our website. You must specify the first name, surname, patronymic; number, series and date of issue of the passport; mobile number. This is enough to remove almost all new restrictions, and also to increase the limit on a one-time payment to 60,000 rubles. To completely remove the restrictions, pass the identification - a one-time limit will increase to 100,000 rubles, and you can withdraw money from the account by any means.
