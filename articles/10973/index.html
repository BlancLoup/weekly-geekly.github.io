Kommersant Publishing House will help launch MSN.ru

The global Russian megaportal MSN.ru should be a serious competitor to "Rambler" and Mail.Ru. Currently, Microsoft is negotiating with the publishing house Kommersant for the delivery of news and photos to the Russian version of the portal. Already, the ID has created a special unit for the development and adaptation of content. The agreement will be signed within a few weeks.

About the imminent launch of MSN.ru openly talking to representatives of the department of public relations of the Russian representative office of Microsoft, so this is a decided fact. Through the portal, search services, instant messaging and other online services will be available.

The commercial director of the Kommersant publishing house Pavel Filenkov in an interview with Vedomosti did not mention the commercial terms of the agreement under discussion. According to independent estimates, the ID can count on $ 50,000-100,000 per month, and subject to the development of original content - even more.

The American portal MSN (Microsoft Network) was opened by Microsoft in August 1995. Since then, the same portals have been launched in almost 40 countries.

Mail.Ru CEO Dmitry Grishin believes that "with the resources and capabilities that MSN has, this portal has every chance to enter the top-10 during the year, having received an audience of 300,000-400,000 people a day."
