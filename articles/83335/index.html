Blender Basics is not just another Blender series of documentation. James Kronister wrote this tutorial based on his two years of teaching 3D modeling and animation to students at Central Dauphin Hight School. The book is a finished course on three-dimensional computer graphics. Each chapter is designed for 2 academic hours and includes a theoretical part, on the basis of which the practical task is given, given at the end of each topic. For teachers of computer science and computer graphics is just a godsend! Putting to the right of each chapter in the table of contents of the book "2 o'clock" they get a ready thematic plan of the course.
