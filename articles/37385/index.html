The first Space Network ad has opened

The other day on the Internet has opened a space network ad 9planet.ru . The project was launched on August 8, 2008.
The site allows placing banners on virtual planets of the Solar System and on the Moon, and for fans of VIP-services there is a special offer on placing banners on billboards.
Now everyone who wants to place an advertisement in virtual space will be able to realize their dream.
The project will be of interest to all who wish to use the new advertising space and are looking for a creative approach to advertising their business.
