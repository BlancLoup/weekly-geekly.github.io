Anomaly on the Volgograd bridge

An unusual oscillation height, reaching about a meter, is observed on the Volgograd bridge.
The President has already ordered to understand this, and also ordered to close the bridge until the reasons for such behavior of the structure are discovered.

The length of the bridge is about 7 kilometers and its construction cost about one billion dollars.

The phenomenon is extremely ambiguous and at the moment there is a check for the correctness of the design, construction and operation of the bridge in Volgograd. Experts cannot yet name the specific reason, but believe that the most likely cause is the aerodynamics of the bridge.
