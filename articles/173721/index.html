Register of stationary objects of observation (complexes of WFM)



For residents of Moscow on the site www.data.mos.ru there is an official list of cameras, photo and video registration of traffic rules. On the tab "For Developers" is a description of the fields. The entire database can be downloaded in CVS format.

www.data.mos.ru/datasets/621_reestr_statsionarnyh_obektov_nablyudeniya_kompleksy_fvf

On the portal "Open Data" published information about more than 600 stationary complexes of photo and video recording of traffic violations, installed on the roads of the capital. The data is available in the form of a table by which car owners will be able to check their usual route, and are also available for unloading in CVS format, which developers can use to create applications, for example, warning the driver about the need to reduce speed on a certain section of the road (such handwritten radar detectors ).

For each set of photo and video recordings, information is available about the exact place of its installation, about which way (to the region or center) it is directed to and what violations it fixes - exceeding the maximum speed, driving to a dedicated lane for public transport or non-compliance with new rules movement of freight transport.
