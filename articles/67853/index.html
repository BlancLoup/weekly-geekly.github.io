The interaction of domestic companies in the telecommunications industry

At the end of July, QTECH representatives made a presentation on the concept of product line development and the interaction of domestic companies in the telecommunications industry.
The seminar, held in Voronezh, reflected the current trends of the Russian telecommunications market and, above all, the success of Russian manufacturing companies. Domestic companies of the new generation are successfully crowding foreign brands, offering innovative technological solutions specially designed for the largest Russian operators.
For a long time, foreign companies dominated the market; Russia did not have its own development and production. It was time to integrate into the world market with the study and application of international experience and technology. Now, new domestic companies are ready to offer their developments on the basis of accumulated experience.
