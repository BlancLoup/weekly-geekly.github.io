International Conference Augmented Human 2016


As well as grants for a trip to the conference as part of a program of scientific cooperation between Switzerland and the countries of Eastern Europe


The Seventh International Conference on Human Empowerment through Technique, Augmented Human 2016 (AH16), will be held in Geneva (Switzerland) from February 25 to February 27, 2016.

The Augmented Human Conference is a unique platform for exchanging scientific experience, the most important achievements in the field of development, designing systems for expanding human capabilities with the help of technology, augmented reality and wearable technology, identifying and developing potential innovative projects, providing new developments and technical solutions to the market. sphere.
The conference accepts scientific papers in English that have not been previously published and have not been submitted to other conferences, journals and other printed publications.

Types of reports
1. Full-text reports - 8 pages
2. Short reports: 4 pages
3. Demonstration projects: 2 pages, project demonstration at the conference
4. Posters: 2 pages, poster placement at the conference
5. Student competition

As in previous conferences, AH16 materials will be published in the Electronic Library of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).

Participation in the conference is free, moreover, within the framework of the program of scientific cooperation between Switzerland and the countries of Eastern Europe, travel grants are provided. More details on the website of the Swiss National Science Foundation.

The conference is organized this year by the University of Geneva .
Conference site: http://www.augmented-human.com/
