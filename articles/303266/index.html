The international conference "Payment Innovations and Alternative Payment Systems" is over



On June 9, an international conference, Payment Innovations and Alternative Payment Systems, was held at the Digital October Center conference hall.

The event was organized by Advapay Events, a company already known to participants in the payment market. The official partner was the company CANOPUS Innovative Technologies. Advapay Events already has several successful international projects, as well as a number of webinars and exclusive interviews with top officials from Russian and European companies.

The past event gathered on its site the most active market participants who follow the latest innovations and strive to develop their business as productively as possible. A large number of speakers allowed to acquaint guests of the project with the practice of leading companies.

The business program of the conference was divided into three blocks, which allowed to touch on the most topical issues. Cryptocurrencies and innovations in traditional payment services, as well as the practice of working in Europe and the foreign experience of colleagues throughout the day, were discussed by the speakers and conference delegates. The most talked about were the presentations of Thales e-Security, Coinfox and MoneyPolo. The working atmosphere of business communication did not leave the venue of the event until its completion.

We sincerely hope that participation in the project allowed the guests of the event to expand the pool of business communication, find a professional solution for business development using the latest innovations and practices of their colleagues.

Stay tuned for future events at www.advapay.eu
