International Energy Agency: electric car batteries are getting cheaper fast

The leader in the global market will be Tesla




The official mission of Tesla Motors is to accelerate the transition of the world to clean electric vehicles. The main reason why electric cars still cost more than petrol cars is the high cost of lithium-ion batteries. This is the most important thing that so far inhibits the mass distribution of electric cars.

Nevertheless, the mission of Tesla Motors is gradually being realized. According to the latest report of the International Energy Agency, sales of electric vehicles are growing rapidly. In 2015, more than 550,000 units (+ 70%) were sold worldwide, so that the total number of electric vehicles on the roads increased to 1.25 million.

The most interesting thing happens with batteries. Since 2008, the cost of batteries has dropped four times, and the energy density has increased five times. Tesla promises that after the launch of Gigafabrika prices will fall sharply.

The fact that the world fleet of electric cars exceeded one million units is a symbolic achievement. The report says that this success was achieved thanks to the efforts of commercial companies and government programs for the benefit of clean cars.

There are some more interesting facts in the report. For example, China is the leader in the number of sold electric vehicles, in the second place is the United States, but in terms of the share of electric vehicles in the car market, Norway is ahead (23%), followed by the Netherlands (almost 10%).
