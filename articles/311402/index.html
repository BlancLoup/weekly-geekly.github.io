Cycle of articles "NetRack Guards: on guard of client IT infrastructure": a story about a man walking on roofs

Have you ever wondered what the cooling system means for equipment, especially in such a structure as a data center? In fact, without refrigeration equipment, the servers rented by you will not be able to work due to overheating, therefore there is always a person in the data center who is responsible not only for the condition of the refrigeration equipment, but also for its modernization to increase the efficiency of the infrastructure and, as a result, improve the quality of in it services. It is worth noting that while the cooling system has never failed neither us nor our customers.



We have already talked about working life of the chief engineer of the data center StoreData , where our company is located. Now we will talk about a person who should not be afraid of heights and low temperatures.

On the roof, leaning on the side, stands a thin man, looking thoughtfully into the distance. He often goes to work on the roof, especially in summer when the weather allows. At the same time gives rest to the eyes, because he often has to sit at the computer. Look straight ahead - in the distance you can see the dome of the Church of the Sorrow. Turns to the right - sees two small red buildings, preserved from pre-revolutionary times (by the way, they were consecrated in 1903). Looks around - and all is green. He says that sightseeing is an important stage for him to climb up.



The roof is a mandatory element of the daily round, which is performed by the refrigeration engineer Yevgeny Kolpashchikov. It is important for him to know not only the state of the equipment, but also the state of the external environment. Therefore, the case is never limited to a visual inspection. On the roof, Eugene should listen carefully (how much the external units of air conditioners are noisy), assess the weather (hot or cold outside), find out if the roof is heating up, or if there is vibration amplification. In addition, he must monitor the condition of the roof as a whole - this applies even to roofing work. The frequency of visits depends on weather conditions: with an average temperature and no precipitation, it is possible to limit one rise. If it is snowing on the street, a sharp increase or decrease in air temperature, it is necessary to check the equipment on the roof much more often: about three times a day. Strange as it may seem, even how much the sun shines, or the sky is covered with clouds, can affect.

Control of weather conditions, in principle, is an important aspect of the work of a refrigeration engineer. Among other things, it is necessary to monitor the amount of precipitation. For example, with the onset of winter, when snow falls sharply overnight, and the air conditioners need to be cleaned, despite the vertical blowing in the cars. This has to be done infrequently, because dual-circuit machines - this means that when it stagnates, it switches from one air conditioner to another. It is harder in the summer when everything is clogged with poplar fluff.
