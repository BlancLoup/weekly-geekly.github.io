MultiTouch Twitter Wall

MultiTouch Ltd., the developer of the world's first modular multi-touch LCD for large-scale displays, announced yesterday (12/13/2010) the launch of the industry's first large-scale multi-touch, multi-user Twitter wall app. MultiTouch Twitter Wall consists of at least six cells MultiTouch 46-inch display (3 in width, 2 in height), which clearly displays on the screen tweets from selected hash tags and keywords. Twitter MultiTouch Wall users can open tweets that are displayed on a rotating sphere by touching the avatar and moving or changing the size of the tweet on the display. MultiTouch Twitter Wall also allows you to work with the sphere of images from Flickr, which will be displayed on the screen using keyword search.

MultiTouch will show Twitter Wall at CES 2011 in Las Vegas, January 6-9
