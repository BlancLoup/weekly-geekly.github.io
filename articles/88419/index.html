We add devices in the sealed case or "How to cram in nevi"

More than once I had to deal with the need to connect an additional hard disk drive or a second CD / DVD drive (or the first one) to a new, sealed system man. This implies the case when the seals are on the side covers of the case, and there is no possibility to get inside.

The method is not difficult, but very much depends on the location and number of seals on (in) the case, and on the configuration of the case itself.

First you need to make sure that you have the necessary connectors on the motherboard, as well as get acquainted with their location. Manual in this should help.

Dale need to carefully remove the front panel. Usually it is mounted on plastic latches. If the front panel could not be removed, you can remove the plugs on the front panel. This can be done with a knife or other similar object, gently prying the cap. After removing one of the plugs with the rest should not be a problem.

Now, showing the extreme flexibility of your upper limb, you need to connect a SATA or IDE cable to the motherboard. This is perhaps the most difficult part of the whole process. If you can't do it yourself, you can ask a colleague or a friend with a thinner hand. The best option if it is a girl.
