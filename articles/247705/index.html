Scannable: a new application from Evernote for scanning documents on iPhone / iPad



Scanning documents using the phone does not happen in a vacuum, it is part of everyday work tasks, and the scanning application should reflect this - to be used for work, not for the sake of the process itself. It should be based on the content of the documents, and not on the mechanical digitization of papers.

Therefore, we are pleased to introduce Scannable. This application is available for iPhone and iPad and is created in order to not only scan, but also quickly share the received files (you probably don‚Äôt need another check in the device‚Äôs gallery).

Speed


Point the device camera at a check, business card or contract. This is enough for Scannable to start scanning. No buttons, no trimming, no work with color - everything happens automatically. Each scanned image will appear in the navigator directly in scan mode to make it easier for you to view documents or create a set of several images in one PDF.



Contacts from business cards


Paper business cards are rather inconvenient in many situations: we can put a card in a box and never remember it again or even take a picture of it, but then we will get just a snapshot of a small piece of paper. All this is not particularly productive. Scannable offers an alternative approach.



The value of business cards lies in the information they contain. After you connect your Evernote and LinkedIn accounts, Scannable will instantly scan the business card and process the data from it. The application will independently complement the contact information with data from the LinkedIn profile, including a snapshot, to make it easier to navigate new contacts. A new contact can be saved on your device and you can safely get rid of paper media.
