Trillian v3.1.8.0

Quite a popular messenger, which was once born, as a free IRC client. At the moment, Trillian in its basic configuration supports AIM, ICQ, MSN, Yahoo! and IRC. In addition to the basic version, there is a paid version of Trillian Pro, which adds support for Novell GroupWise Messenger, Bonjour, Jabber, Skype and video messaging. One of the most well-known advantages of Trillian over other messengers is the ability to simultaneously be connected to several ICQ accounts. In version 3.1.8.0, developers have worked to speed up work, improve file transfer on the Yahoo network, support UPnP, and add support for animated emoticons.

Download Trillian Basic v3.1.8.0: trillian-v3.1.8.0.exe (8.6Mb).
