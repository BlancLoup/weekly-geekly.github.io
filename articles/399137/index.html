Everything you need to know about Hong Kong's fintech ecosystem: startups, regulators, investors, and acceleration programs

The author of the material describes Hong Kong FINTECH ecosystem on the example of projects from the sphere of payments, personal finance management and e-commerce.

Why Hong Kong? Charles D'ossi, head of the Fintech division of Invest Hong Kong , gave a good answer to this question: Hong Kong contains all the elements necessary for this city to become a global and regional Fintech hub. It is one of the international financial centers with the most free economy, the rule of law, solid human resources and strong inherited ethics of doing business and work. In addition, Hong Kong is a very pleasant place to live, allowing its inhabitants to be very efficient and flexible. The city has naturally created an environment conducive to new acquaintances and a fruitful dialogue between partners.



Hong Kong has been an innovation center for many years and boasts a unique position that allows it, with the support of private and governmental organizations, to become a launching pad for all who want to enter the world's largest consumer market.

Moreover, Hong Kong can boast the highest rate of practical use of financial technologies (29%), ahead of markets such as the USA (16.5%), Singapore (14.7%), the United Kingdom (14.3%), Australia (13%) and Canada ( 8.2%). Speaking of access to capital by the Fintech sector, D'ossi noted that the 10 largest investments in the Asia-Pacific Fintech market region were related to projects in China and Hong Kong. Their total value was 90% of the total investment of the region, or $ 8.75 billion. This figure has eclipsed the figure of the European Region, whose countries managed to attract only 1.85 billion dollars for the same period.

Payments, money transfers and mobile wallets


33 Financial Services Ltd. (a subsidiary of China 33 Media Group Limited) is a Hong Kong-based financial services provider. Offers services for the issuance and support of prepaid cards and other financial products, such as commercial and consumer lending, risk management, marketing services, customer service and IT support.

AsiaPay is an accredited payment system and vendor of payment gateways for banks, online payment systems for merchants, a certified international vendor of 3-D Secure for Visa, MasterCard, American Express and JCB.

Cashyou is a free P2P mobile wallet that allows you to make instant direct money transfers using NFC, QR codes or online. The company's goal is to provide a safe and easy way to send and receive small but frequent local payments in Hong Kong.

The platform can convert cash into prepaid cards replenished by balance, RFID / NFC wristbands or electronic vouchers. The list of services also includes the possibility of organizing electronic access to events, tracking the position of devices and integration with social media.
