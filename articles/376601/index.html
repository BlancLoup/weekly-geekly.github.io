Rope Smart Rope can display the number of jumps "in the air" in front of the athlete



Startup Tangram showed a smart jump rope for fitness lovers Smart Rope . An unusual property of the rope is the ability to see the number of jumps in front of you "in the air" during exercise.
The numbers appear in front of the athlete due to the LED diodes built into the ‚Äúrope‚Äù of the rope, the position of which is monitored during the exercise. Smart Rope, in addition to the jump counter, is also able to display the number of calories expended and the total time spent practicing with it. The display mode switch is located on one of the rope's handles, so you can switch right during the jump. On the "indicator" are placed 4 digits, which are clearly visible even in a bright room.

In addition to visualization, Smart Rope can communicate in Bluetooth with a specially designed fitness application that stores and processes data on physical activity.
