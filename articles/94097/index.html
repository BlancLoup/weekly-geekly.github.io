Hero core

Technology moves forward; followed by games. From two-dimensional games, people gradually move to three-dimensional ones: from pixel graphics to realistic ones. Old games are forgotten by most people.
Instead of the old eight-bit palette, they increasingly use High Color, improve the engines, create realistic models, more and more resemble real people, buy more and more powerful computers ...





To be honest, in fact, the game, which this article is devoted to, is not too simple. She has the most simple design and simple control, but try to go through it all and find all the secrets ...

New game from Daniel Remar , author Iji . Hero Core.



In the game you will find:
  • the furious labyrinths of the Tetron base, filled with combat robots who do not know about the pain;
  • nine bosses headed by Tetron himself;
  • unforgettable graphics, drawn in as many as two colors * ;
  • specially created soundtrack;
  • Many secrets and secret abilities for your hero!
* Players with powerful computers can enjoy effects in an eight-bit palette!



Despite its single-bit graphics, the game is able to give you more than one day, full of old school fun. Players who love simple fun can leisurely destroy Tetron on the usual difficulty level, and hardcore lovers will surely remember not one hundred Very Kind Words of all the robots on the asteroid, trying to complete the game on a difficult level with the automatic base map turned off.
The game has many secrets, secrets and, of course, especially powerful enemies. All this is flavored with old-school background music from Brother Android , which gives the final touch to the atmosphere of "the best game on a standard terminal."
Try to cope with the game without editing the save game!

Hero Core works fine on Linux + WINE.
In addition, the game is translated into Russian, so that you can enjoy the old school with younger relatives.
