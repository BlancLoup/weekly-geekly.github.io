Developing a cloud startup: marketing, development and plans



I was inspired to write this post by Megamind in offering free subscriptions to startups who are ready to talk about the development of their project, share successes and packagings, and also have a direct dialogue with Megamy people. Well, although we already have a subscription to our company, we also consider it not superfluous to talk about our work. Below in the text, I will describe who we are, what we did in April on the project and what we plan to do in May.

Who are we?


Since June 2014, we have been developing a virtual PBX for RingCloud business . In August, we conducted a beta test of the product, in which more than 300 testers took part (here you can read more about this) , in October we started selling the service to the first users, simultaneously improving the product based on their responses, and in February of this year we went to full production fully armed.

Post I will break into five parts:
  • Development of new functionality
  • Improving and catching bugs
  • Sales
  • Marketing
  • Plans for may

Development of new functionality

We slightly improved our external number connection management interface by adding call forwarding management on the provider's side to our personal account, now in order to enable incoming calls to be redirected to a SIP connection or vice versa, our users do not need to write HTTP requests to send SMS or call those support Megafon, all this can be done in our personal account.

REST API

In March, we very often heard from various users the question of the possibility of integration with CRM systems. We also received interesting suggestions on using RingCloud in conjunction with other products. For all this, we needed to provide our users with their own REST API. No sooner said than done. In April, this task hit our project manager and its implementation began. Already, we have a ready-made list of functions that we will give to users, and they, in turn, based on them, will be able to do any integration. In terms of API visualization, we decided to use Sphinx . On April 24, I accept the task from the developers, and in early May we will lay out the API in public.

Improving and catching bugs


Facilitate the registration of a legal entity

Once again, going through all the stages of registration and use of the system, testers proposed to simplify the procedure for entering details for legal entities (LLC, JSC).

In the process of development, the billing requirements for the mandatory presence of fields to fill out the details migrated to the project. In fact, for the registration of the sale of numbers, the presence of crossed-out data is not necessary. And when filling out, not every user had on hand the data of the OKVED of his enterprise. Now these data are not required and do not create inconvenience for users to work.

Voice mail bug


Voicemail is activated by switching the toggle switch. Everything would be fine, but at some iteration of the product, we flew the function of saving changes. That is, after switching and exiting the settings section, they were not saved. Since voice mail is not the most popular option, we also found it thanks to testers, not customer complaints. Found and corrected.

Sales and user interaction


Long awaited trial period


Since February, we tested a business model that allowed access to the service only after paying for the first month of the selected tariff. In fact, the results of registrations and payments were quite satisfactory. Only here we have not developed a product for a satisfactory level. In parallel with the users who paid and used, we had a growing funnel of potential users who expressed doubts not about us personally, namely, whether a virtual PBX and / or they can cope with its configuration even with our help. It was for them in April that the option of a free trial period of 10 days became available. Our sales managers reported this to those who registered earlier, and the increase in daily registrations was more than 60%.

Marketing


Article Content Placements

Until this month, we worked only on pay traffic. Our first attempts at writing articles for such portals as Shopologist , e-pepper , Habr and Megamind gave a noticeable increase in registrations and purchases. We also received cool reviews on our product. Some of the comments / suggestions are taken to work. We will continue. For those who are just going to work in this area, I want to advise to distribute a small media plan for 1-2 months for publications. First, think about the topics and who in the team can write. It is so much easier to decide whether to start working or not.

TV series translations (sponsorship)


It is very difficult to record direct metrics through this channel, but the growth of direct transitions indicates that interest in the product increases on the day of the release of the series of various TV series. It is safe to say (and this confirms the bounce rate) that people do not just come to gaze at the site from advertising.
