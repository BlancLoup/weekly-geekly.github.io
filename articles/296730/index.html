40 situations that you will be asked at the interview

Of course, it is impossible to collect all the questions that may be asked to you. But we tried. These are mainly questions related to specific situations from your life. At least you have time to remember these and talk about them.



Do you change jobs again? Can have forgotten how to enjoy it?

// Teamwork


Team spirit is much more important than you think. If people understand each other, if they are willing to make compromises, then everything will be much simpler - this is like a mechanism where everyone knows their business and will never substitute someone who is close, because otherwise the common cause will be useless.

Tell us about the situation when you had to work with a person with whom you were very different.
Give an example of a conflict situation in a team. How did you deal with her?
Describe the moment when you needed to build a relationship with someone very important and you did not immediately succeed. What helped you cope?
We all make mistakes that we would like to correct. Tell us about the situation with your colleague in which you would like to do otherwise.
Tell us about the situation in which you needed to get information from a not very responsive person - how did you do it?


// Customer focus


If you work in the service sector or are engaged in service, then, of course, you are familiar with the situation when you have to cope with a difficult client, when suddenly everything collapses and everyone is unhappy or vice versa - suddenly you managed to save the day.

Describe the situation when it was fundamentally important to make a great impression on the client - what did you do?
Give an example of a situation where you did not meet the expectations of your client. What happened, how did you restore your reputation?
Tell us about the moment when you made sure that the client is very pleased with the service.
Describe the situation when you had to deal with a complex client. What was the situation and how did you deal with it?
When you work with a large number of clients at the same time, it is difficult to provide excellent quality services to each of them. How would you prioritize?


// Ability to adapt


In fact, this is one of the most necessary qualities. If you are able to find an approach to people, if you feel the needs of clients, if you can create a comfortable atmosphere for yourself - this is half the success. The ability to adapt and in nature is considered one of the most important for survival.

Tell us about the situation when you were under strong pressure from outside. What's happened? How did you handle it (or not)?
Describe the time when your company or team experienced some kind of change - how did it affect you and how did you manage to adapt?
Tell us about your first job.
Give an example of a situation where you had to get out of a difficult and unpleasant situation.
Tell us about your failure. How did you handle this?
