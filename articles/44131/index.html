Nokia Smartphone Owners Can Post Blog Posts

Bloghosting Blogs@Mail.Ru was the first Russian service to support the Share Online application on Nokia smartphones. Now our users can quickly and easily publish posts with photos in their diaries on Blogs@Mail.Ru.

Currently, the new functionality is available to owners of 16 Nokia phone models: N-Series, E-Series and others (the list of supported devices will be expanded). On some of them, the Share Online application is preinstalled; in the case of other phones, it must first be installed. In any case, you need to configure the service to work with Blogs@Mail.Ru (a detailed guide is here - mobile.mail.ru/shareonline ).

After installing Share Online, a mobile phone owner gets the opportunity to publish photos (up to six at a time) in his diary on Bloh@Mail.Ru, with a text if desired. You can create posts from both the camera interface and the Share Online application itself.
