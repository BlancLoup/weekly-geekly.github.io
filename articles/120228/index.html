On Friday, the construction of the ISS / ISS was officially completed


In other words, on Friday, the astronauts completed the last scheduled work at the International Space Station, attaching the last module (something like a laser-equipped crane) to the station for a robotic manipulator. Thus, after 13 years and 35-100 billion dollars (the exact amount is unknown), the orbital space station is fully ready. It worked before, we all know, but the work performed by astronauts last Friday is the final stage to complete the construction of the ISS.
