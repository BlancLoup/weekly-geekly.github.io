The program of the cloud track of the DevCon 2016 conference has been finalized



Hello!

We are pleased to present to the participants and non-participants who are still planning the final program of the cloud track. Under the cut - the details of what will be discussed in the track. Since there will be a broadcast on DevCon and then recordings, all this unique content will be available - don't miss it!


Today I wrote that the program of the community track was finalized. Informally, it can be said that the program of the community track is not the main program, however, in terms of the semantic reports, the community is a very important component of the event, since it significantly complements the reports from the thematic tracks. So, one of the thematic tracks was the cloudy one, which has been taking place in DevCon for several years already.

There are only five reports on the cloud track (not counting the plenary). They are chosen in such a way as to logically develop the main ideas that will be voiced in the plenary report of the track from John Galloway , our overseas colleague from Microsoft headquarters. John will tell about all those trends that occur in the cloud ecosystem of Microsoft.

The main topics will be microservices-and-containers, project lifecycle management, Open Source, the Internet of things and visualization . John will give an introduction and tell about the place of these topics in the cloud, about what is happening and how it affects or will affect the future life of the developer.

Vladimir Yunev from Microsoft will continue the thought of John, telling about the new Azure services that can be used in the development process.








Next, I, your humble servant Alexander Belotserkovsky , will tell you about the history of Azure, how everything happened, what services were before, and what is happening now, as well as the prerequisites for what is happening - in order to more clearly understand what services should be used scenarios and what prerequisites guided the developers of these services, offering new options.






The next in the story is Ivan Hudson - a colleague from the corporation, who previously worked at the Argonne National Laboratory and other authoritative organizations on large projects. The code name of Ivan‚Äôs report is IoT E2E S. Or, if in Russian, a story about how to build an I-to-from solution from the devices to the visualization. With concrete, very interesting real stories that are happening right now.






Catalina Gheorghiu , solution architect and multiple MVP owner, I asked to talk about how to use PowerBI to visualize IoT solutions. Katalin decided not to do the report, but to show different scenarios with his hands. So much the better - I responsibly declare that it will be impressive.









Closes the track, Vladimir Gusarov , director of R & D Moscow branch of Dell. Every time we write an obvious moment, that Vladimir is a veteran of the industry, and this time I decided not to write about it (although, of course, I could not miss this moment). Vladimir will tell you about the tasks and opportunities for managing the application life cycle.

We thought about Open Source and decided that it would be nice to do a whole master class that I and Andrei Beshkov and I will conduct on the first day.
