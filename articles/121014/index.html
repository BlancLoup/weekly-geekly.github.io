Diablo III Mouse and Headphones

The company SteelSeries, one of the leaders in the production of gaming peripherals, at the E3 exhibition announced its new devices - a headset and a mouse made in the style of Diablo III. They will appear on the market in October, under the cut - a few details.





Mouse





Even if you were not going to buy a mouse specifically for gaming, this device may change your mind.



It costs a mouse $ 70, it is covered by a guarantee of 10,000,000 clicks (according to the company, this is three times longer than the life span of an average mouse). With the mouse comes a special software that tracks the number of clicks on each of the seven keys and displays the statistics on the heat map. The driver allows you to use several profiles of mouse settings (which should have a positive effect when using different classes of characters in Diablo III).

This mouse seemed to him more comfortable, compared to his own Razer Naga , the sound of pressing the buttons is a bit more muffled than that of ordinary mice.



Braided nylon cord, 2.5 meters + gold plated USB.



Full face / profile.



"Demonic" lights. The user can enable or disable it, as well as adjust the pulsation frequency.



7 buttons.

Headset





The headset will cost about $ 120, this is a modified version of the headset SteelSeries Siberia v2

Features include a sturdy frame, excellent sound quality and a retractable microphone. When checking the headset in the case, Patrick could not appreciate the sound insulation level (as he was near a huge speaker system), but he notes that the headphones were comfortable, sounded great and (most importantly) looked cool.
