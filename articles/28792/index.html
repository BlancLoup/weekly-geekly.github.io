Viruskitty


The creators of viruses in horror, antivirus shocked. Already from one box only. In China, antivirus and firewall came out under the Hello Kitty brand - a popular white toy kitten with a red bow from Japan.

Previously, these were USB flash drives, social networks, charging for phones and laptops. Now Hello Kitty wants to protect users from viruses. Look at her already scary?



Reference: Hello Kitty is a character of Japanese pop culture, a little white kitty (see neko) in a simplified drawing. Invented by Sanrio in 1974 and became a kind of meme. The Hello Kitty trademark, registered in 1976, is used as a brand for many products, has become the protagonist of the cartoon series of the same name, appears in cameo roles in other cartoons. Hello Kitty toys are popular souvenirs in Japan and around the world.

Bonus: Hello Kitty song


Hello Kitty's antivirus and firewall is currently only available in Chinese, and is only sold in China. According to rumors, Custodia stands behind the creation of the program.
