Idea. Literary travel

Essence: the map shows geographical objects that are found in a literary work - settlements, rivers, lakes, routes of heroes, etc. The marks on the map are complemented with descriptions - fragments from the work, pictures of artists about these events, photographs, etc.
The idea itself is not new. I met with literary travels, thanks to Eugene Patarakina. Here is the material on the topic, and this is a detailed instruction for creating .
