Three-dimensional photos - already next year

Recently, the Japanese company Fujifilm introduced its new camera, which implements the technology FINEPIX Real3D . The main feature of this camera is that it is capable of taking three-dimensional photographs. The 3D effect is achieved due to the presence of two synchronized lenses in the device, each of which produces images at its own angle. The captured images are processed by the processor and reduced to a single three-dimensional image, which is projected on a special 3D LCD display camera. The presence of a pair of lens systems also gives the camera a number of additional features, including improved selection of shooting parameters, shooting with a wide angle of capture, shooting panoramas, simultaneous video and photography.

But how can you see these three-dimensional photos? Fujifilm also took care of this. Images can be viewed, for example, using a special digital three-dimensional photo frame FinePix Real 3D Photo Frame with a diagonal of 8.4 inches. Its screen has a resolution of 920,000 pixels. In addition to three-dimensional photographs, such a frame can also display ordinary 2D images.

In addition, pictures taken with a three-dimensional camera can be easily printed on a special printer from Fujifilm, which will create an image with a coating that acts as a lens. If you look at the picture through such a lens, you can see a three-dimensional image.

All these products will be available next year.
