Microsoft unites financiers

Microsoft has announced the creation of Microsoft Dynamics , an online community to which finance professionals can join.

Microsoft Dynamics is positioned as a social network, however, in a certain way oriented. Its members will have the opportunity to conduct columns and blogs, exchange views and experiences.

Interestingly, the current name of the community is temporary. The users themselves take part in creating the name, 45% of which were in favor of the name Finance Forum, and 35% - for Net Knowledge.

via InformationWeek
