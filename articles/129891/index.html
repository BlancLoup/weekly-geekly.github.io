Photos from the exhibition InfoSecurity Russia 2011

Hello to all!

As it was written earlier in our blog, Cloud4y participated in the exhibition InfoSecurity Russia 2011 .

I offer you a small report from this exhibition.

The exhibition was held from September 28 to September 30 at the Sokolniki Expo Center, and more than 120 Russian and foreign companies participated. Over three days, it was visited by more than 4,500 people - representatives of IT services, information security, small and medium businesses, as well as government and corporate customers. A similar exhibition has been held in Russia already 8 times.

At the exhibition, we demonstrated the principle of operation and capabilities of our cloud. Log in under the test account and see how everything works "live" could anyone. We also participated in the Cloud Conference, where we presented our BackUp solution.

Exhibitors include leading Russian developers and integrators in the field of IT and information security: Kaspersky Lab, Security Code, Aladdin RD, SRC SOKB, FSUE Concern Sisteprom, OKB SAPR, CryptoPro, Ankad, InfoTex, Amikon, IVK, Validata, Confident, Maskom, NPO Echelon, MFI-Soft, Jet Infosystems, IT, SIS, Microtest, Almitek, Oversan Group, Avirs Projects, Teleinkom PC, I-Teco and others.
