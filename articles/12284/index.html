In London, earned free WiFi

London residents got free WiFi-access, which was organized for them by Free-hotspot.com, which installed the equipment, and MeshHopper, an Internet provider.

You can use the online services of the Online-4-Free.com network (connection is made at a speed of 256 kbit / s) on both banks of the Thames, as well as in the direction from the central region of Millbank to Greenwich. True, in order to read your favorite sites or friends, you will first have to watch a commercial. Access without advertising will be already paid.
