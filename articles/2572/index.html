Selling on MTV.ru will IMHO VI

The IMHO VI selling agency will exclusively sell advertisements on the main banner positions of the Russian TV channel MTV site. The contract was concluded for the whole of 2007.

According to the publication CNews , IMHO VI plans to optimize the technological and price aspects of selling and advertising on the portal. The advertisers of the telecom, fashion and other industries will be involved in placing banners.

According to the agency's general director Svetlana Gorokhova, MTV.ru has taken a promising niche in the media market and is now actively developing. This is confirmed by a recent study of the audience of the site, conducted by MASMI .

According to statistics from the researcher in December 2006, the monthly audience of MTV.ru was more than 240 thousand people. Its core is young people aged 11 to 34 years. 47% of MTV.ru visitors are students, 15% are specialists and managers of middle or lower level.
