Finally, I became a Yota tester. Officially!

Yesterday (October 16) Yota modems were first issued in the Capital.

Now I am an official Yota tester, which in no way changes my views on the truth and does not inhibit the struggle for real information on the development of such a phenomenon as WiMAX.

By the way, the contingent in Moscow was, let's say, older than I personally expected. Whether such a group (the first) plucked up, or geek enthusiasts are all like that.

The main thing is that the process has begun. And this despite the depressing external conjuncture in the country.

According to the statement of the officials, now the distribution of "gifts" will go faster. Still, they would not delay the commissioning of new BSs - it would be very good.

The kit includes:
- Branded box
- Two documents (on Yota and on the modem)
- Access card with primary login / password
- White usb modem Samsung SWC-U200



For all questions - opened the forum on the tester, collect the installation FAQ and draw a coverage map.
