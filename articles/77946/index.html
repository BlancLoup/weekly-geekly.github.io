Workaround to "classmates" and "in contact" :)

Today, one of the clients complained that during working hours managers are sitting on "classmates", although the proxy is banned. The first reasonable option that came to mind was that they use a third-party proxy server, although the most popular ports for public proxies were closed. However, after logging the logs, I found one interesting thing - an appeal to the od.antizapret.ru website.
Following the link to antizapret.ru - I saw the information that using this site you can go to "classmates" and "in contact".
I think that info is useful to people on both sides of the barricades: both admins and users.

PS: It is interesting that they keep the username and password for both sites or immediately make redirects through their proxy? Therefore, I still advise you to think 7 times before entering your credentials there.
