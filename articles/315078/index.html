"Browser War" Google Chrome continues - news from the fields



At the Chrome Dev Summit 2016 event, Google announced new usage data for its browser. Google Chrome is installed on more than two billion devices worldwide. This figure takes into account both desktop computers and mobile gadgets.

Information about active users of Google Chrome is not disclosed. We only know that the mobile version of the browser uses 1 billion people. But judging by strengthening the position of the browser in the market, its audience should not shrink. Moreover, Google representatives said that The audience of each of the applications Gmail, Android, Maps, Search, YouTube, Google Play exceeds 1 billion users.

In October, Google Chrome developers announced the release of the 53rd version. Among the new features - play video in the background.
