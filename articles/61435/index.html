EXcit!

EXcit is a puzzle built in tables :) The point is to reach the exit without collecting the table edges, collecting logos along the way (although this is not necessary, it will just affect your final score :)).



Management is simple - just arrows. In order not to fly out of the table and get where we need, we have all sorts of blocks, gates, portals, etc. that we can safely knock on and move on :)

Also, each level has its own password so that after leaving the game you can immediately go to the level where you finished.

In general, enjoy and share to where we were able to walk :)
