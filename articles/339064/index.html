MUK becomes an Oracle distributor

The group of companies MUK and Oracle Corporation, a leading global manufacturer of software and server solutions, announced the signing of a distribution contract in Azerbaijan, Armenia, Georgia, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan.



Being a VAD-distributor, the MUK group of companies is actively developing a partner channel in the regions, providing partners with additional opportunities to improve and expand their business with Oracle. This includes joint marketing activities and assistance in training the staff of partner companies - the MUK Training Center is Authorized Oracle University Reseller. The company also provides the ability to use equipment for testing and demonstrations to customers. It is worth noting the possibility of registration and protection of projects, as well as assistance in working on complex projects and technical advice.

One of Oracle's flagship products is the high-performance Oracle Database. In the field of Oracle engineering systems, the most well-known are the Exadata Database Machine - a cluster of servers for managing databases, as well as a Database Appliance software and hardware complex, a Big Data Big Data Complex, Exalogic Elastic Cloud system and a solution for consolidating databases and applications. SuperCluster.

In addition, the Oracle portfolio contains secure servers on its own SPARC architecture, ensuring data security without sacrificing performance. The company also offers All-Flash, NAS, SAN storage systems and tape storage systems.

One of the priorities of Oracle - cloud services. The company offers customers a comprehensive integrated cloud, Oracle Cloud, which helps organizations innovate, improve adaptability, reduce costs and simplify IT systems. For organizations that need an internal private cloud, Oracle provides a comprehensive portfolio of platform and infrastructure solutions. Managed cloud services are available in the company's portfolio for business applications, middleware, database management systems and equipment.
