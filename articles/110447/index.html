Apple removed the WikiLeaks App from the App Store

The WikiLeaks App, an unofficial iPhone, iPod touch and iPad app that allows you to browse the WikiLeaks website and follow WikiLeaks twitter, has been removed from the App Store today.



Google Cache: App Page

Description of the WikiLeaks App:
The WikiLeaks App provides instant access to the world's most documented leak of top secret records and other confidential government documents.
Registered application on December 18 and "lived" there for only three days.

Application developer Igor Barinov ( igorbarinov ) has confirmed deletion of:


According to him, one of the possible reasons for deletion is half the revenue from the sale of the WikiLeaks App (which costs $ 1.99) was transferred to support the WikiLeaks project.

But most likely this is simply caused by pressure on Apple by the American authorities.
