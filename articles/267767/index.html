DropBox distributes place to students / teachers 2



University Dropbox Cup is another promotion with a distribution of up to 25 GB for 2 years (until January 1, 2018).
The promotion ends December 14, 2015.

How to register for the University Cup?
1. Open the University Cup homepage .
2. Sign in to your Dropbox account (if you haven't already).
3. Confirm the email address that you have registered at a college, university or other educational institution (this should be exactly your address, which is located on the domain of your university).
4. Check your email, confirm the address and complete the registration.

  • If you are showing a blank page, disable the ad blocker.
  • Dropbox business accounts cannot participate in the University Cup


Bonuses
For registration / verification email you get 3GB of space.
When registering a large number of participants from your school, these participants receive additional space (up to 22GB). For each admitted school, 3 points are set, at which space is added to the vault. The first threshold is 5GB, the second is 7GB, and the third is 10 GB.

The place can be obtained by completing the following tasks (so you earn points for your entire university):
  • Install Dropbox on your computer and log in to your account - 20
  • Install the Dropbox mobile app and sign in to your account - 5
  • Create a new shared folder - 10
  • Create Access Link - 5
  • Create File Request - 5
  • Invite a friend (this friend must accept the invitation) during the first week of your participation in the cup - 3 (for the invitation)
  • Invite a friend (this friend must accept the invitation) after the first week of your participation in the cup - 1 (for the invitation)


The resulting bonus space can be checked at www.dropbox.com/account/bonus . Additional space will appear in your account an hour after your university reaches the threshold number of points.
