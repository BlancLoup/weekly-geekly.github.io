War of microworld

Users of Opera browser, as a rule, are people with fantasy, which is again confirmed by a computer game created by one of the opera fans. The essence of the game is simple and ordinary: you need to save the world from the invasion of aliens. But how to do it, if the evil aliens are no larger than a poppy seed, and you only have a similar-sized starship in your arsenal? However, there is a way out: you just need to choose the appropriate battlefield in size. For example - use the usual icon of the website of the game Defender of the Favicon .

Rules of the game
Do not allow adversaries to land on their home planet, shooting all the approaching enemy ships from a laser cannon.
Control
To start the game, open the website Defender of the Favicon and press the N key. To move your space fighter, use the arrow keys or the W, A, S, D keys, shooting is done with the N key. If your browser is constantly trying to update the icon, press Enter to move the battlefield to the page.

Here, in general, that's all. And may the force be with us!
