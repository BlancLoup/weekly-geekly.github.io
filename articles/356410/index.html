The Ministry of Communications and Mass Media has withdrawn from Rostelecom in a row on the development of Gosuslug mobile applications

CNews reports that on December 19, the Deputy Minister of Communications, Alexei Kozyrev, sent a letter to the senior vice president of Rostelecom, Andrei Cheglakov, about the unilateral refusal of the customer to execute the mobile development contract for the state services. For 26 million rubles, Rostelecom had to develop applications for smartphones and tablets based on iOS, Android and Windows.
