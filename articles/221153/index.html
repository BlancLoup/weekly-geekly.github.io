About ICAgile
The International Agile Consortium is an independent agency like PMI, IIDA and ScrumAlliance, which issues a limited number of Agile certificates to proven professionals. ICAgile was co-founded and supported by Alistair Coburn, one of the most well-known and influential Agile practitioners, co-author and initiator of the Agile manifesto. ICAgile develops a training program for flexible software development, attracting the best experts from around the world. The consortium builds a training program, accredits courses and trainers, and thus allows students to receive certification. ICAgile does not compare courses with each other, nor does it conduct training on its own.
