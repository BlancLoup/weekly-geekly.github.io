RTC Meetup .Net: welcome to first meeting

The Raiffeisenbank development community actively conducts meetings, prepares workshops, and does much more so that employees can search for expertise and share experience with colleagues from other companies. Development of Raiffeisenbank is located in two cities: in Moscow and Omsk. On our meetings in Moscow one could see many reports from our Omsk colleagues, but we never held a single meeting in Omsk. To restore balance in the universe, we decided to eliminate this injustice and launch meetings of professional communities in Omsk on a regular basis: RTC Meetups.



The first meeting of the RTC Meetup will take place on July 26 at Studio 1901 and will be devoted to .Net . The event starts at 17:00, registration starts at 16:30. We invite you to participate in the meeting. The program of the event is scheduled for 3 reports from the speakers of the internal .Net community of Raiffeisenbank.

Vitaly Yermokhin, Data developer

Breeding rabbits for beginners: features, tips, tricks

Main techniques for working with RabbitMQ (patterns, approaches to work with message queues, guaranteed delivery)
Examples are considered .Net Core, Rabbit MQ, EasyNetQ packages
The main objective of the report is to show what approaches there are in working with message queues.

Gustov Konstantin, architect of the ICDB family

From monolith to microservices: five practical tips

Many projects are now thinking about moving from a monolithic solution to a microservice architecture. We give practical advice on how to make this transition more reliable and simple. Both in terms of code and in terms of infrastructure.

Roman Prosin, ICDB USI developer

The tale of how Cake Dot Net built

The report talks about building a pipeline for building solutions on the .Net platform using the Cake utility. The experience of the team is demonstrated, the possibilities that the use of Cake for building solutions opens up to developers are described. Advantages over assembly using standard tools
