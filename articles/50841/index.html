mp3 bathroom mirror

Everyone knows that a singing voice sounds better in the bathroom. Instead of singing a cappella, the Italian designer, showed ingenuity and came up with an iPod-dock for the bathroom. The dock is built into the mirror, so there will be no problems with erratic and dangerous wires in the sink.

The system will work with any mirror in the steel frame, has a touch-sensitive LED on / off button, conveniently located at the bottom of the mirror. There is also a backlight for "special" evenings in the bathroom.

Price and release date is still unknown.
