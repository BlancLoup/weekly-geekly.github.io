New Facebook news feed - what is it?

Yesterday at a closed press event, Mark Zuckerberg presented a new format of news feeds on the world's largest social networking site, Facebook . You can see what a new news feed looks like on this page, right there you can sign up for a design update waiting list.



In short - this
- more visual content (large images, thumbnails of shared links, activity from third-party applications, etc.),
- several news feeds to choose from (where they promise not to filter the content, but to show everything, everything),
- the same (or, rather, similar) view and functionality on different platforms (desktop, tablet, smartphone), and
- sidebar, which allows you to quickly navigate to different sections (news, groups, chat, applications, etc.)
