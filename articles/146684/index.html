Published reports and interviews from the conference DevCon'12

  • more than 50 reports on 8 key topics;
  • plenary reports of the 1st and 2nd days;
  • recording a question and answer session with Scott Hanselman, chief architect for projects with Microsoft communities;
  • Interviews with conference speakers, which took place directly at DevCon'12 during breaks.
