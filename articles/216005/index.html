Internet technology excellence or one life offline - a look from the past

The reader of this modest historical story is offered not as it usually happens - to plunge into the past, but only to take a closer look at the real present (pun).

Last night I went home and looked at the stars: Jupiter was just above my head and I remembered that I had a telescope hidden in a deep-lined ages in my closet. I came home, went to the site universe-calc.ru , indicated my location on a Google map and received for my locality a calendar of astronomical phenomena for the coming year: from flashes of iridiums to solar eclipses. It turned out that this year there will be 22 coverings of the planets by the Moon and none of them will be visible in the European part of Russia, and on October 8 the Moon will close Uranus, being in total eclipse, an article on this site has already been written with the instruction that what is the easiest way to get to the place of observation, and about the organization of a tourist trip. But what will happen on August 18 will be able to see everything - the rarest convergence of Jupiter, Venus and the M44 star cluster, when the planets are located at a distance less than half the diameter of the Moon from each other (Approx. ). On this site, all astronomical events are ranked by importance, rarity, proximity to the observer and the power of the required telescope, more of them have articles, instructions, video reports and amateur photos. The newest discovered asteroids, comets and flashes of supernovae enter the system on the same day, and all this allows you to plan for 5 minutes the most interesting observations for the next month - three. This morning, while I was brushing my teeth, a message came to the phone, but the phone did not play a ringtone, but read it in a human voice. Messages that come to me start to read out loud themselves, if the home WiFi access point is visible, and they do not need to be specially opened. It said that my companion, who drives me and other guys to work, due to fewer traffic jams will arrive 10 minutes earlier than the agreed time - this forecast was made by his navigator program in the phone and sent to me automatically. I must say that the navigator calculates the route, not only taking into account traffic jams, but, connecting with the database, takes into account all sorts of obstacles that reduce the maximum speed on different parts of the road. The algorithm also takes into account the effect of cars equipped with navigators on the creation of traffic jams. Therefore, all recommended routes on all devices not only minimize travel time, but also minimize traffic congestion as much as possible, and accounting for traffic lights in this database at each time point recommends optimal speed and warns you of a possible passage to yellow or red. This time there was a traffic accident on the route 5 minutes before our journey, so the program did not have time to adjust our route so that we didn‚Äôt waste time on downtime due to the loss of the lane, but we received an offer to move to the right lane in advance.

When I was at work, the task arose to buy another computer and I was given the task to select components for it. This is easily done on the comp-calc.ru site - there the owners of their own assembled computers contribute to the database a set of purchased components and programs, and if any problems arise and / or if their solutions are found, they describe the whole thing, tying it to their configuration. The site engine statistically reveals the most stable causal relationships and, at the user's request, gives a prediction about the possible incompatibility of the hypothetical configuration of the hardware itself or the risk of problems with the software. Those. if you need to assemble a computer, then just go to this site, specify the details you have planned to buy and you will receive a diagnosis: a list of possible problems and ways to fix them. It took me five minutes to find a reliable configuration, select a store that had all the spare parts, place an order and print out the invoice.
