Alisher Usmanov Launches ProNovosti Video Portal

The Center for Media Technologies is completing the beta testing of the video news portal Newstube.ru (ProNovosti). Created along the lines of Rutube.ru, this portal will specialize in professional news stories, and among content providers are Russia's largest television studios, including VGTRK, TV Center and others. The opening of the portal has already been officially announced on the English-language website of the Center for Media Technologies.

The final launch of the portal is expected in the second half of May or in early June.
