The very system of promoting games on the counters of Steam looks tempting, besides, the role is played by the fact that the players will be promoted by the projects that they like.

Desura

Undoubtedly, Desura is one of the best sites for your game. Its advantages are not so high requirements for the quality of games and cross-platform client. From the first plus it is clear that for Indie-developer Desura will be one of the best options for distributing their games, since not everyone can afford to make the game so beautiful that it would be taken to Steam without talking. From the second plus, it is clear that your game can be seen not only by Windows users, but also by the owners of Mac and Linux.

Desura has a convenient game management system, flexible settings and a nice design. When you publish a game on Desura , you automatically create your product page on resources such as IndieDB and ModDB , which is a good thing. All three sites are closely related and there will be no need to run over them if you notice an error in the text or decide to reload the game version - it‚Äôs enough to make an edit in one of the systems.

The game is published on this page (registration is required). To create a page for the game, you need a game icon, boxshot, screenshot preview, and a cap of two sizes. When you submit a game for moderation, you will be asked to upload ten screenshots and one video that demonstrates the gameplay. In order not to waste time, I advise you to take care of this immediately. Moderation of the game can take from several hours to three days, depending on your luck.

Desura has its own API for creating in their games achievements and statistics related to a profile in Desura. A very useful thing, because even such trifles can motivate players to play your creations.

Bottom line: nice service with good attendance and convenient management of published games. Low requirements for games will make a good start in the distribution of your games.
