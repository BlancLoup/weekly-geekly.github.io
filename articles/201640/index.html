Sony Xperia Z1: work on the bugs at the request of workers

We met Sony Xperia Z1 smartphone in September at the IFA exhibition. After listening to the incendiary presentation of Kazuo Hirai, I somehow immediately decided that my next Android device would be Z1. At that time, Xperia Z had already been in my pocket for several months, causing only positive feelings, and his heir seemed a natural and desirable replacement. Moreover, Sony has improved everything in it, really worthy of improvement.



The smartphone quickly got into my hands, I began to use it regularly, and the farther I went, the shorter the text of the article about the Z1, emerging in my head. Not because the device is bad. On the contrary - it is excellent. But this is not a fundamentally new model, but a good work on the bugs. Accordingly, to what has already been said about Xperia Z, it remains to add only a few important touches. And, of course, look inside the device for interesting design features.

Z1 turned out to be even larger than quite rather big Z, and both in length and in width. Given the fact that the screen diagonal remains the same, this can only be explained by the desire to fit a 3000 mAh battery inside. The desire, to be sure, is commendable. However, the extended side frames make it very difficult to control with one hand. So, being a user of the official Twitter application, I can‚Äôt reach out with my thumb to the tweet button (upper right corner) with the left grip. Well, that is, of course, I can, but for this you have to intercept the smartphone in a different way. Nowadays, these little things do not scare anyone, but, for example, the LG G2 with its 5.2-inch screen is very thin frames allow you to reach the same angle without problems.



A very important advantage for me is the dust and moisture resistance of Sony's flagship smartphones. Whether it rains, snows on the street - you always get Xperia without fear of damaging the delicate stuffing. True, before this was achieved with the help of plugs for any and all openings on the smartphone. In the Z1, the headphone jack has finally been freed from it, which will please fans to use the smartphone as a player. I myself constantly listened to music on the Xperia Z, forgiving him not the best expressiveness of sound and not perfect high for the great application Walkman. It is really very convenient and thoughtful, and on other smartphones I really miss it. On Z1, the situation is about the same: the application is excellent, but the sound ... The sound is at a good average level. Better than the absolute majority of devices on Android, but worse than on the iPhone and, especially, LG G2 with its DAC Wolfson WM5110 . So if you do not bother about the sound of plates and copper-nickel harmonics, listen and enjoy the Walkman. If the headphones are cheaper than thousands of dollars, consider useless junk, buy something else. However, you, dear audiophiles, will do it without my advice.

As it was known to absolutely everyone, even those who never held the Xperia Z in their hands, this device had a Terribly Bad Screen. I was amused by this secret knowledge, because I looked at this screen every day and I liked everything about it. Yes, I would probably choose a different matrix for processing photos in Photoshop, but as far as I know, a full-fledged version of this program has not reached Android yet. For everything else, the Xperia Z screen fits perfectly, and the limited viewing angles made you feel quite comfortable even in a crowded subway car, when everyone, out of boredom, starts to pry into the neighbor's phone screen.


As we can see from the portrait of writer Alex Exler, the shades fade at an angle, but are not distorted. Personally, I think this is more of an advantage than a disadvantage of a matrix.

But these lovers of privacy, apparently, not very much, so Sony Xperia Z1 put the screen a little better. He still can not compete in brightness, rich colors and viewing angles with the best products of Samsung and LG, but compared with the Xperia Z progress is evident. Shades have become more succulent, viewing angles slightly increased. Fortunately, not so much so that the neighbors in the car are too happy with the content of your Facebook feed. In my opinion, the Z1 screen is optimal for a mobile device. Everything is as it should, and the eyes do not get tired. But then decide for yourself which approach is closer to you. The main thing is to trust yourself, and not to fool quotes from reviews of some unknown individuals.



On the camera Xperia Z1, I will do a separate material, because so far I have not managed to make an unequivocal impression about it. On the one hand, Sony is almost the main supplier of photo modules on the market, and the freshest 20-megapixel Exmor RS simply cannot be bad.
