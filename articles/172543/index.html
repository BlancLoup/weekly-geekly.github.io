Sony Xperia Z Review



Sony's flagship, the Xperia Z smartphone, is already familiar to Habra, but this time it fell into my hands for a longer test. On the results of several days of living together with this device, I will tell under the cut.

To begin with - a general summary of the characteristics:


Display: 5-inch screen 1080 x 1920 Full HD Reality Display with Mobile BRAVIA Engine 2 technology
Processor: asynchronous quad-core Snapdragon S4 Qualcomm APQ8064 frequency of 1.5 GHz
OS: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) with the subsequent upgrade to 4.2.
Memory: 2 GB RAM, internal flash memory - up to 16 GB, microSD support up to 32 GB
Camera: 13 MP with Exmor RS matrix for mobile devices, support for HDR video, Superior Auto mode and noise reduction, thanks to which it is possible to obtain crystal clear images and video in any conditions
Front camera: 2 MP with Exmor R matrix
Battery: 2330 mAh, Battery STAMINA mode, increasing the standby time at least 4 times

Design and ergonomics


The main principle of Xperia Z design is symmetry. Even the power button is made almost in the center of the side panel. However, in addition to the aesthetic function, this location has a practical function: even with short hobby fingers it is easy to reach it from almost any position - which is very important for a five-inch smartphone. The button itself unexpectedly resembles the wheel of a watch factory - it is quite original. She does not act strongly, and under no circumstances does she ever cling or interfere.



Directly above the button is a slot for a SIM card, closed with a plug. Below is a two-sided volume and speaker key. Just below is the obligatory and inevitable eyelet for the strap.





On the left - contacts for connection with the docking station. The Xperia Z will go on sale with the docking station in the kit, but only the device came to my test, so I can only show photos from the last review:



The contact brought out allows you to connect the smartphone to the dock in one motion, without opening the connectors and other gestures - it is simply put on the base and starts charging. However, at the price of convenience, it turned out that this contact is only suitable for charging, data transmission through it is impossible, so, unfortunately, it is impossible to connect a mouse or keyboard to the docking station.



Above the contact are the connectors for the microSD card and the MHL port, which connects the connector for the HDMI adapter and microUSB. They fit so tightly that they can simply not notice - with a quick glance you can not see that there is something else above the slot for the memory card. At the same time, they themselves are subtle and elegant - those who saw the "armor" of the first Sony Xperia waterproof a couple of years ago, will appreciate this evolution. The best defense is invisible.



At the upper end is also an audio output closed by a plug.



The back panel is covered with a solid piece of glass, in which only the holes of the external microphone and camera are cut out. At the same time, the camera itself is hidden flush with the surface of the phone, without in any way disturbing its symmetry. The glass is additionally closed on top with a thick protective film - just like the frontal one, by the way.

Connection


One of the toughest stress tests for a mobile phone is the elevator at my entrance. As a rule, the network (Megafon and MTS in my case) disappears as soon as the access door closes behind me. In a rare case, catches to the elevator doors. Sony Xperia Z showed slightly better results - it holds the network to the elevator doors and, fragmentary, catches between floors.
In addition to the mandatory Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, the board has already become the standard for top smartphones NFC and LTE (Bands 1, 3, 5, 7, 8, 20).

Battery


The main consumable item of the battery is predictably a huge display. Two useful things immediately answer for the idle appetite in the standby mode: the power saving mode and the Battery STAMINA, which make it possible to discourage the phone from eating the battery in the standby mode, due to which its duration increases to several days.
