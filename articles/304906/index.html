Applications for the VIII Summer School of High-Performance Computing in the Field of Computer Vision have been opened

Computing platforms are showing unceasing growth. The main success we see in industrial robotics and mechatronics. However, the lag in the technologies of artificial intelligence and the component - computer vision for understanding scenes and image analysis is still a deterrent to further development.
