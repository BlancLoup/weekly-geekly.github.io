Touch Z-Distance - the world's first online photo studio


With this project, everyone can feel like a real photographer, you do not need to buy an expensive camera and look for an object to shoot, i.e. models or landscapes, anyone anywhere in the world will be able to photograph professional models. At the moment, the photo studio is equipped with 8 controlled cameras.
Thanks to the special design of tripods, you can control cameras in 6 directions, directly down, up, left and right, as well as change the camera's direction in space, zoom and auto / manual focus are provided, with which you can point the focus point with the mouse.

Anything you need
This is to go to the site of Touch Z-Distance , log in with your VKontakte or Facebook account, select a model from the list and start a photo.

Every five days, the developers plan to change the theme of the photo shoot to diversify the shooting of models.
The project was implemented by two graduates of MSTU. Bauman Lesha Lebedev and Sergei Ivashin, who are developing robotic devices and their controls.
The sponsor of the project was Panasonic taking on the cost of renting and equipment studio for a month during which the project will last. Specialists from Panasonic chose a touchscreen for the Panasonic Lumix TZ-20 project, thereby making the guys a bit more difficult for them because it was interesting for them to bring the capabilities of their robots closer to humans.
For the period of the project, 6 people will be in the photo studio: models, costume designers and technical specialists, the founders of the studio.

They have their own Blog , as well as VKontakte , Facebook and Twitter pages.
