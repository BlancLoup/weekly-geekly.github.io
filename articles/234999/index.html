New media consoles on Rockchip RK3288 from the company Tronsmart already on sale


The Chinese company Tronsmart has finally announced its new media consoles on the Rockhip RK3288 chip called Orion R28. Continuing the tradition of the previous model Vega S89 will be 3 versions: Pro, Meta, Telos. They differ in the amount of permanent memory and Wifi-chips, and in the Telos version they promise to install 4GB of RAM!
Another interesting feature is the positioning of the device as Full Open Source - the first 90 customers of each model will receive a microSD card with a full developer kit. Start shipping devices scheduled for September 3.

The versions differ only in the amount of permanent memory (8GB in Pro version, 16GB Meta version) and different Wifi-chips (AP6210 in Pro version, Broadcom AP6335 in Meta version). It is necessary to highlight the support of 1000Mbit networks, in all previous similar devices only work at a speed of 100Mbit was supported.
After production of these versions is improved, a version called Telos with 4GB RAM and 32GB of permanent memory is planned.

The price and approximate release dates are currently unknown.

What is interesting about Rockchip RK3288?


The RK3288 is a 4-core 28nm Cortex-A17 processor with a maximum frequency of 2GHz and a 4-core Mali T764 video accelerator. 2GHz is theoretically the maximum frequency of the processor; in practice, the Orion R28 will operate at 1.8GHz. This is due to the substantial heating of the chip under heavy load, which the passive radiator can not cope with.
Mali T764 is a powerful 4-core video accelerator with support for OpenGL ES1.1 / 2.0 / 3.0, OpenVG1.1, OpenCL1.1 and Renderscript. According to Mali T764 synthetic tests, Mali-400 is faster by about 50%.

Supported image output in 4K * 2K format and H.265 decoding.

When displaying an image in FULL HD Tronsmart Orion R28 in Antutu 5 scores 37776 points, which is an excellent result.
