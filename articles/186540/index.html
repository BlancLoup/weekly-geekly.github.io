New version of Cubieboard2 Allwinner A20 (Cortex-A7 Dual-Core)

After the success of the Cubieboard development board project, the Cubieteam team continued to develop. And in June, introduced a new version of its product Cubieboard2. The Cubieboard2 debug board is a modified Cubieboard of the first version. The difference lies in installing a new version of the SoC AllWinner A20 ARM Cortex-A7 Dual-Core. Due to the compliance of the AllWinner A20 pads with the previous AllWinner A10 chip, it was not necessary to redo the topology of the printed circuit board.
