The programmer identified his source in a Hollywood movie



An information security specialist from SecurityTube.net learned with pleasant surprise that Hollywood directors used its source code in one of the scenes of the new film.

Naturally, the functionality of the program is not related to the plot of the film, the code is just stupidly inserted into the video sequence. But the author is still satisfied. Not every programmer manages to see his creation in the cinema, even for 3-4 seconds, and the hero of the film expresses the extreme degree of joy (hands are raised up) when he looks at this code. Although in the film the program is executed, it seems, even without compilation.
