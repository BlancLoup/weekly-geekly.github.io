10 reasons to attend the DUMP Developer Conference in Yekaterinburg


It always seemed to us that Yekaterinburg lacked a superconference for full IT happiness, namely events with the best Ural and Russian speakers, some international star. We already have megaprojects, startups, ventures and mentors. In general, we want not only our programmers to go to Moscow, St. Petersburg, but also listeners from other regions come to us. Last year we gathered 300 people, in this we want twice as much. It seems we have a chance, because we are well prepared.

Conference DUMP (Development. Usability. Management. Practice) will be held May 25-27 in Yekaterinburg at the Hotel Angelo. First of all, we focused on developers and PM, engaged in web projects, mobile applications, highload-services. This means no internet marketing, advertising reports, well, about startups - maybe only a little bit.
