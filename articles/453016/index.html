What can teach TPK Foliplast



  • 3D scanning and reverse engineering - scanning of three-dimensional objects and launching production according to models developed in accordance with scans;
  • Small-scale production of products according to the technology of thermal vacuum molding of sheet plastics, molding of volumetric parts from sheet blanks;
  • The introduction of automated technical systems in production - the use of robotics.


About TPK Foliplast



The largest production and engineering center for rapid prototyping, vacuum molding, volumetric machining of parts, 3D scanning and printing in Russia.
It brings together 65 professionals and has 2,500 square meters. m. of production areas equipped with the most modern equipment for plastics processing.

Course program



Part 1: Fast production of plastic products from thermoset plastics


In the first part of the course, you will gain knowledge of the following technical processes and their application:

  • 3D printing. Prototyping and master models using SLA technology;
  • The technology of molding products from thermosets (with the properties of basic engineering plastics and rubbers) under vacuum conditions;
  • RIM - technology. Casting products from thermoset plastics using mixing and dosing equipment;
  • Demonstration of technology in existing production.


Dinner


Part 2: Small-scale production of products using thermal vacuum molding technology for sheet plastics


In the second part of the course, you will learn about the implementation and application of the following processes:

  • 3D scanning, reverse engineering in the design of molded products;
  • Design and manufacture of equipment (including using a milling robotic complex);
  • Quality control of manufacturing equipment using a 3D scanner,
  • Thermal vacuum forming of sheet plastics. The functionality and features of the equipment.
  • Robotic trimming blanks.
  • Demonstration of technology in existing production.
