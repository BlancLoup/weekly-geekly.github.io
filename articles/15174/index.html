My flexible glowing keyboard

About three weeks ago, I decided to buy a keyboard for my laptop, since it is rather inconvenient to work on it myself. Came on Habr and the first post saw the Flexible, shone, waterproof Brando keyboard . Then usb.Brando , order, PayPal, shipping address. That came today parcel.

First about the delivery. In Brando not lied. That delivery will be carried out in 7-20 days. I had a keyboard for 18 days. Although the first week, the order status was "Pending". When asked what the problem was, the answer was that the goods were out of stock, and would be sent soon, upon receipt. The nearest time came in a week, from the day of order.

I'll be back to the device itself. The keyboard itself flew in this form.

Nondescript rumpled and scotch tape rewound.
