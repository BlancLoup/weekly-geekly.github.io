Testing a new VPS platform from Majordomo

Dear Hobrazhiteli!

We start testing cluster equipment for VPS hosting. You have the opportunity to test your own VPS server for a month for free.

Full and high-quality testing can not be conducted without appropriate knowledge and experience. Therefore, we appeal to all habrazhiteli, familiar with systems such as Linux, and with skills in the command line. The best option for testing would be



if you are a web developer or administrator. We will be grateful for any suggestions and comments related to the work of the service.

What do we offer to test?

For testing, we offer two special tariff plans:
T1 (600 MHz CPU, 512 RAM) and T2 (2200 MHz CPU, 2048 RAM)
- unlimited traffic
- virtualization: Xen
- 1 IP address
- 10GB of disk space
- the ability to install any 32-bit OS on the server
- ISPmanager Lite server control panel

What are the limitations?

We do not recommend using your test account for work projects - during testing there may be abnormal situations that we cannot warn you in advance.

What will happen after testing?

After the end of testing, you can switch to one of the current tariffs or wait for the commercial operation of the tested equipment. The most active "testers" will receive a discount on the provision of VPS hosting services under the new tariff plans.

You can learn more and apply on the testing page: www.majordomo.ru/hosting/vps_test.php

We will also be happy to answer any questions related to testing in this topic.
