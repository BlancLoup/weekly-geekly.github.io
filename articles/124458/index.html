Analysis of the SBR (freelance) in the framework of the CEDC

In Enough general management theory there is a remarkable law, which is called the ‚Äú Law of Normal Distribution ‚Äù, its essence is as follows: if you test many identical objects for any one property, the result will be as follows:
  • from 1% to 3% of objects will show excellent results;
  • from 3% to 7% good results;
  • 80% to 90% average, normal results;
  • from 3% to 7% poor results;
  • from 1% to 3% are very bad, the worst results.

It should be noted that this law acts in all natural phenomena, and also applies to society as a whole, social groups within it, and to each individual individually.

Since we consider the activities of distant work exchanges, we will immediately give an example of the justice of this law in the following way, choose the general statistics of remote workers (freelancers) by rating (taken from free-lance.ru) and get the following results:
  • from 1% to 3% very high rating (TOP) above 7000 points;
  • from 3% to 7% high rating of 5000 - 7000 points;
  • from 80% to 90% average rating of 500 - 5000 points;
  • from 3% to 7% low rating is less than 500 points;
  • from 1% to 3% very low rating to 100 points.
