The Eighth All-Russian Meeting of the System Administrators of the ACD 2013



We are glad to announce the holding of the Eighth All-Russian gathering of system administrators!
The event will take place on July 25 - 28, 2013. It is expected that more than 5,000 IT specialists from Russia and neighboring countries will gather at the rally.
