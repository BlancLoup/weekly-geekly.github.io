On the realities of modern mining


Mining is a business with a significant amount of risk and unpredictability. The high volatility of cryptocurrency can almost instantly leave the miner without pants, or vice versa will allow you to rapidly double or triple the investment. There is always a bit of market ‚Äúmagic‚Äù, for example, when you plan to withdraw namnennuyu crypt in fiatnye assets, and some light-Coin adds 30% of the cost over the weekend.

Today, there are disputes about how easy it is to mine as compared to 2016 - 2017. Despite significant skepticism, which naturally changed the cryptotype, I can state with confidence that this is not so. In 2016, there was a problem with the availability of equipment, it was relatively hard to purchase asiki officially, and there were no specialized data centers with mining equipment.

The most common method of mining in 2016 was the construction of a rig from video cards and the installation of a truss in a garage or on a balcony, ideally also with a solar panel, in order to minimize production costs. Now everything has changed, until the prohibitive measures have been taken, the mining equipment is imported officially, has the notification of the FSB and is implemented with full VAT.

Unfortunately or fortunately, the times of the wild garage-balcony mining and reckless crypto-enthusiasm have sunk into oblivion. To start mining today, a sensible calculation, attentive business planning, adequate risk management, strong nerves and a clear budget certainty are needed. Investment strategy for medium and small investments in equipment is calculated for three years.

There are various options for mining strategies and investing. For example, with a one-time purchase of equipment or with a multiple expansion of the park for three years. The latter approach is considered more balanced.

In any case, before starting the production of cryptoactive assets, the equipment market should be studied independently or with the help of outside experts. Based on what you studied, buy ASICs and put them on a hosting. Obviously, it is more profitable to buy the most modern equipment, provided that its PoW algorithms have already been tested, for example, as SHA-256 for bitcoin and SCRYPT for litecoin. Otherwise, the probability of being left without pants increases in proportion to the level of uncertainty of the algorithms and the non-repeatability of the ASIC miner.

It is possible to reduce costs by purchasing used equipment, the same L3 + can be found in decent technical condition in China. It is important to understand that the cost often changes and it is really cheap to find a relatively powerful ASIC-miner only after thorough monitoring of prices on the market. There is also the possibility of buying equipment at home, which will already be on the shelf in the hosting. There are already mining hotels in Russia where you can start mining right after payment. It is natural that in such cases starting costs will be somewhat higher, but on the other hand, there are no risks to buy low-quality used equipment.

The data is provided by Sergey Arestov, co-founder of BitCluster - a network of mining data centers. In this case, we are not talking about investments of less than a million rubles, which is chosen to show the payback of relatively small investments.
