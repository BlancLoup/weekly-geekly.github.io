Twitpic closes

Free hosting of images and videos Twitpic stops working on September 25, 2014 . Until that time, all users can download their files.

Twitpic is a very popular service. Often used by journalists and activists to quickly spread news in the blogosphere and chronicles events almost in real time.

The causes of the conflict are quite understandable. The commercial designation Twitpic uses a derivative of the word Twitter, which Twitter considers unacceptable.
