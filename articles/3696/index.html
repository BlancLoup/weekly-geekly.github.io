How to make my data not stole carders ... The most simple rules.

If for a counterfeiter the most important thing is to forge a drawing on a bill, then for a card reader it is necessary to copy the information contained in the magnetic strip of a credit card. If you are a victim of carders - do not despair. Despite the seemingly hopeless situation, you have a good chance to get your money back. The first thing to do if you find your credit card is missing is to call the bank and block it.
Often, in a card issue agreement, Russian banks state that after a declaration of the loss / loss of a card, all financial costs are borne by the bank. So you should hurry, and as soon as possible to inform the bank about the loss. If a card is stolen from you, then the less time you give the criminal, the more money you have left in the account. It may also be the case that you, as a bona fide cardholder, keep it with you, not having performed any operations, and suddenly, on the day when the account-statement should arrive, you notice with surprise that the funds on your card have "diminished." In turn, it will be necessary to block the card, for one simple reason that the details of your card have become known to the fraudster, and there is a great danger of repeated transactions.

Then you go to the bank, and write a statement of disagreement with the transaction. In the application, in addition to the standard data (last name, first name, patronymic, contact information, type of your payment card, its number and expiration date), you will need to specify the currency and amount of the disputed operation, the date of its execution and the number in the payment system, and also the name or code of the store or terminal where it was held. All this can be found in the account statement, which you need to take with you. After you sign the application, the bank will accept it for consideration. First of all, the bank will try to establish who is responsible for the fraudulent transaction.
