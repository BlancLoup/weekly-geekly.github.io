Start Programming Cup - Russian Code Cup powered by Mail.Ru Group

Russian Code Cup is a contest for the most powerful Runet programmers.

Already on April 7, registration of participants began on the official site russiancodecup.ru

The competition will consist of several Internet rounds, which will be held from May 8 to June 19.
The organizers plan to bring the 50 best programmers to the final round in September 2011.
Top 3 finalists will receive cash prizes of $ 10,000, $ 5,000 and $ 3,000, respectively.

All assignments were developed with the help of an expert group of St. Petersburg State University of Information Technologies, Mechanics and Optics under the leadership of Andrei Stankevich, coach of SPSU ITMO at the ACM ICPC student programming world championships.
