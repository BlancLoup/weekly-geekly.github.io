Universal communication environment

Hi, Megamind!

We have been thinking a lot lately. Over our products - present and future. What can a modern connection do for a company to make it better, easier and more convenient?

In fact, the channels of communication no longer becomes.
All the same audio message, video, chat rooms - only in different formats. Skype, vibers, watches - yes, they grow in breadth, but not deep.
And it is true that here you can come up with a new one?




And then we were visited by such an idea. We decided to focus on the ways of communication.
What if you can communicate with virtually any device that surrounds us? From smartphones, from work phones, from computers, and even from TVs - all in the same environment? One program that brings together a large number of types of terminal equipment and at the same time will support full-scale conferences and international negotiations?

This idea was the basis of our service Meetinger. Service for business environment - for employees and managers of the company. So that they always remain inside the corporate network, including extensions and the phone book.

You can use the Meetinger from various types of devices: from video telephones at workstations of employees, from video terminals in meeting rooms, from mobile devices on Android and iOS, from PC to Windows with the Softfon application installed. And from a computer on which Softfon for PC is not installed, you can use the WEB interface directly from the browser.



With all the devices that surround us in the office. With microwaves and refrigerators yet. We think about it :)

In addition to the usual forms of communication: video calls, text messaging, local services and telephone / international telephone calls - Meetinger allows you to conduct video conferencing sessions with any number of participants. The number of concurrent sessions is unlimited.

There are other plans. In the near future we will make a unique ID with a password, now they are allocated for each device - and will be replaced with a single login with a password for each user. It will be universal for authorization in the Meetinger environment from any device.

We want it to be a unified communication environment for staff. This will be provided by our servers (bandwidth 1 billion minutes per month) and round-the-clock technical support.

In the following posts we will discuss in more detail about the browser WEB-phone and other components of Meetingera.

That's all. Please tell us everything you think :)
