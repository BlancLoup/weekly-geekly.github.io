With the last effort and discarding the beat, you begin your story.



And the programmer saw that the crutch is good for the preservation of nerves, and that once is not scary


Any team of programmers will sooner or later begin to deviate from the specification. And the more redundant the specification is in relation to today's needs, the earlier this process will begin.
