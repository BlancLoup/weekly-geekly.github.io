Service Logs - Users

For a long time, I was worried about the problem that the shared hosting user did not always know what was going on with his account - did anyone log in via ftp , did the cron job run , was there ssh access, where did the email go and did it go. Most hosters (including us) could ask a question to the technical support service and wait for a specialist with the appropriate rights and qualifications to make a selection of the necessary logs.
