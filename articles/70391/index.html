Nethack. Tips for the first fifty steps

I hope the last introductory article about the game Nethack prompted many to download and install this wonderful toy. And the veterans, after reading it, again killed a dozen or two characters. As I write these two articles, I keep score on dozens of entries in the nethaka fame book.
I am sure that your character has suffered from sudden death more than once, and the heels of coyotes lead you into confusion. Cheer up, novice adventurer! Together we will go to the end!

Let's try to give advice to a newbie in netkhakovskih dungeons. Here, run. Enter the name. What's next?


Ask questions! Nethack does not apply to intuitive games. I will say more - nethak refers to intuitively incomprehensible games. How to guess yourself that the corpse of a basilisk can be used as a weapon? Or the fact that the book thrown at the enemy also causes harm? Right! Ask questions.

What is this krakozyabra? Every character on the screen means something. True, the characters are few, therefore, color - additional marking. Due to the similarity of the characters, you can skip the book, which lies in the doorway (and the door and the book are +).
Check for suspicious bugs on the screen. It is very simple to learn the decoding of a symbol: press the colon (:), select the character position with the cursor, press the dot (.). Note that the colon is used to mean shift +;
Also, keep in mind that in one cage there can be more than one object and only one monster.
For example, in the fortress of Ludios there are a lot of soldiers . After stripping, I had twenty-two (!) Screens of things in one cell ( usually the character carries only two screens of things with him).

Do not rush down below. On the first ten levels of the character, the rule works: the number of the floor coincides with the level of the character. Do not climb into the hell at once - your hero and so sweetly do not have to.

Chess! With this word, I emphasize the need for a strategic approach in dangerous battles and unpredictable intrigues. If it smells fried, count the moves. A strong hobbit, for example, can be a serious threat to an elf mage with exhausted forces. But here comes to our aid, oh miracle tactics! The base speed of the hobbit is two times lower than the speed of an elf. Thus, for each of your two moves, the hobbit makes one. It turns out that the winning tactics against Frodo will hit in the first move and move away in the second. The main thing to get into the rhythm. With the right approach to the situation, he will not take away your strength and health from you at all. Probably still need a little luck :)
One more example. In the simplest situation, your character can move his feet to one of the eight squares around. You can move using [cursor keys] and home, end, pgUp, pgDown or play an additional keyboard (if you have one, of course) - where the number is five, there is your character. Exceptions are sokoban levels, and doorways. Being directly in the doorway to the character is not available diagonal cells. Only front.
