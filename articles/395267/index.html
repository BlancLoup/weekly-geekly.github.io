How not to lose an online client?

When choosing an online store, users most often pay attention to the price, the reputation of the trading platform and the range of products. According to the consulting agency 4Service Group, when choosing an online store, price is a determining factor for approximately 29% of users who buy online. Positive reviews, recommendations from other clients can be a decisive criterion for 19% of potential buyers. 15% indicate that they will choose the store that offers the largest assortment and the best choice of goods. But when choosing a product on the website, the decisive factor, as a rule, is the convenience of payment and speed of delivery. It is extremely important to adhere to those delivery times that are voiced by the operator of customer support service or are stated on the site of the store.

How will consumers behave if the stated delivery dates are not met? 63% of consumers agree to a delay in one day, 18% will wait, agreeing to any delay and saving the order on this site. However, 19% of consumers are critical (data of 4Service Group). They will not agree to a delay even in one day and refuse the order. Of great importance is also the cost of delivery and the ability to track the order.
