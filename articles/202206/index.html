Uniregistry launched nic.sexy and nic.tattoo

The big race of new domain names of the first level continues . Uniregistry launched nic.sexy and nic.tattoo .



The founder of both registrars, Frank Schilling, has just broadcast a video about who will take the .tattoo domain and why. Now we look forward to the video with propaganda .sexy.



In total, Frank has plans for more than five dozen new zones, such as:

.auction, .audio, .auto, .blackfriday, .cars, .christmas, .click, .country, .deal, .diet, .family, .fashion, .flowers, .free, .furniture, .game, .garden , .gift, .gratis, .guitars, .help, .hiphop, .home, .hosting, .inc, .juegos, .link, .lol, .love, .marketing, .media, .mom, .news,. photo, .pics, .pizza, .property, .racing, .realestate, .restaurant, .sale, .save, .school, .sexy, .shopping, .store, .style, .tattoo, .team, .tech, .video, and .yoga.


ICANN also added nine other new TLDs of the Demand and Donuts registries to the Internet zone yesterday:
  • nic.bike
  • nic.construction
  • nic.contractors
  • nic.estate
  • nic.gallery
  • nic.graphics
  • nic.land
  • nic.plumbing
  • nic.technology


But most of them are being transferred to registry sites or opened with errors. All 11 new zones can be checked with one link .

It will be interesting to find out which of them will become the most popular, let's say in about 5 - 10 years.
