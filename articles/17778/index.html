License CC + will provide authors a lifetime income

Creative Commons has developed a new contract for the CC license, so that the authors of the ‚Äúfree‚Äù works will finally have the opportunity to earn money from the commercial distribution of their works. The new system (i.e., the CC license plus the new contract) is called CC + (CCPlus).
