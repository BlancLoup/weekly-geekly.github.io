Enhance Google Earth for Android

Google has announced an update to the Earth application for the Android platform. The company announced improvements in 3D graphics, speeding up map updates and simplifying the viewing of KML files in an application.

The new technology of 3D rendering, according to Google, was the first significant improvement in 3D over the past 10 years. Mountains and cities on the map will now become more realistic.




Quote:
"As you fly to Paris or are looking for tours in Tokyo, you can be sure that the globe in your pocket will show you the latest information."

After



If you created your own map on Google Earth - for example, if you need it as a teacher in a history lesson - then you can open KML files on Google Drive right in the Earth application.
