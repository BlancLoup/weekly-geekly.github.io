Top 5 regional portals

With a decent margin from the rest, our Ekaterinburg Portal takes the first place, to which I am extremely happy ... Or not happy (?) ... The portal is really big and really very functional and useful, despite that mess of blocks, and the structure as a whole. It is worth noting the fact that, in my opinion, at least half of the sales transactions are used. cars and real estate in the whole city is carried out through e1.ru.

Average daily attendance: about 160,000 people per day. There are ups to 200,000 per day - not bad for a regional portal, is it not?

Design. It is difficult to call what is there, design. But to be honest, I have somehow got used to it and can hardly imagine it in a different way. By the way, in the design of e1 there is a small feature: earlier it had a different design (in bright orange shades), it was changed, but for some reason not in all sections of the site. We can observe the old design, for example, in the section ‚ÄúCar sales‚Äù.

Advertising. She actually hung everything that could be hung. And it costs very decently. The most expensive place is a banner on the main page on the right, the size of 240x400. It will cost you 64,900 rubles a week or 227,150 rubles a month, with a frequency of 50%! I would have such money :)

2) NGS (Novosibirsk, domain registration date: 05.11.1999).



NGS is very similar to our E1: the same services, the same mess of blocks on the main one and the same popularity. For some reason, it even seemed to me more understandable and more functional. Although the logo, after all, does not interfere with the update :).

Average daily attendance: about 120,000 people per day.

Design. Frankly, I like more than e1, but the logo doesn‚Äôt give me any peace, I just want to change it. It is worth noting that all the main sections are located on subdomains and have an individual design and structure - in fact, as separate sites. For example, the "Real Estate" section.

Advertising. On the completion of banners NGS is not inferior. The most expensive banner is a banner at the top of the screen. With a 100% rotation, it will cost you 66,000 rubles a week and 198,000 rubles a month, with a rotation of 50%: 33,000 rubles a week and 99,000 rubles a month. Which is two times less than the E1.

3) FarPost (Far East, domain registration date: 04/05/1999)



To my surprise, the third place is the project oriented to the Far East (hello to Copernicus !). The main sections are: a bulletin board and a catalog of sites - and all by and large. 4) Sibnet (Siberia, domain registration date: 11/04/1996)



The first impression - a holiday maker or an entertainment site - the roofing structure of the site, the roofing design does its job. The main block contains news, and most of it is non-regional, although the site is focused on Siberia. Services are basically the same as that of the NGS and E1.

5) Electronic City (Novosibirsk, domain registration date: 12/21/1999)



The second representative of the glorious city of Novosibirsk. In contrast to the NGS, the electronic city focuses on the "entertainment": games, movies, blogs, forums. Despite the fact that attendance is many times less than that of the NGS, the impression is exactly the opposite. Most likely, this is due to the greater activity of the audience.

Average daily attendance: about 35,000 people per day.

Design. Modern, very convenient and understandable, although the font of the logo seems familiar to me :). Very pleased with the location of the blocks on the main - everything you need is available and noticeable at the first scrolling.

Advertising. It is like it on most other projects, but in the case of the Electronic City, for some reason it does not hurt the eyes and does not distract much. The most expensive banner is a banner on all pages of the site, except the main one, its cost: 39,000 per week and 118,000 per month. The same stretch on the main page will cost 29,000 per week and 90,000 per month.
