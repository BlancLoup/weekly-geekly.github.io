Why do you need a humidifier?


If everything is clear with a coffee maker, iron or, say, a heater, then the need for air humidifiers is far from obvious to everyone. I explain. If the humidity of the air in your apartment falls below a certain mark, this can lead to a number of extremely unpleasant consequences. Starting with dry skin and eyes, continuing exacerbations in those who suffer from chronic diseases of the respiratory system (for example, asthmatics), and ending with problems with the health of young children. In them, organisms are still being formed, and if an adult will somehow survive dry air, albeit with ‚Äúlosses‚Äù, then it is categorically contraindicated for young citizens (and even more so for infants).
