Keeper update, transaction service integration

Two weeks ago, we updated the WebMoney Keeper Classic program to version 3.9.5.0. After a short time after the release, all random shortcomings and errors were promptly corrected and it was time to tell the audience about what was done and why it might be important for you. By the way - the classic Keeper remains the most popular program among active users of our service.

In addition to minor improvements aimed at improving the stability and security of using the program, one function appeared in it, the addition of which was supported by the majority of our users, namely, the integration of Escrow transaction protection service, the principle of which is simple and straightforward and does not differ from such services other payment systems - to invent a bicycle here is completely useless. Buyers and sellers of various products, but - first of all, freelancers and their customers can secure their own transactions using Escrow.

In short, its essence is as follows: when concluding a transaction, you need to select the item ‚ÄúTransfer Type: Via Escrow Service‚Äù, set your terms of the transaction and pay a deposit, the amount of which will be blocked inside the service until the obligations of both parties are fulfilled. In the event that you are a buyer, this is the moment of receipt of the goods, if you are a seller, then this is the moment of sending the goods. After confirmation of the transfer / receipt of the goods by each of the parties, the deposit is returned (on the part of the buyer, this means full payment of the cost of the goods).

The Events section has also changed, which will no longer strain the user with a constantly flashing window. A link to the profile of a user of the WebMoney system, you can go to his page or start a video call - these same features were added to the chat toolbar.
