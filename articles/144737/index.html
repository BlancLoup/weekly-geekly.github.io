Crash test of the copy of Sonim Land Rover S8 phone

Today we will look at the Chinese copy of the Sonim Land Rover S8, which is not killed, will evaluate its characteristics, conduct a series of crash tests, including leaving the phone from the window, bury it in the sandbox for a day, dip it in the lake, wash it under a stream of water, fill it with cement-plaster solution, then get it out of the monolith using a perforator, fry in the oven and move it over with the machine. The results, of course, will be at the end of the review.

In our century of advanced smartphones and tablets, its characteristics look quite pale if they (characteristics, of course) would not be robed in a durable case, whose protection (as promised by the Chinese) is IP67 certified - in simple terms, this is complete dust protection and protection against immersion in water.
Screen 1.8 ", with a resolution of 176 x 220 (yes, makes you recall the Siemens CX65), GSM, one SIM card, 1.4MB of memory (which, however, compensates for the memory card slot), 1.3M camera (lying 0.3M there ), BT. Battery, really happy - 2000mAh.

Test drive, regarding the declared characteristics.

So what was stated? Carefully put the phone back, go back to the beginning and read in pedivikii:
First digit 6 - Dust cannot get into the device. Full protection against contact
The second number 7 - Short - term immersion to a depth of 1 m, with a short-term immersion water does not fall in quantities that disrupt the operation of the device. Permanent work in immersed mode is not expected.

So, the test report:

1) Falls and throws. From a height of 0.5 meters, 1 meter, 2 meters, 5 meters (from the balcony), a game of football (kick 10 meters).
All is well. The maximum that was achieved was a pair of scratches on the side faces of the phone, but it continued to work, as if it had not happened. The screen is located in the recess created by the overlays, so it was not scratched.


2) Dust and sand. Falling in the sand, sanding, burying in the sand, and as an apotheosis - the phone had to work in a sandbox with a scoop!
Also passed. The phone justified the first number - dry dirt, although it was stuffed in the natural folds of the case, could not cause any damage to the phone. They knocked on the curb (see item 1), brushed it off - and you can use it.


3) Water. Splashing, washing under running water, immersion in water.
Spray - not a problem, washing under the tap - too. A brief dive (at the level of ‚Äúdropped into a bucket of water, and immediately pulled out‚Äù) he also withstood without problems, but when he tried to hold it under water for more than 30 seconds, he showed bubbles (no, not on the screen) and turned off. However, disassembly and drying saved the father of the Russian democracy a dialer, and he was again ready for battle.
