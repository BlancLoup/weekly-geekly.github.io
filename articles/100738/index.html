ChartBeat - Listen to the pulse of your site

Many of the webmasters probably use Google Analytics and Yandex.Metrica. I would like to tell you about the service, which does not cancel the statistics giants, but can be a good and exciting addition.



The service has a trial period (for activation you need a credit card), using which we got involved and decided to stay on a paid service.

So what does this service do?



The main thing is that you can watch in real time what is happening on your site. In particular, the following information is available:
  • The number of users on each page
  • Real time attendance statistics
  • What sites and countries did current users come from
  • How much time is on the site and what they are doing at the moment (read, write, just open the page)
  • Website performance at the moment, page load time
  • Watch for references to your project elsewhere.

This allows you to quickly respond to what is happening on the server, improve the content of popular pages and enhance the positive effect or notice what is happening with the server and correct the situation.

What else is interesting?


  • For almost every parameter (the page of your site or the site from which the user came, etc.) you can turn on filtering and follow precisely these parameters. See the history of visiting this village.
  • There is a record of everything that happened in the last 24 hours, which can be viewed as in real time.
  • Notifications about the occurrence of important events are sent: maximum attendance for the period, inaccessibility of the site, etc.
  • Sharing of the monitoring screen with certain parameters, which will be available to other users through the link with the key, without authorization.
