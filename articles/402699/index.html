Four years later, Microsoft acknowledged the mistake of banning the resale of Xbox One games.



The gaming console of the new generation of Xbox One from Microsoft appeared four years ago. Together with the announcement of the console, it was then announced that the games for this console will be released simultaneously in retail and digital store Xbox LIVE. Initially, it was stated that it is necessary to install games, after which digital copies are assigned to the user account. So, to transfer a disc with a game to another user, it was necessary that this user be added to the Xbox Live friends list at least 30 days ago. Only in this case it is possible to transfer both the disc and the digital copy of the game. But the third owner of the same game can no longer be: after reinstalling the media, no one can transfer it.
