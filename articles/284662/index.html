Do you provide services via the Internet? Is the site hosted outside Belarus? Pay the fine

In my "former homeland" marasmus has reached a new level. Quoting a source:

Business entities engaged in the sale of goods, performance of work, provision of services in the Republic of Belarus using information networks, systems and resources connected to the Internet should pay attention: if these networks, systems or resources are not located in Belarus and (or) not registered in the prescribed manner, administrative penalties of 10 to 30 basic units can be applied to the subjects.


An administrative penalty in the form of a fine (in the amount of 5 to 15 basic units) may be imposed on officials of the points of collective use of Internet services (computer clubs, Internet cafes, home networks and other places in which the collective access of users of Internet services to Internet) in case of violation of legislation on the identification of subscriber devices and users, on accounting and storage of information about them, as well as on Internet services provided.
