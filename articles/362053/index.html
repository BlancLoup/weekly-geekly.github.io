Opportunities


Actually, the most interesting - a set of features. So, the speaker is able to play music through a 3.5mm input, via a stereo-bluetooth connection, well, through microiUSB: in the latter case, the host system provides the power of the audio chip and becomes an external audio card, which allows you to work not only with audio output, but also with input . To do this, Axx'e installed four directional microphones at once (two in front, two, respectively, in the back). In addition to playing and recording sound with a smartphone / computer, the speaker is able to work fully autonomously. On the back panel there is a slot for a MicroSD card, with which you can play music or to which you can write from microphones in voice recorder mode. Opportunities strongly oppose those of ROAR, but if ROAR is more designed to be used as a music center, then Axx is a one-stop solution with an emphasis on rich telecommunications capabilities.
