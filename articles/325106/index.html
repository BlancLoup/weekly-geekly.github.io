Vivaldi 1.8 - Immersion in history


Hello! Spring is in the middle of the yard, and we are in the midst of work. One does not interfere with the other, quite the contrary. And as in the spring the first flowers begin to break through from under the snow, so a new stable version of the Vivaldi browser, now number 1.8, is born from the bowels of our friendly team. Well, in the browser itself, new functions are sprouting abundantly. In today's version of the browser added the following functions:

  • The new system works with the history of views
  • Create notes by dragging the selected text
  • Auto Update System Management (Windows)
  • Search by image from the context menu

Here we will talk about these updates and many other improvements in detail with pictures.

This fascinating story

History is a very interesting subject. She hides so many secrets and mysteries in the depths of centuries. The browser history, or, more precisely, the history of web page views by the user, does not reach such a scale, but this does not make it any less interesting. After all, each of us probably once wondered: but I wonder which site I go to most often? What is my browsing activity by the hour? What is going on in the history of my views and is it possible to erase from the history all the entrances to a particular site? In general, there can be a lot of questions. And we think that many of them will be answered by a new, improved system for viewing and managing the history of the Vivaldi browser:


In fact, it is no longer just viewing the history, but its preparation and in-depth analysis at the same time. Browsing by day, week, month, by periods selected in the calendar, analyzing site entries and identifying the most popular ones - in general, the information here is enough for long hours of study. Even when viewing history in just one day:


Naturally, in addition to viewing the story, you can also edit it. Try the right-click context menu on any link - there are a lot of interesting things. For example, you can filter all entries only from this domain, and then, for example, remove them all in one fell swoop. Pretty comfortable. And in such a story you can quickly find a needle in a haystack. In the video below - an example of such a search:


In general, we hope that you will like this new tool and will help not only to better understand how you work online, but also to change the way you work with websites. For example, I realized that I should rest more and sit less on the network. However, I think this already guessed ...
