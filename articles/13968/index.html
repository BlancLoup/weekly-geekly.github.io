The Russian government has introduced a ten percent duty on game consoles

The Russian government decided to introduce import duties on game consoles. The size of the duty will be 10 percent of the customs value of consoles, according to Interfax.
The duty will take effect from October 2007 and will be valid for 9 months.

Today, in Russian stores, the cost of a new generation of gaming systems (Wii, Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3) is from 9 to 20 thousand rubles.
