Acer Timeline 8000 Series Laptops



Finishing July at Acer decided to celebrate the holiday, and therefore presented to the attention of consumers a new line of ultra-thin business laptops Timeline 8000.

The series consists of three models with diagonals of 13.3 inches, 14 inches and 15.6 inches (LED backlit monitors). Depending on the modification, the buyer is offered to choose between a dual-core Intel Core 2 Duo or Core 2 Solo ULV - the first option is more powerful, the second one is more economical.

Intel GMA 4500MHD or ATI Radeon HD 4330 are responsible for the video.

Laptops are equipped with 4 GB (or 8 GB if using 64-bit Windows) of DDR3 RAM, as well as 500 GB of traditional or 80 GB SSD hard drives.

The 15-inch model has 4 USB ports, and there are three such ports in the 14 and 13-inch versions.

The standard package includes a Wi-Fi module, optionally, you can put the HSDPA module.

The declared work time is up to 8 hours.

The maximum thickness of 25 mm notebooks, weight from 1.6 to 2 kg, the price is still unknown.
