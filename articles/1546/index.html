Hitwise: MySpace is the most popular site in the USA

The MySpace social network has become the most visited site in the United States, having moved the Yahoo portal from the first line of the users' charts, Reuters reported, citing a report from Hitwise .

According to researchers, last week, 4.46% of all visits to sites in the United States were made on MySpace. In addition, this social network accounts for 80% of all visits to social networking sites (76% in April 2006).
