QlikView. Create your first application? ...


In this article I want to describe the basics of using QlikView from QlikTech, a company designed for Business Intelligence. Caution, a lot of spoilers.


Little preface


QlikTech is a business intelligence software development company headquartered in Radnor. Founded in 1993 in Sweden.
QlikView is a BI platform with associative in-memory search with built-in ETL tools.
