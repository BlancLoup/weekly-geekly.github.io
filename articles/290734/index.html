11 tools that really make your life easier

We decided to share with you our findings for planning time, combating procrastination, communicating with colleagues, sleeping, creating. Technology can make your life easier. Otherwise, why are they needed?



1. MakeMe


Application to combat procrastination. Add tasks and goals and achieve them. You can share your successes with friends or together overcome peaks. And the application will cheer you up and mock you if you slow down (immediately says that you are a snail!), Discourage you from drinking more coffee and advise you to go for a walk before fulfilling the next goal. In general, fun and true motivates.

2. Producteev


This is the perfect app for project managers! You can connect with Outlook, mail, calendar - all this program synchronizes. There is also the possibility of scheduling tasks for subordinates, which is very convenient.

3. HoursTracker


C using this application, which can be installed on your smartphone, you can control the time you spend on the implementation of a particular case, and you can still track how much money you spend. Carefully, there is a paid DEAR version, and there is a budget.

4. Wunderlist


This application allows you to regulate all-all-all things that you have planned. You can add all the letters to to-do-list directly from the mail in order to view them later Wunderlist is suitable for those who need more organization of non-working time. Available on all devices that you have (except for the calculator).

5. Paper by Fifty Three


An excellent sketchbook for iPad (for free), which has existed for several years, but nothing has been thought out yet. A wonderful resource for sketching, to illustrate what you can not explain in words. And just for fun (and even children can be reassured). In general, a great app.

6. Upper



Free application in which you can plan a list of tasks for the day / week / month and follow the schedule of their productivity. You can also choose a color design - red, black or white.

7. Dragon Mobile Assistant


And this is for owners of smartphones on Android - voice control is also available there. Write important goals, search the Internet, check your email and just chat with your smartphone.

8. HaikuJam


If you need to write something with the team (or you are just fooling around) - this app is ideal for poetic experiments and for sharing photos. Free for iOS, Android.

9. TripIt


An application for iOS, Android controls all your routes, and you can share them with your mom / colleagues / friends / second half. Ideal for controlling your movements in time and space, if you have a lot of business trips.

10. Sleep Better ( iOS , Android )


You also need to sleep wisely. Or rather, calm and well, so we will advise you to use a smart alarm clock. This one, for example.

11. Resume Designer from icanchoose.ru


We sincerely believe that our designer helps young professionals to find work, present themselves in the most favorable light, find their strengths and weaknesses. We created a constructor for everyone who is ready for change.
