Mail.ru Group began to earn good money on "Vkontakte"



Buying the social network Vkontakte began to bring Mail.ru Group money. For example, in the first half of 2015, about half of the total revenue of the group accounted for the social network. For six months, the revenue of the entire Mail.ru Group increased compared to the same period last year by 6.9%, writes Vedomosti. But the revenue "Vkontakte" for the same period of time increased by 34%. In addition, in the II quarter, the growth of social network revenues was even more active, and amounted to 41.4%. Unfortunately, the quarterly dynamics of income group Mail.ru not provided.
