Overview of e-Learning Trackers or Live and Learn!

Increasingly, one can hear about universal monster trackers such as ThePirateBay.org, torrents.ru, or references to trackers of musical or game themes. But besides them, there have long been torrent trackers of a training orientation, about which little is known to the average user. Basically, these are closed communities of educated people with invitation registration that exchange educational materials, such as e-books, educational videos, CBT (computer based trainings), audio books, presentations from conferences or software for training. The majority of materials are related to IT technologies and therefore will be especially useful for IT professionals, from a student studying Linux, PHP or C # to a guru who is preparing for the CCIE. Also there is a material devoted to the study of foreign languages, psychology, self-development, seduction, development of business skills. If you are thinking of taking CCNA, MCSE, RHCE, CISSP, Network +, PMP, IELTS / TOEFL and so on - then you can find everything you need and even more. Under the cat there is a review of the English-language learning resources.
