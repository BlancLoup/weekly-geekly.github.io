Cricket Lee Webinar - (with) the author of books on DNS and Bind. DNS Security: Threats and Solutions, Best Practices


 DNS is one of the most important network services, the security, reliability and performance of which is often forgotten. By the number of attacks, DNS is in second place after HTTP / HTTPS, and only in the last year the number of attacks has increased by 216%.
Cricket Lee - author and co-author of books on DNS and Bind - will conduct a webinar on DNS security. The main threats to the service (DDoS, cache poisoning, tunneling, amplification, etc.) will be considered and protection recommendations will be offered.

The webinar will be held on August 28, 2014. at 20:00 Moscow time.
