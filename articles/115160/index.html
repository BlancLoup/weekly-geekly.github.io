The first meeting of the Club Innovators in Moscow

It all started with the fact that we decided to hold a contest of interesting projects within the framework of a scientific conference. Then the team had two people: the representative of the conference organizer Vladislav Kudinov and Andrei Dodin from the Zvorykinsky project.

The participants liked the idea so much that they pushed us to organize meetings on a regular basis, and then it was decided to create an Innovators Club in St. Petersburg. A little more than a year has passed since then and we have already managed to hold 18 Club meetings, 5 contests and the Innovation Convention.

For each meeting we invite interesting speakers, which is worth at least one incendiary mini lecture by A. A. Shalyto . Experts at meetings give advice on various issues, for example, regular consultations on the protection of intellectual property from Natalia Petrova have become.

Of course, presentations and discussion of projects occupy a central place. We try a variety of formats for them from presentations in an elevator to brainstorms. And during the coffee breaks with delicious cakes, the participants get acquainted, communicate with each other and continue to discuss projects.

Over time, the number of organizers increased, Alexey Pospelov, Mikhail Kudinov, Alexey Petrov, Maxim Nikitinsky and Ivan Smirnov joined us, each contributing to the development of the Club and helping it to become better.

And tomorrow (March 10) an important event for us will take place - the Innovators Club will now be in Moscow!

The first meeting of the Innovators Club of Moscow will be held on March 10 at 18:30.
Venue: Moscow, Research Institute of Goznak, ul. Mytnaya d.19.
Project specialization: information technology, new materials and protective coatings.

During the meeting, an expert jury will select the best projects that will be presented to a wide range of representatives of science and business in the framework of the seminar "Performance and Effectiveness Management" and on the pages of the scientific and practical journal "Effective crisis management" (included in the list of HAC). Also, several projects will be selected for the next investment session.

Meeting plan:
  • Greetings from the organizers, plans for the year.
  • Alexander Katsay (General Director of the Russian Superconductor Corporation LLC) will share his experience in implementing innovative projects.
  • Project block number 1: New materials and protective coatings.
    Presentation by the authors of their projects in the format of a short presentation (5-6 minutes). Answers to questions and expert comments with recommendations for finalizing projects (7-10 minutes).
  • Coffee break and informal communication.
  • Natalya Petrova (Director General of Neva-Patent, LLC Patent and Law Firm) will talk about the peculiarities of protecting intellectual property in IT and provide express advice.
  • Project block number 2: Information technology.
  • A brief summing up, the announcement of the next meeting, entry into the Club of Innovators.

Participation in the Club meeting is free, but by prior arrangement, as the building has a permit system. Do not forget to bring your passport.
