Digg named 10 Digg API contest finalists

The site Digg.com , after the release of the Digg API and the Flash application toolkit , announced a competition for their most creative implementation.

Among a large number of accumulated different works, the jury consisting of Daniel Burka, Jonathan Gay, Shawn Allen, and Kevin Rose selected the 10 most-most. The list of finalists, along with links to their work, can be viewed on the contest page .

From this point on, the jury does not decide anything - now ordinary users vote for the work. Voices (regular Digg'i) will be taken until May 30. Three winners will receive immodest prizes. For the first place will give a powerful gaming computer with a collection of all PC games from EA and Adobe CS3 Master Collection. For the second - Adobe CS3 Web Premium Collection and Palm Treo 680, and for the third - Adobe Flash CS3 Professional.
