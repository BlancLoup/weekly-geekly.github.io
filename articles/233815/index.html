If you compare K Zoom with S4 Zoom as smartphones (and this comparison cannot be avoided), then literally everything is better in the new product - more powerful processor, more RAM, higher screen resolution. The upgrade is really felt - lags and bugs in the work of the device are rare and unobtrusive. Yes, and benchmarks confirm the evolution of the smartphone.







External changes are also noticeable - a removable cover with a la Galaxy S5 cover, no case thickening under the arm, zoom rings and a tripod jack. Taking into account the resizing - the screen has increased by half an inch - the K Zoom looks somewhat sleeker than its predecessor. We do not specifically evaluate the appearance of the novelty, since both form factors are interesting each in their own way. We only note that the K Zoom is a clear step in the direction of the usual, smartphone design.

Of the interesting modes - quick start from the locked state (simultaneously press "camera" and "lower volume"), the ability to focus and metering at different points, shooting Full HD video at 60 frames per second, manual mode - with the ability to adjust sensitivity, shutter speed and aperture. Let's sum up. On the one hand, the Samsung Galaxy K Zoom is a rather interesting device, with a powerful filling and a good photo module - many gadzhetomany are unlikely to be able to pass by this novelty. On the other hand, we would advise people demanding to purchase two devices separately - a smartphone and a digital photo compact.

A long-term photography will put the K Zoom battery in a matter of hours, a small amount of internal memory, a screen made using PenTile technology (by the way, yes!) - not all are frightened by these minuses. But the price - from 21 thousand rubles - can greatly influence the decision to purchase, especially since the S4 Zoom is now really to be found for 10 thousand.

However, it is impossible to call this model a failure - yet there is a change for the better. The paradox, however, is that the ideal is still far away. And, in principle, probably unattainable.
