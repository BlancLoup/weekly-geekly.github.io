Guitar Hero fans tremble! Guitarist and punk legend Steve Jones from the Sex Pistols will play with three of the best European virtual riffs at the Xtival 07 LIVE online festival. The winners of the Game with the Fame competition, who will impress the musician most of all with their ability to burn hearts and turn over the audience with plastic Gibson, will get a chance to show their skills together with him, a person whose music has become a classic of punk rock.

To participate in the competition, just download the demo version of Guitar Hero III on your XBox 360. After practicing the game and achieving the results that you consider worthy of fame for centuries, send a screenshot with them to sexpistols@gamewithfame.net , adding contact information to it and, very importantly, a detailed description of the reasons why Steve should play with you.

Some of the best candidates will receive full versions of GH III, and the winner will be crowned Jones as the European champion and will get a real autographed guitar from him.
