Overview of the enterprise automation market: solutions for construction and management companies in the housing and utilities sector

The 21st century is the time of information technology. Informatization and computerization today affect all spheres of human life, including real estate. Smart home - technology for housing automation, has long been developing around the world.

Smart homes - volume and market dynamics


According to the research of the analytic agency Strategy Analytics, dedicated to the smart home market, the global expenditures for the corresponding equipment, systems and services in 2017 reached $ 84 billion, an increase of 16% compared to 2016, when sales were measured at $ 72 billion.

By the end of 2017, the number of households using smart home solutions worldwide was 164 million, compared with 136 million a year earlier.

Currently, just under 10% of households in the US and just under 3% of households in Europe have smart home systems installed, the number of which should double or triple over the next few years.

In Russia, the automation market is also gaining momentum - products and technologies are emerging almost as fast as in Europe and the USA.

The Russian market of smart home systems in 2012 was estimated at about 4 billion rubles by the IAA "Rustelecom", and by 2016 the volume had more than doubled and amounted to more than 10 billion rubles.

The market of smart homes is developing and increasing its volume every year. But the sphere of automation is now moving in the direction of a much larger scale and suggests a broader functionality than installing sensors in an apartment.

The market is a relatively new and promising IT platform. Our company has already implemented a project in the development of a mobile application for the residential complex, and our customers continue to show interest in this area, so we decided to analyze the automation systems in the real estate market and utilities. A sample of our research includes solutions in one way or another connected with the automation of real estate and the activities of the management company - from projects "Smart House" for individuals to complex software and hardware solutions covering the activities of housing and public utilities and management companies. In total, 40 products with 31 parameters were analyzed, including the software and hardware functions of the systems.

In the first part of the review, we will look at the structure of the Russian automation market according to various criteria and give a general description of the solutions.

Real Estate Automation Market in Russia


At the moment, the market is filled with a variety of solutions that can be ranked by the following levels of automation:



Let's take a closer look at each level of automation:

  • Individual - automation at this level involves a simple mobile application for a resident with the functions of payment for utility services, informing about events, news of housing and communal services, communication and voting of residents, etc.
  • Apartment - installation of sensors, controllers inside the apartment with the ability to run automatic scenarios in the room, security control inside the apartment. - automated accounting and document management for the Criminal Code.
  • Management Companies (MC) - Software solutions to simplify / improve the activities of the MC, interact with residents, information disclosure, etc.
  • Home - the introduction of engineering automation in the structure of the house - scheduling, installation of sensors, actuators, controllers at the house level, ACS elements. Equipment enabling remote monitoring of systems at home and promptly responding to emergency situations.
  • Residential complex (LCD) - includes 2 previous levels of automation. It assumes the availability of tools for management and monitoring of all houses in the residential complex for the Criminal Code, accounting, document management. Integrated software and hardware solution.
