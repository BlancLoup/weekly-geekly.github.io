RIM announces five new smartphones running the BB 7 OS

It was clear to everyone that RIM was working on a series of new smartphones, and everyone was waiting for their official announcement, which took place today. Just a few hours ago, RIM introduced as many as five new smartphones running the BlackBerry 7 OS operating system.



Among the new products: BlackBerry Bold 9900, BlackBerry Bold 9930, BlackBerry Torch 9810, BlackBerry Torch 9850 and BlackBerry Torch 9860.

In the sale of devices will go in the coming months, and as for prices, RIM is not in a hurry to disclose them. Below you can peep the characteristics of fresh smartphones.

All of the following smartphones are equipped with a 1.2 GHz processor and 768 MB of RAM .

BlackBerry Bold 9900 and 9930





The BlackBerry Bold 9900 series is the thinnest BlackBerry smartphones (only 10.5 mm), offering a perfect union of excellent keyboards and a touchscreen integrated into the iconic design of the BlackBerry Bold. Also, the new BlackBerry Bold 9900 and 9930 are the first smartphones that support NFC technology (Near Field Communications). Outstanding for its design, incredibly efficient keyboard and beautiful touchscreen display, the BlackBerry Bold 9900 series of phones is made from high-quality materials - including a durable case made of stainless steel with a fine polishing texture, with one touch you understand - you have a great smartphone in front of you .
