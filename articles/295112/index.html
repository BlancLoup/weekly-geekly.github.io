Three significant "moment" from the life of the company Twitter over the past five days

Twitter finally launched the service "Moments", which was announced back in June. It allows you to track the most popular events in the social network. To date, the service is available only for residents of the United States. The Moments tab has appeared in a mobile app and on a social networking site.

‚ÄúEvery day people post hundreds of millions of tweets. Among them, there are messages that can only be found on Twitter: dialogues with world leaders and celebrities, news about events received from eyewitnesses in real time, Internet memes, relevant comments about night games and much more. We know that it is difficult to keep track of them, especially for those who are not a follower of well-known accounts. But this should not be so, ‚Äùresults the translation of the company's Slon publication.

Users will be able to get acquainted with the latest collections, regardless of who they are following on Twitter. To do this, go to the "Moments" tab by clicking on a special icon with a lightning. A list of current events will appear here, which will be regularly updated as new topics appear on Twitter. Scrolling through the list to the right or left, you can see the most interesting topics in recent days.

Shaping "Moments" will be a special Twitter team with the participation of partners such as Bleacher Report , Buzzfeed , Entertainment Weekly , Fox News , Getty Images , Mashable , MLB , New York Times , Vogue and Washington Post .

The announcement of the launch of the service appeared on October 6, a day after the company's founder, Jack Dorsey, was approved as a permanent CEO.
