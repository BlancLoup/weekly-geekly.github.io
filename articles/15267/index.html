See invisible

Thanks to the new technology kameraflageTM, with the help of a conventional digital camera, you can see what is not possible to see the human eye. Cameras capture a greater spectrum of light, for example, they see more colors, which allows digital photos to be taken to a new level.




In a special way, the applied text or image can be seen through the screen of your digital camera, but they remain invisible to the naked eye. Invisible messages can be placed both on clothes and on billboards, plain paper and even in video clips.

So, the technology kameraflageTM encodes a layer of information that can only be perceived through a digital camera lens.

In order to see the invisible, no software is required, it is enough to have a digital camera (it does not matter in a separate or in your mobile phone).

The possibilities of using this technology are endless. Starting from design moves and ending with a tool for copyright protection. For example, this technology can be widely used in cinemas. In this way, you can show subtitles or any other information only to those who want to see it, and at the same time it will not interfere with the rest.

KameraflageTM opens up new frontiers for the advertising business. For example, in places where it is simply not ethical to openly advertise (museums, places of historical value), it will be possible to use the form of information that is hidden to the eye.

New opportunities for fun. For example, create a game to search for secret messages in the city, using your mobile with a camera. Such games are already popular all over the world, and with this technology they will make them even more exciting.

For designers, this is a new way to create unique clothes and accessories. KameraflageTM can also be a salvation for people who are forced to wear a uniform, but who have the need for self-expression.
