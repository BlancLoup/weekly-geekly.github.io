Dear readers and writers of Habr!

Before answering the questions about the grant, I decided to make an introduction again to rely on a very important foundation.

Let's understand the objectives of the state in the field of IT sector of the economy. I think that the correct goal sounds like this: The IT sector is considered as one of the 5 extremely important sectors of the economy, requiring accelerated and even advanced development. A specific task on this background is the accelerated creation of new and development of existing domestic IT companies competitive in the global market .

If someone disputes this statement, then it is better to contact the Public Chamber and the Commission for Modernization under the President of the Russian Federation and argue with them. Argue, object, suggest other industries. This is definitely not for us and not for this discussion.

If there is agreement on the above issue, then let's think about what to actually do to accelerate the development of existing and the creation of new IT companies? How to support? What to stimulate? If supporters of the free market come here now who say that there is nothing better than a market for the development of competitive IT companies, then I, excuse me, disagree. Because the free market today has a significant feature. It is open to giant international companies that are hundreds of times more than the most successful Russian IT companies. Free competition between an elephant and a mouse is slyness. And, more precisely, it is a struggle to maintain the current status quo. Someone wants to fight for it?

Total - for the support and incentives need non-market measures and preferences. This means that expenditures from the budget or extra-budgetary funds are coming. Therefore, FOR ANY expenses will be, and someone will receive some funds from the state. If someone cannot live with this, and only requires market ‚Äúsupport‚Äù without spending from the budget, then he needs to find peace with himself. For there is no support without expenses. Therefore - either support and stimulation (with expenses), or the market.

It is also completely clear that , provided that the support target is chosen correctly , the larger and ‚Äúnon-market‚Äù support measures will be, the more significant the result will be. Give a little bit - good too. Somewhere someone will be a little better. Give more - it is reasonable to expect greater returns. We do not forget for a minute about the purpose of the event - the creation of a new or development of an existing IT business. That is, when choosing support measures, we, of course, have to carefully analyze where the funds go, how they are used, what and where they are spent, what are the prospects for returns from such support.

That is, in fact, the key issue is not the degree of non-market, but the correct choice of incentive objects and a set of reasonable conditions imposed on the incentive object.

If you think that among the 10 companies that have already received grants in Skolkovo, there are unworthy ones, then argue and offer Skolkovo experts more worthy.

If, in fact, someone did not want to stimulate anyone, then why now discuss a specific grant of a particular company - it is necessary to discuss the inexpediency of non-market measures in principle.
