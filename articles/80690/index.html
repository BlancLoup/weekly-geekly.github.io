Algo Glide - the mouse of a new generation

The Algo Glide mouse, first introduced at CES, uses an innovative control method that provides amazing ease of use and helps to avoid injuries caused by constant strain when working at a computer. The tiny manipulator needs to be moved with only two fingers, which prevents the brush from overstressing. Two scroll wheels located in the bottom corners of the gadget, along with unusual concave buttons make the device incredibly ergonomic. The device is wired, but the wire and USB-connector is easily hidden in the case, which provides an amazing compactness. Algo Glide is compatible with Mac and PC and comes in white, black, pink, blue and red for about $ 30.








As you can see, the device is universal - suitable for both right-handers and left-handers, which is good news :) When I come, we will definitely buy it.
