Report from the #PostgreSQLRussia mitap on November 3



On November 3, a meeting of the #PostgreSQLRussia community took place in our Moscow office. This time the participants discussed the nuances of switching to PostgreSQL from other DBMS. In Russia, for several years now there has been a tendency to abandon proprietary DBMS. Some large companies have already migrated or are in the process of migration to PostgreSQL. Their experience is interesting not only from the point of view of the migration process itself. Large projects that have switched to Postgres can share a unique new experience, which is certainly useful for those who have been using Postgres for a long time. See our report from the conference under the cut.

Pavel Luzanov, Postgres Professional

"PostgreSQL for Oracle users"



The report will be of interest to Oracle users who want to meet and start working with PostgreSQL. Both DBMSs are well compatible with the ANSI SQL standard. That is why Oracle users will not be so hard to learn how to work with PostgreSQL. The report explores some of the features of PostgreSQL that will make the dating process even easier.
