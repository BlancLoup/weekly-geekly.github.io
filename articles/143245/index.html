Light forces meeting, May 18-20

Very soon, from May 18 to May 20, a conference of the Warriors of Light will be held in the capital's Cosmos Hotel. It will examine ways of dealing with graphic bottlenecks, vampirism of applications sucking forces from ultrabook batteries, the GPA magic amulet and other tools of the Light.

The International Association of the Light, known as INTernational Exalters of the Light (Intel), traditionally acts as a technological sponsor of the conference, and the greatest magicians of the company will present as many as 7 reports , and in the demo zone you can see the powerful artifacts created by them.

People will consider that the next Game Developer Conference is taking place.

Read further, looking through the Twilight

Immersion in the depths of the black abyss with Intel GPA


This report will be devoted to new features of the package Intel Graphics Performance Analyzers 2012 , including the optimization of games for Android x86. Director General of Biart studio, Konstantin Popov, together with Intel engineers will demonstrate the abysses of graphic bottlenecks using the example of Deep Black.

Application Optimization with Intel Parallel Studio XE 2011


Advanced compilers, tools for checking errors and improving code reliability, profiling - the whole complex of Parallel Studio XE tools will be considered to optimize application performance.

Optimization of games for Ultrabook


Three reports will be presented in this category:
  • Ultra-touch technology
  • Features of integrated Intel graphics cards used in ultrabooks, and their effective use when creating gaming applications
  • Code optimization methods that allow for maximum energy savings on mobile platforms, as well as tools for assessing the energy efficiency of applications

Development of cross-platform Android applications


Students will learn how to pack several platform-specific files in one .APK, learn about some of the features that distinguish x86 from ARM, learn how to use SIMD extensions and optimal compiler flags for different ABIs.

OpenCL for Intel platform


OpenCL implementation for latest generation processors, efficient automatic vectorization, automatic multithreading, efficient heterogeneous computing and performance analysis tools.

Demo area


In the demo zone will be presented
  • Ultrabooks
  • Tablets and smartphones on Intel Atom
  • Systems with Ivy Bridge processors
