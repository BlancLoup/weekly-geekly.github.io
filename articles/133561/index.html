VoytPaint - another drawing tool for Android

One day I needed an application on Android, in which you can draw something, after a long search on the market found ScetchBook, and everything ... everything else that came out in the search results was awful: a terrible interface, brakes applies), glitches, drawing with a brush and so on. Since I already studied programming under Android, I decided to think over everything I needed and arrange it as an application.

And that's what happened:

Voytpaint is an application where you can draw !!!
(all other names with the word paint and draw are already taken, so I had to attach a part of the last name to it)


Of course, not everything that I wanted turned out, but what is the functionality now:

brushes:
- ordinary brush
- balloon
-eraser
- fill (solid or gradient)
-line (straight and bezier)
-figures (circle, rectangle, rectangle with rounded edges)
-text


color selection:
-a circle
-pipette
-RGB


rest:
- The maximum size of the picture is 1280 * 1280 (all due to the limited amount of memory in the android)
- you can open a photo from the archive of the device
- undo / redo function
- multitouch zoom




monetization (well, do not disappear as good =)):
Two options - a paid version ($ 1.99) and free.
Free is different from the paid presence of advertising, cancel \ repeat only three times, you can not open the picture from the device and the maximum size of the picture 480 * 480.
