Yahoo expands collaboration with newspapers

As part of expanding cooperation with the print press, Yahoo recently signed advertising contracts with 264 newspapers published in 44 states.

Newspapers will be able to place advertisements on Yahoo sites. In addition, there will be published and journalistic materials on events in the world, sports and finance.

Last year, the number of "paper" Yahoo partners was 176.
