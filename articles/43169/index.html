Age of Heroes Online is a mobile MMORPG + turn-based strategy game.


About a year ago, the first rumors appeared in the network that Megafon decided to create an on-line game for mobile phones. The tender for the creation of the game then won the company "Qplaze". The development of the game began ... Then beta testing came, for which only subscribers of the same "Megaphone" were admitted. And finally, quite recently, the game was opened for other mobile operators. Although the game is still in the test state, it is quite playable. So - Age of Heroes Online .

The official website of the game - http://aoho.qplaze.com

Age of Heroes is a single and carefully developed world, a countless number of big and small stories, a place of exploits, explorations and adventures for thousands of players. The Age of Heroes has no limits in time or in space - join us, and you will see for yourself!

At your service:

* Three game races, each with a unique set of properties and abilities;
* Nine game classes offering completely different approaches to combat and world exploration;
* More than a hundred creatures, warriors and monsters, most of which will be able to join your army and get under your banners;
* Hundreds of skills and abilities of heroes, amenable to development;
* Powerful magic and special abilities of heroes, offering new tactical capabilities in battle;
* Dozens of unique and rare artifacts, and many ordinary armor and weapons sold in stores;
* Unique adventures for representatives of each of the races, a lot of tasks, missions and quests;
* An open and ever-expanding world, a global geographic map with an ever-growing number of accessible locations;
* And finally, many players to explore the world, battles and tournaments!

Also, stay tuned for updates. Soon you are waiting for:

* Dozens of new locations, hundreds of new items and quests;
* Raising the maximum level of the hero to 30;
* And in the near future - two new game races - Gnomes and Demonids - and six new heroes.

If we compare the game with its counterparts - tibia and warspear online, the list of supported phone models is immediately apparent. Since Age of Heroes is a java midlet, anything that cannot be said about warspear can play. Tibia also has customers for any device, but it is already morally obsolete :)

PVP system is more developed in AOHO, because In tibia, for PVP, separate arenas are set aside, and in warspear online, the one who has the Internet faster and more stable drives.

The system of battles in the game - like all the famous "Heroes".
