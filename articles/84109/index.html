EBay sells Anders Hejlsberg's first laptop



An extremely interesting lot appeared on eBay: the first laptop of Anders Hejlsberg ( wiki ). Apparently this laptop is over 20 years old; in the screenshots of the screen, the latest date is 88 years.

The laptop works under PC-DOS 3.02 and contains the built-in Turbo Pascal from Borland. Anders himself, I remind you, is famous for the fact that he wrote and sold to the company Borland the Pascal compiler, and then already in the company he created Delphi. Much later, Anders went to Microsoft, where he created the most popular C # language and still works as lead architect in a team that develops C #.

Such is the artifact on eBay, for which at the time of this writing, they give $ 1026 .
