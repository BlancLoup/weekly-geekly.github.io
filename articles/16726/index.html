Chaos Constructions HackAround 2007

<img src = " habrastorage.org/getpro/habr/post_images/c1d/247/51a/c1d24751a04c6efa06c55600c76de6c6.gif " align = "left" / hspace = "10" vspace = "10"> From November 29 to December 2 (inclusive ) in St. Petersburg, on the second floor of the Manezh Central Exhibition Hall, in the framework of the HiT 2007 exhibition, Chaos Constructions Hack Around 2007 will be held - a festival that brings together highly qualified and very curious specialists who are capable of non-trivial but constructive solutions, in common language called hackers .

During the festival, seminars , contests in real time, video screenings (works sent to the festival, thematic collections and the best works from the archive of the festival) are held. Voting is done by processing profiles filled out by visitors. According to the results of the competition is awarded.

The festival is thematically dedicated to:
  • Aspects of information security;
  • Operating systems and open source software;
  • Nontrivial software and hardware and network solutions;
  • Mnemotechnology and problem solving methodology (LifeHack).


Address of the festival: Manezh Central Exhibition Hall, St. Petersburg, Isaac Square, 1. Metro Sadovaya or Nevsky Prospect). Map From the Moscow railway station (metro station Ploschad Vosstaniya / Mayakovskaya) to the Manezh Central Exhibition Hall can be reached by trolleybus number 5, 22, bus number 3, 22, 27, route taxi number 180, 190.

Duration: from 11 to 18 hours.

The cost of visiting all four days of the festival: 200 rubles.
