$ 5 million will be spent on amateur video financing


It seems that Google has taken a cautious step towards the business of producing original content. The search giant has organized a fund in the amount of $ 5 million, which will be used for financial assistance to the authors of the most original videos on their YouTube service. The company will offer more than 100 popular authors to submit their work in order to win a grant.

YouTube invites both individuals and small companies to join them and upload their own production videos on a regular basis. Mostly in video blog formats, fun news, or short comedies. The company evaluates offers from users based on the audience, taking into account plans for promotion and distribution, and other parameters.
