After thousands and thousands of user complaints dissatisfied with the situation, Apple gave an official response , according to which the problem is a signal indicator failure, I mean in the device software. The company even promised to release an iPhone software update that fixes this problem. At the same time, a number of experts conducted an audit, and the decision is contrary to the approval of Apple. Experts believe that the problem is hardware. Another specialist, Tony Sakkonagi, and Bernstein Research, arrived at the same time, who had calculated how much such a review would cost the corporation.
