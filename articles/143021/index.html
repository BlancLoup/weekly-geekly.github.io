Steve Wozniak greatly praises Windows Phone 7.5 Mango

Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak said a few nice words about the Windows Phone interface. True, these words were spoken not at an official event, but in an informal setting at the Togo diner in Mountain View, where the local journalists accidentally met the legendary Woz. They were not taken aback and did a mini-interview, and under the recording .

Answering a question from journalists, Woz got out of his two pockets his Apple iPhone (both 4S), one Motorola DROID RAZR and, to everyone's surprise, the Nokia Lumia 900 smartphone on the Windows 7.5 Mango platform.

Steve Wozniak noted the "good, fast and reliable" operation of the smartphone, as well as the interface. In general, Steve described the merits of Windows 7.5 Mango so emphatically, as if he were working for Microsoft, and not the founder of Apple.
