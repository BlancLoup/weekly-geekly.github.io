Facebook turned 10 years old



Exactly 10 years ago, a certain Mark Zuckerberg created the social network Facebook, which quickly became popular. Now Facebook is at the peak of its development, in any case, the company's shares are sold at the maximum price since the IPO in 2012.

Since the formation of the social network, as one of the most popular resources in the world, it has been buried more than once. Someone predicted him a quick death due to the small number of media services, someone - because of the transition of young people to modern socials, like Instagram. However, Facebook is still alive and well.

The audience of this social network now has 1 billion users, and sociologists estimate that the number of "friendly connections" on the resource is about 125 billion. In general, everyone is connected to everyone.

At present, everything is fine with Facebook, and its founder, Mark Zuckerberg, has become the youngest billionaire in the world. Now Mark is the permanent leader of Facebook, as well as the ideologue of this social network.

No matter how one of us treats Facebook, yet this resource is worthy of being congratulated on its anniversary, and even ten years old :)
