For the words answer. Droider Hunter Authors Contest

Hello!

We at Droider are confident that there are great writers among our readers, and we decided to identify those through the Droider Hunter contest, the main prize of which is the compact Bliss Pad C7.2s tablet depicted in this picture:



To participate you need to write an article on a given topic and send it to us by mail. All adequate posts we post on the site. In addition, the authors of the best materials will receive encouraging prizes (more about them later) and an offer to participate in the Droider.ru project ! If this interested you, welcome to the sequel for details.

So, the competition will last two weeks: from November 18 (that is, today) to December 2. Requirements for materials:
  • The topic is quite broad: a compilation or comparison of anything related to mobile technologies and gadgets (not necessarily from Android) . These can be applications from a category, operator rates, similar devices, operating systems, or an analytical study - in short, unleash your imagination.
  • Literacy should be on top.
  • Text size : at least 3-4 paragraphs, but a huge canvas is also not needed. Try to stay within 4,000 characters .
  • The title should reflect the essence of the article and be sonorous.
  • Pictures - do not forget to supply them with your post.
  • The file format to send is arbitrary.
Now once again about the prizes. The winners are chosen by the editors: the first place winner is awarded with the Bliss Pad C7.2s tablet , the three following participants will receive a waterproof Waver Tim case (for a smartphone) or Waver Max (for a tablet) to choose from - by the way, an indispensable thing for a gadzhetoman during a shower or bath =)

We are waiting for your articles by December 2 (inclusive) to roy@droider.ru mail (in the subject line please indicate Droider Hunter ). Good luck!
