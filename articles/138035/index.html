The purchase of Motorola Mobility really allows the corporation to do much of what was previously unattainable for this giant. Previously, the devices that were created by the corporation were created in partnership with many manufacturing companies, but now the company is able to cope with all this work itself.

According to The Wall Street Journal, Google is now developing a home entertainment media system that is capable of streaming music and video to any home device. The development is a separate team that does not belong to any of the large branches of the corporation. The system, according to experts, will be somewhat similar to Sonos, but with the ability to work with video. Perhaps the new media system is a direct competitor to Apple's AirPlay.

Now, as before, most of the corporate revenue comes from advertisers, but Google is actively trying to develop other areas of work that allow differentiation of income. The current management of the company, represented by Larry Page, is going to change a lot in the company's work scheme, and make the company more rational to external changes.

Until now, Google has not been a competitor to manufacturers of various kinds of mobile devices, but now we can expect the corporation to enter a relatively new market for itself. And if all goes well, the entry will be fast enough and successful. But it is clear that everything here depends on the activity of the company's management.
