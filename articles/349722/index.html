How we built VDI with 14,000 jobs for the VTB Group

Let's say right away: for us this project is special. VDI implementations for 50, 250 and even 2500 users have already been to our practice. But everything changes when it comes to installations with more than ten thousand jobs. The service automatically becomes business critical, and availability requirements are coming to the fore.




2 years of no-alliance collaboration with the customer, hundreds of approvals, mountains of technical documentation, lots of testing on a large number of diverse application software and peripheral equipment, a huge amount of pitfalls and gray hair ...
But the result is no less ambitious: VDI allowed to upgrade and integrate the IT infrastructures of three banks at once - VTB, VTB24 and Bank of Moscow. And now we, perhaps, any VDI on the shoulder. Details - in the blog VTB.
