These balls are sold in garden stores and are used as soil moisturizers for indoor plants. It is, in fact, the same sodium polyacrylate from diapers (see previous experience). Salty ice



The temperature of the ice can be from 0 degrees (Celsius) and below, at 0 degrees the ice begins to melt. When we add salt, we lower the freezing point, and the ice begins to melt. The thread at this moment is immersed in the formed water. But the more water is formed, the lower the salt concentration and the higher the freezing temperature. Gradually, the thread is frozen into a piece of ice.

It is to reduce the freezing point of water that roads are sprinkled with table salt or special reagents in winter.
