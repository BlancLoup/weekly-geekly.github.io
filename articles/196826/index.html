Lego Specifications Game in Ciklum Odessa, October 16

On October 16, the Odessa office of Ciklum invites everyone to participate in the exciting and educational event of the Lego Specifications Game , which will be held by the IT guru of Odessa - Dmitry Mindra .
ABOUT TRAINING:
From time to time we are confronted with such a situation: the customer sends us a short, but confusing specification that it is completely unclear what we need to get in the end. Or even better, he begins to insist on a particular solution that is illogical and does not fit into the current product.

Why does the customer do this? At our workshop we will learn, first, to disperse the fog of intricate specifications, and second, to help the customer find the lost focus and restore the scope, and, of course, we will use Lego to simulate the development process. Also, we will consider a system for analyzing requirements and you can take a laptop with you, it will come in handy.


ABOUT TRAINER:
For 10 years of software development, Dmitry has been in different projects and different roles. He has gone from a novice programmer to a team leader and a project manager. He speaks at meetings in Odessa. Net User Group, conferences AgileBaseCamp, IT Jam, ITBrunch, XP Days, Agile Eastern Europe, NetWork, Microsoft SWIT. Today, Dmitry is the leading developer of Unity Technologies - the leader in the development of gaming platforms around the world.
