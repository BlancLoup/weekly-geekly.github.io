"Gosznak" announced a tender for the creation of cloud infrastructure

A tender from the Gosznak to perform work on the creation of the first phase of the cloud computing infrastructure appeared on the state procurement website. In this case, the maximum amount of the contract according to the plan should be 2.89 billion rubles, writes "Composition". The winner of the competition undertakes to provide the customer with 80 units of equipment, including servers from Windows, IBM, Cisco and Lenovo, disk arrays and other systems.

All this equipment is required by the "Gosznak" to host its own portal solutions and information systems. In addition, the supplier must provide the necessary documentation for antivirus software, technical support of equipment and other documents. The contractor will have to independently develop technical and operational documentation for the supplied equipment.

Of the 2.89 billion rubles, as estimated by Gosznak specialists, more than 2.7 billion rubles will be spent on the equipment itself, 142 million rubles will be spent on work and services, and 50 million rubles will be spent on the right to use the software.

Applications are accepted until August 20, the results of the competition are scheduled for September 21.
