How can a freelancer accept payment from foreign customers?

When deciding whether to accept payment from a foreign customer, a freelancer needs at a minimum: choose a payment method that will be available to both parties, select a service with an affordable commission and optimal withdrawal conditions. In the case of the method of payment - the most affordable is payment by card. Below are a few nuances when accepting payment cards from customers from abroad in Ukraine, as well as our payment solution for freelancers.




Conversion
According to the legislation of Ukraine, payment is possible only in the national currency. Therefore, when transferring funds from a foreign card, you must take into account the rate at which the conversion takes place. Usually, the conversion when writing off funds occurs at the rate of payment systems. The course is dynamic and may change several times a day.
