In fact, everything is quite simple and ordinary. You are a god. Well, or something like that. You create your own world by joining the neighboring lands. This is what your world looks like:



A large piece of land below is your world. Your goal is to capture and join separate small worlds in order to further create a unified empire. The sweet face of the devil is your humble servant who will tell and tell you everything.

The capture of the world is as follows:

You move into a hero (mage, warrior, archer, commander), you are given a castle and, in the best traditions of step-by-step strategies, you need to capture the surrounding lands, develop the castle and improve the infrastructure in your territory.
