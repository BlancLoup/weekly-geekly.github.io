Imagine Cup 2011 as it was ...

On Saturday, April 16, the Russian final of the Microsoft Imagine Cup 2011 took place. How did the competition go, what was there and what was it, that sarcastically grinning face ... and sobsno general hto it !? All under the cut. Traffic, a lot of photos.


I'll start from far away. Beyond the competition in the lobby was interesting and not boring ...

Beautiful girls played bowling on XBox 360 and Kinekt.


Beautiful guys virtually fought with real friends


Other girls lured to work in the name and for the benefit of Microsoft


Could heartily talk with a poster for a cup of coffee


Everyone was allowed to drive phones on Windows Phone 7


They also told how to quickly concoct an application for a new mobile OS.

In the meantime, teams presented their projects on stage.


social service mutual aid "Dobryashka" from the team Revolution Engine (Volgograd State Technical University)


The origami folding service was represented by the Oricrafter team (Moscow Aviation Institute)


Project of One Hero Mobile Chronometer for Accounting and Organization of Personal Time (Ukhta State Technical University)


Intellectual trash cans Clean City from the Progress team (Saratov State Technical University)


Crystal Ball online activity analyzer represented by Magic Team (Nizhny Novgorod State University)


The SafeFood team (South Ural State University) presented a good quality analyzer for products, but became famous for its logo, which did not fit on the screen and was very successfully clipped to Safe Foo.


The embedded systems contest opened Sunflowers from the Volgograd State Technical University with the project to manage electricity trading.


Although in my opinion they want to take over the world, at least ...


The Calvus team presented a complex for the cultivation and breeding of Ascolot .


And even gave him to stroke the members of the jury!


Smart robo-machines with Kinekt vision from the Finder team (Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology)


The electronic nose project surprised everyone with the be healthy team from Saratov State Technical University.

At this point, the performance of the teams ended and everyone came to rest in the lobby where they could vote for the team they liked.
