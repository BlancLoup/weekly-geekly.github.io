Will powerset google google?

I myself have not yet seen the presentation of the Powerset search engine, but many have already said that after what they see, they will never again use Google.

The goal of Powerset is to give people the opportunity to use familiar phrases when searching, including those words that search engines now ignore. Like many others, I had to learn how to change my traditional language when dealing with search sites. I used only keywords, discarding everything else. In part, this habit has become the norm for me even in communicating with people, to such an extent that negotiations in the messenger or via e-mail sometimes seemed clogged with some unnecessary, meaningless words!

Despite the fact that I do not consider the current search engines perfect, I still did not think about the problem that they do not understand normal human language. Frankly, I would prefer to develop this ability - to communicate in a way that computers understand us, as this will help progress in other high-tech areas.

But Pell gives a convincing argument: a search using a normal language is important from the point of view of understanding meaning.
