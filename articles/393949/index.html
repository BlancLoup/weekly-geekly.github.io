Museum BZHRK "Molodets" with three ballistic missiles

In the light of the new geopolitical situation, the restoration of nuclear weapons becomes relevant again, especially since the START-3 Treaty does not prohibit this.

BZHRK new generation surpasses its predecessor in everything. One Barguzin train will be able to carry six intercontinental ballistic missiles developed on the basis of the Yars ICBM (the Bulava land version) and will be equated with a regiment. The divisional kit will include five regiments.

All Barguzin cars will be sealed and durable, withstanding the explosion of a nuclear warhead a few hundred meters from the train. The autonomy of the train is a month. During the day Barguzin will be able to travel up to 1000 km

The Yars missiles are brought into a combat position within a few minutes. The firing range is 10 000 km, the accuracy of the hit is 100 m.

In December 2015, a source in the Russian defense industry complex reported that, due to the difficult financial situation, the deadlines for the creation of the Barguzin were postponed for more than one year and would end no earlier than 2020. The rocket forces expect that the new complexes will remain in service at least until 2040.
