IBM z / Master contest results

From mid-October to late January, IBM, together with universities and educational organizations in the z / Universities program, held a z / Master competition dedicated to programming for IBM mainframes and working with the DB2 database system - we want to talk about how this useful event went and who, as a result, turned out to be the winner of the IBM competition, at the same time having gained valuable experience that is not so easy to find.

Over the 5 years that z / Master has been doing, tens of thousands of students from different countries around the world, from North and South America, to Europe and Asia, have participated in it. Our students received the first opportunity to take part in z / Master two years ago, and today both Ukrainian and Kazakh students can try their hand at IBM's free educational courses.

This year, all three prizes were taken by students from Russia, moreover, from one university, this is MSTU. N.E. Bauman: Alexey Mayants, Denis Sorokin and Viktor Smirnov. Besides them, there is one student from Ukraine (Sevastopol National Technical University) - Dmitry Kuzmenko, who was also noted by the z / Master jury. The entire competition, stretching over several months, took place in three rounds, and all tasks were performed on real equipment in the remote access mode.
