Twitter founder and owner of ebay arrived in Russia

Yesterday, having turned on the channel to news.ru before going to bed - I found an interesting news - it turns out that the first persons of the Internet came to Moscow. And we do not know anything.

Today, February 18, a large press conference will be held with the participation of members of the American delegation, who came to Russia to exchange experience in the field of technology.

The purpose of the visit is the exchange of experience in the fight against corruption using information technologies. One of the main issues of the conference is the organization of cooperation of mobile operators and other communication companies with law enforcement agencies in the investigation of such crimes. Also a discussion of "e-government", distance education.
Meetings with politicians and scientists in Moscow and Novosibirsk will take place (this Siberian city was chosen because the largest Russian scientific centers and the newest technopark were located in the local Academgorodok).

The conference includes

* John Donahoe, President and CEO of eBay
* Jack Dorsey, Founder and Chairman of the Board of Twitter, Executive Director of Square
* Jason Liebman, CEO and Chairman of Howcast
* Padmasri Warrior, Technology Director, Cisco Systems
* Esther Dyson, Chairman of the Board at EDventure
* Shervin Pishevar, Executive Director, Social Gaming Network (SGN)
* Mitchell Baker, Chairman of the Board of Mozilla
* Ellis Rubinstein (Ellis Rubinstein), President and Executive Director of the New York Academy of Sciences
* Ashton Kutcher, (Ashton Kutcher), actor, producer, executive director of Katalyst, founder of The Demi and Ashton Foundation.
