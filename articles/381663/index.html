Google Maps now has the opportunity to send the destination address from a PC to a phone

In the new version of Google Maps 9.11.0, released at the beginning of July, it became possible to send the found destination address to an Android device.



To use the function, it is necessary, as before, to find the object of interest. In the information window, under the address and working time, select the Share to device item, where the list of your devices will be displayed. Select a device. A notification will appear on the device with a choice of actions: Navigation (car navigator mode) or Directions.


While the function is not available in the Russian version of the interface.

Russian interface is not updated
