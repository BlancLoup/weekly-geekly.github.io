Following the principle of simple ideas, and the advice of minimizing the functional (MVP) and bootstrapping , Eventez appeared .

The idea is simple: people are interested in events (events) that occur around them .



Events are concerts, exhibitions, lectures, seminars, trainings , promotions, discounts, children's matinees, collective bike rides, birthday celebrations of friends and everything else ... The main sign of the event is that they tend to start and end in the foreseeable period of time. To know that there is a concert hall and a pub nearby is certainly useful ... but information about what will happen there is even more interesting. The main difference between Eventez events and points in Foursquare and AlterGeo is that the events self-destruct and do not litter the air (like, for example, 4sq-points ‚ÄúCork on the Enthusiasts Highway ‚Äù, ‚ÄúKolyan wants a beer‚Äù and ‚ÄúSession with Lily‚Äù).

  • We do geo-referencing (via Google, at).

  • Display the list on the map.
