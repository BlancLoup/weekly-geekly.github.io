Epson RIPS - print without cartridges for a large office

In a post in his blog on Habr√© " Epson WorkForce Pro - even more profitable office printing " we promised to talk about the latest line of Epson devices for business printing - Epson WorkForce Pro RIPS . With the release of this series of devices, Epson enters a completely new market segment - a segment of large print volumes. About them and tell in this post.

Quickly learn about the devices and their main features - using very capacious containers with ink instead of cartridges - you can learn from the video presentation:



What is special?


As mentioned above, the main feature of the RIPS (Replaceable Ink Pack System) series devices is that instead of cartridges they use high-capacity ink containers. Very, very high capacity. Up to 75,000 color prints can be made from the ink starter kit! These are 150 packs of office paper or almost a year of printing without refueling with a load of 6,000 pages per month.



Epson engineers were the first to offer solutions for large print volumes based on inkjet technology without using cartridges (first, in the form of Epson Print Factory devices). There are simply no analogues to our RIPS in the market at the moment.

Pigment ink. Therefore, the prints are not smeared, and the printer comes out completely dry - you can immediately make notes with a marker, for example.



That's not all. Since The printer is based on Epson PrecisionCore inkjet printing technology; there is no need to change any other resource parts, except ink containers and waste ink tanks.



If we estimate that from one refill you can print 50 - 75 thousand pages (depending on the model of the device), for all this time to change in our devices you only need the capacity for waste ink. At the same time, on the laser device for the period required to print such a large number of sheets, several times it will be necessary to replace the toner cartridges, the drum unit, and the stove, and other various rollers of the image transfer, paper feeding, etc. Well, and the capacity for waste toner, of course.

What can?


The functionality of the device is quite wide. Duplex printing, copying, scanning of documents (including using the automatic feeder with a two-sided scanner), network connection, Wi-Fi, Wi-Fi Direct function, printing from mobile devices, confidential printing and multi- much more ...



How hardy and fast?


The devices are designed for quite high loads - up to 45,000 pages per month on A4 devices and up to 65,000 pages on A3 devices. This is exceptionally high performance for inkjet technology.

Print speed is quite high - up to 34 ppm. It is important that, unlike laser printers, RIPS series devices do not spend a lot of time preparing for printing (heating up the stove, for example, in the case of a laser). The result is no more than 7 seconds of the time of the first sheet.

And now what?



Now, before buying a printing device designed for printing large volumes of documents (to the office, for example), remember that on laser printers alone the light did not come together. Now, thanks to Epson, you can not only get a color device for printing documents in A4 and A3 format, but also save a lot of money (both time and nerves).

Devices are already available on our website. If you have a desire to learn more about them, then welcome to Epson.ru: www.epson.ru/catalog/workforce-rips.
