New Apple iPod and MacBook - already in September?

Piper Jaffray analysts believe that in September this year, Apple will hold a special press event, during which the announcement of a new range of iPods and MacBook laptops will take place.

Over the past three years, the company has used the September meeting to announce new iPod models. Analyst Gene Munster (Gene Munster) believes that the exception will not happen this year. According to him, Apple will refresh the iPod line, once again, by reviewing the prices and device capacities. In particular, he expects to increase the memory of iPod shuffle and iPod nano. iPod touch is likely to tweak slightly and start selling for $ 199.



Munster also believes that at the same time with the players, Apple will update their laptops. A company must submit updated MacBook and MacBook Pro designs. Probably, new computers will also support multi-touch technology. After 2-3 years, the company can show a fully touchable MacBook.
