Listen trackers

You, probably, faced or already use such services as tvfedor and TorrentMonitor .
And here is a new service for tracking torrent trackers.

Here is a modest list of supported trackers:
- free-torrents.org
- hdclub.org
- kinozal.tv
- lostfilm.tv
- nnm-club.ru
- novafilm.tv
- rustorka.com
- rutor.org
- rutracker.org
- tapochek.net
- tfile.me
- torrents.net.ua
- tr.anidub.com
- tracker.0day.kiev.ua

The length of the list is not the only thing that the service can boast.



For what?


Well, firstly, the existence of these services became known during the development of Listen Trackers . I did not want to quit what had already begun, and neither tvfedor nor TorrentMonitor would satisfy my needs. The first one at that time knew how to work only with rutracker.org, and for the second one to work it is necessary to deploy a server.

What happened?


Probably, one of you is annoyed that some trackers prohibit a direct download of torrent files, forcing each user to open the tracker and scroll up to the link. Someone solved this problem for themselves by installing TorrentMonitor. And if you do not have registration?

Listen Trackers solves this problem in its own way. For this, he is trained to issue a magnet link for each torrent distribution. Thus, you can download without even registering with the tracker.

Separate RSS feeds using a magnet link for torrent clients prevent copying of authorization cookies from the browser.

For each subscription, you can choose how to receive notifications (mail, RSS feeds or all at once).



In the notification you will see not only a link to the topic, but also a poster, a magnet link, changes in the title, if any, as well as a link to .torrent (in case the tracker allows you to download directly).



Is there a killer feature?


Yes! With Listen Trackers you can track the appearance of new products. To do this, you need to create a simple rule by which the distribution you need will be selected.



There are also extensions for Opera, Firefox, Chrome that add a quick subscribe button and a bookmarklet for iOS devices and browsers without extension support.
