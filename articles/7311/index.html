This is not a perfume - it is vodka


This is not a perfume. This is gem-filtered vodka. Blackwood distillers from Scotland decided to produce the most expensive drink in the world. Vodka passes several filters, the first of which is ice, the second is charcoal from Scandinavian birch and, finally, diamond and other precious stones sand. The drink was called Diva vodka. The most impressive thing about this vodka is, of course, its price. In each bottle are stones from semi-precious to precious, there is such a pleasure from $ 70.00 to $ 1,060,000.00.
