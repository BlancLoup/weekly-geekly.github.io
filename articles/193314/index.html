Drive Market: shopping and driving

Hello!

Recently, we in Alconost made a video about an interesting project: a store where you can make purchases without leaving your car. If you do not like to spend a lot of time shopping - you probably will like this store.



Slightly more information about Drive Market under the cut.

The principle of the store


So, the visitor drives into the car shop, follows the light signs and drives up to a free rack with movable trays. The buyer selects the products and puts them on the transport tape. Each tray contains a specific set of products of the same product group. The cashier punches the purchase, accepts payment, transfers the purchase to the client. The purchase process takes just a few minutes.

Features Drive Market


The room is equipped with a system of exhaust ventilation, air is conditioned, exhaust gases are drawn out, and a comfortable microclimate is maintained.

The layout of the premises and the routes of movement of cars are designed to avoid congestion and minimize the possibility of collision of vehicles.

The range of the store is thought out and does not contain ‚Äúextra‚Äù goods. Within each product group, store merchandisers leave one or two product names that best meet the needs of the buyer. Yes, the choice is made for the buyer, and it saves great time.

In the Drive Market, there are no psychological methods for stimulating demand for goods not needed by the buyer, for example, placing goods on shelves at eye level or on shelves near cash desks. All products are in equal conditions.

By automating the processes, Drive Market is more economical than regular stores and makes more efficient use of retail space.

Yes, Drive Market, quite possibly, will not suit lovers of long and thoughtful shopping, but people who value their time will appreciate this idea.
