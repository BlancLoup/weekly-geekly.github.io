"Yandex Browser" will be forced to install on the computers of Moscow officials



This year, the computers of metropolitan officials will necessarily begin to install the browser produced by Yandex, the city news agency Moscow has reported . This was during an interview with the agency told the deputy head of the department of information technology in Moscow, Andrei Belozerov.

The conversation touched upon the issues of informatization, the transition to domestic software and progress in this direction. So, besides the attempt to abandon Microsoft products and the transition to the My Office product, Belozerov spoke about the contract with Yandex, according to which Yandex.Browser will be implemented at the workstations of Moscow officials on a mandatory basis.
