Tips for foreign language learners yourself

These tips are based solely on my experience, above all, studying grammar and memorizing words.

  1. Consider the lack of external motivation. This is a different experience, different from when someone asks you. I do not know whether it is possible to somehow prepare for this, in any case, do not dismiss this factor.
  2. Work on the system, do not invent anything yourself. I learn words with cards. Previously, I simply wrote out unfamiliar words from the dictionary, respectively, to the same word there were several meanings, the words were from different subjects. Then I began to memorize words using the methodology of Pavel Litvinov ‚Äú3000 English words. Technique of memorization ‚Äù (the book is not expensive, 70 rubles). There is nothing difficult, the words are grouped by subject, each word has one meaning, but the performance has increased by 2 times.
