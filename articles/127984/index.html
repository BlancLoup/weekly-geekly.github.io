Changes in the rules for automatic tracking of Russian Post items

For the operation of our online store, we actively used the option of automatic tracking of shipments - a GET request with a 14-digit send code was sent to a page with complete information about the delivery of the package. It is very convenient both for the back office of the store and for customers - they can send not only the tracking code, but also a link for tracking.
