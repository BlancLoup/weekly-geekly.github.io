Video review gaming computer (barebone-system) MSI Nightblade



MSI Nightblade is a Barebone-type personal computer (computer assembled on the basis of a ‚Äúframe‚Äù system designed for self-assembly by the user and called barebone basis) intended for gamers.

In a compact package with dimensions of 345.8 x 277.3 x 175.7 mm, there is a fourth-generation Intel Core processor (i7-4770K in the car from the review) and a Radeon R9-290X video card (in the review) or Geforce GTX 780Ti.
