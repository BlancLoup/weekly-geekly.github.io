Lenovo smartphones at IFA 2014

Hi, Habr!

At the IFA 2014, Lenovo introduced several new products, including two smartphones: Lenovo Vibe Z2 Pro with top features and the currently rare Quad HD resolution and Vibe X2 - its younger cousin, which won the award as ‚ÄúBest Flagship on Android‚Äù .

Read more about the features and prices - under habrakatom.



Lenovo Vibe Z2 Pro


This Lenovo flagship will delight you with a thin metal casing as well as a display. He has competitors with Quad HD (with a display resolution of 2560 x 1440), and they are already on sale. But only the Vibe Z2 six-inch screen occupies more than 85% of the front surface area. For comparison - the LG G3, this is significant in the region of 79%.



He has built-in memory - 32 GB, operational - 3 GB. Another obvious advantage is the 4000 mAh battery, which is very important with such dimensions and performance. Well, he supports two SIM cards - it will definitely come in handy for someone.

With this display, the smartphone is quite compact: the case thickness is 7.7 mm. Weight - 179 grams. For comparison - the Galaxy Note 4 with 5.7 inch display weighs exactly the same.

As for performance: the smartphone is built on a 4-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 MSM8974-AC 2.5 GHz processor, and Adreno 330 is responsible for the graphics.



In this case, the Vibe Z2 is positioned as a phone for "self-defense." Its front camera is wide-angle with a resolution of 8 megapixels. The main camera is 16 megapixel with optical stabilization. The video will shoot with a resolution of 4K.







Lenovo Vibe X2


For Vibe X2 at IFA 2014, Lenovo won the award for Best Flagship on Android.



The case of the device is made of three layers of plastic, which allows you to mix colors. Weight - 120 grams, and the thickness - only 7.27 mm. This phone, built on an eight-core MediaTek MTK6595MBig processor with a frequency of 2 GHz and LTE support, equipped with a five-inch display with a resolution of 1920 by 1080 will cost ... 16-17 thousand rubles. We are waiting in September-October of this year.



The smartphone is equipped with two cameras: front 5 megapixel and the main 13 megapixel. Built-in memory in it - 32 GB, operational - 2 GB. The only negative, in my opinion, is a 2100 mAh battery.

What's interesting: MediaTek MTK6595MBig is the first truly eight-core processor, in which all cores work simultaneously.
