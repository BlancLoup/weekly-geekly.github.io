Legal issues


‚ÄúThe decision to switch to Ubuntu did not require any legal measures, because it is an internal matter of the organization and does not require additional funds. Nevertheless, French law requires certain legal procedures, when partnership with commercial organizations requires additional government spending. The gendarmerie was obliged to take the following measures:
  • Creating an open tender that specifies all requirements and specifications.
  • Sending him [the tender] to the state tender committee.
  • Publication of the tender.
  • Waiting for a response from potential commercial partners.
  • Choosing the most suitable partner.

It is expected that the winner of the tender will be announced at the beginning of 2009. "
