Phyode W / Me: sports bracelet with "health indicator"

Someone reduces the price, someone adds all sorts of "buns" that can be useful to the buyer. One of these, different from the rest, devices is the Phyode W / Me Bracelet (by the way, the name is decoded as Phyode With Me, that is, a device that is always with you).

Main functions:

  • Heart rate data;
  • Data on respiratory rate and respiratory rhythm training;
