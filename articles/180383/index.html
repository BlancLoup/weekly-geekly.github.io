Flickr gives each user 1 terabyte for storing photos and videos.



Marissa Mayer (chief executive of Yahoo) has just published an appeal to users of Flickr photo service with some good news.

  • New interface ( flickr.com/new ).
  • New Android application.
  • 1 terabyte of disk space for each user to store photos, for free.

According to Marissa Mayer, a person should last one terabyte for a lifetime: these are 537,731 photos of 6.5 megapixels, based on the resolution of modern smartphones.

According to the current limits , the user can upload photos up to 200 MB in size and 1080p videos up to 1 GB for up to 3 minutes.
