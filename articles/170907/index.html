Mobility is a guide to business change.

Recently, I have been thinking a lot about the means of data transfer, with the help of which you get or transmit something, as useful advantages of mobile devices. Mobile devices - voice communications. Mobile devices - social media channel. Mobile devices are a means of transmitting e-mail, news, datasets, photos and videos, and now a means of storing electronic money and a payment tool. Quite a large part of our life is already somehow connected with mobile devices.
Over the past couple of weeks I have spent time with dozens of companies interested in the influence of mobility and social trends on their business, because in many cases it is a tremendous influence.
