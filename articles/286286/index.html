On March 9, in San Francisco, Apple organizes a presentation on which new products will be demonstrated. However, while there is no exact information about what exactly they are going to show. Opinions of experts on this subject are divided.

The event was called Spring Forward, as indicated in the sent out invitations. Venue - the center of art Yerba Buena. The presentation will begin at 21:00 Moscow time. But apart from that, the invitation does not indicate anything.
One can only guess what exactly will happen there.



Most experts believe that the event is dedicated to the new Apple Watch. In September, smart watches were first shown publicly. Therefore, the upcoming presentation would be a good reason to remind you that Apple Watch will be available in April.

In other versions, Apple will show a new model of MacBook Air laptop with an improved display, in which the display quality is improved due to the increased pixel density.

Some experts admit that Apple will demonstrate the tablet computer iPad Pro, and someone is sure that the company will finally present an interactive TV Apple TV to the public.

An alternative scenario is on the surface: the presentation can show everything at once. Indeed, in the past 5 years, Apple has not released new products.
