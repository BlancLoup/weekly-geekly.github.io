Wordpress for serious

So called another project with a lot of plug-ins from one box (a little pity that a little bit paid) to deploy your business on Wordpress. All of them are designed for those who want to organize their multi-site service for many visitors, and not necessarily the blog service.

A total of more than 140 plug-ins and 160 themes offered by subscription, I will note here those that can serve as the basis for a new project. You can build such plug-ins one by one, and you can find everything and immediately from some developers. What I really liked, having studied everything in detail.

Let's start with multisite


Pro sites
It will allow you to create your blog service with different paid tariffs, and this differs from the usual multisite content. In the tariffs it will be possible to set limits on the number of posts, access to certain topics, disk space, enable or disable service advertising on client sites.





Here, as part of the blog service, plugins may be useful.


  • Multi-Domains for connecting arbitrary domains to the created sites.
  • Multisite Content Copier for copy-paste between sites.
  • Blogs Directory , Site Categories and Global Site Search for a directory of client sites.
  • Support System to support customers through the ticket system.

For earnings are offered following out of the box


  • Admin Ads for advertising site administrators.
  • In Post Ads for advertising in posts.
  • Global Footer Content allows you to insert anything into all the footers of your network of sites.
  • Membership to be able to provide certain content for a material reward as a monthly subscription. Similarly with the plugin Pay Per View .

Separately, the Ultimate Branding plugin will remove all references to the press for visitors and put their logos, for example.
