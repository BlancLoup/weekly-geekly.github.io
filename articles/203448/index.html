LinkMeUp. Release 9. Intelligent Network Control Systems and the NOC project

The ninth edition of the linkmeup podcast is a continuation of the previous article on Intelligent Control Systems.
The main idea is to remove the person from the network management as much as possible. Setup, checking configs, debugging problems, analyzing logs - everything should be automatic.
There is no need to draw a network diagram manually and monitor its relevance. The network diagram should be combined with an accident monitoring system. Friendly interface, clear messages.

So, on a visit to the podcast Dmitry Volodin ( DmitryVolodin ) and Alexey Shirokikh - engineers of Russian operators and participants of the NOC project. Moreover, Dmitry is the creator of this system.

To some extent, the NOC implements the functions that I mentioned in the article, and, perhaps, is the first step towards the Intellectual Control System.

Also under the heading "News" we raise again the topic of the BRICS cable, we return to the issue of space laser communications. Two other interesting technical news: the free sale of femtochips to subscribers and the Li-Fi technology - wireless communication through conventional LED lamps.
