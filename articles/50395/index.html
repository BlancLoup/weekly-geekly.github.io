Saytofanatov network toodoo decided to help bloggers survive the crisis. Last week, a new service became available to users - donate .

In some ways, this is an analogue of a long-known service from Yandex - Give the ruble! . Plus donate in this case is the ability to collect money in any currency, and not only in Yandex.Money.

The creators of the blog praise their offspring very much - indeed, the idea is quite original in a difficult period, because you have to pay for a website or blog ... However, it was not without tar. A rather big minus of this project is the horrendous design buttons. They can not be customized to your liking: neither the size nor the color will not succeed.

How does all this work? With the help of ROBOKASS (it turned out that toodoo and Robokassa are one owner). Bribes more that you do not need to pay interest from their "profits". But on the other hand, the opportunity to withdraw money is only once a month, which, you see, is not very convenient.
