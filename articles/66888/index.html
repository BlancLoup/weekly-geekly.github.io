Analysis of participants

Some of you may be interested to know that more than 900 unique participants have been assigned to the development of Django since January 1, 2007. Data based on SVN logs.
I must say that the community is very strong, especially in comparison with other well-known projects:
  • Django: 906
  • Pylons: 80
  • PyPy: 240
  • Linux Kernel: 4043
  • PostgreSQL: 150
  • Apache HTTP server: 118
  • SQLAlchemy: 36
  • Python: 428

It is necessary to take into account that all anonymous and guest commits were considered as one participant, unless of course the project allowed those.
