Travel SIM cards: the best alternative for the traveler

It is not surprising that many Russian tourists planning a trip abroad are primarily concerned about the issue of communication with their homeland. Frequently and hotly discussed in the media and blogs, cases involving huge bills for roaming services, force each of us to think carefully about this side of the journey. And this concerns not only telephone conversations, but also Internet traffic. The most famous evidence of this is the conflict between Megaphone and a married couple from Vladimir, which ended in legal proceedings.
All this gives me a great reason for an analytical article devoted to finding the best way of mobile communication for tourists traveling abroad.
To make the result as objective as possible, we will define a number of factors by which the various methods of communication will be assessed.
  1. Tariffs for outgoing and incoming calls (both local and in Russia).
  2. The cost of SMS-messages.
  3. Quick and easy connection.
  4. The need to change the number.
Now imagine the original situation.
You are planning a trip to Italy for a week and do not want to save on conversations with relatives and friends. You are not traveling alone on a trip: you are with family, friends or other pleasant fellow travelers. In addition, right now you are developing a tumultuous SMS-novel with a colleague at work.
If you describe this situation in a dry mathematical language, then your communication needs can be represented as follows:
  • calls to Russia: 5 minutes a day (35 minutes during the trip);
  • local calls: 10 minutes per day (70 minutes during the trip);
  • SMS: 20 SMS per day (140 SMS during the trip).
So, let's try to consider the possible amounts of your communication expenses during the trip.

Option 1. Roaming from the big three


It would seem that this is the most obvious option. But economic considerations will make even the richest and most unreasonable person consider this method as the last thing. Judge for yourself.

Quick and easy connection

Before traveling to the airport, the traveler will have to perform some actions. For example, Beeline subscribers must, firstly, pay off the debt on the SIM card, if there is one, and secondly, replenish the balance by 1,500 rubles. This amount is called a guarantee fee and in theory is returned to the subscriber to the account when the service is disabled. But in practice, this money usually melts as soon as a person makes a couple of calls home.
Megafon and MTS warn their subscribers that the ability to pay for roaming bills is limited, so it is recommended to replenish the balance in advance by the optimal amount.

The need to change the number

According to this criterion, I must state the advantage of the big three. Your number remains the same without additional tricks, and even if you forgot to tell your boss that you are going on vacation, he will find you even across the ocean. Although in this aspect it is worth considering whether this is an advantage?

Our assessment of this method: 1 of 5.
