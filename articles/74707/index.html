Daily interviews for training course CKR 2.0: "Interactive communication in advertising"

The creative agency Red Keds and the advertising agency GRAPE, with the support of ACAR, are conducting an additional recruitment of students for the commercial department of the training course IKR 2.0: "Interactive communications in advertising."

IKRA 2.0 is the first training course in Russia that prepares managers of digital projects and experts of digital advertising.

An additional set of passes for students who are willing to study at the commercial department. This year, the cost of tuition on the course is halved.

In order to enroll in the course, you must first fill out the registration form on the course website www.ikraikra.ru/graduate.php and have an interview before November 14. The interview is held every day at the office of the creative agency Red Keds at the address: Moscow, Stoleshnikov Pereulok, 6, bld. 1, office 21.
