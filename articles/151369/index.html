Ukrainian Silicon Valley at IDCEE 2012

Less than two months left before the main event of the IT industry in Central and Eastern Europe - the international conference IDCEE 2012 . Already on October 18-19, domestic entrepreneurs will be able to evaluate startups for Central and Eastern European developers.



In addition to foreign guests, the BIONIC Hill Innovation Park, already named by experts of the Ukrainian Silicon Valley and having recently received the status of a participant in the National Project TECHNOPOLIS, will be presented at the Conference. They will also talk about BIONIC University - the educational initiative of the Innopark, which will start functioning in October. Programs for students and young professionals are developed in close cooperation with the professors of relevant departments of leading universities in the country, the best trainers specializing in high technology, and companies leading the IT market.


As is known, within the framework of an innovation park located on 147 hectares, it is planned to build comfortable business centers, a modern research and development center, a high-tech production zone, as well as a sports and recreational and social housing infrastructure. The initiator and main investor of the project is the leader of the investment and development market of Ukraine - UDP . The project provides about 35,000 jobs and 10,000 residents.


As Victoria Tigipko, organizer of IDCEE 2012 conference, director of the venture fund TA Venture , notes, such platforms for communication and training like IDCEE and Bionic Hill are very important both for young startups and for the leading and already established players of Internet business.

The conference organizers also maintain a constant dialogue with the professional community through social media. So, everyone who wants to be involved in the organization of IDCEE 2012 can take direct part, who follow the activity on social networks and not just watch, but have the opportunity to create content. For example, according to the results of the voting, which was held on the conference page on Facebook , the topic of the speech of one of the speakers will be: Gamification as part of the business: online training, e-commerce, Saas.
