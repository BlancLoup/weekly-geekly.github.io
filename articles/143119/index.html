+1 extension for Mozilla Firefox

Good day!

A few days ago, Shaida created a post called +1 extension for Google Chrome , where everyone could recommend an extension that ever came in handy to him. It's time for users of firestone to give their answer. So, I'll start!


Web Developer


Web Developer - an extension that will simplify the life of a web programmer (which is engaged in the client-side, of course). It will allow you to change the page styles, disable JavaScript, Java, view cookies, check the page for validity with the latest (and not only!) Standards, and much, much more.



That's all! Wait for your offers! Please do not forget to include a link to the extension.
