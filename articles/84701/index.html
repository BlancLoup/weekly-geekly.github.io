Electronic Designer (EKON-02 vs Expert)

As they talked with a work colleague about electronic designers, it turned out that Ekon-02 was collecting dust on his shelves. In my childhood, there was no such happiness, and I really wanted my son to have some kind of analog Econ-02.
An electronic designer of the Soviet type, the development of the All-Russia Scientific Research Institute "Electro-standard", cost this happiness 14 rubles of Soviet money. I was very interested in the prospect of at least doing something similar on the model. Radio components are fixed on the plane, conventional wiring is used for the connection, the contacts of the radio parts inside the case are inserted into the springs, so all the contacts are brought to the surface with the help of the springs. It is necessary to say that the springs are rather dense, the coils touch each other, if the spring is stretched and the wire is inserted between the coils, the spring will tightly clamp the wire, there will be a fairly stable connection between the radio parts without soldering.
