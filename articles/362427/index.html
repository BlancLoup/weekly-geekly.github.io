King of pigs Nikita Mikhalkov prepared his version of anti-piracy legislation

The Russian Union of Rights Holders (RSP), whose board is headed by the odious leader Nikita Mikhalkov, is ready to end piracy on the Internet. This organization, collecting from producers and importers of audio and video equipment deductions in favor of the right holders (the famous tax on blanks ), offers a similar model for the Network.

At the disposal of RBC was a draft amendment to the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (Civil Code) "in terms of improving the turnover of intellectual rights in information and telecommunications networks." The document, according to two sources of RBC - in the government and in one of the industry associations, was prepared by the CPR with the personal participation of the president of his board, film director Nikita Mikhalkov, and in the near future the draft amendments may be submitted to President Vladimir Putin. A source close to the RSP, confirmed that the organization has its own position on the problem of Internet piracy. The press service of the Union has not yet responded to the request of RBC.
