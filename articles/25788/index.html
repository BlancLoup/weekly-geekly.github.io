2buy.ua - smart consumers community

Halo! Now I will write about the startup I am currently involved in.

It is known that all people, one way or another, are consumers. Of course, there are those who listen to RATM and wear T-shirts with the faces of Cuban revolutionaries, but this is not about them. Although, where do they get these T-shirts from?

So everyone is a consumer, and some of them hang out on the Internet. We also know that people stop believing advertising messages, and this happens on the Internet against the background of turning advertising into an independent entertainment genre, often no longer having lengthy associations with the promotion of some kind of products. The only thing that people truly believe in is the advice of those they trust.

Therefore, it was decided to create 2buy.ua. We see this site as a hybrid of a social network and a collective blog + editorial content. The task of 2buy.ua is to make people spend money wisely and get pleasure from it. And we create the necessary conditions for this:


1) People have the opportunity to share with each other consumer experience and any information that will help them not to throw money down the drain.
2) Users can describe themselves in as much detail as possible, including from a consumer point of view: brand preferences, the desire to purchase this or that thing, already acquired things.

Thus, people will be able to receive useful information and, based on the credibility of its source for them personally, decide whether to believe in what has been written or not.
