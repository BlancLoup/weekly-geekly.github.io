As a result of the abnormal heat of the server, Rambler Telecom data center could not withstand the increased temperature

Hot weather, which is not the first day in Moscow, caused overheating of the servers Masterhost and Rambler, located in the data center Rambler Telecom. Thus, the mail and video hosting of Rambler, as well as some sites running on the Masterhost servers, were interrupted for several hours.

As a result of the abnormal heat that has recently been established in Moscow, the Rambler Telecom data center servers did not withstand the increased temperature conditions and failed. As a result, the postal service Rambler Mail and the video hosting Rambler Vision since mid-day on May 28 turned out to be unavailable to users. The problem affected other companies as well. For some time, the Masterhost servers located in the Rambler data center also did not function. Currently, the functionality of some servers has already been partially restored.
