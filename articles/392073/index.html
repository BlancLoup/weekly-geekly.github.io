PornHub added virtual reality category

One of the largest online resources of the 18+ category has launched a free section with movies for adults that are adapted for viewing in VR headsets. Currently the section is number 1 in the list of all categories.



Everything necessary for the launch was provided by one of the largest players in the porn industry BaDoink. At the moment there are 27 videos on the site.

The objectives of the campaign are very noble, and, in the opinion of the authors of this project, the distribution of 18+ content in VR format will contribute to the speedy penetration of this technology to the masses. In this regard, it is specified that not only the owners of expensive headsets, Oculus or Samsung, but also the owners of any devices will be able to fully enjoy the section.

Earlier, a special series of videos for adults was prepared by the studio Naughty America , also known as a site containing 18+ content in VR format, but paid.
