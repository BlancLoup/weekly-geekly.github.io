Some ideas for using Google Glass (video)



Probably, Google Glass can be called one of the most interesting, and at the same time, controversial gadgets presented relatively recently. Many technology lovers consider them just a good toy, others say that devices of this type are the future. But why can such a device be useful? Agency Playground presented a selection of ideas for working with "glasses" in a creative video.

Apparently, the authors of the video are confident that glasses can be used for communication with the outside world during sports, and for shopping on the Web, and for training.

Of course, all this will not be possible without solving problems with a relatively short battery life of Google Glass, plus the "fine-tuning" of hardware and software points to the user, the final version. And yes, don't forget that some countries and organizations have already banned Google Glass. What will happen next? We'll see.
