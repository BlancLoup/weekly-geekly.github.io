The first P2P browser from the creators of BitTorrent



The alpha testing of a new browser based on P2P technology from the creators of BitTorrent has begun.


It all started with a simple question. What if most of the network would work today as BitTorrent works?

The Maelstrom project begins to answer this question with our first public release of a web browser, which may give rise to a new type of web content publishing, access to it and its consumption. The Internet is truly developing thanks to the people. To people who lower barriers and reject those who guard the gates that hold our future.



If we are successful, we hope that this project has the potential to help solve the most unpleasant problems facing the Internet today. How can we leave the Internet open? How can we leave Internet access neutral? How can we be sure that our personal data will not be abused by large companies? How can we help the Internet scale effectively for content?

For a long time, the power of distributed technology that supports BitTorrent and all our products has been an example of concern. We brought these technologies to the web before these difficulties only got their shape.

Please join us in this project, become a part of this new Internet and help us change the future of the network.
