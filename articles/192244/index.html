Amazon opened the delivery of electronics in Russia



In Soviet Russia:


Today, Amazon's online store has begun shipping consumer electronics to Russia. Corresponding changes were made to the product ordering system on the company's website on September 2. A complete list of countries where delivery is carried out can be found here .


In June, Amazon organized the delivery of home goods, clothing, shoes and accessories to Russia. Prior to this, the retailer was selling books, films and music in Russia. The bulk of sales accounted for electronic options.

Amazon is the largest online store in the world. The company delivers to 65 countries of the world. In 2012, the company reported $ 61 billion in revenue.

Let me remind you that PayPal officially came to Russia not so long ago, and even earlier, eBay, which in many ways expanded consumer opportunities for Russians.

In 2012 in Russia, according to the Association of E-commerce Companies, the volume of the e-commerce market reached 405 billion rubles. The leader in the Russian online trading market is Ozon.ru. In 2012, its turnover reached 15.2 billion rubles. According to experts, up to 44 percent of online sales accounted for consumer electronics. From this we can conclude that large retailers are trying to grab their piece from the dynamically developing Russian online trading market, but what will come of this? ..
