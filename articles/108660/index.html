HackDay: Battle of Companies - December 9-10

HackDay: Company Battle is a new format of competitions between IT companies: you need to think over the concept of the project, declare and implement it in 24 hours. Participate in HackDay: Company Battle can any software company. Participants must set up a team that will be able to fully design and write a prototype of the project for the day. We set only the themes of projects, for example: commerce, games, utilities, entertainment and the like. After that, you will have a day to develop a project from idea to working prototype, ready for demonstration. Developed applications will be evaluated by a jury according to a number of parameters: security, bugs, idea, code quality, etc.

The battle will take place in St. Petersburg, but we are waiting for teams from other cities.

If you consider your company better than others and are not afraid to prove it, you have the opportunity.

In total there are places for 20 teams. 4 companies have confirmed their participation. 16 left.

Spectators are invited on Friday evening for the official presentation of projects.
