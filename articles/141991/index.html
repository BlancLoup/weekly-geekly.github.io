Hide USB-Flash to 3.5 "diskette

If after all previous projects you have left behind free floppy disks, then this Friday project for making stylish USB-Flash media using 3.5 "floppy disks is for you.





So, for the manufacture of the case will need two floppy 3.5. inches The thickness of a single floppy is not enough to fit a USB flash drive. You also need a soldering iron and USB cable, we will solder it to the contacts of the card.



The process is primitive: remove the case of the flash card, disassemble the floppy disk, solder the contacts, paste the flash card into the floppy disk, assemble the floppy disk, run in any order.
