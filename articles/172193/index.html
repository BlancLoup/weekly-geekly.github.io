University and program

Technical University Eindhoven [ Eng. ] In addition to undergraduate, graduate and PhD has a number of post-master's two-year programs Professional Doctorate in Engineering. Professional Doctorate in Engineering
PDEng [ eng. ] this is a post-master degree in the Netherlands, designed to combine science with industry. You can get it at one of the three technical universities of the 3TU association [ Eng. ] . Each participant in the program is an employee of the university, receives a salary and pays taxes and contributions to the pension fund. One of these programs is User System Interaction . ] .
For admission you need to complete a master's degree ( judith suggests, a specialist degree may also be appropriate) in the field of engineering, behavioral sciences or design. As well as pass IELTS Academic with an average score of 6.5 and at least 6 points in each of the parts of the test or 550 points in the TOEFL (on paper).

Paperwork

IND [ English ] Residence Wizard : ‚ÄúProfessional soccer players and minors of religion, are not eligible for a residency as a highly skilled migrant.‚Äù

For admission it was necessary to write a resume, abstract of the thesis and a motivation letter in English. To begin with, I looked for examples of writing these papers, after I found it, I decided that I would write very differently.

Resume and motivation letter should be written for each organization with separate emphasis in the field of activity of this organization, and it is better to redo them entirely for each specific case. For myself, I chose not the most formal style of writing.

The Netherlands has signed the Hague Convention , and therefore, instead of legalizing documents, they must be apostilled , which is said to be easier and takes less time. For the Netherlands you need to put a double apostille, on the document itself and on the certified translation of the document.
Putting apostilles is best done by yourself, because it is cheaper, safer and faster. If the documents are submitted to the Ministry of Justice by you personally, then the deadline is one day, otherwise five working days (relevant for Ukraine).

Do not laminate a certificate of birth, marriage, divorce, change of name. When apostilization stamp is placed on the document behind. If you have already laminated, you will have to get the documents again. Diplomas and inserts are bound with sheets for stamping.

Update : As rightly noted, the apostille of diplomas can be completely optional, most likely I made my own in vain, it is better to clarify it separately at the university.
Algorithm for obtaining documents

The personnel department and the secretariat of the faculty of industrial design simply asked me to provide the documents I had already prepared, they sent me a brochure with tips and checklists of what I should do before and after arrival. Independently, I opened an account at a local bank, sent all documents to the imm immigration office. IND, two weeks later, checked and issued the necessary documents and advised the embassy to issue a MVV ( D visa) visa, which I took in two trips to the embassy in one day. And the program coordinator suggested where good housing appeared, which I chose.

If you have a wife or girl, you need a marriage certificate or proof of civil marriage (a general bank account may be appropriate). They get a work visa, go on city tours, learn Dutch at a discount and integrate into the community in every possible way. At some point, I even thought that it would be necessary to have someone, and then the good disappears.

I received my residence permit nine days after visiting the immigration center upon arrival. To obtain a residence permit, you need to do a fluorography at a local hospital, and you will be able to appoint an appointment at the immigration center.

Interview
It was conducted by telephone in English by two people and lasted 45 minutes. There were no surprises, they asked about plans, interests, and especially about the graduation project and motivation. My interlocutors studied the documents sent by me in detail and clarified a few non-obvious questions (for example, how can you finish school at 16, enroll and go to work, and most importantly, why). Training and work
The group was recruited as multinationally as possible and with experience in various fields from psychology to mathematics and recruited this year 17 people from different parts of the world, most of all from Europe. The average age of my colleagues is around 26‚Äì27 years old (from 23 to 30 years old).

You can talk about the program for a long time, but I will limit myself to general information.
The first week was devoted to professional development, namely, classes were held on intercultural interaction and teamwork in general. One of the classes included training with the actors. There are also additional courses in time management, teamwork, negotiation, etc., which are held throughout the year.

The first year is taught various disciplines (modules 1-2 weeks each) grouped into the following five clusters: User Experience Design, Software Engineering, Interactive Technology and Applications, Understanding People, and Research Methodology. Each module or cluster is one team project. Then the team presents its result. In one of our projects, we were able to visit Philips High Tech Campus and work on UX proposals.
The second year is a more serious team project for 3 months, after which an individual internship for 9 months in the industry.

It is very helpful that in teamwork, which is present in almost every module, everyone is interested in work and training. Naturally, there are no people squinting from the army and those whom parents sent to study. Everyone has a different life, cultural and professional experience. Therefore, if in some field someone has more experience than most colleagues and they want to hear about it, you can easily organize your master class. In this cluster, we already had three such master classes on HTML / CSS (read judith ), Adobe Illustrator and PHP / MySQL. The last I was lucky enough to spend myself.

Our program has a variety of traditions related to celebrations and work, further enlivening the working and learning process. One of them is Cookie Wednesday, which takes place every Wednesday at 3:00 pm, everyone, including guests from previous generations of the program and lecturers, gather in the common room for informal communication and eating cookies brought or prepared by people in charge on that day (for the first time in their lives I baked something).

Prices are relatively high, a one-room apartment or a studio apartment with its own bathroom and kitchen will cost from 450 to 1,000 euros per month, utilities are included in the price, but sometimes it does not. The same with the furniture: either there, or buy it yourself. Up to 400 euros you can rent only the room and share the bathroom and kitchen with someone else.

Shops are open from 8‚Äì9 am on weekdays (on Monday from 12) until the end of the work day. Many purchases can be made the same on the first Sunday of the month and on the evening of every Friday.
