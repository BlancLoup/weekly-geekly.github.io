Twitter enters into a patent agreement with IBM

This Friday, Twitter acquired more than 900 IBM patents, and also entered into a cross-licensing agreement that will help protect the microblogging service from patent trolls.

Such a Twitter move is justified by IBM's autumn attacks, which blamed Twitter for deliberately violating at least three patents for which IBM owns the rights.
