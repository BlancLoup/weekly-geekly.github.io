How to avoid data loss? We make a user manual

The modern world is gradually moving to the storage of all information known to it from a paper form to a digital one, respectively, the main question arises, the question of the protection of this information.




The whole world is a data warehouse
Photo from Lori's photobank.

The responsibility of IT department specialists to neutralize all sorts of risks and ensure a general atmosphere that ensures a sufficient degree of data storage security is growing. Working with information stored in digital form is very comfortable, but, as you know, danger lurks us at every turn. In case of fires and other natural disasters, modern carriers are as defenseless as paper, bark or papyrus. But, if with natural threats of our information everything is more or less clear, for example, compliance with fire safety techniques and keeping copies in a different place, territorial and logical, then with threats that are not of such a global nature, the situation is somewhat more complicated.


Very often, the threat of losing data from electronic media occurs through carelessness, inattention, due to lack of necessary knowledge or, frankly, is a consequence of the carelessness of employees. How else to call the devil-may-care attitude to the problem of organizing secure data storage, which prevails in many firms and companies. Yes, and ordinary users sin. It is more difficult for them, since the amount of information is growing exponentially, and it is not always enough knowledge about organizing archiving.
Every day we are confronted with the despair of specialists who have lost valuable information, with the tears of lovely girls who have lost photos of their loved ones or their essays, we help, comfort, explain. Plunging every day into the problems of our Customers and identifying the causes of information loss, we explain to them what rules to follow, what steps to take if they do not want to become our regular customers.
Based on the data collected, we have compiled and published on our website a small manual of 10 points . If you follow these tips, the probability of losing important data will tend to zero, and, no matter what it is, a family photo archive or an array of results with serious scientific research.
