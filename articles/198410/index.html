The first all-Russian digital-games

Hi, Habr!



We launched a big competition in a new format for the market - the first Digital-Games 2013 . We offer to take part in them studios, agencies and private professionals of our market. Unlike ordinary festivals, when participants submit ready-made works from the portfolio (and, in fact, do not compare them among themselves - such as, for example, compare the Audi promotional website with the shampoo promo website?), We decided to go the other way - and offer to make a competitive work on a single task - in one of six nominations. Almost all nominations go in two tests - personal and corporate.

The games are held within the framework of the tenth anniversary Runet Prize, the awards will be held on November 21, the deadline for accepting papers in nominations is November 14.

So, what competitions will be held in the framework of the Games:

1. CREATIVE & VISUAL
Competition for creatives and visualizers. The task is to design a promotional website for the product / service according to a single task for all participants.

2. USABILITY & DESIGN
Competition for designers and GUI-designers. The task is to develop a convenient interface for the task from a single brief.

3. TREND BATTLE
Competition of visionaries and market analysts. Competition on the knowledge and vision of trends in the development of the industry of interactive communications. Online tours bleed, full-time final.

4. GOVERNMENT & DIGITAL
Nomination for those who want to help our state to create convenient online services. Development of the concept of pages for RosStat. The nomination is organized by the agency Notamedia with the support of the Ministry of Communications.

5. CONCEPT & SOCIAL MEDIA
Nomination for specialists and specialized agencies in the field of communications: development of the concept of product promotion in social networks. Nomination organized by Rostelecom .

6. IDEA & VIRAL
Nomination for professionals in the field of viral marketing: development of the idea and visual of the special project in the banking sector. The nomination is organized by the agency AIC .

Register on the site, see the tasks - and let the strongest win =)
