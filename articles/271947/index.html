Opera Link closes in December

Less than half a year and two years after the closure of My Opera , another service of the Opera company, Opera Link, suffered a similar fate.



For those who do not want to "everything that is acquired by overwork, is gone," I ask under the cat.

Here is a letter came from the development team of Opera synchronization services:
It's time to say goodbye to our good old service Opera Link.

In March, we presented a new way to synchronize data in the latest versions of Opera for computers, Android and iOS. With the transition to the new synchronization service, we were able both to simplify the synchronization procedure and expand the set of data types that are synchronized between computers, phones and tablets.

** Opera Link will stop working in December 2015 **

After that, you will no longer be able to synchronize your browsers using Opera Link. In addition, you will no longer have access to your data through the Opera Link web interface on link.opera.com/ .

However, closing Opera Link does not mean that you can no longer synchronize data in your favorite browser. Download the new version of Opera, and you can - depending on the device - sync bookmarks, Express panels, open tabs, history of visits, settings and passwords.

Go to the new way of synchronization is easy:

1. Download Opera from www.opera.com/ and install it.

2. Log in to the new synchronization service with your username and password Opera Link, or simply create a new account. You can read about how to do this here: www.opera.com/help/account#signIn .

3. After registering an account, use our tool to transfer old bookmarks from Opera Link to your new browser. This tool can be found here: sync.opera.com/web/ .

If you are unable (or do not want) to install a new browser, your local data will remain on your computer, but it will not be updated on the Opera Link server. We recommend transferring your bookmarks so that you still have access to them through the web interface of our new service. The migration tool is here: sync.opera.com/web/ .

We hope you enjoy the new sync services in the latest generation of our browsers.

Thank you for choosing Opera!
Synchronization Services Development Team

And on link.opera.com we are waiting for this warning:
After that, you will no longer be able to synchronize your browsers using Opera Link. In addition, you will no longer have access to your data through the Opera Link web interface .

Worry, however, is not worth it. The new generation of Opera browsers allows you to synchronize bookmarks, express panels, open tabs, browsing history, settings, passwords and some other data through our new synchronization service built right into the browser. We even made a convenient tool for you to transfer bookmarks from Opera Link .

Download the new Opera browser and use synchronization without problems.

So save!
