At the conference YaC 2019 Yandex told what the company will do during the year. The company will have to settle legal issues and bring 100 cars onto public roads, run the Ether advisory service, teach Alice to manage the house, start producing inexpensive dishes and combine this with the delivery of Food.


Arkady Volozh went to the scene on an unmanned car

Yet Another Conference was opened by Arkady Volozh and told how Yandex is doing business with drones - this is now a topic that interests the CEO most of all.

UAVs


In 2019, hundreds of autonomous cars with NVIDIA equipment in the trunk will appear on the roads of Russia (Moscow, Kazan), USA (Nevada, California) and Israel. They will not be engaged in anything special cars - only to collect data on the trips in various conditions. Behind the wheel while driving will always sit the driver, ready to take control.



In fact, by the beginning of the project everything is ready, only in Russia it remains to settle some legal subtleties. According to Volozh, the company is negotiating with the traffic police. As for Moscow specifically, for the time being, Yandex is not sure whether the whole city will be provided for the tests or separate areas of the city will be allocated for this.

Yandex.Auto




The conference also presented a new version of the head unit for cars. Now it can be used not only to control the multimedia system of the machine, but also other systems. For example, you can ask the built-in Auto Alice to open or close windows, unlock the lock using a smartphone, start the engine remotely, etc. When the car is evacuated, the head system will send a notification via SMS, if necessary, will automatically pay for parking and refueling, and in the future will be able to pay for the toll on the toll road itself. If a car model is equipped with sensors in tires, then according to the speed of rotation of the wheels and their direction, the Yandex navigator will be able to determine the position of the car even where there is no GPS signal.



Some functions will be available in the first version of the Auto after the software update, but it may require reconnection for more tight interaction with the car via the CAN bus. The second hardware version of Auto will be on sale in the fall.
