Webinar "Using public clouds in retail"



On Thursday ( July 24 ) at 14:00, IT-GRAD conducts a webinar on the use of public clouds (IaaS in particular) in the retail sector.





During the six years of IT-GRAD company operation, many customers from this industry have appeared among its customers. One of them will share his experience - retail operator IDEAS4RETAIL (chain stores Imaginarium, Hamleys, Mamas & Papas, CookHouse), will tell about what services they use and how they came to this.

Date: 07/24/2014 at 14:00

The program of the event:


What topics will we discuss?
  • Practical realities of many years of experience working with clouds;
  • Centralization of services as a way to save IT budget;
  • Scalability as a means of reducing costs when opening new points of sale;
  • On-demand resources as a means of providing seasonal sales peaks;
  • The presence of SLA as a guarantee of peace;
  • Backup infrastructure in the cloud.
