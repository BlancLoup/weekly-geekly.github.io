ASUS Padfone in Russia



Briefly about the main thing: the ASUS Padfone transforming smartphone has appeared on the Russian market. The complete set of ASUS Padfone will be available from September 10, 2012 in M.Video, Eldorado, as well as in all ASUS brand stores in Moscow.

A smartphone without a tablet will be available in the network of supermarkets for digital DNS equipment. The recommended retail price for a set of ASUS PadFone and PadFone Station with a cover and stylus PadFone Stylus Headset will be 33,990 rubles, a separate ASUS PadFone smartphone - 22,990 rubles. As an accessory, it will be possible to purchase a ASUS PadFone Station Dock mobile docking station, the price for which will be 5990 rubles.

Recall what is the "technological matryoshka" ASUS Padfone.





ASUS PadFone is a 4.3-inch smartphone with Android Ice Cream Sandwich operating system (and then upgraded to Android 4.1), which, when installed in a special ASUS PadFone Station module, turns into a 10.1-inch tablet computer with a long running time talk time (up to 63 hours).

The smartphone has a Super AMOLED display with qHD resolution and a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 dual-core processor with a frequency of 1.5 GHz. The module ASUS PadFone Station does not have its own processor, it uses the capabilities of a smartphone. At the same time, DynamicDisplay technology provides instant switching between smartphone and tablet modes: most applications automatically change their size according to the current mode.

In addition to the 10.1-inch display, the PadFone Station contains a built-in battery that increases battery life. In addition, you can connect a third component to them - the PadFone Station Dock keyboard module, resulting in a kind of netbook version. Another important element of the ‚Äúmatryoshka‚Äù: PadFone Stylus Headset is a stylus combined with a Bluetooth headset. Stylus headset allows you to answer calls without removing the smartphone from the station every time.
