Google has discovered the next version of Android for developers.


TechCrunch N decoding version

On March 9, unexpectedly for all, Google rolled out a preliminary version of the new version of the Android operating system, while it bears the name of one letter - N. Owners of Nexus phones will be able to upgrade even ‚Äúover the air‚Äù. The main novelty of the new version was the support of a multi-window interface.

The announcement was unexpected due to the fact that the announcement of the system was expected at the I / O developer conference to be held in May. Hiroshi Lockheimer [Hiroshi Lockheimer], vice-president of the company for Android, Chrome OS and Chromecast wrote that the team decided to release a new version early, collect more feedback from the developers and give the manufacturers ready OSes this summer.

Those interested can register on a special page and start exploring the system. Nevertheless, the developers emphasize that the current Android N should be perceived as a prototype, on which work is constantly underway.

Among the innovations of the system is the possibility of direct responses to notifications :



But the main novelty, of course, was the ability to show two applications on one screen at the same time, both on tablets and on smartphones. Developers will be able to specify the minimum window size for their application. On one screen, two applications can get along as in the form of two windows of the same size, and in the form of a "preview", where one window is visible in the corner of the other.



Also, the developers claim that they are working on reducing memory consumption by applications and optimizing applications that work in the background, in particular, on expanding the functionality of the Doze mode. The owners of Nexus 6, Nexus 5X, Nexus 6P, Nexus Player, Nexus 9 and Pixel C (but not Nexus 5) will be able to switch to the new OTA version. For other devices and emulators, Google will upload images.
