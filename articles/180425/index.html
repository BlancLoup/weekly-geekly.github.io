What today can be considered compact and modern?


  • Sizes of no more than Motorola RAZR i or Apple iPhone 5 , so that you can comfortably operate with one hand and comfortably carry in your pocket;
  • 2GB of RAM;
  • Screen from 4.2 to 4.5 with a reasonable resolution, like 720x1280;
  • Snapdragon 600 chipset, Exynos 5 Octa;
  • High quality camera;
  • High-quality screen;
  • The presence of protection Gorilla Glass;
  • High-quality speaker + sound in headphones;
  • Multiband UMTS (Europe + America)
  • LTE support
  • Weight up to 130 g

And now try to buy such a smartphone ... You are waiting for disappointments and the fight against compromises. Such characteristics can be found mainly in devices with a diagonal of 5 inches. That is the problem.

And what about the "Chinese"?


During this year, users hoped that many Chinese smartphones will be released on the attractive MTK6589 chipset, the distinguishing feature of which is the possibility of using 2x SIM cards at the same time, rather than alternately. However, at the moment it is very difficult to buy even a Chinese smartphone with 2Gb RAM:
Many Chinese manufacturers of low-end smartphones have long been morally ready to move to the 2 GB RAM bar, but there is one problem - incompatibility with the MT6589 platform. For the absolute majority of budgetary ‚ÄúChinese‚Äù, this platform is already basic and indispensable, therefore, until now, companies such as JiaYu, iOcean and others only promise us devices with 2 GB of RAM, but in fact smartphones with only 1 GB of RAM go on sale.
Another point that should be paid close attention to with Chinese phones is the support of 3G frequencies. Such a great product, like the Xiaomi Mi-2S , for example, having top features comparable to the Nexus 4 (excellent screen 4.3, Quad-core 1.7 GHz Krait 300, 2GB) does not support UMTS 900 (which means that 3G will not work well in premises and areas that, theoretically, cut off most of the buyers).
