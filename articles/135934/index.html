Samsung has released three cameras with Wi-Fi



Wireless communication is becoming more common, in this case Wi-Fi is implied. Wireless modules are now being equipped with devices like refrigerators, televisions, irons , thermometers, and even toothbrushes. Now the series of devices with Wi-Fi replenished immediately with three models of cameras from Samsung. And by the way, for a long time memory cards with a wireless module went on sale. These cards have not become very common, for a number of reasons. But cameras are another matter.

Cameras equipped with a Wi-Fi module are more likely to become popular than memory cards. In any case, Samsung is considered that way. The developers of this company presented immediately three models of cameras equipped with a wireless communication module, these are WB850F ($ 379), WB150F ($ 229) and ST200F ($ 199). This entire model range was presented at the notorious CES. Thus, the owner of such a device can immediately send photos or videos to Flickr or YouTube.

In addition, the company introduced its own cloud storage, naming it AllShare Play.
