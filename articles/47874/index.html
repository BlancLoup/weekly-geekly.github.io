Nigma-feature collection 2008

So, 2008 is inexorably coming to an end - it's time to sum up. This year for Nigma was a real breakthrough. We worked a lot and were able to launch a record number of useful services for our favorite users.

We present to your attention the collection of Nigma-features 2008:
  1. Explanation of abbreviations - the base of 200 thousand English and Russian abbreviations. GDP
  2. Solving Equations Nigma can solve equations, systems of equations - there is a lot more of the list of problems to be solved . x2-3x + 2 = 0
  3. Latest News - the ability to see the latest news related to the query, directly on the search results page. Putin
  4. Autocomplete with the ability to go to the most popular site already at the stage of entering a query into the search bar.
  5. Nigma-chemistry - search in the base of inorganic reactions. KOH + H2SO4
  6. Nigma music. With this feature, all users can listen to their favorite ringtone directly on the search results page. vivaldi seasons
But that's not all! Part of the development is still at the stage of closed testing, but in January 2009, Nigma users will see new useful services, as well as significant improvements to existing ones.
We congratulate every user of Habrahabr with the upcoming 2009! We wish you creative success, bright victories and positive emotions.
