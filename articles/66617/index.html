Bitter experience with smscoin.com

It all started with the desire to fasten to your site the ability to pay for services via SMS. I decided to try the sms: bank service from SmsCoin.
I looked at the tariffs for Ukraine and decided to send an SMS to the short number 4443 for the test. The site states that the cost of sms is 0.67 UAH without VAT and accordingly 0.80 UAH with VAT.



I checked the account before sending and after sending sms. And what was my surprise when 1.06 UAH was withdrawn from the account !!! To my surprise, there was no limit. Although I until recently believed that I must withdraw 80 kopecks.

I decided to contact the site support service and clarify the situation.
