Tips for novice freelancers

A few confused thoughts about freelancing. Do not take every word literally.

I don't know anything, nobody needs my services.


Most likely you really do not know anything. But, believe me, there are so many assholes and idiot people around that if you really want to study and you like what you are doing, then there will be a job for you quickly.

I do not know how much my services cost.


I recommend to start working for free. Make a couple of small projects. Get minimal experience with real customers. Most likely, some of them will want to continue working with you further for the money. At the initial stage of development, experience is much more important than money.

How much to ask for money for services.


Start at any small price. Change the price of services from time to time. Analyze. Do not be afraid to ask a lot. Do not be afraid to discuss the price with customers and bargain.
