Attention, designers! Evernote starts a 100,000 T-shirt contest. The main prize is 100,000 rubles.



Maybe someone does not know, but we make Evernote T-shirts . We carry them ourselves, we give to friends and offer to users. In general, their popularity is constantly growing, and we have matured the idea to update the existing range and offer our fans a T-shirt with a new image. However, instead of once again asking our own employees to strain their imagination and give something out, we decided to approach the process more creatively.

Evernote is used by more than seven million people, among them there are many programmers, businessmen, journalists, photographers and, of course, designers. Fortunately, our service opens up a lot of new possibilities for creative people. So we thought, why not invite our users to make a design for a new Evernote T-shirt?

In general, dear designers, artists and any potential that feels like to participate in such a project, we are announcing the ‚Äú100,000 T-shirt‚Äù contest !

The competition will be held from March 14 to April 14 inclusive.

It is required to come up with, portray and send us a print layout for a T-shirt promoting Evernote service. Keywords: memory, information, brain, elephant, note, idea, productivity, synchronization, storage.

These can be references to some phenomena of culture, cinema, art, design, etc., but the idea displayed on a T-shirt should be universal and understandable to both Russian and American, both Japanese and French. The winning t-shirt will be sold through our online store worldwide. The Evernote logo in the design can be used as you please.

We really want to see unusual, creative ideas that will catch the eye of others, to provoke the thinking process. We want anyone who sees an Evernote T-shirt from someone would want to have the same one.

The winner, who sent the most interesting, beautiful, unusual, in general, the most outstanding layout, will receive 100,000 rubles from Evernote!

Also, the winner's name will be immortalized in the Evernote Gallery , namely on the T-shirt order page, as well as on the T-shirt itself. And by the way, this T-shirt will be sold worldwide.

Layouts of the winner and selected participants will also be published on April 15 on the competition website . In the same place you can familiarize with requirements to sent breadboard models. And please do not forget to read the official conditions of the competition.

We look forward to your layouts on the site !

We wish you good luck and creative success!
