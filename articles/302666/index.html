Google Developers Agency Pro: certification for the best developers of Android applications

Hi, Habr! In December 2015, we shared our plans to launch a unique program for agencies and studios specializing in the development of custom mobile applications. If you have forgotten everything: in the framework of the Agency Program, our team collaborates with specialized agencies and studios around the world, helping them to create convenient and high-quality applications. For companies participating in the program, we offer special training at local events and sessions at the Google Hangouts, case material and early access to products for developers before their official release. In addition, they receive priority support from our product and development specialists.

Over the past few months, hundreds of agencies and studios have shown great interest in the program: we have successfully launched it in several countries, including Russia, the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, India and Indonesia.

During the program, we managed to work with a variety of agencies. Now we are introducing certification for those partners who have successfully completed mandatory training and have demonstrated excellent skills in creating Android applications on our platforms. We expect that our program will simplify for customers the process of finding agencies and studios that they can contact to develop a mobile application. In addition, we hope that the program will give impetus to the development of the market for customized development of mobile applications.

A list of the first certified agencies and studios is available here . Join the Agency Program Community, go through the training program and get certificates.



We are very pleased that already 8 agencies from Russia are taking part in the program, and 3 of them even managed to get certification - Redmadrobot, CleverPumpkin, Surf Studio. By the way, the guys from CleverPumpkin have created an application for Habrahabr . ;) So you have something to strive for, this is a list of the best of the best. Dare!
