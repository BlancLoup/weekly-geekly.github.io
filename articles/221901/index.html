The creators of the free laptop Novena raise funds for the project



The funds are going to start mass production of a laptop with open hardware and software, which was called Novena. This project has repeatedly appeared on the pages of Habr, and last year it was described in some detail .

For a long time, the fate of the project was unclear, but now there is a fundraising for the launch of Novena production , and only need 250 thousand dollars. By the way, already collected 235 thousand dollars, and until the end of the fundraising another 13 days. In general, the likelihood that funds will be collected - almost 100%.

Novena is based on the 1.2 gigahertz quad-core ARM processor and Xilinx FPGA . But a special Linux distribution for this laptop.
