Twitter will open its own data center


Recently, Twitter users have observed that the social network could not cope with the workload and experienced reliability problems. This was partly due to the holding of the World Cup in South Africa, partly due to the rapid growth in recent times - more than 300,000 new accounts have recently been created. This pushed the company to create its own data warehouse in Salt Lake City, USA.
