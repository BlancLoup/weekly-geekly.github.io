However, taking care of the safety of their users, developers are beginning to take the first steps in this direction - for example, Apple in the fifth version of iOS for the Japanese market introduces a unique feature, Earthquake Alert.


Trying to prevent the consequences of strong aftershocks, Japan, like most other countries, has to deal with earthquakes, tracking the situation with the help of an extensive network of complex seismological sensors located throughout the country. Information about abnormal seismic activity is open, but, as is often the case, it does not always reach the consumer on time. Now, by enabling the appropriate option, owners of iOS5 devices in Japan will be able to quickly receive notifications about possible danger.

The technology of push-notifications, which has received widespread in recent years, allows background applications to attract attention and is excellent for alerting in the event of an emergency situation.
