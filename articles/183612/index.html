Webmoney restores work in Ukraine, but with restrictions for users



Today there was an interesting news - the fact is that the company Webmoney today is restoring its work in Ukraine, which was announced earlier. It seems that the authorities nevertheless went to meet this payment system (if you can call it "went to meet"), introducing certain restrictions that Ukrainian Webmoney users, who still withdraw / withdraw money in hryvnias, obviously do not like.

The fact is that it will be possible to withdraw no more than 4 thousand hryvnia per month, and, as far as you can understand, all this will be under the control of the authorities. In any case, it is planned to introduce some special cards with a face value of 500 UAH, which can be used in Ukraine, but not more often than 8 times a month.
