The essence of the story with Volkswagen lies in the fact that journalists have become aware of reliable information that the automaker has underestimated emissions. The scale of this scandal is difficult to overestimate - such stories can become a point of no return for any company. Let's try to analyze the situation.

Special software


An investigation by journalists revealed that Volkswagen had developed a special software that regulated emissions while working on a test bench. Outside the test complex, emissions could be several times higher than the norm.

The fact of the work of the special software was revealed by experts of the International Council on Clean Transport (ICCT). They themselves checked the cars with diesel, but the result surprised them. Diesel engines, which for a long time were considered as a more environmentally friendly option, turned out to be more harmful.

The burning scandal forced the company to recognize the fact of installing a special software, which lowered the level of emissions during the official tests. The company itself said that the problem may be characteristic of more than 11 million cars that were sold worldwide.

Response


Volkswagen said it was ready to solve this situation and sends more than 7 billion euros to fix a technological problem. It is not yet known what exactly will happen - the reprogramming of engines or the installation of an additional air purification system for each car. Both options will cost. At the same time, it will be necessary to review all the processes for the production of new cars.
