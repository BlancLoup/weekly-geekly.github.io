Dell is changing the direction of the company



Now many large companies are radically changing the direction of their work. Many refuse to manufacture computers, printers, laptops and other devices, moving to the creation of specialized software and cloud services. Until recently, HP could be considered the most prominent representative of this class of companies. Now Dell has officially announced its intention to stop being just a computer company and become an IT company.

About the change of the company's development strategy, said one of the leaders of the Brad Anderson Dell. It is noteworthy that this statement was made during the presentation of new models of servers and other devices. True, the company changes the direction of development is not as dramatic as HP. Last completely abandoned the creation of computers and devices that were released earlier, engaging in other areas of activity.

Dell is going to make the production of computers and equipment in general one of the areas of work of the company, and not the fact that this will be the main direction. In exchange, a lot of work will be done to change the course for the development of various kinds of software, as mentioned above. Perhaps this is the right decision, as analysts have long been saying that the PC and laptop market, which is familiar to us all, is changing, and is not particularly predictable.

Now Dell has a place and time to maneuver, because the past year was very successful for the company - it earned about 33% more than last year. And the most profitable was the unit engaged in the creation of products (in particular, software) for corporations and large companies. If you put the accents right now, Dell will be able to earn even more this year, analysts say.
