Radio Tile Slim




Small things are often lost, and not only in a taxi or cafe, but at home. In this case, they can be detected relatively quickly. But if such a thing (wallet, keys) is lost in a public place, it will be extremely difficult or impossible to find it.

In such cases, you can use search tags. One of the most popular on Amazon Launchpad is the Tile Slim label. This miniature device with a thin body, the thickness of which does not exceed two credit cards folded together. If in figures, then its thickness is only 2 mm. Size - 5 * 5 centimeters.

Due to its size, the label can be put in a bag, purse, pocket or attached to the keys. It can also be attached to any device or item of the appropriate size. The label is perceived by others as part of the design of the device, so that it does not catch the eye. In its center - a button to search for things.

With RFID comes an application for your phone (Android, iOS). It shows the signal from the mark in the event that it is within a radius of 30 meters. You can configure the system so that the application will give a signal if you move a certain distance from the same wallet (forget it in the cafe).

All data is transmitted via Bluetooth LTE. The built-in battery tag lasts for 1 year of work, after which it can be changed to a new one in the reTile program.

Droid Sphero BB-8 Droid




This toy robot is a smaller copy of the droid from the seventh episode of "Star Wars". The developers positioned this system as a toy for fans of the Star Wars universe, but the robot turned out so successful that ordinary users, who are not film fans at all, began to buy it.

BB-8 is able to independently study the terrain on which it rolls, to avoid obstacles, to memorize the objects that stand out, and to follow the specific route that the owner has laid. If you wish, you can set the patrol mode, and the droid will explore all corners of the apartment or office.

The robot is equipped with a memory where you can download, for example, a video message by sending it to the addressee. To perform this function, as well as all the others, you need to use the application on your smartphone or tablet. The robot is not afraid of water, it can be rolled on a wet surface.

The robot listens to voice commands, and with the help of the phone, you can use augmented reality. In it, the robot projects various images onto any surface - this is exactly what he did in the film.
