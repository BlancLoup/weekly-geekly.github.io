Eleven - automatic speech to text service

Hey. We, Boris and Polina, will tell about our project Eleven.

We are developing Eleven - an automatic speech-to-text translation service and a plugin for Skype. The service contacts Skype during a call, processes the voice conversation and returns to the user in text form within 5-7 seconds. The text log is divided into familiar blocks: your words and the words of your interlocutor. Communication with Skype is needed to determine the beginning and end of a conversation.

How it works


Everything is very simple: we cut your voice and send it to the server via HTTP. Back we get the answer and save it in the cloud. The plugin itself connects to Skype and tracks the beginning and end of the conversation. But we think it's better to see once.
