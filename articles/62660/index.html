Jabber on your domain

I registered myself a domain. And, of course, I wanted to screw mail, jabber, blog there ... Google Apps does an excellent job with mail. I tried to use all its services, but I came across a nuisance that Google Talk is familiar to many - the lack of meta contacts, bookmarks, imputed user information and, of course, sending status every five minutes from my account. A little googling, I learned about several Russian hosting companies that currently provide Jabber when they rent a virtual hosting, or as a separate service. Separately, Jabber was possible to order only on hc.ru, while it is still free of charge and it is unlikely that in future the price will exceed 50-100 rubles per month, and the geographical location of the servers provides a faster connection than Google. Details about registration under the cut.
1) - To begin, register on hc.ru
2) - Next, go to hc.ru/ru/services/jabber and click "Order".
3) - We see this picture:

Select the service validity period, enter the name of the first account, click "Next".
4) - It turns out the following:

Click "select domain", we see the following:

5) - Put a tick in front of "Use a previously registered domain", click "Next"
6) - Enter your domain name:

7) - Pay attention to 2 lines:
_xmpp-server._tcp SRV 0 0 5269 jb1.hc.ru.
_xmpp-client._tcp SRV 0 0 5222 jb1.hc.ru.
This you have to add to the SRV in the admin panel of your domain (specifically, I registered on webnames.ru , in the same place I conduct all operations with the domain). I will tell about it in more detail:

Something like this you should add SRV records, that is, service - _xmpp-client._tcp, priority - 0, weight - leave empty, the default is 0, port - 5222, target - jb1.hc.ru. (yes, with a point on the end).
8) - Click "Next" and see our order:

Feel free to click "Order"
Well, actually, that's all :) Now you will receive messages to the email box:
- about ordering;
- that, the username jabber server is activated

Now it only remains to wait when all new SRV records are updated and when your server is activated. To know for sure, just try to open your server in a jabber client. If he was found, it means that he is already acting and you can start using it. I registered at night, at 2:00 Moscow time, and in the morning everything was working :) While this service is not suitable for corporate solutions - there is a limit of 10 jid on 1 domain. But if for myself and the family, then this solution is just right.
