Dodgeball creators leave Google



The creators of the Dodgeball service leave Google. According to them , they are dissatisfied with the development of the service, more precisely - its almost complete absence.

As an example of the slow development of the mobile service, we can cite the fact that the first change Google introduced on Dodgeball was the use of Google accounts for authorization on the site. All anything, but only it happened a year and a half after its purchase.
