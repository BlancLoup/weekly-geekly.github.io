CMS systems for highly visited projects. Are there any ?!

Greetings to all. I do not know where exactly with such a question - hardly in the commentary to a topic. :) If not there, then please send me in the right direction. :) I'll get to the point right away - I have a project dedicated to electronic music - DanceGalaxy.net , now there is only a forum. About 7000+ hosts. I would have long wanted to make a full-fledged portal with enhanced functionality and attract a new audience. According to the functionality, a lot of things are planned. That would not list all, etc. - let's take roughly the same Habr + galleries, billboard and all sorts of social things.

Based on the situation - it is clearly not a decision to buy some kind of Bitrix. :)

Actually the question is whether there are similar CMS on the market? Or maybe someone from reading this post has a large project and you can agree on the purchase and support of this engine for the project ?!

I understand that you can write with 0. But why, if there are already a bunch of high-load, time-tested projects created! In principle, there is already a good CMS with basic modules, but still there is still programming and programming. I would like to consider other options.

I would appreciate any information.

UPD1 : Friends, 7000 hosts mean current attendance. If the project will be given a new start with a new engine, then attendance will increase significantly. Therefore, we consider such attendance as the starting one.

UPD2 : And yet I would like to concentrate more on CMS, which are somehow focused on the creation of large portals and social networks and I personally would not like to consider popular CMS: Bitrix, UMI, DLE, Jumla, Drupal, etc. I am sure there are other alternatives.
