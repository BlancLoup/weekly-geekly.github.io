0. We formulate the problem



I do not know how it is with others, but my formulation of an idea begins with the formulation of a problem. Where there is a problem, she may have a solution for which you can ask for money. Examples of that million, you know.

In my case, and specifically in my city, there is one problem. Every quarter, individual entrepreneurs and organizations need to submit reports. There is only one tax inspection in my city; it serves not only my city, but also several nearby cities. It is clear that in this situation huge queues accumulate in the inspection deadlines.

Of course, reporting can be submitted by mail, as well as electronically. We'll talk about the electronic view, but the mail works even worse, since the local telephone operator now accepts payments only through mail, there the queues just never stop.


Of course, what remains is another fairly simple way to submit reports - in electronic form. But there are some problems here too. First, you need to connect (read - fill out a bunch of forms, install the program) and learn how to use it, as well as to think about reporting forms and electronic uploads. Not everyone is capable of this, so they are standing in line.

1. Problem solving

2. Strokes



2.1. Technical part


So, if we buy the software "Tax Representative", we will be able to submit reports. What is worth? Only 1200 rubles for the connection. Payment order, two days of waiting, one form in electronic form and ... welcome to the club.

Of course, even becoming a representative, it will be quite difficult to persuade a client not to go to the tax office, because the stereotypical thinking is strong enough. This means that you need to be located in the tax office. Hm
