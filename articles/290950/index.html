From site to landing: differences and opportunities

Almost everyone today sells goods or services through the site.


Someone website still performs the function of business cards, "to be." But for other entrepreneurs, the site is an important advertising channel that brings the bulk of applications. Even large developers today spend up to 75% of advertising budgets on the Internet, and only 25% on outdoor advertising, flyers, radio and magazines.



If the site is also important to you for sales, you are surely constantly looking for ways to improve it; what to add to the site to catch more customers and get more calls.

And if you are interested in this, you could not help but hear about the landing pages - Landing Page.

There has been a lot of information about landing pages in the past three three years. They write articles about them, talk at conferences. They promise that landing pages give a much higher conversion (a higher percentage of people who went to the page and called or made another useful action for you).

Therefore, the question for the entrepreneur is relevant: is it worth developing landing pages in addition to the site? When should it be done? Or the site is enough, why spend the extra money?

What are landings for?


For stocks, new or important products that need to be most beneficial to the buyer.



The main task of the landing page is to answer all the user's questions within one page:

  • Customer problem shown
  • Explains how a product or service can solve this problem.
  • Many beautiful product photos or video review
  • Answers to standard customer objections are written in advance to prevent all customer fears.
  • There are certificates, reviews of other clients
