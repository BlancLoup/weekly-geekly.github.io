Project for 7 days

We have been working on one project for more than 2 years. It takes all working and free time. And here we are at the finish line and everything is ready with us, but ... one supplier let us down (a very large and slow company) of the last hardware module, without which there is nowhere. Readiness is expected only in September, October. In this regard, there was some free time. Somewhere a week.

And we decided to pull out from a far dusty shelf a good idea that we simply didn‚Äôt have time for.

Every day we come up with different unique and valuable thoughts, we decide that something needs to be corrected in the code, somewhere to write a note, someone to call, something to buy a thread, etc.

The problem is that such thoughts often come at the most inopportune moment when it is simply not possible to make an entry for yourself, in order to return to it later. And that's all, the thought is gone, but the deed is not done.

To solve this problem, we created a small service. Did you do everything?

This is a very simple to-do list, but with one handy feature:

you can add new positions to it by voice

by a simple call from a mobile phone.

The system converts your messages to text and places them in a to-do list.

Briefly how it works:

You have registered an account Have you done everything? There was an idea. Call the number XXX XX XX. Speak the idea. And that's all. A new entry has been added to your list, and a copy of the text of your idea has been sent to your email. Very simple!

Ps 1. since this service is based on technical solutions of another (main) project, we were able to defend the opportunity to use it for free.
