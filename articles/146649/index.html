It will be equipped with a 1.2 megapixel front camera, NFC technology, as well as 8 and 16GB internal memory to choose from. Screen - 7-inch panel with a resolution of 1280x800. The promised work time is 8 hours. The dimensions of the tablet are 198.5 x 120 x 10.45mm. The tablet is manufactured by Asus.

The tablet will be controlled by the new version of the operating system Android 4.1, codenamed Jelly Bean , which was presented at the same conference.



Price for the younger model - $ 199. Model with 16GB memory will cost $ 250. The start of deliveries is mid-July.



It is assumed that the tablet will become the basis for the consumption of content from the Google Play store. It presents not only programs, but also magazines, books, music and TV shows. If so, then its direct competitor - Amazon Kindle Fire, which marked the beginning of the release of low-cost high-quality tablets running on Android, can safely retire.
