Java Cloud Hosting - autoscaling, easy deploy, environment management


Friends, we continue the closed beta test of the cloud platform for Java applications. The most important business moment in our future development are preliminary agreements with one of the leaders of hosting services in the European market. We found an excellent partner - this is one of the most successful hosting providers in Europe. In addition, our team is preparing to launch a new information site and a new unique cloud functionality. For the convenience of creating environments, we have developed a server topology constructor - a visual builder. You will not find a similar solution even with Amazon Beanstalk.
However, today it is not about that. The specified functionality and the name of our partner will be announced next time. Today, for those who participate in closed beta testing, we offer to try our updated functionality. We took into account your wishes and released an interim update.

In the update, several important points were finalized:

Third level domains


Now you can attach a third-level domain based on * .jelastic.com to your environment. The name of your environment corresponds to the domain.



Thanks to this, you can easily link any second level domain to your environment. This can be done using CNAME.

mydomain.ru. CNAME myapp.lejastic.com.

Multiple contexts within a Tomcat


Often, different applications can work on the same server in different contexts. At the request of our users, we added support for deploying to different contexts.

1. Choose the right environment



2. Specify the context



Deploy procedure in different contexts is very simple and convenient.

Contexts can be deleted and renamed.



Editing Tomcat Configs


If there is a need to edit server configs, this can be done very simply through the configs manager.



When editing, try not to shoot yourself in the foot.
