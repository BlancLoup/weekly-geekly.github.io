Amazon introduced set-top box Amazon Fire TV



Amazon introduced its own set-top box Amazon Fire TV . For $ 99, Amazon offers access to Netflix, Prime Instant Video, Hulu Plus, WatchESPN, 200,000 TV shows and subscription movies.

The console is equipped with a quad-core Qualcomm Krait 300 1.7 Ghz, 2 Gb of memory, discrete graphics and 8 Gb of internal memory. Supports 720p and 1080p video at frame rates up to 60 FPS and Dolby Digital Plus sound up to 7.1. Connect to your TV using an HDMI cable.

The console also offers more than a hundred games, including hits such as Minecraft, The Walking Dead, Monsters University. The console for games is bought separately for $ 39.99.
