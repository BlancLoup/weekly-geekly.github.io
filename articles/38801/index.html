Indie games a-la Sierra-on-Line from early 1990s sample

I rarely pay for software, to say the least. But literally in the last few months I did it three times. And for the game. There is such a person in New York - Dave Gilbert . So this man now, after 15 years, is doing quests in the style of the Sierra-on-Line of the early nineties, I would say. An order of magnitude simpler and shorter, but Gabriel Knight: Sins of the fathers and Conquest of the longbow are remembered. A freely distributed 2D gaming engine is taken, a very nice sad detective script is written, there are sane artists who draw backgrounds and sprites of characters. A cool composer for the last time the game was released, Gilbert found somewhere in the region of Holland.
