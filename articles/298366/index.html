$ 4.5 billion for the idea: Alibaba will raise the estimate of Magic Leap augmented reality startup, despite the lack of a product

Startup Magic Leap is developing augmented reality technology, and this has proven to be a very expensive exercise.

In December, The Verge reported that the project raised $ 827 million. After that, the company's valuation reached $ 3.7 billion. The total investment in the company since its inception at that time was $ 1.4 billion.

In October 2014, Magic Leap already raised $ 542 million from Google and other investors. As a result of this transaction, the current head of Google, Sundar Pichai, joined the company's board of directors. Magic Leap investors, in addition to Google, are the microchip manufacturer Qualcomm and the Legendary film studio. In the same month, the Chinese holding Alibaba Group expressed its intention to invest $ 200 million in the project.

Now TechCrunch, citing its own sources, reports that a startup in the new round has attracted additional investment.

The main investor was Alibaba Group, and its founder Jack Ma will join the board of directors of Magic Leap. Source TechCrunch predicts that after the transaction, the company's valuation will rise from $ 3.7 billion to $ 4.5 billion. The Chinese holding will invest $ 793.5 million in a startup.
