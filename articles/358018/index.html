Roskomnazdor, stop: your methods are useless

On the Internet, someone is wrong

No seriously. On the Internet, someone is wrong. And it seems to me that Roskomnazor is wrong today.

On April 16, 2018, the Tagansky District Court upheld the claim of Roskomnadzor demanding to block Telegrams in Russia. The service did not provide the FSB with encryption keys. Because they do not exist.

And this is a telegram problem?

Exactly. Formally, Telegram did not fulfill the requirement of the current legislation, which was adopted by a legitimate authority. Therefore, I propose to consider this as a separate topic for the proceedings and not to touch it in this post.

What is wrong then?

First, the telegram still works. And I just received a message from my grandmother inviting me to visit.

And secondly, half the Internet does not work. And this is a problem.

Over the previous 3 days, the ILS successfully deployed the Scorched Earth tactic and tried to block:

Amazon | Google | Digital ocean

And the telegram is still working

And it will work. Because the push bypass blocking mechanism is designed in such a way that, in order to pacify it, you first have to block all IPv4, and then all IPv6 addresses.

Or disconnect Russia from the Internet?

Yes. Or force all traffic exchange points to prohibit all packets with a destination address on the other side of the State Border. And this may happen .

What to do?

Declare your dissatisfaction: loud and arrogant to hear!
