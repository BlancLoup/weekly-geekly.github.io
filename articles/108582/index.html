Idea Manager - a tool for searching and selecting ideas.

With the development of the information society, the speed of information exchange is growing rapidly and people have the tools to make the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa collective mind a reality. I will tell you about one of these tools - the service of searching and selecting ideas - today.

One head is good, but many are better

Idea Manager is a service with a fairly simple idea: if, for example, you are the head of a company and you need to solve a certain task, but you don‚Äôt have enough ideas, ask your employees to share them. They are the ones who know more about the company than anyone else and can help solve it.

And to unleash the flywheel of the collective mind 100% - motivate them accordingly. Ideas can and should be worth the money - so share them, rewarding all the ‚Äúgenerators of ideas‚Äù for participating in the process of their shares from the money saved or earned thanks to the solution found.

The service is organized in such a way that it does not take time to study it. Idea Manager is the easiest tool to get started right after registration.

The important tasks of the system include increasing employee involvement in the life of the company. They will not just chase the money, working off their hours, but also feel the opportunity to help her with something more, maximizing their abilities.



How does the Idea Manager work?

There are three levels of participation in the system: managers, members and admins.

The function of managers is to set tasks, determine a budget, and then distribute it between ideas through evaluations.

Setting the task, the manager selects departments (all, some or only his) companies that can participate in its solution, and whose managers, respectively, in the distribution of ratings. For the rest, the task will not be visible. The division of users into departments can be arbitrary and does not necessarily have to coincide with the actual structure of the organization.



When the task is set, the participants begin to offer their ideas, as well as comment and evaluate the ideas of other participants. Each of them has 10 stars, which they distribute according to ideas according to their prospects.



Shortly before the closure of the task, managers evaluate the ideas and comments received, and depending on the assessments, the budget is distributed. Everybody is rewarded - and who proposed ideas, and who commented, and who voted for the best ideas. Due to this, the rating of ideas compiled by the participants really reflects their prospects.



It is important to note that the names of the authors of ideas and comments are unknown to anyone until the publication of the results, which makes the voting process as objective as possible.

Administrators have all the rights of managers, and besides this they have a controlling role. They approve the topic budget, regulate users' rights, department lists, and can also edit topics along with their authors.

What gives Idea Manger

In fact, Idea Manager provides a new level of discussion of various tasks. Meetings are short and few in number, and the task within the system can go several months and hundreds of people can participate in it.

In contrast to internal corporate correspondence, all information is structured, messages are anonymous, a functionality has been created for evaluating received ideas and comments, and a well-thought-out bonus scheme for their authors has been implemented.

As a result, management receives a rated list of ideas on a given topic with comments and additions, the best of which can already be discussed at the meeting.

They say ideas are worth nothing. Many rational proposals remain undervalued. Idea Manager was created just to ensure that ideas can find real, expressed in money, value.
