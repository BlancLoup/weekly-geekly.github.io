Seminar on Industrial 3D Printing: March 1, at the Caliber Technopark



We invite you to the seminar ‚ÄúIndustrial extrusion 3D printing. The most important in 2019 "- the first in 2019 seminar on industrial 3D printing. The seminar will consider new opportunities and prospects for the development of industrial FDM and FDF 3D printing.

The event will take place on March 1 at the Caliber Technopark and will begin at two in the afternoon. Participation is free, prior registration is required, we provide assistance in arranging trips to the seminar.



Topics


  • High-precision 3D printing at temperatures up to 500 degrees, refractory polymers, industrial post-processing. Consider the strengths and weaknesses of different models of equipment, as well as new items in 2019.
  • FGF 3D-printers - 3D printing from granules - a wide range of materials with identical properties of injection molding machines, high performance - up to 10 kg per hour. You will learn about Russian developers and the latest systems of additive manufacturing, their capabilities and limitations, economical 3D printing, which consumes 20-25 times less than with standard FDM technology.
  • 3D printing with robust constructional composites - let's talk in detail about the possibility of creating complex objects from high-temperature composite materials without internal stresses. Also we will tell about the 3D-printing of parts withstanding heavy loads, suitable for automotive, aerospace and defense industry.
