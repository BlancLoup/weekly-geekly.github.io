Half a kilometer rechargeable lantern

Sometimes it happens that you find yourself in a bad place with poor lighting, which is not enough to solve an unforeseen problem. As a rule, only a camera flash built into a smartphone can help you. But all this is very sad, since the flashlight of the smartphone can see the maximum of your shoes or a cigarette butt within a radius of 5 meters. In addition, it happened more than once when in a car garage somewhere in the outback of the city the lights were turned off in the whole cooperative and you stay in complete darkness, but you need to come to a logical conclusion - to repair the car and leave, or just go outside and ask for help. And in general, the street lighting of our beloved country leaves much to be desired, even in large towns and cities.

I also took the SX25L2 XM-L2 Rechargeable Flashlight XM-L2 from Eagletac to help me. This American company produces professional flashlights. Most of which are tactical and used by police officers in their work.



As the name implies, the lantern is rechargeable and you are not afraid that the batteries will die and you will have to look for them somewhere and run somewhere. The flashlight includes the XM-L U2 LED of cool white light and has four levels of brightness in normal mode and 4 in tactical, 4 SOS signals and emergency warnings, as well as 3 stroboscope modes. I will say right away that after three seconds of operation of the stroboscope it becomes bad, therefore this type of lamp work can be used as a certain means of self-defense.

It is worth noting that the lamp is IPX-8 dust and water proof and is waterproof and can work up to 10 hours at a depth of 2 meters.


The packaging of the device is quite practical and compact.



The package includes:

1. The lantern itself.
2. Instruction manual.
3. Two rings for fixing grip.
4. Charging with an American plug.
5. Additional cap to hide the charging point.



It looks like a disassembled flashlight with battery.
