Japanese schoolchildren make hybrid machines from bamboo and cardboard



Japanese schoolchildren under the patronage of Toyota collected an environmentally friendly cardboard-bamboo hybrid buggy. The buggy is made from environmentally friendly materials: bamboo and recycled cardboard.
The students of one of the secondary schools in Tokyo, which is known for its projects in the field of creating "cars" on fuel cells and electric vehicles, constructed this "apparatus". The organization of the circles of eco-engineers school helps experts from auto companies in Japan, in this project, students contributed to the representatives of Toyota.

The hybrid buggy was going to schoolchildren for about half a year.
