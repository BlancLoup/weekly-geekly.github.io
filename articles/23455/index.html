NapTV - A mixture of TV and ... benches.

What only do not reach the perverted minds of inventors. Sometimes they bring things into our lives, without which we no longer think of ourselves in the future. And sometimes - completely mysterious devices that are unlikely to find a buyer.
Which category include the following invention will provide you.
Actually: NapTV - TV + bench
The way the creator intended it is easiest to display in the picture:

When looking at the top - this is the usual bench. But below it is built LCD-display and stereo speakers. What is funny, the authors of the concept offer to spend time not sitting on the bench, but lying under it, watching TV at the same time =)
If you want to watch not one, but with one of your friends - please turn NapTV on its side and get a full-fledged TV.
You may want to take NapTV with you on a picnic, but then do we go to nature to watch TV there?
