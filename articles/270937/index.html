Live broadcast of the MBLT} DEV conference on November 17 at 10:00 (Moscow time)

Dear developers!

November 17 at 10:00 (MSK) we invite you to the free online broadcast of the conference for mobile developers MBLT} DEV , which has been gathering for the third year in a row the leading experts of the country's mobile market.



This year the conference program is divided into 2 tracks: for mobile developers and for managers of IT-companies. Viewers of the online broadcast will be able to attend a round table discussion on mobile eco-systems, hear reports from the best mobile developers and interviews with the most interesting speakers of the conference.

The conference will feature presentations by experts from Microsoft, e-legion, Parallels, Kaspersky Lab, Rambler & Co, and many others. More information about the conference program can be found on the website .
