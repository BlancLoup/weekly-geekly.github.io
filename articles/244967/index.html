The explosive growth of downloads after the release of the iPhone 6, what will be the mobile Internet, the new App Store from Xiaomi - and other news of the week for a mobile developer



The release of iPhone 6+ gave a serious increase in the number of application downloads.


The company Fiksu in its new report announced that the launch of iPhone 6 and iPhone 6+ at the end of September increased application downloads in October by 42%. This conclusion was made on the basis of an analysis of the number of downloads of Top-200 applications on iOS, while non-game applications were not taken into account in this report.

During October, there were 7.8 million downloads of applications from the Top-200 list per day against 5.5 million in September. At the same time, the average installation price in October rose by 21% to $ 1.46.

So the launch of Apple devices allows not only to beat records on sales of devices, but also records on the number of downloads per day!

Opera launches stor for operators


Mobile operators can now create their own stores with a wide choice of applications and games thanks to the Opera Subscription Mobile Store solution. Users of these stores will be able to download an unlimited number of applications for a small weekly fee.

Opera Subscription Mobile Store, which is already used by five operators on three continents, has received an extensive catalog of applications and games, Opera Mobile Store - the third largest application store in the world. Opera Subscription Mobile Store provides operators with games like Minecraft, Angry Birds, Star Wars II, My Talking Tom, Ice Age and more. These applications are available on more than 6,000 mobile phone models on various platforms, such as Android, Java, Symbian and BlackBerry. Opera constantly rotates and updates applications in the store, the catalog of which weekly replenishes thousands of developers.
