In my opinion, this is a very serious, and possibly the main problem for the future development of mankind. If, in engineering and technical terms, we are developing quite tolerably, in scientific and fundamental things are not so good. Despite all our greatness, we are still helpless before cancer, aids, runny nose, I'm not talking about aging. And a similar situation in almost all areas of modern science.
