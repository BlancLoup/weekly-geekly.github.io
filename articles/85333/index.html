Russian Producers Guild welcomes the closure of torrents.ru

The Russian Producers Guild is developing a set of measures to combat the spread of unlicensed content on the Internet, Vice President of the Guild Alexander Kotelevsky told RIA Novosti when commenting on the closure of the domain torrents.ru.
