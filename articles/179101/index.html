Mobile conference # MBLT13 in a week!

Next Wednesday, May 15, the annual mobile international conference # MBLT13 will take place in Moscow.

Digital October will bring together visionaries and practical experts from America, Great Britain, the Netherlands, Italy, Finland, Germany, Singapore and Russia to discuss the future of mobile technology in various business areas.



This year more than 30 invited speakers from leading Russian and foreign companies will take part in the conference: TechCrunch, Foursquare, Singularity University, IBM, Frog Design, TripAdvisor, VentureBeat, YOOSE, QIWI, Sberbank and many others. Alexey Volin, Deputy Minister of Communications and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation, will be a special guest.

The program of the event will be divided into thematic sections - finance, medicine, travel, security, advertising, smart city and entertainment. Reports will alternate with discussions and interviews with leaders of the Russian and Western mobile industry. In parallel with the main program, the Success Story section will be held, where the creators of mobile hits Aviasales, LinguaLeo, uBank, Cyto, Documents will tell their success stories.

Register and come!


Loud announcements, live demo of innovative start-ups and the launch of new products from companies such as EyeEm, uBank, Slon, Instabank, Just.me and Angioscan await guests at the conference .

Owners of devices based on the iOS operating system can download the official mobile app # MBLT13 for their devices for free. It provides detailed information on the schedule, speakers and all registered participants of the conference, as well as a list of key partners and the address of the event with the contacts of the organizers.

Details and program of the event are available on the conference website .
