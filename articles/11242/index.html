Latest on TUT.BY

For the third and I hope the last review of illogical elements in the interface of the site TUT.BY I left the most mysterious for me.

Even the fact that the Voting section has long been engaged only in polls and the editors still have not decided to replace the name can be explained. We overlooked or the supplier refused to maintain the interface; there are many reasons for this. But there are things that are inexplicable to me.

First, the BE link at the top of the page. Probably the link is something related to Belgium? To refer to the Belarusian version always use BEL. Unfortunately, the Belarusian version of the site is actually only the Belarusian interface of the main page. When you switch to any section, the interface automatically switches to Russian. The materials in the Belarusian version are also Russian-speaking. What motivated the authors of this version of the site is unclear.

Second, the link with the number of visits "for yesterday" is located next to the link Advertising, on which you expect to see something related to the number of visits, perhaps monthly attendance or hourly attendance, at least something related to attendance. Instead, two adjacent links lead to the same Internet advertising page.

The third link is the most mysterious. At the bottom of the page there is legal information from which you can find out that the site belongs to Trusted Programs UE, is linked to the website of the Electronic Business Center, although the website of the Trusted Programs company is located at relsoft.by and can be found on the Internet. Why the creators of the site were afraid to put a link to your site? Riddle.
