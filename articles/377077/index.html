Call all BlackBerry Torch fans! BlackBerry boasted the new BlackBerry 10 slider at Mobile World Congress. Although the device does not yet have an official name and the characteristics of the device are not yet accurate, we know that the device will have a double curved display and, of course, a great keyboard.

There was a rumor that this device could be a joint development of BlackBerry and Samsung, but so far no comments have been received on this.
Does anyone remember the BlackBerry Blade ? The phone looks the same if you ask me, but there is something from the Samsung Galaxy S6 edge .
As BlackBerry itself writes, it will be a high-quality, expensive phone, developed in partnership with PorscheDesign , codenamed Keian, in honor of Keian Blundell
