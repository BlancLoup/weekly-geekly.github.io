iPhoneDevCamp - Kiev, July 18, 2009



A real technology revolution unfolded in Europe. The next point of deployment is Kiev .
It is here that on July 18, 2009, the iPhoneDevCamp Ukraine Minibarke will take place, which will bring together programmers for iPhone OS 3.0 from all over the CIS.

All this will take place as informally as the programmer feels and behaves while working on the next user story in the project. There will be no division into steep and not steep, our own and not our own, we all work and deal in the same area and must communicate in order to quickly develop ourselves as a professional and for the development of the industry as a whole.

It is assumed that participants in iPhoneDevCamp Ukraine are developers, project managers, and any other specialists directly involved in the development process for the iPhone platform.

On iPhoneDevCamp Ukraine, speakers will talk about iPhone SDK frameworks, accelerometer development principles, iPhone data storage, popular bugs when developing applications, unit testing when developing for iPhone, push notifications, iPhone software promotion in the appStore, and how important Know and understand Apple's Human Interface Guidelines.

This is the first event in the development of applications for the iPhone on the territory of the CIS and, therefore, it is very important to convey information about it to each interested developer.
