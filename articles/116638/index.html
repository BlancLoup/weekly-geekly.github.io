Canterbury

We are happy to announce the appearance of a new free distribution called Canterbury.
Canterbury is the fruit of the combined efforts of distributive developers, previously known as Debian, Gentoo, Grml, openSUSE and Arch Linux.

Our goal is to create a unified community that can compete with developers of proprietary operating systems and demonstrate the ability of the Free Software community to work together for the common good, instead of increasing division.

Distribution features:

The Canterbury distribution will combine all the best created by the Linux world into a product that will change the software market once and for all for the benefit of end users:

Simple as Arch - simple and at the forefront of technology.
Stable as Debian is completely reliable
As Gentoo as flexible as any needs
Live as Grml - ready to use
Open as openSUSE - Easily accessible to anyone
