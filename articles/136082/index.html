Asus Zenbook UX31 Ultrabook Review


Long thought on the topic of buying a laptop. But always could not find a suitable model. That weight is great, the screen is small, the processor is weak. But a miracle happened and I found out about a new branch of laptops - ultrabooks. And perhaps the best known to represent this family, after the brainchild of Apple, is the Asus Zenbook.
Who is interested in the review of this wonderful device, please roll up. Attention: many photos.


Overview


I chose this device from the criteria of performance, weight and price. Now on the market a lot of ultrabooks. But I chose this one because of the screen resolution, very much 1366x768 is not enough for me, but Asus has 1600x900, which is certainly nice.
Here are the specifications:
  • Model: Intel Core i5 / Core i7
  • RAM: 4096 (DDR3-1333)
  • Data storage: SSD 128/256 GB
  • Video: Intel HD Graphics 3000
  • Diagonal (inches): 13.3
  • Resolution: 1600x900
  • Network: Wi-Fi (b / g / n), Bluetooth
  • Webcam: 0.3 MP
  • Ports HDMI, VGA, USB 2.0, USB 3.0, headphone and microphone jack, SD card reader
  • Battar: Li-Ion
  • Width (cm): 32.5
  • Depth (cm): 22.3
  • Height (cm): 0.3-1.7
  • Weight (kg): 1.3

As we can see from the characteristics, this is a very thin and light device with good characteristics.
What really?
Let's start from the beginning.
The ultrabook box was made very nicely and strictly, it was immediately obvious that there would be something unusual in front of you.

All that was in the box is the ultrabook itself, a charger, a power adapter, a cable tie, documents, a mini VGA-VGA adapter, an Enternet adapter, an ultrabook case and an adapter case.
