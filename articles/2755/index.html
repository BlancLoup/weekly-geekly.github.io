The Chinese have made their iPhone!

The Chinese company Meizu announced the release of the communicator M8, which surprisingly resembles the notorious iPhone.

You can control the communicator using the touch screen. Although it is slightly smaller than the display of the iPhone, but its resolution is more: 720x480 against 320x480.

The Chinese communicator is based on the ARM11 stone with video support (the phone even has a TV-out). The operating system of the device, of course, MacOS X will not be - instead, we will see Win CE 6.0.

This device will be equipped with two cameras in 3 and 0.3 megapixels and a Bluetooth module (it would be better if GPS and Wi-Fi were made instead of cameras).

The device supports GSM and TD-SDMA networks.

The price of the device is not yet known, but it will certainly be cheaper.
