Adobe Acrobat 8.1.2 update - security update

One of the best workflow systems has been updated - Adobe Acrobat Professional. Corrections touched mainly:

- closing some security vulnerabilities
- some corrections related to PDF forms
- adding general stability

It remains to add that this is an incremental update and it is suitable for versions 8.1.1 of Adobe Acrobat Professional or Standard. On the Adobe website, the update is presented in two language versions: the first includes Arabic, English, French and Hebrew versions, and the Central European version - Bulgarian, Croatian, Czech, English, Estonian, Greek, Hungarian, Latvian, Lithuanian, Polish, Romanian , Russian, Slovak, Slovenian, Turkish and Ukrainian.
