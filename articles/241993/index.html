#DevNightMinsk



November 22, 2014 DevNightMinsk will be held. DevNight is an international conference for mobile game and application developers. Last time we met in Kiev. And now DevNight comes to Minsk.

What awaits listeners at DevNightMinsk? From 15:00 until the night we will discuss the most interesting cases and dangerous pitfalls of mobile development.
  • Speakers from the company Wargaming | SOOO Game Stream will tell stories about their combat victories on the fields of mobile battles for convenience and performance.
  • Representatives of Heyworks will slightly open a veil of secrecy over the creation of effective and beautiful art for mobile applications.
  • From the head of the studio Alis Games we will hear an instructive story about how to create your hero in the world of mobile games and not be eaten by the dragon of business.
  • Gamemaker Happymagenta will tell you about the special importance of gameplay design in the smallest mobile games.

Free admission. Pre-registration is required for participation in the conference .
