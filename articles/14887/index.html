Freelance. 10 problems when working with customers. Part 1.

In response to habrahabr.ru/blog/pm/27099.html I want to give a look at the situation from a freelancer.

Over the years working with customers, I have accumulated a small ‚Äúcart‚Äù of problems when working in freelancing. It's time to systematize this knowledge a little. Let's get started

Problem 1. Lack of technical specifications.

Unfortunately, 99% of customers have no technical requirements at all. Therefore, it makes the realization of the final product difficult, and generally speaking, it makes it practically impossible to achieve any originally planned result for the simple reason that in words a client may want one thing, and as a result, either another or a lot of problems will turn out.

The fact that the technical specification is missing is a major headache for the implementer, because there is no way you can later prove that what was needed was exactly what was done.

Solution methods:
offer to write a technical task as a separate service and put it into the project budget

ask the client to return when he has a technical assignment

suggest to describe, more or less formally, in textual form, what is nevertheless required (This is an order of magnitude better than if the task is formulated only orally).


Problem 2. Problems with finances.

The main part of customers for this day in freelancing - individuals. This is a limiting factor in terms of payments - very often the budget of an individual customer is lower than if you did it for a company. And firms have their problems with freelancers - they almost don‚Äôt rotate cash, and a freelancer can only accept payment by bank transfer if it is registered as a private entrepreneur. Therefore, in terms of budgets - there are some limiting factors. Fortunately - the situation is changing for the better - those physical persons who previously entered the market have invested some part of their finances - and now they are earning from their own projects, which makes it possible to attract more finance to develop new, refine / improve old projects.

Solution methods:
Register yourself as a private entrepreneur. But not every company has yet to contact a private entrepreneur - there are nuances with payment.

Search for "spun" individuals, offering them their services.

By the way, it will be said - without prepayment you shouldn‚Äôt work with any client at all. Is that only at the beginning of the freelance path. a) do not want to lose time and money
b) this customer is scam and wants to get a finished job and not pay anything

It is very difficult to communicate with a certain number of clients - it is difficult to find a common language - for example, they do not write correctly, are not clearly expressed, are overly emotional, and sometimes the freelancer is not at all respected, considering him almost his servant if he is paid some amount of money.
