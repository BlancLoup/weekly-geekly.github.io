Three-month internships at Microsoft Research in the summer of 2010

Registration of applications from undergraduate and graduate students for a three-month internship at Microsoft Research for the summer of 2010 is open.


Main advantages:
  • Work in one of Microsoft's research centers: in Redmond, Silicon Valley, or Cambridge (UK).
  • Internship is usually held in the summer, but it is possible to settle for another period.
  • Establishing scientific contacts, working together on interesting modern scientific problems - a list of areas is available on the Microsoft Research website.
  • Enough high wages.

The main selection stage starts on November 20, applications can be submitted all year round.

Program website: http://research.microsoft.com/en-us/jobs/intern/default.aspx .
