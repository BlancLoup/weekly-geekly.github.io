Adobe introduced the future of Flash through an application from AlternativaPlatform

The main flasher event - the Adobe MAX conference - is in full swing. Adobe first introduced the new Flash player, using for its presentation the application MAX Racing, created by the Russian company AlternativaPlatform. Four years of hard work of the team on the Alternativa 3D engine were not in vain.



One of the main components of the new Flash-player is the 3D API, code-named Molehill, which radically changes the view of Flash applications thanks to 3D hardware support.
