(Video) Booth Demonstrations: ZyXEL X8004 Universal IP Telephony Communication System

The video focuses on the ZyXEL X8004 IP telephony system. Historically, telecommunication companies have used (now) outdated analog PBXs. But over time, the modest functionality of such PBX begins to cause inconvenience.

Modern realities require a shift towards VOIP, complex IVR scenarios, tight integration with ERP, which can be fully realized on the basis of the IP PBX of the ZyXEL X8004 system.

X8004 implements in one compact device all the functions of traditional and IP-PBX, contact center and call center, IVR and recording of conversations, fax server, sending mail, SMS and IM messages.

Speaker: Evgeny Velichko, teacher of ZyXEL training center in Ukraine
