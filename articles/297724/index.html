Hackathons

Several times I participated in the hackathons. I think that this is a great experience for any startup, you come up with an idea, implement it, manage the development process. Learn to share responsibilities, delegate authority, and make important decisions on the fly.

It was on the hackathons that I understood the main problem that haunts anyone who decided to start their own business - the team. The success of any undertaking depends on people. We will not take as examples unique in nature enthusiasts, day and night, writing code all alone. The results of their work are ultimately needed only by them and they don‚Äôt go further than their computer. This is a very good myth that you can do something alone. No, you can not.

The main thing is the team. You need not just people, you need people who will do it, who will write code on holidays, during a session, at night, at a temperature, with chills and coughs.

The most valuable and most expensive resource is people. If you have people, you have 80% company.

On one of the hackathons, I met one team, they were real professionals, but I remember one - the captain. It was a man who inspired confidence, after talking with him for 15 minutes - I already understood, these guys - they would take the main prize. A clear understanding of what they do, why and for what. Competent and skillful separation of duties and quick making the right decisions. Unfortunately, I lost contact with them, but I think that these guys are no longer in Russia and are doing something in the Californian garage that is possible in the next 2-3 years will create a furore in the field of IT.

Money problem

Often I hear such a good myth that money is needed. We need investors, we need angels, we need huge amounts of money.

It works and the money is really needed if you are going to open a giraffe farm. Yes, you need pens, you need food, you need vitamins for giraffes, you need at least a male giraffe and at least a female giraffe. One giraffe costs 300 thousand euros. Agree, to buy a pair of giraffes for their own savings - it is very difficult.

But, we are creating an IT company. We do not need a giraffe, we need a computer, a bundle of chips and a garage on the outskirts of Moscow with good internet. Everything.

Agree that you can write your own Google sitting in the garage and rented a 5 thousand server on Amazon with 12 cores on board and it will work. The need for money at first is the biggest myth and world deception, which restrains us and makes us doubt our abilities.

But there is one point where money is needed - this is ADVERTISEMENT.
