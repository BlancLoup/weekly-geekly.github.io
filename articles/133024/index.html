Welcome to Windows Phone 7 Student Camp in Tomsk on November 22

Students of Tomsk, you have a good opportunity to learn how to develop applications for Windows Phone 7 in one day, November 22, 2011. On this day, Windows Phone 7 Student Camp will be held at Tomsk Polytechnic University.



During this day you will be able to:

- listen to introductory reports about how to write programs for the phone. Programming skills in languages ‚Äã‚Äãlike C # or Java will be useful, but no other prior knowledge of programming the phone is required;
- to come up with the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe application, find like-minded people in the team and develop a prototype of the application - with the help of Microsoft experts who will be able to prompt and answer many questions;
- register for free on the Windows Phone 7 Marketplace and place a preliminary version of your application there;
- understand how you can continue to make better money on the distribution of your application.
- find out how to participate in the student competition Imagine Cup and in the application contest Windows Phone 7;
- ask questions about developing for Windows Phone, if you have already tried to do it.

In addition, you can get acquainted with active students from different universities of Tomsk, Novosibirsk and Kemerovo and have a good time in the company of people interested in technology.

To get the most out of the event, you must:


- register in advance . When registering, be sure to include your first name, surname, place of study. The absence of these data may cause a refusal to register - because the event is intended for students.
- tell about the event to your friends and bring them with you - it will be much more fun and productive to work together
- come to the event with a laptop, to work on it right on it! Preinstall Windows Phone Developer Tools on your laptop in advance. You can download them from the Tomsk mirror .
- make sure you are registered with DreamSpark , and register for free on the Windows Phone 7 Marketplace. This can be done on the spot (including getting the code for DreamSpark), but it is better to do it in advance so as not to waste time at the event.
- to come up with a few ideas for useful applications for the mobile platform, on which it will be possible to work at the event. But even if you do not have interesting ideas, come anyway, because in the course of communication, ideas are usually born themselves.

Also, a Vkontakte group has been created for you, where you will be told about events and technologies for students.

We are waiting for you on November 22 in the Main building of TPU, aud. 234 (Tomsk, Lenin Ave., 30) to spend a bright and busy day together!
