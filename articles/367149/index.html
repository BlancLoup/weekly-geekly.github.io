Tell manufacturers what kind of smartphone do we need? Poll

Strangely, manufacturers have almost no public user polls on the subject of which smartphone parameters are more important to them. For some reason they decide for us what is better.

So HTC, for example, decided to abandon versions of smartphones with a diagonal of less than 5 inches, arguing that there is no demand for a mini version. True, they did not try to pump mini-versions with powerful stuffing and equip them with a good camera (however, almost all other manufacturers, too), as Sony did with its Z3 Compact and, in principle, this model became quite popular (at least in Japan).

Therefore, I propose to fill this gap and indicate in the survey that you are closer, maybe they are right?
