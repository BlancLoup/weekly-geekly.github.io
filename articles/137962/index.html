How to buy low customer loyalty?

Should customers spend their money on calls to your organization? We are sure not!

Suppose I want to call the bank and find out about the conditions of servicing my account. Usually it happens like this: I dial the number, wait for the connection for a long time, listen to the melody that was annoying by the fourth minute in poor quality ... All this time the mobile operator has scrupulously charged me of the ruble for the ruble, and the charge of the phone loses a couple of divisions. My irritation grows like a snowball, and by the time the operator finally picks up the phone, I can barely restrain negative emotions.

This situation is usual for many: we all have to make such calls from time to time, not only to banks, but also to providers, to business, tax organizations, etc. The costs of them are sometimes higher than the cost of the problem solved with their help.
