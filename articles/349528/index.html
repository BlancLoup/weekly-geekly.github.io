Telegram-bot "Easy Win" - check the history of the car by VIN or state. number

Recently faced with the need to check the history of the car when buying, examining the market for suppliers of this service, came to a disappointing conclusion.



The essence of the problem


All existing services for checking the history of cars have several common drawbacks:

1) the report arrives in the mail after a long time, and when communicating with the seller, data about the history of the car is needed promptly

2) at the time of inspecting the computer machine is not at hand, so you need to install the application (which also needs to be updated periodically), because using the site from the phone is inconvenient

3) uncomfortable payment methods

4) high price

5) there is less convenient AvInfoBot, but for one-time requests it is extremely unprofitable in financial terms and is only suitable for dealers who buy a monthly subscription for $ 1500

6) services that sell data for relatively adequate money provide little information and force you to enter captcha of various types and several times.

Decision


Not finding a convenient and cheap service to check the history of cars by VIN or state. number, I decided to make my - Telegram-bot "Easy Win", which lacks all of the above disadvantages.

The bot can not yet check the right-hand drive car, but I'm working on it.
