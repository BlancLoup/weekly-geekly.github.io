STALKER optimization

STALKER, in many respects, did not confirm our expectations in terms of graphics and engine. Even three released patches did not lead to a serious improvement (elimination) of the main problems. The main changes patches contribute to the network game, or eliminate errors in the gameplay.

But craftsmen do not stand still: they produce fashion and their own improvements. So, having searched through many forums, I found an interesting way to optimize graphics in STALKER'e and will gladly share it with you.

You do not have to put a patch, write codes, or keep a tambourine nearby. My recipe is as simple as two and two.
