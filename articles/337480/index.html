Hi, Habr! October 27 in Moscow will host the International Conference of Mobile Developers MBLTdev 2017 .

MBLTdev is hardcore reports of Middle + and Senior levels, networking, new acquaintances and total immersion in the world of mobile. Invited experts from the USA, Europe and Russia will share their experience with iOS and Android development.

Under the cut - details about the program and the latest tickets at a bargain price!

CodeLabs
