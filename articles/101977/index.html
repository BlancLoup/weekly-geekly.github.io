The new version of the 3D camera Fuji fell to $ 500



Want to feel like James Cameron? Fuji has released the most affordable device to date for shooting 3D video at home. The compact FinePix Real 3D W3 camera is no different in appearance from the same cameras, but it does full 3D photos at 10 MP and 720p 3D video.

The camera is equipped with 3x-zoom and a large 3.5-inch 3D display, where you can watch three-dimensional video without special glasses.

To experience complete happiness, Fuji also offers a remote 3D printing service: photos can be ordered online. True, the cost of the volume of photographs is still high: as much as $ 7 for each photo of 13x21 cm.

FinePix Real 3D W3 is the second generation Fuji 3D camera, which it released last July. Now it has fallen in price on $ 100.

According to experts, due to the growing popularity of 3D-TVs, three-dimensional video may well become a new HYIP. Lacking only amateur content. Such consumer devices should contribute to the emergence of UGC. Not so long ago, Youtube has already started taking 3D videos to the site.
