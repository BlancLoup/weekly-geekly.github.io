Globex GU5011B Review - MediaTek MT6582 quad-core solution

Last summer, the Chinese giant MediaTek told about its plans to release a new 4-core processor MT6582, which differs from MT6589 in a higher frequency and new video accelerator. The review will discuss Globex GU5011B, a smartphone on a new processor from the Ukrainian manufacturer.



The MT6582 is a quad-core solution from the Chinese manufacturer MediaTek, a 1.3 GHz Cortex ‚Ñ¢ A7 processor and a Mali-400 MP2 video accelerator.
Smartphone Globex GU5011B is a middle class model with quite serious characteristics and its own characteristics in its price segment.

Specifications


The smartphone runs on a 1.3 GHz MediaTek MT6582 quad-core processor, this is the youngest model from the MediaTek 4-core processor family on the ARM Cortex A7, but clearly not inferior to the MT6589, and even in some tests it leaves behind. In this processor, the company decided to use the Mali-400 MP2 video accelerator.
