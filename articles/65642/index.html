Philips is preparing to release a smartphone based on Android

Absolutely unremarkable rumors reached the guys from PConline.com.cn : Philips will release its first smartphone V808 based on Android.


In general, the device corresponds to the fashion of the last time, looks like an iPhone, and nothing remarkable is not distinguished by its technical characteristics among its fellows. Judge for yourself:
  • Dimensions of the device: 115x61x14 mm
  • Display 3.2 inches, resolution 320x240
  • 3.2 megapixel camera, video recording guaranteed
  • and this is all that is known so far ...

It also promises to support GPS and a pair of corresponding accessories in the standard package: maps included, a car holder and a charger for the car.
No photos of the device in the on state yet.
