Google Street View will show interior stores and restaurants.



More than a year ago, I wrote the news that in one of New York's shops, Oh Nuts, a photographer was seen photographing the interior of the store from all angles. Then it was also discovered that this photographer works for Google. The conclusion was made simple - it may well be that Google is preparing an extension for its Google Street View service, planning to show interiors of shops, restaurants and other objects for everyone (it is clear that only those objects whose ‚Äúowners‚Äù agree to "). And you know, because this service is really announced by the corporation, after a year and a few months.

All this was reported by the Komsomol clever and beautiful Marissa Mayer , at the conference Social-Loco. Smiling Marissa Mayer informed the conference participants that the new Google Street View service feature was aimed at business representatives. Large and small businessmen who agree to open the insides of their shops, restaurants and other establishments around the world will receive a good bonus, since any potential buyer will be able to get an idea in advance of what the object where the Google Street View user has gathered.

Demonstration of the interior will be panoramic, the same as the corresponding Google Street View. "Floating" on the street of the next city, it will be possible to "go" at the door of the object cooperating with Google, and look at everything that is there. Thus, lazy people who value their time will be able to choose the most suitable object to visit in a couple of minutes.
