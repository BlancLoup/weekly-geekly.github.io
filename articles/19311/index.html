Paulo Coelho - pirates raise sales.

The well-known Brazilian writer Paulo Coelho, not only does not object to the distribution of his books in the network illegally, but also contributes to this. This distinguished husband has opened a blog.
piratecoelho.wordpress.com - Pirate Coelho, on which he himself places links to torrent trackers or direct links to his books already shared on the net. The writer claims that this practice in 2007 significantly increased the sales of paper versions of his books. Here is a network marketing, in spite of all copyright pseudo-fighters.
