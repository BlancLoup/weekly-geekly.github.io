Officials will switch to free software

December 17, Putin V.V. signed an order "On the plan for the transition of federal executive bodies and federal budget institutions to the use of free software (2011-2015)"

The transition plan includes 3 groups of activities for the transition to free software:
  1. Events in the field of education and development of professional skills of federal civil servants
  2. Technical support measures for the transition of federal executive bodies to the use of free software
  3. Arrangements for the organizational transition of federal executive bodies to the use of free software

Among the activities, in particular, there are such interesting as:
  • Study of possible forms (methods) and prospects of state support for Russian developers participating in international projects to develop free software that can be used for the needs of federal executive bodies
  • Creation and maintenance of a single repository of free software used in federal executive bodies
