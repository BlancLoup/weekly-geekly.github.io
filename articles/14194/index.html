HeadHunter and Digital Sky Technologies have invested in Free-lance.ru

Recruitment agency HeadHunter and Digital Sky Technologies direct investment fund each acquired a 20% stake in the Free-lance.ru project. The importance of this transaction is difficult to overestimate, because the largest freelance project of Runet is now directly through the recruitment agency for large customers. For the site created in 2005, this is a great success.

According to a press release from HeadHunter, negotiations between the partners have been going on for quite a long time, and as a result, an agreement on mutually beneficial cooperation was signed. In the joint plans of the companies, the further development of Free-lance.ru services is planned, as well as the involvement of large companies in the search for remote workers.

Attendance Free-lance.ru is from 15,000 people per day, the database contains about 100,000 freelancers and customers.
