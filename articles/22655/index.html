Free wireless internet from google

Do you suffer from having to pay dearly for wireless Internet? Today, Google announced its new service Google TiSP (BETA) - a free home broadband Internet channel. Google TiSP (BETA) is a new, unique service that Google has developed, apparently thinking seriously about providing Internet services to its users.

TiSP is a full-featured all-in-one tool that allows you to quickly and easily install and configure an Internet channel with a Wi-Fi access point in your home.


How does the Google TiSP work?


Register today and you will receive your TiSP kit, which includes installation instructions, a fiber optic cable, a special spindle, a wireless router and an installation CD.

5. Insert the installation disk and run the installation utility, which will automatically detect all system settings
6. After 60 minutes, the Internet will start coming to you.
7. All right, you're online (don't forget to wash your hands before surfing)
