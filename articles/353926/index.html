Reports


Common mistakes at every stage of CRISP-DM and how to avoid them. Ivan Goose (Avito)



CRISP-DM helps to structure the research process and is very easy to understand and learn. However, the apparent simplicity hides a large number of pitfalls that are not so obvious, and the collision with which often leads to the failure of DM-projects. In his report, Ivan will talk about many years of experience in using CRISP-DM in Avito, the main mistakes that were made and how they were fought. Top 10 errors will be presented, which students could easily remember and use in their work.
