MacBook Pro 15 "Unibody Core i5 inside


Having received the long-awaited new MacBook, the guys from ifixit.com turned it on right away!
Under the cut, a lot of pictures. All clickable.

Actually a copy:
  • 2.54 GHz Intel Core i5 with 3 MB cache
  • 4 GB 1066 MHz DDR3 SDRAM
  • 15.4 "glossy LED backlit screen
  • Intel HD Graphics and NVIDIA GeForce GT 330M with 256 MB GDDR3

Apple uses the model number A1286 for the new MacBook Pro. Well, it is necessary to find a new way to distinguish the new from the previous ones.


Remove the cover. It should be noted that replacing the battery is not a trivial task, for this you will need a screwdriver.


Under the hood is nothing surprising, everything is very similar to previous MacBook Pro. As usual, on the battery is a sticker with a warning - do not remove the battery.


Dumb sticker, you can not stop us!
For some reason, Apple stopped using bolts with a 5-sided asterisk and replaced them with 3-sided. Apple can try as much as you want, we have all the screwdrivers here.
Battery capacity is now 77.5 W / h. What is not so much more than the previous 73W / h. In any case, this is clearly not enough to explain the extra 1-2 hours of work (according to Apple).


One of the main changes in the new model was the Wi-Fi / Bluetooth card. Most of all, it reminds MacBook Unibody.


To disable the AirPort / Bluetooth module, you must first disable the antenna.

Unscrew one bolt that holds the entire module.

Two more bolts to detach the AirPort Express card module.


The AirPort Express card itself is different from previous MacBook Pros. In fact, it is from the plastic version of the MacBook Unibody.


Connectors in the photo, from left to right:
  • Wi-Fi / Bluetooth antenna;
  • Camera wire;
  • Optical drive;
  • Connect speakers;
  • HDD;


Turning off the antenna, you can immediately pull out the optical drive.


The new MacBook Pro has all the same Seagate drives as before.


The speakers and subwoofer are fixed on 4 bolts.


Apple slightly changed the design of the audio system. Now the speakers and the subwoofer are in separate acoustic spaces connected by plugs.



Since the AirPort / Bluetooth card is now closed in a metal box, Apple has added an antenna that is located in the cut-out frame for laser discs. Indeed, a smart decision!


After removing two coolers and disconnecting the heap of connectors, the entire motherboard can be removed.



The module of external ports and the second speaker has not changed.


Orange circled graphics chip NVIDIA GeForce GT 330M. The operating system automatically switches to its use, if necessary.
Red marked Intel Core i5 with integrated graphics.
Yellow is Intel BD82HM55 S LGZS Platform Controller Hub. An interesting fact is that there is no radiator on it, apparently, switching graphics is not such a heat-consuming task.




Intel BD82HM55 S LGZS Platform Controller Hub closer. As far as we understand, this chip is a kind of switch between Intel and NVIDIA graphics.


The process of disconnecting the radiator.

And finally, all the insides of a laptop.

In general, nothing particularly interesting in the new Core i5 for iFixit.
