CNET provides detailed information on hacker attacks.

As stated by CNET, the number of hackers is simply amazing. Over the past few months, many computer attacks, network intrusions and information leaks have been committed. Judging from everything, this will happen daily.

CNET has already written about the open season for hackers, and even wrote about those who are behind all this . Now they decided to provide a chronological table of the list of attacks , so that anyone could observe the activities of hackers. Scheduled to constantly update the list.

The list is really quite interesting. According to him, a fairly large number of large organizations were attacked. Such as: Sony, PlayStation, Nintendo, Electronic Arts, Sega, NATO, Apple, Toshiba, Turkish and Danish governments, and many others. Also, hundreds of Gmail users were at risk.
