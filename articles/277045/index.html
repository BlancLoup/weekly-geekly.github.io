We continue the tradition of conducting meetings for WordPress developers in Kharkov, and we invite everyone to WordPress Meetup # 2! As always, our guests are waiting for interesting reports, delicious coffee breaks and a fascinating discussion of news from the world of CMS.

WordPress Meetup # 2


1. How to get a black belt by WordPress?


// Evgeny Kotelnitsky

Eugene will tell you what skills a WordPress Guru (junior, middle, senior) should possess and what growth stages a WP-developer goes through. It will cover all the way from setting up the simplest blog for a friend and small freelancing to participating in large-scale open source projects and building a career in a large company. The report will help beginners to set the right direction for development, and more experienced developers to streamline their thoughts.

2. WordPress Developer Tools


// Nikita Yurov

Nikita will talk about popular services, tools and plugins that modern WordPress developers need in their daily work. The report will help to figure out how to speed up, reduce the cost and ease the development process, as well as get rid of the routine.

3. Block of questions and answers


The third block of the conference will be held in a new format of live communication with the hall: participants will be able to ask questions that will be answered by both speakers and guests from the hall. The authors of the best questions and answers will receive special prizes - annual subscriptions to any IDE from JetBrains.



The meeting will be held on Friday, February 19, 2016, at 19:00.
Check-in is at 18:45.
Venue: hub "Factory", st. Blagoveshchenskaya, 1 (former Karl Marx), Kharkov.
