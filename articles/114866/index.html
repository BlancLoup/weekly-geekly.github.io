Littlecosm: Multiplayer Twitter Client Game



The alpha version of the game Littlecosm , the first of its kind multiplayer twitter client and MMORPG games, has been released.

The game world is inhabited by characters from among the friends on whom you are following on Twitter. The game window displays their latest posts in real time. The characters themselves look like good or bad guys, depending on the content of messages (in Russian does not work).



Day and night in the game space also change in accordance with the settings of the time zone on your twitter.

The essence of the game is to complete quests (although they actually performed themselves after a certain time, the developer has not yet invented any real quests). For each quest, experience is accumulated and give coins, thanks to which there is an increase in levels. Experience points and coins are given simply for keeping the customer open every 5 minutes.

From the game, you can send messages to twitter yourself. They will look like the most common messages, but they also give points for them. The client itself does not spam anything into the tape and the developer promises not to do this in the future.

For coins, you can buy clothes - and other participants in the game will see your character in new clothes.

During alpha testing, the number of participants is limited to 2000 people, at the moment registration is still open. The game is made on HTML5, so it should work on the iPhone and Android.
