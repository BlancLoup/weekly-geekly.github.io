About a time in two weeks I raid the supermarket. For products. It so happened that during the last trip I forgot the list, carefully written by my wife on an A4 sheet of paper in a neat little handwriting. As it turned out, using a mobile phone instead of a piece of paper is much more convenient. That evening I could not fall asleep for a long time, estimating the interface of the future site and reflecting on the existence of services that provide services for sending SMS. And a week later we had a website ...


I do not know how new this idea is - I have not studied the question. It was interesting for me to implement such a task without regard to existing services.

First, it was necessary to find out the possibility of sending SMS from my site - several companies provide such services, they differ in price and quality: only in one of the five I found the necessary combination - sending a newline character to SMS and sending long SMS in one message.

Next, I took the interface. I wanted to make it as convenient as possible to fill the lists, without reloading the pages, with an autocompleted list of previously entered and standard titles.

I also wanted not to burden the user by saving the list, but to keep it in the process of drawing up and give the opportunity to work without registering on the site.

In addition to sending lists by SMS, a printout was naturally needed. I had to break my head over the printout - everything on the sheet should have been compact enough to fit without breaking the sections of the list.
