Top news: 7 Forbes Marketing Trends in 2016

In fast-growing Internet marketing, new players literally annually enter the arena, becoming giants in this sphere and companies that are rapidly winning their place in the sun.
The logical question that arises in any analyst is: how do they do it and is it possible to introduce already proven practices into their business? To overtake competitors dreams of any business owner, regardless of its caliber.




For the coming year is to prepare now. For it is the preparation of the soil that will allow for a further spurt and to snatch prey from the mouth of the "lions". Analysts at Forbes have prepared detailed forecasts, the knowledge from which can be gathered to develop their own business strategy.
  • Video advertising breaks out


This tool is not an invention of this year. And the market giants are known to any experienced marketer. Recall that the largest video hosting service at the moment is YouTube, which also acquired Google back in 2012. One can rightly call Facebook and Bing advertising platforms. Earlier in the articles we already mentioned the micro-moments and the possibilities of pre-roll advertising. What will be the foreshadowed year of 2016 for these channels? Google will contribute to the development of video advertising, launching the latest product on the market, whose name is in-SERP. Why is it profitable? To this channel of promotion there is an impressive coefficient of loyalty of the end user, which makes it payable and in demand. And the release of a new progressive product confirms this data.

In the case of the continued development of the video channel, Google advertising upgrades the possibilities of advertising traffic on YouTube. However, this will become known only after the first quarter of 2016 and the results of further product development.

  • The explosion of popularity of applications due to their indexing


As we know from the latest data, applications also began to be indexed in the Google search engine. It was assumed that it was introduced only temporarily. However, already in 2016 the process of their ranking will be improved and complicated. From here derive their direct visual advantages, a lightweight form of user perception and a virtually unlimited audience. Sites, which we also mentioned earlier, namely, their mobile adaptation will continue to prevail over non-adapted sites, but this is not all. In the near future, the popularity of applications will increase and surpass even the most adapted and user-friendly websites. And everything is quite explicable: this will happen due to their interactivity and even greater clarity on functionality, convenience and accessibility.

Over the course of several years, applications will reach the level that will allow them to become a worthy replacement for applications, but 2016 will be the key in this industry.

Final and unprecedented dominance of mobile devices over stationary PCs
The year 2015 has practically become the key to the development of the mobile sphere and its fundamental specificity. According to Google, mobile traffic has finally exceeded traffic, which is recorded as visits from stationary PCs in more than 10 different countries. We also mentioned that this year was also marked by the update of the Mobilegeddon algorithm, which aims to oust non-optimized and non-adaptive sites from the top search results. At the same time, the search giant completely accepts the presence of only a mobile version of the site, which is also important to have in focus.

So far, this alone is not enough for the data on stationary traffic to finally fall before traffic from mobile devices. But mobile advertising platforms are already receiving huge support from Google. This means a global reorientation of marketing towards mobile devices.   • New milestone in the development of virtual reality


Dozens of different augmented reality devices have already planned out the dates for their releases and mass access to thousands of new users. Undoubtedly, many will be highly specialized, aimed only at one specific industry (for example, only for video games), but many of them will have a common focus. Have you heard of the Oculus Rift? It is likely that at the moment it is the most trendy device at the moment, which, according to the scale of the developed marketing company and PR, is commensurate with the release of the Martian film to the public. The Oculus Rift release is scheduled for the first quarter of 2016, so keep your finger on the pulse. It and many other virtual reality devices will create the conditions for the emergence of a completely new field of online advertising, as they allow to integrate with social media platforms, video channels and even direct messages. In addition, billions of dollars are invested almost every month in the development of augmented reality devices, which predicts their wild popularity in the near future.

Smart gadgets will not compete in scale with augmented reality devices, but their popularity is already gaining momentum. What gives reason to believe about their peak popularity in 2016. There is a well-known entry into the market of another Apple product, namely, the acclaimed Apple Watch, the first-generation smart watch. Following which began to appear and other analogues.

Due to the rapid growth of advertising budgets allocated to Internet marketing tools, it is expected that in 2016 the trend will continue to gain momentum. Higher prices entail a natural decline in demand and decline of companies unable to withstand such shakes.
