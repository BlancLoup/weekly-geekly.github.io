Twietry - write poems together

Hi% habrauser%. I want to tell you about a wonderful project that we worked on at night, and still managed to give birth to something like a beta version. The meaning of the site to the disgrace is simple - to write poems all over the world one by one line. The idea is far from being new, my friend (who, in fact, pushed me to work on this project) played something similar while still at school. Each composed one line, and turned out to be a verse. Interested?
I ask under the cat.



And what is the salt?


All this is written on Ruby on Rails using MongoDB.
For authorization, we use Twitter, hence the name Twietry = twi tter + po etry . Why twitter? Well, it happened.
After authorization, the user is offered 2 options for further actions: either write lines to the already started verses, or take a risk and start your own.

Rules:


Simple as a clear day. Each writes one line, no more than 100 characters (all because after the line is published, the line created flies away to the user's twitter along with a link to the verse). It is advisable to follow the rhyme. That's all the rules.

And where is all this?
