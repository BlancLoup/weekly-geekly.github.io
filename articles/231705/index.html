Blink: Wireless Surveillance System for Home and Office



Different companies have created and are creating surveillance systems for the home / office, and such systems can be very different. Inexpensive and very expensive, professional and amateur, so to speak.

Not so long ago, another surveillance system was announced, the developers of which decided to focus on the simplicity of installation / configuration of the system, as well as on the small size of the cameras / sensors themselves. This system is called Blink.

What can Blink?


The system is equipped with a wireless communication module, a motion detector and a temperature detector. Watching what is happening at home is possible thanks to an HD camera capable of transmitting a good quality image.



If Blink "spotted" the movement, the user is immediately sent a notification to the smartphone. The owner can immediately connect to the device's camera via the Internet, and see what happens at home, maybe the cat runs around, or maybe unexpected guests have visited the house, and it's time to call the police.

When motion is detected, the camera immediately starts recording video, information is stored on Blink servers. In addition, the camera can also broadcast video to the user's mobile device ‚Äî a tablet or smartphone on iOS and Android platforms.

System configuration:


  • HD camera with night vision;
  • Motion Detector;
  • Temperature detector;
  • WiFi module;
  • Built-in microphone.




Several camera modules can be connected to the system, all of them transmit data to the synchronization module (included with the device), and the module itself already analyzes the data and transmits the information to the user's smartphone.



According to the developers, everything works out of the box.



The camera module is powered by a battery, which, according to the developers, is enough for a year of work. Of course, the charge level can always be viewed, information is transmitted to the Blink app for smartphones (iOS and Android).

Price and order


The cost of one set of Blink (camera + sync module) is 69 US dollars. For $ 89 you can get a set of 2 cameras and a synchronization module. For $ 269 you can get 5 cameras and a synchronization module. The maximum set will cost $ 2500 - this is 10 sets with 5 camera modules each (ie 50 cameras and 10 synchronization modules). The device will go on sale in May 2015.

Leave a request for notification when the device is on sale in Russia can be on the site Medgadgets .

There is no license fee for using Blink services . Money is paid only for the device.
