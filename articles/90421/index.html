Withdraw from AdSense to the account of an individual entrepreneur

Yes,% username%, it is possible now!
In short, let me remind you that the problem of many Russian site owners is that they receive money from the AdSense program by mail transfer through the Rapid payment system. But Russian laws , senseless and merciless, allow a somewhat ambiguous interpretation of such operations. If you receive this money and are not registered by an individual entrepreneur, then you may well be attracted under article 171 of the Criminal Code of the Russian Federation ‚ÄúIllegal Entrepreneurship‚Äù, even if you paid 13% of personal income tax. If you are registered as an individual entrepreneur, then for you there will be an article that raises the question of using CCM, since postal orders are cash payments. Thus, it is highly desirable to receive AdSense money not in cash at the post office, but by bank transfer to a bank account. Below I will tell you how to do it.

In accordance with paragraph 46 of the "Rules for the provision of postal services":
46. ( ) ( ) .
Armed with this clause and a postal transfer notification, we arrive at the post office and demand the Application-Directive form for the redirection of the postal transfer. We fill in this form and indicate your bank details as a new address.
We hand over the application to the operator, attach a notice to it. They charged me 20 rubles for the application, maybe in your branch there are a few other rates. We are waiting for the receipt of money in the account.
In my case, the money arrived literally in a couple of days.
Possible problems: mail employees may not know what an Application-Instruction is, what it looks like and what to do with it. In this case, an appeal to the main post office of your city can help. As practice shows, much more competent people work there.
