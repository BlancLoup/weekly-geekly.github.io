IOS 6.1 Video Review



Good night (MSC-time), readers. Very soon you will see dreams, but before going to bed, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with iOS 6.1 by watching this video review.

At the weekend, Apple introduced a new version of iOS 6.1 Beta 5 for developers (which most likely was GM), and today iOS 6.1 has been released to the public. Updates, to be honest, are of little interest to Russia, but not everything can be seen and understood right away. That is why, in the review all the found "chips" of the update, which could come out at number 6.0.3, are described.

As a result of persistent studying of the OS, the following lines came out:

- Support for new LTE networks in 16 countries of the world (including even Africa, but not Russia)
- Increased work autonomy
- Ability to purchase movie tickets through Fandango using Siri (US only)
- iTunes Match users can now download individual songs from iCloud
- A new button to reset the advertising identifier (in the overview tells what it is)
- New player on lockscreen

Phil Schiller, Apple's senior vice president of global marketing, said that iOS 6 is the most popular mobile system available on the market. At the moment, it is used by about 300 million users worldwide. Especially, I want to note the fact that this version of the OS on iPhone 4S / 5, iPad 3rd and 4th generation is still not hacked.
