Your code is decisive

HOUSE.RF conducts its first Hakaton in Voronezh, a programming marathon for solving an innovative task. For the best solution, the team will receive a grant to study or develop their project in the amount of 300,000 rubles. According to the results of Hakaton DOM.RF, the most talented developers are ready to offer cooperation.

Participants will develop an IT solution for one of the tasks:

How can I get advice and complete information about any property on one site?
How can a resident influence the development of the urban environment?
How to track the progress of construction of residential buildings and make
real estate deals just using the app?

Senior students of technical specialties, undergraduates and recent graduates are invited to participate. Hackathon DOM.RF - a real challenge for participants interested in the IT sector, wishing to develop in the industry and work in an innovative company.
In their decisions, participants are invited to use modern technologies: machine learning, augmented reality, blockchain technology. The results will be evaluated not only by the degree of readiness of MVP (minimally viable product), but also by the elegance of the proposed solution and the quality of the code.

Applications are accepted until November 23, 2018. On November 27, participants will be announced on the basis of the examination of applications. Hackathon will be held November 30 - December 2 in Voronezh.

Apply and learn more about the event at the link: clck.ru/EhgHj
