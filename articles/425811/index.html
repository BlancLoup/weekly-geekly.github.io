Another problem with MacBook Pro displays

Apple did not have time to release an extended warranty program for replacing breaking keyboards, as a new, much more serious problem associated with the design flaws of the model appeared on the MacBook Pro 2016 release and newer.



Every time you open and close the laptop, the cable lends itself to slight friction and stress. Over time, the cable is frayed and the display or some of its components, for example, the backlight, simply stop working. Absolutely all laptops are subject to this, but the problem usually manifests itself after many years of use. In the case of the MacBook Pro, based on the available statistics, the problem appears most often after one and a half years of use, when the warranty period has already expired.
