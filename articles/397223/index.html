How will Apple please fans on September 7?




The official characteristics of the new items will be known only during the presentation. At the moment, it is assumed that the updated generation of Apple smartphones will include two models: iPhone 7 and iPhone 7 Plus. Externally, the devices will be similar to their predecessors, including the resolution and screen diagonal.

Important changes are expected in the company's products from Cupertino for the first time: the lack of a 3.5-mm headphone jack and a waterproof case. Instead of two connectors, for audio and for data, the seventh generation of the iPhone will get a single Lightning interface with audio capability. It remains unclear whether it will be possible to purchase an adapter for a classic connector, or if the company will finally force its fans to switch to branded headsets.

Also, devices will get a more efficient processor labeled Apple A10. It is assumed that the change and the amount of internal storage: according to rumors, the company plans to abandon the release of versions with 16 GB and 64 GB of internal memory. In this case, it will be possible to purchase only 3 models: basic, with 32 GB of memory, expanded with 128 GB of storage, and the top model with 256 GB of flash memory.

An interesting decision on the background of a variety of Chinese devices on Android looks like a solution of Cupertino on the memory. It is expected that its volume will be 2 GB for iPhone 7 and 3 GB for iPhone 7 Plus. The resolution of the main camera matrix for the base and Plus models will remain 12 megapixels. But the 5.5-inch model will be equipped with an advanced camera with two modules - one of them will provide optical image stabilization and a wide viewing angle, the second - an optical zoom.
