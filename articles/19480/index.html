The first unofficial prize "The most quoted journalist"

The index of mentioning in the Russian media is an indicator of the authority and popularity of a particular person or company. Journalists, through which famous individuals and companies get on news feeds and ratings, are left out of the game.

The Integrum company found out which journalist is the most quoted and rewarded the winner. The best in 2007 in this unofficial nomination was the financial commentator Jan Art, whose articles are published in the magazines "Profile", "White Strip" and "Career". The top ten also included journalists from Kommersant, Izvestia, Vremya Novostey, and RBC Daily. The company plans to make the presentation of this award annual and official. For now, a database of the best Russian journalists is being created. Thanks to her, every journalist will have the opportunity to see his citation index in the media.
