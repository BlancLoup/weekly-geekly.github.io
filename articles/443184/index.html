From love to control one step



In the previous article ( OK, Google! Are you good or evil? ), Serious disputes related to the ethics of using applications to track the location of other people were developed in the comments.

In this article I will argue in favor of such services and ask to speak, if you do not agree with me.

The first argument. Calm, only calm.

When the child went to school, I panicked, because I was so scared to leave him unattended. And when after a while he already went home with neighbour's children, then I already felt uneasy. Maybe someone will say that I am paranoid or my wife and I have increased anxiety, but using my monitoring program is easier for me, it is really easier for me.

The second argument. Do not get close.

My wife and I trust each other. We are so comfortable - do not reach each other with constant questions where are you, when will you, etc. For these purposes, we use the application between them.

The third argument. Mama.

I have an elderly mother who lives alone, she is 76 years old. She answers the phone from the 3rd time. And sometimes do not get through. Initially, emergency audio and video calls, I came up with to know that everything is fine with her.

These are the reasons that prompted me to create an application.

Try the app! I would be glad to reasoned criticism.

Good luck to everyone, I wish that you and your loved ones did not have to press the panic button in my application!
