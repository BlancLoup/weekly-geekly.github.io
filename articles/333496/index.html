Australian government hints at a possible ban on end-to-end encryption

Last week, Malcolm Turnbull, Australian Prime Minister, stated that the government needed to make sure that there was no criminal activity in its segment of the Network. To this end, expert groups are working on the issue of regulating end-to-end encryption or potential rejection of it within the jurisdiction of Australia.

Malcolm Turnbull said that he has already held consultations with colleagues from the UK and plans to establish cooperation on this issue with a group of other countries.
