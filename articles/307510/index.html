Webinar: acquaintance with Zabbix

The next webinar from the Centos-admin.ru team will be held this Thursday, at 6:00 pm Moscow time. At this webinar, we will talk about the Zabbix monitoring system, describe in detail the installation process, show ways to automate and integrate with other software products using Redmine as an example. At the end of the webinar there will be a short story about troubleshooting, and we will also address the issue of configuring Zabbix for heavy loads. The webinar is designed for system administrators who do not yet have experience with Zabbix.
