The list of games with which I checked the work:
  • NFS Shift - the game has two profiles for this helm with distinction in the pedals

The rest have not had time, a lot of work, so I play in fragments.

Specifications:
  • Force feedback
  • Turn the steering wheel 900 degrees (from stop to stop makes 2.5 turns)
  • 28 cm rubber rim
  • 24-position adjustment knob (red wheel)
  • Gas and brake pedals
  • Sequential shift lever
  • One-piece steering wheel design
  • Customization for various games
  • Optical coding (steering calibrated upon power up)
  • PC compatibility


Keys
Only 21 keys.
Of these (those that are not visible in the picture) - 2 on the back of the steering wheel mounts, 2 on top of them. The central logo key can be used as Horn.
Joystick keys - 4 position switch and 4 standard keys for set-top boxes + PS, Select, Start buttons
The red wheel rotates with clicks, it has no limiters. Used to change the characteristics of the car or the review during the race.

Sensations
Nice steering surface, it is quite large and comfortable to hold.

Feedback
The return pipe is not as rigid as on other steering wheels, so when there is no resistance to it, it turns more easily. But when it works, it works quite noticeably.
In the NFS Shift profile, the steering wheel easily rotates 90 degrees in both directions, after which it rests against the resistance of the return, which shows that the steering wheel is completely turned in the car. If with an effort to twist it further and remove his hands, he himself will return to the nearest extreme position.
In Burnout , there is no ready profile, and probably therefore the steering wheel rotates immediately. But this does not interfere with driving. And when off the ground, the return line is turned off and the steering wheel becomes light. This enhances the feeling of flight and the feeling of landing when the return valve seizes the steering wheel.

About the gearbox to say nothing special. Have not tried yet.

The maximum size of the tabletop, for which you can safely cling the wheel about 3 centimeters. I have it 1 millimeter thicker, so I just made a couple of recesses in the table.

Pedals are like pedals, nothing special, it's a pity that there is no third one to play well with great realism.
One of the reasons why I took the steering wheel is to finally change the automatic box to mechanics in games, so the presence of at least a simple shift lever was mandatory.

Features
I have a lot of devices in the computer, including USB.
After the appearance of the steering wheel. In the morning, when booting the computer, the system fell into a blue screen with a complaint about the food. This problem occurs when the steering wheel is connected to the computer, but not connected to the network.
The steering wheel works without connecting to the outlet, but the pedals and return flow do not work.
