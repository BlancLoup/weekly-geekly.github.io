findings

  1. One HR specialist in the full time mode can process no more than 80 resumes per day.

  2. From sending a resume to an invitation for an interview, it can take more than a week.

  3. A detailed resume is important only at the final stage, too large resume with unnecessary details can get into the trash.

  4. Only a small number of resumes reach the person deciding whether to hire or not, others are eliminated by an HR specialist.

  5. Education and certificates only matter if they are relevant.
