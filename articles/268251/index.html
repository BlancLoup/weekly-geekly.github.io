Welcome to GDG DevFest Voronezh 2015

Hi, Habr, enthusiasts and lovers of Google technology! Most recently, we invited you to GDG DevFest Voronezh 2014. It is time to meet again. We invite you to the main Google-event of the year in Voronezh - the fourth annual GDG DevFest Voronezh conference, which will be held on October 17 in the assembly hall of the VSU.




About GDG DevFest


Conference DevFest Voronezh can rightly be called the annual and one of the most interesting in Russia, if we are talking about Google-technology. For the fourth year in a row, we have gathered hundreds of participants: developers, IT specialists, students of IT departments, teachers, UX and UI designers, and simply Android geeks.

The GDG DevFest conference in Russia gathers only in three cities: Moscow, Voronezh and Omsk.

What will happen in 2015?


This time we will focus on the new visual language Material Design, the new features of Android 6.0 Marshmallow and, of course, we will not forget the still current topic of wearable electronics.
