Fairloris - comment everything, always and everywhere



The main purpose of the project is to give the user a single tool for adding comments to any web page accessible to him. It does not matter whether there are comments on the desired page, whether they are available to the current user or not, he will still be able to leave a comment and read the comments of other users registered in the Fairloris system.

About the idea itself



Often, using this or that web service, reading some article or looking for reviews about the product, I was visited by the thought of some kind of centralized service, where you could read reviews or comments from people or leave yours. Of course, sites with various reviews about anything on the Internet are already quite a few, but they still need to be found, and then you can find information on them about what you are looking for. It would be much easier and more convenient to have the opportunity, being directly on the product page, service or information of interest, to press a button and get a list of comments about it right there, as the saying goes: ‚Äúwithout departing from the cashier‚Äù.

The same applies to the functionality that allows you to leave a review. I do not want to go somewhere to another site, register there and only then have the opportunity to leave a comment. I want to do it right here and now. In this case, registering once, I want to be able to leave a comment everywhere, regardless of what kind of site.

Using cryptocurrency in the project


In any system where the amount of content has exceeded a certain limit, the task of moderating it turns into a very nontrivial, and often practically impossible task. Users of various social networks and other systems with a large amount of user content are initially weakly motivated to generate high-quality content that is useful to other users. The main incentive for them is only the approval or censure of community members, which in most cases is expressed in the evaluation of their content in the form of votes.

Our project to solve this problem involves the use of its own cryptocurrency, which will motivate content creators to improve its quality, and other users to a more thoughtful assessment and even self-moderation.

Speaking in more detail, the project involves the following cryptocurrency applications:

  1. By charging the cryptocurrency, the authors of the comments will be rewarded for every positive vote of other users.
  2. By writing off cryptocurrencies, comment authors will be punished for every negative vote of other users.
  3. Based on the vote for comments, their authors will form a rating, for the amount of which users will either be rewarded or punished on a daily or monthly basis.
  4. Each user of the project can become the initial moderator and moderate the comments of any web-page to which he has access. All comments modeled by him will be checked by moderators at higher levels and, if confirmed, the initial moderator will be rewarded with the project cryptocurrency.

The more users will be in the project and the more actively they will behave, the more project cryptocurrency they will receive, therefore the greater the number of cryptocurrency wallets will be installed on users' computers and the more stable and demand the project cryptocurrency will become.
