Autostart of Loto stratostats in 30 minutes: photo

Google promises in 2016 to bring the number of Project Loon communication stratostats to several hundred pieces and ensure stable access to the Internet via LTE / 4G in the ring around the globe.

Google balloons use that interesting natural phenomenon, that at different heights of 18-27 km in the stratosphere there is a different wind direction, and the wind speed often exceeds 100 km / h, and the flow is quite constant. The vehicles controlled from the Earth change the height in a cunning manner and, thus, are directed in the necessary direction .
