Smart watches that can be useful for both children and their parents



All sorts of smart devices are now very much. A variety of the same "smart-hours" just rolls over. European, American, Chinese manufacturers (oh, these Chinese) every week represent some kind of novelty. Adults who are not strangers to technology and who have children sometimes think about purchasing a similar gadget for their child. And not just for fun, not at all.

The fact is that smart watches can be useful for children, providing them with multimedia features, educational programs and the ability to connect with parents. The device helps the child get comfortable in the world of digital technology. Parents also need these gadgets, since many smart children's watches work as a GPS tracker, which allows you to always be aware of where the child is now. Plus, children's watches are often equipped with an alarm button that sends an SOS signal to pre-registered numbers and email addresses if the child is in trouble.

Elari Fixitime 2


Age group: from 4 to 12 years



This is the second version of the Fixitime smart watch for children. The set of functions can be called standard. These are the SOS button, real-time tracking function and two-way voice communication. There are several voice modules, including GPS / A-GPS / LBS / WiFi. In addition, the developers have equipped the device with a 1-inch color touch screen and added multimedia features.

The device is protected from dust and water according to the IPX5 standard, it is a very important factor for children's watches (if you know what we are talking about :)).

The benefits of hours are not only the ability to contact parents or a color display. There is a built-in alarm clock, speaker, microphone, accelerometer and pedometer. So the child and his parents will always know how actively the child spent her day. If desired, the child can send messages and emoji.

The price of Elari Fixitime 2 is less than the cost of an average smartphone hand. And the probability that the child will lose the watch is much lower than the probability of losing the smartphone.
