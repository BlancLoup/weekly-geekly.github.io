The most interesting at the exhibition Securika Moscow 2019

From March 19 to March 22, the 25th International Exhibition of Security Equipment and Equipment for Safety and Fire Protection Securika Moscow 2019 was held at the Capital Expocenter. Over 400 Russian and foreign companies, including Panasonic, PERCo, Bolid, Dahua, ITV, presented their novelties , Safe Logic, Argus Spectrum, Assa Abloy, Ajax and many others.

So, briefly about the main novelties of the anniversary Securika-2019.

1. Intellectual Atris patrol robot from UBTECH


The Atris patrol robot, whose resemblance to the hero of the cartoon "The Valley" has already been noted by the press more than once, is intended to patrol areas with a heightened terrorist threat. In his homeland, in China, the robot patrols the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region instead of the military, and also keeps order on the streets of Beijing. The face recognition system is able to distinguish potential terrorists from the database from civilians, and, if they are detected, the robot calls into the military. The tracked robot can move along a given route and be controlled manually in real time.

3. Ajax MotionProtect Outdoor Wireless Outdoor Ajax Detector


Ajax MotionProtect Outdoor is a wireless motion sensor with protection against false alarms that can work reliably even at sub-zero temperatures and in conditions of splashes and dust. The intelligent LISA algorithm prevents false notifications. The sensor does not respond to medium-sized animals, accurately identifies a person, has a range of up to 1,700 meters and an anti-masking system that meets the third level of reliability (Grade 3) according to the European standard of security alarms EN 50131-1: 2006. Able to work up to 5 years without replacing the battery.

4. Biometric controller with integrated reader from PERCo


Biometric controller with built-in reader is used in modern access control systems. The new controller from PERCo supports work with fingerprints, and with access cards of HID / EMM and MIFARE formats, and with smartphones. The controller allows you to use several methods for identification in the access control system at once, which allows system users to easily make the transition from access cards to mobile access and biometrics. To enhance access control, you can organize a passage on the map and smartphone simultaneously.



5. Automatic fire extinguishing system based on mini-robots with a system from "EFER"


The fire extinguishing system based on mini-sprinklers with a remote access system was created using innovative developments in the field of sprayed water. The system is able to automatically detect fire, to carry out automatic fire extinguishing of the object, to organize electronic fire supervision with remote control, eliminating the "human factor". According to the developers, the use of such systems in crowded places will avoid the tragedies associated with the incorrect response of the responsible persons in case of an emergency. The system won the first place in the nomination "Means and fire extinguishing systems" of the contest "The Best Innovative Product".
