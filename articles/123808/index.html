Attackers have access to data from users of the Washington Post



Yes, after leaving the virtual battlefield of the hacker grouping LulzSec, hacker attacks on the servers of gaming companies do not seem to be renewed. But at the very end of last month, fans of digging on foreign servers turned their attention to the site of the famous publication The Washington Post. This was announced recently, the newspaper did not advertise the fact of hacking.
