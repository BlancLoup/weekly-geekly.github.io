Gazprom-Media Digital now uses Mail.ru advertising management system

Previously, GPMD worked with the appropriate platform from Yandex. The RB Videomatic technology program, which GPMD has switched to, collects and processes data from social networks Vkontakte and Odnoklassniki, as well as targeting according to socio-demographic data and user interests, Vedomosti reports .
