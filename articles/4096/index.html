Online Storage: Which is Better?

PC World magazine conducted a comparative testing of 17 online file storage services. Convenient and usually free, these services are increasingly used in everyday office work.

The main advantage of online storage is that it is accessible from any computer. You can open your documents from work, from home or from a hotel. Moreover, these documents are also available for your colleagues, that is, with the help of online hosting, comfortable work together on documents is provided. Each of the 17 services tested by PC World magazine has its own characteristics. Some of them are more adapted for individual use, while others are more suitable for sharing files.

Six services specialize in backup storage: IBackup , XDrive , FirstBackup , Data Deposit Box , ElephantDrive and Mozy . Almost all of them are paid (at least a few dollars a month), but have a free trial period. These services work with a special client program and are not well adapted for collaboration. For example, Xdrive requires that other users also register on the site in order to access your files. But the service Xdrive allows broadcast content to mobile phones. Almost all of the above services can back up data from your computer automatically, according to a schedule. For example, every day at 17-30 copy your working folders to a remote server.
