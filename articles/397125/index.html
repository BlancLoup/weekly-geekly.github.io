Unsuccessful launch of Long March 4C

Media reports about the failure of the launch of the Chinese rocket Long March 4C ( Great campaign-4C ). Official news agencies have not yet confirmed the information about the disaster. The rocket was launched on Wednesday from the Taiyuan cosmodrome at 2:55 local time and had to bring the Gaofen-10 satellite to heliosynchronous orbit.

China conducts launches in secrecy, especially in the case of launches in the interests of the military from the Taiyuan cosmodrome, and the imminent launch becomes known after navigational warnings in areas where the exhaust stages have fallen. The launch is announced in the official media after the successful completion of the mission - approximately 40 minutes after the start, but this time it did not happen, and the official media remain silent. US Space Surveillance found no new objects in near-earth orbit, which confirms the first unsuccessful launch of Long March 4C in 2016.
