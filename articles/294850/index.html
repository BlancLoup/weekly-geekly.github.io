goQuests on Kickstarter

Friends, I hasten to inform you that we finally got to kickstarter.


Kickstarter is a global crowdfunding platform for the development of startups. On it you can get support for your project, and those who will financially support it will get good bonuses after launch.



goQuests is a universal quest creation platform. Create puzzles, create tourist routes and share them with friends.

At the moment, we have almost implemented a platform for devices on iOS (iPhone and iPad) and plans to add support for Android and Web. In order for this to be done as quickly and efficiently as possible, we really need your support.

Find out more by following this link: www.kickstarter.com/projects/219870109/goquests-adventure-for-real-life .

Unfortunately, the project description is entirely in English, so if you have questions, you can ask them in the comments to this publication.
