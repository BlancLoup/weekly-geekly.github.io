How to get an American diploma from higher education

The topic of changes in the higher education system is becoming increasingly relevant. There is much talk about online courses as an alternative to university education. One of the main arguments for a traditional university education is the lack of a diploma and real certification after the end of the online course.

In fact, certification can be obtained. And not just a certificate of exam after a certain course, but a very real diploma of an American college after completing a specific program of courses.



I want to note that the information for this post was obtained from the websites of testing centers, reviews on forums and emails with the organizations listed below. But I myself did not try to get an education in this way in practice, and personally I don‚Äôt know anyone who tried and did it.

And further. This method of obtaining education is more suitable for specialties where practical training is not decisive or can be conducted remotely. For example, for IT, business education, marketing, economics, and so on. But not suitable for education in chemistry or biology. Here, without classes in the laboratory can not do.

College credit for what you already know


If you search Google for this phrase you will find several organizations offering to pass exams in many disciplines and get a certificate. This certificate is recognized by most American colleges and you will be credited with a loan for this subject and you don‚Äôt need to learn and pass it again at the college.

Judging by the coverage of subjects and reviews on the Web, 2 organizations can be called the most developed - CLEP and Excelsior College .
Both companies offer to pass exams in a variety of subjects and get a loan for these subjects in almost any college in the United States.

The most interesting. You can pass on this system exams for subjects from a particular program and get a bachelor's degree. This opportunity is offered at Excelsior College. After passing all the exams, you will need to listen to one additional course online specifically from this organization (as I understand it, a special course on the chosen specialty).

You can still pass all the exams in such independent certification centers and go to any college to receive a diploma. But it will be more expensive. And be sure to visit the United States. In any case, saving time and money is substantial.

How to take exams.


The first impression is that the exam must be taken online and skeptics will immediately say that such a system cannot adequately assess the level of knowledge of the applicant, and that there are many ways to deceive the system. But in fact, the system works differently. Such organizations have many certification centers in the United States and around the world.

For example, Excelsior College collaborates with testing centers in Russia and Ukraine. Here you can look for certification authorities . Search according to Moscow, Russia, Kiev, Ukraine and so on.

But there is a condition for passing exams. You need confirmation of the appropriate level of English. For example, IELTS certificate.

Cost of


This way of taking exams and getting loans is not free. But not expensive. At Excelsior College, an attempt to pass one exam costs from $ 95. In CLEP from 80 dollars.
To fulfill the conditions of the program you need to pass about 30 exams. Total - the cost will be from 2500 to 5000 dollars. But if you need to fly to the United States to complete your program, you need to add the cost of the trip. It is quite comparable with the cost of education in a domestic university.

How to prepare for exams


The easiest way is self-education. Usually, such organizations provide materials for training. For example, CLEP sells manuals for $ 20.

But it is possible and an alternative way. These are open online courses which are now a great many. Everyone heard about the courses from Coursera, edX, Saylor.org and so on.

Go to the website MyEducationPath.com to catalog online courses and look for the desired course. We already have more than 10,000 courses in the database from more than 30 providers. You can also use the calendar of courses . Some providers launch courses at a certain time and then close. Others give access to the course at any time.
