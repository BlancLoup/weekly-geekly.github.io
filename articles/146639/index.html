Scholarship at Brainwashing Ruby on Rails and a chance to work on the ocean

Not all employers invest in personnel, and not every developer is able to find money for good training courses on their own. There is a solution - a grant for Brainwashing Rails from the Aviasales project. Send a questionnaire and become one of the 5 fellows, and after the course, go through an interview in Aviasales and fly away to work in Thailand. And say goodbye to the short-sighted employer.



Brainwashing Rails is a two-day intensive course for practicing Ruby on Rails developers aimed at giving developers and team leaders new knowledge, how to apply this knowledge in practice, and also to solve non-trivial, complex tasks of scaling, supporting and developing applications.

The next Brainwashing will be held July 14-15 in Moscow . This time, we have come up with grants for training with the Aviasales project. By qualifying among candidates , you can become one of the five fellows, get into the Brainwashing Rails course for free , and right after the course and short interview you can get a dream job at Aviasales and move on about. Phuket

Let me remind you that Aviasales is an online meta-search engine for airline tickets that finds cheap tickets to all directions of flights. The headquarters (and development office) of the company is located in Phuket, Thailand. If you still doubt whether to go - look at this video, which the guys shot a year and a half ago.



Want to learn new things, become an even cooler specialist and try your hand at Aviasales? Fill out the form and maybe the scholarship will be yours.

Successes!
