Chrome developers become millionaires

Larry Page and Sergey Brin decided to award Chrome browser developers for their outstanding achievements. Programmers will share among themselves the Google Founders Award (a multi-million stock option), which the founders of Google hand at their discretion to the best employees of the company, instantly turning them into millionaires.

The number of active users of Chrome has exceeded 30 million, it is the fastest growing browser on the market and is catching up on the popularity of Apple Safari.

Previously, the Founders Award has already received several development teams, including the creators of Gmail, AdSense for Content, Google Maps, as well as employees of the marketing division and sales department.

According to experts, Google remains one of the most generous companies in Silicon Valley in terms of financial incentives for its employees, despite a slight decrease in new vacancies and even a recent reduction in staff.
