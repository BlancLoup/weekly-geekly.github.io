2007 Open Source CMS Award


Want to support your favorite CMS at the 2007 Open Source CMS Award?

Then we take part in the voting (will last from September 10 to October 26)
on the official site: packtpub.com



On November 1, the three best CMS will be announced and the final winner will be announced, and on November 2, the participants will be awarded (General prize fund: $ 20,000 ).

A bit about the 2006 Open Source CMS Award
The following CMS reached the final five:
  • Drupal
  • e107
  • Joomla
  • Plone
  • Xoops
The final places were distributed as follows (for me, quite unexpectedly):
  1. Joomla!
  2. Drupal
  3. Plone
Joomla and Drupal were on an equal footing, the outcome was decided by the voice of one judge.

For those who want to support Drupal in 2007:
Overall Open Source CMS - vote for Drupal!
Best PHP Open Source CMS - vote for Drupal!
Best Open Source Social Networking CMS - vote for Drupal!
