Enot J101 Tablet: 7 inches for 75 dollars

The tablet was accidentally detected on the network. Price intrigued - at that time was 599 UAH ($ 75), but there were concerns about quality.



Infa on the tablet in the network was not enough, buy it was not so easy. Having written to tech support with the question of why you cannot buy it anywhere, I was told that the first batch was exclusively provided by one of the retail chains in Ukraine (Technopolis) for further retail sales.

Thinking, looking at the characteristics, I decided to buy, because I decided that for $ 100, the risk was small and the risk justified, the tablet turned out to be very, very much for my money.

  • Resolution: 800 x 480 px
  • Processor: TELECHIPS TCC8925 1.2 Cortex A5 ARMv7
  • Video accelerator: Mali 400 MP
  • Operating system: Android 4.0.3
  • RAM: 512MB DDR3
  • Internal memory: 4GB + microSD slot with card support up to 32GB
  • Wireless: Wi-Fi 802.11a / b / g / n
  • Camera: Rear - 2 MP, Front - 0.3 MP
  • Interfaces: Microphone, power connector, Micro USB (HOST), 3.5 jack, MiniHDMI, MicroSD
  • Battery: 2800 mAh 3.7V
  • Body Material: Plastic
  • Dimensions: 190x121x9,5 mm
  • Weight: 290 grams
