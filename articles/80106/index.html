iHome Introduces Portable Audio System for iPod / iPhone - iP49



Last year, iHome introduced the iP1 premium class audio system as part of its new Studio Series lineup. This compact system combines stylish design and good sound. Now the company is expanding the line with more affordable IP2 (worth $ 199) and an iP49 rechargeable battery pack with rechargeable batteries with built-in alarm clock and FM radio for iPhone / iPod.

Like the iP1 and iP2, the iP49 uses the patented Bongiovi Acoustics' Digital Power Station (DPS) technology, as well as an equalizer and full remote control.

IP49 will be released in early spring 2010 for $ 169.
