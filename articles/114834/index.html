Net Applications Browser Market Statistics



Once again, Net Applications, an analyst of various markets and segments related to the Network, published a report on the browser market. This report is quite interesting because it shows a tendency to reduce the popularity of the Firefox browser. Interestingly, the market share of Internet Explorer has ceased to decline, and even some growth has been noted. In the continuation - more detailed information.

So, about Internet Explorer: at the end of February, the market share of this browser increased by 0.08% compared with January, amounting to 56.8%. Perhaps this is a temporary trend, but the decline in popularity of Internet Explorer seems to have stopped. But last year there was a constant reduction in the market share of this browser.
