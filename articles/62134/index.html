Budget Nokia 5530 XpressMusic smartphone

Nokia has officially announced the Nokia 5530 XpressMusic smartphone, equipped with a touch screen and an affordable alternative to the 5800 XpressMusic model.



The novelty is a monoblock without keyboard with a fully touch control, made in the case of stainless steel.

The touch interface of the model allows quick access to 20 selected subscribers (for each of which recent calls and messages are shown), IM messengers and web services Ovi Share, Flickr, Facebook, MySpace, VOX.

The device, working in cellular networks GSM / GPRS / EDGE, is based on the platform S60 5th Edition and is equipped with a 2.9-inch display with a resolution of 640x360 pixels. The 5530 XpressMusic also features a 3.2 megapixel camera with LED flash and autofocus, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 2.0 wireless modules, an accelerometer, a MicroUSB port, and a 3.5 mm headphone jack. The amount of internal memory is small (70 MB), but can be expanded with a microSD card.

Nokia 5530 XpressMusic claimed talk time is 4.9 hours; in standby mode, the device is able to work up to two weeks. Dimensions - 104x49x13 mm, weight - about 107 g.

Nokia 5530 XpressMusic will be available in the third quarter at a price of 199 euros.
