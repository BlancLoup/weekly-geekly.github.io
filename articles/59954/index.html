External HDD Iomega eGo

Iomega has announced the launch of new eGo 2.5-inch portable hard drives. The new line includes models with a capacity from 250 to 500GB.


The devices look nice (the case is made of anodized aluminum, there are three color options - red, blue or silver), connect to a PC via USB 2.0 (they are powered by the same) and are characterized by a data transfer rate of 480 Mbps.

Included with the HDD Iomega provides data backup utilities Iomega QuikProtect and EMC Retrospect Express and antivirus McAfee VirusScan Plus. Also, buyers of devices will be able to access the service MozyHome Online Backup , which provides for the purposes of online data storage 2 GB for free.

Iomega eGo works under Windows and Mac OS (versions from OS X 10.4). HDD cost varies from $ 85 to $ 140.
