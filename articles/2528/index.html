WikiSeek simplifies Wikipedia search

The WikiSeek project presented today is intended to be a search engine that will provide a better search on Wikipedia.

WikiSeek was the first project of the American company SearchMe , established in 2005 and funded by Sequoia Capital. The new search engine indexes only the pages of Wikipedia, as well as those sites to which it refers in the relevant thematic articles. The category cloud located at the top of the search results page will allow you to refine your query. Firefox users can add WikiSeek to the list of search plugins.

The creators of WikiSeek claim that the new search engine indexes pages almost instantly after editing by a particular user.
