Second hacker festival in Paris

Hackers from all over Europe will gather at the end of June (27-30) at the second Hacker Space Festival in Paris.

In the four-day schedule - classes on Metasploit, HostileWRT, FPGA for beginners, a discussion of software patents in Europe, Hadopi and many other topics.

The event will be held on the first in France working hacker site / tmp / lab , which is located in an industrial area on the outskirts of Paris.
