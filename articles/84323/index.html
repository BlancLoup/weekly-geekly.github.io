Startup Tournament Harvest - Preliminary Meeting

Startup tournament Harvest will begin very soon. In the meantime, we offer to participate in the preliminary meeting, which will be held on Wednesday, February 17 at 18.30 in the building of the State University Higher School of Economics on Pokrovsky Boulevard.

Be sure to come if :
  • you plan to introduce your startup - to rehearse in front of a friendly audience
  • you have not yet decided to go to Harvest or not - to meet and ask questions to the organizers
At the meeting, we will talk about how Harvest will be held, get to know each other, and together we will discuss ideas that will be presented on Saturday. For each idea, a small Brainstorm will be conducted to identify strengths and weaknesses, as well as possible improvement options. Experts from among our partners will be invited to the meeting as experts.
If you have a business idea and you want to bring it up for discussion:
  1. tell us which product you offer
  2. tell us which PROBLEM the product solves and how
  3. tell how much money the consumer is ready (if ready) to pay for solving this problem
  4. try to keep within two minutes to leave time for questions
Consider that you are presenting your project to a business angel, only here investments are not made by money, but by the working time of your colleagues. Which is probably even more valuable than just money. Please note that it will not be possible to use the projector.
Sign up for a timepad so we can pre-book a pass for you.
Details about Harvest can be found on the tournament website .
