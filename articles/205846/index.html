FastVPS.ru - company office in St. Petersburg

Hello!

We invite you to a small photo tour of the St. Petersburg office of the hosting company FastVPS





The anniversary year for FastVPS is ending. In 2013, we turned 7 years old.



New Year is coming very soon - so today we have a pre-New Year excursion around the St. Petersburg office of FastVPS (although some photos will be from other seasons)



Indeed, we are always interested in photo reports on how many square meters and kilometers and with what comfort the giants of the IT industry have settled down: Google, Yandex, Facebook and so on. And what landscapes they have outside the window.



Of course, we are much more modest and simpler, but we have tried to create as comfortable conditions for our colleagues as possible.



The St. Petersburg office currently employs more than 25 employees, most of whom are support service specialists. In addition, here is the accounting department of the Russian division, the administrative department, the development department of the billing system.



The office is located at an equidistant distance from the Pionerskaya and Udelnaya metro stations, in a comfortable business center with all amenities.



Like, perhaps, any hoster, we are one of the few companies in the business center working around the clock. If you drive past 27/2 Verbnaya Street at night - look at the windows of the 7th floor - we will definitely work.



St. Petersburg support often consists of "fighters of the invisible front."



Guys and girls (yes, we have beautiful ladies in support!) From technical support are very shy and do not like to be photographed terribly. But not everyone can always get away, although they are carefully hiding behind monitors.



The team in the St. Petersburg office (as, indeed, in the Estonian, which we will discuss later), was selected and continues to be chosen very carefully.



All candidates undergo a rigorous preliminary selection, a personal interview with the head of the department, pass a test task, and in the final - an interview with the general director.



By the way, our corporate tricolor. That is, three flags - Russian, Estonian and corporate.



We sincerely believe that working as a support specialist is not always just the start of a career, it is often a vocation and a permanent profession.



A support is the face of the company, the first line, and we are sure that it is support that is often undeservedly deprived in provider companies attention and care. And, of course, we are trying to fix it.
