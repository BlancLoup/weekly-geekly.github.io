Any business should be efficient. For this, it is necessary to effectively manage resources, including human ones. And an effective resource is primarily a predictable resource.

Therefore, companies resort to the services of freelancers in extreme cases. It is easier for them to take a person to the state, they can do it.

In order to use freelancers effectively, we need the following conditions, which are not there now:

1. Freelancers must learn to manage their time, be able to count the time required to perform a particular task.
2. The freelancer should know how much it costs an hour of work.
3. The level of responsibility should be much higher than now.
4. A system should be built with which the employer could transfer money by wire transfer, and the freelancer could receive it in the way that suits him.
5. It is necessary to learn how to conclude contracts and legally execute documents so that the company can competently report to the state for the money that was transferred to the freelancer.

I think that all these conditions will eventually be fulfilled - and paradise on earth will come. :)
