FAS: Google behaves aggressively



This year, an antitrust lawsuit against Google began - the company was accused of misusing a dominant position in the application market thanks to Google Play. The main reason was the ban on pre-installation of third-party applications for vendors who used Android.

To solve this problem, the FAS offered to get rid of contracts that forbid them to install third-party software in contracts with mobile device vendors, and as a result, the Moscow Arbitration Court took the side of the authorities. Until now, Google has tried to cancel the decision of the FAS, which recognized for the company a violation of the law "On Protection of Competition."

According to official data, the FAS wants to impose a fine of 7%, but even this amount is unlikely to suit Google, exactly as the official admission of violation of the law. This decision can directly affect the company's image in the domestic and foreign markets.
