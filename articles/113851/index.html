All HTC mobile abundance in 4x videos

Today, HTC was an unusual day. At the morning presentation were presented not only 6 new devices, rumors about which have long roamed the Internet, but also a number of interesting services. But let's start in order. Perhaps the brightest novelty was the tablet HTC Flyer , with which the Taiwanese giant debuted in this market.



7-inch tablet has a resolution of 1024 √ó 600, is equipped with a processor with a frequency of 1.5 GHz and ... a stylus, which is called the "pen". The device works on Android 2.3, but, according to the assurances of the company, upgrade to Honeycomb to be. However, we have already heard such promises.

Two new services were presented along with the tablet: HTC Watch (online movie store) and OnLive (cloud gaming platform). Details about them, as well as other comments on today's presentation, see in our interview with Andrei Kormiltsev , the main HTC smartphone in the CIS countries:



Now about the phones. As many as 5 of them were presented. Three of them are the continuation of the 2010 line, which is evident from the names: Desire S , Incredible S and Wildfire S. The devices differ in size and power, while the build quality of all devices remains high.



Incredible is a 4-inch smartphone with an unusual case design, Wildfire is a compact toy with all the features of Android and HTC Sense, Desire is something in between, combining a convenient rounded form factor and powerful hardware stuffing.

But that is not all. Two other mobiles in a sense created a new direction in the smartphone market. The devices with the dance names ChaCha (keyboard maker ) and Salsa (touch monoblock) have a special Facebook button on their bodies.

And she is not just able to launch the corresponding application. Using this button, you can share with your friends any content from your phone, be it a page on the Internet, a musical composition, a photo or something else. "Click on the button, you will get the result," as sang in the song.



500 million users of the most popular social network in the world are obviously very interested in the Taiwanese manufacturer, but the prospects for ChaCha and Salsa in Russia are still vague, because Facebook is in 3-4th place in terms of popularity among our users.
