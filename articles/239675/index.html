New features of IT services in the release of the program Alloy Navigator 7

The quality of work of IT departments of companies plays a significant role in the modern conditions of running a successful business - and this is impossible to disagree with.

At the same time, you should always take into account that users always have the opportunity to choose the best service and put forward such requirements as:
  • uninterrupted operation of technical support services;
  • simple registration and the ability to independently leave requests for the provision of maintenance;
  • fast loading of web applications;
  • safe and error-free processing of transmitted data;
  • the ability to track the progress of processing the left applications and assess the quality of work performed.

And this means that IT service specialists will be in demand for their services only if they are able to fully satisfy customer needs.

And here the task of the service company is to competently organize the work and management of its IT service. How to achieve this?

To ensure a professional approach, we need the appropriate professional tools, in particular, such as, Alloy Navigator, a software package from Alloy Software. I will share my impressions about him today.

And there is a reason. It is being prepared for release and a new release of the version of Alloy Navigator Enterprise 7 will take place very soon .

Let me remind you that Alloy Navigator is a modern software package for managing IT assets and IT services, developed in accordance with the ITIL standard and taking into account the needs of real consumers of IT services.

It is designed to automate business processes and provides a sufficient list of tools to solve the full range of tasks for the provision of IT services.

The high level of flexibility and wide customization options provide this software package with an excellent ability to adapt the entire workflow to the specific needs and priorities of the clients' professional activities of a fairly wide range - from the hotel business, the tourism and entertainment industry to design, production, government and financial enterprises.

I really want to get to the point, and here I suggest a closer look at the functionality of Alloy Navigator 7.

Of course, it is simply impossible to list in full all the possibilities of a large software package in one post. I bring here only the most important and useful, in my opinion, from the point of view of a specialist in the field of IT services.

IT service support


Alloy Navigator 7 provides the means for organizing a rational process of providing IT services, for convenient and accurate management of them, as well as for managing all changes in accordance with the constantly emerging new requirements.



Here, customizable tools for automating workflows and business processes come to the rescue, allowing you to avoid excess manual water and speed up the execution of routine operations.

Due to this, management overhead is reduced, reliable knowledge-sharing processes are established, and the interaction between technical specialists within the company and with external customers is improved.

Service Level Management


This is an integral part of the operational quality control of the service and a means of improving it. About each service provided to external and internal customers, the system allows you to register and store sufficiently detailed information.

Also, to control and improve the quality of the services provided, there is the possibility of flexible adjustment of service levels, including setting up services, functions and processes associated with a particular service.

Change management


Alloy Navigator 7 provides a convenient, customizable mechanism for managing and controlling changes occurring in the IT infrastructure. It helps to significantly increase the transparency and ease of tracking every change. Thus, the risks of adverse effects on the quality of the provision of IT services arising from such changes are minimized.

Asset Management


An important role in managing the work of an IT department is played by managing the full life cycle of the equipment, hardware and software licenses used.

Accounting also applies to procurement contracts, warranty and service contracts, as well as all related tasks and incidents, starting from the moment of acquiring assets until the day they are written off.

This gives a complete picture of the current financial situation of the company, taking into account such significant aspects as, for example, the depreciation of existing assets.

Network inventory


Alloy Navigator Enterprise 7 comes bundled with Alloy Discovery 7, an integrated network inventory tool .

With Alloy Discovery 7, a thorough inventory of all devices on the network is easily made. However, there is no need to install software audit agents or to change the configuration of remote computers.

Alloy Discovery 7 provides a wide range of different audit methods, using which you can get the most complete data on corporate computers, network equipment and installed software, as well as all changes in hardware or software configuration of machines.

Remote auditing is provided: workstations running Windows (starting from Windows 2000 and above), Linux (most common versions, including Debian / Ubuntu, Red Hat / Fedora and Slackware / SUSE) and Mac OS (from 10.3 and above), virtual machines and hypervisors (on Microsoft Hyper-V, VMware ESX, VMware ESXi, Xen, Citrix XenServer platforms), servers and network devices (SNMPv1, SNMPv2c, SNMPv3).

All auditing takes place in real time, and all collected information is regularly synchronized with Alloy Navigator 7 data.

Contact management


For convenient tracking of the sources of requests to the technical support service, the progress of the services provided and the owners of the assets, the system provides access to such important customer data as contact information and a description of their organizations. Contact lists can be imported from external sources or synchronized with Active Directory. All these data are the basis for the effective interaction of specialists, assigning tasks to them and requesting the necessary permissions for execution.

Self-service portal


With it, all customers, using any web browser, have convenient round-the-clock access to technical support and knowledge base. Clients can leave here their applications in self-service mode, track their progress, interact with technical support specialists if necessary, and even evaluate the quality of order execution.

Compatible with most modern devices, including Apple iPad and iPhone, BlackBerry PlayBook, Samsung Galaxy, HTC Desire, etc. It is worth noting that the new version 7 also added support for Windows Phone 8 with Internet Explorer 10 or 11.

Thus, in the Alloy Navigator version 7 system, the self-service portal is equally convenient to use both from stationary computers with wide-resolution monitors and from small portable devices.

Russification of self-service portal

New user licensing model
