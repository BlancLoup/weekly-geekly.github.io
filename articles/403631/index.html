Siberian woman bought a "flash drive" in the online store and went to court

According to the press service of the Novosibirsk regional prosecutor's office, the deputy Barabinsk inter-district prosecutor approved the indictment in a criminal case against 56-year-old Tatyana Belyaeva. A woman is accused of attempting to illegally acquire special technical equipment intended for secretly obtaining information: she has a built-in voice recorder in the USB drive she has acquired on the Ali-Express website.

The examination carried out by the prosecution concluded that the flash drive is classified as special technical equipment intended for secret recordings. A woman ordered it in November 2015 in China, not paying attention to the unusual properties of the purchased flash drive.

On February 5, 2016, officers of the customs post of the Koltsovsk customs of Yekaterinburg, when checking with an X-ray machine, found and seized an international postal item with this device. According to the conclusion of the examination, the digital voice recorder belongs to the category of special technical means intended for covert surveillance, namely, for the implementation of hidden audio recording and storage of information.

According to investigators, Tatyana Belyaeva is accused of committing a crime under part 3 of art. 30, art. 138.1 of the Criminal Code of the Russian Federation (attempted unlawful acquisition of special technical equipment intended for secretly obtaining information). The maximum penalty for committing a crime is 4 years of imprisonment.
