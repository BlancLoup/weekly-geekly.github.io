Life projects after the creator's death

For some reason I remembered today about Anton Skorobogatov . He died on September 29 last year. It was a great loss for me, because while working at Lebrand , he became not only an excellent boss, but also an excellent friend. It was very interesting, what will happen to his favorite project mywordpress.ru , will he fall into the "right" hands ...


And now, eight months have passed, and if you take into account also Anton's illness, then almost a year. Russian wordpress lives and develops. The work on the site is being done by Ivan Kalinin - I don‚Äôt know him, but I hope that he will successfully continue the work of Anton.

It has long been my idea to make a service that will help projects live after the death of the creators. Many have a lot of logins, passwords, access to the database and other "closed" information. What if you make a website where you can order the delivery of letters to the right people in the event of the sudden death of the owner.

For example, I go to the site, enter some information (the same logins, passwords and instructions on what to do with them), indicate the addresses of the recipients. Then I have to go to the site once a day (week, month) and confirm that I did not die (click on which thread to click). If I go on vacation, go to the site, enter the information that I will be back then. And if I went to the site for a certain period, it means everything ... RIP ... It turns out that something like a testament, only more, so to speak, vebdvanolno. The theme, of course, sad, but even worse when the projects (as a memory of the creator) cease to exist.
