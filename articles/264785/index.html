VNXe Storage Family - Small Business Solution at Reasonable Prices



Each company in the process of its growth is faced with the problem of the growth of IT infrastructure.
It is clear that when the whole company has 3-4 PCs and they all stand in the same room, the problem of collaboration and data protection is successfully solved with the help of ordinary USB sticks and external USB-drives.
However, in the process of growth, the organization at one point moves to the next stage of development of the IT infrastructure.
When the number of computers reaches 10-15, it is more convenient to store data on some kind of unified storage, in which the data will be available regardless of whether a particular user PC is turned on or not.
Of course, you can build a similar storage based on a conventional PC with multiple disks and free software for creating a NAS.
However, this causes several problems at once:

1) Such storage has several points of failure at once (for example, a motherboard or a disk controller), which means that at any moment all the work of the company can completely stop.
2) Untested components from different manufacturers do not always get along well in a single solution. This means that unexpected failures can occur at any arbitrary time.
3) In case of failure of the RAID controller, we need to look for exactly the same in order to remove data from disks, which can be problematic 4-5 years after the system is assembled.
4) Support for this decision falls entirely on the shoulders of the sysadmin, and the cost of spare parts - on the company's budget.
Some of these problems are solved in low-cost storage systems from various manufacturers (both eminent and virtually unknown).
However, most of them are still built on a single motherboard per system, and the support leaves much to be desired (especially in terms of time).
A way out of this impasse can be found if you look at storage systems from leading world manufacturers, for example, EMC.
The fact is that in the EMC lineup there is not only storage systems for large companies, but also solutions for those who are just beginning to grow.
These are systems of the VNXe family.
They have all the capabilities of larger EMC storage systems, but they are much cheaper.
Now the main storage systems of this family are VNXe3200 and the VNXe1600 that has just appeared.
Meanwhile, there are still systems of the previous generation on sale: VNXe3150, VNXe3300 and VNXe3100.
By the way, the systems of the previous generation can be bought with a significant discount , which is important for a small company, whose IT budget is not at all rubber.
