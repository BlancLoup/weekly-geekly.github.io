Rest based on Middle-earth



Peter Jackson, the director of "The Lord of the Rings", a true master of his craft. It is necessary to possess remarkable talent in order to make a full-fledged exciting trilogy from a short story that collects full cinema halls. One of the indisputable advantages of Jackson films is the excellent work of the decorators, who create very atmospheric "locations." For example, housing a hobbit. If you are not a fan of techno-style in the interior, then you probably like all these round doors, windows, corridors, a lot of wooden pieces of furniture and the comfort created by all this. Unfortunately, few people can build such a house for themselves for a variety of reasons. But there are still people who are inspired by the hobbit style of housing, trying to recreate it to some degree or another. And - to rent.

Therefore, if you like Peter Jackson's films, then pay attention to our selection of housing in the style of the architecture of Middle-earth (sounds loud, agree). It is much more interesting, going on vacation, to live in one of these places instead of a hotel, and not being afraid to be reputed. In the end, geeks like not only vehicles .

Thailand






The architectural features of Tolkien's imagined hobbit houses make them not very comfortable to translate into reality. However, in this case, the owners were not lazy and built a very convincing in terms of style house. It is located not on the coast, but two hours away from Bangkok. But then it will be much more convenient for you to get acquainted with other areas of Thailand, because not only beaches are interested in this country.

Montana, United States






In the mountains of Montana, in one of the national nature reserves, is located a small residential complex . True, outside this house is more authentic than inside. But if you are tired of the city bustle and noise, then a real dugout in the mountain-hilly terrain, and even with all the benefits of civilization, can be a good option for recreation.

Cornwall, UK






Real tree house . Although not too similar to the dwelling of hobbits, it still looks like it was built somewhere in Middle-earth. The house is located near the coast of the English Channel, in a remote corner of the UK. A real place for a relaxing holiday, preferably without a cell phone and the Internet. By the way, you can get into the house only by a ladder.





California, USA






On the mountainside a small two-story turret was built , which looks like a sentinel outpost. Well, or as a tiny beacon, if you like it more. Although in fact it is a former water tower. Nearby is a small terrace with a magnificent view of the surrounding hills. It is a pity that such houses are not built here in the southern regions, for sure there would be many who want to rest in a similar tower in the mountains of Sochi.





Massachusetts, United States






Studio towers that used to be silo storage, and now turned into interesting housing in a well-kept area. As you have already noticed, all the above houses are located in quite secluded places, in the middle of nature. These towers are no exception, here you also will not find nightclubs and supermarkets. But at your service the beauty of the National Park Birtown (Beartown) and downright medieval entourage of the towers-houses.





Again California, USA




This small mushroom house is located near the coastal resort of Santa Cruz, in a dense forest. You do not have to get a dog for a week, the city is just a few kilometers away. So you can quickly and conveniently get "in civilization", if you suddenly get bored at cafes or shops. Although outwardly the house looks very small, but this is a deceptive impression. And under its strange dome there is a high-grade floor with a double bed.





Granada, Spain






The last accommodation in our selection can not boast a warm wooden lamp interior. This is a house-cave on the outskirts of the city of Granada, lying in the vicinity of the Sierra Nevada Mountains. By the way, this is the least cut off from civilization option on this list, just a few kilometers from home is the campus of the University of Granada. But just a few hundred meters begins a huge forest park, and the house itself is surrounded by a small garden. So this is one of the most gentle ways for a city dweller to touch the exotic of Middle-earth.
