In general, I set out a general idea. On the example of transport and transport systems.
There is such a convenient form of transport - the plane. Fast, convenient, but it has one drawback - the airports are far from each other and to get to a certain point, you still need to go by high-speed train, subway - the transport is not so fast, the distance is less, but it delivers us closer to our goal. But still not straight to her. There is also a bus, trolley buses, trams, minibuses, taxis - each has its own pros and cons. It can be said that not everything ends at them either - there is also an elevator in the building - also a transport. The problem is that each of them functions separately - according to its schedule, with its own rules, with its own characteristics, and this is a huge minus.
In order to get from Deglava street, house 3 in Riga, to Makarova street, house 4 in Moscow, you need to make a long journey on at least 4 types of transport - rates, connection of schedules, etc., etc.
But!
