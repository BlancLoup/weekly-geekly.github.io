Humble Indie Bundle # 4 has started - pay what you want for 5 great indie games



Humble Indie Bundle has launched a new part of its sale of indie games. During this promotion, anyone can purchase 5 games for any cost, and if he pays more than the average payment amount of other participants at the time of purchase, he will receive 2 additional games.

In this promotion are involved:
- Super Meat Boy
- Shank
- NightSky HD
- Bit.Trip Runner
- Jamestown

People who have paid more than the current average amount of payments (at the time of writing the topic is $ 4.58) additionally receive:
- Cave Story +
- Gratuitous Space Battles

Promotional video (in English)




All 7 games are available immediately under the three platforms (Windows, Mac and Linux) and at the same time they do not contain DRM, and can also be added to a Steam account.

You can familiarize yourself with the system requirements of games here: humble.assistly.com/customer/portal/articles/261150-humble-indie-bundle-4-system-reqs

You can pay with Paypal, Amazon Payments, and Google Checkout. At the same time, you can choose how much money the game developers will receive, the organizers of the action, and how much will be donated to American Red Cross and Child's Play organizations for charity. This time, the Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF) is not on the list of organizations where money can be donated to charity, but the Humble Bundle authors promise that in future EFF bundles will definitely return to the number of organizations that can be helped, and this time by a closed vote Bundle game developers instead of EFF involved American Red Cross.

The sale will last another 14 days.

UPD : This bandl beats records. Within an hour of the start of the promotion, the Humble Bundle earned $ 100,000. After a little more than 4 from the start of the sale, they overstepped the mark of 500 thousand US dollars. For comparison, the previous Humble Introversion Bundle for 14 days gained ~ 770 thousand dollars . Humble Indie Bundle # 3 for 2 weeks received 2 million 116 thousand USD, and Humble Voxatron Debut - 900 thousand dollars.
