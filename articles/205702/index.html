A small intermediate result of the year of great work

Hello.
This is my second article on Habr, the first was to penetrate the community. This article will be devoted entirely to the history of business creation in the field of mobile applications. In the article I will share a little experience and tell you what we will do next (and invite you to the team). I must say that the business was done by two people, and it grew from zero to the cost of millions of dollars.



The company, as a business, started with one crazy idea in November 2011. The idea was very simple - why pay users for phone calls and SMS when they can watch ads instead. I will not hide, before that there were a lot of attempts to do this, but for the most part they tried to embed advertising in the conversation or in the application itself. Bad idea ... My grandmother will not understand the pause when I call her, in which she is told to buy powder - she will hang up. In our idea, the user can watch the promotional videos in a completely separate section of the application (or download other applications), get a balance and call where he wants, like from a regular phone.
The idea is good, but who will implement it? I will have to tell a little about myself. I have been involved in various businesses since 1988, and all types of businesses have been connected with telecommunications and the Internet. In 1996, my experience allowed me to quit my job and start a career as a serial entrepreneur (then it was called differently). Behind more than two dozen established businesses in the B2B sector, with an annual turnover of 3-6M USD. One of the brightest businesses is ixc.ua , which is one of the first in the world to sell a billing + softswitch solution for IP telephony.
Starting a new CFC business that works in a relatively new sector for me - B2C for mobile devices, I thought that I did not know programming. We will not write to the Assembler asset on 286 and x86 processors. It was interesting to me not just to do business, it was interesting to write everything from scratch to myself. Half a year was spent studying Obj-C, C, and writing the first, simple client-server application, just for the sake of self-study. A simple recommendation for anyone who follows my example: a book plus the desire to write something, gives far less experience than stackoverflow.com + the desire to write something. From November to April, the CFC application was written. He had one big plus - it really RINGED. And in April 2012, with a sinking heart, the first confirmation was received from the AppStore. At the same time, a second partner entered the project, who started promoting the project site (by the way, we also had a Java version). He believed in the project, did not demand a salary, and together we are developing the project so far.
It is strange to write it, but competitors had obvious things not to go (and even now too). They are obvious because when the client downloads the application, he wants to try the service. Not an abstract test call, he wants to call a friend (relative). We are the only ones who give such an opportunity. Do not ask how much money it costs us, this is a commercial secret.
Perhaps this fact, perhaps others, contributed to the popularity of the project from the very beginning. We spent minimal money on advertising, and users came and came.
