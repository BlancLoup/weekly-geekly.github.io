The company EZ-Robot offers designer robots



In fact, the company EZ-Robot has released a "designer", which allows you to create fairly advanced robots. Yes, you won't get Asimo, but you can get an adequate robotic mechanism capable of reacting to many stimuli. The designer includes several modules, control systems and other components. Its cost is not so high - $ 243.

The kit includes a wireless controller that can be controlled by a robot, then an ultrasonic distance sensor, three servo drives, two electric motors and two wireless cameras. There is also a battery necessary for the work of the created software robots The software included in the kit allows the robot to recognize speech, facial expressions and some gestures.
