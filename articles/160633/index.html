Service of search for components for copters and not only

Collecting a quadcopter ran into a problem: the choice of components is huge, but the search for them is useless.
In my spare time I slowly rivet a search engine for components: www.rcdetails.info
Now the database already contains information about products from hobbyking.com , goodluckbuy and towerhobbies :
  • more than 800 brushless motors;
  • more than 300 internal combustion engines;
  • about 400 regulators for brushless motors;
  • more than 1,300 lithium batteries;
  • nearly 800 servos;
  • almost 1000 screws;




What is implemented:
Search by a bunch of different criteria (max. Current strength, voltage, weight, price, screw pitch, etc.)
Comparison of several results in a pivot table with all known properties


What I plan:
  • Adding information from rctimer.com
