MacOS X 10.7 beta Short Review


Good afternoon, I want to tell you about the first look at MacOS X 10.7. What I saw was very different from what the enthusiasts were saying for the better. At the moment, you can get a disk image in 2 ways: first and with the help of The Pirate Bay. The image weighs 3.59 GB.

Installation


The system was installed on top of Mac OS X 10.6 Snow Leopard. What is most remarkable is that the installation can be done directly from the mounted image, the owners of MB Air without a drive will surely like it. The installation is fully automatic, the installer does not ask any questions. All I did: clicked on "Install" and waited about half an hour. After installing my desktop, bookmarks in the browser, downloads Transmission and even the history of Chrome remained in place.
