The secret of high conversion for online stores

The goal of every online store is to make more money. This is generally and understandably, for this, any commercial project is created. And in order to earn more money, you need to increase sales. Accordingly, in this area of ‚Äã‚Äãonline stores, sales increase is reduced either to an increase in site traffic (usually by increasing advertising budgets) or to improving the effectiveness of advertising already carried out, that is, within the existing budget. The result of improving the effectiveness of advertising will be an increase in the conversion rate of the site at the existing level of attendance. Here, too, everything is clear, we can say that the basics have already been sorted out! :)

Now the question arises - ‚Äúhow can we actually increase the effectiveness of advertising a site?‚Äù Because nobody wants to spend additional funds on advertising, especially considering that its effectiveness in most cases is at a very low level.

There are many different techniques that can improve the effectiveness of advertising on the Internet. On the net you can read a lot of information on this topic. But in any case, in order to track the effectiveness of online advertising, it is necessary to use web analysis. He will help to track all the traffic parameters on the site, help to understand the behavior of visitors, identify its shortcomings and show at what step to purchase a visitor leaves the site or even from a basket full of goods. As an example, you can give one of the most well-known ways to monitor the effectiveness of the site interface - A / B testing. But it is used when there is already a sufficient level of traffic on the site and the site owner understands that any innovation should be tested in conjunction with the existing version of the site.

However, the most effective method, not even the method, but the initial approach to business, is a thorough design of the site interface.

In order for the site interface to really have a positive effect on the effectiveness of advertising, to increase sales and conversion, it is necessary to properly design it initially. This should be done in accordance with the needs of the target audience, while taking into account the paths of visitors through the site, the movement of their gaze and many other factors. You can also read a lot of interesting materials on the topic of proper website design, and thus, you can independently think through the entire interface and structure of your online store.
