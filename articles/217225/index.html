MakeHuman: release after 12 years of development



MakeHuman is free and open source software (distributed under a license of CC0) for creating realistic 3D models of people in illustrations, animations and games. It is possible to export models for Blender, XSI, Max, etc.

Development began in 2001, and the release of the first official version occurred after as much as 12 years. MakeHuman has 1170 different effects of morph modeling: age, gender, height, weight, body and face proportions, eyes, nose, mouth, chin, ears, neck, and more. The program is available on Windows, OSX, Linux. It is worth mentioning that the project is completely written in Python. BitBucket repository.
