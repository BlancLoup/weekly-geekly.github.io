TradeMac.ru Makes Apple More Affordable

Do you have a dream? Stupid question, tell me, how can you live without a dream!
We, the TradeMac team, have set our sights on solving a global problem.

In the age of excess information, it is important to be on the crest of a wave, to use top technologies. It is sad to realize, but many have neither the ability nor the desire to constantly update their equipment, to use something newer. It is even more tragic when the equipment is artificially aged: you have a fairly new look on your hands, but in fact a morally already outdated device. We solve these issues and make Apple more accessible.

Little about the project




About 3 months ago we made the beta version of the site TradeMac.ru .

We collect the maximum amount of Apple-Traffic and give the user the ability to easily select the desired device among their abundance. Everyone can place with us an advertisement for the sale of their favorite Apple-device for free and without registration. For those who want to buy the Best Technique, the technology of Visual Categorization has been implemented, which allows you to choose a product from the pictures, even without knowing its name.



Is it difficult to be a SMM genius? No, you need to get closer to "Freebies" and "Stars".
We put a lot on our group in VK.com . The geniuses of SMM-Marketing told us about several components of success that we want to share:
1) Frequent thematic content
2) Monthly contests for reposts and likes with valuable prizes
3) To be as close as possible to the powerful of this world, and to advertise in the first stages through well-known youtube channels with a target audience.

In the first stages, we recruited moderators into a group of winners.
It was an achievement, because people began to feel their participation and gradually began to fill the group on a disinterested basis.
We are about to give birth to an advertising campaign with one of the well-known video bloggers, and we will wait for the increase in the size of the group a couple of dozen times.
