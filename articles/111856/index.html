SQA Days-9 invites speakers



Colleagues!

We invite you to make an interesting report at the 9th international conference SQA Days (Kazan, April 22-23).

If you are ready to share your experience, knowledge, and feel that your expertise will be of interest to the target audience - send us a request for a speech , we will be glad to cooperate with you.

At the upcoming meeting, you will be able to discuss the problems arising in the workplace and find ways to solve them, having familiarized yourself with the experience of colleagues, or to share your own methods of resolving difficulties. At the conference, both regular speakers and new participants are representatives of Russian and foreign software development companies.

SQA Days is visited by a large number of specialists in quality assurance and control from the CIS and abroad. The previous conference, which took place in November 2010 in St. Petersburg, was attended by about 400 people. A report on the last event and feedback from participants are published here .

At the spring conference will be no less interesting. In addition, it is a good way to personally meet those who know in absentia. At the same time, Alexey Barantsev, Natalya Rukol, Stas Fomin, Alexander Orlov, Alexey Lyanguzov and many, many other interesting speakers will come to the conference.

And among other things, our speakers enjoy the benefits of:
  • free opportunity to listen to fellow speakers, meet interesting people
  • opportunity to declare about the company
  • opportunity to give a 10% discount to colleagues from your company
