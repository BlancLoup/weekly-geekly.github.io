Evernote support on HTC Flyer


We always try to talk about products that are integrated with Evernote. Today's story is a special one, since it will not be about the application, but about the device, the developers of which spent a lot of effort on integrating with Evernote. It's about the tablet HTC Flyer . This pleasant device with a 7-inch touchscreen running Android has a proprietary HTC Sense interface, comes bundled with a pen and HTC Scribe handwriting technology and features easy integration with Evernote right out of the box.
